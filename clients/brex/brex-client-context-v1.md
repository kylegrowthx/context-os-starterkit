## Active Workstreams

1. **Web experience improvements (current primary focus)** — Resources page redesign: navigation, sub-nav, content tagging, hub & spoke architecture, article page consistency, search, tools integration. Georgemaine engineering. Jon pitching to Bango (Head of Design) for final design approval. Prototype live at [brex-hub.onrender.com/resources](https://brex-hub.onrender.com/resources). Design spec: [Notion — Resources Design Spec](https://www.notion.so/growthxlabs/Resources-Design-Spec-3102ba60bc74806b806efdc2b0e90855).

2. **Tools** — 5 tools for /tools hub page: ChargeFinder, Expense Categories, Per Diem Calculator, Invoice Tool, +1 from competitive analysis. Kirkland building. **Currently blocked:** Cloudflare access. Kirkland received a Brex laptop 2/23 and is unblocking final stages of access. Once unblocked, ~1 day per tool. Each tool has custom data requirements being coordinated with the Brex team. Differentiator: fact-checking workflow + PAQ content beats Ramp's hallucination-prone pages.

3. **Experimental lane: Indexes + Templates (biggest untapped opportunity)** — Two initiatives under one lane. No meaningful progress yet — this is the largest growth lever we haven't started executing on. *Indexes:* Ideas: CFO directory for public companies (Tier 1), small business directories by type/location (Tier 2), salary guides (Tier 3). Phased: 50 pages → 500 → 3-5K → prune. *Templates:* Invoice templates = "home run of the century." 600MM search volume. No competitor doing it well. Jon committed to "hundreds of templates" over 2026. Once web experience and tools are unblocked, this lane becomes the priority for scaling organic traffic and leads.

4. **Content engine pilot (new, March 2026)** — Restarting article production as a calibration lane. Yolanda will send 5 article topics with target keywords. GrowthX delivers as Google Docs, Yolanda provides detailed feedback with rationale, we incorporate into system instructions. Goal: minimal-edit articles that can go to legal review. Start at 5/month, scale based on Yolanda's bandwidth. Content will be produced in batches to align with FINRA compliance review cadence. Expansion opportunity: if Yolanda can't keep up with content solo, this lane grows significantly.
