### 4.2 EXPERIMENTAL LANE: INDEX/DIRECTORY STRATEGY (Primary Focus #2)

#### The Ask

We need one lane where Jon can let us **"go do our thing"** without reviewing every page.

> "The path here is tools. He needs to give us at least one experimentation lane... a lane that we can crank over here. If you really care about results fast, that's a way to accelerate results fast."
> — Marcel

#### Why Indexes Work

| Benefit | Why It Matters |
|---------|----------------|
| **Data-driven & factual** | No subjective quality control needed |
| **Infinite scale** | Directories are unlimited |
| **Proven by competitors** | Ramp (40K-100K pages), Wise, Clay, Exa.ai |
| **High search volume** | People searches, salary guides, reviews, pricing |

#### Specific Index Ideas (Tiered)

**Tier 1: CFO Focus (Start Here)**
- **CFO Directory for Public Companies**
  - Search: "Who is CFO of [Company]" — high volume, low competition
  - "We could rank on this all day" — Marcel
  - Direct ICP targeting: CFOs are Brex decision-makers
- **Company Directory (Finance-Focused)**
  - Filterable by: stage, funding, location
  - Each filter combination = new indexable page

**Tier 2: Small Business (Capital One Synergy)**
- **Small Business Directory by Type & Location**
  - Examples: "Accountants in Austin, dentists in SF"
  - Infinite combinations (type × location)
  - Critical given Capital One acquisition focus on SMBs
- **Salary Guides by Role/Industry/Geography**
  - CFO salaries by company size
  - Accountant salaries by city

**Tier 3: Vertical & Alternatives**
- **Vertical Software Rankings** (ServiceTitan approach)
- **Vendor/Alternatives Catalog** (like we did for Ramp)

#### Hub & Spoke Architecture

Cross-link everything:
- Directories → Tools → Templates → Spend Trends
- Industry hubs pulling content dynamically
- Each click keeps user in Brex ecosystem

**Example Flow:**
1. User searches "Who is CFO of Capital One"
2. Lands on CFO profile page
3. Page links to: CFO Salary Guide, Company Profile, Spend Trends article, CFO Budget Calculator
4. Every click keeps them in Brex ecosystem

#### Execution Clusters

| Phase | Scope | Purpose |
|-------|-------|---------|
| 1 | 50 CFO profiles | Prove concept |
| 2 | 500 pages | Expand if working |
| 3 | 3,000-5,000 pages | Full index deployment |
| 4 | Prune | Remove pages with no traffic (Wise's approach) |

#### Prototype Approach

- Marcel can vibe code CFO directory prototype by Monday
- Show visual in next week's meeting with Alex and Jon
- Present as: "This is what we'd do if we were in your shoes"
