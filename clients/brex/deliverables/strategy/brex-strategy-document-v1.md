## 3.2 EXPERIMENTAL LANE: INDEX/DIRECTORY STRATEGY (Primary Focus #2)

### Why This Approach Works

Your standards are high — that's exactly why Brex SEO quality is what it is. You've written 270 articles yourself because you care deeply about getting it right.

But to scale to thousands of pages, we need an approach that maintains quality without requiring page-by-page review.

**The experimental lane concept**: A content type where quality is ensured by data accuracy rather than subjective review — allowing rapid scaling with pre-agreed guidelines.

### What Are Indexes?

Indexes are **enriched databases with public pages**. Not traditional blog content—**dynamic lists** constantly updated with data signals.

> Marcel: *"It's an index of indexes. Each one of them is like a data set, an enriched data set with a description and some narrative and text around it. We can execute on this rapidly with our deep researcher. That has infinite potential."*

Here's how they work:
1. **Enriched tables**: Not just lists—tables with multiple data points
2. **Dynamic filtering**: Each filter state creates a unique indexable page
3. **Auto-generated entity pages**: Each item in the table gets its own page
4. **Constantly updated**: Lists refresh with new signals
5. **Lead magnets**: Make datasets downloadable for capture

### Why Indexes Work for Brex

| Benefit | Why It Matters for You |
|---------|------------------------|
| **Data-driven & factual** | No subjective quality control needed—you can trust the data |
| **Infinite scale** | Directories are unlimited (unlike article topics) |
| **Proven by competitors** | Ramp has 40K-100K pages. Wise grew this way. Clay did company directories. |
| **High search volume** | People searches, salary guides, pricing, jobs, alternatives |

> Marcel: *"Directories are infinite and there's so much volume. People searching for local businesses."*

And this is the kicker:

> *"The 5,000 pages we have on CheckHQ now that we're going to do - if we copy and paste that to Brex, I almost guarantee you close to overnight they would increase their traffic. Almost guarantee."*
