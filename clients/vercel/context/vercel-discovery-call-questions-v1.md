## Company Context & Leadership

**1.** What matters most to leadership right now? What pressures are they feeling, and what would make them say "we nailed it" in 6 months?

**2.** How does the content quality bar get set internally? Who are the key voices we need to be aligned with early on?

**3.** Is there any broader messaging or positioning work happening in parallel that we should be aware of?

---

## Customer Understanding (Pending ICP + Messaging Docs in March)

**4.** How would you define your ideal customer today?

**5.** For the type of customer we're creating content to reach -- who is that, and what's happening in their world when they start looking?

**6.** When you win deals, what's the deciding factor? When you lose, what are prospects choosing instead -- and why?

**7.** Which competitors have the most mindshare right now -- the ones you're watching most closely? What do you admire about what they're doing?

---

## AI Gateway

**8.** Paint the picture for us on AI Gateway. What does the content experience look like today, and where do you want it to be?

**9.** What data and resources do we have to work with on the model pages? Who on the Gateway team should we be talking to?

---

## Index Section (/i)

**10.** What's your vision for `/i`? What kind of content lives there, and how do you think about it relative to the main blog?

**11.** G's quality bar is well known. Even though `/i` bypasses his direct approval, we want to match that standard. Can you point us to 2-3 pieces of existing Vercel content -- blog posts, docs, anything -- that hit the bar? The ones where you'd say "more like this." That'll help us calibrate fast.

**12.** What does the publishing cadence look like in your head -- are we filling the section fast, or being more deliberate?

---

## AEO / AI Visibility

**13.** Tell us about the LLM visibility work you've been doing internally. What are you seeing, and where are the biggest gaps?

**14.** When a developer asks an AI agent for a recommendation, what queries or scenarios do you most want to be winning?

**15.** If you could pick 3-5 prompts that developers type into Cursor or ChatGPT where Vercel shows up as the answer -- what are they?

---

## Bench Projects (Quick Check-ins)

**16.** Where do stacks pages stand? What do you need from us there vs. what's being handled programmatically?

**17.** We talked about the idea of building a high-value AI Chat tool that combines the AI SDK and Gateway -- basically an interactive experience on-site. Any internal momentum on that, or is it firmly a later play?

---

## Current State & Working Together

**18.** What was AirOps doing for you, and where did it break down?

**19.** What are your 3 best pieces of content today -- the ones that actually perform or that you're proudest of? What makes them work?

**20.** Are there any other lanes or assets -- like the open-source project sites -- that you want us thinking about from day one, or is that a later conversation?

---

## Success Definition

**21.** Six months from now, what metric moving would make you feel like this was the best decision you made this year?

**22.** What does "better than AirOps" look like in the first 30 days? What's the bar we need to clear immediately?
