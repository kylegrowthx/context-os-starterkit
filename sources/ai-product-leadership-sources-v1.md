## How This Was Built

Started with 150+ sources from GrowthX sources-index.md, Lenny's Podcast guest recommendations, and Exa deep research. Curated to 75 based on:
1. Practitioner signal (written by people who've shipped AI products)
2. Product leader lens (not pure ML research or AI hype)
3. Actionability (frameworks you can use, not just concepts)
4. Recency (AI moves fast; prioritize 2024-2026 unless foundational)

- **Pure ML research papers** — Important but wrong audience; prefer translated versions
- **AI hype newsletters** — High noise; prefer practitioner-written sources
- **Generic PM blogs covering AI** — Surface-level; prefer depth from specialists
