## How This Was Built

Started with 100+ names from Lenny's Podcast transcripts (AI + product-leadership + leadership indexes), GrowthX people-index.md, and Exa deep research. Curated to 50 based on:
1. Actually shipping AI products (not just writing about AI)
2. Specific, reusable frameworks or mental models
3. Depth of contribution (long-form content, not just hot takes)
4. Relevance to B2B product leaders building with AI

## How to Use This List

**When building an AI feature:** Check what Paul Adams (Intercom), Tomer Cohen (LinkedIn), or Claire Vo (LaunchDarkly) have said about similar challenges.

**When evaluating AI technology:** Read Simon Willison, Chip Huyen, or Andrej Karpathy for technical product implications.

**When shaping AI product work:** Apply Ryan Singer's Shape Up methodology; reference Marty Cagan and Shreyas Doshi for product thinking.

**When thinking about context engineering:** Follow Harrison Chase, Simon Willison, and Phil Schmid.

**When managing AI product uncertainty:** Listen to Ethan Mollick, Marily Nika, and Dario Amodei on navigating what AI can and can't do.
