## What This Is

This folder contains curated indexes of sources I trust and people I respect. These are the foundations for how I think about building companies, leading teams, writing, and staying current on AI/tech.

**Why this matters:**
- Knowing your sources forces clarity about your mental models
- Curated sources accelerate learning (avoid noise)
- Shared with the team, this creates alignment on "what good looks like"
- When evaluating new information, check it against trusted sources

## How to Use This

**For decision-making:** When facing a new challenge, check if anyone on the people list has written/spoken about it.

**For learning:** Pick 5-10 newsletters from the sources list for your regular reading rotation.

**For hiring:** Share relevant sections with candidates to signal what thinking we value.

**For team development:** Point team members to specific sources for their growth areas.

---

## Maintenance

This should be reviewed quarterly:
- Add new sources that have proven valuable
- Remove sources that have declined in quality
- Promote people who've become more relevant
- Note which sources are most frequently referenced

---

## Selection Criteria

**For people:**
- Track record of building or advising at scale
- Original thinking (not just summarizing others)
- Willingness to share specifics, not just platitudes
- Consistency over time (not one-hit wonders)

**For sources:**
- Signal-to-noise ratio (high value per piece)
- Specificity and actionability
- Relevant to our stage and challenges
- Updated regularly (for time-sensitive topics)
