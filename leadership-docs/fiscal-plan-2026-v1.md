## Two Engines

The company runs on two engines. Both need to fire.

| Engine | What It Is | 2026 Target |
|--------|------------|-------------|
| GrowthX | AI-powered services. Strategy Sprints convert to Growth Execution contracts. | $29M ARR |
| CheckThat | AI visibility software. Self-serve + sales-assisted. The data moat. | $4.2M ARR |

**GrowthX** is proven. We know how to sell it, deliver it, and expand it. The work is making it more predictable, scalable, and sticky.

**CheckThat** is the bet. If it works, we have a high-margin software business that feeds leads into GrowthX and builds a data moat competitors can't replicate.

## The Math

### GrowthX Engine

Strategy Sprints are 8 weeks. They convert at 80% into 12-month Growth Execution contracts. That's when they become ARR.

| Input | 2025 Actual | 2026 Target |
|-------|-------------|-------------|
| Kickoffs | 74 | 140 |
| Sprint Conversion | 70% | 80% |
| ACV | $204K | $204K |
| Renewal Rate | TBD | 80% |

At 140 kickoffs × 80% conversion × $204K ACV = ~$23M in new ARR.
Starting ARR of $12.1M × 80% retention = ~$9.7M retained.
Plus ~$2M in expansion revenue.

That gets us to ~$29M from GrowthX alone. Add CheckThat and we hit $33.8M.

### CheckThat Engine

| Input | 2026 Target |
|-------|-------------|
| Self-serve ARR ($200/mo tier) | $2.5M |
| Sales-assisted ARR ($18-20K ACV) | $1.7M |
| Total CheckThat ARR | $4.2M |

CheckThat revenue is incremental. If it underperforms, we still hit $29M. If it outperforms, we have upside.

### Sensitivity Analysis

| Scenario | ARR | Burn |
|----------|-----|------|
| -10% all factors | ~$26M | ~$8.5M |
| -10% Sprint × Conversion | $29.9M | $8.1M |
| Base case | $33.8M | $7.6M |
| +10% all factors | ~$41M | ~$6.2M |

Even in the downside scenario, we still 2x the business with 12+ months runway. The floor is solid.
