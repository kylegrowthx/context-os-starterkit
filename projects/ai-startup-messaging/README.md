# AI Startup Messaging Research

Research project analyzing the messaging, positioning, and story of the most successful AI companies that announced Series A/B/C funding in the last 10 months (May 2025 - March 2026).

## Focus Domains

Sales AI, Marketing AI, GTM platforms, Website Analytics, Legal AI, Customer Research, Brand Research, Vertical AI.

## Analysis Framework

Using April Dunford's positioning framework + Chris Walker's demand creation lens + category design principles. See `context/roles/cmo-v1.md` for full framework reference.

## Research Note

Direct website scraping and browser access were blocked by network restrictions. All company messaging data was gathered through web search-based research (press, funding announcements, third-party coverage, community discussions). Each company .md file contains structured messaging analysis rather than raw scraped content.
