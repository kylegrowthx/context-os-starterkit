### Three audiences. One system.

The shift most companies haven't caught: three audiences now determine whether your brand gets chosen.

**The buyer** researches, evaluates, and decides. They ask ChatGPT and Perplexity before they open Google.

**The AI agent** answers the buyer's question. It picks which brands to recommend and which sources to cite. If the agent doesn't know you, the buyer never hears your name.

**The training bot** shapes what the AI agent knows tomorrow. It reads your website, indexes your content, and builds the knowledge base the agent draws from. Your website is the raw material of AI perception.

GrowthX powers content that wins all three — search rankings, AI recommendations, and the training data that feeds them. That's not optimization. That's ownership of the conversation where buying decisions happen.
