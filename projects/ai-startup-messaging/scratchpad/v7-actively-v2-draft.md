### Three audiences. One growth model.

**The human buyer** researching your category. **The AI agent** answering their questions. **The training bot** shaping what that AI agent knows.

GrowthX trains your growth model on all three. Search rankings, AI citations, and training data influence — unified in a single system. Content that reaches one audience but misses the other two is content that decays.

**1. Researches** — Ingests site architecture, search data, AI visibility, and competitive landscape into a complete growth graph.

**3. Produces** — Deploys content production, decay detection, and opportunity scoring for every page on your site.

**4. Compounds** — Performance data trains the model, strategies sharpen, and results accelerate quarter over quarter.
