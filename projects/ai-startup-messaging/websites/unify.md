# Unify — Website Messaging Research

## Headline / Hero Messaging

**Primary headline:** "Turn Growth Into a Science"

**Supporting copy:** Unify positions as the warm outbound platform that uses AI and intent data to identify and engage the right prospects at the right time.

## Positioning

**Category:** Warm Outbound / GTM System of Action

**Market frame:** Unify occupies the space between cold outbound (spray and pray) and inbound (wait and hope). Their "warm outbound" positioning says: use signals and intent data to reach out to people who are already showing buying intent, but haven't raised their hand yet.

**Competitive alternatives:** Traditional outbound tools (Outreach, SalesLoft), intent data providers (Bombora, G2), Clay (GTM engineering), cold email platforms.

**Unique attributes:**
- Intent signal aggregation from multiple sources
- AI-powered prospect identification and scoring
- Automated multi-channel sequences triggered by intent signals
- "System of action" positioning (similar language to Clay)
- Warm outbound methodology (not cold, not inbound)

**Value themes:**
- Precision: Reach the right people at the right time
- Science: Data-driven growth vs. gut-feel outbound
- Warmth: Prospects who already show intent convert better
- Efficiency: Automate the research and timing, focus reps on selling

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **"Turn Growth Into a Science"** — Positions outbound as a disciplined, data-driven practice rather than a numbers game. Appeals to growth leaders who want predictability.

2. **"Warm outbound"** — Category creation attempt. Not cold (unwanted), not inbound (passive). Warm = signal-driven, timely, relevant.

3. **"System of action"** — Similar to Clay's positioning. Emphasis on doing, not just knowing.

4. **Intent-driven engagement** — The core technical differentiator. Multiple intent signals aggregated to identify the best prospects.

## Social Proof / Credibility

- $40M Series B
- Strong VC backing
- Growing customer base in B2B SaaS
- Intent data integrations with major providers

## Brand Voice & Tone

- Scientific and methodical (data-driven language)
- Growth-focused (speaks to growth teams and revenue leaders)
- Modern and clean
- Less technical than Clay, more strategic than outbound tools

## Narrative Arc

1. **Problem:** Cold outbound is broken — low response rates, burned prospects, wasted rep time
2. **Insight:** Buyers show intent signals before they fill out a form. If you can read those signals, you can reach them when they're ready
3. **Category:** Warm outbound — the middle ground between cold spray-and-pray and passive inbound
4. **Solution:** Unify aggregates intent signals and automates timely, relevant outreach
5. **Outcome:** Growth becomes a science, not an art

## What's Working (Initial PMM Assessment)

- **"Warm outbound" is a smart category frame.** It creates a new bucket that makes cold outbound look outdated.
- **"Science" positioning** appeals to data-driven growth leaders
- **Intent signal aggregation** is a genuine technical differentiator

## What's Missing / Gaps

- "System of action" language overlaps heavily with Clay's messaging — differentiation risk
- "Turn Growth Into a Science" is somewhat generic — could apply to many GTM tools
- Less visible category creation effort compared to Clay's "GTM Engineering"
