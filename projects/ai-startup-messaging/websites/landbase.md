# Landbase — Website Messaging Research

## Headline / Hero Messaging

**Primary headline:** "Find Your Next Customer"

**Supporting copy:** Landbase positions as the AI-powered GTM platform built on their proprietary GTM-1 Omni model, promising to transform sales prospecting through what they call "VibeGTM."

## Positioning

**Category:** AI-Powered GTM / VibeGTM

**Market frame:** Landbase is making a bold category creation play with "VibeGTM" — the idea that GTM should be as intuitive as "vibe coding" (writing code by describing what you want in natural language). Their GTM-1 Omni model is purpose-built for sales and marketing, not a general-purpose LLM adapted for GTM.

**Competitive alternatives:** Traditional prospecting tools, outbound platforms (Outreach, SalesLoft), data providers (ZoomInfo, Apollo), Clay, Unify.

**Unique attributes:**
- GTM-1 Omni: Proprietary AI model purpose-built for GTM
- VibeGTM: Natural language GTM execution
- 825% revenue growth (claimed)
- 4-7x conversion rates vs. traditional outbound
- AI-native architecture (not AI bolted onto legacy software)

**Value themes:**
- Discovery: Find customers you didn't know existed
- Proprietary AI: Purpose-built model vs. general-purpose AI wrappers
- Results: 825% revenue growth, 4-7x conversions (big claims)
- Simplicity: "Vibe" your way to GTM execution (natural language)

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **"Find Your Next Customer"** — Simple, outcome-focused headline. Doesn't talk about features or technology. Speaks to the result every sales leader wants.

2. **"GTM-1 Omni"** — Proprietary model naming. Signals this isn't just another GPT wrapper. Purpose-built for GTM tasks.

3. **"VibeGTM"** — Category creation attempt riding the "vibe coding" wave. GTM execution through natural language description rather than complex setup.

4. **"825% revenue growth"** — Aggressive growth metric. Used to signal both company traction and customer results.

5. **"4-7x conversion rates"** — Specific performance claim that makes traditional outbound look outdated.

## Social Proof / Credibility

- $30M Series A
- Founded by former Salesforce and enterprise sales leaders
- 825% revenue growth
- 4-7x conversion rate claims
- GTM-1 Omni proprietary model

## Brand Voice & Tone

- Bold and ambitious (big claims, big numbers)
- Founder-driven (strong leadership narrative)
- Category-defining (coining terms like VibeGTM)
- Outcome-focused (leads with results, not features)

## Narrative Arc

1. **Problem:** Traditional GTM is broken — too many tools, too much manual work, too few results
2. **Insight:** General-purpose AI doesn't understand GTM. You need a purpose-built model that knows sales, marketing, and buyer behavior.
3. **Category:** VibeGTM — describe what you want in natural language, AI figures out the how
4. **Solution:** Landbase + GTM-1 Omni — the first AI model built specifically for go-to-market
5. **Proof:** 825% growth, 4-7x conversions

## What's Working (Initial PMM Assessment)

- **"Find Your Next Customer" is beautifully simple.** Outcome-focused, no jargon, speaks to every sales leader's core need.
- **Proprietary model naming (GTM-1 Omni)** differentiates from "AI wrapper" perception
- **VibeGTM** is culturally resonant — rides the vibe coding wave into GTM territory
- **Bold performance claims** create attention and urgency

## What's Missing / Gaps

- Performance claims (825% growth, 4-7x conversions) are aggressive and may invite skepticism without detailed case studies
- "VibeGTM" is catchy but may confuse buyers who don't follow the vibe coding trend
- Early stage (Series A) vs. more established competitors
- Limited public customer case studies to validate big claims
