# Hightouch — Website Messaging Research

## Headline / Hero Messaging

**Primary headline:** "AI Decisioning" platform

**Supporting narrative:** Hightouch has evolved from "Composable CDP" to positioning as an "Agentic Marketing Platform" — a platform where AI agents make real-time marketing decisions at scale.

## Positioning

**Category:** AI Decisioning / Agentic Marketing Platform (evolving category)

**Market frame:** Hightouch started as a reverse ETL / composable CDP tool. The pivot to "AI Decisioning" is a deliberate category evolution — moving from infrastructure plumbing to strategic marketing intelligence. They're betting that marketers' role shifts from doing to managing AI agents.

**Competitive alternatives:** Traditional CDPs (Segment, mParticle), marketing automation platforms (HubSpot, Marketo), data activation tools (Census, RudderStack).

**Unique attributes:**
- Built on the data warehouse (composable architecture)
- AI agents that make marketing decisions autonomously
- Real-time audience building and activation
- No data copying — works with data where it lives
- Recognized as Gartner CDP Leader

**Value themes:**
- Data warehouse-native: No data silos, no copying, single source of truth
- AI autonomy: "Every Marketer Should Be a Manager (of Agents)"
- Enterprise-grade: Gartner recognition signals maturity
- Speed: Real-time decisioning vs. batch processing

## Key Messaging Pillars

1. **"AI Decisioning"** — The new category name. Moves beyond CDP territory into a new frame where AI makes the decisions, not just delivers the data.

2. **"Every Marketer Should Be a Manager (of Agents)"** — Provocative thesis about the future of marketing work. Marketers manage AI agents the way managers manage people.

3. **"Composable CDP"** — Legacy positioning that still works as a technical differentiator. Built on the warehouse, not a separate data silo.

4. **"Real-time, not batch"** — Speed as a differentiator. Traditional CDPs process data in batches; Hightouch activates in real-time.

## Social Proof / Credibility

- $1.2B valuation
- Gartner Magic Quadrant Leader for CDPs
- Led by Sapphire Ventures, ICONIQ Growth, Amplify Partners
- Customers include major enterprise brands
- 35+ marketing platform integrations

## Brand Voice & Tone

- Technical and precise (speaks to data teams and marketing ops)
- Thought leadership-driven (publishing POVs on the future of marketing)
- Enterprise-confident (Gartner positioning, enterprise case studies)
- Category evolution language (not disrupting, but evolving)

## Narrative Arc

1. **Problem:** Traditional CDPs create data silos. Marketing teams can't keep up with the speed and scale of personalization required.
2. **Insight:** The data warehouse is already the source of truth — why copy data into yet another tool?
3. **Evolution:** CDPs should be composable (built on the warehouse), not monolithic.
4. **Future:** AI agents should make marketing decisions, not just deliver data. Marketers become managers of agents.
5. **Solution:** Hightouch is the AI Decisioning platform that sits on your warehouse and deploys marketing agents.

## Notable Quotes / Phrases

- "AI Decisioning"
- "Every Marketer Should Be a Manager (of Agents)"
- "Composable CDP"
- "Agentic Marketing Platform"
- "Real-time data activation"

## What's Working (Initial PMM Assessment)

- **Category evolution is smart.** They're not abandoning CDP positioning — they're graduating from it. Composable CDP → AI Decisioning is a natural narrative arc.
- **"Manager of Agents" thesis** is provocative and timely. It gives marketers a vision of their future role.
- **Gartner validation** provides enterprise credibility that most AI startups lack.
- **Warehouse-native architecture** is a genuine technical moat — it's hard to retrofit.

## What's Missing / Gaps

- The "AI Decisioning" category is brand new — unclear market adoption
- Messaging may be too technical for marketing buyers (speaks to data teams)
- The transition from CDP to AI platform creates potential confusion about what Hightouch actually is
- Limited public case studies showing AI agent results vs. traditional CDP results
