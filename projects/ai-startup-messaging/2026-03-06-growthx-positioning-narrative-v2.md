## Three audiences

The shift most companies haven't caught: there are now three audiences that determine whether your brand gets chosen.

**The buyer** — the human researching, evaluating, and choosing your product. They still search Google. They still read content. But they also ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude before they ever visit your site.

**The AI agent** — the model answering the buyer's question. It decides which brands to recommend, which sources to cite, which answers to surface. If the AI agent doesn't know you, the buyer never hears your name.

**The training bot** — the crawler shaping what the AI agent knows tomorrow. It reads your website, indexes your content, and builds the knowledge base the agent draws from. Your content is the raw material of AI perception.

Most companies optimize for one of these audiences. GrowthX optimizes for all three — search rankings, AI recommendations, and the training data that feeds them. That's the difference between showing up on Google and becoming the definitive answer across every channel where buying decisions happen.

Your website is where all three audiences converge. It's where buyers convert, where AI pulls its answers, and where content either compounds or decays. Every page is working for you or against you — in search, in AI, and in the training pipeline that shapes both.
