# Version 1: Polished Narrative

The structured version. Good for decks, investor updates, written positioning.

---

## The shift

AI rewrote how buyers find, evaluate, and choose. People ask ChatGPT before they open Google. If your brand isn't in the AI answer, you don't exist.

Three audiences now matter for every company: the buyer trying to purchase your product, the AI agent influencing that buyer, and the training bot shaping the AI agent. Your website used to be important. Now it's existential.

Content volume exploded 10x. Decay is faster. The job is no longer "publish blog posts." The job is manage, create, and optimize content at scale. Without AI, it's impossible.

## What GrowthX is

GrowthX is the operating system for website-driven growth.

Not a content agency. Not a SaaS tool. A compounding growth engine where AI handles production and optimization, and humans guide strategy, quality, and judgment. The system gets smarter with every output.

The model: AI does 80-90% of production. Humans do the 10-20% that machines can't — strategy, editorial judgment, calibration. The result is outcomes as a service, not just deliverables.

## Three pillars

**1. The Platform (OS)** — Manage, create, and optimize website content at scale. Four layers: infrastructure, data, platform, services. The platform is being decoupled from services so it stands alone. Target: $34M ARR.

**2. CheckThat** — The open AI visibility index for B2B. Tracks how AI talks about brands across 172 categories and 5,800+ brands. Buyer-intent focused, not just mentions. Open by default. Target: $4M ARR.

**3. Output.ai** — Open-source framework for agentic workflows. Powers the platform and services internally. Strategic bet on becoming the standard infrastructure for AI agents.

## The narrative in three beats

1. **The world changed.** AI rewrote buyer discovery. Three audiences now matter: buyer, AI agent, training bot.
2. **Your website is existential.** Content volume is 10x. Decay is faster. You can't keep up without AI-powered systems.
3. **GrowthX is the OS.** A compounding growth engine that improves with every client, every piece of content, every optimization cycle.

## The one-liner

GrowthX is the OS for website-driven growth. AI handles production. Humans handle strategy. The system compounds.

The job of a growth marketer has fundamentally changed.

So here's the thing. The promised land is that AI handles production and optimization and learning, and humans guide the strategy and quality and judgment. That's what we're building. We call it the OS. The operating system for website-driven growth.

It's four layers. Infrastructure. Data. Platform. Services. And on the side, we have distribution through community, CheckThat, and Output AI.

Here's what we're solving. We're building a data layer that has the best data on how AI sees your brand. We're building a platform that lets you manage, create, and optimize content at scale. And then we have a service layer that helps our customers do that.

This quarter, we're decoupling services from platform. The platform stands alone. Services attach to it. Services are optional. A customer can buy just the platform. When you're onboarding a customer, the goal is not to have them sit on the platform and read five Google Docs a week. The goal is to have them be a compounding growth engine that they can't do without.

Three main messages:

1. The platform is the OS for website-driven growth.
2. We have the best data on how AI sees brands.
3. We have the best framework for agentic workflows.
