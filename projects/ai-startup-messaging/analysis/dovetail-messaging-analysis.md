# Dovetail — Homepage Messaging Analysis

## Hero / Headline

**Tagline (banner):** "The best never guess"
**Hero headline:** "Get total clarity from scattered user feedback"
**Sub-hero:** "Dovetail's AI centralizes and analyzes your customer data to pinpoint the work that drives usage and revenue, so your team can build with confidence."

The tagline is identity-based and aspirational — it speaks to the buyer's self-image ("I'm one of the best, so I don't guess"). The hero is a transformation promise: scattered → clarity. The sub-hero names the mechanism (AI centralizes and analyzes) and the outcome (drives usage and revenue).

Two CTAs side by side: "Contact sales" and "Try Dovetail free" — enterprise + self-serve simultaneously.

## Positioning and Category

**Category claim:** "Customer Intelligence Platform"

This is a deliberate elevation. Dovetail could call itself a "user research tool" or "feedback management platform" — instead, they claim "customer intelligence," which implies:
- Intelligence (analysis, not just storage)
- Customer (broader than "user" — includes sales, CX, marketing)
- Platform (system, not point solution)

The page consistently reinforces this with "customer intelligence" language, "single source of truth" framing, and cross-functional positioning (product, sales, marketing, CX).

## Product Hierarchy

Four-step numbered workflow, each expanding into AI-powered sub-features:

1. **[01] Centralize** — "Ingest feedback from everywhere." Auto-import calls, tickets, surveys, interviews, reviews.
2. **[02] Analyze** — "Identify the next thing to work on." AI Projects, AI Channels, AI Dashboards.
3. **[03] Query & Act** — "Go from feedback to code." AI Chat, AI Docs. Generate PRDs and prototypes from findings.
4. **[04] Automate** — "Close the loop with AI agents." Spot changes, generate docs, alert teams.

The hierarchy is a pipeline (like Maze's research pipeline) but goes further — it extends past analysis into action ("Go from feedback to code") and automation ("Close the loop with AI agents"). The progression from passive (centralize) to active (automate) mirrors an intelligence maturity curve.

Sub-features all prefixed with "AI" — AI Projects, AI Channels, AI Dashboards, AI Chat, AI Docs, AI Agents. The naming convention is simple and makes the AI capability feel pervasive without being gimmicky.

## Information Architecture

1. **Banner tagline** — "The best never guess" (identity hook)
2. **Hero** — Problem→solution transformation + dual CTA
3. **Trust bar** — Shopify, AWS, Atlassian, Notion, Canva, Lovable + G2/Capterra ratings
4. **ROI metrics** — 2.3x ROI, 30hrs saved/week, 66% faster shipping + Forrester TEI study link
5. **4-step workflow** — Centralize → Analyze → Query & Act → Automate (with sub-features)
6. **Customer story carousel** — Breville, Atlassian, Itaú Unibanco (with quotes)
7. **"Single source of truth"** — Cross-functional value anchor + CTA
8. **Trust at scale** — SOC2, ISO 27001, HIPAA, GDPR + enterprise features
9. **Best practices / Privacy** — Templates, permissions, redaction
10. **Blog / resources** — Recent posts
11. **Closing CTA** — "Turn customer feedback into product innovation"

The flow: hook → prove (metrics + logos) → show (workflow) → validate (case studies) → anchor (single source of truth) → reassure (security) → close.

## Tone and Voice

Confident but not aggressive. Customer-centric language throughout — "your data," "your team," "your findings." Active verbs: "centralize," "pinpoint," "reclaim," "ship." The tagline "The best never guess" is the boldest statement — everything else is measured.

Notable: the page quantifies ROI before showing any product. The Forrester TEI study is placed above the fold equivalent. This says: "We have third-party proof. Now let us show you how."

## What's Borrowable for GrowthX

1. **"Scattered to X" transformation arc** — Dovetail's "scattered feedback → total clarity" maps to GrowthX's "scattered content operations → compounding growth engine." The before/after is visceral.

2. **Identity-based tagline** — "The best never guess" is aspirational. GrowthX equivalent: "The best never stop compounding" or "The best don't hope their website works."

3. **Quantified ROI above the fold** — Leading with 2.3x ROI, 30hrs saved, 66% faster shipping — before showing any product — is powerful. GrowthX could lead with compounding metrics.

4. **Numbered workflow as architecture** — The 01-04 pipeline shows how the product works as a process, not a feature list. GrowthX could show: Audit → Strategy → Produce → Compound.

5. **"Single source of truth" anchor** — After the workflow, Dovetail drops a bold positioning statement. GrowthX could use "Single growth engine" or "One system, every page" as the mid-page anchor.

6. **Third-party credibility** — The Forrester TEI study is a trust accelerator. GrowthX doesn't have this yet, but the pattern (external validation of ROI) is worth noting for future.

7. **Sub-features with "AI" prefix** — Simple, consistent naming. If GrowthX names features, the pattern of "Growth Signals," "Growth Engine," "Growth Intelligence" creates the same cohesion.
