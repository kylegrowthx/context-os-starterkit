## 1. About GrowthX

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing company with two engines:

- **Services ($12.1M ARR)** — AI-powered content marketing for B2B tech companies. We don't publish articles. We build compounding growth engines on clients' websites — content that ranks in organic search, gets cited by AI models, and drives brand search. One investment, three return channels.
- **CheckThat (launching)** — An AI visibility index that measures how AI models perceive, recommend, and rank brands. Think "surveys for AI engines." Freemium product designed to become the industry standard. Already tracking 172 B2B categories, 5,800+ brands, and 2.6M+ AI responses.

**The trajectory:**
- 2025: Grew 9x ($1.3M → $12.1M ARR). Team grew from 15 → 96. Launched ContentOS platform. 62 customers. 100% word-of-mouth.
- 2026 target: 2.8x growth to $33.8M ARR.

**Capital and backing:**
- Backed by Madrona Ventures (Series A)
- $12.1M cash on hand
- Financial discipline: 0.3x burn multiple — profitable growth, not burn-and-pray
- Recently signed $10M JPM debt facility

**The thesis:** If you're not in the AI answer, you don't exist. AI is eating the middle of the marketing funnel. 89% of B2B buyers use AI for research. AI referral traffic is growing 527% YoY. The companies that show up in AI-generated answers win. The companies that don't become invisible.

## 3. Mission, Vision, Values

**Mission:** Help companies grow through content. Build systems that compound. Make great people incredibly productive.

**Vision:** A world where the best companies don't struggle to create great content at scale — where AI handles the heavy lifting and humans do the work that matters.

### Seven values

1. **Communicate with clarity** — Be direct. Explain complex ideas simply. Tackle issues head-on.
2. **Relentless excellence** — Good enough isn't. Obsess over the highest standards.
3. **Never stop learning** — Curiosity fuels growth. Always reach for the next challenge.
4. **Move fast, stay steady** — Speed with smart execution. Calculated risks, not reckless ones.
5. **Lead with empathy** — Growth is a team sport. Lifting others is how we all rise.
6. **Own the outcome** — Take responsibility. Don't wait. Chase the answer.
7. **Master the process first** — Get the process right before adding technology.

### The writing standard

GrowthX has strong opinions on writing. This matters for a VP of Marketing more than anyone.

- **Voice:** Write like a smart friend explaining something important over coffee. Direct, clear, real.
- **Style anchors:** Paul Graham (conversational precision), Naval Ravikant (compressed wisdom), Hemingway (raw simplicity)
- **Anti-patterns:** AI slop, corporate speak, burying the lead, jargon-heavy content
- **Quality checks:** First-sentence test (is the point in sentence one?), read-aloud test, cut test (can you remove 20%?)

If you write in corporate-speak, it won't survive here.

### Decision-making

- **DACI framework:** Driver, Approver, Contributors, Informed
- **Default bias:** Most decisions are two-way doors — move fast
- **Deadlock breaker:** "Disagree and commit" — voice disagreement with data, then commit fully

### Human-AI collaboration

This is core to the company's operating model:
- AI handles 80-90% of content production
- Humans do strategy, quality, and creative work AI can't do yet
- Three roles: **Process Architects** (systematize), **Input Calibrators** (extract strategy), **Production Refiners** (quality control)
- Every week serving the same client should get easier — that's the system mindset

## 7. The Product Ecosystem

Four products, one flywheel:

### CheckThat — AI visibility index
- **Open index model** — public data, anyone can browse (like Crunchbase or G2)
- Four scores: Presence (are you showing up?), Reputation (what does the world think?), Perception (what story does AI tell?), Influence (how much can you change it?)
- 172 B2B categories, 5,800+ brands, daily tracking across ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, Google AI Mode
- Revenue model: Free → Pro ($249/mo) → Business ($1,500/mo) → Enterprise
- **This is the VP of Marketing's biggest product marketing challenge** — category creation, not feature competition

### ContentOS — content operations platform
- Where client work happens. Two modes: self-operated (client uses it) or GrowthX-operated (we run it)
- Core revenue engine of the services business
- The "closed loop" — opportunities emerge, content gets drafted, performance feeds back

### Output — open-source AI workflow framework
- Technical credibility play and recruiting pipeline
- The infrastructure layer powering everything

### ExpertLayer (future) — expert marketplace
- Connects vetted content experts with companies
- The human quality layer across the ecosystem

**The flywheel:** CheckThat generates visibility data → ContentOS uses it to optimize content → Output powers the AI workflows → ExpertLayer supplies the talent → data from all feeds back into CheckThat. Marketing is the connective tissue.
