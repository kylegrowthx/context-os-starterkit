# VP Marketing / CMO Hiring Research Scratchpad

## Setup

```yaml
topic: "Vetting and assigning VP Marketing / CMO candidates for Series A startups"
learner_profile:
  role: "CEO/Founder of a Series A B2B SaaS startup"
  goal: "Build a rigorous, evidence-based hiring process for first VP Marketing/CMO"
  context: "$10M ARR scaling to $30M+, 90→130 employees. First senior marketing hire. Need to get this right — wrong hire costs 12-18 months and $500K+."
target_sources: 500+
```

## Audience Analysis

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO is learning this? | Series A CEO hiring first VP Marketing/CMO. Technically strong, marketing-naive. |
| WHAT do they care about? | Practical frameworks, real questions to ask, assignments that reveal skill vs. BS, red flags, reference check templates |
| WHY do they need this? | This hire will define their growth trajectory. Wrong hire = 12-18 months lost + $500K+ cost. |
| WHAT sources would they trust? | VCs (a16z, First Round, Sequoia), experienced operators (CEOs who've made this hire), marketing leaders who've been on both sides |
| WHAT should be excluded? | Enterprise CMO advice (different profile), agency hiring, junior marketing manager advice |

## Research Questions

### 1. Foundations (Critical)
- Q1: What distinguishes a great VP Marketing from a great CMO at Series A?
- Q2: What are the core competencies needed at $10M→$30M ARR?
- Q3: What mental models do experienced CEOs use to evaluate marketing leaders?
- Q4: What are the most common misconceptions about hiring marketing leadership?

### 2. Interview Frameworks (Critical)
- Q5: What are the best structured interview frameworks for VP Marketing?
- Q6: What specific questions separate great candidates from good ones?
- Q7: How should you structure a multi-stage interview process?
- Q8: What behavioral/situational questions reveal real competence?

### 3. Assignments & Case Studies (Critical)
- Q9: What are the best take-home assignments for VP Marketing candidates?
- Q10: How should you structure a presentation/pitch assignment?
- Q11: What 30-60-90 day plan exercises work best?
- Q12: How do you evaluate assignment quality without marketing expertise?

### 4. Vetting & Due Diligence (High)
- Q13: What reference check questions reveal the truth?
- Q14: How do you back-channel effectively?
- Q15: What red flags should disqualify a candidate immediately?
- Q16: What green flags predict success in a scaling startup?

### 5. Startup-Specific Context (High)
- Q17: What's different about hiring marketing leadership at Series A vs. later?
- Q18: What profile works best: big company CMO, startup VP, or operator?
- Q19: How do you assess cultural fit for a scaling startup?
- Q20: What does the first 90 days look like for a great VP Marketing hire?

### 6. Expert Practitioners (High)
- Q21: Who are the recognized experts on marketing leadership hiring?
- Q22: What do VCs advise their portfolio companies on this hire?
- Q23: What do marketing leaders themselves say about being evaluated?
