Content pages represent 2.7% of vercel.com's total 168,294 monthly organic traffic. The blog's traffic grew 63.5% in 6 months, driven almost entirely by new product announcement posts (v0, Chat SDK, Series F, AI SDK v6). The KB section is brand new — zero indexed pages 6 months ago.

The full dataset reveals that **93.4% of content pages generate zero organic traffic.** The blog has 412 dead pages (354 current with no traffic + 58 pages that disappeared from the index). The KB has 277 dead pages out of 301 total.
