## How we built this inventory

Every opportunity here is tied to a real competitor page with real traffic, a measured keyword, and a conservative estimate of what Simple could capture.

**Methodology:**
1. Pulled top 300 pages for each of 12 competitor domains via SEMrush
2. Filtered to content pages only (blogs, guides, tools; excluded product, login, help)
3. Pulled keyword-level data for 22 strategic calibration pages
4. Applied traffic estimation model: primary keyword volume x cluster multiplier x CTR at expected position x 80% success rate
5. Deduplicated across domains → 1,000 unique opportunities

### Priority tiers

- **Tier 1 (months 1–3).** Proven topics, highest ROI per page. Simple already has authority here. 230 pages, 62,200/mo addressable.
- **Tier 2 (months 4–6).** Strong traffic potential, builds topical depth. 275 pages, 59,320/mo addressable.
- **Tier 3 (months 7–9).** Adjacent territory, programmatic content. 325 pages, 37,200/mo addressable.
- **Tier 4 (months 10–12).** Long-tail, niche. 95 pages, 11,960/mo addressable.

### Top 5 clusters

**Calculators & Interactive Tools.** 15 pages, 20,400 visits/month potential. IF schedule calculator, fasting zone tracker, calorie deficit calculator, macro calculator. Single pages that drive more traffic than most blogs.

**Fasting Schedules & Methods.** Every schedule has its own search audience. "16:8 intermittent fasting" alone has 49,500 monthly searches. OMAD, 5:2, warrior diet, alternate day, extended fasting, plus dozens of comparison pages.

**Fasting Science & Biology.** "What happens when you fast for 24 hours." "Fasting stages by hour." People want the science. These pages get cited by AI engines and build authority for the whole site.

**IF Meal Plans.** The content type Diet Doctor, Perfect Keto, and Ruled.me use to outperform Simple by 2–5x. Specific plans by schedule, diet type, and goal.

**"Does X Break a Fast?"** Simple already ranks #1 for several variants. 75 searchable variations exist. Low KD, proven template, top cluster for AI citations.
