### Where Simple stacks up

Simple's SEMrush rank (67,802) is better than every direct IF competitor: Zero (169,190), Fastic (100,299), Lasta (136,282). The domain authority is there. What's missing is content volume. Simple has ~50 content pages. Diet Doctor has ~500. Ruled.me has ~200. That's the gap.

Simple's traffic value per visit ($1.86) is the highest of any direct IF competitor. The branded search moat is real. The content engine just hasn't been built yet.
