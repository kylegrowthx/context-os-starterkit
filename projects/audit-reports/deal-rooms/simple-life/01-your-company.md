## Simple.Life

Weight loss and wellness coaching app. Combines intermittent fasting guidance, AI nutrition coaching, photo-based food tracking, activity tracking, and habit building. Their AI coach "Avo" delivered 19M coaching messages in January 2025 alone.

**Founded:** 2015 by Mike Prytkov and Alexander Ilinskiy
**HQ:** Limassol, Cyprus / London
**Funding:** $45M total raised. Most recent: $35M Series B (October 2025) led by Hartbeat Ventures (Kevin Hart) and Liquidity.

### The product

Simple competes across intermittent fasting apps, AI weight loss coaching, nutrition tracking, and wellness. The exercise and weight loss apps sub-segment is estimated at $1–1.3B in 2025–2026, growing at 17% CAGR.

### Where Simple wins

- **Clinical data nobody else has.** Peer-reviewed study of 50,000+ users showed 42% achieved at least 5% weight loss at one year. Most competitors can't point to published research.
- **AI coaching that people actually use.** Avo delivered 19M coaching messages in January 2025 alone.
- **Revenue and profitability.** $100M at 64% YoY growth, operationally profitable. Rare for the category.
- **One app, five tracking surfaces.** Fasting + food + activity + sleep + mood. Most competitors pick one or two.

### Pricing

Free tier available. Premium: ~$15/month (monthly), ~$10/month (quarterly), under $5/month (annual).

### Awards

Top 100 AI Companies (World Future Awards 2025), Best Virtual Health Coach (MedTech Breakthrough 2025). 4.7-star average rating across app stores.
