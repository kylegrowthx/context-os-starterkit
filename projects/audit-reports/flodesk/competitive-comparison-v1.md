## What the data reveals

### SEO and AI visibility are different games

The correlation between SEO rank and AI rank is weak. Companies like Omnisend (SEO #13, AI #2) and Sender (SEO #23, AI #10) dramatically outperform in AI visibility relative to their organic traffic. Meanwhile Kajabi (SEO #8, AI #25) and Campaign Monitor (SEO #7, AI #15) are nearly invisible to AI despite strong organic presence. AI visibility is driven by content strategy and source presence, not organic traffic volume.

### Flodesk's gap is larger than most peers

Flodesk's -7 gap (SEO #10, AEO #17) means it ranks 7 positions higher by organic traffic than by AI visibility. The only companies with bigger negative gaps are Kajabi (-17), Systeme.io (-10), AWeber (-10), Ghost (-9), Constant Contact (-8), Campaign Monitor (-8), and Keap (-7). Flodesk's gap is in the bottom third but not the worst — which means there's a clear path to improvement.

### Six companies are declining on both fronts

| Company | Traffic Δ | KW Net | AI Vis |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Omnisend | -27.7% | -3,913 | 53.70 |
| Flodesk | -5.1% | -825 | 3.51 |
| Moosend | -35.4% | -2,154 | 20.97 |
| SendPulse | -51.7% | -2,438 | 2.76 |
| Sender | -44.8% | -1,993 | 18.47 |
| GetResponse | -15.4% | -2,255 | 23.39 |

These six have negative traffic trends AND net-negative keyword momentum. Omnisend, Sender, and Moosend partially offset this with strong AI visibility. Flodesk, SendPulse, and GetResponse don't have that cushion.

### The companies winning both channels

| Company | Traffic Δ | KW Net | AI Vis |
|---|---:|---:|---:|
| Klaviyo | +11.8% | +5,928 | 61.74 |
| HubSpot | +20.8% | +24,089 | 36.26 |
| MailerLite | +6.2% | +878 | 41.14 |

Klaviyo, HubSpot, and MailerLite are growing organic traffic, gaining keyword momentum, AND have top-7 AI visibility. These are the companies executing on both fronts simultaneously.

### Source Control separates the contenders

Sender (23%), Klaviyo (17%), Moosend (14%), and Brevo (12%) have the highest Source Control — meaning AI engines cite their own domains as reference material. These companies have built content that AI treats as authoritative. Flodesk's 0.06% Source Control is the lowest of any established brand. AI mentions Flodesk occasionally but never uses flodesk.com as a source.
