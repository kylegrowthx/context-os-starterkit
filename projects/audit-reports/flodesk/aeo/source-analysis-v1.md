# Flodesk AEO Source Analysis: Who and What Shapes AI Recommendations

*March 1, 2026 | CheckThat AI Visibility Index*
*Tracking: Dec 18, 2025 – Mar 1, 2026 (74 days) | 201 prompts | 5 AI engines*

---

## Executive Summary

When AI engines recommend email marketing tools, they draw from a narrow, predictable set of sources. This report classifies the top cited domains (consolidated across subdomains) and the top 300 cited URLs to answer two questions:

1. **Who shapes AI's perception?** — Which types of sources does AI trust?
2. **What content format matters most?** — What kind of pages does AI actually cite?

The findings are stark. Flodesk's domain is cited on only 13 of 73 tracked days. Every major competitor is cited daily. The content that drives AI recommendations is overwhelmingly listicle/best-of content — and Flodesk is absent from nearly all of the highest-frequency listicles.

## Part 4: What This Means for Flodesk

### The Source Gap Summarized

| What AI Needs | What Competitors Provide | What Flodesk Provides |
|--------------|-------------------------|----------------------|
| Product pages to describe capabilities | Homepages + feature pages (cited daily) | Homepage (cited 1 day) |
| Listicles to build shortlists | Own "best of" blog posts (cited daily) | Absent from top listicles |
| Glossary content for definitions | Definition pages (cited daily) | None |
| Comparison pages for head-to-heads | vs-competitor pages (cited 60–71 days) | Comparison pages exist but cited 1–3 times |
| Help/documentation for credibility | Help centers and knowledge bases (cited daily) | None |
| Third-party reviews for trust | G2, Capterra listings (cited daily) | Weak review volume |
| Community discussion for authenticity | Reddit threads, forums | Unknown Reddit presence |

### Priority Actions by Content Type

**1. Get on the listicles that run daily.** This is the single highest-leverage action. There are ~25 listicle URLs cited every single day. If Flodesk isn't on them, it's invisible.

**2. Build glossary and definition content.** Mailchimp's glossary pages are cited daily. Create a `/glossary` or `/learn` hub with definitions of email marketing, email automation, newsletter platforms, etc.

**3. Scale comparison content.** Klaviyo has comparison pages cited 66–71 days. Flodesk's existing comparison pages get cited 1–3 times. The content exists — it needs to be better structured, more comprehensive, and more findable.

**4. Make the homepage citeable.** Every competitor homepage is cited daily. Flodesk's homepage was cited once. This suggests the page structure, meta information, or content doesn't give AI engines what they need to extract and reference.

**5. Audit competitor-published listicles.** 9 competitor-authored listicles are cited 61–73 days. Check if Flodesk is listed on each one.

**6. Build review platform presence.** G2, Capterra, and Clutch are cited daily. Review volume directly feeds AI recommendations.
