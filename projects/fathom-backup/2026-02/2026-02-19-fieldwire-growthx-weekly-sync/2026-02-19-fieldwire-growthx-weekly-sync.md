## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Review

  - **Cluster 1 (Plans & Drawings):**
      - **Concern:** The cluster separates "Plans" and "Tasks," which are tightly linked in the product.
      - **Resolution:** The AI content pipeline will be instructed to integrate "Plans" and "Tasks" where relevant, avoiding a structural change.
  - **Cluster 2 (Trade-Specific "Fan-Out"):**
      - [**Strategy:** Create hyper-specific content using the `[Feature] + [Trade]` format (e.g., "job management software for electrical contractors").](https://fathom.video/share/yVz8B3NhEkERPqNwpffPLNyLENo7zVsv?tab=summary&timestamp=579.0)
      - **Rationale:** Captures long-tail search volume and provides tailored content for specific trades.
  - **New Cluster 5 (Field Intelligence & AI):**
      - **Strategy:** An outcome-first approach → focus on efficiency/productivity, then introduce AI as the enabler.
      - **Content Mix:** Balance outcome-focused articles with foundational pillar pieces on "AI in construction."
      - **Action:** Coordinate with Fieldwire's internal team to avoid content duplication.
  - **New Cluster 6 (Industrial Construction):**
      - **Opportunity:** A greenfield area with high search volume and low keyword difficulty (e.g., "data center construction" has 3k searches/mo, KD 8).
      - **Concern:** Search intent is unclear (e.g., looking for a software solution vs. a construction company).
      - **Decision:** Proceed with testing. The cluster will be a mix of branding (1/3) and commercial (2/3) content, with articles prioritized after core clusters.

### Calibration Article Shift

  - **Initial Plan:** A generic, top-of-funnel article ("What is construction management software?").
  - **New Plan:** A specific, mid-to-bottom-funnel article (e.g., "plumbing contractor software that works in the field").
  - **Rationale:** Provides a more effective test of the AI's ability to produce nuanced, trade-specific content.

### AI Analytics Setup

  - **Platform:** Check That, for tracking brand citations and sentiment in LLMs.
  - **Timeline:** Setup next week; requires \~1 week to populate data, providing a full 8-week view by the end of Phase 1.
  - **Input:** Fieldwire will provide a CSV of custom prompts (e.g., "best plumbing construction management software").
