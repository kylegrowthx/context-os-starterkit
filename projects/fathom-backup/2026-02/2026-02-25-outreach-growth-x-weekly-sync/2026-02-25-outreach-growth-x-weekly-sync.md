## Context

Outreach is undergoing a strategic inflection. Internally announced via Slack by leadership (Becky, Eric), the company is adopting a new positioning as an "Agentic AI platform for revenue teams"—reflecting how its AI agents are built and how executives want to market long-term. This repositioning carries material operational risk: a simultaneous domain migration (Outreach.io → Outreach.ai) and platform CMS migration (Craft → Webflow) create compounding SEO risk if not carefully orchestrated. To manage executive expectations and establish accountability, Hadassah and Paul (Outreach) are drafting a pre-emptive risk/mitigation document for leadership, covering SEO impact, contingency steps, and monitoring strategy.

On the tools side, GrowthX has built Check That, a purpose-built replacement for Scrunch (the previous AI visibility tool). Key improvements: historical context (not just forward-tracking), cleaner data (Scrunch had known tracking bugs), and per-LLM granularity (position, sentiment, citations). This enables targeted, platform-specific optimization. GrowthX is running a 20-post pilot to test whether content creation against zero-presence LLM prompts increases overall presence—a data-driven, batch-based approach to validate AEO strategy before scaling.

## Key Topics

### Strategic Brand & Platform Shift

  - **Problem:** A lack of a consistent brand narrative is diluting the company's signal for search engines and LLMs, making it hard to "own" a market category.
  - **Solution:** A major strategic shift is underway to anchor the brand and platform.
      - **New Descriptor:** "Agentic AI platform for revenue teams."
      - **Domain Migration:** `Outreach.io` → `Outreach.ai`.
      - **Platform Migration:** Craft CMS → Webflow.
  - **Internal Communication:** The shift was announced internally via Slack by Becky and Eric; a formal, broader announcement is expected at the SCO meeting.

### Proactive Risk Mitigation Strategy

  - **Rationale:** To avoid reactive panic and manage executive expectations, Hadassah and Paul are drafting a document for leadership.
  - **Document Contents:**
      - SEO risk profile of the migration.
      - Detailed mitigation plan.
      - Execution steps and monitoring strategy.
  - **Goal:** Establish a paper trail to prevent future blame and ensure leadership understands the potential trade-offs.

### New AI Visibility Tool: Check That

  - **Tool:** GrowthX is replacing Scrunch with "Check That," a new tool they developed.
  - **Key Differentiators vs. Scrunch:**
      - **Historical Data:** Tracks brand performance against competitors over time, not just from the start date.
      - **Reliable Data:** Fixes Scrunch's known tracking issues, providing cleaner, more accurate presence metrics.
      - **Granular Analysis:** Shows per-LLM data (position, sentiment, citations), enabling targeted optimization for specific platforms like Perplexity.
  - **Data Calculation:** Presence is a percentage of total possible responses (prompts × 5 platforms × days), providing a clear, consistent metric.
  - **Onboarding:** Jo will invite the Outreach team to the tool this week.

### Pilot Program: Increasing AI Visibility

  - **Hypothesis:** Creating content for prompts where the brand has zero visibility will increase presence for those specific prompts.
  - **Pilot Design:**
      - **Batch Size:** 20 blog posts.
      - **Rationale:** This batch size is large enough to test for statistical significance, not just anecdotal results.
      - **Content:** Focus on topics aligned with prompts where the brand is not currently present.
  - **Timeline:**
      - **Month 1:** Produce the 20 new posts.
      - **Month 2:** Analyze results to see if presence increased for the targeted prompts.

### Team & Process Updates

  - **GrowthX Team Changes:**
      - **Jen:** Moving to a new internal role focused on editorial quality.
      - **Usman:** Promoted to Managing Editor for the Outreach account.
  - **Content Audit Review:** A deep dive on the content audit with Megan (the audit's creator) is scheduled for two weeks from now.
