## Context

GrowthX operates as an agency with a SaaS layer, using agentic AI to deliver SEO and content strategy. The Engagement Manager role is central to client retention: they act as a strategic POC, project owner, and educator throughout a paid sprint phase that bridges sales and long-term contracting. Success depends on converting 8–10 week engagements into annual partnerships; the EM drives this by delivering measurable value (article indexing, rankings) and adapting technical explanations to clients' sophistication levels.

Steph Ortiz is a candidate with demonstrated strength in cross-functional project management. At Docebo, she managed a 400-article help center refresh with embedded team coaching; at Moz, she initiated and owned a data pipeline optimization that cut import time from 8 weeks to near-real-time. Her current role involves using AI tools daily (ChatGPT, Gemini) and building custom AI workflows for operational tasks. Her primary gap is SEO depth—knowledge from Moz (pre-2020) is acknowledged as outdated, and she has not worked with agentic AI or programmatic SEO at scale.

  - **GrowthX Model:** An agency with a SaaS layer, focusing on agentic AI for SEO and LLM citation.
  - **The Sprint:** An 8–10 week paid engagement ($15k–$40k/mo) that serves as a continuation of the sales cycle.

  - **EM Responsibilities:**
      - **Client POC:** Strategic guide and educator, adapting communication to the client's technical level.
      - **Internal Manager:** Oversees the Managing Editor, who is the "production arm."
      - **Results Owner:** Reports on outcomes (e.g., article indexing) and ensures value is demonstrated.
  - **Internal Workflow:** EM → Managing Editor (Content Engineer) → Engineering (for complex AI pipeline tweaks).
