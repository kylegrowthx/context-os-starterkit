## Key Topics

### Nerdio's Content Challenges

  - **Strategic Gap:** The current strategy is reactive ("inside-out"), driven by internal product pushes rather than proactive market analysis.
  - **Production Bottleneck:** Current output is limited to \~10 articles/month (6 glossary, 4 partner guest posts) due to budget and a lack of strategic agency partnership.
  - **Team Bandwidth:** The team needs a partner to lead strategy, as they are already spread thin and lack the bandwidth to manage the current consultant.
  - **Untapped Signals:** Nerdio is not effectively using search data to inform its broader content strategy (e.g., eBooks, campaigns).

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Proactive Strategy:** An "outside-in" approach driven by market data (search volume, difficulty) to identify and capture high-potential topics.
  - **High-Volume Production:** A minimum of 5 articles per week (new or refresh) to accelerate SEO signals and market authority.
  - **End-to-End Service:** Includes strategy, content creation, CMS publishing, and performance tracking (share of voice, conversions).
  - **Strategic Partner:** GrowthX aims to be a strategic extension of the team, freeing up Nerdio for high-value projects like eBooks and case studies.

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - **Proprietary Platform:** Uses an internal platform, not off-the-shelf tools like Jasper.
  - **Custom AI Workflows:** Leverages agentic workflows with models like Claude, which self-correct research and drafting to meet quality thresholds.
  - **Client-Specific Artifacts:** Ingests client resources (style guides, product docs) to create "artifacts" that personalize AI outputs for brand voice and product accuracy.
  - **Phased Partnership:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-Week Strategy Sprint):** Establishes the Content OS (keyword analysis, topic clusters) and publishes 5–20 articles to generate initial signals.
      - **Ongoing Relationship:** Includes weekly strategy calls, a shared Slack channel, and a Looker dashboard for performance tracking.
