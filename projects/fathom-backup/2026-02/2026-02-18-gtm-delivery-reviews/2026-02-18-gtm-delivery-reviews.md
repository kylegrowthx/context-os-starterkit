      - **Performance:** \~300 signups in 2.5 weeks (\~10/day), but 0 self-serve conversions.

### Content Quality & Delivery Bottlenecks

  - **Problem:** Content quality is the primary bottleneck for client publishing, directly impacting renewal rates.
      - **Root Cause:** A disconnect between the content produced and the client's audience/CEO perspective.
  - **Solution:** Hire more fully ramped Engagement Managers (EMs) and Editor Leads (ELs) to ensure the highest quality standards.
      - **New Roles:** Sydney (Sprint team) and Jen (Pods team) are now ELs, dedicated to raising the quality bar across all accounts.
  - **Marcel's Content Philosophy:** Prioritize "effective" over "efficient."
      - **Goal:** Every piece of content must be signal-heavy, delivering high information gain and actionable insights for the reader.

### Renewals & Account Health

  - **Q1 Renewals:** On track at 74% of target.
      - **Key Accounts:** Airbyte, Tamario, and Abnormal contracts are out for signature.
