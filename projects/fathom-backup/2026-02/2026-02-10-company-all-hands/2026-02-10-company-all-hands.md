---

## Context

This all-hands meeting reflects GrowthX's inflection point: the company is transitioning from a pure services business to a platform and product ecosystem. The critical insight driving strategy is that AI agents are now a primary buyer influencer, making the traditional website an asset that must reach three audiences simultaneously—human buyers, agentic systems, and model training data. This fundamentally changes the value proposition from "we'll write your content" to "we'll build a system that compounds growth across all three channels." The business reality is stark: $2.7M in churn validated that non-core service work (sales enablement, agency-like output) wasn't sustainable. The antidote is a three-part ecosystem (Check That for data, OS for platform, Output AI for workflows) that gives clients control and scalability. Check That's unexpected viral success (1.4M impressions) proved market appetite for AI visibility intelligence, creating a new business vector beyond services. The $22M revenue growth target (from $12M to $34M) requires three things: strong service sprint execution to fund the bet, platform momentum that reduces service dependency, and operational excellence that ties everything together.

## Overview

- **Vision:** The company's strategy addresses a fundamental market shift where AI agents now influence buyers. The website is the key asset for brands to achieve compounding growth and leverage by influencing these three audiences: buyers, agents, and training bots.
- **Ecosystem:** A three-part ecosystem is being built to capitalize on this shift:
    - **Check That:** A new product for AI visibility data, serving as a lead-gen engine.
    - **OS (formerly Atlas):** The core platform for managing website growth, decoupling services from the product.
    - **Output AI:** An open-source framework for building agentic workflows.
- **Performance:** Recent churn (e.g., Surge, Okta) created a \~$2.7M revenue gap. The team is closing this with strong sales momentum (10 new deals in 30 days) and a recovery in Strategy Sprint conversion rates to 80-85%.
- **Goals:** The 2026 target is to grow from $12M to $34M ARR. This requires maintaining high sprint conversion and a strong burn multiple to fund the ecosystem's infrastructure, data, and platform development.

## Key Topics

### Company Vision & Strategy

  - **Market Shift:** AI agents are fundamentally changing buyer behavior, making the website an existential asset.
  - **Three Audiences to Influence:**
    1.  **Buyers:** Direct engagement.
    2.  **AI Agents:** Influence the answers they provide.
    3.  **Training Bots:** Shape the models that learn about the brand.
  - **The Core Job:** Drive compounding growth through the website by:
      - **Managing:** Keeping pages organized, current, and healthy.
      - **Creating:** Expanding the surface area of influence (50k+ pages).
      - **Optimizing:** Ensuring every page is fresh and converting.
  - **The Solution:** An AI-powered system is required, as manual execution is impossible. The system must provide strategic guidance, get smarter with every output, and deliver growth across all three audiences.

### Business & Sales Updates

  - **Recent Churn:**
      - **Surge:** $2.7M contract lost. Work was non-core (e.g., sales enablement), validating the company's strategic focus.
      - **Okta:** Similar non-core work.
      - **Superhuman:** Lost post-acquisition to Grammarly, which chose AirOps. This highlights the need for a platform that gives clients more control.
  - **Sales Momentum:**
      - **10 new deals closed in 30 days,** including Talon One ($135M Series B), Tavis ($40M Series B), and Jama Software (acquired for \>$1B).
  - **Upsell Success:**
      - **Webflow:** Success with integration pages led to an upsell for a code components library.
      - **Lovable:** Strong growth ($45k in purchases) drove an upsell from 5 to 25 guides/week. A publishing bot built by Nico enables this scale.

### Check That Launch & Product Updates

  - **Launch Performance:**
      - **Viral Launch:** Marcel's X post generated 1.4M impressions.
      - **Strong Signups:** \~300 signups from companies like iTouch and HC Insights.
      - **Traffic Boost:** Significant, sustained organic traffic to the website.
  - **Product Development:**
      - **Rapid Iteration:** The product evolved from a simple "hot site" in August to a full platform, driven by real-world service-side use.
      - **Agile Roadmap:** New features (e.g., prompt auto-organizer) are shipped quickly based on team feedback.
  - **OS (formerly Atlas) Rewrite:**
      - A major rewrite is underway for a March release, improving the UI and workflow.
