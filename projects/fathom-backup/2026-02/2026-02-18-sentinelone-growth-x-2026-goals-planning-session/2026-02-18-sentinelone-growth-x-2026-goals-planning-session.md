## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Recommendations

GrowthX presented a 4-part strategy for 2026, based on SentinelOne's goals and partnership results.

1.  **Refresh Existing Content:**
    
      - **Rationale:** A quick win. Refreshing existing content leverages its established authority and indexation for faster keyword gains.
      - **Action:** SentinelOne to define a refresh approach (e.g., update stats, links, publish date) and select an initial batch for a calibration test.

2.  **Expand CS101 Series:**
    
      - **Rationale:** A competitor gap analysis found \~150 keywords with 500+ monthly search volume where S1 lacks a presence.
      - **Action:** After the current topic runway, new CS101 content will target these keywords.

3.  **Improve Organic Attribution:**
    
      - **Tool:** A new custom GA4 report tracks organic search touchpoints before key conversions, showing organic contributes to \~38% of events.
      - **Action:** SentinelOne to define key GA4 events (forms, actions, importance) to help GrowthX prioritize content based on its conversion impact.

4.  **Explore Threat Actor Database:**
    
      - **Idea:** A new database, similar to the CVE model, to centralize threat actor info currently buried in blog posts.
      - **Action:** GrowthX to research comps and scope the project. SentinelOne noted this would require collaboration with the Threat Intelligence team.

### CVE Database Localization

  - **Recommendation:** Do not localize the CVE database.
  - **Rationale:** A competitor analysis (WIZ) showed poor UX and low traffic for non-English versions, with English pages always ranking higher.
  - **SentinelOne's View:** Agrees with the recommendation, noting WIZ's localized pages are often not indexed by Google.

### AI Visibility Tool: "Check That"

  - GrowthX demoed its new proprietary tool, "Check That," for monitoring AI visibility.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Competitor Tracking:** Monitor visibility, position, sentiment, and citation share against up to 30 competitors.
      - **Prompt Analysis:** Track \~150 prompts, filterable by persona/stage, with platform-specific breakdowns to guide content strategy.
      - **Source Identification:** See top cited domains and content formats (e.g., listicles) to identify new competitors and content opportunities.
      - **Shared Library:** Access pre-loaded prompts with historical data to get actionable insights immediately.
