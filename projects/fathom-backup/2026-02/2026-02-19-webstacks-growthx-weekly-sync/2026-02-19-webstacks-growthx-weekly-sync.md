## Context

Webstacks' organic traffic has declined over the past six months despite maintaining the keyword-focused strategies that drove growth a year ago. This decline reflects a fundamental shift in how search engines and AI systems discover and rank content. The broader context is the transition from the "search era" to the "AI era," where AI models like Claude, ChatGPT, and others now source information from YouTube, Reddit, blog posts, and community forums rather than relying solely on Google's algorithmic ranking system.

The immediate traffic recovery following a recent crawl fix will be slow, but GrowthX is working with Webstacks to accelerate it through targeted content refreshes, strategic reindexing, and site optimization. However, the longer-term competitive advantage requires a complete strategic reorientation: building thought leadership and community presence on platforms where AI systems and users actively seek information. This involves both technical SEO optimization and broader AEO content strategies that emphasize authentic expertise, product quality, and public visibility.
