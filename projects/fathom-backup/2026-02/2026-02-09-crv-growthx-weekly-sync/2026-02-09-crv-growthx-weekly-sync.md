## Key Topics

### Content Review & Edits

  - **Problem:** The first content batch is blocked pending GP approval.
      - **Rationale:** Proactive approval prevents future "why did we do that?" questions and potential delays.
  - **Edit Decisions:**
      - **Round Size Ranges:** Remove specific dollar ranges (e.g., "$2–5M seed") from articles.
          - **Rationale:** Ranges are quickly outdated by market shifts (e.g., a recent $35M Series A) and risk deterring founders raising larger rounds.
      - **Company References:** Alphabetize all lists of CRV-backed companies.
          - **Rationale:** Avoids the perception of favoritism among portfolio companies.
      - **CTAs:** Standardize all Calls to Action.
          - **Link:** `crv.com/team`
          - **Text:** "Reach out and get to know our team."
          - **Rationale:** Directs founders to a personal touchpoint while avoiding the spam risk of public email addresses.
      - **Portfolio Focus:** Prioritize references to newer, AI-focused companies (e.g., 7AI, CodeRabbit) over older ones (e.g., Zendesk).
          - **Rationale:** Ensures content feels current and relevant to today's founders.

### Publication & Image Strategy

  - **Initial Publication:** Articles will launch with placeholder AI-generated images.
      - **Guideline:** Ensure diversity in founder imagery (e.g., include women).
  - **Post-Rebrand Refresh:** A full image refresh will occur after the Feb 20 rebrand.
      - **New Style:** A "sepia-toned" or "grainy" treatment, inspired by the new website's aesthetic.
      - **Process:** The web development team may apply the treatment, or GrowthX will source new images.
      - **Scope:** All previously published articles will be updated to ensure visual consistency.

### Analytics & Tracking

  - GrowthX will build two dashboards to track content performance:
    1.  **LLM Analytics Dashboard:** Tracks CRV's visibility in Large Language Models.
          - **Metrics:** Visibility, sentiment, citation frequency, and answer position vs. competitors.
    2.  **Looker Dashboard:** Tracks organic performance from Google Search Console (GSC) and Google Analytics (GA).
          - **Metrics:** Keyword rankings and organic traffic growth per article.
  - **Competitor List:** The LLM dashboard will track against the full competitor list provided by SF Three.
      - **Rationale:** This provides a complete market view, including competitors with zero current LLM presence, which highlights CRV's leadership.
