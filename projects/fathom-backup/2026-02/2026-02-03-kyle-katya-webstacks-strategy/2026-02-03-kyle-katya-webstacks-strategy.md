## Key Topics

### Critical Performance Drop

  - **Observation:** Non-brand traffic halved after the Jan website redesign.
  - **Hypotheses:**
      - **Primary:** SEO damage from the redesign (e.g., broken links, ranking loss).
      - **Secondary:** Publishing delays from a team member's illness.
  - **Data Anomaly:** The Looker dashboard shows a near 1:1 ratio of sessions to engagement, which is highly unusual and suggests a data tracking issue.
  - **Action:** Kyle will investigate the root cause today.

### Annual Contract Strategy

  - **Goal:** Secure an annual contract at the current rate by demonstrating long-term strategic value.
  - **Blocker:** CFO Sean demands a 20% discount.
  - **Rationale:** The current rate is already a "screaming deal" for the content volume provided.

### Strategic Roadmap for Annual Contract

  - **Approach:** A flexible 6-month roadmap focused on driving conversions, not just traffic.
  - **Framework:**
    1.  **Identify Strategic Themes:** Focus on key business goals and promising traffic signals.
    2.  **Audit Content Gaps:** Determine if themes require new content or refreshes.
    3.  **Enhance Engagement:** Layer in successful elements from past tests.
  - **Opportunity:** Improve internal linking between articles and underperforming product/solution pages to build topical authority.

### Q1 Initiative: CMS Partner Hubs

  - **Focus:** Building CMS partner hubs (starting with Sanity) to create shareable assets for campaigns.
  - **Goal:** Improve user experience by replacing the old "SEO bet" footer with a dynamic banner featuring relevant blogs.
  - **Future:** Expand this model to other topics (e.g., tools) if successful.

### Fathom Migration Urgency

  - **Issue:** Fathom migration is stalled.
  - **Action:** Katya will ping the executive assistant to schedule a meeting this week.
  - **Backup Plan:** If migration is delayed, Kyle will scope work to refine canonicals.
