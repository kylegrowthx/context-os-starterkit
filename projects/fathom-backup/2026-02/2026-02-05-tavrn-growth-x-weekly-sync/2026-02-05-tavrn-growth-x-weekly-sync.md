## Context

Tavrn (AI legal software) and GrowthX (content marketing agency) are at a strategic inflection point in their six-month partnership. The high-volume, "shooting from the hip" content strategy has proven successful—92 published articles drive consistent organic growth with engaged sessions increasing week-over-week. However, Matt identifies a critical opportunity: moving from acquisition velocity to optimization ROI.

The team is expanding into personal injury law content, introducing new workstreams (glossary for domain authority, case studies as soft conversion events, and templated pages as low-friction CTAs), while maintaining the editorial core. The audit will determine whether these initiatives run in parallel with new content or replace some production cycles. Megan's concern about messaging to stakeholders (particularly CEO Pedro) reflects a common tension in content strategy: the perception that pausing new content signals deceleration, when strategic refresh often drives higher returns than volume alone.
