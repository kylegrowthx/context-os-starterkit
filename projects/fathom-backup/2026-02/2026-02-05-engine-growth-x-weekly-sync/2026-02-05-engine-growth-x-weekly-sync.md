## Context

This sync marks the midway point in GrowthX's content partnership with Hotel Engine (NGINX business unit). The relationship is centered on content-driven visibility for Engine X products (direct billing, NGINX charge card) and new features (Rewards Program, Spaces). Hotel Engine's legal and product teams have strict content governance requirements—particularly around NGINX terminology (charge card vs. credit card)—which necessitates a formal approval workflow. GrowthX is also transitioning visibility tracking from Scrunch to CheckThat, providing clearer insights into organic search performance and topical authority.

The partnership is high-value (Hotel Engine is a significant client) and complex operationally. Carly Piekos (GrowthX) owns content strategy and calendar management; Joel Murphy (Hotel Engine) owns editorial approval, legal coordination, and business context. Success metrics are sessions, engagement rate, CTR, and ultimately, conversion lift—though conversion data is still pending from Hotel Engine.
