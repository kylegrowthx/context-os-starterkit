## Context

Sunbit is a fintech BNPL platform strengthening its thought leadership in AI visibility to attract investors and top engineering talent. The Sunbit.ai blog, now live on WordPress, is the centerpiece of this strategy. GrowthX is helping them scale editorial production via a hybrid model: repurposing Sunbit's in-house AI/ML expertise (including academic papers and webinars from their Israeli team) into English-language explainer content, while GrowthX provides the structural writing and SEO optimization. Early performance signals are strong—clicks up 84%, 8 new auto-tips articles already generating traffic, averaging 80-100 users/day.

The two teams are also optimizing workflows to remove bottlenecks. GrowthX's image webhook wasn't wired to WordPress, delaying publication. Additionally, legal review cycles are slowing B2C content (dental financing, auto repair guides). By templating 50% of B2C page content (branded positioning, competitor comparisons like CareCredit, disclaimers, directories) and wiring it directly into WordPress fields, they can get legal approval once and reuse the templates. Jason Beltran (Sunbit VP of Product/Engineering) will audit the webhook sync and evaluate the template modules with Sunbit's web agency.

  - **Goal:** Build investor credibility by first earning the trust of a technical audience.
  - **Target Audience:** Engineers.
  - **Content Model:** "Smart Explainers" → Break down complex topics with technical depth for engineers, while making the "what" and "why" accessible to non-technical readers.
