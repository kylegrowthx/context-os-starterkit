## Key Topics

### Content Calendar & Production Process

  - The new content calendar is live, organized by cluster and industry vertical.
  - **Action:** The team will use the calendar's priority tab to mark topics as "high" or "low" priority within the next week.
  - **Production Process:**
      - GrowthX uses custom "agentic workflows" that dynamically pull from client artifacts (writing guidelines, case studies) to generate content.
      - Agents self-evaluate and iterate to ensure quality before a human editor performs a final editorial pass.
      - Feedback updates the artifacts, improving future content.
  - **Interlinking Strategy:**
      - New articles are automatically interlinked by an agent that scans the sitemap.
      - GrowthX's managing editor will manually interlink older articles to new, relevant content.
  - **Content Optimization:**
      - The default is to optimize existing articles, as this typically yields faster results.
      - New articles are created only if an old article's URL slug is unoptimized, requiring a redirect.

### "Agentic Commerce" Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** The team questioned whether to continue producing content on "agentic commerce," given its newness and low search volume.
  - **Initial Analysis:** The broad "agentic commerce" topic is highly competitive (SEMrush KD 70), dominated by major players like OpenAI and Salesforce.
  - **Strategic Pivot:** The goal is not to compete with these giants but to own a specific niche.
      - **Target Niche:** "Agentic commerce for loyalty and promotions."
      - **Rationale:** This positions the company as a key solution for businesses already exploring agentic commerce and looking to optimize their loyalty programs.
  - **Execution Plan:**
      - **Internal Team:** Continue creating deep-expertise content (e.g., the upcoming report) that GrowthX cannot easily replicate.
      - **GrowthX:** Create ancillary articles that support the internal team's content, forming a powerful, interlinked cluster.
      - **Research:** GrowthX and Reza will investigate search intent for this niche to validate the strategy.
