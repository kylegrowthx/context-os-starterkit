## Context

Talaera is evaluating artificial engine optimization (AEO) as a strategic channel for B2B visibility, moving beyond traditional SEO to capture the growing share of research that starts on LLMs like ChatGPT and Perplexity. GrowthX's CheckVat platform tracks this visibility in real time by monitoring how frequently Talaera and competitors appear in LLM responses across carefully selected prompts that mirror buyer intent. The "Babel Alternatives" article performance demonstrates that content indexed quickly by Google is immediately picked up by LLMs, and that clear information architecture ranks above traditional SEO metrics in LLM selection logic. This creates a unique opportunity to build long-term authority through a deep content library and strong URL structure (e.g., `/blog/business-english-platforms/`), which competitors cannot replicate retroactively.
