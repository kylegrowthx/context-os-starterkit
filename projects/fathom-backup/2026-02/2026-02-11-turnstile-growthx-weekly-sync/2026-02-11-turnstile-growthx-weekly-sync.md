## Key Topics

### Content Quality Breakdown

  - The "Customer Renewals" article was the primary example of a critical quality failure, with issues including:
      - **Factual Errors:** Misdefining "churn" and misstating what data Turnstile stores.
      - **Awkward Transitions:** Lacking logical flow between paragraphs.
      - **Unsubstantiated Claims:** Asserting patterns are "predictable" without evidence.
      - **Off-Brand Messaging:** Using "addiction" and "contractual lock-in" language.
      - **Irrelevant Information:** Including stats on SaaS pricing updates that don't support the point.
  - Similar issues were noted in other articles:
      - **"Payment Failures":** Article content focused on "collections" instead of the keyword's actual definition.
      - **"Tiers":** Repeated the same problems as a previous draft, indicating feedback was not applied.
  - **Root Cause:** A breakdown in GrowthX's internal review process, where articles are supposed to be reviewed by a managing editor before client delivery.

### Proposed Workflow Solution

  - **Goal:** Embed Turnstile's expert context into the AI writing process to improve accuracy and reduce the need for extensive post-draft reviews.
  - **Pilot Process:**
    1.  Zach will select one topic from the Airtable list.
    2.  Zach will record a 5-minute voice note with expert context.
    3.  GrowthX engineers will integrate this note into the AI pipeline.
  - **Rationale:** Zach's prior test with this method yielded a much higher-quality draft, proving its potential.

### New Engagement Manager & Technical SEO

  - **New EM:** Carly joins next week; William will overlap for 2–3 meetings to ensure a smooth transition.
  - **Carly's Expertise:** Strong technical SEO skills.
  - **Initial Audit Findings:**
      - **Problem:** The blog uses a "load more" button instead of pagination.
      - **Impact:** This hinders search engine crawlers, suppressing the ranking of older articles (e.g., "Quote to Cash").
      - **Recommendation:** Implement pagination.
  - **Next Week:** Carly will present a full technical SEO analysis. Matt Ryder (Turnstile's Demand-Gen Manager) will be invited to attend.

### SEO Performance Update

  - **Authority Score:** Up 4 points in February.
  - **Organic Clicks:** Up 126% week-over-week.
  - **Keyword Rankings:**
      - Now ranking for \>60 keywords (up from \<30).
      - 12 keywords are in the top 20.
      - Valuable keywords now ranking include "recurring invoice software" and "recurring revenue billing software" (difficulty 45).
