## Summary

Discern's organic channel has generated $130k in closed ARR from 13 customers through GrowthX-created PSEO content (5-6x traffic growth, 757 ranking pages). With organic leads closing 2x faster and converting 50% better than sales-driven channels, the partnership now shifts from building coverage to optimizing conversion—proposing bottom-of-funnel content, CTA enhancements, and proactive content refreshes to maintain AI visibility and accelerate ROI beyond the current 12-month payback period.

## Context

Discern is a registered agent services platform competing in a mature market dominated by legacy players (CSC, CT Corp, Northwest). When GrowthX began in June 2025, Discern had minimal organic presence. Over ~8 months, GrowthX deployed a scalable PSEO engine delivering 668 articles (net new + refreshes) targeting state-by-state compliance topics. This strategy proved effective: 757 pages now rank, driving 5-6x traffic growth, and Discern achieved #2 AI visibility ranking (ahead of CSC). Attribution from Discern's "how did you hear about us?" form data confirmed organic search is a high-value channel (~$270k pipeline, 50% conversion = $130k closed ARR). Current bottleneck: traffic growth outpaces lead generation. Discern's organic payback sits at 12+ months but is expected to improve as they add retargeting email automation (new headcount) and explore self-service product options. Renewal is April 2026.
