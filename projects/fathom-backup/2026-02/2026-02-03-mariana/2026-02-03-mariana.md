## Key Topics

### The Core Problem: Reactive vs. Strategic Feedback

Alberto sends stream-of-consciousness feedback via Slack (often on weekends) without seeing the strategic work behind keyword selection. He requests:
- **Duplicate articles for the same keyword** based on Claude-generated drafts with unsourced stats. Mariana notes that this is inefficient: their research process (keyword analysis → Atlas → vetting) already drives keyword selection. Running multiple versions through Claude produces different outputs each time, but the process is deterministic and vetted.
- **Term changes** that conflict with Ambient's own messaging docs (e.g., saying "no vendor jargon" for manufacturing plant article, but those exact terms came from Ambient's messaging doc; deleting "ambient intelligence" despite it appearing verbatim in Ambient's messaging doc).
- **Performance improvements** for gun detection content, but refusing to prioritize it when Ambient deprioritizes the content lane.

### Why the Process Matters

Mariana's workflow is rigorous:
- **Keyword selection** driven by search volume + keyword difficulty ratio (targets 18-20% KD, decent search volume).
- **Competitive analysis** identifies keywords competitors rank for, creating outranking opportunities.
- **Problem-to-keyword matrix** maps each customer problem to multiple keywords, ensuring comprehensive coverage.
- **Vetting** against Ambient's messaging docs, white papers, and threat signature lists (which yield new keywords).
- **Intent research** for refreshes (Mariana types keywords into Google to see SERP intent before writing).
- This process takes longer than 15 minutes, but it's why the content ranks.

Conflict: Alberto's expectation is 15-minute turnaround + manual research for every article. These are incompatible.

### Solutions Agreed Upon

1. **Process Documentation (Notion Doc)**
   - Bailey drafts; Mariana reviews and adds detail.
   - Shows keyword analysis → Atlas → vetting → intent research, justifying time and rigor.
   - Reframes the conversation around why they prioritize the way they do.

2. **Problem-to-Keyword Matrix**
   - Visual mapping of each customer problem to multiple keywords.
   - Demonstrates strategic coverage (not random picking).
   - Shows search volume, KD ratio, and competitor gap analysis.

3. **Webinar-Repurposing Workflow**
   - Turn webinar transcripts into ~9 SEO articles per event.
   - Captures Ambient's narrative and identifies new keywords.
   - CTA links to full webinar, not demo.
   - Model: Use existing Abnormal workflow as basis.

4. **Escalate Conflicting Feedback in Client Syncs**
   - Mariana collects examples; Bailey presents them when Ambient team is in the same room.
   - Forces alignment instead of individual doc reviews creating conflicting edits.

### Additional Requests

- Sales objections/battle cards or top discovery call objections (to inform content around objection handling).
- IT integration documentation (similar to threat signature docs, which yield keywords).
- Existing webinar links and transcripts; monthly webinar schedule going forward.
