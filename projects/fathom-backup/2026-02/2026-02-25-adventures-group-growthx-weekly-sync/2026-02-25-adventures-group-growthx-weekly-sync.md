## Key Topics

### Mexico Safety Concerns

  - Recent safety incidents in Mexico (Vallarta, Guadalajara) caused a surge in traffic to the "Vallarta Safe" blog and cancellation pages.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will create reassuring content (blog posts, reels) to counter "fake news" and provide real-time updates for tourists.
      - **Rationale:** This is a high-interest SEO opportunity to build trust and capture traffic.

### New Content Strategy: Tour Page Priority

  - **Problem:** "Grade D" tour pages have zero revenue and weak SEO (e.g., ranking for only one keyword), making them a key optimization opportunity.
  - **Solution:** Shift content focus to prioritize these high-revenue pages.
      - **Weekly Output:** 4 tour pages optimized + 3 blog posts.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **SEO First:** Optimize on-page content to increase keyword rankings and visibility.
    2.  **UX/UI Second:** After SEO results are measured (e.g., 3-month vs. 3-month comparison), implement CRO changes like an above-the-fold booking button.
  - **Approval Workflow:** All tour page content changes require Adventures' approval, using a change-tracking method (e.g., colored text) to ensure clarity.

### AI Search Visibility with CheckDot

  - **Platform:** CheckDot will replace Scrunch to track AI search visibility.
  - **Setup:**
      - 3 profiles will be created (Vallarta, Cabo, Cancun).
      - 100 prompts per location (300 total) will be created and sent for review.
  - **Strategy:** Include a minority of general "Mexico" prompts.
      - **Rationale:** To see if Adventures' content is cited as a source for national-level topics, building brand awareness against larger competitors like Xcaret.
