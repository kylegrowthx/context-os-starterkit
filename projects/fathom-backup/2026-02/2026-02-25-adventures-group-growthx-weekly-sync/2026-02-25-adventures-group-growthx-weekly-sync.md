## Context

This weekly sync between GrowthX (content marketing agency) and Adventures Group (Mexican adventure tour operator) focused on optimizing the engagement strategy following two major shifts in priority. Recent safety incidents in Mexico (particularly in Vallarta and Guadalajara) created both a challenge and opportunity: traffic to the "Vallarta Safe" blog surged, but cancellations threatened revenue. Simultaneously, analysis of Adventures' tour pages revealed a significant untapped opportunity—"Grade D" pages with zero revenue and minimal SEO presence (ranking for only one keyword). The team structured a two-phase optimization approach: SEO-first content optimization to increase keyword visibility, followed by UX/UI improvements once results can be measured against baseline metrics. The new Notion workspace centralizes all shared documentation, analyses, and the content pipeline, with 44 topics currently in the "considering" stage.
