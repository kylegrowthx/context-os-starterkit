## Key Topics

### Agentic Commerce Strategy

  - **Goal:** Establish Talon.One as the category leader for "Agentic Commerce for Loyalty & Promotions."
  - **Challenge:** The broad "Agentic Commerce" keyword is highly competitive (SEMrush difficulty: 70), with major players like OpenAI and Salesforce.
  - **Solution:** Focus on a specific niche.
      - **Talon.One:** Create deep-expertise content (e.g., reports) that leverages proprietary data and internal knowledge.
      - **GrowthX:** Build a supportive content cluster (e.g., "Agentic Commerce for Retail") to drive traffic and interlink with Talon.One's core pieces.
      - **Coordination:** The teams will align efforts to build authority and interlink all content for maximum SEO impact.

### Content Calendar & Production

  - **New Calendar:** The content calendar is live, organized by cluster and industry vertical.
  - **Prioritization:** Talon.One will assign priorities (High/Low) to topics, starting with composable commerce as a low priority.
  - **Calendar View:** A traditional calendar view is not currently available.
      - **Why:** The team is still establishing a production rhythm (review times, CMS publishing) and is focused on selecting topics for the initial 5 articles/week sprint.
      - **Future:** A calendar view is planned for Phase 2 to help the GTM team coordinate campaigns.

### GrowthX Production Pipeline

  - **Process:** Talon.One feedback → Updates to "artifacts" (writing guidelines, case studies) → Dynamic insertion into AI pipelines.
  - **Agentic Workflows:** Custom pipelines for articles, comparisons, and case studies use self-evaluating agents.
      - **Function:** Agents iterate on tasks (e.g., research) until a quality score is met, ensuring reliable sourcing and structure.
  - **Human Oversight:** Three distinct human quality checks are performed by a managing editor:
    1.  Initial structure review
    2.  Mid-production quality check
    3.  Final editorial pass

### Content Optimization & Interlinking

  - **Optimization Strategy:** Prioritize optimizing existing content over creating new articles.
      - **Rationale:** Faster results. Sunsetting old posts is a last resort for unfixable issues like poor URL slugs, requiring a 301 redirect.
  - **Interlinking Process:**
      - **New Content:** An automated agent scans the sitemap to find relevant internal linking opportunities.
      - **Old Content:** A manual process, managed by GrowthX, will be used to add links from older articles to new content.
