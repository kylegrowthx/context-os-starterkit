## Overview

- **Performance Gap:** Immubit's 220 blogs get only \~150 clicks/week, while a client like Tavern gets 1,000 clicks/week from just 60 blogs.
- **Root Cause:** A "Trojan horse" strategy (broad topic → Immubit pitch) failed. It missed user intent, as seen in the "Hydrocrackers" article, which quickly pivots from the topic to an AI pitch.
- **New Strategy:** Create a lean, focused blog by classifying all articles for a specific action: **Rewrite**, **Refresh**, **Merge**, or **Kill**.
- **Goal:** Build a hyper-focused blog that drives ICP conversion, providing a clear narrative for the client to justify the shift from a high-volume to a high-quality approach.

---

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Underperforming Content Library

  - **Performance:** 220 articles yield only \~150 clicks/week.
  - **Root Cause:** The "Trojan horse" strategy failed.
      - **Execution:** Articles target broad, top-of-funnel (ToFU) keywords (e.g., "Hydrocrackers") but quickly pivot to an Immubit AI pitch, missing the user's initial intent.
      - **Example:** The "Hydrocrackers" article lacks a basic definition and immediately discusses AI optimization.
      - **Result:** \~96 articles have significant keyword/intent mismatches.
  - **Cannibalization:** Multiple articles target the same keyword, diluting authority.
      - **Example:** Two articles on "manufacturing optimization" cover the same topic.

### The Solution: A Lean, Focused Content Library

  - **Goal:** Create a lean, hyper-focused blog that drives ICP conversion.
  - **Audit Framework:** Classify all articles into one of four action categories:
      - **1. Rewrite:** For articles with major intent mismatches.
          - **New Approach:** Fully satisfy the ToFU keyword first (e.g., "What is a Hydrocracker?"), then dedicate a small section to Immubit's AI solution.
      - **2. Refresh:** For high-performing articles that align with intent.
          - **Actions:** Add TLDRs, FAQs, and internal links.
      - **3. Merge:** To consolidate cannibalizing content.
          - **Rationale:** Create one comprehensive article that outranks multiple weaker ones, reflecting top-ranking content that often covers a topic holistically.
      - **4. Kill:** To remove irrelevant or low-quality articles that don't serve the ICP.
  - **Client Narrative:** This framework provides a clear story for the client to explain the shift from a high-volume approach to a high-quality one.
