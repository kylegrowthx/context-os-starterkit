## Summary

The team committed to a 1-2 week sprint of 4 comparison articles per week to fix the citation gap, pausing agentic commerce work. ## Context

Talon.One and GrowthX are partnering on content strategy to improve visibility in AI-driven searches and recommendations. Talon.One operates in the referral and promotions software space for e-commerce, competing against vendors like ActiveCampaign and Yotpo. The client has been investing in agentic commerce content but faces a paradox: strong AI visibility without citations, indicating the AI models see them but don't cite them as authoritative sources.

GrowthX introduced CheckThat, their proprietary AI visibility monitoring tool that runs 120,000 prompts daily to track rankings and citation patterns. The team shared performance audit data showing agentic commerce pages get high impressions but low CTR due to low average search position and AI summarization stripping revenue-generating clicks. The strategic priority is to address the citation gap first (via comparison content) before optimizing for agentic commerce conversion.

## Key Topics

### AI Visibility & Citation Gap

  - **Problem:** High visibility (top 3 in 70% of prompts) but 0% citation share.
  - **Cause:** AI models primarily cite comparison articles for recommendations.
  - **Solution:** Prioritize creating comparison articles to increase citation share.
  - **Tool:** GrowthX will provide Talon.One access to the "Check That" AI monitoring tool.

### Agentic Commerce Content Audit

  - **Finding:** Low CTR on agentic commerce pages despite high impressions.
  - **Cause:** A low average position in search results and AI summarization.
  - **Recommendations:**
      - **Page Structure:** Add FAQs and clear educational content (who, what, why).
      - **Internal Linking:** Use prominent, visual links (like on product pages) to boost user engagement.
      - **Content:** Expand and restructure for better snippet visibility.
  - **Competitor Insight (Yotpo):** Yotpo's success with LLM-specific content (e.g., "Inside ChatGPT's shopping experience") provides a model for Talon.One.

### New Content Strategy

  - **Decision:** Prioritize comparison articles for the next 1–2 weeks.
  - **Pacing:** 4 articles/week.
  - **Rationale:** Fix the AI citation gap before focusing on agentic commerce.
  - **Future Agentic Content:** Will adopt the LLM-specific angle inspired by Yotpo.
