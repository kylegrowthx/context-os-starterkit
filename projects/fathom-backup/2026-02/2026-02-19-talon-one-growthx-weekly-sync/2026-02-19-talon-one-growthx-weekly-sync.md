## Overview

- **AI Visibility Gap:** Talon.One has strong AI response visibility (top 3 in 70% of prompts) but a 0% citation share. This is because AI sources comparison articles, which Talon.One lacks.
- **Strategic Pivot:** The content strategy will pivot to prioritize comparison articles for the next 1–2 weeks to fix the citation gap. Agentic commerce content will follow.
- **Agentic Content Plan:** A 1–2 week sprint will optimize existing agentic commerce pages for CTR by adding FAQs, better internal links, and a more educational structure.
- **Competitor Insight:** Yotpo's successful strategy of creating agentic commerce content for specific LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT) will be adopted by Talon.One.

---

## Key Topics

### AI Visibility & Citation Gap

  - **Problem:** High visibility (top 3 in 70% of prompts) but 0% citation share.
  - **Cause:** AI models primarily cite comparison articles for recommendations.
  - **Solution:** Prioritize creating comparison articles to increase citation share.
  - **Tool:** GrowthX will provide Talon.One access to the "Check That" AI monitoring tool.

### Agentic Commerce Content Audit

  - **Finding:** Low CTR on agentic commerce pages despite high impressions.
  - **Cause:** A low average position in search results and AI summarization.
  - **Recommendations:**
      - **Page Structure:** Add FAQs and clear educational content (who, what, why).
      - **Internal Linking:** Use prominent, visual links (like on product pages) to boost user engagement.
      - **Content:** Expand and restructure for better snippet visibility.
  - **Competitor Insight (Yotpo):** Yotpo's success with LLM-specific content (e.g., "Inside ChatGPT's shopping experience") provides a model for Talon.One.

### New Content Strategy

  - **Decision:** Prioritize comparison articles for the next 1–2 weeks.
  - **Pacing:** 4 articles/week.
  - **Rationale:** Fix the AI citation gap before focusing on agentic commerce.
  - **Future Agentic Content:** Will adopt the LLM-specific angle inspired by Yotpo.
