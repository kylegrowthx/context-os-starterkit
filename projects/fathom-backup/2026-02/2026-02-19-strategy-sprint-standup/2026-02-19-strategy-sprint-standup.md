## Key Topics

### New Notion PM Framework

  - **Goal:** Improve visibility and streamline workflows for the fast-paced sprint process.
  - **Architecture:** Leverages the existing Strategy Sprint Tracker (SST) database, now connected to a new task database.
  - **Automated Task Creation:**
      - A "spin up tasks" checkbox on a new SST record automatically generates a full sprint's tasks, assigned by role and week.
      - This replaces manual Linear ticket creation, which was cited as a major pain point.
  - **Centralized Client Health Tracking:**
      - A dedicated section in the SST record provides a single source of truth for client health (Green, Yellow, Red).
      - **EMs:** Own the overall health signal.
      - **MEs:** Provide input on content quality and publishing issues.

  - **Feedback & Adoption:**
