## Context

Speechmatics is a European-founded speech recognition and voice AI company shifting from a sales-led model to hybrid product-led growth (PLG), driven by the rapid expansion of the voice agent market. They compete directly with Deepgram, Assembly, and Sonioxx, differentiating on strong non-English language support and focus on regulated industries (financial services, legal tech, contact centers). Their current content strategy relies on manual research and creation using three separate tools (Profound for AI search research, Ahrefs for SEO, Surfer SEO for optimization), resulting in slow production and incremental impact on AI search visibility—a critical gap for their PLG motion.

GrowthX is a B2B content services firm founded by a former Deepgram CMO that specializes in scalable, high-quality content production through proprietary agentic workflows and artifact-based knowledge management. Their service model includes an 8-week strategy phase (weeks 1-4: artifact creation, weeks 2-3: content strategy, week 4: first article) followed by 12 months of scale (5 articles/week). They manage competitive conflicts through strict team siloing while systematically rolling out process improvements across all clients. The conversation explores how their workflows avoid "AI slop" through living artifacts (ICP, product info, writing guidelines) and sophisticated multi-step pipelines with human oversight at every stage.

## Key Topics

### Speechmatics' Challenge: Scaling for PLG

  - Speechmatics is shifting from a sales-led to a hybrid product-led growth (PLG) motion, driven by the growth of the voice agent space.
  - **Goal:** Achieve a "step change" in AI search visibility to support this PLG motion, as current efforts are too slow.
  - **Differentiators:**
      - Strong non-English language capabilities.
      - Focus on regulated industries (e.g., financial services, legal tech).
  - **Competitors:** Deepgram, Assembly, Sonioxx.

### GrowthX's Solution: Scalable Content Engine

  - GrowthX's service is a "white glove" content creation engine designed to deliver rapid, high-quality content at scale.
  - **Engagement Model:**
      - **Phase 1 (8 weeks):** Strategy & calibration.
          - Week 1: Create V1 of "artifacts" (ICP, product, writing guidelines).
          - Week 2–3: Develop content strategy & roadmap.
          - Week 4: Publish first article for quality calibration.
      - **Phase 2 (12 months):** Scale production to 5 articles/week.
  - **Time to Value:**
      - Early signals within 3–6 months.
      - Significant traffic uptick expected in Phase 2.

### GrowthX's Process: Artifacts & Agentic Workflows

  - The process uses a custom "artifact" knowledge base and agentic workflows to ensure content is relevant and high-quality.
  - **Artifacts:** A "living" knowledge base (ICP, product info, writing guidelines) that provides context for all content.
  - **Agentic Workflows:** Custom pipelines for each step (research, drafting, SEO, fact-checking) that use the artifacts for context.
      - **Research Example:** An agent synthesizes info from multiple sources and self-evaluates quality, rerunning if a score isn't met.
      - **Human-in-the-Loop:** Experts can edit or give commands at any stage (e.g., "add 3 bullet points for a full-stack developer").
  - **Customization:** Workflows are tailored to client needs (e.g., medical doctor review for Biologica).

### Future Handover & Platform Access

  - GrowthX is building a platform to enable client autonomy post-engagement.
  - **Current State:** A white-glove service where GrowthX's team executes all work.
  - **Future State (6+ months):** Clients can log in, select assignments, and execute workflows autonomously, with GrowthX available for additional services.
  - **Today:** Clients receive all created content, artifacts, and the full content OS if the engagement ends.
