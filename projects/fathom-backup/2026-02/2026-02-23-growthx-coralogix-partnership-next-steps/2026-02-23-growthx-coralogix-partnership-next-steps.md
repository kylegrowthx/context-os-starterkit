## Context

Coralogix is working with GrowthX to transform its content strategy. The company has approximately 1,100 published pages that lack technical depth and corporate voice polish. The goal is to increase content volume and quality while adding personality through developer relations (DevRel) initiatives. GrowthX specializes in AI-powered content production for technical B2B clients, managing a team of 30+ editors and deploying complex AI workflows (fact-checking agents, deep research, internal linking) to ensure quality at scale.

The partnership model involves a collaborative approach: Coralogix defines artifacts (company context, personas, products), prioritizes content topics from thousands of opportunities, calibrates quality standards with initial samples, and provides subject matter expert (SME) review for high-risk content. GrowthX handles the full content lifecycle—research, creation, editing, optimization, and continuous refresh—freeing Coralogix to focus on product insight rather than content management.

## Key Topics

### Coralogix's Content Goals

  - **Problem:** Coralogix's ~1,100 pages are not technical enough, and the brand voice is too corporate.
  - **Solution:**
      - Increase content volume and technical depth.
      - Add personality and a human face via DevRel content.
      - Improve SEO for existing articles.

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Content Engine

  - **Core Function:** An AI engine with human editors that accelerates content creation and learning.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Context Engineering:** GrowthX builds a detailed "artifact" (company, persona, product info) to guide all content.
    2.  **Workflow Execution:** Complex AI pipelines handle research, fact-checking, and enrichment.
    3.  **Human Oversight:** A team of 30+ editors provides judgment, ensuring quality and brand alignment.

### Client Collaboration Model

  - **Input Points:**
      - **Artifacts:** Coralogix reviews the artifact to ensure accuracy.
      - **Topics:** Coralogix helps prioritize from a map of thousands of opportunities.
      - **Calibration:** Coralogix reviews initial content samples to set the quality bar.
  - **Review Cadence:**
      - **Low-Risk Content:** GrowthX publishes directly.
      - **High-Risk Content:** Coralogix's SMEs provide final review.
  - **Rationale:** This model frees Coralogix from managing the content lifecycle (e.g., updating 1,100 pages) to focus on providing product insight.

### Case Study: Augment Code

  - **Context:** A highly competitive space (vs. Cursor, Copilot).
  - **Strategy:**
      - **New Content Sections:** Created `/guides`, `/learn`, and `/tools` to capture non-branded search intent.
      - **High-Intent Topics:** Focused on head-to-head comparisons (e.g., "Cursor vs. Copilot").
  - **Outcome:** GrowthX-generated content drove 3-4x more traffic than the client's entire original blog.
