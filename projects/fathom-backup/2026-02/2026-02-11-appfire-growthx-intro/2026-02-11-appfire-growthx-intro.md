## Context

Appfire is a portfolio of SaaS products built on top of Atlassian, Salesforce, Monday.com, and other platforms. The company has built a strong resale channel but faces existential pressure as Atlassian shifts its partner program away from resale (low-margin) toward direct enterprise sales. Catherine Solazzo, recently hired as CMO (16-year IBM veteran), is leading a strategic repositioning: rebalancing from partner-led to direct-to-marketplace sales, diversifying across other platforms, and establishing Appfire's own brand identity separate from "Atlassian's number one partner." This requires both tactical website fixes (domain consolidation from acquisitions, technical docs indexing, IA refresh) and strategic narrative work to establish standalone brand authority.

GrowthX (founded by Marcel Santilli, former CMO at IBM, HP, Service Titan, DeepGram) combines an AI visibility platform (CheckThat.ai), proprietary "agentic framework" for content execution, and a full-service agency (40+ editors, designers, publishers) to drive compounding organic growth. Marcel built and scaled content hubs at IBM (securedintelligence.com became highest ROI program) and HP (techbeacon.com). GrowthX's flagship offering is a 3-week "Strategy Sprint" (required for all engagements) followed by ongoing execution across keyword research, content production, technical fixes, and AI/SEO tracking.

## Key Topics

### Appfire's Strategic Pivot

  - Appfire is repositioning its brand to stand alone, moving away from a "man behind the curtain" identity tied to the Atlassian ecosystem.
  - **Primary Driver:** Atlassian's chaotic partner program changes, including a shift to direct sales and a de-emphasis on resale partners, which threatens Appfire's existing partner channel.
  - **New GTM Strategy:**
      - Rebalance from partner-led to direct-to-marketplace sales.
      - Diversify into other marketplaces (e.g., Salesforce, Monday) to reduce dependency on Atlassian.
  - **Current Bottlenecks:**
      - Consolidating legacy domains from acquisitions.
      - Indexing a previously unindexed technical documentation site.
      - Refreshing the website's IA and content to reflect the new brand narrative.

### GrowthX's End-to-End Service

  - GrowthX provides a platform and service to drive compounding organic growth by optimizing a company's website for both human buyers and AI agents.
  - **Service Components:**
      - **Platform:** `CheckThat.ai` for AI visibility tracking and an "agentic framework" for content execution.
      - **Agency:** A full-service team providing:
          - Strategy & SEO/AEO
          - Content Production (40+ editors, designers, publishers)
          - Technical fixes (e.g., broken links)
  - **Process:**
      - All engagements begin with a mandatory 3-week "Strategy Sprint" to build a strong foundation.
      - **Deliverables:** V1 brand artifacts, a content strategy, and a keyword universe analysis.
  - **Case Study (Augment Code):**
      - Grew organic traffic significantly in 6–7 months, nearly eliminating paid ad spend.
