## Context

Fieldwire is a mobile-first field management platform for trade and specialty contractors. The company is part of Hilti's portfolio and faces competitive pressure from larger all-in-one platforms like Procore and Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC). This engagement is a strategic bet to establish organic authority and drive ARR growth by repositioning Fieldwire as the best-in-class solution for field management work—where it has a clear product advantage. The engagement spans 8 weeks with deliverables including content strategy, 15–20 new articles, and a calibrated AI content engine. GrowthX will work directly with Fieldwire's marketing team (led by Jessica Miller), with support from product, content (Thais, based in Paris), and paid marketing (Alex, in France).

## Key Topics

### Strategic Context & Goals

  - **Problem:** Over-reliance on paid channels; lack of a cohesive, full-funnel organic content strategy.
  - **Solution:** Build a strategic organic engine to improve lead generation and establish brand authority.
  - **Brand Repositioning:**
      - **From:** "All-in-one tool for the job site."
      - **To:** "Best app for the field."
      - **Rationale:** This focuses messaging on Fieldwire's core strength, avoiding a direct "all-in-one" competition with broader platforms like Procore and Autodesk.

### Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

  - **Primary Target:** Trade & Specialty Contractors.
      - **Trade:** Electrical, mechanical, plumbing, drywall, carpentry.
      - **Specialty:** Civil, facade, solar, fire stopping, security.
  - **Key Differentiator:** Fieldwire excels with increasing complexity, making it ideal for highly coordinated trades like electrical and HVAC.
  - **Secondary Target:** General Contractors (GCs) who perform "self-performing work" (i.e., execute field tasks directly).

### Buyer Personas & Journey

  - **Core Personas:**
      - **Champions:** Project Managers, Superintendents, Project Executives.
      - **New Focus:** C-suite (Head/VP of Ops, CFO, CEO, CIO) to address enterprise-level concerns (data, security, integrations).
  - **Pre-Fieldwire State:**
      - **Analog:** Pen & paper, Excel, WhatsApp.
      - **Competitors:** Procore, Autodesk Construction Cloud (ACC), PlanGrid.
  - **Why Fieldwire Wins:**
      - **Ease of Use & Adoption:** Faster ROI.
      - **Partnership:** Active product feedback loop, white-glove onboarding.
      - **PlanGrid Migration:** A direct replacement for users forced to leave PlanGrid, which ACC is sunsetting.
  - **Common Objections & Responses:**
      - **"Not an all-in-one tool":** Position as a specialized, best-in-class field management solution that can coexist with an office-based PM tool.
      - **GC mandates a different tool:** Emphasize the need for a trade contractor's own tool to maintain control of their information.
      - **Enterprise concerns:** Address data security, risk, and integrations directly.

### 8-Week Sprint Plan

  - **Phase 1 (Weeks 1–2): Discovery & Artifacts**
      - Deep dive on Fieldwire's product, ICP, and goals.
      - Create core data artifacts: Company Context, ICP, Writing Guidelines, and Features.
  - **Phase 2 (Weeks 2–3): Strategy & Calibration**
      - Develop a long-term content strategy (5–7 topic clusters) based on artifacts and competitive analysis.
      - Create a "calibration article" using the new AI workflow for Fieldwire's review to fine-tune the engine.
  - **Phase 3 (Weeks 4–8): Production & Analysis**
      - Ramp up content production to 5 articles/week.
      - Deliver 15–20 published articles by sprint end.
      - Analyze performance using GSC, GA, SEMrush, and GrowthX's proprietary AI citation tool, Check That.

### Paid & Organic Synergy

  - **Paid Channels:** Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, app stores.
  - **High-Performing Paid Strategy:** Competitor campaigns (e.g., bidding on "Procore" keywords).
  - **Organic Opportunity:** Replicate this success with competitor comparison articles (e.g., "Top 10 Construction Softwares for X Persona").
  - **Upcoming Campaign:** A "Blockbuster launch" in May for new AI features (tasks, photo captures).
      - **Action:** Create organic authority content on AI in construction to support the launch.
