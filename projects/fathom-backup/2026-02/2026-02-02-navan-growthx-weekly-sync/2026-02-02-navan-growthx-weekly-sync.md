## Key Topics

### Content Strategy: Refresh vs. New

  - **Problem:** An old article (`AI-powered corporate travel platforms`) ranks for high-intent, bottom-of-funnel (BoFu) keywords but has a search intent mismatch.
  - **Solution:** A "pillar-and-satellite" strategy to capture this opportunity.
      - **Pillar:** Refresh the existing article for its primary keyword, ensuring it doesn't cannibalize other listicles.
      - **Satellites:** Create new, targeted articles for each long-tail keyword to provide specific, in-depth content.

### Legal Review Process

  - **Requirement:** All competitor facts in public content must be sourced and substantiated for legal record-keeping.
  - **Solution:** Leverage Navan's `compare` pages as a verified source.
      - **Rationale:** These pages pull data directly from G2 reviews, providing third-party substantiation that simplifies legal approval.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will create internal "mini battle cards" from this data for consistent, streamlined use.
  - **Internal Battle Cards:** Moran's internal battle cards are too aggressive for public content and cannot be shared externally.
