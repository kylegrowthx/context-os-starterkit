## Key Topics

### Pricing & Contract Negotiation

  - Binti's primary concern is the proposal's price, which is "significantly above" expectations.
      - **Rationale:** Binti is highly cost-conscious and focused on profitability after a large hiring sprint.
  - GrowthX confirmed flexibility on pricing due to the personal connection.
      - **Offer:** A significant discount on both the monthly fee and the one-time setup fee.
      - **Constraint:** The contract's "termination for breach" clause is non-negotiable, as it is standard for all clients.
  - Binti's CFO is particularly concerned about the one-time setup fee, as the company just incurred other large one-time payouts (e.g., hiring bonuses).

### Partnership Model & Workflow

  - **Communication:** A shared Slack channel and Notion workspace for daily updates, drafts, and feedback.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Weeks 1–4:** Develop foundational artifacts (personas, competitive analysis) and calibrate with Binti.
    2.  **Week 4+:** Begin drafting articles. Binti provides feedback via Google Docs comments.
    3.  **Publishing:** GrowthX stages approved articles in Binti's CMS.
  - **Content Strategy:**
      - **Goal:** Drive organic traffic via search-optimized content.
      - **Method:** Keyword analysis targets high-volume terms and opportunities to outrank competitors.
      - **Format:** Text-based articles (e.g., guides, programmatic content). Video is not included but can be discussed later.

### Technical Implementation (WordPress)

  - Binti's site is on WordPress; a potential platform migration is being evaluated.
  - **Requirement:** Binti's contractors must create a simple WordPress template for GrowthX to publish into.
      - **Template needs:** Table of Contents, author/date fields, table support, and a cover image.
  - **Context:** Binti noted past difficulties with its contractors, which may require careful planning for this step.
  - **CMS Recommendations:** GrowthX suggested Webflow and Framer as good alternatives to WordPress for Binti's needs.
