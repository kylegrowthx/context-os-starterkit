## Summary

Marcel and Tyler finalized Vercel's partnership terms with Cody Henshaw: a 12-month contract ramping from $20k/month (months 1-6) to $30k/month (months 7-12), eliminating the original $40k upfront fee. ## Context

GrowthX is executing a content marketing and SEO partnership with Vercel, one of the leading Next.js and Vercel Edge deployment platforms. Vercel manages a sprawling product ecosystem (Next.js, v0, AI SDK, AI Gateway, ShadCN, Nuxt, Svelte, and 100+ open-source projects), and Cody Henshaw was recently tasked with consolidating digital strategy across all properties. The original SoW proposed a 6-month engagement with $40k upfront plus $20k/month, but this conflicted with Vercel's budget constraints and GrowthX's need for annual recurring revenue (ARR) and monthly cash flow. By reworking the deal into a single 12-month contract with a 6-month discount period, both parties achieve their financial objectives while maintaining scope and allowing GrowthX to prove value before full execution in months 7-12.

## Key Topics

### Commercials & Procurement

  - **Problem:** The original SoW's $40k upfront fee and 6-month term conflicted with Vercel's budget and GrowthX's need for ARR.
  - **Solution:** A single 12-month contract with a ramped monthly fee.
      - **Months 1–6:** $20k/month (discounted to align with Vercel's current agency spend).
      - **Months 7–12:** $30k/month (full scope).
  - **Procurement Process:**
    1.  Cody signs the SoW as a letter of intent.
    2.  Cody submits the SoW to Vercel's Zip platform.
    3.  Zip manages the formal vendor onboarding and legal review (MSA redlines).
  - **Timeline:** Cody will confirm the estimated duration of the Zip process.

### Project Scope & Strategy

  - **Initial Publishing Path:** Content will launch on the `/i` index, a copy of the blog with a flexible content model.
      - **Rationale:** Bypasses the high-level approval required for the main blog and product pages.
      - **Requirements:** A dedicated sitemap and footer links are needed to ensure proper indexation.
  - **Growth Levers:**
      - **AI Gateway:** Optimize for model-specific traffic.
      - **Guides:** Target long-tail, buyer-intent questions.
      - **"Stacks" Pages:** Use programmatic SEO for new pages launching soon.
      - **Future Opportunity:** Create a high-value "AI chat" tool combining the AI SDK and Gateway.
  - **Strategic Partnership:** GrowthX will provide feedback and engineering resources for Vercel's LLM visibility tool, which will be open-sourced.

### Kickoff & Pre-Work

  - **Kickoff Date:** Confirmed for **February 23rd**.
      - **Rationale:** Allows time for procurement and GrowthX's pre-kickoff research.
  - **Data Access:** GrowthX needs view-only access to:
      - **Amplitude:** Vercel's primary analytics tool (data via Rudderstack CDP).
      - **Google Search Console (GSC):** For search performance data.
  - **Internal Stakeholders:** Cody identified key contacts for early interviews to map the buyer journey and internal politics:
      - **Dan Fine (Product Marketing):** "Extension of G's brain," critical for understanding the CMO's vision.
      - **Eric Dodds (Product Marketing):** Another key resource.
      - **Head of PR:** Shaping the 5-year brand narrative.
