GrowthX's organic content program delivered strong Q4 results—224 articles published, 250 new keyword positions, and 50% Domain Authority growth—with GrowthX content now driving 30% of site clicks and 80% of impressions. ## Context

Imubit engaged GrowthX in April 2025 to address three core challenges: building a clear market narrative around closed-loop AI optimization, shifting early-stage buyer education from sales-driven activities to scalable organic content, and establishing operational capacity to avoid review bottlenecks. GrowthX deployed its Atlas platform (AI-drafting + human editing) to execute 7 pieces per week. The organic strategy proved effective at the awareness and consideration stages, establishing Imubit as a thought leader in the AI optimization space while supporting the sales team's early-stage lead nurturing. With Imubit now adding New Breed for paid demand generation in Q1 2026, the organic content strategy is being refined to feed into paid campaigns and to leverage AI visibility tracking (CheckThat) as a competitive differentiator in evaluation-stage conversations where LLM citations influence buyer research behavior.
