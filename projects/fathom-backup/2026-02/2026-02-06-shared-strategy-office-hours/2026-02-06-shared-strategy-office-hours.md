## Key Topics

### New Training Program: Data-Driven Growth

  - **Problem:** The team defaults to creating new content, a reactive approach with diminishing returns.
  - **Solution:** A new training program to build a proactive, data-driven growth analysis muscle.
  - **Framework:** Analyze the content lifecycle to diagnose issues at each stage:
      - Indexing → Impressions → Engagement → Conversion
  - **Kickoff Plan:**
      - **Tuesday:** Kyle will lead a deep dive using early-stage and mature account examples.
      - **Friday:** Team members will lead live walkthroughs of their own accounts for discussion.

### Airbyte: Pivoting Content for AI Engineers

  - **Problem:** Low engagement (14–19%) on developer-centric content clusters.
  - **Hypothesis:** The content is too high-level and lacks the specific, technical examples developers need.
  - **Strategy:** Source real-world examples to provide authentic, actionable content.
      - **Internal:** Brainstorm with Mario (SE) and analyze Gong call transcripts.
      - **External:** Find respected voices on platforms like YouTube and TikTok.
  - **Visuals:** Experiment with Kirkland for a custom pipeline to generate repeatable, high-quality architecture diagrams, as NanoBanana Pro is too time-consuming.

### CheckTar Launch Performance

  - **X (Twitter):** High engagement, but suspected bot activity makes the data unreliable.
  - **LinkedIn:** Underperforming typical posts (421 likes vs. 500+).
      - **Context:** The launch coincided with major model releases (Opus, ChatGPT), creating significant market noise.

### General Updates

  - **Reporting:** January reports were well-received. Erik uses Gamma to convert Notion docs to decks, and Kyle noted Claude can now create PowerPoints.
