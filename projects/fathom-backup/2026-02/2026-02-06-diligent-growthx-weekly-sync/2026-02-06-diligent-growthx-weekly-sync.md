## Context

This is a recurring strategic sync between GrowthX and Diligent around content strategy, product research, and technical integrations. GrowthX handles B2B content marketing for Diligent's suite of products, while Diligent develops Check That (an AI visibility index platform) and runs internal research initiatives. The relationship involves coordinating content calendars, leveraging Diligent's proprietary research and templates, managing tool migrations (Scrunch to Check That), and optimizing content for both SEO and AI visibility outcomes.

The broader context: Ahrefs data shows SEO still drives more qualified leads than AI visibility alone. This shifted the strategic direction from aggressive AI visibility tactics toward leveraging original research, addressing real sales objections, and using comparative content more tactically for specific business units where it's most effective. Diligent's new educational content library (previously app-exclusive) opens a new source of SEO-friendly templates and downloadables that can strengthen content pillars across the product line.
