## Context

Yourco is a frontline employee communication platform pursuing rapid growth through improved content marketing and AI visibility. GrowthX is managing their content and Check That (AI visibility index) strategy. The meeting focused on reconciling Yourco's ambitious growth goals (15% monthly growth in 2026) with content performance data, which showed strong January results (~75% of 25-conversion goal) but revealed a strategic misalignment: broad evaluation-stage content wasn't driving conversions. Analysis of recent ChatGPT user conversions showed they came from highly specific, use-case-focused searches—not generic platform evaluations. This insight drove a decision to pivot Check That prompt tracking and new content development toward specific use cases (reporting by function, employee surveys, attendance tracking) and "vs. competitor" comparisons. The team also discussed leveraging customer quotes and case studies early (before full case study launch end-of-month) to boost social proof in middle and bottom-funnel content, and resolving technical indexing issues that are skewing performance metrics.
