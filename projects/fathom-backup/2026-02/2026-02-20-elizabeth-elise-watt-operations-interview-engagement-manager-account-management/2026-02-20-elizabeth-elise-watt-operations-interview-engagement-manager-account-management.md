### Background & Motivation

  - **Career Path:**
      - Intl. Rescue Committee (IRC): Project/Program Manager for an entrepreneurship program.
      - Freelance: SEO, copywriting, and content strategy.
      - AirDNA: Full-time transition to content strategy.

      - **Account Management:** Bridge past experience (working with businesses at the IRC) with current content expertise.
      - **AI Impact:** Excited by the scale of what's achievable with AI, citing a 3-month AI-powered outcome as potentially equivalent to a year of traditional work.

### Content Strategy: AirDNA Case Study

  - **Initial Strategy:** High-volume content (\~200 articles) to build authority and cover broad industry keywords.
  - **Problem:** Diminishing returns from "quick wins" after \~1 year.
  - **Data-Driven Pivot:**
      - **Analysis:** A performance deep dive revealed a dozen articles drove 80% of product conversions.
      - **Insight:** High-converting content focused on product tutorials, unique data insights, and middle/bottom-of-funnel keywords.
      - **New Strategy:** Halved content output to focus on fewer, higher-quality articles and optimizations.
  - **Result:** Doubled the number of converting articles in 3 months with half the output.

### AI & AEO Approach

  - **Daily AI Use:** Employs AI for tasks like creating SOPs and assisting with writing.
  - **Workflow Automation:** Built complex workflows in AirOps, including one that converts podcast transcripts into SEO-optimized blog articles.
  - **AEO (AI Engine Optimization) Strategy:**
      - **Core Principle:** High-quality content with unique data/perspective performs well for both SEO and AEO.
      - **Tactics:**
          - **Structure:** Optimize for LLM snippet preferences.
          - **Technical SEO:** Implement schema to improve crawlability.
  - **GrowthX's AEO Tool:** George introduced GrowthX's new tool, which combines AI visibility tracking with SEO/technical audits and a content workflow layer to connect data to strategy.

### Reporting & Client Onboarding

  - **Reporting Cadence:**
      - **Monthly:** Comprehensive reports tracking keyword growth, rankings, AI citations, engaged sessions, conversions, and bounce rate.
      - **Quarterly:** Audits comparing ROI across content types (e.g., blogs vs. research reports) to optimize resource allocation.
  - **GrowthX Client Onboarding:**
      - **Process:** An 8-week strategy sprint to audit systems (GA4, GSC), define strategy, and calibrate content with a sample article.
      - **Technical SEO:** A foundational audit is performed to ensure content can be indexed and perform.
  - **Client Success Factors:**
      - **Champion:** A marketing-experienced client who trusts GrowthX's process.
      - **Review Process:** A streamlined review process (e.g., one primary reviewer) to avoid becoming "word babysitters."
      - **Access:** Technical access to systems and publishing platforms.
  - **ICP (Ideal Customer Profile):** GrowthX now uses a formal ICP to score and qualify prospects, proactively addressing potential blockers.
