## Context

GrowthX is launching a new AI OS platform that will fundamentally reshape how the company delivers value to clients. However, a critical gap has emerged: the product and business strategies are misaligned. Marcel's recent ELT handbook implies CheckThat (GrowthX's AI visibility index product) will be bundled into the new OS as a core component, but the go-to-market team (Ella and Andi) has been planning to upsell CheckThat as a standalone, premium add-on. This creates two conflicting strategies that must be resolved immediately. The first client renewals are scheduled for May, giving the team only 10 weeks to clarify positioning, pricing, packaging, and customer communication. Without clarity, the team risks confusing clients with contradictory pricing conversations, botching launch execution (as happened with AirOps), or abandoning revenue opportunities. The conversation focused on building a shared mental model between product, operations, and finance to align on what customers can buy and at what price.
