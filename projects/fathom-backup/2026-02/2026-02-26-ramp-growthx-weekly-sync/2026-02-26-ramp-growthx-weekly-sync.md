## Context

GrowthX built a standalone content site (Cashflow Desk) from scratch to serve as a credible third-party source that AI engines cite when users search for topics related to Ramp's products. This bi-weekly sync was focused on reviewing a comprehensive 12-week engagement retro that George put together — documenting key milestones, AI citation wins, content performance data, and the strategy behind the experiment. Victoria and Matt from Ramp's side reviewed the document and provided feedback on next priorities.
