## Key Topics

### EM Onboarding

  - **Problem:** The current onboarding is too high-level, forcing new EMs to rely on ad-hoc questions for tactical work.
  - **Solution:** Formalize a structured, tactical program.
      - **Foundation:** Use Marisol's existing work-in-progress as a starting point.
      - **New Elements:** Add shadowing/sidecar experiences, inspect-and-assign workflows, and clear graduation criteria.
  - **Next Step:** Review the WIP document with Marisol and George in a future session to identify gaps.

### Backlink Strategy

  - **Problem:** Current backlink vendors are low-quality and risky, leading to client churn (e.g., AmiBit, Exec, Interwell).
  - **Decision:** Stop offering backlinks as a service.
  - **Action:** Kyle will investigate new, higher-quality vendors for clients with backlinks in their Statement of Work (SoW). The goal is to offer a pass-through partnership or direct connection, not to manage the workflow internally.

### AmiBit Agency Collaboration

  - **Problem:** AmiBit's new paid agency, New Seed, requested all GrowthX artifacts (Content OS, QBRs), raising concerns about competitive risk and trust.
  - **Solution:** Provide a curated "GrowthX brief" instead of raw files.
      - **Content:** A high-level summary of strategy, content clusters, and goals.
      - **Rationale:** Demonstrates collaboration without exposing proprietary data.
      - **Method:** Use Claude Codework to generate the brief from existing documents.

### OS & Check That Pricing

  - **Problem:** Conflicting guidance on the Check That/OS product relationship prevents final pricing decisions.
      - **Unclear Relationship:** Is Check That a standalone product, a feature within OS, or both?
      - **Bundling Strategy:** How will OS and Check That be packaged for different client types?
      - **Trial Periods:** What are the rules for 90-day vs. 2-week free trials?
  - **Action:** Ella will present a table of potential product scenarios to Daniel and Marcel for clarification.

### Matthew's Claude Skill

  - **Tool:** A Claude skill for EMs to generate reports from Notion and Slack data.
  - **Risk:** The skill can leak private information (e.g., DMs) if not carefully reviewed, as its data access is scoped to the individual user's connected accounts.
  - **Guidance:**
      - Always review reports locally before sharing.
      - Create a dedicated, scoped "EM Clod" to manage data access and mitigate privacy risks.

### Capacity Planning

  - **Problem:** The team is at 79/83 capacity slots, with a 9-slot loss from recent departures and reassignments, eliminating the previous buffer.
  - **Analysis:** Matthew's draft plan shows the team is at full capacity through March 21.
  - **Action:** Matthew will finalize a formal document detailing the capacity crunch and present it to Bridget, advocating for immediate backfills.
