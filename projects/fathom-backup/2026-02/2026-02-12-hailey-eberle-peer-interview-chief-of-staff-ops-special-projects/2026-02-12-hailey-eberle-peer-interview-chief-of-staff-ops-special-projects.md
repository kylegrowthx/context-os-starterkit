  - **Role:** New Ventures team at ThredUp, evaluating new business ideas and running pilots.
  - **AI as a "Personal Assistant":** Uses AI to manage a high workload, from financial modeling to data storytelling for board presentations.
  - **Board Slide Creation Workflow:**

      - **Prompt:** Requests a narrative and 2026 forecast, specifying key metrics (e.g., GMV).
      - **Output:** Receives a draft narrative, which is then refined and integrated into slides.
  - **AI Limitations & Oversight:**

  - **Process Automation Example:**
      - **Goal:** Automate the workflow for cleaning rejected, stained products.
      - **Process:** AI scans product tags/brands → generates a manifest → shared with an external vendor.
      - **Rationale:** Reduces manual data entry and improves efficiency in the distribution centers.
