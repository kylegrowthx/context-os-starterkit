## Summary

GrowthX and Ramp reviewed Cashflow Desk's citation performance, with the Capital One/Brex and Mercury Alternatives articles driving significant citations. The team shifted focus from website traffic to citation rate optimization as the primary metric influencing AI visibility, and established a plan to track articles mentioning RAMP using Scrunch tags to quantify the correlation between citations and RAMP.com's visibility score.

---

## Context

Cashflow Desk continues to demonstrate strong performance as an independent, third-party fintech research platform. The partnership with Ramp leverages this editorial independence to generate valuable citations in AI models without requiring explicit brand mentions. Early evidence from a narrow treasury topic shows a direct correlation: when Cashflow Desk mentions of RAMP increased, the visibility score on that topic shifted from flat to rising—a controlled experiment demonstrating proof of concept. The team is now scaling this insight by adding technical infrastructure (Scrunch tags) and expanding the analysis to answer the central question: how many citations from Cashflow Desk equal an X percent bump in RAMP.com's visibility score?
