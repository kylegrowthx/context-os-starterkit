## Context

Yourco's SMS employee communication platform is driving strong AEO (AI Engine Optimization) results, particularly on Google Search and Perplexity. After months of prompt refinement and content optimization, Yourco has achieved top rankings for "SMS-based employee communication platform" with 20%+ citation share, a leading indicator of content authority and discoverability in AI overviews and LLM responses. However, conversion remains a bottleneck at 3/month, which Jessica and Carrie identified as stemming from generic use-case pages that lack conversion clarity. GrowthX's strategy pivots to two initiatives: (1) redesigned use-case pages with clear value propositions and dedicated CTAs to support Jessica's increased SEM budget, and (2) content promotion through Google Search Console's newly visible longer, conversational queries flowing from Gemini and AI Overview interactions. A recent staging workflow issue (missed handover during team change) has been resolved, and both teams are now ahead of schedule. A batch of new content is not indexing, which Carrie is investigating as a separate operational issue.
