## Context

Vizcom is a software platform that helps industrial designers rapidly visualize and iterate on physical product ideas—bridging the gap between digital conception and real-world manufacturing. The company is navigating a critical market shift: as AI-generated imagery ("slop") has devalued digital renders as a signal of quality, physical prototypes have become the new marker of effort and intent. This positioning requires a content strategy that reaches designers where they evaluate, compare, and research solutions—increasingly through AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity, not just social media.

GrowthX's previous approach focused on social brand-building with Vizcom as a secondary mention. The new strategy aims to create a compounding content asset on Vizcom's website that captures users during intent-driven searches in AI engines, while maintaining social presence for community building and top-of-funnel awareness. This involves expert curation (designer profiles, trend analysis), first-principles learning content, visual inspiration galleries, and product guides—all designed to reduce friction in the designer's research and inspiration journey.
