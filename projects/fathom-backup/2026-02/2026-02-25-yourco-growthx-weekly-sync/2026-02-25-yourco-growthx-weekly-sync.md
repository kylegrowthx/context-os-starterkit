## Context

Yourco and GrowthX are collaborating on content marketing to drive CheckThat visibility and conversions. Yourco handles content production and editing; GrowthX leads strategy, attribution analysis, and SEO/AEO optimization. The partnership focuses on a practitioner-focused content strategy (best tool articles, how-tos) layered with evaluation content (X vs. Y comparisons) to capture both direct conversions and middle-funnel AEO visibility. A recent EIN field addition to Yourco's free trial form has created conversion friction, requiring a shift toward "Book a Demo" CTAs aligned with enterprise sales strategy. Technical SEO improvements (tags, internal linking) and content gap closure are key near-term priorities to defend high-performing content and capture new opportunities.

## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Attribution

  - **February Metrics:** Record high traffic, clicks, and impressions, but only 6 conversions from SEO/AEO.
  - **Attribution Analysis:** A 60-day GA4 analysis of "conversion signals" (not just final conversions) revealed key trends:
      - **Winning Formats:** Question-framed titles perform best for both AEO and SEO.
      - **Strongest Subcluster:** Scheduling and call management content.
      - **Conversion Driver:** Practitioner-focused content (e.g., "Best Tool for X") drives conversions.
      - **AEO Driver:** Evaluation content (e.g., "X vs. Y") drives AEO visibility but not direct conversions.
  - **Actionable Insight:** Optimize CTAs on high-converting practitioner content to maximize conversions.

### Conversion Friction & CTA Strategy

  - **Problem:** A new EIN field on the free trial form creates conversion friction.
  - **Solution:** Prioritize "Book a Demo" CTAs over "Try for Free."
      - **Rationale:** Aligns with the enterprise sales focus and avoids the friction of the free trial form.
      - **Placement:** Move the "Book a Demo" CTA higher on blog pages.
  - **Long-Term Fix:** A 2–3 month project to simplify the free trial form by pre-filling data and using AI/third-party tools to verify EINs.

### Content Gaps & Refreshes

  - **New Content:**
      - "Best Tools for Employee Surveys" → Focus on frontline use cases (e.g., no email access) to differentiate from the existing "Best Automated Survey Platforms" article.
  - **Refreshes:**
      - **Emergency Content:** Optimize for AEO visibility, as it currently has none.
      - **Training & Shift Management:** Add comparison elements and promote the handbook.
      - **Declining Pages:** Refresh high-performing survey pages to defend their position.
      - **Prompt-Driven:** Create new articles or refresh existing ones to directly match specific AEO prompts.

### Technical SEO & Content Structure

  - **Blog Categorization:** Use broad, multi-select tags (e.g., Employee Communication, Safety & Compliance) for categorization.
      - **Rationale:** Tags add keywords to page code without creating new URLs or requiring complex redirects.
  - **Internal Linking:** The internal linking project is complete, with pages now linked within their respective content clusters.
  - **Artifacts:**
      - **Correction:** The "Enterprise Bridge" artifact is misdescribed as an HRIS integration. It is a one-way corporate communication layer.
      - **Process:** Ensure all content edits are immediately reflected in artifacts to prevent future errors.
