## Context

Rimkus paused content publishing three weeks ago due to internal concerns over legal liability from technical building code content. While a less technical content approach is being reviewed by a technical expert, this process is unsustainable because it requires pulling billable experts (roof assessors, inspectors) from client work. GrowthX has successfully used standard disclaimer language with other clients to address similar liability concerns and believes clarification from Rimkus's legal team could be the quickest path to resuming operations without compromising quality or strategy.
