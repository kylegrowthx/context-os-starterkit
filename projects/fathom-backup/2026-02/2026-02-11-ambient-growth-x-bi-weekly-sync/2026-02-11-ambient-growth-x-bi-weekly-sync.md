## Key Topics

### Performance & Reporting Challenges

  - **GA4 Data Pollution:** High-volume, low-quality bot traffic from China is skewing global GA4 metrics.
      - **Solution:** Use "engaged sessions" (10+ seconds, conversion, or multi-page view) as the primary success metric to filter out bots.
  - **LLM Referrals:** Traffic from LLMs (ChatGPT, Perplexity) is low but growing, especially from Gemini.
      - **Rationale:** Gemini's close tie to Google search suggests its traffic will grow with SEO success.
  - **Keyword Tracking:** The current aggregate count of 177 tracked prompts is insufficient for executive reporting.
      - **Requirement:** Report on performance for specific, high-value keywords (e.g., "weapons detection") to demonstrate tangible wins.
      - **Context:** Executives will test keywords, so data must show a high probability of citation, not just a possibility.

### Competitive Landscape & Strategic Pivot

  - **ZeroEyes:** A key competitor focused on gun detection for schools.
      - **Strategy:** Uses programmatic SEO ("PSEO") for state-specific gun violence reports.
      - **Challenge:** Ambient, the inventor of AI gun detection, is not recognized as the leader in this high-visibility use case.
  - **Volt:** A competitor with a 2K Ahrefs traffic rating that is growing slowly (4.5% in 3 months).
  - **Verkada:** The market leader, but its traffic growth is stagnant (3% in 3 months).
  - **Strategic Pivot:** Shift from broad content to focused "content clusters" to build topical authority and communicate a clear strategy.
      - **Rationale:** The current content feels scattered, making it difficult to explain the strategy internally.
      - **Goal:** Create a "mental map" of content clusters tied to the customer journey (awareness → decision).

### New Content Strategy & Tactics

  - **Content Focus:** Prioritize content for high-priority industries and building types.
      - **Industries:** High-tech, manufacturing, data centers (emerging), universities.
      - **Building Types:** Corporate campuses, manufacturing plants, hospitals.
  - **PSEO Opportunity:** Explore PSEO using proprietary data to create a defensible competitive moat.
      - **Data Source:** A customer success data collection exercise is underway, gathering metrics like mean time to response.
  - **Keyword Research:** Use customer language from Clary call transcripts to identify high-intent keywords.
  - **Content Quality:** Improve consistency and creativity.
      - **Feedback:** Content quality varies, and messaging can be repetitive.
      - **Action:** Refine prompt instructions to ensure higher quality and more varied expression.
