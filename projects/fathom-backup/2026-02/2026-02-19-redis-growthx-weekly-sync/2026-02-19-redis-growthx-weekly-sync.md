## Context

This is a strategic checkpoint between GrowthX (content/marketing agency) and Redis (database software company) on quarterly progress. Redis's marketing org is preparing an e-staff review for executives and needs concrete evidence of AI visibility growth and content-driven lead generation impact. GrowthX launched "Check That," an internal AI visibility tool that replaces Scrunch and offers a shared prompt library with historical data across categories. However, the new tool is in a 90-day trial with bugs (jumbled data after category switches), so Erik is using legacy Scrunch data for the e-staff report while the engineering team debugs.

The team also identified a major bot traffic problem that inflated their direct traffic metrics—a known issue affecting the industry that's coming from bots with 1366x1366 screen resolution originating in Singapore and China. This discovery allows them to filter data accurately going forward and trust their organic growth trends.
