## Summary

Deepgram and GrowthX aligned on a strategic pivot from speech-to-text (STT) competitive content to original text-to-speech (TTS) narratives targeting enterprise use cases like healthcare. The team committed to increasing developer-focused content on platform integrations (AWS, Google Cloud) over smaller competitor comparisons, with console signups as the primary North Star KPI tracking product-led growth motion. GrowthX launched a new daily-updated AIO visibility tool providing granular competitor tracking across prompt-level AI search rankings, enabling data-driven content decisions.

---

## Context

This meeting represented a mid-year strategic realignment for the Deepgram-GrowthX content partnership, which began in September 2025 with clear KPIs around organic traffic growth, console signups, and generative AI search visibility. The team had achieved strong momentum—6K+ new sessions from organic content, 400+ referrals from AI search platforms, and $165k in influenced pipeline—but recognized that the content mix was too narrowly focused on STT and competitive articles against 11 Labs. Troy Blanchard (Deepgram's content lead) advocated for a deliberate shift in narrative: rather than chasing voice quality comparisons, Deepgram should establish thought leadership around enterprise-critical use cases where technical precision matters most (healthcare, life-critical applications) while simultaneously expanding developer reach through integration-focused content on AWS Transcribe, Google Cloud, and OpenAI Whisper.

The meeting also introduced two new operational tools: a refined publishing process to preserve SEO authority when refreshing articles, and an AIO (AI/Generative Engine Optimization) tracking system that moves beyond traditional SEMrush metrics to show daily, prompt-level visibility data. This shift from quantity-focused competitive content to quality-driven product differentiation reflects a broader strategic evolution at Deepgram and positions GrowthX to demonstrate impact through both search and AI search channels.
