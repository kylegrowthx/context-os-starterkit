## Context

Spendesk is experiencing an organic traffic and lead decline, a problem compounded by the rise of LLM-based search alternatives (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.). Traditional content agencies and in-house teams no longer meet the dual challenge of SEO ranking and LLM referral optimization. GrowthX differentiates itself from commodity content solutions by building a fine-tuned AI workflow that combines dynamic artifacts (ICP profiles, brand guidelines, product context), human editorial review at every stage, and continuous engine calibration. Rather than shipping raw LLM output, every piece passes through experienced editors who feed systemic improvements back into the pipeline.

The partnership focuses on Phase 1 (8 weeks, English-only): establishing the content engine, launching a content strategy, building a calibration article, and ramping production to 5 articles per week by week 4. Ollie Bryden and Cristina Maria lead for Spendesk; William Leborgne directs for GrowthX. Success is measured by reversing traffic decline and hitting 800 organic/direct/brand leads per month (from ~600) within 6 months, while protecting existing SEO ranking and positioning Spendesk as a go-to answer in LLM responses for finance leaders and accountants.

## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Reversing Organic Traffic Decline

  - Spendesk's organic traffic and leads have been declining, creating an urgent need for a new content strategy.
  - **Primary Goal:** Reverse the traffic decline and increase organic leads from \~600/month to **800/month** within 6 months.
      - **Definition:** "Organic leads" includes direct and brand traffic, not just traditional SEO.
  - **Secondary Goal:** Prepare for the future of search by optimizing for LLM referral traffic.

### The Solution: GrowthX's AI Content Engine

  - GrowthX's engine is a fine-tuned AI workflow, not a simple LLM API, designed for quality and strategic impact.
  - **Key Components:**
    1.  **Dynamic Artifacts:** The AI workflow is fed dynamic artifacts (ICP profiles, writing guidelines, product info) to ensure content is contextually relevant.
    2.  **Human Editors:** Experienced editors review every piece of content to ensure quality and narrative flow.
    3.  **Engine Calibration:** Editor feedback is used to fine-tune the AI pipeline, ensuring systemic improvements (e.g., tone of voice) rather than one-off edits.
  - **SEO & LLM Strategy:**
      - Optimizing for LLMs (e.g., chunked content, FAQ style) also benefits SEO.
      - The strategy will focus on high-quality content to build E-A-T (Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) and protect existing SEO.

### Spendesk's Value & Target Personas

  - **Core Value Proposition:**
      - **Complete Solution:** One platform for all spend management, replacing manual work and point solutions.
      - **User-Friendly:** High NPS from non-finance users due to an intuitive UI.
      - **Empowering Finance:** Enables control without being a bottleneck, freeing up time for strategic work (forecasting, planning).
  - **Target Personas:**
      - **Primary:** Finance Managers, Controllers, and Heads of Finance (champions/economic buyers).
      - **Secondary:** Accountants and Bookkeepers (daily users who need to see positive sentiment).
  - **Key Pain Points Solved:**
      - Loss of spend control in fast-growing companies.
      - Long, chaotic month-end closes due to scattered data.
      - The trade-off between speed and accuracy in financial reporting.

### Project Plan & Localization

  - **8-Week Phase 1 Timeline:**
      - **W1:** Kickoff & Discovery (today).
      - **W2:** Content Strategy presentation.
      - **W3:** Content Calendar & Calibration Article.
      - **W4+:** Production ramp-up (3 articles/week → 5 articles/week).
  - **Localization (Phase 2):**
      - **Focus:** Phase 1 is English-only to perfect the core engine.
      - **Goal:** Produce 1–2 French test articles in Phase 1 to validate the process.
      - **Method:** True localization (culturally relevant content) will be used, not direct translation.
      - **Challenge:** The main bottleneck for localization is finding and training a native-speaking editor for each market.

### Content Approval Process

  - **Stakeholder-Based Approval:**
      - **Content Team:** Handles general topics (e.g., "12 Spend Management Tools").
      - **Product Marketing:** Reviews product-specific content.
      - **Finance Team:** Reviews deep thought leadership pieces (e.g., "CFO's Toughest Role").
  - **SLA:** 24–48 hour turnaround after a 2-week stakeholder notice.
  - **Goal:** Establish a recurring, predictable review cadence (e.g., a 30-min weekly slot) to streamline approvals.
