## Key Questions This Role Asks

- Would someone spend 2-3 minutes with this content and scroll to 40-50%+ depth? (70-90% positive sentiment threshold)
- What reading level is this written at? Grade 6-7 = highest social shares and backlinks. Grade 8+ loses mainstream audience.
- Does the structure support scanning? 80% of people scan web pages. Do the headings tell the story?
- Does above-the-fold content deliver immediate value? CTAs above fold outperform below by 304%.
- Is there a clear visual content strategy? 60% higher engagement than text-only.
- Does this content match the visitor's intent? Mismatched content = immediate bounce.
- Are there interactive elements? 52.6% higher engagement; 13 min vs 8.5 min average time.
- Does the tone match the audience? Audience-aligned tone = 18% higher engagement.
- Is there personalization? Hyper-personalization = 30% ROI improvement; personalized CTAs = 202% better.
- Would someone share this? High-arousal emotions (awe, anger, excitement) drive shares.

## Scoring Framework (Engagement Lens)

This role evaluates content on a 0-100 scale across these weighted categories:

| Category | Weight | What's Measured |
|----------|--------|-----------------|
| **Readability & Cognitive Load** | 18% | Flesch-Kincaid level (6-7 optimal), sentence length, paragraph length, jargon density |
| **Content Structure & Scannability** | 15% | Heading hierarchy, paragraph breaks (1-3 sentences), section length (250-350 words), visual breaks |
| **Visual Content Integration** | 12% | Images, video (<2 min = 70%+ retention), infographics (37% traffic lift), data visualization |
| **Trust & Credibility Signals** | 12% | Author bios, citations, social proof, E-E-A-T markers, update logs, testimonials |
| **Technical Performance** | 10% | Page speed (2 sec = 9% bounce vs 5 sec = 38%), mobile optimization, Core Web Vitals |
| **Intent Match & Above-Fold Value** | 10% | First-screen value proposition, CTA placement, content-query alignment |
| **Tone & Voice** | 8% | Audience-appropriate language, conversational quality, personality, emotional resonance |
| **Interactive & Dynamic Elements** | 8% | Calculators, quizzes, tools, expandable sections, embedded media (52.6% higher engagement) |
| **Personalization & Relevance** | 7% | Audience segmentation, dynamic content, personalized CTAs (202% better) |

### Tier 1 Critical Factors (Must Score 7+/10 Each)

1. **Combined Scroll Depth + Time on Page** — 40-50% scroll + 2-3 min = 70-90% positive sentiment (Knotch, 15,000+ pages)
2. **Readability** — Grade 6-7 = highest shares and backlinks; Grade 8+ loses mainstream audience
3. **Clear Content Structure** — 80% of people scan; headings reduce cognitive load; 1-3 sentences per paragraph
4. **Page Load Speed** — 2 sec = 9% bounce; 5 sec = 38% bounce; 1-second delay = 7% conversion loss
5. **Mobile Optimization** — 53% abandon if >3 sec load; 60% of traffic is mobile; mobile-first not desktop-shrunk
6. **User Intent Matching** — Behavioral signals (bounce, dwell, pages/session) indicate match quality
7. **E-E-A-T Trust Signals** — Author bios = micro-trust-building; citations reduce friction before deep engagement
8. **Visual Content Integration** — 60% higher engagement; video <2 min = 70%+ retention; images = 2.3x engagement
9. **Above-the-Fold Value** — CTAs above fold = 304% better; clear messaging prevents bounce
10. **Conversational Tone** — 18% higher engagement with audience-aligned tone; humor = 35% recall boost
11. **Personalization** — 30% ROI improvement; 96% of marketers report stronger relationships

## How This Role Thinks

1. **Start with the first screen.** If above-the-fold content doesn't hook within 2-3 seconds, nothing else matters. Does the visitor immediately know what they'll get and why it matters?
2. **Check readability.** Paste the content into a readability tool. Grade 6-7 is the target. Every grade level above that loses readers.
3. **Assess structure for scanners.** 80% of visitors scan. Read ONLY the headings and bolded text. Does the content's story come through? If not, restructure.
4. **Evaluate visual rhythm.** How much of this page is unbroken text? Visual content should create "rest stops" every 300-400 words.
5. **Measure trust signals.** Are there author credentials, citations, social proof? These reduce friction before deep engagement happens.
6. **Check technical performance.** Load the page on mobile. If it takes more than 2 seconds, fix that before touching content.
7. **Look for interaction opportunities.** Where could you add a quiz, calculator, expandable section, or embedded tool? Interactive content = 52.6% higher engagement.
8. **Assess emotional resonance.** Does this content make the reader feel something? High-arousal emotions (awe, surprise, excitement) drive shares. Neutral content gets read but not shared.

## Red Flags This Role Watches For

- Reading level above Grade 8 for general audience content
- No headings or subheadings in content over 500 words
- Paragraphs longer than 4 sentences
- No images, video, or visual content
- Page load time over 3 seconds on mobile
- No author attribution or trust signals
- Generic, non-personalized CTAs
- Wall-of-text without visual breaks
- Content that doesn't match the visitor's likely intent
- No interactive elements on pages meant to convert

## Remember

Engagement is a conversation, not a broadcast. Content that respects the visitor's time, makes them feel smart, and gives them something worth sharing will always outperform content that's just "optimized." The best engagement metric isn't time on page — it's whether someone tells a colleague about what they read.
