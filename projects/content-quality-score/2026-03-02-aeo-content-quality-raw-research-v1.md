## SECTION 3: CONTENT FRESHNESS & RECENCY SIGNALS

### Recency Bias by Platform

#### ChatGPT (Strongest Bias)
- **76.4% of most-cited pages** updated in last 30 days
- Content updated in past 30–90 days cited **significantly more** than older pages
- Visibility drops sharply after **3-6 months** without updates (competitive topics)

#### Perplexity
- **50% of citations from 2025 alone** (half from current year)
- **Content decay begins 2–3 days post-publication**
- Over **70% of cited content updated in past year**
- Proactive refreshing essential for maintaining rankings

#### Google AI Overviews
- **85% of citations from last 2 years**
- **44% from 2025 alone**
- Behaves most like traditional search (less aggressive recency)
- More inclusive of well-established older content

### Freshness Data Points
- Content with **visible "last updated" date:** 1.8× more citations
- Pages updated in **past 30–90 days:** significantly higher citation frequency
- Minor cosmetic updates (dates only) = **no AI visibility improvement**
- Substance changes (examples, data, context) = required for boost
- **Evergreen content:** yearly refresh sufficient; competitive/time-sensitive: quarterly or sooner

### Content Length Statistics
| Metric | Value | Notes |

### Section Length Optimization
- **100–150 words per section:** Sweet spot (boosts to 4.7 citations)

- **No padding:** Unnecessary content decreases AI visibility

### Content Structure Performance
- **Clear H2/H3 hierarchical structure:** 40% more likely to be cited
- **Bullet points and numbered lists:** Preferred over paragraph text
- **Q&A format (direct answers):** Perfect for AI extraction
- **FAQ sections:** +89% citation lift; +221% with schema markup
- **Comparison tables:** Perform exceptionally (structured data extraction)
- **HTML tables:** AI extracts easily; JavaScript-heavy interactive elements struggle

### Format-Specific Impact
- **Tables/bullet points:** 40-61% of AI Overview content uses these
- **Lists showing:** Strong extraction rate
- **Multimodal (text+images+video+data):** 156% higher selection rates
- **Full multimodal + schema:** Up to 317% more citations
