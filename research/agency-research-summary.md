## Competitive Positioning for GrowthX

### Direct Competitors (B2B Content + SEO)
1. Omniscient Digital - Similar positioning, revenue-focused SEO
2. Grow and Convert - Pain-point SEO methodology
3. Siege Media - Content-driven SEO, $100M+ results
4. Animalz - Thought leadership for SaaS
5. Foundation Marketing - Content distribution focus

### Differentiation Opportunities
1. **AI Visibility Focus** - GrowthX's CheckThat product offers unique positioning
2. **Full Stack Approach** - Combining content + SEO + AEO
3. **Compounding Growth Model** - Focus on sustainable, long-term results
4. **B2B Tech Specialization** - Deep expertise in tech-enabled companies

### Market Gaps Identified
- Few agencies combine content marketing + SEO + AEO comprehensively
- Limited agencies with proprietary AI visibility tools
- Opportunity for agencies focused on "compounding growth" positioning
