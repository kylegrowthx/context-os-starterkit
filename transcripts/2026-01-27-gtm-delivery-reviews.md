## Key Topics

### Go-to-Market Performance and Pipeline Health

Marcel outlined the GTM framework: volume, throughput, and velocity across all funnel stages. The team agreed pipeline has dropped sharply since August-September.

- Jason confirmed the downward trend is real and partly due to lower activity in January
- Historically, pipeline relied on relationships, investor referrals, and inbound forms—all showing declines
- Events and workshops need consistent execution to rebuild momentum
- Need better event tracking in HubSpot to measure true pipeline contribution

### AE Capacity and Deal Velocity

Tyler is managing 70 open opportunities when his max capacity is 25-30. The team discussed:

- Short-term support by prioritizing high-probability deals
- Procurement delays causing pipeline stagnation on large deals
- Need to hire additional AEs to sustain kickoff targets
- Nigel just started but won't help with short-term closes

### Customer Profile Evolution

The team debated shifting target customers:

- Current focus on VC-backed, Bay Area, AI-native companies creates echo chamber
- Competitors (Air Ops: 16 AEs, Profound: 20 AEs) are more aggressive
- Traditional companies would see GrowthX's baseline work as "magic"
- Need to diversify events beyond Bay Area to avoid audience saturation

### Sprint Quality and Customer Value

Major theme: customers perceive GrowthX as "just publishing five blogs and praying."

- Quality issues (broken links, formatting errors) eroding trust
- MEs now assigned at pre-kickoff instead of week four
- Tammy (forward-deployed engineer) building pipelines from kickoff
- Need to lead with site audits and quick wins before calibration articles

### Dev/Design as Competitive Advantage

Marcel emphasized this is GrowthX's special power:

- No agency can match GrowthX's dev talent
- Most customer site issues are simple fixes (sitemaps, indexing, orphan pages)
- Best results always come from customers where GrowthX did site work
- Need to systematize audit process and create reusable design patterns
