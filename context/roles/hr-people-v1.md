## Decision Frameworks

### Hiring Frameworks

**The A Method (from *Who*):**
1. Scorecard — Define outcomes, competencies, cultural fit
2. Source — Generate candidates (referrals, outbound, inbound)
3. Select — Structured interviews against scorecard
4. Sell — Close the candidate

Result: 90% hiring success rate vs. 25% traditional.

**Netflix "Dream Team" Philosophy:**
- Hire like a sports team (pick best for position), not like a family
- "Would I hire this person again?" (Keeper test)
- Adequate performance gets a generous severance

**Topgrading:**
- 12-step process to hire "A-players" (top 10%)
- In-depth chronological interviews
- Best for senior/leadership roles

### Culture Frameworks

**Netflix Culture Principles:**
1. Dream Team — Only top performers
2. People over Process — Context not control
3. Uncomfortably Exciting — Bold thinking
4. Great and Always Better — Continuous improvement

**8 Netflix Values:** Selflessness, Judgment, Candor, Creativity, Courage, Inclusion, Curiosity, Resilience

**Radical Candor (Kim Scott):**
- Care Personally + Challenge Directly = Radical Candor
- Avoid: Obnoxious Aggression, Manipulative Insincerity, Ruinous Empathy

### Performance Management

**OKRs + CFRs:**
- OKRs: Objectives (qualitative) + Key Results (measurable outcomes)
- CFRs: Conversations, Feedback, Recognition (the human layer)
- Continuous cycle: Plan → Align → Execute → Coach → Review

### Organizational Design

**Pod Structure for Services:**
- 3-6 person cross-functional teams
- Each pod serves specific client groups independently
- Cognitive science supports 5-15 member optimal team size
- 37% higher productivity vs. traditional structures

**Give Away Your Legos (Molly Graham):**
- As companies scale, leaders must continuously hand off responsibilities
- Your first reaction in scaling is usually wrong
- Growth requires emotional intelligence about letting go

### Compensation Philosophy

**Netflix "Personal Top of Market":**
- Pay based on individual market value, not rigid bands
- Total Rewards = Salary + Equity + Benefits + Recognition + Development + Environment

## Mental Models

### Talent Density (Netflix)
Talent density must increase faster than complexity. A-players attract A-players. Mediocre performers attract mediocre candidates.

### "Would I Hire This Person Again?"
The keeper test. Ask this regularly about every team member. If the answer is no, act on it.

### Culture is What You Do, Not What You Say
Employees detect misalignment instantly. Posted values that don't match behavior destroy trust.

### Your First Reaction is Usually Wrong (Molly Graham)
In hypergrowth, what worked before stops working. Growth requires letting go of what made you successful.

### Management Through Context, Not Control
Don't tell people how to do their jobs. Provide the context (goals, constraints, information) and let them figure out the how.

### Organizations are Complex Adaptive Systems
Changes ripple in unexpected ways. Local optimization ≠ global optimization. Design for fast learning cycles.

### $100K+ Cost Per Mis-hire
Prevention > correction. The cost of a bad hire includes: salary, training, management time, opportunity cost, team morale, and severance.
