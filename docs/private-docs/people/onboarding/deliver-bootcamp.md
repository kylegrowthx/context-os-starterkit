# Deliver Org: First 2 Weeks at GrowthX

Hey, welcome to your first two weeks at GrowthX. Get ready for an intense bootcamp to kick things off!

Instead of slowly onboarding you and giving you context, what we want to do is take these next 10 business days to go as deep and as quickly as possible into everything you need to know to be successful here.

---

## Outcome: You Should Be Able To...

- Articulate our story and what we do
- Confidently manage or contribute to onboarding a new client
- Collaborate across editorial, strategy, and operations teams
- Use our tools and workflows without hand-holding
- Hold a high bar for quality and client experience
- Operate with clarity, urgency, and empathy
- Write and communicate your thoughts with excellence

You're not expected to know everything—but you're expected to learn fast, ask smart questions, and care deeply about helping clients grow.

---

## Getting Started

Store all your assignments, journals, and notes in your personal space in Notion. Create a new personal space for yourself there. Name your space, set yourself as the owner, and tag yourself in Team Directory.

---

## The Bootcamp Structure

The bootcamp is structured across your first 10 business days, covering:

### Days 1-2: Foundation
- Company overview and history
- Understanding our mission and values
- Tool setup and access
- Meet your pod and team

### Days 3-4: Client Understanding
- How we work with clients
- The customer journey
- Quality standards and expectations
- Review sample client work

### Days 5-6: Tools & Workflows
- Deep dive into our platform
- Content creation workflows
- Quality control processes
- Practice exercises

### Days 7-8: Hands-On Practice
- Shadow experienced team members
- Participate in client work (supervised)
- Receive feedback and iterate
- Ask questions and clarify

### Days 9-10: Integration
- Take on initial responsibilities
- Continue learning while doing
- Regular check-ins with your lead
- Set goals for the coming weeks

## Key Skills to Develop

### 1. Client Communication
- Clear, professional writing
- Responsive and proactive updates
- Understanding client needs
- Managing expectations

### 2. Content Quality
- Knowing what "great" looks like
- Identifying and fixing issues
- Following client guidelines
- Maintaining consistency

### 3. Workflow Efficiency
- Using tools effectively
- Meeting deadlines
- Managing multiple priorities
- Documenting your work

### 4. Collaboration
- Working across teams
- Asking for help when needed
- Sharing knowledge
- Supporting teammates

---

## Success Criteria

By the end of your first two weeks, you should be able to:

1. **Explain** what GrowthX does and why it matters
2. **Navigate** our tools and workflows independently
3. **Contribute** to client work with supervision
4. **Communicate** effectively with team members
5. **Demonstrate** our values in your daily work
