# Company-Wide Onboarding

Welcome to GrowthX! We are so excited to have you. This page outlines general onboarding tasks that **all team members** (regardless of role) should complete in their first week.

> **NOTE:** Everyone will receive a Linear project with individual tasks (issues) for each item below. It's your job to update your linear project and mark each task complete. Your manager will monitor your onboarding progress in Linear directly.

**Day 1** is about setting up tools.

**Week 1** is about getting to know your team and gathering as much company context as possible, before you focus on role-specific onboarding.

---

## Day 1

Day 1 is all about getting set up on the company tools/systems.

### Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Activate your Okta account
- [ ] Set up Gmail with your GrowthX email
- [ ] Join Slack using the invite link
- [ ] Set up 1Password
- [ ] Join Notion workspace
- [ ] Join Linear

---

## Week 1

Week 1 is a great time to get to know your team and read key company documents (there's a lot, we know!). These docs will give you all the context you need on GrowthX's unique history, vision, and business model.

### Week 1 Checklist

- [ ] Complete Day 1 tool setup
- [ ] Read The Handbook - key company documents
- [ ] Attend Onboarding: Strategy & Vision (hosted by Marcel Santilli, Co-founder & CEO)
- [ ] Attend Onboarding: Company Operations
- [ ] Attend Onboarding: Customer Use Cases
- [ ] Meet your team members
- [ ] Review company culture and values
- [ ] Familiarize yourself with Slack channels and communication norms

---

## Key Documents to Read

During your first week, prioritize reading these foundational documents:

1. **Mission & Vision** - Understand what drives us
2. **Culture & Values** - Learn how we work together
3. **How Our Business Works** - Get the big picture
4. **Priorities for the Next 6 Months** - Know what we're focused on
5. **Human-AI Collaboration Framework** - Understand our unique approach
