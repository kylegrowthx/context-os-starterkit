# Product Lead: Final Round Assignment

As part of the interview process, we're sharing a simplified version of one of our product strategy documents. Use it alongside the live product to put together your take on what we should build next.

---

## What You'll Receive

1. **This assignment brief**
2. **CheckThat Strategy Document** - How we think about the product, the conceptual model, and the direction we want to take
3. **Access to CheckThat.ai** - Sign up and use the product yourself

---

## The Assignment

**Use the product. Read the strategy. Tell us what to do next.**

1. **Sign up for CheckThat.ai** and spend real time with it. Track a brand. Explore the data.

2. **Read the strategy document.** Understand the three-layer model (Context, Instruments, Measurement) and the open questions at the end.

3. **Put together your take on what CheckThat should do next:**
   - What you observed using the product
   - How the strategy doc maps to what's live today
   - Your recommended priorities (what to build, what to skip, in what order)
   - Open questions you'd want to answer before committing

---

## How to Present

You'll present to Daniel (CTO/CPO), Marcel (CEO), and Ella (CCO).

30-45 minutes presenting, 15-30 minutes Q&A.

Format is up to you. Slides, a doc, a whiteboard session. We care about the thinking, not the production value.

---

## What We're Evaluating

We want to see how you think about product. Can you use a product critically, absorb a strategy document, and come back with a clear point of view on what to do next and why?

---

## One More Thing

The strategy doc has "Open Questions" at the end. These are real questions we're wrestling with. If you have strong opinions, we want to hear them. If you don't, say so. We'd rather hear "I'd need to investigate this more" than a half-baked answer.
