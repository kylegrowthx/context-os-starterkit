# Product Lead

We're in the middle of a tectonic shift. Buyer behavior is changing. AI is reshaping how brands get discovered. Entire professions are reinventing themselves.

At GrowthX, we're tackling all three at once:

- **How AI sees brands**: Helping companies monitor and understand how AI engines perceive them.
- **How marketing teams respond**: Giving them the tools to act on insights and win.
- **How AI engineers operate**: Building the infrastructure for teams working at the forefront of AI's impact on go-to-market.

We need someone to build these products with us.

This is a builder role. At GrowthX, product and engineering are mixed. Product managers read code, have informed discussions with engineers, and get in the trenches. You'll mock up features with AI-assisted tools like Claude. You'll lead day-to-day operations. You'll QA new versions and fix bugs yourself. You'll create the content and enablement paths that help the rest of the team ship.

If you want to write specs for other people to build, this isn't it.

---

## What We're Building

GrowthX runs two engines:

**Services**: B2B content marketing for companies that want to win in AI-first discovery. $200k+ engagements. This is the revenue engine.

**Software**: Three products that power the shift:

- **CheckThat**: The open AI visibility index for B2B. Think Crunchbase for funding, G2 for reviews, but for how AI perceive brands.
- **ContentOS**: The system that turns AI insights into content that wins.
- **Output.ai**: AI workflows that power forward-deployed engineering teams.

You'll own product across this portfolio. You might start with CheckThat, then move to scaling our forward-deployed engineering expansion, or take over parts of ContentOS. The work shifts as the company grows.

---

## What You'll Own

**Product Strategy**: Work closely with the CEO and CTO to shape strategy. Convert high-level direction into tactical projects. Make build vs. buy decisions on implementation.

**Bring Clarity Through Building**: Wireframe in ASCII or tldraw. Mock up features in actual code in real repos, not Figma. Use AI-assisted tools like Claude and Cursor to quickly bring ideas to life. Your job is to derisk the early phase and make decisions concrete before the team invests in production code.

**Work with Engineers**: Pair with the team. Review PRs. Make technical trade-off decisions. You need to be credible in the codebase.

**Growth and Metrics**: Own conversion funnels. Track what matters. Run experiments. Kill what doesn't work.

**User Research**: Talk to users. Validate before building. Understand the problem before jumping to solutions.

**Cross-Product Integration**: Connect the dots across CheckThat, ContentOS, and Output.ai.
