## Role Overview

Kirkland serves as the Team Lead for the Client Ops / Forward Deploy team, combining hands-on work on special projects with team enablement and leadership responsibilities. This role leverages his domain expertise in content marketing and pragmatic approach to help scale the team while maintaining quality across client implementations.

**Team Structure:**
- **Team Lead** (Kirkland) - Recruiting, coaching, special projects, team coordination, stakeholder support
- **Product/Project Manager** (Stevie) - Triage, prioritization, cycle planning, client coordination (likely to start helping w/ other Products soon)
- **Senior Engineers** (Marcus, Nico) - Handle complex technical projects, code reviews, etc
- **Forward Deploy Engineers (L1 & L2)** - Standard client implementations and workflow builds

## Core Responsibilities

### 1. Enablement & Coaching

- **Onboarding new team members** - Guide new hires through our platform, processes, and client workflows
- **Knowledge transfer** - Create and maintain documentation for common implementation patterns
- **Skills development** - Help junior engineers level up their abilities in AI workflows, CMS integrations, and client communication
- **Interview support** - Participate in candidate interviews to assess fit for forward deploy roles

### 2. Team Support & Coordination

- **Work closely with Stevie (PM)** on:
  - Cycle planning and capacity allocation
  - Client escalations and strategic decisions
  - Process improvements for team efficiency
  - Quality standards and delivery timelines
- **Unblock junior engineers** - Provide guidance when team members get stuck on technical or client issues
- **Daily standups support** - Help identify blockers and coordinate solutions

### 3. Special Projects

- **High-stakes or complex client accounts** - Work alongside Marcus and Nico on challenging implementations
- **Novel AI workflow or framework architecture** - Work alongside Daniel, Infra team and future ML/AI team on new tech + how to rollout
- **Proof of concepts** - Build and test new workflow patterns, new APIs, etc
- **Client strategy sprints** - Support on pre-engagement discovery to determine what's possible

### 4. Quality & Process

- **Standards maintenance** - Help establish and enforce quality standards
- **Process optimization** - Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies in team workflows
- **Deal desk support** - Assist with scoping and technical feasibility for new client opportunities
