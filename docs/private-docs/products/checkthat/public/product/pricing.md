# Pricing

Three plans. Free is genuinely generous. Pro is for teams that want depth. Business is for companies that want strategy.

---

## Free

**$0/month**

- 50 custom prompts tracked
- Access to 100,000+ shared prompt library
- 14 days of historical data
- 5 AI engines (ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI, Gemini)
- Unlimited users
- Prompt manager
- Full company research database

Free isn't a trial. It's a real product. You get access to the shared prompt library, the open index, and enough custom prompts to understand how AI sees your brand. Most of what you need to get started is here.

---

## Pro

**$249/month**

Everything in Free, plus:

- 500 custom prompts tracked
- 60 days of historical data
- More frequent tracking
- Competitor benchmarking
- Export capabilities
- Dedicated Slack support

Pro is for marketing teams that want to track their brand in depth. 500 custom prompts let you cover your full competitive landscape, your key buyer questions, and the use cases that matter most.

$249 is designed to be approachable. A seed-stage startup can afford it. A Series B company won't think twice. It's enough to filter for serious teams without being a barrier.

---

## Business

**$1,500/month**

Everything in Pro, plus:

- 2,000 custom prompts tracked
- Full historical archive (all data, no cutoff)
- Dedicated AEO strategist
- Ongoing audits and review
- Managed prompt strategy
- Custom reports
- Dedicated engagement manager

Business is for companies that don't just want data. They want someone to tell them what it means and what to do about it.

Your AEO strategist sets up your tracking, reviews your data, and delivers actionable recommendations. They know your category, your competitors, and your goals. They surface the insights that matter and filter out the noise.

---

## What's the Same Across All Plans

Every plan includes:

- Access to the full shared prompt library (100,000+ prompts)
- Unlimited users in your workspace
- The complete open index (browse any category, any brand, any answer)
- Company research database
- Prompt manager for organizing your tracking

We don't charge per user. We don't gate basic features. The open index is free for everyone, always.

---

## The Philosophy

We made Free generous on purpose. The open index only works if people use it. Every user who explores the data, tracks their brand, and contributes custom prompts makes the index richer for everyone.

Pro adds the depth you need when you're serious about AI visibility. Business adds the human expertise for teams that want direction, not just data.
