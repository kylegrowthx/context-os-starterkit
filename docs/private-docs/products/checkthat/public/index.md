# CheckThat

## The open AI visibility index for B2B.

B2B buyers now ask AI before they talk to sales. They type questions into ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Mode. They get recommendations. They make shortlists. They eliminate vendors. All before visiting a single website.

Most brands have no idea what AI says about them.

---

## Three Questions You Can't Answer

Right now, your brand can't answer the questions that matter most:

**1. Do you show up?** When a buyer asks AI "what's the best expense management tool for startups," are you in the answer? You don't know.

**2. How do you show up?** Are you the top recommendation, a safe alternative, or an afterthought? Are you described accurately? You don't know.

**3. What should you do about it?** Which buyer questions matter most in your category? Where are you winning? Where are you invisible? What would actually move the needle? You don't know.

These aren't edge cases. These are the core questions every B2B brand will need to answer in the next two years.

---

## Why Existing Tools Don't Work

The AEO tools in market all follow the same pattern. You pay upfront. You guess which prompts matter. You wait while data trickles in. You get thousands of rows of raw AI responses and a dashboard full of charts.

You don't get direction. You get a CSV.

Worse, every company runs the same research alone. How AI sees your category never becomes shared knowledge. Every brand starts from scratch, guessing in the dark.

---

## What We Built

CheckThat takes a different approach.

### The Open Index

We mapped 1,740 B2B software categories. We researched 5,828+ brands. We built a shared prompt library based on how real buyers ask questions. We track AI responses daily across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google AI, and Gemini.

Then we made it all public. No signup required.

Anyone can browse any category, see which brands AI recommends, and understand how the competitive landscape looks through AI's eyes. That data compounds over time. Historical responses can't be backfilled. Every day of tracking creates data that didn't exist before.

### Track Your Brand

You see the open index. You want your brand tracked in detail. You add it.

All that historical data flows into your dashboard. You layer on custom prompts that reflect your specific buyers, use cases, and competitive landscape. The shared prompt library means you don't start from zero. 100,000+ prompts are already being tracked.

### Get Direction

This is where most tools stop. We don't.

CheckThat answers the three questions: Where do you show up? How are you positioned? What should you do next? Not with more charts. With specific, actionable insights about which gaps to close and which opportunities to pursue.

## What Makes Us Different

**We start with the buyer, not the brand.** Most tools track whether AI mentions you. We track what buyers actually ask and how AI answers across your entire category.

**We're open by default.** Public index. Shared data. See your market before you pay anything. The data gets better as more people use it.

**There's no cold start.** Categories mapped. Brands researched. Prompts built. Historical data already tracked. You plug in and get answers on day one.

**Historical data is the moat.** We've tracked how AI answers buyer questions daily since launch. That history can't be recreated. Competitors who start later will always have a gap.

**Human editorial review, not just scraping.** Every prompt in our shared library goes through human review. Every brand in our index gets real research. We invest in depth, not just volume.
