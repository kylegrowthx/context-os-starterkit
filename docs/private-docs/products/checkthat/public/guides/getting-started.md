# Getting Started

You can get value from CheckThat in five minutes. Here's how.

---

## Step 1: Explore the Open Index

Before you sign up for anything, browse.

Go to [/categories/](/categories/) and find your market. Look at the brands tracked. See how AI engines rank competitors in your space. Get a feel for the data.

This is free. No account needed.

---

## Step 2: Look Up Your Brand

Go to [/brands/](/brands/) and search for your company.

If we track your brand (we track 5,828+), you'll see a profile with research, positioning context, and AI visibility data. See how different engines describe you. Check if the information is accurate.

If your brand isn't in the index yet, you can add it when you sign up.

---

## Step 3: Sign Up

Create your free account at [/signup](/signup).

Free includes: 50 custom prompts, access to the 100,000+ shared prompt library, 14 days of historical data, tracking across 5 AI engines, and unlimited users.

No credit card required.

---

## Step 4: Add Your Brand

Once you're in, add your brand to your workspace. If your brand already exists in the index, all the historical data flows into your dashboard immediately.

You'll see:
- Which buyer questions mention you
- How you're positioned relative to competitors
- Which AI engines favor you (and which don't)
- How your visibility has changed over time

---

## Step 5: Explore the Shared Prompt Library

Before writing any custom prompts, browse the shared library.

100,000+ prompts are already being tracked across the B2B software landscape. Filter by your category. See which buyer questions are tracked. Check your visibility for the questions that matter most.

Most of the data you need to get started is already here.

---

## Step 6: Add Custom Prompts

When you're ready to go deeper, add custom prompts that reflect your specific buyers, use cases, and competitive landscape.

Think about the questions your actual buyers ask. The specific comparisons they make. The use cases that drive your revenue. Write prompts that sound like what a real buyer would type into ChatGPT at 10pm when they're shortlisting vendors.

We'll review your prompts, activate them, and start tracking across AI engines.

---

## Step 7: Review Your Insights

As tracking data accumulates, your insights become more useful.

Check your dashboard weekly. Look for patterns: Where are you consistently visible? Where are you invisible? How does your positioning compare to competitors? Which buyer questions represent the biggest opportunities?

Use these insights to inform your content strategy, messaging, and competitive positioning.
