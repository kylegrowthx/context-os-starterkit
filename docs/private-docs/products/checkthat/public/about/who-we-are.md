# Who We Are

CheckThat is built by GrowthX.

We've spent years helping B2B companies grow through content. Strategy, execution, measurement. We've been inside the machine, doing the work, seeing what actually moves the needle for brands trying to earn attention.

That experience is why CheckThat exists.

---

## The Origin

Marcel Santilli, our CEO, spent 15 years in B2B marketing. He watched content marketing go from a competitive advantage to a broken system. Agencies were slow and expensive. Tools created more work, not less. Internal teams were stretched thin trying to do everything at once.

He built GrowthX to fix that. A company that delivers outcomes, not just tools or services. AI handles the scalable work. Experts handle the work that matters. The system gets smarter over time.

Then AI search changed the game.

We started seeing the shift firsthand, working with clients. Buyers were asking ChatGPT for software recommendations before visiting a single website. Traffic patterns were changing. The old playbook of ranking in Google and running ads was losing ground to a new reality: if AI doesn't recommend you, a growing number of buyers never find you.

We looked at the AEO tools launching to solve this. They all followed the same pattern: closed dashboards, guessed prompts, data dumps without direction. Everyone running the same research alone. Nothing shared. Nothing cumulative.

We thought there was a better way.

---

## Why We Built This

CheckThat came from a simple conviction: AI visibility data should be public, shared, and cumulative.

Not because we're idealists. Because it's the only approach that compounds.

When the data is open, everyone can see how AI views their market. Brands make better decisions. The index gets more complete. Historical data builds up. The whole system improves.

When the data is closed, every company starts from scratch, pays separately, and learns alone. That's slow and wasteful.

We built CheckThat to be the former.

---

## What We Believe

**Content is the atomic unit of marketing.** Every website, every search result, every AI answer, every sales conversation starts with content. The companies that produce great content at scale will win.

**AI should make great people more productive.** Not replace them. Amplify them. You can't delegate to AI what you can't delegate to a human. Start with great, then make it efficient.

**Growth should compound.** Every page is an asset. Every output makes the system smarter. If you're starting from scratch every time, you're doing it wrong.

**Outcomes matter more than outputs.** Nobody wants more data. They want visibility, traffic, conversions, pipeline. They want to be the answer when buyers ask questions.
