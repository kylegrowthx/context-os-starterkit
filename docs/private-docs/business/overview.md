## The Big Picture

We run two engines today: **GrowthX (services)** and **CheckThat (software)**.

Services is the majority of our revenue. That's where we focus most of our energy. CheckThat is the bet that changes our trajectory, but it needs to prove itself first.

There's also a third driver, **Community (AI Led Growth)**, which is smaller but growing.

Until the software and community engines scale, services is the business. Understand services, and you understand GrowthX.

---

## The Simple Mental Model

Our services business comes down to two things: **demand** and **supply**.

### Demand Side

Demand is any company with a website that wants to grow.

We focus on **compounding growth**. That's the outcome we deliver. Not content for content's sake. Growth.

**Why websites?** Websites are the destination. They're where most transactions happen, where you control the most, and what influences everything else: AI visibility, search visibility, traffic, engagement, conversion.

**Who we serve:** B2B tech-enabled companies that have pressure to grow, have found product-market fit, are scaling, and have an established website.

### Supply Side

Supply is how we deliver. We have both the tech (our platform) and people (forward-deployed teams).

We need the right people, the right process, enough capacity, properly trained and ramped, delivering and exceeding expectations.

---

## What We Sell

We sell **workstreams**. Workstreams are lanes of work that build repeatable, scalable, predictable systems for compounding growth.

The atomic unit is **content**. Content isn't just words on a page. It's strategy, how you communicate information, how it's presented, how it's structured.

We are not content publishers for the sake of publishing. We believe content, when done right, becomes a compounding asset.

---

## The Customer Journey

Every customer goes through three phases:

| Phase | Duration | Goal |
|-------|----------|------|
| **Sales** | ~6 weeks | Qualify fit |
| **Strategy Sprint** | 8 weeks | Build trust, calibrate, set up for success |
| **Growth Execution** | 12+ months | Drive compounding growth |

The Strategy Sprint is where trust is built or broken. It's an extension of sales. We're still proving ourselves.

Growth Execution is where we deliver ongoing value. Continuously optimizing. Picking the right shots on goal. Making every week easier than the last.

---

## How We're Organized

Three main teams:

1. **Sales team** - Qualifies and closes new business
2. **Strategy Sprint teams** - Runs the 8-week sprints, converts to Growth Execution
3. **Delivery teams** - Runs Growth Execution in pods

Key roles:
- **Engagement Managers** - Own the client relationship end-to-end
- **Editors / Production** - Own output and quality
- **Directors** - Senior strategists, escalation support
- **Forward-Deployed Engineers** - AI workflow customization
- **SEO Specialists** - Audits and technical analysis

---

## How We Measure Success

Client health comes down to five dimensions:

1. **Output** - Are we publishing? Creating? Refreshing?
2. **Quality** - Is the work good? Would it ruin their reputation or build it?
3. **Performance** - Is it driving growth? AI visibility? Traffic?
4. **Relationship** - Do they love working with us? Would they renew?
5. **Strategy** - Do they understand where we're heading? Are they bought in?

If all five are strong, renewals are inevitable. If any are weak, we're at risk.
