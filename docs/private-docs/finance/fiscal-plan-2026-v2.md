## Why We Believe

2025 was about proving the market. We did. 9x ARR growth. 60+ customers. Every CMO we talk to says the same thing: "Every company needs this."

2026 is different. We're going from 9x to ~3x. The challenge isn't product-market fit. It's leverage and scalability.

**What we proved:** Strategy Sprints convert at 75%+. Quality drives retention. The demand is there.

**What we learned:** Pure services hit a ceiling. Adding bodies doesn't scale—each new hire contributes less than the last. No platform lock-in means customers can leave anytime.

**The shift we're making:** From service company with platform support → Platform company with forward-deployed experts. Work moves into ContentOS. Customers operate the system. Our team delivers the outcomes they can't do alone.

This is the year we build the machine that scales.

## How This Affects You

Numbers don't execute themselves. People do. Here's what the plan means for each function.

**Delivery:** You are the product. Every kickoff that converts, every customer that renews, every expansion that closes traces back to the work you do. Quality drives renewals. Speed drives capacity. When we say 140 kickoffs, we're saying 140 times we need to deliver something a customer would pay $200K for. That's the bar.

**Sales:** 140 kickoffs means closing 11-12 new deals per month. Right now we have one AE and we're booking 4-6 weeks out. That's the bottleneck. The VP Sales hire in Q1 changes everything—building a team, adding capacity, improving conversion. Until then, pipeline coverage at 3x is non-negotiable. Every qualified opp that slips is a kickoff we miss.

**Marketing:** We're competing against 200+ players, many with bigger budgets. The goal isn't just leads—it's building a machine. Channels that compound. Programs that scale. Systems that connect. 420 qualified opps and 1,180 CheckThat paying customers by year end. That's what success looks like. The budget is $3.6M. Use it to build something repeatable, not campaigns that spike and fade.

**Customer Success:** This is your year. First real renewal season. Every account that churns is $200K we have to replace with a new kickoff. Every account that expands is pure upside. Healthy ARR % is your north star. If that number is trending up, renewals will follow.

**Product/Engineering:** CheckThat success determines whether we're a services company with a side project or a platform company with a services arm. 10,000 signups, 500 WAU, first paying customers in Q1. ContentOS needs to be the system our customers can't live without. Ship fast, but ship quality.

**Everyone:** The plan works if we all execute. No single function can carry this. Sales needs delivery to convert. Delivery needs product to scale. Product needs customer feedback to prioritize. We're building something together. Act like it.

## The Math

The plan has to add up. Here's the arithmetic behind the targets.

### The ARR Waterfall

This is how we get from $12.1M to $33.8M:

```
Starting ARR (Jan 2026):          $12.1M
─────────────────────────────────────────
+ New ARR from kickoffs:          +$22.8M   (140 × 80% × $204K)
- Churn (20% of starting):        -$2.4M    (Risk: every 1% worse = $120K lost)
+ Expansion revenue:              +$2.0M    (Existing customers buying more)
- Churn on new ARR (partial yr):  -$0.9M    (Contracts signed later churn less)
+ CheckThat ARR:                  +$4.2M    (Upside, not required for base plan)
─────────────────────────────────────────
Ending ARR (Dec 2026):            $33.8M
```

The two biggest levers: **kickoffs** (volume) and **churn** (retention). Everything else is secondary.

### GrowthX Engine

Strategy Sprints are 8 weeks. They convert at 80% into 12-month Growth Execution contracts. That's when they become ARR.

| Input             | 2025 Actual   | 2026 Target |
| ----------------- | ------------- | ----------- |
| Kickoffs          | 74            | 140         |
| Sprint Conversion | 70%           | 80%         |
| ACV               | $204K | $204K |             |
| Renewal Rate      | TBD           | 80%         |

At 140 kickoffs × 80% conversion × $204K ACV = ~$23M in new ARR.
Starting ARR of $12.1M × 80% retention = ~$9.7M retained.
Plus ~$2M in expansion revenue.

That gets us to ~$29M from GrowthX alone. Add CheckThat and we hit $33.8M.

### CheckThat Engine

## The Bottom Line

We 9x'd ARR in 2025 from $1.34M to $12.1M. That was the "figure it out" phase. We found product-market fit. We proved the model. We built a team that can deliver.

2026 is the "scale it" phase. The playbook exists. Now we run it 140 times without breaking.

Here's what we're NOT doing this year:

- We're not chasing new verticals. B2B tech is our market.
- We're not building features that don't serve delivery or retention.
- We're not hiring ahead of demand. Efficiency matters.
- We're not treating CheckThat as optional. It's the bet that changes our trajectory.

Here's what we ARE doing:

- We're keeping customers. 80% renewal or bust.
- We're scaling kickoffs. 140 is the number.
- We're building systems. What works for 62 customers must work for 110.
- We're proving CheckThat. Software revenue changes everything.

The math works. The risks are known. The plan is clear.

Now we execute. Not perfectly. Relentlessly.

Every week, we ask: Are we on track? If not, what's blocking us? If so, what's next?

That's how we get to $33.8M. That's how we build something that lasts.
