## 01. Q2 Recap

**TLDR:**
1. We've identified an ICP that we can repeatably serve well and retain
2. Revenue growth softened intentionally to pivot to our Strategy Sprint model
3. Retention and happy customers is still the main focus after experiencing significant client turnover

As we head into the second half of the year, we have a lot to be proud of. We more than doubled our run rate from $3.02M to $7.46M YTD, grew to a team of 70 people, and launched our very own platform.

We exited Q2 with a net operating loss of $522K, $10.3m cash on hand, and $1.1m in receivables.

## 03. Deep Dive #1: Retention & Expansion

**TLDR:**
- We have a better idea of our ICP
- We had a lot of pilots not converting and significant churn ($1.5M in potential ARRR)
- We quickly pivoted to new onboarding model and it's working (100% conversion)
- Delivery model is still a work in progress

During Q2, we positioned nearly all engagements with a 90-day pilot period. This proved to be a mistake as customers expected full ROI by day 90.

In Q2, we officially lost 6 customers totaling $732K in ARRR. Of these, 2 were regrettable losses totaling $396K.

**Changes we've made in Q2:**

| At-Risk Trigger | New Mechanism | How It Fixes the Issue |
|-----------------|---------------|------------------------|
| Lack of strategy, alignment, and context gathering | 8 week Strategy Sprint model | Ensures strategic alignment up front |
| Low-ICP clients | New ICP Matrix | Focus on high ICP customers |
| Lack of Client Management SOPs | Hired Head of Customer Operations | Improves client-facing skillset |
| Sloppy onboarding & quality drift | Revamped Hiring Process | Every pod member can hit velocity |
| Execution bottlenecks | Client Ops Engineers embedded | Unblock Delivery org quickly |
| Platform launch | All edits in our platform | Learn from every interaction |

## 04. Deep Dive #2: Product Strategy

**Freemium Trojan Horse → Premium ICP Services Upsell**

- Launch freemium GEO tool at 10x cheaper than Scrunch/Profound

**Efficiency: Atlas Platform Evolution**

- Launch & iterate all core areas of the Atlas roadmap
- AI/ML Team: 2-3 data scientists + ML engineers
- Human Expertise Marketplace

**Create Infra for AI-first world from real use cases**

- Release our AI workflows coding framework ("Rails/Django for AI Operations")
- "Lovable for Knowledge Workers": Natural language workflow creation
- Human-AI Marketplace: Seamlessly blend AI agents with expert humans
