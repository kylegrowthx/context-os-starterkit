# Products

GrowthX's product portfolio documentation.

---

## Quick Summary

GrowthX builds a multi-product ecosystem. CheckThat (AI visibility index), ContentOS (content operations platform), Output (open-source AI framework), and ExpertLayer each serve a specific strategic purpose, target a distinct audience, and operate with their own economic model — but they integrate to create a cohesive platform that compounds in value.

---

## Product Portfolio

| Product | Purpose | Target Audience |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| CheckThat | AI visibility index for B2B | Marketing leaders, brand teams |
| ContentOS | Content operations platform | B2B tech companies, content teams |
| Output | Open-source AI framework | Engineers, technical users |
| ExpertLayer | Expert network (in development) | Research, advisory |
