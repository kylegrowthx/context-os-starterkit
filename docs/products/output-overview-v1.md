# Output

Open-source AI framework for building agents and workflows — GrowthX's technical credibility play.

Output is our open-source AI framework targeting engineers and technical users. It's our technical credibility play, infrastructure layer, and recruiting pipeline rolled into one.

## Strategic purpose

- **Technical credibility** and ecosystem building in the developer community
- **Reduce dependency risk** on infrastructure maintained by small teams
- **Create a recruiting pipeline** of top technical talent
- **Build an ecosystem** that powers ContentOS, ExpertLayer, and future products

## What it does

Output is an open-source framework for building AI agents and workflows. Engineers use it to create, deploy, and manage agentic workflows — from content automation to custom business processes.

## Target audience

- Engineers building AI product features
- Technical users creating automation workflows
- Companies wanting to deploy agentic workflows

## Business model

**Framework** — Free and open source. The adoption driver that builds community and mindshare.

**Cloud platform** — Paid hosting for non-technical teams who want to run workflows without managing infrastructure.

**Marketplace** — Engineers and vibe-coders deploy and monetize agents. Take rate on workflow and agent transactions.

**Enterprise** — Forward-deployed AI Ops Engineers for custom workflows and deployments.

## Strategic roadmap

**Short-term:**
- Open source framework for building agents and workflows
- Gain developer mindshare and contributions
- Increase talent density through recruiting

**Medium-term:**
- Launch marketplace where engineers can deploy and monetize agents
- Provide ContentOS and ExpertLayer with ecosystem of workflows

**Long-term:**
- True network effects through workflow marketplace
- Platform that powers all GrowthX products underneath
- Revenue from cloud hosting and enterprise deployments
