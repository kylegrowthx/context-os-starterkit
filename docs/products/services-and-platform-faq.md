## How do services and the platform relate?

Services isn't going away. It's actually our secret weapon because it's how we figure out what clients really need and let them focus on results instead of learning software. What's changing in March is that we start leading with the platform. The way to think about it: we run the platform, clients come in to review and collaborate. They're not learning a new tool, they're working alongside us in one.

## What's actually changing day to day?

The big practical shift is that we stop doing things in Airtable and Notion and move everything into our own platform. That lets us build in capabilities those tools just can't do. Clients get a login, they see the strategy we're executing, and all our work lives in one place. This is what creates leverage. It's how we scaled CheckThat to 600+ pages a month with just 2 team members. Client meetings become screenshares in the tool, and hands-on clients can jump in and collaborate directly. We're calling this **GrowthX OS**.

## What is Output.ai?

We built our own AI workflow and agent tooling. Four of us on the EPD team have been heads-down improving it for the last 6 months, and we think the broader AI engineering community could benefit from it, and contribute back. That means we get more features, faster. It also helps with recruiting because having Output as a standalone brand makes it easier to attract top AI engineering talent. We have product ideas for Output that tie back to CheckThat and the OS, but more on that later. It'll be free and open source, built for AI programmers.

## What are our three products?

- **GrowthX OS** is our website intelligence layer that helps brands win in the age of AI.
- **CheckThat.ai** is our buyer intent layer. It tells us whether AI search is actually working and how it's impacting our clients.
- **Output.ai** is how we build complex AI agents and workflows that deliver consistent results. Free, open source, built for the AI engineering community.
