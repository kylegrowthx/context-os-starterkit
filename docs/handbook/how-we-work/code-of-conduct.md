# Code of conduct

GrowthX holds itself to the highest standards of ethical conduct. This commitment is only meaningful when each team member shares and upholds these ideals.

This code exists as a guide for behavior and a reflection of our values. It's written broadly — no document could cover every scenario. We trust our people to use good judgment and lead by example.

## Who this applies to

Everyone. Employees, board members, contractors, and consultants. Anyone representing GrowthX is responsible for upholding these values.

## When this applies

In the workplace and whenever you're representing GrowthX in any capacity. In the age of social media, what you say and do online reflects on the company. We expect these values to guide behavior at work and beyond — especially in visible public spaces.

## Questions or concerns

If you have questions about this code or concerns about unethical conduct, report them to your direct manager. If the issue involves your manager, escalate to the next level of leadership. All matters are addressed promptly and respectfully.

## Communication

How we communicate shapes how we work together. We strive for professionalism, empathy, and transparency in every interaction.

- **Encourage, support, and uplift teammates** — don't criticize destructively
- **Be honest** — truthfulness builds trust
- **Respect others' ideas, backgrounds, and beliefs**
- **Speak with kindness**, even under challenging circumstances
- **Stay calm** — avoid reactive or aggressive language
- **Avoid offensive jokes or comments** — this includes anything sexist, racist, or crude

## Integrity

Integrity means acting consistently with our values, being truthful, and taking responsibility for our actions. It requires courage — owning up to mistakes, staying honest, and fostering an environment where others feel safe to do the same.

Kindness is inseparable from integrity. Long after a conversation ends, people remember how you made them feel.

- **Take personal responsibility**: Own your actions and learn from mistakes
- **Align actions with values**: Make decisions you can be proud of
- **Foster honesty**: Be transparent in your work and open with colleagues
- **Extend kindness**: Treat everyone with empathy, understanding, and respect — even when faced with unkindness

Together, integrity and kindness create a culture where trust is paramount, differences are valued, and everyone can do their best work.

## Confidentiality

You'll have access to information not available to the public — business strategies, intellectual property, growth initiatives, new features.

- **Safeguard confidential information**: Keep it secure and never share without permission
- **Be vigilant**: Even casual conversations can unintentionally reveal sensitive details

## Conflicts of interest

Always act in GrowthX's best interests. Conflicts arise when personal interests could interfere with that obligation.

- **Avoid conflicting loyalties**: Personal benefits should never come at the expense of the company or clients
- **Disclose potential conflicts**: If in doubt, speak up and seek guidance

## Inclusiveness

GrowthX is committed to building an inclusive culture. Everyone — regardless of race, gender, religion, or any other characteristic — deserves to feel valued and respected. Discrimination of any kind is unacceptable.

- **Be inclusive**: Create a welcoming environment for everyone
- **Value diversity**: Respect and learn from different perspectives
- **Challenge bias**: Recognize and address unconscious biases

## Harassment

Harassment of any kind is strictly prohibited — verbal abuse, unwanted physical contact, inappropriate comments, or any behavior that creates a hostile work environment.

- **Respect boundaries**: Personal space and consent are paramount
- **Zero tolerance**: Offensive comments, intimidation, or stalking have no place here
- **Support others**: Stand against harassment and support those affected

## Retaliation

We do not tolerate retaliation against anyone who raises a concern or participates in an investigation. Speaking up is encouraged, and everyone has the right to report issues without fear.

- **Report without fear**: Concerns raised in good faith are always taken seriously
- **No retaliation**: We protect those who speak up

---

This code of conduct isn't just guidelines — it's the foundation for the culture we're building. By committing to open communication, accountability, kindness, and respect, we create a workplace where every individual is empowered to grow, contribute, and push boundaries.
