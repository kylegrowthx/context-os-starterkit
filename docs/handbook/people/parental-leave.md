# Parental leave

We're committed to supporting our team during life's most important moments. This policy outlines what to expect when taking parental leave and how to plan for a smooth transition.

---

## Amount of leave

We provide paid parental leave as follows:

- **Birthing parent (or primary caregiver):** up to **16 weeks** of paid leave
- **Non-birthing parent (or secondary caregiver):** up to **6 weeks** of paid leave

Leave is available to any parent welcoming a child through birth, adoption, or surrogacy.

<Note>
For team members based in the **United States** who are eligible for state-sponsored parental leave programs (e.g., CA PFL, NY PFL), leave will be administered through **Deel**, and GrowthX will provide **top-up pay** to ensure your compensation aligns with our policy during your leave period.
</Note>

---

## Eligibility

Parental leave is available to full-time employees and contractors who have completed at least **6 months of continuous service** at GrowthX before their expected leave start date. Both birthing/primary and non-birthing/secondary parents are eligible.

---

## Notice required

Please provide **a minimum of 3 months' notice** prior to the planned start of parental leave. This ensures adequate time for planning and coverage.

---

## Process and planning

<Steps>
  <Step title="Inform your manager">
    Let your manager know as early as possible to discuss timing and resourcing.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your leave request">
    Submit your request in writing to the People team, including your estimated dates.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a coverage plan">
    Work with your manager to create a coverage plan, including:
    - Task handover
    - Point of contact during your absence
    - Tentative return date
  </Step>
</Steps>

Our goal is to support you and your team with a smooth transition both before and after your leave.
