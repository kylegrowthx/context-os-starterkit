# Client ops team lead

## Role overview

The team lead for client ops / forward deploy combines hands-on work on special projects with team enablement and leadership responsibilities. This role leverages domain expertise in content marketing and a pragmatic approach to help scale the team while maintaining quality across client implementations.

**Team structure:**
- **Team lead** — Recruiting, coaching, special projects, team coordination, stakeholder support
- **Product/project manager** — Triage, prioritization, cycle planning, client coordination
- **Senior engineers** — Handle complex technical projects, code reviews
- **Forward deploy engineers (L1 & L2)** — Standard client implementations and workflow builds

## Core responsibilities

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Enablement & coaching">
    - **Onboarding new team members** — Guide new hires through our platform, processes, and client workflows
    - **Knowledge transfer** — Create and maintain documentation for common implementation patterns
    - **Skills development** — Help junior engineers level up in AI workflows, CMS integrations, and client communication
    - **Interview support** — Participate in candidate interviews to assess fit for forward deploy roles
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Team support & coordination">
    - Work closely with the PM on cycle planning, capacity allocation, client escalations, process improvements, and quality standards
    - Unblock junior engineers when they get stuck on technical or client issues
    - Help identify blockers in daily standups and coordinate solutions
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Special projects">
    - High-stakes or complex client accounts alongside senior engineers
    - Novel AI workflow or framework architecture alongside infra and future ML/AI teams
    - Proof of concepts — build and test new workflow patterns, new APIs
    - Client strategy sprints — support on pre-engagement discovery to determine what's possible
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Quality & process">
    - Help establish and enforce quality standards
    - Identify bottlenecks and inefficiencies in team workflows
    - Assist with scoping and technical feasibility for new client opportunities
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Collaboration model

### With the product/project manager

**Delegate to PM:** Ticket management, sprint/cycle planning and prioritization, client pipeline reviews and feedback coordination.

**Team lead complements with:** Technical guidance on scoping and feasibility, team capacity planning, capability assessment and training needs, quality standards, strategic problem-solving for complex clients, technical POV based on status updates.

**Work together on:** Cycle planning, client escalations, process improvements, team growth planning.

### With senior engineers

- **Code review pipeline** — Senior engineers review code from junior team members
- **Special projects collaboration** — Team lead joins complex projects early to ensure the best approach
- **Technical escalation** — When junior engineers need deep technical help, team lead can take it or pull in senior engineers

### With junior forward deploy team

- **First line of support** — Go-to for questions and guidance
- **Quality review** — Reviews work before it goes to clients or senior engineers
- **Hands-on mentoring** — Pair programming or workflow building sessions when helpful
- **Tactical 1:1s** — Skills development feedback (engineering leadership handles career development separately)

## Time allocation

- **30–40%** on team lead work (monitor standups, keep an eye on Linear, help with deal desk, recruiting, training material)
- **60–70%** on focused IC work on whatever is most valuable

## Working principles

1. **Pragmatism over perfection** — Ship working solutions, iterate based on real client needs
2. **Enable** — Help the team grow their capabilities and flag performance issues early
3. **Horizontal support** — Help across deal desk, strategy, delivery, hiring, and coaching
4. **Lead by doing** — Maintain hands-on work to stay connected to team reality
