# Onboarding

## New hire checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Get your accounts set up">
    You'll be set up with access to Slack, Linear, and Geekbot. Make sure you can log in to all three.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the company background">
    Read the pitch deck and company background to understand our story and positioning.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Learn the architecture">
    Get an overview of the technical stack and how systems connect across our products.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch AI-driven development videos">
    Go through the [AI-driven development](/epd/ai-driven-development) training series. This is how we work with AI — it's core to everything we do.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Meet with your team lead">
    Get oriented on your lane, current delivery cycles, and immediate priorities.
  </Step>
</Steps>
