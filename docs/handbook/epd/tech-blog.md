# Tech blog

**Blog:** [Tech @ GrowthX](https://tech.growthx.ai/)

## Publishing process

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set up your author profile">
    Log in to the CMS and add yourself as an author.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a topic">
    Pick a topic from the content board or add your own directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write the content">
    Use one of our internal content pipelines to generate a first draft.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and polish">
    Come back 20–30 minutes later and adjust the final article. Double-check everything — links, formatting, accuracy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish">
    Copy the data into the CMS and publish manually.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update tracking">
    Update the content board with the assignment status.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
There's a Loom walkthrough available for a visual guide of this process. Ask the team for the link if you haven't seen it.
</Tip>
