# Copywriting

Write copy that can't be copied. This skill teaches the three rules of great copywriting — visualization, falsifiability, uniqueness — plus a full creation process with an evaluation rubric and five review personas.

## Trigger phrases

- Writing ads, headlines, taglines, or landing pages
- Creating email copy, newsletters, or sales pages
- Any marketing copy that needs to convert

## What it does

Applies three tests to every sentence: Can I visualize it? Can I falsify it? Can nobody else say this? Includes the zoom-in method for turning abstract language into concrete imagery, the "don't talk, only point" technique for falsifiable claims, and competitive uniqueness checks.

The full creation process has 5 steps (define the wire, core message, say it well, reality test, final review), a 6-dimension scoring rubric (visualization, falsifiability, uniqueness, instant clarity, word economy, conflict/tension), and 5 evaluation personas (The Scanner, The Skeptic, The Creative Director, The Target Customer, The Competitor). Copy must clear 4/5 average across all personas to ship.
