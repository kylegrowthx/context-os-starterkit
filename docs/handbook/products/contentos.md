# ContentOS

ContentOS is our all-batteries-included platform that makes content agencies obsolete. It understands content, personas, and strategy at a deep level, tracking work execution end-to-end from strategy to opportunities to work to outcomes.

## Strategic purpose

- **Deliver high-value enterprise contracts** that fund the business
- **Prove the "service-as-software" model** at scale
- **Build deep integrations** that create switching costs
- **Own the content operations category**

## What it does

ContentOS is a platform that:

- Understands your company, personas, and content strategy at a deep level
- Monitors traffic and finds opportunities based on deep company knowledge
- Collects data from multiple sources to build comprehensive knowledge bases
- Tracks work execution end-to-end: strategy → opportunities → work → outcomes

## Target audience

- Companies with dedicated content teams and serious content operations
- Managing editors who need a "CMO platform" to run content at scale
- Enterprise buyers who value setup, training, and ongoing support

## Two operating modes

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Self-operated" icon="user">
    Customer runs their own content operations. Platform access with native integrations and light-touch support.
  </Card>
  <Card title="GrowthX-operated" icon="users">
    Full-service content operations. Expert account managers, domain experts, forward-deployed engineers for setup, strategic advising and execution.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

**Both modes include:**
- Native integration with CheckThat for AI visibility opportunities
- Executive dashboard showing strategy → opportunities → execution → outcomes
- Deep CMS integration, analytics pixel integration, and training

## Key integrations

- **CheckThat** — connects for LLM rankings and trend opportunities
- **Output** — uses Output workflows (replaceable with custom workflows)
- **ExpertLayer** — integrates for expert hiring directly through the platform
