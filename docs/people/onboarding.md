# Onboarding

Everything you need to get started at GrowthX — your first day and first week.

Welcome to GrowthX! We're excited to have you. This page outlines what every team member — regardless of role — should complete in their first week.

> **Note:** Everyone receives a Linear project with individual tasks for each item below. Update your Linear project and mark each task complete as you go. Your manager will monitor your progress there.

**Day 1** is about setting up tools. **Week 1** is about getting to know your team and gathering as much company context as possible, before you focus on role-specific onboarding.

---

## Day 1: Get set up

1. **Activate your Okta account** — This is your single sign-on for all GrowthX tools.
2. **Set up Gmail with your GrowthX email** — Your primary work email for all company communication.
3. **Join Slack** — Use your invite link. This is where day-to-day communication happens.
4. **Set up 1Password** — Our password manager — use it for all work accounts.
5. **Join Notion workspace** — Documentation and project management live here.
6. **Join Linear** — Task tracking and onboarding progress are managed in Linear.

---

## Week 1: Build context

Week 1 is a great time to get to know your team and read key company documents. These docs give you all the context you need on GrowthX's unique history, vision, and business model.

### Week 1 checklist

- Complete Day 1 tool setup
- Read the Handbook — start with the key documents below
- Attend Onboarding: Strategy and Vision (hosted by Marcel Santilli, Co-founder and CEO)
- Attend Onboarding: Company Operations
- Attend Onboarding: Customer Use Cases
- Meet your team members
- Review culture and values
- Familiarize yourself with Slack channels and communication norms

---

## Key documents to read

During your first week, prioritize these foundational documents:

- **Mission and vision** — Understand what drives us and where we're headed.
- **Culture and values** — Learn how we work together and what we expect.
- **Vision and strategy** — Get the big picture on our market and model.
- **Start here** — The full handbook overview with links to every section.

---

## Onboarding sessions

You'll be automatically added to multiple onboarding bootcamp sessions during your first week. Attend each of these — they'll help you come up to speed much faster.

If you can't make a specific session, let your manager know.

---

## Questions?

If you have questions or need help, don't hesitate to reach out:

- **General questions** — Ask in #general on Slack
- **Your onboarding** — Reach out to your manager
- **Tools and access** — Ask in #it-support on Slack

We're here to support you. Looking forward to seeing you thrive!
