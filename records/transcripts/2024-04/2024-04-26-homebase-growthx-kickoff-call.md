## Key Topics

### Team Introductions and Background

  - Participants from Homebase: Kyle (monetization team), Kerry (content marketing lead), Niraj, Josh, Sachin
  - GrowthX team: Marcel Santilli (extensive content/SEO background), Sadira Sittampalam (AI/content specialist)
  - Marcel shared experiences building successful content hubs (e.g., secretintelligence.com, techbeacon.com)

### Homebase Metrics and Goals

  - North Star metric: 2D30s (2 employee actions on 2 different days over 30 days)
  - Leading indicators: OAM sign-ups, 1D1 (1 employee action within 24 hours)
  - SEO signups trending upward, but facing challenges with brand traffic cannibalization
  - Non-brand organic traffic growth of 150%+ year-over-year
  - Opportunity to diversify beyond time clock and scheduling keywords

### Current Content Production Process

  - Keyword research and opportunity finding
  - Agency-driven content creation (briefs, writing, uploading)
  - In-house review and editing
  - Publishing cadence of 10-15 articles per week
  - Challenges with version control and content repository management

### Content Strategy and Optimization Opportunities

  - Leverage AI for content ideation, briefing, and quality checks
  - Improve internal linking and content refreshes
  - Explore programmatic SEO for labor law and industry-specific content
  - Develop a more robust content distribution strategy (e.g., newsletters)
  - Optimize the editing process to reduce repetitive tasks

### AI Integration and Workflow Improvements

  - GrowthX to provide AI-powered tools for content creation and optimization
  - Focus on augmenting human expertise rather than full automation
  - Explore Airtable or similar tools for improved content workflow management
  - Implement version control and streamline the content refresh process
