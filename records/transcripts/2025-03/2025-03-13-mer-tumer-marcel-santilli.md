## Key Topics

### DiffBlue Background and Challenges

  - 6-year-old AI company focused on software development lifecycle testing
  - Uses reinforcement learning for deterministic results, unlike LLM-based solutions
  - Enterprise-heavy customer base in telco, finance, defense, aerospace
  - Lacking topical authority in Java testing and unit testing
  - Small organic traffic (\~5K visitors) despite valuable insights and expertise

### GrowthX Labs Approach

  - AI-assisted content creation with three layers of human expertise: writers, editors, and managing editors
  - 250-step workflow for each piece of content, combining LLM capabilities with human insight
  - Focus on high-quality, factually accurate content optimized for both traditional search and AI-based search
  - Includes technical SEO support (site speed, indexing, redirects)
  - Successful case studies with clients like Strapi, showing significant traffic growth

### Content Strategy and Collaboration

  - GrowthX Labs creates data-driven content calendars (e.g., 2,500 potential pieces for some clients)
  - Weekly/bi-weekly collaboration with client teams for content ideation and prioritization
  - Flexible approach to incorporate client expertise and adapt to changing priorities

### Pricing and Packages

  - Two main packages: Content (25 articles/month) and Programmatic (100 pages/month)
  - Programmatic package offers more surface area for testing different content clusters
  - 90-day pilot program recommended for initial engagement
