## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Update

HeyGen achieved record impressions last week with clicks following closely. The gap between impressions and clicks is decreasing as content matures. Articles take 1.5-3 months to start gaining traction; one example is a 3-week-old article that's already performing well. Two recently-launched tool pages are gaining momentum. To isolate other tool performance from the deep fake and face swap pages, Mariana excluded those high-traffic pages from analysis and confirmed the rest of the tool portfolio is trending upward.

### Content Strategy and Development

HeyGen is developing content around two main keyword themes: API and video creation keywords. The team identified 10 strong keywords for development over the next two weeks. Currently, there are approximately 140 blog posts in the pipeline, with 34 use-case-related posts ready for publication. For April-May, the team will focus heavily on personalized video use cases. B2B-focused keywords show different volume and performance patterns compared to general audience keywords, but the team is seeing early impressions and clicks on use-case-focused content.

### Technical Challenges and CMS Migration

HeyGen is migrating the website from Webflow to Sanity CMS (expected completion was yesterday but still in progress). This migration is creating validation errors that prevent new tool pages from being published; six pages are currently blocked in draft mode. The validation appears related to SEO settings, specifically whether pages are marked as indexed or not in the new system. Daniel Pak will provide instructions for running redirects in Sanity. Nav Singh is designated as the technical point of contact for troubleshooting publishing issues going forward.

### Personalized Video Use Cases and Benchmarks

Personalized video is a major strategic focus for April-May. Use cases include ABM campaigns, sales outreach (sending one-to-one videos to prospects instead of emails), customer thank-yous from CMO/CEO, and event follow-ups. HeyGen has limited internal data on effectiveness but cited external benchmarks: Nutanix LinkedIn post and an Anheuser-Busch case study showing 1.3-3x engagement improvement for personalized versus non-personalized videos (they sent 50,000+ personalized videos). Holly Xiao will share personalized video data points and industry reports in the GrowthX channel; Tony Faccenda will share the Anheuser-Busch case study.

### Ethical AI Usage

GrowthX and HeyGen are reviewing all UGC, face swap, and deep fake articles to ensure they include tips for using AI video ethically and responsibly. The defake tool page will be deprecated (not migrated to Sanity), but HeyGen will keep related articles live with proper ethical guidance.
