  - Typical client spend: $9,000-$18,000/month, 90-day pilot period
  - Current client base: \~45 clients, aiming for 120 by year-end

### Content Creation Process

  - Use AI for initial content generation and research
  - Human editors/writers refine and fact-check
  - Emphasis on creating high-quality "seeds" (content pieces) efficiently
  - Focus on iterative improvement and nurturing successful content

### Team Structure and Scaling

### Client Onboarding and Management

  - Quick sales process (often 1-2 30-minute meetings)
  - Extensive onboarding documentation and workflows
  - Use of AI-generated research and strategy documents
  - Regular check-ins and strategy calibration

### Future Plans and Challenges

  - Creating efficient processes for scaling (e.g., onboarding, quality control)
