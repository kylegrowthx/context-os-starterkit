## Key Topics

### Equity Allocation and Forecasting

  - Current equity pool ~90% allocated out of 6.5% total (9% out of 6.5% already granted)
  - Forecasting additional 4% allocation based on headcount model reaching 107 people
  - Engineering hires consuming significant equity; critical director-level AI hire requires 30-40 basis points (highly competitive offer from Intuit)
  - Board approval available to increase option pool if needed for strategic AI hire
  - Plan to implement 10-to-1 stock split to make share quantities more psychologically appealing in offer letters
  - Airtable-based system tracks who has equity, forecast model updates as new hires are added

### Bonus Structure Development

  - Created pro-rated bonus model based on roles and tenure with company
  - Engineering bonus revised from 15% to 10% due to already-high base compensation and equity
  - Targeting ~$1M total bonus pool tied to $20M ARR milestone (not a guaranteed bonus)
  - Designed as revenue milestone bonus to align entire team on aggressive growth targets
  - Once revenue ops is stronger, can connect to sales forecast for better predictability

### Managing Director/Editor Compensation

  - Proposed model: 2% of revenue split 50-50 between managing directors and managing editors on their accounts
  - For director managing ~16 accounts at $13k/month each ($2.5M revenue), 2% = ~$50k/year (20% bonus equivalent)
  - Unlimited upside: more accounts, more team capacity, more upsells = larger revenue base and bigger payouts
  - Only paid after client pilot period closes and payment received (cash-in-first incentive)
  - Q2/Q3 ramp: Q2 is stabilization period, Q3 forward books of business become locked for fair measurement
  - Alternative being considered: team-based bonus tied to hitting MRR targets (e.g., 115% growth goal) split across team
  - Challenge: account allocation will shift during first half of year as pods form and team matures

### Headcount and Financial Planning Tools

  - Airtable used for visual headcount planning with departments, roles, titles, levels; exports to spreadsheet for calculations
  - Spreadsheet-based models for equity forecast, bonus calculations, and compensation all tie back to headcount
  - Equity calculator tool needed to show weighted probability outcomes (scenarios for 0%, current valuation, various upside cases) to use in recruiting offers
  - Jason (new operations hire) will help with revenue ops automation to better track and forecast
  - All planning (headcount, equity, bonus, comp) building toward single source of truth tied to sales forecast
  - Once revenue ops mature, will have better visibility into actual vs. forecast and component costs
