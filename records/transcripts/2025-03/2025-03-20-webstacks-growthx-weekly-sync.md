## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Update

  - Google core update rolling out, expected to complete by early April
  - Slight increase in clicks, but no dramatic changes yet
  - Internal linking improvements made to address orphan pages
  - Some blogs (10-15) have lost significant traffic over time, not due to algorithm changes

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Focus on curating existing content ecosystem rather than producing high volumes of new content
  - Emphasis on creating embedded pillars on capabilities pages
  - Goal: Connect high-traffic blog content to pipeline-generating pages (capabilities, solutions, use cases)
  - Experiment with pushing traffic from lower-funnel blog content to capabilities pages

### Conversion Optimization

  - Current email nurture program is basic and not particularly strong
  - Most bottom-funnel conversions happen within single sessions
  - Short-term experiment: Add more targeted conversion panels (e.g., "Book a Call") to bottom-funnel content
  - Prioritize learning about user journeys and quality of traffic over raw lead volume

### New Thought Leadership Content

  - Short-form blog posts sharing insights from stakeholder interviews (e.g., CTO of Gong, CPO of Drata)
  - Not SEO-optimized, but meant to showcase Webstacks' perspective and expertise
  - Can be incorporated into longer-form blog content and used for social media posts
