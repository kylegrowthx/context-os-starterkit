## Summary

Bessemer Venture Partners investors Rayouf Alhumedhi and Bhavik Nagda met with Marcel Santilli to explore investment in GrowthX, an AI-powered content marketing agency that's grown to $6M ARR in just 2.5 months and recently closed a Series A round. Marcel articulated the market opportunity: marketing leaders want small, high-quality teams delivering outcomes with trusted experts—not 12-15 person teams, expensive freelancers, or unmanaged AI tools. GrowthX is winning through word-of-mouth with clients demanding proven expertise applied through AI-powered workflows, aligning with Bessemer's thesis on full-stack AI businesses in sales and marketing.
