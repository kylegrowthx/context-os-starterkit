2.  High-risk and niche industry payments
      - Sensitive topic requiring careful approach
      - Terms like "iGaming" acceptable, but avoid explicit gambling terms

      - Important but slightly lower priority than infrastructure

1.  "Swift alternatives" (Cross-border category)

2.  "How payment APIs support global transfers" (Payment infrastructure)
      - Focus on customized workflows for complex use cases (e.g. Uber, online gaming)

### Competitor Analysis

  - Surprising lack of content from competitor Nuvei in keyword research
  - Team will conduct additional research on Nuvei, Adyen, and payFacs/ISOs
  - Will examine how competitors approach sensitive terms in high-risk industries

  - Currently optimizing for British English, but seeing traffic drop from US
  - Team to investigate whether US or UK English is more effective for target markets

### Content Production Process

  - 7-10 articles per week once calibration is complete
  - Mark to lead review process, involving subject matter experts as needed
