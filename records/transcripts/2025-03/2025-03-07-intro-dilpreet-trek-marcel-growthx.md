## Summary

Trek Health CEO Dilpreet Sahota and GrowthX founder Marcel Santilli connected to explore content marketing and programmatic SEO services as Trek prepares to scale after closing their $10-11M Series A led by Coatue. ## Context

Trek Health is a 3-year-old healthtech company focused on helping health systems and hospitals negotiate contracts with payers (Blue Shield, United Healthcare, etc.) using newly published healthcare pricing transparency data. They pivoted 14 months ago from a different product and have since built strong product-market fit with a sales and engineering team. With Series A financing now in motion ($10-11M led by Coatue), Trek is expanding beyond sales and engineering to build out marketing, customer success, and other go-to-market functions. They currently compete against one venture-backed competitor, Turquoise Health, which has gained significant SEO traction (9,400+ published pages) on healthcare pricing transparency queries. Trek's CEO recognizes the 80-85% of their addressable market is still greenfield and wants to build a stronger marketing presence through programmatic SEO and content strategy before hiring an internal head of marketing.
