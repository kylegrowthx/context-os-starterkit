## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Update

  - 201 published articles to date
  - Significant decline in clicks and impressions last week, specifically for blog articles
  - Overall website metrics stable; issue isolated to blog content
  - Decline mostly in low-quality clicks from India and Brazil
  - U.S. market performance remained strong

### URL Structure Changes

  - Transitioning from '/art' to '/blog' in article URLs
  - Implementing 301 redirects from old to new URLs
  - Change aims to leverage historical SEO value of '/blog' structure
  - May cause temporary discovery delays for new content

### Content Strategy

  - 6 new tool pages launched, including Facebook ad fake video translator
  - 84 use cases published; 44-45 new content ideas pending approval
  - Discussion on handling "cold traffic" articles (e.g., general translation topics)
  - Decision: Keep high-traffic articles, optimize with stronger CTAs for conversion

### Content Refresh and Updates

  - Plan to update "best of" and trend articles for 2025
  - Will include new competitors (e.g., Creative, Vantus) in refreshed lists
  - Strategy to compound existing article authority by updating years and content

### Localization Plans

  - Focusing on Brazilian Portuguese market due to its importance
  - Considering Spanish localization as well
  - Exploring creation of separate website entity or subdirectory (e.g., heygen.com/pt-br)
  - Investigating localization tools: Localize.js, WigLox for potential use with Sanity CMS
