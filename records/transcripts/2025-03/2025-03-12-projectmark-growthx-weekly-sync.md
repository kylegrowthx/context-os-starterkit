### Performance Analysis

  - Click growth: 57 clicks this week vs. 45 last week (+27% WoW); impressions climbing steadily at 20K+ per week
  - Metric discrepancy: impressions and click counts both trending up, but CTR remains flat — indicates search-query-to-content mismatch or weak title/SERP positioning
  - High-impression, low-CTR articles (Jan 1 - Mar 10 GSC data):
      - "Construction Industry Leads": 23,642 impressions, 5 clicks (0.02% CTR)
      - "Ultimate Construction CRM Guide": 23,184 impressions, 14 clicks (0.06% CTR)
      - "SEO for Construction Companies": 17,000 impressions, 0 clicks (0% CTR)
      - Additional low-performers: 6K impressions with only 2 clicks; pre-construction checklist article also underperforming relative to impressions
  - Root cause analysis: older articles have weak titles and generic body copy; Google frequently overrides custom meta descriptions with dynamic snippets based on search intent

### Content Optimization Strategy

  - Dual-track execution: publish ~30-40 queued new articles in parallel with legacy content overhaul (not sequential)
  - Prioritization: target articles with 17K+ impressions and <0.1% CTR as priority rewrites (complete overhaul of titles, body, claims, structure)
  - Quality baseline: new articles have verified claims, proper linking, technical accuracy; legacy content often has unverified stats and gaps
  - Approach: Tom reviews all new content before publication to catch technical/procedural issues specific to construction ICP
  - Metadata optimization ongoing, but title + body copy overhaul is primary lever for CTR lift (metadata alone insufficient given Google's dynamic SERP behavior)
  - Secondary opportunities: downloadable checklists for high-intent keywords (e.g., "pre-construction checklist"), ebook creation deferred until old-content backlog 50% cleared

### Upcoming Content Enhancements

  - Client case study banner in design; Gus to send to Andrej once ready for Projectmark blog integration
  - Demo CTA (watch demo) to be added to all blog posts (positioning at ~50% depth to maximize visibility without intrusive early interruption)
  - Downloadable checklists planned for high-intent content (pre-construction checklist, pre-kick-off meeting checklist) — intent is to convert article visitors into leads
  - Ebook strategy deferred until legacy content backlog is 50% complete and CTR improvements validated
  - Potential automation of fact-checking process using product documentation crawlers (similar to existing headless CMS workflow for other GrowthX clients)
