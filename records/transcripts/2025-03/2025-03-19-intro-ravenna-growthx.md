## Key Topics

### Ravenna Company Background

- Founded by Taylor Halliday and Kevin (co-founder) in July 2022
- YC alumni, raised two rounds of funding (both preempted)
- Second round led by Drona; Tim and Matt from Drona sit on the board
- Product: Enterprise service management (ESM) tool deeply integrated with Slack
- Target market: Internal operations teams (IT, HR, ops)
- Differentiator: Full service experience within Slack, addressing pain points of traditional tools like ServiceNow (incumbent competitors)
- Inspiration: Kevin observed Simple Issue Manager at AWS — a pervasive ticketing system used across entire organization
- Market: Large TAM with many incumbents (ServiceNow, Jira, FreshService, Spiceworks, BMC, ServiceTitan) but fragmented and poor UX
- Luke Thomas: Growth and product lead, background in SEO, former Zapier employee, founder mindset

### GrowthX Company Background

- Founded by Marcel Santilli, former CMO at Deepgram
- Offers managed AI-powered content marketing and SEO services
- Developed proprietary workflow engine for content production (represents workflows as code)
- Grew Deepgram's traffic 24x and achieved major SEO inflection while CMO there
- Founded AImines.com newsletter, scaled to nearly 300,000 subscribers
- Team includes Matthew (former TechCrunch editor-in-chief, 10 years), Dave (former head of SEO at TripAdvisor, helped build Fanatics for 13 years generating $600M/year revenue), Kirk Lin (former ClickUp, built AI internal tooling)
- Pricing: $0.10 per word, 50-100k words/month

### GrowthX Service Model

- 12-week accelerator program followed by 12-month contract
- Rapid content strategy development and publishing (starting week 2-3, not months 1-2 like traditional agencies)
- Comprehensive service including content creation, SEO, distribution, and conversion optimization
- Dedicated team assigned to each client: managing director, editor, SEO specialist, publisher, graphics
- Weekly meetings and continuous strategy refinement
- Process includes 100+ steps per piece of content, millions of tokens processed, multiple review stages
- Clients range from seed stage to public companies (e.g., Reddit is a customer)
- Focuses on organic/SEO first, requires link building for early-stage companies with low domain authority
- Works with Abnormal Security, Webflow, StackBlitz
- Strategy includes content distribution, conversion optimization, and demand generation (AI outbound) but always starts with content

### GrowthX Challenges and Infrastructure

- Started with AirOps as low-code automation platform, but outgrew it at scale
- AirOps limitations: poor retrieval, no task parallelization, slow workflow execution (4+ hours), high costs ($30k/month)
- Built proprietary workflow runtime layer to parallelize, retrieve efficiently, and reduce costs
- Code-based workflows allow for scaling and version control better than low-code tools
- Management complexity: clients don't want to manage/adapt tools; they want outcomes

### Potential Collaboration

- Marcel expressed strong interest in becoming a Ravenna customer to solve internal communication chaos (45 clients, everything in Slack)
- Taylor and Luke showed clear interest in GrowthX's services for Ravenna's content marketing and early GTM
- GrowthX is currently fully booked through April 28th (2 kick-offs per week max due to team capacity)
- Both sides committed to opening a Slack channel for ongoing discussion and deeper product exploration
