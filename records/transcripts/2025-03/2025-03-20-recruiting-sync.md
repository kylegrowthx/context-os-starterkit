## Key Topics

### Company Growth and Financial Health

  - Grew from $2.9M ARR in Q4 to \~$7M ARR currently without burning cash
  - Projecting growth to $23M ARR this year with $5-6M burn (4x growth efficiency)
  - Raised at a conservative valuation to keep employee stock option costs low
  - Transparent financials shared company-wide

### Business Model and Value Proposition

  - Combines strategy, tech, and human expertise to deliver high-value services
  - "AI workflow as code" approach: configurable, adaptable, and continuously learning
  - Services-first model allows for rapid iteration and product development
  - Addressing $500B+ market opportunity in knowledge work automation

### Sales and Customer Acquisition

  - 158 deals generated ($22M pipeline), 15M closed, 48% win rate
  - 29-day average sales cycle, $158K average deal size
  - Primarily inbound leads, no dedicated sales team (yet)
  - Currently doing 2 kickoffs/week, aiming for 4/week by year-end

### Talent Strategy and Company Culture

  - Hiring top industry talent as subject matter experts and team leads
  - Emphasis on agency, proactivity, and raising the bar for colleagues
  - International hiring to access global talent pool and build capacity

### Future Plans and Investor Interest

  - Aiming for Series B later this year
  - Strong interest from top-tier VCs (Iconic, Bessemer, A16Z, Kleiner)
  - Potential to expose workflows as APIs and monetize the platform
