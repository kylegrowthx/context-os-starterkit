## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Update

  - 40 new high-search-volume topics identified, following cluster structure
  - Content pipeline set through end of next month
  - Technical SEO audit feedback: Web vitals improved, but still room for optimization
  - Blog page load times significantly improved

### Programmatic SEO Opportunities

  - Integration pages: Explore traction potential and specific use cases
  - Templates: Email scripts, industry/persona-based content at scale
  - Resource center: e.g., "Top 100 sales chat GPT prompts"
  - Feature-focused content: Leveraging Attention's product capabilities
  - Use case combinations: Utilizing new internal workflow builder feature

### Conversion Optimization

  - Current blog content driving impressions but lacking conversions
  - Need for inline CTAs, newsletter signups, and lead magnets
  - Implement lead scoring to identify high-quality traffic
  - Track ICP traffic sources (blog vs. landing pages)
  - Goal: 0.5-0.8% conversion rate for qualified ICP traffic

### Analytics and Tracking

  - Utilize existing tools (e.g., RB2B) for visitor identification
  - Map form submissions to initial entry URLs
  - Consider implementing more sophisticated tracking (webhooks, enrichment)
  - Tie blog traffic to RB2B data for deeper insights

### New Product Feature: Internal Workflow Builder

  - Zapier-like functionality within Attention's product
  - Enables creation of various use cases and workflows
  - Opportunity for programmatic content around use case combinations
