## Key Topics

### Company Vision & Positioning

  - Addressing pain points of CMOs/growth leaders: expensive agencies, thin internal teams, tool overload
  - Providing full growth function as a service, with transparency into process
  - Combining FOMO, growth stories, and access to top expertise to drive client interest
  - Positioning as premium, viable content solution vs "cheap AI content"

### Market Opportunity

  - $500B+ total addressable market from marketing/content/creative agency work
  - 27,000 mid-market/enterprise SEO tool customers spending $10-40K/month
  - $3-4B serviceable obtainable market from converting those customers alone

### Technology Stack

  - AI workflow engine to represent and scale expert workflows as code
  - ContentOS to guide content creation process
  - Task Hub for efficient task/expert matching
  - Learning engine to evaluate quality and improve over time

### Financial Projections

  - 2025 target: $22.9M ARR
  - Starting at 2 new clients/week, ramping to 4/week by Q4
  - $6M planned burn to reach targets
  - 74% projected margins
  - 3x growth efficiency ($ new ARR per $ burn)

### Client Acquisition Strategy

  - Currently more demand than capacity (50+ open opportunities)
  - Becoming more selective: avoiding clients with no content history, low domain authority
  - Segmenting by company stage (Series A to public) with differing approaches

### Onboarding & Knowledge Sharing

  - Considering week-long bootcamp for new client-facing hires
  - Developing curriculum modules on strategy, content, client communication
  - Leveraging diverse expertise of team to create internal/external education products
