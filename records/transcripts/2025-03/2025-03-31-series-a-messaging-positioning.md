## Context

GrowthX, founded by Marcel Santilli, is a B2B content marketing agency ($200k+/year engagements) with an ambitious product bet: CheckThat, an AI visibility index platform. The company has closed a Series A round and needs to publicly announce it within days. This meeting brings together Marcel (founder/CEO), Jason Gong and Matthew Panzarino (GrowthX leadership), and Bridget McGillivray (operations/organizational structure), to finalize messaging and execution plan.

The timing is urgent: they're balancing Marcel's desire to "get it out of the way" with the practical constraints of press release approval, website updates, and strategic messaging. Madrona (the lead investor) offered PR support, but GrowthX prefers to craft its own narrative. The team has a technical advantage — they've built proprietary internal tooling that powers client delivery — but must decide how much of that story to feature in the announcement versus leading with their growth trajectory and revenue profile.
