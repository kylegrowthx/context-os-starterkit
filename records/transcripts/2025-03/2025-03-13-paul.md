## Key Topics

### Growth X's Content Generation Process

  - Uses a multi-step workflow including brief creation, research, outline generation, and draft writing
  - Leverages AI tools like Claude 3.7 and perplexity for content creation and research
  - Allows for human intervention and customization at various stages
  - Includes SEO analysis, fact-checking, and internal linking

### Improvements for Strapi's Content Strategy

  - Adding a blacklist for specific URLs (e.g., /ecosystem) to exclude from research and internal linking
  - Implementing ability to add resource links for specific content to guide article creation
  - Expanding company and user context fields in Airtable for better brief generation

### Image Generation

  - Growth X can create custom image styles using tools like recraft.ai
  - Strapi team to provide reference images for developer-friendly, on-brand visuals
  - Potential for generating both cover images and in-article visuals

### Localization and Translation

  - Exploring full localization capabilities, considering URL structure and internal linking challenges
  - Potential collaboration on content about handling localization at scale with Strapi and AI

### Future Developments
