## Key Topics

### Role Differentiation: Managing Editor vs. Account Manager

  - Managing Editors:
      - Lead pods of 4-5 accounts
      - Responsible for overall strategy and content quality
      - Often more client-facing
  - Account Managers:
      - Support strategy development
      - Conduct keyword and competitor research
      - Manage content workflows and generation
      - Train and oversee writers
      - Focus on iterating and optimizing processes based on client feedback

### Team Structure and Evolution

  - Recent shift to pod structure (December):
      - 1 Managing Editor
      - 1-2 Account Managers
      - \~2 Writers
      - Support roles (copy editor, production)
  - Currently have 9 Account Managers, with more starting soon
  - Aiming for dedicated full-time support across pods

### Ideal Candidate Profile

  - Content-first background with SEO knowledge
  - 4+ years of experience (average 5-6 years)
  - Intellectual curiosity and willingness to learn AI content creation
  - Experience with or interest in testing and experimentation
  - Adaptable to fast-paced, changing startup environment

### Hiring Process and Assessments

  - Video assessment submission
  - AI-powered initial profile screening (recently adjusted)
  - Workable verbal and comprehension assessment
  - Interview with Kyle (sometimes additional Managing Editor input)
  - Plans to involve Managing Directors in future hiring

### Performance Evaluation

  - Currently qualitative-based:
      - Account growth
      - Proactive problem-solving
      - Ideation and innovation
  - Developing more structured quantitative metrics
  - Managing Directors to help refine evaluation process

### Training and Onboarding

  - Recognized need for improved training programs
  - Current resources: Notion documentation, standard operating procedures, Circle videos
  - Plans to formalize and expand training syllabus

## Action Items

- Develop quantitative performance metrics for Account Managers
- Involve Managing Directors in hiring process and pod management
- Review and potentially adjust Workable assessment process
- Implement more structured performance evaluation system
