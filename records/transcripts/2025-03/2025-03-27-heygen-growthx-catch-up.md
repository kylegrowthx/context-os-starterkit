## Context

HeyGen is a leading AI video generation platform offering face-swapping and video creation capabilities. GrowthX has been executing a content marketing and SEO engagement with HeyGen since October 2024, with a three-month intensive spike agreement starting mid-January 2025 at $30K/month focused on blog post publishing, tools pages, and content repurposing. Nav Singh leads HeyGen's SEO efforts but is stepping back from day-to-day management, with Daniel Pak (newly hired SEO lead) taking ownership. The relationship reached a critical inflection point in late March: HeyGen has not seen the anticipated lift in non-brand organic traffic despite the increased investment and scale of content production, and Nav is unwilling to commit to further spending without demonstrable improvements and clear strategy alignment. This call served as a relationship reset, with GrowthX introducing its new Chief Content Officer (Matthew Panzarino, formerly Editor-in-Chief of TechCrunch) and reaffirming commitment to the partnership through focused prioritization and potential product integrations.
