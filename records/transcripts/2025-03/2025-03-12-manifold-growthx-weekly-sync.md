## Context

Manifold is a biomedical research data platform co-developed by Manifold and the Broad Institute (in collaboration with Verily and Microsoft). Manifold provides a trusted research environment (TRE) for secure access to genomic and biomedical research data, allowing researchers to collaborate and run computational analyses. Manifold will eventually replace the Terra platform — a well-known research environment currently managed by Broad's product team — as part of a strategic transition.

GrowthX is engaged to produce content that explains TRE concepts and Manifold's platform capabilities to a research audience. This is the first recurring sync between the two teams focused on content strategy, process feedback, and quality assurance. Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX leadership) is driving overall strategy and resource planning; David Galic (GrowthX content lead) is managing the article production; and Taylor Gleason (Manifold) is providing subject-matter expertise, feedback, and strategic guidance on content prioritization.
