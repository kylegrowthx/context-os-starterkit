  - Uses AI with human editors in the loop for fact-checking and quality control
  - Multi-step editorial process: concept → outline → content brief → final article (hundreds of steps in workflow)
  - Focuses on brand voice, tone, and factual accuracy; customer feedback applied to calibration articles
  - Scalable: 20-30 articles/month (or up to 40-50/month with calibration) at $6k/month (vs. typical $20k/month)
  - Currently uses AirOps platform; developing proprietary solution to reduce dependency

### Launch Potato's Programmatic SEO Project

  - Targeting auto insurance space with localized, state-level data
  - Currently strong in finance, shopping deals, store closings content
  - Syndication partnerships (e.g., MSN News) establish authority
  - Concerns about how Google will perceive insurance content given current topical focus

### Content Strategy Insights
