## Key Topics

### Airtable Workflow Overview

  - Statuses: Proposed, Review for Following Weeks, Scheduled, Creating Article, Client Review, Edited Meets for Review, Publishing Queue, Published
  - Collaborative process between GrowthX and Prophecy for topic selection and content refinement
  - Additional statuses for special cases (e.g., needs data to sign off, needs SME input)

### Content Research and Creation Process

  - Initial topics derived from JTBDs identified during onboarding
  - Keyword research using tools like Semrush to identify gaps and opportunities
  - AI-powered research flow gathers topic information from various sources
  - Human review ensures Prophecy's narrative is incorporated
  - Recent addition: Incorporating content from Prophecy's webinars to enrich articles

### SEO and Content Strategy

  - Focus on JTBD and topics rather than specific keywords
  - Aim to create content that addresses customer pain points and Prophecy's solutions
  - Balance between SEO-driven content and bottom-funnel content for sales enablement
  - Ongoing adaptation to SEO trends and Google algorithm updates

### Collaboration and Feedback

  - GrowthX team open to feedback and pushback on proposed topics
  - Desire for more input on sales team questions and customer pain points
  - Possibility of including sales team members in future calls for direct input
