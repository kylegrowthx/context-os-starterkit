### JTBD Research Proposal

  - 6-week project to understand core customer motivations for choosing GrowthX
  - Deliverables include buyer motivation clarity, job segments, unified messaging, and interview materials
  - Process involves screening, interviews, analysis, and reporting
  - Potential for additional phases: churn analysis and sales team training
