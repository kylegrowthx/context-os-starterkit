## Summary

George Haikal from Techstars Associates presented a six-week Jobs-to-be-Done research proposal to Daniel Lopes to help GrowthX pinpoint why customers hire them and create a unified messaging framework. GrowthX is experiencing strong demand with high closing rates but wants to move beyond Marcel's intuition to a structured, behavioral framework that the entire team can leverage. The main conversation centered on scope and pricing: George proposed $20k for the full six-week project, but Daniel flagged that internal stakeholders are thinking about $10k or potentially $1k per interview (for ~10 interviews), requiring George to explore adjusted scope options.

## Context

GrowthX has recently raised $12.5 million from Madrona Venture Group and is operating at capacity with 50 team members and strong pipeline momentum. Daniel oversees business operations and is driving the JTBD initiative after being impressed by George's background and mentorship from JTBD pioneers Bob Moesta and Neil Rackham. George works with Techstars companies as a JTBD practitioner and currently consults for another company on the same research end-to-end. The two met at Techstars where Daniel found George's work and passion compelling. This meeting was a proposal presentation with a hard stop at 25 minutes due to Daniel's board call.

  - Current challenge is managing bandwidth and capacity, not lead generation

### JTBD Research Proposal

  - 6-week project to understand core customer motivations for choosing GrowthX
  - Deliverables include buyer motivation clarity, job segments, unified messaging, and interview materials
  - Process involves screening, interviews, analysis, and reporting
  - Potential for additional phases: churn analysis and sales team training

### Value and Implementation

  - Aims to create a framework for distilling customer insights beyond intuition
  - Could help tailor product positioning, accelerate growth, and strengthen market positioning
  - Potential to streamline internal alignment across marketing, sales, and product teams
  - May be incorporated into GrowthX's strategy phase for client engagements

### Budget and Scope Considerations

  - Initial proposal at $20k seen as high by GrowthX team
  - Discussion around potentially reducing scope to align with \~$10k budget expectation
  - Suggestion to workshop different options or adjust the package

### Future Opportunities

  - Potential for JTBD expertise to become a horizontal role within GrowthX
  - Could be offered as an additional service to clients during their strategy phase
  - Opportunity to differentiate GrowthX's approach from traditional marketing strategies
