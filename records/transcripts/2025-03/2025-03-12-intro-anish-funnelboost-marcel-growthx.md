## Summary

Marcel and Anish discussed potential partnership between GrowthX and FunnelBoost — FunnelBoost is building a real-time social listening platform to find buying signals on Reddit, Twitter, LinkedIn, and Discord, while GrowthX creates AI-powered content at scale with a proprietary workflow engine and runtime layer. Both companies recently closed funding (FunnelBoost mid-round at ~$7M transaction volume from one customer; GrowthX at $7M ARR) and are aggressively hiring. ## Context

Anish Chopra is the CEO of FunnelBoost, an early-stage startup founded around the insight that businesses need to find high-intent buyers at the moment they're actively in the market. The company recently closed half its Series A funding round (led by Sequoia investor Stephanie) and is building a platform that searches for real-time buying signals across social platforms — currently focused on Reddit with rapid expansion to Twitter, LinkedIn, and Discord. One of Anish's current customers, a payments processor, closed 10 customers in the first month using FunnelBoost's lead-finding capabilities (generating $7M in transaction volume).

Marcel Santilli is a co-founder at GrowthX, which operates as a B2B content marketing agency (services side) that also builds proprietary software for AI-powered content generation at scale. GrowthX recently closed a Series A funding round (a couple weeks before this call) and has scaled to $7M ARR while working with major brands like Reddit, Webflow, Ramp, and Attention.com. The company has built an AI workflow engine and runtime layer that handles the complexity of publishing hundreds of pieces of content with high quality — including research, strategy, calibration, drafting, optimization, and cross-linking. The connection between Anish and Marcel came through Stephanie, the shared investor, who suggested they talk about potential synergies.
