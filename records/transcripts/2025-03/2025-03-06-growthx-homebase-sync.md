## Summary

GrowthX and Homebase aligned on a major content strategy pivot—shifting from new glossary content to focus on revamping existing calculator pages (especially the hourly salary calculator) and building state-specific programmatic SEO content around payroll calculation guides. Kerry outlined new performance tracking metrics to measure effectiveness: target keyword rankings per page, total organic keyword volume, and keyword clustering by topic (payroll, starting a business) to calculate cost-per-keyword and cost-per-click. The team committed to continued production of "how to start a business" content due to strong conversion signals (1-2 signups per month), and agreed to implement better interlinking strategies, including manual review for glossary placement and AI-assisted discovery of linking opportunities.

## Context

Homebase is a home services business software platform where GrowthX provides SEO and content marketing services. This quarterly sync reviewed content performance and strategy since a major site migration in mid-October. Kerry McCreadie leads content strategy at Homebase and Jess Scott is the primary content lead at GrowthX, with Bisera Stankovska handling analytics and reporting. The team has shipped 60 glossary entries and is seeing consistent organic traffic growth, but Kerry wants to shift investment toward calculator pages and programmatic content around those calculators—a higher-leverage approach that can generate more backlinks and volume. The meeting also discussed internal blog structure challenges that Homebase wants to address in H2 2025.
