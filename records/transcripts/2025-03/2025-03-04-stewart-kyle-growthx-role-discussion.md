### New Pod Structure and Managing Director Role

  - Implemented pod structure in December with Managing Editors leading pods of 2 account managers and 1-2 writers
  - Creating Managing Director role to oversee 2-3 pods each
  - MDs will ensure proper strategy, build client relationships, and free up Kyle's capacity
  - Aiming for pods to manage 7-8 accounts each (up from current 4-5)

### Product and Service Hybrid Approach

  - GrowthX combines powerful tech/AI capabilities with high-touch customer service
  - Unique position as both a product and service company
  - Strong word-of-mouth and "insider" reputation among clients
