## Key Topics

### Programmatic SEO Approach at Wise

  - Three-tiered approach:
    1.  Standalone applications (e.g., currency calculator)
    2.  Editorial content for exploration
    3.  Scalable landing pages via custom CMS
  - Phased launches: start with MVP, iterate based on performance
  - Example: Invoice templates started as basic landing pages, evolved to interactive widgets
  - Success requires balancing aggressive growth with sustainable quality and brand consistency

### Planning and Execution

  - Template mapping: cluster keywords around potential page types
  - Assess template viability: size, difficulty to build, user experience importance
  - Minimum viable set of templates to validate concept before full build-out
  - Flexibility in approach: sometimes full engineering involvement, other times CMS-based
  - Data dependencies and API integrations must be considered early to avoid rework
  - Brand consistency vs. speed tradeoffs need explicit stakeholder alignment

### Resourcing and Technical Considerations

  - Ideal: dedicated engineer(s) familiar with client's systems
  - Alternative: technical SEO specialists with development skills
  - Challenge: balancing brand consistency with speed of implementation
  - CMS flexibility as key infrastructure requirement; rigid systems create bottlenecks
  - Shared knowledge across team members critical—avoid single points of failure

### Performance Measurement and Expectations

  - Early KPIs: indexed pages and pages with clicks (first 3-6 months)
  - Revenue attribution may take longer, especially for competitive verticals
  - Set realistic timelines: 6-12 months for initial traction, potentially longer for significant revenue
  - Tracking performance across template variations helps refine future launches
  - Multiple KPI frameworks needed for different stakeholders (executives vs. operations teams)

### Client Engagement Strategy

  - Educate clients on phased approach and realistic timelines
  - Focus on quick wins and demonstrable progress in first 3 months
  - Balance between aggressive growth and sustainable quality
  - Setting client expectations upfront reduces churn and scope creep
  - Shared documentation and regular communication critical for ongoing alignment

### Real-World Client Examples

  - Stacklets: potential GrowthX client for exploring app/template showcase strategy
  - Wise: model for three-tiered programmatic content at scale
  - Currency calculators and tools as engagement hooks before template-driven pages
  - Domain authority and backlink strategy implications for new domains
