## Summary

Ramp and GrowthX held their weekly check-in with Mara Leighton (new associate director at GrowthX) joining the call. Vivek Shankar reviewed SEO performance across Ramp's blog, per diem, and expense category pages, flagging concerning click losses on blog pages (70% drop over two weeks centered on 6 pages) and secondary keyword drops on per diem pages due to .gov sites appearing in SERPs.
