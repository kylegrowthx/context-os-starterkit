## Context

Breyta is a user research platform exploring content marketing to build audience and visibility. Chris reached out to GrowthX for a pilot engagement worth $40,500 to create content around transcription, AI, and research tools. However, the project stalled during calibration due to content quality issues—articles contained inaccuracies, and the tone-of-voice document wasn't being properly applied to outputs. Chris demonstrated an alternative AI tool that produced higher-quality results and wanted to explore a different approach rather than abandoning the engagement entirely.

Kyle brought up that GrowthX had recently switched research tools from u.com to Perplexity Pro, which may have contributed to quality drops. He acknowledged this was a unique failure point for GrowthX and wanted to work through it transparently to ensure Breyta felt it got value from the investment.
