## Summary

GrowthX and Webflow finalized the structure of a 90-day pilot ($15k/month) followed by a 12-month contract ($24k/month), with GrowthX agreeing to reduce scope proportionally during the pilot phase. The team locked in critical contract terms, including a 7-day cancellation notice during the pilot period and removal of auto-renewal clauses, while Webflow committed to internally review logo usage and publicity rights permissions. Next steps include Webflow sending legal redlines by early the following week and GrowthX updating the contract with separate pilot and 12-month scopes, with kickoff targeted for April 14th or 21st depending on internal approvals.

## Context

Webflow is a no-code website builder and CMS platform. This meeting brought together Webflow's procurement team (Anushri Gupta, Steven Stasevych as procurement specialist) with GrowthX leadership (Marcel Santilli, Bruno Santilli, Bridget McGillivray) to finalize the commercial terms for a high-value AI-powered content services engagement. Anushri was managing the deal closure from Webflow's side before taking a week-long vacation and wanted to move quickly to lock in contract terms. The deal structure had evolved from an initial POC framing into a 90-day pilot with mandatory 12-month renewal, and this call aimed to resolve remaining legal, financial, and commercial considerations so both teams could move toward kickoff and execution.

  - Shifted from POC to 90-day pilot + 12-month commitment structure

  - GrowthX to update contract with separate scopes for pilot and 12-month periods

  - Pilot: $15,000/month for 3 months ($45,000 total)
