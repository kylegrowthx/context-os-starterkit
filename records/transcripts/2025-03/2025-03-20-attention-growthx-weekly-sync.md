## Key Topics

### Current SEO Performance

  - Weekly clicks hovering around 7,500, not breaking out of this range
  - Impressions on upward trajectory, likely not hit by recent algorithm updates
  - Focus on changing trajectory to accelerate traffic and attract right type of visitors

### Backlink Strategy

  - Current backlink profile underperforming; competitors have more backlinks to their articles
  - Tactics discussed:
      - Leverage GrowthX's client network for initial backlinks
      - Outreach to VC-backed companies and incubators for link exchanges
      - Target marketing niche (non-competitive) companies
      - Broken link building: offer replacement content for broken links on target blogs
      - Data-driven journalism pieces using unique company data
      - Thought leadership content for social media traction

### Outbound Email Optimization

  - Current stats: \~60% open rate, 30 emails per mailbox, 180-210 emails per domain daily
  - Recommendations:
      - Reduce to 90-100 emails per domain (max 3 per domain)
      - Split across multiple workspaces to avoid restrictions
      - Continue inbox placement tasks for better deliverability

### Internal Keyword Strategy

  - Main site pages lack clear keyword focus, making it difficult for Google to understand context
  - Key areas for improvement:
      - Optimize page titles with target keywords (e.g., "AI sales agent" instead of generic descriptions)
      - Refine content on product pages to include relevant keywords
      - Align internal pages with blog content for cohesive topical authority

### Programmatic Content Ideas

  - Competitor comparison pages
  - Feature-specific pages
  - Industry and use case pages (e.g., "How to improve sales calls by industry")
  - Integration opportunity pages (with consideration for competition)
  - Buying guides, "best of" lists
  - Sales script templates by industry
  - Focus on content that aligns with decision-maker personas (VPs of Sales, RevOps) rather than individual contributors

### Technical SEO Improvements

  - Add article schema to blog posts for better context and potential ranking boost
  - Include author information on the page (currently only in image) with proper schema markup
