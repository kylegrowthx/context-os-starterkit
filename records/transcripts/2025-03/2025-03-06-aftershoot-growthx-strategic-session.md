## Key Topics

### Current Content Performance

  - AfterShoot's blog has good authority score and organic search traffic
  - Close to competitors' performance, providing a solid starting point
  - Allows for methodical approach rather than desperate tactics

### Content Clusters and Keyword Research

  - GrowthX presented clusters based on competitor analysis and keyword gap research
  - Clusters include: Editing Tools, Equipment Guides, Photography Techniques, Styles/Genres, Workflow Optimization, AI Photo Editing
  - AfterShoot feedback:
      - Equipment Guides and Photography Techniques seen as promising clusters
      - Editing Tools cluster needs significant refinement (70% off-target)
      - Styles/Genres and Workflow Optimization viewed positively
      - AI Photo Editing needs manual filtering but has potential

### Content Prioritization Strategy

  - AfterShoot recommends focusing on:
    1.  In-market keywords
    2.  Out-market keywords focused on ICPs
    3.  Broader experimentation later
  - Emphasis on proving business case with relevant, converting traffic first
  - Balance needed between product-specific content and broader educational pieces

### Calibration Process

  - Will select 2 topics for initial content creation to calibrate tone, structure, and approach
  - Likely starting with Styles/Genres or Equipment clusters
  - AfterShoot to provide feedback on these initial pieces to refine the content strategy

### Keyword Research Refinement

  - AfterShoot to share their internal keyword research sheet and Loom video explaining their approach
  - GrowthX to take another pass at keyword research and clustering based on this new information
  - Focus on more in-market and ICP-relevant keywords
