## Key Topics

### Current Content Performance

  - AfterShoot's blog has good authority score and organic search traffic
  - Close to competitors' performance, providing a solid starting point
  - Allows for methodical approach rather than desperate tactics

### Content Clusters and Keyword Research

  - GrowthX presented clusters based on competitor analysis and keyword gap research
  - Clusters include: Editing Tools, Equipment Guides, Photography Techniques, Styles/Genres, Workflow Optimization, AI Photo Editing
  - AfterShoot feedback:
      - Equipment Guides and Photography Techniques seen as promising clusters
      - Editing Tools cluster needs significant refinement (70% off-target)
      - Styles/Genres and Workflow Optimization viewed positively
      - AI Photo Editing needs manual filtering but has potential

### Content Prioritization Strategy

  - AfterShoot's three-tier framework (Rasheed):
    1.  In-market keywords first (photographers actively searching for solutions — e.g., "best calling software")
    2.  Out-market keywords targeting ICPs (e.g., "best cameras for macro photography" for gear-focused photographers)
    3.  Broader experimentation later (generic keywords with high volume but lower intent)
  - Rationale: prove business case with converting traffic before experimenting with volume-focused content
  - Product placement strategy: in-market articles will lean heavily on AfterShoot screenshots and step-by-step guides; out-market articles lighter; broader content may be educational pillars with peripheral product mentions

### Calibration Process

  - Select 2 topics from preferred clusters (Styles/Genres or Equipment) for content calibration
  - Goals: dial in voice, tone, article structure, and how to address AfterShoot's ICP
  - GrowthX expects "a good amount of feedback" in this phase and will iterate LLM prompts and workflows based on learnings
  - Parallel workstream: Keyword research refinement continues while calibration happens — can unblock GrowthX to start production sooner
  - Kyle highlighted risk: in-market queries may not be blog-friendly; will need to watch for editorial fit

### Keyword Research Refinement

  - Rasheed's core ask: "getting keyword research right is more important than calibration" — wants GrowthX to iterate on clusters based on AfterShoot's internal methodology
  - Monalisa to share keyword research sheet + glossary research list (photographer-level keywords)
  - Rasheed to create Loom video explaining AfterShoot's keyword structure and thinking, synced with Monalisa on data format
  - GrowthX (David) to review new keyword data + product info (AI retouching, masking, cropping features), then re-run clustering analysis
  - Expected outcome: 303 items refined to fewer, higher-precision clusters aligned with AfterShoot's internal categorization
