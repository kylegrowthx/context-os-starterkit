## Key Topics

### Current Work Streams Overview

  - Editorial content (new and refreshes): Most common offering, typically 50k-100k words/month, starting at $9k
  - Programmatic content: Repeatable content following templates, growing to 20-30% of business
  - Link building: Newer offering, reaching about 10% of business
  - Outbound email campaigns: Exploring potential in this area

### Editorial Content Improvements

  - Need better tracking and accountability for progress towards engagement goals
  - Opportunity to improve content refresh processes for greater SEO impact
  - Refining workflows for different content types beyond new editorial

### Programmatic Content Challenges

  - Current processes skew towards editorial, need more repeatable workflows
  - Lack of engineering capacity for automated content publishing is a bottleneck
  - Working with experienced team members (e.g., Dave) to improve processes

### Link Building and Outreach

  - Jimmy helping to leverage existing workflows for programmatic outreach
  - Facing complexities in modern outreach campaigns (e.g., managing multiple mailboxes)
  - Evaluating long-term viability of outbound email campaigns work stream

### Emerging Work Streams

  - Content repurposing: Creating articles from podcasts, webinars, etc.
  - Thought leadership content on platforms like Medium
  - Exploring email content generation to support client SDR teams
  - Considering long-form content creation (e.g., ebooks, whitepapers)

### Client Engagement Strategy

  - Proactively pitching new ideas to clients (e.g., content repurposing for Galileo)
  - Balancing innovation with capacity and compensation considerations
  - Using GrowthX as a "client" to validate new offerings before pitching to external clients
