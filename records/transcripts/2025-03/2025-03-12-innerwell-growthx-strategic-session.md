## Overview

## Key Topics

### Location Page Optimization

  - Revamp location pages to summarize all available services in each state
  - Link location pages to service pages and clinician listings
  - Implement a "join waitlist" feature for unavailable services
  - Create a seamless flow from state-level pages to city-level pages and clinician information

### Knowledge Hub Development

  - Create educational content in a blog-like format with a more formal tone
  - Focus on symptom-based and condition-based content
  - Explore compatibility between treatments, demographic-specific information, and post-care recommendations
  - Ensure clinical accuracy through review process (new head of operations to support)

### SEO Improvements

  - Optimize meta descriptions and tags
  - Enhance internal linking structure
  - Implement FAQ sections on relevant pages
  - Focus on local SEO strategies
  - Refresh existing content as needed

### Technical Improvements

  - Address slow page speed (currently 15s load time on homepage)
  - Ensure mobile-friendliness across the site
  - Investigate image optimization, particularly for the homepage hero image

### Link Building Strategy

  - Leverage existing customer base for initial internal links
  - Scale outreach efforts through Jimmy's work
  - Explore potential content collaboration opportunities in the future
