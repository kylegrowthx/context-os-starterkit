## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

- Three-pronged approach: analyze your website, develop personas using workflows, and identify mindshare competitors already ranking for the terms you want to target.
- Editorial content and programmatic SEO (e.g., glossaries, app catalogs) work in tandem; the editorial creates domain authority and signals, programmatic (templates, directories) scales opportunities across long-tail use cases.
- Weekly strategy refinement meetings; can pivot quickly (example: Ramp pivoted the whole team to Treasury content before Super Bowl ads).
- Access to managing directors: Dave (head of SEO at TripAdvisor, 13 years at Fanatics), Jacob (built J2, first content marketer), Matthew (editor-in-chief of TechCrunch for 10 years, built team to 120 writers/editors).

### Vapi-Specific Strategy Ideas

- Pre-generated voice agent templates with LLM prompts (like Figma UI kits) — if someone searches "voice agent for X use case", the enriched demo page outperforms description-only pages.
- Two-class strategy: blog content (editorial) targets non-technical/broader audience for backlinks and newsletter conversion; glossary, agent catalog, and templates target developers directly.
- Runnable agent demos as SEO lever: every new agent users build can become an indexed, enriched showcase page — creates network effects and internal linking.
- Parallel to StackBlitz Boltz (0→20M growth): instead of template descriptions, create full app descriptions + pre-built, pre-prompted agents ready to run.

### Timeline and Process

- 90-day pilot to de-risk (no long sales cycle; if direction changes, Vapi walks away with value).
- Kickoff + calibration → strategy development → Week 3-4 publish first editorial content.
- Programmatic side: create page templates with Vapi (leveraging existing product experience/demo framework), start enriching agent showcase pages in parallel.
- Weekly strategy sync meetings; entire team (managing editor, managing director) assigned; full access to advisory board.
- Kickoff availability: April 21st (prioritized by Bruno) or April 28th (fallback); GrowthX does 2 kickoffs/week max to maintain quality.
- Programmatic SEO and link building not in initial proposal but can be added mid-pilot as ICP clarity improves.

### Additional Services & Scope Decisions

- **Link building:** Not included in initial proposal but recommended by Marcel. Vapi's low domain authority (44.5k backlinks) means content takes longer to rank; high-authority links (government, industry sources) speed this up significantly. Reference: Wise.com built to 120M visitors/month with dedicated backlinking team + M&A strategy for acquisitions.
- **Programmatic SEO (glossary, agent catalog, template pages):** Not included in initial proposal. GrowthX doesn't need it to kickoff, but can layer it in as ICP discovery clarifies and editorial baseline is established.
- **Pricing:** Transparent, available on website. GrowthX has capacity constraint: 2 kickoffs/week max to maintain quality. SLW (Statement of Work) sent to Vapi for review.

### Mutual Collaboration Opportunities

- **Strategic exchange:** Marcel offers ongoing growth advisory (beyond SEO/content) in exchange for GrowthX building runnable voice agent use cases with Vapi. No cash payment; both teams get content co-marketing and product collaboration.
- **Vapicon content:** Mitch flagged need for Vapicon content; Marcel has connections (Andy from Scale) and can help amplify.
- **Product integrations:**
  - Qualification bot embedded in GrowthX website (replaces manual lead sorting, reduces garbage outreach).
  - AI-powered interview bots for GrowthX's customer research → written content pipeline.
  - Voice agents for GrowthX recruiting (vetting thousands of applicants via workflow + video interview loop; interview-based content with customers).
  - Potential for exec.com (Sean's AI coaching platform) style use cases.
- **Promotion:** Both teams commit to mutual promotion; GrowthX team will promote Vapi through its channels and content; Vapi gives platform and content featuring to GrowthX team and services.
