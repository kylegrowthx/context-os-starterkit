## Key Topics

### Sunbit Business Model and Scale

- Jason Beltran has been with Sunbit for 5.5 years, growing from $20M to $350M+ annual revenue
- B2B2C model: merchants promote Sunbit to their customers at point of need (dentist or auto repair shop)
- Average loan amount: $1,000; average loan length: 12 months
- 30% of customers use Sunbit more than once
- 60% of customers are women; average customer age is 35

### Target Audience and Content Positioning

- Focus on brick-and-mortar services, not e-commerce
- Emphasis on "needs" (e.g., dental work, auto repairs) rather than "wants"
- Avoid terms like "affordability"; prefer "budget-friendly"
- Target both consumers (B2C) and small businesses (B2B), especially dental practices and auto repair shops
- Goal: Associate Sunbit with dentistry and auto services in consumers' minds via informational content

### Regulatory and Approval Constraints

- Avoid direct discussions about financing due to regulatory concerns (bank and legal review required)
- Focus on informational content related to services (e.g., dental care tips, tire maintenance)
- Utilize credible sources like American Dental Association for dental information
- All content must pass legal and banking approval
- Streamlined approval process possible if content stays within certain parameters and avoids financing discussion

### SEO and Traffic Strategy

- Current site has good authority due to merchant backlinks
- Aim to increase keyword volume and organic traffic significantly
- Target: 5x traffic growth
- Primary verticals: dentistry and auto repair
- Competitors to watch: CareCredit and Cherry.com
- Strong SEO foundation enables both B2C consumer acquisition and B2B demo requests from micro-businesses

### Content Creation and Approval Workflow

- WordPress CMS for publishing
- Legal team and bank must approve all content
- Streamlined approval process possible if content stays within certain parameters
- Need to log all published content for potential audits
- GrowthX will manage the submission and approval cycle

### Analytics and Measurement

- Access needed for WordPress, Google Search Console, Google Analytics
- Mixpanel used for tracking from shop directory to purchase (end-to-end funnel)
- Plans to tag entire website with Mixpanel for full funnel tracking
- Strong analytics foundation enables ROI measurement and iteration

### Go-to-Market and Channel Strategy

- Content will be repurposed across social media, video, email campaigns, and traditional media
- Planned brand refresh next year (new logo and look, keeping the Sunbit name)
- Future investments in influencer partnerships, paid social, connected TV, out-of-home, and guerrilla marketing
- Corporate social responsibility initiatives aligned with served verticals
