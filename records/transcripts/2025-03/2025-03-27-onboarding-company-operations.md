## Summary

Bridget McGillivray from GrowthX operations led an onboarding session for new employees (Jason Gong, Nicolás Fernández, and Oluwatimilehin Ademosu), covering key company policies and upcoming initiatives. The company follows a "freedom and responsibility" culture inspired by Netflix, including unlimited PTO, a $10k referral bonus for engineers, and a robust global hiring approach via Deal for 160+ countries. The team discussed gaps in current onboarding materials and committed to building role-specific checklists, Loom videos, and a Deal Engage platform to accelerate onboarding velocity within the next month.

## Relevance

- **To GrowthX Delivery:** The discussion revealed significant gaps in role-specific onboarding materials. Teams identified missing documentation on Client OS setup, AirTable workflows, and content theme configuration. Priority is to build repeatable onboarding artifacts (Loom videos, deal-engage modules) to accelerate productivity—goal is to get new hires productive within two weeks rather than weeks.

- **To GrowthX Business Development:** Company is now hiring across 160+ countries via Deal platform (vs. previous constraints with other EOR providers that only covered ~80 countries). This unlocks hiring in markets like Norway, Argentina, and Canada without workarounds—strategic for expanding the team's global footprint.

- **To GrowthX Operations:** Unlimited PTO policy with emphasis on calendar transparency and PST overlap is live. Company is developing formal expense policy (not yet final). Performance management system will be implemented via Deal Engage platform. Referral program is active ($10k for engineers, $5k+ for other roles) with significant bonuses for niche expertise (healthcare writers, vertical specialists).
