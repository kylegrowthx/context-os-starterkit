## Summary

GrowthX and Innerwell (a telehealth ketamine therapy platform) kicked off their engagement to build a programmatic SEO strategy focused on capturing high-intent local search traffic across state and city pages. Kyle Gaudreau outlined GrowthX's timeline: research and topical opportunities in weeks 1-2, initial calibration articles in weeks 2-3, weekly production scaling by week 4, and a target of 150-200 organic clicks per week by month 3. Anthony Mangia (Innerwell co-founder) shared their current SEO foundation of ~2,000 city/state pages with minimal traffic, minimal content differentiation from competitors, and emphasized the need for clinician-reviewed content where discussing novel therapeutic approaches. The team will meet weekly with Akina Tran as content review lead, while GrowthX will need access to Google Ads data and creative reporting to validate keyword opportunities.

---

## Context

Innerwell is a telehealth ketamine therapy platform offering in-clinic ketamine-assisted psychotherapy (KAP) with licensed therapists, psychiatry, EMDR, and talk therapy. Founded by Anthony Mangia (VP of Growth at Nannette before Innerwell), the company targets adults 18+ in specific states and differentiates on clinical rigor: licensed therapists paired with every treatment, versus competitors who use non-licensed guides or health coaches. The customer journey is direct-response focused (mostly paid ads) with 6-8% lead-to-patient conversion, and the company is preparing to launch a new flow allowing patients to schedule before payment.

This meeting represents GrowthX's first engagement post Series A close (announced Friday before the call). Innerwell has already invested effort in ~2,000 city/state pages but sees minimal organic traffic—competitors (Mindbloom, Better U, Wonder Med, Joyous, Journey Clinical) are also lightly invested in SEO, especially local SEO, creating significant opportunity for a disciplined programmatic approach. Akina Tran joined Innerwell's growth team two weeks prior to this call and will be the primary content review contact.

## Key Topics

### Innerwell's Business Model and Target Audience

  - Offers ketamine therapy, psychiatry, EMDR, and talk therapy
  - Target audience: 18+ in specific states, fairly even gender split
  - Key metric: Net patient starts (patients who complete first appointment)
  - Customer journey: Mostly direct response, with 6-8% of leads converting
  - New flow coming soon: Allowing scheduling before payment

### Current SEO Efforts

  - \~500 city-level pages with 4 services (2000 total pages)
  - State-level landing pages for ketamine therapy
  - Some condition-specific landing pages
  - Limited unique content, mostly dynamically generated
  - Traffic and conversions from these pages are currently minimal

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competitors: Mindbloom, Better U, Wonder Med, Joyous, Journey Clinical
  - Innerwell positioned as more affordable than Mindbloom, but with higher clinical rigor
  - Most competitors not heavily invested in SEO, especially local SEO

### GrowthX's Approach and Timeline

  - Week 1-2: Research and identify topical opportunities
  - Week 2-3: Initial calibration articles and feedback
  - Week 4: Aim to reach consistent weekly production
  - Month 3: Expect first significant traffic spike (150-200 clicks/week)
  - Month 5-6: More reliable conversion data for optimization
