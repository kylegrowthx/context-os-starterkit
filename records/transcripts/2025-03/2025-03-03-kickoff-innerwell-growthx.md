## Key Topics

### Innerwell's Business Model and Target Audience

  - Offers ketamine therapy, psychiatry, EMDR, and talk therapy
  - Target audience: 18+ in specific states, fairly even gender split
  - Key metric: Net patient starts (patients who complete first appointment)
  - Customer journey: Mostly direct response, with 6-8% of leads converting
  - New flow coming soon: Allowing scheduling before payment

### Current SEO Efforts

  - \~500 city-level pages with 4 services (2000 total pages)
  - State-level landing pages for ketamine therapy
  - Some condition-specific landing pages
  - Limited unique content, mostly dynamically generated
  - Traffic and conversions from these pages are currently minimal

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competitors: Mindbloom, Better U, Wonder Med, Joyous, Journey Clinical
  - Innerwell positioned as more affordable than Mindbloom, but with higher clinical rigor
  - Most competitors not heavily invested in SEO, especially local SEO

### GrowthX's Approach and Timeline

  - Week 1-2: Research and identify topical opportunities
  - Week 2-3: Initial calibration articles and feedback
  - Week 4: Aim to reach consistent weekly production
  - Month 3: Expect first significant traffic spike (150-200 clicks/week)
  - Month 5-6: More reliable conversion data for optimization
