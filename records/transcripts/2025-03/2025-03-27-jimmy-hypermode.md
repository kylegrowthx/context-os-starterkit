## Context

Hypermode (formerly D graph) is a graph database company targeting ML engineers and AI researchers. GrowthX has been working with them since they were GrowthX's first client two years ago. The relationship survived a product rebrand (D graph → Hypermode) and initially showed strong results — content written during the early engagement began ranking and driving significant traffic months after publication. However, a recent leadership change brought Jessica as CMO, and the account is now at churn risk due to repeated pivots in content strategy and unclear product positioning. Jimmy Pal leads the account at GrowthX and noted that this is the second time Hypermode has gone through a major identity crisis in the engagement. Mara Leighton recently joined GrowthX and is taking over account execution while Jimmy manages strategy and client relationship.
