## Summary

GrowthX pitched their AI-assisted content engine to Playbypoint, a court-scheduling software company with ~18k monthly organic traffic seeking to compete with Court Reserve (~42k traffic). Bruno Santilli and Jimmy Pal demonstrated GrowthX's 10-12 workflow system with 250 steps per article, 3 layers of human editing, and capacity for 25-500 articles/month, starting at $9,000/month for 50,000 words. Playbypoint expressed interest but indicated they're in foundation-building mode and would evaluate ROI over the next 6-12 months, with no immediate rush to launch given their $1,000/month paid search spend and part-time SEO focus.

## Context

Playbypoint is a court-scheduling software company competing in the legal/court management space. They're trying to establish organic SEO presence but currently rely heavily on referrals and word-of-mouth (strong product, referral network) plus limited paid search ($1,000/month spend). The call was introduced through Ben from Blueprint. Playbypoint has been working on SEO for about 2 years with basic technical work (redirects, keywords) handled by a part-time specialist, but they've never invested in a content strategy—their last blog post was 6 months ago. This is a classic SMB discovery call where the prospect is genuinely interested but needs to build their foundation and justify ROI before committing to a $9k-12k/month content program.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's AI-Assisted Content Creation Process

  - Uses AI workflows and engines feeding into each other with human oversight
  - 250 steps across 10-12 workflows for each content piece
  - Three layers of human involvement: writers, editors, and managing editors
  - Capable of producing 25-500 articles per month
  - Not purely AI-generated content, but AI-assisted with human expertise

### Playbypoint's Current SEO Status

  - \~18,000 monthly organic traffic (vs competitor Court Reserve at \~42,000)
  - 7,500 ranking keywords (vs Court Reserve's 12,000)
  - Part-time SEO specialist managing basics (redirects, keywords)
  - Limited current content production (last blog post \~6 months ago)
  - Spending \~$1,000/month on paid search

### GrowthX's Offering and Implementation

  - Start with 25-30 articles/month (one per day)
  - Week 1: Onboarding, competitor analysis, action plan
  - Week 2: Calibration articles, brand voice alignment
  - Ongoing: 50,000 words/month (25 articles)
  - Access needed: CMS, Google Search Console
  - Focus on month-over-month growth rather than specific traffic targets

### Pricing and Availability

  - $9,000/month for 50,000 words (25 articles)
  - $12,000/month for programmatic SEO (4x volume)
  - Currently at capacity; potential 1-month delay in project kickoff
