## Key Topics

### Programmatic SEO and Template Pages

  - Focus on creating template pages for various app ideas (e.g., CRM for dentists)
  - Utilize AI-generated prompts to kickstart projects in StackBlitz
  - Aim to build a directory of app ideas, similar to Dribble for designers
  - Gradually evolve from ideation pages to more complex starter kits

### Editorial Content Strategy

  - Initially less prioritized than programmatic SEO
  - Potential for future blog posts and listicles to capture different user intents
  - Aim to build topical authority across various app-building topics

### Technical Implementation

  - StackBlitz team to determine how to integrate new pages into their existing Remix app
  - Consideration for creating a subfolder to host the new content/directory
  - Potential to use StackBlitz itself to build and document the implementation process

### Resourcing and Timeline

  - StackBlitz team to discuss internal resourcing to support the initiative
  - GrowthX proposes a 12-month engagement but is open to discussion
  - Emphasis on the need for expert supervision and iterative improvement over time

### Quality and Expertise Concerns

  - Discussion on ensuring high-quality content that demonstrates product knowledge
  - GrowthX highlighted their experience with technical clients and AI-powered workflows
