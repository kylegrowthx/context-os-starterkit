## Summary

FOSSA and GrowthX discussed a content and SEO revitalization strategy for Package Observer, a new subdomain competing with Snyk Advisor that lost all indexed pages after launch. GrowthX proposed a multi-property approach: rebuilding Package Observer with AI-powered content creation while strengthening Stack Share and TLDR Legal through cross-linking to leverage FOSSA's 4.3M existing backlinks—a "rising tide" strategy that prioritizes building traffic and trust before optimizing for lead conversion.
