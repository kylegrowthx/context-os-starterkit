### GPT Integration for Content

  - Sean demonstrated ease of creating and integrating GPT-powered tools

  - Quick implementation (15-20 minutes) once requirements are defined

  - Need to develop a taxonomy for new articles to improve site structure and SEO

  - Integration with Exec's learning management system for seamless content display

### CMS Access and Publishing Process

  - GrowthX team now has CMS access
  - Articles should be left in draft for Sean's review
  - Focus on correct configuration for smooth publishing
  - Sean prefers to monitor and adjust post-publication rather than blocking the process

### Image Quality for Articles

  - Potential to tweak prompts for more color variability in the future
  - Higher-quality images may be considered for high-traffic or important articles
