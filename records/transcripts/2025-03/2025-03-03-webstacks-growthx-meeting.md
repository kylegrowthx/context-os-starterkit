## Key Topics

  - Add relevant screenshots, especially for comparison articles

### Content Refresh and Removal

  - 20 articles identified for complete rewrites due to significant ranking drops
  - Remove or consolidate low-value content (e.g., industry-specific on-page SEO articles)
  - Consider creating comprehensive guides for competitive topics (e.g., on-page SEO)
  - Shift focus to more niche, relevant topics (e.g., SEO for static site generators)

### Internal Linking and Conversion Optimization

  - Address orphan pages using Ahrefs report
  - Add internal links to relevant solutions or solution-focused conversion panels in top-performing blog posts
  - Incorporate natural mentions of Webstacks' products/solutions within content
  - Experiment with AI-generated recommendations for conversion panel placement and copy

### Performance Tracking and Lead Generation

  - Set up separate rank tracking for enriched content in Ahrefs
  - Use content inventory in Notion to track testing and data points
  - Focus on content driving quality leads as the primary metric
  - Analyze HubSpot reports for blog post download performance
  - Current blog conversion rate \~1-1.8%; aim to increase with new strategies
