## Overview

- GrowthX to incorporate AI-generated imagery for article headers, aiming for a style consistent with Superhuman's brand
- CTAs will be tailored based on content funnel stage, moving away from generic "Try Superhuman" for top-of-funnel content
- Superhuman is shifting messaging from "AI-powered" to "AI-native", requiring updates across all content
- Key Superhuman features to highlight: AI capabilities, split inbox, reminders, team collaboration, and keyboard shortcuts

## Key Topics

### Content Imagery Strategy

  - GrowthX can now generate unique images based on article content using AI tools like Recraft
  - Superhuman prefers bold, striking imagery similar to their "inbox zero" visuals
  - Action: Superhuman to send \~10 preferred images as inspiration for GrowthX to create samples

### Call-to-Action (CTA) Approach

  - Current CTAs ("Try Superhuman", "Drop us a line") may be too low-funnel for some content
  - Need for funnel-stage appropriate CTAs (e.g., product info pages for top-of-funnel content)
  - GrowthX to develop 2-3 CTA options with different destinations based on content type
  - CTAs will be added during CMS publishing, not built into the content itself

### Superhuman Messaging and Feature Highlights

  - Shifting from "AI-powered" to "AI-native" messaging across all content
  - Key features to emphasize:
    1.  Superhuman AI: Auto labels, auto drafts, Ask AI, Write with AI
    2.  Split inbox (including new AI-based splitting)
    3.  Reminders
    4.  Team comments and shared threads
    5.  Keyboard shortcuts
    6.  Integrations (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive for sales-focused content)
    7.  Smart Send (AI-powered send time optimization)

### Content Review Process

  - Superhuman's copywriter/managing editor to be looped in for additional style feedback
  - GrowthX to pay special attention to Superhuman-specific language (e.g., "conversations" instead of "email threads")
  - Feedback on product terminology and capitalization is crucial, but GrowthX can push back on non-essential edits
