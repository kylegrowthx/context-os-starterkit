## Context

Superhuman is an AI-native email client designed for power users, and GrowthX is producing a sustained content marketing program (targeting 25 articles per week) to drive awareness and consideration. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's delivery process to ensure content accuracy, brand consistency, and strategic alignment. Superhuman is in a messaging transition period, moving from emphasizing "AI-powered" features to positioning itself as fundamentally "AI-native" — a distinction that requires careful language updates across all content. Alex Rodrigues leads content strategy at Superhuman with support from design/brand leadership (Harmony Anderson) and product input (Morgan Maurer), while Elvis Goren owns GrowthX's content delivery and is working to scale the program to 25 pieces per week with minimal rework cycles.
