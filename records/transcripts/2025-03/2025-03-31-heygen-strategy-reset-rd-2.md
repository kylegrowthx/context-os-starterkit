## Context

HeyGen is a significant GrowthX services client working on AI video generation. The relationship appears to be ongoing engagement where GrowthX is responsible for organic growth strategy and content execution. HeyGen has set an aggressive 10x revenue growth target driven by investor expectations, creating pressure on the team to deliver both quick wins and sustainable long-term growth. The client has already demonstrated solid progress (domain authority +7 over the past year, ranking for significant keywords), but the 10x target requires a step-change in execution strategy. ## Key Topics

### Current Performance and Client Expectations

  - HeyGen has set an ambitious 10x growth target, likely driven by investor/market pressures
  - Recent domain authority growth (+7 in past year) is significant, but 10x still challenging
  - Client mentioned desire for link building, though team sees limited potential impact

### Content Strategy Ideas

  - Update publish dates across older content to boost performance
  - Focus on successful content clusters and scale those topics
  - Leverage HeyGen's product features (e.g. video translation) in content creation
  - Explore editorial opportunities around roadmap-related keywords (\~6,000 monthly searches)
  - Conduct competitor gap analysis to identify high-potential topic areas

### Translation and International Expansion

  - Significant opportunity, but complex to implement (took ClickUp \~1 year with dedicated engineering)
  - Could be a major differentiator and growth driver if executed well
  - Need to address potential client concerns about translation quality

### Programmatic Content Opportunities

  - Identify 2,500-3,000 programmatic content opportunities from competitor analysis
  - Create forward-facing content calendar to visualize growth trajectory
  - Balance volume with quality to avoid potential penalties

### Presentation Strategy

  - Frame ideas into "big bets" with timelines and impact/effort assessments
  - Create visualization of 6-12 month engagement with expected traffic growth curve
  - Prepare two scenarios: achievable growth with current SOW vs. resources needed for 10x
  - Address unrealistic expectations while demonstrating path to significant growth

### Client Management

  - Reschedule presentation from Wednesday to Friday to ensure high-quality delivery
  - Consider upselling to higher engagement level to support ambitious growth targets
  - Be prepared to challenge unrealistic expectations while showing commitment to goals
