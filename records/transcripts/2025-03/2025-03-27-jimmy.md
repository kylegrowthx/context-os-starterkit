- **To GrowthX Delivery:** Jimmy highlighted critical operational risk: growth hires (Kyle, and now Jason) repeatedly get pulled into client services despite their growth mandate. This suggests a need for dedicated account management roles or "pod leads" to shield growth teams from reactive client work. The team is currently handling 12-15+ concurrent clients without sufficient management layer.

- **To CheckThat:** Both Jason and Jimmy are deeply invested in AI-driven content strategy as a replacement for traditional human writing. This validates CheckThat's positioning around AI visibility—they see AI replacing upwork writers at $10/hour, and Google Core Updates in 2024 proved that quality AI content can outperform expensive human content ($20k spent on 200 articles at a previous role, then pivoted to AI).

- **To GrowthX Business Development:** Marcel is currently the primary lead-generation engine (LinkedIn posts fill his calendar), but there's an acknowledged need to build top-of-funnel motion independently. Jimmy is diversifying revenue (backlink acquisition, programmatic advertising, exploring outbound) to reduce single-point-of-failure risk from Google updates. Business exploration includes potential expansion into programmatic and outbound channels, though there's hesitation about outbound due to brand alignment.
