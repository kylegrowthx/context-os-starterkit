## Summary

GrowthX and StackBlitz aligned on a growth partnership focused on dramatically expanding StackBlitz's indexable pages from ~60 to thousands through programmatic template generation. Marcel pitched a two-pronged approach: lightweight template pages for long-tail keyword coverage plus fully-functional starter kits seeded from StackBlitz's user prompt data. StackBlitz committed to sharing ~10,000 initial prompts for analysis and providing API access, with both teams aiming to have initial pages live by end of March for a board presentation. The opportunity is significant — StackBlitz wants to outpace Replit on traffic metrics, and using their proprietary prompt data to create network effects could be a decisive competitive advantage.
