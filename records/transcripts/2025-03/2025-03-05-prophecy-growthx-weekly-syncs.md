Kyle highlighted that publishing velocity is a key success factor and a competitive advantage. Prophecy started slower than the average GrowthX account, and Kyle emphasized the importance of reducing friction in the publishing workflow to accelerate output. The team is focused on learning what works early (certain topics, formats, keyword clusters) so they can double down on winning approaches rather than being limited by a small batch of pieces.

The team is tracking this LLM referral traffic alongside traditional sources as part of the broader visibility picture.

### Content Strategy

The team is focusing on high-level, conceptual content rather than product-specific (JTVD) content. Upcoming topics include governance and self-service data pipeline themes. The team is still refining the balance between combined listicles (covering multiple related topics) and standalone articles, using search volume and content opportunity as the decision criteria.
