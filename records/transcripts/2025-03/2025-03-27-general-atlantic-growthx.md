## Summary

General Atlantic's internet and technology team (Jack Dove, Eldar Safarov, Fred Robson) met with Marcel Santilli and Daniel (co-founders of GrowthX) to explore a potential Series B partnership. GrowthX has built an AI workflow coding engine for knowledge work, starting with content creation, and has achieved 48% win rates, <30-day sales cycles, and 76-79% gross margins post-Series A. The company targets $4B in potential revenue from 25,000 enterprise customers at $160k ACV and plans to expand into recruiting and sales post-Series B through M&A of specialized agencies. GA expressed interest in staying engaged as GrowthX targets $20M ARR by Q3/Q4 2025 and approaches future fundraising with an "open term sheet" approach.

---

## Context

General Atlantic is a $100B growth equity fund with a wide investment mandate ranging from Series C through multi-billion-dollar positions. Jack Dove leads their internet and technology sector team, which has invested in companies like AppsFlyer and Insider. The team became interested in GrowthX through Pix's AI (a portfolio company and GrowthX customer), whose CMO Jason Setchkin connected them. This was an introductory call to understand GrowthX's business model, market opportunity, and team composition ahead of potential future investment consideration. The dynamic reflected GA's relationship-first approach — Jack noted they've held positions with companies for 4-6 years before investing, emphasizing that relationship building matters as much as deal flow.
