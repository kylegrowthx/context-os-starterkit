## Context

GrowthX provides content marketing services to Strapi, an open-source headless CMS platform. The partnership has been active since at least September 2024, focusing on creating integration pages and comparison content to drive organic traffic and visibility. Strapi's content strategy shifted recently to emphasize depth over breadth, moving from broad top-of-funnel content into deeper, vertical-specific pages. This meeting served both as a regular weekly sync and as a transition meeting to introduce Sydney Go, GrowthX's new account manager, to the Strapi team as Andrej Vidovic steps down to handle other priorities.
