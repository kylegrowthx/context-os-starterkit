## Key Topics

### Goals and Objectives

  - Improve SEO visibility and lead generation for targeted industries
  - Establish Rapyd as an industry leader in leveraging AI for marketing
  - Increase site traffic and conversions from organic search
  - Enhance overall website quality score and strengthen lead/sales generation

### Target Audiences

  - High-opportunity industries: iGaming, online gaming, up-and-coming trading platforms, crypto exchanges
  - Partner channels: Referral partners, ISOs (Independent Sales Organizations), payfacs (Payment Facilitators)
  - Focus on attracting partners as gateways to high-value clients in niche industries

### Content Strategy

  - Blend AI-generated content for long-tail topics with human-led content for deeper analysis
  - Implement pillar-cluster approach for content organization
  - Aim for 50,000 words of aligned, high-quality content monthly
  - Balance content to appeal to both direct clients and potential partners (80-20 or 85-15 split)

### Technical Considerations

  - CMS access requires special setup due to high security measures
  - Interim solution: GrowthX to provide HTML markup for Rapyd team to publish
  - Explore potential API integration for streamlined content uploading

### Metrics and Reporting

  - Current attribution model focuses on first-touch
  - Need to establish more specific, measurable goals for the engagement
  - GrowthX to access Google Analytics and Search Console for performance tracking
