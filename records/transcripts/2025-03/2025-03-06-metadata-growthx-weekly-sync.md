## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Performance

  - Shifted focus from content refreshes to net-new content (8-10 pieces/week)
  - Google Search Console data shows growing impressions but low clicks
  - Impressions are a leading indicator of ranking, with room for improvement on click-through rates
  - Too early to assess pipeline impact at 4 months into the engagement
  - Comparison made to other clients (e.g., Galileo AI) showing typical growth patterns over time

### Creative Bottleneck and Solutions

  - Recent bottleneck in image creation due to designer's focus on AI agents project
  - GrowthX offered to take over image creation using:
    1.  Stock image selection
    2.  AI-generated images
    3.  Illustrative AI-generated images
  - Proposal to create a batch of images for editors to select from, rather than per-article creation

### SEO Strategy and Long-term Benefits

  - Discussion on the long-term benefits of SEO vs. paid advertising
  - Example of DeepGram's success: 24x traffic increase after 6 months, leading to significant growth
  - G2.com mentioned as another example of successful long-term content strategy

### Potential New Project: Agency Directory

  - Gil proposed creating an agency/fractional expert directory
  - Suggestion to include B2B performance marketing agencies, content agencies, and fractional demand experts
  - Decision made to postpone this project and reassess in early April
