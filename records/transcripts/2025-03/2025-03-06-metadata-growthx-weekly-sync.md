## Summary

GrowthX and Metadata pivoted from content refreshes to publishing 8-10 net-new articles per week, driving growing impressions in Google Search Console though clicks remain low. The team identified image creation as a bottleneck and agreed that Marcel's team will take over image generation using stock, AI-generated, and illustrative approaches to accelerate publishing velocity. At four months into the engagement (moving toward month five), the team agreed to regroup in early April to assess pipeline impact and decide whether to launch a parallel programmatic SEO effort targeting an agency directory.

---

## Context

Metadata is a B2B SaaS company running with paid advertising as their primary customer acquisition channel; they approached GrowthX in late 2024 to build organic traffic through SEO content strategy. Aida Knežević joined GrowthX in early December and took over the Metadata account in late February, refocusing the content strategy toward customer pain points and net-new pieces where they can win in search, moving away from the initial emphasis on refreshing existing content. The engagement is now four months in, with a critical April checkpoint coming up to assess whether the strategy is generating pipeline impact and to decide on next-phase initiatives like building a programmatic SEO asset.
