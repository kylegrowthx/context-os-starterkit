### GrowthX Service Model

**Technical Architecture**
- AI workflows represented as executable code (not low-code tools like Zapier), allowing parallel execution at scale
- Thousands of processes run continuously, learning and improving from every single run
- Extensive multi-step research process: research hundreds of pages/topics via multiple APIs, outline, plan, title, draft, style adaptation, plus 20+ editor review checkpoints
- Compute investment: ~$10 per article (willing to spend high because margins support it, not trying to squeeze profit)
- Heavy final editing pass from qualified editor before publication

**Client Engagement**
- Focus on 2-week "calibration" or "bullseye" process at project start to align on quality, style, and tone
- Once bullseye is established, clients can request scaling with confidence
- Success metric: Can deliver 100 refreshed pages in one week once calibrated
- Most successful with clients who've experienced pain of slow internal teams or previous agency failures (they value the velocity and quality)
