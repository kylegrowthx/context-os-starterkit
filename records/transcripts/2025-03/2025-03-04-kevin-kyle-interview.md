## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau interviewed Kevin Mo for a client operations role at GrowthX as the company scales from 40 clients and $6M ARR. Kevin presented a structured 90-day onboarding plan focused on building scalable systems for health management, SOPs, NPS, and training—aligning with Kyle's priorities around retention, churn reduction, and operational infrastructure. ## Context

Kevin Mo is an operations and systems builder with deep experience scaling early-stage startups from zero to $10M+ ARR. He worked at an early-stage media startup (zero to $1M in year one, $10M in year two), then moved into consulting at IBM as a senior consultant on generative AI and large-scale implementations. Most recently, he led IBM's generative AI community from 20 to 50+ people and oversaw multiple large SAS implementation projects with 50,000+ users. He's now pivoting back to early-stage startup environments where he can directly influence operations and product strategy.

GrowthX hired Kyle Gaudreau in October 2024 as VP of Client Services. The company has grown explosively—from ~$1M ARR and 20 clients to $6M ARR and 40 clients in roughly five months. They've implemented a pod model with managing editors, account managers, and writers, and are now bringing in managing directors. The explosive growth has exposed operational gaps: onboarding and training processes are ad hoc, client health management is unrefined, SOPs are incomplete or outdated, and the team lacks visibility into churn and Net Revenue Retention metrics. Kyle saw Kevin as a potential hire to build the operational systems the company needs to scale sustainably.
