## Key Topics

### Current Content Strategy and Challenges

  - Publishing \~4 blog posts/week, 1 whitepaper/month, 2 webinars/month
  - Content ideas sourced from various channels (SEO agency, executives, competitor analysis)
  - Two internal content marketers handle 90% of writing
  - Bottlenecks: Jade's review process for major content, slow design turnaround (2-3 weeks)
  - Limited capacity for systematic content refreshes/updates

### Target Audience and Personas

  - Primary: CSOs, SOC analysts, IT security managers in medium to large enterprises
  - Secondary: Business professionals (CEOs, board members) interested in risk mitigation
  - Influencers: Industry analysts, journalists, reporters

### Product Positioning and Value Props

  - Three key elements: Risk reduction, cost savings (vs. competitors), productivity increase
  - Unique selling points: 3-minute deployment, automated triage, significant SOC time savings (95%)
  - Challenge: Some potential users fear job loss due to automation

### Content Optimization Opportunities

  - Enhance SEO performance of existing content (700+ pages)
  - Implement systematic content refresh process
  - Improve fact-checking and competitor citation practices
  - Streamline design processes for faster turnaround

### Collaboration Model

  - GrowthX to supplement and strengthen existing content efforts
  - Focus on optimizing current content and improving organic traffic
  - Potential for cluster content development around pillar pages
