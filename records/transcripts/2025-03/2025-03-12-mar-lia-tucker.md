## Key Topics

### Current Hiring Process Overview

  - Uses Workable ATS integrated with custom AI tools
  - Job postings generate expectations documents and interview questions
  - AI screens resumes and application answers, ranking candidates
  - Video interviews conducted through Hirevue for transcript analysis

### AI-Assisted Screening System

  - Generates job expectations from descriptions (e.g., qualifications, skills)
  - Creates evaluation rubrics for resume analysis and interview questions
  - Ranks candidates automatically in Workable (stars, thumbs up)
  - Provides AI assessment explanations for each candidate
  - Posts top candidates to dedicated Slack channels
