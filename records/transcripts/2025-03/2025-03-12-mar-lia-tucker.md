## Key Topics

### Current Hiring Process Overview

  - Uses Workable ATS integrated with custom AI tools
  - Job postings generate expectations documents and interview questions
  - AI screens resumes and application answers, ranking candidates
  - Video interviews conducted through Hirevue for transcript analysis
  - High application volume (e.g., 700+ for content writer role) necessitates AI assistance

### AI-Assisted Screening System

  - Generates job expectations documents from job descriptions (broad qualifiers, detailed expectations)
  - Creates evaluation rubrics and interview question banks by role (e.g., design tool proficiency, accessibility expertise)
  - Automatically ranks candidates in Workable after analyzing resume PDFs and application answers against the rubric
  - Provides AI assessment explanations for each candidate (key strengths, constraints, potential seniority assessment, recommendation)
  - Posts ranked candidates to dedicated Slack channels (#services-recruiting, #engineering-recruiting) with direct Workable links
  - Can automatically disqualify low-scoring candidates ("no" or "maybe" ratings from AI) to reduce manual review burden

### New Team Member Onboarding

  - Marília: Day 3 of full-time employment, getting hands-on experience with Workable and the hiring workflow; will gradually customize prompts and evaluation criteria as she ramps up
  - Tucker: Part-time contributor (2-3 hours/day, sporadic) until April 7th official start date; currently at previous employer; wants to deeply understand GrowthX's business before jumping into recruiting
  - Both will have full access to AirOps workflows and Notion documents to adjust hiring criteria as they see fit
  - Daniel committed to a written tutorial on the entire hiring system so new team members can reference and build on the process

### Potential Process Improvements

  - **ATS Migration:** Tucker recommended Greenhouse as superior for scaling; Daniel to research Greenhouse pricing/features. Current system built for Workable but portable (can migrate in ~half a day)
  - **Candidate Sourcing:** Tucker to connect with Quantum Talent to streamline candidate sharing pipeline; exploring automated sourcing integrations (could be built in 3 days by a developer)
  - **Video Interview Step:** Using HireVue only for API/transcript capability; team open to removing video screening entirely if deemed unnecessary
  - **AI Prompt Calibration:** Marília and Tucker can customize evaluation rubrics, resume criteria, and interview questions as they identify weaknesses; prompts moved from codebase to Notion for easier editing
  - **Volume Management:** Currently manually moving candidates from "Applied" to "Video Interview" stage (no auto-progression); Daniel open to adding automation if it improves workflow

### Office Space Update

  - Team looking at new office space for events and local team collaboration
  - First floor for events/meetups, second floor for 10-15 desks
  - Current office becoming too expensive ($25k for small space)
