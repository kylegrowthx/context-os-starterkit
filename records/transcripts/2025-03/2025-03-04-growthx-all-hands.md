### Series A Funding Announcement

  - $12.5M raised from Madrona Venture Group
  - Chose Madrona for their trust, reputation, and early Amazon investment
  - Funding will enable aggressive investment (\~$12-14M) in 2023, primarily in hiring
  - Decision to raise driven by strong product-market fit and opportunity to seize market
  - Formal Series A process provides validation and de-risks company for employees/customers

### Market Opportunity & Positioning

  - Addressing pain points of expensive, underperforming agencies and tool fatigue
  - Massive TAM: content marketing, content-related services, marketing/sales salaries
  - Unique value prop: tech-enabled, tier-one talent, outcome-focused vs. tool-focused
  - Strong customer validation: closing 5-6 figure deals in 1-2 short meetings

### Product Vision: "Content OS"

  - Starting with content outcomes, expanding to broader knowledge work productivity
  - Building AI-powered workflow engine to manage complex, scalable processes
  - Collecting valuable "frontier data" from expert human-in-the-loop interactions
  - Long-term vision: AI agents for workflow building, continuous learning/improvement

### 2023 Goals & Growth Trajectory

  - Currently at $6.2M ARR, 39 clients (ahead of projections)
  - Year-end targets: $22M+ ARR, 100+ clients
  - Scaling from 2 to 3-4 new client kick-offs per week
  - Heavy investment ($12-16M) in people and resources to fuel growth

### Cultural Priorities

  - Maintaining trust and reputation through high-quality client interactions
  - Encouraging "builder mentality" - proactively improving processes, documentation
  - Open feedback culture, especially regarding AI tool performance and workflow issues
