## Summary

GrowthX leadership discussed restructuring accounts under new Managing Director roles, with Dave Capone and Jacob serving as initial MDs to oversee account pods and improve oversight. Several key accounts require immediate attention: Metadata faces slow profit growth, Braida may have been saved through a pivot to programmatic, and Tribe is surfacing issues with its new head of marketing. The team identified operational gaps including unsigned contracts (particularly Abnormal), inconsistent freelancer role clarity, and opportunities to introduce a lower-cost "SEO light" package ($6k/month) for price-sensitive prospects while maintaining margin discipline and brand standards.

---

## Context

This was an internal GrowthX leadership review during a period of organizational change. Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX Vice President) facilitated a discussion with Dave Capone (new Managing Director joining from TripAdvisor), Jimmy Pal (GrowthX operations), and Bridget McGillivray (GrowthX RevOps). The call served two purposes: onboarding Dave Capone into his new MD role overseeing account pods, and conducting a quarterly account health review. Several of the scheduled participants (Marcel Santilli, Bruno Santilli, and Jakub Rudnik) were unable to attend, so the team proceeded with a lighter session than planned, though they flagged the need to include Bruno on future reviews given his oversight of churn and pipeline metrics. The meeting represented an inflection point for GrowthX's internal operations — moving from a more ad-hoc review process to a more structured, scalable one as the company brings on managing directors to professionalize account oversight.

- New MD structure and account pod assignments require clear documentation and knowledge transfer (Kyle transitioning Andre's accounts, ramping Dave and Jacob on new ones).

## Key Topics

### Team Restructuring

  - Introducing Managing Director (MD) roles to oversee account pods
  - Dave and Jacob identified as initial MDs, with more to be added (potentially Branca)
  - MDs will manage account managers and editors within their pods
  - Goal: Improve account oversight and resource allocation

### Account Status Review

  - Metadata: Slow profit growth, CEO involvement, shifting focus to new content creation
  - Braida: Likely saved, pivoting towards more programmatic approach
  - Tribe: Potential issues surfacing, need to connect with new head of marketing

### Operational Challenges

  - Contract management: Issues with unsigned contracts and changing terms (e.g., Abnormal)
  - Freelancer management: Clarification needed on roles, responsibilities, and oversight
  - Onboarding process for new marketing contacts at client companies needs improvement

### Pipeline and Pricing Strategy

  - Considering offering lower-cost "SEO light" package ($6k/month) to price-sensitive prospects
  - Need to balance brand reputation, pipeline, and ideal customer profile (ICP) when considering new deals

### Learnings from Challenging Accounts

  - Misalignment on quality expectations and content types can lead to client dissatisfaction
  - Importance of identifying ideal customer profile (ICP) and establishing non-negotiables
  - Caution needed with highly technical content until processes are refined
