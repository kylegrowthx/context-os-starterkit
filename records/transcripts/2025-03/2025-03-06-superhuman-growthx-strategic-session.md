## Context

Superhuman is a high-velocity email client positioning itself as the AI-first email solution for professionals and teams. GrowthX is engaged in a content marketing partnership to build Superhuman's organic visibility. This was a strategic alignment meeting (week 3 of engagement) to present content opportunities based on competitive research and ensure the topic strategy matches Superhuman's brand direction. The meeting included product, marketing, and strategy leads from Superhuman alongside the GrowthX content strategists. Superhuman currently benefits from 77% branded search traffic due to strong domain authority, but organic blog traffic is only 15% of total—a major growth opportunity where content strategy can make an immediate impact.

### Content Strategy Priorities

  - AI and automation to be the main focus (70% of content)
  - Team collaboration as a secondary theme
  - Productivity tools and email management as foundational topics
  - Experiment with various topics to gauge traction and refine strategy

### Keyword Prioritization Approach

  - Consider three factors: keyword difficulty, search volume, and relevance to Superhuman
  - Balance high-volume, competitive keywords with niche, future-focused topics
  - Manually review and rate topic relevance to ensure alignment with brand direction

### AI-Focused Content Opportunities

  - Expand beyond the current 50 AI-related topics identified
  - Explore AI use cases for email-related challenges
  - Position Superhuman as a leader in AI email solutions
  - Act quickly on AI content (next 18 months) before it becomes mainstream

### Feature-Specific Content

  - Highlight unique Superhuman features like snippets and templates
  - Create content around integrations (e.g., Grammarly, HubSpot, Salesforce)
  - Focus on use cases enabled by integrations rather than broad integration topics

### Competitor Analysis

  - Address comparisons with key competitors: Notion Mail, Spark, Shortwave
  - Develop competitor landing pages for Google, Outlook, Spark, Shortwave, and Notion
  - Utilize the Notion Mail comparison document for content creation

### Content Review Process

  - Team to review and approve topics in Airtable (mark as "Ready to start")
