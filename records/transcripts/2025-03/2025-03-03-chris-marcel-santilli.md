## Summary

Chris Rack, founder of two startups (DemandView/SocialView.ai and Mockingbird.ai), met with Bruno Santilli and Jakub Rudnik from fwd.digital to explore programmatic SEO services for accelerating traffic to his directory platform. Chris positioned SocialView.ai as a sentiment-driven alternative to G2 and TrustRadius, with a monetization model based on intent signals rather than direct leads. fwd.digital expressed strong interest, with Bruno positioning programmatic SEO as 2-10x faster than traditional agencies, and both parties agreed to a follow-up demo to explore fit and implementation.

---

## Context

Chris Rack is launching two companies after a long career in demand generation. DemandView is his demand gen services firm, with SocialView.ai as a key differentiator—a WordPress-based directory site that scrapes sentiment analysis from public sources like Reddit to provide unbiased company profiles. Unlike G2 and TrustRadius (which he views as corrupted by paid reviews), SocialView.ai aims to capture intent signals and monetize them through lead generation ABM lists. He has $350k in seed funding and is looking to accelerate traffic growth in the next 6-12 months to prove the MVP concept. Mockingbird.ai, his second venture (rebranded from VoiceReach), is a voice AI tool for lead qualification—which he positioned as a reverse pitch to fwd.digital, noting their high inbound lead volume (20-30 leads/week) and manual qualification process. Jakub Rudnik, who just joined fwd.digital that week, brings valuable context as a former G2 content SEO lead, adding credibility to the collaboration.
