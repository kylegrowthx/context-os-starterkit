## Context

GrowthX is a B2B services company led by Marcel Santilli that recently closed its Series A with strong terms and valuation. The company is currently experiencing explosive demand for its services and is hiring top talent (ex-Airbnb, ex-Stripe, ex-TripAdvisor executives). Marcel is building a proprietary AI agent system that enables workflows as code, which represents a major shift from traditional low-code/no-code tools toward structured, learnable automation infrastructure.

Apex Fractional is a Polish-founded consulting practice started by best friends Marcin Pogroszewski and Tomek Nowinski, both with deep exit experience (they sold an 80-person performance marketing agency to Dentsu/iProspect in 2014). Both founders are Stanford GSB alumni who moved to the U.S. (Marcin in 2016, Tomek in 2018) and have been operating as fractional consultants to Series A-C startups, primarily implementing AI-powered automation. They're at an inflection point—the business is profitable and flexible, but they're exploring whether to scale it, hire a team in Poland, or pursue something else entirely.
