## Summary

Bridget McGillivray, GrowthX's new Operations lead, onboarded five new hires on their first week and walked through critical company policies, tools, and upcoming systems. Key topics included unlimited PTO (targeting ~4 weeks annually), the requirement for a 4-hour overlap with PST for collaboration and company meetings, and upcoming initiatives including Loom for async communication, Deal Engage for structured onboarding, a "freedom and responsibility" expense policy, and performance management tools rolling out within Deal. ## Context

This was GrowthX's first company-wide onboarding session, held during the first week for a cohort of five new hires across different functions (operations, account management, and engineering). Bridget McGillivray, who joined GrowthX in early February as an operations/HR lead, designed and facilitated the session to introduce company structure, operations workflows, and key policies. The meeting was intentionally informal—Bridget noted she "put this together very quickly"—and served as both a practical tool introduction and a chance for leadership to meet new team members. The company is fully distributed across multiple time zones (including Manila, Canada, and the US), which informed discussions about collaboration practices.

## Key Topics

### Tool Access and Onboarding

  - Notion houses the onboarding checklist, which will be refined for team-specific needs
  - Key tools: 1Password, Notion, Slack, Adios CRM (Zapier-based)
  - LastPass contains team vault access; personal vaults available
  - Action item: New hires to review tool list and notify of missing access

### Unlimited PTO Policy

  - Aim for \~4 weeks off annually, no exact amount specified
  - Give 2-week notice for vacations, especially for customer-facing roles
  - Use Google Calendar invites to notify colleagues of PTO
  - Flexible work hours, focus on quality and task completion
  - International employees encouraged to take their national holidays

### Time Zone Collaboration

  - 4-hour overlap with PST required for efficient communication
  - Company and team meetings typically scheduled in PST mornings
  - Flexibility allowed, but consistent overlap is important

### Upcoming Initiatives

  - Loom (or similar tool) for asynchronous communication and training
  - Deal Engage platform for structured onboarding courses and modules
  - Expense policy update based on "freedom and responsibility" principle
  - Annual company offsite and potential team gatherings
  - Performance management system within Deal (30-day, 90-day, ongoing reviews)

### Feedback and Communication

  - Slack preferred for submitting feedback and ideas
  - Plans to create a suggestion box in Notion for centralized feedback collection
  - Encouragement to flag outdated or irrelevant information in Notion
