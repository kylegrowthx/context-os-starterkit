## Summary

GrowthX presented RunPod with a comprehensive programmatic SEO strategy designed to capitalize on RunPod's strong domain authority while addressing declining blog traffic. Sydney Go walked the team through performance analysis showing the domain is growing (traffic and impressions up over six months) but the blog.runpod.io remains flat, alongside a content strategy featuring six main clusters (cloud comparisons, AI deployment, model deployment guides, cost and pricing, framework-specific guides, and GPU pages). The team aligned on launching the new content at runpod.io/articles (separate from blog.runpod.io, managed by Alyssa) and prioritized immediate calibration content on instant clusters and bare metal—topics tied to RunPod's marketing campaign launching Monday—with production ramping to 5-10 articles by the week after calibration feedback.
