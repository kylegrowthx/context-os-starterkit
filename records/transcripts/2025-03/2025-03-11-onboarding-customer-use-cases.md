## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau led onboarding for five new team members, introducing GrowthX's current service offerings: editorial content (50-100k words/month), programmatic SEO, link building and content collaboration, and outbound campaigns. The team discussed how AI-powered workflows enable high-volume content production with human quality control, with editorial and programmatic as the core bread-and-butter services and near-term opportunities in content repurposing, interview-based case studies, and expanded services for existing clients like Galileo.

## Context

Kyle Gaudreau, who leads client services, presented GrowthX's service model and use cases to help the new team understand the company's offerings, revenue drivers, and strategic direction. ## Relevance

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- Editorial content baseline: 50-100k words/month (7-15 pieces/week at 1500-2000 words each) requires human oversight through managing editors for strategy/quality and account managers for workflow iteration
- Programmatic SEO needs process refinement — currently tracking pages/year but lacking repeatable playbooks similar to editorial workflows
- Auto-publishing via APIs (publishing without manual CMS staging) is a key unlock for scale but currently not built into client platforms
- Content refresh workflows need refinement: balance between rewriting for SEO optimization vs. minimal edits for expansion

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- Link building represents the highest profit margin service; started Jan 2025 with 30-35 clients using backlink exchange orchestration then paid links from month 3 onwards
- Outbound campaigns are only 8-10% of business due to scalability challenges (email restrictions, domain warmup) — lower margin relative to link building and editorial
- Galileo identified as expansion opportunity: CMO Jason recently onboarded, requesting GrowthX lead most growth initiatives — testing ground for new use cases and revenue paths
- Content repurposing and interview-based case studies are potential future offerings; Galileo podcast-to-blog conversion is proof of concept

**To GrowthX Product/Operations:**
- AI workflows are key differentiator but require human-in-the-loop: quality degrades without feedback loops, prompt refinement, and understanding of client tone/voice/personas
- Ericksson Abad (Finance/Operations) flagged need for better financial tracking and accounting to support rapid scaling
