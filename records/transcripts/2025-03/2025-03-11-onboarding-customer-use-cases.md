### Current Service Offerings

  - Editorial Content (Base offering):
      - 50-100k words/month
      - 7-15 pieces/week (1500-2000 words each)
      - Managing editors own strategy & quality
      - Account managers refine AI workflows
      - Freelance writers for editing & expansion
  - Programmatic SEO:
      - Creates repeatable templates for scaled content
      - Based on pages/year, needs refinement in process
      - Potential for auto-publishing via APIs (future development)
  - Link Building & Content Collaboration:
      - Started in January
      - Orchestrates backlink exchanges among clients
      - Transitions to paid backlinks after 3 months
  - Outbound Campaigns:
      - Smaller portion of business (8-10%)
      - Challenges with scalability and platform restrictions

### Future Opportunities

  - Content repurposing (e.g., webinars to blogs)
  - Long-form interview-based content (case studies)
  - Expanding services for existing clients (e.g., Galileo)
  - Exploring new use cases and growth paths

### Process & Workflow

  - AI-powered workflows enable high-volume content production
  - Human loop for quality control and refinement
  - Iterative approach to improve AI outputs based on feedback
  - Balance between automation and human touch for optimal quality
