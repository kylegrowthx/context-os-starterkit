## Context

Netwrix is a PE-owned identity and data security company with 18 products, nearly 1/3 of Fortune 500 customers, and a mix of high-volume mid-market and enterprise segments. The company has grown rapidly through acquisitions (8 companies in 3 years), resulting in a complex multi-site portfolio with 14,000 pages across multiple domains and heritage websites. They employ teams primarily in Eastern Europe and are profitable despite the integration challenges. The company is undergoing a major initiative to consolidate brand identity and migrate to a headless CMS, which creates both a content optimization opportunity and technical complexity.

GrowthX Labs pitched their AI-powered content creation platform as a strategic partner for this transition. Marcel Santilli positioned GrowthX as a combination of AI automation and human editorial expertise (managing directors, editors with journalism backgrounds, SEO specialists), contrasting with pure software tools that lack strategy and hands-on guidance. The pitch focused on outcomes (revenue growth, domain authority, organic traffic) rather than just delivering content as a service, with a 90-day pilot model that allows Netwrix to evaluate the partnership with low risk while receiving a comprehensive content strategy upfront.
