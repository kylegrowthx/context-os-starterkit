## Key Topics

### Sales Leadership Gap

Tyler is a machine — closing nearly $2M/month solo, 54 deals in active pipeline. But this creates concentration risk and limits what GrowthX can scale. The sales leader needs to: coach and manage Tyler and future AEs, build sales processes (stages, forecasting, pipeline hygiene), handle renewals and procurement complexity, and sell actively (player-coach, not manager-only). Target: head of sales title, West Coast or NYC, experience selling to marketers and service-heavy buyers. Team should reach 5-6 AEs by year-end.

### Marketing Leadership

Current marketing is run by a senior IC focused on growth and product launches. The role adds structure and management of existing programs: events, content/social, video, external agency. Not a CMO or VP building a department from scratch — a hands-on director-level growth marketer. Must be able to run ABM, nurture campaigns, and explore new market segments. Harmony is Plan A — known to both Marcel and Anna, strong background in demand gen and sales partnership. Uncertainty remains around her availability and expectations.
