## Overview

- Traffic to Arena's site from GrowthX content has been increasing, but conversions remain flat
- There's misalignment on success metrics and expectations for the SEO program
- Strategic discussions and alignment on goals/metrics are needed, rather than just focusing on content production

---

## Key Topics

### SEO Program Performance Review

  - GrowthX reports traffic to Arena site from their content has been increasing (250+ weekly visitors)
  - Arena sees flat overall website traffic (\~2.5-3.2k visitors) and no increase in conversions
  - Discrepancy in traffic data between Google Analytics and Search Console noted
  - Debate over quality of traffic and how to measure true business impact

### Expectations and Success Metrics

  - Original goals included increasing traffic and conversions/signups
  - Misalignment on what constitutes success - traffic increase vs. conversions
  - Need to revisit and clarify success metrics and expectations

### Strategic Approach

  - Weekly calls focused on content production, lacking strategic discussions
  - GrowthX team suggests need to understand Arena's "aha moment" and how to translate that to website
  - Potential to apply learnings from Arena's successful outbound efforts to digital/website strategy

### Program Structure and Communication

  - Arena felt lack of strategic guidance and involvement from Marcel (GrowthX founder)
  - GrowthX explained their team structure and Marcel's indirect involvement
  - Acknowledged need for better alignment and communication
