## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Overview

  - Align with travel industry trends: focus on higher-end products, remote work, and blended business/leisure travel
  - Goal: Increase revenue by 20% by 2025 vs. 2024
  - Prioritize high-intent moments and connect topics to specific tours/services
  - Leverage local expertise to differentiate from competitors like Booking.com and Lonely Planet

### SEO Optimization Opportunities

  - Create comprehensive travel guides for each destination (e.g., Cancun, Puerto Vallarta)
  - Optimize for high-volume keywords: "What to do in \[destination\]" (currently ranking \#30 for Cancun)
  - Develop pillar pages for key topics, incorporating existing content
  - Address duplicate content issues and inconsistent URL structures

### Content Localization Strategy

  - Current traffic: 84% English for both tour pages and blog content
  - Revenue: 90% from English-speaking countries
  - Proposal: Selectively localize top-performing content to Spanish, focusing budget on English content for higher ROI
  - Consider SEO implications of extensive localization (crawl budget, website authority)

### Content Creation Process

  - Utilize AI-assisted workflows to generate initial drafts
  - Implement human-in-loop editing to refine and customize content
  - Start with "calibration articles" to align tone and style with Adventures Group's preferences
