## Key Topics

### New Notion Template for Client Workspaces

  - Simplified sections: Internal Docs, Client-Facing Space (Docs, Meeting Tasks, Weekly Updates, Performance Reports)
  - Template located in Templates section, starred for easy access
  - Encouragement to suggest improvements and alignments on standard practices

### Automated Workflows for Client Research

  - Company Brief: Analyzes intro call transcript, website, generates AI context summary
  - Direct Competitors: Identifies and analyzes main competitors
  - Audience Competitors: Analyzes search queries to find content competitors
  - Kickoff Agenda: Compiles research into structured meeting agenda

### Client Communication Standards

  - Weekly updates mandatory, separate from meeting notes
  - Updates should recap previous week, set goals for current week
  - Meetings focused on relationship building, strategy alignment, not status updates
  - Importance of thorough documentation in Notion, Airtable, and published content

### Account Management Best Practices

  - Proactive communication, especially for bottlenecks or quality issues
  - Building early value and trust to overcome potential early-stage challenges
  - Conducting additional research (e.g., SEMrush analysis) before kickoff calls
  - Properly managing stakeholder information and access in Notion

### Upcoming Process Improvements

  - Plans to improve task and project management tracking
  - Future training on new article generation and assignment creation workflows
