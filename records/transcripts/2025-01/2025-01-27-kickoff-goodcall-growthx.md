  - Expanding into enterprise use cases (e.g., PBMs, insurance companies)

### Content Development Approach

  - Leverage AI for research and content creation

  - Focus on creating high-quality, detailed pages that outperform competitors

### Performance Metrics and Goals

  - Increase domain authority from 41 to over 50
  - Aim for 1-2 million monthly unique visitors
  - Track product activation rate (currently \~15% for PLG)
