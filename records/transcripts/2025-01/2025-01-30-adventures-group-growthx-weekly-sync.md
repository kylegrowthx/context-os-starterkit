## Key Topics

### Content Publishing Workflow

  - Currently publishing manually due to CMS integration challenges
  - Need for automation to improve efficiency, especially for Spanish versions
  - Lucero (content team member) can assist with transferring content from staging to production
  - Exploring ways to optimize the publishing process, including paragraph formatting

### Content Review and Refinement

  - Incorrect links discovered, particularly in Spanish versions (404 errors)
  - Need to avoid mentioning competitors or linking to their sites
  - Suggestion to create a list of negative keywords and competitor websites to exclude
  - Importance of adding unique elements (e.g., photos, tips) to differentiate content from AI-generated material

### Image Strategy for Articles

  - Current approach: 14 images per article
  - Concerns about impact on page speed with high image count
  - Proposal to start with minimum photos and add more later
  - Suggestion to use SEO testing tool to measure impact of image quantity on performance

### Performance Tracking and Reporting

  - Initial focus on impressions, clicks, and queries for newly published content
  - Google Search Console data as primary source in early stages
  - More comprehensive analysis possible as more data becomes available (around mid-month 3)
  - Importance of quick publishing to gather data and refine strategy

### Slack Notification Settings

  - Assistance provided to set up email notifications for Slack messages
  - Instructions given on how to manage multiple Slack workspaces
