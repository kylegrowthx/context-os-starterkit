## Key Topics

### Homebase Product Overview

  - Core offering: Team management app for hourly workers (scheduling, time tracking, hiring, onboarding)
  - Key verticals: Food & beverage, retail (primary); services, hospitality (secondary)
  - Pricing model: Freemium with paid add-ons (e.g., payroll, tip management, background checks)
  - Future focus: Expanding payroll services as a key growth area

### Content Strategy and Positioning

  - Shift from pure readership metrics to "community experience" focus
  - Content funnel model: Pink Cloud (audience-relevant, non-product) → Top of Funnel → Middle of Funnel → Bottom of Funnel
  - Need-pain buckets: Team turnover, payroll simplification, team engagement, scheduling ease, communication efficiency
  - AI content generation challenges: Incorporating "why" behind information, maintaining brand voice

### Upcoming Content Projects

  - Glossary content prioritized (15 pieces by mid-February)
  - Programmatic SEO project delayed for site structure planning
  - Content length guideline adjusted to 700-1400 words, flexible based on topic

### Brand Voice and Style

  - Aim for friendly, approachable tone without overusing analogies
  - Maintain technical accuracy while incorporating brand personality
  - Team to refine AI-generated content to better match desired tone and structure
