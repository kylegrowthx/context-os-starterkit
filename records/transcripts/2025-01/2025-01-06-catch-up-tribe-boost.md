## Key Topics

### Account Health Tracking

  - Implement classification system for all accounts in OS (Operating System)
  - Track performance metrics and stakeholder satisfaction for each client
  - Create triggers/automation to flag issues and next steps
  - Use Notion for initial documentation, refine process later
  - Pod leads to own weekly updates on account status

### Client Communication and Reporting

  - Standardize templates for client updates in Slack and Notion
  - Include key metrics like number of articles published vs. target
  - Ensure blockers and action items are clearly communicated
  - Improve quality of meeting notes and internal updates

### Team Structure and Roles

  - Pod leads to manage 5-8 accounts on average
  - Account managers to focus on day-to-day tasks (Airtable, article generation, writer management)
  - Pod leads to oversee strategy, results, and unblocking team issues
  - Consider adjusting structure as company scales to maintain efficiency

### Hiring and Skill Sets

  - Focus on hiring account managers with writing backgrounds who learned SEO
  - Look for candidates with experience running their own blogs/content
  - Leverage current team for referrals of qualified candidates
  - Consider renaming "Account Manager" role to avoid potential confusion

### Training and Knowledge Sharing

  - Implement mandatory 2-hour Friday morning training sessions
  - Use sessions for templates review, working sessions, and knowledge sharing
  - Record trainings for future reference and onboarding
  - Address gaps in client management skills (e.g., reading body language cues)

### Specific Account Issues

  - Boost: Quality issues stemmed from inaccurate company brief, now being addressed
  - Tribe: Struggling to justify ROI due to lack of traffic impact
  - Arena: Mixed signals on account status, need clear communication on path forward

### Alternative Revenue Streams

  - Jimmy working on email deliverability solutions for potential new offering
  - Facing challenges with Gmail verification processes for account creation
