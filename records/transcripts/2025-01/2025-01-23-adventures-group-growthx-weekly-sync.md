## Key Topics

### AI Search Optimization Strategy

  - Develop longer articles (3000+ words) with numerous questions and answers
  - Consolidate related topics into comprehensive guides (e.g., whale watching guide)
  - Leverage diverse distribution channels, including reviews and social media presence
  - Structure content with easily extractable overviews and tables for AI digestion
  - Monitor potential traffic loss to AI overviews and adjust keyword strategy accordingly

### Content Development Progress

  - Workflows set up for automatic Spanish localization

### Collaboration on Content Creation
