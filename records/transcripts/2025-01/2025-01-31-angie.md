## Key Topics

### Cancun vs. Riviera Maya Distinction

  - Cancun's Hotel Zone:
      - Walkable, easy public transportation
      - More readily available online information
      - Highly competitive for tour agencies (cheaper options, on-site sellers)
  - Riviera Maya:
      - Includes Tulum, Cozumel; \~100km from Cancun
      - Resorts are larger, more isolated
      - Requires car rental for exploration
      - Different nightlife, shopping experiences (e.g., 5th Avenue in Playa del Carmen)

### Content Strategy Implications

  - "Cancun" keyword stronger, but potentially misleading
  - Opportunity to create Riviera Maya-specific content
  - Consider separate articles for Hotel Zone vs. Riviera Maya experiences
  - Highlight logistical differences (e.g., distances, transportation needs)
  - Potential to target lesser-known areas (e.g., Akumal, Puerto Aventuras) for niche content

### Tour Operations Insights

  - Most Cancun Adventures guests stay in Riviera Maya, not Hotel Zone
  - Multiple pickup stops cause longer travel times, occasional complaints
  - Different tour dynamics between Hotel Zone (competitive, on-site sales) and Riviera Maya

### Cultural/Tourist Behavior Notes

  - Some tourists say "Cancun" regardless of specific location (e.g., Tulum)
  - Cancun perceived as more "city-like," catering to American tourists
  - Riviera Maya offers a different, more nature-oriented experience
