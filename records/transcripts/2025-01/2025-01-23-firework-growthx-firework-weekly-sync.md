## Key Topics

### SEO Traffic Growth and Content Performance

  - Organic visitors increased from 800/month in August to projected 3,500-4,000/month in January
  - GrowthX articles contributing \~3,000 clicks, existing Firework articles \~800 clicks
  - 650 clicks from newly published articles, 130 clicks from revamped existing content

### Keyword Rankings and Strategy

  - Prioritizing key buckets: shoppable video, interactive video, video commerce
  - Ranking \#1 for several long-tail keywords (e.g., "shoppable video for electronics")
  - "Shoppable video for e-commerce" improved from position 28 to 6 in one week
  - Need to refocus keyword strategy on enterprise-specific terms and emerging priorities (e.g., 1-to-1 video chat, client telling, AI)

### Backlink Acquisition and Technical SEO

  - Identified need for more aggressive backlink acquisition strategy
  - Suggested outbound email campaigns targeting 500,000 people/day across multiple domains
  - Recommended acquiring 20 high-quality backlinks per month
  - Technical SEO improvements needed, including schema implementation and potential migration from WordPress to Web O

### Analytics and Pipeline Impact

  - Current lack of insight into how SEO traffic translates to pipeline generation
  - Need to implement UTM parameters and improved tracking to attribute leads to specific content
  - Suggested using tools like Apollo to reverse-engineer account information from traffic

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO)

  - Recommended implementing pop-ups, case studies, and other CRO tactics on blog pages
  - Suggested copying successful strategies from competitors (e.g., Ramp's content approach)
  - Need to set up proper tracking to measure conversions from each piece of content

### Team Collaboration and Accountability

  - Identified gaps in marketing automation and insights
  - Need for better internal communication and task allocation
  - GrowthX team to provide more proactive guidance and hold Firework team accountable
