## Key Topics

  - 41 articles published, 13 more by end of week

  - Impressions increased from \~400 to \~1700 in recent weeks
  - Click-through rate benchmark: 5%+ considered excellent

### Outbound Campaigns

  - "Get koala" campaign launched, targeting website visitors
  - Content collaboration campaign: 1 response so far, still iterating
  - Pending campaign focused on paid media managers
  - Strong open/click rates, but response rates need improvement
  - Implementing Gmail to Gmail sending to improve deliverability

### SEO and Link Building

  - Backlinks being built to Datagrid from various sources
  - Focus on holistic SEO approach: backlinks, content, and technical aspects

### Product Integration in Content

  - Discussion on incorporating more product-specific content in articles

### Industry and Use Case Categorization

  - Plan to categorize content by both industry and use case
  - Aim for continuity between blog categories and product interface organization
  - Decision to add industry and use case options in the CMS for easy categorization

### Email Deliverability Challenges

  - Industry-wide challenges in email deliverability discussed
  - Strategies to improve: diversifying domain providers, email providers, and using dedicated IPs
