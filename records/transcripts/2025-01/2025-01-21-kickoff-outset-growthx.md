## Overview

- Outset aims to build content authority in the pre-med/healthcare education space, targeting high school students and parents
- Focus on driving web traffic and SEO rankings initially, with leads as a longer-term goal (12-24 months)
- Content strategy to include deep dives on medical specialties, career paths, and potentially college program rankings

---

## Key Topics

### Outset's Business Model and Goals

  - Summer programs for high school students interested in healthcare careers
  - $3-5M revenue target for this summer, aiming for $10-50M in next few years
  - Multi-year, multi-point product vision (high school, online, gap year, college programs)
  - Building brand authority and expertise to stay ahead of emerging competition

### Target Audience and Customer Journey

  - Primary: High school students (grades 9-10) interested in healthcare careers
  - Secondary: Parents of those students
  - Tertiary: High school career counselors
  - Future potential: Private schools (for group packages)
  - Journey: Interest spark → Summer availability → Parent sees ROI + Student excitement → Application → Interview → Acceptance

### Content Strategy Objectives

  - Build comprehensive blog/content ecosystem
  - Establish authority in pre-med/healthcare education space
  - Diversify from paid channels, build organic traffic
  - Create content similar to NerdWallet's approach, but for medical specialties
  - Potential development of college pre-med program rankings

### Competitive Landscape

  - Direct competitors: Summer Discovery, SLC (broad programs including healthcare)
  - University-run healthcare summer programs (e.g. Wake Forest)
  - SEO competitors: Various pre-med/healthcare education content creators

### Technical Setup and Resources

  - Website built on Webflow
  - Custom blog format inspired by Ramp's content structure
  - Access to paid search/social accounts, analytics, and customer feedback data
  - Recently created customer stories/testimonials
