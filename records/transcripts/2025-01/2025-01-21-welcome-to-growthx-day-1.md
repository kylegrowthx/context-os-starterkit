## Key Topics

### Company Overview and Structure

  - GrowthX has grown from a small team to 20+ employees and 30+ accounts in less than a year
  - Implemented a pod structure led by managing editors to improve scalability and create growth paths
  - Focusing on balancing quality and scale in content production, with a unique selling point of high-volume, high-quality content

### Tools and Workflows

  - Notion: Used for team documentation, SOPs, and onboarding
  - Airtable: Serves as the "Content OS" for tracking content production and client information
  - AirOps: Current backbone for workflow management and content generation, though likely to be phased out
  - Developing proprietary tools to replace third-party solutions and improve flexibility

### Onboarding Process

  - New hires to focus on gaining access to necessary tools and watching training videos
  - Shadowing experienced team members to gain practical experience
  - Emphasis on learning through hands-on application of workflows
  - Continuous feedback loop encouraged to improve processes and workflows

### Client Management

  - Weekly syncs and kickoff meetings with clients
  - Importance of showcasing the "magic" behind the content creation process to clients
  - Balancing client expectations between output quality and the broader value proposition

### Future Plans

  - Potential fundraising within the next month
  - Aggressive hiring for various positions (managing editors, account managers, strategists)
  - Development of new training tools using Circle platform
