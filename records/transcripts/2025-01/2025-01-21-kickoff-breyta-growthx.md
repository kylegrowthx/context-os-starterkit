## Key Topics

### Company Goals and Metrics

  - Primary goal: Reach 700K ARR for break-even, with 100K ARR as initial milestone
  - Current metrics: \~150 sign-ups per week, 5-6% conversion rate from ads
  - Shifting from free model to 14-day free trial with $15/month starting plan
  - Launching general availability within two weeks, supported by micro-influencer outreach

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Core: UX designers, product managers, user researchers
  - Emerging segments: Academia (lecturers, PhD students), marketers (packaging, pricing research)
  - Diverse use cases: Competitive research, mystery shopping, sales enablement analysis
  - Individual users from large organizations (e.g., universities) signing up with personal accounts

### Product Features and Functionality

  - Two core functionalities: Project-based analysis and Research Repository
  - Transcription and video/audio analysis capabilities
  - AI-powered chat interface for querying across multiple files and data types
  - New update improves relevance for user researchers

### Content Strategy and Programmatic Opportunities

  - Focus on creating use case pages and leveraging existing public data
  - Potential for creating a library of questions tagged by personas and industries
  - Explore opportunities for conference-specific content repositories
  - Utilize Breyta's product to generate tailored questions based on content

### Website and Technical Setup

  - Using Webflow as CMS
  - Need for scalable templates to support programmatic content creation
  - Potential to embed Breyta's chat functionality directly into web pages

### Influencer Marketing

  - Seeking micro-influencers in UX/product design space (10k+ followers)
  - Plans to sponsor influencers and leverage their networks
  - Request for assistance in identifying relevant influencers across platforms
