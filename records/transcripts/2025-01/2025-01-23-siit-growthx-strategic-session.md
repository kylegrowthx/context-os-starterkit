## Key Topics

### SEO Analysis

  - Siit's authority score: 28 (average among competitors, room for improvement)
  - Referring domains more important than total backlinks
  - Ranking for 244 organic keywords, primarily informational
  - Targeting keyword difficulty of 30-40 initially

### Content Strategy

  - Clusters identified: ITSM, team building, workplace efficiency
  - Approach: Test 3-4 clusters for \~1 month, then refine based on performance
  - Open to exploring broader topics beyond core ITSM
  - Integration-specific content (e.g., Slack, MS Teams) considered lower priority
  - Comparison articles (e.g., Siit vs. Zendesk) to be evaluated carefully

### French Website Deprecation

  - Recommendation: Phase out French site due to maintenance challenges
  - Current non-brand traffic: France (220 clicks/3 months) vs. US (50 clicks/3 months)
  - Strategy:
    1.  Focus on building English content
    2.  Implement 301 redirects for high-traffic pages
    3.  Create a general redirect rule (French to English)
    4.  Monitor performance for 3-6 months before full deprecation

### Technical SEO Issues

  - Critical: XML sitemap not submitted to Google Search Console
  - Page speed improvements needed (details to be provided)
