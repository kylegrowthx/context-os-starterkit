## Key Topics

### Current Website Performance

  - Blog represents 80% of traffic
  - 50/50 split between mobile and desktop traffic
  - Mobile has higher click-through rate (CTR)
  - Some uncertainty about whether parents or students are primary researchers

### Technical SEO Issues

  - Mobile page speed needs improvement (4.6s First Contentful Paint, 9s Largest Contentful Paint)
  - Main hero image loading slowly on mobile
  - Recommendation to optimize image format (possibly use WebP instead of JPG)

### Content Strategy Development

  - 9 content clusters identified, mimicking customer journey
  - 62 keyword-based assignments created
  - Clusters include:
    1.  Discovering healthcare through summer camp
    2.  Exploring healthcare careers
    3.  Gaining real-world experience
  - Using Airtable for content planning and management
  - Incorporating search volume and keyword difficulty metrics from SEMrush

### Content Optimization Tactics

  - Focus on comprehensive guides for competitive keywords
  - Use listicles and numbered titles (e.g., "8 reasons you should be a pediatrician for teens")
  - Include "for teens" or "for high schoolers" in titles to target audience
  - Create content ecosystems with related articles to keep users engaged
  - Potential to scrape "People Also Ask" questions for content ideas

### Leveraging "Internship" Keywords

  - High search volume for internship-related terms
  - Outset doesn't offer internships but can create content to capture traffic
  - Potential for location-specific internship articles (e.g., "Best medical internships for teens in Charlotte, North Carolina")
  - Include disclaimers and CTAs to Outset's programs in internship-related content
