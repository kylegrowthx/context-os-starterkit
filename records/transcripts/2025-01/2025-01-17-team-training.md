## Key Topics

### Article Generation Workflow

  - Run multiple article generations in parallel at the start of the day to batch research tasks
  - Customize outlines by expanding sections, adding detail, and incorporating client context
  - Use targeted feedback on specific sections rather than broad rewrites
  - Experiment with different "archetypes" and writing styles during calibration

### Differentiating from Basic AI Content

  - Explain sophisticated research process involving hundreds of sources
  - Showcase expert guidance and editorial judgment applied throughout
  - Highlight ability to generate high-quality, ranking content (e.g. D-Graph example)
  - Involve clients in calibration process to build excitement and trust

### Content Strategy Development

  - Identify branching topic ideas while reviewing generated content
  - Use academic papers and in-depth sources to develop comprehensive topic clusters
  - Apply critical thinking to find angles relevant to client's audience
  - Demonstrate deep subject matter knowledge in client meetings

### Troubleshooting and Optimization

  - Document specific problematic examples to help improve the system
  - Experiment with different prompting approaches in tools like ChatGPT
  - Balance depth vs volume based on client priorities
  - Leverage new features like "smart mode" for faster generation
