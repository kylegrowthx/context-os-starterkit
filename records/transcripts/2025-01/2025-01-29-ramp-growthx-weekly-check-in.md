## Key Topics

### Refresher Workflow Improvements

  - New workflow allows for customized research guidance and refresh instructions
  - Supports different types of refreshes (minor enhancements to complete rewrites)
  - Improved processing speed: from 30 minutes to 1 minute for full article generation
  - Enhanced control over voice, tone, and style through post-processing phase

### SEO Performance Update

  - Block pages performance holding steady
  - Per diem pages showing increased impressions, stable clicks
  - Expense category pages experienced highest jump in impressions and clicks
  - Top performers: North Carolina, Texas, Louisiana, and pages related to gifts and clothing for employees
  - Unexpected spike in office cleaning-related searches, but with low CTR due to mismatched intent

### Treasury Content Strategy

  - Initial brainstorming ideas may not align well with Ramp's actual treasury product offering
  - Focus shifting towards business savings accounts and rate comparisons
  - Challenges: SERPs dominated by bank pages for high-volume searches
  - Opportunity: Creating comprehensive comparison tables for business savings account rates

### Airops Workflow Sharing

  - Ramp expressed interest in learning about GrowthX's Airops workflow best practices
  - GrowthX open to sharing non-production workflow examples and setting up a call to discuss configuration best practices
