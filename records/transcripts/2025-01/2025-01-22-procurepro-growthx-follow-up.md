## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Utilizes AI with significant human editor involvement at every step
  - Starts with company research, persona development, and topic ideation
  - Employs custom research agents to gather specific information
  - Allows for multiple feedback points and stylistic adjustments
  - Typical article length: 1,500-3,000 words, sometimes up to 4,000-5,000
  - Includes image generation capabilities, though complex infographics require separate design work

### Onboarding and Calibration

  - Week 1: Deep dive into client's business
  - Week 2: Strategy alignment and opportunity identification
  - Week 3-4: Content calibration and initial publishing
  - Calibration involves creating sample content, gathering feedback, and adjusting guidelines
  - Collaborative use of Notion for information gathering and strategy development

### Performance Metrics and Reporting

  - Focus on indexing, impressions, and clicks as leading indicators
  - Aims for significant traffic increases (e.g., 5x) over 5-6 months, depending on domain authority
  - Considers factors like backlinks (ProcurePro has \~940) and domain authority
  - Offers link acquisition services to boost authority

### Content Strategy Recommendation

  - Marcel recommends starting with 80% editorial content for ProcurePro
  - Suggests validating topics and building authority before considering programmatic content
  - Aligns with ProcurePro's recent hiring of a content marketing person
