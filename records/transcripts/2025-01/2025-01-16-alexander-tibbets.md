  - Multi-step approach: research, content strategy mapping, outline generation, article creation, and post-processing

  - Human editors guide the process and review content
  - Focus on creating content clusters and systematically building out topics
  - Emphasis on continuous content refreshing and enrichment

### Content Quality and Search Engine Performance

  - Aim to produce content superior to Perplexity AI and other AI research tools
  - Clients like DGRF and Strapi have seen sustained growth and high search rankings
  - Gradual approach to content enrichment: start with MVP, then add examples, images, quotes over time

  - Suggest leveraging client's internal knowledge base or documentation as a source of truth
  - Propose using fact-checkers and technical reviewers to ensure accuracy
