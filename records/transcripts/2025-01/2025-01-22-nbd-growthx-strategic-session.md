## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Prioritization

  - Initial strategy covers broad topics related to NoBoringDesign's services (branding, web design, motion design, marketing design, illustration)
  - Team agreed to evenly spread content across main hypotheses to start, refining based on performance data after 3-6 months
  - Potential to explore content angles around client journeys and pain points (e.g., "just closed funding, need a brand")

### Visual Content Integration

  - Challenge: NoBoringDesign lacks resources for custom graphics for each blog post
  - Proposed solutions:
      - Link to relevant portfolio pieces within articles
      - Utilize existing sales deck assets and illustrations
      - Create a shared Google Drive with tagged design assets for easy access
      - Explore programmatic ways to surface relevant visuals efficiently

### Content Production Process

  - fwd.digital team to deliver calibration articles first for review and approval
  - Plan to scale up gradually: calibration articles → 5 weekly articles → 10 weekly articles
  - Emphasis on iterative improvement: publish at 70% quality, then refine based on performance

### Technical Considerations

  - Blog section in Webflow is in development; fwd.digital to provide best practices for blog structure
  - Webflow confirmed capable of handling substantial blog content without performance issues

### Future Optimizations

  - Interlinking strategy to boost SEO performance and user experience
  - Content refreshes and SEO testing to improve existing content over time
  - Potential for email newsletter or lead magnet to nurture prospects not ready to book a call
