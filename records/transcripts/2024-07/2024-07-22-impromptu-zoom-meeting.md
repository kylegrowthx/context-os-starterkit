## Key Topics

### Ideal Candidate Profile

  - Strong writing/communication skills most important
  - SEO knowledge helpful but can be trained
  - Journalism background could translate well

### Sourcing Channels

  - LinkedIn - paid job promotion, manual searches
  - Freelance platforms like Upwork for temp/part-time hires
  - Referrals from existing team

### Hiring Process Optimizations

  - Use video interviews to quickly assess communication skills
  - Streamline assessments for strong referral candidates
  - Prioritize volume of applicants over perfecting process initially

### Cost Considerations
