## Overview

- AI can augment and streamline many tasks like content creation, customer communications, documentation, etc. by breaking down processes into modular steps
- Start small by identifying repetitive tasks that can be automated, don't try to overengineer from the start
- Leverage AI's ability to synthesize information from multiple sources into coherent outputs
- Continuously iterate and adapt the AI workflows based on results and feedback

---

## Key Topics

### AI-Powered Content Creation

  - Demonstrated modular workflows for article creation, optimization, and refreshing
  - Scrapes top ranking pages, analyzes intent, generates drafts tailored to Homebase
  - Adds internal links, formats based on guidelines
  - Allows rapid content seeding that can be enriched based on performance signals

### Broader Applications of AI Workflows

  - Customer onboarding emails personalized with company info and conversation starters
  - Summarizing sales calls, extracting key details into CRM fields
  - Generating documentation by processing product info and demos
  - Potential to create interactive help guides from product usage data

### Getting Started with AI Workflows

  - Identify repetitive tasks and break down into modular steps
  - Start with inputs you already have - data, content, product info
  - Iteratively build out AI components for each step
  - Use examples to steer AI outputs in the right direction
  - Combine AI outputs with human review for quality control
