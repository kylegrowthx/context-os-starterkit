## Overview

- Sadira will create and schedule 10-12 articles per week on WordPress, with Paola reviewing 2 articles per day
- PH will share personas/target audiences to help tailor content
- PH demonstrated the image creation process using Midjourney and Figma, sharing the Figma file with Sadira for future use
- Discussed potentially increasing content volume for a separate community website in addition to Arena's site

---

## Key Topics

### Content Creation Workflow

  - Sadira creates draft articles from AI output, edits them, adds metadata and publishes to WordPress
  - Paola will review 2 published articles per day and provide feedback
  - Articles will be scheduled evenly throughout the week, not all at once, for better frequency

### Image Generation

  - PH uses Midjourney to generate initial images based on article topics
  - He then enhances the images in Figma by adding text, adjusting composition etc.
  - Shared the Figma file with Sadira so she can follow the same process

### Airtable Management

  - Discussed using the Airtable to track content refresh needs for specific pages/keywords
  - Personas and product categories still need to be filled out

### Increasing Content Volume

  - PH proposed creating additional 8-10 articles per week for a separate community website
  - To avoid competing with Arena's site, the content would focus on community engagement tactics
