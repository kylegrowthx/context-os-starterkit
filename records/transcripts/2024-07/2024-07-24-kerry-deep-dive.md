## Key Topics

### Content Production Capacity

  - Currently at \~7 new pieces and 5 refreshes per week
  - Could increase to \~10 new and 5 refreshes by using stock images instead of custom image generation
  - Potential to further increase capacity by adding more resources

### Content Mix

  - 20% "pink cloud" (about the audience, not directly product-related)
  - 60% tofu (how-to, informational)
  - 20% mofu (evaluating solutions)
  - No bofu (product-specific) content for now

### Starter vs. Operational Businesses

  - Main personas: Starters (pre-launch) and operational (recently launched)
  - Focus on starter content for now as easier to acquire and can build loyalty
  - Operational/multi-business owners are a future opportunity

### Zero-Volume Keywords

  - Leveraging zero-volume keywords based on organic traffic patterns
  - Allows targeting of long-tail opportunities competitors may miss
  - Plan to incorporate this into the content strategy

### Reporting/Analytics

  - Currently lack full visibility into conversions from content
  - Need to set up better reporting, potentially using Amplitude directly
  - Will aim to report on keyword/traffic growth by funnel stage
