## Key Topics

### Logo Design Review

  - Option A: Refined letter shapes to match symbol's curves/corners, maintaining symbol hierarchy
  - Option B: Used diamond shape across all letters, with symbol resting on x-height axis
  - Marcel appreciated the thoughtful approach to proportions and aspect ratios

### Feedback and Preferences

  - Marcel liked unique letterforms in Option B (e.g. R reminiscent of rose.com)
  - Suggested using uppercase G for Option B, keeping other elements same as current iteration
  - For Option A, straightening end of G and refining H/T tails for better cohesion
  - Overall, leaning towards Option B with uppercase G and minor refinements

### Homepage Design

  - Prioritize designing a simple, compelling homepage as next main deliverable
  - Marcel can adapt current site himself, swapping logos and branding elements
  - Focus on homepage allows delivering value quickly vs. full site redesign now
  - Subsequent phases to expand to other sections (blog, etc.)
