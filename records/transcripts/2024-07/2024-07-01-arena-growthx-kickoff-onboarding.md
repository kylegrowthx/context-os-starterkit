## Key Topics

### Goals & Metrics

  - Target: 100 signups/day and $600k new ARR from self-service by end of 2023 (currently 4 signups/day)
  - Also aim to increase number of enterprise deals closed via organic traffic (historically 2 per quarter)
  - Success looks like exponential growth in organic rankings/traffic over next 6-12 months

### Target Audience

  - Product managers, digital experience teams looking to increase engagement
  - Companies: Media, publishers, sports, entertainment (e.g. ESPN, Reuters)
  - Have existing lists from ZoomInfo, Apollo of target accounts

### Current Content Strategy

  - No defined strategy/workflow currently - opportunity area
  - Focused on fixing meta tags, titles for high-value keywords
  - Publish 1-2 new articles per week, update old content
  - Bottlenecks: new website launch, creating visual assets, prioritizing keywords

### Next Steps

  - GrowthX to go deep on research/learning this week
  - Arena to share: product demo, sales materials, existing keyword research, examples of great content
  - Schedule deeper dive meetings with relevant stakeholders
  - Establish weekly sync cadence starting week 3
