## Overview

- Arena is launching a new website and "Arena Community" product - an all-in-one solution to help companies build communities on their own websites
- The goal is to move audiences away from social media to company websites for better monetization and first-party data
- Arena's infrastructure can handle large traffic spikes, differentiating it from individual creator platforms like Circle
- The team will provide access to paid ads data to inform the SEO/content keyword strategy

## Key Topics

### New Website and Arena Community Product

  - Shifting to a modern dark mode design while maintaining credibility
  - Main components: AI-powered content feed, live blogs, group chat, user-generated threads
  - Focus on monetization through ads and potential e-commerce integration
  - Using structured data and SEO techniques to improve site's ranking

### Company Background and Goals

  - Founded in 2018 to move audiences to company websites from social media
  - Initial products were live blogs and group chat widgets
  - Now building an all-in-one community solution after raising Series A
  - Targeting media publishers, entertainment, sports, e-commerce companies

### Competitive Landscape

  - Similar value prop to Circle and Sphere for hosting communities
  - But Arena is a white-label version embedded on client's website
