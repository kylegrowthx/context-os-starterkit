## Overview

- The initial focus will be on streamlining the "welcome call" process for new trial signups, including pre-call preparation, conducting the call efficiently, and personalized post-call follow-up emails.
- Leveraging AI can automate time-consuming manual tasks, provide relevant talking points/battle cards, and surface the most pertinent resources for follow-ups based on call context.
- Starting with this end-to-end workflow allows for iterative improvements, quick wins, and learnings that can be extended to other teams like sales and marketing.
- Collaboration between the AI partner and Homebase's customer success team is crucial for understanding processes, refining outputs, and ensuring a seamless adoption.

---

## Key Topics

### Welcome Call Process

  - Customer success reps make "welcome calls" to new trial signups to provide a consultative experience, understand their needs, recommend relevant features, and drive activation.
  - Current process involves manually filling out dispositions, recommendations, and follow-up details in Salesforce after each call.
  - Personalizing follow-up emails by finding and linking relevant resources is very time-consuming.

### Potential AI Use Cases

  - Transcribing calls and using the transcript to auto-populate Salesforce fields and generate personalized follow-up email drafts with relevant resources.
  - Providing pre-call "battle cards" with relevant talking points and customer/company context based on data enrichment.
  - Enabling after-call coaching by analyzing calls for areas of improvement, objection handling, etc.

### Next Steps

  - Marcel to review sample call recordings, transcripts, follow-up emails, and training materials from the customer success team.
  - Identify 1-2 high-impact workflows to build an initial AI-powered solution.
  - Schedule a hands-on working session to collaboratively build and refine the workflows.
  - Explore integrating the AI outputs into existing tools like Salesforce.
  - Pilot the solution with a subset of the customer success team for feedback.
  - Align the project timeline with Homebase's upcoming company-wide hackathon.
