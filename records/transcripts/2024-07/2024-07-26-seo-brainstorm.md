## Key Topics

### Keyword Research

  - Marcel shared research on \~400 relevant keywords with search volumes and difficulty scores
  - Higher volume keywords like "knowledge graph" are very difficult to rank for
  - Lower volume but more specific keywords around AI/ML concepts may be more achievable targets

### Content Approach

  - Create a "concepts" section separate from the blog, with different expectations for depth/quality
  - Start by defining high-level topic clusters aligned with Hypermode's capabilities
  - Generate content drafts using AI and existing materials as inputs
  - Have engineers review drafts, fill gaps, and ensure technical accuracy
  - Adapt content to Hypermode's voice/branding guidelines
  - Approach as "planting seeds" - publish drafts, then iteratively improve content that shows potential

### Multimedia Content

  - Short videos explaining key concepts can be highly engaging and help with rankings
  - Can leverage existing recordings or have team members explain concepts casually
  - Repurpose written content into video formats in the future
