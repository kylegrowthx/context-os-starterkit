## Overview

- Firework provides an enterprise-grade video hosting and streaming platform to enable shoppable videos on websites, emails, and physical stores
- Their solutions cover short-form videos, long-form digital showrooms, live shopping, and 1-on-1 personalized video experiences
- Firework differentiates through its global infrastructure, data privacy/compliance, and services like content strategy and production
- Key customer profiles include direct-to-consumer brands, mid-market to enterprise companies with decent website traffic, industries like beauty, fashion, electronics, health/wellness

---

## Key Topics

### Firework's Video Commerce Platform

  - Enables minimum 30% increase in e-commerce conversions within 14 days without additional IT/content investment
  - Three layers: interactive video player, video infrastructure, and video content management
  - Provides shoppable videos, content import from social media, video streaming from Firework servers
  - Allows video correlation to conversions and shopping integration

### Customer Examples and Use Cases

  - Brands like Beyond Yoga, Higher Education use shoppable product videos on website
  - Samsung used live video shopping for product launch events
  - Brands leverage Firework for user reviews, influencer content (short-form) and brand storytelling (long-form)
  - CPG brands use videos to inspire purchases on e-commerce sites

### Handling Large Video/Product Catalogs

  - Bulk video uploads and imports from social platforms
  - Playlist creation to organize videos
  - Embedding different video feeds on website pages
  - AI-assisted video tagging to product catalog

### Competitive Differentiation

  - Enterprise-grade video infrastructure and global CDN for scalability and low latency
  - Serves 70% of top global Fortune 500 retail/brands
  - Largest engineering team and funding for continuous innovation
  - End-to-end solutions from short-form to live video, not just a video player
  - Video commerce strategy, content production services along with SaaS platform

### Pricing and Customer Success

  - Freemium model based on website traffic levels
  - $39/month for up to 10K monthly visits
  - $700/month for mid-market with customer support
  - $3500+/month for enterprise with data privacy, compliance reviews
  - Co-op services for content strategy, production at additional cost

### Target Customers and Buying Centers

  - CMOs, Heads of Marketing, E-commerce, Digital, Social Media
  - Product Managers for the website/digital experience
  - Brand Marketers and Content Heads (with some reservations on brand priorities)
