## Overview

- Marcel demoed an AI workflow for generating content outlines and articles by combining web research, GPT models like GPT-4, and human review steps
- The approach shows promise but needs calibration - tweaking prompts, guidelines, quality checks etc. based on Homebase's requirements

## Key Topics

### AI Content Workflow Demo

  - Generates content by combining web research summaries, outline from GPT-4, and article writing
  - Allows human review at multiple steps - choosing web sources, reviewing outline, final article etc.
  - Showed examples of generated articles on topics like "tip pooling laws in California"
  - Ability to customize prompts, add guidelines, link to sources, avoid plagiarism etc.

### Opportunities and Tradeoffs

  - Can generate high volumes by extending existing content to different states, industries etc.
  - Faster turnaround than human writers, more cost-effective for lower traffic keywords
  - Tradeoff between volume and quality - more human reviews means lower throughput
  - Risks like making up facts, inconsistent tone need to be mitigated

### Feedback and Concerns

  - Exclude unreliable sources like Wikipedia, competitors etc. from research
  - Check for plagiarism, made-up statistics/stories - human oversight is key
  - Customize tone, structure, guidelines to Homebase's preferences
  - Explore other use cases beyond articles - calculators, Q\&A, refreshing existing content etc.
