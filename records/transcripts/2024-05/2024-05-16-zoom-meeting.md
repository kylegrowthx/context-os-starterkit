## Key Topics

### Content Clustering Process

  - Group related keywords into clusters that will become articles
  - Identify primary keyword (highest search volume/best intent)
  - Tag remaining related keywords as secondary for that cluster
  - Can filter/sort keywords by category, search volume, etc. to prioritize

### Keyword Tagging

  - Keywords tagged by product category (e.g. payroll)
  - Allows filtering to focus on specific topic areas
  - Prioritize keywords without existing URLs first
