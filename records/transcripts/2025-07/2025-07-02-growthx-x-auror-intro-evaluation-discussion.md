## Context

Auror is a New Zealand-founded scale-up in the retail crime intelligence space, operating across five countries (NZ, Australia, UK, US, Canada) with ~250 employees. The company sells software to retailers for crime prevention and recently partnered with Axon (evidence.com) to expand into law enforcement, enabling evidence sharing and automated investigation recommendations. Camille Trent recently joined as a marketer in October to scale Auror's content and marketing efforts alongside Jimmy (field marketing director for North America) and one content person based in New Zealand. The company conducted 52 events last year and is now exploring solutions to scale content creation efficiently across diverse audiences and geographies without adding significant headcount. Heather Gadonniex, their newly hired CMO, recommended evaluating both agency-based approaches (GrowthX) and AI workflow platforms (Tofu, Velocity Engine) to determine which model best fits their needs. Marcel Santilli's relationship with GrowthX and recommendation from Heather made this meeting the right fit among several options Auror was considering.
