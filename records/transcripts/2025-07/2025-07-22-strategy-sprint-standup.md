## Context

This is GrowthX's core delivery standup, led by Andi Bailey with George Haikal handling operations and Aida Knezevic managing client relationships. The team manages a portfolio of 11 active content marketing and SEO projects ranging from site refreshes to new content creation. Most clients are in verticals like construction (Town), industrial (Engine, Hyper Growth), software (Okta, Sentinel-1), and professional services (All, Biologica, Monograph). The meeting is held weekly to track blockers, coordinate across team members, flag urgent issues, and align on next steps. This particular standup occurred during the final days of July, with several projects hitting important deadlines and two clients facing critical technical blockers that threatened delivery.
