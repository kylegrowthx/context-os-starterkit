## Context

Imubit is an industrial AI software company that provides a closed-loop AI optimization platform for process manufacturing. In late 2024/early 2025, GrowthX was engaged as their content marketing partner to build out a top-of-funnel content strategy targeting industrial sectors (cement, mining, chemicals, energy) where AI optimization is transforming operations. The engagement was initiated because Imubit had low domain authority, minimal organic visibility, and were heavily reliant on branded search traffic. Tim Yandel, who recently took on the VP of Sales and Marketing role, recognized this as the highest-leverage area for growth and positioned content marketing as a strategic investment to build awareness in target verticals.

This is Imubit's first serious content marketing push, and the early indicators have been stronger than expected — content published just one month prior is already ranking for relevant keywords and showing strong impression curves. However, the engagement faces internal skepticism from Imubit's board and finance team, who are accustomed to traditional metrics and need to see a clear ROI case and trajectory. This weekly sync serves as both a tactical check-in on content performance and content pipeline priorities, and a strategic planning session for the board meeting presentation that will need to make the case for continued investment in top-of-funnel content initiatives.
