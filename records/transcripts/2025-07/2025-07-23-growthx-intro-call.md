## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Approach to Content Creation

  - Focus on creating high-quality, valuable content for specific target audiences
  - Aim to position clients as publishers in their industry
  - Emphasize deep understanding of customer personas, jobs to be done, and motivations
  - Use AI workflows combined with human expertise for content creation and optimization
  - Developed fact-checking and review processes to ensure accuracy, especially for technical or regulated industries

### GrowthX's Engagement Structure

  - Two-phase approach: Strategy Sprint and Growth Execution
  - Strategy Sprint (6-8 weeks, $20-30K):
      - Develop AI-ready artifacts and context engineering
      - Establish voice, tone, and content roadmap
      - Begin creating initial content pieces (e.g., 23 pieces by week 6 for a current client)
  - Growth Execution (6-12 months, $9-25K/month):
      - Implement and evolve content strategy
      - Scale to daily publishing
      - Continuous refinement based on performance metrics

### Content Performance Measurement

  - Utilize various tools and metrics:
      - SEO: Google Search Console, Google Analytics
      - GEO (AI content visibility): Partner with Scrunch for citations and placement analysis
      - Monitor referral traffic and indirect effects on brand search and direct traffic
  - Acknowledge the complexity of attribution in the evolving AI content landscape
  - Consider incrementality and baseline establishment for performance evaluation

### GrowthX's Team and Platform

  - Editorial-focused team led by experienced professionals (e.g., former TechCrunch editor-in-chief)
  - Managing directors with 20+ years of SEO and growth experience
  - Managing editors oversee day-to-day content creation and refinement
  - Proprietary platform with hundreds of AI workflows for research, content creation, and optimization
  - Human-in-the-loop approach ensures quality control throughout the process
