## Summary

Deep dive with Ankit Pahuja, SEO lead at SentinelOne, to understand current SEO initiatives, challenges, and priorities.

---

## Context

GrowthX recently kicked off an engagement with SentinelOne, a cybersecurity company, as part of a content marketing initiative. Ankit Pahuja leads SEO for SentinelOne and has been in the role for one and a half years after joining the company through the acquisition of a startup where he previously worked. This is a first deep dive with SentinelOne's SEO lead (the kickoff call was held separately with broader stakeholders). GrowthX's engagement focuses on two main areas: refreshing existing blog and editorial pages on SentinelOne's website, and creating new net-new content. The initial phase is exploratory, with George Haikal (GrowthX) conducting deep dives with individual team leads to understand goals, challenges, and daily priorities, and Sydney Go (managing editor) will be the main point of contact for content execution moving forward.

## Relevance

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- SentinelOne's team structure is decentralized: SEO (Ankit), blog (Craig's team), landing page copywriting (Drew/Brand team), PMM. GrowthX will build reusable "artifact" workflows to ensure content is aligned across these siloed teams.
- CS101 (Cybersecurity 101) directory is the primary traffic driver with 900+ pieces across 8 categories built in last 8-9 months. Opportunities to refresh and optimize for LLM visibility and ranking improvements.
- Product marketing integration is a gap. Mansi (CS101 writer) lacks exposure to product positioning. GrowthX can facilitate a product marketing deep dive and incorporate those insights into CS101 refreshes.

**To CheckThat / AI Visibility:**
- LLMs are significantly impacting SEO strategy. Traffic is declining as LLMs answer queries directly. GrowthX's use of Scrunch (LLM tracking tool) will be set up in weeks 4-5. SentinelOne is evaluating Profound as an alternative.
- AI Overview traffic is being misattributed as direct traffic in GA4, making attribution messy.
- Ankit is particularly engaged on AEO (AI Engine Optimization) strategy. This is a key priority for SentinelOne moving forward.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- Strong account health signals. SentinelOne has ambitious goals (45% YoY traffic growth) and is aligned on quick wins (CS101 refresh, CVE database project, localization). Multiple future project opportunities.
- SentinelOne reports to Mahati (web/product leadership) and meets monthly with Drew (VP of Brand) on progress and yearly goals. Clear decision-making structure and favorable positioning for scope expansion.
- Three key projects aligned: (1) CS101 content refresh for LLM/SEO optimization, (2) CVE database (potentially 5M+ pages of AI-generated content), (3) localization to 7 languages. All are traffic-focused and on timeline for Q4 delivery.
