## Key Topics

### ColdIQ's Business Model and Goals

  - Primary revenue streams: outbound system setup, AI sales agency training, tool partnerships
  - Main goal: Increase qualified website traffic across all products

### GrowthX's Approach and Services

  - Focus on building editorial engines for clients, prioritizing long-form content
  - Team includes experienced editors and SEO experts (e.g., ex-TechCrunch, G2)
  - Strategy sprint: 8-week process to create AI-ready artifacts and develop content roadmap
  - Execution phase: Consistent content publishing based on strategy (15+ pieces/week for larger engagements)
  - Custom platform for workflow management and content creation

### AI SEO Strategies and Challenges

  - GrowthX avoids rapid, large-scale programmatic content creation to prevent potential penalties
  - Emphasis on building topical authority through editorial content before exploring programmatic options
  - Careful approach to scaling: start small, monitor performance, then expand gradually
  - Focus on continual content refreshment and enrichment to maintain growth

### GrowthX vs. Other Solutions (e.g., AirOps)

  - GrowthX evolved from using AirOps to developing their own platform
  - Offers more hands-on, managed service compared to pure software solutions
  - Custom workflow engine allows for faster adaptations and feature additions
