## Key Topics

### Outreach's Current Market Position

  - Known for BDR/sales engagement, but platform has evolved significantly
  - Consolidates multiple point solutions (sales engagement, coaching, forecasting, AI) into unified revenue workflow platform
  - Challenge: Market perception hasn't fully caught up with platform's expanded capabilities

### Content Strategy and Execution

  - Recent focus: Refreshing outdated content, implementing SEO best practices
  - Process: Hadassah creates SEO briefs, Maria executes content creation
  - Current output: 2-4 blogs per month, 8-10 days per post
  - Gap: Need more middle/bottom funnel content

### Competitive Landscape

  - Primary competitors: Gong, Clari, SalesLoft
  - Gong perceived as "louder" in marketing; Outreach working on compete pages

### GrowthX Partnership Objectives

  - Scale content production and refresh existing content at high volume
  - Augment Outreach team's capacity for strategic initiatives
  - Improve organic visibility, including in AI-powered search

### Key Personas and Use Cases

  - Priority persona: Revenue Operations Leader
  - Use case: All-day platform usage for team management, forecasting, pipeline health monitoring

### Measurement and Attribution

  - Using HockeyStack and Tableau for content performance tracking
  - Focus on pipeline generation, MQL to SAL conversion, booking ARR tied to organic/content initiatives
