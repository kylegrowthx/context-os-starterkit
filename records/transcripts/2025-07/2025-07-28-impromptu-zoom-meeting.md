## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Efforts

  - 11 SDRs doing cold outreach, working well as lawyers receptive to calls
  - Recently started Google Ads campaign
  - Conferences are key channel, especially for larger firms
  - Little qualification currently, but starting to implement pain-point discovery

### Target Audience

  - Personal injury, medical malpractice, mass tort law firms
  - 10-100 employees, owner-operated firms ideal
  - U.S. only, proportional to state populations
  - Not targeting solos or very large firms yet
  - Looking for growth-minded firms (e.g. investing in marketing, hiring)

### Product Value Proposition

  - Automates intake to demand letter process, reducing manual work
  - Improves predictability, accuracy, and speed of case processing
  - Allows firms to take on more cases and grow revenue
  - Reduces risk of human error in critical legal documents

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competitors: Eveup, Supeo, Evelico (all well-funded)
  - Some competition from horizontal players like Westlaw, LexisNexis
  - Many firms not using any AI solution yet, big education opportunity

### Content Strategy Goals

  - Position Fathom as authoritative resource on legal AI
  - Educate market on AI capabilities and best practices
  - Provide practical, valuable content for law firm operations
  - Differentiate from competitors through depth and quality
  - Mix of technical and business-focused content

### Execution Plan

  - GrowthX to ingest all available info, do competitor research
  - Develop personas, topic clusters, and content strategy in next 1-2 weeks
  - Calibration process to nail tone/style (serious but approachable)
  - Aim for efficient, dense content that's easily skimmable but detailed
