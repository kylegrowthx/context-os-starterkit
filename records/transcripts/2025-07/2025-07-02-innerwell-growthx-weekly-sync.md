## Summary

Innerwell and GrowthX aligned on a major content strategy shift: transitioning from blog-centric content to topic authority hubs organized by medical conditions, with a two-phase approach (V1 indexing existing articles under conditions, V2 full redesign). The team reviewed new blog post templates optimized for conversion, discussed minimum viable content requirements per condition to hit a $300 CAC target, and confirmed Innerwell's quarterly business roadmap prioritizing ketamine-for-pain growth alongside expansion of therapy, EMDR, and psychiatry services.

## Context

Innerwell is a virtual mental health and wellness company offering ketamine therapy, EMDR, psychiatry, and therapy services across multiple states. They're an established GrowthX client working to scale patient acquisition through organic search and content marketing. Current business is heavily weighted toward ketamine (90% revenue) with emerging growth areas in pain management, therapy, EMDR, and psychiatry. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's delivery cadence for their ongoing SEO and content strategy engagement, with Dave Capone as the lead strategist and Andi Bailey managing category page design and implementation. The team is moving from a traditional blog structure to topic-authority hubs, a significant architectural shift that requires coordination between GrowthX's strategy and design work and Innerwell's development and product roadmap.
