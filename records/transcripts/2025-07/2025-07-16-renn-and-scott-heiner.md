## Key Topics

### Current Collateral Gaps

  - Lack of introductory materials for new customers
      - No comprehensive "here's who we are, what we do, proof points" document
  - Insufficient evangelization of capabilities to current customers
      - Underestimation of how little existing clients know about Surge AI's full range of services
  - Existing materials often plain Google Docs, not matching Surge AI's execution level

### Desired Collateral Types

  - Introductory deck for new customers
      - Overview of Surge AI's identity, services, and proof points
  - Monthly capability showcase for existing clients
      - Highlight underutilized services and new capabilities
      - Designed for easy sharing, not requiring sales calls
  - General explanatory materials for common inquiries
      - Improved design over current plain document format

### Design Approach

  - Continuation of hyper-minimalist aesthetic
  - Technical focus, straight to the point
  - Inspiration from 70s-80s design systems and grid layouts
  - Balance between information density and aesthetic appeal

### Content Strategy

  - Maintain technical depth without being overly salesy
  - Showcase data, samples, and let capabilities speak for themselves
  - Challenge: Presenting extensive capabilities without creating overly long decks

### Customization and Editability

  - Materials should be editable and customizable by the Surge AI team

### Example and Inspiration

  - Web Agents deck mentioned as a good reference
      - Spent significant time and effort on creation
      - Maintains technical focus while being visually appealing
