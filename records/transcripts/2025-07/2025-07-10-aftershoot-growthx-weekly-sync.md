## Summary

AfterShoot and GrowthX aligned on a major strategic shift: moving from prioritizing free trial conversions to growing overall blog traffic as the primary north star. With only 200-250 keywords ranking in positions 1-3, the team committed to ramping up content production from 3 articles/week to 10 outlines per batch, targeting 50,000 words/month, with GrowthX focusing on top-of-funnel content while AfterShoot handles mid and bottom-funnel in-house.
