## Context

Diligent is a private governance and board management software company. GrowthX is engaged in an ongoing $200k+/year content marketing engagement to build Diligent's organic visibility. This weekly sync is part of the standard operational rhythm, with Jo Kaminska (GrowthX) and Kezia Farnham (Diligent product/content lead) co-leading the effort alongside Aida Knezevic (GrowthX content strategy).

Diligent's content is created by multiple teams (SEO, content marketing, Diligent Institute, and PR) with limited historical coordination. GrowthX's strategy is twofold: first, to audit and consolidate existing content (identify pruning/refresh opportunities), and second, to build a unified content calendar that incorporates Diligent's internal expertise—ensuring content ranks for non-brand keywords and delivers E-E-A-T signals that both Google and LLMs value. The engagement is in the initial research and audit phase, with content strategy recommendations and a unified publishing roadmap expected by end of week.
