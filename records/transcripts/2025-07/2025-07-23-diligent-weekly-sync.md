## Key Topics

### Current SEO Focus and Challenges

  - Expanding beyond governance to other key solution areas (e.g., audit, risk, compliance)
  - Updating legacy content with more product depth and EAT factors
  - Competitive plays to overtake rivals in specific areas (e.g., 3000+ search terms for governance)
  - Challenge: balancing quality and scale with limited resources

### Content Strategy and LLM Optimization

  - Focus on low-volume, bottom-of-funnel content more likely to be referenced by LLMs
  - Emphasis on readability, clear organization, and unique insights
  - Success seen with zero-volume searches on emerging topics (e.g., AI in dental industry)
  - Diligent's brand authority gives them an advantage in LLM visibility

### Content Management and Collaboration

  - Multiple teams contributing content: SEO, content marketing, Diligent Institute, PR
  - Need for a unified content calendar and better alignment between teams
  - Weekly publish update spreadsheet available for historical content changes
  - GrowthX to create custom artifacts (e.g., information gain doc) to incorporate Diligent's internal expertise

### Site Audit and Content Pruning

  - GrowthX identified over 1000 pages for review
  - Analyzing keyword data from SEMrush and GSC to determine content value
  - Will consider historical performance before recommending content removal
  - Diligent to share existing documentation on content clustering and pruning efforts

### Priority Areas and Industries

  - Boards and governance solution identified as the top priority for content
  - Need for more insights into target industries for broader content strategy
