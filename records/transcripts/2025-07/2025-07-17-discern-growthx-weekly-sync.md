## Key Topics

### Discern Product Overview

  - Offers registered agent services in all 51 jurisdictions (50 states + DC)
  - Automates tracking and handling of filing requirements with Secretary of State offices
  - Simplifies foreign registration process for businesses expanding to new states
  - Provides collaboration tools and unlimited users
  - Handles franchise tax filings, including complex calculations (e.g., Delaware)
  - Supports formation of various entity types (LLCs, corporations, PLLCs, etc.) in all jurisdictions

### User Interface and Functionality

  - Clean, intuitive interface with clear visibility into state registrations and standing
  - Automated creation of filings based on state-specific rules and due dates
  - Pre-fills forms using saved entity data, significantly reducing filing time
  - Offers entity-specific payment methods, crucial for fund managers with multiple entities
  - Provides estimated processing times and expedited filing options

### Content Strategy and Performance

  - Steady growth in impressions week-over-week
  - Top-performing content includes pieces on New Jersey, franchise tax filing, and state-specific information (Illinois, California, Washington, Texas)
  - Increasing referrals from AI/LLM sources, particularly for nuanced topics
  - Perplexity AI noted as a significant source of mentions

### Pain Points and Value Propositions

  - Urgent need: Assistance with registering in multiple states quickly
  - Ongoing challenge: Managing compliance for numerous legal entities and state registrations
  - Key value: Alleviating "existential dread" of non-compliance and lack of visibility

### Technical Details

  - Address and phone number verification to ensure accurate filings
  - Automated handling of complex scenarios (e.g., chain of general partners for LPs)
  - No direct API integrations with most states, but efficient digital submission processes
