## Key Topics

### Tool Setup and Access

- Essential tools: 1Password, Slack, Notion, Zoom, Linear, Fathom
- BITTools Slack channel for tech support (Alice Ochieng or Yusuf can assist)
- Team email (team@growthxlabs.com) setup required; used for logins to ChatGPT, Google Cloud, SEMrush, and other tools
- Linear project management tool with dedicated onboarding project containing day-by-day tasks and week-long roadmap
- 1Password stores all team credentials including team Gmail access credentials and one-time password codes

### Company Policies and Practices

- Unlimited PTO: 20-30 days/year recommended; process is to block time on personal calendar and add team members to the invite (no approval needed)
- $10K referral bonus for engineering roles, $5K for non-engineering roles (bonus paid after referred employee completes 6-month tenure)
- 4-hour minimum overlap with Pacific Standard Time (PST); no fixed working hours beyond that requirement
- Daily Gitbot standups in BEPD Slack channel (prompted at ~8 a.m. or 9 a.m. PST) for sharing priorities, blockers, and status updates
- Bi-weekly all-hands meetings; new employees give brief intro (name, location, interests, one fun fact)

### Onboarding Process

- Notion-based company-wide onboarding guide (shared link provided in Linear project)
- Day 1: Tool setup, Day 1 bootcamp attendance, fill team directory bio, set up Slack profile, attend manager 1:1 with REN, set up Linear and Fathom accounts
- Week 1: Read PTO policy, watch workshops created by Marcel (CEO), attend two bootcamps: Strategy & Vision (Thursday) led by Marcel, Sales (Friday) led by Kyle
- Onboarding buddy assigned by manager REN (role is to answer questions and direct to best resources)
- Complete marketing metrics review: evaluate pages, track GA4 data, test CheckThat prompts for early-signal metrics

### Benefits and HR

- Katia has Rightway set up but needs clarity on full healthcare enrollment process
- Alice Ochieng to contact Bridget McGillivray (operations) for enrollment details and relay back to Katia
- No time tracking tool used at GrowthX; rely on Gitbot updates for visibility
