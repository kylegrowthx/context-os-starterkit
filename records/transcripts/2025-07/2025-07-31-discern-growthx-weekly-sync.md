## Context

Discern is a legal compliance platform helping businesses with entity formation and compliance management. GrowthX is producing a content marketing strategy for Discern focused on state compliance, foreign registration, and other legal entity topics that attract Discern's target customers. This weekly sync is a standing delivery check-in to review content performance, plan upcoming topics, and align on strategy with Discern's founder Mike Bosserman.

The partnership appears to be working: Discern's site recently hit 100k impressions, and the resources section (featuring GrowthX-produced content) is driving 79% of clicks. Mike is also implementing new marketing automation through Unify, targeting companies with specific compliance signals (registered agent count, state registration status) to improve lead quality from web visitors.
