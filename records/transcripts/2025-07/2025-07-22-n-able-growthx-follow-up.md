## Context

N-Able is a software company that's achieved strong product-market fit in the MSP (managed service provider) space but faces market saturation. The company is executing a strategic pivot to become a recognized security brand targeting small and medium enterprises (SMEs) across specific verticals, moving from a "fishing pole on the dock" bottom-of-funnel approach to a more intentional, top-of-funnel visibility strategy. Andrew Beyer leads this effort at N-Able and was following up with Kyle Gaudreau at GrowthX to understand whether GrowthX's content and go-to-market expertise could support N-Able's brand repositioning and help drive awareness in a market where they're currently unknown. This is a business development discussion between a prospective client (N-Able) and a services partner (GrowthX) exploring fit and commercial terms.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Engagement Model and Pricing

  - 8-week initial sprint for deep strategy development and artifact creation
  - Opt-out option available at the end of the sprint
  - Post-sprint engagement typically 12 months, but flexible to 6 months
  - Pricing ranges from $9K/month (5 articles/week) to $15K/month (10+ articles/week)
  - Higher-end engagements ($15K+) include 100+ programmatic pages, 15+ editorial pieces, and custom workflows

### N-Able's Market Position and Goals

  - Historically strong product-market fit in expanding MSP industry
  - Current challenges: saturated MSP market, need to move up the funnel
  - Pivoting to target small/medium enterprises (SMEs) in specific verticals
  - Aiming to establish N-Able as a recognized security brand
  - Seeking content strategies to increase visibility and consideration

### GrowthX's Strategic Process

  - Comprehensive initial brief creation (elevator pitch, ICPs, personas, jobs-to-be-done)
  - Deep dive into persona archetypes, including psychological factors
  - Development of content roadmap and calibration process
  - Artifact creation for AI-readiness and consistent content production
  - Typical timeline: 5-8 weeks for critical artifacts and roadmap setting
  - Emphasis on finding 2+ high-conviction focus areas plus an experimentation lane

### ROI Measurement and Challenges

  - Complexity in measuring impact, especially with generative AI involvement
  - Need to consider incrementality of channels like direct traffic and brand searches
  - Exploration of tools like HockeyStack and Upside for synthesizing insights from multiple sources
  - Importance of adapting measurement approaches to capture full impact of content strategies
