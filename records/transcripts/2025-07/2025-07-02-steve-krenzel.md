## Context

Logic is a Seattle-based AI automation startup that Steve Krenzel (ex-Brex) co-founded after raising their seed round in March 2024. They've built an AI enterprise workflow automation product that is now processing ~250K tasks monthly for a dozen customers. Steve is wearing nearly all non-technical hats (GTM, content, hiring) while the team scales, and they're actively hiring a head of growth to complement Steve's founder-led sales efforts. Earlier this year, Steve connected with Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX) through a mutual acquaintance (Connor at Galileo), who specifically recommended GrowthX for their content strategy work. Logic has already found success with organic content — they've published thought leadership and technical pieces that perform well on LinkedIn and Hacker News — but producing content is consuming time Steve can't spare. This call was Steve's initial exploration of whether GrowthX's AI-enabled content engine could help them scale without hiring a dedicated content team.
