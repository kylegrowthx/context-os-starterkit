## Key Topics

### Geo-Optimization and Content Refresh

  - Workflow article refresh being implemented for Airbyte client
  - Content refresh strategy gaining traction as quick win for clients stuck on content generation
  - Leveraging Scrunch tool to identify strategic content for refresh based on AI citations
  - Refresh particularly useful for ranking but outdated content (e.g., Yerco client)

### Scrunch Tool Usage and Limitations

  - No definitive way to validate search queries; tool uses "peek and poke" method
  - Aim for topic coverage rather than tracking individual prompts
  - Claude AI useful for generating potential queries and categorizing them
  - 1 month of data recommended as minimum for confident trend analysis in Scrunch

### Backlink Strategies

  - Debate on continuing to offer backlinks vs. focusing on citations and organic mentions
  - Current backlink quality could be improved; team not paying enough for high-quality links
  - Backlinks still valuable, especially for new domains or page-level competition
  - Proposal to offer backlinks as targeted packages rather than ongoing service

### Backlink Strategy Refinement

  - Assess current vendors and explore options for better quality at same or lower cost
  - Increase intentionality in selecting link sources and target pages
  - Focus on pages with traffic and existing backlinks for more impactful results
  - Position backlinks as a maturity-based offering, with higher costs for established sites
