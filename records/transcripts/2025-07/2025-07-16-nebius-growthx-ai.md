---

## Key Topics

### Nebius Overview and Product Offerings

  - Two main layers: Nebius Cloud (AI infrastructure) and Nebius AI Studio (high-level AI services)
  - Cloud: Physical infrastructure with AI-centric compute, storage, networking, and managed services
  - AI Studio: Pre-hosted models, fine-tuning capabilities, and developer tools (competing with Fireworks, Together AI)
  - Target customers: AI researchers (Cloud) and AI SaaS companies (AI Studio)

### GrowthX.ai Platform and Workflow

  - Three-layer approach: expert layer, AI workflows, and infrastructure
  - Content-as-a-service model with expert human review and intervention
  - AI coding agent builds workflows as code, with a runtime layer for execution
  - Custom workflows for each client, pulling from various "artifacts" (company info, context)
  - Using multiple AI models (e.g., Claude, Perplexity) for different tasks within workflows
  - Processing 1-2M workflow runs/activities per month

### Technical Infrastructure and Challenges

  - Using Render for hosting, Temporal for runtime, and Langchain for traceability
  - Built custom failovers and fallbacks for quick model swapping and testing
  - Interested in fine-tuning models to leverage accumulated data and feedback
  - Currently not compute cost-sensitive due to high-value outputs for clients ($10-60k/month)
  - Mostly batch processing, with some real-time features (e.g., AI editor in Canva-like experience)

### Future Directions and Potential Collaborations

  - Exploring parallel processing of multiple workflow versions with a "judge" system
  - Interest in Nebius's research on agentic systems and efficient use of open-source models
  - Potential for collaboration as GrowthX.ai moves towards optimizing and scaling their platform
