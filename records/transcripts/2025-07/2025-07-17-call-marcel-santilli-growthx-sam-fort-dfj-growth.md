## Key Topics

### GrowthX Background and Evolution

  - Marcel Santilli's career progression: IBM → HP → HashiCorp/Scale.ai → DeepGram
  - Developed content marketing strategies focused on becoming a "publication first"
  - Transitioned from workshops to full-service offering, then to building an AI-powered platform
  - Bootstrapped to $1.5M revenue while still CMO at DeepGram
  - Raised Series A funding; now projecting $12-15M revenue by year-end

### GrowthX Platform Overview

  - Three-layer approach: service, workflow engine, and expert layer
  - Workflow engine: AI-powered, code-based pipelines for complex content processes
  - Expert layer: Human-in-the-loop system for quality control and continuous improvement
  - Key features: Context engineering, fact-checking, illustration generation
  - Focused on content marketing, but applicable to other domains (e.g., hiring)

### Market Positioning and Competitive Landscape

  - Targeting companies struggling with traditional agency models or in-house content creation
  - Often replacing or supplementing existing agencies
  - Minimal direct competition in AI-powered content creation space
  - Differentiated from general-purpose AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT) by domain expertise and quality control

### Team and Funding

  - 70-person team, including 30 in delivery functions
  - Notable hires from Stripe, Airbnb, HashiCorp, Coinbase, and Grammarly
  - Preparing for Series B funding round in September
  - Seeking strategic partners who understand the business model
