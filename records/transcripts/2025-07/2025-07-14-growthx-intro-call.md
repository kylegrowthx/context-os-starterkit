---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Approach to SEO and GEO

  - Focus on creating high-quality, engaging content that answers audience questions
  - Use of AI workflows to scale content production while maintaining quality
  - Emphasis on understanding business deeply, including personas and pain points
  - Tracking GEO performance using tools like Scrunch to monitor prompt presence
  - Balance between optimizing for search engines and creating valuable content for users

### Content Creation Process

  - 6-8 week strategy sprint to develop deep insights and AI-ready artifacts
  - Creation of detailed personas, writing guidelines, and content structures
  - Use of AI workflows for efficient content generation, with human editing and oversight
  - Focus on editorial content initially, expanding to programmatic and other formats
  - Team includes experienced editors and strategists from major publications and brands

### Measuring Impact and Conversions

  - Challenges in directly attributing GEO impact to conversions
  - Suggestions for tracking:
      - Direct link clicks from GEO sources
      - Monitoring branded search and direct traffic increases
      - Considering incrementality measurements
  - GrowthX doesn't typically set up attribution systems but can advise on approach

### Pricing and Engagement Structure

  - Strategy sprint: $20-30K for 6-8 weeks
  - Ongoing growth execution: $9-25K/month (average $12-15K)
  - No obligation to continue after strategy sprint
  - Pricing varies based on complexity and specific client needs
