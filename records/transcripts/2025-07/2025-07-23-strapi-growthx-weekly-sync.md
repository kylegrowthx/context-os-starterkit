This is a weekly standing sync between the Strapi product and marketing teams (Paul Bratslavsky, Theodore Onyejiaku, Golzar Yaghoubpour) and the GrowthX delivery team (Vivek Shankar, Mara Leighton) to align on content strategy, performance metrics, and integration updates. The relationship is an established ongoing delivery engagement with regular feedback cycles and asset reviews in Airtable.
