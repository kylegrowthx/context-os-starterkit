## Key Topics

### Role Overview and Requirements

  - Blend content expertise with AI/automation tooling
  - Articulate quality standards and why content meets/misses the mark
  - Identify gaps and propose solutions (guidelines, tools, workflows)
  - Translate editorial insights into scalable processes
  - Understand technical concepts to effectively operate content tools
  - Adapt to rapidly evolving startup environment

### Candidate Experience and Skills

  - Diverse background in marketing, content, and project management
  - Experience simplifying complex topics (e.g., blockchain) for broader audiences
  - Strong interest in AI and new technologies
  - Project management skills, including breaking down tasks and contingency planning
  - Branding expertise and understanding of content's role in brand voice
  - Client-facing experience in advertising and blockchain industries

### Startup Environment and Expectations

  - Roles evolve rapidly; need for adaptability and quick learning
  - Balance between execution and strategic thinking required
  - Constant product development alongside client work delivery
  - Emphasis on proactive problem-solving and suggesting improvements
  - Open communication about workflow issues crucial for product enhancement

### Client Interaction and Communication

  - Experience interfacing with external stakeholders and clients
  - Ability to extract detailed information and clarify vague requests
  - Skill in presenting solutions rather than problems to clients
  - Comfort with giving and receiving feedback
