## Summary

GrowthX and Innerwell reviewed website architecture changes to separate condition hub pages from blog content, with Dave Capone walking through a new category/landing page design that emphasizes EEAT signals through authorship, medical review callouts, table of contents, and CTAs. Innerwell's SMS campaign targeting next-day availability across 10 states achieved one of their best-performing days of the year, prompting discussion of scaling via exit pop-ups. ## Context

Innerwell is a telehealth platform offering online and in-person mental health care, with operations concentrated in telehealth across most states. GrowthX is managing their SEO strategy and content development, with a focus on building out a condition hub (distinct from the blog) to serve both search engines and LLM crawlers. This weekly sync is part of ongoing delivery on a content marketing engagement addressing Innerwell's need to improve discoverability across both traditional search and AI-driven tools.
