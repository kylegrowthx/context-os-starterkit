  - Feedback provided on new blog template

### Site Architecture and URL Structure

  - Discussion on separating condition hub content from blog
  - URL remapping in progress, with 301 redirects list to be provided by GrowthX
  - Condition hub pages to be treated as medical web pages, not blog articles

### Content Production

  - Editorial content on track for monthly goals
  - PSEO pages slightly delayed due to automation build time
