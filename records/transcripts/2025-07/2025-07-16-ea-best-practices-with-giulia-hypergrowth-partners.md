## Key Topics

### Executive Alignment and Expectation Setting

  - Quarterly "wish list" review with executives to align on priorities and expectations
  - Establish communication schedules and preferences (e.g., async vs. sync)
  - Set calendar management rules (e.g., buffer times, meeting types per time of day)
  - Regular feedback loops to refine processes and ensure value delivery

### Calendar and Meeting Management

  - Color-coding and emoji system for quick visual understanding of schedule
  - Strategic scheduling based on executive preferences (e.g., back-to-back vs. buffers)
  - Travel buffer indication using emojis (walking, car, etc.)
  - VIP call handling: personalized options vs. calendar link sharing
  - Weekly schedule review on Fridays for the upcoming week

### Meeting Preparation and Follow-up

  - Comprehensive pre-meeting research (LinkedIn, company info, past interactions)
  - AI-assisted meeting summaries (Granola) combined with manual review for nuance
  - Immediate action item extraction and follow-up email drafting post-meeting
  - Use of CRM (ROMA Research) to track all interactions and maintain context

### Communication Management

  - Selective Slack access based on executive preference and privacy concerns
  - Daily digest of important messages and threads for executive review
  - Encourage team to tag EA in addition to executive for better visibility
  - Establish EA as the primary point of contact for executive matters

### Workflow Documentation and Iteration

  - Document all workflows, including purpose, process, and expected outcomes
  - Regular testing and refinement of workflows against initial hypotheses
  - Maintain flexibility to adapt systems as needs and technologies change
