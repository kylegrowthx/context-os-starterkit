## Key Topics

### The Future of E-commerce

  - Prediction: OpenAI to enable purchases directly in ChatGPT within 6-9 months
  - Shift from traditional e-commerce websites to AI-driven interfaces
  - Potential end of traditional e-commerce front-end
  - Challenges for current e-commerce stack and startups

### Voice Technology and Interaction

  - Growing conviction in voice technology (e.g., 11 Labs, Whisper)
  - Possibility of voice replacing keyboards for future generations
  - Potential impact on literacy if voice becomes primary interaction method

### AI's Impact on Consumer Behavior

  - Easier for consumers to write complaints and legal letters using AI
  - Shift in power dynamics between consumers and businesses
  - Increased willingness to address issues due to reduced effort

### Adaptation of Marketing Companies

  - Need for companies like Gorgias, Klaviyo to adapt to AI-native interactions
  - Potential for creating AI-native support platforms
  - Challenges in adapting for larger, established companies

### Changes in Marketing Funnels and Strategies

  - Traditional marketing funnels may become obsolete
  - Loss of retargeting and nurturing opportunities in AI-native world
  - Need for marketers to find new ways to influence and stay close to customers

### Data Sharing and Privacy

  - Unlikelihood of AI companies sharing anonymized customer interaction data
  - Comparison to Google's "original sin" of sharing too much data
  - Expectation of a "black box" approach similar to current social media platforms
