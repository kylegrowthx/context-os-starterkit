## Context

This is a Series B investor pitch from GrowthX's founder Marcel Santilli (who previously held leadership roles at IBM, HashiCorp, Scale AI, and DeepGram) to the Sapphire Ventures investment team based in San Francisco/Menlo Park. Sapphire is a multi-stage venture firm with ~$10B under management that invests across Series B to pre-IPO, with a particular focus on marketing technology and AI. The meeting was rescheduled and happened via Zoom with Sapphire participants joining from both a San Francisco office (Jai Das) and their Menlo Park headquarters (Adam Liu and others). GrowthX started as Marcel's consulting/workshop practice while employed elsewhere, grew to $1.5M ARR with 6 customers, then productized with co-founder Daniel (formerly at IFTTT) to create a scalable AI content platform. At the time of this call, GrowthX had recently raised $12M Series A at $80M post-money valuation from Madrona, achieved $10M+ ARR, and was preparing to raise Series B by September 2023.
