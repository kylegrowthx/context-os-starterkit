## Context

This was GrowthX's internal strategy sprint standup on July 3rd, 2025, led by Marcel Santilli with the delivery and operations team. The meeting tracked progress across eight client accounts in various stages: some in week one (Metronome, Monograph), others in active execution phases (Engine, DogmanCode, Town), and mature accounts reaching expansion decisions (Interwell, Biologica, Hypergrowth). The broader context is that GrowthX is growing but resource-constrained—new designer Katya just starting Monday, and the team is wrestling with common bottlenecks: image creation workflows, blog template standardization, and the need to build Gong call transcript analysis capabilities that multiple clients are independently requesting.
