## Key Topics

### Sunbit Business Overview

  - B2B2C company offering Buy Now, Pay Later solutions for brick-and-mortar services
  - Focus on dental, auto repair, and other service industries
  - Recently integrated with Stripe, expanding into new verticals
  - Offers co-branded credit cards with retailers to enhance loyalty programs

### Content Strategy

  - GrowthX creating top-of-funnel educational content (e.g., cost of dental procedures)
  - B2B content focuses on adjacencies (e.g., hiring dental assistants) to attract industry professionals
  - New AI-focused microsite (summit.ai) in development for investor-oriented content

### Go-to-Market Approach

  - Three main strategies: enterprise sales (DSOs), SMB/micro-business outreach, and consumer-facing branding
  - Strong community engagement through "Sunbeast" program with gamification elements
  - Visible branding in partner locations and employee training programs

### Content Production and Publishing

  - Ramping up to 25 articles/week, currently at 10-15
  - Publishing process to be automated with WordPress connector (in development)
  - Content OS being used to track article status and streamline workflow

### Organic Traffic Challenges

  - Overall organic clicks declining despite growing impressions ("alligator chart" phenomenon)
  - AI-driven search results and LLM usage impacting traditional search patterns
  - Knowledge Center content showing promising early growth in impressions and clicks
