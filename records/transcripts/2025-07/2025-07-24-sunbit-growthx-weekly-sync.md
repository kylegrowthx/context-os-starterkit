## Context

Sunbit is a B2B2C Buy Now, Pay Later fintech serving small businesses and merchants in brick-and-mortar service industries—primarily dental, auto repair, and emerging verticals like veterinary and optometry. The company operates three go-to-market channels: enterprise sales to Dental Service Organizations (DSOs, PE-backed roll-ups now accounting for 15-20% of the market), SMB/micro-business outreach (1-15 employee shops), and consumer-facing brand building. Jason Beltran, VP of Brand and Comms, leads brand strategy and was Sunbit's first marketing hire. Angel Kemper, who recently joined, supports publishing and process automation. GrowthX has been engaged for about four months on a content strategy pivot—from broad B2B marketing content to targeted top-of-funnel educational content (how much does a procedure cost?) paired with adjacent B2B content (how to hire dental assistants) to drive organic awareness and qualified traffic to Sunbit's merchant network and consumer directory.

## Key Topics

### Sunbit Business Overview

- B2B2C company offering Buy Now, Pay Later solutions for brick-and-mortar services
- Primary verticals: dental (13,000 merchants in network, 200,000 total offices in US), auto repair, and expanding to veterinary, optometry, urgent care
- Recently integrated Stripe, opening new BNPL access channels
- Offers no-fee credit card upsell and co-branded retailer credit cards to enhance loyalty programs
- Forecasts DSOs (private equity consolidators) will reach 50% of dental offices within 3-5 years (currently 15-20%)

### Go-to-Market Approach

- **Enterprise sales** to DSOs (top 25 DSOs targeted; one partnership launching with 700 locations)
- **SMB micro-business outreach** (1-15 employee independent shops, many founders nearing retirement)
- **Consumer-facing branding** to build brand awareness and trust at point-of-sale
- **Sunbeast community program**: gamified loyalty for certified merchants with 1,000 weekly live webinar attendees and 100,000 app downloads; visible in-store branding and employee training (LMS video courses + in-person training for DSOs)

### Content Strategy

- GrowthX creating top-of-funnel B2C educational content on service costs (dental veneers, orthodontics, auto repair diagnostics) rather than financing keywords—avoids legal scrutiny and targets broader search audience
- B2B adjacency content targeting decision-makers in dentistry without mentioning financing (e.g., "how to hire dental assistants," retention tactics to fight 20%+ annual turnover)
- New AI-focused investor microsite (summit.ai) for thought leadership on machine learning and AI applications
- All content constrained by finance regulation: Sunbit's legal team and bank scrutinize financing-related claims

### Content Production and Publishing

- Scaling from 10-15 articles/week to 25 articles/week (B2C exclusively)
- Publishing bottleneck: GrowthX handling all legal submissions; Angel Kemper recently joined to reduce load
- Content OS used as source of truth for workflow: "ready to review" → legal approval → "ready to publish"
- WordPress automation connector in development (timeline: next two weeks) for bulk publishing
- Legal review optimized: Chloe Long being added directly to article folder for bulk submission vs. per-article Monday.com handoffs

### Organic Traffic Dynamics

- Overall organic clicks declining despite growing impressions—"alligator chart" phenomenon observed across all GrowthX clients
- Drivers: AI overviews pushing down organic results, LLM usage replacing Google searches
- **Bright spot**: Knowledge Center content showing strong early signals with growing impressions and emerging click growth
- Strong domain authority from 13,000+ merchant backlinks driving immediate indexing and early impressions on published content
