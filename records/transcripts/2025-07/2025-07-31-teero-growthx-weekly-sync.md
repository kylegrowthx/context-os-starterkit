## Key Topics

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Recommendations

Jakub presented six recommendations prioritized by impact and implementation difficulty:

**High Priority (Quick Wins):**
- **Blog sidebar CTA:** Add to sidebar with examples; immediately visible to all traffic, showing proven conversion lift
- **Inline CTAs and banner ads:** Test variations aligned to personas (office managers, hygienists); expand as insurance content launches

**Medium Priority (Moderate Effort):**
- **Table of contents:** Improves page skimmability, helps SEO/AI crawling, increases user engagement with content
- **Slide-ins/pop-ups:** Implement via Framer's built-in conversion tools; slide-ins preferred over pure pop-ups based on performance
- **Reuse existing ad creatives:** Leverage existing ad library (v1, v2, v3 variants) to extend reach on blog traffic for free impressions

**Lower Priority (Higher Dependencies):**
- **FAQs:** Add to backend CMS with schema markup to support long-tail keywords and LLM visibility; requires content population for legacy articles
- **Authority hub pages:** Create "fitness hub"-style page aggregating related content; improves SEO authority, interlinking, and provides foundation for future topic expansion (hiring, insurance)

### Content Performance and SEO

**Lead Generation Momentum:**
- 5 blog-driven leads this month, up from typical 3-4 baseline
- Leads distributed across diverse blog topics, indicating broad content resonance (not single-blog dependent)
- Consistent month-over-month growth trajectory

**SERP Feature Performance:**
- 2000+ keywords ranking in "People Also Ask" — top-tier performance vs. competitor benchmarks
- Also appearing in AI overview, image pack, and "Things to know" features
- Strong indicator of schema optimization and FAQ readiness
- 6-month organic traffic trending consistently upward with no major drops

### Reporting and Metrics

**Dashboard Improvements:**
- New reporting key added explaining metrics categories (SERP features, previous rankings, metric definitions)
- Clarifies terms like "previous detected position" and dot notation for newly-appearing features
- Individual dashboards still in development but coming soon

**SERP Feature Definitions:**
- **People Also Ask (PAA):** Conversational queries appearing in Google's featured box
- **AI Overview:** AI-generated summary of top results for query
- **Image Pack:** Gallery of relevant images for visual search queries
- **Things to Know:** Structured facts and definitions appearing below main results
