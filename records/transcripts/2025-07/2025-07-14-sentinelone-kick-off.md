## Key Topics

### Current Website and Content Status

  - WordPress site with \~613 Cybersecurity 101 pages and \~800 blog posts
  - Major content push started Oct 2022 for Cybersecurity 101 section
  - Product pages need refresh to align with new go-to-market strategy
  - Blog now owned by PR team, focusing on quality over quantity
  - Separate SEO agency working on Cybersecurity 101 content

### Key Business Priorities

  - Driving pipeline generation through website
  - Optimizing for visibility in AI/LLM search results
  - Moving from promotional tool to main business driver
  - Balancing innovation (e.g. AI) with security considerations

### Target Audience and Differentiation

  - Dual focus: CISOs and Security Practitioners
  - CISOs: Efficiency, CFO concerns, IT impact
  - Practitioners: Product details, multiple levels of depth
  - Key differentiators: Protection layer, AI innovation, ease of partnership

### Lead Generation and Tracking

  - High-value forms (demos, product tours, chat) are direct MQLs
  - Other assets contribute to scoring model in Marketo
  - Recently relaunched resources page, gathering initial performance data
  - Working on improving website funnel tracking and analysis

### Vulnerability Database Project

  - High-priority project to create AI-generated vulnerability database
  - Similar to recent launch by competitor (Wiz)
  - Opportunity for GrowthX to own end-to-end development
  - Preference for subdirectory implementation, separate from main CMS

### Ongoing Website Initiatives

  - Full website redesign/rebrand in progress (\~3+ months timeline)
  - New information architecture and solution areas being developed
  - Moving from WordPress to headless CMS (Content Stack)
