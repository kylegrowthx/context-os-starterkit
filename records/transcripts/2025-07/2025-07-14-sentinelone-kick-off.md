## Summary

GrowthX kicked off a strategic engagement with SentinelOne to drive pipeline generation and AI/LLM visibility through content and SEO. Marcel Santilli led the discovery call with Drew Hoffman (VP Brand Marketing) and Mahati Rapol (Web Marketing & SEO) to understand SentinelOne's website challenges: a WordPress site with 613 Cybersecurity 101 pages and ~800 blog posts now fragmented across three teams (Product, PR, SEO agency). Key opportunities emerged including creating a vulnerability database subdirectory to capture organic demand and optimizing for visibility in AI search. A website rebrand and redesign is planned for 3+ months out, and the team agreed to coordinate closely across Slack, Notion, and tech deep dives with SentinelOne product experts.

---

## Context

SentinelOne is a leading cybersecurity company that recently restructured website ownership from a separate department to the brand team (led by Drew and Mahati). The company's massive content footprint (613+ specialized pages, 800+ blog posts) is now split across teams with different priorities: Product manages product pages, PR owns the blog (focusing on quality over quantity), and an external SEO agency handles the Cybersecurity 101 section. The engagement begins with discovery to align GrowthX on business drivers (pipeline generation, AI visibility), technical requirements, and stakeholders before designing a content strategy that bridges these fragmented efforts and supports SentinelOne's go-to-market transformation.
