## Context

This is an internal GrowthX team standup for the delivery organization's strategy sprint. The meeting brings together delivery leads, project managers, engineering leads, and editorial staff to synchronize on nine active B2B SaaS client engagements in various stages (kickoff, early content production, scaling, whitepaper strategy). The context is rapid scaling: the team is working to move multiple clients from planning into production simultaneously while managing internal tool development (Atlas/Canva-like interface for content creation) and competing priorities around content quality vs. speed.
