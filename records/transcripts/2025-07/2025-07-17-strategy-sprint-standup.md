## Summary

GrowthX delivered a weekly strategy sprint standup covering multiple active client engagements across Sentinel-1, Okta, Diligent, Metronome, Monograph, Airbyte, Interwell, Enjin, and Ramp. The team highlighted key progress: Airbyte published 100 articles with SEO testing now active, Enjin showed early positive signals with a published calibration piece already gaining impressions and clicks, and Interwell moved forward with URL remapping strategy ready for implementation. The team identified quality improvements needed for Airbyte refreshes (FAQ placement, linking) and discussed account health concerns with Ramp following AI answer disruptions to their highest-intent pages. Near-term priorities include closing new business (Udemy contract awaiting counter-signature, Superhuman and Galileo in pipeline) and filling remaining July capacity with strategy sprints for existing customers.
