## Context

Strapi is a headless CMS and open-source content management platform. GrowthX has been providing B2B content marketing services to Strapi since late 2024. This is a weekly sync between the Strapi product and marketing team (led by Victor Coisne) and the GrowthX team, which manages Strapi's technical content strategy and generation. The account recently transitioned to a new GrowthX team — Vivek Shankar took over from Jason (who moved to different internal activities), and Oluwatimilehin Ademosu joined as the primary content manager. Strapi tracks conversions through HubSpot using a linear attribution model: sessions, new contacts, and attributed revenue tied to GrowthX-created content. The relationship is collaborative, with Strapi sharing data (ICP, sales calls, community discussions) to help GrowthX create more targeted content.

### Looker Dashboard and KPI Tracking

- **Current dashboard:** Last week before Looker refresh. New dashboard next week will separate LLM referral as a distinct traffic channel.

- **Why it matters:** Engagement time is crucial for LLM visibility — content that keeps users on the page ranks better in AI overviews.

### LLM Visibility and AI Overviews

**Strategic insight:** Basic SEO principles deliver ~80% of LLM visibility gains (brand strength, authority, share of voice, engagement time, impressions). The remaining 20% involves advanced techniques (vector search, chunking), which GrowthX is researching with Scrunch.

**Scrunch integration:** GrowthX working with Scrunch in the background to track where Strapi's products show up in LLM queries, what competitors rank for, and what content can be created to capture those searches.

**Why impressions matter:** Google's AI overviews in search results pull content to display, making impression count more important than traditional CTR.

### Content Production Best Practices

- **Volume vs. depth trade-off:** In-depth articles tailored to Strapi's ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) perform best. Balance between speed and quality is key.
- **Data dependency:** More signals from Strapi (ICP definitions, sales call insights, customer success feedback, community discussions) enable more targeted content.
- **Strapi's new tools:** Strapi is building product marketing agents using "Octave" to manage ICP data and improve marketing efficiency.

### Workflow Automation

**Strapi exploring:** Building internal product marketing agents with Octave for better data management and ICP targeting.
