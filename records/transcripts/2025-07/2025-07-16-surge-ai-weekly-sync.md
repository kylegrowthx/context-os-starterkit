## Key Topics

### Brand Identity and Execution

  - Current bottleneck: Unclear brand guidelines slowing execution
  - Need to define essence of Surge AI and how it translates to visual language, style, tone, and voice
  - Potential direction: Anti-hype, minimalist aesthetic focusing on typography and white space
  - Aim: Create consistent language for faster, more predictable execution

### Technical Workshops and On-site Presence

  - High priority: Organize technical workshops, potentially on-site at customer locations
  - Benefits: Build distribution, create repurposable content, enhance trust
  - Example: Recent workshop post generated 600 comments, 250 registrants
  - Goal: Increase on-site presence to facilitate valuable hallway conversations and relationship-building

### Content Creation and Distribution

  - Blog posts in progress; need to address platform constraints (e.g., Webflow vs. custom solution)
  - Distribution strategy: Leverage existing networks, use tools like Megaphone for amplification
  - Expectation setting: Building reach takes time; consistent quality posting yields compounding results

### Prioritization of Initiatives

  - Benchmarks moved up in priority; open datasets deprioritized
  - Video podcast series lowered in priority due to resource constraints
  - Meetups identified as lower effort, ecosystem-building opportunities

### Collaboration with Meta

  - Upcoming release of reinforcement learning environment
  - Opportunity for Surge AI to write a blog post highlighting early involvement
  - Potential for tech talk or workshop featuring Meta researcher
