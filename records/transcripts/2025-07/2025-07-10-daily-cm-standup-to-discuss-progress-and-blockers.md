## Summary

The GrowthX content team's daily standup covered progress on two main client engagements (Aftershoot and an internal prophecy content series) and surfaced critical challenges with Atlas AI. This creates operational friction: the team must balance using Atlas (to generate improvement data) with delivering quality output to clients on deadline.
