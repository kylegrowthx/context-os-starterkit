## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Offering and Approach

  - Hybrid service-platform model combining AI workflows with human expertise
  - Focus on scaling content engines, primarily editorial/long-form content
  - End-to-end support: strategy, execution, imagery, staging, and publishing
  - Team structure: Chief Content Officer (ex-TechCrunch), Managing Directors (20+ years SEO/growth experience), Managing Editors

### Content Creation Process

  - 8-week strategy sprint to develop artifacts and calibrate approach
  - Deep research into client's business, personas, and content guidelines
  - Use of AI workflows for research, outlining, drafting, and fact-checking
  - Human-in-the-loop at every stage for quality control and refinement
  - Emphasis on maintaining high editorial standards and factual accuracy

### AI Platform Capabilities

  - Custom-built platform for workflow management and content generation
  - Workflow engine with coding agents for quick adaptation
  - Pipeline creation for different content plays (e.g., programmatic, editorial)
  - Inline AI-assisted content refinement tools
  - Rigorous fact-checking process using passage splitting and confidence scoring

### One New Zealand's Context

  - Telco serving all segments in a small market (5 million people, \~500k businesses)
  - More mature in consumer/mass market, less so in SMB and enterprise B2B
  - Challenge: Hyper-personalization across diverse B2B verticals
  - Current focus: Retaining existing customers and mass market acquisition
  - Opportunity: Improve B2B marketing efficiency and owned/earned channel performance
