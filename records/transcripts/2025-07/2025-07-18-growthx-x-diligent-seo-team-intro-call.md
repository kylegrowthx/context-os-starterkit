## Key Topics

### Current SEO Challenges

  - Adapting to AI shifts in search: Weekly SEO roundups help stay informed, but communicating success metrics to leadership is difficult
  - Lack of visibility in AI search conversation data
  - Competitive analysis tools struggling to keep up with AI-first view
  - CTA functionality issues after Sanity migration
  - Scaling SEO best practices (keyword cannibalization, duplicate content) across multiple teams
  - Content inventory management and inter-team communication on existing/planned content

### Content Strategy and Optimization

  - 40+ significant content refreshes in H1 2023
  - Using internal AI tool to surface related content
  - Exploring AI for scaling content inventory management
  - John's team optimizing gated landing pages with AI-generated templates, boosting conversion rates

### Technical SEO and Website Limitations

  - Sanity CMS restricting ability to add alt text, images, and other elements
  - Schema implementation pending, crucial for LLMs
  - Backend limitations in HTML editing and content flexibility

### Experimentation and CTA Optimization

  - CTA optimization at scale has been on "wish list" but deprioritized
  - Using map of high-performing gated content for CTA placement
  - Opportunity to improve traffic to optimized landing pages through internal CTAs

### Cross-Team Collaboration

  - SEO often not incorporated at project outsets, leading to retroactive fixes
  - Need for better communication on SEO impact of technical/security decisions
  - Web team inherited Sanity instance, working through limitations
