  - Published 100+ articles in 3 months, now producing 20-25 pieces weekly

  - Monitoring 88 prompts for Abnormal mentions in AI responses

  - Highly automated process with human oversight at key steps
  - Steps include: assignment brief, research, outline creation, writing, fact-checking, internal linking

### Measuring Impact and ROI

  - Goal: Use content marketing metrics to inform sales team expansion and territory planning
