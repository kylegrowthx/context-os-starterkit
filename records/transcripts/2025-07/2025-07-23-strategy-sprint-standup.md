## Context

This is an internal GrowthX standup where Marcel Santilli (CEO) led the team through account status updates across the Strategy Sprint portfolio. The meeting served as a weekly synchronization point for the delivery team to flag blockers, celebrate progress, and align on resource allocation. The broader strategic context involves GrowthX shifting its reporting model from content volume metrics (articles published) to outcome metrics (clicks, impressions, search performance), and establishing standardized processes for client touchpoints and feedback loops across accounts.

Several key decisions emerged: Augment Code requires immediate SoW signature to determine whether GrowthX continues all four content lanes or reallocates resources (the account is currently losing money at $20k investment vs. $15k monthly contract). For Airbyte, the team acknowledged that refreshed articles can't be re-run through the refresh pipeline without hallucination, requiring manual adjustments. The team also agreed to add a standardized "week four or five" touchpoint for Marcel on accounts where he's less involved, to maintain relationship continuity before major alignment meetings.
