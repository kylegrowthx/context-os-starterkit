## Overview

- Keyword research is a creative, critical thinking process that cannot be fully automated
- Prioritize understanding client goals, target audience, and pain points before diving into keyword research
- Utilize AI tools to expedite research, but always verify search intent and relevance manually
- Focus on delivering high-quality, relevant assignments rather than a large quantity of potentially misaligned content

---

## Key Topics

### Current GrowthX Keyword Research Practice

  - Relies heavily on keyword gap analysis with competitors
  - Extracts relevant URLs and generates assignments using Atlas
  - Limitations include over-reliance on competitors, potential keyword intent mismatch, and missed opportunities

### Recommended Keyword Research Approach

  - Start with understanding client goals and target audience
  - Dig deep into audience pain points and daily challenges
  - Use AI (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude) to generate seed keywords and pain point lists
  - Leverage SEMrush and Google to find actual keywords and verify search intent
  - Be creative and think outside the box to uncover untapped opportunities

### Tools and Techniques

  - SEMrush Keyword Magic Tool for exploring keyword variations
  - Google SERP analysis for verifying search intent
  - AI-assisted research to expedite the process and gain deeper insights
  - Utilize client resources (e.g., sales call transcripts, white papers) for keyword ideas

### Search Intent and Keyword Selection

  - Prioritize informational and commercial intent for blog content
  - Consider zero-volume keywords for potential category ownership
  - Balance between bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) and brand awareness content
  - Critically evaluate keywords against client goals and audience needs

### Client Relationship and Trust Building

  - Demonstrate understanding of client goals and audience through assignment selection
  - Use insights from client conversations to inform keyword choices
  - Build trust to gain more freedom in strategy implementation

---

## Action Items

- Implement a more comprehensive keyword research process for upcoming client projects
- Utilize AI tools to expedite research while maintaining critical thinking and creativity
- Focus on delivering high-quality, relevant assignments that align with client goals and audience needs
- Regularly revisit client goals and priorities to ensure keyword strategy remains aligned
