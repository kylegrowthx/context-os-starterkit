### Feedback on Retail Persona Documents

  - Rachael appreciated the level of detail but suggested using more industry-specific language (e.g. "dev teams" instead of "engineering teams")
  - She emphasized the importance of vertical-specific context in setting the tone for sales conversations

### Okta's AI Initiative for Sales

  - Okta is evaluating tools like Common Room to improve inbound lead response times
  - Current SLA is 24 hours, but actual response time averages 3 days
  - Rachael proposes automating responses for certain lead categories (e.g. existing customers, disqualified leads)
  - Goal is to free up SDRs to focus on higher-value activities like understanding buyer needs and deal strategy

### growthx.ai's Approach to Persona/Company Briefs

  - Creating detailed artifacts for company context, audience personas, and specific verticals
  - Aim to combine these into highly tailored briefs for individual prospects
  - George is working on refining the process for real-time brief creation based on incoming prospect data

### Evolving Role of SDRs

  - Rachael believes SDRs should move beyond just booking meetings to taking on early-stage opportunity qualification
  - This includes understanding buying processes, stakeholders, and deal strategy before passing to AEs
