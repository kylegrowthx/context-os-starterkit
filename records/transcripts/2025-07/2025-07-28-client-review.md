## Overview

- Developed a comprehensive client scoring system (1-5 scale) across 18 criteria to evaluate fit and potential
- Highest scoring clients (55+) tend to be more mature companies with adequate resources and realistic expectations
- Lower scoring clients (\<40) often have mismatched expectations, limited budgets, or overly complex approval processes

---

## Key Topics

### Client Scoring Framework

  - Created 18-point scoring system on 1-5 scale covering areas like:
      - Content complexity (1=expert/credentialed, 5=general)
      - Approval layers (1=complex, 5=trusted)
      - Revenue/funding stage (1=early, 5=mature)
      - Publishing control (1=gated, 5=full access)
  - Total scores range from \~35-60, with higher scores indicating better client fit
  - Framework helps identify potential issues early (e.g. mismatched expectations, limited budgets)

### Notable Client Assessments

  - Engine: High score (58) - trusting relationship, publishing 23 pieces in 7 weeks
  - Aftershoot: Low score (37) - complex approval process with 3+ reviewers, unrealistic expectations
  - Ambit: Challenging but worthwhile (37) - complex content, limited budget, but good collaborators
  - Webflow: Patient builders (49) - slow-moving but not pressuring for immediate results

### Client Relationship Insights

  - Clients scoring 45+ tend to be better long-term fits
  - Sub-35 scores often correlate with higher churn risk
  - Post-strategy sprint scores of 45-50+ indicate good potential for lasting relationship
  - Even challenging clients can be worthwhile if collaborative and aligned on value (e.g. Ambit)

### Competitor Benchmarking

  - Graphite (competitor) quoted $500k/year for strategy only, no content delivery
  - GrowthX delivering higher volume and quality at \~1/3 the price of top competitors

### Team Capacity and Pricing

  - Need to better align client expectations with team capacity, especially for lower-priced engagements
  - Consider raising prices or adjusting scope for clients requiring high volume + high quality
