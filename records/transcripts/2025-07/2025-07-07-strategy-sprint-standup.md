## Context

This was GrowthX's internal strategy sprint standup on July 7, 2025, with the core leadership and delivery team reviewing five active client engagements mid-week. The meeting was led by Marcel Santilli (CEO) and Andi Bailey (Operations), with representatives from content strategy (Jo Kaminska, Sydney Arin Go, Aida Knezevic), technical/programmatic SEO (Jakub Rudnik), and operations (Bridget McGillivray). The meeting occurred during what GrowthX calls the "strategy sprint" engagement model—a structured 8-10 week client onboarding framework that balances rapid value delivery with thorough discovery and input gathering before full strategy rollout. The team convened mid-week to course-correct on early-stage client processes and address contract amendments for an expansion deal (Airbyte upsell), while also planning leadership transitions and MD handoffs for accounts approaching strategic decision points.
