## Context

Imubit is an AI optimization platform helping industrial companies implement closed-loop AI and advanced process control. GrowthX is executing a major content marketing engagement with them, building an authority-driven SEO funnel to establish Imubit as a thought leader and pre-educate prospects before they're ready to buy. This is a strategic shift for Imubit's marketing—moving from brand awareness under departing director Rebecca Murtagh to a more structured demand-generation motion with two new director-level hires expected in about a month. Tim Yandel, who owns the GrowthX spend, is the key internal stakeholder for ROI decisions.

Mariana (GrowthX) leads the content work and is migrating their internal workflow from AIROPS to Atlas to better embed Imubit's brand positioning, writing guidelines, and personas directly into the AI prompts used to generate content. Allison Buenemann (Imubit's current marketing liaison) is the day-to-day point of contact, though a contractor named Maura will handle coordination with the design and execution side. The relationship is strong and collaborative—they're focusing on long-term brand building rather than quick lead generation, recognizing that content leads often take years to convert.
