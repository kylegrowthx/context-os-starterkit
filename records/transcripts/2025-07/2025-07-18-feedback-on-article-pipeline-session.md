## Overview

- Current AI article generation process is time-consuming and produces verbose, disjointed content compared to using Claude directly
- Need to streamline workflow steps, improve prompt engineering, and enhance AI editor capabilities
- Focus on making content creation process more enjoyable for writers while automating tedious tasks
- Plan to review and update core prompts for outlining, brief generation, and article generation workflows

---

## Key Topics

### Current Pain Points with Article Generation

  - Length/verbosity issues - articles are too long and wordy
  - Disjointed content - feels like "potpourri" instead of cohesive piece
  - Time-consuming process - takes \~1 hour in Atlas vs 15 mins with Claude
  - Over-emphasis on SEO at expense of readability/quality

### Proposed Workflow Improvements

  - Reduce number of steps from many to \~3 main stages
  - Allow more freeform assignment input vs rigid SEO structure
  - Implement agentic looping to refine drafts and meet quality metrics
  - Improve internal linking, fact-checking, and image generation steps
  - Surface prompts in studio for easier editing/iteration

### Content Strategy Considerations

  - Balance SEO optimization with human readability
  - Leverage analytics to provide authoritative content performance insights
  - Build tools to help non-expert writers create quality content on diverse topics
  - Focus on making the creative writing process more enjoyable and less tedious
