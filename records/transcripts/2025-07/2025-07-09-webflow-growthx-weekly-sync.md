## Key Topics

### Integration Page Review Process

  - New workflow using Airtable for stages and statuses
  - "Ready for client review" is the key status for Webflow team to check
  - Google Docs used for easy reviewing and note-taking
  - Options after review: "Approved" for minor changes, "Needs revision" for major changes
  - CMS publishing process to be determined once access is granted

### Editorial Content Strategy

  - Initial clusters mapped out, mixing SEO, Reddit, and Hacker News content
  - One article in Luke's voice shared for calibration
  - Plan to generate about 20 integration pages per week, adjusting based on review speed
  - Focus on balancing integration pages with editorial content creation

### Use Case Content Potential

  - Inspiration from "What you can do" sections in integration pages
  - Considering creating blog posts or guides based on these use case ideas
  - Potential for aggregating common patterns across integrations, similar to Zapier's approach
  - Need to balance SEO value with practical usefulness for users and support teams

### AI-Generated Article Feedback

  - Article on APIs and microservices in composable commerce reviewed
  - Feedback: Too flowery, buried lead, excessive use of analogies
  - Need for more direct, developer-focused content that gets to the point faster
  - Suggestion to assume some base knowledge and focus more on relevance to e-commerce

### Technical Considerations

  - Discussed server-side rendering for SEO crawlability of integration pages
  - Confirmed that Webflow CMS should handle server-side rendering appropriately
