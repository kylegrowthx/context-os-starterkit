## Key Topics

### Current Pipeline Metrics & Qualification Barriers

  - Form submissions are tracking at \~9 per week with recent spike (potentially correlated with Marcel's posts)
  - Very low conversion rate from form submissions to booked meetings due to strict qualification criteria
  - Current qualification filters primarily on budget and traffic metrics
  - Marcel wants to "open the gates" by reducing qualification barriers and manually reviewing/declining meetings afterward
  - Need to make outreach feel more human/personal rather than a typical SaaS funnel experience

### Account Targeting Strategy

  - Creating two prioritized lists in HubSpot:
      - High-priority list (\~10 accounts) for Marcel's direct outreach via personal connections
      - Broader secondary list for team outreach through more structured approaches
  - Signals for target accounts include workshop attendance, social engagement, and firmographic criteria
  - Jason will own creation of these lists by Friday
  - Marcel emphasized personal touch effectiveness (example: secured conference speaking slot through network connections within hours)

### Outreach Approach & Sequences

  - Sequences need to be reworked to sound more authentic and "Marcel-like" - shorter paragraphs, casual tone
  - Every touch should provide more value than what's being asked from prospects
  - Need to coordinate multiple touch points across accounts (Marcel working CEO/CMO level while team works director level)
  - Immediate follow-up on event registrations needed (20+ people register daily)
  - Success example: Redis opportunity secured after consistent LinkedIn follow-ups spanning months

### Events Calendar Planning

  - Multiple confirmed events:
      - Madrona dinner (August, targeting founders)
      - Workshop recording (August 1st)
      - WebStacks happy hour (Sept 3-4)
      - Understory event (Sept 17)
      - Amsterdam conference (Oct 29)
  - Planning AI Growth Summit with multiple potential partners (HockeyStick, Hyper Growth, AI Lean, Common Room)
      - Venue options being evaluated (Gallery 308 at Fort Mason, \~$150K for full day)
  - Monthly webinars/workshops as ongoing lead generation
  - Office breakfasts with 20-30 capacity (more flexibility than previous 18-person limit)
