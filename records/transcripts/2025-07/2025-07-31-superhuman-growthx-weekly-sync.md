GrowthX is the content marketing partner supporting Superhuman's go-to-market strategy. This weekly sync is a standing check-in where the Superhuman team (primarily Morgan Maurer, the marketing lead, with occasional input from Alex Rodrigues and Harmony Anderson) gives GrowthX updates on priorities and decisions, while Elvis Goren and Megan Dickey provide strategic content guidance and deliverables. Given the acquisition, Superhuman is in a period of strategic uncertainty—many internal decisions are pending leadership alignment on a major October 6 rebrand that will position the three acquired products (Superhuman, Grammarly, Coda) as a single platform. This creates both opportunity and constraint for content planning.
