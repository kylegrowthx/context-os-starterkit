## Summary

The GTM team held their first meeting to introduce members and align on priorities: Jason Gong (leading GTM), Bianca Nieves (newsletters and events, one month in), Joe Lehr (HubSpot migration from Adio), Rafid (content and ghostwriting workstreams), and Edin Abazi from Raze Growth. ## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency ($200k+/year engagements) with a strategic software bet called CheckThat, an AI visibility index. The GTM team is newly assembled under Jason Gong's leadership and includes both GrowthX staff and external contractors. The team's formation reflects a shift in GrowthX's operational maturity: historically strong at marketing (especially content creation), the company is now building its own marketing infrastructure — tracking, lead gen, publication, and outreach. This meeting marks the first time the dispersed GTM contributors (designers, developers, content creators, ops) are meeting as a cohesive team, though they've been working together on overlapping projects for weeks. The context is urgent: they're shipping The Output publication Friday and beginning HubSpot-powered demand generation while still mid-migration from an older CRM system.
