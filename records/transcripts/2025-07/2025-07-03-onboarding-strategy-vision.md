## Summary

Marcel walked Nicolas through GrowthX's evolution from AI workshops to a software-powered services agency, explaining the company's two core principles: riding the model wave for continuous improvement, and ensuring each workflow/output makes subsequent ones faster and better. He positioned GrowthX's $60k/month contract with Surge AI as validation of their expertise in managing expert networks and AI-powered knowledge work, and outlined the vision of building "GitHub for knowledge work" — reproducible, AI-ready processes that can serve as core IP for companies beyond traditional codebases.

## Context

Nicolas is a new team member joining GrowthX Labs. He previously worked at Airtable/Aerobs and had taken a year off before reconnecting with Marcel via LinkedIn, impressed by GrowthX's growth trajectory and business model. This one-on-one call serves as Nicolas's primary onboarding session, where Marcel provides a comprehensive overview of the company's founding story, strategic pivots, core technology decisions, and long-term vision.

GrowthX Labs (formerly GrowthX) pivoted from running AI workshops into a high-ticket services agency around August 2024, reaching $2M run rate with 12 customers within months. The company's breakthrough insight was that clients didn't just need AI workflows — they needed strategy, expert execution, and process architecture. Marcel founded the company after spending time at Airtable (via DeepGram CMO role), where he experienced the limitations of drag-and-drop automation tools and realized the need for reproducible, code-based approaches to knowledge work.

## Key Topics

### Company Evolution

  - Started with AI workshops, realized workflows alone didn't drive results
  - Pivoted to agency model, quickly reached \~$2M run rate with 12 customers
  - Marcel quit CMO role at DeepGram to focus on GrowthX full-time
  - Partnered with Daniel (ex-IFTTT) for complementary skills and scale experience
  - Bootstrapped until March 2023, engineering team formed in April/May

### Core Technology & Approach

  - Coding agents over drag-and-drop: More flexible, scales better with new AI models
  - Three-layer system: 1) Human expertise for inputs/strategy 2) Process architects 3) Orchestration layer
  - Focus on reusable, extensible components (e.g., scraping, research breakdown)
  - Learning engine improves workflows, human performance, and output quality over time

### Market Strategy

  - Targeting high-budget market with significant needs
  - Balancing service challenges (client expectations, company readiness) with tech development
  - Thoughtful about not overbuilding or getting ahead of immediate needs

### Future Vision

  - Building primitives for expert management (finding, assessing, assigning, evaluating)
  - Creating "GitHub repos for knowledge work" - AI-ready, reproducible processes
  - Aiming to be the core IP for companies, beyond traditional code bases

### Surge AI Partnership

  - $60k/month engagement, validating GrowthX's expertise
  - Surge AI manages 100k+ experts, $1B revenue, powers high-quality AI training data
  - Provides insights into scaling expert networks and quality assurance
