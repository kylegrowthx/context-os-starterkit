## Key Topics

### The RealReal's Current Content Approach

  - Editorial team handles storytelling across all channels (copy, social, campaigns, etc.)
  - Small team looking to optimize and scale content creation
  - Blog content currently thin and lacking substance/authority
  - Authenticity is critical; careful balance needed with AI assistance
  - Data-driven and service-oriented content seen as key opportunities

### GrowthX's AI-Assisted Content Creation

  - End-to-end support from strategy to publishing
  - Custom AI workflows with human oversight at each stage
  - Platform features: content briefs, research, fact-checking, internal linking
  - Can adapt to brand voice, style guidelines, data sources
  - Typical output of 5-15 articles per week during execution phase

### Content Strategy and Opportunities

  - Significant SEO potential identified (2.8M search volume opportunity)
  - Focus on authoritative, ownable, data-driven content
  - Blend editorial with product/shopping content (e.g. "How to shop for vintage Prada")
  - Potential to create complementary video content
  - Opportunity to refresh/improve existing thin content

### Implementation and Integration

  - 6-8 week initial strategy sprint to develop roadmap, artifacts, calibrate content
  - Execution phase aims for daily publishing cadence
  - Pricing typically $12-15k/month, can scale up for higher volume
  - Potential to integrate The RealReal's proprietary data into content creation
