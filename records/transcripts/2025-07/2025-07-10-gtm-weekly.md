## Key Topics

### New Marketing Machine Structure

  - Inspired by information businesses running courses, communities, and newsletters
  - Core focus: valuable content (workshops, courses, teardowns, guides) housed within a community
  - Community membership provides access to all information and additional benefits
  - Everything else in the marketing strategy drives people towards community membership
  - Community serves as a vehicle to surface sales leads for GrowthX services

### Content Strategy and Distribution

  - High-value content pieces (courses, workshops, webinars, written assets) will live in the community
  - Content will be gated, requiring community membership for full access
  - Sneak peeks and breakdowns of content will be used to build awareness
  - Social media efforts will focus on repackaging and distributing community content
  - The Output (publication) will serve as the official editorial surface of the AI-led growth community

### Community Platform

  - Considering Circle as the primary platform for the community
  - Key features needed: payment integration, ability to paywall content, and freemium-style access
  - Team to explore Circle's capabilities for hosting workshops and other content

### Newsletter and Offers

  - Transitioning to a newsletter under "The Output" brand
  - Newsletter will focus on curated content to help marketers and growth professionals
  - Monetizable CTAs in the newsletter will drive to community offers
  - Current offers include an upcoming workshop and tonight's event

### Team Roles and Responsibilities

  - Bianca to take ownership of event management and success
  - Darrell to focus on publication and podcast guest outreach
  - Rafid to continue managing social media content for directors
  - Jason to oversee overall strategy and alignment

### Upcoming Event (Tonight's Happy Hour)

  - Rebranding event materials to reflect AI-led growth community
  - Follow-up strategy to focus on nurturing leads and potentially driving sales conversations
  - Future events to be positioned as part of the community experience
