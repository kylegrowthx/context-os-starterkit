## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau and ResourcePro's Chris Hanson and Kari McCarthy explored opportunities for GrowthX to support ResourcePro's content marketing across their compliance vertical and broader insurance tech business, identifying state-specific regulatory content, timely compliance updates, and industry vertical expansion as key areas where AI-powered content could accelerate growth and visibility. ## Context

ResourcePro is an insurance industry software platform focused on compliance, licensing, and operations management for carriers and managing general agents (MGAs). Kyle had previously connected with Beth and Andrea from ResourcePro's leadership to introduce GrowthX's B2B content marketing services. This follow-up call continues that conversation with Chris Hanson (appears to be in a commercial or product leadership role) and Kari McCarthy, who are exploring whether GrowthX could help ResourcePro accelerate content production and visibility in the compliance space—an area where they've recently made an acquisition (ILSA) and see significant growth potential. ResourcePro currently relies on traditional digital marketing (SEO, paid search, organic social, email) but faces two distinct challenges: (1) the compliance vertical has urgent search intent from customers with pressing regulatory needs, yet the addressable audience is small and fragmented across states, and (2) ResourcePro needs to raise internal awareness of compliance as a cross-sell opportunity within their existing customer base. This is their first direct engagement discussion with GrowthX and follows an initial exploratory call where Kyle outlined the 6-8 week strategy sprint model.
