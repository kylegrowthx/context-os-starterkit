## Context

Prophecy is a data analytics platform that enables business users (non-technical analysts and leaders) to perform advanced data work without deep technical expertise. GrowthX is providing content marketing and strategy services to Prophecy, working on positioning the platform as the tool that lets non-technical professionals become analytics experts within their organizations. This is a regular weekly sync between the GrowthX content and strategy team (Ehtisham, Darrell, Saskia) and Prophecy's leadership and marketing (Matt, Cody, Ashleigh). The relationship includes deep collaboration on content direction, with GrowthX driving strategic recommendations informed by SEO performance and audience research, while Prophecy provides product insights and internal expertise for thought leadership content.
