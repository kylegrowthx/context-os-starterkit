### ROI Calculator, Modeling, and Analytics Infrastructure

  - ROI calculator model introduced with two sides: (1) time from publishing to peak average traffic (influenced by publishing volume, backlinks, authority), and (2) visits per page at peak (baseline 3/month non-branded currently, targeting 50+)

  - Model enables weekly iteration and learning; conservative projections updated as actual data comes in
  - Strategy focuses on rapid testing (throw content at wall and see what sticks) vs. perfecting upfront
  - Setting up Scrunch for query tracking and competitive intelligence
  - Building Looker dashboard to monitor indexation, rankings, traffic, and conversions
  - Newsletter baseline metrics: if 25k recipients, 2% CTR, 10-15% conversion on CTA → strong baseline to optimize upward
  - Manually submitting URLs for indexation (max 10/day to Google Search Console) while awaiting faster indexation
