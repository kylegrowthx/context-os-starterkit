## Overview

## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Mix and Goals

  - Current mix: 30% organic search, 35% direct, rest paid
  - Goal: Increase organic search to 50% of qualified traffic
  - Shifting focus from enterprise to SMB segment for growth
  - Need to build separate motions for enterprise/mid-market vs SMB

### Content Strategy Challenges

  - 1,700+ pages of content managed across different teams
  - Lack of consistent tone of voice and content architecture
  - Need to consolidate approach and align SEO/content teams
  - Opportunity to improve internal linking and user funnels

### Top Performing Content

  - Highly optimized blog posts
  - Gated assets like corporate secretary checklist, GC prep checklist
  - Governance buyer's guide
  - Content on corporate secretary role/responsibilities

### Brand Perception and Positioning

  - Perceived as premium, potentially expensive and complex
  - Reputation for expertise but can seem "stuffy" compared to competitors
  - Aiming to humanize tone while maintaining professional image
  - Highlighting ability to start small and scale with Diligent's solutions

### SEO Opportunity Analysis

  - 1.3M monthly US searches across 887 resource pages
  - Strong brand reputation beneficial for LLM citations
  - Potential to aggressively target long-tail keywords

### Technical Implementation

  - Using Sanity CMS with Okta SSO
  - Recently implemented Optimizely for A/B testing
  - Undergoing website architecture and landing page redesign
