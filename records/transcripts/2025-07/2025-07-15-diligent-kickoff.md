## Context

Diligent is a market-leading GRC platform used by thousands of companies globally for governance, risk management, and compliance workflows. The company has strong brand recognition but wants to increase organic search visibility and be perceived as more approachable (less "stuffy") while maintaining professional credibility. Abi Ravindra (VP of Demand and Operations) drives the engagement, responsible for demand generation, marketing operations, web, SEO, and content. John Habib leads content strategy across the company, working with multiple teams producing everything from blog content to thought leadership to sales enablement. This kickoff marked the official start of a GrowthX engagement to architect a cohesive content and SEO strategy — the team is in the midst of a website redesign and has already started refreshing legacy content. The meeting brought together stakeholders from both organizations to establish alignment on business drivers, content challenges, and the path forward.
