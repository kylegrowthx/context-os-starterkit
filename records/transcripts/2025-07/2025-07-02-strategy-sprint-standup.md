## Summary

GrowthX's strategy sprint team reviewed progress across eight active client projects spanning content strategy, SEO, and AI visibility. ## Context

This was an internal GrowthX strategy sprint standup, a regular tactical synchronization on deliverable progress and upcoming commitments across client accounts. The meeting assembled the core delivery team including leadership (Marcel Santilli), account leads (Andi Bailey), content strategists (Aida Knezevic, Jo Kaminska), and subject matter experts (Matthew Alves-Hill, Marisol Smith, Sadira Sittampalam, Nemanja Simic).

The sprint was focused on managing a portfolio of clients at different maturity stages: some (Augment Code, Engine, Town) in active delivery with discussions beginning around long-term renewal and account expansion, and two newly-kicking-off clients (Metronome, Monograph) requiring strategy buildout and team allocation. A secondary theme concerned workflow optimization—particularly around transforming meeting recordings and podcast transcripts into short-form content, an emerging need across multiple accounts (Hypergrowth, Airbyte, others).
