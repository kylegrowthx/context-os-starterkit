## Summary

Rick Saboia from Get Parallel reached out to GrowthX to explore AI-powered content creation services to build an inbound marketing function from scratch. Kyle Gaudreau presented GrowthX's two-phase approach: a $20K Strategy Sprint to deeply understand Get Parallel's brand, personas, and content strategy, followed by ongoing execution at $9-12K/month delivering ~5 articles per week. ## Context

Get Parallel is a B2B software company generating revenue primarily through referrals and outbound sales. Rick Saboia, Head of Revenue at Get Parallel, is tasked with building an inbound marketing function from scratch despite having no in-house marketer. The company has experimented with Reddit for alternative lead channels (generating ~5 calls/week) and is considering a broader content strategy spanning SEO, blog content, LinkedIn, and case studies. Kyle Gaudreau, VP of Customer Growth at GrowthX, was GrowthX's first customer over a year ago and has since moved into leading the sales side. Rick reached out to explore whether GrowthX could help Get Parallel build a sustainable inbound marketing engine as an alternative or complement to their current outbound motion.
