## Context

CoachList is a newly-funded two-sided marketplace for sports training, fitness instruction, and wellness services. Founded by Jeffrey Idso (a marketplace expert with experience building global platforms in travel), the platform enables independent trainers and instructors to manage their businesses via an in-house vertical CRM, while consumers browse and book services through a sophisticated system that handles family accounts, event booking, and online training. CoachList completed a pilot with 150 trainers, has secured Series A funding, and is now preparing for formal market launch. The company currently has a small team (9-person offshore development team plus founders) and has deliberately chosen not to build in-house content and marketing expertise. Instead, they're outsourcing these functions to specialized agencies and partners to stay focused on platform development, design, and operations. This is the first outreach from CoachList to GrowthX, initiated after the companies discovered their geographic proximity (both in the Bay Area) and apparent strategic alignment around content and growth.
