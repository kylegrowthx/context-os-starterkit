## Summary

GrowthX and Webstacks refined their collaborative content strategy for AI-focused web development, shifting from article quantity to outcome-based, word-count-driven production organized by web development lifecycle stages (discovery, design, content creation, SEO, implementation, launch, governance). The team identified a critical gap: foundational education on AI/ML concepts in web context that clients actually need, not deep technical training. With 9 approved topics ready for August and 9 more in generation, they committed to reviewing the chapter framework by early next week and having Webstacks provide common client questions to inform subchapter structure and content positioning.

## Context

This is a weekly sync between GrowthX's content and SEO team (Jessalynn, Rachel, Jakub) and Webstacks leadership (Jesse, Bruna). GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency working on a strategic content play with Webstacks, a web development/strategy firm. Webstacks brings deep practitioner knowledge of how clients actually engage with web projects and where AI adoption friction exists; GrowthX brings content strategy, SEO expertise, and AI-powered ideation. The meeting focused on validating the high-level content structure and identifying foundational education gaps that Webstacks' client conversations have surfaced.
