## Context

ROAR for Good is a safety and compliance software company operating in regulated industries. The company is in seed stage and relatively early, with a small team. They've achieved traction in small business sales but are pivoting to capture enterprise inbound leads and build partnerships with large business integrators who handle installations and deployments at enterprise clients. This follow-up meeting with Kyle Gaudreau from GrowthX was specifically designed to show ROAR how AI-powered content workflows could help them establish topical authority in their niche, capture high-intent keywords, and serve dual purposes: converting direct enterprise customers and enabling integrator partners with sales and education content. Kyle had previously discussed ROAR's growth challenges (enterprise motion, word-of-mouth reliance, limited content efforts), and this call dove deep into GrowthX's platform capabilities to show whether AI content at scale could be a strategic fit for their business goals.
