## Key Topics

### GrowthX Platform Overview

  - AI-powered workflows for research, content creation, and fact-checking
  - Human-in-the-loop approach for quality control and refinement
  - Integration of multiple AI tools (e.g., Perplexity Pro, Claude) for optimal results
  - Platform features include assignment creation, deep research, outline generation, and AI-powered chat for refinements

### Content Strategy Opportunities

  - Compliance tracker/hub concept similar to Homebase's state labor law pages
  - Potential to capture high search volume and develop topical authority
  - Opportunity to educate audience and convert traffic over time
  - AI content can help scale efforts and build "muscle" for ranking on high-demand keywords

### ROAR for Good's Content Needs

  - Focus on enterprise inbound leads and large business integrators
  - Maintain small business leads without additional investment
  - Potential for dual-purpose content serving both direct customers and enablement for integrators
  - White space in the industry for authoritative content on compliance and safety issues

### Engagement Model and Pricing

  - Phase 1: 6-8 week strategy sprint ($20,000)
