  - Access via Grow \> Boosts \> New Offer

  - Review and approve relevant boost requests

  - Set top 4 recommended publications
  - Potential for reciprocal recommendations
  - Strategically rotate client recommendations

### UTM Parameters and Analytics

  - Keep source as "beehiiv" or "newsletter"
  - Update campaign parameter monthly if needed
  - Utilize Google Analytics for detailed tracking
  - Leverage beehiiv's built-in analytics for client reporting
