## Context

GrowthX is a rapidly growing B2B content operations agency that pivoted from workshops to a hybrid model combining agency services ($12K/month minimum) and platform/education offerings. Founded in Sept 2022 by Marcel (former DeepGram CEO), the company grew from 0 to $7M ARR in under 7 months bootstrapped, raising a $12M Series A from Madrona in April/May 2025. They now have 80 employees across 22 countries with an office in San Francisco. The company serves CMOs and senior content leaders at B2B/B2C companies looking to adopt AI-driven content operations. Their main products include workshops, The Output (a publication), and AI-led Growth (an education platform). Chris Messina is a well-known product strategist, influencer, and founder advocate who has built credibility on Product Hunt and in the startup ecosystem. Bianca recently joined GrowthX (about 1.5 months before this call) and is reaching out to Chris about potential collaboration opportunities around GrowthX's flagship Aug 1 AI workshop focused on GEO and AI SEO strategies, which already had 300+ RSVPs at the time of the meeting.
