GrowthX is a B2B content marketing services firm with multiple active client engagements in various stages—from ongoing high-volume work (Liver) to new opportunity conversations (Weavegrid, Hyper Exponential). - **Blocker:** Without a strategy document, the team can't confidently invite prospects to dinners or clearly communicate event value, slowing pipeline velocity.
