## Key Topics

### growthx.ai Overview

  - Former in-house marketers who've built content programs
  - Focused on creating AI-driven workflows for content creation
  - Offer both a platform and services, with emphasis on end-to-end support
  - Team includes strategy layer (growth/SEO experts) and editorial focus (e.g., managing editors)

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Start with "content strategy foundation" before addressing lower-funnel tactics
  - Focus on speaking to the right people at scale, developing voice/tone
  - Create long-form content (blogs, unique experiences) to dial in strategy
  - Can expand to programmatic opportunities, email, lead magnets, etc.

### Engagement Structure

  - Begin with 8-week "strategy sprint" to develop AI-ready artifacts
  - Deep-dive into customer knowledge, personas, technical details
  - Create roadmap and establish "definition of done"
  - Transition to ongoing content production (5-15+ articles/week)

### AI-Ready Artifacts

  - Detailed briefs on company, personas, jobs-to-be-done
  - In-depth persona/archetype analysis
  - Comprehensive writing guidelines (voice, tone, structure)
  - Content rewrites with explanations to align with guidelines
  - "Content OS" in Airtable for topic/keyword clustering and assignment tracking

### Platform Capabilities

  - Integrates AI-ready artifacts into workflows
  - Multiple "pipelines" for different content use cases (e.g., refreshing, growth, article generation)
  - Leverages various APIs (e.g., SEMrush, ChatGPT) for data and insights
  - Custom workflow engine with coding agent for rapid workflow creation
  - AI shortcuts for content refinement (e.g., tone adjustment, conciseness)

### Pricing and Deliverables

  - Strategy sprint: $20k-$30k (typically closer to $20k)
  - Ongoing execution: $9k-$25k+ per month
  - Low end ($9k): \~5 articles/week
  - High end ($25k+): 15+ articles, programmatic pages, custom workflows
