## Context

Prophecy is a data transformation platform that provides an AI-powered visual interface for non-technical users to work with modern data platforms like Databricks. GrowthX is delivering a $9k/month editorial content service producing approximately 50,000 words per month (roughly five 2,000-word articles weekly) focused on SEO and content generation. The previous content team (Isma and Vivek) used a manual workflow combining Perplexity, Claude, and Gemini to create articles and was working from existing client feedback and strategies.

Saskia recently took over the account and is transitioning it to use Atlas, GrowthX's standardized content generation workflow. However, she's experiencing friction between Atlas output quality, technical topic complexity (data engineering), and client expectations set by the previous team's approach. Additionally, Prophecy recently updated their strategy, and team members (Matthew, Daryl, Eticham) are contributing new topic ideas and feedback that haven't been fully incorporated into the account's artifacts.

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- Atlas pipeline customization is possible through assignment direction field (now supporting rich text), enabling client-specific article structures and formatting rules
- CMs should proactively identify artifacts that are undercooked, work with their director/ME, and understand inputs/outputs (assignment brief, company context, personas, user direction) to troubleshoot output quality

- Prophecy engagement signal: team is actively providing feedback (new topic ideas, strategy updates, specific formatting preferences); incorporating this into artifacts positions GrowthX for upsell and long-term renewal

**To GrowthX Product (Atlas):**

- Assignment direction field is the highest-leverage input for overriding default schemas; personas can be optionally called in brief generation to test impact on outline bloat
