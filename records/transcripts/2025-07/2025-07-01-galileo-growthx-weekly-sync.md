## Context

Galileo is an AI evaluation platform that helps teams benchmark and compare language models, agents, and frameworks. GrowthX provides content marketing and SEO services for Galileo. This is a weekly operational sync where the teams review performance metrics on Galileo's content hub, discuss new content initiatives to expand reach, and align on strategy to increase audience engagement. The relationship has been running for several months, with GrowthX driving organic search growth through AI visibility (AEO) focused content around LLM evaluation, model comparisons, and AI safety topics. Galileo's website migration to Scrunch recently completed, and the teams are coordinating both ongoing content reviews and new programmatic content ideas to capitalize on emerging opportunities in generative SEO and cross-channel content distribution.
