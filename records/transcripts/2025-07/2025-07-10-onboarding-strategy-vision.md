## Key Topics

### Company Structure and Vision

  - Three-layer model: service, AI/technology (workflow), and expert layers
  - Aiming to build a portfolio of incubated ventures, similar to Tiny (owns Dribbble, etc.)
  - Focus on building a common core and community, not tied to a single brand
  - Emphasis on authenticity, vulnerability, and human connection in marketing and community building

### Design Philosophy and Approach

  - Balance between systemization and creativity
  - Create reusable components and guardrails while allowing for creative flexibility
  - Streamline workflows (80%) to focus more on expertise and creative input (20%)
  - Utilize mood boarding and archetype creation for brand and content development
  - Explore AI-assisted tagging and database creation for efficient mood board generation

### Content Creation and Visual Systems

  - Develop visual language for mental models and frameworks
  - Create easily adaptable templates for social posts, article enrichment, and client deliverables
  - Ensure consistency across various content types (social, articles, videos)
  - Example: Splatch PMM's simple yet effective visual style for communicating concepts

### Katia's Motivation and Background

  - Transitioning from traditional agency work to AI-integrated creative processes
  - Seeking opportunities to learn and incorporate new tools into workflow
  - Attracted to GrowthX's unique position between agency and tech startup
