## Summary

GrowthX's delivery team reviewed progress across nine active client accounts in week 4 of their strategy sprint phase, identifying bottlenecks and aligning on next steps. Key wins included Biologica's nearly-complete fact-checker workflow enabling content velocity, Engine's strong performance with 17 topics accepted and 7 articles launching that week, and Augment Code's readiness to go live with guides and the MCP directory. ## Context

This is GrowthX's weekly all-hands for the delivery org, focused on tracking progress across multiple client accounts simultaneously. The team operates in "strategy sprints"—intensive 12-week engagements where weeks 1-4 are foundational (strategy, artifacts, workflows) and weeks 5+ transition to delivery and measurement. The standup includes account leads, subject matter experts, and directors, with George Haikal (VP of delivery) guiding tactical decisions and course correction. The meeting is a real-time status check, issue resolver, and operational hub where teams escalate blockers (e.g., Hypergrowth's stalled custom workflows) and celebrate compound wins (e.g., Biologica's expanding scope from content to email/social copy). Marcel Santilli (founder) and Nem Simic (from GrowthX Labs) join periodically to align on strategy and technical execution.
