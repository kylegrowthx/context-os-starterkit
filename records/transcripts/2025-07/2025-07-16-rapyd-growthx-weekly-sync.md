## Context

Rapyd is a global fintech company processing payments across multiple markets. GrowthX is helping Rapyd produce content targeted at merchants and payment professionals, covering complex payment industry topics like liability shifts, fraud, and transaction approval processes. This weekly sync is a standing operational check-in to review content drafts (artifacts), discuss quality and accuracy, share performance metrics from Rapyd's content, and resolve blockers. Rapyd's team (Mark, Marc, Nicolette, Vanessa) reviews and provides feedback on GrowthX's drafts; the relationship requires deep subject-matter expertise because payment mechanics are nuanced and mistakes can confuse the audience. GrowthX's content strategy relies on detailed "artifacts"—company context, audience personas, and content guidelines—which Rapyd owns and must keep updated to ensure all output stays on-brand.
