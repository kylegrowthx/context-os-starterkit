## Key Topics

### Changing Landscape of Information Discovery

  - Shift from search-driven traffic to AI app-driven information retrieval
  - Anecdotal reports of declining search traffic for some customers
  - Rise in high-converting referral traffic, indicating AI apps' growing influence

### GrowthX's Approach to AI-Driven Content Strategy

  - Monitor AI app mentions of client products using tools like Scrunch
  - Track hundreds of prompts per customer to analyze mentions, citations, and sentiment
  - Use insights to shape content creation and optimization strategies
  - Focus on getting cited by AI to influence responses about client products

### Content Optimization Techniques

  - Create more targeted, long-tail content to match personalized AI searches
  - Regularly refresh and update existing content to maintain relevance
  - Structure content for "chunking" - concise, segmented points for easy AI consumption
  - Implement TLDR or takeaway sections at the top of content
  - Develop better workflows for high-performing content types (listicles, comparisons, alternatives)

### Monitoring and Tools

  - Use Scrunch and Looker dashboards for tracking AI mentions and citations
  - Run regular audits to assess AI performance and optimize strategy
  - Share insights and best practices through Slack channel (D-generative engine optimization)
  - Notion resource with detailed information on delivery methods, monitoring, and writing techniques

### Broader Marketing Implications

  - Shift focus to shaping internet perception of products beyond owned content
  - Consider PR, community engagement (GitHub, Reddit, Hacker News), and link building
  - Emphasize foundational marketing principles in the AI-driven landscape
