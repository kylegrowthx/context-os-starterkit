### GrowthX Platform Evolution

  - Now 70 employees (up from 23 a few months ago)
  - Offering 8-week strategy sprints
  - Key features:
      - Context engineering: Creating detailed company/product descriptions and personas
      - Content strategy development: Cluster identification, gap analysis, assignment creation
      - AI-powered content creation: Automated brief generation, research, fact-checking, and internal linking
      - Analytics integration: Tracking LLM referrals and content performance

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Balances short-term gains with long-term investment
  - Focuses on creating high-quality, targeted content within identified clusters
  - Emphasizes freshness, citation, fact-checking, and organization for LLM optimization
  - Implements systematic cross-linking for improved topical authority
  - Tracks multiple metrics: indexing, impressions, clicks, engagement, and conversions

### Conversion Optimization

  - Identifies micro-conversion opportunities (e.g., newsletter signups, lead magnets)
  - Tracks conversion to next logical step, not just final conversion
  - Monitors LLM referrals and encourages tracking of traffic sources in lead forms
