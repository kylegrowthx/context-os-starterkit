## Key Topics

### Client Feedback and Delivery Org Changes

  - Positive feedback on partnership, trust, and communication
  - Areas for improvement:
      - Goal setting and reporting (metrics tied to higher-level goals)
      - Consistent delivery of promised SEO workflows
      - Strategic direction and industry insights
  - Changes being implemented:
      - Resetting all client artifacts
      - Standardizing reporting and Airtable usage
      - Regular pod stand-ups and weekly activity summaries
      - Hiring in-house publisher and offering more resources

### Atlas Workflow Optimization

  - Four core artifacts: audience personas, company context, writing guidelines, CTAs
  - Key pipeline steps: assignment brief, researcher, outline, draft, fact check
  - Time allocation guidance:
      - 50-60% on polishing brief
      - 25-30% on outline
      - 10-15% on draft passes
  - Common pitfalls to avoid:
      - Using generic keywords instead of specific phrases
      - Waiting to fix voice issues instead of updating writing guidelines
      - Cutting paragraphs late instead of pruning outline

### Workflow Customization and Client Management

  - Custom pipelines can be created for specific client needs (e.g. PSEO workflows)
  - Team encouraged to renegotiate client relationships to remove bottlenecks
  - Engineering support available for highly specific formatting requirements
