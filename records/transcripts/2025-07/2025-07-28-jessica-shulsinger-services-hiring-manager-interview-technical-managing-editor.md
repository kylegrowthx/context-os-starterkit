## Summary

Andi Bailey interviewed Jessica Shulsinger for the Technical Managing Editor role at GrowthX, exploring how her 9 years as Managing Editor at Thread and her deep experience implementing Hypotenuse AI for Adidas' product copy and SEO content would translate to GrowthX's rapidly expanding AI-driven content delivery business. ## Context

This is a hiring interview for an open Technical Managing Editor position at GrowthX, a B2B content marketing services company experiencing rapid growth. Andi Bailey, who leads customer operations (the delivery side), conducted the interview on July 28, 2025. Jessica Shulsinger is an external candidate with a rare combination of editorial expertise and hands-on AI-driven content creation experience. The technical managing editor role at GrowthX is a newly critical position given the company's scale: GrowthX recently signed roughly 20 new clients and hired 30 people in a single two-month window, and now needs experienced editors to manage 3-5 accounts each while providing the engineering team actionable feedback on how the platform is performing in the field.
