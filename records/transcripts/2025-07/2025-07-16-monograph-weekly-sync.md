## Summary

GrowthX and Monograph aligned on a phased content strategy focused on the engineering audience while supporting architects, with a North Star of increasing sales qualified opportunities from content. The team decided to start with one calibration blog post this week to nail voice and tone, then scale to four new blogs and two refreshes next week. ## Context

Monograph is a project management platform for architects and engineers. This weekly sync was the second meeting between GrowthX and Monograph's content and product teams to refine a content strategy that had been drafted the week prior. Aida Knežević, a Senior Managing Editor at GrowthX with seven years of content marketing experience (including prior work at SEMrush and Animals), led the meeting to review the content OS (artifact repository), approve initial assignments for blog creation, and discuss visual content and distribution strategies.
