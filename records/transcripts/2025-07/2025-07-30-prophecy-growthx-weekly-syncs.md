## Key Topics

### Blog Page Redesign

  - Shifting to grid view for better visibility
  - Relocating subscribe button to prevent glossing over
  - Retaining categories display
  - Individual blog layout changes:
      - Mini table of contents added for quicker navigation
      - Narrower view, but minimal overall changes

### CTA Strategy Discussion

  - Bonfire agency proposed removing inline CTAs at blog ends
  - Team's perspective:
      - Prefer tailored resource CTAs over generic newsletter subscription
      - Matt: "I want blogs to end with... go read our five dysfunctions"
      - Ehtisham: "Theoretically, having just one CTA... works out best" but noted subscribe vs. specific resource are different intents
  - Current setup includes demo request CTA at bottom of some blogs
  - Consensus: CTAs should match blog content and reader intent (e.g., Geo blog showcasing product features)

### Newsletter Subscription Placement

  - Team not passionate about prominent newsletter placement until program is solid
  - Ashleigh: "I don't love this here" (referring to newsletter subscription at blog end)
  - Agreed to keep newsletter subscription on main blog listing page for now

### Website Migration Planning

  - Bonfire creating migration plan for potential switch from .io to .ai domain
  - Cody: "I think they're over-engineering it based on what I've seen"
  - Internal confusion among IT resources about requirements
  - Team committed to keeping GrowthX informed of any architectural changes
