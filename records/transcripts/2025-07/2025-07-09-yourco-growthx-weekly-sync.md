## Context

Yourco is a customer communications platform focused on helping traditional industries (field services, HVAC, plumbing, construction) manage employee and customer interactions, particularly through text-based communications and AI-assisted features like real-time translation. This is a regularly scheduled weekly sync between the GrowthX content delivery team (Carrie as the lead, with support from Darrell on SEO strategy and optimization) and Yourco's content stakeholders (Jessica overseeing strategy). The partnership operates under a $50k/month engagement where GrowthX handles content creation, publishing, and performance analysis, with Yourco providing content direction and feedback. The meeting focused on validating the content strategy against actual performance data and planning the next phase of content investments.

## Key Topics

### Content Performance Analysis

  - Impressions have grown steadily since partnership began
  - Clicks peaked in May, but click-through rates are declining (industry-wide AI impact)
  - Best performing content: actionable, communication-focused (e.g., employee texting, translations)
  - Practical guides and templates getting most impressions and clicks
  - Enriched and refreshed content strategy working well

### Content Strategy Adjustments

  - Focus on combining high-impression content (e.g., translations) with high-converting topics (e.g., surveys)
  - Increase emphasis on incident management content (high CTR, low impressions)
  - Include "common pitfalls" and "what not to do" sections in templates and guides
  - Consider incorporating real-world examples and social proof to boost conversions
  - Explore new topics: AI in traditional industries, operations-focused templates

### SEO and Backlink Strategy

  - Investigate page position improvements vs. averages for key converting articles
  - Focus on increasing quality backlinks through organic outreach and utility content
  - Consider targeted outreach to trade publications and service-oriented websites
  - Encourage organic sharing on platforms like LinkedIn to boost visibility

### Technical SEO Updates

  - Some previously non-indexed pages now showing as indexed
  - Yourco's engineering team to run script next week to address URL slash issues
  - GrowthX team to explore bulk submission via XML sitemap
