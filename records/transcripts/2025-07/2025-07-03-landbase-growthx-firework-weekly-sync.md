## Key Topics

### Indexing and Sitemap Issues

  - WordPress sitemap hasn't been updated since October 2024, excluding new Webflow blog posts
  - Many blog posts not properly indexed, affecting visibility and performance
  - Cameron proposes manually updating sitemap and fixing canonical tags as short-term solution
  - Team agrees to test solution with March blog posts before full implementation

### Blog Performance Analysis

  - March-June performance varied widely (e.g., 80k vs. 0 impressions for different posts)
  - April: 7 new blogs led to 8 leads; May: 2 new blogs led to 3 leads; June: 2 refreshed blogs led to 8 leads
  - Overall lead generation from blogs is concerning (e.g., \~0.06 leads per blog in May)
  - Older content still driving majority of leads

### Content Strategy

  - Focus on high-performing categories: card podcast, industry verticals (e.g., beauty, luxury), D2C growth strategies
  - Improve CTAs and include relevant lead magnets
  - A/B testing between pre-trial and book-a-demo CTAs

### Technical SEO Concerns

  - Canonical tag issues identified on important landing pages
  - Migration from WordPress to Webflow causing complications
  - Need for expertise in resolving infrastructure and SEO challenges
