## Key Topics

### SMB vs. Enterprise Content Strategy

  - Separate targeting due to different pain points and governance maturity levels
  - SMB focus: "transaction readiness" message, adapting enterprise content
  - Enterprise content performing well: corporate secretary job, general counsel checklists
  - Challenge: Defining the right story for why SMBs should care about governance

### Product and Sales Approach

  - No self-serve option currently; demo request process being streamlined
  - Pricing not disclosed publicly; new packages created for SMBs
  - White-glove implementation service historically offered, may be changing for smaller packages
  - Common objections: price perception, board adoption concerns, implementation time

### Content Performance and Pipeline Attribution

  - Checklists, buyer's guides, and PDF content historically perform well
  - Experimenting with video content, open to new formats
  - Attribution tracked via Tableau (last-touch), challenges in measuring content contribution

### Content Review and Guidelines

  - Aim for professional yet humanized tone for SMB audience
  - Current writing guidelines are vague; opportunity to develop more specific guidelines
