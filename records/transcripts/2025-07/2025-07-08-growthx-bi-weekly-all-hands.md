### Human-AI Framework

  - Three layers: Service, AI Workflows, Human Expertise
  - Three key roles in delivery team:
    1.  Process Architects: Create blueprints for workflows
    2.  Input Calibrators: Gather and process client inputs effectively
    3.  Production Refiners: Ensure high-quality outputs and raise the bar

### Strategy Sprints and Client Feedback

  - Implementing deeper client understanding through extensive research and artifacts
  - Positive feedback on high-quality, insightful outputs (e.g., Surge AI data quality manifesto)
