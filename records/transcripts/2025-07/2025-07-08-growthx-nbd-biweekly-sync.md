## Summary

GrowthX and Noboringdesign reviewed detailed brand identity refinements including subtle logo adjustments (shortened X arm, unified geometry), updated color palette with increased white and yellow accents, and Rethink typography applied across web hero sections, pricing pages, and social ads. Renato Valdes Olmos approved the overall direction, particularly the new color balance and logo work, but flagged that iconography needs further refinement to balance better with the logo and flagged photography style and subject matter as still undefined. Augusto Gomez Yañez will share the presentation deck with Renato's team, and Renato committed to recording detailed Loom feedback after internal team discussion.

## Context

Noboringdesign (NBD) is GrowthX's external design partner working on a complete brand identity redesign. This biweekly sync brought together the NBD team (Camila Candia leading the design presentation, with Augusto Gomez Yañez managing the project and Juan Pablo Lopez providing input on photographic direction) and Renato Valdes Olmos from GrowthX to review detailed visual identity work. The meeting occurred at the detailed design phase, after an initial concept was approved, to present and validate refined logo adjustments, color palette decisions, typography choices, and initial web and social applications of the new brand system.

## Overview

- Logo refinements approved: subtle X adjustments unify geometry and shorten upper arm while maintaining original thickness and character weight
- Color direction strongly approved: increased white (50% of compositions) with yellow and blue accents for contrast and information hierarchy
- Iconography flagged as requiring refinement: current geometric Bauhaus style too delicate relative to logo weight; needs more contrast and possibly increased roundedness
- Photography direction remains undefined: mix of individual portraits with computers and analog notebook work is under consideration; client to provide input on preferred situations and subject matter
- Next steps: NBD to share presentation deck with Renato's team; Renato to record Loom with consolidated team feedback within ~1 week

## Key Topics

### Brand Identity Updates

  - Logo refinements: Subtle adjustments made to the 'X' in the isotipo, unifying elements
  - Wordmark: Maintained original thickness and proportions with minor adjustments to 'R' and 'W' for a more contemporary, geometric look
  - Color palette: Increased use of white (50% of compositions), with accent colors for contrast and highlighting key information
  - Typography: Exclusively using Rethink font, inspired by modernist sans-serif typefaces
  - Iconography: Geometric, minimalist style inspired by Bauhaus modernist logos (needs further refinement)

### Key Visual Elements

  - Data visualization: Incorporated geometric elements for an visually interesting representation
  - Grid system: Geometric grid from concept \#1 used to structure and unify design elements
  - Photography: Analog-style black and white images of people in context (style TBD)
  - Color accents: Blue used for CTAs, buttons, and to differentiate information hierarchies

### Web Design Applications

  - Hero section: Adapted current content to new graphic style, featuring prominent headline, subheader, and visualized data
  - How it works: Redesigned menu structure using the new grid system and color accents
  - Pricing plans: Updated layout with specific information accessible through navigation menus

### Social Media Ads

  - Flexible system allowing for various combinations of elements (photography, data, CTAs)
  - Adapted key visual elements to smaller format while maintaining brand consistency
