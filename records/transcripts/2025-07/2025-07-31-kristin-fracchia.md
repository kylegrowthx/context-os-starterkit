## Summary

Marcel Santilli (GrowthX CEO) and Kristin Fracchia (Gamma VP of Growth) explored a potential partnership to accelerate Gamma's non-branded SEO and content strategy. Gamma has hit 50M+ ARR with strong conversion metrics but faces competitive pressure and needs to move from 92% branded search traffic to capturing high-intent non-branded keywords. ## Context

Gamma is a generative AI presentation platform that has grown to 50M+ ARR (as of April 2025) with 60% of its user base outside the US. The company competes against traditional tools (PowerPoint, Google Slides), incumbent startups (Figma, Canva), and newer AI agents (ChatGPT, Lovable). Kristin Fracchia is leading Gamma's growth strategy and is actively hiring for in-house SEO and marketing engineering roles to execute on a roadmap that includes content ownership, UGC indexing, and programmatic SEO. This conversation followed a prior dinner meeting and was triggered by Kristin's interest in understanding what GrowthX actually does and whether it could support Gamma's content and SEO goals.
