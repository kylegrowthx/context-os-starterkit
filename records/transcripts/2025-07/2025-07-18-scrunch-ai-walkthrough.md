## Key Topics

### AI Optimization Landscape

  - Websites now need optimization for humans, Google, AND AI/LLMs - each with distinct criteria
  - AI adoption accelerating rapidly; ChatGPT = fastest adopted platform in Internet history
  - Human website traffic likely to decrease; critical to measure and optimize for AI traffic

### Scrunch AI Platform Overview

  - Two setup options:
    1.  Quick 10-min brand creation (URL input → auto-generated context)
    2.  White-glove service for GrowthX (2-day turnaround, custom prompts/tags/personas)
  - Auto-generated brand context: description, personas, competitors, key topics (all editable)
  - Bulk prompt addition with platform selection
  - Analysis features: filter by branded/non-branded, tags, personas

### Leveraging Scrunch for Client Insights

  - Competitive presence analysis across prompted queries
  - Detailed prompt-level breakdowns (e.g., Augment case study)
  - Source citation tracking to identify high-value targets for backlinks/mentions
  - Metadata optimization focus: URL slug, title, description (be highly descriptive for LLMs)

### Upcoming Features

  - New tab for granular AI hit tracking
  - Categorizes hits: retrieval (direct answers), indexing, training
  - Critical for demonstrating AI visibility and impact to clients
