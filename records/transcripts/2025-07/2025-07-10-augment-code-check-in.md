## Key Topics

### Current Organic Traffic Analysis

  - 71%+ of organic traffic is brand searches (85,000 clicks/month)
  - Non-brand organic traffic is minimal (\~150 clicks/month across 50 pages)
  - Brand searches have 15x higher click-through rate (33% vs. 2%) and likely higher conversion rates

### Content Strategy and Goals

  - Increase non-brand content pages by 100+ per month
  - Aim to improve average clicks per page from 3 to 10, 20, then 30 monthly
  - GrowthX to produce \~25 articles/week and 1 newsletter/week
  - Focus on low-competition keywords for quicker ranking potential

### Newsletter Growth Strategy

  - Current estimate: 100,000 subscribers, 6% click-through rate
  - Goal: Increase subscribers to compound weekly clicks (e.g., 200,000 subscribers = 5,000 clicks/month)
  - Potential to purchase subscribers to accelerate growth (20-40K budget discussed)

### Measuring Progress and Impact

  - Track weekly content output (e.g., 10 new blog posts)
  - Monitor Google Search Console data for organic traffic growth
  - Implement keyword ranking tracking for targeted keywords
  - Aim to improve average keyword position (e.g., from current position to top 9)

### Competitive Landscape and Domain Authority

  - Competitor (Kodo.ai) has 4x more backlinks/referring domains
  - Discussed potential for strategic backlink acquisition to boost domain authority
  - Estimated timeline for significant results: 2-3 months (compressed due to AI industry dynamics)

### Budget and Resource Allocation

  - Sylvain to review budget for potential "growth budget" for backlinks and subscriber acquisition
  - GrowthX positioned as "owners" of organic traffic growth strategy
