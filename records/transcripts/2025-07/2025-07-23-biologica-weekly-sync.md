## Context

Biologica is a women's hormonal health supplement product developed by founder Joey and his wife Liz (who is recruiting her mother, a former OBGYN, as a medical reviewer). The product comes in tins containing 30 sachet packets of 6g each, with unique motifs and colors for different life stages (reproductive, midlife, post-menopause). It contains no sugar; the majority is active ingredients plus an effervescent system that also functions as an electrolyte. The product uses several trademarked premium ingredients (e.g., Afron for saffron) as a key quality differentiator. GrowthX is contracted to generate content for Biologica's website launch in late September. Matthew Panzarino recently joined as Chief Content Officer and is focused on refining the content pipeline, while Jess Scott (Managing Editor) and Jakub Rudnik's team handle drafting and editing. Medical reviews are underway from Maggie (current reviewer) and will include Liz's mother starting next week. The team is preparing PDP content and core pages (homepage, Hormones 101, ingredient pages) with deliverables due August 19th for the alpha website build.
