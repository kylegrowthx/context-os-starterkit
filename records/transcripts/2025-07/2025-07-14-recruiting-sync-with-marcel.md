## Key Topics

### Ideal Candidate Profiles

  - Input calibrators: Similar to Ada and George - excellent at relationship building, highly reliable, strong communication skills, and ability to manage 8+ accounts
  - Output bar raisers: Editorial experts like Sydney - meticulous attention to detail, never shipping subpar work
  - Generalists preferred over specialists, with a focus on asking good questions and pragmatic problem-solving

### Sourcing Strategies

  - Target agencies with strong reputations (e.g., Animals, Verbatim, Story)
  - Consider candidates from consulting backgrounds (e.g., McKinsey)
  - Look for people running or writing for reputable newsletters
  - Explore content strategists with AI experience

### Interview Process

  - Use in-depth career path exploration to identify patterns of ownership mentality and growth
  - Implement paid assignments simulating client work (e.g., generating artifacts, presenting strategy)
  - Assess preparation and genuine interest in the company

### Compensation and Location

  - Target range of $80-120k for managing editors, with flexibility up to $150k for exceptional U.S.-based candidates
  - Preference for candidates outside high-cost areas like San Francisco and New York
  - Consider international candidates with experience working with U.S. companies
