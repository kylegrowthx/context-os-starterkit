## Context

Interdynamics is a behavioral health practice founded 25 years ago outside Washington DC, operated by Garth Mitchell's mother. The business serves three distinct audience segments: direct patients seeking therapy or evaluation, referral sources (hospital systems and primary care doctors), and government contractors seeking healthcare staffing and clinical solutions for prisons, schools, and military bases. Garth recently returned to help restructure the business as he recognized major shifts in the industry — behavioral health is now destigmatized and widely understood to reduce overall healthcare costs when integrated with primary care. He sees significant opportunity to automate administrative and operational processes (insurance compliance, scheduling, phone handling) through new technology, which would increase impact and enable scalable, sustainable growth. Garth has a finance background and served as CFO of venture-backed tech companies before consulting and now helping his mother's practice. His marketing team of three people currently lacks clear structure, strategy, or defined GTM process. Garth discovered GrowthX through the website and reached out interested in getting strategic guidance and tooling to build a go-to-market AI stack without having to assemble it piecemeal.
