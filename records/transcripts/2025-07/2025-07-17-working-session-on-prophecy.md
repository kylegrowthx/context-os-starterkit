## Key Topics

### Atlas AI Tool Challenges

  - Outputs tend to be overly long and repetitive
  - Often includes unnecessary sections like "best practices" and "pitfalls"
  - Struggles with thought leadership content that requires original ideas
  - Internal linking feature can shoehorn in irrelevant information

### Content Creation Strategy

  - Create outline externally (e.g. ChatGPT) before inputting to Atlas
  - Reinforce desired outline at multiple stages of Atlas process
  - Focus on editing/condensing Atlas output rather than rewriting
  - Aim for \~2500 words, deleting entire sections if needed
  - Use "Ask AI" tool within Atlas to help with editing

### Quality vs Efficiency Balance

  - Previous manual articles set high quality bar, may see dip with AI
  - Goal is to produce acceptable quality more efficiently, not perfection
  - Think of role as editor managing AI "writer", not manual writer
  - Create tickets for Atlas improvements when output is unsatisfactory

### Client Feedback

  - Cody (client) has approved many proposed topics
  - Added notes on some to avoid straying off-topic
  - Many approved topics are "thought leadership" which may challenge Atlas

## Action Items
