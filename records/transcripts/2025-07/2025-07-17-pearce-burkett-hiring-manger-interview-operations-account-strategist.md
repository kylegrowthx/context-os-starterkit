## Context

GrowthX is hiring an Account Strategist to help manage the post-implementation success and expansion of its client base. The company experienced rapid growth—signing most accounts within a 3-4 month window but then scaling from 5 to 70+ people—which created gaps in client onboarding and expectation management. Early clients churned partly due to lack of training for new hires, insufficient engineering support, and inconsistent delivery as teams were brought online. Andi, who leads customer operations and has background in customer success and Uber's government contracts ops team, is building out systematic processes for implementation (8-week intensive period) and long-term client management.

Pearce is a candidate with directly applicable experience. He graduated in 2017 and spent 6 years at MVF (digital marketing/demand gen) progressing from sales exec to senior AE managing full-cycle sales and a $1.5M MRR portfolio. He then joined Relovo in 2022 as founding AE, where he managed full-cycle sales, account success, and crucially—built methodologies and data workflows to solve discovery and targeting problems for clients. At Relovo, Pearce worked with engineering on AI/ML-based signal detection, tested new data sources and workflows before automation, and managed difficult stakeholder dynamics when internal teams feared replacement. Relovo was acquired and is sunsetting in July 2025, creating the opportunity for Pearce to move to GrowthX.
