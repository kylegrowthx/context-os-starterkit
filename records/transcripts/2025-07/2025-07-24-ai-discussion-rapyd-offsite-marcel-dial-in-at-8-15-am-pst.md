## Overview

- AI literacy requires dedicated, uninterrupted time to explore and decompose processes into first principles
- Effective AI use in marketing starts with creating shared "artifacts" that define company context, personas, and guidelines
- Focus on using AI for research, information synthesis, and accelerating existing processes rather than replacing them entirely
- AI tools should be viewed as assistants to augment human work, not fully autonomous solutions

---

## Key Topics

### Marcel's Background and Journey to AI

  - Started career at IBM, always focused on making marketing more like publishing
  - Successful CMO experiences at Scale AI and Deepgram
  - Transitioned to CEO of GrowthX after exploring AI workflows during personal time
  - GrowthX grew to 70 people and $15M ARR in first year without marketing spend

### Becoming AI Literate

  - Dedicate uninterrupted time (e.g., 2 weeks) to deeply explore AI without distractions
  - Focus on decomposing processes you're expert in into first principles
  - Practice effective delegation by providing clear context and expectations
  - Improve writing skills to better organize thoughts and communicate with AI

### AI Applications in Marketing

  - Create shared "artifacts" defining company context, personas, features, etc.
  - Use AI for deep research and information synthesis (e.g., Perplexity, Claude)
  - Accelerate existing processes like transcript analysis and content creation
  - Implement "expert-in-the-loop" workflows to refine AI outputs

### AI Tools and Models

  - Favors Claude (Anthropic) models for most tasks
  - Uses Perplexity for research, Manus for complex multi-step tasks
  - Recommends experimenting with multiple models in parallel
  - Cautions against over-reliance on OpenAI/ChatGPT

### Marketing Investment Priorities

  - Focus on creating lasting, compounding value (e.g., high-quality content, podcasts)
  - Invest in polished design and presentation to stand out
  - Create educational workshops and events to build trust and relationships
  - Develop programs that foster community and ecosystem growth
