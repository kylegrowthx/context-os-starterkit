## Context

Andi Bailey and Ella Dillon are GrowthX colleagues working on CheckThat and the new OS (operating system) platform. Ella D, an external collaborator or advisor, joined the call mid-session to review their strategic work. The team has been tasked with defining pricing and packaging strategy for CheckThat and the new OS ahead of customer renewals in May 2026.
