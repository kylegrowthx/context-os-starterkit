## Decisions & Commitments

1. **No unrestricted access without contracts**: All customer workspace access to CheckThat requires a signed contract or contract amendment. This controls costs, liability, and revenue recognition.

2. **Three customer action buckets**:
   - Yellow/red risk: Existing customers where CheckThat adds value for retention; upsell via contract amendment.
   - Green: Existing customers willing to pay; incremental revenue opportunity.
   - Net-new/Sprint: Clear onboarding path; trial→conversion path depends on segment (Nigel-led or growth-driven).

3. **Internal team always has full access**: GrowthX EMs must have admin access to all customer workspaces to deliver services effectively. Feature flags can manage what customers see, but internal team access is non-negotiable.

4. **Unified action plan by Ella & Andy**: Merge Jason's and Andy's docs into one comprehensive guide clarifying:
   - Which customers get access and for how long.
   - When/how to upsell (trial length, contract triggers).
   - What is free vs. paid.
   - Access control principles.

5. **EMS enablement session**: After doc finalization, run a live training session for all customer-facing staff (EMs, account leads) covering the new playbook.

6. **90-day trial is not final**: Marcel reserved judgment; team agreed 90 days is reasonable for now but left door open to shorten based on revenue needs and product development progress.

7. **Sprint positioning deferred**: Jason to clarify how CheckThat fits into Sprint sales (platform fee vs. separate line item) and report back; informs contract templates and upsell timing.
