  - **Motivation:** The GrowthX role aligns his marketing and SaaS sales experience, targeting his familiar CMO ICP. He is not actively looking but is open to a role that's the right fit.

### Outbound Strategy: Personalized Demos

  - Max Simon uses a highly personalized outbound strategy to drive meetings.
  - **Process:**
    1.  Create a custom-branded Moxo demo environment for the prospect's company.
    2.  Record a \~1-minute Loom video walking through the tailored demo.
    3.  Send the video in an email to demonstrate deep research and value alignment.
  - **Rationale:** This approach proves the outreach is not generic, building trust and showing a clear understanding of the prospect's needs.

### Deal Example 1: Citizens Bank

  - **Context:** Outbounded Citizens Bank after seeing a press release for their "digital butler" program for high-net-worth individuals.
  - **Problem:** The bank's existing app and portal provided a poor customer experience.
  - **Solution:** Moxo's SDKs were embedded into Citizens' platforms to enable a seamless, high-touch experience with features like secure chat and web conferencing.
  - **Value:** Increased Net Promoter Score (NPS) and customer engagement, reducing churn risk for a critical customer segment.

### Deal Example 2: Kaplan Education

  - **Context:** A \>1-year sales cycle with a large, complex organization.
  - **Problem:** Kaplan was pivoting to a B2B model and needed a platform to streamline interactions with corporate clients.
  - **Strategy:** Built a close relationship with the champion (Raul, Director of Digital Experience).
      - **Weekly Prep Calls:** Raul prepped Max Simon on each stakeholder's priorities and concerns before meetings.
      - **Outcome:** This enabled highly targeted conversations and was critical to navigating the complex deal.
  - **Lesson Learned:** Do not assume alignment within large organizations; proactively confirm internal consensus.

### GrowthX Context & Role

  - **Tyler Pavlas (GrowthX AE):** Hit his annual quota in Q1 due to strong market timing (AI search), CEO Marcel's reputation, and a high-value product.
  - **Enablement:** Proactive and self-directed. Tyler Pavlas learned by shadowing Marcel and connecting directly with EPD and delivery teams.
  - **Sales Model:**
      - **Phase 1:** 8-week pilot.
      - **Phase 2:** 12-month contract, typically $15k–$18k/mo ARR.
  - **AE Involvement:** AEs join kickoffs and check in during Phase 1 to ensure a smooth transition to Phase 2, but their primary focus is closing new deals.
