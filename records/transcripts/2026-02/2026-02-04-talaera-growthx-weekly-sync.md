### Content Generation Process: Atlas Platform

  - Aida demoed Atlas, GrowthX's internal AI platform, to show how content is created.
  - **Workflow:**
    1.  **Input:** Topic, direction, H2/H3 outline (human-led to match search intent).
    2.  **Research:** Tavoli (or EXA for technical topics) creates a detailed knowledge base to prevent "thin" content.
    3.  **Drafting:** AI generates content, applies writing guidelines, and adds internal/source links.
    4.  **Proofreading:** A separate AI checklist enforces rules (e.g., subheading case) to handle LLM limitations.
    5.  **Fact-Checking:** An AI validates claims against research. If a claim lacks a credible source, the AI rewrites it to be less definitive.
    6.  **Manual Review:** A human editor performs a final check for accuracy, links, and overall quality.
