## Overview

- **AI Visibility Gap:** Talon.One has strong AI response visibility (top 3 in 70% of prompts across monitored keywords) but a 0% citation share. This is because AI models cite comparison articles when making recommendations — a content type Talon.One currently lacks.
- **Strategic Pivot:** Content strategy will pivot to prioritize 4 comparison articles per week for 1–2 weeks to fix the citation gap. Agentic commerce optimization work will pause and resume after the comparison-article sprint.
- **Agentic Commerce Page Optimization:** Five key agentic commerce pages (product pages and blog posts live since August or January) are receiving high impressions but low CTR. Root causes: low average search position and AI summarization. GrowthX will provide page-by-page recommendations to add FAQs, clearer educational structure (who, what, when, why), internal linking (especially prominent visual links like on product pages), and content restructuring for snippet visibility.
- **Competitor Insight:** Yotpo (SME-focused but more aggressively pursuing agentic commerce) is successfully creating LLM-specific content (e.g., "Inside ChatGPT's shopping experience"). Talon.One will adopt this angle for future agentic content sprints.
