  - At BrightEdge, Samantha owns the full customer lifecycle, including upsells and renewals.
  - **Upsell Strategy:**
      - **Hook:** Offer a 5-day product demo to give clients a "taste" and build confidence.
      - **Proactive Expansion:** Identify opportunities early, like signing a customer's secondary company to a single package.

      - **Proactive Engagement:** Involve leadership or other stakeholders early to build relationships and mitigate risk from POC turnover.
      - **Direct Communication:** Use phone calls for unresponsive clients; they often answer, providing a direct channel to resolve issues.

### Candidate Experience: Stakeholder Management

  - **Engaging New Stakeholders:**
      - **Show Value:** Use data-driven wins to capture attention.

      - **Clarify Ambiguity:** When a client requests "more leads," define "lead" and quantify the target (e.g., percentage increase, specific number).
      - **Uncover "Secret Goals":** Ask about other tools (HubSpot, Posthog) to find the metrics clients track internally but don't share.
