## Key Topics

### Performance & Visibility Gap

  - **January Performance:** Flat vs. the December peak, which is a positive outcome for a typically slow month.
  - **LLM Referrals:** Tapered slightly from Oct–Jan.
      - **Driver Mix:** Consistent → ChatGPT \> Perplexity \> Other platforms.
  - **Visibility Gap:** A key challenge, as high citation shares are not translating to high visibility.
      - **Goal:** Close the gap with competitors (e.g., Horizon, Smith) by getting Galileo into the right conversations.

### Proposed Metrics for Executive Reporting

  - GrowthX proposed new metrics to provide a clearer picture of visibility and clicks.
  - **GeoScore:** A weighted formula combining various scores into a single metric for executive reporting.
  - **Referral Traffic:** Tracks monthly sessions from citations to measure actual clicks, addressing the gap between citations and traffic.
  - **Competitive Gap:** A metric to track Galileo's performance against key competitors.
  - **Action:** Galileo will review the concepts with Jason to determine the best approach for executive and board reporting.

### Content Strategy: Broadening Focus to "AI"

  - **Problem:** The current "LLM" focus is too narrow, representing only a subset of Galileo's product offering.
  - **Solution:** Shift the strategy to the broader "AI evaluation" and "AI observability" categories.
      - **Rationale:** Better aligns content with the full product offering and messaging.
      - **Execution:** Refresh existing LLM-focused articles and create new content targeting the broader AI category.
      - **Risk Mitigation:** Differentiate content by use case, industry, or persona to prevent keyword cannibalization.

### Content Quality: Improving Internal Linking

  - **Problem:** Internal linking is weak and does not follow SEO best practices.
      - **Impact:** Missed opportunities to drive users to core resources.
  - **Solution:** Guide the AI workflow to link to specific, high-value resources.
      - **Pillar Pages:** For broad topics like "AI observability" or "AI evals."
      - **V2 Docs:** For technical concepts (e.g., agentic metrics) to encourage product exploration.
      - **Product Pages:** For features (e.g., Signals, Luna 2) to drive demos and sign-ups.
  - **Execution:** GrowthX will create a new artifact listing these core resources to guide the AI workflow, followed by manual validation.
