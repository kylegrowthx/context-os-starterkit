## Key Topics

### AI Content Strategy & Rationale

  - **Core Principle:** Reverse-engineer AI citations by creating structured, purpose-built pages for high-intent queries, rather than relying on traditional SEO.
  - **Proof Point:** A GrowthX client saw a \~20% citation rate increase in 18 hours, validating the strategy's speed and effectiveness.
  - **Prophecy Performance:**
      - **Success:** "Data Transformation Journey Guide" has a \~20% citation rate.
      - **Opportunity:** For "Alteryx alternatives" queries, Prophecy is mentioned at \#18 but not cited, indicating a content structure/specificity issue.
  - **Query Selection Process:**
    1.  **Relevance:** Maps to an Alteryx use case (e.g., "what is data aggregation?").
    2.  **Demand:** Has SEO volume or Check That signals.
    3.  **Winnability:** Identifies gaps in competitor content (e.g., Easy Morph's basic comparison pages).
    4.  **Buyer Journey:** Targets the evaluation stage.
    5.  **Trackability:** Can be monitored for mentions.
  - **Content Format:**
      - Listicles are currently effective for AI summaries.
      - Strategy will evolve toward deeper, unique content as AI platforms mature, mirroring the evolution of SEO.

### Alteryx Displacement Campaign

  - **Target Audience:**
      - **Buyers:** Analytics Leaders & Platform Leaders (often with engineering backgrounds).
      - **Influencers:** Analysts, who surface pain points but lack enterprise buying autonomy.
  - **Positioning:**
      - **Focus:** Efficiency and speed (e.g., "work faster, fix faster").
      - **Rationale:** Enterprise rip-and-replace is rare. The goal is to capture budget from Alteryx contract renewals by proving a superior, future-proof solution.
      - **Avoid:** Leading with cost.
  - **Content Briefs:**
      - Informed by sales intel to use buyers' exact language.
      - Example: "I waited two months and it wasn't done right" → Brief for an Alteryx vs. Prophecy comparison page.

### Execution & Process

  - **Content Quality:**
      - **Concern:** Raj noted a "weak link" in the process where non-experts produce content.
      - **GrowthX Fix:** The internal team was restructured so expert managing editors now create the content directly.

      - **Solution:** Cody will use the "Ready to Publish" status for minor edits (typos, etc.). GrowthX will add a "Needs Revision" status for major changes.
  - **Alteryx Content Timeline:**
      - **Request:** Cody needs the first three Alteryx articles by EOW.
      - **Rationale:** Critical for managing Raj's expectations and demonstrating immediate progress.
      - **Plan:** Cody will review and publish next week if delivered on time.
