## Summary

Deepgram and GrowthX aligned on a strategic shift for 2026: pivot content focus from Speech-to-Text (STT) to Text-to-Speech (TTS), prioritize developer-focused integration content over competitive articles, and establish console signups as the North Star KPI. The team launched a new AIO tracking tool providing daily visibility into AI search presence, enabling more data-driven content decisions going forward.

---

## Context

GrowthX and Deepgram have been partnering on content strategy since September 2025, with strong initial results: 6K+ organic sessions from content, $165k in influenced pipeline from 8 opportunities, and 400+ referrals from AI search platforms. The content initially focused on Speech-to-Text (STT) and competitive comparison articles (e.g., Deepgram vs. 11 Labs), achieving a #6 Google ranking for "Speech-to-Text APIs" with organic traffic momentum continuing through January 2026.

Deepgram is now shifting product priorities toward Text-to-Speech (TTS)—a newer, higher-growth product category where the competitive landscape is dominated by 11 Labs. The executive team (particularly Praveen, Deepgram's leadership) has signaled that differentiating on enterprise use cases (healthcare, life-critical applications requiring 99.999% accuracy) rather than voice quality alone is the strategic focus. Simultaneously, Deepgram hired a new DevRel lead (Corey) to expand developer relations, suggesting a broader commitment to integrations and platform partnerships beyond competitive positioning.

## Overview

- **Strategic Pivot → TTS:** Shift content focus from STT to Text-to-Speech (TTS), Deepgram's newer product. The goal is to build an original narrative around enterprise use cases (e.g., healthcare) rather than just competing on voice quality with 11 Labs.
- **Content Mix → DevRel:** Prioritize developer-focused content on integrations (AWS, Google Cloud) over competitive articles. This strategy aims to tap into larger developer ecosystems and boost social media reach.
- **KPI → Console Signups:** Console signups are the primary North Star KPI, as they directly feed the product-led growth (PLG) motion. The current $165k in influenced pipeline is a positive indicator, but the main goal is driving brute-force traffic.
- **AIO Tracking → New Tool:** A new, daily-updated AIO tracking tool was launched to provide granular, prompt-level visibility into Deepgram's AI search presence against competitors, enabling more data-driven content decisions.
