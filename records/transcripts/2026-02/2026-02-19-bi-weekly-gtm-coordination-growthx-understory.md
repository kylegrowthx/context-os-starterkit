## Context

GrowthX and Understory are partners in a bi-weekly go-to-market coordination meeting where both teams review campaign performance and GTM strategy execution. GrowthX drives high-value B2B content marketing and is building CheckThat, an AI visibility tool for B2B. Understory operates as the outbound/campaign execution partner, managing email platforms (Instantly), LinkedIn tools (HeyReach), and audience targeting through HubSpot segmentation. The relationship is collaborative, with both teams aligned on shifting GrowthX's model from pure top-of-funnel lead generation (events and newsletters) toward direct pipeline generation by offering low-friction consulting engagements (AEO/GEO strategy sessions) to highly targeted lookalike audiences within verticals where GrowthX has customer proof points.
