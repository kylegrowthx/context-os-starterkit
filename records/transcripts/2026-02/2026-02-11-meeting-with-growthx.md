## Overview

- **GrowthX is a hybrid agency/platform** that uses AI workflows to produce content at scale (5 articles/week), solving Ataccama's core challenge of limited internal content resources.
- **The model is "AI + Experts in the Loop,"** with GrowthX handling execution and Ataccama providing calibration artifacts (personas, brand voice) and final draft reviews.

## Key Topics

### Ataccama's AI Search Challenge

  - **Primary Need:** Content creation at scale.
      - **Constraint:** Limited internal resources (one part-time writer).
      - **Result:** Inconsistent content quality and approach across channels.
  - **Strategic Goal:** Improve AI search discoverability and compete for market/mind share.
  - **Evaluation Path:** Considering a mix of build, buy, and partner options.
      - **Build:** Internal efforts (led by David Kolinek) are limited in scale and best practices.
      - **Buy:** Current tools include Scrunch for analytics; also evaluating other vendors.
      - **Partner:** Exploring agencies like GrowthX for execution.

### GrowthX's Hybrid Solution

  - **Model:** A hybrid agency/platform combining AI workflows with human experts.
  - **Founder Expertise:** Marcel Santilli built high-ROI content engines at IBM, HashiCorp, and DeepGram (24x traffic, 4x revenue in 18 months).
  - **Platform:** Uses proprietary AI workflows to accelerate content creation.
      - **Client Access:** A client-facing platform is \~3 months away; current clients will gain access within 6 months.

### Execution Process & Pricing

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (Weeks 1–4)**
      - **Goal:** Build a "Content OS" to inform AI workflows.
      - **Activities:** Keyword universe mapping, topic cluster analysis, content roadmap creation.
      - **Deliverables:** Calibration artifacts (personas, writing guidelines) that are continuously updated.
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
      - **Standard Pace:** 5 editorial articles per week.
      - **Programmatic Content:** Can be added to increase volume without increasing the fee.
          - **Example:** Ramp per diem calculator (25+ pages/week from one asset).
  - **Pricing:**
      - **Setup Fee:** \~$40k (one-time).
      - **Execution Fee:** \~$20k/month (fixed for standard scope).
      - **Flexibility:** Scope can be increased (e.g., 10 editorial articles/week) for an additional cost.

### Example: Augment Code Case Study

  - **Client:** Augment Code (AI coding tool).
  - **Content:** 130+ guides, 200+ tool comparisons, 40+ thought leadership articles.
  - **Results:**
      - Organic traffic 3x'd from \~$20k/month.
      - Paid ad spend significantly reduced.
      - Articles now cited for competitor tools (e.g., Cursor).
