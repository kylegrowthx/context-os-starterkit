## Summary

The partnership assessment used a five-point framework (output, quality, performance, strategy, relationship) to evaluate progress. Output volume meets targets with a rating of 5, but effort required for manual corrections and consistency issues brings overall output score to 3. Quality rated at 3 due to repetitive formatting and sameness across articles, though tone and voice are slowly improving. Performance presents challenges—organic traffic increased from 129 to 1500 sessions, but conversions remain unclear and difficult to attribute. The team is shifting strategy from broad, top-of-funnel content to focused mid- and bottom-funnel clusters, dedicating two-month sprints to single clusters for faster wins. Data sharing agreements and structural website changes (including potential separate guides section) are planned to accelerate performance insights and content agility. Client expressed desire for anonymized peer learnings from other GrowthX customers to inform strategy.
