## Summary

Relay and GrowthX conducted a partnership check-in using a five-point evaluation framework. Output volume meets targets (5/5), but required manual corrections for linking issues and repetitive content bring the overall output score to 3/5. Quality is rated 3/5—articles are consistent but lack variety in format and tone. Performance shows organic traffic growth from 129 to 1500 sessions since October, but conversion attribution remains unclear and requires direct data access (Posthog, Fathom, GA). The strategic pivot: moving from broad, top-of-funnel content to focused mid-funnel and bottom-funnel clusters, with two-month sprints per cluster. Relay will share internal research on content performance and is open to learning from anonymized peer insights from other GrowthX customers.

## Context

Relay is a fintech company providing cash flow and financial management solutions for small businesses. GrowthX has been engaged since September 2025 as their primary content marketing partner, with Andi Bailey's customer operations team serving as the day-to-day contact. This is an active engagement with a $200k+ annual contract value. Both teams are invested in proving ROI and scaling the partnership, but face a common challenge: connecting content activity to measurable business outcomes in a multi-touch attribution environment.
