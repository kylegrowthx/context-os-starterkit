  - A new, work-in-progress content strategy is being developed for the QBR, structured around four clusters:
      - Awareness
      - Jobs to be Done (JTBD)
      - Tools / Value Decision
      - Activation / Product Adoption
  - The immediate focus is on scoping JTBDs and testing content hypotheses.
  - Interim content will continue to cover "code review" and "spec-driven" topics.

### New Keyword & Topic Focus

  - New content will target two key areas:
      - **Competitive Intent:** Keywords for tools like "Gaston" and "Claude Team" to capture users researching multi-agent orchestration platforms.
      - **Core Product Concepts:** Topics defining the product's capabilities, including:
          - "Long-running AI tasks"
          - "Multi-agent" & "agent orchestration"
          - "Spec-driven" & "live spec"
          - "Ticket to PR" & "Ralph Wigan loop"

### "Intent" App Promotion

  - **Goal:** Drive as many downloads of the "Intent" app as possible over the next 3 months.
  - **Rationale:** Downloads are a simple, trackable leading indicator of interest, even if the in-app funnel is not yet optimized.
  - **Execution:** All new and refreshed content will feature a clear CTA to download the app.

### Context Wiki Decision

  - The wiki is in a "half dead state" but receives \~80 pages/day, creating a dilemma: invest or kill.
  - **Decision:** A final call is due next Wednesday. Liz and Antilal are evaluating its potential for a JTBD content cluster.
