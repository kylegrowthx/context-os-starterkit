## Context

Adventures Group operates tour and adventure destinations across three properties—Vallarta Adventures, Cabo Adventures, and Cancun Adventures—coordinated from Puerto Vallarta. GrowthX has been supporting their content strategy and SEO efforts, with Andi Bailey stepping in as the primary contact while Jo Kaminska (the usual point of contact) was on vacation. This weekly sync served as both a relationship check-in and a strategic planning session with Saul Sanz (web ops lead) and Isabel Luna (strategy lead) to discuss performance results and align on a major content pivot. The group had observed strong traffic growth (+53%) but weaker revenue conversion (+17%), prompting a shift from awareness-stage content to evaluation and booking-stage content supported by contextual calls-to-action.
