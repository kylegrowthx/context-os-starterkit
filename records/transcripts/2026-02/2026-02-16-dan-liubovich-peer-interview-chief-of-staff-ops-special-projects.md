## Key Topics

### Background & Zipline Experience

  - **Experience:** 4+ years at McKinsey (ops strategy) → 1+ year at Zipline (Biz Ops).
  - **Zipline Role:** Biz Ops (similar to Chief of Staff), focused on special projects.
  - **Key Project: Pricing Model Overhaul**
      - **Problem:** Zipline's simple pricing model (e.g., per-user) failed to support upmarket expansion and new product launches.
      - **Goal:** Create a robust, flexible model to handle market nuance while remaining simple for standard deals.
      - **Process:**
          - **Internal:** Collaborated with Sales and Product to define guardrails and map pricing curves to historical deal data.
          - **External:** Researched competitor pricing and market norms (e.g., for a new contracting module).
          - **Validation:** Pressure-tested concepts with advisory board customers to refine objection handling.
      - **Outcome:** The new model is the standard for all deals (Q3/Q4 revenue), enabling a land-and-expand motion for large accounts.

### AI-Native Productivity

  - **Dan's AI Use:**
      - **Research:** Used Gemini and Perplexity for rapid, first-principles research, cross-referencing AI outputs with manual findings.
      - **Development:** Built an interactive web-based pricing calculator using AI (Gemini, Claude) in a chat window, with no prior web dev experience.
          - **Impact:** The tool is more effective than the company's CPQ and is being explored for automating SoW creation and Salesforce updates.
  - **George's AI Use (GrowthX):**
      - Built an ICP scoring tool for sales to objectively qualify leads based on 5+ matrices, replacing anecdotal feedback.
      - **Impact:** Enables objective pipeline discussions and scalable, adaptable qualification criteria.

### Motivation for GrowthX

  - **Core Driver:** Seeks more foundational, ambiguous problems to solve from scratch.
  - **Contrast:** Zipline's current focus is on refining existing systems, whereas Dan wants to build them.
  - **Analogy:** Seeks a company at Zipline's stage \~2 years ago, where he can have maximum impact.

### GrowthX Culture & CEO's Focus

  - **Culture:**
      - High-impact environment with no hierarchy; decisions are based on first principles.
      - Inspiring team, especially the CEO (Marcel) and CTO (Daniel), who are domain experts but humble and AI-native.
      - Demanding work, as the team is building in a new space.
  - **Marcel (CEO) Focus:**
      - **Vision & Positioning:** Drives the company's narrative in the rapidly changing AI market.
      - **Sales:** Closes most new deals and handles at-risk accounts, leveraging his 25+ years of experience.
      - **Setting Standards:** Builds and scales key functions (e.g., the 8-week onboarding sprint) before staffing them.
      - **Product Strategy:** Guides product prioritization based on user needs, not technical details.
