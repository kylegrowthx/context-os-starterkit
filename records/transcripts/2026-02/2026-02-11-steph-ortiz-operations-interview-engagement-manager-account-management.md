### Candidate Background & Motivation

  - **Current Role:** Enterprise CSM at Docebo (LMS).
  - **Past Experience:**
      - Built the first customer-facing team and processes at Notaro (practice management software).
      - Spent four years at SEO software company Moz.
  - **Motivation:** Drawn to the role as a return to the SEO space, now with an AI focus.

### AI Experience & Mindset

  - **Docebo Context:** The company is "AI-first," requiring regular training and hands-on tool use.
  - **Tool Usage:**
      - **Generative AI (ChatGPT, Gemini):** Speeds up client presentation prep.
      - **Internal Agentic AI:** Centralizes documentation for quick reporting (e.g., renewal reports), saving time.
  - **Key Insight:** Ortiz actively tests internal tools and provides feedback on accuracy and quality—a mindset George Haikal noted as critical for GrowthX's rapid iteration.

### Strategic Client Management Examples

  - **Case 1: New LMS Client (Proactive Strategy)**
      - **Problem:** A new client's platform setup was suboptimal, jeopardizing long-term goals.
      - **Action:** Ortiz secured buy-in for a full rebuild by explaining the "why" and its impact on objectives.
      - **Method:** Presented a clear action plan, timeline, and a "before/after" report to demonstrate the value of accurate data segmentation.
      - **Outcome:** Client aligned on the rebuild; Ortiz increased check-ins to bi-weekly to support the transition.
  - **Case 2: Lyft Renewal (Turnaround & Upsell)**
      - **Problem:** Inherited the Lyft account with a renewal in three months and low seat utilization (100/2000 seats).
      - **Action:** Identified the risk → partnered with the Account Manager → demoed new AI tools to Lyft's executive team.
      - **Outcome:** Lyft was impressed by the time-saving potential → renewed and purchased a paid AI upsell.

### GrowthX's AI-SEO (AEO) Model

  - **Core Strategy:** Optimize client websites for AI models, not just human users.
  - **Key Metrics Tracked:**
      - **Visibility:** Is the brand appearing in AI-generated answers?
      - **Citations:** Is client content being linked and cited as a source? (Most controllable & important)
      - **Prompt Tracking:** Are we answering the specific questions users ask?
  - **Goal:** Use these signals to build a "content operating system" that informs strategy and drives results, moving beyond simple blog posts.
  - **Competitors:** Ahrefs, Graphite (content); Profound (AEO visibility).

### Company Culture & Logistics

  - **Culture:** A "scrappy," nimble environment that rewards initiative and performance.
  - **Travel:** Not required.
      - **Onsite Option:** New hires can visit the SF office for one week for onboarding.
      - **Client Visits:** Optional for engagement managers to attend conferences with local clients.
