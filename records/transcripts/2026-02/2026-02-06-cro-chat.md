## Overview

- **CRO is the top priority.** Hitting the 2026 lead goal requires an unrealistic 8x traffic increase at current conversion rates. The focus must be on significantly improving the conversion rate (CR).
- **The trial form is the biggest blocker.** Its length creates significant friction. A redesign (e.g., multi-step) is the highest-impact, fastest-lift opportunity.
- **Messaging needs a strategic shift.** Move from a feature-focused message to a "pain point → path to relief" narrative to drive larger, 2x+ CR lifts.
- **Paid ads are underfunded for learning.** Current spend ($3k–$5k/mo) is insufficient to gather meaningful data. A budget of \~$10k/mo is the typical threshold needed to find efficiency.

---

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Aggressive Goals & Low Conversion

  - **2026 Lead Goal:** 570 leads (trials + demos).
  - **Current Performance:**
      - Monthly Traffic: \~17,000 sessions.
      - Conversion Rate (CR): \~0.07% (from all traffic) or \~0.26% (from engaged sessions).
  - **The Math:** At the current CR, hitting the goal requires an unrealistic 8x traffic increase. A more feasible 2–3x traffic increase still requires a 2.5x CR improvement.

### Solution 1: Fix the Trial Form

  - **Primary Blocker:** The long, single-page trial form is the main source of conversion friction.
  - **Recommended Fix:** Redesign the form to be more approachable.
      - **Multi-step Flow:** Break the form into smaller, logical steps (e.g., Homebase). This reduces perceived effort and maintains momentum.
      - **Field Audit:** Debate internally which fields are truly critical for trial sign-up versus which can be collected later in the user journey.

### Solution 2: Shift Messaging to Pain Points

  - **Current Approach:** Messaging focuses on product clarity and validation ("what it does").
  - **Recommended Shift:** Adopt a "pain point → path to relief" narrative.
      - **Rationale:** This approach resonates more deeply with user needs and can drive larger, 2x+ CR lifts, unlike smaller tweaks (5–20% lift).
      - **Example Pain Points:**
          - Uncentralized communication (using texting).
          - Lack of frontline visibility for leadership.
      - **Execution:** Use customer research (e.g., sales call transcripts, focus groups) to identify and frame these core pain points.

### Solution 3: Optimize Other Channels

  - **Homepage CTAs:**
      - **Action:** Test a single "Get Started Free" CTA (since the trial is free forever) to reduce user choice friction.
      - **Note:** Dual CTAs can also work; test the impact of removing the "Watch Video" button.
  - **Blog CTAs:**
      - **Action:** Test a persistent, sticky CTA bar in blog posts to increase visibility and capture more traffic.
  - **Paid Ads:**
      - **Issue:** Current spend ($3k–$5k/mo) is insufficient to gather enough data to find efficiency.
      - **Action:** Consider a larger, time-boxed experiment (e.g., $10k/mo) to determine if paid search is a viable channel or if paid social is a better fit.
