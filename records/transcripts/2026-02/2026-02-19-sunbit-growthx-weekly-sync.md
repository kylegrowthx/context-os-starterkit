## Key Topics

### Content Production Bottleneck

  - **Problem:** Publishing is delayed, which negatively impacts performance.
  - **Root Cause:** The GrowthX webhook is not syncing article images to WordPress.
  - **Solution:** Jason will configure the webhook to push images to WordPress.
  - **Process Improvement:**
      - Legal review is no longer required for certain topics, accelerating the workflow.
      - GrowthX offered to assist with publishing to clear the backlog.
      - A duplicate article was sent; Angel will delete it.

### Sunbit.ai Content Strategy

  - **Goal:** Build investor credibility by first earning the trust of a technical audience.
  - **Target Audience:** Engineers.
  - **Content Model:** "Smart Explainers" → Break down complex topics with technical depth for engineers, while making the "what" and "why" accessible to non-technical readers.
  - **Content Sourcing:** Repurpose Sunbit's internal expertise to reduce the team's writing burden.
      - **Sources:** Academic research (including Hebrew-language papers), webinars, podcasts, and "brain dumps" from technical experts.
      - **Rationale:** This approach is less intimidating for engineers than writing from scratch.

### Sunbit.ai Site & Pricing

  - **Site:** Sunbit is already building its AI blog on WordPress, making GrowthX's proposed site build unnecessary.
  - **Design:** The proposal included a dark-mode, monospace-font theme to appeal to engineers.
  - **Pricing:** GrowthX will provide a revised quote for editorial content only.
      - **Initial Quote:** $15k/month for editorial, plus a separate site build/launch fee.
      - **Revised Quote:** A package price for editorial, integrated with the existing GrowthX agreement.

### B2C Content Templating

  - **Proposal:** Template \~50% of B2C page content to bypass legal review bottlenecks.
  - **Templated Elements:** Branded positioning, competitor comparisons (e.g., CareCredit), disclaimers, and shop directories.
  - **Workflow:** GrowthX's pipeline can push content directly to WordPress fields. Sunbit's agency can build the required modules.

### Performance & AI Visibility

  - **User Growth:** Averaging \~80 users/day in Jan, \~100/day in Feb.
  - **Clicks:** Up 84% for search listing breakout pages, driven by the Dental category.
  - **New Content:** 8 new articles in the "auto tips" subcategory are already generating clicks.
  - **AI Visibility:** Sunbit has strong visibility against competitors, though it trails CareCredit, which has a larger B2C ad spend.

### Leadership Update

  - **New Hire:** Sunbit has a new CMO, formerly of Meta (Ray-Ban launch) and Apple (marketing strategy).
  - **Focus:** The new CMO will focus on high-level consumer awareness, not day-to-day operations.
  - **Action:** Jason will introduce the CMO to GrowthX.
