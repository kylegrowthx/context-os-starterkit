## Decisions & Commitments

- **Primary Metric Shift:** Citation rate becomes the primary optimization metric, replacing website traffic focus.
- **Portfolio Optimization:** Apply portfolio approach (high, mid, low performer optimization) to Cashflow Desk articles across all performance tiers.
- **Correlation Testing:** Measure direct correlation between Cashflow Desk citations and RAMP.com AI visibility score using controlled experiments (narrow treasury topic as proof of concept).
- **Data Points to Track:** Citation frequency for RAMP-mention articles, citation frequency for non-RAMP articles, time-to-citation from publish date.
- **Independence Maintained:** Cashflow Desk continues as editorially independent, requiring no disclosure of RAMP relationship for now; RAMP must not be referred to as a bank in treasury discussions.
