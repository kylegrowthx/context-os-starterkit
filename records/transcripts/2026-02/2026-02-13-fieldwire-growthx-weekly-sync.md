## Context

## Key Topics

### Competitor Landscape Analysis

  - GrowthX analyzed 92k+ keywords from 5 competitors (Procore, Autodesk, Racken, Buildertrend, HCSS) to find organic gaps.
  - **Procore:** Lacks content depth and LLM optimization.
  - **Autodesk:** Strong on branded and high-value product pages.
  - **Racken:** Strong on safety and comparison content ("Racken vs. Fieldwire").
  - **Buildertrend:** Focuses on residential; weak on trade-specific content.
  - **HCSS:** Focuses on civil construction; has a dry, enterprise tone.

### Proposed Content Clusters (Initial)

  - **1. Construction:** Target middle- and bottom-of-funnel (MoFu/BoFu) keywords like "construction plan viewer."
      - **Feedback:** This attracts low-quality leads (e.g., "Blueprint app" users) who don't convert. The angle must be refined to focus on Fieldwire's core strength: working off plans *on the job site*.
  - **2. Trade-Specific Content:** Optimize existing pages and create new ones for trades like painting and roofing.
      - **Feedback:** This is a high-priority opportunity to own the trade contractor niche. New trade pages must be prioritized with the sales team.
  - **3. Competitor Alternatives:** Create content for high-intent searches like "Procore Alternatives."
      - **Feedback:** Strong alignment. "Versus" content is a proven success driver.
  - **4. Photo Documentation & Progress Tracking:** Target low-competition keywords.
      - **Feedback:** Split this cluster.
          - **Photo Documentation:** Viable, as it's a core feature.
          - **Progress Tracking:** Not a current product strength.
  - **5. Daily Reports & Field Documentation:** Optimize existing content and create templates.
      - **Feedback:** Important for construction teams; aligned.
  - **6. Task Management:** Target a small, high-conversion cluster.
      - **Feedback:** High priority. Creating a task is the key user engagement metric and the current Google Ads optimization goal.

### New Strategic Priorities

  - **Integrations:** Create content for a connected ecosystem, including "Product A + Product B" comparison pages.
  - **AI / Field Intelligence:** Build thought leadership for the May launch.
      - **Strategy:** Target both direct AI searches and outcome-based searches (e.g., "how to speed up daily tasks") to capture users seeking benefits.
  - **Verticals:** Develop content for high-growth segments like data centers, industrial, and energy.
  - **"Construction Management Software" Cluster:** Re-evaluate this hyper-competitive topic.
      - **Opportunity:** Create deep, trade-specific BoFu content (e.g., "Construction Management Software for Electricians") to capture high-intent users.

### Content Localization

  - **Problem:** Content written for the US market uses terminology (e.g., "Punch List") that differs from other English-speaking countries (e.g., "Snag List" in the UK).
  - **Solution:** GrowthX can create a "localization checklist" artifact for its AI pipeline to automatically adapt content for specific regions.
