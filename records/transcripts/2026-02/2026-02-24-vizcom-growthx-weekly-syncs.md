**To GrowthX Delivery:**

**To GrowthX Business Development:**

- Opportunity to prove deep content strategy value in vertical-focused, authority-building play (vs. surface-level social campaigns).
- UTM tracking and attribution data collection (signups, demos) during this 3-month window provides concrete QBR evidence for renewal negotiation.

**To CheckThat:**

- **New 3-Month Contract:** The contract was cut from 12 to 3 months, creating urgency to prove ROI and secure a full renewal.
- **Strategic Pivot:** The strategy shifts from social media to a long-term organic search play, building authority via a new `/resources` hub and Vizcom University.
- **Automated Publishing:** A Webflow API will automate publishing, removing a manual bottleneck and freeing up Vizcom's time.
- **Diversified Voice:** Content will feature multiple experts (e.g., Product Experts, Product Designers) to build broader credibility, moving away from a single "Jordan's voice" approach.

## Key Topics

### New Contract & Urgency

  - Jordan's involvement increases for the next 3 months to gain clarity on GrowthX's value.
  - The contract was cut from 12 months to 3, creating pressure to prove ROI and secure a full renewal.
  - **Goal:** Use this period to build a compounding asset (organic search) that justifies the long-term investment.

### New Content Strategy

  - **Pillar:** Build authority by teaching designers *how to think*, not just *how to do*.
  - **New Hub:** Create a separate `/resources` hub for guides and thought leadership, distinct from the main blog.
  - **Vizcom University:** A key component for video content, featuring a diversified faculty (Product Experts, Product Designers) to build broader credibility.
      - **Content:** Industry-specific mini-courses (e.g., prompting for automotive) and lessons on design frameworks.
  - **Content Mix:** A phased shift to prioritize high-value editorial content.
      - **Phase 1:** 5 blog articles/week + 2 editorials/week
      - **Phase 2:** 2 blog articles/week + 4 editorials/week
      - **Phase 3:** Editorial content only

### Operational Plan

  - **Automated Publishing:** A Webflow API will automate publishing, removing a manual bottleneck for Vizcom.
      - **Process:** GrowthX stages content → Vizcom reviews → Vizcom clicks "Ready for publishing" → Content auto-publishes.
  - **Author Attribution:**
      - **Interim:** Sophia Silver will be the author on all new content.
      - **Long-Term:** Kim Lu will coordinate with Nico (Head of Product Experts) to assign articles to relevant PEs, leveraging their industry authority.
  - **Tracking & Reporting:**
      - GrowthX will implement UTM tracking on blog CTAs to measure user journeys.
      - Vizcom will provide attribution data (signups, demos) for the QBR to prove ROI.
