## Key Topics

### Wisdom.ai's Content Challenge

  - **Goal:** Scale SEO and AEO content to drive visibility.
  - **Current Agencies:** Two agencies produce content, but their SEO results are limited (\~1k new sessions/month), and they lack AEO expertise.
  - **Competitors:** Snowflake, Databricks, Sigma, ThoughtSpot, Triplo, Looker.

### GrowthX's AI-Driven Solution

  - **Model:** Combines agency expertise with AI workflows to scale content production.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Strategy Sprint (4-week lead time):** Define custom AI artifacts (personas, voice) to personalize content generation.
    2.  **Content Production:** Publish 5 articles/week, with drafts shared via Slack for feedback.
    3.  **Publishing:** GrowthX handles staging and publishing.

  - **Focus:** Prioritizes on-site content (blog, guides) as the most controllable and effective channel for AEO.

### Expected Outcomes

  - **Timeline:** 3–6 months to see significant results.
  - **Case Studies:**
      - **Augment Code:** 3x traffic (25k → 75k sessions/mo), replaced paid ads, and secured top-ranking AEO citations.
