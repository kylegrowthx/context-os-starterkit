## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Feedback

  - **Production:** 16 blogs are published, with 9 in Elaine's review queue and 3 undergoing GrowthX revisions.
  - **Performance:** All published blogs are generating sessions, validating the strategy.
  - **PMM Feedback:**

  - **MDR Topics:** New MDR topics are with Jen (PMM) for approval via Airtable. This is not a production blocker, as the team has 3 weeks of approved topics.

### Strategy: Listicles for LLM Visibility

  - **Problem:** Competitors (e.g., Huntress) are using listicles to capture prime real estate in LLM responses (e.g., Perplexity, Google AI Overviews).
  - **Solution:** Publish N-able-authored listicles comparing platforms to influence LLM outputs.
  - **Internal Concern:** N-able PMMs worry this tactic feels inauthentic or gives competitors "airtime."
  - **GrowthX Rationale:** This is a proven strategy for gaining LLM visibility, as demonstrated by other GrowthX clients (e.g., Tavern, Seat).
  - **Path Forward:** GrowthX will prepare 1–2 test listicle ideas to prove the concept after the current MDR topic batch is approved.

### Tooling: CheckVat for LLM Tracking

  - **Purpose:** Track N-able's visibility in LLM responses across key queries.
  - **Current Wins:** New articles are already being cited in Google AI Overviews for prompts like "best ways to scale my MSP business."
  - **Features:**
      - **Prompt Library:** Tracks performance against a shared library of high-value industry prompts.
      - **Historical Data:** Provides historical performance data, unlike many forward-only tools.
      - **Custom Prompts:** Allows tracking for specific keywords targeted by new content.
      - **Competitor Tracking:** Monitors N-able's presence vs. key competitors.
      - **Tagging:** Categorizes prompts by persona, funnel stage, etc., for granular reporting.
  - **Access:** N-able will receive access next week.
  - **Insight:** The tool confirms that top-cited sources in LLM responses are frequently listicle-style blogs.
