## Key Topics

### Tines's Need & Concerns

  - Tines is considering a \~$250k partnership with GrowthX to drive long-tail traffic for its new self-serve plans.
  - Michael O'Connell requires a meeting with CEO Marcel before committing to the investment, citing the need to vet the leadership behind the proposed "unusual billing terms."
  - Key concerns for Tines:
      - **Quality Control:** How GrowthX maintains quality at scale, especially with a distributed team.
      - **Internal Resourcing:** How Tines can effectively manage the partnership without a dedicated Head of Content.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** An "end-to-end partner" using AI workflows and human experts to deliver content at scale.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks)**
          - Develop "artifacts" (deep-dive documents on personas, products) to serve as custom inputs for AI workflows.
          - Deliver a content strategy and roadmap.
          - Produce 10+ initial articles to calibrate on quality.
      - **Phase 2: Production (Ongoing)**
          - Scale to 5 articles/week.
          - Provide a Looker dashboard for weekly performance review (traffic, conversions).
          - Track AI search visibility and share of voice against competitors.
          - Continuously update artifacts with client feedback to refine brand voice.
  - **Team & Quality Control:**
      - The delivery team is primarily in-house staff with deep organic growth experience (e.g., ex-GitHub, HP).
      - AI workflows handle research and drafting, while human experts provide strategic direction and final edits.
      - GrowthX limits new clients to two per week to ensure quality control.

### Potential Use Cases

  - **UGC Optimization:** Michael O'Connell proposed using GrowthX's model to optimize Tines's user-generated content (UGC) story library.
  - **GrowthX's Relevant Experience:**
      - **Zapier:** Explored building their templates library.
      - **Lovable:** Used their API to create search-optimized apps for a template library.
