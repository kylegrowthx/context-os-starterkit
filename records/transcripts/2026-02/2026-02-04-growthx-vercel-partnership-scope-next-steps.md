## Summary

GrowthX and Vercel locked in a 12-month partnership agreement with a ramped fee structure ($20k/mo for months 1–6, $30k/mo for months 7–12) to accelerate SEO and AI visibility across Vercel's platform. Project kickoff confirmed for February 23rd, with content launching on the new /i index section to bypass approval gatekeeping and enable rapid iteration.

---

## Context

GrowthX and Vercel have been negotiating a content marketing partnership focused on AI visibility and SEO growth. The original Statement of Work proposed a two-phase approach (Phase 1: $40k flat fee for strategy; Phase 2: ongoing execution) with a six-month term. However, this structure conflicted with Vercel's budget constraints and GrowthX's critical need for annual recurring revenue (ARR) to support headcount growth. Cody Henshaw (Vercel's Director of Digital Growth) has budget clearance from Vercel's CMO and COO to move forward but needs a clean procurement path through Vercel's Zip vendor platform. Marcel Santilli emphasized that GrowthX's financial runway depends on ARR contracts and monthly cash flow, making the deal structure as important as the scope. The conversation centered on restructuring the commercial terms to align with Vercel's budget cycle while securing the 12-month ARR commitment GrowthX requires. Cody recently expanded his role to oversee all of Vercel's digital strategy—managing over 100 properties across Next.js, V0, Nuxt, ShadCN, and various open-source projects, with plans to consolidate fragmented sites similar to work Cody had previously led at Redis (which drove significant traffic with minimal SEO loss).

## Decisions & Commitments

**Commercial Terms (Approved):**
- Single 12-month contract (no Phase 1 flat fee)
- Months 1–6: $20k/month (discounted; accounts for Vercel's current agency spend)
- Months 7–12: $30k/month (full scope; Cody has budget approval after other agency contract expires)
- Eliminates procurement friction while hitting GrowthX's ARR requirement

**Scope (Locked):**
- Full editorial + programmatic SEO across all channels for 12 months
- Same scope for all 12 months (no scope reduction during discount period)
- Content launches on /i index (new, flexible section bypassing main blog approvals)
- Initial levers: AI Gateway optimization, buyer-intent guides, emerging "Stacks" pages

**Procurement & Kickoff:**
- Cody signs updated SoW as letter of intent (no pre-signature legal review needed)
- SoW submitted to Zip platform for formal vendor onboarding, legal review (MSA redlines), and PO issuance
- Kickoff date: **February 23rd** (allows time for Zip process and GrowthX pre-work)
- Cody to confirm Zip/MSA/PO turnaround ETA

**Partnership Scope (Strategic):**
- GrowthX provides feedback and engineering resources for Vercel's LLM visibility tool (to be open-sourced)
- Marcel allocated 7 engineers on CheckThat; flexible to shape LLM tool based on Vercel's priorities
- GrowthX mapping: buyer-question research, internal stakeholder interviews, content strategy

**Data & Access:**
- View-only Amplitude access (via Rudderstack CDP) for analytics
- Google Search Console (GSC) access for search performance tracking
- No third-party pixels or tracking scripts (GA4 off-limits per Vercel policy)
- Data ingestion deferred until contracts finalized
