## Context

Discern is a B2B SaaS product in the registered agent services space serving upper-mid-market companies. Discern hired GrowthX in mid-2025 to build organic search presence against legacy incumbents (CSC, CT Corp, and Northwest Registered Agent). Starting with minimal organic visibility, GrowthX implemented a scalable PSEO engine delivering ~25 articles weekly. Eight months into the engagement, Discern has achieved 757 ranking pages, 5-6x traffic growth, and validated organic as a high-value channel (~$130k closed ARR from 13 customers). The April 2026 renewal represents a critical inflection point: Discern must decide whether to maintain current output or invest in conversion optimization and content refresh to accelerate ROI.

      - Dedicated conversion landing pages.

  - **Challenge:** Balance net-new content for growth with refreshes for maintenance.
  - **Rationale:**
      - Mature clusters (e.g., Franchise Taxes) see growth level off (\~5%), requiring refreshes to maintain traffic.
      - AI visibility favors content \<3–6 months old, making refreshes essential for defensibility.
  - **Proposed Solution:** Shift from ad-hoc to a proactive, scheduled refresh cadence.

### Renewal & Spend

  - **Discern's Plan to Accelerate ROI:**
      - Add retargeting email automation (now with dedicated headcount).

  - **GrowthX Renewal Options:**
      - **Flat Renewal:** Sustain current momentum with 25 PSEO articles/week and ad-hoc refreshes.
