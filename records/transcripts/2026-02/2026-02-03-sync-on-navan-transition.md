## Key Topics

### Client Strategy & Priorities

  - **Primary Goal:** Reduce paid search spend by capturing BoFu traffic with organic content.
  - **Content Focus:**
      - **BoFu:** Comparison listicles and vs. pages are the top priority.
      - **AI Cluster:** Supporting a new AI campaign with updated messaging.
      - **Expense Category:** Building authority with credit card-related keywords where Navan has a weak presence.
  - **Client Context:**
      - **Target Market:** Enterprise clients.
      - **Competitors:** Brex and Ramp are investing heavily in SEO; Navan avoids being seen in the same category as smaller players like Engine.
      - **Performance:** Early results show high average position rankings (often on page one) due to Navan's high domain authority.

### Content Production & Legal Process

  - **Legal Review:** Now bypassed, removing the primary production bottleneck.
  - **New Legal Requirements:**
      - Substantiate all facts with Google Doc links.
      - Do not link directly to competitor websites.
      - Use internal comparison pages as the source for competitor data.
      - Include the disclaimer: "Competitive data was collected and subject to change or update."
  - **Artifact Creation:** Create an artifact from Navan's comparison pages (e.g., screenshots) to serve as a pre-approved data source for content.
  - **Content Pipeline:**
      - **Cluster-Level Value Props/CTAs:** Programmatically inserts tailored CTAs based on content cluster.
      - **Statistics Artifact:** Populates articles with data from Navan's gated reports to build authority.

### Content Refresh Strategy

  - **Priority:** New content publication for the next month.
  - **Low Priority:** Glossary content refresh (AI-generated, awareness-focused).
  - **Future Focus:** Refreshing blog content.
      - **Data Source:** GSC clicks and GA4 demo events.
      - **Selection Criteria:** Commercial intent and significant traffic loss.
      - **Action:** Erik to update the refresh candidate list with new GSC data in \~1 month.

### Client Management & Feedback

  - **Feedback Style:** Navan's content team (Rich, Libby) provides line edits but rarely gives high-level strategic feedback.
  - **Action:** Erik must proactively solicit feedback to ensure content quality and alignment.
  - **Nima (Decision-Maker):**

      - **Inquiry:** Asked about the search volume of Check That prompts; Erik should be prepared to explain the "black box" nature of LLMs and the strategy of targeting simple, BoFu queries.

### Internal Team & Content Quality
