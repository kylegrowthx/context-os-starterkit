### Sales Philosophy & Tactics

  - **Core Philosophy:** Act as an educator, not a salesperson.
      - Align solutions to specific business problems, connecting them to high-level initiatives to secure C-suite buy-in.
  - **Outbound Tactic:** Use timely, relevant hooks to drive engagement.
      - **Example:** A World Series-themed campaign offering a hat if a local team lost.
      - **Rationale:** Creates a fun, human connection that stands out from generic outreach.
  - **Deal Management:** Build authentic relationships throughout the cycle.
      - **Example (Common Room):** Created dedicated Slack channels for trial customers to provide real-time value (e.g., sending leads, relevant collateral).

### Case Study: Blue Sight Health System Deal

  - **Context:** A self-sourced deal at Blue Sight, an LA health system the company had long pursued.
  - **Land & Expand Strategy:**
      - **Land:** $80k initial deal for one product at one hospital.
      - **Expand:** \~$400k total revenue from full suite adoption across three hospitals.
  - **Key Success Factors:**
      - **Relationship Building:** On-site visits were critical for building trust and getting key stakeholders in a room.
      - **Partnership Framing:** Positioned Blue Sight as a full partner, not just a vendor, which was more appealing than competitors' cheaper, less comprehensive solutions.
  - **Self-Assessment:** Rated personal performance 7.5/10.
      - **Improvement Area:** Should have pursued a full system-wide deal from the start, involving colleagues earlier to present the complete solution suite.
