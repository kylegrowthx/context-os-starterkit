### Process & Workflow

  - **Feedback Channel:**
      - **Decision:** Use Google Docs for all content feedback.
      - **Rationale:** Ensures immediate email notifications and avoids context-switching between tools.
  - **Author Attribution:**
      - **Decision:** Continue using real author names.

  - **Artifact Maintenance:**
      - The AI pipeline's "artifacts" (knowledge base) are updated based on feedback and product changes.

      - **Blaxel's Concern:** Programmatic content that generates code could be high-maintenance.
