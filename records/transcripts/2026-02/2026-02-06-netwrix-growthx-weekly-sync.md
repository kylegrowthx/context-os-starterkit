### Competitor Comparison Content

  - **Decision:** Netwrix will now create competitor comparison content.
      - **Rationale:** John Knightly confirmed this is a strategic imperative. Mariel will clear any remaining legal concerns.
  - **Publishing Strategy:**
      - **Location:** Publish on Netwrix's own website.
          - **Rationale:** Builds owned assets, drives traffic, and ensures brand control in LLM citations.
      - **Visibility:** Articles can be published but hidden from the main blog index page, making them discoverable by search engines and LLMs without cluttering the user-facing blog.

  - **Status:**
      - **Calibration Article:** Staged in Sanity, ready for Netwrix to publish.
      - **Shadow AI Security Risks:** Delivered.
      - **Team Sprawl:** Delivering EOD.

      - **Process:** Publish articles after Mariel's initial review, before final PMM approval.
      - **Authoring:** GrowthX will manage author approvals directly with Netwrix's pre-approved author list.

### Performance Tracking & Reporting

  - **Looker Dashboard:**
      - **Purpose:** Track organic search performance of GrowthX content.
      - **Data Sources:** Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Search Console (GSC).
      - **Key Reports:**
          - **Engagement Rate:** GA4 data on engaged sessions (\>10s, scroll, click).
          - **Non-Branded Traffic:** GSC data on traffic from non-branded keywords.
          - **Cohort Report:** Performance of GrowthX topic clusters and URLs.
          - **LLM Referral Breakdown:** GA4 data on traffic from LLMs (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  - **CheckThot Platform:**
      - **Purpose:** Measure AI visibility by tracking how LLMs cite Netwrix content.
      - **Methodology:** Uses a shared index of 100k+ evaluation-stage prompts (e.g., "best PAM software") to simulate real-world executive research.
      - **Value:** Proactively shapes Netwrix's narrative in LLMs, preventing competitors from controlling the story.
