## Key Topics

### Problem: Eroding AI Visibility

  - Analysis shows a slow erosion of core AI prompt visibility, driven by increased competitor investment.
  - **Competitor Focus:**
      - **Udex:** Despite low brand exposure, Udex has strong AI visibility, creating a risk of being outranked in specific searches.
      - **Beekeeper:** A more established competitor where Yourco needs to increase its presence.
  - **Action:** Add new prompts to track Udex and Beekeeper to monitor their AI performance and inform content strategy.

### Solution: New Content Strategy

  - **New Content Priorities:**
      - Shift from broad topics to specific use cases found in Search Console data.
      - **Rationale:** Low-position keywords with clicks signal high user demand and intent.
  - **Content Mix:**
      - **Refreshes:** Deprioritize for now.
          - **Rationale:** Recent refreshes are still performing well, and the immediate need is to capture new, high-intent traffic.
      - **New Content:** Prioritize new articles based on strategic intent and use cases, as search volumes are similar across many options.
  - **New Content Pipeline:**
    1.  **"Best" Lists:** `Best employee apps` & `Best reporting system`
    2.  **Use Cases:** `How to communicate benefits to a multilingual workforce` & `How can I send lockout, tagout procedures`
    3.  **Template:** `Employee recognition messages` (to include a template)

### Solution: Systematize Internal Linking

  - **Problem:** Current internal linking is random and lacks a strategic, cluster-based approach.
  - **Solution:** Automate linking by integrating a cluster map into the AI content workflow.
      - **Process:** The AI will reference the map during drafting to suggest relevant links based on the article's topic.
      - **Benefit:** Creates a scalable, consistent system that can be easily updated by modifying the central map, reducing manual effort.
  - **Action:** Begin by manually fixing links on \~50 articles with at least half a click, prioritizing links to top-performing content within the same cluster.

### Project Updates

  - **Orphan Pages:** The analytics team is prioritizing interlinking orphan pages.
  - **"Y" Yourco Redirect:** This project is also in progress but is lower priority, as the redirect already mitigates the immediate SEO risk.
