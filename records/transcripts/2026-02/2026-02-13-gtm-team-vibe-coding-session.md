### AI-Driven Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** Manual content research is slow and inefficient.
  - **Solution:** A new workflow automates content ideation.
    1.  **Input:** Provide a list of competitor websites.
    2.  **Keyword Extraction:** Cursor pulls non-branded organic keywords from those sites.
    3.  **Pillar Creation:** Keywords are grouped into strategic pillars (e.g., "AI Tools," "SEO Optimization").
    4.  **Title Generation:** Cursor creates article titles (listicles, glossaries) for each pillar.
  - **Validation:** The process confirmed competitors Danni had already identified (HubSpot, SEMrush) and suggested new ones (SuperPath).

### Rapid Web App Prototyping

  - **Goal:** Convert the "Fletch Checklist" into a lead-gen web app.
  - **Method:** "Vibe coding" with Cursor and Lovable to create a functional prototype for designers.
  - **Workflow:**
      - **Input:** The checklist content was converted to Markdown.
      - **Cursor Prompt:** "Make a quiz that you make progress in. What do you get? Give it a full breakdown and summary of the results."
  - **Result:** Cursor generated a polished, 3-file (HTML/CSS/JS) quiz.
      - **UX Flow:** Intro → 12 Yes/No questions → Email gate → Results summary.
  - **Rationale:** The Yes/No format was chosen for its low friction, which maximizes completion rates.

### Automated Social Media Publishing

  - **Problem:** Manual social media scheduling is a bottleneck.
  - **Solution 1: Ordinal Integration**
      - A Cursor skill now queues LinkedIn posts directly to Ordinal.

  - **Solution 2: Opus 1 for Video**

      - **Next Step:** Connect Opus 1 to the Ahrefs-driven content strategy to create high-volume, keyword-targeted video content.
