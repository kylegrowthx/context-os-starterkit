## Key Topics

### Website Launch Issues

  - The new website launch introduced critical issues impacting user experience and SEO.
  - **Trial Signup Form:** The form is broken, which directly blocks new user acquisition.
  - **404 Errors:** Top-performing blog pages are returning 404s, causing significant traffic loss and SEO damage.
      - **Example:** The "workforce optimization software" article, a key traffic driver, is inaccessible.
  - **GrowthX Support:** Matt will compile a list of all broken pages and can connect Siit with GrowthX's tech SEO team for support.

### Content Strategy Shift: Traffic → Conversion

  - **Problem:** The current content strategy drives high traffic but has a low conversion rate, as the funnel from content to conversion is too steep.
  - **Solution:** Shift focus to bottom-funnel content to better capture high-intent users and support the Q1 app access campaign.
  - **Execution Plan:**
    1.  **Refresh Top-Performing Blogs:** Optimize existing content for conversion.
    2.  **Build Comparison Landing Pages:** Create dedicated pages to capture traffic from tool reviews.

### Workstream 1: Blog Refresh for Conversion

  - **Goal:** Reposition top-performing blogs to be more actionable and bottom-funnel.
  - **New Elements:**
      - **TLDR Sections:** Add a summary at the top of each article to immediately answer search intent.
          - **Rationale:** Proven to increase visibility in LLM search results (e.g., Homebase case study).
      - **FAQ Sections:** Add to all older articles to improve LLM visibility.
  - **Customer Quotes:**
      - **Goal:** Integrate relevant customer quotes into articles.
      - **Process:** Carmel will provide quotes for specific articles (e.g., a Microsoft Teams quote for the "Microsoft Teams ticketing" article).
      - **Automation:** GrowthX can build a pipeline to automate quote insertion from a database if the volume becomes too high for manual selection.

### Workstream 2: Comparison Landing Pages

  - **Goal:** Create a dedicated conversion step for high-intent traffic from tool review pages.
  - **Rationale:** Tool review pages drive significant traffic (\~1,750 sessions/week), but the current CTA (a generic free trial) is ineffective for users actively comparing software.
  - **Plan:**
      - Build dedicated landing pages comparing Siit to top competitors (e.g., Jira, ServiceNow).
      - **Placement:** Link to these pages from relevant tool reviews via banners or dedicated sections.
      - **Design:** The layout will be standard and functional, not overly aesthetic, to resonate with Siit's IT audience.
