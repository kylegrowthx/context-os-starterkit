## Summary

Company all-hands following Check That's launch and board meeting. Company is scaling from 20 to 91 people in one year, hitting $34M ARR target on core platform with Strategy Sprints converting at 80–85% and Check That capturing 1.4M impressions at launch. Key actions: platform/services decoupling shipping within one month; OS rewrite (formerly Atlas) releasing early March; Check That manual engagement and onboarding rollout underway.

---

## Context

This all-hands marked the company's first full-team sync post-launch of Check That and set strategic frame for 2026. The organization is decoupling platform (AI-powered management, creation, and optimization of website content at scale) from services (managed consulting), making platform the core product and services an optional add-on for clients who want hands-on strategy. Churn from Surge ($2.7M), Okta, and Superhuman in Q1 created an ARR gap, putting Strategy Sprint conversion (targeting 80%+) and pipeline velocity as critical levers. The company transitioned from 20 to 91 people in 12 months, requiring investment in process, narrative (moving away from "content agency" framing toward "compounding growth engine"), and platform-driven retention. Check That launched as lead gen and visibility dataset; 1.4M impressions and ~200 signups established product-market presence.
