## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Word Count

  - **Problem:** Internal feedback questioned the length of the first calibration article (3k+ words).
  - **Decision:** Adopt a dual strategy to match content length to its purpose.
      - **Short Form (800–1.5k words):** For product launches, integration announcements, and other news.
      - **Long Form (3k+ words):** For top-of-funnel SEO articles requiring in-depth coverage.

### SEO Strategy & Keyword Focus

  - **Problem:** Initial keyword targets (e.g., "Agentic Commerce News," KD 60) are too difficult for Talon.One's domain authority (DA 36).
  - **Solution:** Pivot to lower-difficulty, marketer-focused terms.
      - **Example:** "What is agentic commerce for marketers?"
      - **Rationale:** This targets users in the consideration phase who are actively seeking implementation guidance.
  - **Opportunity:** The term "Agentic Commerce loyalty program" is new and underserved, presenting a chance for early authority.

### Competitor Comparison Articles

  - **Rationale:** To address internal requests and capture high-intent search traffic from users in the consideration phase.
  - **Execution:**
      - **Tone:** Factual and neutral, not negative.
      - **Focus:** Emphasize Talon.One's strengths and features.
      - **Assets:** Embed videos to increase engagement.
      - **Data Source:** Use Talon.One's existing battle cards for accuracy, with product marketing oversight.
