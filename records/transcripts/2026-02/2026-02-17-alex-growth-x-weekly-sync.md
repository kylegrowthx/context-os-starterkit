## Key Topics

### SEO Performance & Volatility

  - **Problem:** Significant SEO volatility is causing unstable keyword rankings.
  - **Impact:** Key keyword positions are dropping:
      - "AI recruiter": 12 → 25 (13 positions)
      - "AI interviewer": 19 positions
      - "recruiter AI": 20 positions
  - **Context:** While volatile, this represents progress from a non-ranked position (\~90) in December.

### Competitor Analysis (HeyMilo)

  - **Goal:** Understand how competitor HeyMilo dominates "AI interviewer" and "AI recruiter" keywords despite a lower Authority Score (26) and fewer referring domains (\~500 vs. alex.com's \~800).
  - **Key Findings:**
      - **G2 Award:** A recent \#1 ranking on G2 for "AI Recruiter" likely provided a major, temporary ranking boost.
      - **Targeted Backlink Strategy:** HeyMilo earns hundreds of backlinks on individual blog posts, a key differentiator from alex.com's strategy.
          - Example: "AI Candidate Screening" post has 145 backlinks.
      - **Homepage Keyword Density:** HeyMilo's homepage explicitly uses target keywords in its H1 and H2s.

### Homepage SEO Strategy

  - **Goal:** Stabilize rankings by making the homepage's purpose clearer to search engines.
  - **Proposed Changes:**
      - **Title Tag:** Change from "AI recruiting platform..." to "Your AI recruiter to interview everyone, hire the best | Alex."
          - **Rationale:** Explicitly targets the high-value keyword "AI recruiter."
      - **H1 Tag:** Consolidate the main headline into a single, comprehensive H1.
          - **Rationale:** Provides a clear, singular signal to search engines about the page's primary topic.
      - **Content:** Increase the density of "AI interviewer" and "AI interviewing" keywords.

### Content & Publishing Plan

  - **New Articles:** All approved articles will be published within the next few days.
  - **Existing Articles:** Carly will refresh existing articles with new internal links and images.
      - **Process:** Republish refreshed articles immediately upon completion.
      - **Rationale:** This provides a steady stream of updated content, which can improve SEO.

### Answer Engine Optimization (AEO) Tracking

  - **Tool:** CheckThat, a platform for tracking performance in AI answer engines (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  - **Access:** Alex has a 90-day business plan trial.
  - **Features:**
      - **Historical Data:** Provides immediate historical data for 35 initial prompts.
      - **Competitor Analysis:** Shows alex.com's citation share and sentiment vs. competitors.
  - **Note:** Claude AI is less likely to provide citations than other engines.
