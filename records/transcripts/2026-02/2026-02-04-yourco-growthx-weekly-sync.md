## Summary

Yourco and GrowthX aligned on a strategic pivot from broad topics to granular use-case targeting, essential for meeting the 15% monthly growth goal for 2026. The team validated this approach with recent ChatGPT conversion data, refined Check That prompts to capture niche high-intent queries, and committed to optimizing the three highest-performing pages while integrating customer social proof to drive conversions.

---

## Context

Yourco is pursuing an aggressive 15% monthly growth target for 2026, up from a 25-conversion annual goal in 2025. January actuals reached approximately 75% of target (18-19 conversions), and analysis revealed that roughly half of recent conversions came from ChatGPT, driven by specific use-case searches rather than broad evaluation content. Despite publishing new middle-funnel content around "communication platform for each industry," visibility metrics in Check That are not yet reflecting these gains because the new prompts are still populating with data. This disconnect revealed the core strategic issue: the team was creating broad evaluation content, but customers convert on niche, high-intent use-case queries like "text off reporting," "attendance reporting," and "employee survey reporting." Additionally, several pages remain unindexed by Google, skewing performance metrics and blocking potential conversions.
