## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Low AI Visibility

  - **Problem:** Knox is invisible in AI searches for FedRAMP guidance (e.g., "how to get FedRAMPed"), a critical discovery channel.
  - **Goal:** Consistently appear as the top result for these queries.
  - **Secondary Goal:** Improve traditional SEO for relevant keywords.
  - **Success Metric:** Alexi (Head of Demand Gen) will define specific, measurable targets.

### The Solution: Knox's Differentiated Model

  - **The Problem:** FedRAMP is a massive barrier—3 years, $3M, and requires a federal sponsor.
  - **Knox's "Luxury Condo" Model:** A pre-authorized, secure landing zone where customers deploy their existing code.
      - **"Rent":** $500k/year (up from $350k).
      - **"Utilities":** Pass-through hosting costs (AWS, Azure, GCP).
  - **Key Differentiator vs. Palantir:**
      - **Knox:** "Come as you are" → deploy existing code into a single-tenant subaccount.
      - **Palantir:** "My house, my rules" → requires customers to re-architect their apps, often creating a separate, outdated federal version.
  - **Value-Add:** Knox connects customers directly with government buyers.
      - **Example:** The BigID case study features Carrie Lee (former VA CIO), lending immense credibility for BigID's target market.

### The Strategy: Content & Tools

  - **Current State:** Content is minimal, relying on Irina's LinkedIn and recycled press releases. The blog is "anemic."
  - **New Content Engine: `govcontracts.com`**
      - **Purpose:** A free tool providing prospects with data to build a business case for government sales.
      - **Function:** Replaces unusable government sites (e.g., SAM.gov) by showing:
          - Relevant RFPs
          - Competitor government contracts & revenue
      - **Plan:** Launch as a standalone site with a dedicated paid digital strategy.
  - **GrowthX Support:**
      - **Tool:** Provide a custom workspace on GrowthX's B2B AI visibility platform to track performance.
      - **Guidance:** Use the `govcontracts.com` concept to build a directory of searchable company pages, similar to the Brex "Charge Finder" prototype.

### Upcoming Milestones

  - **March 2nd Week:** New product & other pages go live on the website.
  - **March 10th:** Announce $25M Series A funding (timed to build momentum before RSA).
  - **RSA Conference:** Announce a major CISO hire, expected to be a top-tier event announcement.
