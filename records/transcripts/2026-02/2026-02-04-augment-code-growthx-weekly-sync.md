## Summary

GrowthX and Augment Code aligned on a major content strategy shift: shift from incremental SEO improvements to a programmatic "how-to" content engine targeting technical developers, paired with deep-link CTAs driving users to the Intent app. Liz will draft a full PSEO plan; Sylvain will bring quarterly metrics to QPR in two weeks to validate if this approach can deliver the inflection point needed beyond 3% week-over-week organic growth.

---

## Context

Augment Code is an AI coding agent competing with Cursor. GrowthX is their content marketing partner, handling SEO and demand generation. After a November traffic collapse (40k → 15k page views/week), Augment's organic performance has stabilized with steady 3% week-over-week growth, but this incremental pace won't hit revenue targets. Liz Kushnereit (GrowthX's strategy lead on the account) pulled Sylvain Giuliani (Augment's co-founder) into this call to present a playbook from two similar high-growth accounts — DataGrid and Webflow — and propose adapting it for Augment. The core insight: programmatic SEO (PSEO) for technical "how-to" guides, combined with app download CTAs and pre-loaded prompts, has proven effective at both accounts and could unlock Augment's next growth phase.

## Decisions & Commitments

- **Approved**: Shift from incremental SEO (tool comparisons, guides) to a programmatic "how-to" engine targeting technical developer intent (e.g., "How to build a blog with Next.js & Sanity").
- **Approved**: Pair PSEO content with Intent app deep-link CTAs and baked-in prompts to reduce friction and enable immediate app experience.
- **Approved**: Target "Cursor alternative" competitive keywords as a strategic focus, potentially reducing paid ad spend if organic converts well.
- **Commitment**: Liz will draft a full programmatic use-case plan (topic list, scale, linking strategy, AEO tracking, CTA, deep-link mechanics) by QPR.
- **Commitment**: Sylvain will define quarterly revenue/download targets and work backward to content volume and conversion assumptions needed. Metrics due ~2 weeks for QPR.
- **On Hold**: AEO workstream paused until PSEO strategy is locked and metrics framework is clear.
- **Maintained**: Continue current tool comparison and competitive keyword work while planning the new initiative — no disruption to existing workstreams.

## Open Questions

- **Agent recommendation loop**: How does Augment ensure its content (and the Intent app itself) gets recommended by AI agents (GPT, Claude, etc.) when users ask about blog building, mobile apps, etc.? Content alone may not be enough; requires AEO integration and potentially agent training/partnerships.
- **Interconnection risk**: How do PSEO guides, comparison pages, and competitive keywords avoid cannibalizing each other? Liz flagged this as a potential siloing issue that needs explicit content mapping.
- **Scale economics**: At what content volume does programmatic PSEO become profitable? Webflow scales 30 guides/week; Augment's engineering complexity may be higher. Needs validation.
- **Paywall & conversion**: Sylvain acknowledged uncertainty around app paywall placement. Intent app download is the CTA, but what happens post-download (trial length, conversion path) is still undefined.

## Key Topics

### Current Performance & SEO Health

  - Organic traffic is recovering from a November dip (from 40k to 15k page views/week) with steady \~3% WoW growth.
  - **Technical Issues:**
      - Broken redirects were identified → a fix ticket is being created.
      - A canonical page is needed to resolve a ranking conflict between "cursor" and "augment" keywords.
  - **AEO:** The AEO workstream is on hold pending a clearer strategy.

### New Strategy: Programmatic SEO (PSEO)

  - **Goal:** Create a "big bet" to accelerate growth beyond the current incremental pace.
  - **Model:** A programmatic content engine for technical "how-to" guides, inspired by GrowthX's work with DataGrid and Webflow.
  - **Content Focus:**
      - Highly technical, solution-oriented guides (e.g., "How to build a blog with Next.js & Sanity").
      - Tool comparisons and "best X for Y" lists.
  - **Rationale:** This content directly answers developer search intent, which is consistent across traditional search and AI prompts.

### Conversion Hypothesis: Content → App

  - **Core Idea:** Convert high-intent users from PSEO content into app users.
  - **Mechanism:** Embed deep-link prompts in guides that, when clicked, download the Intent app with the tutorial's code pre-loaded.
  - **Narrative:** Position the app as the most efficient way to execute the tutorial's workflow, building trust by demonstrating value upfront.

### Competitive Strategy: Cursor Alternative

  - **Opportunity:** Target "Cursor alternative" keywords to capture high-intent users actively seeking a switch.
  - **Rationale:** Success here could reduce paid ad spend on competitive keywords, freeing up budget for the new PSEO initiative.
