## Context

This is a weekly sync between GrowthX (lead: George Haikal) and the Ramp finance platform team (leads: Victoria Naef and Matt Angelosanto). GrowthX is executing a 12-week AI visibility engagement for Ramp, starting from a completely new domain with no content, authority, or backlinks. The goal is to establish the domain as a credible source that AI engines (answer engines, ChatGPT, and other LLMs) cite when answering queries related to Ramp and financial tools. The engagement serves as both a strategic proof point for Ramp's AI visibility and a case study for GrowthX's CheckThat methodology.
