## Summary

A new LinkedIn campaign targeting workshop partner followers generated 60+ qualified replies in one day with a 50% connection acceptance rate (30% above benchmark), while operational issues with Luma created an opportunity to test direct-to-demo outreach and event promotion via Understory. ## Context

GrowthX and Understory are in active coordination on GTM campaigns, with Understory serving as the primary outreach and list-building partner. The team is testing audience targeting strategies (specifically followers of workshop partners and related influencers) that have proven highly effective for LinkedIn campaigns. Internally, GrowthX experienced a temporary Luma platform suspension due to a spam-flagged email, which blocks direct attendee invitations but allows self-registration via shared links. This operational constraint accelerates a strategic shift: rather than funneling leads through traditional event registration workflows, the team will experiment with more aggressive direct-to-demo outreach, using intent signals and ICP-based targeting to drive qualified conversations. The Feb 19 breakfast represents the first test of this model.
