## Key Topics

### The Problem: Inconsistent Lead Replies

  - Inconsistent replies to inbound leads across multiple channels (email, LinkedIn) create a fragmented prospect experience.
  - The goal is to build a "decision tree" to guide replies, ensuring every interaction is strategic and moves prospects toward a sales conversation.

### The Solution: A Standardized Reply Playbook

  - **Core Principle:** Always provide value and surface a relevant offer in the first reply.
  - **Reply Scenarios:**
      - **Direct Demo Request:** Route immediately to sales (Tyler).
      - **Proxy Attendee:** Forward the request and CC sales (Tyler).
      - **All Other Replies:** Use the playbook's segmented offers.

### Playbook Framework: Tiers & Personas

  - **Company Tiers:**
      - **Tier 1:** Ideal customer profile (ICP).
          - *Criteria:* Funded, $5M+ revenue.
      - **Tier 2:** Potential customer, but higher churn risk.
          - *Criteria:* Seed/Series A, \<5k organic traffic.
      - **Tier 3:** Unlikely to become a customer.
  - **Personas:**
      - **Senior Exec/Leader:** Founder, CEO, CMO, VP/Director of Growth.
      - **Marketing/Growth Operator:** SEO Manager, Content Strategist, etc.
      - **Other:** Any role outside the above.

### Playbook Framework: Offers

  - **High-Touch Offers (for Tier 1/2 Execs):**
      - **AEO Strategy Session:** A new free session to map AI visibility opportunities.
          - *Purpose:* Qualify leads → route to sales (Tyler) or the free Check That tool.
      - **Exclusive Events:** Dinners, breakfasts, virtual wine tastings.
  - **Low-Touch Offers (for Operators & Tier 3):**
      - **Resources:** AI Growth Vault, workshop recordings.
      - **Product:** Free Check That tool.

### Playbook Implementation

  - **Tooling:** The playbook is live in Notion.
  - **AI Assistant:** Cursor was identified as a more powerful AI tool than Notion's built-in assistant.
  - **Future Automation:** A Clay workflow will be built to automatically categorize inbound leads by tier and persona, streamlining the reply process.
