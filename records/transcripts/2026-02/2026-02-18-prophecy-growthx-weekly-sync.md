## Context

Raj Bains, CEO of Prophecy (a data orchestration platform competing with Alteryx), met with Carrie Chowske and Cody Carmen from GrowthX to address a critical visibility problem. Despite a year-long ~$180K partnership with GrowthX and their stated focus on "Alteryx displacement," Prophecy was invisible in AI search results for its core strategy. This failure had real consequences: a VP of Marketing candidate even pulled up ChatGPT live in the meeting, searched for "Alteryx alternatives," and declined the role after seeing Prophecy missing entirely while KNIME consistently ranked first. The board questioned the investment, and Raj was considering whether to continue with GrowthX or take content creation in-house. The meeting was the first time Carrie and Raj had directly collaborated, despite the extended engagement.

## Key Topics

### Performance Gap & Business Impact

  - **Problem:** Prophecy is invisible in AI search for its core strategy ("Alteryx alternatives"), while an unknown competitor (KNIME) consistently ranks \#1.
  - **Business Impact:** This visibility gap creates significant challenges:
      - **Board Confidence:** Inability to show progress on a key strategic initiative.
      - **Hiring:** A VP of Marketing candidate cited the search failure as a reason to decline the role.
      - **Investment:** Raj questioned the value of the \~$180K investment in GrowthX.

### Strategic Pivot: Reverse-Engineering Success

  - **New Approach:** Shift from a general content cadence to a targeted strategy that reverse-engineers what is already working.
  - **Model for Success:** The KNIME listicle, which consistently ranks \#1, was identified as the model to emulate.
      - **Key Elements:** A single, long-form page with a detailed comparison table and a comprehensive FAQ section.
  - **Rationale:** AI models favor structured data (tables, FAQs) for generating direct answers. Emulating this structure is the fastest path to displacing competitors.

### New Content Plan & Metrics

  - **Content Structure:** New articles will adopt the successful KNIME format, including:
      - **Comparison Tables:** To directly contrast Prophecy features with Alteryx and other alternatives.
      - **FAQs:** To provide clear, structured answers for AI models.
  - **Performance Goal:** Achieve AI search visibility for at least one of 15 high-priority queries by March 15.
  - **Rationale:** This deadline provides a clear, measurable test of the strategy's effectiveness.
      - **Success →** Continue the partnership.
      - **Failure →** Raj will create the content internally.
