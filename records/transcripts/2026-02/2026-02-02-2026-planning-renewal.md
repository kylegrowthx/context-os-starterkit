## Key Topics

### Strategic Pivot: Repositioning Airbyte

  - **Goal:** Shift market perception from a data engineering tool to an "AI infrastructure company."
  - **Rationale:** AI agents need connectors to access business data (e.g., Salesforce) at scale, creating a major infrastructure opportunity for Airbyte.
  - **Audience Shift:** Expand focus from data engineers to AI/software engineers building agents.
  - **Content Strategy:**
      - Create technical content that agents can discover and recommend (e.g., "Use Airbyte for connectors").
      - Compete directly with tools like Composio and Merge as the product matures.

### Performance & Wins

  - **January Traffic:** Near 1:1 sessions:visitors → indicates high user intent.
  - **Content Visibility:** An article published Friday already appeared in Google's AI Overviews.
  - **Programmatic Pages:** Agent Connector pages were built in two days, proving a scalable model for future content.
  - **Editorial Cadence:** "Agentic data" content is doubling to 12 articles/week for Feb-Mar to accelerate growth.
  - **Team Stability:** Mario praised the account stability since Erik took over and requested continuity.

### Content Strategy Evolution

  - **Challenge:** Current "agentic data" content is too high-level for the new AI engineer audience.
  - **Solution:** Create more technical content with code examples and GitHub templates.
  - **Ideation:** Mario will bring in a solution engineer for a technical ideation session.
  - **Research:** Mario will provide Gong call transcripts to inform content strategy with direct customer feedback.

### Renewal Proposal

  - **Cost:** 4DK/month.
  - **Scope:**
      - **Editorial:** 12 "agentic data" articles/week (Feb-Mar).
      - **Programmatic:** 25–100 pieces/week (e.g., Agent Use Cases pages, TLDR refreshes).
      - **Thought Leadership:** 1–2 articles/week.
      - **SEO & Attribution:** Ongoing technical support and semi-annual content audits.
  - **New Tool:** Free, perpetual access to "Check That," a new content intelligence tool.
      - **Note:** Currently feature-parity with Scrunch; will evolve to match Profound's ideation features.
