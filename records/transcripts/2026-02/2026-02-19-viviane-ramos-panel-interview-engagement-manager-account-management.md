## Key Topics

### Candidate Motivation & Background

  - **Experience:** 14 years in tech, including a previous role at Hootsuite.
  - **Current Role:** Senior SEO Specialist, but the role is not client-facing.
  - **Motivation for GrowthX:** Seeks a collaborative environment to escape the isolation of in-house SEO and apply a passion for search.
  - **Personal:** Canadian citizen (lived in Kitsilano, Vancouver) considering a move back from Brazil.

### Ramp SEO Strategy Presentation

  - **Goal:** 3 articles/week for 8 weeks (24 total), focusing on quality.
  - **Success Metrics:** Impressions, CTR, top rankings for prioritized terms, and AI/LLM citations.
  - **Core Insight:** Ramp is a mature domain with thousands of articles and a stronger organic presence than competitor Brex.
  - **Dual-Track Strategy:**
    1.  **Optimization (Short-Term Wins):** Refresh dated content (e.g., from 2022) and improve pages ranking 4–20 to capture top spots.
    2.  **Expansion (Long-Term Authority):** Build a new "CFO" thought leadership cluster to fill a content gap and target high-value decision-makers.
  - **Proposed Clusters & Topics:**
      - **Finance Operations:** Refresh existing content around high-intent terms like "spend optimization."
      - **Enterprise Procurement:** Deepen existing content to focus on the enterprise audience.
      - **CFOs:** Create a new cluster with topics like "90-day CFO playbook" and "CFO priorities" to build authority and capture backlinks.

### Interview Discussion & Feedback

  - **E-E-A-T & SME Expertise:**
      - **Rationale:** Expertise is mandatory for Ramp, a "Your Money, Your Life" (YMYL) site.
      - **Challenge:** Securing time from internal SMEs for content review is difficult.
      - **Workaround:** Use a ghostwriter with topical expertise and credit a high-visibility client team member to lend authority.
  - **AI Visibility (AIO/LLM):**
      - **Approach:** Integrate AIO/LLM considerations into the standard content structure; it is not a separate strategy.
      - **Feedback:** A baseline analysis of current AIO/LLM performance would strengthen the strategy.
  - **Client Research Process:**
      - **Method:** A hybrid of AI for data analysis (e.g., ranking distribution) and manual browsing for qualitative insights and gap validation.
  - **Onboarding Process:**
      - **Week 1:** Absorb documentation, learn the GrowthX platform, and understand the "Marcel way" (the CEO's strategic system).
      - **Week 2+:** Shadow an Engagement Manager to observe client meetings and strategy building.
