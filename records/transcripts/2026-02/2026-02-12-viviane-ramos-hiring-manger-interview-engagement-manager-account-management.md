## Key Topics

### The GrowthX "Sprint" Program

  - **Purpose:** An 8-week paid pilot to showcase GrowthX's capabilities and convert new clients to annual contracts.
  - **Process:**
      - **Weeks 1–4:** Build an AI content pipeline; publish \~3 articles/week.
      - **Weeks 5–8:** Ramp up production to 5 articles/week.
      - **Total Output:** \~20 editorial blog posts.
  - **Key Deliverables:**
      - Build an AI content pipeline ("agentic system").
      - Develop a content and SEO strategy.
      - Drive early traffic and results to prove value.

### Engagement Manager Role

  - **Core Function:** A strategic account manager and the primary client contact.
  - **Responsibilities:**
      - Own the content strategy.
      - Interface with internal teams: Managing Editors (pipeline) and Engineering (pipeline tweaks).
  - **Client Cadence:** 1 weekly meeting (8 total) per client.

### Viviane's Experience & Approach

  - **Project Management:** Uses ClickUp (previously Notion) for organization.
  - **Handling Complex Projects:**
      - **Example:** Led a content consolidation project for a million-page healthcare site.
      - **Action:** De-indexed hundreds of thousands of underperforming pages after a performance analysis.
      - **Outcome:** Successfully pivoted strategy to drive leads from service pages, earning praise from the client and leadership.
  - **Stakeholder Management:**
      - **Method:** Uses shared channels for visibility; avoids DMs.
      - **Escalation:** Follows up 3 times, then escalates to manager.
      - **Client Deadlines:** Sets a buffer by giving clients a later deadline than the internal team's.
  - **Account Management Style:**
      - Builds strong relationships with clients and colleagues.
      - Accountable and energized by sharing results.
      - Diverse background (customer success, translation) informs a people-centric approach.

### AI & SEO Expertise

  - **AI Usage:**
      - "Native user" of ChatGPT for \~2 years, using it for tasks from daily work to generating schema code.
      - Has taken a prompt engineering course.
      - Has not adopted other tools (e.g., Claude, Perplexity) for regular use.
  - **SEO vs. AEO:**
      - **SEO:** Highly confident in ranking content and proving results.
      - **AEO (AI Search):** Actively experimenting but frustrated by unreliable tracking tools.
          - **Example:** Cannot attribute qualified leads from a \#1 ranking "best list" article to specific AI prompts, making performance difficult to prove with hard numbers.

### GrowthX Culture & Challenges

  - **Challenges:**
      - **Pace:** The 8-week sprint is highly compressed, requiring rapid ramp-up and results.
      - **Volume:** Managing 6–10 clients simultaneously can be overwhelming.
      - **Tech Stack:** The internal environment is highly advanced, with widespread use of LLMs and automation tools (e.g., n8n, Make).
  - **Culture & Strengths:**
      - **Meritocracy:** Rapid advancement for high performers (e.g., William was promoted to MD in \~1 month).
      - **Product:** High-demand product with an 8-week client waitlist.
      - **Ethos:** Balances speed with quality, using AI for efficiency and humans for review.
