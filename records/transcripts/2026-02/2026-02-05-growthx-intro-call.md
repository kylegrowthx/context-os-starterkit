## Context

They compete directly with Together AI, Fireworks AI, Nebius, and Novita. An investor mandate requires the company to invest in marketing, creating an urgent need for scalable marketing support. ## Key Topics

### DeepInfra's Marketing Challenge

  - DeepInfra needs comprehensive marketing support, driven by an investor's directive.
  - Leily Moazami is the sole sales and marketing person, creating a need for a scalable solution.
  - **Product:** AI inference cloud for open-source models (e.g., DeepSea, Kimmy), providing the underlying compute.
  - **Target Audience:** Technical roles (Heads of Engineering, CTOs, DevOps).
  - **Competitors:** Together AI, Fireworks AI, Nebius, Novita.
  - **Current State (SEMrush Analysis):**
      - **Traffic:** 4–5x lower than Together AI and Fireworks AI.
      - **Backlinks:** Fewer than competitors, but DeepInfra's traffic per backlink is more efficient than Novita's.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** A managed service combining agency strategy with proprietary AI workflows.
  - **Scope:** End-to-end SEO/AEO, from strategy to publishing.
  - **Deliverables:**
      - 5 articles/week, published directly to the client's CMS.
      - Visibility tracking via a new GrowthX platform (replacing a prior Scrunch partnership).
  - **Process:**
      - **Strategy Sprint:** A 30–40k phase to create foundational artifacts (personas, topic clusters) and a content roadmap.
      - **Execution:** AI workflows (using OpenAI, Claude, Gemini) draft content, which is then manually reviewed and refined by GrowthX's team before client review.
  - **Customization:** Workflows are modular, allowing for custom steps like product integrations or medical claim annotations.
  - **Platform Access:** Clients get enterprise access to the visibility platform immediately; the internal content creation platform is shared after the first year.
  - **Client Examples:**
      - **Augment Code:** 3–4x organic traffic, cut paid ad spend.
      - **Webflow:** Created search-optimized integration pages by ingesting their documentation.
