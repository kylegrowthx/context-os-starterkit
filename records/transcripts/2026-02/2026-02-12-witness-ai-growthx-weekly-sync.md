## Key Topics

### Current State & Opportunity

  - **Pipeline Driver:** Events are the primary driver; organic traffic is the \#2 source but lacks a dedicated content engine.
  - **Website Performance:**
      - Traffic is concentrated on the homepage and press releases.
      - Most content ranks on Page 2, with low CTR on high-volume "governance" topics.
      - Specific topics like "AI model collapse" perform well.
      - \~1/3 of blog posts received \<5 clicks in the last 3 months, creating a future refresh opportunity.
  - **Goal:** Build a sustainable content engine to drive qualified traffic and brand awareness, targeting both Google SEO and LLM visibility.

### Proposed Content Strategy

  - GrowthX proposed 6 content clusters to build authority around the emerging theme of "AI risk management."
  - **1. Agentic AI Security:**
      - **Focus:** Educating buyers on why traditional keyword-based DLP fails with modern AI prompts, positioning Witness AI's intent-based classification as the superior alternative.
      - **Note:** This cluster will be expanded to include general "AI security" topics.
  - **2. AI Governance, Compliance & Shadow AI:**
      - **Focus:** Building authority in governance by targeting specific, high-intent keywords (e.g., "how to enforce AI policies") to support the "Observe & Control" pillars and attract "Trust & Compliance" buyers.
  - **3. Securing AI Tools:**
      - **Focus:** Capturing high-intent searches from users of specific AI tools (e.g., "Google Gemini security") to educate them on risks and position Witness AI as the solution.
  - **4. AI Risk by Industry:**
      - **Subcluster A (FinServ/Healthcare):** Leans on compliance and governance as the entry point.
      - **Subcluster B (Airlines/Retail/Hospitality):** Focuses on securing chatbots and preventing hallucinations.
  - **5. Product Comparisons & Listicles:**
      - **Focus:** Bottom-of-funnel content targeting buyers actively evaluating solutions.
      - **Rationale:** Crucial for SEO and for "training" LLMs on Witness AI's unique technical capabilities, preventing competitors from controlling the narrative.

### Content Execution & Alignment

  - **Persona Mapping:**
      - Sharat requested that the content calendar map articles to specific personas (CISO, security architect) to ensure relevance.

  - **Content Grounding:**

### Defining Success & ROI

  - Stephanie flagged the current KPIs as too "fluffy" and requested more concrete, measurable metrics.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will provide performance benchmarks (e.g., % uplift) from similar past engagements to help define success.
  - **Timeline:**
