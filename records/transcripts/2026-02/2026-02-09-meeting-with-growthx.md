## Context

Michal Ozeri introduced Tyler Pavlas to Alex Lin (the decision-maker) during this meeting. Until early 2024, Priority Gold had no formal digital marketing strategy; they've since built a paid media engine (affiliate traffic, paid search, social) that drives 200-300k visitors monthly but converts at only 0.5-0.75% (1,500 leads/month). Their business model requires 99% of leads to go through a broker call before closing, creating an information gap that limits purely digital conversion. Having stabilized paid channels, they're now evaluating organic content as a strategic lever to reduce ad spend dependency and prepare for AI search visibility. This is their first serious SEO engagement; they have minimal existing content assets.
