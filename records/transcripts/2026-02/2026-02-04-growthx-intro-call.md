## Key Topics

### Gladia's Content Challenge

  - **Primary Need:** Scale content production to meet AI search demands.
  - **Secondary Need:** Optimize content for LLM visibility.
  - **Current State:** A lean team with a hybrid content process (manual + AI).
  - **Goal:** Outsource end-to-end content creation to a single partner.

### Potential Conflict of Interest

  - **Issue:** GrowthX's CEO, Marcel, is the former CMO of DeepGram, a direct competitor.
  - **GrowthX Policy:** Strict siloing of competitive accounts.
      - Separate teams and workspaces.
      - No data or information sharing.
  - **Context:** GrowthX also works with DeepGram, which hired them to get Marcel back.

### GrowthX's Solution & Process

  - **Origin:** Marcel built the solution he wanted as a CMO, inspired by his success at DeepGram (24x traffic, 4x revenue).
  - **Model:** A white-glove service combining an internal platform (Atlas) with a team of experts (e.g., former TechCrunch editor-in-chief).
  - **Two-Phase Partnership:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-week sprint):**
          - **Weeks 1–3:** Ingest resources → Develop custom "artifacts" (brand voice, ICP) → Create content roadmap.
          - **Weeks 4–8:** Publish 10–20 articles.
      - **Phase 2 (Ongoing):** Produce 5 articles/week (new or refresh).
  - **Platform (Atlas):** An internal tool that uses custom artifacts and agentic pipelines to automate content creation.
      - **Agentic Workflows:** Pipelines self-correct (e.g., re-run research if quality scores are not met).
      - **Fact-Checking:** A dedicated step extracts passages and performs deep research to validate accuracy.
  - **Client Access:** The platform is currently internal-only. A future self-serve model will be less expensive.

### Client Examples & Fit

  - **Augment Code:** Grew from 20k to 60k+ monthly organic traffic, enabling them to compete with GitHub.
  - **Lovable:** Built a custom pipeline in 3–4 weeks to create programmatic app templates.
  - **Early-Stage Clients:** Strong results with seed/Series A companies.
      - **Tiro:** Significant traffic growth.
      - **CERN:** Grew from \<5k to 100k+ monthly organic traffic.
  - **Fit for Startups:** The model works well for startups. The main bottleneck is often client perfectionism, which can slow down the necessary volume for AI search.
