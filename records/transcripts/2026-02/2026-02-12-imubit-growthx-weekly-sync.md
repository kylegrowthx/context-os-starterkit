## Key Topics

### Content Strategy: From Traffic to Revenue

  - **Problem:** Current blog articles are "islands" with no clear next steps, making traffic a vanity metric.
  - **Goal:** Drive measurable conversions (demos, gated assets) to prove revenue impact.
  - **Proposed Solutions (for new Webflow site):**
      - **Sticky CTA:** A persistent banner that scrolls with the page.
      - **Gated Assets:** Repurpose blog content into guides (e.g., "refining" guide) to capture leads.
      - **Testimonial Callouts:** Insert relevant customer quotes with links to case studies or demos.
  - **Tracking:** GA4 setup must be updated to track individual CTA clicks for accurate performance measurement.

### New Demand Gen Agency Collaboration

  - **Need:** A direct workflow between GrowthX and the new agency, avoiding Tara as a bottleneck.
  - **Critical Dependency:** The agency requires Imubit's content keywords to launch paid ad campaigns.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will provide a keyword export today and schedule a joint alignment session once the agency is fully onboarded.

### Content Performance & Backlinks

  - **Content Refresh Success:** Refreshing top-performing articles is successfully reviving traffic.
      - **Example:** The "Advanced Process Control" article, refreshed in January, saw impressions rebound after a period of decline.
      - **Process:** GrowthX uses SEMrush to identify articles for refresh, either by updating for LLM visibility or rewriting to target keywords they already rank for.
  - **Backlink Quality:** Imubit flagged current backlinks as poor quality.
      - **Action:** GrowthX's GTM team is investigating a solution.
      - **Reporting:** Backlinks will be included in new monthly performance recaps to ensure transparency.

### CMS Migration & Publishing

  - **Status:** The Webflow migration is delayed as Imubit is refreshing homepage and product page content.
  - **Automation:** GrowthX requires a Webflow API key to enable auto-publishing from its Atlas content engine, which will save significant manual effort.
  - **Article Dates:** Imubit plans to remove dates from articles to avoid signaling batch-produced AI content. GrowthX will present a counter-argument for their value.

### Executive Reporting

  - **Need:** Executive reports must include hard numbers on growth, reach, and traffic, not just qualitative wins (e.g., LLM visibility).
  - **Action:** GrowthX will update the executive snapshot to include these metrics.
