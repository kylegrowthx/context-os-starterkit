## Context

Elementum is a workflow automation platform for enterprise IT operations, currently transitioning from founder-led selling (with Nader Mikhail in every deal) to building a sales organization. GrowthX is supporting their content marketing strategy with the primary goal of building authority around agentic AI and workflow automation, while also enabling the sales team with tactical content they can use in early deals. Andrew Bennett, Head of Content at Elementum, is leading this content initiative and working closely with Aida Knezevic, the GrowthX content lead. The sales team is new to the platform and lacks enablement assets, creating both a risk (skepticism about content relevance) and an opportunity (quick wins from tactical content). The market narrative around AI agents and automation is shifting rapidly, so the team expects to pivot topics frequently rather than lock a rigid quarterly calendar.
