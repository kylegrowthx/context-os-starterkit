## Key Topics

### Traffic Recovery & Site Optimization

  - **Problem:** Traffic recovery from the recent crawl fix is projected to be slow (4–6 weeks).
  - **Solution:** Accelerate recovery with minimal content refreshes on high-priority pages.
      - **Action:** Update a few stats and move sections.
      - **Goal:** Trigger intentional re-crawls via re-indexing requests.
  - **Execution:** Webstacks and GrowthX will share re-indexing duties to bypass Search Console's daily limits (e.g., 10 requests/account/day).
  - **Related Optimizations:**
      - **Sitemap:** Review the current sitemap, which requests a daily crawl for all pages.
          - **Rationale:** This is likely inefficient, as most content (e.g., capabilities pages) is static.
          - **Action:** GrowthX will consult its internal SEO experts for best practices.
      - **Internal Linking:** Review and optimize internal links during content refreshes.
      - **Blog Tags:** Simplify the tag structure to improve content discovery and dynamic component functionality.

### Strategic Shift: SEO → AEO

  - **Core Thesis:** AI is ending the era of "gamified" SEO. Future visibility depends on an exceptional product/service and a strong public presence, not just keywords.
  - **Evidence:** Webstacks' traffic has declined over the last six months, despite using the same keyword-focused strategy that was highly effective a year ago.
  - **New Focus:** Build a public community on platforms where AI sources information.
      - **YouTube:** A top driver of AI visibility and citations.
      - **Reddit:** An experimental area for building karma and engaging in conversations.

### Content Direction & Process

  - **Gong Call Analysis:** GrowthX analyzed transcripts to find key topics and messaging.
      - **Jesse's Feedback:** The core narrative is "AI is happening regardless." Content must convey that the answer to visibility is an exceptional product, not just SEO tactics.
  - **Blog Imagery:** Webstacks is experimenting with an AI tool to generate post-publish images.
      - **Decision:** GrowthX will continue its current process (uploading illustrated images) to avoid workflow disruption. Webstacks will overwrite them as needed.

## Decisions & Commitments

- **Traffic Recovery Approach:** Prioritize minimal content refreshes (stat updates, section reordering) on high-priority pages impacted by the crawl fix, combined with coordinated re-indexing requests split between Webstacks and GrowthX (bypassing daily GSC limits of ~10 requests/account/day).
- **Content Strategy Pivot:** Shift from keyword-focused SEO to AEO, with emphasis on public community presence (YouTube, Reddit) where AI sources information for citations.
- **Gong Analysis Integration:** Use Gong call transcript analysis to inform content strategy, messaging, and identify trending topics relevant to Webstacks' market.
- **Featured Image Process:** GrowthX continues uploading illustrated images with content; Webstacks is free to overwrite with AI-generated alternatives as the tool evolves.
- **Velocity Work:** Revisit velocity-based content production strategy (which was effective a year ago) as a potential lever for accelerating traffic recovery.
