  - **Siemens Mexico (Dr. Churis):**
      - **Deal:** Multi-year, six-figure contract.
      - **Challenge:** Multi-threaded across legal, procurement, and IT.
      - **Value Prop:** Tailored to each department's pain points.
          - **Legal:** Faster contract review (weeks → days).
          - **Procurement:** Tracking auto-renewals to avoid costly slips.
          - **IT:** Data security and AI performance.
  - **Sekisui (Plangistics):**
      - **Deal:** \~$400,000 service contract (9.5-month cycle).
      - **Challenge:** Revived a lost deal by reconnecting with a new POC.

      - **Learning:** Involve the economic buyer earlier to shorten the sales cycle.
