## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Performance

  - **Recent Google Update Impact:** A recent algorithm update caused significant ranking drops (e.g., from top 5 to \>30) for some Brex pages.
      - **Hypothesis:** The update is targeting AI-generated content patterns.
      - **Action:** Jon will analyze the SERP for the reviewed article's keyword.
          - **Initial Finding:** Top results are dominated by large banks with high domain authority, suggesting the article may not rank for SEO but remains valuable for G-O-A-I-O.
  - **Content Uniqueness:** The strategy must prioritize unique value to stand out from the "groupthink" of AI-generated content.
      - **Example:** The "Hidden Cost of Business Banking" article succeeded by exposing competitor fees that were difficult to find, even for AI.
      - **Goal:** Replicate this success by finding and sharing information that is not easily discoverable.

### Article Refresh Review

  - **Objective:** Improve G-O-A-I-O performance by making the article more LLM-friendly.
  - **Key Changes:**
      - **Style:** "Inverted pyramid" structure with a "finance reporter" tone.
      - **Internal Links:** Increased from 3 to 8.
      - **Data:** Added reputable statistics and tables for authority.
  - **Feedback & Refinements:**
      - **Keyword Cannibalization:** Avoid creating content that competes with existing articles.
          - **Action:** Remove one FAQ suggestion that targets a keyword already covered by a Brex article.
      - **Header Hierarchy:** Use H3s for sub-sections if they are not planned as standalone articles. This helps Google and AI understand the content structure.
      - **Meta Description:** The current suggestion feels generic. Jon prefers a more human-written, two-sentence description, as Google often overwrites them for SEO anyway.
      - **External Links:** Jon will verify all sources and noted a potential new article idea: "Operating Agreement."

### Proprietary Data Initiative

  - **Problem:** Brex lacks a centralized bank of proprietary statistics.
  - **Opportunity:** Create unique data from Brex's user base (e.g., spending trends, application times) to build authority and provide content that AI cannot replicate.
  - **GrowthX's Role:** Suggest data points that would strengthen specific articles.
      - **Precedent:** GrowthX built a custom knowledge base for client Biologica to ensure all cited data was proprietary and factually accurate.
