## Key Topics

### Goal: Drive Click Growth via SEO Refreshes

  - **Objective:** Increase traffic by improving the SEO performance of existing articles.
  - **Target:** Articles ranked 5th or lower, as these have the highest potential to reach the \#1 spot.
  - **Scope:** All work is for SEO refreshes only.

### Process: SEO-Driven Content Enhancement

  - **1. Keyword Analysis (Ahrefs):**
      - Check the article's URL in Ahrefs Organic Research to confirm its current ranking.
      - Use the Ahrefs plugin on Google SERPs to analyze the AI Overview and top competitors.
  - **2. Content Enhancement:**
      - **Add Missing Sections:** Use the AI Overview to identify and add critical sections, such as a "What is..." definition, to better match search intent.
      - **Improve Skimability:** Enhance readability with bullet points, tables, and infographics.
      - **Add Infographics:** Include 1–2 infographics per article (e.g., for frameworks, benefits).
          - **Process:** Write an image direction in the `Infographic Generation` project.
          - **Requirement:** Any data in an infographic must first be cited in the article's text.

### Product Integration: Natural Feature Placement

  - **Strategy:** Integrate Abnormal features naturally as solutions to pain points, avoiding a salesy tone.
  - **Resource:** Use the `Relevant Features` project (in pod D) for accurate, approved messaging on each feature.
  - **Inspiration:** Model the integration style on the successful Outreach account work, which effectively wove in platform modules.
  - **Conclusion CTA:**
      - For mid-funnel articles → Link to other blog posts or a newsletter.
      - For bottom-funnel articles → Use a "Book a Demo" CTA.

### Workflow: Agentic Generation & Manual Stitching

  - **Tooling:** Use Agentic Article Generation to create new sections.
      - **Rationale:** This method is more effective for generating specific content than the Agentic Refresher tool.
  - **Execution:**
    1.  Copy the existing article into a Google Doc.
    2.  Use Agentic Article Generation to create content for new sections.
    3.  Manually stitch the new sections into the Google Doc.
    4.  Highlight all new content (additions, edits) in green for easy review.
  - **Required Metadata:**
      - Meta Title
      - Meta Description
      - Slug
      - Alt Text (per image; placeholder "alt text" is acceptable)
