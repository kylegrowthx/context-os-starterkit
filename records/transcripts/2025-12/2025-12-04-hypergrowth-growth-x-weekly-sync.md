## Key Topics

### The Problem: Production Inefficiency

  - **Goal:** Maximize co-founder impact with minimal time investment.
  - **Blockers:** Articles are not "ready to ship" due to two consistent issues:
      - **Weak Story Arc:** Repetitive structure; fails to build a compelling narrative.
      - **Missing Tone Guidance:** Lacks specific direction (e.g., controversial, diplomatic), forcing extensive rewrites to match the desired voice.

### The Solution: A Strategic Pivot

  - **Insight:** The "CMO1" article performed best because its controversial take sparked conversation.
  - **Decision:** Pivot from disjointed topics to a cohesive series on "Advisory."
      - **Rationale:** This topic is a unique strength where the team can "absolutely shine," unlike the crowded "Growth" space.
  - **Series Focus:** Picking winners, building a collective, and getting paid in equity.

### The Model: Learning from Competitors

  - **Analysis:** Study competitors (e.g., Elena Verna, Hypolder) for successful themes and distribution tactics (frequency, tone, graphics).
  - **Inspiration:** Airbyte's thought leadership series provides a relevant model.
      - **Goal:** Create a new category ("agentic data infrastructure") using co-founder interviews.
      - **Method:** A 12-week series builds a story arc, starting with market changes and evolving into the need for new standards.
