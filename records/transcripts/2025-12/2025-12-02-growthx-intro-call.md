## Key Topics

### Squint's Content Gaps & 2026 Goals

  - **Objective:** Accelerate pipeline growth for 2026 planning.
  - **Core Challenge:** Squint lacks a content engine to articulate its value, creating three critical gaps:
    1.  **Website:** Outdated content (3 years old) is actively losing leads.
    2.  **Sales Enablement:** No battle cards or decks to differentiate Squint from competitors.
    3.  **Lifecycle Marketing:** No content to support nurture campaigns.
  - **Strategic View:** Content is the foundational layer for all marketing channels. Investing in content is necessary before scaling channels like performance marketing.
  - **Current Content Efforts:** Squint works with an agency for LinkedIn posts, which is effective and would not be replaced.

### GrowthX's Content Engine Solution

  - **Service Model:** An end-to-end partner for SEO/GEO strategy and execution, positioning itself as a hybrid of an SEO agency and an analytics platform (e.g., Profound).
  - **Process:** An 8-week strategy sprint to create foundational artifacts (personas, brand voice) precedes a 12-month execution phase.
  - **Execution:**
      - Publishes 5 editorial articles per week.
      - Focuses on long-tail keywords to build domain authority.
      - Uses AI workflows with expert oversight for research and drafting.
      - Tracks performance via Google Analytics/Search Console and a proprietary platform (Checkback, replacing Scrunch).
  - **Scope:** Primarily editorial content (blogs, guides). Other website pages (e.g., programmatic directories) are possible but secondary. Sales decks are out of scope.

### Hiring vs. Agency: A Strategic Decision

  - **Squint's Dilemma:** The scope mismatch forces a decision between hiring a full-time content creator and using a specialized agency.
  - **GrowthX's Rationale for Agency:**
      - **Speed:** Faster time-to-value (3–6 months vs. hiring).
      - **Scale:** Achieves higher content volume (5 articles/week) than a single hire.
      - **Cost:** Potentially lower than building and maintaining the necessary AI workflows and API calls in-house.
