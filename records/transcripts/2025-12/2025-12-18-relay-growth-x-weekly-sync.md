### Comparison Article Strategy

  - **Problem:** The standard content pipeline struggles with the subtle positioning required for comparison articles.
  - **Solution:** Dave will review one comparison article to assess the pipeline's output.
      - **If it fails:** GrowthX will build a custom pipeline for this article type.
      - **If it passes:** No change is needed.
  - **Compliance Constraint:** Relay cannot use definitive claims like "Relay is best."
      - **Approved Framing:** Factual statements and tailored recommendations (e.g., "good for X needs").

### Content Refresh Project

  - **Decision:** Refresh outdated SEO content (2021–2022) on existing URLs instead of creating new pages.
