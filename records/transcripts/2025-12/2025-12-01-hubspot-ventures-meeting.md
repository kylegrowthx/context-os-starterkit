## Transcript

**Marcel Santilli:** Thanks for making time today. I wanted to walk through how we're positioning the company and some of the work we're doing with customers that I think could be interesting for HubSpot.

**Adam Coccari:** Absolutely. We're excited to hear more about the platform approach.

**Marcel Santilli:** So we're shifting from being seen as an agency to a platform-led business. Customers like Lovable are tripling engagement within 2.5 months by focusing on content quality and rapid experimentation.

**Adam Coccari:** That's impressive. What's driving that engagement increase?

**Marcel Santilli:** It's about giving customers control and visibility into content opportunities. They can choose how involved they want to be—fully managed or fully self-serve. For Lovable, they have over 3,000 potential content opportunities, and we're helping them prioritize and execute efficiently.

**Jenn Van Dam:** How does that translate to the platform experience?

**Marcel Santilli:** The platform presents opportunities, tracks progress, and provides analytics. We're using context engineering—capturing company-specific knowledge about products, markets, and customers—to generate relevant opportunities at scale.

**Adam Coccari:** Context engineering is interesting. Can you expand on that?

**Marcel Santilli:** For example, with Clerk, we manage technical product details and compliance requirements. With Biologica, we handle medical and legal compliance. That context feeds into content generation and optimization. We're compressing what would be six months of product marketing into about two weeks.

**Adam Coccari:** That's significant efficiency. What about the editorial side?

**Marcel Santilli:** We're reducing human editorial intervention to roughly 30 minutes per piece of content while maintaining quality and compliance. We're also expanding beyond blogs—integrating content with product catalogs and APIs, like Ramp's vendor pages and Lovable's template libraries.

**Jenn Van Dam:** How does this align with your growth goals?

**Marcel Santilli:** Our goal is to position GrowthX as a strategic partner to HubSpot. Growth in site traffic directly benefits HubSpot by expanding marketing automation reach and pipeline opportunities. We can offer content audits, platform trials, and integration to improve lead capture and nurturing.

**Adam Coccari:** I like the collaboration angle. What would the presentation focus on?

**Marcel Santilli:** A 20-minute talk showcasing customer success stories like Lovable, Brex, and Redis, followed by live demos. We'll frame it as a journey from AI-enabled agency to a platform empowering customers across a spectrum of involvement.

**Adam Coccari:** And the timing?

**Marcel Santilli:** We're targeting an investment announcement for January. The presentation will highlight our platform power, customer involvement, and the evolving marketing landscape driving this shift.

**Jenn Van Dam:** Sounds good. I'll get you that attendee list, and we can coordinate the final presentation slides.

**Marcel Santilli:** Perfect. I'll share iterations as we progress. Looking forward to exploring collaboration opportunities with HubSpot.

**Adam Coccari:** Great. This should resonate well with our teams. Let's keep the momentum going.
