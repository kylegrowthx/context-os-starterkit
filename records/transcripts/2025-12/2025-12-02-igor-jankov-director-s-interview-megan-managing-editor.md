## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Strategic Shift

  - GrowthX is transitioning from a services-heavy model to a hybrid one.
  - **Goal:** Move from 95% services to a 70% services / 30% self-serve platform mix.
  - **Rationale:** Diversify revenue and serve clients who don't need full-service support.
  - **Focus:** Engineering is enhancing the production tool, analytics, and AI visibility to enable this shift.

### Managing Editor Role & Team Structure

  - The Managing Editor (ME) role is now end-to-end content production.
  - **Workload:** 3 client accounts, with weekly volume ranging from 5 editorial posts to 26 programmatic SEO posts.
  - **Team:** MEs report to an Engagement Manager, who oversees ~8 total client accounts.
  - **Tools:** GrowthX uses Linear, Notion, Airtable, Google Sheets, and Slack.

### Igor's Experience & Problem-Solving

  - **Current Role (Proof):** "Service Delivery" lead, managing 6–10 clients.
  - **Responsibilities:** Style guide creation, editor vetting, QA, and client feedback integration.
  - **Workflow:** Groups similar clients (e.g., SEO automotive) to create efficiency templates.
  - **Key Project (Web Novels):**
      - **Problem:** A 50% client feedback rate on machine-translated Chinese web novels.
      - **Cause:** Inconsistent character/plot details across 900+ chapters.
      - **Solution:** Created detailed character/plot guides for editors to reference.
      - **Outcome:** Drastically reduced feedback and inconsistency issues.
  - **Feedback Response (Time Tracking):**
      - **Feedback:** Inconsistent time tracking due to adjusting to global freelancer schedules.
      - **Action:** Implemented a rigid schedule and required editors to conform, improving efficiency.
