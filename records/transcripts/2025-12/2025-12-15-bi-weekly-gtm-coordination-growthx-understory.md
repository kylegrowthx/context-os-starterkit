## Context

GrowthX (a B2B content marketing agency) is partnering with Understory (a GTM services firm) on coordinated outbound campaigns. Understory's team manages execution—email and LinkedIn outreach, campaign setup, tracking—while GrowthX owns the offer strategy and event marketing. This meeting was a bi-weekly sync to assess what's working, troubleshoot tracking gaps, and plan the next wave of campaigns. The underlying context is that GrowthX's LinkedIn-first strategy is emerging as dramatically more effective than email for founder/executive audiences, which is prompting a strategic shift toward smarter list sourcing and segmentation rather than broader volume.
