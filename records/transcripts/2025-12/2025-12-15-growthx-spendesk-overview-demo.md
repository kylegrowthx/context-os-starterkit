## Overview

- **Service Model:** GrowthX delivers a "white glove" service, managing the entire content workflow (strategy, creation, publishing) to produce 5 articles/week.
- **Process Foundation:** The workflow is built on four "artifacts" (company, ICP, brand voice, guidelines) created in an 8-week strategic Phase 1.
- **Human-Led Optimization:** Performance data (Looker dashboard) is analyzed by GrowthX's team, who manually refine the artifacts to improve future content quality.
- **Primary Blocker:** Spendesk is concerned about GrowthX serving direct competitors (Ramp, Brex). GrowthX will provide its competitive client policy to address this.

## Key Topics

### Spendesk's Content Challenge

  - Cristina (incoming Head of Content) needs to scale content production from a lean team.
  - Goal: Reconcile high quality with increased quantity, moving beyond manual ABM workflows.

### GrowthX's Solution: AI-Powered Content Service

  - **Focus:** SEO-driven editorial content, with programmatic opportunities explored later.
  - **Phase 1 (8 weeks): Strategy & Calibration**
      - Goal: Create four foundational "artifacts" that define the content engine.
      - Process: Kickoff → product deep dive → CMS deep dive.
      - Rationale: The system's output quality directly depends on the quality of these inputs.
  - **Phase 2: Production & Optimization**
      - Cadence: 5 articles/week (new or refreshed).
      - Feedback: Spendesk provides feedback on drafts (2 rounds post-calibration).
      - Optimization: GrowthX's team manually refines the artifacts based on performance data to improve future content quality.

### Workflow & Technology

  - **Artifacts:** Define the content engine's core identity.
      - Company (positioning, differentiators)
      - ICP/Personas (jobs-to-be-done)
      - Brand Voice (tone, style)
      - Writing Guidelines (competitor mentions, terminology)
  - **Knowledge Graph:** An internal tool for GrowthX's team to accelerate research.
      - Purpose: Store and connect research findings, not for SEO linking.
      - Function: Feeds the AI research agent to speed up iterations and improve accuracy.

### Team & Communication

  - **Client Team:**
      - Managing Editor: Content expert, end-to-end owner.
      - Content Manager: Daily comms, execution, publishing.
      - Pooled Managing Directors: Strategic guidance.
  - **Communication:**
      - Slack channel for daily comms.
      - Weekly/bi-weekly data review meetings.
      - Monthly business reviews.
      - Shared Notion workspace and Airtable ContentOS.

### Design & Visuals

  - **Standard Service:** AI-generated cover images for all articles.
      - Process: GrowthX's team selects the best AI-generated image.
      - Optimization: GrowthX's "designer" role is a prompt engineer who refines workflows based on client feedback to ensure brand alignment.
  - **Custom Design:** Requires an additional, separate workstream and cost.
      - Example: Ramp's interactive vendor directory charts were built by a human design engineer.

### Competitive Client Policy

  - **Issue:** Spendesk is concerned about GrowthX serving direct competitors (Ramp, Brex).
  - **GrowthX's Policy:** Strict SOPs prevent information mixing between competing clients.
      - Example: No team member works on two competing accounts.
  - **Precedent:** GrowthX successfully serves other competing pairs (e.g., Ramp & Brex).
