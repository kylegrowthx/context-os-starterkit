## Key Topics

### Content Review Bottleneck

  - **Problem:** Brooke's 30-minute review time per article is the main production bottleneck.
  - **Goal:** Reduce review time to 5–10 minutes by improving GrowthX's content quality.
  - **Root Causes:**
      - Repetitive sentences and robotic phrasing.
      - Bulleted lists "flattened" into paragraphs without transitions.
  - **New Review Strategy:**
      - **Prioritize New Content:** Review the most recent articles first.
          - **Why:** This provides faster feedback on the latest pipeline improvements, enabling GrowthX to apply fixes to the entire backlog.
      - **Provide Actionable Feedback:**
          - **Granular:** Specific examples of issues.
          - **High-Level:** Overarching patterns (e.g., "choppy sentences").
          - **Why:** This allows GrowthX's managing editor, Jana, to update the AI pipeline's writing guidelines, preventing recurrence.

### Workflow & Tooling

  - **Problem:** The current approval process is fragmented across Slack and Airtable, causing confusion.
  - **Solution:** Centralize all approvals in Airtable.
  - **New Airtable Workflow:**
      - **New Statuses:** `Client Approved` and `Needs Revisions`.
      - **Automated Notifications:** Slack notifications will alert Brooke when an article is ready for review.
      - **Revision Handling:**
          - **Minor Edits:** Use the `Needs Revisions` status. Add a doc comment: "Good to publish after edits."
          - **Major Edits:** Use the `Needs Revisions` status. The article will return to Brooke for a second review.
  - **Airtable Interfaces:** Bailey will create custom interfaces to simplify Brooke's view of the Airtable base.
      - **Why:** To provide a clear overview of the content pipeline and pending tasks, reducing information overload.

### Content Strategy & Logistics

  - **Topic Prioritization:**
      - **Problem:** The topic queue is low, but this is acceptable while Brooke catches up on reviews.
      - **Solution:** Brooke shared a keyword analysis doc to align on priorities.
      - **Action:** Bailey will cross-reference this with GrowthX's research to build a new topic list.
  - **TLDR Strategy:**
      - **Problem:** The "TLDR" tag feels off-brand.
      - **Solution:** Replace it with a custom, branded summary.
      - **Why:** To preserve LLM visibility (which favors front-loaded summaries) while maintaining brand voice.
  - **PSEO Pipeline:**
      - **Decision:** Postpone the programmatic SEO (PSEO) discussion until January.
      - **Why:** Brooke's current workload makes scaling content a low priority.
  - **Reporting Cadence:**
      - **Schedule:** Monthly deep-dive reports will be delivered the last week of the month for the prior month's data.
      - **Why:** To align with Brooke's internal reporting cycle.
  - **Image Resizing:**
      - **Problem:** Images in Webflow require manual resizing.
      - **Action:** Bailey will check with the publishing ops team to ensure correct sizes are pulled in automatically.
