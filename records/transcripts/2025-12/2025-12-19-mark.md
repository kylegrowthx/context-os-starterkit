## Key Topics

### Clay: Beyond List Enrichment

  - Jesus's goal: Learn Mark's lead generation process, starting with Clay.
  - Mark's advice: Use free resources to master Clay's full capabilities.
      - **Clay University:** The platform's official training program.
      - **Eric Novoslawski:** A key influencer whose videos cover list building, cleaning, and enrichment.
  - Clay's advanced features include:
      - Intent signal scraping
      - Data cleaning
      - CRM enrichment
  - Mark noted a paid course, "Clay Boot Camp," is not necessary, as its creators share similar content for free on LinkedIn.

### Instantly: Scaling Email Outreach

  - Instantly's role: A powerful email platform for managing and sending from hundreds of accounts.
  - Key function: Automated account warming.
      - **Purpose:** Simulates human sending behavior to build a positive sender reputation and avoid spam filters.
  - Understory's infrastructure setup:
      - **Domains:** Purchased from various providers.
      - **Email Accounts:** Purchased separately.
      - **Connection:** Both are linked to Instantly for campaign execution.
      - **Rationale:** This method provides more control than buying accounts directly through Instantly.
