## Summary

Renato reviewed two initial brand concepts for growthx.ai with design studio Renn. Concept 1 (Contrast) — based on visualizing input-output transformation through visual contrast (texture, color, typography, layout shifts) — emerged as Renato's personal favorite for its quality and boldness, though he flagged risk that its strong "audio media" aesthetic might feel too far from an engineering framework. Concept 2 (Wormhole/Funnel) — a traditional tech approach using funnel-shaped forms and glowing line animations — felt safer but less fresh. Next step: Renato will review with the GrowthX team and provide directional feedback to guide the design studio toward the final direction.

## Context

Renato Valdes Olmos from GrowthX met with Dutch design studio Renn (Giel Cobben and Nol Cobben) to review initial brand concepts for growthx.ai. ## Overview

- **Concept 1 (Contrast) is the clear favorite for its quality, but its bold style may be too "audio media" for an engineering framework.** Renato will test this with the team.
- **Concept 2 (Wormhole) is less fresh and feels like a safer, more traditional tech approach.**
- **The core "input-output" metaphor must be conveyed through animation or form, not a literal symbol.**
- **Next step:** Renato will share the Figma file with the team to gather feedback and provide a consolidated, directional response.

---

## Key Topics

### Concept 1: Contrast

  - **Core Idea:** Visualize the "input → output" process using visual contrast (texture, color, typography).
  - **Web Application:**
      - **Header:** Bold, using a clipped image/video within the logo's last three letters.
      - **Body:** Shifts to a calmer, minimalist layout (e.g., split-screen) for content.
      - **Footer:** Returns to the bold, full-width image/video.
  - **Renato's Feedback:**
      - **Quality:** "Super strong" and a personal favorite.
      - **Concern:** The style feels more like "audio media" than an "engineering framework," which may trigger internal pushback.
      - **Logo:** In the second website concept, the logo felt too hard against the monotype font.

### Concept 2: Wormhole / Funnel

  - **Core Idea:** A "wormhole" or "funnel" shape represents the transformation process.
  - **Web Application:**
      - **Style:** A "safer," more traditional tech aesthetic (light mode).
      - **Layout:** The funnel shape is integrated throughout, with lines guiding the user down the page.
      - **Animation:** Potential for glowing lines that animate on scroll.
  - **Renato's Feedback:**
      - The website concept feels less fresh and has been seen before.
