## Key Topics

### Content Accuracy & Value Prop

  - **Problem:** Recent drafts (e.g., "Guide to Construction Travel Management") misaligned with Navan's value prop, promoting generic solutions instead of key differentiators like dynamic policies.
  - **Solution:** Create a new artifact for the content generation pipeline.

      - **Owner:** Aida will draft a framework this week.

### Content Prioritization Framework

  - **Problem:** The current Airtable refresh prioritization is purely SEO-driven (traffic/position), lacking business context.
  - **Solution:** Develop a new framework combining:
      - **SEO Metrics:** Traffic, position, etc.
      - **Business Impact:** Lead generation data and relevance to strategic goals.
  - **Owner:** Aida will draft a framework this week for review.

### Internal Linking Strategy

  - **Problem:** Current internal linking is ad-hoc, missing a strategic, consistent approach.
  - **Solution:** Implement a new process using a priority URL list from GSC.
      - **Owner:** Moran will provide the initial list and anchor text.
      - **Process:** The list will be reviewed quarterly.
      - **Guideline:** Aim for 8–10 links per article, avoiding link-dense paragraphs.

### Legal Review Process

  - **Context:** Navan's legal team reviews all blogs, requiring external substantiation for claims (e.g., "top choice" status).
  - **Solution:** Navan will clarify legal guidelines to inform a new artifact that flags content for review, similar to a process used for a healthcare client.
  - **Owner:** Libby/Rich will get guidance from the legal team and share it with Aida.

### Tone & Personality

  - **Problem:** Content feels too "straightforward" and lacks personality.
  - **Solution:** Use Rich's feedback on drafts to refine writing guidelines.
  - **Contingency:** If a single guideline proves ineffective, Aida proposed a separate artifact to add tone at the draft stage without sacrificing informational quality.
