  - **Search Visibility (GSC):**
      - **Position 1 Keywords:** Increased from 32 to 51.

  - **Competitive Presence (Scrunch):**
      - Alex's presence grew from 0% to \~4% in three months.
      - **Insight:** This steady growth validates the content strategy and suggests continued publishing will increase market share.

### New Messaging: "Agentic Recruit"

  - **Repositioning:** From "AI recruiter" to "agentic recruit."
  - **Four-Agent System:**
    1.  **Alex Interview:** Conducts interviews.
    2.  **Alex Verify:** Detects candidate fraud (e.g., resume/LinkedIn mismatches, spoofed addresses).
    3.  **Alex Connect:** Integrates with ATS (e.g., Bullhorn) to sync data and automate interview creation.
    4.  **Alex Discover:** Uses natural language to find candidates in the ATS.

### Accelerating Growth

  - **Paid Ads for Keyword Testing:**
      - **Strategy:** Test commercial keywords with paid ads to validate their lead quality and acquisition cost.
      - **Rationale:** This provides fast feedback to inform the organic content strategy, which has a longer lead time.
  - **Leverage Social Proof:**
      - Use customer stories and case studies on landing pages to support product claims and build trust.
  - **Target Competitor Alternatives:**
      - [Create content and ads for "alternatives to \[competitor\]" searches to capture high-intent users actively seeking a new solution.](https://fathom.video/share/zFBPuXrT2rfPqgDJSfH9vAL3dPxjAp1C?tab=summary&timestamp=1494.0)
  - **Webinars (Limited):**
      - Webinars with ATS partners are now difficult, as many ATS companies are developing their own AI features.
