## Context

GrowthX is delivering content marketing and SEO visibility strategy services to RAMP, a finance operations platform. The partnership centers on CashflowDesk, a resource site designed to educate finance operators on cash flow, accounting, and operations-related topics. This is part of a broader strategic initiative to establish CashflowDesk as an independent authority in the financial operations space, separate from RAMP's core brand. The team has been moving at an accelerated pace to get the site live and establish the foundational content strategy before the end-of-year holidays.

---

## Relevance

- **To GrowthX Delivery:** CashflowDesk site requires complete brand independence from RAMP (removing all "backed by RAMP" mentions and RAMP tool integrations). GrowthX is implementing its proprietary AI visibility tool to replace Scrunch for tracking performance, organized by topic and funnel stage (awareness, evaluation, decision). Priority is setting up the tool ASAP before scaling content production to 5 articles/week.
- **To CheckThat:** The AI visibility tool deployment on CashflowDesk is a key testing ground for CheckThat capabilities. - **To GrowthX Business Development:** CashflowDesk represents expansion potential beyond core RAMP services. The team is exploring strategic backlinks from RAMP.com to accelerate indexing and authority (a few links approved for initial traction). Victoria and team are also building cross-promotion with the CashflowDesk YouTube channel. 13 articles in production with target of 5/week indicates strong engagement and content velocity.
