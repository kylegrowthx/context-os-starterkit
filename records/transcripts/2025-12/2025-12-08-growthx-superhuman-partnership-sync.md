## Key Topics

### Superhuman's Content Challenge

  - **Strategic Goal:** Broaden Superhuman's website focus and audience as part of a major rebrand.
  - **Key Constraint:** The Grammarly website will be maintained due to its high search equity; the Coda site is under evaluation.
  - **Primary Need:** Rapidly scale high-quality content for the main Superhuman site.

### GrowthX's Solution: The Atlas Workflow

  - **Function:** An end-to-end partner for SEO/AEO content, acting as an extension of the client's team.
  - **Method:** Combines sophisticated AI workflows in the "Atlas" platform with expert human oversight.
  - **Core Principle:** Content quality is directly tied to the quality of the inputs ("artifacts") provided by the client.

### Human-in-the-Loop Review Process

  - **Mandatory Human Touchpoints:**
      - **Brief:** Editor validates the AI-generated assignment brief.
      - **Outline:** Editor reviews and edits the AI-generated outline.
      - **Draft:** Editor performs a deep read and final review.
  - **Goal:** Keep final human editing time under 30 minutes per article. If it exceeds this, it signals that the initial inputs (artifacts) need refinement.

### Fact-Checking for Technical Accuracy

  - **AI Fact-Checker:** A workflow step that validates content against client-specified, authoritative sources.
  - **Human Verification:** An editor manually clicks all links and verifies information to prevent errors, especially for highly technical or compliance-related topics.
  - **Product Accuracy:** The initial strategy sprint builds a "company context" artifact with product details. This artifact is the source of truth, reducing the need for client review over time as trust is established.

### SEO & AEO Integration

  - **SEO (Traditional Search):**
      - **Strategy:** Keyword gap analysis (SEMrush) to find opportunities where competitors rank.
      - **Performance:** Weekly/bi-weekly meetings to review metrics (clicks, impressions, conversions) and pivot strategy.
      - **Optimization:** Content refreshes (meta titles, content updates) to improve click-through rates.
  - **AEO/GEO (AI Search):**
      - **Tracking:** Uses Scrunch and LLM referrals in GA4 to monitor AI visibility.
      - **Execution:** Workflows are engineered with GEO best practices (e.g., structured data, clear definitions) to create content favored by LLMs.

### Production Timeline & Logistics

  - **Production Volume:** 10 articles/week for Superhuman (5 net new, 5 refreshes).
  - **Workflow:** Content is delivered for review on a rolling basis throughout the week via Google Docs.
  - **Review Cadence:** A clear, agreed-upon review schedule is critical to prevent publishing bottlenecks.
  - **Content Series:** A dedicated workflow pipeline is used to maintain context and ensure consistency while preventing overlap between articles in a series.
