## Context

N-able (also referred to as "Enable" in the transcript) is a cybersecurity company transitioning from a managed service provider (MSP) focus to a "cyber resilience" positioning, anchored by their acquisition of AdLumen MDR. GrowthX was engaged for a content marketing engagement focused on SEO content to support this strategic repositioning. The team initially generated 6-7 content pieces for N-able's blog, but when those drafts were shared with N-able's Product Marketing Managers (PMMs) for review, the PMMs flagged accuracy issues and product feature misrepresentations. This prompted GrowthX to implement a new 2-step review workflow ensuring product correctness before editorial review. Matthew Alves-Hill is newly assigned to the account; Aida Knezevic has been leading strategy and client relationship management, while Lawrence Neves is the primary writer/producer.

## Key Topics

### Account Context & Strategy

  - **Client:** N-able, a cybersecurity company.
  - **Strategic Shift:** Repositioning from an MSP-focused vendor to a "cyber resilience" provider.
  - **Growth Driver:** The newly acquired AdLumen MDR product, which enables a full suite of services: prevention, detection, response, and restoration.
  - **Content Strategy:**
      - **Goal:** Create SEO content that supports the new brand positioning.
      - **Focus:** How-to guides, listicles, and thought leadership.
      - **Exclusion:** Foundational "what is" content, which belongs in N-able's internal "cyber encyclopedia."
      - **Process:** Before writing, Google the primary keyword with `site:enable.com` to check for existing content to refresh.

### New Review & Production Process

  - **Problem:** Initial drafts were flagged as inaccurate by N-able's Product Marketing Managers (PMMs).
  - **Solution:** A new 2-step review process was implemented to ensure product accuracy before editorial review.
  - **New Workflow:**
    1.  **PMM Review:** Drafts are routed to the relevant PMM based on the `Product Focus` field in Airtable.
    2.  **Editorial Review:** After PMM feedback is addressed, the draft moves to Elaine for stylistic review.
    3.  **Publishing:** N-able publishes the final article (no CMS access for GrowthX).
  - **Airtable Updates:**
      - PMMs now have editing access to comment on topics.
      - A `Product Focus` field was added to route drafts correctly.
      - A `Brief Description` (generated by Claude) now precedes each topic to give PMMs context.

### Gated Assets

  - **Requirement:** Gated assets are a contractual deliverable, replacing 1–2 standard articles each.
  - **Execution Plan:**
      - Repurpose content from N-able's existing reports (e.g., Annual Threat Report).
      - Create a \~2,000-word asset (standard article length) from extracted chapters.
      - **Design:** Katya will design the asset.
      - **Approval:** The first asset's outline is approved; it now requires PMM input on where to mention N-able solutions.

### Production & Communication

  - **Production Status:** No articles have been published. All 6–7 completed drafts are now "In Production" and require revision to meet the new PMM review standards.
  - **Communication:** N-able is an email-only client (no Slack). They are responsive, and this has not caused delays.
  - **Image Workflow:** Lawrence will select images from the shared N-able image folder. Katya is merging this workflow into the main agentic pipeline.
