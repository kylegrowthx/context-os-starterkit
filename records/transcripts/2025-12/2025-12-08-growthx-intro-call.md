## Context

Federato is a property & casualty (P&C) insurance software platform serving insurance carriers and Managing General Agents (MGAs). The company recently secured $100M in Series D funding and is rapidly expanding its product line beyond its original underwriting focus to cover the full insurance policy lifecycle: underwriting, claims, billing, and distribution. Jenn DeRome, their content lead, is being stretched thin as the content team shrinks to one person. Federato's CMO recommended GrowthX as a partner to help scale content operations. The company has already been using a custom GPT to generate content, but finds it unengaging and unconverted—they need higher-quality, thought leadership-driven content that ranks in both traditional search and AI search engines, particularly to capture traffic from their narrow but high-intent insurance carrier audience.
