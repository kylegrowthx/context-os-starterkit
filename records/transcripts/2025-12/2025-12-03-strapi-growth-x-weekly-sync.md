## Key Topics

### Monthly Performance Review (Nov)

  - **Traffic & Impressions:**
      - Sessions and engaged sessions increased MoM.
      - Non-branded impressions also increased, maintaining a positive trend.
      - Top 10 pages are all blog posts, including recent publications.
  - **Conversions & Contacts:**
      - Overall converted events declined MoM.
      - A significant drop in new contact acquisition was observed in HubSpot.
      - **Hypothesis:** Over-indexing on informational content → attracting top-funnel users.
      - **Action:** Investigate the cause and consider creating more bottom-funnel content.
  - **Cohort & Page Performance:**
      - Cohort performance remained stable, with no major ranking volatility.
      - GrowthX-generated integration pages lost the most sessions MoM.
      - **Hypothesis:** Low total addressable market (TAM) for these specific integrations.
      - **Action:** A refresh report is due this week to confirm this.
  - **Competitive Landscape:**
      - Scrunch data shows Strapi remains well ahead of Contentful and Sanity across all tracked queries.
      - No major ranking positions were lost in key categories.

### Content Pipeline & Issue Resolution

  - **Comparison Page Table:**
      - The large table will be removed.
      - The agency is building a backend toggle to control its display on a page-by-page basis.
  - **Comparison Page Videos:**
      - Theo will review the list of pages needing video refreshes.
  - **FAQ Components:**
      - Capabilities & Features pages are complete.
      - Integrations pages are being fixed (resolving layout issues from long FAQs). **ETA: Friday.**
  - **Integration Pages:**
      - New pages are being generated in parallel with the FAQ updates and will be published this week and next.
      - Internal cross-linking is also being fixed to improve SEO.
  - **LLM "About Strapi" Page:**
      - The page is not rendering markdown formatting (e.g., H2s) correctly.
      - Theo will investigate the cause.

### Q1 2026 Content Strategy

  - **Core Focus:** Double down on the developer persona with a "fewer but better" content strategy.
  - **New Initiatives:**
      - **Backlink Generation:** Create integration pages for emerging tech and partner with those companies for backlinks in their official docs.
          - **Model:** Successful partnerships with Expo and Resend.
      - **"Strapify" (Codename):** Identify popular templates and add Strapi as the backend.
          - **Collaboration:** Paul (code) + GrowthX (blog post).
      - **Agency Persona:** Research agency-specific queries to fill content gaps.
          - **Action:** Vivek to check Octave for agency persona data.
      - **Social Listening:** Create timely content on new releases (e.g., Next.js 16) and answer questions on subreddits.
  - **Blocker:** The Kappa/Octolens content gap integration is on hold.
      - **Reason:** Engineering is prioritizing reusable, tool-agnostic workflows.
      - **Timeline:** Likely unblocked when Atlas opens to customers in late Q1.

### Image Workflow & Feedback

  - **Process:** Enhanced workflow uses a Figma template and generates images based on highlighted text in the content.
  - **Feedback:**
      - **Quality:** Images are a significant improvement and are highly technical.
      - **Quantity:** Only one image per post is being included.
  - **Action:** Vivek will ask Usman to increase the number of images per article.
  - **Tracking:** Victor will grant GrowthX Hotjar access to analyze user interaction with the new images via heatmaps.
