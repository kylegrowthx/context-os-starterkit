## Context

Adventures Group operates three tour destinations (Cabo, Puerto Vallarta, and Cancun) offering specialized tours including whale watching, dolphin experiences, cultural excursions, and water activities. GrowthX has been supporting their organic content strategy, which has grown to drive 60-62% of revenue in 2025. However, the client is experiencing a decline in e-commerce revenue (projected -10% for the year), primarily driven by underperforming paid media, while organic continues to be their strongest channel. Jo Kaminska, the new Engagement Manager, joined the team to replace the previous support structure with a more specialized setup: she handles strategy and analytics while Mariana Montezzana (existing team member) focuses on content production and editing. This meeting marks the first coordination call with the new structure and addresses immediate business challenges, particularly the drop in dolphin tour sales in Cabo and Vallarta.

## Key Topics

### Team & Workflow Updates

  - **GrowthX Team Structure:**
      - **Jo Kaminska:** Engagement Manager → Strategy, analytics, topic selection.

  - **Content Feedback Loop:**
      - Mariana will convert recurring client feedback (e.g., tour sequence errors) into formal guidelines to streamline the workflow and prevent repeated issues.
  - **AI Content Direction:**
      - **Principle:** Human oversight is critical for AI content.
      - **Rationale:** AI can misinterpret intent, as seen when it turned a niche keyword ("AI call system QA") into a generic topic ("QA framework").
      - **Action:** Provide detailed briefs with H2/H3 structures and semantically related keywords to ensure accuracy and capture user intent.

  - **Primary Goal:** Expand content scope to capture broader search traffic and funnel it to specific tours.

      - **Problem:** Significant sales drop for Dolphin tours in Cabo and Vallarta.
      - **Solution:** Launch a content campaign linking Dolphin programs to the popular Whale Watching season.
      - **Support:** Lorena will provide content on the "saving sea lions" campaign to create a positive narrative.
  - **New Tour Content:**
      - **Cabo:** New "catastrophe" boat tours and a Whale Watching/Dolphin combo.
      - **Cancun:** No new tours; focus on refreshing existing content.
          - **Update:** Re-add the detail about climbing the Coba pyramid, as it is a key highlight for visitors.
  - **"Shibauba" Keyword Article Approved:**
      - **Decision:** Publish the article targeting the high-volume keyword "Shibauba" (~9k monthly searches).
      - **Rationale:** The former competitor park is closed, creating an opportunity to own the term by framing it around the local legend of the "magic rain."

### Performance & Scaling Initiatives

  - **2025 Performance Review:**
      - **Organic Channel:** Strong growth; now drives 60–62% of revenue.
      - **Overall Revenue:** Projected -10% e-commerce revenue, primarily due to underperforming paid media.
  - **Crunch Dashboard Optimization:**
      - **Goal:** Improve AI visibility tracking for more granular, location-specific data.
      - **Action:** Reset the dashboard to tag prompts by location (Cabo, Vallarta, Cancun) and funnel stage (Awareness, Evaluation).
  - **Marieta Islands Landing Page:**
      - **Success:** The manually built travel guide for Marieta Islands is performing well.
      - **Challenge:** The manual build process is not scalable.
      - **Goal:** Automate the creation of similar travel guides for other destinations to replicate this success efficiently.
  - **Social Media Support:**
      - **Request:** Help identifying blog posts suitable for social media repurposing (Reels, Stories).
      - **GrowthX Offer:** Provide content suggestions for the client's team to execute.
