### Messaging Strategy: "Pipelines" vs. "Workflows"

  - **Directive:** Raj wants to use "data workflows" instead of "data pipelines" for analytics-focused content.
  - **Rationale:** The terms may appeal to different user personas.
      - **"Pipelines"** → Users with deep SQL/warehouse experience.
      - **"Workflows"** → Users primarily in BI tools.
  - **Challenge:** "Data pipelines" has significantly higher search volume, which conflicts with the SEO goal of driving top-line traffic.
  - **Resolution:**
      - **Primary Strategy:** Continue targeting high-volume "data pipelines" keywords.
      - **Secondary Strategy:** Test a few "data workflow" topics to find low-competition opportunities.
      - **Editorial Tactic:** Use qualifying clauses (e.g., "data analytics pipelines, sometimes referred to as data analytics workflows") to bridge the terminology gap.
