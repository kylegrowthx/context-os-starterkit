Bridget McGillivray conducted a hiring interview with Andre Webster for an Account Executive role at Profex (formerly GrowthX). ## Context

Profex (formerly GrowthX) is a content marketing and AI visibility company undergoing a strategic pivot from a pure services business to a platform-driven model. The company has achieved $14M in ARR by end of 2025 (up from $2M at the start of that year) and is targeting $50M by end of 2026. Bridget McGillivray runs the ops teams and works closely with Tyler (Head of Sales) to structure the go-to-market motion. The company is in a critical hiring phase, adding an Account Management team (starting December 1st, 2025) to own renewals and freeing up sales to focus entirely on new business. Andre Webster is being evaluated as a potential Account Executive to accelerate growth as Profex transitions to the platform phase. The current sales team is small (one dedicated AE, Tyler) with strong win rates and large deal sizes ($200K+ 12-month service engagements), but stretched thin managing net new, expansions, and renewals simultaneously.

  - **Business Model Shift:** From a services-only agency to a platform company, a strategy funded by services revenue to de-risk product development.
  - **New Product Launches:**
      - **LLM Visibility Tool (Dec 2025):** Tracks brand ranking in LLM search to inform content strategy.

  - **Go-to-Market Evolution:**
      - **Dual Motion:** A self-serve, lower-ACV PLG motion for the LLM tool, alongside a high-touch, white-glove service offering.
      - **Sales Focus:** Renewals are now handled by a new Account Management team, freeing sales reps for pure new business and upsells.
      - **Comp Plan:** An uncapped, open-ended 3% commission on all closed deals, designed to maximize rep earning potential.

  - **Cypress (\<50 employees):** Built the GTM process for a new UI/accessibility product from scratch, including:
