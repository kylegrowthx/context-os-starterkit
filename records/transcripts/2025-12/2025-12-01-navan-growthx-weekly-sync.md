  - The content calendar is in Airtable with 140+ blog ideas for review.
  - **Prioritization Strategy:**

          - **AI Search:** Higher likelihood of appearing in AI-generated summaries (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).

  - **Content Cadence:**
      - **Week 1:** 1 calibration blog
      - **Week 2:** 3 blogs
      - **Scaling:** 5 blogs per week

  - **Featured Image Examples:** Two concepts (environment photography, human imagery) were shared for Navan's brand team to review.
