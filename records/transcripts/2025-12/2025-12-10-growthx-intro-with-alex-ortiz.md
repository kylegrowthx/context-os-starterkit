  - **Current State:**

  - **Evaluation:** The CEO was already evaluating Aerops, prompting Alex to add GrowthX to ensure a comprehensive review.

### GrowthX's Solution & Process

  - **Core Value Prop:** A full-service partner that handles the entire AI content pipeline (strategy, execution, publishing, monitoring), freeing client teams from technical overhead.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** Produces 5–10+ articles to establish quality and define the long-term roadmap.
    2.  **Growth Execution (Ongoing):** Executes on the roadmap, starting with editorial content (blogs, guides) and potentially expanding to programmatic use cases (e.g., Ramp's vendor directory).
  - **Onboarding:** A low-friction process requiring only a 1-hour kickoff and a 45-minute product deep dive. GrowthX ingests client resources (brand guides, transcripts) to build "artifacts"—detailed context documents that guide the AI.
  - **Platform:** An internal tool that uses artifacts and a knowledge graph to power agentic pipelines for research, drafting, and optimization. Human writers fine-tune each step and use AI-assisted editing.

### Scoping & Pricing

  - **Content Scope:**
      - **Primary Focus:** SEO/AEO content (editorial, programmatic).
      - **Out of Scope:** Product/use case pages (best handled in-house).
      - **Potential Add-on:** CEO thought leadership for LinkedIn.
  - **Pricing:** The initial quote was a significant concern for Alex.
      - **Onboarding:** $40,000
      - **Monthly Retainer:** $23,000
