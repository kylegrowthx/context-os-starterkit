## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Prioritization

  - **Goal:** Balance high-volume SEO topics with deep-dive product content to build authority and capture less-known use cases.
  - **Rationale:** The blog is new, so a balanced content mix is needed to avoid focusing too heavily on a single type (e.g., theory, competitive).
  - **SEO vs. Conversion:**
      - **Strategy:** Use high-volume topics (e.g., "product design") to build visibility and rankings.
      - **Rationale:** Even a small conversion percentage from this large audience is a win.
      - **Tactic:** Link to specific Vizcom resources and CTAs within these articles.
  - **AI Search Optimization:**
      - Prioritize topics where existing content is poor or incomplete.
      - **Rationale:** This allows Vizcom to stand out and be cited by LLMs quickly.

### Looker Dashboard Demo

  - Aida demoed the new Looker dashboard, which combines GA4 and GSC data for performance tracking.
  - **Key Reports:**
      - **Sessions:** Tracks overall vs. engaged sessions (duration \>10s, scroll, or click).
          - **Goal:** Increase the engaged session ratio by publishing more educational content.
      - **Landing Page Engagement:** Shows engagement rate (inverse of bounce rate).
          - **Significance:** Higher engagement rates improve SEO ranking.
      - **Non-Branded Traffic:** GSC data on non-branded queries.
      - **Conversions:** Lists all GA4 conversion events.
          - **Action:** Vizcom to clarify what each event tracks for accurate measurement.
      - **Cohort Page:** Tracks GrowthX content performance by topic cluster.
          - **Rationale:** Enables strategic refinement by showing which clusters drive traffic and engagement.
      - **Queries Report:** Shows keywords driving traffic.
          - **Use Case:** Discover new content opportunities from low-volume keywords not found in SEMrush.
      - **LLM Report:** Shows traffic from LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).
          - **Finding:** ChatGPT is the top referrer, a common pattern for GrowthX clients.
          - **Significance:** Publishing accurate content prevents LLMs from misrepresenting Vizcom's features.
