## Key Topics

### Content Workflow & Review Process

  - **Problem:** The previous JSON-only delivery format was inefficient for review, making it difficult to track updates and share content with external stakeholders like OpenAI.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will now deliver full Google Docs for all content.
      - **Rationale:** This provides a single source of truth, enabling consistent reviews and easier sharing.
      - **Format:** Each doc will contain the main content and the "Metronome's take," potentially on separate tabs.
  - **New Cadence:**
      - **Mondays/Tuesdays:** GrowthX sends a Slack update on the week's work.
      - **Fridays:** GrowthX sends a Slack roundup with links to all completed documents.

### Content Pipeline & Categorization

  - **Tagging:** Will will sync with Chris on content categorization, which directly informs the tagging requirements GrowthX needs to implement.
  - **Airtable Update:** GrowthX is creating a new Airtable tab for companies with \>2 products, as these require a different publishing process. Katya will provide a Loom walkthrough.

### Content Hold: `character.ai`

  - **Issue:** A recent 60 Minutes segment painted `character.ai` in a negative light, creating a sensitivity risk.
  - **Decision:** The `character.ai` page will be held from publication.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will investigate if a replacement is available from its 70-company pipeline. If not, Metronome will provide a new company.

### Stripe Acquisition

  - **Status:** The deal is not yet finalized.
  - **Guidance:** Metronome is operating "business as usual."
  - **Action:** Metronome will notify GrowthX if messaging changes are required post-acquisition.
