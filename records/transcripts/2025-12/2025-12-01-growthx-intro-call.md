## Key Topics

### Autodesk's AEC Data Model: The Awareness Gap

  - **Team:** Alexine's team manages the AEC data model, a platform providing granular data access (e.g., room data, single walls) without opening heavy BIM models.
  - **Audience:** Existing Autodesk customers, ranging from high-tech developers (using SDKs) to low-tech users needing simple buttons.
  - **Core Challenge:** A severe awareness gap. Only 3–10% of customers know the data model exists, despite high demand for related topics (e.g., data standardization) at industry events.

### The Content & Marketing Bottleneck

  - **Current State:** Content creation is ad-hoc, handled by product managers and one part-time contractor.
  - **Volume:** Only \~1 blog post per month.
  - **Goal:** Increase output to weekly blogs and thought leadership content.
  - **Constraint:** The team operates outside the official marketing org, limiting access to channels like email and social media. Content must be funded via contracting dollars.

### GrowthX's Solution & Budget Mismatch

  - **GrowthX's Model:** An end-to-end partner combining AI workflows with human experts to produce daily content for AI search.
      - **Phase 1:** 8-week strategy sprint ($30k).
      - **Phase 2:** 12-month execution ($15k/mo).
  - **Budget Conflict:** The AEC team's budget ($8k/mo now, $25k/qtr next year) is insufficient for a standalone engagement.
  - **Service Mismatch:** The AEC team needs thought leadership for existing customers, while GrowthX's core model is for net-new customer acquisition via AI search.

### The Path Forward: A Joint Project

  - **Opportunity:** A potential joint project with Sharon Perdue's SEO team, which is already in talks with GrowthX for strategy.
  - **Rationale:** A larger, cross-departmental contract could absorb the AEC team's budget and provide the execution support they need.
  - **Benefit for AEC Team:**
      - **Strategy:** Gain insights from the strategy sprint at no cost.
      - **Execution:** Potentially receive weekly content for the data model use case.
  - **Constraint:** Autodesk's "my money, your money" culture could complicate budget sharing.
