## Key Topics

### Phase 1 Progress & Benchmarks

  - GrowthX reviewed progress after 3 weeks of publishing, benchmarking against a client who saw consistent week-over-week traffic growth after 3 months.
  - **Traffic Forecasts:** Projections are based on 90 target keywords and a 5-article/week cadence.
      - **Limitation:** Forecasts are for organic search only (not LLMs) and are estimates, as articles often rank for more keywords than initially targeted.
  - **Seasonal Impact:** Holiday traffic is currently lower across all GrowthX clients.

### SEO vs. LLM Optimization

  - **Dual Focus:** The strategy optimizes for both SEO and LLMs, as best practices are largely the same.
      - **Core Tactics:** Clear headlines, chunked paragraphs, TLDRs, and FAQ sections.
      - **LLM-Specific:** Comprehensive topic clusters (e.g., productivity) establish authority, and original data (e.g., the "20% productivity improvement" stat) increases citation likelihood.
  - **LLM Traffic Volatility:** LLM referral traffic (currently 3–5% of total organic) is less predictable than traditional SEO, as platform algorithms change (e.g., ChatGPT's citation policy shift in late 2023).

### LLM Performance Tracking (Scrunch)

  - The Scrunch dashboard tracks Read.ai's performance against competitors across 336 prompts and 7 LLM platforms.
  - **Current Metrics:**
      - **Visibility:** 11% (up from \~0% in early Dec)
      - **Citation Rate & Position:** Both have increased over the last 12 weeks.
  - **Competitor Analysis:**
      - **Top 3:** Otter, Fireflies, Fathom.
      - **Action:** Add Granola to the tracking list.
  - **Prompt Strategy:** The current prompt list is meeting-centric. It must be expanded to cover new strategic areas like enterprise search and productivity.

### Strategic Alignment & Partnership Feedback

  - **Content Strategy Expansion:**
      - **Goal:** Shift brand perception beyond "meeting notetaker."
      - **Method:** Build authority via 8–9 new topic clusters (e.g., enterprise search, productivity) and persona-based content (e.g., sales, recruiting).
  - **Website Optimization (Phase 2):**
      - GrowthX will advise on adding prominent CTAs to blog posts to improve conversion.
      - **Challenge:** Implementing this on Webflow will require experimentation, possibly using code blocks in the CMS.
  - **Partnership Conflict:**
      - **Issue:** GrowthX's internal use of competitor tools (Fathom, Fireflies) undermines Read.ai's credibility.
      - **Significance:** This is a high-level strategic concern for Read.ai.
