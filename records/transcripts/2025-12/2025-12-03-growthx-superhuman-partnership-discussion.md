## Summary

Lake Tahoe and Tyler Pavlas pitched GrowthX's content and AEO services to Grammarly's organic growth lead Kelly Morr and product lead Jess Mordo, focusing on Grammarly's need to 3x content output across editorial, programmatic SEO, and paid landing pages. GrowthX proposed $30k/mo for 10 editorial pieces/week (double standard volume) plus a $10k/mo programmatic add-on ($5k discount), with an 8-week strategy sprint validating the partnership before a 12-month commitment. ## Key Topics

### Grammarly's Content Scaling Challenge

  - **Primary Goal:** 3x content output while maintaining brand quality.
  - **Required Content Types:**
      - Editorial content (guides, comparisons).
      - Programmatic SEO (hundreds-to-thousands of page variations).
      - Paid landing pages (high-volume copy for specific keywords).
  - **Key Concern:** The platform's ability to handle programmatic scale, as Grammarly's use cases are far larger than typical.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Hybrid Model:** Combines AI workflows with human experts (e.g., Matthew Panzerino, former TechCrunch EIC) for strategy, execution, and AEO monitoring.
  - **Programmatic Execution:**
      - **Pricing Model:** Cost depends on who creates the templates and provides data inputs.
          - **GrowthX Creates:** Higher cost.
          - **Grammarly Provides:** Lower cost.
      - **Engineering:** GrowthX has forward-deployed engineers for template creation (additional cost), but this is scoped during the 8-week strategy sprint.
  - **Onboarding Process:**
      - **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** Defines strategy and publishes \~10 pieces to validate the partnership before the 12-month term begins.
      - **Scope:** Typically covers editorial and one programmatic workstream to avoid overcommitment.

### Proposal & Pricing

  - **Context:** The proposal is timed with the Superhuman Mail contract renewal in February.
  - **Special Offer for Grammarly:**
      - **Editorial:** 10 pieces/week for $30k/mo (double the standard volume).
      - **Programmatic:** Add-on for $10k/mo (a $5k discount).
  - **Business Model Alignment:** Kelly clarified Grammarly is self-serve, not enterprise. Lake confirmed GrowthX's focus is on content, which aligns with this model.
