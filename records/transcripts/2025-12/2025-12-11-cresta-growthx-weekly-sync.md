## Key Topics

### Content Calendar Feedback

  - **Target Persona:** Shift from basic topics to advanced content for senior decision-makers.
  - **AI Agent Cluster:**
      - **Focus:** Explore use cases, best practices, and human-AI team management.
      - **Voice AI:** Increase representation to reflect market momentum.
      - **Positioning:** Differentiate Cresta from traditional chatbots.
  - **Competitive Cluster:**
      - **Focus:** Target direct AI competitors (e.g., Genesys AI, Nice AI), not general CCaaS platforms.
      - **Rationale:** Cresta integrates with CCaaS platforms, rather than competing directly.
  - **Macro Theme:** Reframe "contact center" content as "customer experience" to broaden appeal and target a wider audience.

### First Sprint: Blog Selection & Messaging

  - **Goal:** Use the first blogs to calibrate product messaging early in the sprint.
  - **Initial Selection:**
      - Cresta vs. Nexidia
      - Cresta vs. Verint
      - [Cresta vs. \[3rd competitor\]](https://fathom.video/share/nvrMv_EatoQbpSZ-M4vWfZPKGC1xVfTd?tab=summary&timestamp=898.0)
  - **Correction:** The Nextiva comparison was removed.
      - **Rationale:** Nextiva is a UCaaS platform, not a direct competitor.
      - **Replacement:** The Verint comparison was selected.
  - **Resource:** A product feature matrix (in Notion) will ensure accuracy.
      - **Maintenance:** Cresta will add comments with links to announcements for new product launches.

### Content Deployment & QA

  - **Placement:** A new "Guides" section will be created under Resources, separate from the main blog, to house all GrowthX content.
  - **Process:**
    1.  GrowthX stages content in Webflow.
    2.  Cresta reviews and provides feedback.
    3.  Cresta publishes the final version.
  - **Rationale:** This process ensures Cresta retains final QA and publishing control.

### New Looker Dashboard

  - **Purpose:** Unify GA4 and GSC data for performance tracking.
  - **Key Reports:**
      - **GA4 Overview:** Sessions, engagement, landing page performance.
      - **Non-Branded:** Traffic from non-branded keywords.
      - **Conversions:** Tracks GA4 conversion events.
      - **Cohort:** Tracks performance of GrowthX content by cluster and URL.
      - **LLM Traffic:** Shows referral traffic from sources like ChatGPT and Perplexity.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will grant Cresta edit access to the dashboard.
  - **Idea:** Automate alerts for high-intent keywords with low rankings to identify new content opportunities.
