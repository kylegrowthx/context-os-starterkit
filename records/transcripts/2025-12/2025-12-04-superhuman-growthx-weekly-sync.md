## Relevance

### Content Strategy & Execution

  - The primary focus is refreshing existing content, not creating new articles.
  - **Goal:** Update messaging for the Superhuman Mail rebrand and integrate new product angles.
  - **Grammarly & Coda Integration:**
      - Mentions will be natural and value-focused, not promotional.
      - **Rationale:** Avoid keyword competition with Grammarly's SEO team.
      - **Action:** Link directly to product pages (e.g., Grammarly.com), not to partnership content.
  - **Topical Pillars:**
      - The strategy will be refined into 5–6 core clusters to better track performance.
      - **Process:** Jo will create a Notion doc for review by Morgan, Alex, and Harmony.

### November Performance Review

  - **Traffic & Engagement:**
      - **Sessions:** 6,500 (flat vs. Oct).
      - **Engaged Sessions:** \~4,000 (\~60% rate).
      - **Rationale:** Flat traffic is a positive signal for a typically slow B2B month.
  - **Google Search Console (GSC):**
      - **Top 10 Rankings:** Grew 24% (500→640 queries).
      - **Rationale:** This is a strong signal of increasing organic visibility.
  - **LLM Traffic:**
      - **Trend:** Declined to \<300 sessions in November, down from a September peak.
      - **Source:** Primarily ChatGPT, with Perplexity and Gemini contributing.
      - **Rationale:** LLM traffic is a small fraction (1–5%) of total sessions.
  - **Scrunch (LLM Visibility):**
      - **Superhuman Visibility:** 26% (1,100+ responses).
      - **Rationale:** Superhuman leads the pack, but the channel is highly volatile and inconsistent.

### Competitive Analysis: Fixer

  - **Problem:** Fixer is winning LLM visibility for Gmail/Outlook-related prompts.
  - **Context:** Fixer is a more affordable, easier-to-adopt tool that works within existing email clients.
  - **Insight:** Superhuman Mail is a full email client replacement, not an add-on.
  - **Action:** The team will review Superhuman's competitive positioning (Notion doc) to refine its strategy against Fixer.
