## Key Topics

### Artifact Review & Pricing Correction

  - Jordan noted the competitor list artifact contained incorrect pricing info, likely AI-generated.
  - **Correction:** Turnstile's model is a platform fee + percentage, not a fixed $100/mo.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will update the internal Feature Matrix (the source for content generation) to reflect this.

### Content Calendar & Strategy

  - GrowthX presented the content calendar in Airtable, containing \~70 blog topics.
  - **Structure:** Topics are organized into clusters:
      - Foundational & Educational
      - Lower-Volume, Persona-Specific
      - High-Intent Commercial (e.g., solution comparisons)
  - **Review:** Turnstile will review the list to prioritize topics for the 3-post launch and suggest additions.

### Calibration Blog & Launch Plan

  - The "Revenue Leakage" calibration blog draft is ready for review.
  - **Purpose:** To calibrate on writing style, voice, and Turnstile's integration into content.
  - **Status:** This is a real post and will be one of the three launch articles.
  - **Launch Strategy:**
      - **Goal:** Launch the blog with three posts to appear more substantial.
      - **Rationale:** The three posts can internally link to each other, which helps Google index the new content faster.
      - **Constraint:** Direct competitor comparisons (e.g., vs. Stripe) are deferred to avoid a "bullseye" on one company.

### Review Process & Timeline

  - A fast-track review process was established to meet a press announcement deadline.
  - **Process:**
    1.  Core team (Jordan, Seema) reviews drafts.
    2.  Others (Mike, Lillian) are notified and have a 48-hour window for feedback.
    3.  Post is published if no feedback is received.
  - **Rationale:** This "publish and refine" approach accelerates learning from performance data (indexing, clicks) within the 8-week sprint.

### Technical Setup & Tools

  - **Looker Dashboard:** Build is unblocked by Mat Rider granting GA4 access. The dashboard will expand from 3 to 7 pages to include GA4 data.
  - **CMS:** Mat Rider sent an invite to the CMS.
  - **Atlas Platform:** Aida demoed GrowthX's internal AI content platform.
      - **Process:** An agentic pipeline uses a researcher (Exa) for deep searches → drafts content → validates against guidelines → human review.
      - **Availability:** Aida will ask Marcel if Turnstile can get access to Atlas.
