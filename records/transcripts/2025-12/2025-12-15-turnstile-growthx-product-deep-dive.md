## Overview

- **Replaces Manual Processes:** Turnstile replaces error-prone, ad-hoc workflows (spreadsheets, DocuSign, Slack) with a single source of truth for sales-led startups.
- **Flexible Quoting:** The WYSIWYG quoting tool supports complex pricing (e.g., usage-based tiers, custom payment schedules) without rigid plans, adapting to a startup's evolving business model.
- **Granular Automation:** The invoicing workflow allows per-customer automation levels—from fully manual review to auto-charge—enabling teams to focus on high-value accounts.
- **Content Focus:** Key themes are professionalizing operations, starting early to prevent pain, and empowering non-finance founders with an intuitive tool.

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Manual, Disconnected Workflows

  - **Current State:** Startups use a patchwork of tools (Word, DocuSign, Stripe, Slack) for quoting and billing, leading to:
      - **Revenue Leakage:** No single source of truth to track contracts vs. invoices.
      - **Manual Errors:** Copy-pasting data from CRMs like HubSpot.
      - **Process Gaps:** Contract amendments often fail to update billing systems.
  - **Turnstile's Solution:** A unified quote-to-cash platform that guides users through a "foolproof" process, ensuring consistency and control.

### The Product: A Unified Quote-to-Cash Platform

  - **Quoting:**
      - **WYSIWYG Editor:** A Notion-like interface for building quotes from scratch, offering more flexibility than rigid, wizard-based CPQ tools.
      - **Complex Pricing:** Supports flat fees, usage-based tiers (e.g., API calls), and custom payment schedules (e.g., paid pilot + annual plan).
      - **CRM Integration:** Syncs with HubSpot, Salesforce, and Adio to pull customer data.
  - **Subscription Management:**
      - **Unified Customer View:** A central page for all customer data, subscriptions, and invoices.
      - **Self-Service Portal:** A shareable portal for customers to manage billing info and view history.
      - **Amendment Flow:** A built-in process to backdate/forward-date contract changes, automatically updating billing.
  - **Billing Automation:**
      - **Swimlane Workflow:** Invoices move through stages (Draft → Review → Issued → Paid).
      - **Per-Customer Automation:** Teams can set different levels of automation for each customer, from fully manual review to auto-charge.
      - **Payment Provider Flexibility:** Integrates with Stripe for payments and can optionally use Stripe for invoice delivery.
  - **Reporting:**
      - **Investor Dashboard:** A standard view of key metrics (ARR, customer count, outstanding invoices).
      - **Explorable Data:** All metrics are clickable for drill-down analysis.
      - **Custom Reports:** Supports SQL queries for advanced reporting.

### AI Capabilities

  - **PDF Ingestion:** An AI tool to parse external contracts (e.g., customer paper) and automatically create subscriptions in Turnstile.
  - **Anomaly Detector:** A background AI system to flag billing errors (e.g., duplicate invoices, fat-fingered usage).
  - **Orchestration Chatbot (Q1 2025):**
      - **Slack Chatbot:** An internal tool for orchestrating tasks (e.g., "send invoice," "which invoices are due?").
      - **Intercom Chatbot:** A public-facing bot for answering general questions, trained on Turnstile documentation.

### Content & Marketing Strategy

  - **Key Message:** Startups should adopt Turnstile early to professionalize operations and prevent future pain, rather than waiting for a billing crisis.
  - **Target Audience:** Non-finance founders (e.g., engineers) who are novices in quote-to-cash processes.
  - **CMS:** Webflow. The blog and resources section will be live by the end of this week, enabling GrowthX to begin publishing.
