## Key Topics

### Content Workflow & Cadence

  - **Problem:** The current Slack-based workflow is inefficient for managing the 5-article/week review cadence.
  - **Solution:** A new `Content OS` workflow will centralize status tracking.
      - **Process:**
        1.  **Udemy:** Mark articles `Revisions Needed` → notifies GrowthX.
        2.  **GrowthX:** Apply revisions → mark `Revisions Complete`.
        3.  **Udemy:** Publish → mark `Published` → notifies GrowthX to grab the URL.
  - **Goal:** GrowthX to take over publishing in mid-January, pending Udemy team onboarding to the CMS.
