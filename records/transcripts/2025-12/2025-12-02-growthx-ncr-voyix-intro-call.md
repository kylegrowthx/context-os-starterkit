## Key Topics

### NCR Voyix's Content Challenge

  - **Context:** NCR Voyix is a smaller, more focused company following a split from NCR.
  - **Team:** A lean 2-person team manages all content, not just blogs.
  - **Production:** Currently zero blog posts due to focus on other priorities.
  - **Agency Silos:** Multiple agencies handle specific content types (website build, PR) but none focus on ongoing SEO/GEO content.
  - **AI Adoption:** Internal use is elementary; legal/procurement hurdles block advanced marketing AI tools.
  - **Goal:** Improve performance in all search types (SEO, GEO, AEO, TEO, LLMs) via a new content partner.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** An end-to-end service combining proprietary AI workflows with human experts.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Context Engineering:** GrowthX uses client resources (ICP docs, brand guides) to build internal "artifacts" that inform its AI workflows.
    2.  **AI-Powered Drafting:** Workflows generate initial drafts, briefs, and research.
    3.  **Human Refinement:** A 60+ person team of SEO/content experts refines drafts for brand voice and accuracy.
  - **Features:**
      - **Fact-Checking:** AI validates every passage; editors review suggested rewrites for low-confidence claims.
      - **Content Refresh:** Proactive refresh of existing content every \~3 months to maintain relevance.
      - **Publishing:** Handles staging and publishing directly in Webflow.
      - **LLM Visibility:** Monitors content performance in AI answers to inform strategy.

### Partnership Model & Pricing

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint**
      - **Cost:** $30,000
      - **Duration:** 8 weeks
      - **Deliverables:**
          - Context artifacts (brand, audience, company)
          - 10–20 published articles
          - A content roadmap
  - **Phase 2: 12-Month Retainer**
      - **Cost:** Estimated $15,000–$18,000/month
      - **Term:** 12 months
      - **Decision Point:** NCR Voyix can opt out after Phase 1.
