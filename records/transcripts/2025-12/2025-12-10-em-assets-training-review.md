## Key Topics

### Problem: Disconnected Account Health Data

  - Account health data is disjointed across Notion, making it hard to connect risk signals (Red/Yellow status) to specific actions.
  - This structure hinders proactive risk management and prevents the team from identifying patterns across accounts (e.g., product issues, training gaps).
  - **Desired Workflow:** A "golden thread" connecting risk status to:
      - A clear action plan.
      - A request for help (if needed).
      - A closeout/follow-up to drive accountability.
  - **Tooling Evaluation:** The team is evaluating platforms (Vitally, Monday, Pylon) to unify this data. Vitally is a strong candidate, as it can also integrate Fathom call recordings for deeper analysis.

### Solution: Structured EM Training & Assets

  - **Goal:** Standardize EM workflows to ensure consistent, high-quality customer experience and elevate the team's strategic value.
  - **Identified Training Gaps:**
      - **QBRs & Renewals:** No formal templates or agendas exist for these critical conversations.
      - **GrowthX Positioning:** EMs need a consistent talk track to frame GrowthX's value as a strategic partner, not just an editorial agency.
      - **Product Demos:** EMs need training to demo the Atlas platform, a key differentiator that showcases the complexity and value of GrowthX's AI-driven systems.
      - **AI Industry Expertise:** The team must become AI experts. This requires a formal program (e.g., internal newsletter, lunch-and-learns) to curate and deliver industry trends to EMs.
  - **Training Program Ownership:** Andi has started a Notion board to map the customer journey and identify asset gaps. This will be shared with Marisol and George to ensure a collaborative and comprehensive training program.

### Solution: New KPI Framework for EMs

  - **Current State:** The primary KPI is "don't churn." Formal CSAT surveys were ineffective, and health scores are not yet calibrated enough to be a reliable KPI.
  - **Q1 "Bingo Card" Pilot:** A gamified model will be used to track completion of core EM tasks (e.g., running a QBR, discussing AI trends).
      - **Rationale:** This pilot ensures EMs perform all key functions, providing the data needed to define formal, outcome-based KPIs for Q2.
      - **Incentive Ideas:** Non-monetary rewards like "dancing bananas" or a "Marcel cameo" were suggested to keep the pilot lighthearted and fun.

### Director Role Refinement

  - The Director role is being refined to focus on three distinct areas, providing a clear framework for their contribution:
    1.  **Proactive Services:** Delivering specific, billable services like SEO audits.
    2.  **Escalated Support:** Stepping in to support at-risk accounts with critical strategic needs.
    3.  **Training & Enablement:** Leading team-wide training to build expertise (e.g., data analysis, AI trends).
