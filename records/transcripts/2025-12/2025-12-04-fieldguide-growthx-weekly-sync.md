  - **Context:** This is Week 7 of Phase 1, prompting a summary document for the renewal decision.
  - **Early Results:** The first 2 published articles show strong initial performance.
      - **Avg. Position:** 6.5 (page 1) and 13 (page 2).
      - **Significance:** This validates the strategy, as Google quickly indexed and ranked the content, demonstrating quality.
  - **Renewal Timeline:**
      - **GrowthX:** Deliver Phase 1 summary document today.
      - **Fieldguide:** Provide renewal decision by early next week.

### Competitor Content Strategy

  - **Proposal:** Create a competitor content cluster to control the narrative in LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  - **Rationale:** Competitors may publish negative content that LLMs then cite, as seen in a GrowthX client case.
      - **Example:** A competitor's article claimed a client wasn't an "enterprise solution." This was cited by LLMs, forcing a reactive content strategy to counter the narrative.
  - **Benefit:** Proactively publishing accurate, product-focused comparisons ensures LLMs have the correct information about Fieldguide's features and capabilities.
