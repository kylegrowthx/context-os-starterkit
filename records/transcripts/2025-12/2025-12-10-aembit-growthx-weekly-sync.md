## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Strategy Rationale

- **Performance Metrics:** Sessions and Engaged Sessions are the primary metrics.
  - **Session:** A single visit to the site.
  - **Engaged Session:** A session lasting more than 10 seconds or including a click. The benchmark is 50% of total sessions.
- **Current Strategy:** Focus on niche topics (e.g., "agentic AI security") is effective but has a small audience, limiting top-funnel growth.
- **New Strategy:** Broaden reach by targeting high-volume cybersecurity topics.
  - **Goal:** Drive top-funnel traffic and associate Aembit with broader cybersecurity conversations.
  - **Format:** High-level topic overview → Dedicated Aembit POV section.
  - **Benefit:** Reduces Aembit's editing burden, increasing content velocity.
- **Breach Analysis:** A potential new content type for topical authority.
  - **Rationale:** Capture audience attention on trending topics and recent security events.
  - **Condition:** Must connect to Aembit's core non-human identity principles; Dan will only approve breaches related to identity/authentication issues, not generic data breaches.
