## Context

Lovable is a rapid AI-native design and development platform where users can build fully functional web applications and landing pages using natural language prompts. Ceci Stallsmith leads product marketing at Lovable. GrowthX (led by Jason Gong and including Will Proctor and Danni Roseman) is a B2B content marketing and AI visibility agency that has been partnering with Lovable on co-marketing initiatives. Marcel Santilli, a key GrowthX strategist, was on the calendar but unavailable during the call due to competing client work.

This was a planning meeting to finalize the structure and content for an upcoming live workshop featuring both Lovable and GrowthX. The workshop aims to showcase how AI-native tools can drive rapid growth and teach marketers and builders how to effectively use Lovable for landing page creation, prototyping, and product design. The 500+ sign-ups before any major promotion signaled strong organic interest, making this a high-visibility event for both teams.
