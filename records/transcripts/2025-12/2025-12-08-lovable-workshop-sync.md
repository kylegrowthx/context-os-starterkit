### Shaping Loop Framework

The "shaping loop" is a systematic approach to improving AI-generated content by iterating on discrete design components (e.g., header font choice, image style, color palette). Rather than regenerating entire pieces, teams iterate on individual elements and measure impact. This breaks down complex tasks into smaller, repeatable loops. The framework applies to both product design and marketing execution, eliminating traditional boundaries between the two disciplines. Marcel is shaping the overall loop direction; Jason is implementing it in content templating for Lovable and CheckThat.
