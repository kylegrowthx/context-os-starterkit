### Content Strategy: Hub & Spoke Model

  - **Goal:** Build Google authority for core topics by publishing foundational "Hub" articles first.

  - **Cadence:** Hubs (first 3 articles) → Spokes (this week's 5 articles) → Hubs (next week's articles).
  - **Review Scope:** Sarah's review focuses on tone and correct Read references, not deep content edits, as GrowthX handles SEO-optimized length (\~1,500 words).
