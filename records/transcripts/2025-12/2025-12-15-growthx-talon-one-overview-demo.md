## Key Topics

### Talon.One's Content Challenges

  - **Capacity:** 2-person team handles all content (blogs, e-books, social, PR).
  - **Output:** Limited to \~5 blogs/mo, 1 gated asset/qtr, 2 research reports/yr.
  - **Quality:** Past freelancers produced generic content lacking deep business/persona understanding.
  - **Goal:** Increase content quantity and quality, reduce paid ad spend, and improve GEO visibility.
  - **Key Concern:** Simon (Product) needs a process for handling the subtle nuances between Talon.One's product features (e.g., promotions vs. loyalty) to prevent AI hallucinations.

### GrowthX's Solution: AI-Driven Content Engine

  - **Model:** Combines custom AI workflows with human experts (e.g., ex-TechCrunch EIC, ex-SEMrush).
  - **Output:** 5 articles/week (new or refresh), with Talon.One's role shifting to review and feedback.
  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (Pre-Commitment)**
      - Deep dive to create foundational artifacts: ICP profiles, brand guidelines, writing style.
      - Delivers \~10 calibration articles to align on quality before a 12-month commitment is required.
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12-Month Commitment)**
      - Scales content production to 5 articles/week.
      - Includes CMS publishing support and a Looker dashboard to track performance (traffic, engagement, conversions).

### GrowthX's Process: Custom Workflows & Fact-Checking

  - **Core Engine:** Custom workflows built in code, not off-the-shelf tools, allowing for deep customization.
  - **Knowledge Graph:** Ingests client resources (decks, transcripts) and agentic research to build a semantic knowledge base, ensuring content accuracy and nuance.
  - **Fact-Checking Workflow:**
      - Breaks final drafts into passages.
      - Validates each passage against a confidence interval (-2 to +2).
      - Suggests rewrites for low-confidence passages, with human experts making the final decision.
  - **Future Scope:** Simon asked if the engine could analyze Gong call transcripts for voice-of-customer reports. Tyler confirmed this is a possible future workstream after the content foundation is built.
