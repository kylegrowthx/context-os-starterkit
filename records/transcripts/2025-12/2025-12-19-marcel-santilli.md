## Key Topics

### Reframing Success: From Process to Outcome

  - Marcel clarified that success is defined by outcomes, not just operational cadence. An efficient process is the "how," but it must deliver the "why."
  - **The "Why":** The business model depends entirely on organic growth. The current burn rate is unsustainable with paid acquisition ($15–$20/click).
  - **Two Core Success Metrics:**
    1.  **Organic Growth:** Validate a sustainable organic growth model.
    2.  **Feature Completeness:** Prove the private workspace is usable end-to-end and delivers value.

### Validating the Private Workspace (Top Priority)

  - The immediate focus is validating the private workspace with real clients to prove end-to-end value.
  - **Brex Demo Feedback:** A demo with Brex revealed critical gaps that are blockers to adoption.
      - **Missing AI Engines:** No support for AI modes/overviews; Claude is irrelevant for search.
      - **Inadequate Visualizations:** Key data visualizations are missing.
      - **Poor Prompt Management:** The admin panel is "choppy" and inefficient.
      - **Limited Category Views:** Users see only one category (e.g., "expense management"), which misrepresents the product's full capabilities.
  - **Validation Process:**
      - Compare metrics against competitors (e.g., Scrunch) for the same clients (Brex, Webflow).
      - Any team confusion with the product signals a guaranteed user experience problem.

### Improving the Public Site for Organic Growth

  - The public site's goal is converting impressions into clicks to drive organic growth.
  - **Current State:** The site gets impressions but zero clicks, indicating a problem with content quality and navigation.
  - **Key Issues:**
      - **Poor Navigation:** Too many clicks to find brands; no intuitive way to see all categories for a brand.
      - **Orphaned Pages:** Lack of cross-linking hinders indexing and user discovery.
  - **Required Fixes:**
      - **CMS:** Implement a CMS to enable rapid testing of page content and structure.
      - **Cross-linking:** Improve site navigation to create a cohesive, indexable experience.

### Stevie's Role & Responsibilities

  - Stevie's role is to connect all team work to the strategic goals of organic growth and feature completeness.
  - **Key Responsibilities:**
      - **Strategic Alignment:** Ensure all work directly contributes to validating the core business model.
      - **Process Ownership:** Own the process for creating and testing new categories, then delegate execution to the team (Kavishka, Carrie).
      - **Communication:** Resume weekly Friday updates to provide team-wide clarity and alignment.
