## Key Topics

### Chanel Project: Driving Non-Branded SEO Growth

  - **Problem:** Chanel's organic traffic was unsustainable, relying almost entirely on branded search (98% of clicks). The client was risk-averse and focused only on branded metrics.
  - **Solution:** Proposed creating thousands of new PLPs to target non-branded, long-tail keywords (e.g., "red lipstick").
  - **Execution:**
      - **Buy-in:** Secured approval after a SERP change caused a performance dip, proving the unsustainability of the status quo.
      - **Process:** Agency handled research → vendor used product feed to auto-generate pages.
  - **Impact:**
      - **Non-Branded Growth:** Significant increases in visibility, traffic, and revenue.
      - **Branded Lift:** Also improved branded CTR, exceeding a key client goal.
      - **Expansion:** Success led to buy-in for the strategy across other site divisions.
  - **Lesson Learned:** A limited product data feed required manual workarounds, reducing efficiency.

### Strategic Approach to Stagnant Accounts

  - David specializes in reviving long-term, stagnant client accounts.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Diagnose:** Identify client pain points and the questions their internal stakeholders are asking.
    2.  **Refine Reporting:** Adjust reporting to tell the story the client needs to hear, establishing a better baseline.
    3.  **Scale Proven Tactics:** Shift from small, isolated optimizations (e.g., 40-50 PLPs/month) to scalable strategies for top-line impact.
    <!-- end list -->
      - **Example (Ace Hardware):** Using AI to increase PLP output by 20-30% and applying the same optimization logic to all underlying PDPs.

### AI Overviews (AEO) Strategy

  - **Client Education:** Reset expectations on traffic from AEO vs. traditional SERPs.
  - **Prioritization:** Maintain focus on Google's 80-90% market share while preparing for AEO's increasing prominence.
  - **"Future-Proofing":** Emphasize foundational SEO that benefits both traditional search and AEO.
      - High-quality, citable content
      - Optimized product feeds
      - Strong local presence
  - **Performance Measurement:**
      - Track prompt visibility and referring traffic.
      - Introduce new KPIs like "brand presence" in AEO responses.
      - Differentiate strategy based on prompt type (e.g., on-page copy for product queries vs. PR for review-based prompts).

### GrowthX Vision & Service Gaps

  - **Vision:** To be a "knowledge base for your web experience," unifying opportunity discovery, action, and data tracking in one platform.
  - **Business Model:** A hybrid agency/product company.
      - **Agency:** A "white glove" service model that funds product development.
      - **Product:** Aims to create a lower-touch, self-serve model for knowledge workers.
  - **Service Gaps:**
      - **Off-Page SEO:** Not a core offering due to negative experiences with shady vendors. This is a potential gap, especially as PR and brand mentions influence AEO.
      - **Video:** Not a core offering, though a talented video person is on staff for internal projects. YouTube content is a known driver for AEO, representing another potential gap.
      - **Custom Work:** GrowthX is selective with custom projects, prioritizing repeatable, scalable offerings to avoid past issues with scope creep.
