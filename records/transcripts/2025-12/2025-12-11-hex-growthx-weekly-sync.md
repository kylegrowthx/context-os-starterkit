## Key Topics

### Content Review & Pipeline

  - **Problem:** PMM James requested earlier input on content topics to guide the narrative before drafts are generated.
  - **Solution:** A two-part review process was established:
      - **Topic Feedback:** PMMs will comment in Airtable on approved topics (22 currently) to guide storytelling *before* drafting.
      - **Strategic Feedback:** For high-level messaging, PMMs can update the "product services artifact" via Loom or a meeting.
  - **Pipeline Status:**
      - **Live:** 4 articles
      - **Approved for Staging:** 5 articles (all feedback implemented except for one final review by Jessica)
      - **In Progress:** 5 articles, due EOD Friday
  - **Staging:** Approved for Monday, as Hex's internal blog post launch was moved.

### AI Workflow & Artifacts

  - **Tone & Accuracy Challenge:** Initial attempts to make the AI tone more "human" sacrificed technical accuracy by oversimplifying concepts.
  - **Solution:** The workflow was split into two artifacts:
    1.  **Writing Guidelines:** Focuses solely on technical content accuracy.
    2.  **Writing Style Guide:** Applied *after* drafting to refine tone without altering facts.
    <!-- end list -->
      - **Result:** This separation successfully generates human-sounding drafts without losing critical context.
  - **"Examples of Good" Artifact:**
      - **Function:** Guides the AI's structure and voice.
      - **Constraint:** Limited to \~1 article due to token length.
      - **Action:** Jessica will identify specific elements from the "Embedded Analytics" article to combine with the current example.
  - **Automated Case Study Insertion:**
      - **Function:** A "customer spotlights" artifact automatically inserts condensed case studies (e.g., Figma) into relevant sections.
      - **Decision:** Keep all case studies, as the benefit of proving a use case outweighs the risk of using lesser-known companies.

### Performance Tracking & Attribution

  - **Challenge:** Hex lacks page-level attribution for signups and gated downloads.
  - **Root Cause:** GA4 data is inflated by unfilterable bot traffic, making it unreliable.
  - **Solution Paths:**
      - **UTMs:** Add UTMs to CTAs (e.g., "Request Demo") to track blog-sourced leads.
      - **Inline CTAs:** Build reusable Contentful blocks for prominent CTAs, which are proven to drive higher engagement than text links.
  - **Reporting Walkthrough:** Aida will record a Loom video explaining the Scrunch and Looker dashboards.
  - **Duplicate Content:**
      - **Problem:** GrowthX is drafting new articles for topics where Hex has existing, non-ranking content.
      - **Action:** Jessica will identify these URLs in Notion. GrowthX will then either rewrite the existing post or publish a new one with a redirect.
