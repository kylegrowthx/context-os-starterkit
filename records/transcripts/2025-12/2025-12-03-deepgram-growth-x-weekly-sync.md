## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Reporting Gaps

  - GrowthX content traffic is recovering after a post-Thanksgiving dip, with strong engagement (\~50% rate).
  - **Key Gap:** The Looker dashboard lacks per-page conversion data.
      - **Why it matters:** This data is essential to prove content ROI and justify the investment.
      - **Action:** Erik will add a view to filter conversions by GrowthX-produced pages.
  - **LLM Referral Drop:** A recent drop in ChatGPT traffic is a wider market trend (correlated with college breaks), not a Deepgram-specific issue.
      - **Deepgram's Cause:** A single free transcription page, which generates low-quality leads, lost its high ranking.
      - **Significance:** This drop requires a proactive strategy to maintain executive confidence in LLM visibility.

### Strategy Pivot: Competitor Comparisons

  - The team will pivot from broad topics to bottom-funnel competitor content.
      - **Rationale:** Directly addresses sales team needs for competitive answers and captures high-intent, decision-making traffic.
  - **New Content Focus:**
      - Detailed comparison charts and infographics.
      - Vendor-specific breakouts (e.g., Deepgram vs. Google Text-to-Speech).
  - **Future Topics:**
      - Voice agent building and conversation analysis.
  - **Technical Plan:** Deepgram's upcoming CMS migration will support this pivot.
      - **Goal:** Use SEO-friendly URLs (e.g., `/compare/assemblyai`) to improve discoverability and authority.
      - **Support:** A new website planner will ensure proper implementation.

### LLM Visibility Tracking (Scrunch)

  - GrowthX uses the Scrunch platform to track Deepgram's presence across 7 LLMs.
  - **Current Status:**
      - **Overall Presence:** 28% (outperforms competitors).
      - **Citations:** 92% from third-party sources (e.g., Wikipedia, Reddit), indicating high authority.
  - **How it works:**
      - Tracks \~75 "seed prompts" (e.g., "AI tools for sentiment analysis").
      - Monitors competitor citations to identify content gaps (e.g., AssemblyAI's URL for "best API models for real-time speech recognition").
      - **Action:** Erik will share the list of 75 seed prompts with Troy.

### Speech-to-Text Language Pages

  - **Goal:** Create dedicated pages for each supported language.
  - **Technical Feasibility:** GrowthX's engineering team confirmed the project is a low-lift, feasible via manual template copying.
  - **Content Questions for Deepgram:**
      - Should page copy be consistent across all languages?
      - Are the four features in the template the only ones to include?
      - Who is the best contact for FAQ content?
      - **Action:** Erik will post these questions in Slack, tagging Hassan and Patricia.
