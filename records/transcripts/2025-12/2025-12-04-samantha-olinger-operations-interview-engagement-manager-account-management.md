## Key Topics

### Client Renewal Case Study

  - **Problem:** A landscaping client, despite recent project success (site migration, 4 acquisitions), planned to churn for an agency offering a broader scope.
  - **Root Cause:** The client's leadership wanted AI capabilities for brand mentions and citations, a need not communicated by the day-to-day contact.
  - **Solution:** Offered the client a free 3-month beta test of a new, unlaunched LLM visibility tool.
      - **Goal:** Partner with the client to build the tool's initial workflow and playbook.
      - **Focus:** Used the tool to research winter-specific terms (e.g., "snow removal"), a known client pain point.
  - **Outcome:** The client renewed, keeping Samantha's team for organic work and hiring the agency for paid media, creating a collaborative partnership.

### Account Management Philosophy

  - **Proactive Discovery:** Meticulously tracks client usage patterns to differentiate normal fluctuations from true red flags.
  - **Strategic Questioning:** Asks "why" behind client topics to uncover underlying struggles before they escalate.
  - **Leadership Development:** Building a track record by creating and sharing resources for niche client problems, positioning Samantha as an expert.

### Company Context & Challenges

  - **Business Model Transition:** Shifting from a services-only model to a product-first one.
      - **Product:** A new platform (LLM/SEO visibility, content creation) launches early 2025.
      - **Future Delivery:** Will offer both self-serve platform access and managed services.
  - **Current Challenges:**
      - **Perceived Value:** The services-only model is often valued less than a product.
      - **Expectation Setting:** Learning to identify and avoid clients with unrealistic expectations (e.g., overnight results, complex review processes).
