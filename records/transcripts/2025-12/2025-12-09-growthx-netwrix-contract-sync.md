## Key Topics

### AI & Data Security

  - **Netwrix Concern:** Data security, specifically preventing sensitive info (ICPs, personas) from training public LLMs or being commingled with other clients.
  - **GrowthX Solution:**
      - Uses private, enterprise-licensed LLMs (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini).
      - Data is isolated: Netwrix documents are for team reference only and are never fed into the LLMs.
      - Workflows use only GrowthX-created artifacts, ensuring no commingling.
      - Account teams are partitioned to prevent competitive overlap.
  - **Contract Language:** The contract will be updated to reflect this, specifying enterprise-licensed tools and excluding public models.

### IP Ownership (Sec 2.1)

  - **Resolution:** Agreed to revert Tyler's redline and accept Sarah's latest version.
  - **Rationale:** This maintains a clear distinction: Netwrix owns its pre-existing IP and data, while GrowthX owns its underlying platform. Netwrix owns all final "work product deliverables."

### Confidentiality Cap (Sec 6.2)

  - **Resolution:** Netwrix confirmed acceptance of the 2x cap on breach of confidentiality.

### Phase 2 Billing

  - **Resolution:** Agreed to quarterly billing for Phase 2.
  - **Rationale:** This aligns with the original agreement and reduces administrative overhead for both parties.
  - **Language:** "Billed on the effective date and then subsequently on a quarterly basis."

### Logistics & Staffing

  - **Netwrix Request:** James asked about the account manager's time zone to help staff Netwrix's Europe-based team.
  - **GrowthX Response:** Tyler will request a manager who can accommodate earlier meetings, prioritizing quality over time zone.
