## Overview

- **Strategy Revision:** Broaden topic clusters to 30–40 articles each (for 3–4 months of content) and target competitor keywords to steal traffic, not just avoid them.
- **Content Focus:** Prioritize educational, foundational content over deep technical guides, which are harder for the team to execute.
- **Airtable Workflow:** Consolidate all keywords into one tab for efficiency. Use linked records to auto-populate data (e.g., search volume) in the `Assignments` tab.
- **Client Presentation:** William will lead the strategy review, using the Content OS to show the data foundation (keywords, URLs) that informs the recommendations.

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Feedback

  - Aida provided feedback on the strategy doc to ensure a sustainable content pipeline.
  - **Topic Clusters:**
      - **Goal:** Broaden clusters to support 3–4 months of content.
      - **Rationale:** Each cluster needs to contain 30–40 articles to maintain a consistent publishing cadence.
  - **Keyword Targeting:**
      - **Action:** Target competitor keywords to steal traffic.
      - **Rationale:** The current strategy of only targeting topics where competitors are absent is too limiting.
  - **Execution Feasibility:**
      - **Focus:** Prioritize educational, foundational content.
      - **Avoid:** Deep technical guides and dev tutorials, which are difficult for the team to produce.
      - **Action:** Consult Sydney (Base 10, Rumpod) for a technical perspective.

### Airtable Content OS Workflow

  - Aida demonstrated the Content OS setup, which streamlines content planning.
  - **Unified Keyword Tab:**
      - **Change:** Consolidate all keywords (gap analysis, manual research) into a single `Keywords` tab.
      - **Rationale:** The previous two-tab system was inefficient and created linking issues.
  - **Linked Records:**
      - **Function:** Link records between tabs (e.g., `Assignments` to `Keywords`) to auto-populate data.
      - **Benefit:** Automatically pulls keyword data (volume, difficulty, intent) into the `Assignments` tab, ensuring accuracy and saving manual entry.
  - **Client View:**
      - **Setup:** Group the `Assignments` tab by `Topic Cluster` for client approval.
      - **Rationale:** This view clearly organizes topics by strategic theme.

### Client Presentation Plan

  - **Presentation Flow:**

          - **`Keywords` Tab:** Demonstrate the depth of research (e.g., 80k records).

          - **`Assignments` Tab:** Preview the initial content calendar.
    3.  **Rationale:** Explain that the fast-paced process is designed to publish quickly and get performance signals faster.
