### Content Strategy: CTAs & Imagery

  - **Call-to-Action (CTA) Strategy:**
      - **Problem:** Current CTAs ("Get a Demo," "Contact Us") are all bottom-funnel, missing opportunities to capture leads earlier.
      - **Solution:** Add a middle-funnel, ungated CTA.
          - **Example:** A free MDM handbook.
          - **Rationale:** Provides value and captures interest without requiring a form submission.
      - **Future:** Link CTAs directly to article topics.
  - **Imagery Strategy:**
      - **Problem:** Mike T. flagged current article images as low quality.
      - **Solution:** Remove existing images. Mike T. will create a set of 10–12 generic, high-quality images to be cycled through.
      - **Rationale:** The primary purpose of images is for social sharing; the low probability of a single user seeing the same image repeatedly makes this approach viable.

  - **Action:** All competitor comparison topics are paused.
  - **Rationale:** To ensure content aligns with existing battle cards and avoid creating conflicting information.
  - **Next Step:** Erik will connect with Ashish, who is already working on comparison data, to align on the strategy.

### Website Performance & SEO

  - **Client-Side Rendering (CSR):**
      - **Finding:** The CSR issue flagged in a recent audit is not a problem.
