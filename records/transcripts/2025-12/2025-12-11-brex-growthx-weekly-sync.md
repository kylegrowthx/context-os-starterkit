## Key Topics

### Content Strategy: Leads Over Traffic

  - **Goal:** Support Jon Kowieski (Organic Growth Lead) by expanding the content library, freeing him for off-page SEO and experiments.
  - **Evaluation Metric:** Leads, not traffic. This drives a focus on commercial, high-intent keywords over top-of-funnel content.
  - **Execution Plan:**
      - **High-Priority Clusters:**
          - Business Banking & Treasury → **Focus on DDA (operating accounts)**, a strategic shift from the historical yield-forward treasury focus.
          - Credit Cards & Credit Management
          - Expense Management & Receipt Tracking (to build authority)
      - **Experimental Clusters:**
          - Startup Finance & Funding
          - Industry-Specific Finance → Initial verticals: construction, medical/dental, multi-location retail.
              - **Note:** Brex will share its 8 target verticals for FY27 to ensure alignment.
      - **De-prioritized:** Vendor management, corporate travel, and procurement due to existing partnerships.

### Content OS & Ideation

  - **Platform:** Airtable, serving as the Content OS.
  - **Current Status:** \~40 ideas generated from a competitor keyword gap analysis.
  - **Goal:** Reach 80–90 ideas for a comprehensive calendar.
  - **Feedback:** Jon requested secondary keywords be added to each idea record.

### CMS & Dev Support

  - **Problem:** Brex's Sanity CMS is "clunky" and "no longer best practice," creating a bottleneck for the organic growth team.
  - **Opportunity:** GrowthX can provide dev support, starting with custom blocks and CTAs to increase lead conversion on high-value pages (e.g., "best credit card" pages).
  - **Collaboration:** GrowthX will scope a plan to help modernize the CMS, potentially partnering with Brex's external agency, Loof.

### Segment Marketing Sync Debrief

  - **Key Learnings:**
      - **Information Organization:** The segment marketing team has extensive documentation but struggles with organization; GrowthX will synthesize it.
      - **Execution Gap:** The team has strong content ideas (e.g., how-to guides) but lacks the resources for execution.
      - **Value Prop Clarity:** The team directed GrowthX to existing documentation for value prop details, indicating a potential internal communication challenge.
