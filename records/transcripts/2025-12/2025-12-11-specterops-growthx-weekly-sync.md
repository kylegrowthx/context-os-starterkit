The deal is currently in decision-making phase—content has been drafted and reviewed internally by SpecterOps' product marketing team, but now hinges on whether the PMM team has sufficient bandwidth to continue reviews. ### Content Quality & Proposed Fix

  - **Positive:** Content is consistently technically accurate.
  - **Area for Improvement:** The tone can be inconsistent.
  - **Proposed Fix:** Feed GrowthX's AI more SpecterOps-owned content (e.g., white papers, style guides) to better align the output with the brand voice.

### GrowthX's Process & Solution

  - GrowthX's AI pipeline uses "artifacts" to guide content generation.
  - **Example:** A SpecterOps white paper was summarized into a custom artifact, which the AI now references to ensure accuracy on topics like "identities at rest."
  - **Benefit:** This process reduces review time by embedding client-specific knowledge directly into the draft, requiring no developer support.
  - **Suggestion:** Highlight product-specific sections in drafts to further streamline PMM reviews.
