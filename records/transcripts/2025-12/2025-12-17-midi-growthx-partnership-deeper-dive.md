## Context

Midi Health is a telehealth company focused on menopause care for middle-aged women, operating both a direct-to-consumer (D2C) platform and a B2B arm that works with healthcare providers and insurance plans. The company has recently identified an urgent market opportunity as competitors like HERS and Weight Watchers have launched menopause-focused products, creating competitive pressure to expand content and capture search traffic. GrowthX has been engaged to help Midi build a scaled content marketing strategy that would increase editorial output from their current 5-8 articles per month to 50 per month by Q2 2026, while maintaining human-like quality and avoiding Google penalties related to AI-generated content.

## Key Topics

### Midi's Content Goals & Constraints

  - **Primary Focus:** Dominate content for middle-aged women's health, with a specific emphasis on peri- and post-menopause.
  - **Scaling Target:** Increase editorial output from 5–8 articles/mo to 50/mo by EOY Q2 2026.
  - **Content Quality:** Must be human-generated in tone to avoid Google penalties, despite using AI for acceleration.
  - **Review Process:** A critical bottleneck. The goal is to streamline reviews to a 2–3 day turnaround to enable high-volume publishing.
  - **Programmatic Content:** A separate workstream (e.g., location-based SEO) not counted toward the 50-article/mo editorial target.

### GrowthX's Competitive Analysis & Opportunity

  - **Competitors Analyzed:** HERS and Weight Watchers.
      - **Rationale:** Both have recently launched menopause-focused products, creating an immediate market opportunity for Midi.
  - **Analysis Method:** Identified keyword gaps between Midi and its competitors, filtering out branded terms and irrelevant topics (e.g., "diet").
  - **Projected Traffic Lift (Year 1):**
      - **Refresh:** \~25k additional visits/mo from optimizing existing articles.
      - **Net New:** \~210k additional visits/mo from targeting new keywords.
      - **Total Projected Lift:** \~235k additional organic visits/mo.
  - **Assumptions:**
      - **Refresh:** Assumes a conservative 1% traffic capture rate on new keywords, compared to Midi's current 8% rate on its existing keywords.
      - **Net New:** Focused on keywords where Midi ranks 2–40, targeting realistic gains.

### GrowthX's Proposal & Pricing

  - **Contract Structure:**
      - **Phase 1 (Strategy):** Paid phase for setup (artifacts, calibration, 5–10 articles), with an opt-out option before Phase 2.
      - **Phase 2 (Editorial):** 12-month term at 5 articles/week.
  - **Pricing Flexibility:**
      - **Volume:** Weekly output can increase to 10 articles at no extra cost if Midi's review process is efficient enough to support it.
      - **Payment:** GrowthX is flexible on payment terms (e.g., monthly vs. annual upfront).
      - **Scope:** Additional workstreams (e.g., tools, microsites) would be added to the scope and cost.
