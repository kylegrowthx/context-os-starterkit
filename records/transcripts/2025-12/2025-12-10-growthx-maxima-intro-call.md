## Key Topics

### Maxima's GTM & Content Strategy

  - **Company:** Maxima, a Series A AI-native alternative for financial close & consolidation.
  - **GTM Motion:** Top-down, sales-led enterprise/mid-market sales to a conservative finance audience (Controllers, VPs of Accounting).
  - **Content Goal:** Differentiate from legacy vendors (e.g., Blackline) with high-quality, proprietary thought leadership.
      - **Rationale:** Avoid generic content easily copied by competitors.
      - **Model:** Emulate Zip HQ's focus on deep guides and whitepapers.
  - **Initial Plan:** Use an SEO consultant for research, then hire SMEs for high-value content and an intern for blog/newsletter execution.

### GrowthX's AI-Driven Content Model

  - **Core Service:** End-to-end SEO/GEO content creation to build domain authority and rank in search/LLMs.
  - **Process:** An agentic AI workflow with human oversight at each stage.
    1.  **Context Artifacts:** Create detailed profiles (personas, brand voice, product) to guide the AI.
    2.  **Brief Generation:** AI analyzes SERPs and competitor content to create a brief.
          - **Human Role:** Editors can modify the brief before drafting.
    3.  **Drafting & Refinement:** AI drafts content, which is then evaluated for groundedness, brand alignment, and audience focus.
          - **Human Role:** Editors review, accept, or modify AI suggestions.
    4.  **Fact-Checking:** AI validates every passage against deep research.
          - **Human Role:** Editors can accept or rewrite AI suggestions.
  - **Team Structure:** A dedicated 3-person team for each client: Managing Director (strategy), Managing Editor (writing), and Content Manager (execution).

### Pricing & Engagement Model

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint**
      - **Duration:** 8 weeks
      - **Cost:** $30,000 (one-time fee)
      - **Deliverables:** A content roadmap and 5–10 published articles.
      - **Terms:** Client can opt out before Phase 2.
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
      - **Duration:** 12 months
      - **Cost:** $15,000/month
      - **Deliverables:** 5 articles per week.
