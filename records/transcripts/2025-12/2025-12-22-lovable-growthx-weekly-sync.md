## Summary

Lovable and GrowthX approved scaling content production from 10 to 15 articles per week for January and February 2025, with performance re-evaluation at the end of February to decide whether to continue. The team addressed critical operational blockers including lost GA4 access that broke Looker dashboards, a meta tag bug affecting SEO on template pages, and publishing workflow constraints that require pull requests for every change. The strategy is shifting from keyword-chasing to building authority around specific content clusters (e.g., "safe vibe coding for enterprises"), and syncs will move to bi-weekly starting in February.

  - **Decision:** Approved the proposal to scale content production to 15 articles/week.
  - **Rationale:** To capitalize on a high-opportunity market window.
  - **Timeline:**
      - **Jan–Feb 2025:** 15 articles/week.

  - **Budget:** Sam has internal approval and will finalize with finance.

### Operational Blockers & Solutions

  - **GA4 Access:**
      - **Problem:** Access was lost during a weekend incident, breaking Looker dashboards and blocking performance analysis.
      - **Solution:** Megan will provide the required access level and email address; Sam will restore access ASAP.
  - **Meta Tag Bug:**
      - **Problem:** Template pages have a generic meta title ("Lovable") instead of a descriptive one, which negatively impacts SEO.
      - **Context:** This is a known, site-wide issue Lovable is investigating.
      - **Solution:** Megan will flag the issue with the GrowthX engineering team.
  - **Publishing Workflow:**
      - **Problem:** The current workflow requires a pull request (PR) for every change, creating a bottleneck.
      - **Context:** Lovable is actively exploring alternatives to the current CMS.
      - **Solutions Under Investigation:**
          - A Cloud Code integration with Slack for easier publishing.
          - Building a custom CMS within Lovable.

### Performance & Content Refresh

  - **Performance (Dec 1–16):**
      - Impressions and clicks for "guides" increased significantly from Week 49 to Week 50.
      - The top-performing page is "best AI app builders in 2025."
  - **Content Refresh:**
      - **Action:** Update the top-performing page to "best AI app builders in 2026."
      - **Rationale:** LLMs and Google favor recent content.
      - **Process:** GrowthX will manage the update via a PR.

### SEO Strategy Shift

  - **New Strategy:** Shift from targeting individual keywords to building authority around specific content clusters.
  - **Rationale:** To establish Lovable as a thought leader in key areas (e.g., "safe vibe coding for enterprises").
  - **Ownership:** Johanna will define the initial list of clusters.
  - **Execution:** GrowthX will map existing keywords to the new clusters and conduct new research to fill gaps.
