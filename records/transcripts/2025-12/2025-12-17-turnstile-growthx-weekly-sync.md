### Content Strategy: 6 Topic Clusters

  - **Goal:** Reach 500+ customers by EOY 2026 by becoming a core part of the startup toolkit.
  - **Competitive Moat:** Competitors (Chargebee, Maxio) target established companies ($10M+ ARR) with sales teams. Turnstile's strategy focuses on founders and early ops teams.
  - **1. QuotaCache Fundamentals (High Priority)**
      - **Goal:** Rank #1 for foundational founder questions (e.g., "tools for my first contract").
      - **Rationale:** Low competition, high winnability. Jordan noted the audience is new to these concepts, so content must be educational.
  - **2. Revenue Operations (High Priority)**
      - **Goal:** Capture founders hitting a billing crisis (e.g., 10-15 hrs/week on billing, data mismatches).
      - **Feedback:** Seema flagged "RevOps" is ambiguous. The focus is on billing/provisioning, not the broader sales/marketing ops function. Jordan suggested "Sales Ops" as a more precise term.
  - **3. Solutions Comparisons (High Priority)**
      - **Goal:** Capture high-intent users comparing tools.
      - **Rationale:** Aida noted these articles drive fast results (e.g., a similar article for another client was cited by ChatGPT in 2-3 weeks).
      - **Feedback:** Jordan questioned comparing Stripe vs. Chargebee. William clarified the goal is to join the conversation and build authority, not just promote Turnstile.
  - **4. Sales-Led Billing Complexity (High Priority)**
      - **Goal:** Target companies adding enterprise sales, who realize manual Stripe work is unsustainable.
      - **Rationale:** Highly topical and directly addresses the moment Stripe users become Turnstile prospects.
  - **5. Subscription Management**
      - **Goal:** Target founders managing subscriptions with spreadsheets.
      - **Feedback:** Jordan clarified "Dunning" is for unpaid bills, not just failed payments.
  - **6. Financial Foundations for SaaS**
      - **Goal:** Educate technical founders on SaaS metrics (MRR, AR) for investor conversations.
      - **Rationale:** Builds trust and provides internal linking opportunities to strengthen SEO.
