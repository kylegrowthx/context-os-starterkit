## Key Topics

### HyperExponential Corporate Marketing Context

- Deyan leads the Corporate Marketing team, which is shifting focus from demand gen to brand building.
- The team is in a transitional phase, building out five key pillars:
    - **Brand Marketing:** Defining brand strategy, standards, and creative production.
    - **Comms & PR:** External media relations, with US investment planned for 2026.
    - **Content & Website:** Top-leadership content, website operations, and social media.
    - **Campaigns:** Running the first integrated brand-to-demand campaign in 2026.
    - **Events:** Corporate and Tier 1 events.
- **Future State:** A separate "Growth" team will eventually own demand-driven activities like ABM and campaigns.

### GrowthX's AI Content Engine

- GrowthX is a strategic growth partner using an AI engine to produce SEO/AEO-optimized content.
- **Process:**
    1. **Strategy Sprint:** Defines company context, personas, and SEO/AEO strategy.
    2. **Artifacts:** Creates foundational documents (writing guidelines, competitor briefs) in Notion.
    3. **AI Pipeline:** The engine uses these artifacts to generate content, combining tools like Claude (drafting) and Exa (research).
    4. **Human Oversight:** An editor (Rachel) reviews inputs and outputs at key steps.
- **Key Insight:** Updating the artifacts directly improves output quality and reduces the need for manual review.

### Current Content Blockers

- **Outdated Messaging:** The content backlog uses old terminology (e.g., "HX Renew"), requiring a full review and refresh.
- **Undefined Brand Strategy:** The lack of clear guidelines for tone of voice and visual style prevents content from being published.
- **Website Operations:** No clear owner for publishing content on the new Framer site.
- **Image Strategy:** No decision on using stock vs. graphic images, which blocks the AI image generation pipeline.

### Future Collaboration: "Underwriting Workbench" Campaign

- HyperExponential is launching a major campaign around the "underwriting workbench" category.
- **Goal:** Use GrowthX's engine to support a multi-format content strategy built from a central piece of proprietary research.
- **Potential Formats:**
    - Interactive landing pages
    - Infographics
    - Guides
    - Blog series
- GrowthX can also build pipelines to repurpose other HyperExponential content, such as podcast transcripts, to enrich blog posts and improve search performance.
