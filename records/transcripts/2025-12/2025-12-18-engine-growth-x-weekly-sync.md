## Key Topics

### Content & Performance Review

  - **Overall Performance:** Sessions are up 55% and engagement is up 51% since mid-October, rebounding from a post-Thanksgiving dip.
  - **Bet Performance:**
      - **Expense Management:** "Policy Enforcement" is the top performer. "Receipt Chaos" shows strong growth, though from a small base.
      - **Industry-Specific:** Energy/Utilities and Construction/Infrastructure content has more than doubled in sessions. New "Technology & Software" content has 224 sessions.
  - **Engagement Insights:** Bet content has fewer sessions but higher events-per-session than general guides, signaling stronger intent.
  - **LLM Referrals:** Traffic is leveling off, possibly due to seasonality or increased competition among AI tools.
  - **Concur Citations:** Citations are down, likely due to seasonality. This will be monitored in the new year to ensure rankings rebound.

### CMS & Workflow Improvements

  - **Amplitude Tracking:** The CMS dropdown for content clusters now serves as the source for Amplitude tracking, replacing the Airtable list.
      - **Action:** Notify Joel of any new clusters to add to the CMS.
  - **"Travel Trends & Tech" CMS Field:** This field is required but links to old, low-performing articles, causing broken UI.
      - **Solution:** Joel will create a ticket for an empty state, allowing the field to be left blank without breaking the page.
  - **Webflow API Integration:** An API key was granted to automate content staging.
      - **Permissions:** Read/write for CMS and Sites (for staging), and read-only for Assets (for screenshots).
      - **Benefit:** Reduces manual CMS work and prevents login conflicts.

### Strategic Alignment & Planning

  - **Contract Renewal:** The 6-month contract renewal discussions will include Joel and Luke.
  - **Concur Integration:** A new integration launching \~Feb 2025 will allow enterprise Concur users to access Engine's inventory and reporting from within Concur.
      - **Strategy Pivot:** Content will now position Concur as a platform that Engine *enhances*, not just an alternative.
  - **Barry's Content:** Barry's content is complementary and not a priority over GrowthX's strategy. GrowthX will flag any conflicts.
  - **GrowthX Pilot Program:** Carrie will push to include Engine in a pilot for a new internal analytics tool to replace Scrunch.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will consult James (Telescope) for feedback on his negative experience with the competitor tool, Peek.
