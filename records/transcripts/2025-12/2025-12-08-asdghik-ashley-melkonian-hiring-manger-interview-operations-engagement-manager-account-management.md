## Key Topics

### The Role: Engagement Manager at Growthx

  - **Company Context:** Growthx is a 1.5-year-old agency using an internal AI platform for organic blog content. It is currently undergoing a reorg and defining new roles.
  - **Delivery Model:** A traditional pod structure.
      - **Engagement Manager (EM):** Manages 8 accounts (scaling to 12).
          - **Focus:** Client strategy, relationships, and renewals.
          - **KPIs:** Relationship strength → renewals and expansions.
      - **Production Layer:** Content Managers/Managing Editors own end-to-end content production for 2–3 accounts.
      - **Specialist Layer:** Directors/specialists provide support for technical SEO or major strategy shifts.
  - **Client Onboarding:** All clients start with an 8-week "strategy sprint" where EMs define the initial engagement.
  - **Future Vision:** The internal AI platform will eventually become an external product offering.

### The Candidate: Ashley Melkonian's Background

  - **Experience:** 10 years in marketing, primarily as an SEO Manager.
  - **Agency Experience:**
      - Built SEO teams from scratch.
      - Scaled an agency team from 20 to 100+ clients.
      - Hired, onboarded, and trained SEOs.
  - **In-House Experience:**
      - First SEO Manager at Teachable.
      - Built the entire SEO structure and KPIs from scratch.
  - **Consulting Experience:**
      - Works with travel brands (e.g., Norwegian Cruise Line, American Airlines) on content and technical SEO.
  - **Client-Facing Skills:**
      - Managed VIP clients; ran onboarding and ongoing account management.
      - Presents complex SEO strategies to diverse audiences (e.g., 20-person client teams vs. small startups).

### Candidate's Approach to Key Challenges

  - **Managing Stakeholders (Teachable Example):**
      - **Challenge:** Introduced SEO to a company unfamiliar with its value, requiring buy-in from multiple teams (engineering, content, dev, brand).
      - **Solution:** Conducted a deep audit → created a 6-month plan with projected ROI → secured executive buy-in and budget.
      - **Key Insight:** Success hinged on connecting SEO efforts directly to business outcomes (leads, ROI), not just vanity metrics.
  - **Organization & Prioritization:**
      - **Method:** Works backward from deadlines using PM tools (Monday.com, ClickUp, Jira).
      - **Prioritization:** Uses a matrix to focus on high-impact, low-lift tasks first.
      - **Tool Philosophy:** The workflow built *within* a tool is more critical than the tool itself.
  - **Handling Client Pushback:**
      - **Example:** A client's dev team resisted a high-lift SEO recommendation.
      - **Solution:** Offered alternative solutions while clearly setting expectations on the trade-off in results.
      - **Outcome:** The client ultimately decides, sometimes accepting a less impactful option for easier implementation.
