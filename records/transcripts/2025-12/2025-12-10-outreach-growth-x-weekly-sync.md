## Key Topics

### Q1 Strategic Pivot: AE-Focused Narrative

  - **New Narrative:** Shift from an SDR tool to an "AI revenue workflow platform" for CROs.
  - **Core Message:** Position Outreach as AI that "asks and then acts for you," not a simple chatbot.
  - **Content Focus:** Align all content with this narrative, using themes like:
      - AE workflows & productivity
      - Forecasting & deal execution
      - Pipeline inspection & conversational intelligence
  - **Supporting Materials:** GrowthX requested internal docs (e.g., product matrices) to ensure messaging accuracy.

### Content Operations & Workflow

  - **Listicle Production:** A new process will accelerate production.
      - GrowthX will deliver a single doc containing 5 listicle outlines.
      - Outreach will review it with PMMs and Maggie before splitting it into 5 separate articles.
      - **Competitor Links:** The team will use no-follow links to G2 or competitor sites to cite information without legal risk.
  - **AI Image Workflow:** The image generation process is a "0-to-1" function.
      - **Limitation:** Minor edits (e.g., moving elements, changing text) are not possible.
      - **Solution:** For any issues, Outreach will request a full image regeneration.
      - **Listicle Images:** GrowthX can use vector logos for a more on-brand look.

### Website Structure: AI Agents

  - **Problem:** The current AI agent hub-and-spoke model is effective but raises UX/SEO concerns as the agent roadmap grows to \~30.
  - **Solution:** The current structure is the correct SEO approach.
      - **Hub Page:** Ranks for broad terms (e.g., "AI agents").
      - **Individual Pages:** Rank for specific agent terms (e.g., "Research Agent").
      - **Navigation:** Avoid adding all agents to the main nav.
          - **Recommendation:** Use a category dropdown or footer links for key agents.
      - **Internal Linking:** SEO content will continue to link to agent "money pages" to drive traffic and authority.

### Q1 Content & SEO Initiatives

  - **LLM Visibility Experiment:** GrowthX is developing a controlled experiment to improve content visibility in AI Overviews and LLMs.
      - **Goal:** Test one variable at a time to measure its impact.
      - **Timeline:** More details will be shared in early January.
  - **Upcoming Priorities:**
      - **February Flagship Content:** A major piece (e.g., e-book) using proprietary Outreach Insights data.
      - **February 16 Product Launch:** Content will support a new agent or feature release.
      - **Year-End Review:** A "Spotify Wrapped" style piece using BI metrics.
  - **CRM Content:** This is a lower priority than the new AE-focused narrative.
