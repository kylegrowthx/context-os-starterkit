## Context

This was a deal-closing meeting between GrowthX (represented by Marcel Santilli) and Fleet (John Jeremiah, Ashish Kuthiala, and Erik O'Brien), following earlier scoping conversations about Fleet's content marketing needs. Fleet, which reached $13M ARR in 2025, has strong organic traffic but lacks strategic positioning for enterprise buyers—the company has been engineering-focused but needs balanced content that speaks to both developer and C-suite audiences. The relationship moved from exploration to active engagement, with the goal of establishing a content production partnership that bridges Fleet's product expertise with GrowthX's SEO and content distribution capabilities. This call solidified pricing, scope, and next-step timeline for initial content delivery and stakeholder alignment within Fleet.
