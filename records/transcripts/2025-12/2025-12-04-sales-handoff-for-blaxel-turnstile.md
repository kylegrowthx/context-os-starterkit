## Key Topics

### Blaxel Handoff

  - **Client:** Blaxel, a seed-stage AI infrastructure company.
  - **Product:** A platform for running AI agents with instant-booting VMs, secure sandboxes, and usage-based billing.
  - **Goal:** Build awareness and trust in a new market to preempt future competition.
  - **Contacts:**
      - **Nico:** Main POC (engineer/product manager).
      - **Paul:** CEO (engineer).
  - **ICP Score:** Low (below 13), but approved as an exception due to a VIP referral and the CEO's commitment to a 12-month content investment.
  - **Content Strategy:**
      - **Focus:** Top- and mid-funnel awareness content (e.g., "What is an AI agent?").
      - **Exclusions:** Technical content (e.g., engineering blog posts) will be handled by their new technical writer.
  - **Risks:**
      - **Review Process:** Potential for delays from two co-founders reviewing in parallel.
      - **POC Experience:** Nico has no content marketing background, which may complicate the calibration phase.

### Turnstile Handoff

  - **Client:** Turnstile, a pre-launch quote-to-cash platform for SMBs.
  - **Product:** A unified platform for proposals, billing, collections, and reporting.
  - **Goal:** Launch with high content velocity (daily publishing) to support paid campaigns.
  - **Contacts:**
      - **Seema Kumar:** Fractional CMO (ex-New Relic).
      - **Matt:** Marketing support, will manage paid campaigns.
      - **Lillian:** Co-founder with a marketing background.
  - **Risks:**
      - **Knowledge Gap:** Minimal client materials provided → team lacks a clear understanding of the product.
      - **CMS Access:** Blog platform is not yet built; Matt promised it for next week.
      - **POC Longevity:** Seema is a fractional CMO; the relationship must be built with the founders to ensure continuity.

### Process Improvements

  - **Problem:** Client review processes and time commitments are often unclear before kickoff, leading to delays.
  - **Solution:** Secure a clear review process and time commitment during the sales cycle.
      - **Rationale:** Founders often underestimate the time required for effective collaboration (est. 5–10 hrs/wk).
      - **Tactic:** Set expectations for a time-intensive calibration phase (first 8 weeks) to build trust.
  - **New Workflow:** A comprehensive research workflow is being developed to proactively fill knowledge gaps.
      - **Output:** A detailed report on competitors, social perception, and ICP pain points.
      - **Benefit:** Enables the team to engage clients with superior knowledge, even when client materials are limited.
