## Key Topics

### Content Governance & CMS Audit

- Abnormal disabled a list of URLs for a CMS audit to improve brand alignment and user experience.
- **Disabled Content Categories & Actions:**
    - **Permanent Disable (Red):** Content too far outside Abnormal's core business.
    - **Review & Tweak:**
        - **Platform Capabilities:** Verify product messaging with PMM.
        - **Threat Intel:** Ensure content aligns with Abnormal's capabilities (review with Head of Threat Intel).
        - **Insider Threats:** Remove sections not tied to misdirected email prevention.
    - **Re-enable:** Content that needs minor tweaks.
- **Workflow:** GrowthX will import the list to Content OS. Mariana will highlight specific sections for Abnormal's review in Google Docs, simplifying the approval process.

### New Content Strategy & Messaging

- **Primary Focus:**
    - **Email Security:** Stake a claim as the leading provider.
    - **Misdirected Email Prevention:** Leverage the new product launch.
    - **AI Phishing Coach:** Build traction for this new offering.
- **Messaging Nuance (per Gartner MQ):**
    - **Legacy SEGs:** Frame traditional, signature-based Secure Email Gateways (SEGs) as inadequate on their own.
    - **Abnormal's Role:** Position Abnormal's behavioral AI as the necessary complement to a built-in Microsoft/Google SEG, not as a replacement for the SEG itself.

### Website Redesign & Content Migration

- **Context:** A full website redesign and replatforming is underway.
- **Timeline:** MVP launch is mid-to-late February. Content migration (blog, resources, glossary) begins in January.
- **Content Pruning:** The migration will be selective to improve user journeys and focus on high-quality content.
- **Redirect Strategy:**
    - **Preference:** Use 404s for low-performing content.
    - **Rationale:** This avoids redirect chains and allows for a custom 404 page that guides users to relevant content, improving UX.

### Content Volume & Performance Goals

- **Tension:** Balancing content quality with the volume needed to hit aggressive traffic goals.
- **Abnormal's Goal:** Hit aggressive monthly visit targets for the current quarter, which were missed last quarter.
- **GrowthX's Role:** Deliver high-quality content at scale, targeting ~15-20 articles/week.
- **Abdallah's Rationale:** A high-volume, AI-driven workflow is required to hit targets. A low-volume approach would negate the value of the AI investment.

### Content Pipeline & Approvals

- **Threat Intel Content:** Paused. Callie will define a new workflow for GrowthX support once her role focuses exclusively on Threat Intel.
- **Content Refresh Plan:**
    - **Priority 1 (Low-Hanging Fruit):** 16 articles ranking 11-20.
    - **Priority 2 (Rewrites):** 17 articles with no traction but high relevancy.
    - **Priority 3 (Rewrites):** 135 articles with no traction.
- **Approval Workflow:**
    - **New Topics:** Emily is the primary approver.
    - **Webinar Repurposing:** Abdallah can approve directly.
