## Summary

Marcel Santilli presented GrowthX's AI-driven content marketing framework and case studies demonstrating measurable ROI—including a Ramp engagement that supported $50M+ in annual ad spend with reduced acquisition costs. Key outcomes: Cresta team gained concrete frameworks for evaluation-stage buyer targeting, visibility into Check That AI's competitive benchmarking capabilities (launching Q1 2026), and actionable content strategies for integration into their own programs. ## Context

This fireside chat was part of Cresta's broader initiative to deepen partnerships with leading AEO and content strategy experts. GrowthX and Cresta have an active partnership focused on AI-driven SEO and content marketing strategy. The Cresta team (approximately 20+ attendees) sought to understand how content-first approaches scale with AI, how to measure AI visibility in search and AI assistant citations, and how evaluation-stage buyer targeting drives growth. Marcel is a proven founder (IBM's SecurityIntelligence.com generated $17M attributed revenue) with a track record of building scalable content operations and AI-powered workflows at both enterprise and startup scales.
