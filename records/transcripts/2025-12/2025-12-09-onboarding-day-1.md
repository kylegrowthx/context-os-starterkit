### GrowthX Tools & Platforms

  - **Slack:** Primary communication hub.
      - **Channel Naming:** `D` (Delivery/client-facing) or `B` (Build/internal ops) + `Int` (Internal) or `Ext` (External) + Client Name.
  - **Geekbot:** Asynchronous stand-ups in Slack.
      - **Process:** Prompts arrive daily; respond during your shift.
      - **Purpose:** Share progress, blockers, and questions with your team.
      - **Note:** Geekbot is pre-integrated; no installation is required.
  - **Linear:** Project management tool.
      - **Use:** Track personal onboarding tasks and assign tickets to IT/engineering.
  - **Notion:** Central knowledge base for documentation, SOPs, and client files.
  - **Other Tools:**
      - **Loom:** Screen recording
      - **Zoom:** Meetings
      - **Fathom:** AI note-taker

### Onboarding Process & Resources

  - **Onboarding Plan:**
      - **Location:** Notion → Handbook → Onboarding Resources → Company-Wide Onboarding.
      - **Structure:** Day 1 tasks (tool setup) and Week 1 tasks (reading, webinars).
  - **Task Management in Linear:**
      - **Location:** `Workspace` → `Projects` → `My Projects` → Your Onboarding Project.
      - **Process:** Each task links to its Notion resource. Update task status (`In Progress`, `Done`) in Linear.

### Key Policies & Information

  - **Time Off (PTO):**
      - **Policy:** Unlimited PTO; no approval required.
      - **Process:** Block time on the calendar and invite your team.
      - **Notice:** Provide 1–2 weeks' notice.
  - **Employee Referral Program:**
      - **Bonus:** Up to $10k for engineering roles; up to $5k for non-engineering roles.
      - **Condition:** Referral must remain employed for 6 months.
  - **IT & Tool Support:**
      - **Channel:** `#it-and-tools` in Slack.
      - **Contacts:** Alice Ochieng or Yusuf (Head of Cybersecurity & IT).
  - **Bi-weekly All-Hands:**
      - **Next Meeting:** Next week.
      - **Action:** New hires will introduce themselves (name, location, fun fact).
  - **Onboarding Buddy:**
      - **Assignment:** Your manager will assign a buddy to help you learn processes and tools.

### Team Email Setup (`team@growthxlabs.com`)

  - **Purpose:** Shared access for tools (e.g., ChatGPT) and client communication.
  - **Setup Process (in Gmail):**
    1.  Add account using credentials from 1Password.
    2.  When prompted for 2FA, select "Try another way."
    3.  Choose "Get a verification code from the Google Authenticator app."
