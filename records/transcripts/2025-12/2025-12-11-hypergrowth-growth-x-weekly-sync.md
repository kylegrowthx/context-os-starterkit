## Key Topics

### Influencer Content Analysis

  - An analysis of top influencers (e.g., Lenny Rachitsky, Kyla) revealed a consistent formula for high engagement.
  - **Key Engagement Drivers:**
      - Personal stories \> pure theory
      - Controversial takes
      - Specific company examples for credibility
  - **Identified Formula:** `Bold Claim → Personal Story → Old vs. New → Specific Examples → Framework → Hot Take`.

### "Portfolio Operator" Content Series

  - A new content series, "Portfolio Operator Playbook," will break down Guillaume's advisory model into its core components.
  - **Rationale:** This approach replaces a stalled, monolithic piece on "10 years of advisory" with smaller, digestible articles and posts.
  - **Core Components to Cover:**
      - **Equity Focus:** Explain the model's goal of aggregating equity and why it drives specific client selection (e.g., the "three-circle advisory test").
      - **Operator Credibility:** Emphasize the value of hands-on experience over pure consulting.
      - **VC Network:** Highlight the role of the network in sourcing opportunities.

### New Content Angles & Positioning

  - **"Why are there so many French people in growth?"**
      - **Hypothesis:** A unique intersection of factors.
          - **Culture & Education:** High-quality engineering schools + a Latin culture of working around rules.
          - **Entrepreneurship:** A higher startup density in France.
          - **Early Influence:** Guillaume's role in popularizing growth in France.
      - **Action:** Validate the hypothesis with data on French representation in growth teams and tools.
  - **"The 7-Year Wasteland" / Hero's Journey**
      - **Angle:** A narrative of the advisory practice's evolution, including early failures and lessons learned.
      - **Rationale:** Admitting failure creates a relatable "hero's journey" arc that resonates with audiences.
  - **Positioning Shift:**
      - **From:** Competing on "most advanced" growth tactics.
      - **To:** Owning the "sage at the top of the mountain" narrative, leveraging 15+ years of experience.

### Content Cadence & Distribution

  - **Influencer Cadence:** 2–5 posts per week.
  - **Strategy:** Repurpose long-form content (e.g., newsletter) into multiple LinkedIn posts to drive curiosity and traffic.
  - **Geotargeting:** LinkedIn's lack of geotargeting is a limitation for French-specific content, but posting in both English and French will be explored.
