## Key Topics

### Q1 Content & Campaign Support

  - **Goal:** Create a high-value asset to support a Q1 campaign focused on a core pain point: app access and automated onboarding.
  - **Asset Idea:** A data-driven report (e.g., white paper, trend report) based on Siit's internal data.
      - **Rationale:** Provides a unique, non-blog asset for lead generation and strengthens SEO by offering proprietary insights.
  - **Timeline:** Target launch is February 2025.
  - **Action:** Carmel will provide Matt with available internal data (e.g., top integrations, key requests) for analysis.

### Advanced Growth Projects

  - **ROI Calculator:** A tool to calculate time/cost savings from using Siit.
      - **Rationale:** Leverages the success of existing tools, which are a major traffic driver.
      - **Inspiration:** Ramp's "growth tools" section.
  - **Interactive Product Demo:** A landing page where users can select their tech stack (e.g., Slack, GitHub) to see a custom workflow simulation.
      - **Rationale:** Provides a dynamic, personalized product experience, inspired by Vapi's custom agent builder.
  - **Status:** Both are "blue sky" ideas requiring a custom add-on to the partnership. Matt will investigate GrowthX's internal capabilities and cost.

### Q1 Strategic Analysis & Content Refresh

  - **Competitive Gap Analysis:** A deep dive into competitor marketing strategies is planned for Q1.
      - **Rationale:** To proactively counter anticipated marketing pushes, especially from Serval, who recently started paid ad campaigns (Nov 28, 2024).
  - **Content Cluster Refresh:** GrowthX is already scoping a refresh of the Service Desk and AI content clusters.
      - **Goal:** Tighten clusters by eliminating redundant topics and creating a clear TOFU → MOFU → BOFU user journey.
      - **Strategy:** Expand into industry-specific content (e.g., ITSM for Healthcare) to capture high-intent, low-volume traffic.
  - **Customer Proof in Content:** A new initiative to add customer quotes to blog posts.
      - **Rationale:** Boosts E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness) and provides tangible proof points without requiring full case studies.
