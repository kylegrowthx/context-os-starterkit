## Key Topics

### 2025 Performance Review (Feb–Dec)

  - **SERP Visibility:**
      - **Position 1 Keywords:** 19 → 388 (+1,942%)
      - **Position 2–10 Keywords:** 68 → 1,500 (+2,106%)
  - **Traffic & Conversions:**
      - **Organic/Referral Sessions:** 0 → 4,000+ per month.
      - **Conversions (Aug–Dec):**
          - Signups: 178
          - Booked Demos: 27
      - **Insight:** Users strongly prefer self-serve signups over demos. A future CRO strategy must align with the primary business goal (PLG vs. Enterprise).
  - **AI/LLM Visibility:**
      - **Overall:** \<1% → 20%
      - **"AI Roleplay" Category:** 5% → 43%
      - **Significance:** This growth establishes strong topical authority, which is critical for influencing how LLMs cite the website and for capturing high-intent leads.

### 2026 Renewal Proposal

  - **Strategic Shift:** The strategy will pivot from the niche "AI roleplay" category to a broader "learning platform" focus, aligning with the upcoming course platform and call scoring features.
  - **Proposed Package ($12k/mo):**
      - **1. Landing Page SEO:** On-page optimization and backlink support for new commercial pages.
      - **2. Editorial Content:** 5 high-quality articles/wk (vs. 10).
          - **Rationale:** Prioritize quality over quantity. Articles will be more in-depth (e.g., 2,000 words), with FAQs and detailed examples to improve LLM performance.
      - **3. Backlinks:** 10 backlinks/mo, focused on supporting new landing pages.
          - **Note:** The current 24/mo cadence ends in January.

### Renewal Negotiation

  - **Exec's Position:**
      - **Goal:** Reduce the $12k/mo cost to $10k/mo to manage expenses.
      - **Rationale:** GrowthX is the most expensive agency partner. Past under-delivery on backlink strategy created a "sour taste" and justifies a price adjustment.
      - **Alternative:** Consider hiring an in-house writer for \~20 articles/month at a lower cost (e.g., \~$600/article).
  - **GrowthX's Position:**
      - **Value:** The $12k/mo package already includes landing page SEO and backlinks as "extras" compared to the standard $12k/mo price for content alone.
      - **ROI:** The current lead volume suggests the value exceeds the cost; one closed enterprise deal could cover the annual investment.
  - **Path Forward:** Jo will explore options, including a potential credit for past under-delivery or an increased content volume (e.g., 7 articles/wk) to justify the $12k price.

### Immediate Priorities

  - **AI Roleplay Page On-Page SEO:**
      - **Action:** Garett to secure 30 mins with Sean this week for approval.
      - **Rationale:** Capitalize on recent off-page SEO gains by implementing on-page optimizations before the holidays to compound growth.
  - **`sales-roleplay-software` Redirect:**
      - **Action:** Garett to secure Sean's approval to implement the pre-built JavaScript code.
      - **Rationale:** The old and new articles are nearly identical; the redirect is needed to consolidate authority.
  - **Placement.com Article Images:**
      - **Action:** GrowthX to use general, existing Placement-style images (e.g., magnifying glass) for new articles.
      - **Rationale:** Avoid creating a new image generation system to save effort while maintaining brand separation from Exec.
