## Key Topics

### The Bottleneck: Aida's Role

  - Aida's time is consumed by meetings (10–12 hrs/wk) and acting as a secondary QC layer for all clients, preventing her from owning a dedicated portfolio.
  - **Weekly Time Allocation:**
      - **Meetings:** 10–12 hours
      - **Strategy Dev:** 1–1.5 days for two clients
      - **Content Editing:** 1 full day for 3+ clients
      - **Topic Ideation:** Varies by CM/ME skill

### The Problem: QC Risks & Strategic Gaps

  - Aida's QC role is essential to prevent critical errors and strategic flaws that damage client results and company credibility.
  - **P0: Critical Errors**
      - Citing outdated data (e.g., from 2015).
      - Citing fake data (e.g., a PDF marked "This is not real data").
  - **P1: Strategic Flaws**
      - **Duplicate Keywords:** Targeting synonymous keywords dilutes results (e.g., Hyper-Exponential case).
      - **AI "Slop":** Unquestioned, confident but empty AI statements.
  - **P2: Refinements**
      - Flow, transitions, and tone are subjective and can be iterated with the client without high risk.

### The Solution: Training & Process Improvements

  - **EM Training:**
      - **William:** Strong on content strategy; needs training on keyword research.
      - **Kathy:** Needs coaching on content strategy.
      - **All EMs:** Must understand the keyword process to advise clients credibly, even if CMs/MEs execute it.
  - **CM/ME Process:**
      - **Coverage Calendar:** Implement a system to ensure continuous coverage, preventing EMs from having to fill gaps.
      - **Training:** Provide formal training on keyword research, search intent, and source verification.
  - **New Sprint Cadence:**
      - **Goal:** Create a forum for real-time problem-solving, enablement, and knowledge transfer.
      - **Proposed Agenda:** Status updates, workshopping client signals, and live enablement sessions.
  - **Marcel Review Gate:**
      - **Proposal:** Make a mandatory internal strategy review with Marcel a gate before client presentation.
      - **Rationale:** Transfers Marcel's deep industry knowledge and ensures alignment.
      - **Logistics:** A kickoff on Monday allows for an internal review by the following Monday, fitting the typical one-week strategy development cycle.
