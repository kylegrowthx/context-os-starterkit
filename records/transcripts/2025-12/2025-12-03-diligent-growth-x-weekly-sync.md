  - **Overall Traffic & Engagement:**

      - November was the second-best month for traffic in 2023, behind October.
  - **Organic Search (GSC):**

  - **Top Content & Conversions:**
      - **Top Post:** "Singapore AI regulation" → confirms strong performance for regulation-focused topics in both organic and LLM results.
      - **Top Converting Page:** "Enterprise risk compliance" (11 lead form submissions).
      - **Action:** Verify the "lead form submission" event tracks high-intent actions (e.g., demo requests) vs. top-funnel downloads to assess lead quality.
  - **LLM Performance:**
      - Diligent remains a dominant citation in non-branded prompts, significantly outperforming Onboard.
      - **Opportunity:** Refresh content in the "Board meeting management techniques" cluster to counter recent performance drops.

### New SEO Priority: Page Consolidation

  - **Problem:** The sunsetting of legacy "solution" pages has caused a loss of rankings for high-intent commercial terms (e.g., "board management software").
  - **Goal:** Consolidate SEO authority from old pages into the new "product" pages to regain rankings.
  - **Action Plan:**
      - **Audit:** Review all legacy solution pages to identify consolidation opportunities.
      - **Redirects:** Update all internal links to point directly to new product pages, bypassing redirects.
      - **Keyword Strategy:**
          - **Product Pages:** Target both product-specific and commercial terms.
          - **Diligent1 Page:** Target broad "GRC platform" terms to reinforce topical authority.
      - **Content Strategy:** Align all content refreshes with the new page structure and GRC messaging.

### Competitor Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** Internal legal feedback requires competitor claims to be sourced from neutral, external voices to mitigate risk.
  - **Solution:**
      - Cite user reviews and data from third-party sites like G2 and Capterra.
      - Use `nofollow` links for all outbound competitor citations.

### Image Production Process

  - **Problem:** Internal bandwidth constraints limit the ability to create custom images for content.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will now create on-brand infographics using AI tools (e.g., Nano Banana).
  - **Process:** Diligent provides a blank Canva template and a completed example for style guidance.
