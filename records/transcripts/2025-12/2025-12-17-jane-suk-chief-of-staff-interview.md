## Transcript Summary

**Background and Career Journey**

Jane has extensive experience as Chief of Staff, currently at Rasa supporting the CEO with annual planning, company strategy, OKRs, executive communications, board meetings, and organizational rhythms. She values the dynamic, generalist nature of the Chief of Staff role and wants to deepen her mastery in this capacity.

Her previous experience includes consulting, go-to-market strategy, and multiple Chief of Staff roles supporting C-suite executives. She develops and manages annual plans, CEO communications, and operational frameworks that enable executive effectiveness.

**Motivation for Joining GrowthX**

Jane is motivated by:
- Alignment with a compelling company mission focused on AI
- Working with a leader deeply connected to the AI space
- Opportunity to improve inefficient agency-driven content production that costs $10,000+ per piece at Rasa
- GrowthX's phased approach: starting with services delivery and transitioning to scalable platform

She's excited by the potential to help GrowthX scale from operational chaos to structured, efficient systems.

**GrowthX Vision and Strategy**

Marcel outlined GrowthX's multi-product ambition:
- Core platform "Output AI" supporting all operations and client services
- Two main products: CheckThat AI (AI visibility tool) and content marketing services
- Strategic goal to transition clients from fully managed services to platform ownership within 2-3 months
- Organic growth focus: B2B companies with $5-50M revenue committed to content-driven website optimization
- Organic growth as sustainable moat vs paid acquisition

**Current Operational Context**

GrowthX's challenge: Despite strong sales (nearly $5M in deals recently with 85% conversion rate), operations limit them to two client kickoffs per week. This bottleneck prevents scaling. The company operates with 90 people across delivery, build, and engagement management with minimal management structure.

Financial health: 70% gross margins, well-capitalized with $12M in bank, recent investment from HubSpot at $250M valuation. Burn rate approximately $2.3M/month.

**Jane's Leadership Philosophy**

Jane's approach centers on:
- Three Cs: consistency, clarity, and care
- Empathy and direct feedback with leaders
- Building clarity from organizational chaos through frameworks and people engagement
- Adaptability, proactive communication, and strong executive presence
- Data-backed decision making and customer insight

Mentors like Nate Vogel (former VP Go-to-Market Enablement at Gong) shaped her belief in maintaining executive presence while staying connected to team dynamics.

**Desired Leadership Impact**

Jane aspires to:

- As hypothetical CEO, would first establish clear operating principles and 2-year North Star vision
- Break down silos with clear accountability and outcome orientation

**Why Leaving Rasa**

Jane prefers to leave Rasa because:

- Turnover disrupting personal connections
- Desire for faster pace critical in competitive AI space
- Preference for direct communication and feedback with leader

**Cultural and Strategic Fit**

Jane values:
