## Context

Ambient is a security/AI visibility product company working with GrowthX on content strategy and SEO. The engagement started in September 2025 and focuses on building thought leadership through high-volume, technically accurate content. Ambient's team is lean (CMO Alberto, product marketers Atul and Mauricio) and depends on this GrowthX partnership to generate content that drives organic traffic and establishes credibility. This meeting introduces the new GrowthX structure and addresses that concern while delivering board-ready performance metrics.
