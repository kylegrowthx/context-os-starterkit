## Context

Hex is a data analytics platform client engaged with GrowthX for B2B content marketing services. The partnership is in its early phase, with a December 15, 2025 Phase 2 opt-out deadline that will determine whether Hex continues the relationship. This meeting brought together Hex's internal stakeholders (Jessica Schimm and Peter Ryan) with GrowthX's content and business development team (Aida Knežević and Tyler Pavlas) to review early content output, align on production cadence, and address concerns about the content review process that was bottlenecking publication and preventing the team from generating performance signals needed to make a confident Phase 2 decision.

## Key Topics

### Content Review & Cadence

  - **Problem:** The current review process is a bottleneck, delaying publication and preventing the generation of performance signals needed for the Dec 15 Phase 2 decision.
  - **Solution:** Adopt a 5-article/week production cadence.
      - **GrowthX:** Delivers 5 drafts on Friday.
      - **Hex:** Provides feedback by the following Thursday.
      - **GrowthX:** Implements revisions and stages content for publication on Friday.
  - **Review Team:** Reduced to three stakeholders to accelerate feedback.
      - **Jessica:** Tone (expected to become a light review).
      - **Rachel:** Technical accuracy.
      - **Peter:** Positioning and gut check.

### Atlas Platform Deep Dive

  - Aida demonstrated Atlas, GrowthX's proprietary platform, to build confidence in the content's quality and accuracy.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Agentic Pipeline:** AI self-grades against guidelines and fact-checks every passage, ensuring consistency and accuracy.
      - **Research:** Uses Tavali (replacing Perplexity) for more powerful, high-quality information gathering.
      - **Fact-Checking:** Divides articles into passages, researches each claim, and edits the draft in real-time.
      - **Customization:** The pipeline is modular; new steps can be added for product-specific checks or organic product mentions.

### Logistics & Content Details

  - **Author:** Jessica will confirm if Rachel is comfortable being listed as the author.
      - **Rationale:** A real person's name is preferred over a generic company name.
  - **Featured Images:**
      - **Issue:** The first article's image uses a color associated with a specific Hex solution.
      - **Decision:** Publish the first article as-is. Peter will get brand approval from Annie for future images.
      - **Process:** Aida will export image samples to Figma for review.
  - **Contentful Uploads:**
      - **Requirement:** GrowthX must add author initials (e.g., RH) to drafts in Contentful.
      - **Rationale:** An empty author field blocks other users from publishing content.
  - **Source Control:**
      - **Issue:** Hex requested control over source quality, citing past issues with another agency using unknown publications.
      - **Solution:** GrowthX can configure Atlas to pull from a client-approved list of primary sources (e.g., McKinsey, Gartner).
