## Key Topics

### Content Production Strategy

  - **Sprint Goal:** Get diverse feedback on content quality and product messaging.
  - **Selection Approach:**
      - Approve 3–5 topics from each of the 8 clusters.
      - Mix commercial keywords (e.g., listicles) with informational ones.
      - **Rationale →** Commercial topics provide more opportunities for Navan product mentions, yielding crucial feedback on messaging.
  - **Timeline:**
      - Navan to approve initial topics by EOD Tuesday.
      - GrowthX starts writing Tuesday/Wednesday for internal editing.
      - Drafts delivered to Navan by end of week.

### Calibration Draft Review

  - **Draft:** "How to Create a Travel Policy"
  - **Purpose:** Replace an outdated 2023 post.
  - **Key Improvements:**
      - **Structure:** Better organization of H2s/H3s for clarity.
      - **Integration:** Product mentions peppered throughout, not just at the end.
      - **SEO:** Added FAQ section (targets "People Also Ask") and TLDR (improves AI search visibility).
  - **Feedback Process:**
      - **Actionable Feedback:** Specific edits (flow, grammar) can be accepted directly.
      - **Strategic Feedback:** High-level comments on tone, style, and product messaging are critical for refining the Atlas platform's artifacts (e.g., writing guidelines, product context).

### GrowthX's Content Generation Process (Atlas)

  - Aida demoed the "agentic article generation" pipeline in Atlas, GrowthX's in-house platform.
  - **Human-Led Workflow:**
    1.  **Briefing:** Manual input of keyword, word count, and structural templates (e.g., "how-to," "comparison").
    2.  **Research (Tably):** An agent generates a research plan, asks questions, and evaluates source quality.
    3.  **Drafting:** An agent creates the draft.
    4.  **Self-Correction:**
          - **Validate Writing Guidelines:** An agent grades the draft against rules (0-1 score, e.g., 0.92) and auto-corrects issues like choppy flow.
          - **Fact Check:** An agent verifies claims paragraph-by-paragraph.
  - **Integration:** Atlas connects to the SEMrush API to find related keywords, ensuring comprehensive topic coverage.

### Content Refresh Analysis

  - GrowthX is analyzing existing content for refresh opportunities.
  - **Sources of Analysis:**
      - **Paid Keywords:** Analyzing Nima's list for blog potential.
      - **Underperforming Content:** Identifying posts on pages 2–4 that could be pushed to page 1 with a refresh.
  - **Data Anomaly:** A significant traffic drop on the "What is a virtual card?" post was caused by a URL structure change (category removed from URL), not poor performance.
      - **Fix →** GrowthX will combine data using the slug to get an accurate view.
  - **Prioritization:** The sprint focuses on new content. Refreshes will be balanced with new content in Phase 2, excluding time-sensitive news posts.
