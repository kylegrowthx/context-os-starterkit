## Key Topics

### Background & Motivation

  - **Career Path:** Started at Meta & Google (ads/cloud), then moved to startups for remote flexibility.
  - **Current Role:** Account Executive at WriteSonic, an AI content platform (SEO/GEO).
  - **Motivation for GrowthX:**
      - Original interest from a job search in June.
      - Desire to join a "rocket ship" with exponential growth.
      - Interest in the AI content space.

### WriteSonic Experience: Building Sales from Scratch

  - **Context:** Joined as the 3rd AE; the lead AE was on a 40-day vacation, requiring immediate self-sufficiency.
  - **Process Building:** Implemented the sales infrastructure from scratch to enable forecasting and automation.
      - **HubSpot:** Set up the CRM, pipeline stages, and lead-routing workflows.
      - **Sales Assets:** Developed scripts and battle cards against competitors (Profound, Scrunch).
  - **Sales Cycle & Post-Sale:**
      - **Inbound-Heavy:** Strong brand generates leads; price is the main objection.
      - **Post-Sale Involvement:** Creates contracts/invoices and sets up client Slack channels to ensure a smooth handoff to the lean CS team.

### Deal Examples & Sales Methodology

  - **WriteSonic: Paradigm Agency (Win)**
      - **Method:** Used the SPICE framework (Situation, Pain, Individual Contributors, Context, Champions).
      - **Tactic:** Held individual "office hours" with team members to build consensus before group calls, avoiding surface-level agreement.
      - **Value Prop:** Positioned WriteSonic as the only tool offering both SEO and GEO content creation, not just AI search tracking.
  - **Google Cloud: marketing.ai (Win)**
      - **Context:** $5M multi-year deal with a prospect (CEO Kevin Wassum) already engaged with Amazon and Azure.
      - **Strategy:**
          - **Personal Connection:** Met the CEO at a conference.
          - **Doubt Creation:** Planted seeds of doubt by asking if the CEO wanted to risk missing a better opportunity.
          - **Resource Leverage:** Brought in AI specialists to provide a human connection and build trust that competitors lacked.
      - **Self-Critique:** Could have secured a larger deal ($7-8M) with more patience; also missed opportunities for case studies and network expansion.

### GrowthX Context & Sales Structure

  - **Current State (Tyler):**
      - **Role:** The sole AE, building the sales function without a direct manager.
      - **Challenge:** Lacked SEO expertise initially; now focused on personalizing the sales process to show specific client ROI.
      - **Goal:** Build custom demo workspaces and perform keyword analysis to help prospects visualize their future with GrowthX.
  - **Sales Process Status:**
      - **Established:** ICP scoring, demo process, handoff process, and proof points (case studies, references).
      - **Opportunity:** Personalize the sales process to show specific ROI, moving beyond generic demos.
  - **Prospect Profile:** A mix of educational-stage and shopping-stage leads, all eager to understand GEO.
