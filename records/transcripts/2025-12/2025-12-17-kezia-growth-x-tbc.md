## Key Topics

### Content Audit Challenges

  - An AI-powered audit using Glean is failing on bulk web fetches, getting stuck in loops and unable to process the large content volume (12–15 years of content).
  - **Goal:** Use AI to map content clusters, identify outdated products ("Governance Cloud"), and prioritize pages for refresh.
  - **Proposed Solution:** Pre-scrape all URLs and feed the static data to the AI. This bypasses the tool's web-fetch weakness and should prevent context loss.
  - **Contingency:** If the Glean tool remains unreliable, the audit will proceed manually, prioritized by product.

### LLM Visibility Strategy

  - **Problem:** A Scrunch report shows Diligent is missing from key LLM queries (Google AI Overviews, GPT, Perplexity) where competitors like Onboard and BoardVantage appear.
  - **Approach:** Treat this as a "marginal gain" initiative, not a core content project.
      - **Google AI Overviews:** Fix with simple SEO updates (e.g., FAQ insertions).
      - **Other LLMs:** Requires guidance from Diligent on prioritization.
  - **Rationale:** The data is directional and fluctuates, so the goal is to "plug holes" at scale with light-touch optimizations.

### Reporting & Analytics Overhaul

  - **Goal:** Create a new reporting dashboard for Tyler to track competitor gaps.
  - **Proposed Metrics:** Share of Voice, Top Positions, and Average Position.
  - **Tooling:** Use Ahrefs, Diligent's new standard SEO tool, for consistent reporting.
  - **Refinements:**
      - Focus on **Page 1 rankings only** for a consistent baseline, as tracking beyond page 1 is unreliable.
      - Segment data by **product/pillar cluster** and track against the closest competitor for that specific area.
      - Include consideration-stage terms to evaluate the performance of newly approved legal pages.

### Scrunch Data Migration

  - **Request:** Tyler wants to migrate historical performance data and/or settings from growthx's Scrunch account to Diligent's new in-house instance.
  - **Rationale:** Ensures reporting consistency and avoids starting from scratch.

### 2025 Content Priorities

  - **Top Priority:** Refresh content for the core "Governance" product, which is showing performance decay.
  - **Secondary Priority:** Expand to newer products like "Vault" with light-touch user journey tests (e.g., 8 high-potential pieces).
