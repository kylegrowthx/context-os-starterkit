## Key Topics

### Publication Context & Audience

  - **Positioning:** Occupy the space between basic bookkeeping and high-level CFO strategy, focusing on the 20% of finance knowledge that drives 80% of value.
  - **Editorial Style:** "Wealthsimple Magazine meets Investopedia" → human, empathetic, and practical, using concrete examples over definitions.
  - **Content Exploration:** Users can self-identify by stage (e.g., Starter, Survival Mode) to find relevant content.
  - **Audiences:**
      - **Primary:** Founders & operators who have inherited finance.
      - **Secondary:** Overwhelmed finance managers (an existing RAMP ICP).

### Content Strategy & Topic Clusters

  - **Methodology:** Analyzed \~4,000 URLs from competitors (QuickBooks, Airwallex) to identify low-difficulty, high-intent keywords.
  - **Core Clusters:**
      - **Finance Operations:** Foundational content for SEO/LLM authority.
      - **Modern Finance Stack:** Software (AP, international payments, banking).
      - **Strategic Finance:** Niche topics (budgeting, forecasting, cash flow).
  - **Priority Verticals:**
      - Professional Services
      - E-commerce & Retail
      - SaaS & Software
      - **Rationale:** These verticals have sufficient keyword material and identified content gaps (e.g., MRR tracking for SaaS).
  - **RAMP Feedback:** Approved the strategy. E-commerce is not a core RAMP vertical, but its inclusion is acceptable for building domain authority.

### Website V1 Review

  - **Review Scope:** The website layout and structure, not the placeholder copy or cover images.
  - **Outcome:** The layout was approved.
  - **Next Steps:** GrowthX will apply the new branding, create final cover images, and load initial articles for a final review before launch.

### Go-to-Market Plan

  - **Goal:** Achieve site indexing and impressions within two weeks of launch to validate the strategy.
  - **Execution:**
      - GrowthX will set up Search Console/Bing Webmaster for speed.
      - GrowthX will perform initial backlinking.
      - RAMP will provide backlinking from RAMP.com to accelerate indexing.
