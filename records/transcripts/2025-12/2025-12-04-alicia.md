## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Business Model & Challenge

  - **Mission:** Define the future of knowledge work using AI.
  - **Strategy:** Use a white-glove agency to discover what "good looks like," then productize the scalable components.
  - **Current State:**
      - **Agency:** High-touch service for Series B+ B2B SaaS.
          - **Success:** $12-15M revenue in \~1 year, 100 people.
          - **Challenge:** Unscalable due to unproductizable human elements (e.g., CMO alignment).
      - **Community:** A paid learning hub ($30/mo, 200+ members) that serves as the primary GTM channel.
      - **Platform:** A new product launching soon (mid-hundreds/mo) to track AI visibility and guide strategy.
  - **Core Problem:** The agency is at capacity (waitlist until Feb). The key challenge is building a scalable GTM flywheel to convert the community audience into customers for the new platform.

### Alicia's Background & Motivation

  - **Experience:** 14 years in product marketing (PMM), transitioning to Head of Marketing.
      - **Ravio:** Head of Marketing at the compensation data platform ($5M ARR, 50 people).
          - **Achievement:** Built a community-led GTM, shifting budget from performance ads to a more cost-efficient channel.
      - **Deliveroo:** Experience in a hyper-growth environment.
  - **Motivation for Change:**
      - **AI Interest:** Strong passion for AI; Ravio lacks internal AI curiosity.
      - **Pace:** Seeks the intensity and scale of a Deliveroo-like environment.
      - **Location:** Desire to be more connected to the SF tech scene.

### The Proposed Marketing Role

  - **The Gap:** GrowthX's marketing team is composed of specialists (Events, Content, Production). The missing piece is a generalist leader to build the overarching GTM system.
  - **Core Responsibilities:**
      - **System Building:** Connect the community, events, and product into a cohesive flywheel.
      - **Offer Creation:** Leverage a product marketing foundation to create compelling "offers" that deliver value and drive the customer journey.
      - **Strategic Judgment:** Select and execute marketing channels with a clear purpose, avoiding unnecessary complexity.
  - **Strategic Alignment:** Alicia's experience building community-led GTMs and her product marketing background are a direct fit for this challenge.
