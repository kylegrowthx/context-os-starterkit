## Key Topics

### PSEO Strategy for New Product

  - **Opportunity:** A high-volume PSEO campaign for the new agent product, targeting use cases where users need to connect data to AI agents.
  - **Initial Scope:** ~80+ pages, based on a list from Tanmay.
  - **Page Structure:**
      - **Focus:** Connectors-based use cases, not generic connector pages.
      - **Example:** "Fetch Salesforce data for marketing agents" or "Fetch Salesforce data for sales agents."
      - **Content:** Must be technical, featuring templatizable code snippets (e.g., Python) to provide value. Same code structure across pages, only changing connector names and variables.
  - **Why Quality Matters for Zero-to-One:** Unlike Airbyte's established data infrastructure product (which has existing traffic and brand awareness), the new agent product requires high-converting pages from day one because there's no baseline awareness.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Inspiration:** Jakub to share high-converting landing page examples by tomorrow.
    2.  **Template Draft:** Tanmay to create a page template doc this week (start with doc, then wireframe in Room with Mario and Michelle).

    5.  **Wireframing:** Jakub to create wireframes post-holidays.
    6.  **Build:** Engineering begins building the pages after wireframes are approved.
  - **Parallel Opportunity:** While the first PSEO set is being built, research and wireframe a second PSEO opportunity (e.g., vertical agents like marketing or support) to maintain momentum.
