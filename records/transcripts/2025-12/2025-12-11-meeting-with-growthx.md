## Key Topics

### Midi Health's Content Strategy & Goals

  - **Current State:** Publishing 5–8 articles/month; inconsistent cadence ("herky-jerky pattern") due to small team and competing content needs.
  - **Goal:** Scale to 50 articles/month by end of Q1 2026 to determine optimal volume before diminishing returns.
  - **Content Preference:** Human-generated or heavily human-edited AI content to maintain unique brand voice.
  - **Partnership Model:** Seeking strategic guidance and AI monitoring (GEO/AEO), not end-to-end content creation. Open to custom projects like quizzes, tools, calculators, or separate content properties.

### Content Review Process

  - **Requirement:** A single-round medical review is mandatory for all content.
      - **Rationale:** Build EEAT authority for Google search and ensure clinical accuracy.
  - **Current Process:** Reviewers provide all feedback in one pass (1-hour effort, 1-week turnaround).
  - **Future State:** A dedicated reviewer could reduce turnaround to 1–2 days.

### GrowthX's Model & Capabilities

  - **Content Creation Process:** AI-first workflow (initial drafts, research, fact-checking via AI) with significant human editing and quality assurance. 2–3 kickoffs/week with human involvement throughout.
  - **Partnership Structure:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-week Strategy Sprint):** ~5 articles for Midi Health (quality over quantity during review process setup); establishes quality bar and roadmap.
      - **Phase 2 (Ongoing):** 5 articles/week (mix of new and refreshed content); same price as Phase 1.
      - **Programmatic Content:** Can shift from editorial to programmatic (vendor directories, calculators) without price change; combining both increases cost.
      - **Custom Workstreams:** Quizzes, tools, calculators cost $9k–$15k each; scope varies with web dev/design involvement.
  - **Established Client Portfolio:**
      - **Ramp:** Long-term client; evolved from blog content → programmatic (vendor directory, per diem calculator) → separate authority domain ("Cashflow Desk" for CFO-focused thought leadership).
      - **Webflow, Airbyte, Augment Code:** Multi-month+ engagements for high-volume editorial content and strategy.
