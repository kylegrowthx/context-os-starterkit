## Key Topics

### Integration Page SEO Deep Dive

  - **Performance:** Nearing 500 published pages; a recent traffic dip is not a concern, as clicks remain healthy and key keywords perform well.
  - **Branded Search:** Webflow dominates branded search terms (e.g., "Webflow Stripe integration").
  - **Non-Branded Opportunity:** Pages are capturing high-intent non-branded searches, revealing a new opportunity.
      - **Examples:** "add social feed to Webflow," "migrate Squarespace to Webflow," "Stripe integration."
      - **Significance:** This indicates users are finding Webflow for broader, high-intent tasks, not just specific integrations.
  - **Next Steps:** GrowthX will deliver a formal "version two" optimization strategy for Webflow approval.

### New Content Engine for Use Case Guides

  - **Goal:** Address a key feedback gap by producing more technical content than the current integration pages.
  - **Methodology:** A proprietary content engine with a multi-step workflow (agents, fact-checking, human QA) to generate technical content at scale.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Codify Feedback:** Convert specific feedback into engineering specs to set a clear quality bar.
    2.  **Build Specs:** Create detailed engineering specs (e.g., TOCs, input/output benchmarks) to guide the engine.
    3.  **Generate & Iterate:** Use the engine to produce content for calibration and refinement.
  - **Benchmark:** The SoundCloud guide is the target for technical depth, as it successfully shows data structure (e.g., "what a SoundCloud record looks like").
