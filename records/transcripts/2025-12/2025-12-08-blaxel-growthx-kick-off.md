## Summary

GrowthX kicked off an eight-week content strategy sprint with Blaxel, a Series A AI infrastructure company providing production-grade sandbox environments for coding agents. Nicolas Lecomte and Paul Sinaï outlined Blaxel's "perpetual sandbox" technology—which achieves near-instant (25ms) resume times by snapshotting memory to standby storage—and identified their core challenge: low market visibility despite superior technology. The team aligned on GrowthX's role: creating educational content targeting VP-level engineering decision-makers to establish Blaxel as the authority on agentic infrastructure, with a long-term goal of making Blaxel synonymous with running AI agents in production.

## Context

Blaxel is a Series A infrastructure startup founded a year and a half ago, currently funded with a $7 million seed round from First Round Capital after completing Y Combinator earlier in 2025. Co-founders Nicolas Lecomte (product and go-to-market) and Paul Sinaï lead the company alongside four other co-founders, with the team now at nine people including a full-time technical content hire. Their core product is a "perpetual sandbox" platform—a managed environment for coding agents to run code—addressing a critical pain point in the emerging AI agent market where managing compute infrastructure manually results in either high costs (always-on VMs) or poor user experience (cold starts). GrowthX initiated an eight-week strategy sprint to help Blaxel build market education and mindshare, working directly with Nicolas and Paul on content calibration in the initial phase before moving to a streamlined publishing process.

## Key Topics

### Blaxel's Challenge: Low Visibility & Market Education

  - Blaxel's core problem is low visibility, not product inferiority.
      - **Competitors:** Have more mindshare and appear higher in search results.
      - **"Build it Yourself" Projects:** A common alternative, especially for companies with high compliance overhead.
  - The market needs education on the complexities of production-grade AI agent infrastructure.
      - **The Pitch:** Blaxel highlights the hidden costs and risks of DIY (scaling, reliability, security) to make the value of a managed service clear.
  - The long-term vision is that latency will be the key differentiator for agents, mirroring the web's evolution.
      - Blaxel's platform is designed to provide the sustainable speed required for agents to win in the future.

### Blaxel's Solution: The Perpetual Sandbox Platform

  - **Core Product:** A "perpetual sandbox" platform providing micro-VMs for AI agents to run code.
  - **Key Differentiators:**
      - **Automated Sandbox Lifecycle:**
          - **Problem:** Manual lifecycle management leads to high compute costs or slow cold starts.
          - **Solution:** Sandboxes snapshot to standby storage (compute-free) when inactive and resume in **25 milliseconds** when reconnected.
      - **Co-Hosting:** Host the AI agent itself on Blaxel's backbone for near-zero network latency.
      - **High-Touch Support:** A dedicated Slack channel for each customer provides real-time, technical support.
      - **Advanced Features:** Fully managed custom domains (one-click setup) and other production-grade tools.

### Customer Profile & Buying Journey

  - **ICP:** AI startups and scale-ups building agents that run code.
  - **User:** Technical roles (Software Engineers, Product Engineers).
  - **Buyer:** VP of Engineering, Head of Engineering, or Director of Engineering.
  - **Buying Moments:**
      - **1. New Infrastructure Setup (2/3 of customers):** Adopting a vendor for a new GenAI product.
      - **2. Vendor Switch (1/3 of customers):** Switching from a competitor due to reliability issues (e.g., Webflow).

### Content Strategy & Workflow

  - **GrowthX's Role:** Create educational content for decision-makers (VPs of Eng, CEOs) to build trust and mindshare.
  - **Internal Technical Writer:** Focuses on deep-dive engineering blog posts (e.g., Fly.io style) to build credibility.
  - **Content Review Process:**
      - **Initial Phase:** GrowthX will work directly with Nicolas and Paul for content calibration.
      - **Long-Term Goal:** A streamlined process with minimal review before publishing.
  - **Blog Structure:**
      - **Option 1 (Preferred):** A single blog with tags to differentiate content types.
      - **Option 2 (Alternative):** A separate "Guides" section for GrowthX content, mirroring the Augment Co. model.
