## Key Topics

### November Performance Review

  - **Traffic Dip:** November click traffic dipped to September levels.
      - **Cause:** Likely holiday seasonality and the lingering impact of Google's September algorithm update.
      - **Trend:** A positive traffic uptick in early November was cut short by Thanksgiving week.
  - **LLM Visibility:** Down from its September peak.
      - **Rationale:** This dip validates the new content strategy's focus on LLM-friendly formats like comparison tables.
      - **Positive Signal:** Recent Scrunch data shows Webstacks is being cited for both awareness and evaluation-stage prompts, indicating strong performance across the funnel.

### New Content Strategy: Tech Partners

  - **Goal:** Capture LLM traffic by creating objective, comparison-based content.
  - **Approval:** Webstacks approved direct comparisons between tech partners.
      - **Rationale:** Aligns with Webstacks' platform-agnostic positioning.
      - **Constraint:** Content must remain objective and never speak negatively about a tool.
  - **New Content Type:** "Implementation Guides" will be added.
      - **Rationale:** To address the business impact of tech decisions, which often lead to expensive migrations when teams lack the processes and skills to use a tool effectively.
  - **LLM Optimization:** Comparison tables are a key format for LLM traffic.
      - **Action:** Webstacks will develop a custom comparison module in Sanity.
      - **Timing:** This is now feasible during the holiday site redesign.

### Strategic Shift: Site Redesign & Content Structure

  - **Webstacks Redesign:** A holiday site redesign is underway to elevate the brand to an "agency studio feel."
  - **Content Restructure:** The redesign provides an opportunity to reorganize the blog.
      - **Proposed Model:** A "hub and spoke" structure built around evergreen pillar pages.
      - **Rationale:** To improve recency and topical authority for Google and LLMs.
      - **Benefit:** Allows for content refreshes by adding new modules instead of writing full articles.

### Logistics & Planning

  - **Holiday Schedule:**
      - **GrowthX:** Out the week of Christmas; light Slack monitoring.
      - **Webstacks:** Quiet week of New Year's.
      - **Production:** No new content will be produced for the week of New Year's.
  - **Meeting Cadence:** Shifting to biweekly in January to focus on high-level strategy.
