## Key Topics

### Background & Motivation

  - **Experience:** Balanced career in pre-sales, sales, and post-sales.
  - **Current Role (Sanity):** Account Manager with significant AE overlap (discovery, multi-threading, contracts, upsell).
  - **Motivation for AE Role:** The current AM role is already AE-like, making a formal AE transition logical.
  - **Interest in GrowthX:**
      - **Content & AI:** Strong background in the content space (Sanity, Storyblock) and AI (chatbots).
      - **"Building in Public":** Followed GrowthX's journey since July 2024.

### Sales Process & Playbook

  - **Storyblock (Founding AE):**
      - **Challenge:** Navigating complex enterprise deals (e.g., 4-month T-Mobile RFP) without dedicated support teams.
      - **Solution:** Built the first sales playbook and knowledge base to standardize processes and centralize information.
  - **Sales Process Framework:**
      - **Multi-threading:** Identify and build relationships with 3–4 key stakeholders across departments (champion, influencer, economic buyer, budget holder).
      - **Discovery:** Book next calls live during meetings to avoid email delays.
      - **Scoping:** Define user seats, environments, and data usage to structure the proposal.
      - **Procurement:** Anticipate and buffer for contract redlining and legal reviews.

### Deal Example: Sanity Expansion

  - **Deal:** Expansion at The Motley Fool (Q2).
  - **Source:** Proactive discovery with an existing champion uncovered a new business unit in Australia.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Discovery:** Initial call with the new unit.
    2.  **Technical Deep Dive:** Solutions Engineer call on frameworks and hosting.
    3.  **POC Workshop:** Hands-on session to scope needs.
    4.  **Scoping & Proposal:** Structured a proposal based on user seats and data SKUs.
    5.  **Negotiation:** Standard sales tactics to close by quarter-end.
    6.  **Procurement:** Navigated contract redlining (e.g., auto-renewal, liability) and an AI security addendum.
  - **Outcome:** Closed on the last day of the quarter after a \~2.5-month cycle.
  - **Self-Critique:**
      - **Rating:** 8/10.
      - **Gap to 10:** Could have discovered more use cases for future pipeline and buffered for procurement to close earlier.

### GrowthX AE Role & Compensation

  - **Role Evolution:**
      - **Phase 1 (Product Launch):** High-velocity, lower-ACV ($15–20k) sales.
      - **Phase 2 (Services):** Higher-ACV, white-glove deals.
  - **Services Deal Structure:**
      - **Phase 1:** 8-week strategy sprint ($30–40k fixed fee).
      - **Phase 2:** 12-month term, $15k/mo ($180k ACV).
      - **Example:** Brex deal was over $400k ARR.
  - **Compensation:** 3% of revenue brought in, with kickers up to 5%.
