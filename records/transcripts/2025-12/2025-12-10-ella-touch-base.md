## Key Topics

### The Scaling Challenge

  - **Problem:** A 2-onboardings/week limit on strategy sprints is the primary growth constraint.
  - **Goal:** Scale beyond this limit without linear hiring while maintaining an 85%+ conversion rate.
  - **Solution:** Codify best practices into repeatable processes and training to reduce reliance on "hero" individuals (e.g., Ada, Marcel).
  - **Process Gaps:**
      - No formal retrospective process to capture organizational learning from strategy sprints.
      - No proactive system to identify at-risk accounts before they become renewal problems.

### Strategy Team's Evolving Role

  - **Proposed Model:** The Strategy team shifts from owning client health metrics to an "elite forces" unit.
      - **Ownership:** Account health and strategy shift to Engagement Managers (EMs).
      - **Function:** The Strategy team provides high-leverage services:
          - **Proactive:** Training, playbooks, and best practice codification.
          - **Reactive:** Escalation support and health remediation for at-risk accounts.
          - **Future:** A paid service SKU (e.g., quarterly SEO audits) to fund the team.
  - **Rationale:** This model ensures EMs develop the intuition and self-sufficiency needed to handle client issues directly, rather than creating a ticket for every small request.

### Operational Gaps & Opportunities

  - **Account Health Tracking:**
      - **Problem:** No central, actionable system to track account health.
      - **Impact:** Critical risks are missed. *Example: A CSM reported an account as "green" while the client was running a parallel beta with a competitor.*
      - **Need:** A formal process for documenting risks and tracking remediation plans.
  - **EM Handoffs:**
      - **Problem:** High-risk handoffs are managed informally (e.g., via Slack) and lack a structured process.
      - **Need:** A playbook for gradual transitions to build trust and prevent client churn.
  - **Fathom Data:**
      - **Problem:** Valuable data in Fathom call recordings is underutilized.
      - **Opportunity:** Use AI to analyze recordings for health signals, sentiment, and training insights.

### Capacity & Process Management

  - **Current Process:** Kyle manually triages all incoming tickets, biasing towards immediate action over a large backlog.
  - **Challenge:** No repeatable method exists to assess team capacity. The current low volume (due to holidays) is not representative of future demand.
  - **Goal:** Develop a rubric for ticket types and escalation scenarios to enable more accurate capacity planning.
