## Key Topics

### The Problem: Manual Revenue Processes

  - Turnstile's origin is the founders' experience at their previous company, Second Measure, where manual revenue processes became a bottleneck.
  - **Core Issue:** No structured system of record for commercial terms (pricing, entitlements), which are buried in PDFs.
  - **Resulting Pain Points:**
      - **Time Drain:** Founders spend too much time on "undifferentiated heavy lifting" (e.g., manual quoting, billing, usage reconciliation).
      - **Data Inaccuracy:** Discrepancies between sales and billing numbers create confusion, especially during fundraising.
      - **Revenue Leakage:** Manual processes lead to forgotten invoices and lost revenue.
      - **Scalability Block:** Manual work prevents hiring AEs or standardizing processes.

### The Solution: Turnstile's Quote-to-Cash Platform

  - Turnstile automates the entire quote-to-cash workflow, from proposal to payment and accounting.
  - **Key Differentiator:** Stores commercial terms as structured data, enabling automation and analytics across the customer lifecycle.
  - **Pricing:** Starts at $100/mo + 0.6% of billings (vs. Stripe Billing's 0.7%), making it cheaper and more feature-rich.
  - **Strategic Goal:** Become the "day-one revenue platform" that startups never outgrow, building a business on the success of breakout winners.

### The Shift: From Sales-Led to PLG

  - Turnstile's product was built with core flexibility, enabling a shift from a sales-led model to a scalable, low-touch PLG motion.
  - **Self-Serve Launch:** Enabled last Friday, allowing for rapid customer onboarding.
  - **Go-to-Market Strategy:**
      - **Primary ICP:** Founders of B2B SaaS companies \<$2M ARR.
      - **Secondary ICP:** Operations leads at companies $2M–$10M ARR.
      - **Disqualified:** B2C companies, static businesses, and pure PLG models without a sales component.
  - **Strategic Timing:** Catch founders early to establish good habits and avoid painful "data audits" later.

### The Goal: Content-Led Growth

  - The product is ready; the current gap is marketing and messaging to drive adoption.
  - **Overall Objective:** Exit 2025 with 500 customers.
  - **Content Objective:** Be the top-ranked answer for founder questions on LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT), which are considered more influential than Google for this tech-forward audience.
  - **Key Event:** A press launch is scheduled for Jan 21, 2025, creating urgency for content production.
