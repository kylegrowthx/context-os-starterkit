## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Scope

  - **GrowthX Focus:** SEO/GEO content to drive visibility.
      - **Method:** Analyze competitors (e.g., Wiz) and data (search volume, keyword difficulty) for quick-win opportunities.
      - **Collaboration:** GrowthX provides data-driven topics; Panther adds nuance (e.g., competitive wins) for prioritization.
  - **Panther's Use of Profound:**
      - **Value:** Excellent for content ideation, brief generation, and optimization of existing content.
      - **Limitation:** Poor for full article generation due to context limitations (e.g., 500-character prompt limit).
  - **GrowthX's Generation Advantage:**
      - **Method:** Uses agentic research and fact-checker agents to feed unlimited context into drafts, ensuring quality.
      - **Overlap:** GrowthX will provide a similar prompt/AI visibility tool for comparison with Profound.

### Strategy Sprint Roadmap

  - **Goal:** Build a "Content OS" (roadmap) and publish \~5 articles by sprint end.
  - **Phase 1: Kickoff & Deep Dive**
      - Kickoff call with core team.
      - Product deep dive with product marketing for platform nuance.
      - **Output:** Brand/audience artifacts and the Content OS.
  - **Phase 2: Calibration**
      - Publish one "calibration article" to align on quality and process before scaling.
  - **Phase 3: Operating Cadence**
      - Scale production to 2–3 articles/week, with a goal of 5/week.

### Logistics & Process

  - **Platform Access:**
      - **Google Analytics & Search Console:** Ana to provide access.
      - **Framer (CMS):** Ana to coordinate access.
  - **Publishing Workflow:**
      - **Owner:** GrowthX will stage and publish approved content.
      - **Constraint:** Framer's deployment freezes (up to 1 day) block publishing.
      - **Coordination:** GrowthX will join the Panther Slack channel tracking freezes to plan around them.
  - **Review Process:**
      - **Problem:** Over-engineering reviews is the primary bottleneck to scaling content.
      - **Solution:** Katie is the primary reviewer. She will tap technical experts (e.g., Michael) only for specific fact-checks, not full article reviews.
