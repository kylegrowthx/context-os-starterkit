## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Tracking

  - **Tracking Setup:** Looker is now tracking all "Book a Demo" button clicks.
      - **Limitation:** GA4 logs clicks from the navbar and sticky CTA as a single event, preventing source-specific attribution.
  - **Performance:** Recent content is performing strongly, with listicles and tool reviews gaining significant organic traffic.
      - **Google Features:** Articles are appearing in Google's "People also ask" and "referenced by" sections, a key step toward earning direct featured snippets.
      - **Strategy Validation:** This confirms the current content strategy is effective.

### Q1 Content Strategy: App Access

  - **Goal:** Create new content formats to support a Q1 campaign on "app access" (automating employee software requests).
      - **Rationale:** Past tests showed "app access" content drives high open rates.
  - **Proposed Asset:** A gated whitepaper with proprietary customer data.
      - **Method:** Survey Siit customers to gather unique statistics (e.g., time/money saved).
      - **Rationale:** Proprietary data is a powerful differentiator against AI-generated content and builds authority.
  - **Execution:** Matt will investigate adding whitepaper creation as a custom service.

### Website UX Revamp

  - **Context:** Siit is undergoing a full site redesign (new branding, simplified structure, rewritten core pages).
  - **Blog/Tools Page Redesign:** The pages will be revamped for a wider, less condensed layout, inspired by Healthline and Atomic Work.
      - **Healthline Inspiration:** Use hero section buckets for direct jumps to comparison, alternative, etc.
      - **Atomic Work Inspiration:** Structure content into "chapters" for better user flow and SEO.
  - **Image Workflow:** Matt will reintroduce image generation to the workflow.
      - **Rationale:** To improve visual appeal and break up text.
      - **Constraint:** Must not create production bottlenecks as it did in the past.
