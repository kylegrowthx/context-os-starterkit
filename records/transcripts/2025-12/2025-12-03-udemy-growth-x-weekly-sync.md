## Key Topics

### Blockers & Resolutions

  - **Looker Access:**
      - **Blocker:** Access is stalled because Udemy's policy prohibits signing the required Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
      - **Resolution:** Justin is working with procurement to find a workaround, citing the Statement of Work (SoW) that grants GrowthX access to "CMS and analytics tools."
  - **Image Creation:**
      - **Decision:** Udemy's production team will continue creating header images through EOY.
      - **Rationale:** The decision was made to avoid stepping on the production team's toes.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will create mockups in the background for a potential reevaluation in Q1/Q2.
  - **Localization Link Issue:**
      - **Problem:** Google search results for Udemy Business articles sometimes redirect to localized pages (e.g., German) instead of the English version.
      - **Workaround:** Jay confirmed a known fix: translate the page twice in Chrome.
      - **Pipeline Fix:** GrowthX is adding a check to its pipeline to ensure only the main `udemy.com/business` link is used, stripping out localization tags.

### Content Strategy & Pipeline

  - **AI Category Definitions:**
      - To resolve overlap, the team defined the scope for two key AI categories:
          - **AI Upskilling:** Training on specific tools and use cases.
          - **AI Literacy:** Foundational knowledge, concepts, and ethics.
      - **Rationale:** This distinction will guide content creation and keyword targeting.
  - **Pipeline Enhancements:**
      - **Tables:** Implementation begins the week of Dec 15.
      - **In-Text Images:** Requires pipeline customization; mockups are expected in the next few weeks.
  - **B2B Focus:**
      - **Goal:** Ensure content is distinct from the consumer blog.
      - **Method:** Use a B2B lens and target lower-volume B2B keywords to avoid direct competition.

### Process & Goals

  - **Content Review Feedback:**
      - **Request:** Justin will ask the review team (Jen) to provide consolidated feedback on recurring patterns.
      - **Rationale:** This allows GrowthX to refine its AI pipeline and reduce revision cycles.
  - **CMS Access for GrowthX:**
      - **Goal:** Grant GrowthX CMS access for direct posting of supporting articles.
      - **Timeline:** Q1/Q2 2026.
  - **Communication:**
      - **Request:** Use the main external Slack channel for all strategic discussions.
      - **Rationale:** Ensures context is shared with the full team, preventing knowledge silos.
