### Sales Philosophy & Experience

  - **Marketing Strategy:** Views all marketing channels (paid, organic, social) as connected; the core goal is to drive traffic and action with a consistent message.
  - **Prospecting:**
      - **Inbound-Driven Outbound:** Uses inbound wins to fuel targeted outbound campaigns to competitors.
      - **Personalization:** Prefers handwritten emails and creative tactics (e.g., drawing logos) to humanize outreach.
      - **Strategic Targeting:** Focuses on a top 50–75 account list, understanding the "good, better, best" customer profiles.

### Case Study: Coinbase Deal (Sprout Social)

  - **Problem:** Coinbase needed a scalable way to handle customer care and monitor its brand perception during rapid growth.
  - **Solution:** A two-phase deal that grew from a social care pilot to a full social listening platform.
  - **Value Prop:**
      - **Phase 1 (Social Care):** Improved customer response times and CSAT scores.
      - **Phase 2 (Social Listening):** Provided real-time sentiment analysis and crisis management, informing future content strategy.
  - **Outcome:** A 1-year deal, growing from a typical $10k–$20k deal to $90k, becoming the team's largest at the time.
  - **Key Learnings:**
      - **Success:** Persistent champion management and using a personalized demo environment to prove value.
      - **Improvement:** Engage power (VP of Marketing) and commercial discovery (legal/procurement) earlier to shorten the sales cycle.

### GrowthX Context & Needs

  - **Sales Team:** Currently a team of one (Tyler Pavlas), handling high inbound demand.
  - **Win Factors:**
      - **Expertise:** Marcel's background (ex-CMO at Scale AI) and the team's AEO/GEO knowledge.
      - **Product Superiority:** A scalable, end-to-end platform that outperforms custom GPTs and typical SEO agencies.
  - **Loss Factors:**
      - **Customer Mismatch:** Prospects wanting services beyond GrowthX's core content offering.
      - **Skepticism:** Doubts about the ROI of organic content or the technical capabilities of AI-driven creation.
  - **Key Needs:**
      - **Process Improvement:** Build a better demo experience and a more robust search opportunity analysis process.
      - **Sales Capacity:** Support two distinct sales motions:
          - **White-Glove Services:** Collaborate on larger, consultative deals.
          - **New Product Launch:** Lead sales for a new, higher-volume, lower-ACV product.
