## Summary

Yousef Hamade (GrowthX) evaluated Read.ai's platform with Alison Barker (Read.ai Customer Success) as a potential replacement for Fathom. Read.ai can provide immediate beta access to API/MCP support and intelligent sharing (shared folders), but critical gaps remain: Global Access lacks editor permissions for urgent sharing changes, and the Loom-like clip/trim feature is not yet available. Yousef requires formal pricing, firm delivery dates for roadmap features (Linear integration, Vitality integration, agentic editing), and a test script for Fathom data migration before committing to evaluation.

---

## Context

Read.ai is a video collaboration and meeting intelligence platform (read.ai). Yousef contacted them as GrowthX seeks a replacement for Fathom — a primary tool for recording and analyzing customer conversations. This was a product fit and feasibility assessment, driven by GrowthX's need to move ~1 year of archived Fathom recordings to a new platform and establish ongoing recording capabilities. Alison Barker manages customer success at Read.ai and was introduced to help with account setup and feature discovery. The meeting exposed a critical tension: Read.ai has many of the features GrowthX needs, but several are in beta or on the roadmap, and Yousef wants concrete timelines and pricing before investing time in evaluation.

---

## Relevance

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- Switching recording platforms requires migrating ~1 year of Fathom data and retraining the team on new workflows.
- Global Access with editor permissions is critical for urgent ops (sharing interviews while team members are OOO, unsharing sensitive conversations).
- Shared Folders + Smart Folders will enable automated distribution of client calls to internal stakeholders (execs, CSMs).
- Read.ai's Search Copilot and chat features align with GrowthX's content intelligence workflows.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- Linear integration (in development) would connect Read.ai recordings to issue tracking, improving ops velocity.
- Vitality integration (exploratory) would feed customer interaction data into CS tooling — strategic for Customer Success workflow efficiency.
- Clip/trim feature is a dealbreaker blocker: GrowthX will need to share short clips of customer conversations with stakeholders. Absence of this feature may force GrowthX to stay on Fathom.

**To CheckThat:**
- Read.ai's transcript accuracy and AI-powered summary generation could be valuable for AEO/content research tasks if integrated into GrowthX's analysis pipeline.

## Key Topics

### Feature Gaps & Status

  - **API MCP Support:** In beta; growthx.ai can get access and documentation.
  - **Global Access (Super User):**
      - **Current:** Viewer-only access to all workspace reports (recordings).
      - **Gap:** Lacks editor permissions to change sharing settings.
      - **Rationale:** Critical for urgent sharing changes (e.g., sharing an interview while a colleague is on PTO, unsharing sensitive data).
      - **Status:** Requires a quick fix to add editor permissions.
  - **Intelligent Sharing (Folders):**
      - **Requirement:** Auto-share calls based on rules (e.g., title, participants).
      - **Status:** "Shared Folders" feature enables this; growthx.ai can get access next week.
      - **Clarification:** Need to confirm if "Smart Folders" (auto-categorization) work with shared folders.
  - **Call Groupings & Search:**
      - **Requirement:** Search/chat across specific call groups (e.g., all calls with growthx.ai).
      - **Status:** Supported via "Search Copilot" and folders.
  - **Bi-directional Integrations:**
      - **Linear:** On roadmap; team is actively working on it.
      - **Vitality:** growthx.ai's future CS tool; Read.ai is open to exploring an integration.
  - **Call Snipping & Trimming:**
      - **Requirement:** Extract and share short clips; trim call start/end.
      - **Status:** Not currently available. A top-requested feature on the product roadmap.
  - **Table Stakes Features:**
      - **Private Notes:** Available in the desktop app and browser extension.
      - **Shareable Private Notes:** Status unknown; Alison to confirm.
      - **Summary/Action Item Editing:** Supported via manual edits and "Custom Vocabulary" (for auto-correction).
      - **Agentic Editing:** Not yet available. The product team is building "taking action" capabilities (e.g., "update summary with X").

### Data Migration

  - **Requirement:** Migrate \~1 year of recordings from Fathom.
  - **Options:**
      - **Self-Service:** Manual upload via credits (impractical for a full archive).
      - **Bulk Upload:** Read.ai engineering can perform a bulk upload from a shared drive (e.g., Dropbox, Drive).
  - **Test Plan:** growthx.ai needs a script to export a single meeting from Fathom and upload it to Read.ai to validate the process.

### Pricing & Evaluation

  - **growthx.ai's Position:** Requires a formal proposal with pricing and firm delivery dates for all features before committing to testing.
  - **Rationale:** Avoids "vaporware" and ensures a clear understanding of the product's current state and future roadmap.
