## Context

Abnormal Security is a B2B enterprise security company (cloud-native security, email security, insider threat detection) that engaged GrowthX for content marketing services. Jo Kaminska (GrowthX) and Mariana Montezzana (GrowthX) lead delivery, partnering with Callie Baron (Abnormal's head of content strategy) and Emily Burns (content reviewer). ## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Process Alignment

  - **Problem:** The previous high-volume (20+/wk) production model lacked sufficient review, creating messaging risks. A recent pause to fix this has stalled growth.
  - **Solution:** A new process balances quality and scale by segmenting content and defining clear review paths.
      - **Net-New Blogs:** GrowthX provides outlines for Emily's review before drafting.
          - **Volume:** 3 articles/wk.
          - **Support:** A product-focused content manager may assist with product-specific topics.
      - **Content Refreshes:** Emily conducts a lightweight review of existing URLs.
          - **Volume:** 2 articles/wk.
          - **Process:** GrowthX uses green highlights to show all changes.
      - **Webinar Repurposes:** Abdallah's team (Nian, Kelsey) will conduct a final check before publication.
          - **Rationale:** This is low-risk content, as it is derived directly from approved webinars.
          - **Volume:** Scalable, targeting \~10 articles/wk.

### January Production Plan & CMS Migration

  - **January Goal:** Maintain production velocity (\~15 articles/wk) to hit growth targets while calibrating the new process.
  - **CMS Migration Freeze:** A \~2-week content freeze is planned for late Jan/early Feb during the website migration.
      - **Impact:** Only imperative content for active campaigns (e.g., RSA) will be built.
      - **Workaround:** GrowthX can build non-essential content directly in the new CMS for publication post-launch.

### Project Management & Tracking

  - **Proposal:** Use Asana to centralize project tracking.
      - **Rationale:** Provides a single source of truth, avoiding status updates getting lost in Slack.
      - **Implementation:** Create a dedicated Asana board linking to Airtable records and Google Docs.

### Performance & Projections

  - **Concern:** Abdallah is concerned the new process will stall growth and impact team metrics.
  - **Context:** The previous forecast was based on 50 URLs that were recently taken down. The new forecast is for *additional* traffic from new content.
      - **Projection:** \~11k total clicks by Feb EOM (average scenario).
  - **GA4 Status:** Meaghan confirmed GA4 tags are firing correctly. Low session counts (1-2) are likely due to a low Looker Studio reporting threshold, not a tracking error.
