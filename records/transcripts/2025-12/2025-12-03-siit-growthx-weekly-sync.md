### Content Strategy: Refresh & Gap Analysis

  - **Plan:** A major content refresh will begin in Q1 2026.
      - **Rationale:** Optimize existing assets for faster results by improving content already ranking on pages 1-2.
      - **Actions:**
          - Add TLDRs and FAQs to articles.

          - Consolidate or prune underperforming articles.
  - **Gap Analysis:** A new content gap analysis is planned for Q1 2026.
      - **Rationale:** Refresh the initial analysis to account for market shifts (e.g., AI) and new competitors.

### CTA & Video Strategy

  - **Video Embed:** The YouTube demo video will be embedded directly into content pages.
      - **Placement:** Above the final section on Alternatives pages, between the "why not this tool" and CTA on Reviews pages.
      - **Action:** Carmel will provide the Vimeo embed link.
  - **CTA Link Change:** All "Free Trial" CTA buttons will link to the "Book a Demo" page.
      - **Rationale:** The demo page converts better than the slow-loading trial form, and demos are the primary business goal.
      - **Action:** Carmel will implement this change.
  - **CTA Copy Refinement:** The CTA copy will be refined to differentiate the embedded video from the live demo.
      - **Suggestion:** Use phrases like "Get a custom demo" or "Book a live demo."

### Reddit Channel Performance

  - **Decision:** Continue Reddit activity for one more quarter.

  - **Tracking Suggestion:** Ask "How did you hear about us?" at the end of successful demo calls for directional data.
