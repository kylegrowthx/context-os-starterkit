## Key Topics

### Strategic Repositioning & Content Roadmap

  - **Goal:** Shift brand perception from an SDR-only tool to a full RevOps platform.
  - **Strategy:**
      - **New Clusters:** Create dedicated content clusters for Forecasting and Conversation Intelligence to build deep topical authority.
      - **SDR Content:** Optimize existing SDR/BDR content (a key revenue driver) by adding a broader platform angle, rather than removing it entirely.
      - **Tool Comparisons:** Implement a two-stage approval process to vet tool lists with PMMs before drafting, preventing delays.
  - **Key Asset:** A commissioned IDC report on scaling AI and revenue intelligence launches Dec 8. Its data and quotes will be integrated into new and refreshed content to add authority.

### November Performance & Analytics

  - **Traffic & Engagement:**
      - **Sessions:** Dipped seasonally vs. a strong October, but remained higher than September.
      - **Engagement:** Rates were very high (60–80%), confirming content quality and success in driving users to other pages.
  - **Search Console Data:**
      - **Position 1 Queries:** Grew 2% to over 1,400.
      - **Positions 2–10 Queries:** Declined 11% to over 6,000, indicating a shift of queries into Position 1.
  - **LLM Visibility (Scrunch):**
      - **Traffic:** November was the highest-performing month for LLM-driven visits this year.
      - **Drivers:** ChatGPT remains dominant, but Gemini and Perplexity are growing sources.
      - **Sentiment:** 100% positive, up from 99% in October.
      - **Opportunity:** Increase presence for CRM-related prompts, a strategic focus for Outreach.

### Technical & Reporting Updates

  - **Looker Dashboard Issue:** A tab is broken, blocking data.
      - **Cause:** Likely related to recent GA4/Amplitude tracking changes.
      - **Impact:** Prevents accurate reporting and establishes a single source of truth.
  - **Reporting Metric Change:** The dashboard will switch from tracking "Views" to "Engaged Sessions."
      - **Rationale:** "Engaged Sessions" is a more valuable metric, measuring active interaction (10+ seconds, 2+ page views, or a click/scroll) over simple page refreshes.
  - **Webflow Migration:** The site migration from Craft to Webflow is underway, with Jasper assisting. This is the current priority, so landing page revamps are on hold.
