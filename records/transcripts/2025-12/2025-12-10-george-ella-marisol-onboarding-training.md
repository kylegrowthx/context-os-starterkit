## Key Topics

### The Problem: Growth is Constrained

  - **Onboarding Bottleneck:** The Strategy Sprints team is limited to onboarding only two clients per week.
      - **Causes:**
          - Lack of scalable documentation and training.
          - Over-reliance on key experts (Marcel, Ada, George) for all strategic work.
          - Understaffing (e.g., only one EM until last week).
  - **Performance Gap:** The sprint-to-production conversion rate dropped to \~50% in November/December, far below the 85% plan.
      - **Cause:** A recent client conversion issue was addressed, but the team still lacks systems for early risk detection.
  - **Documentation Chaos:** Essential knowledge is scattered and disorganized, creating a high-friction experience for new hires and forcing experts to answer repetitive questions.

### The Solution: A Scalable Operating System

  - **P1 Project: Productize Marcel's Strategic Discovery Process**
      - **Goal:** Enable EMs to conduct deep customer discovery and tailor strategy without direct input from Marcel or Ada.
      - **Deliverables:**
          - Video explanations from Marcel.
          - How-to guides for building the Airtable Content OS.
          - Structured kickoff questions to uncover client goals.
  - **Long-Term Vision: Company-Wide Transformation**
      - **Goal:** Build a scalable operating system to support the company's growth and new product launches.
      - **Scope:**
          - Create role-specific training modules (e.g., for EMs, MEs).
          - Systematize workflows in a PM tool with triggers and reminders.
          - Build a new, structured onboarding experience for all employees.
