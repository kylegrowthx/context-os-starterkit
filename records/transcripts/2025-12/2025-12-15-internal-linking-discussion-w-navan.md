## Overview

- **Combined Strategy:** The approach will blend growthx.ai's automated linking (via the Atlas platform) with Navan's strategic manual linking.
- **Navan's Strategic Focus:** Use Google Search Console (GSC) data to find "low-hanging fruit"—pages with high impressions but low average rankings—and link to them to improve their performance.
- **Action Plan:** Navan will provide a priority URL list with sample anchor texts for growthx.ai to integrate into the workflow. The list will be refreshed every 2–3 months.

---

## Key Topics

### growthx.ai's Current Process

  - **Automated Linking (Atlas Platform):**
      - Uses the site map and URL slugs to identify linking opportunities.
      - Anchors links to relevant keywords connected to the destination URL's slug.
  - **Manual Linking:**
      - Supplements automation based on the broader SEO strategy.
      - Follows best practices: anchor text must accurately describe the destination content.
  - **Link Density:**
      - No strict rule; avoids over-linking.
      - Typical range: 5–10 links per 1,500–2,000 word article.

### Navan's Strategic Approach

  - **Goal:** Drive performance by targeting "low-hanging fruit" pages.
  - **Method:**
    1.  Analyze GSC data for a specific keyword (e.g., "expense management").
    2.  Identify the page ranking for that keyword.
    3.  Link to the destination with the highest impressions and lowest average ranking.
  - **Rationale:** This method leverages existing keyword authority to boost pages that are already getting traffic but need a ranking push.
