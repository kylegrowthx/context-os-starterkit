## Overview

- **Holistic CX Strategy:** Cresta's platform is modular, enabling customers to start with any of three pillars—Analyze, Augment, or Automate—to solve specific, funded problems.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** AI Analyst provides natural language root-cause analysis of 100% of conversations, replacing manual sampling to identify systemic issues (e.g., a bad website flow) and inform business strategy.
- **Agent Augmentation:** Cresta automates 60–68% of Quality Management (QM) tasks, freeing supervisors to coach more effectively and enabling a 40% increase in the agent-to-supervisor ratio.
- **Seamless Automation:** AI Agent handles transactional tasks, using an Agent Operations Center to enable human supervisors to provide real-time guidance on complex issues, preserving containment and preventing escalations.

## Key Topics

### Cresta's CX AI Philosophy

  - Cresta's strategy is to match the right AI solution to each conversation type, avoiding a "automate everything" approach.
  - **1. Eliminate:** Solve root causes of unnecessary conversations (e.g., product bugs, policy issues) using insights to build an ROI case for business-wide fixes.
  - **2. Automate:** Use AI Agents for efficient, transactional tasks where speed and accuracy are the primary customer value drivers.
  - **3. Augment:** Equip human agents with AI tools for high-value or emotionally complex conversations, where human empathy is critical.
  - **4. Proactive:** Enable new, value-add customer engagements that are too expensive for humans alone.

### Pillar 1: Analyze (Cresta Insights)

  - **Topic Discovery:** Visualizes conversation topics, correlating them with KPIs like AHT and CSAT to find inefficient or low-resolution areas.
  - **AI Analyst:** A natural language query tool for deep root-cause analysis.
      - **Function:** Asks open-ended questions (e.g., "Why are conversations unresolved?") to analyze 100% of transcripts and provide a summarized report with evidence.
      - **Impact:** Replaces manual sampling (1–2% coverage) to identify systemic issues.
      - **Use Case (United Airlines):** Identified a confusing website flow for basic economy fares, which was driving millions of dollars in contact center costs and was subsequently fixed.

### Pillar 2: Augment (Performance & Coaching)

  - **Performance Insights:** Automates behavior adherence scoring for 100% of interactions against a custom scorecard.
      - **Function:** Identifies coaching opportunities by comparing individual agent performance to team averages.
      - **Impact:** Reduces QM team overhead by 50–60% and increases the agent-to-supervisor ratio by \~40%, reallocating staff to higher-value coaching.
  - **Coaching Hub:** Provides supervisors with a proactive view of coachable moments, enabling them to build and monitor targeted coaching plans.
  - **Opera (Real-time Agent Assist):** A no-code engine to deploy real-time guidance based on insights.
      - **Function:** Delivers contextual hints, guided workflows, and knowledge articles to agents.
      - **Behavior Detection:** Uses Small Language Models (SLMs) trained via natural language and user feedback to accurately detect nuanced behaviors (e.g., "overcoming an objection") in real time.

### Pillar 3: Automate (AI Agent & Seamless Handover)

  - **AI Agent Architecture:** Uses a sub-agent approach, combining specialized, task-specific agents (e.g., for lost cards, address changes) with a routing agent to handle complex, multi-intent requests.
  - **Agent Operations Center (AOC):** A human-in-the-loop management system for AI Agents.
      - **Function:** Allows AI Agents to "raise their hand" for supervisor guidance on complex or out-of-bounds issues (e.g., compliance, pricing exceptions).
      - **Impact:** Preserves containment and prevents unnecessary escalations to human queues, allowing one supervisor to manage hundreds of AI conversations simultaneously.
  - **Seamless Handover Demo:**
      - **Scenario:** A customer's chat request for a lost card was handled by the AI Agent, which also identified a fraudulent transaction and updated the customer's address.
      - **Escalation:** The customer then requested an HYSA account, triggering a seamless voice handover to a human agent.
      - **Context Transfer:** The human agent received the full conversation history and context, enabling a smooth transition and eliminating the need for the customer to repeat information.
