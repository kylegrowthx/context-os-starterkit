## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Production

  - **Strategic Keyword Focus:**
      - Prioritize high-intent keywords that drive leads over high-volume keywords with low conversion.
      - **Example:** Target a keyword with 50 visits/mo → 10 leads, not one with 1,000 visits/mo → 1 lead.
      - **Rationale:** "Card" and "banking" topics drive leads; "expense management" does not, despite strong rankings.
      - **Proposed Allocation:** 66% lower-funnel, 33% top-funnel.
  - **Production Status:**
      - 3 articles in production this week:
          - "How to create an expense report" (ready for review)
          - "American Express Platinum vs. Business Platinum" (ready for review)
          - "Best Software Tools for Automated Account Reconciliation" (in production)
      - \~30 new topics added to the calendar for review.
      - **Constraint:** Do not target "accounts receivable" (per Brex team).
  - **Review Workflow:**
      - Jon will review all drafts within hours to maintain a rapid pace.
      - **Focus:** SEO fundamentals (internal links, URL structure) and overall sanity checks.

### `pry.co` Website Project

  - **Goal:** Redesign the `pry.co` domain into a standalone content hub for finance professionals (e.g., CFOs).
  - **Rationale:** Leverage the domain's strong backlink profile and topical authority on startups.
  - **Execution:**
      - **Redesign:** Strip all product pages and old content; model the new site after `thecfoclub.com`.
      - **SEO:** Implement 301 redirects to preserve domain authority.
      - **CMS Migration:** Migrate from Prismic to Sanity.
          - **Rationale:** Prismic is difficult to use, especially for programmatic content.
          - **Scope:** Migrate only valuable articles; refresh others.
  - **GrowthX Involvement:**
      - **Initial:** Assist with the redesign.
      - **Future:** Potentially produce content for `pry.co` once the site is live.
  - **Priority:** Content production for `brex.com` remains the top priority.

### Sanity CMS Access & Improvements

  - **Access Status:** GrowthX's Sanity access is blocked.
      - **Blocker:** The Brex email and Okta verification are complete, but the user is not added to the Sanity organization.
      - **Impact:** Prevents GrowthX from scoping and recommending improvements.
  - **Required Improvements (Identified by Jon):**
      - **Slow Publishing:** Content takes hours to appear live, hindering quick sanity checks.
      - **Poor Search:** "Spend Trends" articles are not searchable from the main Sanity interface.
      - **Redirects:** The current setup is functional but complex.
