### Pricing & Engagement Model

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint**
      - **Cost:** $30,000
      - **Duration:** 8 weeks
      - **Deliverables:** A content OS (strategy, roadmap, topic clusters) and initial articles.
      - **Pace:** \>10 articles possible, contingent on a fast review process.
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
      - **Cost:** $15,000/month minimum (flexible payment terms, e.g., quarterly)
      - **Duration:** 12-month term
      - **Deliverable:** \~20 articles/month (5/week)

### GrowthX's Differentiators & Workflow

  - **Core Focus:** Scalable SEO and GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) content.
  - **Strategy:** An audience-first approach that connects every assignment to a keyword opportunity and competitor analysis.
  - **Proprietary AI Workflow:**
      - **Human Role:** Defines inputs and context via "artifacts" (e.g., ICP profiles, competitor analysis).
      - **AI Role:** Generates outlines, drafts, meta tags, and on-brand images.
      - **Editor Role:** Manually reviews and edits every draft for quality and brand voice.
  - **Scope:** GrowthX publishes to Outtake's existing blog template but does not perform custom web development.
