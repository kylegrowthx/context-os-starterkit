## Key Topics

### Q1 Priorities & Strategic Context

  - Monograph's Q1 priorities will guide content strategy:
    1.  **De-risk the team:** Provide organic "air cover" for SDR cold outreach.
    2.  **Product marketing/story:** Shift to an "all-in-one" product narrative.
    3.  **Engineers/non-architects:** Expand market reach beyond architects.
    4.  **New product launch:** Support the Leads product launch.
  - This strategy is driven by a planned website messaging overhaul.
      - **Current:** Benefit-led (e.g., "Leverage reports"), which lacks SEO value.
      - **Future:** SEO-driven, framing Monograph as an "all-in-one" platform with keyword-aligned sub-products (e.g., "architect document management software").
      - **Rationale:** Replicate the successful model from Allison's previous company, Canopy, to align organic content with high-intent keywords.

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - **Leads Launch:** The launch is now targeted for mid-February (original Q1 plan).
      - Product updates require a messaging shift to an "all-in-one" narrative.
      - Growth X will continue building authority around the "CRM" keyword, using mid-funnel content to frame Monograph's product as a viable alternative to a full CRM.
  - **Software Comparison Content:** Double down on this high-opportunity area.
      - **Focus:** Competitor comparisons, which are driving leads.
      - **Constraint:** Avoid cannibalizing existing main navigation comparison pages.
  - **Salary Content:** Pivot from civil engineering to Monograph's ICP.
      - **New Targets:** Structural engineers and MEP engineers.
      - **Guidance:** "Engineers" is sufficient for MEP; "structural engineers" must be explicit.
      - **Opportunity:** Create broader content for key roles (Principals, Owners, Operations Leaders).

### Performance Reporting & Site Maintenance

  - **Reporting Accuracy:** Growth X will filter product pages (e.g., `/projects`, `/overview`) from performance reports.
      - **Rationale:** To provide a clearer view of marketing page performance.
  - **Page Refreshes:** Monograph has a list of \~2-year-old, high-traffic pages needing simple date updates.
      - **Proposal:** Growth X to handle updates (e.g., "2023" → "2025").
      - **Constraint:** This work may reduce net-new content volume unless pricing is adjusted.
