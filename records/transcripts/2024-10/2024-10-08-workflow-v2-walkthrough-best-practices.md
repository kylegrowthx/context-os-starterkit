## Key Topics

### V2 Workflow Overview

  - New columns: company name, company about, brand guidelines (copy for each article)
  - Six-step process: keyword research, title generation, outline generation, article generation, post-processing, internal linking
  - Improved output: more internal/external links, better fact-checking, research depth

### Content Structure and Formatting

  - Avoid walls of text; use short paragraphs (3-4 lines max) and bullet points
  - Place "Benefits" sections near the end, not immediately after introduction
  - Use action-oriented H2/H3 titles (e.g., "How to Implement" vs. "Implementing")
  - Avoid question-based headings; opt for declarative statements
  - Include primary keyword in H2s where appropriate
  - Number steps or list items when applicable to improve readability

### Outline Generation Best Practices

  - Critical step for final article quality
  - Adjust word counts for sections based on importance and content needs
  - Add/remove H3s as needed; not all H2s require H3s
  - Order sections logically based on user search intent and topic importance

### Content Writing and Editing

  - Craft concise, direct introductions (about 2-3 lines)
  - Begin each section with a clear definition or main point
  - Break up long paragraphs, add bullet points, and use bold text for emphasis
  - Ensure internal link anchor text accurately reflects the linked page content
  - Writers encouraged to conduct additional research (e.g., analyzing top-ranking articles for common H2s) to enhance content, time permitting

### New Workflow Implementation

  - V2 workflows to be used starting this week
  - Writers to expect new assignments using updated grids
  - Further guidelines and knowledge base to be added to Notion
