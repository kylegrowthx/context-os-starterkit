## Key Topics

### Website Transition and SEO Impact

  - Firework transitioning from WordPress to Webflow, using Nginx reverse proxy
  - Temporary solution: redirecting firework.com to firework.com/index.php
  - SEO impact minimal, but branding affected due to visible "index.php" in URL
  - Future reversal: need to implement reverse redirect (index.php to firework.com)
  - Google will update rankings within \~1 month after redirect changes
  - Critical to manage redirects carefully to avoid issues

### WordPress Staging Environment Issues

  - Navigation between lists of pages not working properly on staging
  - Cache clearing and permalink reset attempts haven't resolved the problem
  - WP Engine's cache described as "relentless"
  - Concern about allowing authors to write directly in production environment

### Content Publishing Strategy

  - 10 articles ready for publishing before Monday
  - Decision to put new content publishing on hold for a couple of days
  - Prateek to check with Carolyn before publishing anything to staging
  - Aim to avoid causing additional problems during the transition period
