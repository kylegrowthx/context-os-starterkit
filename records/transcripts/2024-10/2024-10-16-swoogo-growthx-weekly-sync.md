## Key Topics

### Blog Performance Overview

  - 15 new articles published last week, total of 82 articles published by GrowthX
  - Last 28 days: 1,700 clicks (60% increase), 266k impressions (100% increase)
  - 200 total blog posts on Swoogo website:
      - 118 existing articles: 177 clicks, 22k impressions (weekly)
      - 67 new articles: 238 clicks, 48k impressions (weekly)
      - 25 content refreshes: 56 clicks, 11k impressions (weekly)

### Traffic and Engagement Trends

  - Traffic doubled from 1,400 to \~2,800 visits in September
  - Engagement rate remained relatively flat despite increased content volume
  - Maintaining flat engagement with increased traffic viewed positively by internal SEO expert (Hamilton)

### Keyword Strategy and Rankings

  - Current focus on long-tail keywords to build authority and gain "quick wins"
  - Plan to transition to short-tail keywords (e.g., "event registration", "event management") in coming months
  - Will monitor short-tail keyword performance for 1-2 months before considering backlink strategies
  - Team to set up position tracking for key terms in SEMrush for weekly reporting

### Content Performance

  - Significant growth in impressions and clicks over past 7 weeks
  - Projecting to achieve same 7-week results in just 2 weeks moving forward
  - Content refreshes showing positive results: from 1-2 clicks/day to 15 clicks/day

### Lead Generation Strategy

  - Discussing implementation of lead magnets to capture emails from blog traffic
  - Considering offering eBooks or PDFs related to popular content topics (e.g., school/party planning)
  - Goal: nurture leads through email sequences before pushing for demo bookings
  - Plan to A/B test different lead magnet types, placements, and messaging

### Team Updates

  - New Director of Branding & Content starting October 21st
  - Large customer event (On Con) occurring first week of November
