### Target Audience and Pain Points

  - Security leaders scrambling to secure board-level AI initiatives

### Content Strategy

  - Establish PromptFoo as an authoritative voice in AI security
  - Develop repeatable process for high-quality content production

  - Opportunity to build authority beyond direct product offerings due to nascent market

### Content Development Approach

  - Create topic clusters and themes aligned with product features and industry pain points
  - Incorporate external research, PromptFoo documentation, and emerging standards
