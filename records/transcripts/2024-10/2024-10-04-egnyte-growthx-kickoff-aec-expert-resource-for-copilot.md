## Key Topics

### Nadia's Background and Expertise

  - 20+ years in construction, progressing from engineering to project management and sales
  - Extensive experience with project documents, specifications, bids, and proposals
  - Currently consulting part-time while focusing on family

### Egnyte Product Introduction and Use Case Mapping

  - Plan to conduct a comprehensive product demo for Nadia
  - Nadia to provide an A-to-Z overview of project lifecycle from conception to completion
  - Goal: Align Egnyte's capabilities with real-world AEC workflows and pain points
  - Focus on connecting features to daily tasks of job site workers and project managers

### Document Management and AI Integration

  - Egnyte stores all project documents, including financial and accounting information
  - Opportunity to use AI for sorting, categorizing, and processing billing/accounting data
  - Nadia to upload sample project files to a demo Egnyte account for hands-on exploration
  - Specific focus on RFP to proposal conversion workflow

### Change Management and Document Comparison

  - Identified as a major pain point in construction projects
  - Need for AI-powered comparison between old and new document sets (drawings, specs)
  - Goal: Automatically highlight and outline all changes to save time and reduce errors

### Building Codes and Regulations Integration

  - Explore ways to incorporate building codes and safety regulations into Egnyte
  - Two potential approaches:
    1.  Egnyte purchases and maintains a library of codes (resource-intensive)
    2.  Allow customers to upload their own purchased codes for AI-powered interpretation
  - Need to determine optimal workflow for code reference and interpretation

### Existing Tools and Beta Programs

  - Smart Specs tool: Nadia to review and provide feedback
  - Contracts Analyzer beta program: Potential to refocus on AEC-specific needs
