## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - Moving away from shallow SEO-focused articles to deep, expert-driven content
  - Utilizing Tribe AI's internal expertise, presentations, and workshop materials as primary content sources
  - Aiming to scale Jackie's in-person impact through high-quality, insightful content
  - Focus on creating content that enterprise customers find valuable in their buying journey

### Content Creation Process

  - Start with ingesting existing materials (presentations, transcripts, internal docs) into a knowledge base
  - Use AI tools to process and structure information from various sources
  - Create a custom vector database for efficient information retrieval and cross-referencing
  - Implement a workflow to decompose presentations into individual articles, then recompose into larger pieces

### Content Formats and Distribution

  - Create concise, insight-packed articles similar to "cliff notes" for easy consumption
  - Develop a regular cadence of social media posts, particularly on LinkedIn
  - Aggregate content into newsletters for broader distribution
  - Explore the possibility of starting a podcast to further leverage expert knowledge

### Automation and Scaling

  - Utilize AI tools for content generation, structuring, and improvement
  - Implement systems to build and maintain knowledge bases from curated expert sources
  - Use tools like Notion's transcription and AI-powered organization features for efficient content creation

### Measuring Success

  - Focus on generating high-quality leads and opportunities rather than just SEO metrics
  - Aim for content that assists in the buying journey and showcases Tribe AI's expertise
  - Track the impact of content on generating interest and demand, especially through social sharing and direct referrals
