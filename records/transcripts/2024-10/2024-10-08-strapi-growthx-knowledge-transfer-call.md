## Overview

- GrowthX has implemented significant upgrades to their V2 content generation workflow, improving quality and SEO optimization
- New workflows include enhanced keyword research, search intent mapping, and more sophisticated outline generation
- Strapi team expressed interest in having more control over content fine-tuning and source selection

---

## Key Topics

### V2 Workflow Improvements

  - More skimmable content with better formatting (bullet points, numbered lists, H3s)
  - Enhanced search intent analysis using top-ranking Google pages
  - Improved outline generation with better section structuring
  - Increased internal and external linking for SEO benefits
  - Manual human review at critical stages (title selection, outline approval, final editing)

### SEO Optimization

  - New workflow automatically optimizes meta keywords, descriptions, and titles
  - Incorporates Strapi's SEO plugin requirements
  - Utilizes perplexity API for citations and fact-checking
  - Plans for an optional fact-checking workflow at the end of the content generation process

### Content Customization and Sources

  - Working on ability to select different sources for keyword research (e.g., Google, Strapi dev docs)
  - Regularly updating knowledge base with Strapi's sitemap for accurate internal linking
  - Exploring options for manual assignment of specific resources for particular articles

### Quality Control and AI Detection

  - Efforts to "humanize" content by removing obvious AI-generated phrases
  - Focus on balanced presentation of topics (e.g., equal treatment of decoupled vs. headless CMS)
  - Extensive human review process to ensure quality and natural language

### Future Plans

  - Developing fact-checking and custom source selection workflows
  - Shifting strategy towards more top-of-funnel content and integration pages
  - Aim to increase content output and publishing frequency once quality concerns are addressed
