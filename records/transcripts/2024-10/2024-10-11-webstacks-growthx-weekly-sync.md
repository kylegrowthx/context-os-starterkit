## Key Topics

### Content Performance and Strategy

  - 2.5% of overall traffic from blog articles within one month of indexing
  - 78 articles indexed; 10-15 performing well (mix of design and development topics)
  - Contentful Builder blog post generated a lead; focus on comparison articles
  - Upcoming comparisons: Sanity vs. Webflow, DataCMS vs. WordPress, HubSpot vs. Builder.io
  - Implementing fact-checking workflow for middle/bottom funnel content
  - New set of refreshes launched (5 more articles)

### Workflow Improvements

  - V2 workflow includes:
      - Internal linking (sourced from sitemap XML)
      - External linking to credible, non-competitive sources
      - Deeper keyword research and content outline development
      - Manual input for word count and section structure
      - Post-processing (details to be provided by CTO Daniel)
  - Customization for outlines, including manual review and addition of unique sections
  - Flexibility for combined human and AI input in content generation

### Technical Issues

  - Hyperlink anchor text visibility issue on the frontend (known issue, being addressed)

### Google Discover Performance

  - Spike in visibility for "Modern Website Design" article on September 19-20
  - Google Discover explained:
      - Appears in Google app news feed
      - Content recommendation based on user engagement and search history
      - Requires high-quality images, non-clickbait titles, and evergreen/trending topics
      - Performance tied to user engagement, not directly related to search engine rankings
