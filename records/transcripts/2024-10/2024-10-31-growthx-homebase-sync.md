## Key Topics

### Content Performance Update

  - 112 new blog posts published, 148 total pieces worked on
  - Generated 11% of total blog traffic Oct 7-29 (51K clicks, 6M impressions)
  - 8,600 keywords ranking per SEMrush
  - Team has 6,000 additional content ideas in pipeline

### Content Opportunity Analysis

  - Analyzed 34,000 keywords from competitors
  - Created 3,100 potential content assignments
  - Total potential search volume of 29M monthly (US desktop)
  - Assignments scored on relevance, difficulty, and volume
  - Opportunity to create high-quality briefs and assignments at scale

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Moving from high volume to high-value, conversion-focused content
  - Maintain some broader "pink cloud" topics (5-10%) for overall site authority
  - Focus on specific, high-intent topics related to Homebase offerings
  - Balance editorial quality improvement with programmatic content opportunities

### Engagement Optimization

  - Explore adding lead capture forms/widgets to blog posts (2-3 week project)
  - Develop nurture programs for captured leads
  - Consider broader content topics once core funnel is optimized
