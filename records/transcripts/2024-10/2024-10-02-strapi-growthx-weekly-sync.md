## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Adjustment

  - Shift focus from new content to refreshing existing articles (10/week)
  - Prioritize less technical articles for V5 updates
  - Reduce new content production to improve quality
  - Explore creating content for integration pages

### Quality Improvement Initiatives

  - New AI workflows being implemented next week
      - Incorporates external sources and competitor content
      - Includes obligatory internal and external links for SEO benefits
  - CMS workflow improvements
      - New tags: "Ready to Review", "Content Review", "Metadata Review"
      - Streamlined process for feedback and revisions within Strapi CMS

### Performance Metrics

  - 104,000 impressions (up from 2,600)
  - 44% increase in clicks
  - 19-20 articles published and indexed by Google
  - 11 drafts ready, 9 more to be uploaded this week

### SEO Optimization

  - Need to optimize meta tags, descriptions, and titles
  - Character limits: 160 for meta descriptions, 60-65 for meta titles
  - Focus on cross-linking and SEO optimization for existing content

### AI Content Generation Improvements

  - Current system uses Perplexity AI to scan SERPs for relevant, up-to-date information
  - Exploring integration with Strapi's Capa AI models for more accurate content generation
  - Aiming to increase AI-generated content quality from 50% to 70-90% in future versions

### Upcoming Events

  - Knowledge transfer calls scheduled for October 8th and 22nd
  - Separate call scheduled for webinar planning
