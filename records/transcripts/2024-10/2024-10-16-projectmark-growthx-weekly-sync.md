## Key Topics

### Content Review Process

  - Tom acknowledges being a bottleneck in content review due to lack of marketing team
  - Agreement to publish content at 70-80% quality to accelerate indexing and data collection
  - Strategy to refine high-performing articles post-publication based on traffic metrics

### SEO Performance

  - 22 published articles have generated 1.3K impressions and 10 total clicks
  - Business development-related articles performing relatively better
  - Existing AEC (Architecture, Engineering, Construction) company articles showing good performance

### Content Strategy Adjustment

  - Shift focus from exclusively bottom-of-funnel to include more top-of-funnel content
  - Goal: Build domain authority and increase overall traffic to boost rankings
  - Utilize high-performing BoFu content as lead magnets for ToFu visitors

### Blog Optimization for Conversion

  - Implement lead capture elements (email forms, pop-ups) throughout blog posts
  - Add CTAs, case studies, and product trial offers to increase conversion opportunities
  - Improve interlinking between articles to guide visitor journey

### Content Relevance and Accuracy

  - Ensure CRM-related topics accurately reflect Projectmark's capabilities in construction
  - Adjust article titles and content to avoid misrepresenting CRM functionality in construction
  - Convert some CRM-specific topics to more general construction industry topics where appropriate
