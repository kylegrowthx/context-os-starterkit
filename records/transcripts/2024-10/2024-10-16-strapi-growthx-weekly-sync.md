## Key Topics

### Integration Page Updates

  - 10 integration pages refreshed, following Strapi's suggested outline
  - Focus on adding broader, top-of-funnel content rather than technical details
  - Andrej added missing SEO fields (meta descriptions, canonical URLs) to improve rankings
  - Team to review updated pages for accuracy and relevance

### Content Strategy for WordPress Drama

  - Paul suggested creating content comparing WordPress and Strapi
  - Victor cautioned against appearing opportunistic; focus on highlighting Strapi's strengths without directly addressing the drama
  - Team agreed to accelerate related topics while maintaining a neutral stance

### Content Refresh Process

  - Concerns raised about QA for refreshed content and tracking changes
  - Current Strapi v4 lacks draft/publish versioning (coming in v5)
  - Exploring options for backing up old versions and analyzing refresh impact
  - Need to ensure refreshed v4 content points to v5 documentation or tutorials

### Draft Articles and New Topics

  - Theodore reviewed draft on Strapi and Angular, suggested title change
  - Waiting for new topics to be added to Strapi board
  - Andrej to re-run drafts through V2 workflow for review

### Performance Reporting

  - 2.7% overall CTR reported, considered good performance
  - Jimmy suggested benchmarking against in-house content after one month of indexing
  - Golzar requested additional metrics like bounce rate and user journey data
  - Team to explore tagging system to differentiate GrowthX-written articles

### Click-Through Rate Analysis

  - Marcel noted 2.7-2.9% CTR is significantly higher than the 1.7% blog average (excluding branded searches)
  - Suggested quick wins by optimizing meta descriptions and titles for underperforming pages
