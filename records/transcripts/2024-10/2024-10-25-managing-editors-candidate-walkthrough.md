## Overview

- The team is balancing between finding experienced candidates now and developing a pipeline for future talent

## Key Topics

### Candidate Screening Process

  - Implemented AI-powered initial screening for applications, categorizing as "Yes," "Maybe," or "No"
  - AI screening looks for recognized companies, practical experience, SEO experience, and writing quality
  - Manual review still necessary, especially for borderline cases
  - $500 spent on job postings across multiple boards to increase applicant pool
  - Well-Found noted as a potentially good source for candidates

### Evaluation Criteria

  - U.S. candidates held to highest standards due to salary expectations (e.g., $60k/year insufficient for NYC)
  - Slightly lower bar for Canadian applicants
  - Emphasis on unique, engaging, and concise application responses
  - Technical background (e.g., ML observability, AI) viewed favorably for potential technical editor roles
  - Writing samples and past work (e.g., fiction writing) considered in evaluation

### Outbound Recruiting

  - Marcel conducted targeted outreach to potential candidates
  - Shared 5 LinkedIn candidates in leadership channel for review

### Application Response Analysis

  - Standout responses:
      - Use of AI (e.g., Nick Yaml bot) with transparency
      - Concise, to-the-point answers
      - Engaging, "cliffhanger" style writing
      - Unique openings that grab attention
  - Red flags:
      - Generic or AI-generated responses without originality
      - Lack of relevant experience combined with bland answers
      - Potential language barriers (e.g., heavy accents) considered for certain roles

### Future Hiring Strategy

  - Current focus on finding experienced candidates (3-4 years in)
  - Long-term goal to develop a pipeline for identifying talent earlier in their careers
  - Considering adjusting expectations (e.g., 50,000 words/month) in job postings
  - Potential to become known as a "sales university" equivalent for content creation

---

## Action Items

- Continue reviewing applications using both AI screening and manual assessment
- Refine AI screening prompt to better capture uniqueness and originality in responses
- Follow up with promising candidates, particularly those who submitted video responses
- Consider expanding the talent pool by looking into technical editor roles for candidates with strong domain expertise
- Review and potentially adjust job posting details (e.g., word count expectations) for future listings
