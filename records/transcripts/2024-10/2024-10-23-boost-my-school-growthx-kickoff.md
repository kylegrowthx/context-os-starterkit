## Key Topics

### Company & Product Overview

  - Boost My School helps K-12 schools with fundraising, currently at seed stage
  - Primary ICP: private schools, with potential to expand to lower ACV public schools
  - Key features: annual fund management, alumni relations, events/auctions

### Growth Goals & Metrics

  - Grow from 200 to 1000 paying schools
  - Focus on driving SQLs (sales qualified leads) through inbound website traffic
  - Track conversion funnel: hand-raisers \> SALs (scheduled appointments) \> SQLs
  - Aim for low-touch growth to demonstrate scalability for investors

### Target Audience & Buying Process

  - Primary: Development/Advancement offices in K-12 schools
  - Key roles: Director of Development, Annual Fund Managers, Alumni Relations Directors
  - Decision-makers: Director of Development, Principal (smaller schools)
  - Secondary audiences: volunteers, board members

### Content Strategy

  - Leverage thought leadership approach that has worked well so far
  - Create content addressing specific jobs-to-be-done (e.g. live vs. silent auctions)
  - Utilize webinar Q\&As and transcripts as content source material
  - Balance between high-volume "awareness" content and targeted mid-funnel pieces

### GrowthX Capabilities Demo

  - Showcased competitive analysis across 62,000+ keywords
  - Demonstrated AI-powered content ideation and relevance scoring
  - Outlined multi-pronged approach: competitive gaps, persona-based, product-driven

### Technical Considerations

  - Transitioning from end-based blog to Webflow
  - Need to optimize site structure, analytics setup
