## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Avoid expressing uncertainty about competitors during presentations
  - Research topic clusters thoroughly to benchmark against content competitors
  - Aim to become an expert in the client's domain through the research process
  - Focus on quality over quantity, especially in early stages of technical engagements

### Research and Ideation Process

  - Utilize tools like Perplexity.com and ChatGPT for deep learning and ideation
  - Create comprehensive knowledge graphs around client's ecosystem
  - Generate topic clusters and potential article types using AI-assisted brainstorming
  - Explore persona-based approaches for certain clients (e.g., small business owners)

### Keyword Research and Content Backlog Creation

  - Use SEMrush for competitor keyword gap analysis
  - Import keyword data into Airtable and create URL clusters
  - Apply relevance scoring to filter and prioritize content ideas
  - Present large content backlogs (e.g., 2,500+ potential assignments) to impress clients

### Technical Content Approaches

  - Define terminology and create "What is" articles for complex topics
  - Develop beginner's guides and practical guides for technical concepts
  - Create listicles for courses, research papers, people to follow, and conferences
  - Focus on highly relevant, specific topics like LLM red teaming, prompt injection, etc.
