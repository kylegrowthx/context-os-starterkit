  - New features include:
      - Updated navigation bar
      - Categorized content
      - Sidebar CTAs
      - Author pages
  - Infrastructure team working on final implementation details

### Conversion Optimization

  - Focus shifting to improving conversion rates from blog traffic
  - Implementing intent signals:
      - Track users who read blog posts, submit email, and view pricing

### Keyword Strategy

  - Prioritizing improvement for key terms like "shoppable video" and "video commerce"
