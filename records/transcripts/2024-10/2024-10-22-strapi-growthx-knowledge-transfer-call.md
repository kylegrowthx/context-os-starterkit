### GrowthX's AI Content Creation Process

  - Uses multiple AI workflows for keyword research, title/outline generation, and full article writing
  - Incorporates Strapi-specific knowledge bases (site map, docs) for contextual relevance
  - Iterative AI self-checking and human editing to ensure quality
  - Aims for 70-80% AI-generated with 20-30% human refinement

  - Utilizes various GPT models with different temperature settings for different tasks
  - Employs "COSCAR" prompting framework: Context, Objective, Style, Tone, Audience, Response format
  - Uses XML/markdown formatting to structure prompts and specify variables

  - Scrapes top-ranking articles for competitive analysis and context
  - Uses Perplexity API and Google search for relevant citations and external links

### SEO Strategy and Google Updates

  - Balances AI-generated content with human oversight to avoid penalties
  - Monitors impact of Google's AI overviews on click-through rates
  - Emphasizes brand-driven searches and high-quality content to maintain traffic

### Ethical Considerations in AI Content

  - Discusses responsible use of AI vs. low-effort content flooding
  - Highlights importance of adding genuine value beyond basic AI outputs
  - Notes challenges in balancing efficiency with maintaining content quality
