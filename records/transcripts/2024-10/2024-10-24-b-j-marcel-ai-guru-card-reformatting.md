## Key Topics

### Current State of Guru Knowledge Base

  - Several hundred cards in support collection
  - Written by dozens of people over 5 years
  - Inconsistent formats, lengths, and styles
  - Need for continuity and standardization

### Proposed Solution Framework

  - Define 3 card types: Inform, Troubleshoot, Procedure
  - Create templates for each card type with specific sections
  - Use AI to fit existing content into new template structure
  - Key constraint: Preserve original content without rewriting or altering terminology

### AI Workflow Setup (Air Ops platform)

  - Imported Guru card data (CSV export with HTML content)
  - Configured comp config for easy variable management
  - Defined initial problem statement and guidelines
  - Created basic template structure for troubleshooting cards (6 sections)
  - Implemented content preservation rules (e.g., "Keep the content of substance the same. Don't change any terminology.")

### Initial Test Results

  - Demonstrated reorganization of content into defined sections
  - Showed potential for improvement in readability and structure
  - Highlighted need for further refinement and testing
