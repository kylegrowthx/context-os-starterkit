## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Overview

  - GrowthX is mapping out testable content ideas, focusing on what works with Google
  - Initial content may overlap to test effectiveness and gather data
  - Strategy aims to build topical authority in construction CRM
  - Content will be refined and scaled based on performance metrics

### Topic Clusters and ICPs

  - Developed topic clusters based on competitor analysis and ProjectMark's current strategy
  - Key ICPs: General contractors, specialty contractors (avoid architects/engineers)
  - Focus on roles close to winning work: pre-construction director, business development director, marketing director, senior project managers
  - Avoid content for field staff, junior roles, and engineers

### Content Templates and Keywords

  - Three main templates proposed:
    1.  "Benefits of using construction CRM for \[ICP\]"
    2.  "Benefits of construction CRM for \[role\]"
    3.  "CRM vs alternatives for construction projects"
  - Emphasis on positioning CRM for winning projects, not construction management
  - Keywords focus on construction CRM, business development, and project acquisition

### SEO and Topical Authority

  - Aiming to gain "striking distance" for core keywords and ICPs
  - Some content may target adjacent roles/industries for brand awareness and Google's semantic understanding
  - Plan to break down construction CRM functionalities and combine with ICPs in future content

### Content Publishing Process

  - GrowthX will publish directly to ProjectMark's Webflow site
  - ProjectMark to provide CMS access to <team@cortexlabs.ai>
  - Juliano from ProjectMark will guide GrowthX on the exact publishing process
