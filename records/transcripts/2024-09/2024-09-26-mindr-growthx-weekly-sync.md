## Key Topics

### Content Management System and Workflow

  - Airtable set up as central content OS for tracking, assignments, and approvals
  - Includes sections for personas, topic clusters, product categories, and competitors
  - Process for feedback: comments in Airtable, revisions based on severity of changes
  - WordPress CMS access provided, but site not live yet for publishing

### Content Strategy and Production

  - 10 new articles/week: 5 programmatic (state-level) + 5 generic DUI topics
  - State-based content temporarily paused due to lack of rules engine
  - Focus shifted to keyword-based blog content and content refreshes
  - Three-pronged keyword strategy: land and expand, ad keyword long-tail, competitor gap analysis

### Legal Review and Compliance

  - Need to develop streamlined process with legal team for content approval
  - Proposal to create a checklist of review flags to expedite the process

### Graphics and Visual Content

  - Current process: submit ticket to Stephanie for graphics
  - Considering creation of pre-approved graphics library for efficiency
  - Team to handle image sourcing; no need for external support on this

### Content Refresh Strategy

  - Implementing 5 content refreshes per week starting week 2-3
  - Using SEO testing tool to identify and prioritize content for refresh
  - Focus on articles with biggest drop in clicks over last 6 months

### Multi-Brand Content Strategy

  - Plans to create separate content for other brands (Intoxalock, Breathe Easy)
  - Proposal to alternate focus between brands on a bi-weekly basis
  - Separate Airtables to be set up for each domain
