## Overview

- Becoming "AI native" requires dedicated time to experiment and learn how to effectively delegate tasks to AI
- AI workflows can dramatically accelerate work by automating repetitive tasks while still leveraging human expertise
- Start small by decomposing jobs into steps, identifying where AI can assist, and iterating to improve workflows
- The workshop will provide hands-on training to build custom AI workflows without requiring coding skills

---

## Key Topics

### Marcel's AI Journey

  - Started exploring AI tools like AirHog to build workflows and save time
  - Created an AI-generated apps directory that drove significant traffic for his company
  - Began offering workshops to teach others how to build AI workflows
  - Founded GrowthX to help companies implement AI-assisted productivity techniques

### AI Workflow Examples

  - Content generation: Analyzing competitors, generating outlines, writing articles
  - Sales outreach: Scraping prospect data, analyzing psychographics, drafting personalized emails
  - Customer success: Analyzing call transcripts, scoring account health, enriching CRM data
  - Marketing: Scoring keyword relevance, analyzing user intent

### Approach to Building AI Workflows

  - Decompose jobs into steps (inputs, processors, variables, planners, doers, critics, adapters)
  - Provide context and clear instructions to the AI
  - Identify where human judgment/expertise is critical
  - Start small and iterate to improve results
  - Focus on applying AI to energizing high-impact tasks

### Workshop Preview

  - Will cover how to build workflows for various business functions
  - Hands-on training, no coding skills required
  - 6 sessions planned over next few months
  - First session on October 7th (in-person in NYC) and October 11th (virtual)
