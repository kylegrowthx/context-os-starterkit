  - Aim to drive sales and implementations while diagnosing SEO issues

### Future Website Migration

  - Firework planning migration to Webflow in medium term
  - GrowthX to provide comprehensive SEO checklist for migration
  - Key considerations: maintaining URL structures and internal links
