## Key Topics

### Content Publishing and Updates

  - 13 articles currently in drafts, with new batch ready for this week
  - Revalidation process needed after updating articles in CMS to reflect changes on frontend
  - Discussing best practices for publishing dates to avoid SEO penalties
  - Exploring addition of "original published" and "last updated" dates on articles

### Content Strategy and Quality

  - Focusing on less technical, top-of-funnel content for refreshes
  - Implementing strategy to update intros/outros without touching technical core of articles
  - Addressing concerns about repetitive patterns in article structures
  - Enhancing knowledge base with specific sources (e.g., YouTube transcripts, blog posts) for more targeted content creation

### SEO and Performance Metrics

  - Metrics for published articles now available in Airtable for easy access
  - Discussing implementation of RB2B JavaScript for better visitor identification and ICP scoring
  - Planning to run content cannibalization reports on high-converting keywords

### Workflow Improvements

  - Working on transferring some workflows from GrowthX to Strapi's AirOps account for increased autonomy

### Traffic Analysis and Lead Generation

  - Introduced Clay for Contacts tool to analyze quality of ICPs visiting blog articles
  - Discussed potential for advanced sequencing and targeted outreach based on visitor data
  - Considering integration with existing RevOps tools (Common Room or Rio) for account scoring and enrichment
