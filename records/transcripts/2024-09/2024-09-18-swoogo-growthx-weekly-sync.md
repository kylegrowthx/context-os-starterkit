## Key Topics

### Content Performance Update

  - New articles (40 published) generated 106 clicks, 15k impressions (17% of blog clicks, 20% of impressions)
  - Content refreshes (10 articles) showed 3x increase in clicks, 6x increase in impressions
  - Overall impact: new + refreshed content driving 20% of clicks, 27% of impressions for entire blog

### Content Cleanup Strategy

  - Identifying duplicate/similar articles for redirection (e.g., virtual event topics from 2021 and 2022)
  - Plan to review 5-10 articles weekly, optimizing \~150 total articles
  - Prateek to send list of articles for redirection to Will for implementation

### Lead Tracking and Reporting

  - Discussed journey mapping approach in HubSpot
  - Challenge: distinguishing new content performance from overall blog performance
  - Suggestion: Add unique identifier to new blog URLs for better tracking
  - Limited data available due to recent implementation (started Aug 26)

### B2B Visitor Identification

  - Discussed implementing tools like Koala or RB2B (estimated $200-500/month)
  - Benefits: Identify high-value visitors, enable targeted follow-ups
  - Stacey considering for 2025 budget, but interested in piloting free version

### SEO Impact and Expectations

  - Jimmy highlighted importance of increased branded search queries as content efforts mature
  - Emphasized patience: full impact on lead generation takes 3-6 months
  - Swoogo performing well compared to other GrowthX clients in early stages
