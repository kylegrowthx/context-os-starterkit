  - Approximately 40 visitors to the blog

  - Experienced team members (Jimmy, Pratik) providing valuable SEO insights
  - Educating Firework on the importance of Google Search Console metrics

### Areas for Improvement

  - Need for deeper analysis of engagement metrics (bounce rate, session time)
  - Lack of access to Google Analytics data by GrowthX team
  - Potential for implementing AI-driven content optimization techniques

### Case Study: Deep Brand Glossary

  - Demonstrated slow initial growth followed by significant improvement
  - First click occurred \~2 months after first publication
  - Gradual increase in impressions and clicks over time

### Content Optimization Strategies

  - Rewriting "zombie" blog posts with AI showed 15x growth in impressions
  - AI-adjusted content (45-80% changes) performed better than purely human-written content
  - Hypothesis: Google may have stricter restrictions on newly AI-written content vs. optimized existing content

### Technical SEO Considerations

  - Google's indexing process and limitations in accessing site engagement data
  - Importance of technical factors (mobile responsiveness, site speed, authorship) for indexing
