## Key Topics

### Galileo's AI Content Creation Approach

  - Uses custom AI workflows built with tools like AirOps for content generation
  - Combines AI-generated drafts with human editing and quality control
  - Focuses on "planting seeds" quickly, then iterating and optimizing based on performance
  - Can handle technical content but requires more collaboration for highly specialized topics

### Content Optimization and SEO Strategies

  - Utilizes site maps for intelligent internal linking and content clustering
  - Implements custom scoring frameworks to prioritize content updates
  - Emphasizes rapid publishing and iteration over perfecting content before release
  - Incorporates user intent analysis and adaptive workflows for relevance and conversion

### Galileo's Service Offerings

  - Full-service content creation, from ideation to publishing
  - Custom workflow development for specific use cases (e.g., case study generation from sales calls)
  - Gradual transition from full-service to advisory role as client teams build capacity
  - Handling of technical tasks like CMS staging to free up client resources

### Potential Partnership Structure

  - GrowthX could start with SEO-oriented content supplementation
  - Potential to expand into thought leadership and product marketing content over time
  - Galileo offers transparency and knowledge transfer to help clients build internal capabilities

### AI Integration Beyond Content

  - Galileo has developed workflows for sales enablement, customer onboarding analysis, and more
  - Emphasis on using AI to augment and retool knowledge work across various business functions
