## Overview

- Primary goal: Rapidly scale relevant content for DUI.org to capture top-of-funnel traffic and nurture leads
- Target: 24,000 unique monthly visitors to DUI.org by end of Q2 2024 (currently \~500 organic sessions/month)
- Content focus: State-specific DUI information, aiming for high accuracy and authority
- Challenges: Legal review process, sourcing accurate state-specific details, competing with attorney websites

---

## Key Topics

### Project Objectives

  - Create authoritative DUI-related content to capture organic traffic
  - Position DUI.org as a trusted resource for DUI offenders and their families
  - Drive more leads and installations for Intoxalock's products (IIDs, insurance)
  - Improve rankings for DUI-related terms (currently strong only for product-specific keywords)

### Target Audience

  - Primary: First-time DUI offenders seeking information post-arrest
  - Secondary: Family members researching on behalf of offenders
  - Characteristics: Likely lower income, younger, without attorney representation
  - Emotional state: Confused, overwhelmed, seeking guidance on next steps

### Content Strategy

  - Focus on state-specific DUI information and processes
  - Create personalized user experiences based on individual situations
  - Develop content addressing common questions and concerns of DUI offenders
  - Aim to outrank attorney websites by providing comprehensive, user-friendly information

### Production Process

  - Utilize AirOps platform for content ideation, creation, and management
  - Implement legal review process within the workflow (likely via Google Docs initially)
  - Publish approved content to WordPress-based DUI.org (new site launching later in the year)
  - Leverage AI for content generation, with human editing and quality control

### Challenges and Bottlenecks

  - Legal review process for ensuring accuracy and compliance
  - Sourcing and verifying state-specific DUI information
  - Competing with well-established attorney websites in search results
  - Updating outdated content and tracking down internal product information

### Integration and Tools

  - AirOps platform for content management and workflow
  - WordPress CMS for DUI.org
  - Potential use of Reddit Pro for audience insights and topic ideation
  - Custom scoring system for keyword relevance and intent analysis
