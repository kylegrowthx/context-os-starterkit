## Key Topics

### Holiday Content Strategy

  - Create content around holiday shopping trends, strategies, checklists, and stats
  - Replicate content structure across various holidays (e.g., Black Friday, Cyber Monday, Christmas)
  - Focus on B2B angle: e-commerce strategies for businesses, not B2C shopping tips
  - Include industry-specific variations (e.g., fashion, electronics, beauty)

### Content Optimization

  - Incorporate Firework's product features (e.g., video shopping, one-to-one shopping) into holiday-focused content
  - List complementary tools (e.g., email marketing) alongside Firework's offerings
  - Restructure blog with hub-and-spoke model, categorizing content by topics (e.g., e-commerce, brand, shoppable video)

### AI-Powered Content Enhancement

  - Experiment with Google's Notebook LLM to generate audio summaries of blog posts
  - Consider converting audio summaries to YouTube videos for embedding
  - Explore multi-channel content distribution (blog, podcast, video, slides)

### SEO Considerations

  - Mix up content structure to avoid AI-generated content fingerprinting
  - Include infographics and varied media to improve perceived content quality
  - Note: Time on page is not a direct ranking factor, but click-through behavior on search results pages is important
