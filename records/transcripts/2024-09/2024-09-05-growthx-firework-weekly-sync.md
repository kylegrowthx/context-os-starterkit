## Key Topics

### Page Structure Optimization

  - Current issue: H1 buried, multiple unnecessary H2s at top
  - Ideal structure: H1 at top, followed by relevant H2s-H4s
  - Action: Jorge (web admin) to be involved in implementing fixes
  - Potential impact: May resolve mobile indexing issues and improve overall site performance

### Content Performance Analysis

  - GrowthX content: 24 clicks, 1100 impressions, 2.1% CTR (vs 0.6% overall blog CTR)
  - Content refresh strategy: Showing 3x increase in impressions for updated low-performing articles
  - Indexing challenges: \~1/3 of recently published articles not indexed after a month

### SEO Strategy Discussion

  - Shopify-related keywords: Challenging due to Shopify's high domain authority
  - Suggestion to continue including Shopify keywords (5-10% of articles) despite difficulties
  - Focus on backlinking to Firework's Shopify app to improve rankings

### Metrics and Reporting

  - Request to include on-page metrics (time on page, bounce rate) in future reports
  - Suggestion to use Google Analytics (connected to Search Console) for comprehensive metrics
  - Agreement not to benchmark against previous non-marketing team's blog posts

### Backlink Strategy

  - Current referring domains: \~2,000 (400 less than competitors)
  - GrowthX perspective: Technical SEO more crucial than backlinks at this stage
  - Backlinks become more important for highly competitive keywords and scaling traffic
