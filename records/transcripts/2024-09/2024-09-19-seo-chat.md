## Key Topics

### GrowthHax.ai's Content Creation Process

  - Uses AI to generate high-quality, SEO-optimized content at scale
  - Human editors involved for quality control and final touches
  - Can adapt to brand voice and incorporate existing company materials
  - Typical workflow: keyword research → outline → content generation → human editing
  - Aims for 2-3k word articles, publishing 1-2 per day

### Potential Partnership Structure

  - White-label option for Metadata to offer content services to clients
  - Suggested starting with one pilot client to validate process
  - Pricing could be adjusted based on level of strategy/management required
  - Possibility of revenue sharing model after proof of concept

### Metadata's Content Needs

  - Current website content is outdated and limited
  - Interest in leveraging existing video/podcast content for blog posts
  - Need for consistent, high-volume content production
  - Desire to improve organic search presence and lead generation

### Technical Considerations

  - GrowthHax.ai doesn't currently offer full website development services
  - Discussion of potentially moving Metadata's site to Webflow for easier management
  - Metadata looking to reduce reliance on current agency for site management

### Content Strategy and SEO

  - Focus on creating "halo effect" around brand with varied, relevant content
  - Importance of lead magnets and conversion optimization alongside content
  - Potential for cross-linking between non-competing GrowthHax clients
