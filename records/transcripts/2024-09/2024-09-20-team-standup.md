## Key Topics

### Content Indexing and Ranking Issues

  - 100 articles published, 50% not indexed by Google
  - Remaining 50% not ranking high enough for efficient impressions/clicks
  - Two potential causes:
    1.  WordPress Elementor setup causing slow loading, wasting crawl budget
    2.  Excessive SEO debt pages diluting site value

### Technical Solutions

  - Moving to WebFlow next week from WordPress
  - Implementing 301 redirects to consolidate pages
  - Aiming to reduce from 400+ to 200-300 high-value pages
  - Will compare indexing data after two weeks on WebFlow

### Team Expansion and Workflow Improvements

  - Daniel (new full-time engineer) joining to enhance workflows
  - Team members encouraged to schedule 1-on-1s with Daniel
  - Four designers added to create Canva templates
  - Elina and Bianca reviewing articles for web stacks, Strapi, and tribe projects

### Content Quality Improvements

  - Manual content creation process to be automated
  - Scheduled content quality session with Daniel to brainstorm improvements
  - Observed issues:
    1.  Repetitive article introductions (e.g., "Are you...")
    2.  Limited depth in subheadings (3-4 H2s max)
    3.  Lack of comprehensiveness in longer articles

### Project Updates

  - Tribe launching first articles this week
  - Some projects experiencing slight delays in content refreshes and publishing
