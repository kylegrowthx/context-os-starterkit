## Key Topics

### Growth Goals and Metrics

  - Current: \~1M monthly site visitors, \~400k monthly signups
  - Target: 5x signups (2M monthly) by end of January
  - Company ARR: $37M now, aiming for $70M-100M by end of January
  - Sales-led ARR: $10M now, targeting $20M by end of January
  - Key metric: 800k paid videos/month on prosumer plans (currently \~250k)

### Content Strategy and SEO Focus

  - Build best-in-class tools directory to rank for non-branded keywords
  - Prioritize high-volume, strategic keywords (e.g., video translation)
  - Create landing pages for each tool, following successful competitor formats
  - Develop editorial blog content to support directory pages
  - Leverage existing newsletter audience (7-8M emails, 1M monthly active)

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Horizontal product with diverse use cases (marketing, L\&D, religious orgs, etc.)
  - Key verticals: Customer education, courses, sermons, general marketing
  - Focus on US/EU markets for higher conversion rates
  - Roles: Video creators, marketers, content teams, localization specialists

### Product and Content Gaps

  - Need for better onboarding and "magic moment" within first 3 minutes
  - Lack of up-to-date product demos and video content
  - Outdated help center articles being refreshed and condensed

### Brand Voice and Tone

  - Aspiring to be like Canva: simple, approachable, creator-focused
  - Exploring unique brand voice using TV/movie character inspiration
