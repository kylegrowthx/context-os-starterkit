## Key Topics

### Website Platform Transition

  - Moving from Webflow to Framer for faster campaign deployment
  - Need to verify Framer's capability to handle 20,000+ pages
  - Importance of API access and negotiating enterprise-level pricing
  - Potential need for dedicated dev resource for marketing website

### SEO Strategy Overview

  - Two-pronged approach: Editorial and Programmatic content
  - Editorial: Starts with competitive gap analysis and content strategy
  - Programmatic: Focuses on scaling successful content themes rapidly
  - Importance of continuous content refreshing and value addition

### Editorial Content Strategy

  - Develop comprehensive content plan (e.g., 3,400 assignments for Ramp)
  - Stack-rank assignments based on keyword difficulty and business relevance
  - Start publishing quickly (by second week) to test and learn
  - Double down on successful content themes

### Programmatic Content Approach

  - Build on validated content lanes from editorial success
  - Focus on unique information sources and rigorous quality control
  - Dedicate resources to continuous content creation and improvement
  - Aim for constant value addition to maintain rankings

### Case Study: Steadily

  - Grew organic traffic from 20k to 150k monthly visits in 6-7 months
  - Utilized both editorial and programmatic approaches
  - Created content clusters (e.g., state-specific articles) for scalable content
  - Prioritized high CTR content for scaling

### School AI's Current Situation

  - Starting from scratch with marketing efforts
  - No existing SEO tools or analytics setup
  - Opportunity to align tool selection with fwd.digital's preferred stack
