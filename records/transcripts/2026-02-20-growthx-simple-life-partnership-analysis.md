## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Scaling
Simple Life wants to scale from 120 blog pages to 5,000+ pages to achieve 10x organic sales. Medical content is bottlenecked at 3 articles/month due to doctor review requirements, so the plan pivots to non-medical content categories (~4,500 pages identified). Marcel proposed a phased approach: start with 5 articles/week, test indexing and engagement signals, then ramp up. Content will be AI-assisted but human-reviewed, with citations and reputable sources to build search engine trust.

### Traffic, Conversion, and ROI Analysis
Current baseline: 45.5k organic visits → 20k downloads → 757 purchases (3.8% conversion). Average blog page gets 40-50 monthly visits with potential to double or triple. LTV is ~$70/year per customer. ROI projections are conservative — first year unlikely to show strong returns, but organic traffic compounds and becomes profitable by year two. Dennis needs granular CTR and conversion data by content category to build internal business cases.

### LLM and AI Traffic Opportunity
Simple Life's site is currently blocking AI indexing, resulting in zero LLM traffic. Marcel estimates LLM traffic could reach 0.5x to 2x organic traffic once unblocked — a critical untapped growth channel. Competitors have lower quality content in this space, creating an opportunity to dominate AI-driven app recommendations and health queries.

### Technical SEO and Site Issues
Immediate audit needed for crawlability, page speed, mobile optimization, and indexing. AI bot blocking must be resolved. Marcel will assign a dedicated engineer and designer during the pilot to fix infrastructure issues that limit both organic and LLM traffic potential.

### Pilot Partnership Structure
An 8-week no-risk pilot starting in April includes site audits, new section creation, content publishing, and content refreshes. Cost will be below the $45k/month eventual retainer. Pilot results and Marcel's ROI model will be used to secure stakeholder buy-in and navigate Dennis's procurement process.
