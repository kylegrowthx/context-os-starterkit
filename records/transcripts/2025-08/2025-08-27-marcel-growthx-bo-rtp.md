## Key Topics

### RTP Ventures Overview

- Global VC firm with 27+ years history, offices in 8 cities (NY, SF, London, Paris, Bangalore, Dubai, Amsterdam, Israel)
- Fund 4: $1B vehicle with >50% reserved for follow-on investments in portfolio companies
- Investment thesis: Pre-seed to Series A stage, $1-15M initial checks, compound wins through aggressive follow-on participation
- Success case study: Datadog (RTP first institutional money, subsequent presence in every round, became 2nd largest shareholder at IPO)
- Bo Berluti: Early-stage investor since Nov 2021, based in NYC, sourcing deals and building relationships

### GrowthX Service Model

- "Services as software": Blending quality expert services with AI/software speed and scalability
- End-to-end content partnership: Strategy (SEO/GEO) → Research → Creation → Fact-checking → Publishing → LLM tracking/optimization
- Internal platform with AI workflows + experts in the loop at every stage (no black-box AI)
- Leadership expertise: CEO Marcel built content engines at scale, distilled his DeepGram workflows (drove 24x traffic, 4x revenue) into GrowthX product
- Team: Ex-TechCrunch CCO, G2.com editors, TripAdvisor/Fanatics SEO directors, expert content managers
- Scale: 10M+ ARR, 70+ clients across SaaS/B2B, Series A ($12M from Madrona), Series B actively fundraising

### GrowthX Engagement & Workflow

- Two-phase commitment: 8-week strategy sprint ($20K fixed for Series A) + 12-month growth execution ($12K+/mo for Series A)
- Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint) artifacts: Deep company context research, persona development (fears, pain points, motivations), ICP analysis by vertical
- Phase 2 (Growth Execution): Continuous content creation, optimization, distribution (newsletters, programmatic SEO)
- Workflow for each piece: Brief (with keyword research, competitor analysis) → Deep Research (on custom research questions) → Draft (AI-generated from persona context) → Fact-check (per-statement validation) → Cross-linking (SEO rewards) → SEO meta (titles, descriptions)
- Technology: Proprietary workflow engine (coded in-house), API integrations for keyword traffic, search analysis, competitor intelligence, research validation

### Client Results & Proof Points

- **AugmentCode**: Started with 5K organic traffic, reached 50-100K in 6 months (5-10x uplift)
- **DeepGram**: Founder Marcel's previous company; workflows drove 24x monthly traffic increase + 4x revenue increase (proof concept for GrowthX model)
- **Ramp**: 1+ year GrowthX client; built business expense classifier (SEO win) and vendor directory leveraging Ramp's proprietary spend data (programmatic content strategy)
- **Town.com**: Seed-stage client, accounting software, GrowthX built entire blog with 100% accuracy fact-checking (high-stakes domain)

### Pricing & Commercial Model

- **Strategy Sprint**: $20,000 fixed fee (baseline for Series A companies)
- **Growth Execution**: Starting $12,000/month for Series A stage companies (12-month commitment)
- **Premium tiers**: Higher pricing for expanded services beyond editorial (e.g., distribution, programmatic plays, data integration)
- **Positioning**: ROI measured in traffic uplift (100-1000% in 6 months typical) rather than direct revenue attribution; clients build trust via content, then optimize conversion downstream
