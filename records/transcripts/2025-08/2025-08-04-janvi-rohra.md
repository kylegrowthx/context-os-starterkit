## Context

Pepper Content is a 200+ person content and AI services company founded in 2017 by Anirudh Sinla and co-founders from his engineering batch. The company initially focused on content services, then pivoted to include creative, SEO, and AI verticals after successfully launching PepperType, an AI product that acquired 6,000+ users in its first six months. Pepper is now rebranding from "Pepper Content" to simply "Pepper" with multiple vertical lines. The company operates from India (Mumbai headquarters) with teams expanding to the US and Singapore.

Pepper launched a webinar series last month targeting entry-level and manager-level professionals learning about SEO in the AI era. The series addresses a key question many professionals have: "Is SEO dead or still working in an AI world?" Previous webinars covered topics like "Are we optimizing for AI or search?" and "AI in the LLM world." Each webinar reaches 50-60 live attendees and approximately 45,000 post-publication views across their website, newsletter, and social media. Co-founder Kishan Panpalya hosts from the US. Janvi reached out to Jakub based on his depth in SEO strategy before and after AI adoption.
