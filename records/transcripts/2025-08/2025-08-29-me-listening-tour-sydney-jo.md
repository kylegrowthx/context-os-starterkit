## Overview

- Standardization of processes and CM skill sets is needed to improve efficiency
- Atlas needs improvement; Cloud Projects currently offer a better user experience
- More training and documentation are required for onboarding and consistent output

---

## Key Topics

### ME Workload and Responsibilities

  - MEs are juggling multiple clients with varying needs and complexities
  - Time split between client-facing work and internal team management (roughly 50-50)
  - Editing content still takes significant time, impacting capacity for strategic work
  - MEs often absorb tasks that should be handled by CMs (e.g., keyword research)

### Content Manager (CM) Performance

  - Inconsistent skill levels among CMs lead to variable output quality
  - Need for better onboarding and training, especially in SEO and content writing
  - Ideal CM can take strategy input and produce client-ready content with minimal ME editing
  - Suggestion to map CM skills and standardize their capabilities across the team

### Platform and Tools

  - Atlas currently has limitations, often "fighting" users with hard-coded elements
  - Cloud Projects offer more flexibility and ease of use for content creation
  - Need for more granular control over individual steps in content creation process
  - Upcoming agentic workflows and assistant features show promise for improvement

### Process Improvement

  - Lack of standardized processes and SOPs slows down work and onboarding
  - Need for better documentation on strategies, Content OS setup, and other key processes
  - Suggestion to create step-by-step guides for tasks like keyword research
  - Challenge in finding time to create these processes while managing current workload

### Client Management

  - Some clients (e.g., Sunbit) have extremely high content volume expectations (25 articles/week)
  - Need to better manage scope creep and small requests that accumulate over time
  - Importance of setting clear expectations in statements of work (SOW)
  - Suggestion to focus on impact and prioritization rather than just output volume

## Action Items

- Develop and document standardized processes for key tasks (e.g., keyword research, Content OS setup)
- Review and potentially adjust high-volume client expectations (e.g., Sunbit)
- Explore ways to give MEs more time for strategic work and process improvement
- Continue improving Atlas platform based on user feedback and comparison with Cloud Projects
- Implement skill mapping and standardization for CMs to ensure consistent quality
