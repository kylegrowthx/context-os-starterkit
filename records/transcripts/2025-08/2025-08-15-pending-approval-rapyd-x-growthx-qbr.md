## Key Topics

### Content Performance Overview

  - 28 blog articles published in 22 weeks
  - New content showing stronger early performance than pre-GrowthX baseline
  - Non-branded clicks and impressions increasing from March to August
  - Legacy content from early-mid 2024 losing traffic steadily

### Content Strategy Adjustments

  - Refreshing high-potential pages from 2023-2024 that are losing traffic
  - Optimizing content for AI search behavior (e.g., problem-solution framing, clear structure)
  - Focusing on content recency to leverage AI's preference for recent information
  - Consolidating similar pages to reduce overlap and improve conversion paths

### Industry Trends and Content Opportunities

  - Cross-border FX cost mitigation: Potential to discuss stablecoin settlement benefits
  - Payment rail evolution: Focus on academic/foundational content about crypto/stablecoin integration
  - Retail trading expansion: Emphasis on prediction markets, overlap with gambling industry
  - Targeting white-label brokerage platforms as potential referral partners

### AI Search Optimization and Tracking

  - Implementing LLM referral tracking through GA4
  - Using Scrunch for brand presence monitoring in AI-generated content
  - Creating product-focused prompts for competitive analysis and brand awareness
  - Optimizing content format for AI crawling (e.g., chunking information, FAQs, listicles)

### ICP Refinement

  - Discussed breaking down ICPs into more specific segments:
    1.  Mid-enterprise payment ops professional
    2.  Payfac/ISO (finance professionals, owners)
    3.  Physical retail (partners/ISOs or small business owners)
    4.  High-opportunity industries (gaming, online trading platforms)
  - Potential to target ISVs separately, especially in high-opportunity verticals

### HubSpot Integration

  - Need to integrate HubSpot data for better lead and conversion tracking
  - GrowthX to provide requirements for HubSpot data integration into reports
