## Context

GrowthX is actively hiring for a Technical Managing Editor to operationalize its AI-driven content production at scale. Matthew Panzarino, who previously served as Editor-in-Chief at TechCrunch, explained that the role requires roughly equal parts editorial judgment and process articulation—understanding not just what good content is, but how to systematize its production and continuously improve workflows. Jessica Shulsinger is an experienced content operations leader who spent 10 years at Thread Creative managing e-commerce copywriting at scale, then led the implementation of AI content generation at Adidas using Hypotenuse AI, training teams on adoption and managing stakeholder expectations. Her hands-on experience optimizing processes and understanding when and how to deploy AI for content production makes her a strong fit for GrowthX's objectives.
