## Key Topics

### GrowthX Business Overview

  - Recently hit $10M in annual revenue, with \~70% gross margins
  - Currently have \~50 clients, aiming for 250 clients and $45M revenue next year
  - Started as a content service, now building scalable AI-powered platform
  - Transitioning from deterministic workflows to more agentic AI approaches
  - See content as entry point to broader knowledge work automation

### Current Challenges and Priorities

  - Improving efficiency and scalability of content delivery as client base expands
  - Building out platform features and instrumentation to support growth
  - Transitioning more manual steps into the automated platform
  - Aiming to reduce human intervention costs while maintaining quality

### Product and Technology

  - Built workflow engine and moving executions to internal "Atlas" platform
  - Developing advanced AI capabilities like fact-checking and content generation
  - Creating strategy sprint process to improve onboarding and retention
  - Exploring open-sourcing some infrastructure components

### Future Vision

  - Evolve into "holding company" that can incubate/acquire agencies and build software products
  - Potential to spin out individual platform components as standalone products
  - Open source core infrastructure with cloud-hosted version

### Candidate Background and Motivations

  - Estevão previously worked at Koala, acquired 3 months after he joined full-time
  - Seeks dynamic startup environment to solve open-ended problems
  - Enjoys quick research, prototyping, and shipping solutions with minimal constraints
  - Wants to maintain excitement and challenge in work over next 12 months
