## Summary

Marcel and Abhishek (CMO at Firework since March 2025) reviewed how significantly both companies have evolved since Firework became GrowthX's third customer in June 2024—GrowthX has shifted from content consulting to AI-driven content strategy and LLM optimization, while Firework pivoted from broad SMB reach to deep enterprise targeting where content is no longer a primary driver. After assessing their original partnership scope, Abhishek identified that 3 of 4 original partnership aspects no longer align, and both leaders mutually agreed to end the engagement amicably without renewal.

---

## Context

Firework is a video content platform serving 250 employees across five regions with significant revenue. Abhishek Anjanappa joined as CMO in March 2025 after advising the company since early 2022 through a PE firm that had invested in Firework. This approach gave him institutional understanding and pre-alignment on Firework's vision before taking the full-time role. GrowthX became Firework's customer in June 2024 (their third customer ever) under a different strategic mandate than what Firework now pursues. The partnership was structured around content generation and reach, but Firework's market strategy has fundamentally shifted toward enterprise customers where depth and embedded solutions matter more than content reach.
