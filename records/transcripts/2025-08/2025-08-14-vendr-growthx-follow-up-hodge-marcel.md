## Summary

Vendr and GrowthX Labs negotiated down the initial $250k/year content strategy engagement to a $12k/month pilot for 6 months, starting with a strategy sprint in early September to identify quick wins and demonstrate value. Marcel emphasized that Vendr's 2,400 pages — many stale and losing traffic — need both programmatic generation and editorial refresh, and that editorial content provides the foundation for SEO success; Matt agreed to move forward once the strategy sprint outputs show clear metrics on shrinking content areas and growth opportunities. Both teams resolved contract issues around approval rights, auto-renewal removal, and a termination-for-breach clause.

---

## Context

Vendr is a B2B procurement intelligence platform that helps enterprises navigate vendor negotiations and contracts. GrowthX Labs was brought in to develop a content strategy focused on organic growth — both programmatic content generation (buyer guides, vendor comparison pages) and editorial content refresh. The initial engagement scope was ambitious ($250k/year), but Vendr's leadership wanted validation before committing to that level of spend. This call resolved the commercial sticking point by agreeing to a phased approach: a $12k/month, 6-month pilot starting with a strategy sprint to map Vendr's content gaps and demonstrate early results. Both sides also worked through standard contract terms (approval rights for logo use, removal of auto-renewal, addition of a termination-for-breach clause) to get the deal across the finish line.
