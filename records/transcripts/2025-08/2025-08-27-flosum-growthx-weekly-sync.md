  - Sidebar issues:
      - Long table of contents pushes other elements below the fold
      - Recommendation to trim spacing and update sticky nav functionality
      - Consider moving author and share buttons to blog header
  - Chat popup:
      - Current implementation needs improvement (e.g., showing when no one is online)

      - Need to align blog categories with Flosum's internal product/solution interests

      - Suggestion to surface more gated assets (white papers, ebooks) within blog content
      - Add more prominent CTAs and buttons for downloads or demos
      - Incorporate social proof elements (e.g., G2 reviews, case studies) within blog content
  - Bottom of page enhancements:
      - Reduce duplication of elements (share buttons, etc.)
      - Add more prominent CTAs related to the blog's topic or product alignment

### Analytics and Tracking

  - Jakub reported issues accessing comprehensive conversion data in Google Analytics
  - Beau to investigate potential tagging issues on blog pages
  - Team to explore using Marketo for better tracking and personalization capabilities
