## Context

Understory Agency is a growth and demand generation firm hired by GrowthX to drive lead generation and pipeline growth. GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency with a $200k+/year engagement model, combined with CheckThat (an AI visibility/AEO product). The company is at an inflection point: sales capacity is constrained on the delivery side (limited to two kickoffs per week, aspiring to four to six), and the team has historically relied on organic inbound from founder-led relationships rather than proactive outreach. The engagement is also strategic for Understory, which views GrowthX as an ideal customer (fresh to Understory's services, with "blank canvas" potential) and has similar capacity constraints as a growing agency.
