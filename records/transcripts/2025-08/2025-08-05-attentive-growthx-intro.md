## Key Topics

### Attentive Company Update

  - Competitor sunset email/SMS product, driving customer acquisition for Attentive
  - Launching major Black Friday campaign (biggest of the year)
  - Company size \~1,100 employees (post-layoff); small marketing team of \~4

### Content Creation Challenges

  - Bottlenecks: accessing data and SME input for unique insights
  - Publishing cadence: historically 1 SEO-focused piece/week + higher-caliber projects
  - Desire for AI tools to review/evaluate content post-creation
  - Using external briefs (possibly from Profound) for SEO/AI optimization

### GrowthX Services Overview

  - Offer 8-week strategy sprint to ensure mutual fit
  - Produce 1-2 high-quality content pieces daily for most clients
  - Use custom AI workflows, research agents, and human editors
  - Cost efficiency: \~10 cents/word, typically less than one full-time hire
  - Focus on scaling quality through iterative process improvement

### Industry Landscape

  - Attentive's main competitors: Klaviyo, Iterable
  - Shifting focus towards orchestration (beyond just SMS)
  - Importance of both LLM optimization and traditional SEO

### Content Strategy for LLMs and SEO

  - Value in creating top/middle-of-funnel content for brand awareness
  - LLM training data consideration for long-term brand visibility
  - Balancing efforts between LLM optimization and traditional SEO
