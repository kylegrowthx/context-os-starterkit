## Context

Kyle Doherty is GrowthX's fractional RevOps consultant, committed to 10-15 hours per week, responsible for building out HubSpot infrastructure, integrations, and reporting. This was a working session to align Bridget (COO-level ops owner) and Jason (GTM, event marketing) on what he should prioritize, given that a lot of their existing RevOps setup is undocumented and scattered across Zapier, Airtable, and manual processes. The immediate pressure is two-fold: (1) fundraising prep (potential October 1 date means due diligence two days later, so they need clean pipeline data ASAP), and (2) a fundamental operational friction where most deals flow through Marcel's personal network and texts, making it hard for the team to see what's happening without constantly asking him. Tyler Pavlas joined toward the end to discuss custom HubSpot views to surface only the deals Marcel is actively pushing to close each week, since he can only handle 2-3 kickoffs weekly.
