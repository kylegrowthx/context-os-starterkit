## Overview

- GrowthX operates on two levels: customer-facing service layer and underlying AI/expert workflow platform
- Platform aims to scale expertise, improve with better models, and learn from each interaction
- Current focus: consistently delivering high-quality output, scaling capacity non-linearly with human resources
- Developing client-facing dashboard (Atlas) for analytics, opportunity tracking, and eventual self-service capabilities

---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX Business Model

  - Two-sided approach: customer-facing service layer and underlying AI/expert workflow platform
  - Service layer helps understand customer needs and market demand
  - Underlying platform consists of AI workflows and expert layer for scalable expertise
  - Common core being built to potentially expand into multiple service areas beyond content

### Platform Architecture and Vision

  - Three key layers: service, AI workflow, and expert
  - Goal: reproduce expert thought processes in scalable workflows
  - Principle 1: Improve as AI models advance (faster, better, cheaper)
  - Principle 2: Learn and improve with each iteration
  - Aiming for no sunken costs by building solutions for immediate, paid use-cases

### Workflow and Improvement Process

  - Inputs, outputs, workflows, context generation, and expert interventions
  - Learning from expert feedback to improve workflows
  - Comprehensive evaluation of outputs (e.g., groundness, writing guidelines)
  - Agentic planning and improvement based on evaluations

### Client-Facing Platform Development (Atlas)

  - Phased approach to client access:
    1.  Analytics dashboard
    2.  Opportunity inventory view
    3.  Commenting and improvement capabilities
    4.  Self-service execution and AI assistant access
  - AI assistant for content creation (demo'd with abnormals example)

### Current Challenges and Focus Areas

1.  Ensuring consistent high-quality output and customer experience
2.  Scaling capacity without linear human resource growth
3.  Managing rapid growth and market demand
