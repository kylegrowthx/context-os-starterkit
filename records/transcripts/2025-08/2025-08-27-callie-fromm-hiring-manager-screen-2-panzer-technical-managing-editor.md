## Context

GrowthX is hiring for a Technical Managing Editor role on the delivery team that sits between account strategists (client-facing) and content managers (CMs, the people who shepherd AI workflows). The ME role requires someone who is excellent at content AND excellent at understanding how content workflows function—identifying where inputs need adjustment (prompt engineering, context engineering, artifact refinement) versus where outputs are failing because of system issues. This second skill—the "workflow thinking"—is the delta between good and great at GrowthX, and it's not easy to find. Callie Fromm is an internal candidate or prospect (context suggests she was not yet an employee but was being evaluated).
