## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

  - Combines AI tools with human expertise for scalable, high-quality content production
  - Creates detailed artifacts (writing guidelines, brand voice, personas) to ensure consistent quality
  - Uses custom workflows and knowledge bases to handle complex, technical subjects
  - Focuses on both creating new content and refreshing existing pages
  - Aims to cover a wide range of topics relevant to target audience

### AI Search Optimization

  - Emphasizes the shift from traditional SEO to AI-powered search
  - Creates content that addresses more specific, long-tail queries
  - Monitors brand visibility and citations within AI search results
  - Adapts content strategy to match changing user behavior with AI search tools

### Content Quality Control

  - Employs experienced editors and content managers for each account
  - Implements multi-step review processes, including AI-powered fact-checking
  - Calibrates content to match client's brand voice and technical requirements
  - Offers flexibility in client involvement for content review and approval

### Alloy's Current Content Situation

  - Strong existing content base with 286 pages, including 11 guides and 113 blog posts
  - Some high-performing content, like guides, driving significant traffic
  - Team looking to enhance lead generation and adapt to AI search impacts
  - Potential need for more efficient content refreshing and updating

### Collaboration Model

  - GrowthX acts as an extension of the client's team
  - Uses Slack for regular communication and progress updates
  - Offers weekly check-ins and goal alignment
  - Flexible in adapting to client's specific needs and workflow preferences
