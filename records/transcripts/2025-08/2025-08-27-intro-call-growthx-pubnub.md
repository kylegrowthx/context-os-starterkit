## Summary

GrowthX CEO Marcel Santilli, alongside Kyle Gaudreau and new sales hire Tyler Pavlas, connected with PubNub VP of Growth Joshua Carr and VP of Product Todd Greene to explore collaboration on content strategy and developer growth. GrowthX pitched their service model—an 8-week $20K strategy sprint followed by daily content publishing—focused on organic growth, AI visibility, and high-quality content that drives conversions, not just traffic. Joshua raised the critical question of conversion quality over vanity metrics, and both teams identified potential synergies around agentic workflows that PubNub is exploring internally.

---

## Context

PubNub is a 10-year-old infrastructure company providing real-time data synchronization APIs that power 800M devices and process 3T transactions monthly. They operate in highly regulated sectors including healthcare, and maintain strong inbound developer motion—they hit $6M ARR before ever hiring a sales team, driven purely by word-of-mouth and developer attraction. Todd Greene, VP of Product, was introduced to GrowthX through Jay Hoag (Sapphire Ventures), a mutual board member whom both companies trust deeply. The warm introduction signals potential fit: PubNub is exploring AI and agentic initiatives internally and looking for strategic partners to accelerate their marketing motion around these emerging capabilities. GrowthX is currently in Series B fundraising and positioning themselves as the service-plus-software partner for organic growth and AI visibility strategy.
