## Context

Aembit is a cybersecurity identity and access management company; GrowthX is providing content marketing services as part of a multi-month engagement. This is a weekly sync between the delivery and client teams to track content production velocity and performance, with the goal of identifying topics that resonate with Aembit's technical audience and drive organic traffic. By the four-month mark (end of August), the team expected to see significant traffic uplift and sufficient data to inform topic strategy, but performance has lagged behind typical baselines. The core challenge is sourcing content that captures Aembit's unique perspective—not available through public sources—while maintaining production velocity.
