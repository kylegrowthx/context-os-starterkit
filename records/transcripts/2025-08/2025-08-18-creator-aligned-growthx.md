## Key Topics

### GrowthX Overview and Approach

  - Utilizes AI workflows combined with human expertise for content creation
  - Focus on creating content that answers user questions, optimized for AI-driven search
  - 8-week strategy sprint to develop AI-ready artifacts and de-risk long-term engagements
  - Ongoing monthly engagements for content production and optimization
  - Works with various team structures (founders, established content teams, to-be-hired teams)

### Client Results and Examples

  - Augment Code: Nearly 10x increase in monthly organic traffic since working with GrowthX
  - Created comprehensive guides and comparison charts (e.g., "13 Best AI Coding Tools for Enterprise Codebases")
  - RAMP: Developed interactive tools like per diem calculator and expense classifier
  - Quick indexation and high engagement rates for new content

### GrowthX Platform Capabilities

  - Hundreds of AI-driven steps in content creation workflows
  - Includes keyword research, competitor analysis, SERP ranking factors, and deep research
  - Generates research questions and validates with legitimate sources
  - Optimizes content for both traditional and AI-powered search engines

### Pricing and Engagement Structure

  - Strategy sprint: $20-40k for 8 weeks
  - Ongoing monthly engagements: Average $12-15k/month
  - Minimum engagement typically 6-12 months, with 18+ months ideal for significant results
  - Custom, higher-priced engagements available for complex projects

### Multilingual Content Production

  - Capability to produce content in multiple languages (e.g., English and Spanish for Ventures Group)
  - Utilizes native speakers for quality assurance
  - Considers strategic value of localization vs. focusing on primary language opportunities
