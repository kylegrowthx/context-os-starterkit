## Key Topics

### Content Performance Overview

  - Blog pages saw a significant traffic spike, especially per diem pages (Washington page leading)
  - Expense category pages maintained steady performance
  - Tuning pages: Assets and liabilities page had a large jump in sessions
  - Treasury pages remained stable
  - Vendor pages:
      - Overall performance maintained
      - AI cluster increased traffic despite reduced Google referrals
      - Content creation, AI, and AI infrastructure in top 10 average Google position
      - Observability entered top 15 position

### Vendor Page Enrichment Strategy

  - Proposed additions:
      - New sections to current pages
      - UX improvements (e.g., horizontal tables for alternatives)
      - Industry vertical expansion (CRM tools as primary focus)
      - Business maturity level content (startup, enterprise, mid-market)
  - AI model comparisons:
      - Focus on industry-specific use cases rather than business size
      - Potential high-value traffic from product managers and researchers
  - CRM vertical expansion priorities:
      - Healthcare, finance, and nonprofits show high search volumes
      - Monday.com example: dedicated nonprofit website

### Content Creation Plans

  - Start with CRM tools for vertical expansion
  - Explore project management, email marketing, and CMS tools
  - Create templates for easy scaling of new content
  - Focus on tool-specific AI content (e.g., Jasper AI, growthx.ai) rather than foundation models
  - Compile list of vendors based on search volume to identify gaps in current coverage

### Product Team Involvement

  - Expected to take over core vendor landing pages
  - May require moving existing content to subpages (e.g., "details")
  - Developing strategy for pages with insufficient transaction data
  - Aiming to add hundreds to thousands of new vendors
  - Different editing workflow needed for various vendor page types

### Content Restrictions and Partnerships

  - Avoid creating content for accounting platforms and partners
  - Develop separate page experience for partners, showcasing only positive data
