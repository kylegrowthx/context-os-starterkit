## Key Topics

### GrowthX Overview

- End-to-end content partner: strategy to CMS publishing
- Team includes ex-TechCrunch editor-in-chief (Chief Content Officer), former Fanatics/TripAdvisor CEO, and managing editor who built g2.com
- Founder Marcel's background: Built content engines at IBM (securityintelligence.com, 10+ years), HP (1000+ expert contributor network), and HashiCorp (scaled from $6M to $100M+); also led strategy at DeepGram
- AI workflows for efficient, high-quality content creation with editorial focus as foundation

### GrowthX Process

- Strategy sprint: 1-hour kickoff, bi-weekly or weekly 30-minute meetings (minimum)
- Develop artifacts: company context, personas (with fears, uncertainties, motivations, trust factors, job-related priorities, pain points), writing guidelines
- Content OS: Define topic clusters, create content assignments, prioritize by search volume and difficulty to identify winnable opportunities
- Publish ~30 articles by week 4-5 of strategy sprint (when artifacts are locked)
- Phase 2 (Growth Execution): Execute at scale, 10+ articles/week, daily publishing target, refresh opportunities, refine hypotheses and artifacts over 12 months

### Otto's Current Status and Goals

- AI-powered corporate travel assistant in beta for 9 months (transactions and user actions already working)
- Targeting GA/press launch mid-November for multiplier effect in SEO and AI visibility
- Created ~40 basic Q&A articles (800 words each, unpublished) independently using ChatGPT and Claude
- Seeking 15-20 more long-form articles before launch; Michael will handle testimonials and non-content marketing website elements

### Timeline and Pricing

- Strategy sprint: $20K for 6-8 weeks (GrowthX at low end to support startups)
- Growth execution: $12K/month (industry range $12-15K; Otto quoted at lower end)
- Current demand: GrowthX typically 4-6 weeks out on strategy kickoffs
- Proposed start date: September 22nd (if signed next week)
- Otto prefers earlier: September 2 or 11 (ideally ~September 5) — Tyler to check with Marcel for feasibility
- No long-term commitment required; decision on growth execution phase made after strategy sprint completion

### Expectations Management

- GrowthX emphasized no "silver bullet" for immediate results — this is a long-term investment
- 1-2 quarters typically needed for significant traffic/domain authority increase from ground zero
- Otto starting with ~200 monthly visitors (near zero domain authority)
- SoW language: Don't engage if you need immediate results, don't believe in content as long-term asset, or your website is brand new — but GrowthX does work with early-stage startups
- Otto acknowledged their pre-launch status and understands the long-term nature of the commitment
