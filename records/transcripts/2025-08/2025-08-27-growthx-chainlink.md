## Context

Chainlink is a major blockchain protocol and publisher that has invested heavily in organic content strategy to build authority in crypto/Web3. The company has achieved significant visibility (White House mentions, major publication features, strong owned-domain SEO) and recently built a custom AI content generation system over six months. However, Chainlink is now shifting strategy due to the rise of AI-powered search—they're finding that while they generate lots of content, other sites are cited more frequently in LLM answers about their own products. This is the core tension driving the meeting: Chainlink wants to understand how to optimize for AI discovery and increase citation mentions, and whether GrowthX's publishing-first platform can help. The meeting was introduced through Francois (an advisor at Chainlink) and represents both companies' philosophy that content is a core business asset.
