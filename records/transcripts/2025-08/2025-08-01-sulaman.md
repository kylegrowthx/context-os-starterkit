- Sulaman should use Slack channels for direct feedback requests instead of Linear mentions, which can feel slow and impersonal.
- Deployed dashboards should be posted to group channels for broader team review, enabling multiple reviewers to provide feedback upfront rather than iterating with one person at a time.
- This approach prevents post-launch discovery issues and ensures comprehensive feedback coverage.

- Dave's template had code that would become obsolete within weeks and contained bugs that eventually broke half the dashboard—discovered during this meeting.

- BigQuery integration could streamline the process, but Vivek noted this is a future priority, not immediate.

- Completing School AI and Monograph Looker dashboards before client meeting early next week.
- Addressing Home Base dashboard issues: GA4 data not appearing (likely sampling issue due to very high traffic volume—1M+ active users/month, 73K/minute).
- Publishing Galileo articles: high priority for today (Friday).
