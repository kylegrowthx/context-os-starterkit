## Key Topics

### Apollo.io's Core Functionalities

  - 231 million contact database, updated every 32 days
  - 98% accuracy on verified data (green check marks)
  - Filters for job postings, hiring status, and ideal customer profiles
  - Buying intent data from LeadSift and Bombora, updated weekly
  - Website visitor tracking with potential contact suggestions
  - Workflow automation for ongoing lead generation and sequencing

### Data Enrichment and Integration

  - Bidirectional HubSpot integration for pushing/pulling contacts
  - Chrome extension for LinkedIn profile data capture
  - Ability to enrich existing HubSpot data through Apollo
  - Limitations on historical data import; focuses on new interactions within Apollo

### Communication Tracking and Management

  - Sequences for outreach management within Apollo
  - Conversation logging for calls made through connected platforms (Zoom, Google Meet, Teams)
  - Meeting scheduling similar to Calendly, with Google Calendar integration
  - Lack of comprehensive contact summary across all touchpoints

### Pricing and Trial

  - Annual pricing: $4,284 for 3 users (paid upfront)
  - Quarterly option: $5,000/year
  - Credit system: 1 credit per email, 8 credits per mobile number
  - 10-day trial arranged for thorough evaluation
