## Context

Bubble is a no-code-to-AI platform company that recently transitioned from being the market leader in no-code tools to competing in the AI app development space. At the start of 2025, they conducted extensive competitive analysis and built strong personas and market understanding. They're now seeking execution help to accelerate their go-to-market strategy. Tatiana Afanasyeva (leading the partnership evaluation) and Deedi Brown (content leader) brought concerns about commitment length and the scope of research work GrowthX was proposing—specifically, they felt Bubble had already completed much of the personas and competitive analysis in-house and wanted to shift the engagement toward execution. GrowthX, represented by Marcel Santilli with support from Tyler Pavlas, positioned the initial 8-week engagement as an opportunity to build trust and prove value before committing to a longer-term relationship. Both sides expressed strong interest in making the partnership work if terms could be negotiated.
