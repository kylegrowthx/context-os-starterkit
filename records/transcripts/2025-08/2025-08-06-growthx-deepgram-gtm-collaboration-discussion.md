## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Landscape and Trends

  - Traffic patterns changing: fewer clicks but higher intent visitors (3-5x more valuable)
  - Shift from broad searches to in-depth queries and direct brand searches
  - Content must be well-researched, comprehensive, and show strong brand authority
  - AI-driven search optimization becoming crucial (AI answers, AI engine optimization)

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

  - Create detailed "artifacts" capturing company context and audience insights
  - Use proprietary platform for content creation, powered by AI coding agent and custom workflows
  - Combine editorial content with strategic programmatic SEO approaches
  - Focus on audience-first content and building a "knowledge graph" of relevant topics
  - Monitor content performance and brand visibility in AI-powered search results

### Case Studies and Examples

  - Augment Code: Created high-quality listicle ("13 best coding tools for enterprise code bases")
  - VAPI: Developed 2,600 AI voice agent use case pages with custom designs and prompts
  - Ramp: Built vendor catalog comparing business tools (e.g., Asana vs. Monday)
  - Other examples: CodeLibrary.ai, per diem calculator, landlord resource hub

### Potential Strategies for Deepgram

  - Refresh and expand AI apps catalog, focusing more on voice-related applications
  - Create comparison pages for competing products (e.g., WhisperFlow vs. SuperWhisper)
  - Develop deeper, more relevant content connecting to Deepgram's core offerings
  - Implement custom CTAs, cross-linking, and callouts to better tie content to Deepgram's products
