## Key Topics

### The Voice-First Content Problem

Guillaume's core challenge: he spends all day on Zoom calls where he occasionally makes insightful observations, but lacks the time to develop those into published thought leadership. He sees a broader trend—CMOs and executives increasingly relying on voice tools (Granola, Whisper Flow) for capturing insights. The business opportunity: build a workflow to aggregate Guillaume's voice snippets across calls/recordings, curate them, and turn them into published pieces—a repeatable model for other advisors at HyperGrowth.

### The Atlas Workflow

The current workflow (built by GrowthX's dev team) transforms podcast transcripts or snippets into LinkedIn posts. Inputs are customizable: writing guidelines (can be tuned for different voices), user context (LinkedIn profile URL), and example posts (2-3 posts that exemplify desired structure/tone). This flexibility means the team doesn't need separate workflows for different people—they can adapt the same tool for Gonta or other advisors.

### Short-Form vs. Long-Form Content Challenges

Short-form content (LinkedIn posts) is crisp and publication-ready with minor edits. Long-form content struggles with narrative coherence—Fathom/AI outputs sometimes loop through the same points without connecting dots or building story arcs. Guillaume emphasized: don't stretch thin snippets into long-form where there's not enough source material. Instead, be discerning about which pieces become LinkedIn posts vs. blog posts based on available input.

### Quality Control and Risk Management

LLMs hallucinate technical details, which damages credibility. Guillaume flagged an incorrect technical statement in one piece that made him look uninformed—this is unacceptable for thought leadership. Solution: add a fact-checking layer to the workflow and bring in writers (Megan, Jenn) to review for accuracy and narrative before publishing. New idea: pre-writing clarification questions—have the model ask for more details/context before generating output, rather than hallucinating answers.

### Scaling and Iteration

Start by posting the first piece and 2 LinkedIn posts, monitor audience reaction, and iterate. Once the workflow is proven on Guillaume, scale to other HyperGrowth advisors who presumably have the same problem. Initial commitment: 1-2 LinkedIn posts per week to build Guillaume's presence and generate learnings for the model/workflow.
