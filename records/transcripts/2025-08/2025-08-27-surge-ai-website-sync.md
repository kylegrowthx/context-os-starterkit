## Key Topics

### Webflow vs Custom Development Debate

  - Pros of Webflow:
      - Already implemented and working
      - Easy for non-technical edits (e.g. job postings, blog posts)
      - Proven scalability (e.g. 4M views/month, hundreds of landing pages for data annotation site)
      - Built-in CMS, security, CDN
  - Pros of custom dev:
      - Better collaboration via Git-based workflows
      - Easier to implement rich, interactive content
      - Cleaner long-term maintenance of styles/components
      - GrowthX team more comfortable with custom stack

### Interactive Content Examples
