## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Hybrid Agency-Platform Model

  - Combines marketing leaders, editors, writers, and SEO experts with an AI-powered platform
  - Currently powers their services, with plans to expose more platform features to clients
  - Aims to be an extension of the client's team, handling strategy to execution

### Content Strategy and Creation Process

  - Starts with audience-first mapping and topic clustering
  - Uses AI workflows for research, drafting, and fact-checking
  - Editors guide the process and ensure quality output
  - Publishes daily, with 42 pieces created for Engine.com in 8 weeks

### AI Visibility and Authority Building

  - Focus on becoming the best answer and resource for target audiences
  - Creates comparison content to bias AI answers towards the client
  - Emphasizes fresh content updates every \~3 months for evergreen topics
  - Demonstrated quick AI visibility gains (e.g., Augmented Code appearing in AI results within 4 days)

### Engagement Model and Team Structure

  - 8-week strategy sprint to set foundation and prove value
  - Ongoing growth execution with daily content production
  - Team includes managing director, managing editor, content managers, and specialists
  - Collaboration via Slack for regular updates and interactions
