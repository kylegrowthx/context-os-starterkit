## Summary

GrowthX kicked off a strategy sprint with HockeyStack to drive a 90% traffic increase in 90 days through content repurposing and AI visibility improvements. The client is targeting top-10 rankings in LLM search results and higher pipeline generation by leveraging their strong existing content assets (research reports, template library, video series) with better distribution strategies. GrowthX will develop a content strategy focused on breaking down dense reports into high-intent question-aligned videos and shorts, set up weekly tracking via Search Console and GA4, and schedule deep-dive sessions to deepen understanding of HockeyStack's products, customers, and competitive positioning.

---

## Context

HockeyStack is a B2B SaaS attribution and account intelligence platform targeting mid-market to enterprise software companies (200–2,000+ employees). The company is positioned as a premium solution due to its superior product and built-in data foundation, competing against Visible, Bizible, 6sense, Demandbase, and Common Room. HockeyStack's core offering combines marketing attribution with a newer Account Intelligence product (powered by the Odin AI agent), giving customers visibility into the full customer journey for data-driven decision making.

The engagement is strategic for both sides. HockeyStack is growth-focused but resource-constrained—they have strong content assets and LinkedIn presence but struggle with distribution and traffic generation. They're currently running 10,000 outbound calls daily via Thundersory, but aren't capturing the AI-driven search opportunity despite having the product and content to win there. GrowthX sees an opportunity to accelerate growth through content strategy, distribution, and AEO/AI visibility work—core competencies that align with CheckThat's strategic focus on AI-driven search rankings.

## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Challenges

  - Content production not the main issue; distribution and driving traffic are key priorities
  - Existing content breadth is strong, but needs better distribution strategies
  - Time/capacity constraints limit marketing team's ability to execute
  - Recent dip in website landing page conversion rates

### Content Strategy & Performance

  - Research reports and template library performing well
  - LinkedIn content driving engagement
  - Video content (e.g., "Can You Dashboard It?" series) shows promise
  - Case studies, especially when shared by sales team, are effective
  - Opportunity to break down dense research reports into more digestible, targeted content pieces

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competitors: Visible, Bizible, 6sense, Demandbase, Common Room
  - HockeyStack positioning as a premium solution due to superior product
  - Key differentiator: Built as a data platform from the ground up vs. competitors using white-labeled tools

### Target Audience & ICP

  - B2B software companies, 50-10,000 employees (focus on 200-2,000+)
  - 70% North America, 30% EMEA (UK third-largest market)
  - Key titles: CMOs, CROs, VP Marketing, Growth Marketing, Performance Marketing, Marketing Ops

### Product & Value Proposition

  - Core offering: Marketing attribution + newer Account Intelligence product
  - Key value props: Visibility into full customer journey, data-driven decision making, AI-powered insights (Odin agent)
  - Unique selling point: Built-in data foundation for high-performing go-to-market teams

### AI & LLM Strategy

  - Aim to improve visibility and rankings in AI-driven search results
  - Focus on long-tail, niche keywords related to core offerings
  - Opportunity to repurpose existing content for AI-friendly formats
