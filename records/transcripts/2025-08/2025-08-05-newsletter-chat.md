## Summary

George walked Maddy and Megan through GrowthX's newsletter strategy for Augment Code, showcasing impressive early results: 40% open rate and 10% CTR on a cold audience of 20,000 product users. The Metronome team is deciding whether to create a standalone thought leadership newsletter (using CK's purchased domains) or distribute Metronome's existing blog content via newsletter, with the option to do both. The critical blocker is HubSpot integration—George committed to investigating Beehive's compatibility with HubSpot lifecycle tracking and engagement data sync, which Maddy emphasized is essential for their lead scoring and nurture automations.

## Context

Metronome is a customer data platform client of GrowthX. CK (head of marketing) acquired several branded domains interested in thought leadership initiatives and reached out to GrowthX to explore newsletter strategy. Megan LaFlamme (at Metronome) and George Haikal (GrowthX) had discussed GrowthX's successful Augment Code newsletter work the previous Friday, which impressed the team. The Metronome team is building out lifecycle-based nurture automations—they need newsletter engagement data (opens, clicks) flowing into HubSpot to power lead scoring and stage transitions for prospects at the top of their funnel.
