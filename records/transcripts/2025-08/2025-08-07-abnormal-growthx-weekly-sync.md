## Summary

GrowthX's content marketing for Abnormal achieved a dramatic 300% growth in search impressions over three weeks, climbing from 28,000 to 114,000 impressions across nearly 60 published articles. The team identified a critical misalignment: some high-performing content (particularly "Data Loss Prevention Strategies for Small Businesses") covers product capabilities Abnormal doesn't yet offer, which created legal and positioning concerns. Mara, Emily, and Callie aligned on a solution—GrowthX will focus on high-impact "star keywords" (AI security, email security, behavioral AI) and vertical-specific content while Emily implements more thorough topic approval review; Abnormal will share finalized brand guidelines and meet with Mariana and the team to clarify which topics to pursue and how to position ones adjacent to future roadmap items.

## Context

Abnormal Security is a cybersecurity company focused on email and cloud threat detection. GrowthX has been running a content marketing engagement for them, building SEO-driven awareness content across security topics. After publishing nearly 60 articles in three weeks (a major production effort), the team is seeing exceptional results—search impressions tripled—but the rapid pace created a coordination gap: some content got approved and published without full alignment on whether it properly represents Abnormal's actual product capabilities. This meeting was Mara bringing the team together to review what's working (the metrics are strong), fix what's misaligned (data loss prevention content without a real DLP product), and establish clearer processes going forward.
