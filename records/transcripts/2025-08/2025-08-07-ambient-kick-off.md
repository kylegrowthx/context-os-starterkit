## Key Topics

### Company & Product Overview

  - Founded 2017, AI-powered video analytics for physical security
  - 60+ enterprise customers including Tesla, SpaceX, NVIDIA, Salesforce
  - Key use cases: threat detection, access control optimization, forensic investigation
  - Differentiator: Most advanced solution but poorly marketed vs competitors

### Current Marketing State

  - Limited content/digital presence (only recently started regular blog/social posts)
  - Primarily outbound sales motion via SDRs
  - Weak SEO, branding, website
  - No paid advertising due to lack of creative resources

### Target Audience

  - Primary: Head of Physical Security
  - Secondary: IT teams (for technical validation)
  - Focus industries: Tech companies, manufacturing, museums, universities/schools
  - US-focused, not targeting federal gov (no FedRAMP)

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competitors: Zero Eyes, Vault.ai (startups with better marketing)
  - Legacy player: Verkada (successful with SMBs)
  - Point solutions for specific use cases (e.g. gun detection only)

### Sales Process & Challenges

  - Typically replacing/augmenting legacy video management systems (VMS)
  - Main objection: "do nothing" despite acknowledging need
  - Some losses due to price (premium solution) or perceived complexity

### Marketing Goals

  - Dramatically increase inbound pipeline/demo requests
  - Expand brand awareness and thought leadership
  - Enable rapid, high-quality, personalized campaign execution
  - Improve ABM capabilities for enterprise accounts

### Current Tech Stack

  - ZoomInfo (list building)
  - Outreach (SDR outbound)
  - HubSpot (marketing automation)
  - Salesforce (CRM)
