## Context

GrowthX is supporting Udemy on a content strategy transformation. Udemy is a major online learning platform facing competitive pressure from ChatGPT and other AI education tools, and is actively repositioning to emphasize its skills-first narrative and instructor expertise. This internal GrowthX sync between Kyle (leading the strategy) and Matthew (focused on content execution) came after an initial client kickoff with Udemy stakeholders and follows feedback from Jennifer at Udemy on audience targeting. The team is working toward a major strategic brief for Udemy leadership due within 48 hours, with plans to establish a regular cadence for alignment and move toward defining concrete content execution plans.
