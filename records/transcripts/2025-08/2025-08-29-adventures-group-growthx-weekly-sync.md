## Key Topics

### New Destination Pages Development

  - Marietta's Islands page nearly complete, praised for its visuals
  - Next destinations: Yelapa and Caletas (videos ready)
  - Strategy: Reproduce format across destinations (Vallarta, Cabo, Cancun)
  - Header videos crucial; only Sayulita's pending
  - Plan to interlink new pages with high-performing content (e.g., "cities to visit vacation destinations in Mexico")

### SEO Performance Update

  - Slight decline in impressions and clicks past week
  - Possible factors: competition, seasonality, volatility
  - Vallarta showing steady performance due to recent article launches
  - Cancun's performance heavily influenced by popular Sargassum article

### Content Publishing Challenges

  - Sanity CMS issue: links not preserved when pasting content
  - Potential solution: enable custom paste rules (requires developer intervention)
  - Time-consuming process of manually re-adding links post-paste

### Content Review Process

  - Need for Lucero to review articles before publication
  - Issues found: mentioning competitors (e.g., Rio Secreto) unknowingly
  - Aim: Maintain image diversity, avoid repetition in thumbnails

### Technical Updates

  - Schema plugin v1 published by Oscar
  - Carlos testing and implementing schema markup, starting with homepage
  - Instagram embeds may slow page load; suggestion to limit to 1-2 per article
  - New feature: Instagram feed can be added to Enriched Text for blogs (in beta)

### Tour Updates

  - Cancun: Adventure tours reopened (2 out of 4 original tours)
  - Food tours category removed; Taco Tour moved to "Bucket List" category
  - Isla Mujeres: Two tour options available, including private vessel rentals
