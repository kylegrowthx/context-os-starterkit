## Context

Braze is a leading customer engagement platform, and this call was a follow-up to earlier partnership discussions where GrowthX and Braze had explored a content marketing engagement. Braze had previously received feedback from references (including Airbyte) questioning whether GrowthX's maturity and approach were the right fit. This call was Kyle Gaudreau and Marcel Santilli's opportunity to directly address Braze's concerns and clarify how GrowthX's onboarding process works in practice.

The core issue blocking the deal isn't a content marketing strategy question—Hedy and Antonio confirmed GrowthX had answered those adequately. Instead, Braze faces genuine internal constraints: the content team is depleted (one person is on leave), they don't have a senior leader driving content ownership and learning models, and they're running 10 interviews per week to fill the role. Before taking on an external vendor, Braze wants to ensure the internal team is in place to execute. This is a realistic concern, and both parties recognized it's a timing and resourcing problem, not a partnership problem.

  - Actively interviewing (10 interviews/week) for senior content position

### GrowthX Onboarding Process

  - Week 1: 1-hour kickoff
  - Week 2: Shape writing guidelines, content strategy (\~1hr total + 30min review)
  - Week 3: Mostly aligned, start execution
  - Week 4+: Begin publishing content, calibrating quality
  - Time commitment: 1hr/wk for first 2 weeks, then as low as 30min/wk
