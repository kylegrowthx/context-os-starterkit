  - Actively interviewing (10 interviews/week) for senior content position
  - Hesitant to onboard new vendor without proper internal support
  - Roles open in New York and San Francisco

### GrowthX Onboarding Process

  - Week 1: 1-hour kickoff
  - Week 2: Shape writing guidelines, content strategy (\~1hr total + 30min review)
  - Week 3: Mostly aligned, start execution
  - Week 4+: Begin publishing content, calibrating quality
  - Time commitment: 1hr/wk for first 2 weeks, then as low as 30min/wk
