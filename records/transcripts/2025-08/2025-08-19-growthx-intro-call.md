### GrowthX's Offering

  - End-to-end AI-powered content partner
  - 2-phase partnership: 2-month strategy sprint + 12-month growth execution
  - Daily publishing (\~10 articles/week)
  - Expert team: Managing Director, Managing Editor, Content Manager
  - Dedicated Slack channel and weekly planning
  - Focus on organic growth via editorial content (not paid ad optimization)
  - AI workflows for efficient, accurate content creation

### GrowthX's Process and Technology

  - Deep initial strategy work: customer personas, market analysis
  - AI-driven content creation workflow: brief, research, drafting, fact-checking

  - Integration of visuals (charts, images) in content
  - Fact-checking against third-party sources with confidence scoring

### Pricing and Timeline

  - Growth execution: $12K/month

  - 4-6 week queue for strategy sprint kickoffs
