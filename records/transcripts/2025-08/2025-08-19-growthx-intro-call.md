## Context

Atto is a field service workforce management platform offering time tracking, scheduling, and payroll solutions, with roughly 60% of their customer base in the construction industry. They previously worked with siloed agencies (separate SEO, paid, and content vendors) but have since moved operations in-house with a lean team — a content person and content designer handling most marketing without dedicated scale or focus. Etnik, a decision-maker at Atto, reached out to GrowthX after seeing posts or materials online and was interested in exploring a partner who could provide integrated, multi-channel growth support. This call was an initial qualification meeting to assess whether GrowthX's content-first model could meet Atto's broader needs across paid lead gen, organic content, and channel optimization.
