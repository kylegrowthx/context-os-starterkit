## Context

This is a weekly internal standup within GrowthX's Strategy Sprint program — a structured cohort of client engagements running in parallel. Andi Bailey leads these standups to review delivery progress, client feedback, and blockers across all accounts. The team delivers B2B content marketing and SEO strategy, with clients ranging from startups (Understory, Alex AI) to established SaaS firms (Okta, Clerk, Outreach). Each client typically gets 1-2 dedicated team members plus specialist support. The meeting focuses on account momentum, content cadence, client feedback on quality and strategy, and dependency management.
