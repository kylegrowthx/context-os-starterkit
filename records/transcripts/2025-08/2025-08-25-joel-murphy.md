## Overview

- RoomBlocks aims to capture B2C group travel market (weddings, sports teams, etc.) separate from Engine's B2B focus
- Current growth is primarily paid traffic; goal is to build organic search presence for new domain
- GrowthX.ai proposed a strategy sprint to scope out content approach, blending editorial and programmatic content

---

## Key Topics

### RoomBlocks Context and Goals

  - Separate from Engine.com to target consumer group travel (weddings, sports teams, church groups)
  - Currently relies on paid traffic; aims to build organic search presence
  - Strong conversion rates on existing landing pages
  - Need to avoid conflict with hotel partnerships (can't list specific hotels)

### Content Strategy Ideas

  - Start with wedding-focused content, scalable to other industries
  - Blend of informational content, tools (e.g., calculators, checklists), and hotel listings
  - Potential for custom recommendation engines based on event descriptions
  - Leverage existing partnerships and data (e.g., hotels with meeting spaces, pricing info)

### Technical Approach

  - New domain: "RoomBlocks by Engine" or "powered by Engine"
  - Marketing pages as entry point, leading to product team-owned booking pages
  - GrowthX.ai can potentially build initial MVP experience

### Strategy Sprint Proposal

  - 4-8 week engagement to scope out and prove content approach
  - Blend of editorial and programmatic content, focusing on quality signals
  - Opportunity to build case for internal buy-in at Engine
