## Context

This was a brief internal standup between two GrowthX content strategists (Saskia Wartnaby and Ehtisham Hussain) working on the ProfitD account. ProfitD is a data analytics/workflow tool that GrowthX is delivering content strategy and article production for. The meeting was triggered by significant shifts happening at ProfitD's end: they're pivoting their go-to-market strategy away from data engineers and enterprise customers toward broader end-users (marketers, sales teams, anyone analyzing data), and simultaneously migrating from a Databricks-specific platform to Prophecy.ai. ProfitD is also facing internal chaos due to their CMO leaving and losing market differentiation as Databricks released competing features. The team needed to align on whether their existing content strategy still worked for ProfitD's new direction.
