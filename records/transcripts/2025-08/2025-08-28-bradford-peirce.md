## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX) met with Bradford Peirce (Okta Product Marketing) to explore how GrowthX can help Okta improve sales enablement for retail accounts. Bradford explained that customer identity is where Okta has the strongest retail differentiation—positioning it as a marketing and loyalty tool rather than just security—while workforce identity offers minimal retail-specific advantages except for retail floor workers (partnered with Oloid). The key challenge: SDR teams operate separately from product marketing, and there's significant drop-off at stage two of the sales pipeline where prospects learn Okta may not fit their needs. Bradford suggested that helping SDRs better understand and communicate the retail customer identity story could be valuable, and recommended Kyle reconnect with Rachel (who initiated the engagement) to clarify focus areas and potentially connect with Okta's SDR team directly.

## Context

Kyle Gaudreau is exploring how GrowthX can support Okta's go-to-market efforts around retail identity solutions.
