## Key Topics

### Okta's Identity Solutions for Retail

  - Workforce identity:
      - Limited industry-specific differentiation for retail
      - Exception: Retail floor workers solution (partnered with Oloid) for high-turnover, seasonal staff without dedicated devices
      - Potential to explore retail-specific application integrations
  - Customer identity:
      - More retail-specific differentiation and momentum
      - Positioning as marketing/loyalty tool rather than just security burden
      - Resonating well with recent customer accounts

### Okta vs Auth0 Positioning

  - Ongoing challenge within the company since February split
  - General guidance:
      - Sell Okta to IT and security teams
      - Sell Auth0 to developers and technical product teams
  - Separate sales teams for Okta and Auth0, unclear coordination at SDR level
  - Potential for conflicting outreach to same companies
  - Unclear process for inbound lead routing between products

### Sales Enablement Challenges

  - SDR team operates separately from product marketing
  - Significant drop-off at stage two of sales pipeline
  - Limited interaction between product marketing and SDRs
  - Opportunity to better equip SDRs with retail-specific customer identity story

### Potential Areas for GrowthX Assistance

  - Help SDRs understand and communicate the retail-specific customer identity story
  - Explore ways to reduce stage two drop-off in sales pipeline
  - Investigate retail-specific application integrations for workforce identity positioning
  - Clarify Okta vs Auth0 positioning for SDRs in retail context
