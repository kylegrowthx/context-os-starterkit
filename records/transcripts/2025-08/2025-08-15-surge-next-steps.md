## Summary

The GrowthX team debriefed a brand positioning workshop with SURGE and evaluated three iterations of brand pillars. The post-workshop version—five pillars including Momentum, Frontier, Precision, Talent, and Post-Training—emerged as the clearest and most differentiated, addressing core insights from SURGE's founders about their data-driven research model. ## Context

GrowthX is working with SURGE, an AI data labeling and research platform that sources highly trained researchers to deliver custom training data for AI models. The relationship is in active delivery phase, with GrowthX developing brand and positioning strategy. Earlier in the week, GrowthX conducted a full-day brand workshop with SURGE's leadership, including founder Edwin, to define brand pillars and positioning framework. This debrief call followed to synthesize workshop outputs and plan next steps before sharing refined work back to the client for async feedback. The team is working toward a final pillar set that SURGE will sign off on, which will anchor all downstream marketing content, messaging, and positioning work.
