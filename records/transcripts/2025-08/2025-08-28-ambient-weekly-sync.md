## Key Topics

### Article Feedback and Refinement

- Overall positive reception; tone and structure well-received by subject matter experts
- Key areas for improvement:
  - **PII and Privacy Stance**: Rephrase "who is holding the gun" to "is the weapon brandished" to avoid facial recognition implications. This aligns with Ambient's "privacy by design" positioning—focusing on the action, not identity of perpetrator.
  - **Acoustic Sensors**: Remove or downplay due to reliability issues (unreliable detection) and misalignment with preventive approach (gun would already be fired if acoustic sensors are the detection method). Not part of Ambient's recommended solution.
  - **Modern AI Capabilities**: Tone down emphasis on capabilities Ambient doesn't currently have. Avoid claiming capabilities that competitors may have or that overstate Ambient's current state.
- Minor stylistic adjustments in the first section (shortening bullet-list-style paragraphs to feel less AI-generated)
- Writing guideline updates will prevent these issues in future articles

### Content Publishing Process

- GrowthX handles staging in Ambient's CMS; requires a quick walkthrough video from Ambient showing category selection and staging details
- Hero images provided by GrowthX in two styles: simple graphic (logo + title) or AI-generated illustration background
- Publishing can be handled by GrowthX or left to Ambient for final approval check
- Header images can be updated later without SEO impact (purely illustrative, no ranking factor)

### Content Strategy and Timeline

- Phase 1 engagement: 8 weeks total, started early August, 4.5 weeks remaining as of this meeting
- Gun detection content lane launches next week (execution phase after calibration)
- Target length: 1,500–2,000 word articles. Shorter articles (800 words) may not rank in Google; longer pieces (3,000+ words) limit review capacity.
- Publishing cadence: 4–5 articles/week balances comprehensive coverage with manageable Ambient review workload
- SydneyGo (GrowthX editor) and Aida Knezevic (GrowthX account lead) managing content; Ambient adding internal team member to workflow

### AI Visibility Tracking (Scrunch)

- Scrunch dashboard (LLM/AI visibility index) live as of this week; 175 AI-generated prompts uploaded based on customer personas asking about gun detection
- Mechanism: Scrunch pings ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, etc. with prompts and tracks which companies appear in responses. Ambient currently showing as only cited company across available prompts (early data, competitors not yet appearing).
- Competitor list seeded incorrectly (unrecognized companies); Everest to provide accurate competitor list for better benchmarking
- Dashboard access limited to GrowthX email addresses; Aida to provide weekly screenshot insights and analysis during syncs
- Identified opportunity: Prompts based on real customer objections and sales calls can be added to track and inform content strategy

### Brand Presence on Review Platforms

- G2 and TrustRadius profiles are critical for LLM citations (Scrunch research shows LLMs reference these platforms heavily)
- Ambient's G2 profile status unknown (may not exist or severely outdated); TrustRadius status also unclear
- Opportunity: Update profiles with key differentiators (preventive approach, privacy design, real-time detection, school/venue security focus) to increase LLM citation likelihood
