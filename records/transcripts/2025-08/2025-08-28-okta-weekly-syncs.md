## Summary

Kyle, Sydney, and George from GrowthX worked with Rachael and Erick from Okta to align on account prioritization and tiering for both Okta and OddZero in the retail sector. The team agreed on a simple three-tier model based on signal percentage (70%, 50%, and under 50%) and decided to expand from OddZero-only targeting to comprehensive Okta products, starting with retail and expanding to other sectors. GrowthX will leverage its research capabilities beyond Clay to validate critical signals like security breaches and loyalty programs.

## Context

Okta is a GrowthX client working on account-based expansion targeting in the retail sector with their OddZero product. This meeting follows ongoing work to refine targeting and prioritization strategies. The team has established OddZero as their initial focus (with a two-month onboarding already one month in) and is now planning to expand simultaneously into Okta products to improve cross-selling opportunities. Rachael Tiow leads strategy and prioritization work on the Okta side, working closely with GrowthX's Kyle Gaudreau (research and workflow), Sydney Arin Go (research execution), and George Haikal (technical implementation). The urgency stems from Okta's goal to demonstrate clear, defensible reasoning to internal executives for account selection, moving beyond gut-feel decisions.

  - Develop a clear, justifiable method for selecting target accounts
  - Focus on key signals like loyalty programs, security incidents, and industry-specific factors
  - Aim to demonstrate process and reasoning to executives for strategic decisions

### Tiering System Implementation

  - Implement 3-tier system based on percentage of signals met:
      - Tier 1: 70%+ signals met
      - Tier 2: 50-69% signals met
      - Tier 3: \<50% signals met
