## Context

Prophecy, a data engineering and AI tool company, is in the midst of a strategic pivot away from a buy-committee-focused enterprise sales model toward product-led growth targeting individual, hands-on practitioners. Matt Turner (CEO) is leading this shift with aggressive timelines: launching a simplified "Express" offering immediately, rebranding from prophecy.io to prophecy.ai by September with a radical website redesign, and rolling out major product features (including "replays" that let users jump into real pipelines without registration) by end of October. GrowthX—specifically Darrell Etherington and Ehtisham Hussain—is embedded as the primary growth and content partner, responsible for tuning the pipeline, managing the SEO implications of the domain switch, and building out persona-targeted content for the launch. The relationship is strategic and ongoing, with plans to escalate the engagement once Prophecy hits growth-marketing phase in October.
