## Key Topics

### Express GTM Motion

  - Shifting to enterprise outward sales motion with compact, deliverable version under $50K/year
  - Focusing on hands-on users interested in achieving specific outcomes
  - Moving away from backend data engineering focus to target more hands-on, tool-savvy personas

### Prophecy.ai Launch

  - Planning to launch prophecy.ai in September (aggressive timeline)
  - Radically changing company look and positioning
  - Implementing "replays" feature allowing users to jump into real pipelines without registration
  - Aiming to erode friction between marketing site and product experience

### Content Migration Strategy

  - Considering how to maintain SEO value while pivoting to new positioning
  - Exploring options for archiving or redirecting old content
  - Planning to create new, persona-targeted content library for launch
  - Balancing CEO's desire for clean break with need to maintain valuable traffic

### Growth Marketing Plans

  - Shifting to growth marketing phase starting in October
  - Exploring community-building and rewards systems for engaged users
  - Considering how to leverage events and signals for more effective lead qualification

### Website Redesign

  - Planning major overhaul of website structure and content
  - Implementing one-page design with enterprise tag, features, and pricing
  - Focusing on showcasing "replays" and real product experiences
