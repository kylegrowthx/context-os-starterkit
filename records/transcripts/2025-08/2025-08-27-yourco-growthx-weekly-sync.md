## Summary

Yourco's SEO program is delivering massive 10x YoY impression growth (1.3M impressions), but conversion rates remain flat at 0.5% with only 7-8 leads/month — Kyle and Carrie diagnosed that newsletter CTA placement is too low on pages and should be repositioned higher or sticky to boost signups, while templates and call-off management content are performing best and should be prioritized for the fall launch. GrowthX and Yourco agreed to focus on refining content targeting, running CTA placement experiments, completing dashboard fixes, and preparing 1-2 survey templates to publish within two weeks while investigating why the call-off management page remains de-indexed.

## Context

Yourco is a B2B SaaS platform, and this is their weekly sync with the GrowthX content marketing team. Carrie Chowske (GrowthX delivery lead) runs the weekly performance reviews, sharing Search Console data and Looker dashboards that track SEO impressions, clicks, and downstream lead generation. Jessica Yip (Yourco's side) participates in content strategy planning and leads approvals on Yourco's end.

The core challenge is a classic top-of-funnel bottleneck: Yourco's content strategy generated exceptional visibility (10x growth YoY), but the downstream conversion machine isn't firing — leads have stalled at 7-8/month despite 1.3M impressions, and the self-reported attribution methodology likely underrepresents true SEO contribution. The GrowthX team believes the leverage points are: (1) improving CTAs and lead capture mechanisms on high-traffic content, (2) accelerating template-based content (surveys, call-off management guides), and (3) resolving a persistent indexing issue with call-off management content that's driving the most conversions.
