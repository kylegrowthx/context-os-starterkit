## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Overview

  - 4 main content buckets: AI threat detection, industry solutions, incident intelligence, competitive intel
  - Goal: Position Ambient as leader in physical security space
  - Mix of top and bottom-funnel content, targeting both search engines and LLMs
  - Aim to publish \~5 blogs/week once in full flow

### Campaign Approach: "Beyond Gun Detection"

  - 6-week sample campaign outlined
  - Weekly themes: foundation problem, solution overview, case studies, etc.
  - Need for integrated distribution strategy (social, email, SDR outreach)
  - Team to explore ways to automate/streamline content distribution

### Content Creation and Distribution Workflow

  - Current focus: Produce SEO-optimized content to build baseline
  - Will develop social/email distribution approach incrementally
  - Existing AI tool can convert blog snippets to LinkedIn posts
  - Need to develop email marketing capabilities (newsletter, SDR templates)

### Metrics and Goals

1.  Improve SEO rankings from current non-existent state
2.  Support SDRs with intent-based, personalized outbound content
3.  Group content into themed campaigns (e.g., gun detection, campus security)

### SEO Progress Tracking

  - Using SEMrush for keyword position tracking
  - Looker dashboard to visualize Google Search Console & Analytics data
  - Scrunch dashboard for LLM visibility measurement

### Content Illustrations

  - Currently capable of creating featured hero images
  - Team to explore options for in-article illustrations
