## Key Topics

### Hyperexponential's Market Position and Challenges

  - Historically sold within Lloyd's of London market, achieving 50% market share
  - Entering US market with more distributed customers (New York, Bermuda, Chicago, Philadelphia)
  - New competitors in US: Federado, Sitora, Earnix (strong SEO presence)
  - Average deal size: $1 million, with ~500 potential global accounts
  - Key personas: underwriters (P&L owners), actuaries (math support), IT/digital transformation leads

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

  - Develops comprehensive content strategies tailored to client needs
  - Uses AI-powered workflows for efficient content creation and optimization
  - Focuses on both SEO/AEO (AI Engine Optimization) and thought leadership content
  - Creates various content types: blog posts, newsletters, product pages, social media content
  - Emphasizes fact-checking and high-quality, industry-specific content

### GrowthX's Technology and Workflow

  - Transitioned from AirOps to custom-built platform for better scalability and flexibility
  - Uses multiple AI models and complex prompting for content generation
  - Implements fact-checking and quality control measures
  - Provides collaborative tools for client feedback and content approval

### Proposed Engagement Structure

  - 8-week strategy sprint ($20K)
      - Research and context gathering
      - Artifact creation
      - Content strategy development
      - Initial execution and proof of concept
  - Optional ongoing services at $9K/month after sprint completion

### Content Examples and Use Cases

  - Augment Code: SEO-optimized content leading to increased organic traffic
  - Biologica: Thought leadership content for healthcare supplements
  - Town.com: Detailed, fact-checked content for small business taxes

### David's Perspective on Timing and Approach

  - Initially expected consulting services, not a platform-based approach
  - Prefers iterative validation: "hack it" with 5-10 articles first
  - Wants to establish early wins before committing to systematic platform engagement
  - Open to engagement but wants to see proof of concept before major commitment
