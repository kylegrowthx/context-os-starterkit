  - Desire to increase B2B SEO content production
  - Need for converting webinars into blog posts for maximum impact
  - Limited content writer resources for nurture programs
  - Potential need for digital advertising artifacts
  - Concerns about content accuracy and alignment with product capabilities

### GrowthX's Engagement Model

  - 8-week strategy sprint ($30,000) followed by growth execution (starting at $15,000/month)
  - Typically produces 35 pieces of content in 8 weeks
  - Can handle both content refreshes and new content creation
  - Flexible in balancing refresh vs. new content based on client needs
  - Integrates with various CMS platforms, including Contentful

### Resource Requirements and Workflow

  - Initial weeks require 1-3 hours of client time for kickoff and deep dives
  - Ongoing engagement typically needs about 30 minutes per week from the client
  - GrowthX handles publishing, with options for manual or automated workflows
  - Can accommodate legal review processes by working in batches

### Pricing and Flexibility

  - Strategy sprint: $30,000 (one-time fee)
  - Growth execution: Starts at $15,000/month
  - 12-month total commitment (8-week sprint + 10 months execution)
