## Key Topics

### GEO Experiment Planning

  - Three main experiments in progress:
    1.  Reddit posting/seeding discussions (Monograph with Matthew, AugmentCode with Jason)
    2.  LLMs.txt file impact (Smith.ai — Joe and Megan merging Dave's version with Smith's existing file)
    3.  On-page components for increased citations (FAQ sections, TLDRs, exec summaries, tables of contents)
  - Critical design requirement: track baseline prompts and keywords before making any changes so you can measure before/after impact
  - Sample size varies by experiment: early-indicator signals may need just 5-10 blog posts, but formal experiments may require 30-40+ pages and 100+ tracked prompts
  - Aim to start rolling out experiments with customers in 1-2 weeks, pending experiment design and client selection

### Client Selection for Experiments

  - Criteria for selecting clients:
    1.  Willingness to participate and tolerance for uncertainty (not requiring guaranteed results)
    2.  Appropriate dev resources if experiment requires code changes (though text-based components may be better for crawlability)
    3.  Relevant content/website structure that matches the experiment (e.g., glossary pages for on-page component tests, published content for A/B testing)
  - Flagged client matches:
    - **School.ai**: Testing key takeaways placement (moving from bottom to top of posts); already rolling out programmatic content (300/month target); can test within different teacher-benefit clusters
    - **Smith.ai**: LLMs.txt file refinement and deployment
    - **Abnormal**: Large glossary page portfolio; good fit for on-page components experiment; expressed willingness to experiment
    - **Rapid**: Potential fit for experiments (mentioned by Mara)
  - Broader approach: first identify willing, healthy clients, then match them to specific experiments rather than designing experiments first

### SEO Experiment Considerations

  - Many experiments overlap between GEO and SEO; same framework and design rigor applies to both
  - Current blocker: SEMrush Enterprise setup for tracking (Jason to contact Marcel to unblock)
  - Alternative metrics can be used when SEMrush isn't available: GA4 for on-page experiments (time on page, traffic), publishing performance data for searchability
  - Scribe example: adding influencer video to blog post increased traffic and time on page, measurable in GA4 even without SEMrush Enterprise
  - Near-term focus is GEO; SEO experiments can follow once SEMrush Enterprise is unblocked and teams flag specific ideas

### Knowledge Sharing and Case Studies

  - Team recognizes need to surface and scale winning plays across the 50+ client portfolio; currently directors work in silos and repeat research
  - Goal to create a shared knowledge base of experiments, results, and playbooks so directors don't reinvent "zero to one" every time
  - Potential to use insights for external thought leadership and LinkedIn content to position GrowthX as GEO/SEO experts and attract new business
  - Split roles: Jakub handling strategy and hypothesis definition; Jason handling prompt tracking and consistent measurement; team flagging experiments and client opportunities
  - Future: automate repeatable processes (SEO table-stakes work via workflows) rather than relying on manual spreadsheets that teams won't maintain
