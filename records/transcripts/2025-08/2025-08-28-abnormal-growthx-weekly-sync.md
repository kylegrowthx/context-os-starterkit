## Key Topics

### Content Performance Metrics

  - Clicks increased by 27% week-over-week
  - Impressions grew from 49,000 to 52,000 (8% increase)
  - AI presence metric (via Scrunch) shows 18% comparative presence, 27% total presence
  - Team discussing how to interpret and utilize Scrunch metrics for benchmarking

### Content Refresh Strategy

  - 32 legacy articles (2021-2023) identified for refreshing
  - Focus on bottom-performing posts first, then those with potential to rank better
  - Process: Identify main keyword, analyze competitor content, preserve original voice (especially for opinion pieces)
  - Update publication dates for significantly revised content to boost SEO
  - Consider redirects for outdated content (e.g., old webinars, product updates)

### Refresh Workflow

  - Preserve original voice, especially for opinion pieces
  - Update images to align with current branding
  - For author-assigned posts, provide optimization suggestions rather than rewriting
  - Aim to refresh content every 6 months, with a 3-4 month initial performance evaluation period

### AI Presence and SEO

  - Correlation noted between Google rankings and AI mentions
  - Recent publication dates important for AI mentions
  - Team recognizes value of AI in speeding up content refresh process
