## Summary

Marcel Santilli (GrowthX CEO) and Garrett Kamps (Third Bridge co-founder) explored a strategic acquisition of Third Bridge by GrowthX to combine GrowthX's AI-powered content platform with Third Bridge's decades-old network of music and entertainment experts. GrowthX is raising a $40-60M Series B and looking for specialized domain expertise to open new verticals—music, film, podcasts, record labels—while Third Bridge is struggling to adapt to AI-driven content creation and exploring exit options. Both companies see significant synergy: GrowthX's 70%+ gross-margin platform could turbocharge Third Bridge's premium services, and Third Bridge's relationships with Apple, Spotify, Amazon, and Netflix could provide entry points for GrowthX's workflow engine across the entertainment industry. Next steps include deeper technical discussions with Third Bridge's CEO Sam, packaging strategy, and follow-up meetings after GrowthX completes pitch meetings with Series B investors.

## Context

Third Bridge, founded in 2013 by Garrett Kamps and Sam, is a niche B2B content agency specializing in music and entertainment expertise. They built a global network of hundreds of subject matter experts (former music journalists, industry veterans) and won flagship clients including Apple (their first client in August 2015), Spotify, Amazon, Sonos, and Netflix. Over the past 2-3 years, AI disruption has dramatically shifted their revenue model—clients who once needed 10,000 custom playlists now need QA and output validation for AI-generated content, and some clients (like Apple and Netflix) are either reducing engagement or building their own LLM pipelines. Garrett stepped back from daily operations about 18 months ago due to health issues (cancer), and Sam took over operations. When Garrett recovered, they began exploring strategic options, including M&A, because their business model is under pressure from AI adoption.

GrowthX is a 2-year-old AI-powered content services company founded by Marcel Santilli (former CMO at IBM, HP, HashiCorp, ServiceSight, ScaleAI, DeepGram) and a co-founder who previously led engineering at IFTTT. They hit $11M in revenue (all inbound), achieved profitability early, and are now raising a $40-60M Series B with Iconic and other investors. GrowthX operates a proprietary workflow engine that combines strategy, AI, experts-in-the-loop, and execution. Their clients (Webflow, Ramp, SentinelOne, Lovable) pay $15-200K/month for recurring content services. Gross margins are 70%+ despite human involvement in every engagement. Marcel views this call as an exploratory discussion about acquiring specialized domain expertise (music/entertainment) that could unlock new verticals and provide immediate access to high-profile client relationships.
