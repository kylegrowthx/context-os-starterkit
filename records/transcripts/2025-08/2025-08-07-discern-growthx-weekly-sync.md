## Context

Discern is a B2B SaaS platform targeting professionals who need to file regulatory filings and reports. GrowthX is executing a content marketing strategy for Discern, building out educational resource pages that rank in search results for state-specific deadlines and compliance requirements. This is a brand-building exercise focused on organic visibility and content quality over immediate lead conversion. The partnership is tracking content velocity (200+ pieces per week) and search performance metrics (clicks, impressions, CTR, average position) as primary success indicators. As traffic scales, the client is now interested in understanding conversion funnel performance—specifically how to turn organic search visitors into demo bookings.
