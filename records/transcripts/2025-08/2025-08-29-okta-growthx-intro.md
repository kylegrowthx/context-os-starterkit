## Summary

George Haikal from GrowthX introduced AI-powered account research and sales enablement tools to Cristian Munteanu, a Strategic SDR at Okta, to understand his research workflow and gather feedback on whether GrowthX's account briefs and call prep documents could streamline the research process. Cristian walked through his current manual process—analyzing company websites for authentication signals, using Sales Navigator to identify stakeholders across five buyer personas, and then using GEM AI to draft personalized cold emails—while George showcased examples of GrowthX's research output, including executive summaries, stakeholder mapping, and competitive intelligence. Both agreed that GrowthX's consolidated research could save significant time and be particularly valuable for junior SDRs learning what effective prospecting looks like.

GrowthX is working with Rachel (Okta's organization contact) to enable both the Auth0 SDR team and the broader Okta sales team. **To GrowthX Delivery:**
- Cristian's workflow identifies critical pain points in current SDR research: manually finding people who've used products at previous companies (high-signal leads but currently stumbled upon by chance), validating technology stacks without losing credibility, and searching for people with specific keywords in LinkedIn profiles that Sales Navigator misses. ## Key Topics

### Current SDR Workflow

  - Cristian uses a custom Excel sheet to track accounts, leads, and territory information
  - Research process involves:
    1.  Analyzing company website for multiple login portals (key signal for Auth0)
    2.  Searching Sales Navigator for ICPs (Ideal Customer Profiles)
    3.  Manually identifying stakeholders across 5 buckets: technology, marketing, developers, security, and business
  - Uses GEM AI tool for generating email outreach, but finds it somewhat rigid

### Pain Points in Current Process

  - Difficulty identifying stakeholders who have previously used Okta/Auth0
  - Sales Navigator's built-in filters for past company experience are inaccurate
  - Manual and time-consuming research process
  - Data quality issues for prospecting (e.g., obtaining correct email addresses)

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Solution

  - Comprehensive account briefs with executive summaries, stakeholder mapping, and tech stack information
  - Shorter, pre-call resources with talk tracks, objection handling, and email templates
  - AI-driven research process that consolidates information from multiple sources
  - Potential to save significant time in account research and prioritization

### Feedback on GrowthX Solution

  - Cristian found the executive summary and competition overview particularly useful
  - Emphasized the importance of tech stack validation to maintain credibility
  - Suggested expanding stakeholder profiles to match Okta's target personas
  - Noted the potential value for junior SDRs in setting expectations and best practices
