## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency running a strategic co-marketing event with Webstacks, a web development agency. This sync between Jason Gong (GrowthX Lead Events), Marissa Cordes (GrowthX Operations), and Jesse Schor (Webstacks) covers venue logistics, budget, guest list strategy, and marketing approach for an October event targeting high-intent marketing and agency decision-makers. The event serves dual purposes: brand awareness and lead generation with aggressive targets of 1-2 closed deals per company. Both companies see strong potential for partner referrals and social proof, particularly leveraging Webstacks' 10-12 existing clients and GrowthX's funnel of 50+ warm leads and 100-200 prospects.
