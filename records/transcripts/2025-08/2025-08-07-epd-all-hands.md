## Overview

- GrowthX is refining its Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) to focus on B2B companies needing high-volume, complex content
- The company is developing a new framework ("Flow") to make workflow creation more modular and flexible
- Several new features are in development, including a knowledge base, AI-assisted editing tools, and improved analytics

## Key Topics

### Company Strategy and ICP Refinement

  - Board meetings went well; investors are excited about GrowthX's positioning in the AI-driven marketing shift
  - Refining ICP to focus on B2B companies with high content needs, decision authority, and realistic expectations
  - Implementing ICP scoring across tools (HubSpot, Linear) to prioritize high-fit clients
  - Goal: Onboard clients quickly to strategy sprint and retain for at least 6 months

### Product Development Horizons

1.  GEO (Generative AI Optimization) tracking and adaptation
      - Developing a freemium GEO tracker product to help find more ICP-fit clients
      - Integrating SEO and GEO analytics within Atlas
2.  Efficiency improvements for content marketing service
      - Developing BI reports to identify time sinks and bottlenecks
      - Converting linear workflows to agent-based workflows for flexibility
3.  Future infrastructure development
      - Building AI Ops engineering infrastructure
      - Exploring potential for creating an ecosystem of shareable tools

### New Framework Development ("Flow")

  - Creating a modular, componentized system for workflow creation
  - Abstracting away complexities of underlying systems (e.g., Temporal)
  - Key modules: Flow Core, Prompt, LLM, Evaluator, and API
  - Improved developer experience with simpler syntax and reduced boilerplate

### Knowledge Base Development

  - Building a retrieval pipeline for private data content creation
  - Using Neo4j for knowledge graph storage
  - Features include markdown source processing, context retrieval, and graph-based querying

### AI Editor Improvements

  - Restructured TipTap editor for more flexibility and modularity
  - New features: resizable chat sidebar, automatic inclusion of client rules/guidelines, multi-editor support

### IDE Concept for Workflow Creation

  - Prototyping an IDE-like experience for creating and managing workflows
  - Features include workflow visualization, testing scenarios, prompt playground, and evaluation tools
  - Designed to be used by both human developers and AI agents
