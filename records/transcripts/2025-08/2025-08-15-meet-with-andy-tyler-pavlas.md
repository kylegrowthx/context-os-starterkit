## Key Topics

### Proposal Scope and Pricing

  - Initial proposal included 3 main content lanes: net new editorial, programmatic SEO, and content refresh
  - Original pricing was $330-350k annually, reduced from initial $40k/month for sprint and $30k/month for execution
  - GrowthX offered to remove programmatic SEO, bringing cost down to \~$18k/month
  - Redis currently spends $7-10k/month on an SEO agency and $2k/month on Profound

### Content Strategy and Execution

  - GrowthX aims to publish 5 pieces of content per week, separate from refresh efforts
  - Strategy includes both SEO and AI-driven content optimization (GEO)
  - GrowthX emphasizes daily content publishing and refreshing to stay competitive
  - Team includes experienced professionals like former TechCrunch editor and TripAdvisor SEO lead

### ROI and Performance Metrics

  - GrowthX provided projections based on keyword difficulty and potential traffic increases
  - Redis requested more concrete metrics on expected impact on traffic, leads, and revenue
  - GrowthX highlighted 1,100% brand visibility increase for client Augment Code in 9 weeks
  - Difficulty quantifying exact ROI due to evolving nature of AI-driven search (GEO)

### Redis's Current Position and Needs

  - Redis has 95% unaided brand awareness in their space
  - Need to grow organically in new AI-related categories (e.g., vector databases)
  - Current content team updating only \~5 pages per quarter
  - Large existing site with 1,300 pages needing refresh and optimization

### GrowthX's Value Proposition

  - Blend of AI-powered platform and human expertise for faster, more efficient content creation
  - Ability to handle high volumes of technical content at scale
  - Integrated strategy, execution, and optimization across SEO and AI-driven search
  - Potential cost savings compared to building equivalent in-house capabilities
