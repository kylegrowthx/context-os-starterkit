## Key Topics

### PagerDuty's Current AEO Efforts

### PagerDuty's Content Creation Process

  - 90% of content created internally, 10% outsourced

  - AI-powered agency model delivering "services as software"
  - 8-week strategy sprint (30K) followed by ongoing growth execution phase

  - Ability to create and refresh large volumes of content efficiently
  - Tracking and improving AEO/SEO metrics using tools like Scrunch

### Potential Collaboration Areas

  - Short-term focus on content production support
  - Possible assistance with tracking and reporting AEO/SEO metrics
  - Potential for expanded scope if initial engagement proves successful
