They raised from Indrecent in February 2025 and are at $20M run rate with 55-60% gross margins, targeting $60M EOY (seasonal business). The B2B2C model generates 90-95% of user acquisition from merchant partners (Nike, Peloton), with a 7% average take rate plus a new affiliate channel generating $30K/month.
