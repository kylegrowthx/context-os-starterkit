## Key Topics

### Content Refresh Strategy

The team initially debated the scope of content refreshes for older technical articles (mainly Strapi v4 and earlier docs). Paul flagged that fully updating code examples and screenshots to Strapi v5 would be extremely time-consuming. Vivek explained that GrowthX's standard refresh approach prioritizes freshness (Google's ranking factor), then UX/formatting, then SEO keywords. Mara reframed the question: could GrowthX deliver a lighter refresh to boost SEO while Strapi engineering decides which pieces merit deeper investment?

The consensus: lighter refreshes with SEO optimization, outdated content flags, and links to newer resources. Vivek will create a separate "redirect needed" status in the refresh table so Paul and Theo can prioritize which articles to rewrite versus redirect versus archive. This approach lets GrowthX move faster while preserving user experience and SEO value.

### New Content and Comparison Pages

Seven new content pieces are on track for delivery this week. Comparison pages (e.g., Strapi vs. Sanity) are bottom-funnel conversion tools. Theodore noted they lacked internal links to product features (Cloud, Marketplace). Vivek clarified the strategy: these pages sit low in the funnel where the goal is conversion, not navigation. Too many internal links risk users leaving before converting.

Final agreement: no external links, single strong CTA (Launchpad demo), placed once or multiple times for maximum impact. Strapi's backend team is finalizing publishing infrastructure. Once live, GrowthX will automate comparison page creation using an established template to scale delivery.

### Workflows and Automations

**Image generation:** Vivek needs Figma template/design guidelines. Theodore will add team@growthxlabs.com to the blog banner Figma template.

**Staging/formatting:** Strapi engineering is automating staging to fix formatting issues that have plagued recent deliverables. Safeguards now in place.

**Octave integration:** GrowthX gained access last week but is manually updating artifacts. Team needs an API key to automate. Mara interjected that Victor may have already shared it; engineering may just need to confirm key distribution. Golzar will check the Octave channel.

### Performance Report

**Overall trajectory:** Strapi is near all-time highs in traffic and conversions. Performance leveled off slightly over the past month, likely seasonal. No major ranking changes. Google is the top referral source.

**Non-branded impressions anomaly:** Vivek flagged a significant drop in non-branded impression data. However, clicks and positions are stable. This pattern has occurred on other GrowthX clients. Conclusion: Search Console data fluctuation, not a ranking issue.

**Conversions:** Pricing link clicks increased this past week. HubSpot demo form submissions dipped slightly week-on-week, but month-on-month they're up—good news overall.

**Content cohorts:** Integration pages leveled off with a 3.7% monthly dip. Other cohorts improving. LLM referrals holding steady. Paul flagged potential meta title/description gaps on integration pages; Vivek will provide concrete SEO recommendations by end of week.
