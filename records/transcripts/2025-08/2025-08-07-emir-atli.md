## Key Topics

### Implementation Process Overview

  - Week 1: Kickoff meeting with deep discovery questions
  - Weeks 1-2: Generate key artifacts, develop content strategy, start calibration
  - By Week 8: \~32 pieces of content published across defined topic clusters
  - Transparent workflow in Ocean platform, showing all interactions and progress
  - Time commitment from client: \~30 min/week + 30 min async checks

### Content Strategy and Output

  - Aim for daily publishing, including new and refreshed content
  - Topic clusters mapped to personas/audiences
  - Flexibility for additional workstreams (e.g., Airbyte's refresh lane for 5000 pages)
  - No strict content limit, but large projects may require trade-offs

### Competitive Research Approach

  - Identify competitive gaps through audience mapping and query monitoring
  - Analyze competitors' evergreen content and keyword rankings
  - Create comparison content with deep research on each topic
  - Strategic insertion of client's solution in content for quick LLM pickup

### Design and Implementation

  - Varies based on client needs and existing resources
  - Can provide best practices, full design, or work with existing design team
  - Examples: full design for VAPI, best practice recommendations for Interwell
  - Key elements: CTAs, Table of Contents, related content, author info, last updated

### Technical Setup

  - Webflow access needed; Enterprise version not required but helpful for multi-user editing
  - Automated publishing available (as drafts, with human QA)
