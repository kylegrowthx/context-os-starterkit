## Key Topics

### Content Creation Challenges

  - Difficulty in replicating the depth and perspective of engineer-produced content
  - Industry-specific terminology (e.g., non-human identities vs. credentials) causing confusion
  - Content often lacks Aembit's inherent perspective and voice
  - Previous attempts with technical contractors yielded unsatisfactory results

### Potential Solutions

  - Leverage internal resources:
      - Record engineer demos and all-hands meetings
      - Use these recordings to extract deeper technical insights and unique perspectives
  - Explore adjacent topics:
      - Identify issues of interest to the target audience beyond direct product promotion
      - Use social listening tools (e.g., Brand24) to inform content strategy
  - Improve communication:
      - More direct feedback on rejected topics
      - Increase proactive communication between GrowthX and Aembit teams

### Content Strategy Evolution

  - Shifted focus from "workload identities" to "non-human identities" based on industry trends
  - Now aiming to differentiate Aembit's approach within the non-human identity space
  - Balancing between owning the topic and exploring adjacent areas of interest to the audience

### Audience-Centric Approach

  - Goal: Create content that makes life easier for the target audience
  - Focus on solving challenges without overtly promoting Aembit's solutions
  - Identify and address topics that resonate with DevOps and security professionals dealing with non-human identities
