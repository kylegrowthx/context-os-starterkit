## Summary

GrowthX demonstrated an AI-powered SDR call brief workflow for Okta's retail segment that generates comprehensive account research, competitive intelligence, and cold-calling briefs in 30 minutes per account, pulling from internal artifacts, online sources, and LinkedIn. Rachael provided critical feedback on improving output (LinkedIn profile links, email vs. call focus, child brand research) and proposed a three-tier account classification system based on loyalty program presence, recent breaches, and fragmented SIAM systems to help SDRs prioritize efforts across 44 target accounts. The team agreed to run the workflow for initial accounts like Harvey Nichols, export outputs to Google Drive for Okta's review, and connect GrowthX with Okta's top-performing SDRs Nick Winkler and Christian for real-world testing and feedback.
