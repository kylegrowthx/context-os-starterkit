## Key Topics

### RAMP's Current SEO and LLM Optimization Challenges

  - 4-person SEO team (plus 1 open backfill) with traditional growth strategists
  - Brought in a full-stack engineer for LLM work, but they're temporary
  - Identified three key areas for improvement:
    1.  Winning in LLM visibility (currently doing "hacky stuff," technical optimizations, and content/external citations)
    2.  Optimizing site/funnels for bots and agents, not just humans
    3.  Improving traditional SEO performance against competitors like Brex

### GrowthX's Partnership and Achievements with RAMP

  - Partnership started in August 2022, now at 1-year mark
  - Published 100 articles and 100 refreshes over \~7 months
  - Success with vendor pages and custom workflows
  - Created per diems and expense categories content
  - Focusing on visibility across various platforms, including AI-powered search engines like Perplexity

### GrowthX's Strategy and Approach

  - Proposed "strategy sprint" to create a universal map/knowledge graph of audience interests
  - Focus on deep understanding of personas, including fears and uncertainties
  - Create content that mentions the brand naturally and adapts messaging for long-tail queries
  - Utilize a combination of AI-powered workflows and human review for content creation and fact-checking

### Content Creation and Optimization Techniques

  - Demonstrated success with Augment Code use case:
      - Created detailed, interactive content based on actual usage data
      - Achieved high click-through rates and time on page metrics
      - Content being cited in LLMs
  - Emphasis on personalizing content for specific use cases and industries
  - Importance of refreshing content regularly (ideally every 3 months) for improved visibility

### Potential New Initiatives for RAMP

  - Creating an industry-focused newsletter similar to CFO Club
  - Utilizing platforms like Beehiiv to acquire newsletter subscribers cost-effectively ($1-2 per subscriber)
  - Exploring opportunities for net new editorial content
  - Considering a separate content section (e.g., "/guides") for experimentation
