## Context

This is a weekly internal sync between Marcel Santilli (GrowthX founder) and Jason Gong (GTM lead) to align on go-to-market execution. The meeting focused on three operational areas: (1) swag and merchandise strategy for workshops and events, reflecting a broader effort to move away from generic corporate giveaways toward premium, memorable items that actually appeal to marketers and builders; (2) tactical promotion of an upcoming workshop, building on learnings from previous workshops that convert at healthy ratios (500 visits to 200+ registrants); and (3) building out the community and events infrastructure at GrowthX, specifically how to hire and structure roles to maximize community engagement and content leverage. The team is working within the constraint of limited bandwidth — Marcel carries much of the strategy and content creation work — and the underlying question is how to systematize and scale the community/content pipeline without losing quality or authenticity.
