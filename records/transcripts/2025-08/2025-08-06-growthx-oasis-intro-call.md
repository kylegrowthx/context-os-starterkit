## Key Topics

### Oasis Background and Challenges

  - Series A company with \~85 employees and small marketing team (1 PMM, 1 demand gen, 1 event manager)
  - Struggling to stand out in crowded market; everyone copying similar messaging
  - Current content efforts: weekly blog posts, but reactive and lacking strategic approach
  - Need help with content strategy, production, and brand differentiation

### GrowthX Offering Overview

  - End-to-end AI-powered content marketing service
  - Process includes creating company context artifacts, audience mapping, and content strategy
  - AI-assisted content production with human editing and quality control
  - Focus on LLM visibility factors (well-researched, attributed, structured content)
  - Tracking of SEO traffic, organic traffic, and LLM referrals

### Engagement Model and Pricing

  - 8-week strategy sprint for $20,000 (one-time fee)
  - Includes artifact creation, content strategy, opportunity identification, and calibration
  - Aim to publish daily content by week 5-6
  - Ongoing engagement after sprint (pricing not specified)

### Potential Concerns and Clarifications

  - Competitive conflicts: GrowthX uses separate pods and maintains strict information separation
  - Integration with existing tools (e.g., Ahrefs): GrowthX prefers to use their own platform for efficiency
  - SLAs and responsiveness: Highly responsive during work hours, balance between reactive and planned work
  - Content types: Focus on website content (evergreen, thought leadership, informational) rather than product announcements or presentations
