## Summary

GrowthX and Understory kicked off a strategic engagement to accelerate Understory's growth from $2.5M to $5-6M this year and $10M next year. Understory is a B2B SaaS-focused marketing agency (22 people) that blends go-to-market engineering with paid media—a unique differentiator in the market. The team is heavily reliant on LinkedIn for lead generation (~85% of pipeline) and needs GrowthX to help diversify demand channels, improve organic search rankings, and scale content production beyond the founders. George outlined GrowthX's eight-week strategy sprint approach and discovery methodology, while Ali and Alex walked through their service offerings, key clients (Rivial Security, Yofi, WorldPay, Expensify), competitive landscape (Cold IQ, Directive, Refine Labs), and growth challenges (capacity constraints, client reporting standardization, founder-dependent content).

## Context

Understory is a performance marketing agency founded by Ali Yildirim and Alex Fine (who have known each other for 15 years). The company specializes in paid media and go-to-market engineering, serving B2B SaaS companies with $20K+ ACV and established product-market fit. Their unique value proposition is blending modern GTM tactics (leveraging tools like Clay for audience building and outbound automation) with traditional paid media channels—an approach that resonates with technically sophisticated clients like Rivial Security, a cybersecurity company, and Yofi, a financial services platform. Ali brings deep ad tech experience (previously at Metadata, where he worked with custom audience databases), while Alex is a Clay expert who can orchestrate complex multi-channel campaigns. The agency has grown 8x year-over-year and is hungry to scale further, but current growth is bottlenecked by two factors: a heavy reliance on founder-led content and LinkedIn for pipeline generation, and capacity constraints (they've had to pause outbound work to focus on current client delivery). This is why they're engaging GrowthX—to develop a systematic content marketing and SEO strategy that can drive pipeline at scale.
