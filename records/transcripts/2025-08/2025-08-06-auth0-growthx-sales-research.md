## Key Topics

### Current SDR Workflow

  - SDRs manage inbound (QLs) and outbound leads for enterprise accounts (1,000-5,000 employees)
  - Daily tasks include reviewing QL reports, account mapping, and coordinating with AEs
  - Research involves examining 10Ks, social posts, and using tools like Gemini and ZoomInfo
  - Sixth Sense used for buying intent, but considered "funky" and not entirely reliable

### Pain Points and Challenges

  - Manual research is the most time-consuming and challenging aspect of the SDR role
  - Information overload: Wealth of knowledge available, but difficult to navigate and find relevant data
  - Need for better automation in gathering and presenting account information
  - Lack of efficient signal-based tools for tracking buying intent and account focus

### GrowthX AI-Powered Solution

  - Developed custom artifacts for Auth0's retail segment, including persona documents and call prep guides
  - Created a comprehensive call brief example for Jersey Mike's, showcasing potential AI capabilities
  - Proposed solution includes detailed account information, discovery questions, value props, and objection handling
  - Feedback: Information is valuable but needs to be more concise and tailored to SDR-specific needs

### Improvement Suggestions

  - Focus on creating email sequences with discovery questions for SDR use
  - Develop separate resources for cold calling and account overviews
  - Incorporate competitor information and Auth0 customer success stories
  - Integrate past interaction data from Salesforce CRM
  - Align content with SDR stage criteria (stage one and two goals)
