## Summary

Marcel Santilli (GrowthX CEO) presented GrowthX's AI-augmented content creation model to Gur Singh and Amanda from CodeRabbit, a developer-focused code review platform facing content production bottlenecks. CodeRabbit needs immediate capacity for technical content but is concerned about AI-generated quality and the 4-6 week lead time for GrowthX kickoffs. GrowthX offers an 8-week $20k strategy sprint followed by $12k/month growth execution with dedicated 4-6 person pods, leveraging AI for research and initial drafts while keeping human editors in control—a model they've successfully applied to technical companies like Augment Code, Sentinel One, and Strapi, delivering 1000%+ LLM visibility gains.

## Context

CodeRabbit is an early-stage developer-focused platform in the code review and dev workflow space, founded by Gur Singh (technical founder leading go-to-market). The company has been growing rapidly but faces a critical content gap—Amanda, their Head of Content, joined three months prior but is already overextended managing not just traditional content strategy but also higher-level initiatives like video production. CodeRabbit's content strategy is content-led with a strong emphasis on developer-written technical guides (demonstrated by their high-performing CoPilot guide with 20+ screenshots), but they lack the execution capacity to scale. The introduction came through mutual contacts in the industry. GrowthX reached out to explore a content partnership that could fill CodeRabbit's immediate capacity gap while proving value in the first 8 weeks before committing to longer-term growth execution.
