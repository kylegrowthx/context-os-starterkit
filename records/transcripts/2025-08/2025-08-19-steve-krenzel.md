## Summary

Steve Krenzel, CEO of Logic (a 5-person process automation startup), aligned with GrowthX on a partnership to launch their content marketing and GTM strategy ahead of a Series A raise in early 2026. GrowthX proposed an 8-week strategy sprint ($20k) followed by ongoing monthly execution ($9k/month), positioning content as a cost-effective alternative to hiring internal teams or multiple agencies. Steve committed to signing the Statement of Work immediately to secure a September 8th start date, which gives his new head of growth (joining September 2nd from dbtlabs) one week to onboard before GrowthX begins full execution.

## Context

Logic is a 5-person Seattle-based startup that automates business processes from SOPs or natural language descriptions, generating UI, test cases, and integration documentation automatically. The company raised a seed round last year and is now preparing for go-to-market launch with roughly 20 white-glove onboarded customers. Steve Krenzel, the CEO and founder, is a repeat founder (previously at Brex running office of CTO and at Convoy in shipping logistics) with deep experience in operations and LLM integration. The meeting was triggered by two previous conversations between Logic's Growth Lead Kyle and GrowthX, plus independent recommendations from Connor at Galileo and Sudivy, who both praised GrowthX's work. This was the first call between Steve and the GrowthX team (Marcel and Tyler) to move a preliminary discussion into a concrete partnership proposal.
