Bubble required more specific language about Phase 1 outputs before moving to Phase 2 since they cannot cancel in Phase 2, and GrowthX agreed to revise the SOW to clarify cross-platform strategy considerations (forum, YouTube, Reddit) while committing to producing at least 5 articles per week during execution. ## Context

Bubble is a low-code/no-code platform that enables users to build web applications without coding. GrowthX is engaged in a two-phase engagement: a $20k Strategy Sprint in September 2025 followed by a $180k Growth Execution phase in November 2025. This SOW sync meeting represents the strategy sprint phase where both teams are aligning on Phase 1 deliverables and expectations. Bubble has significant internal procurement requirements and cannot proceed without finalized language in the SOW, as their process involves finance, compliance, and legal reviews before kickoff.
