## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

  - Uses audience-first approach, mapping pain points and jobs-to-be-done across service areas
  - Creates custom knowledge bases and artifacts to capture company context and expertise
  - Employs agentic research tools for comprehensive, high-quality content creation
  - Adapts content for different audiences while maintaining consistent brand voice
  - Focuses on both SEO and AEO (AI Engine Optimization) to appear in AI-generated answers

### Rimkus's Content Challenges

  - Strict language guidelines due to adjacent litigious industry
  - Need for technical accuracy and compliance in content
  - Diverse service areas with potentially conflicting client needs
  - Current piecemeal SEO efforts and limited internal bandwidth
  - Multiple voices across existing website content

### GrowthX's Engagement Model

  - 8-week strategy sprint ($20,000) to set up workflows, calibrate, and start publishing
  - Ongoing growth execution starting at $12,000/month
  - 100% conversion rate from strategy sprints to full engagements in last 3 months
  - Collaborative approach, integrating with client teams and experts

### SEO/AEO Performance Metrics

  - Rimkus's current domain authority: 34/100
  - Significant opportunity to grow non-branded traffic
  - GrowthX tracks metrics like average visits per page, brand lift, and LLM referrals
  - ROI model considers content volume, peak traffic, conversion rates, and LTV

### Technical SEO Considerations

  - GrowthX focuses on foundational issues: site speed, core web vitals, site map updates
  - Will provide audits and recommendations but not full web development services
  - Emphasis on creating valuable content assets and monitoring performance signals
