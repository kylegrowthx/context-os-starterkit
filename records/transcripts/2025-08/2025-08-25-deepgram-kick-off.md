## Key Topics

### Current Business Priorities and Challenges

  - Drive more relevant organic traffic to the website
  - Increase organic console signups for PLG program (currently \~6,500/week, 85,000 run rate)
  - Improve SEO and GEO performance
  - Boost overall awareness in the voice AI space
  - Low conversion rates from signups to PQLs/SQLs/new logos
  - Increased competition from new players (e.g., Eleven Labs, Mistral)

### Product and Market Positioning

  - Three core products: Nova 3 (speech-to-text API), Aura 2 (text-to-speech API), and Voice Agent API
  - 90% of ARR from speech-to-text, gaining traction in text-to-speech
  - Key differentiators: low latency, accuracy, cost-effectiveness, enterprise-grade scalability
  - Target market: AI-native companies and technology firms building voice solutions
  - Competitive edge: More enterprise-oriented, offering self-hosted deployments and optimized latency

### Use Cases and Customer Segments

  - Top industries: Healthcare (30%), Call Centers (20%), Sales, Insurance
  - Notable use cases: medical transcription, call summarization, sales coaching, claims processing
  - Key customers: CVS (via CoreAI), Jack in the Box (pilot), Florida Blue
  - Open-source frameworks integration: LiveKit, PipeCat

### Content and Marketing Strategy

  - Product content (tutorials, launches) performs well
  - Regular publishing of SEO-focused blog posts
  - Two newsletters: AI Minds and Deepgram-specific
  - Competitive ad campaign against Eleven Labs (1,200 signups/week)
  - Opportunities: partner content, guest posts, gated content, technical content from internal experts

### Areas for Improvement

  - Lack of developer relations team
  - Limited technical content from internal experts (PMs, engineers, founders)
  - Need for more active social media presence
  - Opportunity for comparison content (e.g., Granola vs. other note-takers, Eleven Labs vs. OpenAI)
