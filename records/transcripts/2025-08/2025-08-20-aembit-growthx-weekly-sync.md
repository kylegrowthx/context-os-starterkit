## Key Topics

### Team Transition and Account Management

Darrell Etherington is departing GrowthX at the end of the week after managing Aembit's account. Kyle Gaudreau, who has been at GrowthX since October in various roles (client services, sales), is taking over. Kyle brings 20+ years of digital growth experience and a background focused on building organic channels and topical authority. Ehtisham Hussain will continue as the execution lead, ensuring continuity. Kyle emphasized the need to understand Aembit's account deeply and offered weekly 1:1s with Dan Kaplan to establish strategy and alignment.

### Content Performance Metrics

Analytics show strong momentum on the blog. Average position improved to 16.74 from 17.91 (tracked over weeks 32-34), now at best all-time performance. The team is tracking position as the North Star metric, with a goal of reaching below position 10 to capture more high-ranking placements. Impressions and clicks are on an upward trajectory, though the click-through rate remains stable at 0.41%. Technical how-to articles continue to be the primary traffic drivers. Kyle noted the importance of tracking broader organic metrics beyond individual article performance, including topical authority contribution and brand search growth.

### Content Strategy and Asset Repurposing

The team discussed a hybrid approach combining volume-based content with handcrafted pieces. Aembit has on-demand webinars and videos that could be repurposed into SEO-optimized articles. Kyle proposed a pillar-and-cluster model where Aembit publishes broader educational pieces with deeper supporting articles underneath, potentially done in collaboration with GrowthX's content teams. Darrell and Kyle also discussed repurposing the "five trends" webinar content that previously performed well. The strategy acknowledges that while individual repurposed pieces may not outperform handcrafted content one-to-one, the aggregate volume could exceed the performance of handcrafted articles alone at lower cost.

### Frontier Data Challenge

A key discussion point is Aembit's position in an emerging market where not much public discussion exists around the framing they're using. Kyle suggested exploring whether all avenues for sourcing content have been exhausted, and proposed leveraging LinkedIn and Reddit as research sources for AI identity and AI security discussions. Kyle offered to explore Reddit using tools like Perplexity to identify emerging topics and community discussions that could inform content strategy. Apurva noted seeing significant discussion on LinkedIn around AI identity and security but acknowledged the difficulty of sourcing LinkedIn content directly.

### Q4 Product Launch Planning

Aembit is planning to launch an AI identity security product late in the year. Apurva emphasized building topical authority pre-launch as a key goal, positioning Aembit as a thought leader in AI security before the product becomes available. She plans to invest significant budget in interviews, podcasts, and videos that can be repurposed into blog content, webinars, and other assets. Apurva offered to draft an initial positioning document for the product (still in development) to guide content planning. She also clarified that competitive positioning is secondary to establishing Aembit's fundamental association with AI security.

### NHICON 2026 Event

Aembit is planning NHICON 2026, a late-January AI-focused event (following the successful NHICON event last January that had 1000+ registrations and 150+ attendees from quality organizations). The team discussed how to build content momentum around the event both before (pre-seeding) and after (repurposing). Apurva sees significant time and budget allocation for this event and wants GrowthX to explore content strategies that support event promotion and leverage event content for ongoing authority building.

### Publishing Process and Automation

The team discussed a transition in publishing responsibilities. Currently Ehtisham handles publishing, but GrowthX is working to automate the publishing process and hand it off to an internal Aembit resource to streamline the workflow.
