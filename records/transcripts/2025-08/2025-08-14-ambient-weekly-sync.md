  - Migrating from Strapi to Webflow CMS
  - Need for composable CTAs and improved user experience
  - Opportunity to implement best practices for modern websites

  - Using Atlas platform for content generation
  - Workflow includes brief generation, research, outline, draft, fact-checking, and meta tags
  - Potential for automation with Strapi CMS integration

### SEO and Competitive Landscape

  - Competitors like ZeroEyes have strong SEO presence
