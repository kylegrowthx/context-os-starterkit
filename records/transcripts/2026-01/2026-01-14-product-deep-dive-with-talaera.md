- **Strategic Pivot:** Talaera is shifting from a services-first model to a software platform, with the new AI coach, "Tali," as the key differentiator.
- **Tali's Edge:** Tali is trained on Talaera's proprietary 900-point communication framework, derived from tens of thousands of hours of real coaching data. This makes it a highly specialized, business-focused tool, unlike generic AI.

## Key Topics

### Strategic Pivot: Services → Software

  - Talaera is repositioning as a software platform at the intersection of language, culture, and business communication.
  - **Goal:** Combine AI efficiency with human coaching, framing it as a "GrowthX model" for language learning.
  - **Product Stack:**
      - **Software:** E-learning catalog, Tali AI coach, HR analytics dashboard.
      - **Services:** 1-on-1 coaching, group courses, workshops, webinars.

### Product Deep Dive: Platform & Features

  - **English Level Test:** A free, adaptive 10-minute diagnostic for organizations of any size (e.g., 10,000 employees).
  - **Speaking Club:** Live, low-pressure group practice sessions several times a week.
  - **E-learning Catalog:** Searchable content on business topics (Meetings, Confidence) and culture (e.g., U.S. business norms).
  - **1-on-1 Coaching:**
      - **Matching:** A human-curated process uses an algorithm to recommend a top 5 list of teachers based on learner background, role, and goals.
      - **Rationale:** This ensures a strong fit, resulting in a very low teacher-switch rate.

### "Tali" AI Coach: The Differentiator

  - **Core Function:** An AI coach for practicing business conversations in a safe, low-pressure environment.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Multilingual Support:** Switches languages on demand for recaps (e.g., Bosnian demo was successful).
      - **Integrated Ecosystem:** Creates exercises, adds terms to a personal dictionary, and maps progress against the 900-point skill framework.
  - **Proprietary Training:** Tali's effectiveness comes from being trained on Talaera's unique 900-point communication skills framework, which was built from tens of thousands of hours of real coaching data.
  - **Usage Limit:** 60 minutes/month.
      - **Rationale:** This is the recommended time for optimal results (3x 5-min sessions/week), not an arbitrary cap. It also manages costs for an expensive AI model.

### B2B Value: HR Dashboard & Analytics

  - **Primary Benefit:** Provides L\&D leaders with autonomy to manage their team's learning.
  - **Key Capabilities:**
      - **Session Reallocation:** Move coaching sessions between employees to ensure budget efficiency and reward engagement.
      - **Detailed Reporting:** Monthly reports on engagement, content usage, and learner progress.
  - **Account Support:** Dedicated account managers provide same-day responses, a key differentiator from competitors like Preply.

### B2C Strategy: Building Trust & Community

  - **"Momentum" Plan:** A cost-effective, scalable subscription offering Tali AI, the Speaking Club, and the e-learning catalog.
  - **Core Narrative:** The combination of Tali (safe practice) and the Speaking Club (real-world connection) builds confidence and trust.
  - **Community Appeal:** Highlighting the professional network (e.g., managers from Microsoft) attracts aspirational learners.
  - **Persona Focus:** The current priority is B2C managers at a B2 level who want to project authority. The long-term goal is to expand to other segments (analysts, junior roles).
