## Key Topics

### Competitor Analysis & Market Gaps

  - Analysis of \~80k keywords across 6 competitors (Eleven Labs, Sierra AI, Cognigy, Decagon AI, Core AI, Poly AI) informed the strategy.
  - **Key Findings:**
      - **Sierra AI:** Focuses on thought leadership and high-profile case studies.
      - **Decagon AI:** Owns the glossary space. Parloa can compete directly, as its Domain Authority (35) is close to Decagon's (39).
      - **Cognigy:** Positions itself as the enterprise leader, leveraging Gartner recognition and competitor comparisons.
      - **Core AI:** Differentiates with deep technical content.

### Proposed Content Strategy & Topic Clusters

  - **1. Conversational AI & Voice Tech**
    
      - **Goal:** Establish Parloa as the trusted educator, reinforcing its voice-first differentiator.
      - **Narrative:** Most vendors start with chat; Parloa is built voice-first for superior quality.
      - **Status:** Pending CEO approval. Dora will present data showing high search volume (\~250k/mo) to make the case.

  - **2. Agentic AI & Technical Thought Leadership**
    
      - **Goal:** Position Parloa at the forefront of agentic AI and demonstrate technical expertise.
      - **Audience:** CX leaders, transformational AI leaders, and product teams.
      - **Keywords:** Agentic AI, multi-agent, AI agent architecture, human in the loop.

  - **3. Enterprise Contact Center Transformation**
    
      - **Goal:** Position Parloa as the partner that scales AI from pilot to production, avoiding "failed AI pilots."
      - **Audience:** CX leaders and heads of AI.
      - **Keywords:** Enterprise AI platform, contact center automation, scaling agentic AI.

  - **4. Operational Metrics & Glossary**
    
      - **Goal:** Own operational metric conversations to show deep CX/contact center understanding.
      - **Format:** Glossary-style content (Phase 1 articles, Phase 2 dedicated glossary).
      - **Benefit:** Creates internal linking opportunities to boost SEO.

  - **5. Industry Vertical Solutions**
    
      - **Goal:** Demonstrate deep expertise within specific verticals.
      - **Rationale:** Hyper-specific content drives higher-intent traffic.
      - **Keywords:** AI voice agent in healthcare, AI for insurance, retail AI solutions.

  - **6. Competitive Comparisons & Alternatives**
    
      - **Goal:** Own the conversation for middle/bottom-of-funnel buyers in the consideration phase.
      - **Rationale:** High-intent traffic, even with lower search volume.
      - **Action:** Create SEO-optimized versions of paid-only comparison pages (e.g., CIRA).

### Paid & Organic Strategy Integration

  - **Rationale:** Tightly integrated paid and organic efforts are more effective and cost-efficient.
  - **Organic → Paid:** Create content supporting paid landing pages to improve their SEO authority and Google Quality Score.
  - **Paid → Organic:** Reallocate budget from expensive keywords to SEO, reducing overall CAC.
