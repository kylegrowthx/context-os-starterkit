### New Content Strategy: Blog vs. Education Hub

  - **Rationale:** The new strategy addresses past internal blockers by creating a clear path for different content types.
  - **Two-Track Model:**
      - **Education Hub:** High-volume, broad content (e.g., K-12 cybersecurity) for general traffic.
          - **Review Process:** Lighter editorial review.
          - **CTAs:** Contextual links to relevant Abnormal pages (e.g., industry solutions).
      - **Blog:** Targeted, technical content for ICPs.
          - **Review Process:** Thorough review by Emily and Kali.
          - **CTAs:** Stronger, direct calls to action (e.g., "Get a Demo").
  - **Action Item:** Jo will draft initial definitions for "Blog" vs. "Education Hub" content for team iteration.

### CMS Migration & Content Tagging

  - **Interim Solution:** An "EDU" tag will be added to all GrowthX content in the CMS to prepare for the future "Education Hub" migration.
  - **Migration Plan:** Meaghan is deciding whether to migrate all content and then unpublish, or to filter first.
  - **Unpublished Content:** Emily will work with Kali to create a definitive list of content to be permanently unpublished vs. content to be republished under the new "EDU" tag.

### FY27 Strategic Alignment

  - **Key Initiatives:**
      - **Public Sector Growth:** Support the goal of 100% YoY revenue growth in the Public Sector (K-12, Fed/State, Higher Ed).
      - **New Industry Pages:** Create content to support the launch of new "Solutions by Industry" pages (e.g., Education, Retail/E-commerce, Healthcare).
      - **Product Focus:** Support new products like "AI Phishing Coach."
  - **Content Cadence:** Publish content supporting these initiatives *before* integrated campaigns launch to build topical authority and drive baseline traffic.

### Webinar Repurposing & Lead Gen

  - **Traffic Projections:** For planning purposes, Abdallah can model a 5–10% traffic capture rate from the total search volume of webinar topics.
      - **Timeline:** Expect a 3-month lag for new content to rank, with results becoming visible by the end of Q2.
  - **Lead Gen Experiment:** Begin experimenting with stronger, contextual CTAs in high-intent articles (e.g., competitor comparisons) to test lead generation.
  - **Web Team Handoff:** The primary goal remains driving high-volume traffic to put pressure on the web team to improve on-site conversion paths (e.g., pop-ups, chat).
