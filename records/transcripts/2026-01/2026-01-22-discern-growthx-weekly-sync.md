### New Tool: "CheckThat"

- **Purpose:** GrowthX's proprietary AI visibility tool, developed in-house to replace Scrunch for future client reporting.
- **Focus:** "Evaluation stage" LLM prompts—i.e., queries where users ask LLMs to compare options, identify best-fit solutions, or evaluate trade-offs. These prompts drive actual user decision-making.
- **Key Advantages:**
  - **Historical Data:** Provides 90 days of historical data immediately upon account activation. Scrunch requires weeks of waiting for data to accumulate.
  - **Data Depth:** More granular, focused data collection on evaluation-stage prompts is expected to yield more actionable insights.
  - **Custom Prompts:** Supports custom prompt libraries (all Scrunch prompts have been ported over).
  - **Open Data:** Some data will be visible without login, allowing the knowledge base to grow as more users contribute.
  - **Client Access:** Scheduled for early February rollout.
