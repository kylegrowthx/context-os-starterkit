## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Data Needs

- GrowthX is evaluating data vendors for a new initiative on AI visibility metrics (mentions, citations, coverage across LLMs).
- **Required Data**:
  - AI mentions and citations across ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, and other models
  - Keyword and search volume data
  - Internal search queries (query fanout) that LLMs use to generate answers
- **Key Evaluation Criteria**:
  - AI platform coverage (ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, etc.)
  - Data collection methodology (panel, probing, or network-based)
  - Pricing and licensing for embedding and redistributing data to end customers

### Bright Data's Solution

- **Scale & Compliance**:
  - Scrapes approximately 2.5 petabytes of data daily—only Google scrapes more.
  - Strict compliance (no login screens, transparent terms of service) has survived legal challenges from Elon Musk and Facebook.
  - Operates as the world's largest proxy network with contracted devices for reliability and compliance.
- **Technical Foundation**:
  - All tools are API-based, charged per successful record (not 404 errors).
  - Built on contracted proxy network where every device is contracted for reliability.
  - Offers four tiers of capability: pre-built APIs, browser APIs and unlockers, IDE environment for custom scrapers, and managed service.
- **Tooling Options**:
  - **Pre-built APIs**: For popular sites (ChatGPT, Claude, Amazon, search engines).
  - **Browser APIs & Unlockers**: To bypass CAPTCHA and anti-bot blockers on sites without dedicated APIs.
  - **IDE Environment**: For building custom scrapers (customer manages maintenance as sites change).
  - **Managed Service**: Bright Data can build and maintain custom scrapers for a list of sites.
- **Positioning for AI**:
  - 70% of Bright Data sales calls are now AI-related use cases.
  - Largest AI models in the world are using Bright Data for training and public data sourcing.
  - Query fanout feature is in active development due to high customer demand.

### Licensing & Pricing

- **Licensing**: GrowthX owns all scraped data in perpetuity with full rights to redistribute to end customers without additional licensing fees or restrictions.
- **Pricing**:
  - Standard rate card: $1.50 per 1,000 successful scrapes (not per attempt).
  - Flexible pricing for embedded use cases negotiated based on customer volumes and profitability scenarios.
  - Pricing can be adjusted downward if Bright Data understands the downstream economics and customer profitability.

### Feature Gap: Query Fanout

- **Need**: Track the internal search queries an AI model makes to generate its answer (e.g., "best golf clubs" → ChatGPT searches Google for "best golf clubs," "cheapest golf clubs," "most forgiving golf clubs," then synthesizes results).
- **Status**: Not currently available but actively in development.
- **Demand**: 70% of Bright Data sales calls involve query fanout requests—highest customer interest signal in the company's portfolio.
- **Potential Impact**: Critical for understanding LLM bias, training sources, and competitive positioning in AI visibility metrics.
