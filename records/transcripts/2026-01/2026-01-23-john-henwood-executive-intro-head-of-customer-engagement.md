- **Drafting workflows:** Content generation from research outputs
- **Fact-checking workflows:** Validation layer on generated content
- **Image generation:** Article images and infographics (e.g., Lovable examples shown)

**Operational model:** All platform operation is currently done by GrowthX team (fully-managed service). Customers do not directly access platform interface; they receive curated demos and fully-managed outputs.

**Technical foundation:** Coding agents as core abstraction (Daniel's conviction: "code is the best abstraction; it's what technology is built on"). Agentic framework with runtime infrastructure, full observability, same architecture Daniel built at IFTTT.

**Platform evolution question (current, next 6 weeks):** Decision on go-to-market model: (1) platform-only sales? (2) platform with required managed service? (3) hybrid options (managed vs self-serve apps based on customer maturity)? John's Writer experience is directly relevant: Writer sold both "managed apps" (hands-on customer collaboration) and "self-serve apps" (customer-led usage) depending on customer sophistication and preference.
