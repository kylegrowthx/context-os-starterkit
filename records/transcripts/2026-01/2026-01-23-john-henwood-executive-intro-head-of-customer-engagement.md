## Key Topics

### John's Background

Four-time customer success leader focused on scaling teams through growth stages (typically Series B to late growth). Career path: account management → senior IC at Hootsuite → manager → director → head of North American enterprise CS. Then built 0-to-1 CS functions at Productboard, Writer, and Pocus.

Key achievements:
- Hootsuite: 30 CSMs, $100M+ ARR, commercially consultative org
- Productboard: Scaled from $5M to $55M ARR in 3 years, team of 3 to 45
- Writer: 15x revenue in 18 months, team of 3 to 50, Fortune 100 clients (KPMG, Accenture, United Health)
- Pocus: Sold into his own persona for the first time

### GrowthX Business Model

Started from Marcel's "24x traffic in 6 months" success at Deepgram using AI workflows + human editors. First workshop had 33 attendees, two became customers. Initial insight: customers wanted three things bundled:
1. Fractional CMO strategy/advisory
2. AI content workflows (not DIY tools)
3. Last-mile human editing and publishing

Combined all three for $9K/month (10¢/word vs $2-3/word traditional). Hit $1.5M revenue while Marcel was still CMO. Quit September 2024. Partnered with Daniel (ex-IFTTT head of product/engineering) who built the runtime infrastructure.

### Platform Capabilities

The platform runs agentic workflows including:
- Research supervisor (ran 83 minutes on one topic)
- Drafting workflows
- Fact-checking workflows
- Infographic generation
- Article image generation

Currently operated internally. Evolution question: sell platform-only, platform + required managed service, or hybrid options.

### Financial Position

- $12M ARR, ~70% gross margin
- Cash flow positive last 2-4 months
- $12M+ in bank, $10M credit line
- Burned only $3.5M while growing from $2M to $12M
- Series A at conservative valuation (low exercise costs)
- Target: $12M → $36M ARR in 2026

### Market Opportunity

Marcel's TAM calculation: Semrush has 25,000 enterprise/mid-market customers paying ~$40K/year for a tool that just reports rankings. Those are exactly GrowthX's target customers. 25,000 × $120K ACV = $3.2B ARR.

Vision: Websites will be 100x more valuable because they influence AI search, deep research, and agents. Companies will go from 1,000 pages to 10,000 to 1-to-1 personalization. Need an AI-native system that manages context, prioritization, and execution.

### John's Criteria for Next Role

- AI-native company transforming workflows
- Growth stage (not pre-product-market-fit)
- Multidisciplinary CS org scope (implementation through expansion)
- Product he believes in and feels the pain points
