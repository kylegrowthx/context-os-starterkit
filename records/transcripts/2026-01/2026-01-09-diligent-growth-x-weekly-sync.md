## Key Topics

### December SEO Performance Review

  - **GRC Keyword Declines:** The December algorithm update caused major ranking losses for key GRC terms.
      - "GRC tools": #1 → #24
      - "GRC risk management": #10 → #24
  - **Branded Impression Drop:** Significant MoM impression losses for branded keywords.
      - "Diligent Platform": -24%
      - "Diligent 1 platform": -62%
      - **Hypothesis:** A paused brand marketing campaign, not an SEO issue, is the likely cause.
  - **Competitor Analysis:** SEMrush data shows Diligent held its Share of Voice.
      - **Significance:** Keyword losses were not to competitors, simplifying recovery efforts.

### Q1 Content Strategy

  - **GRC Guide Refresh:** The guide page, which previously ranked #1 for "GRC tools," will be refreshed to include a new "GRC tools" section.
  - **AI Governance Article Refresh:** The "AI board governance" article will be refreshed to maintain ranking in a fast-moving topic.
      - **Process:** GrowthX will refresh the article. Kezia will ask SME Dale for new quotes, which will be added post-publication.
  - **"Speak Up" (Whistleblowing) Content:** Content for this acquired product will be woven into the Jan/Feb schedule, not prioritized over existing work.
      - **Context:** The product is listed under "Vault" in the nav but has a different URL, which initially confused GrowthX's research.
  - **"Budget-Friendly Governance Software" Query:** Diligent will compete for this term to target smaller deals (e.g., school boards) and align with products like AI Risk Essential.

### Inline Images & Branding

  - **Tool:** GrowthX uses Nano Banana for AI-generated inline images.
  - **Customization:** The tool replicates styles from a sample image.
      - **Limitation:** Images are static (not editable like Canva files), so a new sample is needed for any brand updates.
  - **Icons:** The tool can incorporate custom assets, including Diligent's Google Material Icons.

### Fathom Data Migration

  - **Proposal:** Diligent will create a new Fathom account and grant GrowthX full editing access.
  - **Goal:** Centralize data and prevent reporting silos from two separate instances.
  - **Risk:** This is a new process for GrowthX, requiring internal approval from Marcel.
