## Key Topics

### Q1 Content Strategy: Partner Focus

- **Rationale:** Q1 is historically a slower lead-gen period. The strategy is to use this time for experimentation and creating targeted content that directly supports sales and partnerships, rather than chasing high-volume keywords.
- **Sanity Priority:**
  - **Pipeline Impact:** Sanity is the top lead-generating partner. High-quality content strengthens this relationship and provides sales reps with valuable resources for cross-promotion and client conversations.
  - **Content Angle:** Focus on emerging topics like Sanity AI features, which are less saturated and highly compelling to clients. The AI Agent tool is a key example—a feature that allows editors to query the CMS and auto-generate content based on component libraries.
  - **Content Inspiration:** Monitor partner product roadmaps for new features and beta programs to identify timely content opportunities. Jesse will provide talking points and may organize short (1-min) developer videos to capture authentic insights on how Sanity AI features work in practice.
  - **Existing Assets:** Reference the "AI capabilities" page on Webstacks and a thought-leadership blog by Jesse and Nikan for initial messaging frameworks.
- **Partner Content Roadmap Priority:** 1) Sanity (top), 2) Storyblok (revival target), 3) Contentful

### Landing Page Optimization

- **Goal:** Transform CMS capability pages (e.g., Sanity capability page, Storyblok capability page) into focused conversion hubs that guide visitors toward high-intent content.
- **Problem:** The current dynamic "Additional Resources" section pulls content via a filter or query, creating an uncontrolled list that risks keyword cannibalization with dedicated blog posts and dilutes conversion focus.
- **Solution:** Redesign with two components:
  1. **Static Featured Cards:** A curated set of 3–5 high-value articles (e.g., "What is Sanity," "Benefits of Sanity," "Migrating to Sanity"). These remain on the page permanently and are manually managed.
  2. **Scrollable List:** A new component (similar to the blog listing page's featured posts carousel) for other relevant articles, keeping the page length manageable while providing deep-dive options.
- **Implementation:** Webstacks will manually update the pages. Katya will provide the list of articles to feature on each capability page.

### Content Audit & CMS Alignment

- **Issue:** A few URLs in the initial content audit were not in the recent CMS export, creating a mismatch between planned content refresh targets and actual available pages.
- **Resolution:** Katya will reconcile the audit list with the CMS export to ensure all content refresh plans are based on live, existing URLs.

### Tagging & Organization

- **AI-Assisted Tagging:** Use Claude to generate new tags for consistency, cross-referencing with existing tags to avoid duplicates.
- **Webstacks' Approach:** Consolidate "Industries" and "Technologies" taxonomy into a single "Tags" field to simplify the blog filter UI.
- **Threshold Rule:** Add industry-specific tags (e.g., SaaS, FinTech) only when there is a significant batch of related articles (5–6+) to justify the new filter option and support dynamic filtering on resource components.

### Task Management & Communication

- **New System:** GrowthX is implementing a new task tracking database to centralize content and CMS work assignments.
- **Notification Channel:** Webstacks will receive task updates via Slack, as this is the most effective channel to ensure visibility and timely action compared to email or other tools.
