## Key Topics

### Partnership Disengagement Rationale

  - The partnership's focus on high-volume content and traffic was misaligned with SchoolAI's need for a comprehensive, revenue-driven strategy.
  - **SchoolAI's Need:** A strategy covering technical SEO, off-page factors (backlinks), and content focused on revenue-driving personas (administrators).
  - **GrowthX's Insight:** The initial content-heavy focus may have stemmed from SchoolAI's own request, rather than a GrowthX consultation on optimal strategy.

### New Agency & Strategy

  - **Agency:** 97th Floor (local, performance-focused).
  - **Retainer:** $8,000/month.
  - **New Strategy Pillars:**
      - **Website Migration:** Framer → Webflow to resolve significant technical SEO debt.
      - **Content:** Reduced volume (\~10 pieces/mo), focused on revenue-driving personas (administrators).
      - **SEO:** Technical fixes, backlinking, and internal linking to build authority.

### SEO Strategy Insights from GrowthX

  - **Keyword Analysis:** A spreadsheet was shared, scoring keywords by business potential (1-3) to create a revenue-aligned content funnel.
  - **Competitor Gap Analysis (Magic School):**
      - **Problem:** Magic School's homepage ranks \#1 for high-intent commercial keywords (e.g., "AI Platform for Education"), while SchoolAI is on page 2+.
      - **Opportunity:** SchoolAI can capture this revenue by outranking Magic School for these terms.
  - **Keyword Targeting Strategy:**
      - **Challenge:** Many high-intent keywords are teacher-focused, but SchoolAI's buyers are administrators.
      - **Solution:** Target these keywords, but tailor the landing page content to administrators.
          - **Rationale:** Admins search for solutions for their teachers and will consider platforms that address their needs, even if the keyword is teacher-centric.
      - **Validation:** Use qualitative analysis of sales calls to confirm admin search intent before executing.
  - **Hypothesis-Driven Approach:**
      - **Test:** If SchoolAI outranks Magic School for a key commercial term, will it drive a measurable increase in revenue?
      - **Method:** Measure homepage conversion rates, deal open rates, and customer LTV during a test period after achieving a \#1 ranking.
