### Witness.ai's SEO Challenges

  - **Bottleneck:** A single person (Dave) manages SEO as a secondary role, limiting output to 3-4 articles/week.
  - **Content Quality:** Current articles are surface-level, lacking the depth and value needed to build authority.
      - **Process:** Ahrefs → ClearScope → ChatGPT.
      - **Constraint:** Product marketing lacks bandwidth to add depth.
  - **Keyword Saturation:** The team has exhausted high-volume keywords and now targets low-tier, low-impact terms.
  - **AI Search (AEO) Blind Spot:** No insights into AI model search behavior, preventing effective content targeting.
  - **Competitive Pressure:** Rapidly evolving market requires higher content volume to compete with large players (Zscaler, Palo Alto).

### GrowthX's AI-Driven SEO Solution

  - **End-to-End Service:** Manages the entire content lifecycle: strategy, execution, publishing, and performance monitoring.
  - **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):**
      - Develops a 3-6 month content roadmap based on competitor analysis and keyword difficulty.
      - Publishes 10-20 articles to quickly calibrate the workflow.
  - **Execution:**
      - Delivers 5 articles/week (new, refreshes, programmatic).
      - Uses "artifacts" (e.g., case studies, product details) to ensure content is deeply integrated with the brand.
  - **Robust AI Workflow:**
      - **Agentic Research:** Deeply researches topics using artifacts and API calls.
      - **Agentic Drafting:** Iteratively refines drafts to meet writing guidelines and address gaps.
      - **Human-in-the-Loop Fact-Checking:** Breaks articles into passages for verification against a confidence interval, ensuring accuracy and nuance.
  - **Performance Tracking:**
      - Monitors visibility in traditional and AI search.
      - Connects content performance to KPIs (traffic, conversions) via weekly reviews.
