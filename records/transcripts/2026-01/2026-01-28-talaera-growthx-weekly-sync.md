### CTA Strategy

**Goal:** Drive blog readers to the next conversion step (demo booking or free account signup).

**Problem:** Current banner CTAs are ineffective due to "banner blindness." Users are conditioned to ignore advertisements on website sidebars.

**Proposed Solution:**
- **Inline CTAs:** Use visually engaging graphics (inspired by Webstacks) for product-specific calls to action within article content
- **Sticky Banner:** Reserve this prime real estate for the highest-impact CTA (e.g., "Book a Demo," "Start Learning"), leading to pricing page or demo booking
- **End-of-Post CTA:** Reserve for the most important conversion, as readers who finish an article are highly engaged and more likely to convert

**Implementation:**
- Aida will create individual CTAs for each product page
- CTAs will be prepared in Notion for review by Paola and Anita
- Process: Extract copy from existing pages, feed into AI for variations, iterate with stakeholder feedback
- Talaera dev team to assess feasibility of personalized sidebar CTAs

### Performance Tracking & Looker Dashboard

**New Dashboard:** A Looker dashboard combining GA4 and GSC data is live and accessible to Talaera team.

**Key Features:**
- **GrowthX Cohort:** Isolate and measure traffic/conversions from GrowthX-generated content via topic cluster tagging
- **Non-Branded Traffic:** Track organic search for non-branded queries (e.g., "virtual meeting tips"), showing which blog posts drive qualified awareness
- **Conversion Events:** Monitor three key events:
  - `sign_up`: B2C free account creation on platform
  - `purchase`: B2C product purchases within platform
  - `managers`: B2B demo booking (tracked via calendar booking event)
- **Engagement Metrics:** GA4 engagement sessions (>10 seconds, page scroll, or link click) showing Talaera's blog has engagement ratio higher than typical ~50% baseline
- **Landing Page Performance:** View impressions, clicks, and click-through rate (CTR) by page; identify high-performing non-branded articles
- **LLM Referrals:** Track traffic from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other LLM sources; identify which content pieces are being referenced in LLM outputs

**Dashboard Pages:**
1. Overview: Sessions and engagement by traffic source
2. Non-Branded Traffic: GSC data for organic search queries excluding brand terms
3. Conversion Events: Tracked events by campaign cohort
4. GrowthX Cohort: Performance metrics isolated to GrowthX-published content
5. Landing Pages: GSC data by URL
6. Queries: Keywords Talaera ranks for in Google Search Console
7. LLM Referrals: Traffic from ChatGPT, Perplexity, and other AI sources

**Branded Misspellings Issue:**
- Searches for "Talera" (no 'a') rank Talaera 5th behind another entity with the same spelling
- Google is generally good at correcting intent, but this specific case is a problem
- GrowthX to consult team for technical SEO recommendation; likely approach is creating comparison/differentiation content

### Technical SEO & Rank Math + LLMs.txt

**Goal:** Maximize blog optimization for ranking in LLMs.

**Tool:** Talaera uses Rank Math WordPress plugin, which includes llms.txt file feature for LLM discoverability.

**Action:** GrowthX will consult its technical SEO team on best practices for configuring Rank Math to improve LLM visibility and ensure all optimization opportunities are leveraged.
