## Key Topics

### Problem: Misaligned LLM Tracking

  - The current Scrunch setup tracks 25 unique prompts across 7 LLMs (175 total tracked items).
  - **Core Issue:** The prompt list is outdated, focusing on RevOps and SDR angles instead of Outreach's new "AI Revenue Platform" positioning.
  - **Specific Gaps:** No prompts for core terms like "AI revenue workflow," "revenue intelligence," or specific AI agents.
  - **Outdated Competitor List:** Tracks irrelevant players (e.g., Reply) while missing key benchmarks.

### Solution: Scrunch Tracker Reset

  - GrowthX will reset the Scrunch tracker to align with Outreach's new strategy.
  - **Prompt Curation:**
      - Retire irrelevant prompts (e.g., security, BDR-focused).
      - Add 15–20 new, high-relevancy prompts across three tiers:
        1.  **Core Positioning:** "AI revenue workflow," "AI agents."
        2.  **Future Differentiation:** Forecasting, conversation intelligence, deal execution.
        3.  **Competitive/Consolidation:** Pain point solutions.
  - **Competitor Focus:** Reduce the list to key players for leadership visibility: Gong, SalesLoft, Clari, Apollo, and HubSpot (for benchmarking).
  - **Enhanced Reporting:** Add tags (e.g., funnel stage, persona) to enable more granular data filtering and analysis.

### Context: Leadership Mandate & Internal Alignment

  - The reset is driven by CMO Holly's directive for a GTM + LLM visibility plan.
  - **Internal "Tiger Team":** Hadassah, Becky (Comms), and Andrea (PMMs) will use the new data to inform Outreach's narrative and analyst strategy.
  - **Strategic Goal:** Establish a clear POV on how Outreach appears in analyst reports and thought leadership, supporting the transition away from its RevOps-only perception.
  - **Long-Term View:** Leadership acknowledges this is a "long game." For example, forecasting is a key focus, despite having only one current customer for that feature.

### Supporting Actions: Content & Persona Updates

  - Outreach will provide GrowthX with updated artifacts to ensure all content is aligned.
  - **Materials to be Shared:**
      - Messaging and positioning document.
      - First-call decks and persona-specific decks.
      - Product explanation guides.
  - **Persona Updates:** The current list (CRO, VP Sales, multiple RevOps) will be revised.
      - **Proposed:** Consolidate RevOps roles and add Account Executive and CFO personas.
      - **Action:** Outreach will provide the official, updated persona playbooks.

### Future: Content Experiment Framework

  - Once the tracker is reset and data is stable (est. 2 weeks), GrowthX will run experiments to improve LLM visibility.
  - **Experiment Cycle:**
    1.  **Identify Gaps:** Find prompts with 0–2% presence/citation.
    2.  **Analyze Intent:** Use query fanout to see how LLMs interpret the prompt.
    3.  **Create Content:** Write net-new content or refresh existing articles to target the identified intent.
    4.  **Measure Impact:** After two weeks, re-evaluate presence and citation for the targeted prompt.
  - **Rationale:** This framework provides a structured, data-driven method to directly test and improve Outreach's LLM visibility.
