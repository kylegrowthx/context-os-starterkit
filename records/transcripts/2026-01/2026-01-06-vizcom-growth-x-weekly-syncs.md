## Key Topics

### GSC Tracking Issue

  - **Problem:** No organic search data was appearing in Google Search Console (GSC).
  - **Cause:** A hostname mismatch was identified.
      - GSC was tracking `https://vizcom.com`.
      - The correct property should be `https://www.vizcom.com` (a specific URL prefix) or `vizcom.com` (a domain property).
  - **Resolution:** Carly will connect with Vizcom's CTO, Kaelin, who owns the DNS, to fix the configuration.

### Vizcom Website Redesign & Launch

  - **Timeline:** The new site launches Thursday, Jan 8, to unblock delayed marketing campaigns.
  - **Scope:** The launch includes a new sitemap with new pages and URL slug changes.
  - **SEO Support:** Carly will provide SEO guidance for the migration, focusing on:
      - Redirect strategy to prevent SEO issues.
      - Submitting the new sitemap in GSC post-launch.
  - **Staged Rollout:** Some sections (e.g., case studies, docs) will be hidden at launch and rolled out later.

### Content Review & Feedback

  - **Status:** 10+ drafts are awaiting review; 5 new drafts are in progress.
  - **Key Feedback:**
      - **Visuals:** Content must better align with Vizcom's visual brand identity.
      - **Competitor Mentions:** The CEO is sensitive to competitor listicles. Content must "lightly thread the needle" on this topic.
  - **Author Tags:** The new blog format requires author tags.
      - **Recommendation:** Use real people (e.g., internal reviewers) instead of "Team Vizcom." This provides a stronger SEO authority signal.
  - **Image Workflow:** Maura noted missing images in some drafts.
      - **Clarification:** GrowthX provides images in the GSC doc. Featured images are a publishing requirement.
      - **Enrichment Strategy:** Deeper enrichment (e.g., product images) is prioritized for top-performing articles post-launch to maximize ROI.

### Team & Process Updates

  - **New GrowthX POC:** Carly Piekos is the new Engagement Manager.
      - **Background:** 8 years in B2B SaaS SEO/content (Conductor, agency).
      - **Focus:** Accelerating results, maintaining quality, and refining the visual strategy.
  - **Vizcom Team Updates:**
      - Emily Lonetto (Marketing Lead) is on mat leave starting Jan 13.
      - Kim Lu will manage marketing operations.
      - Chris (Product Designer) and Jordan (CEO) may provide content feedback.
