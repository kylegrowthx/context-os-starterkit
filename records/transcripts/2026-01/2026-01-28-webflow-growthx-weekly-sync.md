## Context

This is a mid-engagement sync between GrowthX (B2B content marketing) and Webflow (no-code website builder) ahead of a scheduled QBR. The engagement centers on developer content strategy across three workstreams: integration use case guides (16 live), code components POC (contract pending), and exploration of new opportunities. The relationship has matured over several months, but the integrations workstream is naturally reaching capacity limits as available integrations exhaust. This sync flags a critical strategic decision: whether to explore programmatic SEO (comparison pages like "Webflow vs. X") as an adjacent play, or continue deepening existing workstreams. The conversation also surfaces a historical tension—a 2020 AI-generated developer blog failed due to marketing team quality feedback—that now shapes how the teams assess whether improved AI modeling and processes justify renewed confidence in programmatic approaches.
