## Key Topics

### Check That: New AI Visibility Tool

  - GrowthX is replacing Scrunch with "Check That," its proprietary tool for tracking AI visibility.
  - **Key Features:**
      - Tracks performance across 5 LLM platforms (Claude, Google AI, ChatGPT, Perplexity).
      - Shows exact citations (specific page URLs, not just homepages).
      - Identifies top-cited pages to reveal competitor content strategies and gaps.
  - **Access:** GrowthX clients receive free access for up to one year.

### Performance Gap: Check That vs. Scrunch

  - A significant gap exists between Galileo's visibility scores: 7.2% in Check That vs. 20% in Scrunch.
  - **Rationale:** The prompt sets differ significantly.
      - **Check That:** Uses a curated library of evaluation-stage prompts (e.g., "best X for Y") that reflect high-intent buying decisions.
      - **Scrunch:** Uses custom prompts provided by the client.
  - **Conclusion:** The tools measure different things, making the scores non-comparable.

### Refining Check That for Actionable Reporting

  - To make Check That data actionable for Jason, the tool's setup requires refinement.
  - **Category:** The current "AI Monitoring and Observability" category is too broad, grouping Galileo with general solutions like Datadog.
      - **Proposed:** "AI Evaluation and Observability" to better reflect Galileo's core focus and provide a more relevant competitive comparison.
  - **Competitors:** A competitor error (wrong Braintrust logo) must be fixed to ensure accurate data.
  - **Metric for Jason:** A succinct performance metric is needed.
      - **Proposal:** A weighted formula combining Position, Sentiment, and Citation Share, with relative competitor positioning (Arise, Langsmith) as a key factor.

### Content Strategy & Performance

  - **Traffic Dip:** January traffic is down, especially on "evals" and "miscellaneous" pages that were top performers mid-last year.
  - **Solution:** Prioritize refreshing these pages.
      - **Rationale:** Refreshes are the most effective lever for improving AI visibility and capitalizing on algorithm recency bias.
  - **Content Production:** On track. New content from the report will be integrated next week, with a lower priority than LLM performance topics.
