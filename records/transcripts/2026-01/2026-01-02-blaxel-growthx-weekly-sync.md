### Analytics Strategy

  - **Original Plan:** Use Google Analytics (GA) for web data → Build a Looker dashboard for reporting.
  - **Revised Plan:** Use PostHog directly.
      - **Rationale:** PostHog is Blaxel's primary data warehouse and already provides both BI and web analytics, eliminating the need for a separate Looker dashboard.

### Content Strategy & Topic Clusters

  - **"MCP" Topic Cluster**
      - **Decision:** Deprioritize.
      - **Rationale:** MCP hosting is an auxiliary feature with minimal revenue impact. The strategic focus is on the full agentic framework, not this single feature.
      - **Action:** Reallocate relevant MCP content to the "AI Agent Foundations" cluster.
  - **Topical Focus**
      - **Primary:** "Code Execution & Sandboxes" and "AI Agent Foundations."
      - **Secondary:** "Comparisons & Mysticals" (e.g., alternatives articles) to capture competitor search traffic.
  - **Keyword Research Process**
      - **Method:** SEMrush analysis of competitor keywords and pages.
      - **Goal:** Identify high-volume, low-difficulty keywords and fill content gaps.
      - **Refinement:** Manually include high-value topics (e.g., "Sandbox Lifecycle") even if their search volume is low, to establish Blaxel as an authority.

### Article Review & Production

  - **Upcoming Articles (for next week's delivery)**

      - **"RunPod alternatives" → "CodeSandbox alternatives" or "Daytona alternatives"**
  - **Featured Images**
