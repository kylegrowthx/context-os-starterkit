## Key Topics

### Growthx.ai's Strategy Sprints Model

  - **Purpose:** An 8-week accelerated program to prove value and secure a long-term client partnership.
  - **Structure:**
      - **Weeks 1–3 (Foundation):** Build deep client knowledge via artifacts (product docs, personas) to inform workflows.
      - **Weeks 4–8 (Execution):** Calibrate voice/tone, create writing guidelines, and publish content to hit a \~15-article-per-week cadence.
  - **Success Metric:** Secure a "hell yes" from the client, who has a mutual opt-out option at the end.
  - **Reporting Focus:** Emphasize early signals (indexing, impressions, clicks) to show progress before full SEO traction develops.

### Candidate Experience & Approach

  - **Background:** 10 years in SEO (agency, in-house, consulting), specializing in building functions from scratch for startups.
  - **Teachable Case Study:**
      - **Problem:** \>80% of organic traffic was branded, missing new users.
      - **Solution:** A 6-month plan to target top-of-funnel, non-branded keywords with the blog.
      - **Result:** Significant organic traffic growth that also boosted service pages and the homepage.
  - **AI Philosophy:** Uses AI as a support tool (e.g., for keyword generation) but believes human oversight is essential to avoid generic, "obviously AI-generated" content.

### Client Management & Reporting

  - **Handling "Pushy" Clients:**
      - **Strategy:** Build trust early by acting as a proactive partner, not a supplier.
      - **Tactics:** Provide frequent updates and highlight small wins to build positive momentum.
  - **Handling Out-of-Scope Requests:**
      - **Process:** Acknowledge the request's validity → frame it as a valuable addition → scope it for a contract add-on.
      - **Example:** A client's daily requests for extra content were converted into a paid quarterly hours package.
  - **Reporting Philosophy:**
      - **Core Metrics:** Traffic, impressions, clicks, CTR, position changes.
      - **Business Impact:** Tailor reports to client goals, showing how SEO drives leads, conversions, and revenue.

### Growthx.ai's Challenges & Culture

  - **Primary Challenge:** Scaling the Strategy Sprints into a repeatable system.
      - **Hiring:** Finding high-quality Engagement Managers ("the quarterback").
      - **Enablement:** Systematizing training to transfer expert knowledge (e.g., from Marcel).
      - **Efficiency:** Improving workflows and automation to reduce manual work.
  - **Culture:**
      - **Proactivity:** Initiative and new ideas are highly rewarded.
      - **Collaboration:** Open to feedback and incremental improvement; no ego-driven "one best solution."
