- Q1 focus: messaging/positioning overhaul, brand redesign, website refresh

### GrowthX Overview
- Origin: Marcel built organic growth engines at IBM, Hashicorp, ServiceTitan, Deepgram
- Model: AI workflows + human editors to scale quality content
- Results: 24x traffic at Deepgram in short period
- Current: 60+ brands, two kickoffs per week, 6-8 week booking lead time
- Philosophy: Content is the atomic unit. Websites are the destination. Pages influence visibility, traffic, conversions.

### How GrowthX Works
- **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** Deep research, interviews, demos, context gathering, calibration
- **Daily Publishing:** Humans in loop reviewing every piece, constant iteration
- **Platform:** Custom workflows per client (research, drafting, compliance, fact-checking, image generation)
- **Quality vs. Signals:** Balance based on domain authority and audience needs

### Pricing and Work Streams
- Starting at $15k/month per work stream
- Work stream = distinct content program (editorial, guides, templates, newsletter, etc.)
- Includes: end-to-end production, imagery, CMS publishing, fact-checking, design support
- 8-week sprint with 80% conversion to 12-month contracts
- No separate platform fee; AI visibility tracking included

### Examples Shared
- **Lovable:** Templates library (integrated into product), guides (ROI positive in 2 months)
- **Ramp:** Vendors section, treasury content ramp-up pre-launch, microsites
- **Webflow:** Integration pages, developer pages (also investor)
- **Biologica:** High-bar medical content with two-doctor review

### Why GrowthX vs. Agencies
- Most clients consolidate to GrowthX, fire previous SEO agencies
- Avoids "scraps" problem when splitting work
- Dedicated engagement manager and editors become extension of team
