## Key Topics

### ASU Learning Enterprise Context

  - **Mission:** Owns all non-degree offerings (career learning, certificates, K-12 pathways) across three pillars:
      - Universal Pathways
      - Career & Professional Learning
      - Emerging Learning Solutions (grant-funded)
  - **Goal:** Build an intentional AEO strategy to drive registrations for key offerings, starting with Universal Learner Courses (ULC).
  - **Tech Stack:** Complex, but AEO is expected to be a standalone service, not requiring deep integration.
      - **B2C Marketing:** Salesforce Marketing Cloud
      - **B2B Marketing:** HubSpot
      - **Data:** BigQuery
      - **CRM:** Salesforce Sales Cloud

### GrowthX's AEO Solution

  - **Service Model:** An end-to-end service for AEO content, not a tech platform for internal teams.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** Develop a content roadmap and publish 5–10 initial articles.
    2.  **Growth Execution:** Publish 5 new articles per week.
  - **Platform Workflow:**
      - **Inputs:** Custom "artifacts" (personas, style guides, voice/tone) provide deep context.
      - **Research:** A research agent performs 30-minute deep dives, generating a bibliography.
      - **Fact-Checking:** A separate agent validates every passage against a confidence interval.
      - **Human Oversight:** Experts make manual edits to ensure quality and nuance.
  - **Pricing:** Standard enterprise software pricing (5 articles/week) may be too high for ASU's initial test. GrowthX is open to a custom model for a single offering.
