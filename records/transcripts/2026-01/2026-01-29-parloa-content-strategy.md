## Context

Parloa, a conversational AI platform for enterprise contact centers, is entering a highly competitive market dominated by well-established players (Decagon AI, Core AI, Poly AI, Sierra AI, Cognigy). The market opportunity is significant—enterprise buyers are actively searching for AI contact center solutions—but Parloa's visibility in the US market is limited. GrowthX was engaged to define and execute a content-driven SEO strategy that leverages Parloa's technical capabilities and domain authority (DA 35, comparable to Decagon's DA 39) to capture share in this market. The analysis of ~80k keywords across six competitors revealed specific gaps Parloa could exploit, particularly in glossary-style content, vertical-specific solutions, and the emerging agentic AI category. This meeting served as Week 2 checkpoint to align on the six-cluster content strategy before moving into Week 3 execution and calibration.

## Key Topics

### Competitive Landscape Analysis

  - Analysis of \~80k keywords across 6 competitors (Sierra AI, Cognigy, Decagon AI, Core AI, Poly AI, 11 Labs) informed the strategy.
  - **Key Competitor Insights:**
      - **Sierra AI:** Focuses on thought leadership via its high-profile founder.
      - **Decagon AI:** Owns the glossary space; Parloa's similar DA creates a direct competition opportunity.
      - **Cognigy:** Positions itself as the enterprise leader, leveraging Gartner recognition.
      - **Core AI:** Sets the standard for technical depth in content.
  - **Paid vs. Organic Strategy:**
      - For high-difficulty keywords (e.g., "Voice AI," KD 100), the strategy is to:
          - **Paid:** Drive immediate traffic and conversions.
          - **Organic:** Build long-term authority and interlink to paid landing pages.
      - This integrated approach ensures cost-effectiveness by avoiding paid spend on keywords where Parloa already ranks well organically.

### Proposed Content Strategy (V1)

  - The strategy is built on 6 topic clusters designed to address market gaps and Parloa's business needs.
  - **1. Conversational AI & Voice Tech**
      - **Goal:** Establish Parloa as the trusted educator in conversational AI.
      - **Angle:** Position Parloa as voice-first, built from the ground up for quality, unlike chat-first competitors.
      - **Status:** On Hold. Requires CEO approval to use the term "Conversational AI," which is critical for capturing high-volume searches (\~250k/mo).
  - **2. Agentic AI & Technical Leadership**
      - **Goal:** Position Parloa at the forefront of agentic AI and demonstrate technical chops.
      - **Audience:** CX leaders, transformational AI leaders, and product teams.
  - **3. Enterprise Contact Center Transformation**
      - **Goal:** Position Parloa as the partner that scales AI from pilot to production.
      - **Angle:** Address the pain point of failed AI pilots by demonstrating expertise in enterprise deployment.
  - **4. CX Operational Metrics & Glossary**
      - **Goal:** Capture informational searches and own conversations around contact center operations.
      - **Phase 1:** Create glossary-style articles.
      - **Phase 2:** Build a dedicated glossary page.
  - **5. Industry Vertical Solutions**
      - **Goal:** Create high-intent content for specific industries (e.g., healthcare, insurance, retail).
      - **Context:** Aligns with a planned roadmap for creating dedicated verticalization pages.
  - **6. Competitive Comparisons & Alternatives**
      - **Goal:** Own the middle-to-bottom-of-funnel conversation for prospects in the consideration phase.
      - **Context:** Two paid-only comparison pages (Sierra AI, Decagon AI) are launching next week. Organic versions will be needed to rank effectively, as paid pages are typically not SEO-optimized.
