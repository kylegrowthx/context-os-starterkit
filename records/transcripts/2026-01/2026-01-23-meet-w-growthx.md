## Key Topics

### Cresta's Core Differentiator: Outcome Inference

  - The central thesis: Cresta's AI infers business outcomes (e.g., CSAT, sales, resolution) from conversations, a capability competitors lack.
  - **Why it matters:** This allows contact centers to identify and prioritize agent behaviors that directly drive business results.
      - **Example:** Acknowledging a customer's poor experience predicts a \~30% increase in CSAT. This data-backed insight enables targeted coaching.

### Product 1: Insights

  - **Purpose:** Analyze contact center conversations at scale to uncover business-critical information.
  - **Three-Phase Process:**
    1.  **Discovery:** Unsupervised LLMs find "unknown unknowns" (e.g., emerging topics, customer questions).
    2.  **Tracking:** Users monitor specific trends with custom dashboards and intent maps.
    3.  **Action:** Insights inform four key areas:
          - **Automating Conversations:** "Automation Discovery" identifies topics suitable for AI agents and maps their workflows.
          - **Augmenting Human Agents:** Real-time hints guide agents toward high-impact behaviors.
          - **Augmenting Customer Journeys:** Fixing friction points outside the contact center.
              - **United Airlines Case Study:** Identified an app flow issue → fixed it → saved millions by deflecting calls.
          - **Making Better Business Decisions:** Informing policy or messaging changes.
              - **CVS Health Case Study:** Spotted customer anger over a drug's coverage change → issued new agent talking points same-day.
  - **New Content Focus:** "Automation Discovery" is a new feature and a key differentiator that needs more content.

### Product 2: Quality Management (QM) & Coach

  - **Purpose:** Automate and improve the traditional manual QA process.
  - **Problem with Manual QA:**
      - Low coverage (1–5% of calls) creates an inaccurate picture of agent performance.
      - Coaching based on this limited data feels unfair and demotivating for agents.
  - **Cresta's Solution:**
      - **Data-Driven Scorecards:** Scorecards are built from behaviors proven to drive outcomes, not just a static checklist.
      - **100% Conversation Coverage:** AI scores all conversations, eliminating sampling bias and providing a complete, fair view of performance.
      - **Targeted Coaching:** Managers get specific data (e.g., "Agent X missed 8 opportunities to verify a device reboot this week") to deliver actionable, data-backed feedback.
  - **Key Differentiators vs. Competitors:**
      - **Outcome Inference:** The core advantage.
      - **Real-time Capabilities:** A newly validated differentiator against Observe AI.
      - **AI Agent Oversight:** Cresta's 7+ years of QM experience for human agents applies directly to monitoring generative AI agents, a key advantage over newer players (e.g., Sierra, Decagon).
