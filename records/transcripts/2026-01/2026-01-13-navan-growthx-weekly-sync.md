## Key Topics

### Content Feedback & Process Refinement

  - **Problem:** Drafts had choppy introductions and "Key Takeaways" that became inaccurate after editing.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will focus on writing smoother intros and ensure takeaways are updated post-edit. Navan will provide examples of strong intros for reference.
  - **Problem:** The Airtable workflow was confusing and didn't match Navan's internal process.
  - **Solution:** The workflow was simplified to:
      - `Drafts Ready for Review` (first pass)
      - `Second Draft Review` (final check before publish)
      - `Published`
  - **Legal Review:** Navan will manage legal review internally for competitive comparison articles (3 currently in process), which will have a longer publishing timeline. All other SEO articles can be published directly after the final review.

### Image Sourcing

  - **Problem:** The process for sourcing cover images was unclear.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will add free stock photos to the Airtable `Cover Image` field for each draft, adhering to Navan's existing visual style. AI-generated imagery is not permitted.

### Performance Reporting & AI Visibility

  - **Looker Dashboard:** The dashboard is live, tracking performance for the first 4 published articles.
      - **Initial Data:** New articles are ranking well (avg. position 4–6), but it's too early for definitive conclusions.
      - **Refreshed Article:** The "10 Essential Steps..." article shows a 28-day traffic dip, likely due to the holiday season and a recent Google Core update.
  - **AI Visibility Platform:** GrowthX is upgrading from Scrunch to a proprietary tool.
      - **Rationale:** The new platform provides a pre-built index of \>100k prompts, eliminating the need to build them from scratch.
      - **New Prompts:** GrowthX created new prompts using target keywords to directly link content performance to AI visibility.
      - **Baseline:** The start of January will serve as the baseline for measuring future AI visibility growth.
