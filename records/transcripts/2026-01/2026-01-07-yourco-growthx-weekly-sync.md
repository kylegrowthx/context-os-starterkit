## Key Topics

### AEO Strategy & Performance

  - **Goal:** Dominate the AEO space in 2026, driven by high-quality mid-market/enterprise conversions from LLM traffic.
  - **Problem:** Existing content that ranks on Google is not being cited by LLMs (e.g., "how to communicate when employees don't have email").
  - **Performance:**
      - LLM referrals are \~5% of overall sessions (GA4).
      - Keyword rankings dropped in December, possibly due to seasonality.
      - Competitor UDEX is outperforming Yourco in LLM citations despite a weaker brand.

### Technical & Content Solutions

  - **Technical Fixes:**
      - **Remove JavaScript:** The top priority, as LLMs cannot read it. Jessica has been given authority to push this with the tech team.
      - **Use Question-Based Headers (H2s/H3s):** Helps LLMs parse content hierarchy.
  - **Content Structure:**
      - **Pillar-Cluster Model:** Reorganize content around high-traffic pillars to create a clear, interlinked architecture.
      - **Categorization & Schema:** Add categories and schema markup to improve structure and LLM readability.
      - **Interlinking:** Focus links on high-performing resource and use-case pages.
  - **Content Optimization:**
      - **TLDR Summaries:** Add a bulleted or paragraph summary at the top of articles to provide an easy-to-digest overview for LLMs and users.
      - **Bottom-of-Funnel Content:** Prioritize comparative listicles to capture users with high purchase intent.
      - **Target Specific Queries:** Create content for long-tail, problem-solving queries (e.g., "mobile communication without internet for deskless workers").

### GrowthX Role & Support

  - **Account Management:** Carrie will manage the account while GrowthX finds a long-term fit.
  - **Strategic Focus:** GrowthX will focus on content optimization for AEO, not broader social strategy.
  - **Tools:** GrowthX is building an internal tool to provide better visibility into LLM performance and competitor tracking.
