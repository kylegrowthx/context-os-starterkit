## Key Topics

### Q1 Content Strategy: Tool-Based Pillar Content

  - **Goal:** Build SEO authority by creating \~100+ articles on tech tools (e.g., Sanity, Contentful).
  - **Structure:** Content will be organized into "chapter" landing pages for each tool.
      - Each "chapter" (e.g., "What is Sanity?") will be a separate article.
      - Topics will be prioritized by search volume and partner status.
      - "Alternatives" articles will capture audiences using non-partner tools.
  - **Implementation:**
      - **Location:** Webstacks will build a simple card-deck template for the landing pages.
          - **Placement Options:** `/resources`, `/technologies`, or root-level slugs (e.g., `/sanity`).
          - **Decision:** Jacob (GrowthX) will advise on the best SEO structure.
      - **Workflow:** GrowthX will create a content calendar from the blog audit to identify missing articles.
