## Context

Daylight is an early-stage B2B cybersecurity services company offering 24/7 managed detection and response (MDR) with a hybrid model of AI agents and expert humans (15–25 years experience). The market is characterized by dormant innovation in services ($200B TAM) despite heavy VC funding in cybersecurity tools. Daylight's bottleneck is converting prospects to POC trials, despite achieving 100% close rates post-POC. The engagement builds GrowthX's expertise in Daylight's product and markets, positioning GrowthX to accelerate content production, authority building, and visibility tracking. This is a strategic engagement on Daylight's GTM roadmap, with content as the primary lever for moving prospects into discovery and POC.

## Overview

- **Daylight's Pitch:** An AI-agentic platform paired with 15–25 year security experts (threat hunting, incident response) to reinvent cybersecurity services, which have seen minimal innovation in 20 years.
- **Primary Challenge:** The sales bottleneck is getting prospects to a POC. Daylight has a 100% close rate post-POC, but prospects hesitate due to startup size, scalability concerns, and skepticism about AI.
- **Content Strategy:** Build authority by targeting a specific audience with opinionated, conversational content. The initial focus is 70% on MDR (Managed Detection and Response) and 30% on future services to build a content backlog.
- **GrowthX's Role:** Execute an 8-week accelerated onboarding to become Daylight experts, build a content engine, and track AI visibility using a proprietary tool.

---

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Stagnant Cybersecurity Services

  - The $200B cybersecurity market has seen 20 years of innovation in tools, but services have been neglected due to low VC interest.
  - Recent AI funding (\>$1.5B in the last year) is now driving innovation in this space.
  - Daylight's model is a hybrid of an agentic platform and expert humans, contrasting with pure AI SOC tools that promise full automation.

### The Solution: Daylight's Hybrid Model

  - **Product:** A 24/7 MDR service, with new services launching quarterly (Threat Hunting, Managed Phishing, Managed DLP, Incident Response).
  - **Technology:** An agentic platform built for rapid integration.
      - **Integration Layer:** Built on founders' Torque experience; adds new integrations in days.
      - **Agentic Platform:** Uses multiple, specific AI agents managed by an orchestration system.
  - **Human Element:** A global "follow the sun" team of experts (15–25 years experience) in complex tasks like threat hunting.

### The Challenge: Sales & Marketing Bottlenecks

  - **ICP 1:** High-growth tech companies (\~1,000 people) needing 24/7 monitoring.
      - **Trigger:** A board-level decision to address accountability gaps.
  - **ICP 2:** North American tech companies (4k–10k people) using expensive MDRs (Expel, ReliQuest) but are dissatisfied.
      - **Trigger:** End of annual contract.
  - **Sales Cycle:** \~3 months, with an Average Selling Price (ASP) of \~$120k.
  - **POC:** A free 1-week trial is offered. The "aha moment" occurs post-POC, when skepticism about AI and startup size is overcome.

### The Strategy: Content & Growth

  - **Content Goal:** Build authority with opinionated, conversational content that addresses real pain points, not just defines terms.
  - **Initial Content Mix:** 70% MDR, 30% future services.
  - **Marketing Channels:**
      - **Events:** Focus on smaller, community-based events and Daylight's own "SOC Innovation Days" series.
      - **Paid Search:** A future, highly focused effort targeting competitor keywords.
      - **Partnerships:** Building relationships with two large companies.
  - **Content Operations:**
      - **Approvals:** Founder review is required for all calibration articles.
      - **Authorship:** Continue building expert author bios for all content.
      - **Engineering Blog:** A separate blog on AI engineering will run on the main domain.
