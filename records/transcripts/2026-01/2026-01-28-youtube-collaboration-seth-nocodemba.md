## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Marketing Challenge

  - **Business Model:** GrowthX is a growth agency ($10M+ ARR) specializing in AI SEO and content systems for tech clients (e.g., Webflow, Stripe).
  - **Community as a Funnel:** The agency uses a paid community (~400 members @ $30/mo) as its primary marketing engine to build an audience.
  - **Lead Generation Gap:** Current marketing relies on one-off events (e.g., workshops) that create traffic spikes but lack the consistent, evergreen lead flow that YouTube can provide.

### Proposed Collaboration Models

  - **Newsletter Sponsorship:** A direct ad in the No Code MBA newsletter (21k subscribers).
  - **Mid-Roll Sponsorships:** Evergreen ads in new videos.
      - **Rationale:** Guarantees a permanent slot in the description and pinned comment, providing a long-term lead source as videos gain views over time.
  - **Back Catalog Links:** Adding GrowthX links to relevant older videos.
      - **Rationale:** Taps into the channel's largest source of views for a scalable, low-effort lead stream.
  - **Co-Created Content:** Joint workshops or a YouTube series.
      - **Rationale:** Builds authority and generates high-quality leads through a more organic, educational format.

### Compensation & Financial Alignment

  - **The Challenge:** Seth must weigh the financial benefit of promoting GrowthX against the opportunity cost of promoting his own No Code MBA membership.
  - **GrowthX's Flexibility:** Jason is open to various models to ensure the partnership is financially compelling for Seth.
      - **CPL (Cost Per Lead):** A flat fee for each email signup.
      - **Rev-Share:** A percentage of revenue from members sourced through the partnership.
      - **Full Affiliate:** All revenue from sourced members is passed to Seth.

### YouTube Strategy & Evergreen Content

  - **Evergreen Video Value:** Seth's strategy focuses on SEO-targeted, evergreen content that accumulates views over months. Example: a video may get 1k views in the first week, but 10k within six months.
  - **Mid-Roll Advantage:** Multiple mid-roll placements increase chances of viral moments while providing consistent lead flow as videos age.
  - **AI Trend Alignment:** AI services are increasingly sourcing YouTube over Reddit, strengthening the value of YouTube placement for both parties' positioning.

### Seth's Business & Opportunity Cost

  - **No Code MBA Model:** Two business lines—paid educational content and rapidly growing YouTube channel focused on no-code and AI.
  - **Opportunity Cost:** Seth's bandwidth is split 50/50 between No Code MBA and another business, making partnership terms critical to justify allocating YouTube real estate to GrowthX over his own offerings.
  - **Background:** Data analytics leader turned entrepreneur; marketing operations expertise; self-taught no-code/AI developer.
