## Context

Clear Street is a fintech platform founded with institutional trading DNA (via CenterPoint legacy) that is now launching Clear Street Active, a retail trading platform targeting sophisticated traders with accounts above $50K. The company is at a strategic inflection point with a brand new CMO and organizational focus on retail growth. Currently, their web presence is outdated and institutional-focused, leaving a substantial content gap. Mahmoud Sherif leads the retail product division and is actively seeking a content partner to help shape the narrative around three key personas: day traders, short sellers, and algo traders.

GrowthX positions itself as an end-to-end content partner that combines AI-driven production workflows with human expertise (CEO's background scaling content at HashiCorp, ScaleAI, and DeepGram; CCO former TechCrunch EIC). The company has demonstrated success with similar plays—most notably Augment Code, which grew from less than 1% to 20-25% AI search visibility in six months and tripled organic traffic while cutting ad spend. GrowthX's engagement model is designed to minimize friction on the client side: upfront collaboration to gather personas, brand guidelines, and product context, followed by a low-touch review-and-approve workflow for ongoing content delivery.
