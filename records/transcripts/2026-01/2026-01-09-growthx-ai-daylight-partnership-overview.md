## Key Topics

### Daylight's Content Challenge

Daylight needs to build domain authority from a starting point of zero organic traffic. Maya's primary concern was ensuring the content would be technically accurate and sophisticated enough for Daylight's audience, given the company's lack of existing content or domain authority.

### GrowthX AI's Solution & Process

**Service:** An end-to-end content partner providing strategy, production, and publishing.

**Team:** 90+ person company with a 65-person delivery team led by Matthew Panzarino (former TechCrunch Editor-in-Chief).

**Technical Content Model:**
- **Platform:** Proprietary internal platform (not Airtable-based or third-party) for custom workflows with fine-tuned prompts and multi-step validation.
- **Staffing:** A technical managing editor is assigned to each account to ensure editorial quality and technical accuracy.
- **Inputs:** Client-provided resources (personas, brand guides, competitor examples) are used to create detailed "artifacts" (e.g., persona profiles with fears and motivations, competitor analysis, terminology guides) that guide content creation.

**Case Study (Augment Code):**
- Grew from ~20k to 60k+ monthly organic traffic in the space for "AI + coding."
- Achieved top-ranking keywords and secured multiple citations in AI search answers (7 out of 10 citations were GrowthX-created articles).
- Demonstrates success in both traditional search and AI search visibility.

### Engagement Structure & Pricing

**Phase 1: Strategy Sprint ($30k)**
- **Duration:** 8 weeks.
- **Deliverables:**
  - Content OS setup with keyword analysis and topic clusters (based on search volume and difficulty).
  - Content roadmap identifying priority topics and authority areas.
  - 5–10 calibration articles published to align on quality.
- **Opt-out:** Daylight can terminate the engagement after Phase 1 with no obligation to continue.
- **Goal:** 10–20 articles published by the end of the sprint, depending on calibration pace.

**Phase 2: Scale ($15k/month)**
- **Production:** 5 articles per week (approximately 20–25/month).
- **Pricing:** $3k/month discount from the standard $18k/month rate, offered to support an early-stage startup.

### Content Creation Workflow

GrowthX uses a multi-step, artifact-driven workflow:

1. **Research:** Generates research plans and evaluates quality using specialized tools (beyond OpenAI/Anthropic).
2. **Drafting:** Uses writing guidelines and artifact inputs to draft articles.
3. **Validation:** Fact-checks every passage, deep-researches claims, and validates against writing guidelines.
4. **Optimization:** Generates optimized meta tags, URL paths, and cover images; adds cross-links and source citations.
5. **Publishing:** Stages and publishes directly to the client's CMS with all formatting and infographics.

### Collaboration & Logistics

**Daylight's Role:**
- Provide initial resources: personas, brand guides, competitor examples, style guide.
- Maintain a clear, timely feedback loop on calibration articles (5–10 drafts).
- Review content and provide feedback during Phase 1 to prevent bottlenecks.

**GrowthX AI's Role:**
- Handle all publishing logistics: staging, meta tags, images, and infographics.
- Requires CMS access with the ability to insert tables, which benefit LLM visibility for comparisons and structured data.
- Optionally stand up custom content sections (e.g., Daylight could create a `/guides` subdirectory instead of posting to the main blog, similar to Augment Code).

**Timeline:** Organic traffic signals are expected within 3–6 months, with longer timelines for companies starting from zero.
