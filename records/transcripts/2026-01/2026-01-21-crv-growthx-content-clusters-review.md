## Summary

CRV and GrowthX reviewed and finalized the content cluster strategy for CRV's SEO initiative, approving three core clusters (VC Selection & Due Diligence, Series A Preparation, Technical Founder Resources), merging two overlapping clusters into "Building & Running Your Board," and introducing a new AI-Native Companies cluster to address a critical gap in CRV's founder-focused content strategy.

---

## Context

This is the second planning meeting between GrowthX and CRV to refine the content clusters that will serve as the foundation for a ~100-keyword content OS. GrowthX is conducting SEO-focused content marketing for CRV, targeting early-stage founders (pre-Series A through Series A) with founder-centric guidance on fundraising, board governance, and technical leadership. The content strategy aims to build CRV's brand visibility and thought leadership in a relatively underserved space, with realistic expectations: 18–20 articles in the sprint (5/week over 4 weeks) targeting 2,000–4,000 total ranked keywords within 6 months. All content will be attributed to "Team CRV" to build a collective brand rather than individual investor profiles.

---

## Relevance

- **For GrowthX:** Clarifies content clusters and scope, enabling execution on the SEO engagement with well-defined targets and realistic deliverables.
- **For CRV:** Validates keyword strategy, identifies content gaps (AI-Native Companies), and ensures content remains founder-focused (not institutional investor content).
- **For Content Strategy:** Emphasizes the importance of founder lifecycle targeting (earliest touchpoints create the strongest partnership opportunities) and the distinction between AI-native companies and AI-integrated legacy products.

## Overview

- **Clusters Approved:** "VC Selection & Due Diligence," "Series A Preparation," and "Technical Founder Resources."
- **Clusters Merged:** "Investor Partnership Value" and "Board Governance" will combine into a single, comprehensive "Building & Running Your Board" cluster.
- **New AI Cluster:** A new cluster on "AI-Native Companies" will be developed to capture founders building the next generation of AI, not those integrating it into legacy products.
- **Realistic Expectations:** The engagement will target \~100 keywords in the content OS, aiming for 2-4k total keywords ranked within 6 months, not the 800k+ of competitors like YC.

---

## Key Topics

### SEO & Content Strategy

  - **Competitive Analysis:** A quick check of top search results (e.g., Reddit, Medium) for a keyword indicates low competition from major VCs, signaling a strong opportunity.
  - **Keyword Difficulty (KD):** This metric measures how hard it is to rank for a keyword.
      - **"Best VC Firms":** KD 43 (Medium) → Pursue.
      - **"Venture Capital":** KD 73 (Extremely Hard) → Avoid, as top results are dominated by high-authority sites like Investopedia and Harvard Business Review.

### Content Cluster Review

  - **1. VC Selection & Due Diligence (Approved)**
      - **Goal:** Help founders pick the right partner.
      - **Rationale:** Low competition from other VCs; covers both seed and Series A.
  - **2. Series A Preparation (Approved)**
      - **Goal:** Guide founders on preparing for Series A.
      - **Rationale:** Targets founders *before* they are actively fundraising, creating an earlier touchpoint.
  - **3. Investor Partnership Value & Board Governance (Merged)**
      - **Issue:** The keyword "investor relations" is ambiguous (founder-VC vs. VC-LP).
      - **Solution:** Merge clusters 4 & 5 into one focused on "Building & Running Your Board."
      - **New Focus:** Practical guidance for founders on board selection, operations, and evolution.
      - **Caveat:** Ensure content is founder-focused, not for junior investors.
  - **4. Technical Founder Resources (Approved)**
      - **Goal:** Build brand visibility with technical founders *before* they seek funding.
      - **Rationale:** Establishes CRV as a thought leader for its ideal customer profile at the earliest stages of their journey.
  - **5. AI-Native Companies (New)**
      - **Rationale:** A critical omission, as AI is a top investment focus for CRV.
      - **Angle:** Target founders building new, AI-native companies (e.g., Periodic Labs, Skilled AI), not those integrating AI into legacy products.
      - **Strategy:** Use a technical thought-leadership approach (e.g., "Leveraging the ChatGPT API") to attract the right founder profile, integrating CRV founder stories.

### Project Scope & Expectations

  - **Content OS:** \~100 keywords.
  - **Sprint Output:** 18–20 articles (5/week for the final 4 weeks).
  - **6-Month Target:** 2,000–4,000 total keywords ranked.
  - **Author Attribution:** All content will be attributed to "Team CRV" to build a collective brand, not individual investor profiles.
