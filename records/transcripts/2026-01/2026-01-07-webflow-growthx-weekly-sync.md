## Context

This is an internal-external sync between GrowthX (the content strategy and execution partner) and Webflow (the platform). GrowthX manages Webflow's content marketing across two main workstreams: Use Case Guides (technical how-to content) and Integration Pages (connector documentation). The meeting reflects a post-holiday reset and mid-project recalibration, with key focus on optimization rather than pure volume production.

The backdrop here is Google's recent algorithm shifts favoring experience-driven, authoritative content over AI-optimized keyword density. GrowthX is actively testing a new content model (exemplified by their work on "Augment Code" for another client) that prioritizes standalone, example-driven sections—a departure from traditional pyramid-structure writing.

## Key Topics

### Use Case Guides: Strategy Shift

  - **Problem:** Current performance is "okay," but the broad SEO approach is underperforming compared to the successful Integration Pages.
  - **Solution:** Pivot to a high-intent keyword strategy.
      - Split long guides into smaller, focused pieces.
      - Add FAQs and improve internal linking to capture high-intent keywords.
  - **Process:** Raymond is reviewing guides; Liz will manage the rework and republishing.
  - **Blocker:** Meta images remain unfixed. Liz will continue to follow up.

### Integration Pages: Scaling & Enrichment

  - **Status:** 520 of 676 pages are published.
  - **Phase 2 (v2):**
      - **New Content:** Find new high-ranking integration topics.
      - **Enrichment:** Retroactively improve top-performing existing pages.
  - **v2 Quality Benchmark:**
      - FAQ enrichment
      - Clear screenshots & lightweight media
      - Improved internal linking
  - **Performance:** A holiday dip in clicks has already recovered.

### Partner Edit Process

  - **Need:** A formal process for partners to request edits to integration pages.
  - **Context:** The current ad-hoc system is informal but functional, with only 2–3 requests in December.
  - **Plan:** Liz will draft a process by the end of January, considering a potential partner-facing interface.

### 2026 Content Strategy

  - **Developer Hub:** In progress (Owner: Jason).
  - **Partnership Request Process:** In progress.
  - **New Content Model:** A resource-heavy, experience-driven model (e.g., "Augment Code") was presented as a potential direction for Use Case Guides.
      - **Rationale:** Aligns with Google's focus on authoritative content and better detects AI-generated text.
      - **Method:** Sections must be standalone and example-driven; removing an example breaks the section's logic.
  - **Category Refresh:** Blocked pending approval from Vivian. Zach will investigate the status.
