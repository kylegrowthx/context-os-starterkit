## Key Topics

### Crux's Content & SEO Challenge

  - **Goal:** Maximize value from in-house SEO and content initiatives.
  - **Problem:** Current agency content is too generic, not audience-specific, and lacks the flexibility required for Crux's sophisticated market.
  - **Audience:** A niche, two-sided marketplace.
      - **Supply:** Clean energy developers & manufacturers (enterprise/Fortune 5000).
      - **Demand:** Corporate taxpayers & financial institutions.
  - **Keyword Strategy:** Focus on specific, high-intent clusters (e.g., "carryback tax credits"), not broad, high-volume terms.
  - **Content Cadence:** Currently 2 SEO articles/month; the 2026 goal is 1 blog post/week.
  - **Growth Marketing Need:** Improve efficiency for integrated campaigns, which are currently manual in Asana.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** A white-glove service combining AI workflows with human experts.
  - **Origin:** Founded by Marcel (ex-CMO of HashiCorp, Service Titan) to automate the organic content engine he built at DeepGram.
  - **Service Scope:**
      - **Strategy:** SEO & GEO (AI Answer Engine Optimization).
      - **Execution:** 5 articles/week (new or refresh), starting with editorial content.
      - **Publishing:** Handles all CMS publishing and optimization.
      - **Monitoring:** Provides LLM visibility and competitive tracking.
  - **Process Inputs:** Brand documents, audience personas, product marketing materials, and a kickoff call.

### GrowthX's AI Workflow & Examples

  - **Core Process:** Agentic AI workflows (self-correcting until quality scores are met) are guided by human experts.
  - **Key Differentiator: "Artifacts"**
      - Deep, verticalized context documents (e.g., construction operations manager persona) that detail fears, motivations, and how to win trust.
      - Weave in customer stories and unique data to differentiate content.
  - **Workflow Steps:**
    1.  **Research Plan:** Generate research questions based on keyword data and artifacts.
    2.  **Drafting:** Create an initial draft.
    3.  **Human Review:** Editors add specific instructions (e.g., "add 3 bullet points for a female entrepreneur").
    4.  **Fact-Checking:** Break the article into chunks → perform deep research → suggest rewrites with specific attributions (e.g., citing a person's quote instead of a general statement).
    5.  **Optimization:** Add cross-links, source citations, SEO metadata, and on-brand images.
  - **Client Examples:**
      - **AugmentCode:** 3x organic traffic in 6 months, enabling them to shut off ad spend.
      - **Ramp:** Published 700+ pages, including a programmatic vendor directory and interactive per diem calculator.

### Scope & Future Possibilities

  - **Initial Focus:** On-site content (blogs, guides) to build authority.
  - **Future Scope:** After 3–6 months of on-site success, explore other workstreams like:
      - Repurposing content for social media.
      - Creating sales enablement materials.
      - Third-party thought leadership (Nate's suggestion).
  - **Backlinking:** Not a core service; GrowthX prioritizes content creation ROI over backlink acquisition.
