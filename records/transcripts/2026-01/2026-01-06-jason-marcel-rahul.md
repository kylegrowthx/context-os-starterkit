## Key Topics

### The Problem: LLM Visibility for Brands

  - LLMs are now a top priority for marketers, with CEOs and boards focusing on their teams' ability to drive visibility.
  - **Key Challenge:** Brands need to appear in mid-funnel, category-level searches (e.g., "best expense management tools"), but LLMs rarely cite brand websites for these queries.
  - **Solution:** Get mentioned in the third-party sources (publishers, other brands) that LLMs *do* cite.
  - **Why Mentions \> Backlinks:**
      - LLMs value the copy and context of a mention, not just the link.
      - LLM updates are fast (days/weeks), providing immediate impact, unlike the slow (months/quarters) effect of traditional SEO.

### Noble's Solution: An Automated Mention Marketplace

  - **Core Strategy:** Automate brand mention placement at scale.
  - **Traction:** Launched in Sept; now has \~50 brands (Ramp, Webflow, Zendesk) and is moving upmarket (Adobe, Dell).
  - **Publisher Network:**
      - **Quality Control:** Noble only targets sources already cited by LLMs for relevant prompts, ensuring high-value placements.
      - **Recruitment:** Cold email outreach yields an 8–10% reply rate, with \~50% converting to mentions.
      - **Monetization:** Publishers are either paid or receive reciprocal mentions from Noble's brand network.
  - **Future Vision:** A two-sided marketplace where brands and publishers manage offers in a dashboard, replacing cold email.
      - **Pricing Model:** An auction system will price mentions based on demand from competing brands.

### Agency Partnership Models

  - **GrowthX's Concern:** Backlink services have poor margins for agencies (e.g., a $500 cost marked up to $1k is only 50% margin).
  - **Noble's Proposed Models:**
    1.  **Cost Pass-Through:** Bundle Noble's service into a client package to improve overall results and stickiness.
    2.  **Upsell:** Mark up Noble's cost (e.g., $200 cost → $400 client price) to generate direct margin.
    3.  **Referral:** Earn a pure-margin fee for introducing clients to Noble.

### GrowthX's "Check That" Initiative

  - **Product:** A new, free open index for AI visibility, launching in a few weeks.
  - **Scale:** Already includes 6,000+ brands with pages that are gaining LLM citations (e.g., a page for "Mentally Fine pricing" is already cited).
  - **Business Model:** A loss leader (costs GrowthX hundreds of thousands/mo) to corner the market on AI visibility data, with revenue from custom tracking and data licensing (e.g., to Webflow).
  - **Strategic Fit:**
      - **As a Customer:** GrowthX can use Noble to get its own content and client pages mentioned in LLMs.
      - **As a Publisher:** GrowthX's 6,000+ brand pages provide a massive, high-quality inventory of "reverse mentions" for Noble's marketplace.
