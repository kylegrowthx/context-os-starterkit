- **Top Priority:** Immediately add target keywords (`AI Recruiter`, `AI Interviewer`) to H1s and meta descriptions on core pages. This is the fastest way to signal relevance to search engines.

## Key Topics

### SEO for Target Keywords

  - **Goal:** Rank for high-value keywords like `AI Recruiter`, `AI Interviewer`, and `AI for staffing`.
  - **Challenge:** High competition and a lower backlink profile than competitors require a focus on topical authority.
  - **Action Plan:**
      - **On-Page Optimization:** Add keywords to H1s and meta descriptions on core pages.
      - **Content Strategy:** Create content clusters around the parent keyword (`AI Recruiter`) using related keywords to build authority.
      - **Internal Linking:** Link all blog mentions of target keywords to their respective conversion pages (e.g., `AI Recruiter` → Homepage).
      - **Schema Markup:** Implement JSON-LD schema (Organization, Product) to improve visibility for both search engines and LLMs.

      - **Removed:** 2 products

  - **Performance:** Engaged sessions are trending up post-holiday, but the article "Best AI Recruiting Tools" is underperforming and will be refreshed.

### Backlink Strategy

  - **Action:** Carly will submit a ticket to the GrowthX director team to explore backlink strategy options.
  - **No-Cost Tactics:** Donald can pursue guest blogging and leverage network connections for mentions.
