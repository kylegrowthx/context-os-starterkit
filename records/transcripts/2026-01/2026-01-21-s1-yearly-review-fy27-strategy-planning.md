## Summary

SentinelOne (Ankit, Mahati) and GrowthX (Bailey) reviewed the CVE database and CS101 editorial content performance from S1 FY26. The critical shift for FY27 is pivoting from organic traffic to conversion rate as the primary metric (target >2.5%, up from 1.9%). The team identified three strategic priorities: fixing CVE indexing issues, expanding behavior-focused content, and exploring off-site AI discoverability (Reddit, Discord, Quora) to maintain LLM authority leadership.

---

## Context

GrowthX provides content marketing services to SentinelOne, a cybersecurity company. The engagement spans two content properties: the CVE (Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures) database—a programmatic, at-scale vulnerability resource—and CS101, an editorial content hub focused on AI security topics. In S1 FY26, the team published 25 CS101 articles and grew the CVE database while defending SentinelOne's #1 position in LLM mentions (up from ~30% to 35% share).

The meeting context is critical: it marks SentinelOne's fiscal year boundary (FY26 ending, FY27 beginning on Feb 1). Marketing strategy is shifting from volume-based metrics (organic traffic) to conversion efficiency (CR). This requires deeper alignment on product lines, attribution tracking, and more "radical" content strategies to stay ahead of narrowing competitive gaps.

## Key Topics

### FY27 Strategic Shift: Conversion Rate

  - **New Primary Metric:** Conversion Rate (CR) is now the single goal for web, user testing, and SEO.
  - **Performance & Target:**
      - FY26 Avg. CR: \~1.9%
      - FY27 Target: \>2.5%, ideally 3% (vs. 2.5% industry standard).
  - **Attribution Challenge:** Tracking the true source of conversions is difficult, especially for users who visit via organic search and convert later via a paid channel.
      - **Action:** GrowthX's technical SEO team will investigate solutions.

### S1 Performance Review

  - **CVE Database:**
      - **Issue:** A recent site-wide change to trailing-slash URLs broke CVE indexing, causing GSC errors.
      - **Fix:** Marcus is implementing a fix today.
      - **Success Metric:** A tech company requested a CVE entry update, validating the database's authority.
  - **CS101 Editorial Content:**
      - **Performance:** 25 articles published; "AI threat landscape" is the top driver of traffic.
      - **Opportunity:** Prioritize "behavioral AI" content to highlight key product differentiators and capture bottom-of-funnel traffic.
  - **LLM Authority:**
      - **Status:** Holding the \#1 position for LLM mentions, with share growing from \~30% to 35%.
      - **Risk:** Competitors are narrowing the gap, requiring a proactive strategy to maintain the lead.

### FY27 Strategic Direction

  - **Product Alignment:** A session with the product team is needed to align content with product lines and the ongoing brand refresh (which is IA-focused and won't change goals).
  - **Out-of-the-Box Content:** The team wants to move beyond standard SEO content to explore "radical" ideas.
  - **Off-Site AI Discoverability:** Investigate influencing LLMs on platforms like Reddit, Quora, and Discord.
      - **Example:** Automate monitoring and semi-automate responses to build brand presence.
