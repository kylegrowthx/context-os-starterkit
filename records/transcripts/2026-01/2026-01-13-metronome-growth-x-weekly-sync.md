### Content Quality & Process Improvements

  - **Feedback-Driven Refinements:** GrowthX is implementing specific changes based on Will's feedback:
      - **"Pricing at a Glance":** Improve accuracy by identifying and applying recurring patterns.
      - **"Metronome's Take":** Shift from promotional language to a use-case-focused, analytical tone.
  - **Source Tracking:**
      - **Problem:** Google Docs currently list only "seed sources," not all sources used in deep research.
      - **Solution:** GrowthX will update its pipeline to provide a consolidated list of every source used in the final analysis.
      - **Rationale:** To create an audit trail for pricing data, enabling Metronome to validate information and respond to inquiries (e.g., from sales) about potential discrepancies.
  - **Indexing Issue:**
      - **Problem:** New content pages are not being automatically indexed by Google.
      - **Solution:** GrowthX is manually requesting indexing via Google Search Console (GSC) to prevent traffic loss and will monitor for automatic indexing to resume.

### Future Audit & Expansion

  - **Plan:** A full content audit will be conducted in \~4 weeks, after the initial 40 companies are covered.
  - **Scope:** The audit will assess the pricing index's:
      - **Breadth:** Market coverage (e.g., AI vs. other verticals).
      - **Depth:** Vertical-specific analysis (e.g., voice/image generation).
