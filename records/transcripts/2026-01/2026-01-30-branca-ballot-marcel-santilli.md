## Key Topics

### Client Engagement and Project Structuring

Both agreed that a phased, time-boxed client engagement model with clear deliverables maximizes impact and efficiency.

**Branca's model:**
- Onboard one new client per month with an intense first month to define story, messaging, and positioning
- Initial phase focuses on clarifying the client's narrative, differentiators, and market angle
- Follow-up work includes website updates and targeted launch initiatives
- After 2-3 months, transition to a light advisory role, handing over the roadmap for execution

**GrowthX's strategy sprint:**
- Eight-week process with deep discovery, context building, and product marketing
- Kickoff calls, deep dives documented in templates and SOPs
- Post-sprint deliverables: AI workflows, quality frameworks aligned to buyer's journey
- Helps clients decide on distribution channels and hiring needs

### Market Readiness and Client Qualification

Both emphasized pre-qualifying clients to ensure they're ready for strategic growth initiatives.

- Branca's approach: "pre-massage" companies before they're ready for GrowthX
- Example: current client is a "cluster" now but will be ready in three months
- GrowthX already declines companies not ready for growth acceleration
- Focus on clients with some product-market fit but unclear positioning or story
- Avoid extremely technical or early-stage clients lacking clear market needs
