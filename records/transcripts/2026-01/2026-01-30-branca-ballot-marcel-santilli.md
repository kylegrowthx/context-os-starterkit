## Key Topics

### Branca's Engagement Model: Intense, Time-Boxed, Hands-On

Branca is moving away from advisory-only ("tell you what to do") work toward hands-on, limited-duration engagements.

- **Month 1 is dense:** Story definition, messaging, positioning clarity, market angle validation, website planning, channels research and roadmap
- **Months 2-3 lighter:** Advisory as client executes on Month 1 outputs (website build, launch initiatives, channel setup)
- **Hard exit:** No retainer, no ongoing relationship—client gets full roadmap and hands off to internal team or hires. Branca moves to next client.
- **Client qualification:** Target companies with product in market + some product-market fit, but unclear story/differentiators. Avoid purely technical/early-stage without clear market need.
- **Workflow:** Discovery (story, what are we selling, differentiators, market angle) → website/positioning clarity → channels and launch roadmap → client execution and hiring

### GrowthX's Strategy Sprint: Deep, Systematic, 8-Week Program

Marcel detailed GrowthX's process as a complement and follow-on to Branca's work.

- **Phase 1 (deep discovery):** Kickoff call with prepared questions (documented in Notion templates). Stakeholder deep dives with everyone so all feel heard. Company audit. Space analysis.
- **Phase 2 (context & roadmap):** Synthesize context from Phase 1. Map buyer personas and buyer's journey. Identify topics and questions buyers care about. Create 2-year content and distribution roadmap.
- **Phase 3 (systems & execution):** Define AI workflows and quality frameworks. Address distribution decisions (LinkedIn outbound, social, content channels, hiring needs, rev ops fixes, recruitment support).
- **Output:** Actionable roadmap. Not just strategy—templated processes clients can execute on immediately.
- **Philosophy:** Help clients clarify what they want before hiring expensive functions. Avoid firing CMOs every 12 months by getting decision-making right upfront.

### Pre-Qualification Pipeline: Branca ➜ GrowthX

The partnership closes a gap: companies needing "pre-qualification" before they're ready for GrowthX.

- **The problem:** GrowthX gets asked constantly for fractional CMO/strategic help. GrowthX declines unqualified deals (too early, no product-market fit, messaging still messy).
- **Branca's role:** Works with companies in the "messy" stage—clarifies narrative, messaging, positioning. After 3 months, company is ready for GrowthX's deeper strategy sprint.

- **Mutual benefit:** Branca gets deal flow + warm referrals. GrowthX gets qualified pipeline and fills the gap it can't serve itself.

### Operational Excellence: SOPs, Templates, Structured Discovery

Both speakers converged on systematization as the key to scaling without chaos.

- **Marcel's example:** Kickoff call is sacred and templated. Every client starts with the same structured questions (documented, repeated, iterative). Questions are Notion-based with audit, deep dive templates, context synthesis—all reusable.
