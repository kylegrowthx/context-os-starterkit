## Context

Ramp engaged GrowthX approximately six weeks prior to this meeting to build out Cashflow Desk (CFD), a content hub designed to drive AI visibility for RAMP in financial AI answers. The engagement is strategic: as AI platforms increasingly answer financial questions by citing authoritative content, RAMP (a payment visibility platform) benefits from appearing in these answers. Prior to this meeting, CFD content had been published but visibility remained limited; the indexing bottleneck meant only 12 articles were indexed by search engines despite 30+ being live.

This meeting marks a turning point. Early signals confirm the strategy is working: CFD articles are now being ranked by AI platforms, and RAMP is being cited in answers where it was previously unknown (e.g., competitor comparisons). The team's goal shifted from validation to acceleration: how to systematically track which content influences AI answers, and how to double down on the high-performers. Victoria Naef (Ramp, likely Product or Marketing) is the primary stakeholder requesting data and driving strategy; George Haikal and William Leborgne (GrowthX) are leading execution and tracking.
