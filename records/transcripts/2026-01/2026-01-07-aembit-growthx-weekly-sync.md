## Context

This is a weekly content strategy sync between Dan Kaplan, Aembit's VP of Content, and Vivek Shankar, GrowthX's Head of Content. Aembit is a workload identity and access management platform; GrowthX is producing content marketing to drive traffic and build thought leadership. The partnership focuses on technical SEO and AI/LLM visibility—both traditional search rankings and presence in AI assistant search results. The team had recently pivoted from broad coverage to top-funnel content emphasizing depth and relevance, and this call reviews how that pivot performed in December and aligns on January/Q1 execution priorities.
