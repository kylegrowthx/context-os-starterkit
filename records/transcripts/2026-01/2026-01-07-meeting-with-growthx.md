## Summary

ALSAC (St. Jude's fundraising arm) met with GrowthX's Tyler Pavlas to explore a hybrid AI content service designed to scale production from 5 blog posts/week to 5-10+ articles/week across stjude.org while monitoring visibility in AI search answers. The core question: can GrowthX's "context engineering" workflows produce accurate, on-brand content for a highly regulated nonprofit without compromising trust or accuracy.

---

## Context

ALSAC is the fundraising arm of St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. The organization's primary content focus is optimizing stjude.org (owned domain) for both traditional search and emerging AI search (AEO) to drive donations and support. The team publishes ~5 patient/survivor stories per week on their "Inspire" blog and uses Conductor's platform for AI visibility tracking and content optimization. Aimee Hall, the content strategist, initiated contact with GrowthX after learning about the agency's work from a mutual connection (Otto, on a St. Jude board) who works with a GrowthX client. Tyler Pavlas, GrowthX's founding AE, presented the agency's hybrid model—combining AI workflows ("context engineering") with a team of content experts—to address ALSAC's core challenge: scaling content creation beyond manual capacity while maintaining accuracy for a highly regulated, trust-dependent brand.

## Key Topics

### ALSAC's Content & SEO Challenge

  - **Mission:** ALSAC is the fundraising and awareness arm for St. Jude Children's Research Hospital. The brand is St. Jude, not ALSAC.
  - **Current State:**
      - Focus is on optimizing existing `stjude.org` content for AI search (FAQs, schema), not new article creation.
      - A separate team creates \~5 patient/supporter stories per week for the "Inspire" blog.
      - Primary tool is Conductor's "Writing Assistant" for optimization, not full-scale generation.
  - **Core Problem:** Past efforts to target non-branded fundraising topics failed due to low search volume. The team needs creative strategies for new audiences and content formats, especially for sensitive topics.

### GrowthX's Hybrid AI Content Service

  - **Model:** A platform-powered service combining AI workflows with human experts (e.g., Chief Content Officer ran TechCrunch).
  - **Standard Engagement:**
      - **Phase 1 (8 weeks):** Strategy sprint to build custom AI workflows ("context engineering") using client assets (personas, brand guides).
      - **Phase 2 (Ongoing):** Growth execution, targeting 5 articles/week.
  - **LLM Monitoring:** Uses the partner tool Scrunch to track visibility and sentiment in AI answers against competitors.
  - **Client Examples:**
      - **Augment Code:** 3x organic traffic in 6 months, enabling them to shut down paid ads.
      - **Biologica:** Created specialized health content with a custom AI workflow to simulate a chief medical officer's review, ensuring accuracy for a highly regulated industry.

### Proposed Path Forward for ALSAC

  - **Initial Focus:** Educational content for high-intent donors (e.g., Donor Advised Funds, tax-efficient giving).
  - **Customization:** GrowthX is open to custom projects beyond standard articles, like glossaries or directories.
  - **Key Questions for Next Call:**
      - **Revenue Impact:** Timeline from content creation to donations/leads.
      - **Risk Mitigation:** Process for ensuring accuracy for a highly regulated brand.
      - **Scope:** Focus on owned domains vs. other channels (social, Reddit).
