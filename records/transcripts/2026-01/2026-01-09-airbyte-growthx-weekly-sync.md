## Context

This is a routine weekly sync between Airbyte's content team (Tanmay, Nithin, Mario, Kushal) and GrowthX's content strategy leads (Erik, Kyle). The meeting occurs at the end of the first week of January, immediately following the holiday break, making it a strategic planning session for the year ahead. The primary driver for this meeting is a major content pivot: transitioning from a diverse portfolio of articles across old product and agentic AI content to a singular focus on Sonar, Airbyte's upcoming AI-native platform requiring substantial pre-launch visibility.

The broader context includes industry headwinds—a market-wide 45% decline in AI-driven traffic since July, primarily driven by ChatGPT reducing citations in responses. Despite this macro trend, Airbyte's competitive position remains solid, with strong citation share across both Profound and Scrunch tracking platforms. The GrowthX team is using this session to align with Airbyte stakeholders on realistic expectations, confirm the new content cadence, and establish clear ownership of new initiatives (use cases pages, content enhancements, analytics improvements) ahead of a planned rebrand in the next 1-2 months.

  - **AI-Driven Traffic Decline:**

      - Initial traffic is strong for a new subfolder: 150 peak sessions, now at 95 weekly.

      - Continued growth, reaching 1.8K weekly sessions.
      - Data Integration remains the top cohort; Data Pipeline grew 120%.
  - **Content Tracking:**
      - **Profound:** Tracks individual page citations and integrates with Cloudflare for direct traffic data.
      - **Scrunch:** Lacks direct traffic data and individual page tracking.
      - **Action:** Erik will update Scrunch prompts to track the new product.

### Content Strategy & Production

  - **Strategic Pivot to Sonar:**
      - **Rationale:** Build pre-launch traffic and visibility for the new AI platform.
      - **Cadence:** 15 articles/week for Sonar, replacing the current 5 agentic + 12 old product mix.
      - **Timeline:** Effective February 1.
  - **New Project: Agent Use Cases Pages:**
      - **Goal:** Capture high search volume with programmatic pages, inspired by sites like Lindy.
      - **Status:** Blocked pending Tanmay's delivery of the page template doc and use case list.
      - **Team:** Jacob will be involved in the engineering setup.
  - **Content Enhancements:**
      - **TLDRs:** Will be added to all Data Engineering Resources, replacing AI Summaries for consistency.
      - **AI Widgets:** Will be added to all agentic articles to signal relevance to LLMs.
  - **Rebranding:**
      - A full rebrand and new website are planned for the AI-native shift.
      - **Timeline:** \~1–2 months.
      - **Impact:** New pages must be built to accommodate the new visual identity.
