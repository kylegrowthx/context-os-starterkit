  - **Strategic Shift:** A sprint to regain SEO ground is approved.

      - **Focus:** Prioritize SEO-driven content (e.g., listicles, FAQs) over other topics.

### Content Quality Issues

  - **Problem:** Recent blogs have critical errors and inconsistencies.
  - **Specific Issues:**
      - Dead links.
      - Missing internal links to Galileo.
      - Poor anchor text placement.
      - Outdated product messaging and docs.
      - Inconsistent CTAs focused only on "guardrails," ignoring the full platform (observability, evals).
  - **Action:** Galileo will provide detailed feedback via comments in the review queue.

### Competitor Landing Pages

  - **Blocker:** The original Framer design is not replicable in the CMS, requiring bespoke development for each page.
  - **New Plan:** Simplify the design to a pillar-page style.
      - **Proposed Features:** A different header and a sticky "Book a Demo" CTA in the table of contents.
  - **Action:** Galileo will resend the existing wireframe for feedback to unblock the design process.

### New AI Visibility Tool

  - **Context:** GrowthX is transitioning from Scrunch to an internal tool for AI visibility.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Immediate Data:** Backloads 90 days of historical data, providing instant insights.
      - **High-Intent Focus:** Library of pre-loaded prompts targets "evaluation stage" searches.
      - **Clearer UI:** Shows full AI-generated answers, brand mentions, and direct citations.
      - **Custom Prompts:** Allows for private, Galileo-specific prompts.
  - **Timeline:** Client access is targeted for the end of next week.
