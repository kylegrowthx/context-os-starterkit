## Key Topics

### ModelOp's Content Challenges

- **Primary Need:** Automate lead attribution reporting, a manual process since migrating to HubSpot.
- **Secondary Need:** Address inconsistent content production, a key challenge for scaling.
  - **Current Mix:** Freelancers, in-house team (Jay Combs, Demand Gen Director), internal experts (Product/CTO), and AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  - **AI Limitation:** AI is used only for brainstorming and editing, as it produces inaccurate or bland content for ModelOp's new, technical market.

### GrowthX's Solution: A Hybrid Agency Model

- **Model:** A hybrid agency/platform service where GrowthX acts as a full content team, managing strategy, execution, and reporting.
- **Core Technology:** A proprietary internal platform with agentic AI workflows for research, writing, and optimization.
  - **Inputs:** Workflows are guided by client-specific artifacts (personas, brand guidelines) and deep product knowledge.
  - **Quality Control:** Workflows self-correct (e.g., re-running research) to meet quality thresholds, ensuring accuracy for technical topics.
- **Process:**
  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint ($40K fixed fee)**
    - Develops custom AI workflows and a content roadmap based on search volume and competitor analysis.
    - Delivers 5–10 articles, with publishing starting by week 4.
  - **Phase 2: Retainer ($18K/month)**
    - Delivers 5 net-new or refreshed articles per week.
    - Includes forward-deployed AI engineers for custom pipeline work.
- **Commitment:** Phase 1 is a standalone engagement; clients decide to opt into Phase 2 at the end of the sprint.

### Pricing & Fit

- **Pricing:**
  - Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint): $40,000 fixed fee.
  - Phase 2 (Retainer): $18,000 per month.
- **Target Client:** Companies with strong conviction in content as a primary growth channel, typically Series A–C startups or enterprises.
- **ModelOp's Position:** Interested in the solution's power and consistency but must evaluate the budget.
