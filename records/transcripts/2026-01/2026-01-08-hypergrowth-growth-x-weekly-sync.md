## Context

This meeting kicked off GrowthX's transition from bottleneck-prone, low-velocity content production to a nimble, short-form cadence optimized for social impact. Over the past year, GrowthX struggled with multi-week review cycles on long articles that often failed to gain traction. The team recognized that large pieces created strategic friction and killed momentum. The solution: ship short-form LinkedIn content weekly, validated through brief-first approval workflows, with Substack expansion only for proven performers. Guillaume Cabane (HyperGrowth founder) emphasized the need for discipline around voice—mild controversy, candid tone, and impactful delivery—while Erik O'Brien (GrowthX) brought tactical execution frameworks from client work and new generative capabilities (Nano Banana for custom graphics). Giulia Caracausi (HyperGrowth) bridged strategy and production, ensuring the team could move from planning to shipping within days.

## Key Topics

### Problem: Low Velocity & Impact

  - **Core Issue:** Large, full-length articles create review bottlenecks, leading to lost momentum and low shipping velocity.
  - **Goal:** Shift from strategy debates to execution, focusing on shipping content that builds team confidence and impact.

### Solution: New Workflow & Cadence

  - **Brief-First Model:**
      - GrowthX will create a \~0.5-page brief for review before writing the full post.
      - **Why:** This ensures alignment on the story arc, tone, and key points upfront, reducing major revisions later.
  - **LinkedIn-First Cadence:**
      - **LinkedIn:** 1 post/week.
      - **Substack:** 1 article/bi-weekly, based on successful LinkedIn posts.
      - **Why:** Short-form content is faster to review and has a higher effort-to-impact ratio on LinkedIn.

### Solution: Defined Voice & Visuals

  - **Post Structure:**
      - Controversial hook
      - Personal story
      - Prediction
  - **Tone:** Short, candid, impactful, and synthetic.
  - **Visuals:** GrowthX will create custom graphics (infographics, diagrams) to increase engagement, using new AI capabilities (e.g., Nano Banana).

### First Topics & Timeline

  - **Topics:**
      - "Equity Multiplier Formula"
      - "Three-Bin Circle of Advisory"
  - **Equity Multiplier Graphic:**
      - The post will use a graphic based on a provided table showing the relationship between startup equity value and exit likelihood.
      - **Why:** This visual illustrates the "bell curve" of optimal equity value vs. risk, peaking at the Series B stage, and is central to the personal story.
  - **Timeline:**
      - **Mon/Tues:** GrowthX delivers briefs for both topics.
      - **Thurs:** Target date to post the first LinkedIn piece.
