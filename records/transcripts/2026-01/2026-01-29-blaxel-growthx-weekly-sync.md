## Key Topics

### Content Production & Review Process

  - **Problem:** Blaxel's review process is a bottleneck, with initial reviews taking 20–30 mins/article.
  - **Solution:** A new workflow improves efficiency:
    1.  GrowthX delivers articles via Slack notification.
    2.  Blaxel dispatches articles to an in-house writer (Vikram) for review.
    3.  Nicolas consolidates feedback for GrowthX.
  - **Rationale:** The Slack notification is critical, as the main table tool is difficult to navigate without filtering access.

### Content Strategy & Topics

  - **Article Mix:** Shift to a majority of non-technical articles to reduce review time.
      - **Target:** Max 2 technical articles per week.
  - **New Topic Idea: Sandbox Gateways**
      - **Angle:** Warn against using gateways, as they limit users to the "lowest common denominator" of features from underlying sandbox providers.
      - **Keyword Research (Aida):** Low volume (20/mo) but high opportunity, as Google struggles to serve educational intent.
  - **Content Ideas:** Blaxel will add all future ideas to the shared Notion page.

### Writing Guidelines & Style

  - **Feedback (from Vikram):**
      - **Readability:** Articles use long sentences and complex words, making them hard to read.
      - **Specificity:** Content is too generic; it needs more nuance specific to AI/ML.
  - **GrowthX Response:**
      - **Readability:** Will update guidelines for shorter, simpler sentences.
      - **Specificity:** Will refine instructions to ensure AI-specific nuance.
  - **Rationale:** LLMs prioritize content structure (H2s, H3s), allowing for flexibility in tone and style.

### Blog Enhancements & CTAs

  - **Standardization: "microVM"**
      - **Decision:** Remove the dash from "microVM" to align with common usage.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will update all relevant blog posts; Blaxel will update its docs and website.
  - **New Feature: Reusable CTAs**
      - **Goal:** Drive traffic to key landing pages (e.g., "Get Started," "Contact," "Sandboxes").
      - **Implementation:** Blaxel will build an MVP system with a default CTA that is overwritable per article.

### Engagement Status & Renewal

  - **Renewal Intent:** Blaxel would renew based on the strong process and partnership, even though it's too early for results-based metrics.
  - **Phase Timeline:** The calibration phase ends Feb 16, transitioning to a steady production cadence.
  - **Publication Process:**
      - **Default:** Articles publish once feedback is addressed, unless explicitly flagged for a second review.
      - **New Workflow:** Blaxel will flag articles requiring a second review in the draft comments.
