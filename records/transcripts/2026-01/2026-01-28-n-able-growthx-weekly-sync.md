## Context

This is the fourth content sync between N-Able (endpoint management platform) and GrowthX (B2B content marketing agency). Since the partnership began, GrowthX has published 14 blog articles covering endpoint management, security operations, and patch management. The partnership centers on two key initiatives: building organic visibility through content marketing and tracking N-Able's mentions in LLM outputs (AI model visibility), which increasingly influences how IT professionals and platform evaluators find solutions.

In January 2026, N-Able launched the AdLumen initiative, an internal push for managed detection and response (MDR) coverage. This meeting reflects the partnership's shift toward strategic content planning around high-intent MDR topics. The introduction of Check That—GrowthX's proprietary tool for tracking LLM citations and visibility—signals a maturation of the relationship and a move toward data-driven competitive tracking. Matt Alves-Hill, GrowthX's content lead, is the primary driver of editorial strategy and performance metrics, while Elaine Duhaylungsod (N-Able) manages content intake, feedback, and coordination with N-Able's product marketing teams.
