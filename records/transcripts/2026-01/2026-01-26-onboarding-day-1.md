## Key Topics

### Onboarding Process & Tools

  - **Linear (Project Management):**
      - Manages onboarding tasks via the "Account Executive Onboarding" project.
      - **Workflow:** Complete an "issue" → mark "Done" in Linear.
      - **Issue Statuses:** Backlog, To Do, In Progress, Done.
      - **Tickets:** Use the `/linear create` Slack command to report engineering issues.
  - **Notion (Knowledge Base):**
      - Central hub for all documentation, SOPs, and client workspaces.
      - Onboarding tasks link directly to relevant Notion pages.
  - **Slack (Communication):**
      - Primary tool for internal and client communication.
      - [**Channel Naming:** `D-EXT-[ClientName]` for external/delivery; `D-INT-[ClientName]` for internal/delivery.](https://fathom.video/share/1HLGdxBChSQxw_LmovyqkpKc9YKsXpBo?tab=summary&timestamp=386.0)
  - **Geekbot (Asynchronous Standups):**
      - Slack-integrated bot for daily standups, replacing live meetings.
      - **Process:** Respond to the prompt at the start of your shift → updates post to the GTM channel.
  - **Other Tools:**
      - **Loom:** Screen recording.
      - **Zoom:** Meetings.
      - **Fathom:** AI note-taker.

### Company Expectations & Policies

  - **Time Zone Overlap:** 4-hour minimum with Pacific Time (PT).
  - **Daily Geekbot:** Mandatory for all team members.
  - **Time Off (PTO):** Unlimited, no approval needed.
      - **Process:** Block calendar with "OOO" event → invite your team (GTM).
  - **Employee Referral Program:**
      - **Bonus:** Up to $10k (engineering) or $5k (non-engineering).
      - **Condition:** Referral must remain employed for 6 months.
  - **IT Support:** Use the `#ITN-tools` Slack channel for access issues, CC'ing Alice or Yusuf.
  - **Bi-Weekly All-Hands:** Next meeting is tomorrow.
      - **Action:** Prepare a brief self-introduction (name, location, fun fact).

### Account Setup: `team@growthxlabs.com`

  - This shared email is the central login for most tools and the address clients use to grant system access.
  - **Troubleshooting:** A 1Password extension issue was resolved by refreshing the browser, which forced it to load the correct "Internal Team" vault.
