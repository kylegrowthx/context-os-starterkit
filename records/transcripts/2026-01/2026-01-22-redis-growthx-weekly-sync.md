## Context

Redis and GrowthX are executing a content marketing initiative aimed at improving SEO visibility and driving conversions. GrowthX has been publishing articles for Redis at a pace of 5 per week (20/month, 60/quarter), with the goal of establishing topic clusters that can be analyzed for performance and audience fit. As of late January 2026, 15 articles are published, 5 are in PMM review, and GrowthX continues pre-processing new articles using AI tools to reduce technical inaccuracies before review. The primary bottleneck is Product Marketing Manager (PMM) reluctance to attach their names to published content without personal review, which prevents the planned "Arabite process" of auto-publishing content that meets quality thresholds. Reet Mand (Product Strategy) identified an opportunity to engage DevRel and Sales Engineers as additional reviewers and contributors, both to accelerate the review cycle and unblock content production bottlenecks.

  - **Problem:** PMMs are the primary bottleneck, hesitant to attach their names to articles they haven't reviewed due to quality concerns (e.g., technical inaccuracies, "marketing fluff").

  - **Proposed Solution:**
      - **Low-Risk Content:** Fung-Lin will ask PMMs to approve skipping brand reviews for article refreshes, as their technical accuracy is already established.
      - **New Reviewers:** Engage DevRel and SAs to assist with reviews.
          - **Rationale:** More reviewers → faster cycle times.
