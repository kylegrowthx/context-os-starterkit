## Summary

Content production remains on track at 5 articles per week (17 published to date), but the extensive product team review cycle is becoming a bottleneck for category topics like Quality Management. GrowthX and Cresta agreed to address this by having Oren record 1-hour briefings on key topics to create reusable artifacts, which could save 4-5 hours of review time over the coming months. Additionally, Aida discovered 130 URLs in Google Search Console that are discovered but not indexed—critical content like blogs and case studies affected—and Cresta granted GrowthX owner access to GSC to manually resubmit high-value pages for indexing.

## Context

GrowthX is in Phase 1 of a content production engagement with Cresta, delivering 5 articles per week to support Cresta's content marketing strategy. The onboarding sprint team (led by Aida) is responsible for establishing the foundational content OS, review process, and quality standards before handing off to the main delivery team and the new engagement manager (Bailey) in Phase 2. This sync focused on removing bottlenecks, preserving the work done during review cycles by formalizing tacit knowledge into reusable artifacts, and addressing a critical technical SEO issue discovered in Cresta's Webflow site.
