## Key Topics

- **Product Priorities:** MDR and Cove are the top 1A/1B products for business growth; N-Central (Lumen) should not be neglected but is ranked third.
- **"Attack Resilience" Campaign:** A central content theme linking N-able's platforms to the attack lifecycle:

  - **MDR:** During-attack response (e.g., incident response, threat hunting).
