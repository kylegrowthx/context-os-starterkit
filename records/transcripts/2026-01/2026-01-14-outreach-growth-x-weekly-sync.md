**Content OS Business Score System**
- **Score 3:** Directly related to a product capability (prioritized first).
- **Score 2:** Tied to the product but not a specific capability.
- **Score 1:** Adjacent topics for audience expansion.

- **Current State:** 2% brand citation rate (competitors at 5-6%).
