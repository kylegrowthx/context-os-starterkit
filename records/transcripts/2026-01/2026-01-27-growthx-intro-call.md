## Key Topics

### ThreeKit's Repositioning & Content Gap

  - ThreeKit is shifting from 3D visualization to an AI agent that simplifies complex product configuration for B2B manufacturers.
  - **Problem Solved:** The AI bridges the gap between a customer's "fuzzy inputs" (e.g., measurements) and the precise configuration data required for manufacturing and order entry.
  - **Target Audience:** Marketing, sales, and IT leaders at B2B enterprises (300+ employees) in the US.
  - **Content Gap:** The current content is optimized for the old visualization story. The new strategy must:
      - **Target CPQ & B2B Commerce keywords** to compete in a larger market.
      - **Differentiate** by highlighting ThreeKit's core strengths: AI-powered CPQ and visualization for complex products.
      - **Maintain compatibility** as a front-end for existing platforms (Salesforce, SAP) to avoid direct competition where unnecessary.

### GrowthX's Solution & Methodology

  - GrowthX is an end-to-end content partner using a proprietary AI platform with expert oversight.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** Develops a keyword-driven roadmap, builds context artifacts (personas, guidelines), and publishes \~10 articles to establish early signals.
    2.  **Growth Execution (12 months):** Scales content production to 5 articles/week, with ThreeKit's team providing feedback to refine the AI engine.
  - **Core Differentiator:** The platform's sophisticated, agentic workflows are designed to produce higher-quality, more consistent content than generic tools, while avoiding their scaling costs and maintenance issues.

### Proof Points & Examples

  - **Origin Story:** Founder Marcel's organic growth playbook (IBM, HashiCorp, Deepgram) is the foundation of the platform's methodology.
  - **Augment Code Case Study:**
      - **Goal:** Compete with GitHub and Cursor for AI coding tools.
      - **Result:** GrowthX content is now cited in AI search answers for competitors, positioning Augment Code as "built for large code bases."
      - **Metric:** AI visibility for key prompts rose from 0% to 28%.
  - **DataGrid:** An investor referral; Marc requested the same account team, citing their similar business model.
