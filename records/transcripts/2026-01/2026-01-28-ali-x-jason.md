## Overview

- **Strategy:** Use hyper-targeted campaigns (e.g., ad + content for one industry) to generate high-quality leads, as Understory's internal campaigns prove the model works.
- **Tactics:** Use LinkedIn Lead Forms for high conversion and UTMs on direct Calendly links for precise tracking.

## Key Topics

### The Understory Playbook: Hyper-Targeted Campaigns

  - Understory's internal campaigns (~$3k/mo spend) generate qualified meetings by targeting specific personas within a single industry.
  - **Example:** A cybersecurity campaign (ad + organic content) generated a call from a publicly traded company.
  - **Cost per Qualified Meeting:** ~$300–$350.

### Core Tactics: Lead Capture & Tracking

  - **Direct-to-Calendly:**
      - **Process:** Ad → Calendly link with UTMs.
      - **Why:** UTMs enable precise tracking of booking source.
  - **LinkedIn Lead Forms:**
      - **Process:** Ad → Pre-populated form → Confirmation message with Calendly link.
      - **Why:** High conversion rates; can validate for work emails only.
      - **Integration:** Connects directly to HubSpot or via Zapier.

### Audience Targeting: Precision & Automation

  - **Company List Uploads:**
      - **Process:** Upload a list of target companies → overlay job titles.
      - **Why:** Highly predictable and reliable targeting.
  - **Dynamic HubSpot Lists:**
      - **Process:** Connect HubSpot to LinkedIn Ads.
      - **Why:** Automatically syncs audiences, enabling retargeting of warm leads (e.g., website visitors, event attendees) with specific content.

### Experiment Plan for GrowthX

  - **Goal:** Validate if LinkedIn ads can drive value for GrowthX.
  - **Structure:**
      - **Focus:** One service line, one goal (e.g., demo bookings).
      - **Budget:** $5k–$10k/mo.
      - **Tracking:** Install the LinkedIn Insight Tag (pixel) and set up conversion tracking.
  - **Potential Bottleneck:** GrowthX's single salesperson is already at capacity with 20+ open deals.
      - **Rationale:** Validate the channel first; the bottleneck is a good problem to solve.

### Advanced Tactic: Message Ads

  - **Function:** Sponsored messages appearing in the LinkedIn inbox.
  - **Flow:** Message with CTA → LinkedIn Lead Form.
  - **Why:** Effective for retargeting users who have already engaged with a standard ad.

### Understory Agency Services

  - **Model:** Staffing with a dedicated channel expert and senior oversight.
  - **Pricing (6-month term):**
      - **$8k/mo:** One channel (e.g., Paid Social)
      - **$12k/mo:** Two channels (e.g., Paid Social + Paid Search)
      - **Spend Tiers:** Up to $50k/mo, $50k–$150k/mo, etc.
