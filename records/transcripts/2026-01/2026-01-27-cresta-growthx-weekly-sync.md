### Content Strategy & Prioritization

- **3-Month Plan:** Aligned on a phased content approach to support Cresta's goals.
  - **Months 1–2 (Immediate Priority):**
    - Competitive Comparisons (listicles, Cresta vs. Sierra, Cresta vs. Decagon, etc.)—high-intent, bottom-of-funnel content that's performing well.
    - AI Agents & Customer Experience—foundational topics to build authority and support thought leadership.
  - **Month 3:**
    - Vertical-Specific Guides: Travel & Hospitality, **Insurance** (new priority, replacing broad Healthcare), Financial Services.
    - Contact Center Operations—high-volume, top-of-funnel topics to fill content gaps and drive organic visibility.

- **Competitive Content Strategy:**
  - **Goal:** Create multiple unique articles targeting different keywords for key competitors (Sierra, Decagon, etc.) to maximize ranking opportunities for different search phrases.
  - **Rationale:** Capitalize on competitor brand spend and capture branded search volume. Companies like Sierra are advertising heavily, generating high branded search volume that Cresta can target.
  - **Example Keywords:** "Decagon Alternatives," "Sierra vs. Cresta," "Sierra Alternatives," "Cresta vs. Decagon."
  - **UX Concern:** A guides landing page dominated by competitive articles creates poor user experience. Solution: Redesign guide page to showcase thought leadership prominently and hide competitive content lower in the list. Annie will add this to the next Web Strategy sync agenda.

- **Product Positioning Clarification:**
  - **AI Agent:** A specific Cresta product (newer offering).
  - **Agent Assist:** Cresta's oldest and most established product. Focuses on real-time augmentation: behavioral guidance, knowledge assistance, and workflow automation (note-taking, summarization). Core value prop in market leadership.
  - **QM & Coaching:** Separate product pillar for post-call analysis and agent performance evaluation. Synergistic with Agent Assist but distinct product pillar (paired with Insights for "Conversation Intelligence").

- **New Topic Idea: AI Regulations**
  - **Proposal:** Create authoritative content on emerging AI regulations relevant to contact centers (EU AI Act, US proposals like state-level laws, AI voice regulations for outbound).
  - **Rationale:** Emerging, high-relevance topic for Cresta's ICP. Potential for early ownership and long-term evergreen authority as regulations evolve.
  - **Next Steps:** Bailey to research search volume via SEMrush and Reddit to assess viability. Propose as a subcluster under AI Agents if volume/competition is favorable.

### Vertical Focus Shift

- **Healthcare → Insurance:** Devon clarified that the healthcare vertical should focus specifically on **Insurance** (including healthcare payers like UHG and standard insurance: auto, life, etc.).
  - **Deprioritize:** Healthcare provider topics (patient experience, hospital systems, dental) are lower priority and not in top 3.
  - **Why:** Cresta has clear market leadership with healthcare payers and insurance companies; limited track record with healthcare provider systems.
  - **Note:** Dental is interesting as a traction segment but should be positioned as insurance/staffing adjacent, not healthcare provider.

### Performance & Tracking

- **LLM Performance (Check That):**
  - **Methodology:** GrowthX runs 200+ target keyword prompts to simulate user queries and track which sources are cited in LLM responses. Not optimized for Cresta—prompts are straightforward and represent actual decision-maker queries.
  - **Findings:** Cresta guides appearing in LLM responses for commercial keywords like "best AI agent platform." Multiple guide articles showing up across different LLM queries. Citation rates currently ~1% per URL, but validates that content is being discovered.
  - **Timeline:** Check That access for Cresta team coming in February 2026 (no exact date yet).

- **Direct LLM Traffic (GA4):**
  - **Methodology:** Looker dashboard tracks real user traffic from LLM sources (ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini) to specific landing pages.
  - **Findings:** Cresta receiving traffic from ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, and Google Gemini. Traffic flowing to comparison posts and educational content.
  - **Access:** Annie set dashboard to public; Aida will confirm access works with team account.

- **HubSpot Integration Opportunity:**
  - **Suggestion:** Import published blog URLs into HubSpot to track customer journeys from organic search → product pages → case studies → demo requests.
  - **Owner:** Cresta can set up if interested; GrowthX provided list of published URLs with dates.
