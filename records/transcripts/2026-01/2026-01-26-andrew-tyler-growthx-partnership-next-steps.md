## Context

Elementum is a lean, AI-powered company evaluating a two-phase partnership with GrowthX for content marketing. Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint) is a 30-day engagement to establish baseline performance and content roadmap. Phase 2 is the 12-month Growth Execution phase where GrowthX invests significant resources to build domain authority and drive measurable outcomes.

The primary friction point is Elementum's desire for quarterly payment flexibility in Phase 2 (driven by CFO constraints), while GrowthX maintains a standardized 12-month term across all clients to justify the substantial Phase 1 resource investment and ensure long-term strategic alignment. Additionally, Elementum's unique technical architecture—an AI-generated website built on Vercel without a traditional CMS—creates a risk around content automation, requiring validation before contract finalization.
