## Key Topics

### Growth Cafe Promo Analysis

  - Analysis of the Growth Cafe event promo revealed key targeting insights:
  - **Positive Replies by Seniority:**
      - Head: 35%
      - VP: 30%
      - Director: 27%
      - C-Level: 8.5%
      - Manager: 0%
  - **Positive Replies by Department:** Marketing, Brand, Partnership, Growth (in order).
  - **Action:** Future event campaigns will prioritize Head, VP, and Director titles.

### Event Strategy & Performance

  - Event performance varies by type, requiring different strategies:
      - **Breakfasts:** High registrations, low show rates.
      - **Dinners:** High-touch, text-message-driven invites.
      - **Workshops:** The key growth opportunity.
  - **Fletch Workshop (Feb 26):**
      - **Goal:** Drive attendance from Rob Fletch's \~60k followers.
      - **Rationale:** The Casey workshop proved this tactic works, with followers responding 4x better than cold leads.
      - **Ownership:** Understory will own all outreach (email/LinkedIn copy, execution).
      - **Constraint:** Email volume is limited to \~2,000/week, requiring a phased approach to reach the large follower base.

### SIFT Campaign (LinkedIn Router)

  - The automated router for LinkedIn post engagement is nearly ready for launch.
  - **Process:** Post engagement → Clay table segments by intent → Instantly/HeyReach campaign.
  - **Critical Update:** A HubSpot suppression step is required to prevent messaging contacts in active sales conversations.
      - **Action:** Mark Lim to add a check against the `Contact Understory Exclusion List` segment.
  - **Copy Workflow:** Jason Gong will write copy in a Google Doc for Mark Lim to implement, bypassing direct access issues with Instantly.

### Sales Handoff Process

  - **Problem:** No formal process exists to hand off warm event leads to sales.
  - **Solution:** A new process will send all qualified leads event invites, regardless of sales funnel status.
      - **Rationale:** This ensures maximum outreach and provides Tyler Pavlas with a list for personal follow-up, creating a high-touch experience.
  - **Lead Handoff:** For strong fits (e.g., Together AI), the team will use the most frictionless path (e.g., email intro with Tyler CC'd).
  - **Goal:** Increase new open opportunities from \~20 to 60+ per month.
