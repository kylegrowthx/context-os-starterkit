## Key Topics

### The Ideal EM Profile

  - **Strategic Foundation:** Deep SEO/AI knowledge is non-negotiable.
      - **Why:** EMs must independently build and troubleshoot content strategies using tools like SEMrush and Claude, positioning GrowthX as a strategic partner, not just a blog creator.
  - **Commercial Acumen:** The sprint is a sales process; EMs must be revenue-minded.
      - **Why:** They must build rapport, articulate value, and identify retention risks to convert clients to long-term pod engagements.
      - **Signal:** Look for experience in quota-carrying or retention-based roles.
  - **"Human X-Factor":** Strong communication and charisma are more critical than deep technical knowledge.
      - **Why:** It's easier to train strategy than to teach someone how to build client trust and excitement.
  - **Sourcing Signals:** Active self-development (e.g., AROPS certifications, Pavilion courses) indicates a candidate's passion and fit.

### Sprint vs. Pod EM Roles

  - **Strategy Sprint EM:** A "house builder" role.
      - **Focus:** Scrappy, fast-paced, and strategic.
      - **Why:** Requires building a complete content strategy from scratch under tight timelines.
  - **Pod EM:** A "house maintainer" role.
      - **Focus:** Long-term relationship building and value creation.
      - **Why:** Requires refreshing existing strategies and maintaining intellectual curiosity over a multi-year client relationship.
  - **Decision:** The profiles are similar enough to source from the same candidate pool, with interview questions tailored to each role's nuances.

### Hiring Plan & Urgency

  - **Goal:** Hire 3 EMs for Strategy Sprints ASAP.
  - **Rationale:** This creates a stable team of 4 (Ada + 3 hires), enabling William to transition to a management role.
  - **Interim State:** William, George, and Cathy are currently acting as EMs.
      - **Assumption:** George and Cathy will be redeployed once new hires are ramped.
  - **Internal Sourcing:** No viable internal candidates were identified.
      - **Why:** The production team lacks the required commercial and client-facing experience.

### Interview Process Updates

  - **New Flow:**
    1.  **Recruiter Screen:** Marília
    2.  **Hiring Manager Interview:** William (tests strategic depth)
    3.  **Commercial Acumen Interview:** George (tests client-facing skills)
    4.  **Panel Interview:** Ella & Bridget (tests connecting work to business outcomes)
  - **Take-Home Assignment:** The current assignment is outdated and requires revision.
