## Key Topics

### LLM Traffic Analysis & Strategic Pivot

  - **Finding:** LLM traffic is a developer activation signal, not a top-of-funnel driver.
      - **High Engagement:** 10 views/session, ~2 min duration, low bounce rate.
      - **Primary Destination:** The console, not discovery pages.
      - **High Conversion:** 5x higher rate than organic search.
  - **Strategic Pivot:** Focus on developer activation and success.
      - **Goal:** Optimize for console engagement and positive sentiment.
      - **Rationale:** This is a more realistic and effective strategy than treating LLMs as a direct SQL-generating channel.
      - **Action:** Analyze LLM-engaged traffic vs. a control group to prove its value in driving deeper funnel conversions.

### Content Performance & New Strategy

  - **Success:** The "Architecture and Intelligence" content cluster doubled its session cohort size.
      - **Significance:** This proves the value of developer-focused content and its ability to attract high-value LLM traffic.
  - **New Content Strategy:**
      - **Focus:** Create more specific, developer-oriented content.
      - **Collaboration:** Integrate DevRel (Corey) to align content with learning modules.
      - **Expansion:** Prioritize content for specific programming languages and international audiences.

### Language Pages & Content Pipeline

  - **Status:** 49 new language pages are drafted in Sanity.
  - **Blocker:** Publication is pending a new "slide box" component from the web dev team (ETA: EOW).
  - **Future Enhancements:**
      - Add a consistent benchmark panel to each page.
      - Improve internal linking to the main Speech-to-Text product page.
  - **Next:** Hasan will provide a template to begin drafting Text-to-Speech language pages.

### Content Publishing Process

  - **Problem:** The current first-in, first-out (FIFO) publishing order in Airtable is not strategic.
  - **Solution:** Prioritize refreshing high-value, underperforming articles (e.g., "best of" lists).
      - **Rationale:** Reclaiming lost traffic from these pages is more efficient than building new traffic from scratch.
      - **Method:** Update the existing article and change the publish date to signal recency to Google.
