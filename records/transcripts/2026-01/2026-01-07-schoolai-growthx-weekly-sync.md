## Key Topics

### Strategic Misalignment & Performance Gaps

  - SchoolAI's 3x business growth goal for 2026 requires a performance-focused SEO partner with a revenue lens, which GrowthX is not currently providing.
  - **Core Gaps Identified:**
      - **Technical SEO:** No support for site-wide technical issues.
      - **Off-Page SEO:** No strategy for link building or authority.
      - **AI/LLM Visibility:** No clear strategy to win in AI search, a key competitive area where SchoolAI has fallen behind.
  - **Traffic vs. Revenue:** The current focus on traffic is misaligned with business goals.
      - **Example:** A top-ranking article on "AI math tools" attracts high traffic but also a high volume of non-decision-makers, diluting its strategic value.

### Cost & Service Model

  - The current engagement is perceived as expensive compared to competitor quotes, which are all under $100k/year.
  - Competitor models offer a more cost-efficient approach:
      - They provide detailed content briefs.
      - SchoolAI's internal SMEs (former educators) then write the content, leveraging their deep subject matter expertise.
  - GrowthX's base rate (\~$12k/month) is above this threshold, reflecting a full-service model that includes content writing and execution.

### Critical Technical Need: Site Migration

  - SchoolAI plans to migrate its site from Framer back to Webflow.
  - This requires significant technical SEO expertise to:
      - Address existing site debt from a previous messy import.
      - Ensure a clean, SEO-optimized migration.

### GrowthX's Response & Path Forward

  - Kyle Gaudreau acknowledged the strategic misalignment as a GrowthX miss, attributing it to:
      - **Initial Scope:** The engagement was originally scoped for complex programmatic SEO, leading to a higher price. When that plan shifted, the scope was filled with high-volume editorial content to match the price, rather than being reset.
      - **Onboarding Process:** The current "strategy sprint" onboarding was not in place at the time, which would have better aligned the strategy with business goals from the start.
  - GrowthX confirmed it has the technical SEO expertise for the migration and can reset the strategy to be more performance-focused.
  - Kyle will discuss internally to determine if a proposal can be created that meets SchoolAI's needs and budget.
