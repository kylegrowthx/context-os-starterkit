## Key Topics

### JFrog's Challenge: Data-Rich, Content-Poor

  - **Primary Objective:** Win the AI search visibility race, a key KPI for the current and next quarter, to reach enterprise buyers using LLMs for research.
  - **Core Problem:** JFrog has extensive internal data but lacks the means to synthesize it into actionable content narratives.
  - **Resource Constraints:** Limited internal resources prevent scaling top-of-funnel content production, including video.
  - **Process Gaps:** No formal brand kits or content style guides exist, creating a need for guidance on defining content identity.

### GrowthX's Solution: An End-to-End Content Engine

  - **Positioning:** An extension of JFrog's team, handling the entire content lifecycle.
  - **Differentiation:**
      - **Aerofs:** GrowthX's founder built workflows on Aerofs before creating a proprietary platform to solve its limitations (e.g., high costs, refactoring).
      - **Profound:** GrowthX offers a competing AEO monitoring product launching in two weeks.
  - **Methodology:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks)**
          - **Context Engineering:** Interviews with SMEs (DevOps, DevSecOps) and ingestion of all internal resources to build a precise brand/product context for the AI models.
          - **Strategy Development:** A 3–6 month roadmap is created, prioritizing topics with the highest potential for AEO/SEO visibility and conversions.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12 months)**
          - **Production:** Daily content publishing, including text, images, code snippets, and cross-linking.
          - **Monitoring:** Continuous tracking of LLM visibility against competitors.
  - **Workflow & Collaboration:**
      - **Current:** JFrog reviews drafts in Google Docs, providing feedback that GrowthX's editors manually ingest to refine the AI pipelines.
      - **Future (Feb 2026):** A platform feature will allow JFrog experts to directly calibrate the AI by labeling content and flagging issues.

### Case Studies & Pricing

  - **Augment Code:** Achieved 19% AEO visibility against GitHub and Cursor by publishing \>300 technical pages.
      - **Result:** GrowthX-created articles are now cited by competitors and appear as expert answers in AI search for terms like "enterprise AI coding tools."
  - **Pricing Model:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint**
          - **Cost:** $30,000–$40,000 (one-time fixed fee)
          - **Term:** 8 weeks
          - **Opt-out:** JFrog can cancel the engagement if not satisfied.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
          - **Cost:** $15,000–$18,000/month
          - **Term:** 12 months
          - **Deliverable:** 5 articles per week (mix of content types)
