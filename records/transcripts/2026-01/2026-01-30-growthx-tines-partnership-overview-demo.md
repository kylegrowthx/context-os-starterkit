## Context

Tines is an automation platform for security and operations teams, with a strong practitioner community. The Tines leadership team (product marketing, growth, and content) is resource-constrained and faces competing demands from growth marketing, product marketing, and customer advocacy. Despite owning a rich "Story Library" of use cases and builder stories, Tines struggles with SEO and AEO visibility—partly due to technical constraints (non-crawlable UI patterns) and partly due to insufficient bandwidth to produce discoverable, long-form content. GrowthX, a B2B content marketing firm, positioned itself as a partner that could augment the team's capacity with proprietary AI workflows, human editorial expertise, and end-to-end service (strategy, production, optimization, publishing). This call evaluated fit and timeline for a potential engagement.

## Key Topics

### Tines's Content Challenges

- The team is resource-strapped, struggling to meet internal requests from growth marketing, product marketing, and customer advocacy.
- **Primary Gaps:**
  - **Discoverability Content:** SEO and AEO efforts have been under-resourced and have not yielded desired results.
  - **Technical/Practitioner Content:** The team lacks capacity to create content for a new go-to-market motion targeting builders and practitioners.
- **Strategic Opportunity:** Tines's "Story Library" is a rich asset—use cases and builder stories—but is not discoverable due to technical SEO blockers (e.g., non-crawlable pop-out UI). GrowthX could help enrich these pages with discoverable content to drive traffic.

### GrowthX's Solution & Platform

- GrowthX provides a managed service combining proprietary AI workflows with human experts ("experts in the loop").
- **Service Scope:**
  - End-to-end content creation: strategy, production, publishing, and LLM visibility monitoring (via Scrunch or GrowthX's proprietary platform).
  - Focus on editorial and programmatic content (guides, comparisons, how-tos), not custom case studies, custom research, or social posts.
- **Platform Overview:**
  - **Artifacts:** A "Content OS" is built from Tines's brand docs, product marketing materials, and a deep product dive. This creates a knowledge base for AI workflows to reference and cite.
  - **Pipelines:** Custom workflows use multiple LLMs (Claude, OpenAI, Gemini) and research tools (Exa, Tavli) to automate content creation, fact-checking, and optimization for SEO/AEO.
  - **Human Checkpoints:** Editors are notified of fact-checking flags, review all drafts, and use the platform to make edits and generate suggestions based on the artifacts and workflow data.
  - **Brand Compliance:** Image generation is fine-tuned to brand kits; GrowthX can also handle staging and publishing directly in CMS systems.

### Partnership Model & Engagement Structure

- The engagement is structured in two phases to de-risk the partnership:
  - **Phase 1 (8 weeks):** Focuses on quality and strategy. Produces 10+ articles to demonstrate value, build a content roadmap, and establish workflows. Low bandwidth; requires one primary approver (Jason or Mark).
  - **Phase 2 (12 months):** Scales production to 5 articles/week (new or refreshed content). Higher volume, managed editorial cadence.
- **Team & Bandwidth:**
  - **Phase 1 Team:** Senior-level engagement managers (e.g., former directors of organic growth, heads of growth) with deep content expertise. One-on-one relationship with client.
  - **Client Bandwidth:** Designed to be minimal. Requires one primary approver for priorities and draft reviews. Jason noted internal bandwidth is not a concern, especially with a new hire joining in 4-6 weeks.

### AEO & AI Search Optimization

- GrowthX's CEO, Marcel, is developing proprietary AI Search (AEO) workshops and methodologies—different from traditional SEO.
- Tyler noted this is a strategic area for GrowthX and mentioned sending Tines longer-form examples and Marcel's workshop materials for reference.
- AEO is relevant to Tines's story, as AI search engines (Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude) are increasingly used by practitioners to find solutions, and content optimized for AI indexing can drive significant traffic.
