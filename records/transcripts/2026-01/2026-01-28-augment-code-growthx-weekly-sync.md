## Key Topics

### SEO Performance & Bofu Optimization

  - SEO performance is strong, with page one rankings climbing across all clusters.
  - **Bofu (Tools) Cluster:**
      - Performing well; strategy is sound.
      - **Goal:** Increase CTR to \>1%.
      - **Tactics:**
          - Update CTAs on older articles.
          - Optimize meta titles.
          - Conduct competitor analysis.

### TOFU Strategy: Data Science/ML Proposal

  - **Proposal:** A high-volume TOFU cluster on Data Science & Algorithms.
      - **Rationale:** Drive massive traffic via educational content on high-volume, high-consensus topics.
      - **Expected Outcome:** High traffic, low conversion.
  - **Objection (Sylvain):** The persona is wrong (researchers, not developers), creating traffic that won't convert.
  - **Decision:** Reject the proposal. GrowthX will research alternative TOFU clusters targeting the core developer persona (e.g., language-specific content).

### TOFU Strategy: Hacker News Experiment

  - **Proposal:** A "churn and burn" experiment for the underperforming "Learn" cluster.
      - **Tactic:** Rapidly publish content on trending Hacker News topics.
      - **Rationale:** Capture first-mover advantage on viral topics (e.g., CloudBot) to dominate search results.
  - **Decision:** Approved. Sylvain noted the infrastructure built for this experiment is reusable.

### Product & Conversion Updates

  - **Sign-up Flow Revamp (EOM Feb):**
      - **Problem:** The current flow has high friction (credit card required) and poor conversion.
      - **Solution:** A new flow will introduce free plans and remove friction for specific products.
      - **Impact:** Expected to significantly increase conversion rates from content.
  - **Product Launches:**
      - **Intent App:** Early access launched; full GA in \~3 weeks.
      - **Context Engine MCP:** Launches next week with a free plan.
  - **Context Wiki (/open-source):**
      - **Status:** An existing site with low traffic and poor SEO.
      - **Opportunity:** Could be repurposed as an SEO asset (e.g., indexing docs for trending tools) to boost other content.
      - **Decision:** GrowthX will analyze the opportunity and present options.
