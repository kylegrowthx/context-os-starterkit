## Key Topics

### 2026 Goals & Performance

  - **Primary 2026 Goals:**
      - **New Users:** 2.9M → 3.5M
      - **Organic New Search Users:** 4.2k → 4.5k
      - **Shop Directory Loans:** 80k → 130k
  - **Shop Directory Loans:** A core revenue driver, each loan is \~**$1,000 GMV** and \~$10 profit.
  - **B2B Demo Requests:** A low priority, as the B2B sales funnel is effective (60% close rate, 8-day cycle) and driven by paid media.
  - **Legal Review:** The primary bottleneck. All content requires review by Sunbit legal and their bank for compliance and audit purposes.

### Strategic Pivot: B2C Programmatic & B2B Editorial

  - **B2C Programmatic Content:**
      - **Problem:** Manual blog creation creates a legal review bottleneck, slowing publishing velocity.
      - **Solution:** Use templates for programmatic pages. After the initial template review, only small, variable units require review, accelerating publishing.
  - **B2B Editorial Content:**
      - **Rationale:** Low search volume for B2B topics makes a programmatic approach ineffective.
      - **Solution:** Focus on high-quality, in-depth editorial content (e.g., case studies, media kits) to support the sales team.

### New Initiative: `sunbit.ai`

  - **Purpose:** Position Sunbit as a fintech AI leader.
  - **Audiences & Goals:**
      - **Investors:** Improve the valuation multiple for fundraising/IPO.
      - **Analysts:** Showcase deep technical expertise.
      - **Tech Talent:** Attract engineers by demonstrating advanced AI work.
  - **Content Focus:**
      - **Thought Leadership:** Beyond Sunbit's products, cover AI topics like computer vision, LLMs, and synthetic data.
      - **Human-Centric AI:** Emphasize AI as an augmentation tool, not a human replacement.

### New Tool: CheckTat

  - **Function:** GrowthX's proprietary LLM visibility tool, replacing Scrunch.
  - **Key Differentiator:** Tracks competitive presence and sentiment across categories, not just individual brand prompts, to inform strategy.
  - **Rollout:** Demo for Sunbit in early February.
