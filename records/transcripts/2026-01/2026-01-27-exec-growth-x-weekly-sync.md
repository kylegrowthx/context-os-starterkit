## Summary

GrowthX engagement with Exec is ending this week. The client is pausing mass AI-driven content production to focus on a website relaunch and a higher-touch, human-first content strategy. Despite the non-renewal, both parties expressed strong satisfaction with collaboration. Asset handoff is underway, and teams will reconnect in March to discuss progress.

---

## Context

Exec had been working with GrowthX since August 2025 to build SEO visibility and develop content strategy. During the engagement, a major win emerged: the "AI role plays" solution page rose to #1 organic rankings from a much lower initial position. This validated the strategic content approach and demonstrated tangible business impact. However, in October–November 2025, Exec leadership initiated a strategic review of the contract amid budget considerations and internal capability building. The decision to not renew reflects a broader pivot toward in-house SEO ownership rather than any dissatisfaction with execution.

Garett McDonald (VP at Exec) noted that while he initially recommended renewal given GrowthX's deep knowledge of their business, leadership elected to pause external services and rebuild internally. The client plans to maintain one key win (the AI role plays page), implement a new human-centric content model, and explore their own AI workflows via platforms like Aerofs and Profoundhouse.

- **Highlight:** The "AI role plays" solution page achieving #1 organic ranking.
  - **Significance:** Tangible win that validated strategic work. Page was far from top when engagement began.
  - **Duration:** Engagement ran August 2025 to January 2026 (5 months).

  - **Implication:** Gap between tactical link-building and PR-driven visibility strategy.
