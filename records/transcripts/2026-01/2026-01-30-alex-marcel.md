### Site Architecture Problems

Current state is worse than content issues:
- Global nav links to abandoned pages (Resource Center, Webinars)
- No consistent taxonomy or breadcrumbs
- 300+ pages with no authors, no dates, no CTAs
- Spend Trends (their growth driver) only accessible via footer
- Traffic dropped from 60K to 10K monthly on neglected pages

Marcel's proposal: Create a navigation layer with:
- Two page types: index pages (hubs) and article pages (leaves)
- Consistent breadcrumbs and taxonomy
- Hub-and-spoke model (like Healthline's 80K pages)
- Cross-linking via tags, not just categories

Timeline: Weeks, not months. No design resources needed from Brex.
