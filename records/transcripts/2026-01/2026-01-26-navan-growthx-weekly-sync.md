## Context

This 11-week Phase 1 engagement between GrowthX and Navan focuses on building organic content assets for the travel and expense management software market. Navan currently relies heavily on paid search and wants to diversify through SEO to reduce long-term acquisition costs. GrowthX is delivering both top-of-funnel awareness content (targeting travel manager personas) and bottom-of-funnel comparison listicles (targeting decision-stage buyers). The initiative is meant to demonstrate the value of organic content and help Navan decide whether to continue beyond the 12-week sprint.

The meeting occurs at a critical juncture: early organic signals are positive (articles ranking page 1/2, already cited by ChatGPT and Perplexity), but legal review is creating a bottleneck for the high-impact comparison content. The team must resolve citation requirements and clarify sourcing policy to unblock publication and hit the February 2nd continuation decision deadline.
