## Context

GrowthX and Rimkus are in month two of a content marketing partnership. Rimkus, a national engineering services firm serving building owners and insurers, offers two distinct service lines: Building Engineering Services (BES, preventive inspection and assessment) and Forensics (failure analysis for insurance claims). GrowthX is building organic visibility for Rimkus across both service lines, with recent focus on rebalancing content output. A critical insight emerged: Rimkus's competitive positioning differs between BES and Forensics (BES competitors are different from Forensics-only firms), yet GrowthX's LLM visibility tracking was mixing both cohorts, inflating reported performance. The team is also transitioning from Scrunch (current LLM tracking tool) to GrowthX's proprietary "Check That AI" platform to enable segmented, accurate competitive benchmarking aligned with Rimkus's "Profound" platform.
