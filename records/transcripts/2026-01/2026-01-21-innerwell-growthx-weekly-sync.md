## Context

Innerwell is a digital mental health platform offering ketamine-assisted therapy, psychiatry, and EMDR treatment. The partnership with GrowthX ($12k/mo) focuses on scaling organic acquisition through treatment-led content (editorial and PSEO) to reduce customer acquisition cost. ## Key Topics

### 2026 Strategic Alignment

  - The team confirmed alignment on 2026 business goals:
    1.  **Reduce CAC:** Build a scalable organic channel (editorial, PSEO) to decrease reliance on paid media.
    2.  **Expand Brand:** Position Innerwell as a full-spectrum practice, not just a ketamine provider.
    3.  **Drive LTV:** Use insurance coverage as a key patient acquisition and retention driver.
  - **Organic Acquisition Goal:** Scale organic acquisition from its current 5–10% of total acquisitions.

### Performance Review & Key Learnings

  - **Editorial Content:** The new treatment-led strategy is driving significant growth in total and *engaged* sessions since September 1st.
      - **Why it works:** Content aligns directly with user search intent, signaling high value to Google and reducing bounce rates.
  - **PSEO Pages:** Traffic has rebounded to \~700 weekly sessions after the October URL migration.
      - **Key Learning:** State-level pages are the clear winner, driving \~600 sessions/week, while city-level pages have not recovered.
  - **LLM Visibility:** Innerwell's brand presence in AI responses increased from October to December, with several articles now appearing in Google's AI Overviews.

### 2026 Strategic Plan & Priorities

  - **Core Workstreams:**
      - **Knowledge Hub:** Scale the proven editorial strategy, starting with ketamine and expanding to psychiatry and EMDR.
      - **PSEO Expansion:** Focus new location pages on state-level content for psychiatry and EMDR.
  - **New Initiatives:**
      - **Insurance Build-out (P1):** Create content and potentially a tool to help users check eligibility.
          - **Why:** To drive LTV and improve patient quality.
          - **Execution:** Start with editorial content, then scope a V2 eligibility widget.
      - **Treatment Space Hub (P2/Q2):** A lower-priority condition hub, inspired by Talkspace.
  - **Homepage Real Estate:** Akina questioned the value of the current homepage dropdown listing all 40+ states.
      - **Suggestion:** Reallocate this space to higher-value content like insurance or medication hubs.

### Check That: AI Visibility Tool

  - Andi demoed "Check That," a new proprietary tool replacing Scrunch for tracking LLM visibility.
  - **Key Features:**
      - Tracks brand citations across a library of 100k+ prompts.
      - Identifies citation sources (URLs) to reveal competitive strategies.
      - Provides a collaborative workspace for Innerwell to customize tracked prompts and competitors.
  - **Rollout:** Innerwell will receive access next week; the tool officially launches in early February.

### Renewal & Backlinking Discussion

  - **Renewal Package:** The $12k/mo renewal includes 5 articles/week, PSEO support, technical support, quarterly audits, and Check That access.
  - **Backlinking Request:** Noah requested backlinking be reinstated, citing a competitive gap.
      - **Context:** The previous backlinking effort was paused due to low-quality results.
      - **Decision:** GrowthX will provide a quote. Reinstating it may require reallocating budget from other deliverables.
