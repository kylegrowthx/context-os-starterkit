## Key Topics

### Content Strategy: Feature Mentions

- **Problem:** An article positioned "field filtering" as a key feature for "best tools," but it was unclear if You.com offers it, creating a potential credibility gap.
- **Solution:** Establish clear rules for feature mentions:
  - **If positioned as a "best practice":**
    - Confirm You.com offers the feature.
    - Link directly to the relevant documentation.
  - **Future Idea:** A glossary of technical terms could capture traffic and serve as a central linking resource.
- **Discussion:** Bailey explained SEO content serves a tactical purpose and shouldn't be blocked by perfectionism; Brooke agreed quality matters but acknowledged the balance. Bailey noted the new pipeline step (checking product features) will help. Brooke confirmed she'll check internal experts before publishing and is pro-linking to documentation when mentioning features.

### Workflow & Process Improvements

- **Airtable Status Updates:**
  - **Problem:** Brooke cannot edit article statuses in Airtable, forcing her to use Slack for status updates, which is inefficient.
  - **Solution:** Bailey will create an Airtable interface for Brooke to directly update statuses, enabling faster workflow visibility.
- **"Composable API" Article Revision:**
  - **Problem:** You.com's API SME reviewed the article and flagged that it should focus on "composable architecture," not "API," requiring a significant rewrite.
  - **Solution:** Brooke will manage the revision with the SME, adding comments to a new tab in the same Google Doc. She'll share both the original (with SME comments) and final draft with GrowthX.
  - **Rationale:** This allows GrowthX to capture foundational feedback that applies to future content, avoiding duplicate feedback on similar topics.
- **Image Creation:**
  - **Decision:** You.com's design team will now create all article images.
  - **Rationale:** Internal brand control; not a quality issue with GrowthX's images.
  - **Process:** GrowthX will continue providing images as backup. Brooke will replace them with you.com designs.
