## Key Topics

### CheckThat Product and Value Proposition

- **What It Is:** Free AI SEO monitoring tool from GrowthX that shows how SaaS products and tools rank in AI responses across 100k+ AI prompts.
- **Hook:** Free access to 100k+ prompts tracked in real time, versus competitors (Meridian, Profound, Scrunch) who charge ~$300/month to track ~500 prompts.
- **Positioning:** Differentiated by offering free access to massive prompt library plus downstream educational content (courses, workshops) to teach users how to act on the data.
- **Comparison to Competitors:** Meridian launched recently with positioning "Get Your Brand Ranked by ChatGPT" and targets similar audiences (e-commerce, AI tool creators). CheckThat is more specific to AEO/SEO and offers greater prompt coverage.
- **Launch Goal:** Drive awareness and sign-ups, then onboard users into GrowthX's Circle community and educational content (courses on how to optimize for AI, workshops with industry experts).
- **Launch Date:** January 28, 2026 (may shift one week if needed).

### Launch Campaign Strategy and Creator Activation

#### Phase 1: Post Amplification (~$5k)
- **Tactic:** GrowthX publishes the launch post on X and LinkedIn; Ori's creator network amplifies it (reposts, shares, engagement).
- **Rationale:** Ori notes this is a standard, proven tactic for SaaS launches. At $5k, this budget can fund amplification across both platforms.
- **Deliverable:** Amplification examples (posts from similar launches) and a one-pager showing what the package includes.

#### Phase 2: Dedicated Creator Posts (Variable Cost)
- **Tactic:** Creators publish original posts about CheckThat on X and LinkedIn, positioning it within their own commentary.
- **Cost:** Varies by creator tier and platform. For smaller/mid-tier creators: ~$1k per post. Budget can scale from 10k to 20k to 30k depending on creator count.
- **Rationale:** Drives credibility via third-party endorsement and reaches creator-specific audiences.
- **Jason's Request:** Before committing, wants to see a curated list of potential creators to vet and decide.

#### Phase 3: YouTube Partnerships (Variable Cost)
- **Two Approaches:**
  1. **Mid-Roll Ad Integration:** Larger channels (100k+ subs) get paid to feature CheckThat as a mid-roll ad in existing videos. Cost: ~$1k+ per integration.
  2. **Dedicated Videos:** Smaller creators (sub-100k subs) create dedicated videos about CheckThat. Cost: ~$1k each. Example: Julian Goldie (SEO YouTube channel) charges $4-5k for combined X and YouTube promotion.
- **Jason's Preference:** More interested in mid-roll integrations and amplification of GrowthX's own content rather than lower-quality dedicated videos from high-volume channels like Julian Goldie.
- **Evergreen Value:** Ori notes that dedicated videos can have long-tail value as evergreen content, especially given the current AI/AEO hype. However, for a launch moment, the impact depends on budget and creator selection quality.

### Long-Term YouTube Partnership Model

- **Vision:** Build a recurring revenue stream via YouTube creators, moving beyond short-term LinkedIn workshops.
- **Proposed Structure:**
  - **Co-Creation:** GrowthX and a YouTube creator collaboratively build an educational asset (e.g., workshop, mini-course) hosted on the GrowthX Circle community.
  - **Evergreen Promotion:** The creator promotes the asset across all their videos (new and old), driving consistent referrals over time.
  - **Compensation:** Creator receives an upfront payment plus recurring fees per referred community member (or CPL model). GrowthX's community has a $200 joining fee + $30/month subscription, so the creator gets a portion of that revenue share.
- **Example Model:** Creator and GrowthX co-create a workshop on "AEO Fundamentals" or "AI Prompt Optimization." Creator promotes it to their audience, driving members into the community. Creator receives, e.g., $50-100 per referred member or a share of joining/subscription fees for 3-6 months.
- **Target Creators:** Smaller to mid-sized YouTube creators (10k-500k subs) in AI, automation, marketing, or AEO niches who don't already have large paid communities (Skool, Circle, etc.) but want recurring revenue.
- **Rationale:** Creators get passive income without managing their own community. GrowthX gets consistent lead generation and community growth. Model addresses creators who've monetized audiences via Skool but want to diversify or who want to monetize audiences they can't serve directly.
- **Jason's Real Example:** GrowthX recently co-created a workshop on "A/B Testing and Conversion" with an unnamed LinkedIn creator. Despite the topic being outside GrowthX's core expertise, the collaboration worked because the creator promoted it to their large LinkedIn audience, driving qualified signups.
- **Skool Communities:** Discussion of Skool as a competing platform. Some creators (e.g., Liam, N8N-adjacent creators) use Skool to build large free and paid communities. Ori shares that paid Skool communities tend to be more engaged than free ones, and some creators generate substantial revenue (~$120k/month for 2,000 members at $60/month). Ori mentions knowing a Lindy-affiliated creator (Nick Sarek) who's building a large Skool community around AI automation and may be open to partnerships.

### Creator Network and Vetting

- **Ori's Approach:** Uses Airtable interface where clients can browse and select creators from a pre-curated list. Ori filters by platform (X, LinkedIn, YouTube), audience size, engagement quality, niche fit, and pricing.
- **Research Capability:** Ori has a team that researches creators, analyzes recent video/post performance, engagement metrics, and audience demographics to recommend the best fits.
- **Platform-Specific Creators:**
  - **X/LinkedIn:** AI, SaaS, e-commerce, marketing, AEO creators. Ori works with networks of 50+ creators across these platforms.
  - **YouTube:** AI, automation, SEO, marketing education creators. Smaller, niche creators preferred for dedicated videos. Larger channels (100k+) preferred for mid-roll integrations.
- **Example Creators Mentioned:** Julian Goldie (SEO, high-volume videos, ~$4-5k for combined X/YouTube), Nick Sarek (AI automation, Skool community, Instagram reels, large YouTube channel), Liam (AI/automation, Skool community), N8N creators (various, building Skool communities).
- **Budget-to-Creator Mapping:** For $5-10k launch budget, Ori suggests:
  - $5k for X/LinkedIn amplification (covers post reposts and engagement).
  - Additional $1-5k for 1-5 dedicated creator posts (depending on creator tier).
  - $10k+ for YouTube (either multiple mid-roll integrations or 5-10 dedicated videos from smaller creators).

### Competitive and Market Context

- **Evergreen Opportunity:** CheckThat's free, value-driven positioning can generate ongoing organic interest, especially if supported by creator endorsements and educational content.
