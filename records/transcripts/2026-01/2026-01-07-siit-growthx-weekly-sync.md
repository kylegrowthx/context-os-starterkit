## Context

Siit is launching its first major product campaign focused on "App Access"—automating the provisioning of employee access to tools like Okta, JumpCloud, and Microsoft Entra ID. The problem is widespread: manual provisioning is slow, creates security risks (Shadow IT), and delays employee onboarding. Siit's AI agent solves this by automating approval workflows and integrations with IDPs, reducing a multi-step manual process to two minutes in Slack or Teams.

Carmel presented a comprehensive campaign strategy with four target segments (Okta, JumpCloud, Entra ID, and Google users) across email, paid ads, social, Reddit, LinkedIn, YouTube, and case studies. However, the initial GrowthX proposal to convert high-traffic tool pages was rejected as too narrow and non-ICP-aligned. The team realigned on a broader content strategy: creating ICP-targeted editorial and educational content split 50/50 between App Access and always-on service desk topics, with a new distribution mechanism—a universal ROI calculator for service desk productivity.

## Key Topics

### App Access Campaign

  - **Objective:** Generate 200–230 MQLs (trials & demos) this quarter.
  - **Organic Goal:** 60 MQLs from search.
  - **Problem:** Manual app provisioning is slow, creates Shadow IT, and delays employee productivity.
  - **Solution:** Siit's AI agent automates provisioning via Identity Providers (IDPs) like Okta and JumpCloud.
  - **Target Audiences:**
      - Okta users
      - JumpCloud users
      - Microsoft Entra ID users
      - Google users (managing access via Slack/spreadsheets)
  - **Channels:** Email nurture (segmented by IDP), Google/Bing Ads, Reddit, organic social, LinkedIn ads (test), YouTube, and case studies.

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - **Initial Proposal (GrowthX):** Create tool-specific landing pages to convert high-traffic tool review pages (\~800 organic clicks/mo).
  - **Rationale:** Piggyback on existing demand for adjacent problems.
  - **Feedback (Carmel):**
      - **Niche Focus:** "App access for Linear" is too narrow; the pain point is general provisioning automation.
      - **ICP Mismatch:** Tool pages attract non-ICP visitors; editorial content is more effective for reaching the target audience.
  - **Decision:** Pivot from tool-specific pages to creating ICP-focused content for the App Access campaign.

### New Conversion Test: ROI Calculator

  - **Idea:** Build a universal ROI calculator to convert traffic across the entire site.
  - **Rationale:** A single, high-value asset can be embedded everywhere (blogs, ads, email signatures), solving the distribution challenge that causes many calculators to fail.
  - **Execution:** Siit will provide the logic; GrowthX will handle the build.
