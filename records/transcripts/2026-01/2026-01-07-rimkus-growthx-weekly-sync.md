## Summary

GrowthX and Rimkus reviewed findings from a recent content audit targeting Rimkus's forensics and building engineering services (BES) blogs. The team identified a critical strategic shift: moving from highly technical, standards-focused content to accessible, situational topics that reduce legal risk while expanding audience reach. Rimkus will implement a state-specific building code pillar for BES and refocus forensics content on investigation processes using expert webinars as source material.

---

## Context

Rimkus is a leading global engineering and environmental consulting firm. GrowthX manages their B2B content strategy focused on two pillars: Forensics (investigation and inspection services) and Building Engineering Services (BES). Content performance has been strong (34 of 40 new blogs receiving organic traffic), but the audit revealed a fundamental misalignment: content was too technically dense for lay audiences (e.g., Florida condo owners) and created legal exposure through unverified claims about engineering processes.

This meeting marks a strategic pivot toward audience-centric, accessible content while maintaining technical credibility by sourcing from Rimkus's own expert webinars. The shift directly supports Rimkus's market expansion into Florida and aligns with Google's move toward AEO (Answer Engine Optimization), where direct, scannable answers rank higher.

## Key Topics

### Content Audit Feedback

  - The recent content audit successfully improved quality by addressing recurring issues.
  - **Primary Concern:** The content's highly technical focus on engineering standards and legal vetting.
      - **Risk:** Unverified claims create legal exposure. A past instance involved a client being questioned in a deposition about a specific process mentioned in a Rimkus blog post.
      - **Audience Mismatch:** The technical depth is inaccessible to lay audiences (e.g., Florida condo owners) and risks alienating technical experts who might spot inaccuracies.

### New Content Strategy

  - **Forensics (Jen):**
      - **Shift:** From technical standards to accessible, situational topics.
      - **Focus:** Investigation processes, common problems, and what to expect from an inspection.
      - **Topics:** Align with key practice areas: Global Energy (industrial, renewables) and Transportation.
      - **Source Material:** Use Rimkus expert webinars to create AI-summarized artifacts, ensuring technical accuracy.
  - **BES (Nohora):**
      - **Strategy:** Create a scalable, programmatic content pillar for building codes.
      - **Format:** A central pillar page linking to state-specific "spoke" articles.
      - **Goal:** Capture high-intent search traffic for queries like "2026 building code updates Florida."
      - **Alignment:** Supports Rimkus's push into the Florida market, targeting senior condo owners with relevant safety information.

### Readability & SEO Enhancements

  - **Intros:** Expand on introductory scenarios throughout the article to maintain reader engagement.
  - **Tone:** Use more natural, digestible language.
  - **"TLDR" Summaries:** Add a "Too Long; Didn't Read" summary to the top of all posts.
      - **Rationale:** Boosts AEO by providing direct answers for Google's featured snippets.
  - **Performance:** Current SEO metrics are strong (34 of 40 new blogs getting clicks, high engaged sessions). The strategy shift is expected to improve these results.
