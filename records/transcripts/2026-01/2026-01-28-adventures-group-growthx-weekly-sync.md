## Context

They face seasonal weather challenges—particularly Nov–Mar wind closures at Cancun's port—which disrupt tour operations and require guest rescheduling. The business generates significant blog traffic (~9k organic sessions across all destinations) but experiences poor conversion, with blogs outranking tour pages for commercial keywords. This meeting brought together Adventures Group's marketing and product teams (Isabel, Saul, Angie) with GrowthX's content strategist (Jo) to align on a new SEO and content marketing strategy to improve ROI.

GrowthX is positioning content strategy as a three-part engagement: blog UX redesign (desktop and mobile), SEO audit to resolve keyword cannibalization, and off-page SEO (backlinks to tour pages). The business opportunity is clear: Adventures Group's top 20% of content likely drives 80% of revenue, so optimizing high-impact pages offers measurable returns.

## Key Topics

### Problem: Blog Traffic Isn't Converting

  - **Keyword Cannibalization:** Blogs outrank tour pages for commercial keywords, diverting high-intent traffic from conversion-focused pages.
  - **Poor UX:** The current blog layout pushes conversion elements below the fold, causing high bounce rates as users leave before seeing them.
  - **Weather Impact (Cancun):** High wind during Nov–Mar forces port closures, disrupting catamaran tours and creating guest rescheduling challenges.

### Solution: New Content Funnel & Blog Redesign

  - **Content Funnel:** Align content with user intent to guide them from research to purchase.
      - **Awareness:** Informational blogs (e.g., "Weather in Cancun") build brand awareness.
      - **Consideration:** Blogs on attractions (e.g., "Things to Do") link to relevant tour pages.
      - **Decision:** Tour pages and commercial blogs ("Best Tours") drive direct conversions.
  - **Blog Redesign:** Place conversion elements above the fold.
      - **Desktop:** Replace the "Related Articles" sidebar with "Recommended Tours."
      - **Mobile:** Swap the order of the blog and tour carousels at the bottom.
      - **In-Content CTAs:** Add compact, relevant CTAs (e.g., "Add to Cart") within commercial blogs.

### Solution: SEO Audit & Optimization

  - **Goal:** Ensure tour pages rank highest for all commercial keywords.
  - **On-Page SEO:** Optimize tour pages with commercial terms to improve search visibility.
  - **Off-Page SEO:** Build backlinks to tour pages from travel sites to signal authority to Google.
  - **Prioritization:** Focus optimization on top-performing pages (top 20 blogs & tours) to maximize impact.

### New Opportunity: AI Search

  - **Growth:** AI search traffic grew from \~9k to \~119k sessions, creating a new high-intent awareness channel.
  - **Tracking:** Isabel tracks AI search traffic (ChatGPT, Perplexity, etc.) in Google Analytics via a custom segment.
  - **Action:** Structure content to be easily cited by AI models, driving more traffic to the website.
