## Summary

GrowthX and HubSpot explored a strategic partnership to address HubSpot's push toward productized partner models. While GrowthX's service-based business doesn't fit HubSpot's Solutions Partner Program (which requires selling HubSpot licenses), three promising paths emerged: co-marketing GrowthX's upcoming standalone platform to HubSpot's agency network, building a HubSpot Marketplace app for direct publishing to Content Hub, and establishing an agency referral program. ## Context

HubSpot is actively guiding its partner network to evolve from manual services (like content creation and SEO) to productized, technology-driven models to maintain margins in a competitive market. This conversation brought together Jenn Van Dam and Justin Graci from HubSpot with GrowthX's Bridget McGillivray and Tyler Pavlas to explore how GrowthX's proprietary platform for high-volume AEO content creation could fit into HubSpot's partner ecosystem. The discussion revealed structural constraints with the formal Solutions Partner Program but identified alternative models that could unlock significant value for both sides.

## Key Topics

### HubSpot's Partner Strategy

- HubSpot's Solutions Partner Program is evolving beyond traditional agencies to include technical partners (e.g., CRM implementers).
- **Strategic Push:** HubSpot is guiding partners to productize services to maintain margins as manual models (e.g., content/SEO) become unsustainable.
- **Referral Models:**
    - **Partner-Sourced:** Partner brings a deal to HubSpot and co-sells.
    - **Partner-Assisted:** HubSpot direct reps refer clients to partners.

### GrowthX's Productized Service Model

- **Core Offering:** End-to-end AEO/SEO content creation, from strategy to publishing and LLM monitoring.
- **Delivery Model:**
    - **Phase 1 (8 wks):** Strategy sprint to develop client-specific "context engineering" (personas, brand voice).
    - **Phase 2 (12 mos):** Growth execution, delivering 5 articles/week.
- **Internal Platform:** A proprietary platform automates workflows using APIs (e.g., SEMrush, SERP) and research tools (e.g., Exa, Tavli).
- **Human-in-the-Loop:** 50+ in-house editors ensure quality and provide feedback to continuously improve platform workflows.
- **Target Client:** Series B+ companies with established ICPs, seeking a volume play.
- **CMS Agnostic:** GrowthX publishes to any client CMS, with automated publishing capabilities that could be adapted for HubSpot.

### Partnership Opportunities & Blockers

- **Blocker:** GrowthX's service model doesn't fit the Solutions Partner Program, which requires partners to sell HubSpot licenses to tier up.
- **Opportunity 1: Co-Marketing GrowthX's Platform**
    - **Action:** Co-market GrowthX's standalone platform (launching next month) to HubSpot's agency network.
    - **Benefit:** Enables HubSpot partners to resell AEO services without building their own tech.
- **Opportunity 2: HubSpot Marketplace App**
    - **Action:** Build a technical integration for GrowthX's platform to publish directly to HubSpot's Content Hub.
    - **Benefit:** List GrowthX as an app in the HubSpot marketplace for direct purchase.
- **Opportunity 3: Agency Referral Program**
    - **Action:** HubSpot refers clients needing AEO services to GrowthX for a referral fee.
    - **Benefit:** Provides a non-technical partnership path.
