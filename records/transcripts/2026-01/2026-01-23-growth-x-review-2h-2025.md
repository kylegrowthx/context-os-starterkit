## Key Topics

### 2025 Performance & Strategic Rationale

  - **Strategy:** Prioritized refreshing 80 existing articles over creating 16 new ones.
  - **Rationale:** This approach delivered faster, incremental lifts and was more effective for improving LLM visibility.
  - **Results:**
      - **Search Rankings:** Top-3 positions increased from 4,099 → 6,800.
      - **LLM Visibility:** Top recommendation for 76% of tracked queries (+12% increase).
      - **Competitive Gap:** Widened against OnBoard but narrowed against MetricStream in Q4 2025.
  - **Organic Mix Goal:** The 50% organic mix goal was not met (current: 32-33%), a direct result of the refresh-heavy strategy.

### 2026 Strategic Shift: BU-Centric Approach

  - **Problem:** General SEO metrics (e.g., traffic) don't align with Diligent's BU-level business goals.
  - **Solution:** Shift to a BU-centric strategy with tailored plays for different market maturities:
      - **Mature Markets (e.g., BLC):** Focus on capturing existing demand and winning competitive SERP gaps.
      - **Immature Markets (e.g., DMI):** Build a "moat" via PR/AR partnerships to capture future search volume.
  - **Reporting:** Realign all reporting to BU performance, integrating SEO with SEM to show a blended view of competitive distance.
  - **Zero-Click Experience:** A key 2026 bet is capturing the zero-click experience (snippets, deals) to control the narrative for the 95% of the audience not yet in-market.

### Q1 2026 Priorities

  - **Re-establish Top-3 GRC Positioning:** Counter MetricStream's Q4 2025 surge.
  - **Expand Presence:** Focus on Boards, AI Governance, and DMI Vault.
  - **Maintain Presence:** Protect rankings for DRM and Compliance.
  - **LLM Visibility:** Use new internal tooling to find fresh topics and refresh opportunities.
