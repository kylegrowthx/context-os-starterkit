## Key Topics

### Partnership Decision: GrowthX vs. Directive

  - Brooke confirmed the decision to partner with GrowthX, citing its new model as a better fit for a fast-moving startup than Directive's traditional approach.
  - The primary goal is to increase web traffic by \~40% to hit inbound revenue targets.
  - Brooke will manage the separate goal of increasing site conversion by \~50% internally, believing it requires a distinct, specialist focus.

### GrowthX's AI-Driven Content Model

  - GrowthX's model is designed for speed, publishing content in weeks, not months.
  - **Phase 1 (8-Week Strategy Sprint):**
      - **Week 1:** Create "artifacts" (personas, Jobs to be Done, workflows) for deep context.
      - **Weeks 2–3:** Develop a "Content OS" (keyword analysis, topic clusters, 500+ assignments).
      - **Weeks 3+:** Publish 5–20 articles to calibrate quality and gather initial data.
  - **Phase 2 (Growth Execution):**
      - A dedicated account lead (e.g., Kathy, William) provides consistent communication via a shared Slack channel and bi-weekly meetings.
      - A team of Strategy Directors and the CCO (former TechCrunch EIC) are available for strategic support.

### Content Strategy: Specialty-Specific Depth

  - Brooke emphasized the need to move beyond generic claims of "handling complexity" to specific, specialty-focused examples (Ortho, Derm, Women's Health).
  - **Example Patient Journey (Derm):** A patient with a new spot and a history of skin cancer faces a 20-minute hold, gets stuck in a "cosmetic vs. medical" loop, and struggles to find an available provider.
  - GrowthX's artifacts will capture these nuances, using Assort's internal database of workflows to create content that resonates with specific audiences.

### Scope and Logistics

  - **Initial Scope:** Content creation only.
  - **Web Dev:** Assort's internal Webflow contractor will handle pillar page templates. GrowthX's partner, Webstacks, was noted as a potential option for an extended scope.
  - **Collaboration:** Assort will provide first drafts for core/specialty pages; GrowthX will optimize them for SEO.
  - **Company Context:** GrowthX was founded in mid-2024 by CEO Marcel (ex-CMO at DeepGram) and grew to $12M ARR in its first year, working with clients like Ramp and Brex.
