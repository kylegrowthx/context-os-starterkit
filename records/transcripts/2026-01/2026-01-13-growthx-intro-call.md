## Context

Louise Ng was referred to GrowthX by Jesse (from Tavis/Ramp), who is currently in final contract negotiations with GrowthX. Securitas Healthcare operates without an internal content team, relying instead on freelancers to support their content production. They are seeking tools and services to accelerate content creation for both traditional SEO and AI SEO visibility. Louise had previously tried an AI content generation tool that produced volume at scale but failed to achieve meaningful search rankings, which reinforced her preference for a structured, thoughtful approach to content creation. In early January 2026, Securitas signed a contract with Aerobs (an AI-powered platform that enables teams to build custom workflows) as their primary solution for content acceleration, positioning this as a more budget-conscious alternative to hiring a full-service agency.

## Key Topics

### Securitas's Content Needs & Current Solution

  - **Primary Goal:** Accelerate content production for SEO & AI SEO.
  - **Internal Resources:** No content team; relies on freelancers.
  - **Current Solution:** Just signed a contract with Aerobs.
      - **Rationale:** Securitas will build its own workflows on the platform, a more budget-friendly approach than a full-service agency.
  - **Past Experience:** A previous AI tool failed to rank content, reinforcing the need for a thoughtful, structured approach.

### GrowthX's Differentiated Service Model

  - **Model:** A full-service agency combining a proprietary platform with expert-in-the-loop workflows.
  - **Value Proposition:** Delivers higher-quality, faster-ranking content than self-service tools by eliminating the client's learning curve and workflow-building effort.
  - **Leadership:** Founded by Marcel, who built Deepgram's content engine (24x traffic, 4x revenue) using Aerobs, then created GrowthX to solve the "zero-to-one" problem for other companies.
  - **Pricing:** \~$15k/mo minimum, reflecting the cost of creating high-quality, results-driven content at scale.

### GrowthX Case Studies

  - **Augment Code:**
      - **Goal:** Establish enterprise AI coding tool authority.
      - **Action:** Created \>300 guides.
      - **Result:** 3x organic traffic in 6 months; articles outrank competitors (Cursor, GitHub) and are cited in their content.
  - **Ramp:**
      - **Action:** Published \>700 pages.
      - **Result:** Expanded from editorial content to programmatic projects (e.g., Spender Directory) using internal data, establishing Ramp as a business spending authority.

### AI Visibility Tooling

  - **Securitas's View:** Visibility tracking tools (e.g., Profound) are overpriced and lack a moat. The core challenge is content creation and ranking, not just monitoring.
  - **GrowthX's Response:**
      - Agrees with this assessment.
      - Uses Scrunch for internal tracking but is launching its own tool next month.
      - **New Tool:** Will offer "better bells and whistles" and a head start on tracking prompts/citations.
      - **Availability:** Will be a SaaS product and integrated into white-glove service engagements.
