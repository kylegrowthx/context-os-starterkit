## Context

Abnormal (abnormalsecurity.com) is investing significantly in organic content strategy through GrowthX, paying ~$20k/month. In December 2025, Abnormal made strategic changes—disabling irrelevant glossary URLs and pivoting toward mid-funnel blog content (fingerprinting, phishing, behavioral AI) designed to drive higher-intent business traffic and increase visibility in LLM responses. December's metrics revealed a concerning trend: clicks dropped nearly 30% to 5,332, attributed primarily to URL disablement but complicated by holiday seasonality. Additionally, interim content leadership (Callie Baron) became more risk-averse, slowing the publishing cadence and halting momentum built earlier in the year. A new Content Director, Warren, joined the team in January, creating both opportunity and uncertainty around process alignment. Abdallah needs to demonstrate business impact and justify program ROI to leadership, particularly given the click decline and program cost. Jo Kaminska (GrowthX) is tasked with designing a production and SEO strategy that scales reliably, incorporates AI visibility as a new lever, and generates the forecast data Abdallah needs for stakeholder conversations.
