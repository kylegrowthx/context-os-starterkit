## Key Topics

  - **Problem:** Content is becoming "samey," prompting a desire to experiment with new topics and formats.

  - **Format Exploration:**
      - **Relay's Interest:** Listicles, quizzes, interviews, and other dynamic structures.
      - **GrowthX's Capability:** Strong at programmatic content (e.g., building large, templated content libraries for specific search intent). Less suited for high-touch, dev-heavy formats like interactive quizzes.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will provide examples of their programmatic work to serve as thought starters for Relay.

### Operational Updates

  - **Disclosure Format Change:**
      - **New Rule:** Thread approved a cleaner format using a superscript link to a dedicated disclosure section at the bottom of the article.

      - **Solution:** Create a single source of truth—a running doc for Dave to update—to ensure all teams work from the latest guidelines.
      - **Action:** Bailey will update all Notion docs to reflect the latest guidelines and share them with Dave for review.

### Content Pipeline & Performance

  - **Topic Pipeline:** Dave approved 10 new topics, creating a sufficient runway.
  - **Topic Balance:** The focus on "accountants and bookkeepers" will shift from primary to an equal cluster. Dave will review the pipeline to ensure a healthy spread.
