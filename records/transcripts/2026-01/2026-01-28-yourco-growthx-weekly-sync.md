## Context

Yourco's lead generation baseline is approximately 10 monthly leads, with a target of 25 by month-end—a near-2.5x increase focused on tracking completed demos, free pro activations, and free trial sign-ups. The team recognizes that current content and AI visibility strategies are insufficient to support this aggressive growth, particularly given Yourco's 100% customer retention rate and strong product-market fit in the frontline workforce communication niche.

GrowthX recently transitioned Yourco's AI visibility tracking from Scrunch to an internal proprietary tool that provides granular citation data, exact linked URLs, and co-mention analysis. Initial findings revealed a critical visibility gap: Yourco appears in Google AI Overviews and Perplexity consistently but is entirely absent from ChatGPT results—a high-priority investigation area. Additionally, Yourco's "frontline intelligence" content cluster underperforms because the framing doesn't align with user search intent in LLMs, which favor specific use cases (e.g., "call-off reports," "safety concern reporting") over broad product categories.

## Key Topics

### AI Visibility & Tool Transition

- **New AI Visibility Tool:** Transitioned from Scrunch to internal proprietary platform.
  - Provides exact URLs of citations and co-mentioned brands.
  - Offers 7-day and 45-day rolling views with competitive benchmarking.
  - Shows position, sentiment, and citation share by competitor.

- **Visibility Gap Analysis:** Yourco is cited in Google AI Overviews and Perplexity but is absent from ChatGPT results—a critical gap requiring investigation.
  - Early listicle and comparative content showing traction in AI visibility metrics.
  - Hypotheses: ROI framing mismatch, missing relevance signals, content structure gaps.
- **Competitive Monitoring:** Staffbase is the primary competitor winning SERP positions and will be tracked as a co-mention benchmark.

### Content Strategy Shift

- **Problem:** "Frontline intelligence" subcluster underperforms because it doesn't match LLM user search intent.
- **Solution:** Reframe content around specific use cases and workflows.
  - Target phrases: "call-off reports," "safety concern reporting," "workforce communication without smartphones."
  - Integrate "frontline intelligence" narrative into refreshes for SEO continuity while supporting LLM visibility.
- **Structure for LLMs:** Use tables and bullet points to create parseable, structured data that LLMs can easily extract and cite.
  - Headers should read as an outline when skimmed independently.
  - Sections should be self-contained for context isolation.

### Content Production Plan (Two-Week Sprint)

- **Prior Baseline:** 2 refreshes + 5 new pieces per week.
- **New Target:** 4 refreshes + 4 new pieces per week for two weeks.
  - Refreshes prioritize articles that lost SERP position and competitive displacement (especially Staffbase).
  - New content targets high-intent LLM queries from the keyword gap analysis.
- **Refresh Rationale:** Existing content with SEO traction, optimized for LLM structure and findability, yields faster momentum than new content alone.
- **Production Oversight:** Joe (production lead) committed to maintaining content buffer and quality review; Carrie (account lead) owns editorial direction and competitive analysis.

### Technical SEO & Non-Indexing Fixes

- **Article 1—"ROI of Streamlined Call-Off Communication":**
  - **Issue:** Non-indexing, likely due to ROI framing mismatch (LLMs expect monetary ROI; content discusses time/efficiency benefits).
  - **Fix:** Refresh content to clarify benefits angle; update meta title and opening paragraph to signal "operational benefits" vs. direct financial ROI.
- **Article 2—"How Can I Manage My Workforce to Most Employees on Smartphones?":**
  - **Issue:** Non-indexing, unwieldy URL slug.
  - **Fix:** Shorten URL to `/employees-without-smartphones/` or similar; slug must stay under 60 characters per new standard.
- **Broken Links:** 83 broken internal links on Prismic require manual remediation this week.
- **URL Slug Standard:** All new URLs must be ≤60 characters to ensure proper indexing and Prismic auto-slug reliability.

### Lead Generation Tracking

- **Metrics:** Book a Demo completion, Free Pro activation, Free Trial sign-up.
  - These three events are the most accurate lead indicators.
  - ~20% spam discount applied across the set (empirically validated).
- **Lead Baseline:** ~10 leads in January (verified from GA4).
- **Monthly Process:** Jessica (Yourco) shares actuals end-of-month; GrowthX tracks against 25-lead target for February and beyond.
- **ARR vs. Lead Focus:** Company maturity and product constraints limit ARR scaling; lead generation growth is the strategic priority.
