## Context

This meeting occurs in week six of a year-long GrowthX engagement with Turnstile, a company focused on financial automation software. The engagement follows a structured approach: the first eight weeks constitute a "strategy sprint" focused on understanding Turnstile's business, target ideal customer profile (ICP), and competitive positioning. The goal is to move toward full production—publishing one blog post daily—by week eight and beyond.

Turnstile's strategy centers on AI visibility: creating content optimized for both human readers and large language models (LLMs). Unlike ChatGPT-only approaches, GrowthX layers detailed context artifacts (company profile, ICP, product specifications) into its content creation workflows across multiple AI tools, delivering higher quality and consistency.

Key milestones: Turnstile completed the Content OS Airtable documentation and strategic artifacts in early January. Content production began in week five but faced calibration delays; the team aims to deliver three revised articles by EOD January 14, approve a seven-article batch this week, and launch the next batch of five articles. The CMS is live and ready for publication; all articles follow a structured taxonomy aligned with topic clusters defined in the strategy.

Turnstile's blog analytics are tracked via Looker, integrating GA4 and Google Search Console data. The platform supports real-time monitoring of organic search performance, LLM referral traffic, and content performance by topic cluster.
