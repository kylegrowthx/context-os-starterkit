## Key Topics

### Assort Health's SEO Challenge

  - **Goal:** Build SEO/AEO from scratch to drive long-term growth.
  - **Current State:** The website is minimal (13 indexed pages), lacking core content for specialties like cardiology or pediatrics.
  - **Strategic Concern:** High-volume editorial content is ineffective if visitors land on a page and immediately bounce due to a lack of relevant product/service information.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** An end-to-end partner using AI workflows to scale content creation.
  - **Origin:** Founded by Marcel, who pioneered this model at DeepGram, achieving 24x traffic and 4x revenue.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-Week Sprint):** Strategy development, audience/brand context engineering, and a 3-6 month content roadmap.
      - **Phase 2 (12-Month Execution):** High-volume content creation (target: 5 articles/week).
  - **Tooling:** Includes a proprietary LLM monitoring platform (built to fill gaps in tools like Profound/Scrunch).
  - **Case Study (Augment Code):** 3x traffic, eliminated paid spend, and achieved AI search visibility over competitors.

### Reconciling the Two Approaches

  - **Assort Health's Need:** Foundational pages for specialties (e.g., cardiology) to provide a clear user journey and improve conversion.
  - **GrowthX's Position:** The standard model is editorial content. However, a custom scope for foundational pages is possible if Assort Health handles design/dev.
  - **The Alternative (Directive Consulting):** A traditional agency that prioritizes building out core pages *before* scaling editorial content.
