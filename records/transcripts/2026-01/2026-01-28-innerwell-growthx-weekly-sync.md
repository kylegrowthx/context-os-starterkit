## Summary

Matthew Alves-Hill from GrowthX presented January performance results with Akina and Noa from Innerwell, addressing renewal negotiations, the ketamine content cluster completion, and a new general mental health content strategy. Key friction points include Innerwell's budget constraint ($8k vs. GrowthX's required $12k/mo), and GA4 tracking gaps preventing accurate CTA-to-conversion attribution.

---

## Context

Innerwell and GrowthX are in the renewal cycle for their content marketing engagement. The relationship has driven strong organic performance in January—blog traffic doubled to 1,260 sessions/week, with top performers including the Ketamine Laws by State reference guide and evergreen pieces like "emotional safety" and "fear of happiness." However, budget expectations have shifted on Innerwell's side, creating tension in the renewal. Additionally, the engagement data infrastructure remains incomplete: GA4 doesn't distinguish between general page clicks and "Take the Assessment" CTA clicks, making it impossible to measure downstream conversion impact from content. This meeting balances wins (traffic, content strategy expansion) with operational challenges (budget negotiations, analytics setup).

## Key Topics

### Renewal & Budget

  - **Innerwell Request:** Reduce monthly budget to $8k.
  - **GrowthX Position:** The $12k/mo core package is non-negotiable and cannot be itemized.
  - **Innerwell Request:** Switch from annual to quarterly or biannual renewal for flexibility, citing past performance volatility.
  - **Action:** Matt will present these requests to Andy but set expectations that a budget reduction is unlikely.

### Content Strategy & Production

  - **Ketamine Cluster:** Nearing completion with 3 articles left to refresh.
      - **Refreshes:** Content updates only (SERP analysis, internal linking) → no URL changes.
      - **Exclusions:** Articles Innerwell uses for sharing were intentionally excluded.
  - **New Strategy: "General Mental Health" Cluster**
      - **Rationale:** Replicate the success of evergreen articles (e.g., "emotional safety") that consistently perform well.
      - **Proposal:** 51 new topic assignments added to Airtable for Innerwell review.
      - **Goal:** Drive traffic to general mental health landing pages, with potential for specific service funnels.

### Performance & Tracking

  - **January Traffic:**
      - **Blog:** Doubled traffic to \~1,260 sessions/week.
      - **Top Performers:** "Ketamine Laws by State" and evergreen articles.
      - **Engagement:** Strong on top blogs (60–70% rate); learnings from lower-performing pieces will inform future strategy.
  - **PSEO Landing Pages:**
      - **Redirects:** Successfully restored traffic to pre-URL-break levels.
      - **Top States:** Georgia, California, Texas.
      - **Challenge:** Engagement on these pages remains low post-redirect and requires improvement.
  - **Conversion Tracking Issue:**
      - **Problem:** The "Take the Assessment" CTA click is tracked as "user engagement" in GA4.
      - **Impact:** This event is indistinguishable from any other click on the page, preventing accurate ROI measurement for content.
      - **Solution:** Create a unique, dedicated conversion event for the CTA.
  - **Mixpanel:** Innerwell also uses Mixpanel; a recurring report on editorial pages was requested to align on conversion metrics.
