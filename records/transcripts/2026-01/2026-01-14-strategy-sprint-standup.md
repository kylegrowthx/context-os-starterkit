## Summary

Sprint team held the weekly standup to review six active client accounts (Cresta, Ramp, Nivon, Brex, BlackSol, Turnstile) and align on strategic priorities. Two critical SEO issues emerged: Cresta's indexing failure (3/11 articles indexed due to robots.txt misconfiguration) and Ramp's slower indexing (12/30 articles). The team discussed positioning GrowthX as a "strategic content machine partner" rather than a commodity agency, with premium pricing ($40k+/month) justified by proprietary insights and rigorous SEO expertise. Jason Gong introduced Check That, the proprietary AI visibility platform replacing Scrunch, with rollout to all Sprint clients by end of January.

## Context

The Strategy Sprint is GrowthX's highest-value engagement model targeting high-growth B2B companies with complex content and SEO requirements. This meeting occurred at a critical juncture: multiple clients are in mid-engagement decision points and facing competitive pressure from lower-cost vendors. The introduction of Check That signals GrowthX's shift from service-only to tech-enabled services, providing proprietary data advantages that justify premium positioning.
