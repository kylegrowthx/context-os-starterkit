## Summary

GrowthX presented a strategic pivot for Talaera from B2C-heavy content to a B2B-focused 8-cluster strategy designed to improve AI visibility and capture high-intent buyer traffic. The engagement centers on a data-backed content program, technical SEO remediation, and AI visibility tracking via GrowthX's proprietary "Check That" platform.

---

## Context

Talaera, a corporate language and cross-cultural communication platform, has a visibility problem rather than a product problem. With approximately 140 existing blog posts heavily weighted toward B2C topics (grammar, vocabulary), Talaera's ideal customer profile (ICP)—L&D professionals and corporate training buyers—cannot easily find the platform in organic or AI search. GrowthX's engagement aims to reposition Talaera in high-intent B2B segments (competitive positioning, cross-cultural workplace communication, support & CX team training, and HR/L&D strategy) while leveraging Talaera's unique anonymized platform data as a content differentiator. This is part of a broader GrowthX initiative to expand AI visibility optimization (AEO) capabilities through their "Check That" platform, which tracks AI search visibility across 100k+ evaluation-stage prompts.

## Key Topics

### Technical SEO Audit & Fixes

  - Mal is responsible for implementing these fixes.
  - **Action:** Anita will provide Mal with a prioritized list of fixes from the audit doc and explain their importance for the new content strategy's success.

### Content Strategy: Problem & Analysis

  - **Core Problem:** Talaera's primary challenge is low visibility for its B2B platform, not conversion. When the platform gets in front of its ICP, it wins deals.
  - **Current Content Analysis:**
      - **Volume:** \~140 blog posts.
      - **Focus:** Heavily B2C-oriented (e.g., grammar, vocab), attracting solo learners who are not the ICP.
      - **Performance:** Culture-focused content is the most effective at driving B2B conversions.
  - **Competitor Analysis:**
      - **B2C:** Preply dominates organic search but has limited B2B content.
      - **B2B:** Voxy, Spex, and LearnLite are active in corporate training and L\&D content.

### Content Strategy: Proposed Clusters

  - The strategy is built on 8 topic clusters (4 B2B, 3 B2C, 1 Hybrid) to build authority and target specific funnel stages.
  - **B2B Clusters:**
    1.  **Competitive Positioning & Buyer Guides:** High-intent, low-volume content (e.g., "Talaera vs. Preply") to capture users actively evaluating solutions.
    2.  **Cross-Cultural Workplace Communication:** Addresses real-world client pain points (e.g., "how to work with German colleagues").
          - **Key Principle:** Content must be nuanced and bridge understanding, not perpetuate stereotypes.
    3.  **Support & CX Team Communication:** A unique differentiator, leveraging Talaera's contact center case studies as proof points.
    4.  **HR & L\&D Strategy:** Targets L\&D professionals with content on measuring training ROI, competing with Voxy and Spex.
  - **B2C Clusters (to build on existing content):**
    5\.  **Meetings & Presentations:** Taps into professional insecurity around speaking skills.
    6\.  **Business Writing:** Addresses insecurity around emails and Slack, highlighting Talaera's self-paced courses.
    7\.  **General Speaking:** Focuses on everyday confidence and accent improvement.
  - **Hybrid Cluster:**
    8\.  **Business English for Specific Functions:** Targets roles like engineering and marketing.
    \*   **Format:** Can be a combination of blog posts and dedicated landing pages.

### Content Strategy: Differentiation & Data

  - Talaera's content will stand out by incorporating two unique elements:
      - **Case Studies:** A library of proof points will be programmatically inserted into articles.
      - **Unique Data:** Anonymized platform data will be used to create original stats and insights.
          - **Available Data:** Learning goals, job titles, country, lesson types, and completion rates (e.g., 87% completion rate).

### AI Visibility Tracking: "Check That" Platform

  - GrowthX's proprietary platform, "Check That," will track Talaera's performance in AI search results.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Shared Index:** Tracks 1,500+ companies and 100k+ evaluation-stage prompts.
      - **Metrics:** Overall position in AI lists, sentiment, and citation share.
      - **Tracking:** Currently monitors 45 custom prompts for Talaera against 14 competitors.
  - **Access:** Talaera will gain access to the dashboard within a few weeks.
