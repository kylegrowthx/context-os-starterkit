## Context

N-able is working with GrowthX (a B2B content marketing agency) to support a strategic brand shift from "RMM House" to positioning N-able as a cybersecurity leader. The engagement focuses on content clustering, editorial workflow optimization, and visibility in large language models (LLMs). At N-able's leadership level, there is emphasis on improving brand discoverability through LLM searches for terms like "cybersecurity software." The N-able content team operates across multiple products (AdLumen, N-central, Cove) with distributed product marketing review, which creates both alignment challenges and opportunities for content positioning.

GrowthX is introducing LLM visibility as a key strategic lever—particularly through comparison listicles and tool reviews—which represents a departure from N-able's historical hesitation around competitor mentions. The partnership includes content pipeline management through Airtable, editorial workflow streamlining, and performance tracking across organic channels. Both teams are working to balance speed-to-publish with content quality and strategic alignment across product lines.

## Key Topics

### LLM Visibility & New Strategy

  - **Goal:** Improve N-able's visibility in LLMs for broad searches like "cybersecurity software" to support the brand shift from "RMM House" to a cybersecurity leader.
  - **Proposed Tactic:** Publish comparison listicles (e.g., "Top 5 Tools for X").
      - **Rationale:** This content format is highly effective for LLM citation and allows N-able to own the narrative around its competitive set.
      - **Challenge:** N-able has historically avoided this format due to PMM alignment issues.
  - **GrowthX Tool:** GrowthX is creating an LLM visibility profile for N-able in its new tool to provide granular tracking data.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will provide a strategic rationale and examples to help N-able build an internal case for this content.

### Content Pipeline & New Clusters

  - **Publishing Cadence:** 10 articles published; 5 in production this week (including 3 "Attack Resilience" pieces).
  - **PMM Review Process:** Elaine is streamlining the review queue by assigning articles to specific PMMs (e.g., vulnerability prioritization → Joel) to reduce bottlenecks.
  - **New Topic Clusters Approved:**
      - **CMMC Compliance:** Based on topics provided by Joel (N-central PMM).
      - **MDR Verticals:** Keyword research is underway. Existing MDR topics will be moved from "Considering" to this new cluster.

### Blog CTA Strategy

  - **GrowthX Proposal:** Use existing website "blocks" to create dynamic, product-specific CTAs at the end of blog posts.
      - **Block Examples:** AdLumen, N-central, Cove.
      - **Requirement:** Webflow CMS access for GrowthX to build the blocks.
  - **N-able Decision:** Rejected the proposal for now.
      - **Reason:** The required security review for CMS access is too time-intensive.
      - **Alternative:** N-able will use static banners from its internal creative team.

### Airtable Image Export Issue

  - **Problem:** Inconsistent image exports from Airtable.
      - **Issues:** Mixed file types (PNG/JPEG), varying sizes, and occasional incomplete downloads.
  - **Request:** Standardize exports to JPEG to ensure consistency and smaller file sizes.
