### Process Overhaul: Project Management Tool

**Current Problem:** The team uses Linear as the PM tool, but it's largely unused due to complexity. Teams don't track artifacts (Content OS, decision docs, client feedback) consistently, making it hard to scale operations and hand off work.

**Decision:** Replace Linear with a more user-friendly tool.

**Options Under Consideration:**
- **Low-cost SaaS PM tool (~$300/mo):** Faster, simpler, and more cost-effective than building custom tools.
- **Custom Claude-based tool:** Panzer recently built an EM tracking tool in Claude that works well; the team discussed whether a similar custom build could work for Sprint PM. Consensus: the SaaS route is likely faster.

**Next Steps:** The team will research alternatives and present options next week.

---

### Process Overhaul: Workflows & Responsibilities

**Goal:** Formalize all workflows to scale operations, reduce Aida's workload, and prevent chaos as the team grows.

**Key Areas for Formalization:**

#### ME Responsibilities (Sydney & Team)
- Proactively manage content status in Airtable: flag items in "production," "client review," or "stuck" states.
- Check for client feedback on edited drafts and flag delays.
- Update Looker dashboards weekly with content status.
- Provide structured weekly updates to leadership: what's published, what's in review, what's blocked.

#### Handoff Documentation (All)
- Document all handoffs explicitly: client onboarding, sprint-to-full engagement transitions, and scope changes.
- Prevents breakdowns when team members are unavailable or when accounts transition between EMs.

#### Communication Templates (Sydney to Share)
- Sydney will distribute Marisol's client communication templates to standardize outreach, kick-off messaging, and feedback requests.

#### Weekly Reporting
- MEs and EMs must provide structured status updates every week (not ad hoc).
- Format: by client, by content status (published, in review, in production, blocked).

---

### Process Overhaul: Automation Plan (Sydney)

**Draft Consolidation:** Create a single Slack channel where all drafts from Airtable appear automatically. Simplifies visibility for EMs and MEs.

**Status Reminders:** Send internal reminders on Wednesday and Friday afternoons for any content in "production" or "client review." Prompts action before Friday crunch.

**Editing Guidelines:** Sydney and Sucheta are creating client-specific editing guidelines (example: Abnormal already has guidelines). This reduces back-and-forth and sets expectations up front.
