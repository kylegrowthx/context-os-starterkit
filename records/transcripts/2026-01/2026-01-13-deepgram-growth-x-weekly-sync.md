## Summary

GrowthX and Deepgram reviewed content performance driven by Dreamdata attribution, confirming that competitive content (especially "11 Labs Whisper" and "Gladia" comparison pages) generates the highest-value console sign-ups. ## Context

This is a strategic sync between GrowthX (content marketing services) and Deepgram (AI speech-to-text platform) following three months of engagement (since September 2025). Deepgram is in a critical growth phase, having just announced a Series C funding round on the day of this call, which temporarily paused content publishing but validated the investment in their platform expansion. The meeting occurred on the heels of this announcement while the team managed recovery publishing (1-2 articles daily) and addressed infrastructure issues (Netlify performance problems).

GrowthX has been producing ~5 articles weekly focused on SEO-driven content spanning top-of-funnel voice AI topics and competitive comparison content (targeting users evaluating alternatives to Deepgram). The partnership is shifting from initial foundation-building to data-driven optimization, leveraging Dreamdata attribution to understand which content types drive actual console sign-ups and downstream B2B deals. Deepgram's product marketing function is scaling with the addition of Martine Katz as a new product marketing manager, integrating internal SME-driven content (product-focused, technical) with GrowthX's long-tail SEO strategy.

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Performance

  - **Performance Data:** Dreamdata attribution confirms competitive content drives the highest-value conversions.
      - **Top Performers:** "11 Labs Whisper" and "Gladia" comparison pages.
      - **Underperformers:** "Twilio" and "Amazon Transcribe" pages.
      - **Significance:** This data validates the initial strategy and provides clear direction for future content prioritization.
  - **Strategic Focus:** Prioritize content that directly addresses high-intent user searches (e.g., "compare 11 labs and Deepgram").
      - **Rationale:** This approach aims to capture users evaluating alternatives and influence AI models (like Google's AI Overviews) that synthesize information from top-ranking pages.
  - **New Topic:** A prescriptive guide on evaluating speech recognition models using open-source datasets (FLIRS, Common Voice).
      - **Angle:** Position as a reference guide, not a one-off article.
      - **Title:** Leverage the high search volume for "FLIRS" directly in the title.

### Programmatic SEO (PSEO) Initiative

  - **Goal:** Launch language-specific landing pages (e.g., "Spanish speech-to-text") to capture low-hanging SEO traffic.
  - **Status:** Launch is pending finalization of the Sanity integration, expected this week.
  - **Rollout Plan:**
      - **Staging:** All pages will be staged in Sanity for review before going live.
      - **Batching:** Pages will be published in batches of \~10 per week.
      - **Interlinking:** A language-selector panel will be added to all pages to improve site navigation and SEO.
  - **Scaling:** The pipeline is built to support rapid scaling, accommodating the 50+ new languages expected by EOY Q1.

### Operational Updates

  - **Series C Announcement:** Deepgram is pausing blog publishing for 2–3 days to focus on the announcement.
      - **Recovery Plan:** Publish 1–2 articles daily next week to catch up.
  - **Website Issue:** A Netlify issue is causing slow page loads and broken images on Deepgram's blog.
  - **New PMM Onboarding:** Martine Katz (new PMM) was onboarded and granted access to the shared Notion content backlog.
