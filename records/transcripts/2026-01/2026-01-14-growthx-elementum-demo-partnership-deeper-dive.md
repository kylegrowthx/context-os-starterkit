## Key Topics

### Elementum's Content Gap & Needs

- **Problem:** Elementum has no formal product marketing assets (messaging, battle cards, personas), relying on ad-hoc sales decks.
- **Target Audience:** CIOs/CDOs and their directs, plus business owners in procurement, help desk, IT, and finance.
- **Sales Motion:** Secure buy-in on the platform vision → land an initial use case (e.g., software license management) → expand from there.
- **Goal:** Produce high-quality content that the go-to-market team can immediately use and share.

### GrowthX's AI Content OS

- **Foundation:** A "Content OS" built on keyword research and topic clusters to establish authority.
- **Inputs:** Client artifacts (personas, writing guidelines, product differentiators) are ingested directly into the AI workflow.
- **Workflow Pipeline:**
  1. **Agentic Research:** Uses specialized tools (Equity, Tavly) for deep, cited research. The process reruns if quality scores aren't met.
  2. **Drafting:** Uses research and client artifacts to generate the article.
  3. **Validation:** The draft is checked against writing guidelines and optimized with internal/external links.
  4. **Fact-Checking:** Passages are extracted and re-researched to validate accuracy against a confidence interval, correcting any AI hallucinations.
- **Human Oversight:** Editors can intervene at any stage to guide the AI, ensuring quality and strategic alignment.

### Partnership Scope & Timeline

- **Initial 4-Week Engagement (Weeks 1–4):**
  - **Week 1:** Ingest Elementum resources (sales decks, positioning, product differentiators); kickoff calls to refine V1 artifacts (personas, writing guidelines, content priorities).
  - **Week 2:** Finalize artifacts and build out the "Content OS"—keyword research, topic clusters, persona-specific frameworks.
  - **Weeks 3–4:** Publish initial "calibration articles" for feedback and quality validation.
- **Post-Kickoff Production (Weeks 5–7):**
  - **Weeks 5–6:** Publish 5–10 articles/week to the Elementum CMS; content tailored to CIO/CDO buyers and functional stakeholders (procurement, IT, finance).
  - **Week 7:** Checkpoint review; Elementum decides on 12-month "Phase 2" engagement or go-live with live dashboard.
- **Product & Platform Access:**
  - **Month 1:** Access to GrowthX's "AI visibility product" (AEO/SEO tracking for Elementum's content).
  - **Months 3–6:** Potential access to GrowthX's internal orchestration platform (for advanced editorial control and workflow customization).
