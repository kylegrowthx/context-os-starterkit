### Introducing "Check That" Platform

  - GrowthX is replacing Scrunch with "Check That," its new internal AI visibility platform.
  - **Core Strategy:** Focuses exclusively on high-intent, evaluation-stage prompts (e.g., comparisons, alternatives) to provide more valuable data than general topic searches.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Instant Data:** Access to a 100k+ prompt library provides historical data from day one, eliminating Scrunch's data-collection lag.
      - **Contextual Tracking:** Searches are based on context, not just brand names, ensuring accurate comparisons (e.g., "Sage Intacct" vs. "Sage").
      - **Source Tracking:** Shows the exact page cited by an LLM, enabling direct traffic attribution and content optimization.
      - **"Sources" Page:** Functions like a SERP for AI, listing top-cited pages (often "best of" lists) to identify content opportunities.
  - **Discern's Performance in "Check That":**
      - High visibility: 78% of brand mentions in tracked prompts.
      - Strong citation share: One article has a 1% share, which is significant as the platform's observed maximum is \~6%.
  - **Access & Pricing:**
      - GrowthX will use "Check That" for all client reporting.
      - Discern will receive a year of direct platform access upon renewal.
