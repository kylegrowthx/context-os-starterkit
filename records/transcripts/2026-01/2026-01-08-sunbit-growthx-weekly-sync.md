## Key Topics

### December Performance Review

  - **Traffic:** Sessions outpaced November and nearly matched October, a strong result given the holiday period.
  - **Keyword Rankings:** A raw Google Search Console export showed a decline in total keywords ranking (1,943 → 1,700 for top 10 positions).
      - **Rationale:** This is not a red flag, as the decline is due to GSC's inclusion of irrelevant, long-tail keywords. Core keyword performance is stable.
  - **LLM Visibility (Scrub):**
      - **Competitor Gap:** Sunbit trails CareCredit significantly but is closing the gap with Cherry.
      - **Citation Gap:** Sunbit's citation rate is 2% vs. 9% for competitors combined, largely due to CareCredit's dominance.

### Strategic Opportunities

  - **Local SEO ("Near Me" Searches):**
      - **Problem:** The Shop Directory receives high impressions but has a low CTR for local queries like "auto repair shops near me."
      - **Proposed Solutions:**
        1.  [**Programmatic Pages:** Create template-based pages (e.g., "Best Dental Providers in \[City\]") to capture this traffic.](https://fathom.video/share/PqByCaym5CDz8THNxrar_M1U5gQnq_rq?tab=summary&timestamp=700.0)
        2.  **Partner Promotion:** Ask partners to mention Sunbit on their sites and Google My Business profiles to build credibility.
  - **LLM Content Gap:**
      - **Problem:** Sunbit is missing from 98 ChatGPT queries where CareCredit appears.
      - **Action:** Enrich \~39 existing articles with these missing queries to improve visibility.
      - **Challenge:** This requires a compliance review for each update, creating a workflow bottleneck.

### Process & Planning

  - **Content Refresh Workflow:** A formal process is needed to handle content refreshes at scale while navigating compliance review.
      - **Rationale:** Essential for maintaining performance and LLM visibility as content volume grows.
  - **Sunbit AI Strategy:** An AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) strategy for the `sunbit.ai` domain will be presented before the end of January.
