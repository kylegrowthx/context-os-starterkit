## Overview

- **Customer Pain Point:** VC-backed firms get stuck after exhausting paid search headroom. They need repeatable organic growth to meet aggressive targets, often under pressure from CMOs with \~1-year tenures.
- **Our Value Prop:** Provide "speed to signal" by rapidly testing hypotheses and delivering data. This accelerates customer learning cycles and helps them demonstrate value before their CMO's tenure ends.
- **Core Strategy:** Start with editorial content to build deep business/persona understanding. This foundation informs all subsequent work, including programmatic SEO and content repurposing.
- **Key Tactic:** Use a `/guides` subfolder to bypass slow client blog processes. This creates a dedicated, fast-moving surface area for testing without diluting the client's thought leadership content.

## Key Topics

### The Customer Problem: Stuck After Paid Search

  - Our customers are VC-backed firms needing repeatable growth to hit aggressive targets.
  - They start with paid search for its predictable modeling but quickly hit its natural headroom (limited search volume).
  - This leaves them stuck, unable to scale beyond demand capture.
  - **The CMO Pressure Cooker:**
      - Average CMO tenure is \~1 year.
      - A typical organic growth cycle (assessment → quick wins → data validation) consumes most of this time.
      - This creates intense pressure to show results fast, making "speed to signal" our core value.

### Our Solution: A Phased Approach

  - We start with editorial content (high-quality articles) to build a deep understanding of the client's business and personas.
  - This foundation informs all subsequent work, ensuring strategic alignment.
  - We then layer on other services, like programmatic SEO (PSEO), as the client's program matures.
  - **Note:** We avoid one-off projects and highly custom work, which have historically been unprofitable.

### Use Case 1: Programmatic SEO (PSEO)

  - **Definition:** Template-based content creation to capture long-tail search intent at scale.
  - **Our Role:** We build the PSEO system from scratch for clients, as they rarely have one.
      - This includes template design, data sourcing, and technical implementation.
  - **Example: Ramp Per Diem Calculator**
      - **Opportunity:** High search volume for per diem rates, with poor user experiences from government sites.
      - **Solution:** Scraped government data to build a superior, interactive calculator.
      - **Result:** Created a scalable template for hundreds of city-specific pages.
  - **Example: Vapi Use Cases**
      - **Opportunity:** Vapi (voice AI) lacked clarity on its own use cases.
      - **Solution:** Analyzed Vapi's data to identify \~3,000 use cases.
      - **Result:** Built a PSEO system to create pages for each use case, including a forkable system prompt for the Vapi tool.

### Use Case 2: The `/guides` Subfolder Tactic

  - **Problem:** Client blog processes are often slow, hindering our ability to test and learn quickly.
  - **Solution:** Create a dedicated `/guides` subfolder on the client's site.
  - **Rationale:** This provides a fast-moving surface area for our content, separate from the client's thought leadership blog.
  - **Goal:** Enable rapid testing and learning without diluting the client's core brand voice.

### Use Case 3: Content Repurposing

  - **Strategy:** Extend the life and reach of existing client assets (e.g., webinars, podcasts).
  - **Example: Galileo Podcast Repurposing**
      - **Process:** Transcribed a weekly podcast into a detailed article.
      - **Result:** Created a more engaging experience by offering both video and a scannable article, increasing content accessibility.
