## Key Topics

### Agenda Timing Strategy

  - **Problem:** Posting agendas too early triggers pre-meeting questions and "nitpicking."
  - **Solution:** Post agendas 30–60 mins pre-meeting.
      - **Rationale:** This window allows participants to add items without enough time to initiate lengthy discussions before the meeting.
      - **Precedent:** This strategy was a best practice from a previous managing editor role.
  - **Parallel Experience:** Carly encountered similar challenges at Conductor, where stakeholders asked questions days in advance if the agenda was visible too early.

### Meeting Recording Tool Comparison

  - **Fireflies:**
      - **Pro:** Highly accurate transcripts, even with diverse accents and international teams.
      - **Con:** Occasional misspellings of names.
      - **Feature:** Supports specific note-taking within transcripts.
  - **Fathom:**
      - **Pro:** Centralized recording and speaker-identification capabilities; can tag people in calls.
      - **Con:** Auto-joins all meetings; no option to disable on a per-meeting basis.
      - **Con:** Speaker identification fails when logged into the team email, as it attributes all speech to the generic "team@growthxlabs.com" account instead of individual users.
  - **GrowthX Context:** Team is evaluating whether to switch tools; Carrie is agnostic on the choice as long as direction is clear.
