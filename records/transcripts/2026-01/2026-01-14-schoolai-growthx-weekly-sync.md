## Context

SchoolAI is a platform targeting school administrators to improve student outcomes using AI. The company is driving for 3x business growth, with approximately 70% of that goal dependent on new business acquisition. Marketing channels include organic/direct search, paid search, events (30%+ of revenue), email, social, and out-of-home. The engagement with GrowthX (part of their marketing mix) has underperformed due to a fundamental audience mismatch: content attracts teachers seeking general education resources rather than school administrators and directors with budgets to make purchasing decisions.

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency specializing in demand capture and full-funnel content strategy. Kyle Gaudreau leads the account and has prior experience with Webflow migrations and SEO-driven growth at companies like Homebase. Jo Kaminska is the strategic lead on SchoolAI's account, working on ICP refinement and positioning integration (using SchoolAI's positioning deck from December 2025).

## Overview

- **Problem:** The $18k/mo investment is underperforming due to poor traffic quality (teacher-focused content) and a lack of revenue impact, with zero deals opened in the Buster CRM.
- **Solution:** Pivot to a "quality over quantity" strategy, shifting from 10 articles/week to 5 high-impact pieces/week ($12k/wk) focused on the ICP (school administrators) to capture demand.
- **Measurement:** Success will be measured by deals opened in Buster, not just traffic. New Looker dashboards will track content's influence on signups and space launches.
- **Decision:** Nikki will review the proposal with the CRO today and provide a final decision by EOW.

## Key Topics

### Current State & Challenges

  - **Performance Gaps:**
      - **Traffic Quality:** Content attracts the wrong audience (teachers, not buyers).
      - **Revenue Impact:** Zero deals opened in Buster CRM from GrowthX content.
      - **Cost:** The $18k/mo investment is not justified by current results.
  - **Technical SEO:**
      - **Visibility:** Declining visibility in AI answer engines.
      - **Migration Risk:** Webflow migration poses a risk of losing SEO value.

### Proposed Solution: Strategic Pivot

  - **Goal Alignment:** The strategy will align with SchoolAI's 3x business growth target, focusing on the new business portion (\~70% of the goal).
  - **New Content Strategy:**
      - **ICP Focus:** Shift from teachers to school administrators (buyers with budgets).
      - **Full-Funnel Approach:** Map content to the buyer journey (Awareness → Consideration → Decision).
      - **Content Mix:** Prioritize refreshes of high-potential existing content and create net-new pieces for strategic gaps.
      - **Content Pruning:** Redirect or remove underperforming teacher-focused content to improve crawl budget and focus SEO authority.
  - **Measurement & Funneling:**
      - **Primary Metric:** Deals opened in Buster CRM.
      - **Secondary Metrics:** Track engaged sessions (time on page, pages per session) and user flow from blog to "money pages" in Google Analytics.
      - **Tactics:** Use empathetic CTAs (e.g., newsletter signups) to nurture users, rather than pushing for immediate demos.

### Proposal Details

  - **Pricing:** $12k/week for 5 high-impact editorial pieces.
      - This is GrowthX's standard "editorial" package, offered to reflect the pivot to quality over quantity.
  - **Scope:** Includes landing page optimization advice and full SEO support for the Webflow migration.
  - **Webflow Migration Support:**
      - **Pre-Migration Audit:** Identify risks and requirements.
      - **Post-Migration Audit:** Verify redirects, fix broken links, and monitor indexation.
      - **Ongoing Monitoring:** Quarterly technical audits.
      - **Rationale:** Proactive support is critical to prevent SEO value loss, as migrations often cause unexpected issues (e.g., Webflow's handling of trailing slashes).
  - **Optional Add-on:** Backlink building to core "money pages" to boost their demand-capture rankings.
