## Context

This is a strategic business review between two B2B vendors: GrowthX (providing content marketing and AI visibility platform services) and Ambient Intelligence (the client, developing AI-powered computer vision). The meeting sits midway through a 5-month engagement (Sep 2025–Jan 2026) that started at 1 month into Q3 2025. The engagement is in execution phase—shifting from pure content volume to quality and brand consistency. Ambient is preparing for a major sales kickoff on Feb 17, 2026, requiring a quarterly rollup showing progress in website traffic, LLM visibility, and SEO. The meeting also marks a tooling transition: GrowthX is replacing the Scrunch dashboard (which was tracking too many irrelevant AI prompts) with its own proprietary B2B-focused AI visibility platform, which will give Ambient direct access.
