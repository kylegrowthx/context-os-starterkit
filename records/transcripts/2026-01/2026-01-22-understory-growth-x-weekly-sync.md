## Context

This is a strategic alignment call between GrowthX's content and strategy team (Katya Luscomb) and Understory's leadership (Ali Yildirim, Alex Fine) to review three months of content performance post-launch. The session focuses on validating the organic content strategy, demonstrating that post-holiday traffic recovery and first-page keyword rankings signal Google's recognition of site authority. The discussion centers on two key initiatives: expanding into Reddit as a new traffic and authority channel, and implementing lead tracking infrastructure. Both partners are building toward a content engine that compounds growth through organic and LLM-driven discovery while maintaining quality and avoiding platform friction (e.g., Reddit bot flags).

---

## Relevance

- **Content Performance Validation**: Post-holiday traffic hit all-time highs, proving organic strategy durability and that seasonal dips don't indicate momentum loss.
- **LLM Traffic as Proof of Relevance**: Higher engagement from LLM-referred users (ChatGPT, Claude) vs. Google traffic shows the content engine is correctly positioning Understory for AI-native discovery—a differentiation from traditional SEO.
- **Reddit Strategy De-risks New Channel**: Rather than ad-hoc Reddit posting, the structured karma-building approach mitigates bot-flag risk while creating a long-tail SEO asset that search engines cite.
- **Lead Attribution Gap**: Inability to track blog CTA leads to specific articles is a critical blind spot that UTM parameters will resolve, enabling content ROI measurement at the article level.
- **Keyword Research Expansion**: Benchmark and B2B SaaS growth strategy content proven high-performers; upcoming services (LinkedIn thought leadership, HubSpot, outbound) require bespoke keyword research.

## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Optimization

  - **Performance Highlights (3-Month Review):**
      - **Sessions:** Post-holiday traffic hit an all-time high, fully recovering from a minor seasonal dip.
      - **Keyword Rankings:** First-page rankings (positions 2–10) have skyrocketed, showing Google is recognizing site authority.
      - **LLM Referrals:** Traffic from LLMs is growing and shows higher engagement (dwell time) than Google traffic.
  - **High-Performing Content:**
      - **"Trends & Benchmark" Content:** Extremely popular (e.g., "benchmark conversion rate").
      - **"B2B SaaS Growth Strategy" Content:** Also performing very well.
  - **Future Optimization:**
      - **Content Refresh:** In March, refresh existing articles with TLDRs and FAQs to improve LLM visibility.
      - **New Content:** Focus keyword research on the high-performing "benchmark" and "growth strategy" topics.
  - **Indexation Issue:**
      - **Unindexed Pages:** Search Console shows 82 pages not being indexed; likely causes include 404 errors, sitemap issues, or non-optimized page setup.
      - **Investigation Required:** Katya will analyze patterns and report findings to Ali.

### Reddit Strategy

  - **Goal:** Establish a presence on Reddit to drive traffic and build authority.
  - **Timeline:** Content pipeline launches mid-to-late February.
  - **Execution:**
      - **Account Setup:** Understory to accept GrowthX's email transfer request and provide a banner image (size to be provided by GrowthX).
      - **Content Cadence:** GrowthX will post weekly articles to the Understory profile.
      - **Strategy:** Build karma before posting in subreddits to avoid bot flags and bans.
      - **Reporting:** GrowthX is developing a Reddit-specific reporting method.
      - **Long-term Vision:** Model after SEMrush's MuteDash Reddit account, which has extensive karma and diverse topics to leverage SEO citation value.

### New Initiatives & Lead Tracking

  - **Lead Tracking:**
      - **Problem:** Inability to attribute leads from blog CTAs to specific articles.
      - **Solution:** Add UTMs to Calendly links in all blog CTAs.
      - **Future Idea:** Test a more prominent, tailored CTA (e.g., sidebar) to improve conversion rates.
  - **Backlinks:**
      - **GrowthX Service:** GrowthX is investigating a service to secure ~5 backlinks/month via a partner network. Pricing is pending.
      - **Understory Initiative:** Understory will leverage its own network for informal backlink asks. GrowthX will provide messaging templates.
  - **New Service Keyword Research:**
      - **LinkedIn Thought Leadership:** GrowthX will research keywords for this upcoming service once Understory provides messaging.
      - **HubSpot & Outbound Sync:** GrowthX will add keywords for these tools to the content calendar.
