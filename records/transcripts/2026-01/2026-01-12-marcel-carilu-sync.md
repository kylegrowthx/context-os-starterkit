## Key Topics

### Business Model Mechanics
- All revenue currently comes from services (not platform sales)
- Customers want visibility and analytics, not DIY platform access
- Two kickoffs per week = ~88 kickoffs/year at 75% conversion = 2x ARR growth
- Each kickoff requires 4 FTEs with 2-month ramp time
- Pod capacity (engagement manager + editorial) is a key bottleneck

### Growth Levers
1. **Kickoffs** (primary lever) - Scale from 2 to 3+ per week
2. **Conversion** (secondary) - Improve from 75% to 80%
3. **ACV** (limited) - Already high; retention not strong enough to push further

### Check That Strategy
- Feature complete, finishing sign-up and onboarding this week
- Self-serve monetization launching next week
- Separate from GrowthX brand to avoid cannibalization
- Low bar to ROI: just 2 customers/month pays for itself
- Replaces $11K/month Scrunch dependency

### Financial Reality
- $12M+ in bank
- Plan burns ~$8M to reach $40M ARR (good growth multiple)
- Securing line of credit for safety
- Fundraising possible in 2026 but not urgent until scaling proof points validated

### Brand Architecture
- Carilu suggested unifying brands under "X" family (Check That X, OS X)
- Marcel prefers separation to protect $200K+ service pricing from perceived $200 alternatives
- Fish from Check That pond for service leads without brand confusion
