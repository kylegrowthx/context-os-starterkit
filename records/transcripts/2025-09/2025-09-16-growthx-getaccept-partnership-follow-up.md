## Context

GetAccept is a digital sales room platform that helps enterprise and mid-market sales teams manage deal cycles, centralize communications, and provide a better buyer experience through interactive, AI-powered sales collateral. Their product is sophisticated and all-in-one — spanning e-signatures, proposal software, and deal collaboration tools — making them compete on breadth rather than single-point solutions. They're building category awareness for "digital sales room" as a concept and recently lost their content manager, leaving a gap in organic marketing that had already flatlined at 10-15k monthly visits for the past 1-2 years.

This follow-up call was the second conversation between GrowthX and GetAccept on a potential partnership. Tyler Pavlas, an account executive at GrowthX, presented the case for GrowthX's services: moving GetAccept beyond generic awareness content (like "Top Qualities of a Sales Manager") to buyer-segment-specific, consideration-focused content that targets the pain points of enterprise, mid-market, and SMB buyers separately. The core tension: Bastiaan, as a product-focused leader, wanted proof that GrowthX could deliver ROI on a 12-month, $12k/month commitment — not just traffic lifts, but actual signups and conversions — especially when cheaper SEO agencies pitch the same work.

### GetAccept's Current Situation and Goals

  - Content manager recently left
  - Building category of digital sales room
  - More MQLs coming through AI search
  - Organic traffic flat at 10-15k monthly visits for 1-2 years
  - Aim to move from general awareness content to consideration-focused, segmented by buyer type/segment
  - Need to improve funneling from content to conversion, especially for ICP

  - Focus on educational content and thought leadership
  - Create content OS with topic mapping and keyword-informed prioritization

### Content Strategy and SEO Insights

  - Opportunity to widen gap with competitors like Trumpet and Align
  - Increase content velocity and target keywords Align ranks for
  - Focus on editorial content with potential for programmatic play
  - Audience-first approach, mapping topics to personas and pain points

### Competitor Landscape

  - PandaDoc has 27x more traffic but broader solution set
  - Trumpet and Align stronger on organic social media
  - GetAccept more sophisticated but potentially more expensive due to all-in-one platform

### ROI and Performance Measurement

  - GrowthX provides insights on LLM performance against specific prompts
  - Measures core website vitals, activity (articles published/refreshed), impressions, clicks, engagement, and conversions
