## Key Topics

### Company Tools and Platforms

  - Slack: Primary internal communication tool
  - Geekbot: Integrated with Slack for daily stand-ups (9am local time)
  - Linear: Project management and IT ticket system
  - Notion: Knowledge management for SOPs and shared workspaces
  - Additional tools: Airtable, SEMrush (for managing editor), Loom, Zoom, Fathom

### Onboarding Resources and Process

  - Notion handbook contains detailed onboarding information
  - Linear tracks onboarding tasks with day/week tags
  - Key onboarding activities:
      - Tool setup (1Password, Zoom, Slack, Notion, etc.)
      - Meet managers and onboarding buddy
      - Read company articles and watch workshops/webinars
      - Attend strategy/vision meeting with Marcel (CEO)
      - Customer use cases meeting with Kyle

### Company Policies and Programs

  - Minimum 4-hour overlap with Pacific time required
  - Unlimited PTO policy (no approval needed, just calendar blocking)
  - Employee referral program: Up to $10K for engineering roles, $5K for non-engineering (after 6-month retention)
  - Bi-weekly all-hands meetings (next one on Tuesday)

### Team Email Setup

  - <team@growthxlabs.com> email used for accessing shared tools and scheduling client calls
  - Setup process involves adding account in Gmail using credentials from 1Password
