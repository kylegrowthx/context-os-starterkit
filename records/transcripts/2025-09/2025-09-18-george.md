## Relevance

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- The agentic research pipeline (using Perplexity or similar tools with iterative research criteria) outperforms legacy comparison article generation; this should become the standard for competitor research artifacts at the start of any PSEO engagement.
- Augment Code playbook: comparison articles and listicles driven by performance signals, minimal content review, rapid iteration — achieved 225 clicks/day in 2 months. Replicable if client buy-in and content velocity are high.
- Growth forecasting framework: breaking down inputs (articles/month), ramp-up periods, average visits per page, traffic capture rate, and conversion metrics helps clients internalize why organic building is a long-term play and justifies month 4+ results.
- Content review cycles are a key success variable: zero/minimal review (Augment Code) enables fast iteration; slow review (HockeyStack ~2 weeks initially, now urgent) creates bottlenecks.

**To CheckThat / AI Visibility:**
- HockeyStack is tracking Perplexity citations and visibility for attribution-related queries; George showed Scrunch data showing the team getting cited within an hour of publishing targeted long-tail content. Fast feedback loops from AI models create real optimization signals.

- Augment Code achieved rapid AI visibility (listicles and versus articles citing them) through volume and long-tail keyword coverage — direct proof of AEO working.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
