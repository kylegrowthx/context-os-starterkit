## Key Topics

### AI Narrative Misalignment

  - Competitor (RSCAL) creating content portraying HockeyStack as SMB-focused
  - Google and ChatGPT results show HockeyStack lacking AI features (opposite of reality)
  - HockeyStack's own blog posts contributing to misalignment (e.g., "Dream Data for Enterprise")
  - Strategy: Create multiple posts mentioning correct narrative to influence AI answers

### Content Strategy Correction

  - Focus on bottom-of-funnel, comparison-oriented articles (e.g., "Dream Data vs HockeyStack")
  - Update content generation artifacts to explicitly highlight enterprise focus
  - Revise existing blog posts that may portray HockeyStack as a small business solution
  - Avoid cross-posting (duplicate content) as it can harm long-term SEO despite short-term benefits

### ContentOS Topic Refinement

  - Many current topics in ContentOS not relevant to HockeyStack (e.g., forecasting, lead qualification)
  - Need to align with updated ICPs and actual product offerings
  - Remove topics like influencer marketing, affiliate marketing, and cross-device tracking

### Performance Tracking

  - HockeyStack using Profound for AI visibility tracking
  - GrowthX set up Scrunch dashboard for mention tracking
  - Pending access to GA4 and GSC for creating Looker dashboard
  - Looker will show referral traffic from LLMs (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude)
