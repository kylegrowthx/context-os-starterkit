  - Primary objective: Position HX as leading AI transformation platform for commercial P\&C insurance

    1.  AI transformation platform for commercial insurance (targeting chief AI officers, digital transformation leads)

  - David suggested researching "underwriter workbench" as important competitive area that prospects consider platform-like

    1.  Editorial content for thought leadership (upper funnel)

  - Potential content distribution: 70% focus on branded environment with newsletter aggregating industry news, 30% on competitive content
