  - Internal writers: 1.5 FTE in India (\~$60k/yr), 0.5 FTE in US (\~$70k/yr)

### GrowthX's Offering

  - AI-powered content creation with expert editors in the loop
  - Proprietary platform for content strategy, creation, and performance tracking
  - Daily publishing (12-15k words/mo), high-volume content refreshes
  - Comprehensive service: research, writing, fact-checking, graphics, publishing, optimization
  - 8-week strategy sprint ($20k) followed by ongoing execution ($9k-$15k/mo)
  - Expertise in technical content (e.g., IT, security, ITSM)

### Content Strategy and AI Visibility

  - Focus on creating comprehensive, well-researched, up-to-date content
  - Emphasis on authority signals and reputable sources
  - Structured for both SEO and reader experience
  - Aim to increase visibility in AI answers (e.g., Perplexity, ChatGPT)
  - Regular content refreshes (ideally every 3 months) to maintain relevance

### Case Studies

  - Augment Code: 120 guides in 60 days, 10x AI visibility increase

  - Webflow: All integration pages
  - Town.com: High-volume, technical blog content
