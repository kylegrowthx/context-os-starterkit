## Context

Imubit is an AI-driven process control and optimization software company for industrial manufacturing, with a strong focus on verticals like cement, mining, chemicals, and polymers. GrowthX is running Imubit's content marketing strategy, generating vertical-specific educational content designed to drive organic search visibility and demonstrate Imubit's expertise across use cases (e.g., batch consistency, margins, operational excellence). This is a weekly operational sync where Mariana (GrowthX SEO/content lead), Mara (GrowthX account manager), Dan (Imubit marketing), and Tara (Imubit product/strategy) review publishing velocity, SEO performance metrics, website structural decisions, and content quality. The relationship is healthy with strong engagement from both teams, though decision-making is occasionally fragmented across product, marketing, and engineering at Imubit, requiring careful communication around review processes to avoid scope creep.
