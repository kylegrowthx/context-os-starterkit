  - Current content strategy: $4-5k/month spent on freelance authors, language-specific content

### GrowthX's AI-Driven Content Approach

  - Built on Marcel's extensive experience in helping brands become publishers
  - Uses a custom-built platform with advanced AI capabilities:
      - Deep research using Exa AI for high-quality information gathering
      - Knowledge graph storage for better semantic understanding
      - Agentic workflows for content creation and improvement
      - Human editors in the loop for quality control and refinement
  - Focuses on creating a high volume of quality content with strategic intent
  - Aims to optimize for both search visibility and AI citations

### Case Study: Augment Code

  - 100+ pieces of content produced in 3 months
  - Achieved top organic search results for key terms
  - Multiple citations in AI-generated answers (up to 6 in one example)
  - Demonstrates the potential for rapid content scaling and SEO impact

### Build vs. Buy Considerations

  - DIY approach challenges: need for strategy, human oversight, and last-mile execution
  - Costs of piecing together solutions (e.g., hiring freelancers, purchasing tools like AirOps, hiring GTM engineers)
  - GrowthX offers an all-in-one solution combining technology, strategy, and execution

### Content Strategy Philosophy

  - Balance between thought leadership and search optimization
  - Portfolio approach: create a high volume of quality content, learn from signals, and optimize
  - Importance of being cited in AI-generated answers and search results
