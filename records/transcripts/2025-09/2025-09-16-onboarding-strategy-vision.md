## Overview

- GrowthX is building an AI-first platform to revolutionize marketing, bridging the gap between AI development and human expertise
- The company has achieved $11.5M in run rate within 14 months, demonstrating strong market demand and capital efficiency
- GrowthX's approach focuses on delivering outcomes through a unique blend of AI technology and expert-led services, targeting a $3.2B serviceable obtainable market
- The core product, Atlas, is an internal platform that combines context engines, knowledge bases, and AI-powered workflows to scale high-quality content production

## Key Topics

### Company Culture and Goals

  - Three main goals for every team member:
    1.  Do the best work of their lives
    2.  Inspire everyone around them and outside the company
    3.  Achieve more than thought possible
  - Emphasis on execution, effectiveness, and efficiency
  - "Best hire mentality" encouraged for all team members

### Business Performance and Market Position

  - $11.5M run rate achieved in 14 months
  - $1.7M closed in the current month (10 deals)
  - 7% gross margins
  - 237K burn rate last month, with over $1M in NetNew added
  - Notable clients include N-ABLE, Spectre Ops, and Lovable

### Product Vision and Development

  - Building the future of marketing with an AI-first approach
  - Atlas: Internal platform powering service delivery
      - Features: Context engine, knowledge base, AI-assisted editing, analytics (in development)
      - Workflow-as-code engine for scalable, agentic processes
  - CheckThat.ai: Upcoming visibility play

### Market Opportunity and Strategy

  - Targeting the intersection of sales/marketing labor and content marketing services
  - $3.2B serviceable obtainable market based on current tool spend
  - Focus on high-profile clients to build trust and credibility in various categories

### Competitive Advantage

  - Outcomes-based approach vs. traditional agencies or AI workflow tools
  - Unique position to understand and describe work, then build scalable AI-powered solutions
  - Generation of valuable "expert frontier data" as a byproduct of service delivery
