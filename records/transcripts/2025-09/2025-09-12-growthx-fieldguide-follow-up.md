### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Uses AI-powered platform with human expertise

  - Employs agentic workflows for research, drafting, and fact-checking
  - Produces high-quality, contextually relevant content at scale

  - GrowthX maps all relevant topics based on audience needs and competitor analysis

  - Suggests bid modifiers for retargeted audiences on expensive keywords

  - GrowthX acts as an extension of the client's team
  - Incorporates client's brand guidelines and voice
  - Offers a review process before content publication
  - Customizable workflow to match client's specific needs and complexity

### Performance Tracking

  - GrowthX provides dashboards for insights into improvements and trends
