## Key Topics

### 35's Background and Capabilities

Frank Slangen (designer) and Bob Wassermann (developer) founded 35 as a hub for freelance multidisciplinary expertise in design, development, AI, and 3D. They operate with a flexible model: can work as a unified team on complex projects or provide individual subject-matter expertise to clients. Both have deep product experience — Frank originally a designer who gravitated toward product thinking; Bob evolved from designer to full-stack developer over years of collaboration starting in 2016.

### AI Image Generation Case Study: Bose

Bob led an innovation sprint for Bose during COVID-19 when physical product photography became impractical. They built a virtual try-on tool (AI in browser, Snapchat-filter style) for sunglasses, letting customers preview fit on their face shape. Success led to a new business unit: generative AI product photography. They fine-tuned Flux on Bose product data, generating lifestyle shots (people in airplanes, subways, privacy-sensitive contexts) too expensive or complex to shoot in-camera. Two weeks before this meeting, they ran an A/B test on 4M recipients: half saw AI photos, half didn't. Result: zero complaints, no performance difference in conversions — first successful mainstream deployment of AI images for a company historically cautious about AI.

### GrowthX Overview and Business Model

Renato shared that GrowthX is an AI-native B2B marketing agency founded by Marcel (ex-CMO at Scale AI, HashiCorp, Deepgram). Marcel took his playbook of AI-powered workflows (Airtable, Zapier, off-the-shelf tools) and scaled it into an agency. Revenue grew from $6M to $11M since Renato joined as COO, with $20M projected by year-end. Recent major clients: Surge ($1B ARR, post-training lab, no funding yet) and Lovable. Now building proprietary software (internal tool for managing projects, clients, delivery) with the goal of eventually selling it as standalone SaaS.

### GrowthX's Three Support Areas

**1. Client Work:** Renato needs brand and web support for high-profile clients. Example — Surge rebrand (mid-flight): working with Surge's internal designer, but hitting a crunch point where they need senior designers to refine brand guidelines, create exemplar assets, template assets system-wide. Also building new marketing website with experiential content. Lovable project starting soon with similar scope.

**2. Internal GrowthX Branding:** Recent refresh was "disjointed." Quality hasn't reached Renato's standards yet. Need senior-level brand guidelines and website rebuild.

**3. Software Suite Branding:** GrowthX registered output.ai as the umbrella brand for new software products (distinct from the GrowthX agency brand). Need to brand output and child products from scratch.

### Collaboration Model and Engagement

GrowthX prefers monthly retainer agreements with preset hours and availability — they already work this way with two agencies. 35 offers the same structure: monthly or yearly retainers with hourly rates (€110 standard rate), plus flexibility for project-based work. All work is remote, async-first via Renato with 4-hour overlap window to San Francisco (GrowthX is fully remote, core team distributed across time zones). 35 has team augmentation experience (embedded staff innovation sprints at clients), which appeals to Renato's team-extension model.
