## Context

M12, the corporate venture fund of Microsoft, met with Marcel Santilli (GrowthX CEO) for the first time after bumping into him at a conference in San Francisco earlier that week. Peter Lenke, a new analyst at M12, and Monica Lim, his colleague, requested this intro call to understand GrowthX's market positioning, product architecture, and capital raise plans.

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing services company founded 12 months prior (as of September 2025) by Marcel Santilli and a CTO co-founder who previously built product at IFTTT and was CTO of a company spun from Basecamp (37 Signals). The company targets the $500B marketing services market—specifically the dollars spent on agencies and freelancers, not software tools—and is directly addressing a pain point Marcel identified through his own experience as a CMO: traditional agencies are expensive, underperform, and lack AI capabilities, while most marketing tools don't actually drive results. GrowthX raised a Seed round and is currently evaluating a Series B raise while managing strong organic growth driven entirely by inbound and word-of-mouth (no outbound sales).
