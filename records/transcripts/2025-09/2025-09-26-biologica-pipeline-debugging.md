## Key Topics

### Pipeline Performance Assessment

  - Pipeline performing better than expected, despite detailed documentation of issues
  - Most problems are minor and fixable; output quality is generally good
  - Biologica pipeline outperforming Digital pipeline in terms of content quality

### Knowledge Base Functionality

  - Current knowledge base not ready for consistent, high-quality answers
  - Researcher component working adequately with knowledge base
  - Main issue: inability to query specific documents from knowledge base
  - Workaround: Use ingredient checker as a separate step, outputting as an artifact

### Pipeline Optimization Strategies

  - Convert older workflows to agents where possible (e.g., medical expert review)
  - Remove redundant steps (e.g., first "add medical source links" step)
  - Simplify and consolidate steps (e.g., combine Biologica conventions and product dossier validation)
  - Move "add medical source links" step to end of pipeline to prevent link removal in later steps
  - Adjust research instructions in assignment direction for more focused queries

### Writing Guidelines and Rules

  - Split writing guidelines into three components: article directions, writing rules, and proofreader checks
  - Writing rules now handled by an agent, working well for hyperlink optimization and trademark symbols
  - Remove writing guidelines validation from proofreader checklist, as it's redundant and lacks context

### Proofreader and Post-Processing

  - Separated sentence structure optimization into its own step, improving readability
  - Proofreader checklist simplified, focusing on specific checks rather than broad guideline compliance
  - Post-processing agent approach more effective than previous workflows for most tasks
