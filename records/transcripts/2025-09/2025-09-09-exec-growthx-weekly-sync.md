## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Moving from role-play focused content to top/middle funnel content addressing user pain points
  - Competitive advantage: Diverse scenarios, but need to catch up on pain point-focused content
  - Competitors like Hyperbound and Second Nature already targeting top/middle funnel content
  - New strategy aims to cover the current gap in Exec's content

### Topic Cluster Restructuring

  - Initial proposal of four clusters received feedback for improvement
  - Sean suggested splitting the first cluster (customer-facing pain points) into two:
    1.  IC-focused content (e.g., handling objections)
    2.  Organizational leadership content (e.g., team management)
  - Merging the last two clusters (manager and executive effectiveness) into one
  - Garett emphasized focusing on the sales enablement and training persona
  - Agreement to create more granular content within chosen sales methodologies (e.g., MEDIC vs. SPIN)

### Looker Dashboard Updates

  - New dashboard includes more structured and flexible reports
  - Data sources: Google Search Console and Google Analytics 4
  - Key metrics: Sessions, engaged sessions, query distribution, non-branded traffic, events by landing page
  - LLM visibility report added to track traffic from AI platforms

### Content Audit Plans

  - Goals: Improve content quality, optimize existing content, address cannibalization issues
  - Three main actions for URLs: Keep as-is, redirect/merge, or refresh
  - Garett introduced Rival Flow tool for content optimization; team to coordinate efforts
  - Timeline: Focused audit work over the next two weeks, with limited new content production

### Technical SEO Considerations

  - Team to implement infrastructure for merge redirects when needed
  - Addressing \~30 404 errors, mainly in the /explore section
  - Sean to provide database export with URL and last updated date for content audit
