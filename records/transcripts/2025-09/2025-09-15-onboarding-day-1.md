## Overview

- Employees are expected to have 4 hours overlap with Pacific time and provide daily Geekbot updates

## Key Topics

### Company Tools Introduction

  - Slack: Primary tool for agent chats and team communication
  - Geekbot: Integrated with Slack for daily stand-ups and updates
  - Linear: Project management tool, used for raising IT tickets and tracking tasks
  - Notion: Knowledge management platform for databases and client workspaces
  - Additional tools: Airtable, Loom (screen recording), Zoom (meetings), Fathom (call recording)

### Onboarding Process

  - Resources available in Notion handbook and duplicated in Linear for task tracking
  - Day 1 focus: Setting up tools, meeting managers and onboarding buddy
  - Week 1 includes reading materials, watching videos on company history and working model
  - Three key meetings: current onboarding, CEO strategy/vision, and customer use cases

### Company Policies and Expectations

  - 4 hours minimum overlap with Pacific time required
  - Daily Geekbot updates are mandatory
  - Unlimited PTO policy: No approval needed, just calendar blocking with adequate notice
  - Employee referral program: Up to $10k USD for engineering roles, $5k USD for non-engineering (after 6 months)

### Team Communication

  - <team@growthxlabs.com> email setup for accessing tools and scheduling client calls
  - Biweekly all-hands meetings where new employees introduce themselves
  - IT and tools Slack channel for technical support
