This SEO Specialist role is new and addresses a key gap Andi identified: while delivery team members have deep growth marketing backgrounds, they struggle to step back from day-to-day client management to conduct the high-level, objective SEO research and optimization analysis that multiple clients need on a periodic basis. The role sits across the entire delivery organization, meaning internal pods become the SEO specialist's clients. GrowthX is fully remote and focuses exclusively on organic/SEO and content production—they don't offer paid advertising services.
