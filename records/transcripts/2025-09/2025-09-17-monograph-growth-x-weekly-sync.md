## Key Topics

### Reddit Content Strategy

  - Developing three post formats: utilization rate, time tracking, and architect salary
  - Posts use declarative language, minimal linking, and aim for engagement
  - Experimenting with "we" vs. factual statements for Monograph's voice
  - Designing metrics to measure LLM visibility impact and traffic from Reddit posts

### Content Generation Pipeline

  - EXA researcher creates comprehensive research plans based on topics and personas
  - Pipeline ingests various sources, including Monograph's customer stories
  - Knowledge base built from past research and one-time ingestions
  - Evaluation process ensures adherence to guidelines and rules

### Knowledge Management Challenges

  - Chris exploring solutions for managing large context and diverse content sources
  - Interest in developing a system allowing efficient loading of content "packets"
  - Desire for ability to adapt source context and push to queryable system of record
  - Need for both raw data storage and meaning synthesis capabilities

### EXA Research Tool

  - Matthew recommends exploring EXA for Chris's use case
  - EXA outperforms Perplexity in detail-orientation and depth of information retrieval
  - Potential for quick front-end development using EXA API for local database queries
