## Key Topics

### Pando.ai's Current Marketing Situation

  - Successful outbound and event marketing, but underdeveloped inbound strategy
  - Creating 1-2 pieces of content weekly, published on blog, LinkedIn, and website
  - Lack of in-house expertise for digital marketing and inbound strategies
  - Seeking to scale both organic and inorganic (paid) search efforts

### GrowthX's Offering and Approach

  - End-to-end partner for GEO/SEO strategy, content creation, and analytics
  - Focus on AI-powered content optimization for LLM visibility
  - Use of AI workflows with expert oversight for comprehensive, fresh, and well-structured content
  - Team includes managing director, managing editor, and content manager with impressive industry experience
  - Two-phase engagement: 8-week strategy sprint followed by 12-month growth execution
  - Daily publishing during growth execution phase

### GrowthX's Differentiators

  - Founded by Marcel, former marketing leader at major tech companies (IBM, HashiCorp, Scale.ai)
  - Emphasis on brands operating like publishers for long-term value
  - Proprietary AI workflows and upcoming client-facing platform
  - Track record of success with clients like Augment Code and Ramp

### Content Strategy and Examples

  - Creation of comprehensive guides, thought leadership pieces, and programmatic content
  - Focus on being the first citation in AI-powered search results
  - Examples: Augment Code's tool comparison guides, Ramp's vendor database and calculators

### Measurement and KPIs

  - Primary focus on organic traffic growth and domain authority
  - Emphasis on AI search visit quality (3x longer site visits, 4.4x more likely to convert)
  - Example: 10x organic traffic growth for Augment Code in 6 months
