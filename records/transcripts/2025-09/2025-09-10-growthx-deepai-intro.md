### Potential Benefits for DeepAI

  - Higher-value advertising content aligned with site visitors' interests

  - Potential for long-term partnerships with reputable brands

  - Content collaboration and syndication opportunities

  - DeepAI's decision-making process requires Kevin's involvement and number-crunching
  - Need to ensure new content/links don't interfere with current SEO strategy
  - Balancing potential long-term benefits against current reliable AdSense revenue
