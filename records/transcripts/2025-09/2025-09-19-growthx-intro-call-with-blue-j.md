## Context

Blue J Legal is an AI-powered tax research platform experiencing rapid hypergrowth, having recently completed a Series D funding round. The company serves a wide market—from Big Four accounting firms to solo practitioners—positioning itself as the category leader in AI-driven tax research. Lindsay Cournoyer, their CMO, is nine months into building out the marketing function from scratch after inheriting an immature program that focused primarily on events and webinars.
