## Overview

---

## Key Topics

### LLM Presence Tracking

  - Using Scrunch to track LLM presence across various topics
  - Presence ranges from 2% to low 20s across different accounts
  - No established benchmarks due to the novelty of LLM tracking
  - Focus on improvement over time rather than specific targets
  - Need to be intentional about selecting and updating tracked keywords

### Content Strategy Reevaluation

  - Current focus on Condition Hub challenged by Noa
  - Concerns about competing with established health institutions (e.g., Oxford University)
  - Suggestion to focus on more specific, situation-based content
  - Example of successful content: "Will Ketamine show up on my drug test?"
  - Proposal to invert strategy: focus on solutions (e.g., ketamine) first, then branch into conditions

### Site Performance Issues

  - Core Web Vitals problems identified on both mobile and desktop
  - GSC showing four different error types on mobile pages since early August
  - Page speed improvements needed for both mobile and desktop versions

### Location Pages Optimization

  - Improve internal linking by adding buttons linking to city pages from state pages
  - Create a directory-style page in the footer with links to all location pages
  - These changes aim to improve indexing and crawlability of city and state-level pages

### Review Page Enhancement

  - Suggestion to add a prominent bar linking to a case study or G2 review page
  - This could improve conversion rates and boost the visibility/ranking of the review page
