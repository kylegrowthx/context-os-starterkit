## Key Topics

### GrowthX Platform Overview

  - Atlas: Internal platform for tracking editor activities and workflows
  - Artifact creation: Defines business context, personas, writing guidelines
  - Knowledge base: Graph-based storage of research and ingested client information
  - Workflow engine: Code-based (not low-code) for flexibility and scalability
  - Multiple API integrations: Exa AI, Perplexity, Tavly for deep research
  - Fact-checking process: Splits content, extracts passages, verifies against reputable sources

### Content Strategy and Execution

  - Audience-first approach: Maps opportunities to personas and business goals
  - Topic cluster creation: Organized into assignments and opportunities
  - Daily publishing with full-service execution (staging, graphics, etc.)
  - Calibration process: 4-week alignment on voice, style, and content types
  - Ongoing collaboration: Weekly meetings, Slack communication for feedback

### Results and Case Studies

  - Engine.com: 100 guides in 3 months, 2x more traffic than entire blog
  - Ramp: 700+ pieces of content, significant visibility increase
  - Augment Code: 100 guides in 3 months, multiple citations in AI search results

### Future Platform Development

  - Near-term: Customer-facing dashboards, LLM visibility tools
  - Mid-term: Operating system with expert marketplace
  - Long-term: Open-source visibility data (similar to Crunchbase meets G2 for content)
