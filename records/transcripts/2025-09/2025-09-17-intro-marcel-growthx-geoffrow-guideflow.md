## Summary

Marcel Santilli and GrowthX's first AE Tyler Pavlas met with Geoffroy d'Halluin from Guideflow to explore SEO and inbound lead generation services. Guideflow is currently generating leads through outbound sales and product network effects (watermarks), but has significant untapped potential in organic search with their new website and "Demo Tutorial Hub" launching soon—they're targeting 100 programmatic tutorials per week. GrowthX outlined its approach: an 8-week strategy sprint ($30K) followed by ongoing growth execution starting at $15K/month, with a current 6-week waitlist. ## Context

Guideflow is a no-code automation platform that helps enterprises and marketers create interactive product tutorials and demos. Founded about 2 years ago, the product is mature but their website hasn't been updated in over a year. They have strong enterprise customers (Gartner, Cisco, DocuSign) but make almost nothing from marketing and SEO—their growth comes almost entirely from outbound sales, network effects (product watermarks showing "powered by Guideflow"), word-of-mouth, and sales team efforts at events. Guideflow is a team of about 20 people based between San Francisco and Paris. They recently launched a "Demo Tutorial Hub" strategy that's generating strong SEO traffic by creating 5,000+ interactive tutorial demos showing how to use specific software tools (targeting 100/week). This early success signals significant opportunity for inbound-driven growth, which is why Victor (a mutual connection from the "French growth mafia") recommended they connect with GrowthX. The meeting took place as Marcel and GrowthX were onboarding their first AE, Tyler Pavlas.
