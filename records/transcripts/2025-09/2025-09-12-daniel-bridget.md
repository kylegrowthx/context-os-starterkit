## Key Topics

### Pitch Deck Structure

  - Team slide: Focus on founders and key builders, emphasizing past roles/companies
  - Logo snapshot: Include recent additions (Bubble, Base10, Lovable)
  - Journey/traction: Show growth without overemphasizing specific metrics
  - Problem statement: Marketing complexity, need for outcomes vs tools
  - Solution: Forward-deployed experts + AI workflows/agents
  - Deployment model: Customer-centric view (e.g. Augment case study)
  - Expansion path: Visual representation of additional use cases/jobs to be done
  - Scalability: Address how to reach 2000+ customers (separate from expansion)

### Messaging Strategy

  - Meet market where they are: Emphasize rapid changes in marketing capabilities
  - Highlight need for expert guidance and execution support
  - Position GrowthX as solving overwhelm from tools/agencies
  - Frame content as "mouth of the river" for broader GTM potential
  - Avoid positioning as competing directly with specific tools (e.g. Clay, Customer.io)

### Product Demo Approach

  - Brief 3-minute flythrough of key features
  - Have multiple tabs open to quickly showcase different aspects
  - Focus on impressing without getting bogged down in details

### Investor-Specific Considerations

  - Tailor depth based on investor's background (e.g. more high-level for Altimeter's Jamin Ball)
  - Be prepared to address questions about scalability and services component
  - Leverage team's connections/reputation where applicable (e.g. Marcel's relationship with ClickHouse CEO)
