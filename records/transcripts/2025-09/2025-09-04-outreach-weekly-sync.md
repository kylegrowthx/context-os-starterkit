## Summary

GrowthX's Aida Knezevic and Vivek Shankar met with Outreach's team to discuss Looker dashboard enhancements for tracking analytics across channels, custom events for conversion tracking from Qualified chatbot data, and user journey visualization options. The team reviewed Scrunch AI visibility data showing mixed results over 12 weeks but recent improvement in the past 7 days, with most citations coming from third-party sites rather than Outreach's own content. Key decisions included staging completed blog content immediately rather than batching, resolving a Craft CMS glitch affecting refresh publications, and planning external amplification of Outreach's AI agents through PR, G2, and Trust Radius.

## Context

Outreach is a GrowthX client engaged in a content marketing partnership focused on SEO, thought leadership, and AI visibility strategy. This is an internal operational sync among Outreach's marketing team (Hadassah Pegado, Maria Akhter, Andrew Arasaki, Paul Galvin) and GrowthX's delivery team (Aida Knezevic, Vivek Shankar, and Andi Bailey as head of customer ops). The meeting happened as part of Outreach's ongoing optimization of their analytics infrastructure and content strategy, with a specific focus on how their new AI agents—a recently launched product—are being positioned and cited in AI model outputs. GrowthX had previously built Looker dashboards for Outreach's organic traffic analysis, and this call extended that work to address more granular conversion tracking and user journey analysis needs. The content strategy discussion reflects a broader push to increase brand visibility in generative AI citations through amplification on review platforms and earned media.
