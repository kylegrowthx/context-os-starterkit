## Key Topics

### PSEO Pipeline Development

  - Two major components: pipeline setup and design work
  - Modular pipeline architecture with separate generators for each content block
  - Data sources: company websites, community platforms, review sites, historical archives, industry publications
  - Building system for quarterly (or faster) refreshes to keep pages current
  - Fact-checking integrated into each pipeline

### Design Collaboration

  - GrowthX can handle full design or collaborate based on Metronome's preferences
  - Need to determine division of labor for hub page, sub-pages, and templates
  - Chang to schedule call between GrowthX (including Ren) and Metronome's design team (Chris)
  - Deployment options: GrowthX pushes to Metronome's GitHub/CMS or establishes steady-state publishing process

### Metronome's Input Needed

  - List of target companies to start with
  - Catalog of pricing models Metronome tracks/cares about most
  - Internal data: analysis docs, sales team insights, customer feedback, competitive analysis
  - Rough categorization of internal data to appropriate pricing model "buckets"
  - GrowthX to create shared Notion space for Metronome to input this data

### Content Quality and Review Process

  - Initial test with 10 companies in \~2-3 weeks (production-quality pipeline targeted)
  - Focus on copy quality over design for first review
  - Iterative feedback to fine-tune the model and reduce revision cycles
  - GrowthX to conduct internal reviews before sharing with Metronome
