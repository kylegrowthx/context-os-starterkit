## Context

Overtime is a sports and lifestyle brand focused on creating content and consumer products, particularly basketball shorts and athletic apparel. They have a significant social media and league presence that drives awareness and e-commerce traffic. Since spring 2025, Overtime has experienced a decline in organic search traffic, which they believe is connected to the broader shift in consumer behavior toward AI-powered search (particularly LLM-based engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and others). The company has only recently begun optimizing for search and currently operates at a basic level (e.g., product naming on TikTok Shop). This introductory call was facilitated by Tiffany from Sapphire Capital and positioned as an opportunity for Overtime to learn about GrowthX's approach while providing feedback on whether GrowthX's services could extend beyond B2B SaaS into consumer goods and e-commerce.

## Key Topics

### AI Search Optimization

  - AI search is disrupting traditional SEO and content marketing strategies
  - GrowthX focuses on creating high-quality, audience-first content to appear in AI search results
  - AI search visits are 4.4x more likely to convert compared to traditional search (SEMrush data)
  - Overtime has seen organic traffic decline since spring, potentially due to this shift

### GrowthX's Approach

  - Two-phase partnership: 8-week strategy sprint followed by growth execution
  - Create artifacts like company context, audience personas, and voice/brand guidelines
  - Use sophisticated AI workflows with expert oversight for content creation
  - Offer end-to-end services from strategy to content creation and CMS publishing

### Overtime's Current Strategy

  - Limited optimization for search (e.g., basic product naming on TikTok Shop)
  - Main awareness drivers are leagues and social media platforms
  - No traditional brand awareness studies conducted

### Potential Application for Overtime

  - Explore creating website content (not necessarily a blog) to connect with search queries
  - Consider developing thought leadership content or expanding social media strategy
  - Investigate how to position Overtime's products against competitors like Nike, Adidas, etc.

### GrowthX's Workflow and Tools

  - Detailed research process including keyword analysis, SERP factors, and competitor content
  - AI-powered brief generation and fact-checking
  - Agentic researcher for executing and validating research questions
