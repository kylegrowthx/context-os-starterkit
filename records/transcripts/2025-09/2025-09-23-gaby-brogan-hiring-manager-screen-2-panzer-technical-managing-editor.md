## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Generation Platform

  - Internal tool called Atlas for building workflows and pipelines
  - Focuses on delivering high-quality content at scale for clients
  - Uses AI and LLMs but requires human oversight to ensure quality and accuracy
  - Collects data on what works, down to word choice level, to improve content performance

### Role of Technical Managing Editor

  - Manages content managers (CMs) who oversee client pipelines
  - Identifies process issues and articulates fixes or engages resources to improve
  - Balances client expectations with internal quality standards
  - Requires understanding of content, workflows, and AI tooling

### Client Management and Content Quality

  - Work with \~70 clients across various industries
  - Need to balance SEO, readability, and client-specific requirements
  - Example: Ensuring medical accuracy and appropriate tone for women's health content
  - Challenging hallucinations and inaccuracies from AI-generated content

### Process Improvement and Innovation

  - Opportunity to directly impact product development
  - New features often born from client needs, articulated by MEs
  - Goal to make the platform usable even for those with limited content experience
  - Continuous iteration and learning from editing and content improvements

### Long-term Vision

  - Become the best platform for end-to-end content generation and performance understanding
  - Eventually expose the platform to external users
  - Expand beyond content to architect other business processes (e.g., sales)
  - Create a dynamic, self-regulating rulebook for AI to improve content quality automatically
