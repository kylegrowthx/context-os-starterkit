## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

- "Comprehensive AI platform" not an accurate framing; focus on specific solution areas:
  1. Submission ingestion & triage
  2. Pricing & underwriting (HX's core strength)
  3. Portfolio optimization
- Underwriting workbench emerging as a known category (e.g., SEND, Federado)
- AI transformation a key high-level topic, but consider traffic potential carefully
- Pricing transformation less used in U.S. market

### Keyword Research Insights

- AI-related keywords likely underreported in tools due to rapid growth
- Many AI topics have low difficulty, presenting opportunities to rank well
- Competitor analysis:
  - Sierra: 11,000 keywords (broad focus beyond insurance)
  - Earnix: 1,300 keywords
  - Indico: 1,400 keywords
  - Federado: ~500 keywords
  - HX: ~100 keywords (significant growth potential)

### Content Prioritization

- Top of funnel: Market intelligence, AI transformation
- Middle/bottom funnel: Guides, comparison content, data submission/ingestion for P&C
- Avoid deep technical content (e.g., Excel tutorials) initially due to resource constraints
- Focus on AI-related content clusters first, then expand based on performance

### Target Personas

- Newer personas (e.g., Cycle) best suited for initial content focus
- Actuaries (core persona) potentially for deeper, second-phase content
