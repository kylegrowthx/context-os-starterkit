## Context

Third Bridge Creative is a premium content production agency that builds custom solutions for major media and tech clients including Spotify, Apple, YouTube, and Sonos. Their work spans content marketing, music curation, playlist creation, and editorial services — often requiring them to work within client CMSs and training remote teams on bespoke workflows. GrowthX is actively fundraising for Series B (targeting $40-60M) with a platform that automates content workflows through agentic research, AI editing with company context, fact-checking, and custom knowledge bases. This follow-up meeting explored whether GrowthX's technology could help Third Bridge Creative scale their high-margin, expert-driven services by introducing AI leverage into the creative process itself.
