## Key Topics

### Role Overview and Requirements

  - Content manager runs AI systems to generate content at scale (10-20 pieces/week)
  - Light editing before sharing with managing editor
  - Key skills: Strong editorial background, AI experience, prompt engineering
  - Company growing rapidly, high demand for this role

### Patricia's Background and Experience

  - 10+ years copywriting experience (Farfetch, e-commerce)
  - Recent focus on AI content generation and training
  - Freelance AI trainer for US companies
  - Personal projects using ChatGPT, Grammarly, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity
  - Developed content pipeline for scaling: ChatGPT \> Perplexity \> Grammarly \> Trello/Notion \> Word

### AI-Assisted Content Creation Process

  - Uses Google Trends for keyword research and topic selection
  - Detailed prompting in ChatGPT for initial draft
  - Iterative refinement with AI
  - Fact-checking with Perplexity
  - Grammar and tone adjustment with Grammarly
  - Final edits and formatting in document editor

### Quality Assurance and "Definition of Done"

  - Creates detailed checklists for content requirements
  - Focuses on proper structure, headings, meta info, hyperlinks
  - Balances perfectionism with deadlines
  - Sets maximum number of edits/reviews to avoid endless tweaking

### Team Structure and Collaboration

  - Content managers work on 1-2 accounts
  - Managing editors oversee 2-3 content managers
  - Account directors/strategists manage \~10 accounts, provide long-term strategy
  - 40-ish people on delivery team, 60-70 overall in company

### Client Interaction and Tools

  - Shared Slack channels with clients
  - Airtable for project management
  - Microsoft Word for comments and edits
  - Weekly client meetings led by managing editors (content managers typically don't attend)
