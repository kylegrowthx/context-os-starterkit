## Key Topics

### syftdata Product Overview

  - Integrates data from LinkedIn, website visits, and ad engagements
  - Enriches contacts with work email and company information
  - Allows filtering based on custom ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) criteria
  - Syncs data to HubSpot and other tools for streamlined operations
  - Provides a unified view of customer interactions across platforms

### LinkedIn Data Capabilities

  - Tracks post engagements, comments, and DMs
  - Offers connection request management
  - Ensures compliance with LinkedIn's terms to avoid account issues
  - Provides more comprehensive data compared to tools like Phantom Buster

### Pricing and Usage Model

  - Base plan: $500/month for 5,000 credits (activated contacts)
  - Credits consumed only when new, qualified contacts are exported
  - Unlimited team members can use the platform
  - Flexible scaling options for additional credits

### Integration with Existing Stack

  - Potential to replace multiple tools (e.g., Phantom Buster, partial HeyReach functionality)
  - Complements HubSpot by providing richer data and automation capabilities
  - Offers API integrations with various marketing and sales tools

### Future Product Direction

  - Aiming to be a GTM-focused CDP (Customer Data Platform) for B2B companies
  - Plans to expand data collection from other platforms (e.g., Substack, WhatsApp)
  - Focus on meeting customers where they are, beyond traditional inbound marketing
