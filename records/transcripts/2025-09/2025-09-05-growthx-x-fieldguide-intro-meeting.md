## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Approach and Services

  - End-to-end partner for SEO strategy and content creation
  - AI workflows combined with human expertise for daily publishing (10+ articles/week)
  - Strategy sprint to develop artifacts (personas, topics) followed by growth execution
  - Team includes managing director, managing editor, and content manager
  - Platform handles LLM monitoring and analytics (partnered with Scrunch)

### Content Creation Process

  - AI-driven workflows for research, writing, and fact-checking
  - Human intervention for quality control and brand voice alignment
  - Flexibility in content placement (blog, new website sections, microsites)
  - Focus on editorial content as foundation, with potential for expansion

### Client Concerns and GrowthX's Solutions

  - Accuracy and brand voice preservation, especially for B2B companies
  - Strategy sprint develops rich artifacts to capture company voice and persona details
  - Custom workflows can incorporate expert review steps (e.g., chief medical officer)
  - Initial close collaboration to calibrate content quality, transitioning to more autonomous production

### Pricing and Timeline

  - Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint): $20,000 fixed fee, 8 weeks, includes 20+ published articles
  - Phase 2 (Growth Execution): $15,000/month for 12 months
  - Option to opt-out before Phase 2 if not satisfied
  - Typical 4-week lead time for strategy sprint kickoff
