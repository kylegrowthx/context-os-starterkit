  - End-to-end partner for SEO strategy and content creation

  - AI-driven workflows for research, writing, and fact-checking
  - Human intervention for quality control and brand voice alignment
  - Flexibility in content placement (blog, new website sections, microsites)
  - Focus on editorial content as foundation, with potential for expansion

  - Accuracy and brand voice preservation, especially for B2B companies
  - Strategy sprint develops rich artifacts to capture company voice and persona details
  - Custom workflows can incorporate expert review steps (e.g., chief medical officer)
