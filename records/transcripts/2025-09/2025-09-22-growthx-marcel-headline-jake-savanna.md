## Context

This is an investor pitch from Marcel Santilli, founder and CEO of GrowthX (a B2B content marketing services company), to Jake Horwitz and Savanna Silver at Headline VC, a venture capital firm currently building their growth portfolio. Marcel quit his role as CMO 12 months ago to start GrowthX after decades of building content-driven growth programs at companies like IBM's Secret Intelligence, HP, HashiCorp, and ServiceTitan. He observed that the traditional content marketing workflow was broken—agencies were expensive, slow, and required assembling fragmented teams of writers, editors, SEO specialists, and designers—and built GrowthX as a tech-enabled services company to solve this problem with AI-driven workflows and forward-deployed expert teams.

Headline VC is interested in GrowthX as a potential investment opportunity, particularly because GrowthX already demonstrates strong product-market fit with profitable unit economics, a prestigious customer base (Bubble, Base10, Lovable, Augment Code), and word-of-mouth growth with zero outbound sales. This call serves as an initial deep dive to help Jake and Savanna understand GrowthX's technical approach, business model, and fundraising strategy before presenting to their investment committee.
