## Key Topics

### BaseTen's Product and Market Position

  - Specializes in high-performance, reliable inference for AI applications
  - Focus areas: model performance, reliability in partnership, and infrastructure expertise
  - Positioning inference as the backbone of the AI economy
  - Competitors: Together AI, Modal, Fireworks, and proprietary hardware solutions (e.g., Grock, Cerebras)
  - Differentiators: flexibility in model support, performance optimization, and cost reduction

### Target Customers and Use Cases

  - Venture-funded AI startups (seed to growth stage)
  - AI-native product companies (e.g., Fathom, Abridge, Mercore, Open Evidence)
  - Use cases: code generation, scribes, audio/music generation, chatbots, voice bots
  - Customer triggers: new model releases, margin concerns, product launches, performance optimization needs

### Content Strategy and Marketing Challenges

  - Current focus: technical, in-depth content performing well (e.g., GPU comparisons, LLM guides)
  - Need for earlier-stage awareness content to capture companies earlier in their journey
  - Bottleneck: limited capacity for producing complex, technical content
  - Goal: position BaseTen as the "fast company for inference" in the market

### Sales and Customer Acquisition

  - 70% of leads come from events and inbound channels
  - Field events and deep technical content are effective
  - Challenges in reaching early-stage startups (e.g., Y Combinator crowd)
  - Focus on high-leverage accounts for potential 7-8 figure contracts

### Performance Metrics and Goals

  - Improving AEO/SEO rankings for inference-related terms
  - Increasing engagement with top 100 target accounts
  - Measuring brand association with speed and performance in the inference space
