### Content Strategy Best Practices

  - Utilize Airtable for organizing content clusters, keywords, and assignments; Marcel walked through Bappy/DeepGram example showing how Bappy clusters and assignments copy nearly 1:1 to other AI tool clients
  - Implement strategy communication techniques: show live Airtable with clusters, sample assignments, and quantified opportunities in client meetings rather than sending raw docs; helps clients conceptually understand strategy
  - Follow HockeyCode recipe for high-intent content success: prioritize listicle/comparison pieces over broad top-of-funnel content; HockeyCode achieved 300+ signups and now gets more traffic from /guides than /blogs
  - Always include both refresh and net-new content in strategy: refresh opportunities easier to publish, but net-new content essential for long-term growth; communicate this trade-off clearly
  - For technical clients (especially VIP), implement EPD technical review as part of calibration approval process to catch depth/nuance issues before client escalation
  - Requirement to understand audience vs. product approach: review AI growth content strategy training materials (2-hour video) on audit methodology and topic mapping for comprehensive strategy understanding

### Operational Challenges

  - Need for improved technical review process, especially for VIP technical clients
  - ImageGem pipeline issues causing publishing delays for Outreach
  - Potential need for additional client ops engineer to handle increasing setup requirements
