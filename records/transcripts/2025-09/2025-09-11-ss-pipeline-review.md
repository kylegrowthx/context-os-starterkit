## Overview

- Need to improve client qualification process and expectation setting during sales to prevent misalignment and potential churn
- Revive and refine the ICP (Ideal Customer Profile) matrix to better qualify prospects and inform sales decisions
- Balance between maintaining growth and ensuring quality delivery; team agrees to prioritize process improvement over increasing weekly kickoffs

---

## Key Topics

### Client Qualification and Expectation Setting

  - Current process shows gaps in communicating client expectations from sales to delivery team
  - Need for a document outlining key information required before client contract signing
  - Suggestion to incorporate more qualifying questions from kickoff into sales conversations
  - Importance of understanding client's existing content, CMS, and resources to better prepare for delivery

### ICP Matrix and Sales Process

  - Existing ICP matrix needs refinement and consistent application in sales process
  - George and Tyler to collaborate on updating and implementing ICP criteria
  - Discussion on whether to disqualify companies based on specific criteria (e.g., traffic, funding stage)
  - Caution against blanket disqualification of early-stage companies, as some have been successful clients

### Pricing and Scope for Additional Services

  - Debate on whether to establish pricing for additional lanes beyond editorial in phase two upfront
  - Suggestion to test including additional services in initial discussions and pricing
  - Current disconnect between sales commitments and delivery team awareness for some clients (e.g., Lovable, Understory)
  - Need to improve documentation and communication of agreed work streams in HubSpot

### Capacity and Growth Considerations

  - Team leans towards maintaining current capacity (2 kickoffs/week) rather than increasing to 3
  - Focus on improving qualification and handoff processes before expanding capacity
  - Concerns about potential churn if team overextends without proper processes in place

### Examples and Case Studies

  - Need for more diverse examples beyond Augment Code to showcase capabilities to potential clients
  - Suggestion to find and highlight wins from other clients, especially for editorial-focused engagements
