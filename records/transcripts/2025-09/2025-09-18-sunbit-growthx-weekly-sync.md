## Key Topics

### Traffic Analysis and Content Performance

  - Early September metrics show positive trends:
      - Sessions: 482 → 530
      - Active users: 450 → 489
      - New users: 404 → 422
      - Shop directory views: 49 → 76
  - Top traffic drivers: Car and dental cost/tips articles (TOFU)
  - Shop directory views mainly from Sunbit-focused, bottom-of-funnel content

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Proposal to include more finance-focused articles alongside TOFU content
  - Challenges: Legal approval for finance articles may cause delays
  - Solution: Flag finance articles in Airtable for separate legal review
  - Blog updates: Separating consumer vs. merchant articles, adding "find somebody near you" side panel

### Merchant Engagement with Shop Directory

  - High merchant interest in directory listings (photos, hours, visibility)
  - Potential for UGC-style content loop, similar to Zapier's integration pages
  - Exploring ghostwriting opportunities for merchant-associated content

### SEO and Review Strategies

  - G2 Crowd investment for reviews, noting their increasing SEO impact
  - Emphasis on reviews, recency, and social proof for search engines

### Technical Updates

  - Automated publication testing ongoing; Nikola currently offline
  - Blog UI changes planned, leveraging WordPress categories without URL changes
