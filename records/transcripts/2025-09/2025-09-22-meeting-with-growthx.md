## Key Topics

### Ataccama's Current GEO Challenges

  - Experiencing a drop in SEO traffic due to changes in Google's passage retrieval (now using Gemini AI summary)
  - Competitors (Informatica, Collibra) dominating in LLM space
  - Ataccama not prominently featured in ChatGPT responses
  - Seeking agency support to measure and improve stack ranking against competitors in LLMs (GPT, Gemini, Copilot)

### GrowthX's Approach and Offerings

  - End-to-end agency partner for SEO, GEO strategy, and content creation
  - Focus on editorial content (e.g., blogs) to build domain authority
  - AI-driven content creation process with multiple models and human oversight
  - Fact-checking and deep research agents ensure accuracy and quality
  - Capability to create programmatic content at scale (e.g., thousands of pages for specific use cases)
  - Provides visibility and analytics for LLM performance

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Strategy sprint to understand audience, product, and company
  - Creation of personas and artifacts to inform content strategy
  - Robust brief generation considering keywords, traffic, SERP factors, and competitor content
  - Deep research and fact-checking processes to ensure accuracy
  - Ability to stage and publish content directly in client's CMS

### LLM Visibility and Analytics

  - GrowthX provides visibility and analytics for LLM performance
  - Currently partnered with Scrunch, but building analytics into their platform
  - Tracks performance in ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity (possibly Claude)
  - Clients can set up and track custom prompts

### Success Stories and ROI

  - Augment Code: 10x increase in organic traffic, improved AI Citations
  - Tiro: Quick ROI with improved domain authority and blog performance
  - Exec: 46x growth in LLM referral traffic (16 to 741 sessions)
  - AI search visits are 4.4 times more likely to convert than traditional search visits
