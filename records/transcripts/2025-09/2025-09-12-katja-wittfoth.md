## Key Topics

### Synthetic Data Generation Approach

  - Proposed using LLMs to generate diverse search queries for companies (e.g., Ramp) and competitors
  - Synthetic data will simulate real user searches and LLM responses, as actual data is inaccessible
  - This approach allows for large-scale data generation and controlled experimentation

### Study Objectives and Methodology

  - Primary goal: Identify tactics most effective for improving visibility in LLM search results
  - Plan to use statistical methods (e.g., linear regression) to analyze correlations between tactics and mentions
  - Will leverage LLMs for data labeling to assess qualitative factors like content quality and authoritativeness

### Parallels with SEO Research

  - Recognized similarities between proposed study and historical SEO optimization efforts
  - Discussed challenges in quantifying qualitative factors like "helpfulness" and "authoritativeness"
  - Agreed to research existing SEO studies for insights and to avoid duplicating work

### Content Quality Assessment

  - Identified need to develop a scoring system for content quality
  - Discussed potential features: uniqueness, expert opinions, new information, overall helpfulness

### Publication and Marketing Potential
