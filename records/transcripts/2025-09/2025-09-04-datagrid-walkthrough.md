## Key Topics

### Metadata Generation Process

  - Use dedicated ChatGPT space (search for "DG" in folders)
  - Copy article assignment name, ask AI for meta title, description, URL, and slug
  - Provide 10 previous examples from published articles for context
  - Fill Airtable with generated metadata (meta title, description, URL including slug)

### Article Creation in Atlas

  - Navigate to Blog and Integrations \> Article Generation Workflow
  - Fill in fields using data from Airtable (slug, topic, industry, etc.)
  - Industry field determines template to use (e.g., "AI agents for finance")
  - Use Claude to create content structure based on template and company context

### Content Generation and Editing

  - Allow AI to generate article draft (typically 1,000-1,500 words)
  - Quick review for template adherence and link appropriateness
  - No heavy editing or polishing required
  - Check and count internal links (aim for 6-7)
  - Select cover image from AI-generated options

### Final Steps and Documentation

  - Create Google Doc in appropriate Drive folder (e.g., "September 1st")
  - Copy article content and title into Google Doc
  - Add Google Doc link to Airtable in "Full Article Final" field
  - Ensure correct CTA link is added (copy from template)
