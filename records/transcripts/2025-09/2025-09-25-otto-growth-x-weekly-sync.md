## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Keyword Research

  - Dual approach: audience pain points/interests + competitor keyword gap analysis
  - 72 assignments grouped into topic clusters, balancing high-volume and high-intent keywords
  - Example: "virtual travel" (110 searches/mo) vs. "AI travel assistance" (30 searches/mo, high intent)
  - SEMrush used for competitor analysis, focusing on blog content to avoid product keywords

### Content Calendar and Workflow

  - Airtable setup with client views: Considering, Approved, In Production, Ready for Review, Published
  - Otto to review/approve topics via comments (can use emojis or provide additional context)
  - GrowthX updates statuses and manages content pipeline
  - Aim for 3-4 weeks of content planned ahead

### Blog Development and Best Practices

  - Otto developing test homepage, planning blog page and single post template
  - GrowthX shared examples (WebStacks, SEMrush) for effective blog layouts
  - Recommendations: featured articles, CTAs, simple layout, dark font on light background, sharing buttons
  - Include table of contents (left/right side) to improve user engagement and reduce bounce rate
  - Incorporate call-out sections, tables, and mid-content CTAs

### Performance Tracking

  - Looker dashboard in development, combining Google Search Console and GA4 data
  - 7-8 page report breaking down organic traffic, GrowthX blog performance, and LLM referral traffic
  - Complements Scrunch report for AI visibility insights
