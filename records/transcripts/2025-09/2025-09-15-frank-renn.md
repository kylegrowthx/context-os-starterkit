## Context

Frank Slangen is the primary designer at 35, a freelance design agency contracted to overhaul GrowthX's brand identity. This is the project kickoff meeting. GrowthX has been through multiple iterations of branding work with different agencies over the past couple of years—initial guidelines from an unnamed first agency, then work with Ray's agency, and most recently refinement work with No Boring Design. Despite this investment, the brand guidelines remain disjointed and incomplete. The core issue: GrowthX's brand doesn't visually reflect the company's actual differentiation, which is the combination of human expertise (client strategy, research, content) paired with AI-powered tools. Renato is framing this redesign as similar to the late-1970s/early-1980s shift to desktop publishing—a moment where new tools (AI) make previously manual work scalable, requiring fresh systems thinking.
