## Key Topics

### Project Context & History

  - Initial brand guidelines created by previous agency - logo, basic color palette
  - Subsequent work with Ray's agency produced updated logo and some concepts
  - Recent work with No Big Deal/No Boring Design yielded further logo refinement
  - Current guidelines need significant expansion and improvement

### Brand Direction

  - Moving away from software-centric branding
  - New focus: Showcasing combination of human expertise + AI tools
  - Drawing inspiration from late 70s/early 80s transition to desktop publishing
  - Aiming for modern interpretation, not overt retro aesthetic

### Current Assets & Needs

  - High-quality office/client footage and photography available
  - Need templates for case studies (slides + web)
  - Require illustrative style for infographics/statistics
  - Iconography needed to communicate platform concepts
  - Color system open to reimagining (current CMYK theme not mandatory)

### Deliverables & Scope

  - Expand current guidelines 1.5-2x in content
  - More applied examples for typography, web, etc.
  - Develop cohesive visual style across all elements
  - Create templates for various use cases (case studies, slides, web)

### Tools & Access

  - Frank to receive access to Notion, Linear, and Slack
  - Figma files shared; Frank has viewer access currently
