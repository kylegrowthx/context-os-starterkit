## Context

GetAccept is a Swedish B2B SaaS platform (based in Madrid, with offices in London) that provides a digital salesroom solution — a centralized platform where sales teams can manage the entire sales evaluation and document execution process. The company recently lost its content manager, creating an urgent need to re-evaluate their content marketing strategy. Calle Svan discovered GrowthX through Marcel's LinkedIn content and saw an opportunity to leverage AI-powered workflows to improve both content quality and commercial output, particularly given the shift toward AI-powered search (AEO) that GetAccept's buyers are increasingly using. Bastiaan Haghuis, who has prior experience with category-building companies, brought deep strategic perspective around thought leadership and awareness-building campaigns — areas where GetAccept feels they have significant upside, as they operate in an emerging "digital salesroom" category that many sales leaders aren't yet actively evaluating.
