## Summary

Tyler Pavlas introduced GrowthX's end-to-end content marketing and AI-powered strategy approach to Matt Danna at Boulevard, a $600M+ vertical software company serving salons, spas, and med spas. Boulevard is struggling with declining inbound/organic traffic and high cost per lead, and is looking to optimize SEO and AI visibility. ## Context

Boulevard is a ~10-year-old SaaS company providing booking and point-of-sale software exclusively for established brick-and-mortar salons, spas, and med spas (targeting businesses with 6-9 professionals and $1M+/year revenue per location). They have ~20% of the med spa market and ~6,000 locations across the US. Currently, Boulevard's content strategy is fragmented across multiple agencies: a content agency producing 1-2 blog posts/week, a Meta/Google ads team, and a Reddit organic specialist. While they have strong domain authority and traffic on their website, inbound/organic acquisition is underperforming and cost per lead/account is high — forcing them to rethink their current agency partnerships. Matt Danna is evaluating whether to consolidate content strategy with a partner who can help with SEO, AEO (AI-enhanced organic), and larger-scale content creation. This call was Tyler's first touchpoint after Boulevard found GrowthX through LinkedIn posts from Marcel (GrowthX's CEO).
