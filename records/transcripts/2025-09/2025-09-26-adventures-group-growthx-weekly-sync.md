## Summary

GrowthX and Adventures Group agreed to shift from weekly to biweekly meetings with async Slack updates in between, with flexibility to revert if needed. The team reviewed strong performance across destinations — Vallarta's "What to Pack" series achieved 7% click growth and is now comparable to top general articles, Cancun hit record impressions with 4% click growth, and Cabo's hurricane season article ranked as the third most-trafficked content. The team finalized strategy for Tierra Sagrada Beach Club, positioning it as a stylish, family-friendly recovery and relaxation spot with appeal to friend groups, and committed to refreshing existing content while developing new articles targeting non-branded keywords like "Cabo with Friends," "Bachelorette in Cabo," and "Cabo Girls Trip" to avoid cannibalizing tour pages.
