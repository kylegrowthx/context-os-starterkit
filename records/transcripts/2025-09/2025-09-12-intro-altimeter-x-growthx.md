## Key Topics

  - Founded in 2008, evolved from late-stage/public investing to include venture capital
  - $750M venture fund for Series A-C investments
  - Crossover fund for late-stage private and public investments
  - Small, tight-knit team of partners focusing on 1-2 high-conviction investments annually
  - Check sizes typically $10-50M, but flexible

### GrowthX Origin and Evolution

  - Marcel's background: IBM, HP, HashiCorp, Scale AI, DeepGram
  - Started with AI workflows for content creation at DeepGram
  - Pivoted after a successful workshop revealed demand for outcomes, not just workflows
  - Addressing pain points: expensive agencies, complex workflow platforms

### GrowthX Platform and Approach

  - Context engine: AI-ready product/feature descriptions
  - Knowledge base: Semantic graph for efficient information retrieval
  - Workflow engine: Code-based framework for reproducible processes
  - Human-in-the-loop expertise for quality control and continuous improvement

### Market Opportunity and Customer Traction

  - Shifting landscape: AI answers changing content distribution, making high-quality, scalable content crucial
  - Notable clients: Augment Code, RAMP, Webflow, Bolt

### Team and Financial Metrics

  - Capital efficient growth, considering fundraise due to inbound interest

### Fundraising Considerations

  - Exploring $30-60M raise, potentially last round
  - Seeking the right partner who understands their unique model
  - Capital would accelerate go-to-market efforts and engineering initiatives
