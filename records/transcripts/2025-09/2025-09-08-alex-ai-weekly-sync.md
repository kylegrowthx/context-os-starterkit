## Context

Alex AI is an AI recruiting platform that automates interview scheduling, candidate evaluation, and hiring workflows for staffing firms and recruitment teams. GrowthX is delivering content marketing services as part of a strategic engagement to build visibility and thought leadership in the recruiting technology space. This call was a weekly check-in between the GrowthX content team (Nathalie Schrans, Andi Bailey) and the Alex AI client (Donald Donckers) to review keyword research, refine topic clusters based on customer pain points, and establish production timelines and approval workflows. The relationship is early-stage enough that they're still defining voice and publishing processes—hence the calibration article approach before scaling production.
