## Summary

Marcel and the HubSpot Ventures team discussed concrete next steps for a Series B investment. ## Context

GrowthX is in Series B capital raising after a strong first full month of operations. The company closed 14 deals in September (including Lovable, CodeRabbit, Bubble, and BaseStand) and collected $1.2M, reaching cash-flow positive status despite burning $230–500K/month. Marcel has been almost entirely focused on partnerships and operations, stepping away from direct sales. HubSpot Ventures, led by Nate Morgan and including Adam Coccari and June Zhang, has done diligence on GrowthX and its customers. Karan (presumably Daniel's co-founder) had independently connected with Madrona investor Brian, which validated the investment thesis. Marcel sees HubSpot as the ideal strategic partner for the capital round and is open to a SAFE structure rather than a traditional Series B, though he's also fielded interest from other investors including fractional CMOs and prior angel backers.
