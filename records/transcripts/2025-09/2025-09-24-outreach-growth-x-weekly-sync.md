## Summary

GrowthX and Outreach confirmed an ongoing partnership to manage Outreach's content strategy, with focus on content clusters, SEO performance, and workflow optimization. The team discussed an unexplained drop in search impressions across multiple accounts (likely due to Google's parameter changes), rising CTR and average position metrics, and the challenge of integrating Airtable task data into Asana's editorial calendar—settling on a tagging workflow rather than a third-party automation tool. GrowthX committed to providing deeper LLM visibility reporting through Scrunch and Looker Dashboard by the following week.

## Context

Outreach is a major GrowthX engagement partner focused on sales engagement software. GrowthX was formally assigned as Outreach's official content team, replacing a handoff period where Ada (GrowthX) had been onboarding the account before taking PTO. The team (Jenn Peters on GrowthX's delivery side, Megan Dickey on strategy) now works directly with Hadassah Pegado and Cade McNelly on Outreach's side. The meeting is part of the weekly content sync cadence to align on strategy, performance metrics, editorial workflow, and LLM visibility—a key selling point of GrowthX's services for Outreach. Outreach is particularly interested in understanding how their content is performing in AI/LLM search results, an emerging channel that traditional SEO tools like SEMrush and Ahrefs don't capture well yet.
