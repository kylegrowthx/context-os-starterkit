## Summary

Marcel Santilli introduced GrowthX's AI-powered organic growth platform to Jeff Beckham (Head of Marketing at Gem, an ATS platform), demonstrating how GrowthX uses workflows, human editorial oversight, and AI models to produce daily content that achieves 10x visibility increases in AI-generated answers. Jeff shared that Gem has 200 blog posts generating only 1-3% of traffic and expressed skepticism about AI-generated content quality—but Marcel showed how human expertise prevents the low-quality content flooding the market. Both agreed to schedule a deeper dive meeting with Gem's extended team to explore partnership potential in the ATS/recruiting space, where competitors like Ashby and Greenhouse lack comparable content strategies.

## Context

He's looking to build a meaningful organic growth strategy but acknowledges his team lacks expertise in emerging AI-visibility tactics. ### GrowthX's Approach to Organic Growth

  - Utilizes AI-powered content creation with human expertise in the loop

  - Creates daily content across various formats (blogs, newsletters, catalogs)
  - Emphasizes visibility in AI-generated answers (e.g., ChatGPT) as a key strategy
  - Implements detailed workflows for research, writing, and fact-checking

  - Most traffic driven by brand searches rather than non-branded organic traffic

  - Jeff acknowledges the need for faster progress and expertise in new organic growth methods

  - Showed examples of detailed, informative content created for clients in various industries

  - Marcel noted that many competitors in Gem's space are not fully leveraging content strategies, presenting an opportunity for Gem
