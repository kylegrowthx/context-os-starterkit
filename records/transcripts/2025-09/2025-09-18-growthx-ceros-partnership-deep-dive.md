## Summary

GrowthX and Ceros explored a potential 8-week strategy sprint to accelerate Ceros's content strategy, with particular focus on the upcoming Ceros Flex product launch in late October. Tyler Pavlas and Marcel Santilli addressed deep questions about GrowthX's approach to content creation at scale, AI-driven workflows for content refreshing and optimization, and how to build content strategy that drives visibility in both traditional search and AI-powered answers. ## Context

Ceros is a visual content and marketing technology company facing a pivotal moment: their flagship product is undergoing a major refresh called Ceros Flex, launching in late October 2025. The launch represents a significant company initiative that requires comprehensive content support before, during, and after go-to-market. Internally, Ceros has Mary Mattingly leading content creation and web management, while Hani Durzy (external advisor from Red Dog Strategies) and the broader team (Sig Watkins, Lauren O Donnell, Brad Paterson, Alison Engel, Reyna Lopez) manage strategy and product go-to-market.

GrowthX was brought in to explore a partnership focused on accelerated content strategy and execution. Ceros's existing content—created by a traditional SEO agency until earlier this year—doesn't align with their visual brand identity and has generated minimal traffic. Their Contember CMS has performance issues affecting technical SEO. The core opportunity: build a holistic, brand-aligned content strategy that drives both traditional search visibility and AI-powered search visibility while supporting the Flex product launch and longer-term business growth.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Approach

  - Focus on turning companies into publishers at scale with high-quality, credible content
  - Emphasis on audience-first strategy, mapping topics to personas and business goals
  - Capability to create various content types, including interactive elements and programmatic content generation
  - Use of AI workflows for content creation, refreshing, and optimization

### Ceros' Content Challenges and Goals

  - Current blog content misaligned with ideal customer profiles (ICPs) and brand identity
  - Need for more thought leadership and visually appealing content
  - Desire to leverage Ceros' interactive capabilities in content strategy
  - Upcoming Ceros Flex product launch requiring comprehensive content support

### Technical SEO and CMS Considerations

  - Ceros using Contember CMS, facing performance issues affecting SEO
  - GrowthX can assist with technical SEO improvements, including sitemap updates and page optimizations
  - Potential move to Contentful CMS in the future to address performance concerns

### AI Search Optimization (AEO)

  - GrowthX focuses on content attributes beneficial for both humans and AI systems
  - Key factors: well-researched, comprehensive, up-to-date content with authority signals
  - Differences observed between ChatGPT (quicker indexing) and Perplexity (slower updates)

### GrowthX's Workflow Demo

  - Showcased content refreshing workflow using AI-powered analysis and generation
  - Ability to add cross-linking, update research, and generate FAQs automatically
  - Customizable workflow adaptable to client needs and brand guidelines
