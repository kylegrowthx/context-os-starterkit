## Context

Lisa Aguilar is a technical marketing leader at DataRobot, an AI/ML platform company. She reached out through mutual connection Jai to explore whether GrowthX could support DataRobot's next stage of growth, specifically around content creation. Her core challenge is that DataRobot operates at the cutting edge of AI — they're defining concepts like "agent AI lifecycle" and "Pareto-optimized workflows of LLMs" that don't yet exist in standardized form in the market. She's currently working with another vendor on organic and LLM strategy, but is evaluating whether GrowthX's sophisticated AI workflows plus expert oversight could accelerate output while maintaining technical accuracy and authority. Tyler Pavlas, GrowthX's founding account executive, led the call because Marcel Santilli (GrowthX founder and CEO) had a conflicting Series B investor demo.
