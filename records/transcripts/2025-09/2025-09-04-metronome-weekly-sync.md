## Summary

GrowthX and Metronome aligned on the scope and execution of an AI Pricing Index project — a comprehensive directory of AI company pricing models designed to position Metronome as an authoritative resource and drive organic traffic through search. The team decided to start with 20 companies representing diverse pricing model types as a refinement batch, then expand to 50+ and eventually 100+ companies, with quarterly updates focused on structural changes rather than minor price adjustments. Metronome will provide seed content on their perspectives about different pricing model types (credits, per-unit pricing, enterprise models, etc.) for GrowthX to integrate into individual company pages.

## Context

Metronome is a billing and pricing platform that helps companies manage complex pricing models. This was a weekly sync between GrowthX (the external marketing agency driving this initiative) and Metronome's product and strategy team to define the scope and execution strategy for a major content project: building a comprehensive public index of AI company pricing models. Matthew Panzarino (GrowthX) came prepared with a detailed scope document outlining page structure, data sources, update cadence, and resource requirements. The goal is to create a link magnet and organic traffic driver that could also funnel interested companies back to Metronome's services. This is a high-priority project that requires close collaboration on resource allocation, editorial strategy, and design/development resourcing.
