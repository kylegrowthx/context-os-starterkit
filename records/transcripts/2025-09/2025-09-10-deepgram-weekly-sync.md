## Summary

GrowthX and Deepgram aligned on a content strategy refresh focused on quality over volume. The team presented ContentOS (an Airtable-based content management system) with 50+ potential blog topics across four clusters: Voice AI/API evaluation, industry-specific (medical), DevRel "how to build" content, and enterprise focus (QSR and pharmacy). A critical discovery: much of Deepgram's existing top-of-funnel content is irrelevant (e.g., idiom explanations with high traffic but no connection to voice AI). Next step: GrowthX will conduct a deeper audit to reassess what constitutes quality top/middle/bottom funnel content, with a focus on the four target clusters before moving to SEM strategy and programmatic SEO.

---

## Context

Deepgram is a speech-to-text API platform. GrowthX is delivering content marketing and SEO services to drive organic traffic and console signups. This meeting is part of an ongoing engagement where GrowthX is building a strategic content roadmap. The team previously refined personas and company context based on feedback, completed initial keyword research, and identified four main content clusters. In this meeting, they reviewed the ContentOS Airtable system and discovered a significant issue: Deepgram's existing blog content includes articles with high traffic that are not relevant to the business (e.g., explanations of idioms and general AI glossary terms), likely from an earlier phase when content strategy was not formalized. This finding prompted a larger conversation about defining what "quality" top/middle/bottom funnel content means for Deepgram's specific goals: driving traffic and signups.
