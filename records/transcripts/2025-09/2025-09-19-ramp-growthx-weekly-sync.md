## Context

RAMP is a financial operations platform that GrowthX is providing B2B content marketing services for, including SEO optimization across multiple topic clusters (AI, DevOps, expense management, treasury, vendors). The strategic new initiative emerged from RAMP's goal to expand their content reach through YouTube—they're producing short-form video content for their CashFlow Desk channel and now want a dedicated blog publication to house these videos alongside text content, optimizing for faster indexing and reaching different audience segments through dual content formats.
