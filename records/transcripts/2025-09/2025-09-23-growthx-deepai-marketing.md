### Potential Partnership Opportunities

  - GrowthX interested in two channels: developer-targeted advertising via contextual/in-chat ads, and SEO/authority plays through glossary collaboration
  - DeepAI willing to build custom ad solutions if budget justifies the engineering work (e.g., targeted banners by user segment or page type)

  - GrowthX budgeting ~$20-30K/month for experimental backlinks and partnership spend; client itself spends ~$30K/day on advertising (indicating significant marketing budget scale)

### Ad Integration Options

  - Complex integration: Become a bidder, write code to place bids on specific messages

  - Custom solutions possible for significant budgets (e.g., targeted banners for specific user types/pages)

### SEO and Content Collaboration

  - DeepAI's glossary/docs originally built for SEO, now accept paid links
  - Potential to update/improve glossary content quality
  - GrowthX to evaluate traffic (via SEMrush) and propose collaboration terms
