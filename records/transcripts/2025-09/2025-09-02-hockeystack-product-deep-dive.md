## Overview

- HockeyStack offers two main products: Marketing Intelligence (attribution, reporting, AI analyst) and Account Intelligence (sales enablement, scoring)
- Core value prop: Unified data ingestion across marketing/sales touchpoints to map full buyer journey and extract actionable insights
- Key differentiators: Anonymous user tracking, granular data analysis, AI-powered insights (Odin for marketing, Nova for sales)
- Primary ICP is marketing teams seeking attribution and data-driven decision support

---

## Key Topics

### Platform Overview & Data Ingestion

  - Connects to CRM, marketing automation, paid channels, website (pixel)
  - Tracks anonymous + known user activity across entire buyer journey
  - Uses fingerprinting (not cookies) for anonymous user tracking
  - Creates AI-powered account context as foundation for both products

### Marketing Intelligence Product

  - Attribution modeling (multi-touch, predictive)
  - Custom reporting with flexible data slicing
  - Odin AI analyst for natural language queries
  - Incrementality/lift reporting to measure true campaign impact

### Account Intelligence Product

  - Sales-focused view with account scoring, signals, and engagement data
  - AI-generated account plans and personalized outreach suggestions
  - Workflow automation for scaling signal-based actions
  - Stakeholder mapping and enrichment capabilities

### Implementation & Adoption

  - 3-4 days for initial attribution dashboard
  - Fully operational within 5 weeks
  - Dedicated CSM support for setup and custom dashboard creation
  - Products can be purchased separately or bundled
