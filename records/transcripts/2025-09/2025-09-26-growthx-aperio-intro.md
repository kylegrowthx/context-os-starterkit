Aperio is a Series A-stage startup solving industrial data quality at scale using unsupervised machine learning and AI/LLM-based scoring and remediation. The company works with major industrial operations teams (Chevron, Procter & Gamble, Devon Energy) to clean millions of sensor data streams before they're fed to AI and analytics models. With 30 employees and 30 early customer logos (mix of paying and non-paying), Aperio is navigating a critical go-to-market transition: shifting from selling IT features to IT teams (quick deals, low revenue) to selling operational value to ops leaders like process control engineers and maintenance managers (higher revenue, strategic impact). Jonas Hellgren is the CEO. The call was introduced by Yusuf (likely a mutual connection). GrowthX proposed editorial content marketing as a path to establish Aperio as a thought leader in the data quality space, citing strong LLM/AI search metrics (4.4x conversion lift, 3x longer time on site vs. traditional search). Aperio recognized the strategic value but deferred engagement pending resolution of foundational go-to-market and messaging challenges.

  - Addresses data quality issues in OT (Operational Technology) data using ML/AI
  - Target clients: Industrial companies (e.g., Chevron, P\&G, Devon Energy)
  - Current focus: Transitioning from IT feature sales to value-based ops team sales
  - Challenge: Identifying urgent use cases and refining go-to-market strategy

### Content Marketing Strategy Considerations

  - AI search visits convert 4.4x more, spend 3x longer on site
  - Aperio needs to establish thought leadership in data quality space
  - Current lack of subject matter expert-created content
  - Potential to differentiate from competitors through educational content

  - Phase 1: Rich persona/company/voice artifacts development with deep research and expert interviews

  - AI-powered research and fact-checking workflows with confidence scoring and rewrite suggestions
  - Human editors in the loop for context-aware refinement and fact validation
  - Custom knowledge base and agentic analytics workflows (currently in beta, rolling out to customers in next 2 months)
  - Able to integrate client team expertise and quotes into content for authenticity and accuracy
