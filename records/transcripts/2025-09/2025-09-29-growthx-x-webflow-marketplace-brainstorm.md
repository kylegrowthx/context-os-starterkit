## Overview

- GrowthX proposes creating an enriched, SEO-friendly marketplace with easily remixable templates
- Webflow's CodeGen product (launching in October) presents opportunities for AI-generated starter kits
- Current challenges: no API access, inability to clone/duplicate sandboxes, and differing tech stacks between Webflow and CodeGen
- Next steps involve deep-dive sessions with Webflow PMs to align on possibilities and constraints

---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Marketplace Vision

  - Inspired by Mobin, Dribbble, and Notion templates
  - Focus on easy navigation, remixing, and SEO-friendly descriptions
  - Aim to create a curated experience with high-quality starter kits
  - Potential to incorporate user-generated content later, following Notion's model

### Webflow's Current Situation and Goals

  - Marketplace is "a mess" with mixed Webflow and coded elements
  - Launching private beta of CodeGen product in October
  - Targeting freelancers and agencies, focusing on practical integrations
  - Aiming for a showcase of remixable/clonable templates contributed by Webflow creators

### Technical Challenges and Considerations

  - Webflow templates use internal language, not easily translatable to HTML/CSS
  - CodeGen works better with Tailwind, creating a disconnect with existing Webflow templates
  - No current API access for CodeGen, limiting automation possibilities
  - Need to respect community promises regarding template usage

### Proposed Strategy and Next Steps

  - GrowthX suggests an 8-week strategy sprint to dive deep into possibilities
  - Need for product deep-dive on CodeGen to understand capabilities and limitations
  - Explore potential to translate Webflow's internal language to a format usable by GrowthX's systems
  - Consider phased approach: start with static websites, then translate to Webflow-ready templates

### Market Positioning and Competition

  - Lovable's upcoming features (easy database and AI service connections) seen as potential threat to Webflow
  - Opportunity for Webflow to get ahead by integrating similar features
