## Context

This is an ongoing weekly sync between GrowthX (the content marketing and AI visibility services provider) and Strapi (an open-source headless CMS platform). The relationship began with GrowthX providing content marketing and SEO services to boost Strapi's visibility and drive traffic to their website. GrowthX created dedicated integration pages and content hubs (particularly around headless CMS and integration topics) that have become Strapi's highest-performing content assets by traffic and engagement. The relationship has evolved to include workflow automation projects—GrowthX is exploring how to automate the publication of Strapi content to Medium, which requires solving technical challenges around markdown conversion and image generation.
