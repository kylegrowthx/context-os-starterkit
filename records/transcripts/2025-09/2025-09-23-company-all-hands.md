## Context

This is a monthly all-hands meeting for GrowthX, a B2B content marketing and AI visibility company. The meeting serves as a company-wide update covering people (onboarding and promotions), business development (new customer wins and pipeline), and product development across both the content services business and the CheckThat.ai strategic product initiative. The company is experiencing significant momentum—having just signed 14 deals in September and being booked out nearly two months in advance—while undergoing internal restructuring to scale delivery through a new agentic workflow system. The engineering organization has been split into three dedicated lanes: Client Ops (led by Stevie), AI-Info (working on framework improvements), and Atlas/CheckThat (building the product layer), reflecting the company's dual focus on delivering world-class content services while building CheckThat.ai as a standalone B2B SaaS platform for AI visibility tracking.

**To CheckThat:**

- Target launch: end of October. Will be integrated into Atlas eventually but starting as standalone product. Tier-one sources prioritized (official documentation); tier-four sources (blogs, medium posts) deprioritized to maintain quality.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
