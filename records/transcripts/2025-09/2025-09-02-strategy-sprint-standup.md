## Summary

## Context

The Strategy Sprint is GrowthX's core delivery pod, led by Andi Bailey. This standup occurs during a particularly active period: two simultaneous client kickoffs (Relay and HyperExponential) are launching this week, HockeyStack and DeepGram onboarding is imminent, and the team is managing eight concurrent client accounts in various content production stages (Ambient, Clerk, Tavern, Understory, Abnormal Security, Udemy, Okta, Diligent). The AI visibility tool, being built in parallel, will become a critical competitive differentiator.
