## Summary

Sunbit and GrowthX reviewed strong performance on shop directory traffic (up from 137 to 252 organic views Jan-Aug) and discussed upcoming publishing automation (ETA Sep 8) that will significantly improve deployment efficiency. The teams also identified a drop in content quality over recent months—formatting inconsistencies, brand guide violations, and lack of human editorial review—and agreed GrowthX would investigate root causes (artifacts, pipelines) before automation goes live. Finally, Sunbit shared plans for a social content strategy overhaul, moving from generic posts to more engaging, humorous content developed with an outside agency, while maintaining their focus on top-of-funnel B2C content to drive shop directory conversions.

---

## Context

Sunbit is a fintech platform providing buy-now-pay-later (BNPL) services across multiple verticals including auto, dental, and home services. GrowthX has been delivering SEO and content marketing services to drive organic traffic to Sunbit's Knowledge Center, which serves both B2B merchant partners and B2C consumers. This weekly sync is part of an ongoing engagement where Kyle Gaudreau and Ehtisham Hussain report on content performance, content production quality, and upcoming product changes. Sunbit is planning a major rebrand (new logo, visual identity, increased AI focus) rolling out mid-2026, and is exploring ways to improve social media engagement and strategy alongside the content work.

## Key Topics

### Content Performance Metrics

  - Shop directory views from organic traffic on Knowledge Center articles:
      - Consistent month-over-month increase (137 in Jan to 252 in Aug)
      - Correlated with overall site traffic uptick since May
  - Merchant partner signups:
      - Lower conversion rates (9 in Jan, 1 in Aug)
      - Primarily from middle/bottom funnel content
  - B2C focus:
      - \~95% of website visitors are consumers
      - Shop directory conversions show clear ROI (\~$2M GMV, \~$200k profit last year)

### Content Quality Concerns

  - Issues identified in recent months:
      - Formatting inconsistencies (e.g., header tags, capitalization)
      - Brand guide violations (e.g., use of exclamation points)
      - Occasional content structure problems (e.g., bulleted lists instead of paragraphs)
      - Discrepancies in list articles (e.g., "10 reasons" with only 7 listed)
  - GrowthX to investigate causes and refine processes

### Automation Progress

  - Publishing automation in final stages
  - Expected completion date: September 8th
  - Anticipated to significantly improve content deployment efficiency

### Social Content Strategy

  - Current social posts deemed "generic and boring"
  - Sunbit planning follower growth campaign with more engaging, humorous content
  - Exploring unique voice and imagery with help from external agency
  - Aim: evolve from informative to entertaining while maintaining value
  - Challenge: Balancing diverse audience needs (B2B vs B2C, various verticals)

### Sunbit Rebrand

  - Planned rollout by mid-next year
  - Retaining company name but updating visual identity
  - Increased focus on AI aspects of the business
  - Challenges include resource allocation and budget approval
