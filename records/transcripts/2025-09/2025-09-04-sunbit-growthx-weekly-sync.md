## Key Topics

### Content Performance Metrics

  - Shop directory views from organic traffic on Knowledge Center articles:
      - Consistent month-over-month increase (137 in Jan to 252 in Aug)
      - Correlated with overall site traffic uptick since May
  - Merchant partner signups:
      - Lower conversion rates (9 in Jan, 1 in Aug)
      - Primarily from middle/bottom funnel content
  - B2C focus:
      - \~95% of website visitors are consumers
      - Shop directory conversions show clear ROI (\~$2M GMV, \~$200k profit last year)

### Content Quality Concerns

  - Issues identified in recent months:
      - Formatting inconsistencies (e.g., header tags, capitalization)
      - Brand guide violations (e.g., use of exclamation points)
      - Occasional content structure problems (e.g., bulleted lists instead of paragraphs)
      - Discrepancies in list articles (e.g., "10 reasons" with only 7 listed)
  - GrowthX to investigate causes and refine processes

### Automation Progress

  - Publishing automation in final stages
  - Expected completion date: September 8th
  - Anticipated to significantly improve content deployment efficiency

### Social Content Strategy

  - Current social posts deemed "generic and boring"
  - Sunbit planning follower growth campaign with more engaging, humorous content
  - Exploring unique voice and imagery with help from external agency
  - Aim: evolve from informative to entertaining while maintaining value
  - Challenge: Balancing diverse audience needs (B2B vs B2C, various verticals)

### Sunbit Rebrand

  - Planned rollout by mid-next year
  - Retaining company name but updating visual identity
  - Increased focus on AI aspects of the business
  - Challenges include resource allocation and budget approval
