## Overview

- Relay offers up to 20 checking accounts and 2 savings accounts with no minimum balance requirements
- Platform focuses on cash flow clarity, backed by the Profit First method
- Extensive card controls allow granular spending limits, vendor restrictions, and approval workflows
- Integrated invoicing, bill pay, and expense management features streamline financial operations
- QuickBooks, Xero, and other key integrations available for seamless accounting workflows

---

## Key Topics

### Platform Overview and Account Structure

  - Dashboard shows total and available balances, pending/posted transactions
  - Up to 20 checking accounts, 2 savings accounts with no minimum balance
  - Easy fund transfers between accounts, external account linking via Plaid
  - Auto-transfer rules support Profit First method for allocating income percentages

### Card Management and Controls

  - Physical and virtual cards with customizable designs
  - Granular spending controls: limits, vendor restrictions, category blocks
  - Backup account feature to prevent declines by sweeping funds
  - Pre-approval workflows and international usage controls

### Payments and Invoicing

  - ACH, wire, and check payment options with tiered transaction fees
  - Built-in invoicing tool with customizable templates and online payment acceptance
  - Bill management features for tracking due dates and approval workflows
  - Third-party check writing support with integrated dashboard

### Expense Management and Integrations

  - Receipt capture and categorization tools
  - Direct integrations with QuickBooks, Xero, Gusto, and other platforms
  - Auto-syncing of chart of accounts, vendors, and expense categories

### Team Management

  - Customizable permission levels for employees and external advisors
  - Roles include read-only, cardholder, manager, administrator, and super administrator
