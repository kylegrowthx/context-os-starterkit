## Key Topics

### Current Webinar Structure

  - October 10th webinar currently planned for 90 minutes
  - \~200 registrants, primarily practitioners (website builders, content creators)
  - Considering adjusting to 60 minutes of pure thought leadership
  - Will serve as lead generation for subsequent, more in-depth events

### Practitioner-Focused Workshop

  - Planned for late October, coinciding with Webflow's product GA and State of the Web report
  - 90-minute hands-on workshop for practitioners
  - Content to include:
      - Detailed AEO strategies
      - AI tools demonstration (Claude, ChatGPT)
      - Webflow-specific activation techniques
  - Rachel suggested open-sourcing Webflow's internal work and potentially featuring Vivian (from Graphite)

### CMO/Leadership Summit

  - Proposed as an in-person event in San Francisco, potentially first week of November
  - Invite-only, application-based attendance
  - Focus on high-level strategy, future of web experiences, and ROI of AEO investments
  - Potential to feature case studies from respected brands and CMOs
  - Aim to provide clear insights on costs, growth potential, and risks/benefits of AEO adoption

### Content and Themes

  - Emphasis on the future of web experiences
  - Interactive, AI-enabled content becoming increasingly important
  - No trade-off between content quality and scale in the AI era
  - Importance of creating a "knowledge graph" for audience understanding
  - Blurring lines between websites and products (e.g., Canva)

### Event Logistics and Support

  - GrowthX offered significant support, including potential venue and speaker connections
  - Webflow to check on internal event support capabilities
  - Aim to use events to generate valuable content and B-roll for future marketing efforts
