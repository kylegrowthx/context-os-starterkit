## Summary

GrowthX and Webflow aligned on an ambitious three-event series launching in October-November to establish thought leadership around AI and the future of web experiences. The strategy kicks off with a high-level webinar on October 10th, followed by a practitioner-focused hands-on workshop in late October timed to Webflow's State of the Web report and AI SEO GA, and culminates in an invite-only CMO summit in San Francisco in early November featuring case studies and industry leaders. Marcel emphasized the market need: customers are paralyzed by website redesigns and AI chaos, wanting validation on whether to invest in AEO and how to approach it.

---

## Context

Webflow is a leading web design and development platform with ~200k users across practitioners, agencies, and enterprises. GrowthX is a B2B content marketing services firm specializing in AI-driven SEO and content strategy (AEO). The two companies have been collaborating on AEO initiatives, with GrowthX helping Webflow develop frameworks, content, and thought leadership while Webflow provides platform-specific insights and customer access.

This meeting happened after Rachel Wolan and Marcel had an initial conversation about expanding the scope of what was originally a narrow "Own the AI Front Page" webinar into a larger collaborative series. Meg Murray joined as Webflow's event lead to assess internal support capacity. The backdrop is urgent: Webflow is launching its "State of the Web" industry survey and AI-powered SEO tool in late October, creating a natural anchor point for content and campaigns. Both teams see an opportunity to position Webflow and GrowthX as thought leaders navigating the seismic shift in how websites will need to be built and optimized in an AI-native world.
