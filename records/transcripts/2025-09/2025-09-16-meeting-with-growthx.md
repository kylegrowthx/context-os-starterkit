  - Pricing: $12-15K/month for editorial, additional $9-15K/month for custom work

### Pylon's Content Strategy

  - Recently engaged a freelancer for initial "war chest" of content (2 pillar pieces, derivatives)
  - Seeking long-term strategy post-freelancer engagement
  - Content is a critical pillar in Pylon's broader marketing strategy
  - Raised Series A; budget for content partnership not a concern

### GrowthX's Approach and Deliverables

  - Create, stage, and publish blog content in client's CMS
  - Develop SEO and GEO strategy
  - Generate specialized, technical content as needed
  - Focus on building domain authority and audience-specific content
  - Programmatic SEO examples: city-specific pages (Ramp), vendor pages based on spend data

### Measuring Content Success

  - KPIs: keywords, organic traffic, eventual MQL conversion
  - GrowthX uses Google Search Console, Looker dashboard
  - Metrics: articles published, impressions, clicks, rankings, AI search visibility
  - Track engagement and conversion (signups, sales conversations)

### Potential Partnership Considerations

  - Long-term view on content strategy (potentially years)
  - GrowthX could replace need for in-house content person
  - Pylon needs to evaluate if partial content services meet their needs
  - Decision between full-stack outsourced solution vs. partial service
