## Summary

GrowthX held an all-hands meeting featuring introductions of eight new global hires and updates on major business wins, including the signing of Lovable (fastest company to reach $100M in history) and CodeRabbit. ## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing services company ($200k+/year engagements) with a strategic software bet in CheckThat (an AI visibility index for B2B SaaS). The company operates with a dual-revenue model combining high-touch managed services (content creation and optimization) with emerging software/platform offerings. This all-hands meeting brought together a geographically distributed team spanning the US, Mexico, Brazil, Serbia, and Colombia.

The meeting comes at a significant inflection point: strong sales momentum (multiple marquee clients signed), successful implementation of AI-powered workflows that are dramatically improving delivery efficiency, and progress toward Series B funding. The leadership team (Marcel, Daniel, Bridget McGillivray, and Andi Bailey among others) used this venue to align the team on customer wins, product progress on the Atlas platform and CheckThat, and the company's investment readiness.
