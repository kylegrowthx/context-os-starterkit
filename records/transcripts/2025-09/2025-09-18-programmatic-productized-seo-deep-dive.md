### Current Lovable SEO Issues

  - Core pages not being indexed due to 5xx server errors
  - Low-quality content from previous agency work
  - Shallow website with limited depth and internal linking
  - Poor blog quality needing complete overhaul

### Proposed SEO Strategy

  - Start with competitor comparison pages as quick win
  - Develop comprehensive template library categorized by function, industry, etc.
  - Improve existing how-to content or create from scratch if quality is too low

### Template Library Concept

  - Combine elements of Dribbble and Mobbin for inspiration

  - Aim to capture traffic currently going to template marketplaces

### Content Analysis and Generation

  - Use AI tools (e.g. Exa AI) for deep research and content creation
  - Analyze user sessions and prompts to understand intent and categorize content
  - Generate detailed, engineer-vetted prompts for high-quality app templates
