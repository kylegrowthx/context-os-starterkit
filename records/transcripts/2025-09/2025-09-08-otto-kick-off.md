## Overview

- Otto is preparing for GA launch in mid-November, transitioning from limited beta to broader availability
- Target audience: frequent business travelers (6+ trips/year) managing own travel, especially at SMBs without corporate travel management
- Content strategy aims to build authority in search/AI, educate on Otto's unique AI assistant approach

---

## Key Topics

### Otto Product Overview

  - AI-powered executive assistant for booking/managing travel
  - Learns user preferences through calendar integration, past bookings
  - Focuses on business travelers, not leisure
  - Handles common pain points (flight changes, upgrades, personalized recommendations)

### Target Audience & Positioning

  - "Road warriors": 6+ business trips per year
  - Primary segments:
    1.  SMB employees without corporate travel management
    2.  Dissatisfied users of existing corporate travel tools
  - Positioning: "Executive assistant for every traveler"
  - Not targeting: Leisure travelers, companies with strict travel policies

### Current Marketing & Growth

  - Limited beta with hundreds of users
  - Main acquisition: Instagram video ads (\~$10-15 cost per lead)
  - Artificially constrained growth, pending fraud validation
  - Basic website with beta signup, expanding content strategy

### Content Strategy Goals

  - Build search/AI authority on business travel topics
  - Educate on Otto's unique AI assistant approach
  - Address common travel pain points, questions
  - Highlight benefits vs. leisure travel sites, outdated corporate tools

### Technical & Security Considerations

  - Uses VGS for secure payment/personal data storage
  - Multiple authentication options (Google, Apple, Microsoft, email)
  - No reported user concerns about security/privacy

### GrowthX Engagement

  - 8-week strategy sprint to accelerate content development
  - Will provide access to analytics, CMS, brand guidelines
  - Flexible on content structure, page hierarchy based on Otto's needs
  - Primary focus on written content, open to exploring other formats
