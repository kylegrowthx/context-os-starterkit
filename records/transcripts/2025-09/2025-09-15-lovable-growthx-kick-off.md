## Overview

- Lovable's growth is primarily driven by word-of-mouth and branded search; non-branded search presents a significant opportunity
- Current user base is horizontal (from students to VPs) with key segments including tech professionals and solopreneurs
- Main challenges: user education, retention beyond initial prompts, and scaling quality content production
- Goal: Position Lovable as the leader in the "vibe coding" category, emphasizing brand, capabilities, and ecosystem integrations

---

## Key Topics

### Current Growth Channels and Challenges

  - Word-of-mouth and branded search are primary drivers
  - Paid performance not as effective due to mobile tracking issues and scale limitations
  - YouTube, influencer marketing, and paid search showing promise
  - Need to improve non-branded search performance

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Horizontal audience: from students to VPs in tech companies
  - Key segments:
    1.  Individuals at tech companies (product, marketing, sales)
    2.  Solopreneurs building businesses or initiatives
  - Best-performing use cases: internal tools, SaaS, and websites
  - Challenge: Identifying high-value users among broad audience

### User Behavior and Retention

  - 80-90% of users drop off after 5-10 prompts
  - "Rage quitting" due to getting stuck is a major issue
  - Some users supplement Lovable with more technical tools (e.g., Codex, Cursor) to overcome limitations

### Competitive Positioning

  - Aim to be perceived as the leader in the "vibe coding" category
  - Key differentiators: brand appeal, "it just works" functionality, and upcoming integrations (Shopify, Notion, HubSpot, Atlassian)
  - Need to address perception of being "just a prototyping tool" vs. capable of building full-stack apps

### Content Strategy

  - Video content has performed well, with some reaching millions of views
  - Blog needs significant improvement in usability and publishing process
  - Opportunity to create a network of content addressing various user intents and queries

### SEO and Growth Objectives

  - Focus on non-branded search terms and comparison pages
  - Target meaningful impact on signups (currently 250,000-300,000 per week)
  - Establish clear metrics for success in visits, signups, and ranking for key terms
