## Context

Unblock is a developer platform that aggregates tribal knowledge from Slack, Jira, Confluence, and source code, giving developers on-demand access to high-quality answers. The company, led by CEO Dennis Pilarinos, recently discussed its growth challenges with Radical Ventures board members, which led to the GrowthX introduction. Despite strong product metrics (high NPS, strong POC-to-customer conversion), Unblock faces a recognition problem in the noisy developer tools space and needs to build awareness through top-of-funnel content. Dennis has been the primary content creator, but the workload is unsustainable—he's expecting his first child within weeks and needs to delegate. This conversation was an exploratory sales call with GrowthX to evaluate whether an end-to-end content partnership could help Unblock establish thought leadership and drive organic traffic through GEO/SEO content focused on technical topics relevant to their audience.
