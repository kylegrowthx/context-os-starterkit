## Key Topics

### Company Background & Traction

  - Founded by Marcel Santilli, former marketing executive at IBM, HP, HashiCorp, Scale AI
  - Built content hubs/engines that drove significant revenue/traffic at previous companies
  - Started as workshops, evolved into service + platform model
  - Current metrics:
      - $11M ARR
      - 70% gross margins
      - 70+ employees (40 in delivery, rest in engineering/product)
      - 50+ customers including Ramp, Webflow, Surge AI
      - $9k-$200k/month contracts

### Product & Technology

  - AI-powered workflow engine for content creation
  - Context engine ingests company info, audiences, competitors
  - Workflows for research, drafting, fact-checking, etc.
  - Analytics dashboard integrating LLM visibility, search console, GA
  - Forward-deployed experts for strategy and quality control

### Business Model

  - Initial 8-week strategy sprint ($20-30k)
  - 12-month growth execution contracts based on work streams
  - Work streams include editorial, newsletters, programmatic pages, etc.
  - Most customers $9k-$30k/month, some up to $200k+/month

### Market Opportunity

  - Targeting 32,000 US marketing agencies, 300,000+ freelancers
  - Aiming to be operating system for growth for top brands
  - Seeing strong demand, all growth organic/word-of-mouth so far

### Fundraising & Future Plans

  - Exploring $40-60M Series B raise
  - Main goal is finding right partners who understand services+software model
  - May continue bootstrapping if ideal partners not found
  - Looking to accelerate platform development, expand engineering, invest in sales/marketing

### Competitive Differentiation

  - Focus on codifying knowledge work within organizations
  - Emphasis on learning from every interaction to improve system
  - Ability to quickly leverage new AI models across entire workflow
