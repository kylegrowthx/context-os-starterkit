## Summary

Strapi's content received strong performance gains — sessions up 9.5% week-over-week, SERP position improved from 25th to 15th place, and conversion events up 6% — driven by high-quality comparison content attracting longer page visits. The team aligned on next steps for three focus areas: Strapi will provide specific design examples to improve AI-generated image quality; the engineering team will update 130 integration page meta tags within the next 1-2 weeks; and GrowthX will convert Medium articles with incompatible tables to image-based workarounds, using text metadata to preserve SEO value.

## Context

Strapi is a leading open-source headless CMS platform. GrowthX is providing B2B content marketing services to drive visibility and customer acquisition for Strapi's product offerings. This is an ongoing engagement where GrowthX creates content, optimizes SEO, and develops image generation workflows to support Strapi's content distribution across their website and platforms like Medium. The weekly sync keeps both teams aligned on content performance, upcoming deliverables, and technical integration work. The team is actively investing in automation — particularly AI image generation — to scale content production while maintaining quality standards that match Strapi's brand.
