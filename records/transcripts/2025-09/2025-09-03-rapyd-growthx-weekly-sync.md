## Key Topics

### Content Production and Performance Update

- 31 articles ready for review on Airtable
- Slight drop in overall sessions and non-branded sessions from last week's peak
- Cross-border payments cluster lost some traffic, mainly from Payment Trends article
- Other clusters (compliance, payment infrastructure, processing) held steady
- LLM referrals: GPT and Perplexity remain major sources; Gemini referrals increased

### Content Scope and Guardrails

- Mark flagged recurring mentions of "intelligent routing" across multiple articles (AI trends, auth rates, gateway selection)
- "Intelligent routing" is misaligned with Rapyd's position: it recommends sending transactions to another processor, but Rapyd wants to be the destination
- Solution: Use Rapyd's official API documentation as a guardrail; tie all capabilities mentioned to documented features
- Avoid going "blue sky" on advanced capabilities that exceed Rapyd's actual offering

### Audience Persona Development

- Current composite persona (professional in payment ops) is too broad and lacks industry/level specificity
- Plan to create 4-5 new personas: general catch-all (refined), iGaming/Forex/online trading, crypto, ISO/payfac, gateway/orchestrator tech bundle
- GrowthX will provide audience template example with categories and word count guidance
- Recommendation: consult Rapyd sales team to identify highest-priority personas

### SEO Considerations for New Personas

- Not all personas search the same way: integration partners may not use Google; deals often happen face-to-face or through relationships
- Content for non-search personas (ISO/payfac, gateways) can serve as sales enablement material instead of organic traffic drivers
- Key insight: focus SEO effort on personas that actually search; create awareness/enablement content for others

### Content Localization

- Occasional inconsistencies between British and American English; Rapyd operates in UK/Europe so British English is preferred
- Issue creeps in during editing; team will double-check moving forward
