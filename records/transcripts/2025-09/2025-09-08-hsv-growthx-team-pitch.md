## Context

GrowthX is an AI-native content marketing platform founded by Marcel Santilli and Daniel Lopes. The company has achieved $11M ARR through a services-first model combining AI workflows with domain expert oversight—fundamentally disrupting how B2B content gets created at scale. HubSpot Ventures has been exploring GrowthX as a potential investment and strategic partner, particularly given the overlap with HubSpot's customer base (content creators, marketing leaders) and the strategic potential around AI-powered content and visibility. This meeting is a formal pitch to the HubSpot Ventures team on Labor Day 2025, with the company actively fundraising at a Series A stage.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX Platform Overview

  - Built custom AI framework and runtime for content workflows
  - Combines strategy, AI workflows, expert oversight, and last-mile execution
  - Platform includes knowledge base, artifact management, and workflow engine
  - Fact-checking and research capabilities powered by parallelized AI agents

### Business Model and Traction

  - $50k/month for larger customers like Augment Code (5 workstreams)
  - $9-15k/month for earlier stage startups (1 workstream)
  - $20-30k upfront strategy sprint, then 12-month recurring contract
  - 100% conversion from strategy sprints to recurring contracts so far
  - Artificially constraining growth to 2 new customer kickoffs per week
  - No outbound sales or marketing spend to date; mostly word-of-mouth growth

### Team and Expertise

  - Founders have deep experience in content, growth, and AI from companies like IBM, HP, HashiCorp
  - Engineering team focused on AI-native, experienced developers
  - Building out expert marketplace to scale domain expertise

### Competitive Positioning

  - Positioned as disrupting marketing services, not building a SaaS tool
  - Significantly cheaper than traditional agencies while delivering higher quality
  - AI enables small team to perform work of much larger agency

### Future Vision

  - Launch CheckThat.ai as commoditized visibility layer
  - Expand into adjacent services like sales enablement
  - Build "common core" technology applicable to multiple use cases
  - Potential to generate valuable frontier expert data as byproduct
