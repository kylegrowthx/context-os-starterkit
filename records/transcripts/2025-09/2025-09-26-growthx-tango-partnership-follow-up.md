## Context

Tango is a software tool for creating how-to documentation and software training guides, used primarily by enterprise buyers implementing new systems. The company has a broad horizontal ICP (IT Project Managers, L&D professionals, Sales Enablement, Directors of Business Applications) rather than a vertical focus, which has complicated their marketing in the past. Tango's previous content marketing efforts in 2023-2024 largely failed despite significant investment — they tried agency engagement, product-generated landing pages, and even successful initiatives like G2 reviews and Reddit campaigns, but none drove the consistent organic traffic they needed. Tyler Pavlas, who is in week 6 at GrowthX, brought this opportunity forward because Andy (GrowthX team member) previously worked at Tango. This meeting was a discovery call to help Tyler and the team determine if GrowthX's methodology is a good fit for Tango's specific challenges — mainly: how to create quality content at scale for a wide audience without falling into the generic-content trap that hurt their previous efforts.
