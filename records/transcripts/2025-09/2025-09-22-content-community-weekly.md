## Summary

GrowthX team finalized the strategy to launch the AI-led Growth community platform on Circle, targeting launch within two weeks with an initial cohort of 5-10 "founding members" recruited this week. The team restructured the platform into three content buckets (Growth Toolkit for bite-sized content, Courses for longer-form material, and Discussion threads) and settled on a freemium model where users can create free accounts to access workshops and community features, with paid course access. ## Context

The AI-led Growth community platform is a strategic product initiative to drive engagement and retention for GrowthX's target audience around AI-powered content strategy and growth methodologies. The team is building the community using Circle (a community platform) as the foundation, redesigning content organization to emphasize discussions and peer learning alongside educational content. The Raze Growth team (external partner) is handling the promotional strategy and founding member recruitment, while the internal GrowthX team (Jason, Will, Claudia, Brandon) is responsible for platform setup, content restructuring, and launch coordination. This meeting was focused on aligning on timeline, platform architecture, and the founding member value proposition before the soft launch push.

## Key Topics

### Community Platform Structure

  - Reorganize content into clearer buckets:
      - Growth Toolkit: Bite-sized content like teardowns
      - Courses: Longer-form educational content
      - Discussions: Ongoing conversation threads
  - Implement tiered access:
      - Free tier with limited content visibility
      - Paid tier with full access
  - Improve navigation and user experience:
      - Consider changing default landing page from "Feed" to curated content overview
      - Add "Start Here" and "Say Hello" sections to boost initial engagement

### Content Strategy

  - Leverage existing workshop/course content to drive discussions
  - Create permanent, topic-centered discussion threads vs. short-lived conversations
  - Develop a job board to attract active community participants
  - Produce high-quality video content for landing page and promotional materials

### Founding Member Strategy

  - Target 10-20 high-profile founding members for initial launch
  - Develop clear value proposition and expectations for founding members:
      - Co-host an event
      - Produce a piece of content (podcast, webinar, course)
      - Evangelize the community
  - Create a one-pager highlighting exclusive member list and community benefits
  - Plan coordinated social media push with founding members upon launch

### Social Media & Promotion

  - Primary posting strategy:
      - Post on Jason's personal account
      - Repost (after 5-10 min delay) on AILedGrowth and GrowthX accounts
  - Aim for multiple weekly posts (no set limit) as content allows
  - Mix of serious, value-driven content with occasional memes/humor
  - Coordinate team to engage immediately on posts for algorithm boost
  - Develop landing page and promotional video highlighting community value and exclusive member list

### Launch Timeline

  - This week: Recruit initial 5-10 high-profile founding members
  - Clean up and optimize community platform structure
  - Create promotional assets (landing page, one-pager, video)
  - Next week: Target full launch with 10-20 founding members and coordinated social push
