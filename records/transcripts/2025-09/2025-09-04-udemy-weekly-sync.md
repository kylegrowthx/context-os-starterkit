## Summary

Weekly sync between Udemy and GrowthX to align on web resource development, content strategy, and next steps for their collaborative microsite project.

---

## Context

Udemy is a leading B2B skills platform with a massive library of hands-on courses. GrowthX is supporting them on a content marketing play: building a dedicated microsite and newsletter that showcase skills insights—how roles and job requirements are shifting in response to emerging technologies like AI. The relationship is about 2-3 months into the engagement at this sync. This particular meeting happened during Inbound 2025 in San Francisco, where multiple GrowthX team members were present for brand building and partnership work. The core challenge Udemy faces is credibility differentiation: they need to move beyond generic "top 10 skills" lists (which competitors like Coursera and McKinsey already publish) to specific, actionable insights that show how skills are changing, why those changes matter to business leaders, and how Udemy courses uniquely address them.

---
