## Key Topics

### Microsite Prototype and Direction

  - GrowthX presented a rough prototype microsite design showing how the newsletter content could be expanded into a broader resource hub
  - Design includes areas for featured content, leadership spotlights, trending skills data visualization, and downloadable assets
  - Veronika emphasized the need to focus on Udemy-specific skills that deliver business outcomes, rather than generic upskilling frameworks
  - Team discussed differentiating from competitors like Coursera by highlighting hands-on projects and real-world applications of skills

### Content Depth and Differentiation Strategy

  - Discussion about finding the right "altitude" for content—avoiding both overly generic (already covered by McKinsey/HBR) and hyper-specific content
  - Need to build Udemy's credibility by showing deep understanding of how skills and roles are evolving (e.g., cybersecurity transitioning to LLM pen testing)
  - Key opportunity: highlighting the gap between AI investment and actual production deployment (only 2% making it to production)
  - Challenge of balancing thought leadership content vs Udemy-forward messaging that showcases their unique data and perspectives

### Data Utilization and Skills Insights

  - Agreement that generic "top 10 skills" lists are too surface-level and don't provide meaningful differentiation
  - Better approach: show why skills are trending, what business outcomes they drive, and how they're evolving over time
  - Veronika shared example of an instructor (Donner) whose students at major companies build real projects and share them on LinkedIn
  - Team discussed leveraging these success stories to demonstrate credibility and real-world application of Udemy courses

### Content Planning and Resource Audit

  - GrowthX has begun a content audit with \~60 pieces categorized, but more comprehensive inventory needed
  - Kyle will follow up with Glenn regarding an existing content audit that might accelerate this process
  - Need to identify content gaps based on prioritized themes and topics
  - Goal to start calibrating on content creation by end of next week to build momentum
