## Key Topics

### Current AI Content Workflows at Obsidian

  - Using LLMs to inform briefs, content templates, and SEO formulas
  - Hybrid approach: AI suggestions + human writing/editing
  - Example: AI-generated integration pages with human-written bullet points
  - Concerns about AI content being too easily replicable by competitors

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Leverages multiple LLMs (Claude, ChatGPT, EXA) in custom workflows
  - Creates detailed artifacts on company context, personas, and brand guidelines
  - Multi-step process: brief generation, deep research, content creation, fact-checking
  - Human experts can make manual edits and work with live chat for adjustments
  - Fact-checking involves splitting content into chunks and validating against sources

### Content Types and Scale

  - Editorial content (e.g., blog posts) and programmatic content (e.g., integration pages)
  - Target of 5 articles per week in Phase 2 (post-strategy sprint)
  - Capability to scale up to 100-200 articles/month, likely through programmatic content

### SEO and Quality Assurance

  - Focus on SEO best practices and scientific approach to topic/keyword selection
  - Multiple validation steps to ensure accuracy and brand alignment
  - Structured data and internal linking capabilities
  - Emphasis on creating high-quality content that avoids Google penalties
