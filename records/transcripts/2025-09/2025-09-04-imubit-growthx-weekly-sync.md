## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Overview

  - "Stalking" Imubit across platforms (LinkedIn, webinars, events) to identify key terms and opportunities
  - Translating Imubit's voice into evergreen content that compounds over time
  - Expanding content across verticals (e.g., oil & gas, cement industry)
  - Utilizing call transcripts and competition gap analysis
  - Focusing on process optimization, margins, ROI, and workforce transformation

### High-Performing Content Examples

  - "Golden batch for the chemical industry" article performing well
  - Content on specific metals (e.g., gold, copper) in the mining industry
  - Ethanol article in renewable fuels category showing good results

### Workforce Transformation Content

  - New pillar focusing on skills and mindset needed for successful AI implementation
  - Examples: "7 AI Skills That Build the Future-Proof Plant Operator", "How AI Optimization is Transforming Workforce Development"
  - Addressing challenges in attracting young workforce to manufacturing

### Content Creation Process

  - Using stat-backed claims from reputable sources (e.g., PwC, Harvard Business Review)
  - Organizing content into 4 topic clusters: Industrial AI and Digital Transformation, Optimization and Operational Efficiency, Sustainability/Safety/Compliance, Empowering the Workforce
  - Balancing high-volume keywords with niche terms (40 monthly searches) for strategic advantage

### Imubit Vertical Analysis

  - Tim shared a framework for assessing new vertical fit
  - Factors considered: process-intensive nature, digital culture, energy efficiency, growth phase, price volatility, executive momentum
  - Top potential verticals: LNG, biofuels/renewables, metals/mining
  - Lower priority: pharma, food & beverage (due to regulatory constraints and price stability)

### Content Management and Collaboration

  - GrowthX uses a Content OS to manage published and upcoming articles
  - Shared Notion space for meeting notes, context, and resources
  - Clear distinction between Imubit's blog (opinion pieces) and GrowthX-created articles
