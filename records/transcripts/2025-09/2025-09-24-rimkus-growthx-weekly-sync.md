## Summary

GrowthX and Rimkus aligned on a content strategy built around four clusters—structural engineering and code compliance, due diligence for real estate and investments, mechanical/electrical/life safety consulting, and energy efficiency and resilience—prioritized toward Rimkus' Building Engineering Services (BES) business.   - Writing guidelines will be updated to reflect these nuances

  - GrowthX can assist with content refreshes, starting with analyzing \~9 blog posts
