## Context

RAMP is a fintech company and long-standing GrowthX client that recently launched Cashflow Desk, a YouTube channel focused on content for CFOs and finance leaders. The channel is roughly 1 month old and producing 3 videos per week. Rather than drive traffic directly to RAMP's main domain (which already has high brand recognition), RAMP wants to create a separate, owned third-party affiliate domain that covers the same topics—positioning the content as unbiased editorial advice while still benefiting from RAMP's YouTube audience and proprietary video library. The separate domain is a strategic play to appear in organic search results for financial and cash management topics without the "RAMP branding" tag, which can limit SEO performance. This is a new engagement opportunity for GrowthX: a standalone project separate from RAMP's existing content work, requiring end-to-end delivery (domain setup, infrastructure, design, content strategy, and initial content production).
