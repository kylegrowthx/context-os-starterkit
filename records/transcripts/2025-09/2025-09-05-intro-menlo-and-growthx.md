### Product and Technology

  - AI-powered platform for content strategy, creation, and optimization
  - Four layers: strategy, workflows, experts-in-loop, execution
  - Internal platform evolving into customer-facing dashboard

  - 8-week paid strategy sprint ($20-30K) with 100% conversion to 12-month contracts

### Traction and Financials
