## Summary

Mara from GrowthX and the Rapyd social/content team (Arianna, Mark, Nicolette) aligned on how to integrate GrowthX's SEO and GEO content into Rapyd's social media strategy, moving from siloed operations to collaborative content flagging. They agreed to add a "Good for Social" checkbox in Airtable to identify high-performing content types (pain point-focused, product-focused, and listicles), while Mara will create a framework for repurposing long-form blog content into carousel posts (which achieve 30%+ engagement for Rapyd vs. 8% for regular posts). Given Rapyd's recent 450 iGaming follower growth from the SBC event in Lisbon, they're prioritizing repurposing iGaming content with Mara providing a compiled list of published and pipeline pieces.

---

## Context

Rapyd is a B2B fintech/payments platform (with a strong iGaming vertical) that recently engaged GrowthX to develop SEO and GEO content strategy. Arianna Cernadas, who has been at Rapyd for nearly three years, owns social media strategy and is in the process of breaking down silos between social and the content team. The meeting was triggered by Mara's (GrowthX) onboarding at the company six months prior and her desire to understand how Rapyd's social team works so GrowthX can help feed high-impact content into their social channels. Rapyd has recently implemented more sophisticated social selling infrastructure through Octopost (launching in two weeks) which will give their sales team better content distribution and attribution capabilities.
