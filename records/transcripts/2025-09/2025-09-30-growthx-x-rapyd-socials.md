## Key Topics

### Current Social Media Approach

  - Ari pulls content from blog, white papers, and reports for social calendar
  - Campaign launches and events drive content, with evergreen content filling gaps
  - Moving towards better integration between social and content teams
  - Focus on creating content for brand accounts and sales enablement

### Content Collaboration Strategy

  - GrowthX primarily focuses on SEO and GEO content
  - Not all SEO content suitable for social media promotion
  - Will flag content with strong pain points, product focus, or technical value
  - Consider repurposing content for carousels (30%+ engagement rate vs. 8% for regular posts)
  - Add "good for social" checkbox in Airtable for easy filtering

### Sales Enablement and Social Selling

  - Implementing Octopost tool for sales team social media activity
  - Tool will provide better tracking and data on content performance
  - Automatically applies tracking parameters to all shared content

### iGaming Content Focus

  - Recent event (SBC in Lisbon) brought 450 new iGaming followers to Rapyd LinkedIn
  - Need to capitalize on new audience with targeted iGaming content
  - Will identify and flag existing iGaming content for social media use

### Content Performance Insights

  - Top-performing content includes:
      - "Top 10" lists
      - "How-to" guides
      - Trend analysis pieces
  - Listicles and easily digestible formats work well for engagement
