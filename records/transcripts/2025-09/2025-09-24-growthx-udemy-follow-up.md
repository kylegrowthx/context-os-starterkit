## Context

This follow-up call continued a multi-day engagement between GrowthX and Udemy Business, the enterprise learning platform within Udemy. Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX) is working with Krista Chism and Justin Luebke (both Udemy) to develop SEO content strategy for the UB website, which currently lacks organic visibility. GrowthX's role is to figure out how to systematically extract and leverage that differentiation at scale.

## Key Topics

### Content Sourcing Strategy

  - Webinars identified as primary source of UB thought leadership
      - Extensive library and ongoing production of webinars with cutting-edge content
      - Easier to access and utilize compared to instructor-led content
  - Lecture transcript database ("lecture articles") available as secondary source
      - Requires SQL queries to access; may need to train Justin on usage
      - Already being utilized successfully for consumer-side content

### Instructor Involvement

  - Direct instructor involvement not strictly necessary for UB content
  - When involving instructors:
      - Consumer side offers course links as value exchange
      - UB side faces challenges with aligning instructor incentives to lead gen focus
  - Recent changes to instructor compensation model (minutes consumed vs. per-purchase) may impact willingness to participate

### Content Differentiation

  - Emphasis on avoiding "voice of the universe" content
  - Leverage Udemy's unique resources: instructor knowledge, internal expertise, webinars
  - Concern about potential future Google penalties for "sludge" content

### Technical Considerations

  - UB website facing performance issues; impacts user experience and potentially SEO
  - Initiative underway to holistically address technical SEO across Udemy subdomains
  - Prioritize engineering capacity towards improving page speeds and core user experience

### Content Guidelines

  - H2, H3, and other subheadings (excluding titles) should use sentence case, not title case
  - This differs from current consumer blog implementation
