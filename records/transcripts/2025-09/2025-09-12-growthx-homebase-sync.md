## Summary

GrowthX and Homebase synced on content strategy and delivery challenges. Homebase glossary content is performing well with sustained growth despite AI overview competition, but deeper analytics are needed to understand what's working. Two critical quality issues emerged: TLDR summaries don't match source articles, and "How to Start a Business" state guides lack localization depth and uniqueness. Jakub will bring a new Managing Editor into the delivery team next week to improve quality control and process workflows, starting with fixing these content issues and building guides for interlinking strategy.

## Context

Homebase is a GrowthX content marketing client focused on HR and workforce management software. This is a regular sync between Jakub Rudnik (GrowthX director) and the Homebase content team (Sonia Urlando, Mark Velarga, Kerry McCreadie) to review content performance metrics, discuss workflow issues, and align on strategy. Homebase has been building glossary content successfully, with a secondary focus on TLDR summaries and localized "How to Start a Business" guides across US states. The relationship runs on a weekly sync cadence; the team is sensitive to Friday meeting load and looking to optimize scheduling going forward.
