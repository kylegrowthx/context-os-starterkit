## Context

Attention.com is a software company that previously engaged with GrowthX for content marketing services for approximately one year before churning six months ago. The primary reason for churn was lack of measurable impact on sales pipeline—content wasn't generating meaningful meeting bookings. Since then, Attention.com has observed increased inbound traffic mentioning AI sources (LLMs like ChatGPT), which has refocused their content strategy on AEO (AI Engine Optimization). Stewart White leads the business development discussion and Harrison Tracy would be the day-to-day owner of a renewed engagement. Tyler Pavlas, GrowthX's founding account executive, leads the reconnect conversation and is positioning GrowthX's evolved service delivery model, which now includes a proprietary platform component (still in beta) and a more prescriptive, research-intensive content creation process grounded in audience personas and company context artifacts.
