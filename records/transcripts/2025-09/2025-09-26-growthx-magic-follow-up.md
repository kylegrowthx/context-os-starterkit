## Summary

GrowthX and Plus Labs (Magic) discussed a potential partnership to scale Magic's content marketing and SEO performance. Magic currently produces 2 blog posts/week with flat organic traffic (~6,000-7,000/month US) and is seeking to increase output to 5-7 posts/week while gaining SEO expertise, LLM visibility optimization, and white-glove CMS management. GrowthX proposed a $15k/month engagement (8-week initial phase plus 12-month commitment starting Nov 3-10), with comparable case studies showing 2.5x traffic gains in 3 months (Augment) and 6.5x in 6 months (Tiro). The decision hinges on Magic's commitment to invest $150k+ annually in content as a growth lever versus their current $5k/month agency spend.

---

## Context

Plus Labs (Magic) is a profitable, self-funded executive assistant platform with ~$35M in annual revenue and 10+ years in market. They compete with Athena and Wing Assistant but differentiate through flexibility (no minimum commitments), the Magic 24/7 app (providing round-the-clock AI-powered assistance beyond business hours), and placement of 10,000+ assistants. This is a follow-up call from an initial GrowthX discovery meeting; Tyler Rogers (lead marketing) is now stepping in to own SEO strategy after realizing Magic's organic performance has been flat despite their strong domain authority (67, with 30,000 backlinks). The agency they currently work with ($5k/month) produces ~2 blog posts/week but Magic lacks dedicated internal resources—their only content contributor is an executive wearing multiple hats. Tyler Pavlas from GrowthX presented case studies and GrowthX's comprehensive service model (managing director + managing editor with SEO backgrounds, keyword research, technical SEO, LLM visibility tracking) to explore whether Magic is ready to make a larger investment in content.
