### GrowthX Partnership Structure

  - Earliest start date: November 3rd or 10th

### Decision Factors for Magic

  - Evaluating $150k annual investment vs. current $5k/month agency spend
  - Potential for exponential results and higher ROI than paid marketing
  - Need for internal discussion on prioritizing content as a growth channel
