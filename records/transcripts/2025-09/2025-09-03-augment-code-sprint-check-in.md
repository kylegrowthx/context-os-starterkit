## Context

Augment Code is a software company focused on developer tools and AI-powered code completion products (Augment CLI). GrowthX is supporting their growth through content marketing, SEO, and backlink strategies — a multi-month engagement focused on driving developer signups and enterprise adoption. This meeting was a sprint check-in with Sylvain Giuliani (CEO) and Molisha Shah reviewing content performance metrics, resource allocation, and upcoming product launches alongside GrowthX's Jason Gong.

The engagement is at an early inflection point: comparison articles are driving outsized conversions (7-9% vs 1-2% baseline), and the team is exploring whether to double down on SEO/content or diversify into product launches (CLI template marketplace), paid ads, and enterprise outreach. Strategic decisions here will shape resource allocation for the next 4-6 weeks.
