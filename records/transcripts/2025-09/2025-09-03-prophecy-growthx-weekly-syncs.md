## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Approach to Content Creation

  - Combines AI automation with human expertise for rapid, high-quality content production
  - Team structure: Managing editors, content managers, and strategy-level support
  - Focus on getting market signals quickly through experimentation and learning
  - Aim to find 2+ high-conviction content areas + 1 experimental area
  - Use AI workflows to break down content creation steps (research, outlining, writing, fact-checking)

### ContentOS Platform Overview

  - Organizes content opportunities into clusters and topics
  - Allows prioritization and assignment of content pieces
  - Incorporates persona artifacts, company context, and writing guidelines
  - Features AI-powered pipelines for content development (e.g. keyword research, brief creation, fact-checking)

### Content Strategy and Planning

  - Conduct deep research on client's personas, competitors, and industry trends
  - Organize topics into clusters based on product pillars and target audience
  - Use various sources (Reddit, competitor analysis, SEO tools) to identify relevant topics and keywords
  - Collaborate with clients to refine and prioritize content opportunities

### Full-Funnel Content Approach

  - Not limited to blog posts; can create various content types (guides, buyer-focused pieces, etc.)
  - Balance between awareness-level content and solution-specific pieces
  - Build topical authority to support ranking for competitive, high-intent keywords
  - Consider content's role in nurturing leads through the funnel

### Prophecy's Content Challenges

  - Recent company pivot necessitates rethinking content strategy
  - Past reliance on events for quick awareness, which can be inefficient
  - Need to bridge the gap between awareness and solution-specific content
  - Importance of creating discoverable content for ICP at various funnel stages
