## Key Topics

### Market Positioning and Investor Interest

Bryan mentioned that multiple investors have approached him for customer perspective on GrowthX's growth and market positioning. He noted that CMOs across his network are increasingly focused on AI-driven content creation and market research. The CMO AI group he participates in has shown strong interest in GrowthX's approach, and he has personally referred colleagues (e.g., Vitaly) to GrowthX based on his positive experience. Marcel emphasized that this investor interest validates GrowthX's inflection curve and the critical role of AI Answers in the modern sales and discovery process.

### AI Answers as First Sales Call

Marcel explained the core insight driving GrowthX's growth: AI Answers is becoming the first sales call for technical and complex topics. Prospects spend significant time in AI Answers (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude) before engaging with sales. To win visibility and citations, companies must become the most credible, useful, well-cited, well-structured answers to the questions their buyers ask. This requires not just AI but also subject matter expertise, high-quality content, and rigorous fact-checking — particularly critical for public companies.

### Atlas Platform Capabilities and Roadmap

Marcel demoed the Atlas platform, showing:
- **AI Visibility Dashboard:** Consolidates AI visibility, search console, analytics, and other data in one place
- **Context Layer:** Stores personas, guidelines, rules, and context (similar to Cursor rules or Claude.md files)
- **Research Pipelines:** Breaks down topics into questions, performs deep research on each, includes fact-checking with confidence scoring and suggested rewrites
- **Fact-Checking Example:** For public companies, Atlas extracts passages, verifies accuracy, flags confidence scores, suggests regulatory-compliant rewrites (e.g., "satisfy data sovereignty" → "align with data resilience and sovereignty objectives")
- **Knowledge Base:** Perplexity-like interface for researching topics ingested during content creation
- **Activity Feed:** Full transparency of GrowthX team actions and effort
- **Analytics and ROI Modeling:** Measuring impact and demonstrating value
- **Assistant Features:** Context-aware assistant for content generation and iteration (future roadmap)

### Vulnerability Catalog and SentinelOne Project

GrowthX created a comprehensive vulnerability catalog for SentinelOne based on the company's security offerings. The team developed deep audience personas through interviews with SentinelOne team members and experts, then expanded the catalog to 100+ content pieces ready for production. This proactive work differentiated GrowthX and demonstrated value-add beyond content creation alone.

### Content Publishing Bottlenecks and Solutions

SentinelOne faces constraints around content review, approval, and publishing access. Marcel shared solutions from other clients (e.g., Augment, Webflow):
- **Augment:** After 10 weeks, GrowthX published 100+ guides on enterprise AI topics. The guides page now has more traffic than Augment's entire blog and achieved 10x visibility lift on key queries like "best AI coding tools for enterprise" (Augment now ranked #1). This growth despite resource constraints shows the power of solving publishing bottlenecks.
- **Webflow:** GrowthX created their entire integrations catalog (every page under /integrations), removing friction between content creation and production
- **Secure Process for SentinelOne:** GrowthX team members undergo security clearance and access via private browser (no copy-paste allowed) for SentinelOne production publishing

### CheckThat.ai: Competitive Intelligence Tool

CheckThat.ai is launching in ~2 weeks (as of Sept 3, 2025) as an open visibility index. Features include:
- Index of every software category and competitor in each category
- Publicly available data on all companies and competitors
- Similar to SimilarWeb for web traffic or Crunchbase for company data
- Transparent methodology: users can see which queries and prompts are used to determine visibility rankings
- Free tier available to all users
- Atlas customers get premium analytics features and more robust visibility tracking
- Positioning: democratizing data that competitors like Profound/Crunchbase charge $2,000-$4,000/month to access

### SDR-Focused Content Expansion

Bryan raised GrowthX's potential to support SentinelOne's SDR team. Marcel shared the Okta/Auth0 example:
- **Company Briefs:** Deep research on target accounts with industry-specific contextualization
- **Methodology:** Interview SMEs in the target industry, develop industry-specific guidelines, create call briefs and cold calling resources
- **Automation:** Briefs are auto-populated into email templates for scale
- **Example Brief Contents:** Company background, pain points, timing signals, points of contact, LinkedIn profiles
- **Differentiation:** Most data tools (ZoomInfo, HG Insights, DemandBase, G2, Lucia) provide standardized data; GrowthX does live web research to capture recent product announcements, features, customer use cases, and context that static databases miss
- **Implementation:** Only for customers at mature stage of partnership (e.g., Okta/Auth0); not done at scale across 20 customers

### Product Suite Clarity and Prescriptive Positioning

Bryan provided feedback from investor conversations: they ask which parts of GrowthX's suite SentinelOne uses and how to upsell or expand. He noted that GrowthX's website presents too many options, making it hard for buyers to understand. He contrasted this with Evidenza's approach of being more prescriptive (showing core offerings but allowing custom configurations). Marcel responded that GrowthX prioritizes proving value first before expanding services, as many customers request things they don't fully understand and don't see results from. The focus is on defining clear problems to solve rather than feature-driven selling.
