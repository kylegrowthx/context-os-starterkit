  - Familiar with AI-assisted keyword research, drafting, and editing processes

### GrowthX Content Creation Process

  - Uses proprietary Atlas tool, more advanced than Aeolus
  - AI pipelines handle research, drafting, fact-checking, linking, metadata, and even publishing
  - Content managers guide AI outputs, intervene at key points for quality control
  - Aim for 5-10 minutes of human intervention per piece for efficiency

### Content Manager Role Details

  - Focus on managing AI pipelines, not direct client interaction
  - Provide feedback on keyword strategies and content goals to Managing Editors
  - Identify gaps between pipeline output and client needs
  - Document recurring issues and suggest pipeline improvements
  - Comfort with evolving tools and active development environment is crucial

### Client Onboarding and Workflow

  - 8-12 week sprint to understand client needs and build custom pipelines
  - Content managers may be involved in sprint teams or steady-state client groups
  - Continuous feedback loop for improving efficiency and output quality

### Technical Aspects

  - No engineering required, but understanding of pipeline processes is important
  - Can prototype ideas in Claude or ChatGPT before requesting engineering implementation
  - Ability to articulate pipeline improvement needs clearly is valuable
