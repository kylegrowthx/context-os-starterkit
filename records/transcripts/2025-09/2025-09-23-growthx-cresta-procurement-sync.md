## Summary

Tyler Pavlas (GrowthX) and Kevin Kiernicki with Shreha Jain (Cresta) discussed contract structure, risk mitigation, and pricing for a potential 12-month content marketing partnership. Cresta requested a 3-6 month pilot period within Phase 2 and a budget of ~$7K/month, while GrowthX holds firm on an 8-week Phase 1 with a 7-week opt-out window, a 12-month Phase 2 commitment with no opt-out, and a $15K/month starting price. Kevin committed to sending redlined contract terms reflecting Cresta's requested changes for Marcel's review, with the understanding that the partnership hinges on contract alignment.

## Context

Cresta is a prospect considering GrowthX's content marketing services to build AI visibility and organic traffic. This was a procurement-focused negotiation call between GrowthX (Tyler Pavlas) and Cresta's procurement and product teams (Kevin Kiernicki and Shreha Jain). The meeting centered on reconciling different expectations around contract structure, engagement phases, risk mitigation mechanisms, and budget. Cresta was seeking flexibility to validate results before committing to GrowthX's full 12-month model, while GrowthX was defending its standard delivery structure based on resource allocation and client success patterns. The conversation was civil but revealed a significant gap in both contract structure expectations and pricing budget that threatened deal closure.

## Key Topics

### Contract Structure and Risk Mitigation

  - Cresta wants 3-6 month pilot period in Phase 2 for risk mitigation
  - GrowthX's structure: 8-week Phase 1 (7-week opt-out window), then 12-month Phase 2
  - GrowthX unwilling to offer opt-out in Phase 2 due to resource allocation constraints
  - Cresta suggests contingency contract with KPIs for first 2 months of Phase 2
  - GrowthX open to considering termination for breach clause instead of pilot period

### Deliverables and Performance Metrics

  - Phase 1 (8 weeks): Strategy sprint, limited content creation, alignment on goals
  - Phase 2: Daily content creation, weekly reporting, focus on long-term growth
  - Key metrics: Content refreshes, new content pieces, web traffic (esp. AI-specific), LLM visibility
  - GrowthX emphasizes long-term investment vs. short-term vanity metrics

### Pricing and Budget

  - Current GrowthX pricing: $15K/month for Phase 2
  - Cresta's budget: \~$7K/month
  - Significant gap in expectations, potential dealbreaker

### Confidence in Results

  - GrowthX expresses high confidence in delivering results
  - Cites churn as critical metric for their business, emphasizing commitment to client success
  - Cresta views confidence as reason to offer pilot period; GrowthX disagrees
