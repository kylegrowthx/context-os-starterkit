## Summary

Tyler Pavlas presented GrowthX's SEO and content strategy services to LlamaIndex, where Murtaza Khomusi (product marketing) and Jerry Liu (CEO) are seeking to boost non-branded traffic by 50% over 6 months, particularly for their Llama Cloud document parsing product. GrowthX outlined a two-phase approach: an 8-week strategy sprint ($20K) to develop audience personas and content frameworks, followed by ongoing growth execution at $15K/month with AI-assisted content workflows and human editorial expertise. LlamaIndex expressed interest in a potential start two weeks out and asked Tyler to confirm with Marcel on pricing flexibility, Sanity CMS integration capabilities, and the feasibility of setting up attribution dashboards for content-to-signup and content-to-MQL tracking.

---

## Context

LlamaIndex is an open-source framework for building AI agents with retrieval-augmented generation (RAG), but has evolved into a suite of products including Llama Cloud, a specialized document parsing and OCR solution. The company has strong branded search visibility through the LlamaIndex brand itself but only 10-20% of traffic comes from non-branded keyword searches. Murtaza Khomusi, the sole product marketer at LlamaIndex, reached out to GrowthX after having worked with the company previously when he was at Galileo AI, where he was involved in content review and strategy. He was attracted to GrowthX's rapid execution speed and AI-assisted content workflows, though he noted that GrowthX's pricing had roughly doubled from the $8K/month he remembered to the current $15K/month. Tyler Pavlas, who recently joined GrowthX (5-6 weeks prior to this meeting), represented the company alongside a note-taker, as Marcel Santilli was unavailable due to Series B fundraising.
