## Context

Create is a no-code app development platform founded by Dhruv Amin (CEO) that launched about a month before this meeting. The company just closed a $10M Series A funding round and is in hyper-growth mode, targeting $40M run rate by end of year—they hit $2M run rate within two weeks of launch. Create competes with much larger players like Lovable, Bolt, and Replit (all with hundreds of millions in funding). GrowthX was engaged to help Create acquire product-market-fit customers through a three-month content strategy engagement beginning September 29th. Dhruv will be the primary point of contact for the initial kickoff and strategy phase; he plans to transition ownership to Zarya (Create's marketer) once the content process is established and running smoothly.
