## Summary

GrowthX and HockeyStack aligned on a comprehensive content strategy to significantly increase HockeyStack's content volume from 5 articles per month (current agency) to 5 per week, using GrowthX's AI-powered Atlas platform. The strategy focuses on four content clusters—go-to-market and account intelligence, marketing attribution, revenue operations, and industry-specific verticals—with an emphasis on proprietary HockeyStack data to drive both SEO and LLM visibility. Key implementation details included reviewing custom artifacts (Writing Guidelines, Audience Personas, Company Brief), establishing analytics dashboards, and developing processes to integrate HockeyStack's customer highlights and case studies into the content pipeline.

---

## Context

HockeyStack is a B2B SaaS platform providing marketing attribution and go-to-market intelligence. The company is currently dissatisfied with its existing SEO agency, citing slow content production (5 articles/month), generic messaging that doesn't align with HockeyStack's brand voice, and lack of strategic vision. GrowthX has been engaged to transform HockeyStack's content strategy through AI-powered generation, focusing on building topical authority and positioning HockeyStack as a thought leader in marketing data and revenue operations.

This weekly sync brought together HockeyStack's content and product leadership (Amulya Vadrevu) with GrowthX's delivery team (Aida Knežević, Andi Bailey, Erik O'Brien) to establish the operational framework for the engagement, review initial strategy documents, and clarify execution processes. The meeting demonstrates the complexity of aligning AI-generated content with brand voice while meeting both traditional SEO and emerging LLM discovery requirements.
