## Overview

- Lovable's current focus is on retention, particularly for paid users, due to its high potential impact
- First-time user experience is a major drop-off point, with only 20-30% of users sending 5+ prompts after logging in
- growthx.ai to analyze first 5-10 prompts per user to identify issues and develop solutions (e.g., templates, starter kits)
- Collaboration will prioritize activation/conversion improvement, with potential retention benefits as a bonus

## Key Topics

### Current Data Analysis Capabilities

  - Lovable can categorize projects by type (SaaS, internal tools, websites, prototypes)
  - AI-generated descriptions and categorizations for user projects
  - Analysis focused on retained, paid users due to resource constraints
  - Basic last-touch attribution setup for top-of-funnel analysis

### Key Metrics and Challenges

  - Major drop-off in first-time experience: \<30% of users send 5+ prompts after login
  - Mobile conversion rate is very low (1-2% page view to sign-up)
  - High volume of sign-ups (250-300k/week) but low conversion to active users
  - Limited analysis on users with \<5 prompts due to cost constraints

### Potential Areas for Improvement

  - SEO optimization and showcasing user projects
  - Mobile conversion rate enhancement
  - Template marketplace development
  - Algorithmic high-quality project discovery on explore page
  - Scalable use case analysis for user projects

### growthx.ai's Approach and Offerings

  - Experience with similar projects (e.g., Bolt)
  - Creation of starter kits and templates based on user data
  - Churn analysis and user behavior insights
  - Content strategy tied to SEO and search intent
