  - Current site has substantial improvement opportunities potentially yielding double-digit conversion lifts

  - Homepage structure buries critical information that should be prominent
  - Customer testimonials/success stories need strategic placement for conversion impact

### Engineering Resources Bottleneck

  - Website changes consistently deprioritized behind product development and support
  - Resource allocation hierarchy: 1) support tickets, 2) product development, 3) growth initiatives
