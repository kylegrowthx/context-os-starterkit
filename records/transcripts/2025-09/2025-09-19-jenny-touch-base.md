## Key Topics

### Content Accuracy Concerns

Fieldguide operates in a compliance-driven industry where misinformation about rules, regulations, and guidelines could damage their credibility. They wanted assurance that GrowthX isn't just using ChatGPT to generate content. Tyler explained GrowthX's two-layer accuracy approach: fact-checker agents that validate every passage against credible sources, plus client team review before staging and publishing. Jenny asked for specific data on edit frequency and how "finished" the articles typically are (60% vs 90% quality), but Tyler didn't have statistics on hand. He acknowledged this would only become clear after the trial period.

### Content Creation Efficiency

Jenny raised concern that if GrowthX outputs require extensive rewriting, the efficiency gains disappear. The core question: how much refinement does the content need before it's publish-ready? Tyler confirmed this will become clear during Phase 1 when Fieldguide sees actual output quality.

### Phase 1 Content Production Timeline

Content production typically starts in the second half of Phase 1. For motivated clients like Fieldguide, it can begin as early as week 4. Tyler committed to targeting ~10 publish-ready articles by week 7, flexible to 15-20 if Fieldguide needs more examples to make a confident Phase 2 decision.

### Phase 2 Commitment and Terms

GrowthX maintains a strict 12-month commitment for Phase 2, committed to 5 articles per week. Tyler is firm on this due to high demand: 10+ clients competing for available slots through mid-November. He emphasized that this isn't a revenue-maximization constraint—it's about ensuring GrowthX can dedicate resources to client success. Quarterly billing is available as an option.

### Fieldguide's Internal Decision-Making

Finance approved the budget increase, removing that barrier. The remaining decision requires alignment among Jenny (lead decision-maker), Yasine (department head), and Mark (executive stakeholder). Fieldguide committed to providing a go/no-go decision by early next week to either lock in the October 20 start date or release the slot.

### GrowthX's Positioning

Tyler positioned GrowthX as the best partner for creating content to appear in AI answers at scale. He referenced town.com as a successful example—a compliance/tax information platform with similar accuracy requirements to Fieldguide, using GrowthX content that now appears in AI answers.
