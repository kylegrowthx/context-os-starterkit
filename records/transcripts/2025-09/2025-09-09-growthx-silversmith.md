## Key Topics

### GrowthX Overview and Traction

  - Founded by Marcel Santilli, former CMO with track record of building content engines at IBM, HP, HashiCorp, etc.
  - Launched Sept 2022, raised $12M seed in Feb 2023, now at $11M ARR run rate with 50+ customers
  - 72% gross margins as of August, burning only $3M total to reach current scale
  - Key clients include Webflow, Sentinel One, Abnormal Security, Ramp, Clerk
  - Seeing strong word-of-mouth growth and referrals, including from investors

### Product and Service Offering

  - AI-powered content creation platform with human experts in the loop
  - Combines strategy, AI workflows, and "forward deployed experts" for execution
  - 8-week strategy sprint + 12-month work stream model, $9K-$230K MRR per customer
  - Internal platform manages workflows, research, editing, fact-checking, etc.
  - Evolving toward customer-facing dashboard and expert marketplace

### Competitive Landscape

  - Not losing deals to agencies or other AI tools like Jasper, Copy.ai
  - Main competition is internal teams or DIY attempts with workflow tools
  - Differentiated by end-to-end service model vs. self-serve AI writing tools
  - Planning to launch CheckThat.ai as first standalone product for AI visibility

### Fundraising Plans

  - Considering $40-60M raise to accelerate growth and product development
  - Not demand constrained - could grow faster by increasing sales capacity
  - Want to invest more in go-to-market and engineering
  - Evaluating growth equity partners who can go deep on the business model

### Customer Success and Expansion

  - 120%+ net revenue retention, with multiple recent upsells
  - Long-term customers like Ramp (12+ months, 700+ content pieces)
  - Proactive upsells as customers expand use cases (e.g. Metronome, Superhuman)
  - Focused on ideal customer profile - firing some early misfit customers
