## Key Topics

### Tango's Current Situation and Goals

  - Tango has evolved through several product phases: documentation capture, team collaboration, and now UI automation
  - Recent focus on sales-led growth for automation product has flattened self-serve growth
  - Aim to reinvigorate self-serve engine, exploring new channels and tools for hybrid go-to-market approach
  - Current ICP: Sales teams, expanding to operations teams in software without robust APIs (e.g., ERPs, internal tools)

### GrowthX's Offering and Approach

  - Founded by Marcel Santilli, leveraging experience from successful content engines at IBM, HP, and other tech companies
  - Combines AI-powered workflows with human expertise to create high-quality, targeted content at scale
  - Platform features:
      - Artifact creation for brand guidelines and context
      - Knowledge base for deep research and fact-checking
      - AI coding agent for flexible, code-based workflow management
      - Parallelized research and fact-checking processes
  - Engagement model:
      - 8-week strategy sprint ($30k) for initial setup and calibration
      - Ongoing growth execution starting at $15k/month
      - Replaces multiple roles: head of content, writers, SEO agency, graphic designer, and various tools

### Content Creation and Distribution Process

  - GrowthX handles end-to-end content creation, from research to publishing
  - Content primarily published on client's website to build authority
  - Weekly 30-minute calibration meetings and ongoing Slack communication for feedback and adjustments
  - Potential for expansion into third-party channels and newsletters

### Results and Case Studies

  - Augment Code example: 3x traffic increase, 10x visibility score improvement in \~4 months with 120+ content pieces
  - Improved search rankings and citations in AI-generated answers for relevant queries
