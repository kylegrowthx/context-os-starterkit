## Context

Yousef Hamade from GrowthX has been in discussions with Liam Moore at Vanta about SOC 2 Type II compliance for nearly six months. GrowthX is a rapidly scaling B2B content marketing agency (75 employees, growing customer base and ARR) that works exclusively with publicly available information. While major enterprise customers like Ramp and Service Titan are part of the client base, they don't currently request SOC 2 because GrowthX operates as a marketing agency with no access to privileged or confidential data. SOC 2 is strategically important to GrowthX's roadmap but not urgently blocking new business. Management bandwidth is currently stretched managing the company's rapid growth, so the project keeps getting deferred.
