## Key Topics

### BES Overview and Positioning

  - BES is one of three main Rimkus service groups, focusing on proactive services for the built environment
  - Positioned as a high-quality, well-known player in the industry (analogous to Nordstrom in retail)
  - Comprehensive suite of services for commercial buildings, institutions, and property portfolios
  - Key value proposition: in-house expertise across multiple disciplines, reducing coordination costs for clients

### Priority Service Areas

1.  Structural Engineering
      - Condition assessments
      - Building code compliance
      - Garage inspections and repairs
2.  Due Diligence
      - Real estate transactions
      - Property evaluations for investors, brokers, and insurance companies
3.  Mechanical and Electrical Consulting
      - Energy efficiency
      - System design and upgrades

### Regional Focus and Regulations

  - Services often driven by local/state regulations (e.g. Florida's HB 913, New York's Local Law 97)
  - Rimkus acquired companies with expertise in region-specific regulations
  - Challenge: Keeping content up-to-date with evolving regulations while maintaining SEO performance

### Client Types and Needs

  - Building owners, property managers, real estate developers, investors
  - Sometimes other technical experts (e.g. architects hiring Rimkus architects)
  - Clients value Rimkus for comprehensive services, reducing need to coordinate multiple vendors

### Content Strategy Considerations

  - Emphasize sustainability, resilience, and maintainability in all services
  - Focus on long-term value and cost savings for clients
  - Highlight Rimkus' ability to provide end-to-end solutions for complex projects
  - Consider creating micro-pages for specific regions/regulations using Foleon platform
