## Overview

- Logic is pivoting from positioning as an ops-focused tool to a broader process automation solution for various business functions
- Content creation has been successful for Logic in the past, but time-consuming; they seek help scaling production while maintaining quality
- Logic's key differentiator is rapid time-to-value for automating processes, often within minutes or hours
- Current customer acquisition is primarily through the founder's network; they aim to expand reach

## Key Topics

### Logic's Product and Positioning

  - Logic helps teams automate enterprise processes by turning documents into automated workflows
  - Key differentiator: Rapid time-to-value, often automating processes within minutes/hours
  - Shifting positioning from ops-focused to broader process automation for various business functions
  - Target company size: 10-500 employees, focusing on Series A/B tech-forward companies
  - Unique selling points:
      - Can automate existing SOPs without rewriting
      - Provides testing, versioning, and rollback features
      - Generates UIs and APIs for non-technical users

### Current Customer Acquisition and Challenges

  - Primary source: Founder's network and referrals
  - Content campaign (12-day series) drove significant interest
  - Waitlist of 100+ from content and fundraising announcement
  - Challenges:
      - Overcoming "not invented here" syndrome with engineering teams
      - Clarifying Logic's capabilities vs. custom-built solutions
      - Integrating with specific tools (e.g., QuickBooks) for some prospects

### Content Strategy and Past Success

  - Previous successful 12-day content campaign on LinkedIn
  - Process: Team brainstorming, prioritization, assignment based on expertise
  - Content focused on engineering topics; need to expand to industry-specific content
  - Time-consuming to produce high-quality, original content with working code examples
  - Desire for guidance on content type, frequency, and distribution strategies

### Engagement Goals with growthx.ai

  - Develop consistent content output to increase visibility and establish topical authority
  - Focus on industry-specific content (fintech, e-commerce, logistics) beyond engineering topics
  - Guidance on content strategy, creation, and distribution across channels (LinkedIn, Reddit, Hacker News)
  - Assistance in refining messaging and positioning as Logic expands its target market

### Next Steps in the Engagement

3.  Logic to provide access to analytics and any existing CMS
4.  Begin defining initial artifacts (company description, product, audience, writing guidelines)
