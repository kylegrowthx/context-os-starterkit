CMO advisory service requires new capability assessment.

**To GrowthX Business Development:** Packaging restructure is directly tied to positioning and pricing strategy. ## Key Topics

### Website Content Strategy

  - Current website lists individual work streams with pricing (e.g., content at 9K, programmatic SEO at 12K)
  - New approach: Create outcome-based packages (content, distribution, conversion) without specific pricing
  - Maintain a separate page detailing all work streams and team expertise
  - Use hover effects to showcase team member profiles for each work stream
  - Consider adding a VIP/advisory service option to anchor pricing and increase perceived value

### Pricing Transparency

  - Aim for transparency without overwhelming potential clients
  - Use "starting at" pricing for packages to maintain flexibility
  - Include "Included in all plans" section to highlight core offerings
  - Balance between providing enough information and maintaining curiosity to drive inquiries

### Community and Lead Generation

  - Explore creating an AI-led growth community as a marketing tool and potential revenue stream
  - Consider offering workshops or webinars as lead magnets and upsell opportunities
  - Leverage community access as an added value for clients (e.g., up to 10 team members per client)

### Service Packaging

  - Frame engagements as 12+ month programs with initial 12-week boot camps
  - Emphasize the interconnected nature of services and how they reinforce each other
  - Consider offering fractional CMO or advisory services as a high-value add-on

### Brand Positioning

  - Position GrowthX as a strategic partner rather than a traditional agency
  - Emphasize access to high-level expertise and advisory services
  - Create a sense of exclusivity and high value through pricing and packaging structure
