## Context

Yourco is a frontline workforce management platform that helps organizations manage hourly and shift-based workers. GrowthX provides B2B content marketing services focused on SEO, SEM, and demand generation. This weekly sync is part of an ongoing delivery engagement where GrowthX handles all content production, SEO optimization, and strategic guidance. The meeting reflected Yourco's shift from purely SMB targeting toward enterprise accounts (500+ employees) and discussed implementation bottlenecks in email marketing operations (CRM hygiene, list quality, integration complexity) alongside strategic questions about whether outbound cold email is still viable given increased market noise from AI-driven tools.
