## Overview

- Becoming "AI native" requires dedicated time (100-1000 hours) to deeply explore and experiment with AI tools and applications
- First principles thinking and strong writing skills are crucial for effectively leveraging AI
- AI should elevate human thinking and collaboration, not replace it or make it lazy
- Decomposing tasks and providing clear context/examples are key for getting quality AI outputs

---

## Key Topics

### Becoming AI Native

  - Carve out significant time (e.g. 2 weeks) to go deep on AI exploration
  - Apply AI to real work tasks that matter to you to learn through practical application
  - Experiment constantly, testing different prompts and approaches
  - Audit your calendar and activities to identify areas for AI optimization

### Effective AI Delegation

  - Process of delegating to AI is similar to delegating to humans
  - Can't delegate what you don't understand yourself
  - Provide clear context, examples, and instructions
  - Show good/bad examples to calibrate AI outputs

### First Principles Thinking

  - Break problems down to fundamental components
  - Example: For audience research, imagine a day/year in target persona's life
  - Helps generate deeper insights AI can build upon

### Writing Skills

  - Strong writing organizes thoughts and improves AI collaboration
  - Study and emulate different writing styles to develop your own
  - Use AI to polish and iterate on human-generated content

### AI for Content Creation

  - Use AI to generate editorial concepts, outlines, and drafts
  - Provide style examples and "polishing instructions" to refine AI outputs
  - Combine multiple AI tools/models for best results (e.g. concept generation + image search + layout tools)

### AI for Image Generation

  - Current tools have limitations, especially with brand consistency
  - Break down image creation into components (concepts, styles, layouts)
  - Use AI to generate/select concepts, then human design for final polish
  - Tools like Add to Sale allow programmatic image creation with variable inputs

### Staying Up-to-Date on AI

  - Use AI to summarize long-form content (podcasts, articles, books)
  - Extract key frameworks, mental models, and takeaways
  - Experiment with different summarization approaches as a learning exercise
