## Context

Webstacks is a web design and development agency that works primarily with B2B and FinTech companies. This is an ongoing weekly sync between the Webstacks delivery team (Devon Wood leading strategy, Jakub Rudnik on technical strategy) and GrowthX's SEO team (Aida Knežević). The relationship focuses on helping Webstacks scale their content and organic visibility strategy, moving from reactive content creation to a more structured, capability-driven approach. Webstacks has recently seen success with keyword-targeted campaigns that drive high-quality leads, and they're now trying to double down on this by reorganizing their content architecture to feed directly into their capability pages and create more intentional content pillars. GrowthX is supporting this with keyword research, competitive analysis, and potential programmatic SEO work.
