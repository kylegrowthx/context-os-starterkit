- Matthew's vision: Log every content interaction (similar to how code revision history works). Google Docs revision history is discoverable but isolated; WordPress sidecars are invisible. Neither institutionalizes learning.

- Both leadership approaches are service-oriented — enabling people's growth and skill development — and they see recruitment as part of the role
