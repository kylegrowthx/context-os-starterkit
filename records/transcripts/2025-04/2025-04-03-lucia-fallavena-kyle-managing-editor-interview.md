  - Recently implemented a pod structure with Managing Editors as pod leads
  - Content Managers support multiple accounts (4-5) under a Managing Editor
  - Directors manage multiple pods, providing strategy support and process development

### Lúcia's Background and Experience

  - 7+ years in B2B content marketing and product marketing for tech/SaaS companies

  - Experience with various content types: e-books, blogs, SEO-driven content, and videos

  - Iterative approach to content creation, using human-in-the-loop steps to refine output

### Content Manager Role Specifics

  - Contributes to strategy development alongside Managing Editors and Directors
  - Conducts research and provides insights for content improvement
  - Iterates on workflows to enhance content quality and efficiency
  - Some client-facing responsibilities, including participating in calls and Slack communication
