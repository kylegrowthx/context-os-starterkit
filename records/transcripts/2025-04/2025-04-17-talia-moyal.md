- Current AI-generated content isn't meeting quality standards, leading to a backlog of unpublished pieces
- Gitpod is considering involving industry experts for pre-publication review to ensure technical accuracy and brand alignment

## Key Topics

### Content Quality Challenges

  - Gitpod unlisted all GrowthX-produced content due to quality concerns
  - Issues include:
      - Outdated sources (2018-2019) for a rapidly evolving field
      - Contradictions and redundancies within articles
      - Technically inaccurate statements requiring expert verification
      - Misalignment with Gitpod's specific messaging (e.g., avoiding "cloud platform" terminology)
  - Only 2 people at Gitpod can currently review content, creating a bottleneck

### Current Content Review Process

  - Talia and one other person (ex-engineer, now PMM) handle all reviews
  - Recent company offsite has created a backlog of \~10 pieces awaiting review
  - GrowthX's editor (Jen) has improved based on feedback but can't fully bridge the technical gap

### Potential Solutions

  - Integrate an industry expert into the content creation process for technical accuracy
  - Consider creating separate content streams for SEO-focused vs. high-stakes technical content
  - Improve the initial style guide and messaging guidelines provided to GrowthX
  - Explore how other companies (e.g., Abnormal Security) handle varying content quality bars

### Additional Content Needs

  - Gitpod interested in outsourcing technical tutorials and "how-to" guides
  - High stakes due to instructing users to run code
  - GrowthX exploring similar solutions for other clients (e.g., Webflow's developer docs)
