## Key Topics

### School AI Product Overview

  - Core offerings: teacher tools, assistants, and student-facing "Spaces"
  - Spaces: adaptive learning environments with 140+ languages, accessibility features
  - Teacher dashboard (Mission Control) for real-time student engagement tracking
  - Upcoming: student product, parent dashboard, admin dashboard
  - Free tier available with usage limits (e.g., 75 daily space sessions)

### Competitive Landscape

  - Saturated market with numerous entrants; schools deciding on AI products within 18 months
  - Key competitors: Magic School (teacher productivity focus), Brisk (Chrome extension)
  - School AI aims to be a full-stack platform for schools
  - Expanding into higher ed, alternative education, and corrections

  - Launching June 2025
