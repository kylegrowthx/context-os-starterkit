## Summary

GrowthX and Teero discussed expanding their engagement from editorial content ($9K/month) to a comprehensive $18K/month package including editorial, programmatic SEO (1,200 pages/year), and lead magnets (PDFs with exit and intent popups). While Teero is seeing healthy results from current editorial efforts (nearly 500 clicks/week), Nate Anderson expressed concerns about ROI on editorial-only work and requested more concrete examples and volume estimates for programmatic SEO implementation before committing to the expanded scope.

## Context

Teero is a dental services platform that matches hygienists with employers. The company has been experiencing rapid growth across the Northeast and is now preparing for expansion into Texas, with Austin selected as their U.S. hub ahead of New York and Chicago. Teero has been working with GrowthX for editorial content for the past 12 months and is approaching renewal. GrowthX recently raised a $12M Series A at a healthy valuation and has grown to 60 employees and nearly $8M ARR with 50 customers. The meeting brought together GrowthX's leadership (Marcel Santilli, CEO) and delivery team (Kyle Gaudreau, Jakub Rudnik) with Teero's Nate Anderson to explore deeper partnership opportunities as both companies scale.
