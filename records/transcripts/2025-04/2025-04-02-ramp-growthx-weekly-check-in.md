## Context

Ramp is an expense management platform, and this is a standing weekly check-in between Ramp's content and product teams and GrowthX's delivery team. GrowthX is managing Ramp's SEO content strategy, which includes a mix of editorial and programmatic pages covering expense categories (blog, per diem, vendor pages), treasury topics (liquidity position reports, cash flow projections), and comparison pages (alternatives, versus). The engagement has been active for several months, with content performance tracked in Looker and managed through Airtable workflows and a Sanity CMS structure. This particular meeting focused on reviewing SEO performance trends from the previous weeks and determining next steps for underperforming content.
