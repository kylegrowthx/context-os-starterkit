## Key Topics

### Vertical-Focused Strategy

  - Concentrating efforts on 4 key verticals: grocery, fashion, footwear, beauty, home improvement
  - Creating more blogs and content specifically tailored to these verticals
  - Identifying and liberally using video e-commerce-specific keywords across all content
  - Consolidating backlinks to generate higher traffic (5000+ hits vs. 50) and maintain updated content

### SEO Optimization Tactics

  - Sprinkling identified keywords across SM, SEO content, and sales enablement materials
  - Website changes to improve SEO performance
  - Creating quality backlinks to generate substantial, recognizable traffic
  - Future phase: associating backlinks with specific content (e.g., fashion playbook) for better SEO performance

### Content Calendar and Publishing Strategy

  - Integrating SEO articles into the larger content calendar
  - Weekly focus on specific categories, publishing targeted articles
  - Tracking performance and adjusting strategy based on results

### Existing Content Audit

  - 40-50 articles published across beauty and fashion industries
  - Prateek to conduct audit on rankings, clicks, impressions, and topics for tomorrow's meeting
  - Results will inform strategy for new industry-specific topics and content

### Footwear Industry Content Ideas

  - Broad topics: industry statistics, trends, AI usage in footwear brands
  - Product-relevant topics: digital showrooms, video marketing benefits, e-commerce personalization
  - Conversion-focused content: increasing average order value, reducing bounce rates

### Brand Integration in Content

  - Emphasis on showcasing Firework directly or indirectly in articles
  - Mentioning Firework keyword and including phrases like "Firework helping clients like Nike"
  - Incorporating current customer examples and recent deals in content

### Active Footwear Clients and Use Cases

  - DSW: Implemented shoppable videos on homepage and product detail pages
  - JD Sports (parent company) and Finish Line (sub-brand): Active partners
  - Potential upcoming launches: Adidas, Nike (not to be discussed publicly yet)
