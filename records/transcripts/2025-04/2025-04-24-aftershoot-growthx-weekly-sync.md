## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - Shift focus to out-market educational content
  - Easier for AI/writers to produce and edit
  - Allows for standardization of style and guidelines
  - Lower risk compared to product-specific content
  - Builds foundation for future product-led content

### Editorial Process Optimization

  - Develop comprehensive style guide and editorial guidelines
  - Aim to reduce revision rounds (goal: 1 round of minor edits)
  - Implement checklist for common edits
  - Consider reviewing outlines before full drafts to catch misalignments early
  - Improve internal linking strategy, both within new content and to existing pages

### Content Production Goals

  - Set minimum word count / post count targets (e.g. 30,000 words/month)
  - Track drafted vs. published content more closely
  - Add word count and draft/publish dates to content tracking system

### Keyword Research and Prioritization

  - Refresh keyword list with focus on out-market topics
  - Implement AfterShoot's prioritization matrix:
      - Factors: search volume, business relevancy, topical authority, audience relevancy
      - Calculate quality score (multiply relevancy factors)
      - Set minimum quality score threshold (e.g. 40)
      - Prioritize based on total score (quality score \* volume)

### Distribution Strategy

  - Explore channels beyond SEO (e.g. social, email, community)
  - Consider distribution potential during content planning phase
  - Focus on tip-based content and articles with expert quotes for wider appeal

### On-Page SEO and Publishing

  - Improve process for optimizing meta descriptions, titles, etc.
  - Show internal linking work earlier in the draft process
  - Fact-check all AI-generated content and quotes
