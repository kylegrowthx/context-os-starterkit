## Summary

AfterShoot and GrowthX aligned on a major content strategy pivot — shifting from product-led content to focus exclusively on out-market educational content, which the team found easier to produce, edit, and scale through AI and writers. Rasheed proposed three core improvements: establishing comprehensive editorial guidelines to reduce revisions from 2-3 rounds to 1, implementing a prioritization matrix to select keywords based on search volume and business relevancy, and ensuring internal linking happens earlier in the workflow. The team committed to setting a minimum production goal of 30,000 words per month and preparing detailed outlines for review to catch misalignments before drafting.

## Context

AfterShoot is a photo editing and visual content platform (photography-focused SaaS). GrowthX is supporting them with a comprehensive content marketing program that includes both product-led content (feature-focused articles) and out-market educational content (broad photography advice unrelated to their product). The engagement was launched about a month prior to this call, and the team had been testing both content tracks while iterating on the production workflow. However, AfterShoot's editor Monalisa flagged that product-led content required 2-3 rounds of revisions and wasn't scaling efficiently, while out-market educational content moved faster and generated better results. This weekly sync was called to reset strategy and address workflow bottlenecks blocking publication velocity.

## Overview

- **Pivot to out-market content:** Stop trying to do product-led and educational content in parallel. Focus exclusively on out-market educational content, which moves faster through the production workflow, requires fewer revisions, and is easier for LLMs and writers to handle.
- **Establish editorial guidelines:** Create 10-12 concrete editorial and writing guidelines (intro structure, tone, style, brand voice) that both teams agree to upfront. This reduces feedback loops and revision cycles from 2-3 rounds to 1.
- **Implement keyword prioritization matrix:** Use AfterShoot's scoring model (quality score = business relevancy × topical authority × audience relevancy, minimum threshold 40; total score = quality × search volume) to select high-impact keywords before writing.
- **Set production velocity targets:** Establish a minimum monthly commitment of 30,000 words (approximately 10-20 blog posts) and a target of 1 revision round maximum to drive urgency and track progress.
- **Improve internal linking process:** Ensure internal linking is visible and discussed in content drafts before publishing, not added retroactively. Include relevant article links in writer briefs so they can link naturally during drafting.
- **Review outlines before drafting:** Have AfterShoot (Rasheed/Monalisa) review 5 content outlines by Monday before full drafts are written, catching misalignments early and reducing downstream editing burden.

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - Shift focus to out-market educational content
  - Easier for AI/writers to produce and edit
  - Allows for standardization of style and guidelines
  - Lower risk compared to product-specific content
  - Builds foundation for future product-led content

### Editorial Process Optimization

  - Develop comprehensive style guide and editorial guidelines
  - Aim to reduce revision rounds (goal: 1 round of minor edits)
  - Implement checklist for common edits
  - Consider reviewing outlines before full drafts to catch misalignments early
  - Improve internal linking strategy, both within new content and to existing pages

### Content Production Goals

  - Set minimum word count / post count targets (e.g. 30,000 words/month)
  - Track drafted vs. published content more closely
  - Add word count and draft/publish dates to content tracking system

### Keyword Research and Prioritization

  - Refresh keyword list with focus on out-market topics
  - Implement AfterShoot's prioritization matrix:
      - Factors: search volume, business relevancy, topical authority, audience relevancy
      - Calculate quality score (multiply relevancy factors)
      - Set minimum quality score threshold (e.g. 40)
      - Prioritize based on total score (quality score \* volume)

### Distribution Strategy

  - Explore channels beyond SEO (e.g. social, email, community)
  - Consider distribution potential during content planning phase
  - Focus on tip-based content and articles with expert quotes for wider appeal

### On-Page SEO and Publishing

  - Improve process for optimizing meta descriptions, titles, etc.
  - Show internal linking work earlier in the draft process
  - Fact-check all AI-generated content and quotes
