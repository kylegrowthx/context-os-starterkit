## Context

Galileo is a generative AI evaluation and benchmarking platform working with GrowthX on a content marketing program focused on LLM-related topics (LLM evals, agents, multimodal AI, metrics, security). This is a recurring weekly sync between Vivek Shankar (GrowthX delivery lead) and Conor Bronsdon (Galileo's CMO/head of growth), established to track content performance, align on strategy, and discuss capacity and expansion opportunities. The program has shown strong early results: organic search traffic doubled in five months, LLM referral traffic grew from 13 to 62 sessions (with VB2 as the largest traffic source, indicating healthy brand visibility), and key content clusters like agents are consistently ranking in the top 10.
