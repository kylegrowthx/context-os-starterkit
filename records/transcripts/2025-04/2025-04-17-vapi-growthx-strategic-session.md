## Overview

- GrowthX is developing a comprehensive strategy focusing on editorial content, programmatic page creation, and website health optimization
- The approach aims to establish Vapi as an authority in voice AI, targeting both high-volume and high-intent keywords
- Plans include creating use case pages, feature pages, and potentially a catalog of customer applications to drive traffic and showcase Vapi's capabilities

---

## Key Topics

### Current Website Audit and Quick Wins

  - Over 90% of traffic comes from branded searches
  - Many pages are not properly indexed due to linking directly to homepage sections
  - Immediate fixes: Create separate pages for better indexing, add CTAs, improve internal linking

### Content Strategy Overview

  - Aim: Be effective before efficient, focusing on thoughtful, strategic content creation
  - Approach: Combine editorial content with programmatic page generation
  - Goal: Create a "knowledge graph" for Vapi, similar to successful examples like GoodRx (30,000 pages)

### Programmatic Content Generation

  - Creating thousands of landing pages based on various combinations (roles, functions, verticals)
  - Focus on zero-volume opportunities that anticipate future search trends in voice AI
  - Structure: Flat hierarchy with tags for easy navigation and search engine understanding

### Editorial Content Strategy

  - Develop feature pages, use case pages, and in-depth technical content
  - Balance between broad topics (e.g., "AI voice") and specific, high-intent keywords
  - Potential topics: compliance (e.g., HIPAA), benchmarking, technical deep-dives

### User Experience and Demonstration

  - Plan to create interactive demos of voice agents on use case pages
  - Challenge: Need to work on embedding functional agents directly on pages

### Competitive Analysis

  - Brief mention of competitor (Retail AI) having basic pages for various use cases
  - Acknowledgment that Vapi's strategy will go beyond this level of implementation
