Awais' team conducts daily email outreach to acquire backlinks from qualifying websites. Existing requirements are AS30+ (Ahrefs Score) and 10K+ minimum monthly traffic. The approval workflow involves a shared spreadsheet where Jason/team review and approve recommended sites. Domain ratings are tracked using SEMrush metrics.

### Expanding Backlink Quantity and Quality

Jason requested a significant increase in both the number of websites and quality of backlinks. The new target is DR 50+ from SEMrush (with 40+ as a fallback if supply is insufficient). Awais committed to adding 100+ new website options to the "Price Request" tab for Jason's review and approval, focusing on scaling while maintaining or improving link quality.

### Workflow and Spreadsheet Management

The shared spreadsheet has three key tabs: "Website Suggestion" (approved sites awaiting publication), "Price Request" (new sites pending approval), and "Live Link" (sites with pending article publication). The workflow is: Awais suggests sites → Jason approves by moving them to Website Suggestion tab → Awais publishes articles on those sites. Sites move to Live Link when articles are published and pending payment.

### Ongoing Projects and Pace

SmithAI is the active project in progress, with a standard pace of 2-3 articles per week preferred. Seven websites in the Website Suggestion tab are waiting for confirmation that clients still want articles published on them. Awais has demonstrated capacity to accelerate to 50 articles in 2-3 days if urgent projects require it.

### Payment and Publication Blockers

Two articles per project in the Live Link tab are awaiting payment confirmation before publication can proceed. Seven additional sites are awaiting confirmation from Jason's team that the respective companies/projects still want the articles published.
