## Key Topics

### Current and Projected Device Count

  - Currently at 50 users, not all on company devices (some BYOD)
  - Projecting growth to 110 devices by year-end
  - Kandji licensing works in 25-device packs after initial purchase

### Pricing Negotiation

  - Mosul offering $3/unit/month
  - Kandji's initial quote: $8/device/month ($81.90/device/year for 75 devices)
  - GrowthX AI target: $75/device/year
  - Final agreed price: $75.60/device/year ($37.80/month for 50 devices)
  - 40% discount applied to reach this price point

### Contract Terms and Requirements

  - GrowthX AI requests:
    1.  No auto-renewal clause (Kandji needs approval)
    2.  Restricted use of company name in marketing (pre-approved clause available)
    3.  California or Delaware as governing law venue (California confirmed in Kandji's MSA)
  - Kandji unable to accept redlines for 50-device plan
  - Net 30 payment terms confirmed as standard
  - Annual upfront billing structure expected

### Signing Process

  - Kandji to send link for electronic signing through DealHub
  - Only GrowthX AI's CEO currently has signatory approval
  - Kandji will pre-populate billing info in the deal room
