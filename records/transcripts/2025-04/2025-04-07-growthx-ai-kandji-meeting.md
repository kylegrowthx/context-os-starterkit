## Context

GrowthX AI (Yousef Hamade's company) is evaluating Mobile Device Management (MDM) solutions for employee devices, currently at 50 users with plans to scale to 110 devices by year-end. They're in active negotiations with Kandji, a vendor who handles vendor sales (Chris Bradshaw). The main tension: Kandji's APIs and capabilities are superior to competitors like Mosul, but Kandji's pricing ($8/device/month = $81.90/device/year) is higher than Mosul's offer ($3/device/month). GrowthX AI's goal is to get Kandji's price closer to $75/device/year while securing favorable contract terms (no auto-renewal, restricted marketing use of the company name, and California-based dispute resolution).
