## Summary

GrowthX and Yourco aligned on a major content strategy pivot to balance broad search appeal with ICP targeting. The core issue: Yourco's heavy focus on "non-desk" keywords was limiting visibility for broader terms like "best texting app for business" while ranking for low-volume keywords that drive minimal traffic. The team agreed to use general article titles paired with dedicated sections addressing non-desk workers and specific industries (manufacturing, construction, healthcare) — driving higher impression counts without sacrificing ICP relevance. Sadira has regenerated 10 articles with this new approach; Jessica will review for fast-track publishing by week's end, with 10 more articles incoming. Next week's meeting will cover Yourco's sales process and lead journey to identify content gaps where GrowthX can add targeted support.

---

## Context

Yourco is a B2B communication platform for frontline workers and non-desk employees. GrowthX is their content marketing partner, responsible for building out Yourco's organic visibility in competitive SaaS keywords. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's standard content delivery cadence. Yourco's business depends on capturing high-intent search traffic from HR leaders and operations managers in transitional industries (manufacturing, construction, healthcare), but current content strategy was over-indexing on "non-desk" modifiers, which was limiting discovery for broader buyer searches that don't explicitly use that language. The data showed this was costing them significant search visibility and click volume — a strategic problem that needed immediate correction.
