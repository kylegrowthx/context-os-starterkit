## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Review

  - Cancun showing growth in impressions and clicks for GrowthX articles
  - "Things to do in month" articles performing well across destinations
  - Nightlife content for Cancun showing good traction with room for improvement
  - Vallarta seeing success with "Is it safe?" article, driving significant traffic

### ROI Tracking and Reporting

  - Current challenge: attributing revenue directly to blog content
  - Approximately $50,000 in revenue linked to articles since January (vs. $54,000 in agency fees)
  - Team to develop more comprehensive reporting method, including:
      - Dividing organic traffic between main pages, tours, and blog
      - Tracking first-click vs. last-click attribution
      - Cumulative revenue tracking from the start of engagement

### AI Search Optimization

  - AI overviews (e.g., from Gemini) becoming more accurate and comprehensive
  - Only 5% of AI overviews contain exact search queries, suggesting need for broader keyword targeting
  - Transactional searches still primarily happening on traditional Google search
  - Adventures Group showing well in AI results for tour-specific queries, less so for general travel tips

### Content Strategy Improvements

  - Leverage Instagram content (videos, images) in blog posts
  - Create more tour-specific content (e.g., packing lists, experience descriptions)
  - Develop local insight articles (e.g., best dishes according to locals, restaurant guides)
  - Use data to create engaging infographics about tour popularity and traveler preferences
  - Implement schema markup to enhance search visibility (to be discussed with technical SEO consultant)

### Content Production Updates

  - Team working on refreshing existing content (e.g., Things to do in Tulum)
  - 8 new articles planned for completion by end of week 15
  - Initiative to generate original photos and content for destinations (e.g., malecon in Puerto Vallarta, murals)
