## Context

Strada is a 2.5-year-old AI voice platform founded by Amir Prodensky and others, operating in the insurance industry space. The company went through Y Combinator's Summer 2023 cohort and has moved past an early "bumpy" phase to now focus on scaling. Strada works with major customers including the largest trucking insurance broker in the US, major insurtech providers, and Turo hosts—helping insurance brokers and carriers automate inbound calls (customer inquiries about coverage) and outbound calls (finding new agents, selling policies). The company has grown its team and is now ready to scale aggressively across hiring, marketing, and business development.

GrowthX Labs is an AI-powered content marketing agency built by Marcel Santilli that serves B2B companies. The team includes growth experts like Kyle Gaudreau (who transitioned from customer success to sales after being GrowthX's first customer), directors of growth and SEO who've run programs at major brands, and infrastructure/engineering teams. GrowthX's approach combines AI-powered content creation with human expert oversight to solve what it sees as the core problem in organic growth: scaling requires hiring 7-10 specialists, which is expensive. The agency uses workflows (represented in code) to automate parts of the process while keeping experts in the loop for quality and strategy.
