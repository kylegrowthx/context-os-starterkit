## Summary

Marcel and Matthew presented GrowthX's scaled structure—now 50 customers and 50 employees with a Chief Content Officer, industry-expert Managing Directors, and expanded graphic design capabilities—while Maddy gave blunt, specific feedback on Smith.ai's content execution. Key issues: thin imagery in blog posts, lack of data-driven reporting (need weekly SEO metrics on keyword rankings, not just new-content KPIs), duplicates and outdated topics cluttering Airtable pitches, and generic tone that Maddy wants replaced with friendly, clever, sarcastic voice.
