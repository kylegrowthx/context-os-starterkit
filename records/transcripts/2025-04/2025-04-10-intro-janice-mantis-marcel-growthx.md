## Context

Janice Liu, CEO of Mantis Group, connected through Candy (their shared contact from Homebase, GrowthX's first customer). Mantis operates as a bootstrapped, 13-person data consulting and commercialization firm, pivoting aggressively toward AI-driven solutions after downsizing from 26 employees. The company serves two main customer segments: brands looking to optimize marketing through data-driven approaches (with a focus on transforming them from cost centers to profit centers) and media owners seeking to monetize their audience data. Mantis owns access to Vertical Scope's network of 400+ domain-specific forums (RateMD, Toyota Nation, etc.), creating rich behavioral and intent data in verticals like healthcare, automotive, and finance. GrowthX, by contrast, is a content and organic growth services firm that recently raised $12M Series A and operates a proprietary workflow engine for enterprise content production—effectively serving as an outsourced content and growth team for B2B customers. Both companies operate in the data/content space with complementary but previously disconnected capabilities: Mantis has data and monetization expertise but lacks content production; GrowthX has world-class content expertise (chief content officer Matthew previously ran TechCrunch) but has historically relied on customers to provide their own data and insights.
