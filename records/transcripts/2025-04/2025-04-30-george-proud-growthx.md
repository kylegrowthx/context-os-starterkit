## Context

Gendo is a UK-based, seed-stage startup building an AI design tool for architects and designers. The platform streamlines image production—a historically time-consuming and expensive step in architectural workflows—by generating images that respect the designer's intent rather than producing random AI artifacts. They target small-to-medium architecture firms (average 7 people) with a product-led growth (PLG) motion and self-serve subscription model. Currently an 8-person team expanding to 12-13, they've seen strong organic traction with minimal marketing effort but recognize they need to increase their market presence and differentiate online. George Proud, their founder, reached out to GrowthX after being referred by the RunPod growth team, whom Gendo uses as their serverless provider. GrowthX is pitching an opportunity to own Gendo's content strategy before their new growth marketer (Joe) joins at the end of May.
