## Key Topics

### Current Semrush Usage at growthx.ai

  - Team heavily uses Semrush, especially for keyword gap analysis
  - Semrush is part of onboarding documentation for new team members
  - Primarily monitor blog content rather than entire website for most customers

### Semrush Enterprise Features Demo

  - What's Happened Report: Provides quick (5-minute) overview of website performance changes
      - Identifies traffic changes, keyword winners/losers, and SERP feature changes
      - Can focus on specific URL paths or subdomains (e.g., blog only)
  - Keyword Clustering:
      - Uses AI to group semantically related keywords
      - Aggregates search volumes for clustered keywords
  - Content Analysis Tool:
      - Compares content performance against top competitors
      - Provides content score and recommendations for improvement
  - Content Brief Generation:
      - Creates detailed outlines and writing instructions
      - Includes AI prompts for content generation (less relevant for growthx.ai's custom AI workflows)
  - Forecasting Tool:
      - Projects potential traffic and revenue gains based on ranking improvements
      - Useful for demonstrating SEO value to clients

### Google Analytics Integration

  - Can incorporate conversion data, revenue, and other key metrics from GA
  - Customizable dashboards to focus on specific KPIs (e.g., non-brand traffic, conversion events)
  - Particularly valuable feature for growthx.ai team and their clients

### growthx.ai's Specific Needs

  - Ability to create custom dashboards for individual client needs
  - Interest in conversion tracking and event data visualization
  - Need for efficient processes, especially for newer team members
