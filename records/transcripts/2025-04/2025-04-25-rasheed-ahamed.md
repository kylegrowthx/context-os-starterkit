## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Reduce scope and increase efficiency by focusing on fewer content categories
  - Team feeling overwhelmed with multiple programs and content types
  - New hire (Minus) joining to support growth efforts
  - Hiring for Head of Content position to strengthen content function

### Contract Terms Discussion

  - Current contract: 12-month term (including 3-month pilot) starting Feb 24, 2025
  - AfterShoot proposing 6-month term post-pilot (effectively 9 months total)
  - Rationale: Internal changes, team restructuring, potential new product launch
  - Kyle to discuss flexibility with GrowthX leadership

### AfterShoot's Marketing Mix

  - Social media (organic + paid): Major focus, very active on Instagram and Facebook
  - Brand campaigns: Events, sponsorships (e.g., $1M fund for event organizers)
  - Referrals/word-of-mouth: Strong due to product's impact
  - Partnerships: Influencers and ambassadors creating promotional content
  - Search (organic + paid): Minimal impact, 90% branded searches

### Video Content Challenges

  - Struggle to scale long-form video content production
  - Tried in-house production (team too busy) and agency production (lack of subject expertise)
  - Currently testing having video creators on payroll as spokespersons
  - Continued investment in video, but workflow still a work in progress

### Future Opportunities

  - Potential for GrowthX to expand offerings beyond pure SEO
  - Explore ways to incorporate existing video content into SEO strategy
  - Consider programmatic content opportunities for educational purposes
