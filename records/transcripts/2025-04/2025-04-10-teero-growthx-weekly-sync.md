## Context

Teero is a marketplace platform connecting temporary dental hygienists with practices and DSOs across the United States. The company is based in Amsterdam (where founders and their partners are based) and operates a two-sided marketplace for hygienist staffing. GrowthX is providing content marketing services to drive organic visibility and conversions from search engine traffic. This weekly sync is part of an ongoing engagement to develop content strategy, analyze SEO performance, and refine content that converts high-intent searches (particularly around employment classification and compensation) into app signups and hygienist placements. Teero's business model depends heavily on supply-side attraction (getting hygienists to use the platform), making content about employment benefits, regulatory clarity, and competitive wages critical to their acquisition strategy.
