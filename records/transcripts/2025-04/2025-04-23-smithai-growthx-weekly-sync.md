  - Potential impact from March 2023 core update; may indicate duplicate content issues

### Content Optimization Strategy

  - Plan to review and optimize existing content weekly, starting with high-value pages ranking for multiple keywords
  - Maddy suggested tiered approach: categorize optimization efforts into Tier 1, 2, and 3 based on impact and effort required, focusing on high-intent keywords and deprioritizing generic keywords like "receptionist" that may attract careers traffic
  - Strategy emphasizes finding true signal in small sample sizes and avoiding low-ROI cleanup work

### Programmatic Page Wireframe

  - Elvis proposed a hub page structure with sections on marketing strategies (SEO, social, ads), AI tools for executing those strategies, and AI receptionist for handling inbound demand
  - Maddy flagged that the current structure lacks narrative coherence — the page needs a unified theme that explains how all three pillars connect for a small business owner
  - Suggested reframing page title to "How to leverage AI to grow your [industry] business" or similar to create an umbrella concept
  - Maddy outlined ideal narrative flow: (1) prove marketing channels work, (2) show tools to execute at scale, (3) explain demand capture/fulfillment via AI receptionist
  - Emphasized need for small business-friendly language (avoid "lead generation" jargon, use "ads" instead, etc.)
  - Julian (Webflow specialist) can help source appropriate design components; priority is strategy and content, not design polish at this stage

### Backlink Reporting

  - Previous backlink efforts (15-20 mid to high DA) still pending
  - Jason (Head of GoToMarket) now managing backlink process
  - Team to provide updates on backlink progress and add to future reports
