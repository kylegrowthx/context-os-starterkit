## Summary

Gitpod and GrowthX aligned on a revised content delivery model: shifting to a bi-weekly cadence (10 articles every two weeks) delivered by EOD Thursday for Friday review. Following internal brand positioning discussions, Gitpod temporarily removed AI-focused content from their blog but will republish it once messaging strategy solidifies; the team confirmed future content will focus on developer productivity and security topics. Site performance remains positive with 17k impressions and improved average position despite recent content changes, with stabilization expected as the March Google core update volatility subsides over the next 1-2 weeks.

---

## Context

Gitpod is a development platform company that GrowthX is providing content marketing services for. Jenn Peters leads the account from GrowthX, with support from Deya Bhattacharya and Dave Capone on the team. On the Gitpod side, Talia Moyal (head of content/marketing) and Louis Bichard (leading website structure and messaging strategy) are the primary stakeholders. The account is maturing past initial content ramp-up; the team is now refining how Gitpod positions itself, particularly around AI and automation—topics that intersect with Gitpod's offering but don't yet align with their brand positioning. GrowthX is helping Gitpod navigate the tension between SEO content volume (10-15 posts monthly) and careful brand messaging, while also solving for authorship strategy (how to attribute posts without creating quality expectations misalignment internally).
