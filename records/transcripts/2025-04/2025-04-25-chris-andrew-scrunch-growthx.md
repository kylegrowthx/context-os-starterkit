## Summary

GrowthX explored a partnership opportunity with Scrunch AI, an AI search monitoring platform founded 18 months ago. Chris Andrew, Scrunch's CEO, demonstrated their persona-based monitoring tool that tracks presence, position, sentiment, and citations across GPT, Claude, Perplexity, Meta, and other AI search engines — viewing monitoring as a "commodity wedge" into broader capabilities for content creation and distribution. GrowthX is interested in integrating Scrunch's API data into client dashboards (via Looker Studio initially) to track AI mentions and attribute referral visits, while both parties explored deeper co-marketing opportunities including webinars, white papers, and thought leadership positioning.

---

## Context

GrowthX is exploring how to integrate AI search monitoring into its client delivery and internal reporting. The company works with approximately 50 B2B customers, primarily helping with SEO and growth strategy — a service that inherently requires tracking performance across search channels. As AI search becomes increasingly important, customers are asking GrowthX what it's doing to help them maintain visibility and drive referral traffic from AI engines like ChatGPT and Claude. Kirkland Gee and Jason Gong are building proprietary performance dashboards that will eventually consolidate GSC data, AI search metrics, and attribution into a single unified view for clients.

Scrunch AI is a 11-person seed-stage startup (founded 18 months ago, raised $4M from Mayfield) offering AI search monitoring and planning to move into broader capabilities around content creation and distribution infrastructure. Chris Andrew, the CEO, has significant experience with enterprise data platforms and is positioning Scrunch as a data provider to agencies and growth teams rather than solely as direct-to-customer software. The partnership would involve GrowthX consuming Scrunch's API to feed client dashboards, with potential for mutual referrals and co-marketing (webinars, white papers) as the space evolves.
