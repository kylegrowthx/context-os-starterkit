## Key Topics

### Current Onboarding Challenges

  - New hires experiencing information overload with numerous documents and videos
  - Some facing technical issues with tools like Fathom for accessing training materials
  - Notion documentation extensive but not well-organized by role, causing confusion

### Upcoming Onboarding Improvements

  - Slack cleanup to clarify channel purposes and auto-add new hires to relevant channels
  - Creation of dedicated onboarding channel for each cohort
  - Implementation of Linear for project management, integrating with Slack
  - Development of structured 12-week onboarding checklist in Linear
  - Clearer role descriptions and org chart to be released next week

### Tool Access and Expenses

  - 1Password TeamVault as first stop for shared tool accounts (SEMrush, Ahrefs, Surfer SEO, etc.)
  - Wits supporting customer-facing team with tool access escalation
  - "Freedom and responsibility" expense policy: spend company money to do your job better, but spend responsibly as you would your own
  - Directors receive vendor cards for tool purchases; one-time cards available for specific projects
  - Example: Nem and Elvis approved for Superhuman monthly trial to evaluate the product for content work

### PTO and Work Hours

  - Unlimited PTO policy; no tracking or approval forms required
  - Recommended process: give team and manager 1-2 weeks notice, add all-day calendar events for absence, alert account team
  - Employees encouraged to take national holidays of their own country to preserve team coverage
  - Global collaboration requires 4-hour morning overlap with Pacific Time Zone (e.g., Bridget works Canadian holidays, takes days off when half the US team is absent)
  - Rationale: prevent week-long delays when waiting for responses across time zones

### Referral Program

  - $10,000 bonus for referring hired engineers (6-month retention)
  - $5,000 bonus for referring other hired roles (6-month retention)
  - Google form available for submitting referrals

### Company Culture and Growth

  - Emphasis on first-principles thinking in designing company processes
  - Openness to employee feedback and ideas for improvement
  - Acknowledgment of rapid growth and ongoing development of structures
