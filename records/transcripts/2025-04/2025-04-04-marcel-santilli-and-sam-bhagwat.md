## Summary

Marcel and Sam discussed SEO and growth strategies for Mastra, a JavaScript agent framework that raised $12M Series A and is targeting 300k-400k monthly downloads by February 2026 to compete with LangChain. Sam outlined Mastra's current status (12k downloads, viral growth in mid-February via GitHub and news, new cloud product, ~300 new signups) and core challenge: driving non-brand SEO traffic to complement open-source adoption. Marcel outlined GrowthX's expertise—team includes former TechCrunch editor Matthew Pensarino and SEO leaders from ClickUp, TripAdvisor, Walmart, and Nordstrom—and proposed a user-generated content ecosystem with AI-enriched, SEO-friendly showcase pages, topical authority building around Mastra's book, and creative programs (contests, bounties) to drive engagement.
