      - Updating metas, adding internal links, adjusting content when necessary
      - Exploring related keywords (e.g., "model agnostic") to boost traction

### PR Pitching Strategy

  - Key elements for effective tech reporter pitches:
      - Attention-grabbing, beat-relevant headline
      - Clear newsworthy angle (why now? why important?)
      - Framing against provocative industry trends (e.g., power shift, democratizing force)
      - Insights reporters can claim as their analysis
      - Focus on themes like labor or power dynamics
      - Supporting materials: day-in-life demo, specific use cases, early data
      - Compelling founder quote
  - Timing challenges due to "drip launch" process

### Thought Leadership Content

  - Publish technically deep, well-structured content addressing real industry challenges
  - Focus on unique insights and "hot takes" from internal experts and network
  - Leverage notable figures for expert guides and borrowed authority
  - Active promotion across LinkedIn and email drips
  - Study peer and large tech company examples for structure and formatting
