## Summary

Unstructured.io kicked off a content marketing engagement with GrowthX to dominate search results and own the conversation around enterprise data extraction for AI/ML applications. Unstructured outlined their current approach (generating ~30 articles/month via AirOps) and target personas: technical "champions" within enterprises who recommend the product, and C-suite "buyers" making procurement decisions. GrowthX will conduct an SEO audit, develop a content strategy with topic clusters in Airtable, and create calibration content (examples for both ICPs to align on voice and tone). Unstructured to provide detailed ICP profiles, access to analytics, and content examples; GrowthX will share a style guide and schedule a follow-up to review strategy and calibration pieces.

---

## Context

Unstructured.io is a developer-focused platform for enterprise data extraction and processing, with 60,000 companies using their open source and 28 million downloads. Their immediate focus is converting open source users and inbound leads from Fortune 1000 companies to paid enterprise customers. The product targets data engineering teams handling unstructured data (PDFs, images, documents) for AI/ML applications. This is GrowthX's first engagement with Unstructured; the kickoff establishes the scope: GrowthX will develop a content-driven strategy to position Unstructured as the default answer for data extraction in Gen AI use cases, competing against tools like Reducto, Llama Parse, AWS Textract, and Azure Form Recognizer. Unstructured's website is currently on Framer and transitioning to Sanity CMS over the next ~2 months, which will create an opportunity to launch a redesigned content experience.

## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Approach

  - Using AirOps platform to generate \~30 articles/month for insights page
  - Focusing on keywords/phrases around connectors and organic search terms
  - Tarun uses AirOps and has trained other models (Claude, ChatGPT) for content generation

### Unstructured's Business Model & Target Audience

  - 60,000 companies using open source, 28 million downloads
  - Primary focus on Fortune 1000 enterprises
  - Two key personas: "champion" (technical recommender) and "buyer" (decision maker)
  - Self-serve product for smaller companies/startups, but not primary focus

### Content Goals & Strategy

  - Own search results for unstructured data and Gen AI data topics
  - Create "popcorn-like" easily digestible content for quick understanding
  - Develop content that feels like a "default answer" for data transformation in Gen AI use cases
  - Leverage podcast, webinar content by repurposing into multiple formats

### GrowthX's Approach

  - Human-in-the-loop content creation, augmenting writers with AI tools
  - Will conduct SEO audit, competitive gap analysis
  - Develop "Content OS" in Airtable to organize topic clusters
  - Create calibration content to align on voice/tone for different ICPs

### Voice & Tone Guidelines

  - Simple, concise, matter-of-fact with a slight smile (similar to Apple's approach)
  - Technical content should be easy to digest, light on marketing terms/buzzwords
  - Avoid exaggeration, especially for technical audience

### Competitive Landscape

  - Key competitors: Reducto, Llama Parse, Textract, Azure Form Recognizer
  - Some overlap with traditional ETL tools (Airbyte, AWS Glue) but differentiated by AI focus

### Website & CMS

  - Currently on Framer, transitioning to Sanity CMS in \~2 months
  - New site aims for simple, scrollable "popcorn" experience
