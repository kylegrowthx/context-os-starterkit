## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Service Overview

  - AI-powered workflow engine breaks down content creation tasks

### Conductor's Motivations and Goals

  - Market changes, especially AI's impact on search, driving renewed focus on content and SEO

### Engagement Structure and Expectations

  - Initial "pilot" period focuses on strategy alignment, editorial quality, and early performance indicators
  - Performance metrics: GSC impressions, AI visibility (using Conductor's own product)

### Budget Considerations

  - Conductor expressed preference for a smaller initial engagement
  - GrowthX open to exploring scaled-down options to align with Conductor's budget constraints
