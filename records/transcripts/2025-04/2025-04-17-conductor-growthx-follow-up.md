## Context

Conductor is an established player in SEO and search intelligence (10-15+ years in the market) that has seen the industry fundamentally disrupted by AI search services. The team — led by AJ Kieffer and including Shannon Vize and Lindsay Boyajian — is doubling down on content and SEO as core business drivers in an AI-first world. They want to rapidly build authority and stake a claim in multi-modal, AI-driven search environments (including LLM visibility on platforms like Perplexity), which requires ramping content production beyond their existing in-house capabilities. GrowthX and Conductor have complementary assets: Conductor has deep API access to search/AI data products and 800+ existing pages needing optimization; GrowthX has the AI workflow engine and editorial team to execute at scale. This was a follow-up deep-dive after Lindsay Boyajian's prior conversation with the GrowthX team.
