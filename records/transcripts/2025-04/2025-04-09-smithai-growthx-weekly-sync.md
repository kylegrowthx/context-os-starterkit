## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Moving towards a hub-and-spoke model for content organization
  - Creating comprehensive landing pages that combine AI tools, marketing strategies, and industry-specific information
  - Focusing on high-utility content that provides actionable information for users
  - Incorporating visual elements like diagrams to show how different tools and strategies connect

### Programmatic Content Approach

  - Identified clusters for content creation, including AI tools for various industries/job functions
  - Exploring feature + industry combinations (e.g., "24/7 answering for real estate lawyers")
  - Aiming to create templated, scalable content that can be easily customized for different industries or use cases
  - Planning to start with top 10 industries based on search volume for each content type

### SmithAI Product Evolution

  - Developing a hybrid solution combining AI receptionists and virtual receptionists (to be launched this quarter)
  - Shifting focus from separate products to a modular approach where clients can choose deployment options
  - Need to adjust content strategy to reflect this upcoming change in product offering

### Technical Implementation

  - Utilizing Webflow for creating customizable components and landing pages
  - Planning to create wireframes for both hub and spoke pages before developing content
  - Considering bringing in Julian (new senior growth lead) for Webflow expertise and guidance

### Content Performance Tracking

  - Setting up a Looker studio dashboard for tracking published content performance
  - Planning to include a monthly check-in during meetings to review content traction and traffic
  - Currently working on improving data presentation methods for easier analysis
