## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau conducted a recruiting conversation with Megan Rose Dickey, a journalist with 15+ years of experience covering tech and San Francisco issues, to explore her fit for a content role at GrowthX. Kyle outlined GrowthX's evolution from AirOps-based workflows to a proprietary platform, the company's strategic focus on storytelling to help clients move beyond tactical SEO, and potential opportunities in client-facing content, developing GrowthX's own media presence, and helping clients launch customer story initiatives. Both discussed how AI can enhance journalism without replacing core reporting work, and the conversation highlighted the value of Megan's journalism background in helping software companies connect with audiences through human-centered storytelling.

## Context

GrowthX is recruiting for content roles as it scales its delivery operations and expands its service offerings beyond SEO-focused content production. Megan Rose Dickey is a seasoned journalist and content strategist who was referred through Matthew (a GrowthX leader also with a TechCrunch background). She has spent her entire post-college career in journalism and content creation, including roles as Chief Content Officer at Backstage Capital (managing newsletters, blogs, and podcasts) and at Kite (a consulting company that connected large enterprises like Visa and Kimberly Clark with startups). Most recently, she was covering business and technology at Axios with a focus on San Francisco issues. This conversation was Kyle Gaudreau's initial evaluation of whether Megan's journalism background and storytelling expertise could fill emerging needs at GrowthX—specifically around client-facing content creation, developing GrowthX's own thought leadership, and helping GrowthX's clients tell their customer stories beyond just SEO tactics.

Recruiting non-traditional candidates (like Megan, with journalism rather than growth-marketing background) reflects GrowthX's strategy to bring diverse perspectives to avoid conventional marketing traps and deliver deeper strategic insight to clients.
