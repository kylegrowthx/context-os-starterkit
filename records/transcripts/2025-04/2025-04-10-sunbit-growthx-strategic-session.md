## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Keyword Research

  - Developed extensive keyword research for dental and auto repair clusters
  - Prioritized upper-funnel content to build brand awareness, differentiating from competitors' lower-funnel focus
  - Identified opportunity for merchant-facing content (e.g., running a dental practice, auto service center management)
  - Plan to use CTAs linking to Sunbit's shop directory, enhancing merchant value proposition

### Content Creation Process

  - Will start with two articles on clear aligners (cost-focused and top-of-funnel) for calibration and legal review
  - Aim to scale quickly after initial approval, moving cluster by cluster in weeks 3-5
  - Content will be delivered in batches for easier review and publication
  - Careful approach to cost-related content, using ranges and multiple sources for accuracy

### Technical SEO Audit

  - Overall strong technical foundation with minimal errors
  - Areas for improvement:
      - Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) optimization for better site speed
      - Adding redirects for \~50 404 errors
      - Considering selective indexing of partner/location pages for potential long-tail traffic

### Backlink Strategy

  - Current strategy leveraging merchant partnerships is effective
  - Each new merchant adds a backlink to their Sunbit pre-qualification page
  - PR efforts contribute to backlink profile, though many are behind paywalls

### Collaboration and Next Steps

  - GrowthX to send weekly plans and content deliverables through established channel
  - Potential collaboration with Sunbit's web agency (Adora Digital) on technical SEO improvements
  - Ongoing discussions needed for indexing strategy of partner/location pages
