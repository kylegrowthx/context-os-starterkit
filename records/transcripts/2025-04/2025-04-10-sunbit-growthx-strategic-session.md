## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Keyword Research

  - Developed extensive keyword research for dental (75 articles) and auto repair (35+ articles) clusters, with care credit as a competitive benchmark
  - Prioritized upper-funnel content to build brand awareness and consumer familiarity, explicitly differentiating from competitors' lower-funnel-only focus — Jason flagged this as a key priority
  - Identified cost-related subtopic (e.g., "How much do clear aligners cost?") as separate high-priority cluster due to search volume and conversion intent, but flagged as requiring careful legal handling
  - Identified secondary opportunity for merchant-facing content (e.g., running a dental practice, career paths in auto service) — lower search volume but B2B2C value-add; Jason to provide examples
  - Plan to use CTAs linking to Sunbit's merchant shop directory within content, creating a value add for both consumer readers and merchant partners

### Content Creation Process

  - Calibration phase: Two clear aligner articles (one cost-focused, one top-of-funnel) selected from content OS for David to draft and Sunbit to review with legal and banking teams
  - Batched delivery model (not one-off): Content published in batch cycles to streamline legal review and publication workflow
  - Rapid scaling plan post-approval: Weeks 3-5 will see cluster-by-cluster rollout across dental and auto repair, with speed calibrated based on calibration phase learnings
  - Cost-related content sourcing strategy: Use ranges (e.g., "$X to $Y"), cite dental associations and brand websites directly, note factors affecting price variation, include disclaimers (e.g., "check with your provider before agreeing")

### Technical SEO Audit

  - Overall strong foundation: no GSC errors in thousands, strong link profile from merchant partnerships, clean site structure
  - Areas for improvement to discuss with Adora Digital (Sunbit's web agency):
      - Largest Contentful Paint (LCP) optimization — minor but addressable site speed issue
      - Add redirects for ~50 404 errors currently present
      - Selective indexing of partner/location pages: Currently all 10,000+ pages are no-indexed to prevent clickjacking; Jakub suggested experimenting with indexing small batches of high-relevance pages to capture long-tail traffic (e.g., "[Merchant Name] financing" searches), though this requires product team alignment on click-through handling

### Backlink Strategy

  - Current strategy leveraging merchant partnerships is highly effective: Every new merchant that joins Sunbit links to their pre-qualification page on Sunbit's site, creating a natural, scalable backlink flywheel
  - PR efforts contribute to backlink profile (similar playbook to G2 approach), though many outlets are now behind paywalls limiting their SEO value
  - No additional aggressive link-building initiatives planned; current merchant and PR strategies are performing well and require no further optimization

### Collaboration and Next Steps

  - GrowthX will send weekly Slack updates on current week's work plan and upcoming content deliverables in batches for review
  - GrowthX will coordinate with Adora Digital (Sunbit's web agency) for technical SEO implementation (LCP, 404 redirects, indexing decisions)
  - Jason to send merchant-facing content topic examples to inform secondary content expansion planning
  - Sunbit to review content OS and select couple of articles for calibration; David to draft clear aligner articles; Jakub to complete keyword research on clear correctors/aligners
  - Product team discussion needed on partner/location page indexing strategy (clickjacking vs. long-tail opportunity trade-off)
