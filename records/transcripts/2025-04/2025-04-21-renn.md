## Key Topics

### Current System Overview

  - Two main components: AI workflow engine and content marketing platform
  - AI workflow engine:
      - Built-in IDE for workflow creation and management
      - 41 existing workflows, potential for exposure as API endpoints or micro-apps
      - Similar to Hugging Face Spaces, but more specialized
  - Content marketing platform:
      - Currently using Airtable (strategy) and Airops (production)
      - High learning curve, complex processes
      - Goal: simplify and integrate these tools

### Content Marketing Platform Development

  - Five planned areas:
    1.  Production (replacing Airops)
    2.  Strategy (replacing Airtable)
    3.  Context storage
    4.  Client interaction
    5.  Performance tracking
  - Aiming to differentiate from competitors by addressing all five areas

### AI Workflow Engine

  - Includes framework, runtime wrapper on Temporal, and IDE
  - Potential for client-specific workflow creation
  - Considering gradual opening to more users
  - Competitors: Restack (more stable) and Maslo (building own runtime)
