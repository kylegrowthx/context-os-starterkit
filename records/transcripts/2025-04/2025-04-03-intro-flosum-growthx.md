## Context

Flosum is a Salesforce DevOps and backup platform founded to solve friction points around data backup and deployment automation. Drew and Girish drive product and strategy—Girish emphasized Flosum is "one of the best-kept secrets" in the Salesforce ecosystem, often excluded from RFPs (they call them "dance parties") despite having competitive products in DevOps and data backup. The company is sales-led with a small marketing team. They're releasing a new freemium product later this quarter or early Q4 to reduce friction and expand their go-to-market motion, but currently use freelance content writers who lack the velocity and quality needed. Flosum's domain authority is around 30, borderline for GrowthX's typical engagement threshold. They've engaged a separate SEO agency for backlinks, but Girish felt the work lacked intensity and throughput. The pain is acute: when customers move from Salesforce solutions to Flosum's data backup offering, Flosum doesn't show up in searches—not in Google, ChatGPT, Grok, or other AI search engines.
