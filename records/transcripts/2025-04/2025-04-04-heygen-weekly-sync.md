## Summary

GrowthX presented a comprehensive SEO reboot strategy to HeyGen designed to drive 275k additional monthly clicks, with three core initiatives: a new content strategy (tools pages, programmatic SEO, content refreshes targeting 81k clicks), a major localization opportunity (64-93% growth via Spanish, Portuguese, German, and French translations), and content enrichment using HeyGen's AI avatars. HeyGen is an AI video generation company. GrowthX has been working with HeyGen (led by Nav Singh and Daniel Pak) on SEO content strategy to drive organic traffic growth. The meeting brought together GrowthX's delivery team (Dave Capone, Marcel Santilli, Kyle Gaudreau) and Mariana Montezzana with HeyGen's product leadership to align on a phased execution plan leveraging HeyGen's product capabilities and new team members with localization expertise.
