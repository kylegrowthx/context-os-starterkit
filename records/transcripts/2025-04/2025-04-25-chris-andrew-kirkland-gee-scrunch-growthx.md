## Context

GrowthX is an AI-powered growth agency serving ~50 customers with SEO as their primary offering. The company combines human expertise with AI to deliver growth outcomes at scale. As GrowthX expands their services, virtually all customers are asking them about AI SEO strategy — specifically how to track and improve visibility in AI search platforms and secure citations in AI-generated answers. Chris Andrew is the founder and CEO of Scrunch AI, a 18-month-old startup that monitors web search results across AI platforms (GPT, Perplexity, Meta, Claude, soon Gemini). Scrunch started before most platforms had web search and has grown to 60+ customers since launching sales in late January 2025. This meeting was set up because GrowthX identified a need for a reliable data partner to provide AI visibility metrics, and Scrunch is actively building a partner program where growth agencies and programmatic SEO shops can access monitoring data via API to embed into their own client dashboards.
