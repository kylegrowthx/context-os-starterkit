### Client Management Tools and Processes

  - Clients database in Notion: maps accounts to team members, includes SOWs and work streams

  - Linear being implemented as new project management tool for account task tracking

### Onboarding Resources

  - Onboarding tracker in Notion: suggested timeline for completing various tasks
  - Circle platform: contains training videos and walkthroughs

  - Notion: central hub for company information, client data, and collaboration
