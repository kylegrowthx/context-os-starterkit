## Key Topics

### Current Website Status

  - Built on Framer in 5 weeks, 2 years ago; was never meant to last this long
  - New site being built on Sanity CMS, expected launch June/July
  - Current site has limitations, including inability to add new users

### SEO and Content Strategy

  - GrowthX performed keyword gap analysis vs competitors
  - Created topic clusters in Airtable for review and approval
  - Content will target both technical "champions" and non-technical "buyers"
  - Articles aim to resonate across personas while nudging down conversion funnel
  - Up to 50,000 words per month (roughly 30-70 articles) once at full scale

### Content Creation Process

  - Will start with a calibration article for thorough feedback
  - Style guide created based on reference articles: down-to-earth, not too technical
  - Each topic includes competitor reference, keyword volume, and search intent
  - Tarun (Unstructured) will review and approve final content

### Integration with Sales and Marketing

  - New weekly marketing meeting to share sales team insights
  - Content can be flagged for sales team use in outreach
  - Aim to create a feedback loop between content, sales, and marketing efforts

### GrowthX Operations

  - Dedicated content manager for Unstructured
  - Separate teams for competing clients to avoid conflicts of interest
  - Encourage frequent communication via Slack for questions and feedback
