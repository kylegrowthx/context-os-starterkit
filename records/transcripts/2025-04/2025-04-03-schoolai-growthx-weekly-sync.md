## Summary

GrowthX and SchoolAI celebrated the launch of their first batch of content live on SchoolAI's website and discussed critical SEO and content strategy improvements. ## Context

SchoolAI is an AI education platform that GrowthX has been contracted to support with content strategy and SEO optimization. This weekly sync is a core touchpoint where both teams align on content publication progress, website optimization, and SEO performance. SchoolAI's product centers on AI-powered "spaces" — interactive learning environments that users can create and customize. The content strategy involves publishing guides and blog posts to drive organic visibility, while optimizing SchoolAI's spaces pages (of which 150,000+ exist but only 210 are currently indexed) for search engines. This week marked a significant milestone: the first batch of published content went live on SchoolAI's site after what Jenn described as "a little bit of dry spell."
