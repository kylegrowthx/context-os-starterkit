  - Thin content: Several pages (e.g., industry pages) lack sufficient text content

  - Indexing: 95 pages not indexed by Google

### Technical Fixes

  - Connect title tags for blogs and case studies to auto-populate correctly
  - Remove old, unused pages after the call

  - Consider standardized template for case study pages to improve content depth

### Content Strategy

  - Continue normal content publication schedule (10 articles/week by Martha)

  - Expect improvements once technical issues are resolved
