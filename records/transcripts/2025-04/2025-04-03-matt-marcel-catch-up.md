  - Service-first approach: Solved immediate customer needs, built trust through workshops
  - Organizational structure: Pods led by managing directors (e.g., ex-TripAdvisor, G2, ClickUp leaders)
  - Hiring strategy: High-caliber talent, leveraging international hiring for cost efficiency
  - Key metric: MRR per dollar of headcount cost

### Technology and Product Development

  - Three layers: Service packaging, workflow streamlining, and underlying AI engine

  - Balance between service delivery and automation/tech development
