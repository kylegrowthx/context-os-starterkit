## Key Topics

### Company Overview & Growth Story

The unnamed AI content production company is sub-1-year-old but experiencing explosive growth. Bridget joined in January when headcount was 15; by April it had reached 50. They serve 40 customer accounts including major brands with strong revenue metrics. Their core service pairs proprietary AI workflows with human QA/editing for content production at scale. The long-term roadmap is to move from agency model (all work done internally) to a self-serve platform where customers can log in and execute tasks autonomously, though they acknowledge AI limitations mean human QA will always be needed. Bridget is currently hiring specialized assistants for five organizational units: sales, marketing, operations, tech, and the CEO office.

### Bored Panda: AI Workflow Optimization at Scale

Mariana started at Bored Panda (a Lithuanian entertainment/media company) as an AI text proofreader, checking ChatGPT output for human quality before integration into video scripts for YouTube/Facebook. She was promoted to "Virtual Assistant" and now owns the audio generation workflow using ElevenLabs and Lovo AI, plus checks the end-to-end Monday.com production pipeline. Key achievement: developed a custom ChatGPT bot that formats translator files to spec, saving ~10% of team time. Bigger win: created a comprehensive style guide (designed in Canva) and trained 30 translators from 15 countries on how the AI audio system works, specifically showing them the Studio feature's voice assignment and the unpredictability of language handling. Result: 50% reduction in her own audio-checking workload because translators now understand the "why" and deliver properly contextualized text. She measures success by faster deadline adherence and higher output quality.

### Flax Hospitality: Operations Bridge and Multi-Function Leadership

Mariana is full-time EA to Flax Hospitality's two co-CEOs (a married couple running a multi-business hospitality operation: 2 restaurants, 2 food shops, a winery, a farm, and a catering company). She divides her role into four functions: (1) Administrative—schedule/email/meeting management, Basecamp coordination; (2) Marketing—full autonomy over Instagram/Facebook for all brands, content creation via Canva, analytics-driven posting strategy; (3) Operations—financial bridge (invoicing, tips division via Excel/Google Sheets, R365 accounting system), customer service (catering quotes via Toast, reservation management), vendor relationships; (4) PR—fielding media inquiries and awards-related communications for the owner (a sommelier with industry recognition). Her explicit job is to remove operational burden so her CEOs can be on-site for the physical/customer-facing work.

### AI + Teaching: How Mariana Approaches Scaling Knowledge

Mariana's breakthrough insight from translating work was that if you understand how AI behaves, you translate differently. She didn't receive that teaching herself, so she invested in creating it for others. Her style guide communicated workflows visually (Canva screenshots, examples of what works/fails) and her training showed live AI demos. She explicitly references her ESL teaching background (taught kids, teens, adults for ~5 years) and fashion industry EA experience as foundations for this pedagogical approach. She's now exploring Airtable to automate catering client follow-ups—a specific pain point where she currently manually tracks "follow up after X weeks" conversations.

### Career Trajectory: From Execution to Leadership

Mariana has bounced across industries (Netflix/Amazon/HBO translation, ESL teaching, fashion industry EA, Bored Panda, Flax Hospitality) but the pattern is clear: she moves from tactical work into optimization and team impact. She explicitly stated she doesn't want to be an assistant her whole life. Her ambition is to lead teams in operations and marketing, leveraging her teaching background. She wants one full-time role instead of juggling two because current pay doesn't cover living expenses. She's drawn to positions with growth potential and the ability to scale her impact beyond personal execution.
