## Summary

Holly Simmons, newly appointed CMO at Outreach (joined December 2024), connected with Marcel Santilli from GrowthX to discuss a potential organic content marketing partnership. Outreach is pivoting from SMB/mid-market focus to enterprise customers and shifting its product narrative from XDR-centric to a full revenue platform targeting CROs, RevOps leaders, CMOs, and CIOs—a strategic change that requires more sophisticated marketing approaches. GrowthX pitched its AI-powered content production system (capable of publishing 100k words at $0.10-0.20/word) combined with expert editors and proposed a 12-week pilot project to develop strategy, calibrate style and voice, and begin publishing high-volume topical authority content across Outreach's domain. Holly committed to pulling together her SEO and growth marketing leaders (Deanna Morris, Hadassah, and Hadassah's manager Lex) for a follow-up discussion after receiving GrowthX's proposal.

## Context

Outreach is a mature, well-funded revenue orchestration platform serving enterprise customers like SAP, Siemens, and MX. Holly Simmons joined in December 2024 as CMO to drive growth during a critical strategic transition. Over the past three years, despite maintaining strong domain authority, Outreach has experienced flat or declining organic traffic despite publishing roughly 400 blog articles—with only about 10 driving meaningful traffic. Holly identified this as a key area for investment and is building out her marketing team with new leaders like Deanna Morris (growth marketing) to support more sophisticated, data-driven initiatives targeting enterprise decision-makers.

Marcel Santilli founded GrowthX after previously building organic growth engines at multiple companies, including secretintelligence.com at IBM (which generated $17M in first-year revenue) and AI Mind Institute (which reached 300k newsletter subscribers in six months). Marcel brings deep operational experience with B2B content production at scale, and Karan (GrowthX's lead investor) was on the board at Marcel's prior company, giving Holly and Marcel a shared investor connection and credibility anchor.
