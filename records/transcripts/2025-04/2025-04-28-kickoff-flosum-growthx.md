## Summary

GrowthX and Flosum kicked off their content partnership with a focus on scaling Flosum's organic presence in the data backup space. The team aligned on producing 50,000+ words monthly (roughly 8 articles per week) targeting data backup and DevOps content, with a primary push into data backup to capture customers migrating away from Salesforce's OwnBackup following their acquisition price increase. GrowthX will complete keyword research and gap analysis by next week's strategy session, then enter a calibration phase with initial articles for thorough review before ramping up production velocity.

---

## Context

Flosum is a Salesforce-native DevOps and data management platform that has primarily owned the DevOps space with strong content but lacks visibility in the larger data backup market. Their main competitors in DevOps are Gearset and Capado; in data backup, they compete with OwnBackup (now Salesforce-owned), Broadly, and others. Following Salesforce's acquisition of OwnBackup and resulting price increases, approximately 7,000 OwnBackup customers are seeking alternatives—a major opportunity for Flosum. The partnership begins as Flosum transitions from human-only content creation (a bottleneck) to a hybrid approach with GrowthX handling scaled content production. Flosum's existing website redesign in November improved performance, and a January PR campaign drove significant traffic spikes. They have strong attribution infrastructure in place (Bizible/Marketo Measure and Salesforce CRM integration) to track organic search contributions to pipeline.
