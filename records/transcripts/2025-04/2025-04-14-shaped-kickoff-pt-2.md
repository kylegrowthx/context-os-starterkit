## Context

Shaped is a B2B AI/ML platform focused on recommendation systems for enterprises. Nic Scheltema is leading the go-to-market effort for Shaped, with Tully (co-founder/colleague) setting aggressive growth targets. This is GrowthX's initial kickoff meeting after being engaged to develop and execute a content and SEO strategy. The company is at an early stage of content marketing — they've been running research article summaries and building pillar content (10-20 posts per pillar on topics like connectors, competitors, use cases, models, features, datasets, metrics) at 5 posts per week, with code snippets and human review to ensure accuracy. The core challenge is that their niche market (ML engineers needing recommendation infrastructure) is small and sparsely documented online, making AI-generated content prone to hallucinations. The highly technical audience demands specificity and has low tolerance for marketing fluff, requiring GrowthX to balance content volume with technical depth and accuracy.
