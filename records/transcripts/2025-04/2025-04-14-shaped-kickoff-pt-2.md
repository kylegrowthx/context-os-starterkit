## Key Topics

### Growth Goals and Expectations

  - Tully (colleague) set ambitious goal: 100k visitors by end of Q2 (June)
  - Current traffic mainly branded searches; big growth opportunity in non-branded
  - Kyle (GrowthX): Goals extremely aggressive; typical growth more gradual
      - Early: 20-50 clicks/week
      - Month 3: 150-200+ clicks/week
      - 100k visitors usually seen at 1+ year mark
  - Small serviceable addressable market (SAM) for ML engineers needing recommendations

### Target Audience and Segments

  - Decision makers: CTO, Head/VP of ML, Head of Data Science
  - Implementers: More junior versions of above roles
  - Key segments:
    1.  Marketplaces (e.g., Airbnb, eBay)
    2.  Social media companies
    3.  E-commerce (Shopify integration)
    4.  Media platforms
  - Focus: Personalization features (notifications, email campaigns, homepages, recommendations)

### Current Content Strategy

1.  Research article summaries:
      - Recent research summarized by grad students
      - Posted on Webflow, Reddit, LinkedIn
2.  Pillar content:
      - 10-20 posts per pillar (connectors, competitors, use cases, models, features, datasets, metrics)
      - Human-edited, code snippets, images
      - Publishing 5 posts/week (could potentially increase)
3.  Creative stream:
      - Working with agency + graphic designer
      - Developing infographics for social media

### Content Challenges and Considerations

  - Limited public information on niche topic
  - AI-generated content often hallucinates/inaccurate
  - Highly technical audience requires specificity, averse to fluff
  - Webflow CMS limitations (e.g., code snippet formatting)
  - Time-consuming manual processes for publishing/distribution

### Competitor Landscape

  - Two main competitors (unnamed)
  - Alternative: Building in-house recommendation systems
  - Advantage: Immediate implementation vs. 2-year build time

### GrowthX.ai Next Steps

1.  Conduct competitive research
2.  Develop content/SEO strategy in Airtable
3.  Present recommendations for content clusters and keywords
4.  Begin calibration process for style/format alignment
