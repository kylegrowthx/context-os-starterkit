## Context

This is an ongoing weekly sync between Shaped (a discovery/personalization technology company) and GrowthX (a B2B content marketing and AEO services firm). Shaped is both a GrowthX client and collaborative partner — GrowthX is helping Shaped develop content strategy and blog content, while Shaped is integrating its technology into Shaped's blog as a proof-of-concept. The meeting focused on calibrating GrowthX's content deliverables and getting Shaped feedback on approach, quality, and strategic direction before ramping production. Both teams are strategically conscious that Shaped's target audience (technical professionals in search and recommendations) is highly skeptical of self-promotional content, so the approach emphasizes authentic, unbiased knowledge-sharing with minimal explicit product mentions.
