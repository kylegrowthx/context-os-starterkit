## Key Topics

### Sales Process Overview

  - Inbound leads typically come through the website, often via referrals or LinkedIn posts
  - New qualification process implemented: leads answer questions before booking, get enriched, then routed to sales or DQ sequence
  - Color-coding system for lead qualification: red (no-go), yellow (potential), green (high-quality)
  - Aim to add 4 new proposals, eliminate 2, and close 1-2 deals daily

### CRM (Atio) Management

  - Create new deals in Atio with company details, contact info, and deal specifics
  - Use "handover notes" template for important deal information and context
  - Update deal stages, values, and dates as the sales process progresses
  - Attach relevant documents (e.g., signed contracts) to deal records

### Proposal Creation (PandaDoc)

  - Use specific template (current V3) for creating Statement of Work (SOW)
  - Customize SOW with client details, work stream specifics, and contract terms
  - Adjust pilot period from standard 90 days to 30 days when applicable
  - Send SOW via PandaDoc for client review and signature

### Client Onboarding

  - [Create Slack channels for new clients (prefix: EX-\[ClientName\])](https://fathom.video/share/cHczwx_mczksvws4Gks1_sRR_sBHhQh6?tab=summary&timestamp=5709.0)
  - Set up client workspace in Notion using template, customizing with client info
  - Share Notion workspace with client stakeholders (as guests, not workspace members)
  - Schedule and prepare for kickoff meeting, sharing agenda and asking clients to fill out their sections

### Follow-up Process

  - Send follow-up email after initial call with SOW, Slack channel invite, and call recording
  - Schedule follow-up calls to discuss SOW and address any questions
  - Continue following up if clients go silent, adjusting kickoff dates as needed
