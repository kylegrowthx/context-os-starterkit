## Overview

## Key Topics

### Team Introductions

  - GrowthX team: Kyle (strategy), Jakub (content/SEO), David (managing editor), Nicolas (account manager)
  - Shapes AI: Nic Scheltema (marketing lead), background in AI/ML, product at Uber

### Goals and Priorities

  - Increase website traffic significantly
  - Convert visitors to MQLs (anyone from ICP visiting site considered MQL)
  - Create sales enablement content (lower priority)
  - Prove marketing's value to board

### Current Marketing Status

  - Recently implemented lead capture tools (HubSpot, Koala, Clay)
  - Moving from "zero to one" in lead generation and tracking
  - Content distribution challenges on platforms like Reddit and Hacker News
  - Paid ads on Reddit yielding \~$0.17 CPC

### Content Strategy Considerations

  - Balance between marketing site (for decision-makers) and docs (for implementers)
  - Potential to repurpose docs content into traffic-generating articles
  - Need for engaging content to keep visitors on site

### Measurement and Conversion

  - Basic funnel: Traffic → MQL → SQL → Closed deal
  - Working on implementing proper tracking and attribution
  - Aiming for \~50,000 visitors as a soft target based on paid ad performance
