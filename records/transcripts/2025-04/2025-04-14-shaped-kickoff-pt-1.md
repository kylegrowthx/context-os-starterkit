## Summary

GrowthX kicked off its content marketing engagement with Shapes AI, a vector database and semantic search startup. Nic Scheltema outlined three core goals: drive significant website traffic, convert ICP visitors to MQLs, and prove marketing's value to the board — with an 80/20 focus on top-of-funnel content over sales enablement. The team discussed Shapes' dual-audience challenge (CTOs vs. ML engineers), their transition from zero-to-one in lead capture (HubSpot, Koala, Clay), and strategies for repurposing technical documentation into organic traffic assets across Reddit and Hacker News, with a soft target of ~50,000 visitors based on Reddit paid ad performance ($0.17 CPC).
