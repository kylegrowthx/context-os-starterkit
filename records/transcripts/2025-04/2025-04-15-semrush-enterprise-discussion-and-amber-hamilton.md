## Summary

GrowthX and Semrush discussed upgrading from the Business plan (38 users) to Semrush Enterprise, a customizable platform designed for agencies managing multiple clients' SEO strategies. Jason Gong outlined GrowthX's core workflow: competitor analysis and gap identification during initial strategy phase, followed by content creation using a mix of AI and human experts, with progress tracked through Search Console and GA4. Kevin Batchelor presented the Enterprise platform's key advantages — streamlined workflows for GrowthX's use case, GSC/GA4 integration, forecasting tools, and dedicated Customer Success Manager support — and proposed a detailed demo using Abnormal Securities as an example of a well-configured 100-client agency setup, scheduled for April 22nd at 9am Pacific.

---

## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency providing growth consulting services to clients, with SEO as a core offering. They manage SEO strategies for multiple clients simultaneously, handling keyword research, content creation at scale, technical audits, and progress reporting. GrowthX currently uses Semrush's Business plan with 38 users but feels underutilized — the team primarily uses it for keyword research and basic site audits, while building custom workflows for content generation (mixing AI with human expert review) and reporting in parallel systems like Looker. Semrush had previously approached GrowthX founder Marcel Santilli in November 2024 about the Enterprise platform. This follow-up meeting brought together GrowthX's operations/finance lead Bridget McGillivray and Jason Gong (recently hired, formerly in growth) with Semrush's Amber Hamilton, Kevin Batchelor, and Dorian Foster to evaluate whether upgrading would streamline GrowthX's platform usage and training challenges as the team rapidly scales.
