## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau and Bruno Santilli evaluated a potential content marketing partnership with Simon Huang from Plutaro, a Toronto-based dev agency with 6 people offering "Silicon Valley Grade" automation services. While both parties saw strategic alignment on automation, a fundamental mismatch emerged: Plutaro seeks performance-based pricing to manage risk, while GrowthX operates on fixed $9k/month 12-month contracts. ## Context

Plutaro is a development agency based in Toronto founded by Simon Huang and a co-founder with prior experience in San Francisco. The company focuses on delivering high-quality software products and AI automation at cost-effective pricing, with a team of 6 that deliberately avoids headcount scaling in favor of automation-driven growth. Simon reached out to GrowthX after encountering them on LinkedIn, exploring whether to partner with an external vendor or build sales/client communication automation capabilities in-house.

GrowthX brought Kyle Gaudreau (CEO/co-founder with 19 years in growth and formerly leading customer engagements) and Bruno Santilli (VP of Sales with 16+ years in B2B complex selling) to the call. The conversation centered on potential overlap in automation philosophy, but revealed that Plutaro is primarily focused on sales and operations automation, while GrowthX specializes in organic content creation and top-of-funnel growth.
