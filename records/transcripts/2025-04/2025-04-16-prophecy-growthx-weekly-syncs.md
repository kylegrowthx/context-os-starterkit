## Context

Prophecy is a data transformation platform company focused on self-service data preparation. GrowthX has been hired to execute a content marketing engagement, running a weekly sync to discuss SEO performance and content strategy. The team has published articles that rank well in search results but mostly for low-volume, long-tail keywords — a function of the narrowly-tailored, product-focused approach to article writing. This week, the discussion centered on expanding content scope to capture higher-volume searches while still aligning with Prophecy's positioning, and on long-form content initiatives.

## Key Topics

### SEO Performance and Content Strategy

  - Current articles ranking well, but mostly for low-volume keywords
  - Need to expand scope to target broader, higher-volume searches
  - Balance between product-specific content and general industry topics
  - Focus on fundamentals and one-on-one articles to funnel readers to more specific content

### Target Audience Refinement

  - Shift focus from business analysts to more technical data analysts
  - Prioritize topics relevant to data infrastructure and practical applications
  - Emphasize connection between analytics and underlying data pipelines

### Content Priorities

  - Advanced analytics and embedded analytics identified as high-priority topics
  - Databricks SQL and Spark SQL content to be developed further
  - Reduce focus on basic business analyst topics (e.g., data visualization, hypothesis testing)

### Long-form Content Initiative

  - Plan to convert existing thought leadership blog into an e-book
  - Team to provide detailed brief and chapter outlines for efficient content creation

### Communication and Reporting

  - Streamline weekly updates to reduce noise for broader team
  - Share performance reports before meetings for more productive discussions
  - Maintain open communication channels for strategy points and brainstorming
