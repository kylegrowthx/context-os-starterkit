  - SEMrush acquired multiple brands (e.g., Backlinko, Traffic Think Tank) to dominate SERP real estate

### Content Strategy for GrowthX

  - Potential to involve community members or GrowthX team as contributors
  - Showcase AI-led growth strategies and tools, similar to Backlinko's SEMrush integrations
