## Summary

Jason and Branko discussed GrowthX's strategy for chosendata.com, a recently acquired domain with DR 46 authority, trademark, and existing brand assets. They explored building a portfolio of branded content hubs modeled on SEMrush's acquisition strategy (Backlinko, Traffic Think Tank) — which generated 400k-600k monthly sessions and 7-8x ROI — to showcase GrowthX's AI-led growth methodologies. ## Context

GrowthX recently acquired chosendata.com from Branko Kral as part of an intellectual property clause in his contractor agreement. The domain — which Branko operated as a mostly dormant personal brand during his time running a content marketing agency and later working at SEMrush — carries significant inherent authority (DR 46, trademark registration, social handles, logo files) and complements GrowthX's business model. Jason Gong, GrowthX's director managing the emerging AI-led growth practice, brought Branko onto the team and wanted to validate the strategic potential of building this asset into a flagship content hub. Branko brought deep expertise in portfolio brand strategy from his time building Backlinko, Traffic Think Tank, and other SEMrush-owned properties, where he personally drove content operations and achieved outsized ROI by combining high-authority assets with strategic attribution to the parent company's tools.
