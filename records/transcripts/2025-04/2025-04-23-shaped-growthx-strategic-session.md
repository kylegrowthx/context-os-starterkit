## Summary

GrowthX and Shaped aligned on a multi-pronged content strategy to drive traffic and visibility for Shaped's AI personalization platform. Shaped clarified their developer-first positioning, and GrowthX presented a three-part approach: targeting three key verticals (e-commerce/retail, content companies, B2B) with 30-100 content pieces in month one (blogs, glossary items, case studies), starting with 2-3 calibration articles to nail tone and depth. Shaped's recent success (3,000 visits from a Reddit post, demo from Best Buy discovered via ChatGPT) pointed to demand and the need for better attribution tracking, with first-month success measured qualitatively by content quality and cultural fit.

---

## Context

Shaped is an AI personalization platform founded three years ago by Tullie Murrell, formerly of Meta where he worked on Instagram Reels ranking and ads algorithms for over 10 years. The startup competes in the recommendations and personalization space but positions itself as developer-first and horizontal (like Algolia and AWS Personalize), distinct from marketing-first, e-commerce-focused competitors (Bloomreach, Constructor.io). Shaped recently hit a turning point: after years of sales-driven growth, they're seeing organic traction (3,000 site visits from a single Reddit post, an unsolicited demo request from Best Buy discovered through ChatGPT research). The team—based mostly in Williamsburg, Brooklyn—wants to capitalize on this momentum but lacks mature content marketing and attribution infrastructure. GrowthX was brought in to establish a repeatable content strategy that builds traffic and credibility while Shaped investigates what's actually driving demand (Reddit posts vs. ads vs. organic search remain murky in their PostHog analytics).
