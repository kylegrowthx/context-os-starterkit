## Overview

- Traffic growth is the primary goal, with a focus on building visibility and demonstrating results to the board
- Content strategy will target three main areas: e-commerce/retail, content companies, and B2B, with a developer-first horizontal approach
- Initial focus on 2-3 high-level topics, producing 30-100 pieces of content in the first month (mix of blog posts, glossary items, and case studies)
- Success criteria for the first month will be largely qualitative, based on content relevance and quality

---

## Key Topics

### Recent Marketing Performance

  - Shaped experiencing recent success with inbound leads and site visits
  - 3,000 website visits from a single Reddit post
  - Demo booked from Best Buy, discovered through ChatGPT research

### Content Strategy and Competitors

  - GrowthX presented a three-pronged approach to content creation
  - Shaped clarified their position relative to competitors:
      - Developer-first, horizontal approach (like Algolia, AWS Personalize)
      - Distinct from marketing-first, e-commerce focused competitors (e.g., Bloomreach, Udify)
  - Suggestion to focus on well-known use cases (Netflix, Spotify, TikTok) for content inspiration

### Content Types and Topics

  - Blog posts for in-depth content
  - Glossary items for shorter, definition-style content to cast a wide net
  - Case studies or pseudo case studies on major companies' personalization efforts
  - Repurposing successful Reddit posts into organic content
  - Leveraging research papers and industry data (e.g., Meta attributing $70B+ revenue to personalization)

### Content Production and Evaluation

  - Goal of 30-100 pieces of content in the first month, depending on content type and depth
  - Initial focus on calibrating tone, depth, and style with a few draft articles
  - First month evaluation will be qualitative, focusing on content relevance and quality
  - Quantitative metrics (keywords, impressions) will be tracked but not primary success criteria initially

### Analytics and Attribution

  - Current challenges with accurate channel attribution in PostHog
  - Request for GrowthX's expertise on improving tracking and attribution
