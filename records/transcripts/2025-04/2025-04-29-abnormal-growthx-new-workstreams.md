## Summary

GrowthX (Jason Gong) and Abnormal Security (Abdallah AlHakim, Sara Itzkovics) explored new workstreams to improve lead activation and outbound efficiency. Abnormal receives 30,000-40,000 inbound leads per quarter from content syndication, events, and webinars, but most don't meet MQL criteria—they're redefining MQL definition to reduce volume by 80% while identifying high-quality leads in the remaining pool. GrowthX proposed signal mining, lead enrichment, and trigger-based outreach to activate these dormant leads, with typical booking rates of 2-3% for website de-anonymization and 5-10% reply rates for webinar attendees.

## Context

Abnormal Security is a cybersecurity company using GrowthX for SEO content creation (10-15 articles/week for about 8-9 weeks). Abdallah AlHakim, VP of RevOps/Growth, leads the inbound and outbound efforts; Sara Itzkovics is the newly hired sales ops/marketing ops/content ops leader (8-9 weeks in) responsible for SDR enablement and automation, sitting between marketing and the SDR organization. The relationship is strategic: Abnormal is looking to expand GrowthX's scope beyond content to include full-funnel outbound and inbound lead activation. Abnormal's sales motion is ABM-focused with 6sense integration for intent data on target accounts. Current challenges include low MQL-to-meeting conversion (0.5-1%), a backlog of unqualified inbound leads from high-volume channels (events, webinars, content syndication), and lack of systematic outbound infrastructure across SDRs and account executives—particularly the 150-person mid-market team that has no SDR support.

## Overview

- Abnormal Security receives 30,000-40,000 inbound leads per quarter (from content syndication, events, webinars) but is redefining MQL criteria to reduce volume by 80%, leaving a large pool of unqualified but potentially qualified leads
- GrowthX and Abnormal identified opportunity to triage and activate this pool using signal mining, enrichment, and trigger-based outreach—leveraging GrowthX's existing content relationship and data infrastructure
- Current conversion rates are low: MQL-to-meeting is 0.5-1%, but webinar follow-up (when executed well) can achieve 5-10% reply rates; de-anonymized website traffic shows 2-3% booking rates
- No systematic outbound program exists; Abnormal is looking to accelerate outbound enablement for SDRs and 150-person mid-market account executive team using AI and automation

## Key Topics

### Current Abnormal Security Setup

  - SDRs sit under marketing, not sales
  - Using 6sense for intent data on target accounts
  - Inbound leads come primarily from content syndication, events, and webinars
  - Currently changing MQL definition to improve quality and reduce volume by \~80%
  - No systematic outbound program in place for AEs or SDRs

### GrowthX Capabilities

  - Currently supporting Abnormal with SEO content creation (10-15 articles/week)
  - Can provide end-to-end outbound support: signal mining, enrichment, campaign creation, and execution
  - Flexibility to work within existing tools or set up separate infrastructure
  - Experience with de-anonymizing website traffic for warm outreach
  - Typical success rates: 2-3% booking rate for de-anonymized traffic, 5-10% reply rate for webinar attendees

### Potential Workstreams

  - Triage and activate the large pool of inbound leads that don't meet MQL criteria
  - Implement trigger-based outreach for leads showing specific intent signals
  - Enhance lead enrichment to better qualify and segment inbound leads
  - Develop nurture campaigns for leads not ready for direct sales outreach
  - Improve webinar follow-up process to increase conversion rates

### Abnormal's Current Challenges

  - Low conversion rates from MQLs to meetings (0.5-1%)
  - Lack of systematic approach to handling non-MQL inbound leads
  - No trigger-based outreach processes in place
  - Need for better segmentation and personalization of outreach efforts
