## Overview

- Abnormal Security is looking to optimize their inbound lead process and potentially activate cold leads from events and webinars
- GrowthX could help with signal mining, enrichment, and automated outreach for leads that don't meet MQL criteria
- Abnormal receives \~30,000-40,000 leads per quarter, with only a small percentage currently converting to MQLs
- GrowthX will provide a proposal for potential workstreams after gathering more information about Abnormal's current systems and processes

---

## Key Topics

### Current Abnormal Security Setup

  - SDRs sit under marketing, not sales
  - Using 6sense for intent data on target accounts
  - Inbound leads come primarily from content syndication, events, and webinars
  - Currently changing MQL definition to improve quality and reduce volume by \~80%
  - No systematic outbound program in place for AEs or SDRs

### GrowthX Capabilities

  - Currently supporting Abnormal with SEO content creation (10-15 articles/week)
  - Can provide end-to-end outbound support: signal mining, enrichment, campaign creation, and execution
  - Flexibility to work within existing tools or set up separate infrastructure
  - Experience with de-anonymizing website traffic for warm outreach
  - Typical success rates: 2-3% booking rate for de-anonymized traffic, 5-10% reply rate for webinar attendees

### Potential Workstreams

  - Triage and activate the large pool of inbound leads that don't meet MQL criteria
  - Implement trigger-based outreach for leads showing specific intent signals
  - Enhance lead enrichment to better qualify and segment inbound leads
  - Develop nurture campaigns for leads not ready for direct sales outreach
  - Improve webinar follow-up process to increase conversion rates

### Abnormal's Current Challenges

  - Low conversion rates from MQLs to meetings (0.5-1%)
  - Lack of systematic approach to handling non-MQL inbound leads
  - No trigger-based outreach processes in place
  - Need for better segmentation and personalization of outreach efforts
