## Context

Adventures Group is a travel and tourism business operating in Mexico's Caribbean and Baja California regions (Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Cabo San Lucas, La Paz). They offer tour experiences including dining experiences and water activities, and run companion operations across multiple domains (Cancun Adventure, Vallarta Adventures, Cabo Adventures). GrowthX has been engaged to drive organic growth through content marketing and SEO optimization, including a strategy to scale editorial content around destinations and attractions.

This weekly sync represents ongoing delivery work on a content and SEO engagement. The team is tracking month-over-month organic revenue growth, iterating on template and publishing workflows, and planning a major initiative to implement structured data (schema markup) and build programmatic travel guide pages that aggregate content dynamically. Analytics data accuracy issues that existed before mid-March have now been resolved, allowing the team to confidently track organic channel performance going forward.
