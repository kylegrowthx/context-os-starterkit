## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Move away from broad hub pages to more targeted, bottom-funnel content
  - Focus on creating interactive tools, calculators, and templates
  - Aim for content that directly addresses user intent (e.g. "should I hire or outsource?")
  - Prioritize content that can show clear ROI and move the needle quickly

### Inspiration from Competitors

  - ServiceTitan's approach with interactive tools and templates viewed positively
  - Good Call's content clusters (e.g. answering service, appointment scheduling) noted
  - Aim to fill gaps in Smith.ai's current content offerings compared to competitors

### Interactive Content Development

  - Preference for HTML-based interactive content over static PDFs
  - Examples: ROI calculators, cost comparisons (hiring vs. outsourcing), business valuation tools
  - Ensure all interactive elements are on one page for better user experience
  - Potential to repurpose existing blog content into interactive formats

### AI-Focused Content

  - Maintain AI-related content, but ensure it's closely tied to Smith.ai's specific offerings
  - Position Smith.ai as experts in both AI and answering services
  - Consider content on AI tools that complement Smith.ai's services (e.g. AI copywriting tools for social media)

### Design and Presentation

  - Emphasize clean, professional design for all content
  - Aim for visuals that are appealing to small business owners and easy to understand/use
  - Consider leveraging existing Smith.ai design templates (e.g. integrations page layout)
