### GrowthX Pricing Model and Transparency

- Starting price: $9K/month for 50K words of editorial content
- Typical engagement: $9K-$15K/month for editorial
- Programmatic content starts at $12K/month (roughly 1,200 pages/year; Kyle noted a typo in his materials)
- Flexible word count: can do 25K increments (50K, 75K, 100K, etc.)
- 95% of GrowthX accounts include editorial content as the core service
- GrowthX updates pricing periodically but nothing imminent

### Editorial vs. Programmatic Content Strategies

- **Editorial content:** longer-form, usually blogs but not limited to blogs; focus on creating "network experience" for ICP and answering their key questions; more strategic and branded
- **Programmatic content:** template-based, highly repeatable, scalable; less editorial oversight; higher volume, lower per-unit editorial touch
- Most customers start with editorial; some with mature content functions may prioritize programmatic expansion instead
- GrowthX can layer services: not just keyword-targeted blog posts, but webinar content, ABM amplification, podcast content distribution
