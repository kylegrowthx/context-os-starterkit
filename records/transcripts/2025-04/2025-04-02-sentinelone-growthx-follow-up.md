  - 12-week "boot camp" to set strategy and build momentum
  - Comprehensive site audit, including deep-dive on platform pages
  - Weekly optimizations, content creation, and performance tracking
  - Persona-based content strategy (e.g., CISO, security practitioners)

### Balancing Focus and Strategy

  - Drew flagged that past engagements failed due to scope creep ("boiling the ocean")—emphasis on focused execution for this engagement

  - Key tension: SentinelOne wants immediate platform optimization; GrowthX wants to ensure long-term strategy isn't missed by focusing too narrowly
