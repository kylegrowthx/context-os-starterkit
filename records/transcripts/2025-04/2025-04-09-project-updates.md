### About Us Page Planning

Guille shared Resolve.ai as a reference—simple design with culture tags, values, open positions, and investor info. Jason will gather office b-roll footage and photos for video/visual content. The scope depends on design complexity (simple vs. animation-heavy). Jason confirmed he needs more research time before proceeding, so this task is deprioritized.

### Project Management and Team Setup
