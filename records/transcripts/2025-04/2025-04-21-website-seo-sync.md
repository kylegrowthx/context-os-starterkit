## Key Topics

### SEO Content Buckets

  - Two main buckets identified: retail-related keywords and footwear-specific keywords
  - Retail keywords not directly targeting Firework, but relevant to overall retail industry
  - Footwear keywords focused on footwear-related topics
  - Connected commerce keywords suggested for incorporation into SEO strategy

### Target Industries and Regions

  - US-focused categories: grocery, beauty D2C, beauty retailer, grocery retailer, CPG brands, home improvement retailer, home improvement brand
  - EMEA focus (Europe, Middle East, Africa) with emphasis on EU, UK, France, Germany, Sweden
  - Plan to localize site for EMEA region, potentially launching French and Spanish domains

### Strategic Approach to Content Creation

  - Need for a more holistic approach with clear steps (e.g., publish 200 articles in 4 weeks, create lead magnets)
  - Importance of understanding industry-specific pain points (e.g., tariffs affecting sourcing and margins for fashion retailers)
  - Focus on creating content addressing conversion rate improvement and sales growth
  - Targeting multiple stakeholders within enterprise brands (e.g., digital product, merchandising, paid media, marketing technology, design, IT)

### Website Schema Implementation

  - Comprehensive global schema added to every page on the site
  - New features: rating section, related-to section, social links, 50 FAQs
  - Potential issues with global FAQ schema implementation; need for page-specific schema instead
  - Expected improvements in SEO scores, rich search results, and overall site credibility

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Need for better storytelling and analysis of why specific topics are selected
  - Importance of aligning content with ICPs and addressing specific pain points for each industry
  - Plan to develop a strategic game plan for the next four weeks
