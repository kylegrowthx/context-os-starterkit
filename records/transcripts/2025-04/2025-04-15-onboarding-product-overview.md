## Overview

- GrowthX focuses on "service as software", using AI to reduce manual work while maintaining human creativity
- The company is building a flexible AI workflow engine and content creation platform to replace multiple existing tools
- Engineering team is split between platform development and client-specific workflow/integration needs
- Continuous feedback from the delivery team is crucial for product development and improvement

---

## Key Topics

### Company Strategy and Product Philosophy

  - Shifted from traditional product development to "service as software" model
  - Focus on outcomes and client success rather than specific tools
  - Leveraging AI to reduce manual work while maintaining human creativity in the process
  - Building flexible solutions to accommodate diverse client needs and rapid AI advancements

### Current Technology Stack

  - Multiple tools in use: Notion, AirOps, AirTable, Slack, Google Docs, various CMS
  - Custom-built workflow engine for AI-powered content creation tasks
  - Integration with third-party services (e.g., Perplexity, SEMrush) for research and SEO

### AI Workflow Engine

  - Allows rapid creation of custom workflows for content creation and other tasks
  - Engineers can quickly develop workflows using a simple text-based description
  - Future plans to create a user-friendly interface for non-technical team members to create and modify workflows

### Content OS Platform (In Development)

  - Aims to replace AirOps, AirTable, and potentially Notion
  - Will provide an integrated environment for content creation process
  - Features include customizable pipelines, chat-like interfaces for content iteration, and seamless workflow integration

### Upcoming Features and Improvements

  - Task Hub: Integration with freelancer marketplaces for specific workflow steps
  - Learning system: Automatically collect and analyze edits and improvements for model fine-tuning
  - Micro-apps: Standalone tools (e.g., researcher, image search, fact-checker) for potential use by other agencies

### Engineering Team Structure

  - Split into two groups:
    1.  Platform development team (Content OS, AI Workflow Engine)
    2.  Client ops engineers for workflow needs, client-specific integrations, and micro-app development
