## Key Topics

### Marcel's Background and GrowthX Origin

  - Brazilian-born, moved to Texas at 11
  - Built organic content engines throughout career (IBM, ServiceTitan, HashiCorp, Scale AI, Deepgram)
  - Started GrowthX by teaching workshops, evolved into full-service offering
  - Partnered with CTO Daniel (ex-IFTTT) to build underlying tech platform

### GrowthX Business Model

  - Two sides: customer-facing content services + underlying tech platform
  - Focuses on delivering outcomes, not just tools or partial solutions
  - Uses expert-driven "managing director" model for service delivery
  - Underlying platform: workflow engine, runtime layer, IDE experience, services/workflows
  - Learning engine captures insights from workflow execution and expert input

### Customer Base and Offering

  - Diverse customer base: Series A startups to public companies (e.g., Webflow, Ramp, Reddit)
  - $160K ACV, 48% win rate, \<30-day sales cycle
  - Full-service approach: strategy, execution, and "grunt work" (e.g., content staging, optimization)
  - Team structure: Managing Directors lead pods of specialists (editors, strategists, researchers)

### Growth and Funding

  - Crossed $7M ARR, still profitable (may start burning cash due to rapid growth)
  - Raised $12M Series A at $80M post-money valuation
  - Aiming for $20M ARR, exploring new verticals (e.g., sales) and platform exposure
  - Considering future Series B for M\&A, geographic expansion, and talent acquisition

### Future Plans and Strategy

  - Three-pronged approach:
    1.  Scale current offering to $20M ARR
    2.  Expand into new vertical/domain (possibly sales)
    3.  Further develop and expose the underlying platform
  - Exploring international opportunities (e.g., Mexico, UK)
  - Considering M\&A to acquire expertise and accelerate growth in specific niches
