## Key Topics

### Current Performance Analysis

  - Organic traffic performing well, showing growth
  - Paid channels (primarily Google, then Meta) experiencing issues:
      - Higher cost-per-click
      - Increased traffic but lower conversion rates
      - Resulting in expensive customer acquisition
  - Overall company sales up, but online channel underperforming
  - Spending approx. $1 million/year on paid advertising

### Recent Changes and Variables

  - New website design implemented ($700,000 investment)
  - Changed paid media agency
  - Multiple simultaneous changes creating difficulty in isolating problem variables

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Considerations

  - Agency proposed CRO plan costing $18,000-$21,000/month
  - Marcel suggests this might be overpriced for the scope
  - Emphasis on matching landing page content to user intent for better conversions
  - Potential for AI-driven dynamic page creation to match search intents

### Paid Media Strategy Recommendations

  - Reduce reliance on paid as primary channel due to volatility
  - Focus on retargeting strategies:
      - Pixel visitors for future targeted ads
      - Use search modifiers to increase bids for previous site visitors
      - Implement abandoned cart campaigns
  - Minimize spend on cold audiences, optimize for warm audiences familiar with the brand

### Organic and Paid Synergy

  - Leverage strong organic performance to create retargeting pools
  - Use content to attract visitors, then retarget with paid ads on platforms like Instagram
  - Expand organic reach to increase the pool of potential customers for paid retargeting

### Collaboration and Next Steps

  - Plan a brainstorming session with new paid agency and GrowthX to align strategies
  - Adventures Group to send previous agency reports for GrowthX review
  - GrowthX offers to provide free audit and recommendations
  - Potential introductions to trusted paid media partners for second opinions
