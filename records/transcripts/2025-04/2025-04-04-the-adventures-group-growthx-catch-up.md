## Summary

Isabel Luna and Marcel Santilli discussed The Adventures Group's March sales decline (down 10% overall, but down more significantly in paid channels) despite strong organic growth. While Google and Meta paid channels are driving higher costs and lower conversion rates, organic traffic is thriving after a recent $700,000 website redesign. Marcel offered a free audit of the paid strategy and recommend a dual approach: reduce reliance on cold-audience paid spend while doubling down on organic growth to build a larger pool for retargeting, plus implementing search modifiers and abandoned cart campaigns to optimize warm audience spend. They agreed to schedule a brainstorming session with The Adventures Group's new paid media agency and GrowthX to align organic and paid strategies.

---

## Context

The Adventures Group is a destination experiences company operating tour and activity businesses in multiple Mexican locations (Vallarta, Cabo, Cancun). They are an existing GrowthX customer with whom Marcel has worked on organic growth initiatives. Isabel Luna, who leads operations or marketing decisions for the company, reached out to discuss a performance issue: despite overall company growth across other channels, their online sales have declined 10% in March specifically in paid advertising.

The timing of this decline is complicated by multiple simultaneous variables. The Adventures Group recently invested $700,000 in a website redesign and simultaneously changed their paid media agency. These overlapping changes make it difficult to isolate what's causing the paid channel underperformance. Isabel is considering a $18,000-$21,000/month CRO (conversion rate optimization) proposal from an agency but is unsure if it's justified given the noise created by the recent changes.

## Key Topics

### Current Performance Analysis

  - Organic traffic performing well, showing growth
  - Paid channels (primarily Google, then Meta) experiencing issues:
      - Higher cost-per-click
      - Increased traffic but lower conversion rates
      - Resulting in expensive customer acquisition
  - Overall company sales up, but online channel underperforming
  - Spending approx. $1 million/year on paid advertising

### Recent Changes and Variables

  - New website design implemented ($700,000 investment)
  - Changed paid media agency
  - Multiple simultaneous changes creating difficulty in isolating problem variables

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Considerations

  - Agency proposed CRO plan costing $18,000-$21,000/month
  - Marcel suggests this might be overpriced for the scope
  - Emphasis on matching landing page content to user intent for better conversions
  - Potential for AI-driven dynamic page creation to match search intents

### Paid Media Strategy Recommendations

  - Reduce reliance on paid as primary channel due to volatility
  - Focus on retargeting strategies:
      - Pixel visitors for future targeted ads
      - Use search modifiers to increase bids for previous site visitors
      - Implement abandoned cart campaigns
  - Minimize spend on cold audiences, optimize for warm audiences familiar with the brand

### Organic and Paid Synergy

  - Leverage strong organic performance to create retargeting pools
  - Use content to attract visitors, then retarget with paid ads on platforms like Instagram
  - Expand organic reach to increase the pool of potential customers for paid retargeting

### Collaboration and Next Steps

  - Plan a brainstorming session with new paid agency and GrowthX to align strategies
  - Adventures Group to send previous agency reports for GrowthX review
  - GrowthX offers to provide free audit and recommendations
  - Potential introductions to trusted paid media partners for second opinions
