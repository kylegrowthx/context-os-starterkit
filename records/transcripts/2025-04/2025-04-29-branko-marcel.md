## Context

This is a GrowthX internal strategic alignment meeting between Branko (VP, managing the delivery operations team) and Marcel Santilli from GrowthX Labs, with Matthew Panzarino and Bridget McGillivray. GrowthX has been experiencing growth challenges as it scales from a services business ($200k+/year B2B content marketing engagements) into new service lines (backlinks, PSEO, programmatic SEO) without fully developed systems or workflows. The conversation reflects a company in transition, attempting to systematize delivery while managing client expectations and team morale.

## Key Topics

### Current Team Structure Challenges

  - Misalignment in roles: CMs sometimes communicating with clients, MEs trying to drive strategy
  - New team members (directors, MEs, CMs) onboarding simultaneously while trying to deliver for clients
  - Some key team members (e.g. Jess, Jürgen, Pratik) working long hours to compensate

### Ideal Team Structure and Workflow

  - Directors: Lead strategy, client relationships for first 3 months
  - Managing Editors (MEs): Gradually take over from directors, delegate to CMs
  - Content Managers (CMs): Focus on execution, not client-facing
  - Protected time needed for team training and workflow learning

### Content Strategy Development

  - Need for more thoughtful, research-based approach to content strategy
  - Example given of FathomAI strategy development process
  - Utilize tools like Perplexity, ChatGPT, and internal workflows for research
  - Directors should drive initial strategy, then delegate to MEs and CMs

### Client Onboarding and Expectations

  - More time needed upfront to understand client's product, audience, and competitive landscape
  - Develop clear content clusters and assignments before presenting to clients
  - Show progress across multiple areas for high-budget clients

### New Work Streams and Services

  - Backlinks and PSEO sold before fully developed
  - Need to position new services more robustly (e.g. combine backlinking with distribution, PR, ghostwriting)
  - Balance between selling new services and optimizing for retention/expansion

### Team Member Development

  - Pratik (senior CM) needs support in client communication and strategy presentation
  - Sadira (experienced CM) has potential for promotion to ME, needs evaluation
  - New ME starting May 19th, will need training time
