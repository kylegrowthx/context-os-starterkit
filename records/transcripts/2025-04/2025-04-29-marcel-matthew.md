## Key Topics

### Current Team Structure Issues

  - Roles misaligned: CMs doing ME work, MEs trying to drive strategy
  - New team members at all levels simultaneously onboarding
  - Some key team members (e.g. Pratik, Jess) working long hours
  - Lack of time for team members to fully learn workflows/tools

### Ideal Team Structure

  - Directors: Lead strategy, client-facing in first 12 weeks
  - Managing Editors: Handle execution, gradually take over client management
  - Content Managers: Focus on production, minimal client interaction

### Content Strategy Development

  - Need more intentional approach to understanding client/audience
  - Use tools like Perplexity AI for research
  - Develop clear content clusters and assignments before client meetings
  - Show progress across multiple areas for high-budget clients

### Work Stream Challenges

  - Backlinking and PSEO sold before fully developed
  - Need to balance selling new capabilities with ability to deliver
  - Considering repositioning offerings (e.g. backlinking as part of broader distribution strategy)

### Client Onboarding Process

  - Directors should spend more time deeply understanding product/space
  - Develop initial strategy and get alignment in early weeks
  - Hand-carve initial assignments to set direction before scaling production

### Team Member Spotlight: Pratik

  - Senior CM managing 5 clients ($4k-$9.5k budgets)
  - Strong execution but lacks ME support
  - May need additional support for higher-budget clients and client communication
