## Summary

Matthew Panzarino, GrowthX's Chief Content Officer, conducted his third interview with Maximiliano De Benedetto for the Content Manager position. Matthew highlighted the strategic nature of the role, emphasizing that GrowthX seeks content managers who think beyond writing—balancing 40% client management with 30% SEO and content strategy development. The critical differentiator for this role is willingness to engage with automation workflows (using tools like AirOps) and provide continuous feedback to engineering to improve systems across the organization. Matthew outlined the daily workflow through Notion-based ClientOS, weekly client calls, and a step-by-step content pipeline from keyword research through polished drafts to publication and monitoring. Matthew will forward his positive recommendation to the hiring team, with HR to contact Maximiliano regarding next steps.
