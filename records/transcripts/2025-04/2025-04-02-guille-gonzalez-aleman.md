## Summary

Jason Gong explored both immediate and long-term website redesign options with Guillermo Gonzalez Aleman of Litebox Strategies to support GrowthX's Series A announcement. The short-term need is a redesigned pricing/positioning page within 1-2 weeks featuring a single-entry-point layout showcasing GrowthX's five work streams (content, programmatic SEO, link building, outbound/demand gen, analytics).
