## Key Topics

### Performance Metrics

  - 17% decrease in organic traffic last week, mostly affecting design-related pages
  - Overall traffic has been flat since February
  - Lead growth down in recent months, but MQLs (sales form submissions) have increased
  - Need for better visibility into lead quality and traffic from updated content

### Content Updates

  - Working on new content agreed upon earlier in the week
  - Updating existing content with new conversion panels, SEO titles
  - 17 assignments under website redesign cluster available for next 2 weeks
  - Devon to provide another pillar and guidelines for keyword research next week

### Webstacks Process Overview

  - Web strategists involved from sales process through project completion
  - Comprehensive website audit categorizes work and page types
  - Modular, component-based approach allows for parallel work streams
  - Design discovery process educates clients on atomic design principles
  - Development starts with individual components before full page builds
  - Process subtly educates clients on composability for websites

### Client Challenges

  - Clients often come with issues from previous agencies or freelancers
  - Common problems include inability to make updates, lack of design systems
  - Webstacks' approach addresses these issues through education and flexible implementation

### Marketing and Development Collaboration

  - Traditional agency processes create bottlenecks and inflexibility
  - Webstacks' composable approach allows for greater agility and faster iterations
  - Importance of bridging the gap between marketing and development teams
