### Link Building Operations

  - Jimmy manages link building for 8 clients
  - Process:
    1.  Month 1: Exchange links with existing clients
    2.  Months 2-3: Use link brokers ($100-150/link based on domain authority)
    3.  Months 3+: Create "link magnet" content to organically attract backlinks
  - Example: Steadily spent $70k on links over 8-10 months to rank \#1 for key term
  - Firework case study: Created stats pages, attracting 40-60 backlinks/month organically

### Pricing and Packaging

  - Jason developing flexible packaging for 10-50 work streams

  - Challenge: Can't pitch specific outcomes beyond backlink quantity
