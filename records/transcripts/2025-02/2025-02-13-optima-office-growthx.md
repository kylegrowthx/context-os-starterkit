## Context

GrowthX Labs is a newly formed AI services company founded by Marcel, structured as an AI consulting agency but building toward a SaaS model. The company transitioned from LLC to C-corp three weeks prior to this call and is actively fundraising for Series A. With $500k MRR and 24 contractors distributed across 15 countries, GrowthX needs to establish proper financial infrastructure and move from contractor-based to a mixed full-time/contractor model. Bridget, who joined 10 days before this call, serves as Chief of Staff and VP Operations and inherited accounting systems that are "really muddled" from six months of pre-C-corp operations under the LLC structure.

Optima Office is a 100-person accounting and finance services firm based primarily in San Diego with additional staff across the US. They offer flexible, hourly-based accounting, finance, and HR services with CFOs, controllers, staff accountants, and HR specialists. Their business model centers on scalability—clients start with minimal hours and expand services as they grow.
