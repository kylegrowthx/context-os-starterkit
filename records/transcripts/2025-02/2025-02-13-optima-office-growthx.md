  - Currently pursuing Series A funding
  - $500k Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
  - 24 contractors across 15 countries, planning to mix contractors and full-time employees
  - AI services company with an agency-like model to customers, aiming to build more SaaS-like backend

### Optima Office Services and Structure

  - 100 employees, primarily in San Diego with 20 across the US
  - Offers hourly-based services: bookkeepers, staff accountants, controllers, CFOs, and HR specialists
  - Scalable model allows clients to adjust service levels as they grow
  - CFOs focus on forward-looking strategies, forecasting, and high-level decision-making
  - Controllers handle historical data, financial reporting, and systems/processes

### Accounting and Finance Options for GrowthX

  - Bridget considering Pilot ($700/month) for basic bookkeeping, partnered with their VCs
  - Optima suggests potential combination of their services (controller, staff accountant, CFO) could be more comprehensive for $6-7k/month
  - Discussion of trade-offs between full-time hires vs. fractional services

### HR and HRIS Considerations

  - GrowthX needs to choose an HRIS system, considering Rippling and Deel for international and EOR capabilities
  - Optima uses Perform Yard for HRIS and Paycor for payroll, recommends separating these systems
