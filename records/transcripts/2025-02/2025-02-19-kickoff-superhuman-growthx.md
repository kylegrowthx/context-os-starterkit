## Overview

---

## Key Topics

### Current State and Goals

  - 80% of sales conversations are with existing customers; aim to increase net new leads
  - Shifting focus from individual to team/enterprise solutions
  - Expanding into new markets, verticals, and personas (e.g., professional services)
  - Key metrics: Discovery Call Occurred (DCO), high-intent leads/users
  - Organic traffic contributes 60-70% of overall traffic, lower for DCOs

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Primary focus: Tech/SaaS, VCs and portfolio companies, Professional Services
  - Professional Services priority: Management consultants (enterprise), Marketing/Advertising (mid-market)
  - Evolving use cases: Sales, Recruiting, Operations, Founder/Executive
  - Need to address various personas in buyer groups (e.g., IT decision-makers)

### Content Strategy and Production

  - Current approach is campaign/product-launch driven, lacking holistic content strategy
  - Moving towards use case and persona-specific content across customer journey
  - Webinar strategy: Minimum one webinar per month, opportunity for content repurposing
  - Short-form video content initiative for demos and use cases
  - Plans to overhaul resources section on website for better user education

### Technical Considerations

  - Blog currently on subdomain; plans to move to main domain
  - Using Ghost for blog, Webflow for main site; considering consolidation to single CMS
  - Website re-architecture planned, starting in March

### AI and Product Evolution

  - Recently launched beta AI solution; expecting significant changes in user experience
  - AI features performing well in non-brand SEM
  - Shifting from individual-focused to team-oriented solution
