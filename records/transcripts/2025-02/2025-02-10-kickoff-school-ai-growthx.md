## Key Topics

### Company Background and Goals

  - School AI closed Series A funding recently
  - Aiming to become category leader in AI for education
  - Currently behind competitors in organic traffic, backlinks, domain authority
  - 80% of new users come through organic channels currently
  - Focused on generating pipeline and closed-won ARR, not vanity metrics

### Target Audience and Positioning

  - Users span K-20 education (kindergarten through PhD)
  - Primary users are teachers across all levels
  - Buyers include administrators, tech directors, instructional coaches, librarians
  - Positioning as end-to-end platform for personalized learning, differentiating from competitors focused on teacher productivity or browser extensions

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Balance between demand capture (60%) and demand generation (40%)
  - Need to address spectrum of AI adoption in education - from skepticism to enthusiasm
  - Opportunity to leverage user-generated content and use cases
  - Potential to create content tied to School AI's training courses
  - Focus on differentiating School AI's comprehensive platform capabilities

### Competitive Landscape

  - Primary competitors are Magic School and Brisk
  - Magic School strong in teacher productivity use cases
  - School AI aims to showcase broader, more innovative classroom applications

### Collaboration Process

  - GrowthX to provide initial content opportunities and strategy for review
  - School AI to share existing resources like tone/voice guidelines and training materials
  - Need to determine internal review process as content production ramps up
