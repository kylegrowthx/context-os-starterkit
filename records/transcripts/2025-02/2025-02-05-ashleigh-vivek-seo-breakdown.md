## Overview

- FWD.digital uses a topic-first, semantic SEO approach vs. traditional keyword-centric methods
- Strategy built on product/audience analysis, creating broad topics, then iterating based on performance
- Expect 3-4 months for significant traction; 6 months for exponential growth in organic traffic

---

## Key Topics

### Modern SEO Approach

  - Shift from keyword-centric to semantic, topic-based strategy
  - Google's algorithm now understands related concepts (e.g., "ETL modernization" ≈ "roadblocks to ETL modernization")
  - Focus on addressing topics comprehensively rather than targeting specific keywords
  - Strategy: Start w/ product & audience → develop topic pillars → create broad content → iterate based on performance

### Content Development Process

  - Use AI/LLM to analyze personas, jobs-to-be-done, daily tasks
  - Identify 5-10 key tasks relevant to Prophecy's offerings
  - Create broad, foundational content (e.g., "ETL vs. ELT")
  - Measure performance to determine which subtopics to explore deeper
  - Iterate content strategy based on data & client feedback

### Performance Expectations

  - 2-3 months: Initial traction
  - 3-4 months: Significant growth in traffic
  - \~6 months: Potential for exponential growth (based on Galileo case study)
  - Early indicators: High impressions & engagement rates
  - Conversion focus comes later, typically end of month 2 into month 3

### Content Optimization Strategy

  - Initial broad topics cast wide net for potential searches
  - Analyze best-performing articles for optimization opportunities
  - Refresh process includes updating internal linking to revenue-generating assets
  - Balance between top-of-funnel content and mid/bottom-funnel conversions

---

## Action Items

- Continue executing current content strategy, maintaining publication cadence
- Monitor early performance indicators (impressions, engagement rates)
- Prepare for deeper performance analysis at 2-3 month mark
- Plan for potential content refreshes and link optimizations as data becomes available
