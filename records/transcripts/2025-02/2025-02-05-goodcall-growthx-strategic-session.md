## Summary

GrowthX and GoodCall reviewed initial content cluster research (industry guides, best tools, answering services, and potential expansions into call center/contact center integration with platforms like Genesis). The team identified that "best software" framing was weak compared to service-oriented language, discussed experiments around city-based keywords and AI-specific modifiers (automation, productivity, efficiency), and agreed that the core opportunity lies in the call center and contact center space where GoodCall has strong capabilities in scheduling, lead capture, and authentication. Next steps include deepening research on query volume and difficulty for proposed clusters (especially call center/Genesis integration), continuing article refresh work (5 per week with priority on dropped rankings), and reconvening after GrowthX validates cluster selections with research data.

## Context

GoodCall is an AI voice agent platform that handles inbound calls for businesses, with core capabilities in appointment scheduling, lead qualification, and call routing/authentication. GrowthX is building out GoodCall's content and SEO strategy as part of a $200k+ engagement. This was a strategic follow-up to an earlier kickoff meeting, with Kyle Gaudreau (client strategy lead at GrowthX) returning after recovering from COVID to review initial research and course-correct the content strategy. The meeting happened at an awkward time (just after 9:59 PM UTC) due to timezone constraints with team members across different locations. Bob Summers and Daniel Lannon from GoodCall were joining to provide market feedback on proposed content clusters and help GrowthX align on which market segments (SMB vs. mid-market, specific verticals) make the most sense to target given GoodCall's positioning and where actual search volume exists.
