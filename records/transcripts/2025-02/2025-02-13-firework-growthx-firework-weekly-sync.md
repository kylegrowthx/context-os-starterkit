## Overview

## Key Topics

### Content Performance Metrics Realignment

  - Current focus on clicks and CTR is misaligned with business goals
  - Need to track leads generated per article, distinguishing between SMB (PLG) and enterprise (SLG) leads
  - Implement UTM parameter for blog post names to enable granular tracking
  - Retroactively analyze which articles attracted enterprise accounts that converted

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Shift from volume to quality: produce fewer, more impactful articles
  - Focus on enterprise pain points and industry-specific content
  - Create "anchor" content pieces with multiple industry-specific derivatives
  - Develop comparison guides and vendor evaluation content to capture bottom-funnel searches

### Industry Focus

  - Prioritize top industries: beauty, consumer electronics, apparel, fashion, athletic wear, grocery
  - De-emphasize lower-priority sectors like healthcare
  - Adapt successful content (e.g., Easter campaigns) across priority industries

### Upcoming Thought Leadership Content

  - "2025 Video Commerce Research Report"
  - "2025 Video Commerce Global Blueprint"
