## Context

This was an internal operational onboarding call between Marcel Santilli (founder) and Bridget McGillivray (newly hired operations/finance lead). GrowthX has been operating with financial systems under multiple entity structures (FWD Digital LLC as the original parent, with Growth X as a new C-corp entity for the core services business). Marcel walked Bridget through the full stack of financial access and tools, explaining each system's role, setup quirks, and next steps—essentially a comprehensive handoff of financial system management from Marcel to Bridget. The meeting reflects a growth-stage company restructuring: increasing from bootstrap/founder-managed operations to a more formal operational structure with dedicated finance leadership.
