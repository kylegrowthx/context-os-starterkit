## Key Topics

### Company Overview and Growth

  - Currently at $6M ARR, aiming for $15-22M by year-end (5-7x growth)
  - 35 accounts now, targeting 100 by end of year
  - Recently raised funding from tier-one VC (Madrona)
  - 62 deals in pipeline, growing without active marketing
  - Q4 gross margin was 71%, now have $12M in bank to fuel growth

### Service Offerings

  - Primary focus (80-90%) on long-form editorial and programmatic SEO content
  - 50-100k words/month for editorial, few hundred pages/month for programmatic
  - Additional services: link building, AI outbound, content repurposing
  - Plans to expand into social content, newsletters, research, product marketing

### Workflow and Technology

  - Developed AI-powered workflows for client onboarding, research, and content creation
  - Workflows generate company research, persona development, ICP analysis, and competitive research automatically
  - Content strategy and assignment creation workflows reduce manual work and human error
  - Engineering team rapidly building internal platform (ContentOS) to replace AirTable and AirOps
  - Fast iteration loop: engineering and operations use the same tools as customers, enabling rapid feedback and improvement

### Client Engagement

  - 90-day pilot period, followed by 12-month contracts
  - Multiple work streams per client (editorial, programmatic, etc.)
  - Aiming to be an extension of client's team, using Slack for communication

### Potential Roles for Mara

  1. Client Operations:
     - Heavy focus on systems and process setup
     - Less client-facing than other roles

  2. Managing Editor:
     - More hands-on with content strategy and client work
     - Leading a team, but still involved in day-to-day operations
     - Calibrating quality standards and client expectations during the 12-week pilot

  3. Associate Managing Director:
     - Overseeing 2 pods (approximately 8 accounts total)
     - Mix of client-facing work, team management, and business development
     - Focus on opening up new work streams (content repurposing, social, newsletters, etc.)
     - Building workflows and processes for new service offerings in collaboration with engineering team
     - Managing account health and business metrics across the pods
     - Potential for revenue-based commission structure on new work streams
     - More executive-level strategic leadership

### Company Culture and Benefits

  - Netflix-inspired culture with employee-friendly equity terms
  - Extended exercise windows for stock options (1 year vs. 90 days)
  - Guaranteed severance for any termination case
  - Emphasis on work-life integration and family-friendly policies
  - Recruiting from high-performing agencies and in-house teams (recent hires from ClickUp, TripAdvisor/Fanatics)

### Candidate Profile and Job Fit

  - Mara has stepped back from day-to-day editing to focus on strategy and client relationships
  - Energized by small, fast-growing teams with distributed problem-solving
  - Prioritizes growth, strategy, and client-facing work
  - Values early ownership and startup environment
  - Associate Managing Director role aligns with her ideal: strategic depth, client engagement, team building, and the opportunity to develop new service lines
