## Context

Prophecy is a data engineering platform (Prophecy.io) that is an early GrowthX B2B content marketing client. GrowthX is delivering a content strategy focused on SEO and AI visibility for Prophecy's data engineering and ETL audience. The weekly sync is the regular operational check-in between the GrowthX team (Vivek Shankar and Kyle Gaudreau) and Prophecy's stakeholders (Ashleigh Blalock, Raveena Kapatkar, Mitesh Shah) to review content output, performance metrics, and strategy adjustments.

This particular sync surfaced a discovery: despite solid article quality and completion velocity (3-4 articles/week), Prophecy's website has underlying technical SEO problems that are preventing full indexation. The discovery came from investigating why one high-value article wasn't being indexed, which revealed HTML markup issues in their Webflow CMS. This represents a critical intervention point where technical fixes could unlock the organic and AI visibility gains that the content strategy is designed to achieve.
