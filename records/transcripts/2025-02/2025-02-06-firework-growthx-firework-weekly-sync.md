## Summary

GrowthX reported strong SEO performance for Firework (4.5k monthly visitors, 6x growth; 35% increase in impressions), with new articles generating 866% more clicks than existing content. However, the team agreed that current content strategy is too broad and generic (e.g., "cart abandonment," "customer segmentation"); the next phase requires pivoting to use-case-driven, industry-specific content that directly demonstrates how Firework's video capabilities solve problems in grocery, beauty, apparel, and retail. Key initiatives include developing 20-30 new topics, creating lead magnets with content-specific CTAs, moving the "Book Demo" button to the right sidebar for better visibility, implementing UTM tracking, and establishing a seasonal holiday content calendar to capture high-intent search traffic around Valentine's Day, Easter, Mother's Day, and Prime Day.

## Context

GrowthX is providing SEO and content marketing services to Firework, a video commerce platform used by e-commerce and retail brands to drive conversions and personalize customer experiences. This is a weekly sync between Prateek Gupta (GrowthX content lead) and Ritesh Kewlani (Firework's VP of Product) to discuss organic traffic performance and refine content strategy. The call follows up on previous discussions about broadening Firework's content beyond generic e-commerce topics to use-case-driven content that showcases how Firework's video capabilities solve specific industry problems (grocery, beauty, apparel, fashion). GrowthX has successfully driven a 6x increase in monthly visitors and 35% increase in impressions over the past month, but the team needs to shift from broad, generic topics to specific, conversion-focused content that directly ties to Firework's unique value proposition.
