## Summary

Dimmo and GrowthX Labs aligned on a content strategy pivot to drive sales conversions through high-value, targeted content. Troy Munson from Dimmo pushed back on generic "pointless blogs," advocating instead for in-depth content like "10 CRMs you need to know about" (segmented by company size) and novel content formats including "Integration Station" (AI-scraped integration databases) and competitive comparison matrices. GrowthX committed to delivering 80 high-quality articles per month, organized into four content clusters (individual software reviews, tool comparisons, alternative listicles, and job-to-be-done articles), with special focus on the sales category. The team also identified ~500 unindexed blog articles and agreed that GrowthX would investigate technical SEO fixes (HTML sitemaps, internal linking improvements) before scaling content production.

---

## Context

Dimmo is a software review and comparison platform with approximately 400-500 product demo pages. The company is building a content strategy to drive sales professionals to product comparison and review pages, with the ultimate goal of monetizing through traffic-dependent business model. This is an onboarding engagement with GrowthX Labs to shift Dimmo's content from low-value generic blog posts to high-intent, specialized content. Troy Munson represents Dimmo's product/strategy side and is attuned to Lucas Swartsenburg's (the CEO) skepticism about blog effectiveness, though Troy himself is open to well-executed content strategies. Jimmy Pal from GrowthX is leading the engagement and brought examples from comparable clients (Strapi, a headless CMS) who scaled content-driven traffic using templated, data-informed workflows. The team has access to existing Dimmo demo videos and raw transcript data that can be repurposed into step-by-step guides and enriched articles.

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

- Avoid generic, low-value blog posts ("pointless blogs")
- Explore unique content ideas:
    - "Integration Station": AI-scraped integration info for marketing tools
    - Competitive matrix: Interactive tool comparison feature
- Focus on in-depth, valuable content like "10 CRMs you need to know about" with market segmentation
- Utilize existing demo videos as a basis for enriched, step-by-step guide content

### Technical SEO Improvements

- Recent efforts improved rankings (e.g., "Sindoso demo" now on page 1)
- ~500 blog articles currently not indexed by Google
- GrowthX to investigate and potentially implement fixes:
    - HTML sitemap inclusion
    - Improve internal linking structure

### Content Production Plan

- Aim for 80 high-quality articles per month
- Content clusters:
  1. Individual software reviews
  2. Tool comparisons (e.g., "Tool A vs Tool B")
  3. Alternative listicles
  4. Use case/job-to-be-done articles
- Prioritize sales category content initially
- Adapt strategy based on performance metrics (CTR, impressions, ICP scoring)

### CTA and User Journey Optimization

- Primary goal: Drive users to product pages
- Implement contextual CTAs within article content
- Explore widget implementation for improved conversion
