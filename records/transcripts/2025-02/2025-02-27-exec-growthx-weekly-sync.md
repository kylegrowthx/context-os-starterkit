## Key Topics

### Content Refinement

  - Avoid overuse of em-dashes and phrases like "It's not just X, it's Y"
  - Balance SEO needs with reader engagement
  - Structure articles with impactful openings (250 words) followed by denser, list-style content
  - Reduce use of bullet points and long H-tag headers with colons

### AI Writing Tools

  - Claude (3.7 version) performs better for certain tasks, especially outlines and structure
  - GPT-4 excels at rewriting company copy
  - Combining different AI models yields best results for various content needs

### Graphics and Imagery

  - Current Midjourney-generated images resemble outdated "corporate Memphis" style
  - Aim for clean vector art aesthetic, similar to Exec's new website design
  - Sean to provide a "swipe file" of inspiring examples for future graphics

### Interactive Content Idea

  - Develop a tool where users input product details and receive potential sales objections with responses
  - Integrate this interactive element into blog posts to increase engagement and provide practical value
