## Overview

- Manifold seeks high-quality, authoritative content on trusted research environments (TREs) and related topics to establish thought leadership
- Focus on differentiators like AI capabilities, cloud flexibility, and solving data management/analysis challenges for life sciences R\&D
- Initial emphasis on TRE content to support upcoming campaign, with goal to expand into more specific technical and scientific topics
- Aim to produce 2-4 high-quality articles per month initially, prioritizing quality over quantity

## Key Topics

### Manifold's Content Needs and Goals

  - Lacking volume of authoritative content on key topics
  - Need educational content for highly educated, discerning audience
  - Focus on trusted research environment (TRE) space initially
  - Aim to become go-to source for TRE and related information
  - Quality of content critical - audience will quickly spot inaccuracies
  - Quantity important but secondary to quality at outset
  - Goal to at least double current output of \~1 article/month

### Key Differentiators and Messaging

  - AI capabilities as major differentiator
  - "Bring your own cloud" flexibility
  - Solving data management and analysis challenges
  - Speed and efficiency in drug development process
  - Unified platform vs. fragmented point solutions
  - Strategic partnership with Broad Institute

### Target Personas and Industries

  - Chief Research Informatics Officer
  - Chief Scientific Officer
  - IT/Security decision makers
  - Biotech, pharma, diagnostics companies
  - Contract research organizations (CROs)
  - Shifting focus from academic to industry customers

### Content Strategy and Approach

  - Build pillar content on TREs, branch into specific topics
  - Leverage upcoming TRE campaign as foundation
  - Focus on educational content addressing buyer pain points
  - Aim for thought leadership on technical and scientific topics
  - Use trusted sources (e.g. .gov sites) for credibility
  - Align with Broad Institute partnership messaging

### GrowthX Process and Timeline

  - Week 1-2: Build content strategy, get feedback
  - Week 3-4: Produce calibration articles, refine approach
  - Week 4-5: Aim for 2-4 quality articles/week with less oversight
  - Flexible to adjust pace and process as needed
