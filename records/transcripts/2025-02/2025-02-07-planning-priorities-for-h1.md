## Overview

- Critical need to rapidly scale client operations and support functions to prevent bottlenecks and maintain quality as company grows
- Emphasis on hiring AI-native talent and building automation/leverage into all processes
- Immediate priorities: hire head of talent acquisition, client operations leader, and build out key support functions (finance, legal, GTM ops)

---

## Key Topics

### Organizational Structure and Hiring Priorities

  - Current headcount \~40 (including freelancers), rapidly growing
  - Key hires needed:
      - Head of Talent Acquisition: To build recruiting engine and processes
      - Director/VP of Client Operations: Experienced in scaling agencies/professional services
      - Business Operations Manager: To support data-driven decision making
      - Engineering/product roles (under Daniel's leadership)
  - Considering unconventional early hire of Head of People (typically 50-100 employee milestone)
  - Emphasis on hiring AI-native talent comfortable with rapid change

### Client Operations and Delivery

  - Urgent need to improve onboarding, training, and support processes
  - Considering pod structure vs. specialists for client teams
  - Exploring ideal ratios and team compositions as company scales
  - Need for better project management and client success frameworks
  - Goal to reduce reliance on founders for client relationships/retention

### Product Vision and Defensibility

  - Building workflow marketplace with network effects
      - Allow users to create, publish, and fork AI workflows
      - Aggregate data from workflow usage to improve offerings
      - Potential to spin out individual workflows as standalone products/APIs
  - Emphasis on building leverage and automation into all processes
  - Exploring international hiring to optimize for talent/cost

### Financial Planning and Forecasting

  - Need to develop comprehensive financial model
      - Hiring plans tied to revenue/client growth
      - Scenario planning for different growth trajectories
  - Balance growth targets with maintaining quality and margins
  - Prioritize retention and expansion of existing clients

### Culture and Operations

  - Implement "rhythm of the business" practices (weekly/monthly reviews)
  - Develop training programs to quickly ramp up new hires
  - Build automation and AI usage into core company processes
  - Create clear success metrics for all roles, especially new hires
