## Overview

- Reddit aims to increase awareness and consideration for its ad platform among B2B buyers (tech, gaming, fintech) through influencer partnerships that tap into decision-maker networks
- The strategy will run a 2-activation pilot over 6-8 weeks (Q1 2025, target end of March) with industry leaders and agency owners to generate positive sentiment and content for organic amplification
- Activation ideas include co-branded webinars, vertical-specific "Navigating Reddit" guides, and branded white papers with people like Alex (Understory agency owner) and Silvio (platform education expert)
- Aggressive incentives will drive sign-ups: $5,000 in ad credits for the first 100 webinar registrants, tiered credit matching, early access to features, and direct account manager support
- Success will be measured on reach (30,000-50,000 people via webinar + email + influencer amplification) and engagement (10% engagement rate), not ROAS; secondary metrics include webinar attendance and credit usage

## Key Topics

### Influencer Activation Strategy

  - Target: B2B influencers, industry experts, agency owners with large networks
  - Goals:
    1.  Increase awareness and consideration for Reddit ads
    2.  Access decision-maker networks
    3.  Generate positive content and sentiment around Reddit ads
  - Activation ideas:
      - Co-branded webinars
      - Navigating Reddit guides for specific verticals
      - White papers or insight reports
  - Potential incentives:
      - Ad credits (e.g., $5,000 for first 100 webinar registrants)
      - Early access to alpha features
      - Direct account manager support

### Potential Influencer Partners

  - Alex from Understory (agency owner)
      - Has strong testimonial from Adam (CEO of retention.com), who runs Reddit ads and is a compelling reference
      - Manages outbound and paid media for brands like Clay (just raised at $1B valuation)
      - Operates as an agency owner influencer with access to B2B decision-makers
  - Silvio (platform education expert)
      - Built comprehensive learning platform covering how to use all ad platforms, including Reddit
      - Has existing Reddit curriculum that he's already recorded
      - Positioned as a "master of craft" with deep technical platform knowledge

### Measurement and Success Metrics

  - Primary focus: Reach and engagement
      - Target: 30,000-50,000 total reach
      - Aim for 10% engagement rate
  - Secondary metrics:
      - Webinar attendance
      - Credit usage and account signups
      - Content creation and amplification

### Pilot Program Structure

  - Run 2 activations over 6-8 weeks to gather sufficient data
  - Create "crazy" incentives to generate buzz (e.g., $5,000 in ad credits)
  - Tiered credit system to encourage continued usage
  - Emphasize brand association and distribution opportunities for influencers
