## Overview

- Reddit aims to increase awareness and consideration for its ad platform through B2B influencer partnerships
- The strategy will focus on high-impact activations (e.g., webinars) with industry leaders to reach decision-makers
- A pilot program will be developed with aggressive incentives (e.g., ad credits) to create buzz and prove concept
- Success metrics will primarily focus on reach and engagement, not immediate ROAS

---

## Key Topics

### Influencer Activation Strategy

  - Target: B2B influencers, industry experts, agency owners with large networks
  - Goals:
    1.  Increase awareness and consideration for Reddit ads
    2.  Access decision-maker networks
    3.  Generate positive content and sentiment around Reddit ads
  - Activation ideas:
      - Co-branded webinars
      - Navigating Reddit guides for specific verticals
      - White papers or insight reports
  - Potential incentives:
      - Ad credits (e.g., $5,000 for first 100 webinar registrants)
      - Early access to alpha features
      - Direct account manager support

### Potential Influencer Partners

  - Alex from Understory (agency owner)
      - Has testimonial from Adam (CEO of retention.com)
      - Works with high-profile B2B brands
  - Silvio (platform expert)
      - Built learning platform for various ad platforms, including Reddit
      - Has existing Reddit curriculum

### Measurement and Success Metrics

  - Primary focus: Reach and engagement
      - Target: 30,000-50,000 total reach
      - Aim for 10% engagement rate
  - Secondary metrics:
      - Webinar attendance
      - Credit usage and account signups
      - Content creation and amplification

### Pilot Program Structure

  - Run 2 activations over 6-8 weeks to gather sufficient data
  - Create "crazy" incentives to generate buzz (e.g., $5,000 in ad credits)
  - Tiered credit system to encourage continued usage
  - Emphasize brand association and distribution opportunities for influencers
