## Summary

GrowthX and Seamless AI explored a potential partnership to accelerate content scaling and programmatic page optimization. Seamless AI, a 2-person team in the lead generation space, is experiencing strong organic growth (7x in the past year, targeting 10x) but is resource-constrained in both content production and programmatic page enhancement. Marcel walked through GrowthX's AI workflow-plus-human-editors approach, which has proven successful with clients like Ramp, Reddit, and Webflow, and outlined how GrowthX could help Seamless AI enrich thin content pages, improve programmatic company profile pages, and accelerate overall content velocity.

---

## Context

Seamless AI operates in the B2B lead generation and sales intelligence space. The company is led by CEO Brandon Bornancin and has a small in-house team including Salvatore Surra (VP of SEO/Content), who brings nearly 30 years of SEO and content publishing experience from companies like Angie's List, MotorTrend, and a background in programmatic SEO at scale. About 18 months ago, the team took over content responsibility and immediately removed roughly half of the low-quality, AI-generated content that had been published, freeing up resources to focus on improving the remaining ~150 high-value pieces. They've also begun exploring programmatic page generation (search results and company profiles) launched 6-12 months ago with 20,000-30,000 pages, but lack developer resources to iterate and improve. Marcel Santilli, founder of GrowthX, recently closed a $12M Series A and wanted to connect with Seamless AI to explore how GrowthX's workflow infrastructure and editorial team could help accelerate Seamless AI's content strategy.
