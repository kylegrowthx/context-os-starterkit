## Summary

Marcel Santilli (fwd.digital) introduced his AI-powered content services company to Michael Weisbaum, an experienced SEO and growth leader at Dot Dash, pitching a Managing Director opportunity. fwd.digital has achieved 35 clients and $6M ARR in under two years by building proprietary AI workflows that handle 80% of content work with human expert guidance, and is aiming for 100 clients and $22M ARR this year.
