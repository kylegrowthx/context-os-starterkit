## Key Topics

### Company Background and Growth

  - Started in April 2023, first providing content services to clients
  - Developed proprietary AI workflow engine and infrastructure starting September 2023
  - Rapid growth: 35 clients, $6M ARR, targeting $22M ARR and 100 clients by end of year
  - Recently closed Series A funding round

### Technology and Approach

  - Built engine to create AI-powered workflows represented as code
  - Workflows handle 80% of work, with human experts guiding the process
  - Constantly evolving components and assembling methods
  - Long-term vision: translate outcomes into AI-understandable workflows
  - Collecting data to improve workflow assembly and outcomes

### Business Model and Strategy

  - Currently delivering outcomes as a service (e.g., editorial, programmatic SEO)
  - Plans to expand into other knowledge work areas beyond content
  - Three-phase growth strategy:
    1.  AI-powered content services to build platform and modules
    2.  Expose APIs for individual tools (e.g., researcher, fact-checker)
    3.  Expand into other areas like hiring and recruiting

### Team Structure

  - Hiring managing directors to own books of business
  - Roles combine general management, product management, client strategy, and sales
  - Seeking experienced professionals who have scaled operations at multiple companies

### Market Opportunity and Differentiation

  - Content and marketing services market estimated at $1 trillion
  - Approach differs from other AI companies by starting with real-world outcomes
  - Generating valuable "frontier data" through service delivery to improve AI models
