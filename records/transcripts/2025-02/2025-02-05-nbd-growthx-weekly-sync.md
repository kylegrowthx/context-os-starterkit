## Context

GrowthX is producing a high-volume content strategy for NBD's website, starting with a blog built on Webflow. This meeting represents the final pre-launch sync after GrowthX and NBD aligned on blog strategy and design in previous calls. GrowthX has been implementing NBD's brand guidelines and voice direction, while NBD finalized the Webflow blog section and design system. The teams are now ready to begin publishing the first batch of articles and establishing workflows for ongoing content production.
