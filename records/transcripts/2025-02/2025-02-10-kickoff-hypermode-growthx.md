## Key Topics

### Goals and Objectives

  - Build category awareness for Hypermode's approach to AI-native/model-native apps
  - Migrate content and domain authority from Dgraph to Hypermode
  - Develop foundational knowledge assets for target audience
  - Test various marketing messages (e.g. "model-native" vs "AI-native") to refine positioning

### Target Audience and Positioning

  - Spans ML engineers familiar with models to traditional app developers
  - Seeing more traction with enterprises and innovation labs
  - Shift from self-service to focus on organizations with complex needs and multiple stakeholders
  - Emphasize knowledge graph + AI use cases, moving beyond traditional graph database positioning

### Content Strategy and Process

  - Currently limited formal process, working to revamp blog publishing
  - Will's technical content typically starts with demo/code, then expands to video, blog post, social

  - No CMS currently - using MDX files in GitHub, but evaluating options

### Website Migration

  - Plan to deprecate Dgraph website by end of month
  - Need strategy for 301 redirects, canonicals, and handling SEO transition
  - Special consideration needed for Dgraph Q\&A forum content

### Competitive Landscape

  - Current list focuses on database competitors
