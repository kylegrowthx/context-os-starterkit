## Context

Prophecy is a data integration and orchestration platform that is a content marketing client of GrowthX. This is part of their ongoing weekly syncs to align on content production, performance, and strategy. Prophecy has published two initial articles (ELT and ETL topics) with strong early engagement metrics, and both teams are focused on establishing reporting baselines while refining the content approach going forward. The underlying tension in this call is how to balance Prophecy's desire to demonstrate thought leadership and neutral positioning against the SEO reality that Google currently ranks tool-comparison articles higher for informational queries, requiring a careful strategy to serve search intent without appearing biased or promotional toward Prophecy's own solution.
