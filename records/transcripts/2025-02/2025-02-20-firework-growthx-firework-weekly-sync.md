## Overview

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - Moving away from generic keywords to more specific, use-case driven content
  - Leveraging Firework's existing playbooks and industry reports as source material
  - Creating "bite-sized" SEO content from comprehensive e-books and reports
  - Focus on quality leads over high click volumes

### Lead Generation Goals

  - Target: 5 quality inbound opportunities per week from mid-market and above
  - Aim for 0.5-1% conversion rate on lead magnets
  - Track form completions and lead quality, not just clicks
  - Analyze which content pieces drive high-quality leads vs. low-quality leads

### Content Distribution and Promotion

  - Promote content on LinkedIn with paid advertising
  - Create industry-specific reports similar to other successful B2B companies
  - Implement pop-ups and banners for lead capture on blog posts
  - Consider adding conversational search functionality to improve lead quality

### SEO and Paid Ads Strategy

  - Prioritize organic SEO efforts over paid ads initially
  - Use paid ads data to inform SEO strategy when implemented
  - Focus on long-tail variations of best-performing keywords
  - Track how SEO efforts reduce need for paid ad spend over time
