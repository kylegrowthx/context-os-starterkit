## Summary

GrowthX and Firework agreed to pivot their content strategy from high-volume generic keywords to targeted, use-case-driven content sourced from Firework's comprehensive playbooks and industry reports, with a clear goal of generating 5 quality mid-market inbound leads per week. The team will experiment for 1-2 months creating 10-20 bite-sized articles based on use cases (e.g., shoppable video, video commerce, digital showroom) and optimizing existing beauty/fashion content to funnel readers toward the playbooks, while implementing lead capture mechanisms (pop-ups, banners) when users scroll 70-80% or spend 10 seconds on blog posts. Success hinges on tracking lead quality over click volume and identifying which content clusters drive high-value ICPs versus low-quality leads.

---

## Context

Firework is a video commerce platform helping brands leverage shoppable videos and live shopping to drive engagement and revenue. This is a key engagement account for GrowthX, with a joint goal of scaling inbound lead generation from Firework's own website and blog. GrowthX has been focusing on broad SEO content targeting high search volume, but discovered through page-level analysis that generic content (e.g., "shoppable video examples") is converting better than industry-targeted content (e.g., "shoppable video examples for beauty brands"). Rather than abandon vertical-specific content, the team is taking a curated-content approach: extracting use cases and statistics from Firework's extensive playbooks and industry reports to create hundreds of smaller, SEO-optimized articles that showcase real brand examples (L'Oréal, IKEA, Gucci) without requiring explicit brand permission. The expectation is that mid-market and above-mid-market brands searching for "how to increase revenue," "how to use video commerce," etc., will land on these articles, see relevant examples, and convert to inbound leads—which they can then plug into CRMs and nurture sequences.

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - Moving away from generic keywords to more specific, use-case driven content
  - Leveraging Firework's existing playbooks and industry reports as source material

  - Aim for 0.5-1% conversion rate on lead magnets
  - Track form completions and lead quality, not just clicks
  - Analyze which content pieces drive high-quality leads vs. low-quality leads

  - Promote content on LinkedIn with paid advertising
  - Create industry-specific reports similar to other successful B2B companies

### SEO and Paid Ads Strategy

  - Prioritize organic SEO efforts over paid ads initially
  - Use paid ads data to inform SEO strategy when implemented
  - Focus on long-tail variations of best-performing keywords
  - Track how SEO efforts reduce need for paid ad spend over time
