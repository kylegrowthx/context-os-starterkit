## Key Topics

### SEMrush Data Accuracy Concerns

- SEMrush significantly overestimated traffic (estimating 5k/month for pages that received only 18 clicks in 3 months in Google Search Console)
- Recent JavaScript rendering changes are making third-party tools less accurate at tracking rankings and traffic
- Google Search Console is the most reliable source of truth for actual clicks and impressions; SEMrush useful for directional data but not absolute numbers
- GrowthX will educate Goodcall and other clients on using GSC as primary source and building custom dashboards in Looker Studio for easier trend visualization

### Content Strategy and Clusters

- Approved content categories: appointment scheduling software, best AI tools (by vertical), answering services
- Total of 1,400 potential topics with 5,000+ search volume across all categories

- Enterprise-focused content to follow, targeting companies with 3,000+ employees and X million ARR; 271 such companies already visiting site via business productivity database
- Two-phase approach: publish 20-25 articles in SMB-focused categories first to signal ranking authority, then double down on categories showing clicks and impressions

- Target: 25-30 new articles on answering services within 1.5 weeks (by approximately late February/early March)
- Goodcall to provide Apollo data export showing 271 enterprise leads by page to help prioritize content verticals

- Appointment language revision needed: update articles to say "with an integration to your booking system" rather than current phrasing
- Expected milestones: March 15 (initial indexing and impressions), end of March (significant clicks arriving)

### Website Architecture and Navigation

- Goodcall using Apollo on-page tracking to capture enterprise visitor segments
- Business productivity database category is driving majority of enterprise traffic (not traditional organic search)
- Site visitors include education data (legacy from previous goodcall.com incarnation) mixed with viable enterprise clients
- Content strategy leverages business productivity database as a discovery mechanism for upmarket segment

### Reporting and Tracking

- Primary tracking via Airtable: all published pages with real-time performance data (clicks, impressions by page)
- Secondary tracking: weekly GrowthX calls where performance is reviewed week-over-week
