## Key Topics

### fwd.digital's Business Model and Growth

  - Started by automating content creation workflows using chained LLM steps
  - Evolved from teaching workshops to offering services due to client demand
  - Reached $6M ARR, up from $2.9M at end of Q4 (2x growth in 8 weeks)
  - 54 deals in pipeline, including Webflow; all growth through word-of-mouth
  - Recently closed $25K/month, 12-month contract with Abnormal Security

### Platform Architecture and Capabilities

  - Uses modular, chainable AI workflows similar to Terraform providers
  - Runs on Temporal, with very low per-task costs (e.g., 20-30 cents for research task)
  - Can process large amounts of data cheaply due to service-based pricing model
  - Examples: candidate resume analysis, deep research workflows
  - Potential to expose APIs and allow users to create/modify workflows

### Vision and Future Directions

  - Expand beyond content creation to other knowledge work domains
  - Build internal platform and AI modules exposable as individual products/APIs
  - Create marketplace for sharing and monetizing workflows (similar to Hugging Face)
  - Focus on scalability, modularity, and continuous improvement of workflows

### Technical Challenges and Priorities

  - Need to improve infrastructure to handle growth and enable scalability
  - Build with modularity and reusability in mind to make future work easier/faster
  - Design for model-agnostic architecture to benefit from advancements in AI
  - Adopt "everything as a service" approach for internal and potential external use
