  - Team investigating which pages gained more clicks, but improvement seems broad across many pages
  - Matt noted some share of voice metrics falling off for expense categories
  - Vivek observed similar impression drops across other clients, possibly seasonal

### Treasury Management Content Strategy

  - Team sent templates for category pages for review
  - Ali's feedback:
      - Hold off on "best bank account" or "best banks" content due to regulatory concerns
      - Focus on treasury cash management, operating accounts, and specific competitors (e.g., "mercury account alternative")
      - Skip "best credit unions per state" for now
      - Proceed with metric tracking topics (e.g., how to calculate days cash on hand, earn rate)
  - Vivek explained the rationale behind bank-focused content, citing search volume and product relevance
  - Agreement to create one sample write-up (e.g., "best banks for small business in California") for treasury team review

### Keyword and Content Concerns

  - Ali highlighted issues with search volume data, noting many keywords show as zero volume
  - Vivek clarified the focus is on broad topic coverage rather than specific high-volume keywords
  - Team discussed the need for:
    1.  Target keywords to understand topic intent and SERP landscape
    2.  Confirmation of no existing competing articles
    3.  Differentiation between similar topics (e.g., cash flow projection template vs. how to calculate cash flow)

### Recent Content Updates

  - Tuning pages published last Friday (2025-02-07)
