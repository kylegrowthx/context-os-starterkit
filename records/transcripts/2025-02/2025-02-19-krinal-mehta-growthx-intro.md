## Context

fwd.digital (formerly known contextually as GrowthX in this transcript, though Marcel clarifies it's fwd.digital) is a B2B content services company founded by Marcel Santilli and a CTO who previously built If This Then That. The company started with workshops, evolved into content marketing services, and is now a series-A backed (closing the week of this call) AI-native platform that builds AI workflows represented as code. With 35 clients and 60 deals in pipeline, they're scaling rapidly—from near-zero to $6M ARR this year—by addressing a large market pain: enterprises and mid-market companies that need content strategy, execution, and AI-powered production but can't afford or manage hiring a fractional CMO, in-house team, and all the infrastructure that entails. Their average deal size is $150k/year with short sales cycles (under 30 days, mostly 1-2 meetings). Krinal Mehta is a career content and SEO professional with 15+ years in the industry (since 2008), ranging from agency work to in-house roles at brands like Pressbooks, Freshbox, and currently Newfold Digital. He's at Newfold overseeing SEO for Bluehost and Hostgator globally—a stable, well-resourced position with strong leadership buy-in for SEO. He's currently on parental leave with a newborn and returns to work March 24. The call was a recruiting exploration: Marcel showing Krinal the fwd.digital opportunity and early technical infrastructure, Krinal evaluating whether the startup risk, founder identity, and growth potential offset his current stability.
