The company builds proprietary AI workflow infrastructure (code-based, not GUI-based) that handles massive-scale content generation while maintaining quality through human-in-the-loop processes.

Kirkland is actively considering a career transition from SEO management into software engineering. He currently spends 80-90% of his time coding at ClickUp and is a Cursor power user, but he's become increasingly interested in engineering-focused roles where he can solve novel problems rather than execute repetitive SEO tasks. The conversation explores whether fwd.digital offers the right combination of technical challenge, product influence, compensation, and work flexibility that would appeal to him.
