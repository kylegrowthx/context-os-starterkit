## Key Topics

### Current SEO Status and Opportunities

  - fwd.digital uses AI workflows and human editors for end-to-end content creation
  - Process includes research, outline generation, article creation, and fact-checking
  - Custom knowledge bases created for each client to ensure accuracy
  - 5-10 pieces published weekly, adaptable based on agreed strategy

### Collaboration and Resource Requirements

  - Initial 12-week process: 1-hour kickoff, 1-2 rounds of content calibration
  - Ongoing 30-minute weekly meetings (flexible frequency)
  - fwd.digital can work directly with Abnormal's content marketers
  - Abnormal to provide access to CMS for publishing (Craft CMS)

### Handling Proprietary and Changing Information

  - Abnormal undergoing rebrand, frequent product updates pose challenges
  - fwd.digital can adapt workflows for mass content updates (e.g., company name change)
  - Custom instructions and knowledge bases can be continuously updated

### Additional Services and Considerations

  - Content refresh workflows to add SEO value to existing content
  - Potential for fwd.digital to assist with optimizing Abnormal's internally-produced content
  - Need to balance new content creation with optimization of existing assets
