## Summary

GrowthX Labs (24 people, recently funded) met with Shannon Anderson, Madrona's talent partner, to discuss hiring strategy for a dual-track approach: (1) hiring a principal engineer and 2-3 more senior technical hires through Quantum Talent, with Daniel leading; and (2) scaling the delivery team by hiring ~10 managing directors and two critical in-house recruiters (senior technical recruiter and senior go-to-market recruiter) to manage 1,000 daily applicants. ## Context

GrowthX Labs is a recently funded B2B content marketing and software company (CheckThat) led by Marcel Santilli and Daniel (co-founders). The company is in rapid growth mode at 24 people and aggressive hiring phase, having just closed funding and secured a major investment from Madrona Ventures. Bridget McGillivray, head of people/talent at GrowthX Labs, reached out to Shannon Anderson, Madrona's lead talent partner, to introduce the GrowthX team and establish a working relationship for scaling hiring. GrowthX's hiring needs fall into two distinct buckets: (1) building a world-class technical team starting with principal engineers and relying on Daniel's network plus Quantum Talent for senior hires, and (2) scaling the delivery/services side by hiring managing directors (heads of growth, VP of growth, directors of SEO/content) and establishing an internal recruitment function to manage their applicant pipeline. Shannon manages talent for 130+ portfolio companies across Madrona's West Coast-focused footprint and organizes her team (3 talent advisors based in Seattle) by managing director alignment.
