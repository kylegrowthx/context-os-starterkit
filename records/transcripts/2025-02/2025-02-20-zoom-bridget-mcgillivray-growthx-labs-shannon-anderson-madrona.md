## Key Topics

### GrowthX Labs' Hiring Model and Priorities

  - Dual focus: high-caliber technical team (product/engineering) and volume hiring for client-facing roles
  - Aiming for "Netflix mentality" of talent density for technical hires: senior, top-of-market talent
  - Partnering with Quantum Talent for key technical hires
  - Hiring \~10 managing directors (heads of growth, VP of growth, director of SEO/content)
  - Receiving \~1000 applicants daily, using AI-driven screening process

### Recruiter Hiring Strategy

  - Seeking two key recruiter roles: senior technical recruiter and senior go-to-market recruiter
  - Prioritizing recruiters with strong networks, hustle, and experience scaling companies (20-100 employees)
  - Location-agnostic, but U.S.-based candidates might have an edge for network strength
  - Emphasis on resourcefulness and ability to leverage AI/automation in recruiting processes

### Madrona's Talent Support Offerings

  - Access to Coda document with resources, compensation playbook, and benchmarks
  - Weekly executive resume distribution
  - Potential introductions to specialized recruiting agencies
  - Amplification of job postings and key hires through Madrona's channels post-funding announcement
  - Connections to other VC talent partners (e.g., Greylock, Iconic, Battery)

### Current Team and Notable Hires

  - 24 people currently in the company
  - Recently hired Felipe (ex-Airbnb and Stripe) as principal engineer, starting officially in March
  - Marcel (co-founder) has a strong network for growth marketing roles (70% InMail response rate)

### Compensation and Benefits Resources
