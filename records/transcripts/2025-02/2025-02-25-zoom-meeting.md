## Key Topics

### Client Feedback and Content Quality Issues

  - Client unhappy with specific use cases and accuracy of content
  - Team struggling to match client's expectations despite using multiple LLMs and human input
  - Concerns about word count and SEO opportunities conflicting with suggestions to simplify content

### Proposed Solution: Programmatic Content Approach

  - Shift focus to creating factual, resource-based content (e.g., UX researcher profiles, book summaries)
  - Example given: clay.com's approach to creating simple, factual pages
  - Potential to create a UX resource library with TLDR summaries and takeaways
  - Client initially resistant to scaling back content, preferring longer articles for SEO

### SEO Strategy Considerations

  - Kyle warned about potential risks with broad-based content strategy
  - Google algorithm changes targeting sites building domain authority for competitive keywords
  - Importance of relevance and specificity in content to avoid traffic loss

### Workflow and System Improvements

  - Team acknowledges need for calibration and system enhancements
  - Considering use of ChatGPT or other tools to improve output quality in short term
  - Plans to leverage post-processing steps to refine formatting and style based on client preferences

### Calibration and Example-Based Approach

  - Proposal to create a high-quality calibration article as a benchmark
  - Use of examples to show potential improvements over current client content
  - Focus on style, tone, and formatting in calibration process

### Brief Adjustments

  - Removal of emphasis on accuracy in the content brief
  - Incorporation of client feedback into company brief
  - Need for further refinement of brief to align with client expectations
