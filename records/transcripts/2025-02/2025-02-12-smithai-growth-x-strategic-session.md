## Summary

GrowthX and Smith.ai held a strategic session to refine Smith.ai's content and SEO strategy, narrowing focus from their broad existing portfolio to high-priority topical opportunities. Kyle Gaudreau and Elvis Goren walked Maddy Martin through their analysis of content gaps, emphasizing AI receptionist and automation topics for SMB owners over consumer-oriented keywords, and mapping out a plan to refresh existing high-potential pages and develop calculators to drive conversions. ---

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Shift focus from broad topics to specific, tactical content for SMB owners
  - Avoid consumer-oriented keywords (e.g., "how to block spam on iPhone")
  - Target keywords with 200+ monthly searches, but consider emerging trends
  - Prioritize content that helps demand-gen managers run effective ads
  - Smith.ai's size and breadth of existing content requires disciplined prioritization to avoid scope creep

### AI Receptionist Opportunities

  - Significant potential identified in AI receptionist-related keywords
  - Plan to incorporate AI terminology into existing content to improve search engine understanding
  - Develop content around "7 productive ways to use AI for lawyers" and similar targeted topics
  - AI receptionist is positioned as Smith.ai's core offering and should be the lens through which other topics are evaluated

### Content Refresh Workflow

  - Identify existing pages ranking on 2nd page or lower for improvement
  - Extend blogs with new information, internal links, and backlinks
  - Enrich industry-specific landing pages (e.g., answering service for realtors)
  - Use AI to analyze competitor content gaps and inform expansions
  - Refresh approach requires fewer new pages and more strategic expansion of high-potential existing assets

### Lead Capture and Conversion Optimization

  - Develop calculators to demonstrate virtual receptionist value (e.g., cost savings)
  - Create single-page calculator experiences optimized for SEO
  - Improve CTAs on blog posts to guide users to next steps (e.g., "Calculate your savings")
  - Focus on motivating SMB owners to prioritize virtual receptionist adoption

### Competitor Analysis & Imagery

  - Reviewed competitors listed in shared document
  - Identified unique keyword opportunities not currently targeted by Smith.ai
  - Design resource constraints require clear creative guidelines; Smith.ai has in-house designers but will provide direction rather than outsourcing from scratch
  - Mix of approaches: stock photos, AI-generated images, brand elements, and minimal custom design to optimize for time/cost
