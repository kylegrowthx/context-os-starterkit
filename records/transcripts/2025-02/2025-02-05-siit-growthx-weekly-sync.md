## Context

Siit is a B2B SaaS company that provides IT workflow automation software for managing operations like onboarding, password resets, and other mission-critical tasks. Carmel Schetrit (Siit) leads product marketing and is working with GrowthX's Kyle Gaudreau, Jess Scott, and Sadira Sittampalam to build out a content strategy. Siit has exceptional product-market fit (70%+ demo-to-customer conversion rate, near-zero churn) but faces a significant challenge: brand awareness is critically low. In customer interviews, prospects cite concern that Siit is a small startup controlling deeply integrated IT workflows. This is Carmel's first time working with a content marketing agency at scale, so the team spent time discussing realistic workflows, content formats, and how to balance brand awareness (top-of-funnel) with action-driven content.
