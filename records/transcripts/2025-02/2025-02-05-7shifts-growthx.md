## Key Topics

### Kyle's Experience with GrowthX at Homebase

  - Implemented SEO changes at Homebase, achieving 140-170% YoY non-brand traffic growth
  - High content costs ($400k+ per quarter) led to exploring GrowthX's services
  - GrowthX reduced content costs from $600-1000+ per piece to \~$130
  - Focus shifted to driving "starter" signups, which had higher payroll adoption rates

### GrowthX's Approach and Capabilities

  - Acts as an extension of the client's team, plugging into existing processes
  - Can work across the marketing funnel, not just top-of-funnel content
  - Offers programmatic solutions for various content types (e.g., podcast transcription, webinar content)
  - Aims to free up marketing teams for more creative thinking and strategic work
  - Uses AI-powered workflows to generate high-quality content at scale

### 7shifts' Current Content and SEO Situation

  - Experiencing declining organic traffic and flat direct traffic
  - Has a 4-person content team with diverse responsibilities
  - Currently uses an external SEO strategist
  - Exploring channel diversification beyond paid acquisition
  - In early stages of payroll product offering, facing challenges in market positioning

### Potential Partnership Structure

  - GrowthX can adapt to 7shifts' existing content structure and team
  - Opportunity to increase content production and explore new content types
  - Flexible engagement models based on client needs and budget
  - Focus on providing true user value and avoiding AI-generated content pitfalls
