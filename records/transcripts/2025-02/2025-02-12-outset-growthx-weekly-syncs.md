## Context

Outset is an educational publishing platform focused on high-search-volume career and educational content, particularly in healthcare and pre-medical fields. Mariana Montezzana from GrowthX leads content production for Outset, working directly with Lawson Kuehnert (Outset's founder/leader) to develop and approve article strategies. This is part of GrowthX's content marketing services engagement with Outset—GrowthX creates the editorial calendar and article outlines, Mariana produces the content, and Lawson approves the strategy and reviews sample articles before rollout. The relationship is ongoing with weekly syncs to discuss production progress, new content ideas, and keyword opportunities.

The current phase focuses on scaling production: Outset has high-volume keywords in the "how to become a [specialty]" vertical that perform well, and the team is simultaneously exploring adjacent content opportunities in summer programs and pre-med education. A key constraint is that Outset's AI tools (and standard AI in general) struggle with finding accurate, specific information about university summer programs, so the strategy emphasizes university-run programs over competitor mentions and leverages Lawson's direct research to fill gaps.
