Kyle laid out the company's agency model with pod-based structure and significant market demand (over 12 deals in a single call, contracts ranging $9k-$40k+), while Jakub shared extensive experience scaling content operations at G2 (50k to 1.5M blog traffic), Scribe, and Active Campaign.
