## Context

Madrona Ventures' Shannon Anderson met with GrowthX Labs' Bridget McGillivray and Marcel Santilli to discuss how Madrona's talent team can support GrowthX's scaling phase. GrowthX Labs is a recently funded startup (Series A not yet closed as of Feb 2025) with two sides: a delivery business focused on B2B content marketing and AI visibility (CheckThat), and a rapidly growing engineering team building proprietary AI-powered tools.
