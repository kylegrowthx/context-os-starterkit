## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Creation Approach

  - Uses AI-assisted workflows (250+ steps) for content creation, not pure AI-generated content
  - Employs multiple AI models (GPT-3.5, GPT-4) and cloud models for different tasks
  - Human experts (writers, editors, strategists) involved throughout the process
  - Claims to increase content output from 1-2 articles/week to 10-15 articles/week per writer

### SEO and Google Algorithm Concerns

  - GrowthX emphasizes quality over pure AI content to avoid Google penalties
  - Shared case study of steadily.com rebounding from a traffic dip (40k to 20k) using their approach
  - Acknowledged that no method guarantees protection from Google updates
  - Highlighted examples of high-quality sites (HubSpot, Fit Small Business) also experiencing traffic drops

### Onboarding and Implementation Process

  - 1-week onboarding to gather client information and understand goals
  - 2-3 week ramp-up period to calibrate content and start production
  - Weekly calls and Slack communication for ongoing collaboration
  - Custom reporting on content performance and impact

### Pricing and Services

  - 50,000 words per month published
  - Includes strategist, pod lead, and multiple layers of human review
  - Custom imagery and stock photos with basic editing
  - Potential for scaling to higher volumes (e.g., 500 pages/month for some clients)
