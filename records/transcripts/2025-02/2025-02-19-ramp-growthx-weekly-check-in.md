## Context

Ramp is a fintech/payments platform providing spend management solutions for businesses. GrowthX is providing content marketing and SEO services for Ramp, with a focus on creating pages targeting specific financial and operational topics. Vivek Shankar leads the GrowthX delivery on this account, while Ali Mercieca (Ramp) reviews and approves content strategy and topics. This weekly check-in covers the performance of recently published pages across several categories (Block, Perdian, Expense Category, and Tuning pages) and discusses next steps for programmatic content expansion, particularly around treasury and bank-specific solutions.

---

## Relevance

- **To GrowthX Delivery:** Confirmed programmatic approach limits for treasury content (primarily bank-specific templates rather than treasury-general topics). Flow generator may need to handle unique subtopics per page to improve content quality, but this impacts production velocity. Team is assessing feasibility before scaling.

- **To GrowthX Business Development:** Ramp account showing active engagement on content strategy with weekly performance reviews and topic approval workflows. Opportunity to expand programmatic content at scale if template-based approach with local customization succeeds. First template (California bank treasury) launching next week.

- **To Delivery Processes:** Airtable workflow being formalized with checkbox column for topic approvals, creating a structured gate before content generation. This reduces back-and-forth and clarifies what topics Ramp approves before GrowthX invests in editing/customization.

## Overview

- Block pages showing positive momentum: clicks increased week-over-week, with "Preventing Payment Delays" page gaining the most clicks; impressions stabilized; other pages distributed clicks evenly
- Perdian pages experiencing significant impression drop across most pages; rankings remained stable, pointing to seasonality (peak in January, leveling off in February) rather than migration or ranking issues
- Expense Category pages holding steady in a narrow range over the past 4 weeks with no major CTR pattern changes
- Tuning pages (refreshed 1.5 weeks ago) showing strong results: one page saw a huge impression and click bump, contributing to all-time high impression levels; deeper analysis planned for next week
- Treasury programmatic campaign strategy narrowed to bank-specific templates (banks like PNC and Capital One); limited opportunities for state-level content beyond California and New York; California bank treasury management page selected as the first template to test with Ramp's product team
- Reporting and metrics pages need customization: template-based approach may not be sufficient; evaluating whether GrowthX's flow generator can handle unique subtopics identified from top-ranking competitor articles
