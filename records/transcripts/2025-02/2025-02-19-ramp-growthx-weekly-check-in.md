## Key Topics

### Performance Update: Block Pages

  - Clicks increased while impressions leveled out
  - "Preventing payment delays" page gained the most clicks
  - Capital metrics page had a slight CTR increase
  - Clicks were evenly distributed among other pages

### Performance Update: Perdian Pages

  - Significant drop in impressions across most pages
  - No clear patterns or migration-related issues identified
  - Possible seasonality effect (January peak, February leveling off)
  - Rankings remained stable, no major concerns

### Performance Update: Expense Category Pages

  - Clicks holding steady in a range for the past 4 weeks
  - No major CTR patterns observed
  - Page on "five prime" showed a slight increase in CTR

### Performance Update: Tuning Pages

  - 1.5 weeks since refresh of pages
  - One page saw a huge bump in impressions and clicks
  - Impressions at an all-time high post-refresh
  - Deeper analysis planned for next week with more data

### Treasury Programmatic Campaign

  - Template creation for bank-specific pages (California bank as first example)
  - Limited programmatic opportunities found beyond bank-specific treasury management solutions
  - Focus on larger banks appealing to corporates
  - Some small community-oriented banks offering similar services to Ramp
  - Low volume for state-level opportunities (mainly California and New York)

### Content Strategy: Reporting and Metrics Pages

  - Discussion on incorporating unique subtopics beyond the general template
  - Balancing between programmatic approach and editorial strategy
  - Plan to identify topics requiring unique subtopics and assess flow generator capabilities
