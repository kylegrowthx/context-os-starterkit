## Key Topics

### Company Background and Growth

  - GrowthX (formerly LLC) provides AI workflow services
  - Rapid growth: Reached $5.3M ARR
  - CTO joined as co-founder in Sept, initially as contractor
  - Bootstrapped by Marcel, now closing Series A

### C-corp Formation and Equity Structure Issues

  - Transferred assets from LLC to new C-corp
  - Key mistake: Co-founder received options instead of founder stock
  - Current split: 70/30 (Marcel/Co-founder), diluted by ESOP and Series A
  - Co-founder faces high tax burden on option exercise (ordinary income vs. QSBS)

### Proposed Solution for Co-founder's Equity

  - Cancel options, replace with shares purchased via partially-recourse promissory note
  - Example structure:
      - 5-year note, matching 5-year vesting
      - Annual forgiveness of 20% ($200K on $1M example)
      - Company grosses up co-founder's taxes (\~$50K/year in example)
  - Allows co-founder to lock in current value, potentially qualify for QSBS treatment

### Series A Details and Investor Pressure

  - $80M post-money valuation
  - Raising: $12M from Andreessen Horowitz, $250K angels, $250K SAFE note
  - A16z pushing for quick close, complicating restructuring efforts

### Current Compromise for Co-founder Equity

  - Auto-vest 50% of shares upfront for both founders
  - Remaining 50% vests over 2 years
  - Co-founder considering loan to exercise 50% upfront
  - 51% recourse loan, \~$2M liability (down from $8M for 100%)

## Action Items
