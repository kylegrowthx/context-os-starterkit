## Key Topics

### Company Overview and Growth

  - GrowthX Labs has grown from $2M to $3M in annual recurring revenue last month
  - Currently at 25 clients, aiming for 100 clients by end of 2025 ($12-20M revenue)
  - Transitioning from overlapping roles to more defined structure and processes
  - Leveraging AI for efficient service delivery, aiming for "software margins" in services

### Company Values and Principles

  - Core values: Do best work, be energized, push boundaries of what's possible
  - Key principles: Communicate with clarity, relentless excellence, never stop learning, own the outcome
  - Emphasis on AI-native approach and continuous learning to stay ahead of competition

### Team Structure and Roles

  - Moving to a pod-based structure for client management
  - Pod lead: Owns strategy, delegates tasks, manages resources
  - Account manager: Handles day-to-day client interactions, contributes to strategy
  - Writers and other specialists dedicated to specific pods

### Tools and Processes

  - Notion: Primary documentation and knowledge base
  - Slack: Main communication tool, with channels for each client and internal discussions
  - AirOps: Workflow tool for content research and generation
  - Airtable: Client content operating system for organizing assignments

### Onboarding Process

  - New hires to review personal training section in Notion
  - Familiarize with assigned client accounts, watch past meeting recordings
  - Explore AirOps and Airtable workflows
  - Holiday break coming up (weeks of Dec 23 and Dec 30)

### Client Management

  - Weekly calls with most clients, transitioning to less frequent for some
  - Opportunity to improve agenda and data presentation in client meetings
  - Need for better process in reviewing and updating client briefs with feedback
