## Key Topics

### Client Context and Expectations

  - Data Grid (formerly Torik) pivoted from construction ETL to AI agents on data
  - Founded by Tiago, who had previous $60M exit; well-funded and connected
  - Client wants to see fast execution to prove product-market fit for fundraising
  - Budget: $39k/month, potential to grow to $1-2M/year account

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Focus on programmatic SEO, not highly editorialized content
  - Target sales/marketing personas, RevOps, and Salesforce/HubSpot users
  - Use competitor keyword gap analysis (e.g., Bardeen, Gong, HubSpot, Zapier)
  - Cluster topics into themes/buckets for easier presentation and planning
  - Aim for 1000+ content pieces over the year, focusing on volume and speed

### SEO and Topic Research Process

  - Use tools like Ahrefs for competitor keyword analysis
  - Filter keywords by volume and difficulty
  - Import data into Airtable for organization and clustering
  - Create assignments based on competitive URLs
  - Use roll-ups to show total volume and average difficulty for clusters

### Content Production and Scaling

  - Consider hiring inexpensive freelancers for examples and screenshots
  - Leverage Data Grid's platform for some analysis tasks to save on AirOps costs
  - Balance between programmatic approach and selective manual review
  - Potential to create new web experiences (e.g., "Answers" section) in the future

### Outbound and Additional Strategies

  - Jimmy to set up outbound campaigns
  - Explore GPTs people have built for additional topic ideas
  - Consider creating calculators or tools for sales/marketing metrics
