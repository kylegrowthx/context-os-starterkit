## Key Topics

### Teero's Business Overview

  - Dental staffing platform, currently focused on hygienist staffing
  - Growing 7x year-over-year, operating in 10 states
  - Plans to expand into other dental practice management products (payroll, scheduling, patient booking, insurance verification)
  - Target customers: dental practices in the US, ranging from small private practices to larger DSOs (Dental Service Organizations)

### Content Strategy Goals

  - Drive inbound traffic from qualified dental practices
  - Build credibility in the dental industry beyond staffing
  - Generate interest for future product offerings
  - Measure success through inbound dental practice signups and cost-effective customer acquisition compared to outbound sales

### Target Audience and Personas

  - Primary: Dental office/practice managers (80% focus)
  - Secondary: Dentists, regional managers, directors of recruiting at larger DSOs
  - Tertiary: Hygienists (20% focus for basic, high-impact topics)

### Content Themes and Opportunities

  - Location-based content (e.g., specific to states or cities)
  - Private practice vs. DSO-specific content
  - Hygienist staffing challenges and solutions
  - Dental practice management advice (e.g., payroll, benefits, hiring)
  - Compliance and legal issues (e.g., 1099 vs. W-2 employment)
  - Future product-related topics (e.g., payroll, insurance verification, patient booking)

### Content Creation and Publishing Process

  - GrowthX to handle content creation and publishing
  - Initial phase: Teero team (Nate/Christian) to review content before publishing
  - Long-term goal: Increase autonomy for GrowthX team to publish directly
  - Frank (Teero) to provide CMS access and discuss category structure with GrowthX

### Collaboration and Communication

  - Weekly sync meetings, preferably on Wednesdays or Thursdays
  - Ad-hoc communication via Slack for quick updates and document sharing
  - GrowthX to build content workflows in Teero's Airtable instance for transparency
