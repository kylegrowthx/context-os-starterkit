## Key Topics

### Client Meeting Reflection

  - Initial concerns about meeting performance, but ended better than anticipated
  - Challenges in keeping client focused and managing expectations
  - Recognition of need to refine approach for similar future engagements
  - Despite difficulties, team reached a good outcome with the client

### Strategic Value of Reddit Partnership

  - Reddit's brand strength makes it a uniquely valuable client
  - Partnership can significantly raise fwd.digital's profile and attract other clients
  - Potential to leverage Reddit association in outbound marketing (e.g., "Reddit's growth partner")
  - Opportunity to run programs and webinars that benefit both Reddit and fwd.digital's growth

### Resource Allocation and Pricing Strategy

  - Considering dedicating 2-3 full-time staff to Reddit account, despite potential initial losses
  - Proposed pricing around $40k/month, with the possibility of growing to $200k/month
  - Viewing Reddit engagement as part of fwd.digital's marketing budget
  - Willingness to offer services beyond typical scope due to Reddit's strategic importance

### Scope and Deliverables

  - Focus on content creation, potentially 3-5,000 pages
  - Email outreach capabilities, sending thousands per day
  - Potential for lifecycle marketing and email automation experimentation
  - Need to clearly define guardrails for experimental aspects of engagement

### Concerns and Considerations

  - Balancing ambitious offerings with realistic expectations and resource constraints
  - Importance of setting clear expectations about pilot nature of some elements
  - Potential challenges in scaling dedicated resources for an experimental engagement
  - Need to ensure repeatability and profitability of services offered
