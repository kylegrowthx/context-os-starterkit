## Key Topics

### Current SEO Strategy at Hims

  - Traditional focus on blog content and storefront pages
  - Recent expansion into tools, calculators, and drug-related landing pages
  - Seeking to shorten time-to-publish for new pages significantly
  - Exploring efficiency gains while maintaining quality content and brand voice

### GrowthX's AI-Assisted Content Creation Process

  - Utilizes complex workflows with over 100 steps
  - Incorporates custom researchers and knowledge bases
  - Human-in-the-loop approach with experienced managing editors
  - Post-processing for brand voice adaptation and formatting
  - Fact-checking and legal review integration possible
  - Estimated 80% AI workflow, 20% human involvement (varies by project)

### Potential Use Cases for Hims

  - Drug comparison pages
  - "Near me" pages for doctors or health specialists by location
  - Possibility of leveraging public data on doctors and specialties
  - Consideration of pharmacy-related content (similar to GoodRx model)

### Integration and Scalability

  - GrowthX can integrate with Contentful (Hims' CMS)
  - Ability to scale up content production rapidly
  - Option for iterative approach: start with "good" content, then enrich based on performance signals
  - Potential to automate CTA placement, conversion modules, and analytics integration

### Pricing and Workflow Structure

  - Programmatic content typically priced at $9,000/month per workstream
  - One workstream produces about 1,200 pages annually
  - Editorial content priced at 10-20 cents per word
  - Workstream includes dedicated managing editor and writer team
