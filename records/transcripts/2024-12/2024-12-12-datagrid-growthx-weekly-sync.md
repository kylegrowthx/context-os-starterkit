## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Overview

  - GrowthX presented Airtable with high-level content opportunities focused on GTM use cases and productivity enhancements
  - Major categories: AI-powered sales/marketing, Google Workspace tips, email management, video conferencing
  - Estimated search volume for identified topics: 8.9M+ monthly searches in the US alone
  - Plan: Start with 2-3 initial articles next week for tone calibration, then ramp up production in January

### Datagrid's Target Industries and Differentiators

  - Focus on heavy document industries: construction, real estate, healthcare, finance, government, energy, insurance
  - Key differentiator: ability to process large volumes of documents (Word, PDF) efficiently
  - Example use case: VP of Sales at Equinix (data center company) interested in RFP automation
  - Datagrid's strength: Loading and analyzing thousands of multi-page PDFs for easy querying

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Inject industry-specific use cases into broader content categories
  - Potential focus areas:
    1.  RFP automation and analysis
    2.  PDF data extraction and manipulation
    3.  Excel automation for complex calculations
    4.  Document workflows for bid management
  - Balance between general AI topics and specialized industry applications

### Outbound Campaign Progress

  - Similar client in enterprise AI automation secured first meeting after 2 weeks
  - Holiday season may limit initial results, but full ramp-up planned for January
  - Starting with 5 emails/day/account (200-300 total), increasing to 1200-1500/day in January
