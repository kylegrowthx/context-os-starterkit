## Key Topics

### Content Performance and Strategy

  - Seeing consistent growth in organic traffic and keyword rankings
  - Block-level performance shows even better results
  - Daily clicks reaching 2,000; some clients achieve up to 10,000 daily clicks
  - Focus on event registration and related keywords as per previous discussion with Stacey

### Lead Generation and Tracking

  - Suggestion to include conversion panels and checklists in articles (0.5-2% conversion rate)
  - Considering implementation of RB2B or similar tools for visitor identification (5-20% identification rate)
  - Need to track not just clicks and impressions, but lead quality and ICP alignment
  - Discussion of potential tools like 6sense, RollWorks for enhanced tracking (planned for Q1)

### Content Review and Optimization

  - Liz (contractor) to review event registration content for product alignment
  - Will add Liz to Airtable for commenting and feedback
  - Suggestion to prioritize review of best-performing blog articles that align with key topics

### Brand-Specific Content

  - Discussed possibility of creating more brand-specific, comparison-style content
  - Swoogo team indicates this would likely be handled internally, not outsourced

### Visual Content Enhancement

  - Suggestion to incorporate design elements and custom graphics into blog posts
  - Will to provide Figma file with brand guidelines and past charts/graphs
  - Example shared of Reddit's approach using a Google Drive folder with named images

### Call-to-Action Implementation

  - Current blog template lacks robust CTA mechanisms
  - Discussed adding offers, ebooks, or data reports as lead magnets
  - Will and Rebecca to explore options for standardizing CTAs across blog post templates in 2024
  - Potential use of OptinMonster for WordPress implementation
