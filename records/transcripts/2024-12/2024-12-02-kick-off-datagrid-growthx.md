## Key Topics

### Engagement Overview

  - December focus: Content creation and gaining context
  - January onwards: Additional workstreams (editorial, programmatic SEO, backlinking, outbound)
  - Co-marketing and product advisory components included
  - GrowthX to leverage 25 existing customers for use case validation and potential Datagrid clients

### Goals and Metrics

  - Increase awareness and relevancy among target audience
  - Achieve 5 major non-construction logos on website
  - Reach 100 new users per day (current best: 100/week)
  - Attain $1M ARR faster than previous product (took 2 years previously)
  - Current ARR: \~$100K (combining credit card signups and Toric deal)

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Operations teams across various industries
  - Founders and entrepreneurial roles (including sales)
  - Focus on tasks involving multiple data sources and context
  - Examples: Product marketing, board meeting preparation, information retrieval and synthesis

### Content Strategy

  - Balance between broad SEO topics and specific AI agent use cases
  - Programmatic approach for high-volume, tactical content (e.g., "How to connect GitHub to ChatGPT")
  - Create content around common tasks in popular tools, introducing AI as a solution
  - Explore vertical-specific content (e.g., construction, insurance) without becoming overly specialized

### Product Differentiation

  - Customizable and flexible AI agents
  - Ability to choose AI models and toggle specific tools/features
  - Strong performance in "needle in a haystack" type problems (e.g., finding specific info in numerous PDFs)

### Current Sales and Marketing Efforts

  - Primarily inbound leads from trade shows
  - Positive response to vision/image support feature
  - Limited outbound efforts currently
