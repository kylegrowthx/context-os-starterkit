## Key Topics

### Scaling Challenges

  - Team is spending too much time on account delivery vs. building scalable processes
  - Need to shift from "services mentality" to "product mentality" with defined work streams and outputs
  - Lack of clear, documented processes for new hires to follow
  - Leadership team is too involved in day-to-day account work

### Process Improvements

  - Create detailed 12-week client onboarding template with weekly tasks/deliverables
  - Implement task management system to track progress across accounts
  - Define clear roles/responsibilities for team members at different levels
  - Consider specialized roles (e.g. onboarding specialist) or program manager to oversee processes

### Staffing Model

  - Discuss optimal pod structure and staffing ratios
  - Clarify account allocation process
  - Ensure proper training and enablement for new hires

### Technology Needs

  - Daniel to focus on building "Content OS" platform to systematize workflows
  - Automate repetitive tasks like Airtable setup
  - Improve tools for keyword research, topic clustering, etc.
