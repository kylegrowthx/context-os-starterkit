## Key Topics

### SOW Overview and Content Strategies

  - Editorial content and programmatic COPs targeting 3,000 pages (minimum)
  - Focus on templatization to increase efficiency and volume
  - Content repurposing, nurture sequences, and automated emails planned
  - Localization experiments proposed, targeting city-level content and small business ecosystems

### Influencer and Agency Program

  - Unique approach combining distribution, access, and AI outbound
  - Goals: drive awareness, mindshare, direct sign-ups, and agency reach
  - Strategy: recruit influencers for co-created content and webinars
  - Emphasis on growth stories "brought to you by Reddit" rather than solely Reddit-focused content

### Localized Growth Strategy

  - Inspired by successful app growth models (e.g., Tinder's campus approach)
  - Target influential local business organizations (e.g., Chambers of Commerce, Hispanic business associations)
  - Create "domino effect" by influencing micro-influencers in smaller communities
  - Scale through hyper-focused approach, moving from one locality to the next

### Link Building Initiative

  - Aim for 20-30 high domain authority backlinks per month
  - Leverage Reddit's desirability as a backlink source
  - Focus on strategic, high-value targets for maximum impact

### Team and Resource Allocation

  - GrowthX planning to hire and dedicate more team members to Reddit
  - Seeking clarity on scope to inform investment decisions
  - Current contract ends January 18, adding time sensitivity to discussions
