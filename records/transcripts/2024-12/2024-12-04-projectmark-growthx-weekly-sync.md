## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Update

  - 6,000 impressions, 40 clicks since September 4th
  - Best performing blog: "Instructions Sales Process" (8 clicks, 2,000+ impressions)
  - 25 organic leads generated (HubSpot data), with 17 in November alone
  - Current performance focused on valuable bottom-of-funnel traffic

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Agreed to adjust content mix: 60-70% top-of-funnel, 30-40% middle/bottom-of-funnel
  - Goal: Increase overall traffic while maintaining valuable targeted content
  - Example keyword progression: "What is CRM software" (top) → "Best CRM tools" (middle) → "Buy CRM tools" (bottom)

### Publishing Process Improvements

  - Expedite content approval process, especially for top-of-funnel pieces
  - Publish articles more frequently to identify high-performing content angles
  - Implement tagging system to manage blog homepage display
  - Add publishing dates to articles for SEO benefits
  - Schedule articles for consistent publishing (e.g., 5 per day, including weekends)

### Meta Description Optimization

  - Identified need to improve meta descriptions for high-impression, low-click articles
  - Example: "Ultimate Guide" article with 18,000 impressions but only 1 click
  - Team to review and optimize meta descriptions, especially for high-potential content

### Competitor Backlink Caution

  - Be mindful of backlinking to competitor websites in content
