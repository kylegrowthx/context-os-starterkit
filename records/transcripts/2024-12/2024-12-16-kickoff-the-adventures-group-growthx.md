## Key Topics

### Company Overview and Growth Goals

  - The Adventures Group operates in three destinations: Puerto Vallarta, Cabo, and Cancun
  - 2025 goal: 20% year-over-year growth (achievable based on past performance)
  - Cancun is the most challenging market due to strong competition (e.g., Xcaret)
  - Organic traffic is particularly important for Cancun (74% of visitors)

### Customer Demographics and Booking Patterns

  - Majority of customers are couples/adults (25-45 years old) with high income
  - Families make up a smaller portion (e.g., 18% for dolphin programs in Cabo)
  - Booking windows vary: Cabo \~17 days, research starts 1-2 months before trip
  - 90% of revenue from English-speaking audience, but significant Mexican traffic

### Content Strategy and Opportunities

  - Focus on becoming the "source of truth" for destination information
  - Expand content beyond tour descriptions (e.g., restaurant guides, itineraries)
  - Create content linking all three destinations to boost overall traffic
  - Explore interactive content ideas (e.g., quizzes, trip planners)

### Technical SEO and Website Structure

  - Recent migration to GA4 has caused tracking issues; working on fixing analytics
  - Using SEMrush and Ahrefs for technical SEO monitoring and backlink analysis
  - Plans to improve blog structure with better categorization and tagging
  - IT team can develop new functionalities, but there may be timeline constraints

### Content Creation and Approval Process

  - Start with a small volume of articles to refine the process
  - English content created first, then translated to Spanish
  - Need for a dedicated team member to review content for accuracy and local nuances
  - Photo selection process to be streamlined, possibly using AI for tagging and categorization
