  - Using keyword research to identify potential topics related to Prophecy's offerings

  - Goal: Identify 10-20 initial topics to start content production in January
  - Strategy includes both bottom-funnel (product-related) and top-funnel (educational) content

### Keyword Analysis Process

  - Considered Prophecy's offerings, customer needs, and competitor topics
  - Analyzed keywords for search volume, difficulty, and relevance
  - Average keyword difficulty of 43 for sample ETL topic, with 27,000 monthly searches

### Content Clustering Approach

  - Building topic clusters to establish thought leadership and improve ranking chances
  - Creating interconnected content pieces (e.g., definitions, guides, listicles) around core topics
  - Focus on depth and quality to enhance ranking potential over time

  - Team to review and rate topics on a 1-4 scale

  - Vivek's team to refine categories and rerun analysis based on feedback

  - Plan to produce first calibration articles in early January for review

### Extended Content Opportunities
