## Key Topics

### Current Marketing Approach and Goals

  - Heavily reliant on ABM and events, looking to diversify channels and lower acquisition costs
  - Aiming for 4x pipeline coverage in Q1 2025, organic efforts to help fill gap
  - Key metrics: New ARR, overall ARR growth (doubling/tripling range), pipeline contribution, cost per lead
  - Shifting focus to data platform/analytics leaders vs end users

### Target Audience and Pain Points

  - Enterprise Databricks customers (2000+, 300 with $1M+ spend)
  - Key pain: Data access bottlenecks despite major investments in data infrastructure
  - Audience includes data engineers, data scientists, analysts
  - Gartner and other analysts have significant influence on target audience

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Build out "content clusters" around key topics, campaigns, and audience pain points
  - Leverage existing assets (e.g. CEO whitepapers) as starting points for new content
  - Focus on problems/outcomes vs technical jargon (e.g. "ETL modernization")
  - Aim for mix of timely, link-building content and evergreen, pipeline-driving pieces

### GrowthX Process and Tools

  - Will use AI to increase content velocity while maintaining quality
  - Building custom knowledge base from Prophecy materials
  - Can add unique POV/enrichment as post-processing step
  - Will present initial content strategy next week, start calibrating with 1-2 sample pieces
