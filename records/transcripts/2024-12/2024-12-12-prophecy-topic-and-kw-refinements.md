## Key Topics

### ETL Modernization Overview

  - Central theme: Modernizing pipelines and compute for cloud platforms (e.g., Snowflake, Databricks)
  - Key aspects: Speed to insight, automation (not real-time processing)
  - Subtopics: Cloud readiness, process changes, cost efficiency
  - Shift from ETL to ELT: Extract and Load are simple, Transform is the critical step
  - Goal: Enable flexible data usage for AI/ML and advanced analytics

### Legacy System Pain Points

  - "Black box" nature of old systems causing governance and visibility issues
  - Difficulty in adapting to new cloud environments and AI/ML demands
  - Cost inefficiencies (e.g., per-user licensing models in legacy tools)
  - Limited ability to handle unstructured data (e.g., documents, content)

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Focus on educating about problems and solutions without overtly pushing Prophecy
  - Create content addressing specific use cases (e.g., "How to do X in Informatica")
  - Develop hierarchical content structure: broad topics (e.g., AI readiness) down to specific pain points
  - Balance between highly targeted, low-volume keywords and broader topics for traffic

### Persona Targeting

  - Primary focus: Platform leaders and line-of-business leaders (not individual data engineers)
  - Address decision-makers' concerns: cost savings, governance, flexibility for AI/ML
  - Highlight the "renaissance of data" due to AI, but emphasize the need for modernization
