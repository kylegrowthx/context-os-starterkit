## Key Topics

### Editorial Content Strategy

  - Recommended as the fastest way to start delivering value and generating leads
  - Can validate topics/keywords of interest through blog posts, guides, etc.
  - Allows learning what resonates before investing more in a potential directory
  - AirOps can support cost-effectively producing high-quality content at scale

### Potential Directory Strategy

  - Could create listings/directory of event services companies to drive relevant traffic
  - Needs careful evaluation of search demand, ability to convert traffic to leads, and resources required
  - Different from DeepGram's strategy of capturing their customer's audience
  - More of a long-term, resource-intensive play than editorial content

### Programmatic SEO Workflows

  - Marcel's team has developed AI-powered workflows to generate content at scale
  - Covers all steps from research to writing to publishing and optimizing
  - Allows rapidly creating large amounts of content aligned to search demand
  - Dedicated specialist is assigned to manage the workflows for each client
