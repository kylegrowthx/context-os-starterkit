## Overview

- AI can help identify unique content opportunities, do research, and elevate content quality
- Building custom AI workflows allows for more control and adaptability vs black box solutions
- Start with lower risk content like glossaries before trying end-to-end article generation
- Use a central repository like Airtable as a "content workflow management system"
- Focus on providing unique value to your target audience with your content strategy
- Measure ROI based on cost savings and rising domain authority, not immediate revenue

---

## Key Topics

### Using AI for content opportunity research

  - Use tools like perplexity.ai, chat.openai.com to identify questions, pain points, topics to cover
  - Analyze competitors' content with SEMrush to find keyword opportunities
  - Feed research into ChatGPT to brainstorm angles, cluster topics, generate ideas

### Building AI workflows in AirOps

  - Two types of apps: agent apps (chatbots) and workflow apps
  - Key components: inputs, outputs, AI steps, logic, memory, utilities, integrations
  - Start simple, test and iterate to refine prompts and improve output quality
  - Use human review steps for QA
  - Manage costs by running in batches, using your own API keys

### Content workflow management with Airtable

  - Use as central database to track content production - articles, images, metadata
  - Integrate with AirOps, Zapier to automate workflows
  - Enables collaboration, version control, analysis of content performance

### Improving efficiency and reducing costs

  - Classify and analyze existing content to identify improvement opportunities at scale
  - Automate repetitive tasks like meta descriptions, internal linking
  - Adapt AI-generated content to be more human-like with LLM prompts
  - Aim for "good enough" to publish quickly, then enrich top-performing content

### Driving traffic and conversions

  - Focus on site speed, visuals, answering user questions well
  - Keep content fresh with frequent updates
  - Use lead magnets and newsletter to capture audience
  - Cross-link extensively to boost indexing and domain authority
