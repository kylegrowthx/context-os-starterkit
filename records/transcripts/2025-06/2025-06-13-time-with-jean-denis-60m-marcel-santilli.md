## Key Topics

### Company Context and Positioning

  - Town provides tax preparation services for SMBs, focusing on businesses with $250K-$5M annual revenue
  - Differentiators: Responsive CPAs + AI-powered contextual tax advice
  - Rebranding and new website launch in August
  - Targeting e-commerce, startups, professional services (doctors, dentists, law firms, consultants)

### Growth Strategy and Objectives

  - 2025 goal: 2,000 businesses, $2-6M revenue (up from $350-400K this year)
  - Primary focus on partnerships, aiming to lock in 2,000 customers
  - Direct acquisition goal: additional 1,000 customers (would be "ecstatic")
  - Building capacity for 3,500 customers to allow for overperformance

### Content Strategy Priorities

  - Build credibility through comprehensive, high-quality tax content
  - Create a "tax library" covering 35 most important business tax topics
  - Focus on SMB content strategy initially, partner content strategy later
  - Improve domain authority and search rankings for key terms
  - Explore creative, unique content ideas to stand out in the market

### Customer Acquisition and Seasonality

  - Tax season (Jan-Apr) is critical, with 80% of customers acquired then
  - Current CAC in industry: $300-400
  - CPC: $3-4 normally, jumps to $8-12 in Jan-Apr
  - Considering monthly subscription model to build loyalty pre-tax season

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main competition: local CPAs (90% of market)
  - Other players: TurboTax for Business, H\&R Block (lower-end)
  - Emerging competitors: Fifteenth (high-end focus), Rivets Pass (offshore model)
