## Key Topics

### Advocate's Content Goals and Challenges

  - Targeting late-stage case leads, worth \~$1,000 per lead
  - Competing with sites like Atticus (130k+ monthly organic traffic)
  - Need for lawyer-written or lawyer-edited content to maintain credibility
  - Current success with Google Ads, but looking to diversify channels
  - Potential internal resources: EDPNA (non-lawyer advocate) and former Forbes editor (lawyer)

### GrowthX AI's Content Creation Process

  - Develops a "Content OS" using AirTable for topic organization and planning
  - Utilizes persona analysis, jobs-to-be-done framework, and competitor gap analysis
  - Implements fact-checking tools (e.g., Checkr) to ensure content accuracy
  - Offers flexible attribution options for published content
  - Typical output: 5-10 articles per week, depending on complexity and review process

### Proposed Engagement Structure

  - Strategy Sprint (6-8 weeks): $30,000
      - Collaborative process to set foundation and align on content strategy
      - Deliverables include customer insights, competitor analysis, voice/tone guidelines, and editorial roadmap
  - Execution Phase: $15,000/month
      - Content creation, fact-checking, and publishing support
      - Volume and cost may adjust based on Strategy Sprint findings
