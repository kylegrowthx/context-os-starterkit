## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Service Overview

  - Combines AI-powered workflows with expert human oversight
  - Focus on editorial content (10-15 pieces/week) and programmatic content (50-75 pages/week at higher tiers)
  - Uses modular workflows adaptable to different AI models (e.g., Claude for article generation)
  - Offers end-to-end service from strategy to content creation and publishing

### Pricing and Engagement Structure

  - Strategy sprint: $20K upfront for 6-8 weeks
  - Execution phase: Starts at $15K/month, rarely exceeds $30K/month
  - 12-month agreement with opt-out option after strategy sprint
  - Includes all compute and API costs

### Content Creation Process

  - Develops "Content OS" with detailed keyword and topic strategy
  - Uses Airtable for content tracking, Slack for communication, and Notion for documentation
  - Calibration process to ensure content meets client standards
  - Flexible review process based on client preferences

### GrowthX vs. Competitors (e.g., AirOps)

  - GrowthX focuses on service delivery rather than providing a self-serve platform
  - Currently doesn't offer direct access to workflows, but considering future options
  - Emphasizes collaboration and strategy over purely technical solution

### Finder's Interests and Concerns

  - Seeking AI-first approach to rebuild content strategy
  - Interested in increasing output while reducing headcount/costs
  - Wants visibility into content creation process and ability to influence workflows
  - Values GrowthX's experience across multiple clients
