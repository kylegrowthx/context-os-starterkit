## Context

Neighbor is a hyper-local marketplace enabling individuals and businesses to monetize unused space (storage, parking). The company operates at scale with 30,000+ dynamic landing pages targeting renters across geographies and use cases (monthly parking in Chicago, long-term parking at LAX, RV storage in Topeka, etc.), with strong organic traction through programmatic SEO and Google Maps. Reid Anderson leads SEO efforts and is exploring new growth channels, particularly editorial content targeting B2B hosts (multifamily property owners like Equity Residential, hospitality operators, retail property owners) who have seen meaningful ancillary revenue by listing on Neighbor.

This call was a first meeting to explore GrowthX's capabilities for host-side editorial SEO. Reid had tracked GrowthX's founder Marcel from DeepGram to Aerox, has network overlap (worked with Alex at Masterclass, who connected to Aerox), and reached out proactively. The call occurred just as Neighbor was hiring a new SEO team member, creating timing uncertainty around project start.
