## Context

This meeting brought together GrowthX's newly formed pod for the first time as a cohesive unit. The team spans eight people across multiple time zones (Brooklyn, Lisbon, Lagos, GMT+4, Florianópolis, Bangalore, and Montevideo) ranging from 3-7 hours of overlap with San Francisco time. The pod includes six content managers (CMs) and two managing editors (MEs) who work with multiple clients. The context for this kickoff is critical: GrowthX is migrating from its legacy content delivery system to Atlas, a custom-built platform designed to be faster and more efficient. This migration is happening concurrently with onboarding new clients (Abnormal Security and Immobit), creating inherent tension between learning new tools and delivering on client commitments. Mara Leighton, the pod lead based in Brooklyn, was brought in to manage the team through this transition period and advocate for their needs as engineering addresses inevitable bugs and issues.
