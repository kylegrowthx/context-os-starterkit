## Context

GrowthX is the content marketing and AI visibility strategy partner for Adventures Group, a portfolio of tour companies including Vallarta Adventures, Cancun Adventures, and Cabo Adventures operating in Mexico's tourism market. These are weekly syncs between Mariana Montezzana (GrowthX Lead) and the Adventures Group team to align on content strategy, performance metrics, and implementation of tour-focused AI visibility improvements. The relationship centers on creating detailed, location-aware tour content that captures search demand from both general travelers and repeat visitors (snowbirds and expats) seeking local knowledge. This meeting occurred during a system transition from GrowthX's legacy Aerox platform to a custom Atlas-based system, temporarily reducing content publication capacity but enabling more scalable content workflows.
