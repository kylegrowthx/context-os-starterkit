## Summary

GrowthX kicked off a strategy sprint with Monograph to accelerate content marketing and drive marketing-qualified leads from 30 to 40-50 per month by year-end. The engagement focuses on leveraging Monograph's unique benchmark data, customer testimonials, and "Monograph Method" best practices to overcome a content creation bottleneck and directly compete with BQE (the primary competitor). GrowthX will deliver AI-assisted content production, webinar strategy, and thought leadership development while Monograph provides access to proprietary data assets and customer success insights.

---

## Context

Monograph is a venture-backed SaaS platform serving US-based architecture and engineering firms with 5-50 employees. They've built an operating system for managing project delivery, financials, and operations using the "Monograph Method" — a framework emphasizing daily time tracking and monthly invoicing/collection for improved cash flow. Robert Yuen (VP Sales) has restructured the sales organization from remote to on-site over the past three quarters, achieving predictable scaling at 140-150 qualified opportunities per month (with marketing currently contributing 30 per month). The company is growing 70-100% year-over-year and sees a clear growth ceiling unless marketing performance improves. Their primary competitor is BQE, a PE-owned SaaS business with $30M ARR growing at 15% annually. This kickoff with GrowthX was initiated to break through the marketing bottleneck and increase lead generation to hit aggressive growth targets. GrowthX is bringing strategic expertise in content marketing, demand generation, and go-to-market strategy, with George Haikal (from Techstars background) and Aida Knezevic leading the engagement alongside Marcel Santilli and Kyle Gaudreau.

  - \~140-150 total SQLs/month, with marketing contributing 30 consistently
