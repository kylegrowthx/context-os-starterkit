## Summary

Marcel and Kady reconnected to explore strategic partnership opportunities between GrowthX and You.com. Marcel demonstrated GrowthX's content OS platform with workflow engines that ingest research from APIs like You.com and Perplexity, running millions of workflow activities monthly.
