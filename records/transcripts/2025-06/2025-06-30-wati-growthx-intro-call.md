## Key Topics

### Wati's Content Challenges and Needs

  - Current team of 3 content creators struggling with depth and volume
  - Launching new product with insufficient content resources
  - Need help with competitor analysis, content gap identification, and SEO strategy
  - Desire for more human-like, differentiated content than pure AI solutions
  - Operating in multiple global markets (LATAM, GCC, Hong Kong) requiring multilingual content

### GrowthX's Offering and Process

  - End-to-end managed service: strategy, content creation, images, publishing
  - 6-8 week initial strategy sprint to develop personas, guidelines, and content roadmap
  - Ongoing collaboration via Slack, Notion, and weekly meetings
  - Custom AI workflows with human oversight for quality and brand alignment
  - Content OS in Airtable for planning, tracking, and transparency
  - Capability to incorporate first-party data and customize workflows

### Technical Platform and Workflows

  - Custom-built platform with visual workflow engine
  - AI-powered content generation with human editing capabilities
  - Fact-checking and citation workflows
  - Integration with analytics tools (GSC, GA, SEMrush) for performance tracking
  - Developing comprehensive content dashboard (estimated 2 months away)

### Engagement Structure and Pricing

  - 6-8 week strategy sprint at $20K, no long-term obligation
  - Typical engagements aim for 12-month programs
  - Currently booking 4+ weeks out for new client kickoffs
