## Summary

Jason Gong and Stephen Oladele explored a partnership where Neurl's engineer-creators would provide technical content review and feedback for GrowthX's client work (Gitpod for DevOps, Webflow and Strapi for web development). The model starts with reviews at $20-30/hour, with experts providing feedback at the outline and final review stages, and potential for later involvement if they're needed earlier in the process. They agreed to start with a test using Gitpod and Strapi content, with Stephen bringing DevOps and web development experts into a shared Slack channel and Jason providing detailed briefs on GrowthX's customers and content types.

## Context

Stephen Oladele founded Neurl, a platform that connects companies with engineer-creators for content production. Stephen previously worked with Keith Graham at GrowthX as a contractor while also contracting with DeepGram, so this meeting was a direct introduction via Marcel to explore a new partnership. Neurl maintains a network of full-time engineers across cloud, data, AI, and web technologies who create technical content on the side, with talent distributed across the US, Canada, Nigeria, Botswana, and Ghana.

GrowthX's core challenge is that writers on its content delivery team often lack the technical background to verify accuracy in specialized topics like DevOps, data engineering, and web development. Jason Gong wanted to find a way to inject technical expertise into their content review workflow without the high cost of full-time specialists or creating bottlenecks. The conversation centered on designing a lightweight, efficient collaboration model where Neurl experts would review near-final content and provide feedback that GrowthX writers could iterate on before publication.
