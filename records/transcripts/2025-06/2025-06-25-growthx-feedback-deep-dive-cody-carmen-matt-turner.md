## Overview

- GrowthX is viewed as a strategic partner, not just for SEO execution but overall content strategy

## Key Topics

### Partnership Background and Goals

  - Prophecy.io engaged GrowthX in Nov 2024 to scale content creation
  - Wanted to move from manual process (1-3 articles/month) to AI-assisted (40-50/week)
  - Initial concerns about volume vs quality and ability to review high output
  - Normalized to 5-7 articles/week, focusing on right technical level and topics

### Content Generation Process Evolution

  - Added upstream topic approval step to ensure relevance
  - Improved calibration on technical depth and Prophecy-specific content
  - Saw consistent improvements in writing quality after feedback
  - Shifted from keyword-focused to topic cluster approach for SEO

### Performance Reporting and Benchmarks

  - GrowthX responsive to requests for new performance metrics
  - Challenge in providing industry benchmarks due to Prophecy's niche
  - Desire for more context on performance relative to Prophecy's historical data
  - Overall positive trend in organic traffic attributed to GrowthX partnership

### New GrowthX Platform Preview

  - Moving workflows from AirOps to proprietary platform
  - Building comprehensive, editable artifacts (personas, guidelines) into system
  - Will allow partners more visibility and control over content generation process
  - Potential for partners to experiment with different inputs and see results
