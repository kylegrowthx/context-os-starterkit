## Overview

- GrowthX combines AI software and expert services to deliver high-quality content and marketing solutions for companies across various stages and industries.

- GrowthX is preparing for a Series B round in August/September, focusing on connecting their expert hub, workflow engine, and learning engine.

---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Business Model and Services

  - Evolved from building publications inside companies to a full-service AI-powered content and marketing solution
  - Combines AI software with expert services, allowing for scalable, high-quality outputs
  - Works with a range of clients from pre-launch startups to public companies
  - Services include content creation, SEO optimization, thought leadership, and custom growth strategies

### Technology Platform and Workflows

  - Developed a proprietary "Content OS" with a coding agent that builds complex workflows
  - Platform includes strategy sprints, artifact creation, and AI-powered content generation and fact-checking
  - Enables rapid, high-quality content creation and marketing strategies that are difficult to replicate with low-code tools

### Team Structure and Expertise

  - Organized similar to a law firm with managing directors, managing editors, and supporting roles
  - High-profile experts from companies like TechCrunch, Shopify, and G2 lead strategy and oversee quality
  - Includes AI engineers and design engineers for end-to-end implementation

### Client Engagement and Capacity

  - Currently doing 2-3 client kickoffs per week, focusing on quality over quantity
  - Replaces multiple agencies for some clients, augments in-house teams for others
  - Provides a more cost-effective solution compared to niche players in specific industries

### Future Plans and Funding

  - Preparing for a Series B round in August/September 2025
  - Focusing on connecting the expert hub, workflow engine, and learning engine
  - Aiming to open up the platform to more customers while maintaining quality and avoiding false signals
