## Overview

- Finder is considering GrowthX and Aerox as top contenders for AI content automation, with speed to market being a key factor
- GrowthX differentiates through a comprehensive sprint process, custom platform, and high-touch strategic approach vs. Aerox's more self-service model
- GrowthX pricing (\~$0.20/word for editorial, \~$100 for programmatic pages) is positioned as cost-effective vs. traditional freelancers ($1+/word)
- Finder weighing build vs. buy decision; GrowthX emphasizing their specialized expertise and ability to move faster than in-house development

## Key Topics

### GrowthX Platform Overview

  - Recently transitioned off Aerox to custom-built platform
  - Platform enables modular workflows, fact-checking, style adaptation
  - Includes features like in-line content editing and image generation
  - Aims to give customers more transparency/access in the future

### GrowthX Sprint Process

  - 2-week calibration phase to deeply understand client's audience, voice, content needs
  - Deliverables include persona development, writing guidelines, content examples
  - Sets roadmap with 2 high-conviction content lanes + 1 experimental lane
  - Involves senior team members (VP Growth, Managing Editor, SEO Director)

### Pricing and Positioning

  - Editorial content: \~$0.20/word
  - Programmatic pages: \~$100/page
  - Positioned as more cost-effective than traditional freelancers ($1+/word)
  - Additional value through strategic guidance and specialized team

### Finder's Decision Factors

  - Speed to market is critical due to closing organic traffic window
  - Weighing GrowthX's agency model vs. Aerox's platform approach
  - Considering internal build option; need to justify external solution
  - Some internal hesitation around full AI adoption

### Potential Content Opportunities

  - Local content (e.g., mortgage rates by city/state)
  - Milestone-based content (e.g., first home, new baby)
  - Persona-specific content (e.g., students)
