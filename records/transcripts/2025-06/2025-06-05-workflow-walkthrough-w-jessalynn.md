## Key Topics

### Workflow Overview and Structure

  - Keyword should be directional (e.g. "modular vs monolithic AI native architecture")
  - Key fields: company context, user context, CTA, writing instructions
  - Check input mappings to ensure proper data flow between workflow steps
  - Editing workflow structure: can add/remove columns, drag to reorder

### Assignment Brief and Research

  - Spend 10-15 minutes editing the brief for accuracy and relevance
  - Research gen pulls from edited brief - only edit research if major issues
  - Title gen is a standalone tool, currently a workflow quirk

### Content Outline and Draft

  - Edit outline to ensure solid structure (20% of effort)
  - Initial draft often lacks formatting variety
  - Apply writing instructions/polishing guidelines to improve structure

### Feedback and Final Edits

  - Use feedback feature for specific edit instructions
  - Can shorten by deleting entire sections rather than line editing
  - Internal linking and final draft viewing added as workflow steps

### Client-Specific Workflows

  - Workflows vary between clients (e.g. Good Call's image generation)
  - Writing instructions critical - 20+ detailed guidelines recommended
