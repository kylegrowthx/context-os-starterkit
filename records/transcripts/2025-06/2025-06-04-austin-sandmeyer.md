### GrowthX's Process

1.  Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):

      - Create content strategy, artifacts, and content OS

      - Ongoing content production and optimization
      - Weekly meetings and progress reports

### GrowthX's Differentiators

  - Eliminates need for large in-house content team
  - Provides high-quality content at \~10-20 cents per word
  - Combines AI efficiency with expert human oversight
  - Flexible approach tailored to each client's needs and timeline
