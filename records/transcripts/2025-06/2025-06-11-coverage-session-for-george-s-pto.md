## Context

GrowthX is rolling out a new strategy sprint model to handle increased client demand and pipeline growth. Strategy sprints are 8-week engagements where GrowthX develops a full content strategy and 3-month content plan for clients, with clients signing long-term contracts but retaining the option to exit after the sprint. The model replaces the previous "pilot" approach that left clients expecting perfect results by week two. Two sprint types are running: custom sprints (like Augment Code, starting with loose briefs and Marcel doing much of the technical work) and editorial sprints (like Gitpod, Engine, Town—more defined scope around content lanes and editorial strategy). George had been running this end-to-end, but the team is now expanding with new hires Jo and Sadira to share the load. This coverage session established what each team member owns during George's absence and clarified the workflows they'll use to avoid dropping tasks while George is out for 10 days.
