## Key Topics

### Token Security's Current Situation and Needs

  - Series A startup with 35 employees, raised $20M in January; NHI is trending topic in cybersecurity (consistently top 10 Gartner list but increasingly crowded market)
  - Current marketing engine heavily dependent on founder network and outbound SDR-driven sales; limited inbound channels
  - Ad spend: Some LinkedIn ads running, minimal Google Ads, no significant paid search strategy yet
  - Website and content: Attractive design but minimal content; significantly behind in SEO rankings and domain authority relative to competitors
  - Russell Miller context: Recently joined as VP Marketing (1+ month in role); building marketing team from scratch with Head of Product Marketing and Head of Demand Gen coming in coming weeks; seeking 1-2 low-risk pilots before larger commitments
  - Marketing priorities: Build out website content infrastructure (every capability and use case needs dedicated pages), determine right balance between SEO, GEO, and generative AI visibility, establish domain authority before competitors consolidate market position

### GrowthX's Approach and Offerings

  - Combines AI-powered workflows with human expertise
  - Team structure:
      - Managing directors oversee engagements
      - Chief Content Officer (former TechCrunch editor-in-chief)
      - Managing editors and content managers embedded in client teams
  - Process:
    1.  Strategy sprint: Calibrate strategy, voice, tone, and quality definition
    2.  Build custom content OS for client
    3.  Create content clusters and topic opportunities
    4.  Produce, optimize, and publish content
    5.  Continuous learning and iteration
  - AI-powered workflow engine automates research and content creation processes
  - Human editors review, edit, and ensure quality

### Content Strategy and Execution

  - Core philosophy: Quality over volume, especially for low-domain-authority startups; counterpoint to competitors like Daydream offering 1,000-page programmatic scalability
  - For Token Security specifically: Start editorial (not programmatic), 1-3 topic lanes, single persona initially; resist urge to publish 300 pages by day 10 as this triggers negative SEO signals
  - GrowthX process workflow: AI-powered research agents fetch context and citations → editing and fact-checking → publishing and CMS optimization → ongoing refreshes and signal monitoring
  - Approach emphasizes incremental daily improvements (1% gains stacking) over massive one-time content dumps
  - Example: VACI programmatic pages—took 3-4 weeks to design and calibrate each page template before publishing; first batch of 100 generated 6,000+ impressions and 98 clicks (6% CTR) in under a week, validating approach before scaling
  - Parallel optimization: While publishing new editorial content, simultaneously refresh and optimize 4+ year old content to accelerate domain authority signals
  - Reporting cadence: Weekly updates via Slack integration, embedded team members (director, managing editor, technical editors) reviewing drafts and prioritizing topics based on business direction

### Pricing and Engagement Model

  - Strategy sprint: $20,000 for 8 weeks (foundational phase, low-risk for client evaluation)
      - Includes: Keyword and opportunity analysis (1,000+ topics identified like Abnormal Security), competitor research, calibration workshops to establish voice/tone/quality definition, content OS build-out, style guide and editorial standards, initial content creation and publication testing, performance monitoring and reporting
      - Designed as bi-directional evaluation: GrowthX assesses fit and execution capability; Token Security assesses whether approach works for their business
      - Baseline deliverable: Full strategy framework, content calendar, team structure, and definition of success metrics
  - Growth execution: $12,000 to $15,000 per month (ongoing scaling phase)
      - Pure delivery: Daily publishing, continuous optimization, editorial management, team collaboration and reporting
      - Flexibility: Can adjust scope based on calibration insights from sprint phase
  - Booking and capacity: Currently booked 6-8 weeks out due to demand; new kickoffs targeting mid-to-late July 2025 window
  - Business model advantage: Low upfront commitment reduces risk for startups like Token Security; aligns incentives (GrowthX incentivized to deliver results to justify growth-phase commitment)
