### GrowthX's Approach and Offerings

  - Combines AI-powered workflows with human expertise
  - Team structure:
      - Managing directors oversee engagements
      - Chief Content Officer (former TechCrunch editor-in-chief)
      - Managing editors and content managers embedded in client teams
  - Process:
    1.  Strategy sprint: Calibrate strategy, voice, tone, and quality definition
    2.  Build custom content OS for client
    3.  Create content clusters and topic opportunities
    4.  Produce, optimize, and publish content
    5.  Continuous learning and iteration
  - AI-powered workflow engine automates research and content creation processes
  - Human editors review, edit, and ensure quality

### Content Strategy and Execution

  - Tailored approach based on client's current situation and goals
  - For new sites with low domain authority:
      - Start with editorial content, not programmatic
      - Focus on 1-3 topic lanes and a single persona initially
      - Gradually increase publishing frequency
      - Optimize existing content in parallel
  - Emphasis on daily publishing and refreshing to provide consistent signals to search engines
  - Regular reporting and collaboration with client team

### Pricing and Engagement Model

  - Strategy sprint: $20,000 for 8 weeks
      - Includes comprehensive strategy, keyword analysis, competitor research, and initial content creation
      - Low-risk opportunity to assess fit and set strategy
  - Growth execution: $12,000 to $15,000 per month
      - Ongoing content creation, optimization, and publishing
  - Currently booking 6-8 weeks out (mid to late July for new engagements)
