- The platform uses AI to stitch together data from various sources (CRM, marketing automation, etc.) and extract insights from emails, call transcripts, and more

- GrowthX may explore using Upside for its own attribution needs and potentially recommend it to clients once they complete their HubSpot migration

## Key Topics

### Upside's B2B Measurement Platform

  - Uses "forensic attribution" to analyze thousands of touchpoints across the B2B sales cycle
  - AI-powered agents extract insights from various data sources, including emails and call transcripts
  - Provides visualizations of account history, engagement, and key moments that influenced deals
  - Offers channel-level reporting and campaign effectiveness analysis

### Data Integration and Security

  - Integrates with Salesforce, HubSpot, Marketo, Gong, and other data sources
  - Uses graph data model to stitch together information from multiple systems
  - SOC 2 compliant with extensive security measures to protect client data

### Potential Applications for GrowthX

  - Could help attribute results from content marketing and SEO efforts
  - Might be useful for showing ROI to clients, especially for hard-to-track channels like LinkedIn
  - Agency access model could allow GrowthX to pull reports for clients

### Pricing and Implementation

  - Starts at $36K/year for companies with $10M+ revenue
  - Pricing scales based on data processing needs, which can be significant for large enterprises
  - Implementation requires access to various data sources, which can be a challenge with some clients
