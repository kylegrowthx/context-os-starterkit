## Key Topics

### Current Workflow and Tool Integration

  - Replacing SEMrush and Airtable functionalities within Atlas
  - Concerns about data capacity and replicating custom Airtable functionalities
  - MVP approach: Focus on core experience, assume complex setup handled by experts

### Content Inventory Process

  - Two-sided approach: sitemap analysis for evergreen content + competitive research
  - Manual selection of relevant subdirectories for monitoring (MVP stage)
  - Key metrics: page performance, ranking keywords, LLM queries (future enrichment)
  - Tracking impact: 40 refreshes out of 1,000 URLs to measure work effectiveness

### Tagging and Categorization

  - Custom AI columns for tagging based on categories (e.g., content themes, audience groups)
  - Example taxonomies: topic clusters, group types, personas, industries
  - Human-configured tag structure, AI-assisted classification for large-scale tagging (5,000+ items)
  - Flexibility to add/modify tags as needed

### Competitive Analysis and Keyword Research

  - Keyword gap analysis between competitor domains and client domain
  - Example: Interwell therapy platform - 1,000 potential assignments, 5M searches/month
  - URL-based keyword clustering for efficient assignment creation
  - Leveraging competitor content to identify keyword opportunities (e.g., 49 keywords for "biography therapy")

### Opportunity Scoring and Prioritization

  - Factors: total volume, keyword difficulty, relevance, conversion value, persona fit
  - Calculated score (0-100) based on relevance, volume, and difficulty
  - Current assignment creation already includes some scoring elements
  - Goal: Combine scoring with opportunity volume and ranking difficulty

### Data Set for Development

  - Engine recommended as the best client example for Atlas development
  - Includes manually curated assignments, tagged clusters, and numerous existing pages
