## Context

This is Webflow's weekly sync with GrowthX, their content marketing partner working on a major developer-focused content initiative. Webflow is investing heavily in developer audience growth—recognizing that developers are a critical constituency for adoption and expansion, yet remain underserved by current content strategy. The relationship spans multiple workstreams: integration content pages, developer blog strategy, and messaging alignment around new products like Webflow Cloud. Rachel Wolan, Head of Developer Content at Webflow, joined this call to push for a more opinionated, separate brand identity for developer content, arguing that the current approach conflates developers with marketers and sales buyers—losing both audiences in the process. GrowthX is producing high-quality integration pages (using Webflow docs and integration tool docs as source material, with AI-assisted generation and human review), and is actively mapping developer content topics and user journeys.
