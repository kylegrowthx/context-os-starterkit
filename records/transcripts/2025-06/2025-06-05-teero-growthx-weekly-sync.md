## Context

Teero is a staffing platform focused exclusively on dental hygienist placements. This is a recurring weekly content sync between Teero's CEO Nate Anderson and GrowthX's content team (Aida, Jakub, and Jessalynn) to review content performance on the Teero blog, lead generation from GrowthX articles, and align on content strategy and keyword priorities. Teero launched this content partnership with GrowthX in March 2025 to drive organic traffic and dental office lead generation. The relationship is working — leads have grown from 1 in March to 4 in April to 3 in May — but performance is uneven across content types and requires ongoing refinement to match Teero's narrow ICP (hygienist staffing pain, not general dental practice management).
