## Context

Prophecy is a GrowthX services client engaged in a high-touch content marketing engagement worth $200k+/year. The company is a data integration and transformation platform—technical in nature but deliberately positioning itself as process-focused rather than tool-focused (core message: "It's not the tooling that's broken, it's the process"). This positioning shapes everything GrowthX produces for them. The account was initially rocky: when Vivek joined two weeks after the original kickoff, the client was dissatisfied with the initial strategy because GrowthX hadn't properly aligned SEO outputs with Prophecy's product marketing narrative. That friction resolved once Vivek and Ismail implemented a feedback-driven process where Prophecy's detailed editorial guidance shaped topic selection and messaging. The relationship is now strong—Cody (recently hired as a point of contact) is supportive and transparent, positioning himself as an internal champion for the partnership.
