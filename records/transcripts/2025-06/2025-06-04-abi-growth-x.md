## Key Topics

### Current LLM Rank Tracking Approaches

  - GrowthX partnering with Scrunch:
      - Identifies prompts and competitors
      - Tracks rankings, mentions, positioning, sentiment
      - Analyzes competitor presence, content evolution needs
      - Limited in category-level aggregation (team discussing improvements)
  - Diligent exploring free tool (unnamed):
      - Provides positioning and category-level aggregation
      - Shows discrepancies (e.g., Diligent not top-ranked despite market leadership)

### Challenges in LLM Rank Tracking

  - Numerous SEO tools emerging (esp. YC-backed)
  - Inconsistencies between API results and direct model queries
  - Early-stage tools with inherent flaws
  - Difficulty in accurately representing category-level rankings

### Diligent's Search Performance Trends

  - Organic click-throughs dropping significantly
  - Impressions increasing (mostly zero searches)
  - Strong presence due to longevity and brand strength
  - Competing with own nonprofit product (BoardEffect)

### Tool Landscape

  - Scrunch: One of the better options, similar to others
  - Positional: Mentioned as interesting (2-year-old company)
  - Many YC-backed SEO companies with unclear differentiation
