## Key Topics

### Current Content Strategy Workflow

  - Research process:
      - Uses AI-powered prompts for company research, customer quotes, and writer style analysis
      - Manual curation of AI outputs to create presentable client artifacts
      - Creation of voice and tone guidelines with examples (e.g., "Blue-collar directness")
  - Content inventory and keyword analysis:
      - Ingests client URLs and filters keywords to focus on evergreen content
      - Uses SEMrush data, removing brand mentions and homepage to isolate relevant pages
  - Assignment generation:
      - Editors manually review and approve content opportunities
      - Import approved assignments into Airtable for tracking (statuses: backlog, considering, approved to start, etc.)
      - Metadata tagging (topic clusters, group types, personas, industries) for strategy communication

### Client Feedback and Challenges

  - Rapid (client) mentioned frequent business changes affecting content relevance
  - Need for regular updates to company context, CTAs, and writing guidelines
  - Importance of calibrating tone and style to match client's brand voice
  - Client appreciation for comprehensive research and content strategy presentation

### Potential Improvements

  - Integration of Notion-based artifacts directly into GrowthX.ai's system
  - Automation of manual processes (e.g., editor review of content opportunities)
  - Enhanced visualization of assignment data (relevant score, volume discrepancies)
  - Streamlined calibration process for tone and style preferences

### Tool Integration

  - Current use of multiple tools: Notion for strategy docs, Airtable for assignments, Atlas for client presentation
  - Opportunity to create a unified system for a more seamless workflow and client experience
