## Summary

GrowthX and Yourco aligned on a strategy shift to increase conversions by combining content refresh initiatives with utility-focused resources tailored to their ICP. Key focus areas include moving CTAs higher in existing top-performing content, creating downloadable templates and checklists (especially around employee surveys), and leveraging RB2B visitor data to create industry-specific content for construction and transportation sectors. The team agreed to start with low-lift wins—refreshing existing content and converting blog guides into downloadable PDFs—while exploring longer-term plays like building a resource destination hub and using LLM monitoring tools to track query performance across AI-powered search engines.

---

## Context

Yourco is a SaaS product company that sells frontline employee communication and engagement solutions. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's ongoing B2B content marketing engagement, where Yourco aims to drive organic traffic and lead generation through blogging and downloadable resources. The relationship appears to be several months in, with Yourco running content through GrowthX's Atlas platform (a proprietary ICP-targeting and content generation tool that uses learned embeddings of customer data to generate tailored assets). Yourco's core challenge is converting high-impression blog traffic into qualified leads—they're seeing strong page views and ranking impressions (50+ search positions) but very low click-through rates and minimal conversions (their white paper approach yielded only 3 leads in 6 months). This meeting focused on reframing their content strategy to align with how modern searchers and LLM-based answer engines consume information.
