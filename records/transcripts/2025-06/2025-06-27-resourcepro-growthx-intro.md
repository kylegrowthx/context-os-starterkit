## Key Topics

### ReSourcePro's Compliance Business Context

  - Recently acquired compliance business, operating as a separate unit within ReSourcePro (11,000 person company)
  - Shorter sales cycles compared to other insurance tech offerings (similar to website security purchases)
  - Highly specialized, expert-driven field with constant regulatory changes
  - Currently maintaining separate website, gradually integrating into main ReSourcePro site
  - Opportunity to modernize marketing approach in a traditionally paper-based industry

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Content Platform

  - Utilizes a combination of AI workflows and human editors/strategists
  - 8-week strategy sprint to develop deep understanding of client's business and content needs
  - Creates AI-ready artifacts that power automated content creation processes
  - Flexible workflow adaptations for industries requiring legal/compliance reviews
  - Publishes over 10 million words monthly across various industries and company sizes

### Engagement Structure and Pricing

  - Initial 8-week strategy sprint: $20K flat fee
  - No obligation to continue after sprint phase
  - Typical ongoing engagements start at $12K/month for execution
  - Customizable based on content volume and specific client needs identified in sprint

### Content Distribution and Beyond

  - Initial focus on editorial content to establish foundation
  - Potential for programmatic, template-based content for scale (e.g., city-level or industry-specific pages)
  - Additional distribution channels like newsletters, lead magnets, and social posts can be explored after initial phase
