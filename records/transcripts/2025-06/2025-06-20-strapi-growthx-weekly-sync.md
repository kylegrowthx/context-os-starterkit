## Key Topics

### Content Optimization Strategy

  - Identified 986 URLs from Strapi's sitemap, categorized into:
      - Duplicate content: Old/similar URLs needing redirection
      - Refresh candidates: Content with declining traffic (\>20% drop) but good impressions
      - Monitor: Well-performing content to keep an eye on
      - Keep: New or consistently performing content
      - Low-performing: Consider redirecting or removing
  - Plan to merge duplicate content on important topics to improve overall performance
  - Adding canonical tags to handle blog series and similar content

### LLM Citation Strategy

  - Tracking prompts in various categories (e.g., "CMS for developers")
  - Identified 50-100 content pieces to prioritize based on prompt analysis
  - Focus on product comparisons and alternatives, which are impactful for LLM citations
  - Refreshing comparison section of the website, which currently doesn't get picked up

### Content Production Plans

  - New publishing cadence: 8-10 high-quality articles per week
  - Potential for higher volume of programmatic comparison content
  - Strapi team to add expert quotes and references during the review process

### Workflow Changes

  - GrowthX bringing in new team members:
      - Mara (Director) and Vivek (ME) to focus on day-to-day content production
      - Jason shifting focus to SEO strategy and workflow improvements

### Comparison and Integration Pages

  - Agreement on the importance of comparison pages for high-intent traffic
  - Current comparison pages need optimization (less table-heavy, more SEO-friendly content)
  - Short-term plan: Update comparison pages to follow blog post structure for easier SEO optimization
  - Integration pages: Pausing major efforts after recent updates, focusing on other priorities
