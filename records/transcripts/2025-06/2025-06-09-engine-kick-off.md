## Overview

- Engine.com primarily serves blue-collar, project-based industries (construction, manufacturing, transportation) with discounted hotel bookings and travel management.
- Current organic traffic is minimal; goal is to reach 100 high-quality first-time bookings (FTBs) from organic content monthly within 6 months.

- GrowthX to provide strategic guidance on content production, SEO optimization, and leveraging Engine's unique market position.

## Key Topics

### Engine.com Business Overview

  - Spun out of Travelers Haven, focusing on direct hotel relationships
  - Offers 20-30% discounts off public rates for business travelers
  - Primary customers: SMB and mid-market, blue-collar, project-based industries
  - Revenue model: Take-rate based on margin between hotel rates and resale price
  - Multiple products: Core travel platform, Groups booking, Partner hub for hotels, upcoming Charge Card

### Target Audience and Use Cases

  - Primary users: Operations managers, finance/accounting, travel admins in blue-collar industries
  - Key verticals: Construction, manufacturing, transportation, logistics, energy, utilities
  - Specific use cases: Disaster relief, corporate offsites, weddings (via Groups product)
  - 80% of customers don't have existing travel management solutions
  - 50% close rate from discovery call to first-time booking

### Content Strategy Challenges and Opportunities

  - Current content mostly upper-funnel, educational, with limited direct conversions
  - Recent focus on AI-generated overviews, but engagement metrics are unclear
  - Opportunity to shift towards more targeted, vertical-specific content
  - Potential to explore white-collar use cases (e.g., startup offsites) for diversification
  - Need for higher volume content production while maintaining quality

### SEO and Traffic Goals

  - Current organic traffic and conversions are minimal
  - Aim for 100 high-quality FTBs from organic content monthly within 6 months
  - Recent launch of 7,000 programmatic SEO pages, but indexing challenges exist
  - GrowthX recommends a more measured approach to page launches and indexing

### Competitive Landscape

  - Main alternatives: Traditional Travel Management Companies (TMCs), not direct competitors
  - Engine.com serves smaller businesses often overlooked by larger TMCs
  - Unique value proposition: No contracts, easier onboarding for SMBs
