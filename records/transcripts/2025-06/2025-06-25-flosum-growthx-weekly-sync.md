## Context

Flosum is a Salesforce-focused data backup and recovery platform. This is a recurring weekly sync where the Flosum product and engineering team meets with GrowthX's content and SEO team to align on content strategy execution. GrowthX won the engagement on a $200k+ annual contract to build Flosum's organic visibility through content marketing and AEO (AI-Generated-Output Optimization), with a focus on backup/archive topics that address Flosum's core product value. Matt Lyman, Flosum's VP of Product, leads strategy conversations on their end while Jakub Rudnik (GrowthX SEO lead) and Jessalynn Jones (GrowthX delivery lead) manage execution. A major inflection point this week: Flosum has a product release rolling out next month that achieves feature parity with competitive alternatives, which is reshaping how the content team positions the backup product in upcoming articles.
