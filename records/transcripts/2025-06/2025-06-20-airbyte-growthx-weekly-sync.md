## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Updates

  - Shifting focus from keyword-centric to question-based content
  - Aiming for concise paragraphs (2-3 lines) to improve readability
  - Including examples and use cases to validate points
  - Adding comparison charts for listicle-type articles
  - Using H1, H2, and H3 headers, with H3s formulated as questions
  - Numbering items in listicles for better AI citation

### SEO Tool Usage

  - Lowering Surfer SEO score threshold from 80 to 65
  - Recognizing Surfer as one of many metrics, not the sole determinant of content quality
  - Observed top-ranking articles with scores of 60-70, supporting the decision to lower the threshold

### Operational Changes

  - GrowthX adding two new Content Managers (CMs) to the team
  - David and Jakub freeing up bandwidth by moving clients off their teams
  - Tiandra transitioning off due to Spanish-speaking client needs, but helping train new CMs

### Content Production Goals

  - Aiming for 25-30 articles per week
  - No strict word count limits, focusing on comprehensive topic coverage
  - Typically ranging from 1,500 to 2,000 words per article

### Interlinking Strategy

  - Emphasizing importance of 3-4 relevant internal links per article
  - Exploring programmatic solutions for better interlinking suggestions
  - Avoiding links to glossary pages, focusing on main domain content

### AI-Friendly Formatting

  - Using question-based H3 headers to help LLMs understand content structure
  - Including examples and use cases to provide context for AI comprehension
  - Ensuring proper table formatting for AI readability (using ChatGPT for table creation)
