## Summary

## Context

The RealReal is a peer-to-peer resale marketplace specializing in authentication and curation of luxury pre-owned fashion. Kristen Cho, who previously led content strategy at an Etsy subsidiary in the musical instruments space, recently joined The RealReal and identified a gap: the company has a wealth of proprietary data (search trends, regional pricing, inventory velocity, authenticity expertise) but hasn't leveraged it to build differentiated editorial content. Their flagship fashion blog, RealStyle, lacks the editorial polish and data-driven insights their brand team is producing via their successful Substack newsletter, The Real Girl. Meanwhile, their product catalog of over 1 million SKUs represents a massive enrichment opportunity. GrowthX, founded by Marcel Santilli (former CMO at DeepRam), specializes in building AI workflows that automate content research, generation, and enrichment while maintaining human editorial voice. This intro call tested fit and explored how GrowthX could help The RealReal punch above its weight in the competitive fashion content space while scaling product description quality.
