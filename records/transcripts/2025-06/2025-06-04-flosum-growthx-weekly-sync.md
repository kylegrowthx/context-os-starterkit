### SEO and Technical Improvements

  - No-index page strategy (post-calibration phase):
      - 2,000 no-indexed pages identified; 1,500 returning 404 errors
      - Priority focus: 190 pages with organic search traffic in last 16 months — these need redirects to preserve link equity and prevent organic visibility loss
      - Broader follow-up: Identify no-indexed pages with backlinks (second phase) to ensure link equity is preserved across migration
      - Recommendation: Skip redirects for pages with zero SEO value (never linked to, no impressions/traffic)
      - Collaboration needed: Flosum to provide spreadsheet of where pages migrated or clarify why content was removed; Saakshi Jain familiar with redirect mapping
  - Webflow technical capabilities:
      - Matt can implement individual redirects and wildcard redirects (e.g., `/blogs/*` → `/blog/*`) for mass rewrites
      - No SEO impact expected from adding missing structure (e.g., resources page) if content already categorized there
  - Content refresh calibration: Jakub to schedule meeting with Jessalynn on content refresh recommendations based on SEO analysis
  - Future reporting: Dashboard tracking URLs created by GrowthX with traffic, impressions, and performance metrics (coming soon)

### AI and LLM Strategy

  - GrowthX developing beta platform for AI/LLM visibility optimization and tracking (different from traditional SEO, though with overlapping tactics)
  - Flosum selected as early test partner — no upsell, included in standard engagement
  - Core thesis: Transition from "web as destinations" to "web as databases for LLM consumption"; LLMs best understand LLM-generated content
  - Best practices for LLM-friendly content (alignment with traditional SEO):
      - Summarizing articles with clear questions (similar to featured snippet strategy)
      - Using bullet points for skimmability and readability — benefits both humans and LLMs
      - Clear question-answer pairing early in article (like featured snippet targeting)
      - Implementing schema markup in CMS to clarify content structure
  - Multi-channel "surround sound" visibility strategy:
      - Content on owned site is only one lever
      - Expand presence on Reddit, Quora, Spotify transcripts, review sites, and other platforms where LLMs source information
      - Different from traditional backlink strategy — drives LLM visibility rather than domain authority
  - Matt Lyman designated as Flosum point of contact; Girish Jashnani (founder) bullish on AI/LLM research
  - Competing opinions in market: some advise against LLM optimization; Darrell Etherington and Jakub exploring what actually matters vs. noise

### Cloud-First Strategies Content

  - Blog piece delivered; Matt Lyman initially questioned whether to rewrite entirely from Flosum perspective (Salesforce-native focus) vs. meeting existing search intent
  - Decision: Keep content aligned with search intent but weave in Flosum's Salesforce-native perspective throughout (bolded sections in H2, benefits, and vendor lock-in risk sections)
  - Saakshi Jain to review and provide feedback by next day; approach allows both SEO ranking and brand differentiation
