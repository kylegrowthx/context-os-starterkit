## Context

Dimmo is a software review platform that aggregates demos and comparisons across 500+ products. GrowthX is providing ongoing SEO strategy and content services to improve Dimmo's organic visibility and user acquisition. The meeting is a weekly sync to review performance data (Google Search Console impressions vs. click-through rates), identify opportunities, and align on content execution. A key strategic problem emerged: Dimmo has been focusing heavily on product comparison pages but seeing an impressions-to-clicks mismatch (a trend across the industry, partly driven by AI overviews claiming content). GrowthX is proposing a shift toward blog-based thought leadership combined with search optimization to educate visitors on software categories and guide them back to product reviews.

## Key Topics

### Current SEO Performance

  - Mismatch between impressions and clicks observed across clients
  - AI overviews potentially reducing click-through rates
  - Some pages (e.g., Titan X, FlashDocs AI) driving clicks despite low overall performance

### Short-term Optimization Strategy

  - Target top 10 pages with highest impressions
  - Finesse meta titles and descriptions
  - Monitor click improvements over next few weeks/month
  - Enhance interlinking, especially for well-performing pages (e.g., FlashDocs AI)
  - Suggest related tools to visitors, creating a portfolio approach

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Previous focus on product pages over blog content
  - GrowthX suggests reintroducing blog for thought leadership
  - Aim: Improve domain authority and educate on broader software categories
  - Troy's concern: Blog traffic not converting to product page visits
  - Proposed solution: Optimize blog-to-product page funneling with strategic CTAs and pop-ups

### Long-tail Keyword Opportunities

  - Target ultra-specific, question-based searches
  - Identified keywords with manageable difficulty and good search volume
  - [Potential for programmatic content creation (e.g., "best \[category\] software")](https://fathom.video/share/cQyEZFZ9sxGNJP2Fy-aszQevicrBg3sB?tab=summary&timestamp=806.0)

### Website UX Improvements

  - Better clustering and searchability for software categories
  - Potential for AI chatbot integration for personalized recommendations
  - Upcoming feature: AI-powered search for specific tool features within demo videos

### Content Workflow

  - GrowthX team to publish content without requiring Troy's approval
  - Will seek approval only when necessary
  - Troy to share email updates on new demo video creations
