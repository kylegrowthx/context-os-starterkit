## Overview

---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Business Model and Brand Vision

  - Delivers AI-first growth marketing services at scale
  - Building proprietary AI-enabled workflow software for marketing professionals
  - Seeks brand identity reflecting both human expertise and AI capabilities
  - Core values: bold, experimental, simple, refined, bursting with energy, premium

### Current Brand Challenges

  - Logo lacks refinement despite having appropriate weight
  - Typography inconsistent and contrasting
  - Desire for more uniformity in overall brand presentation
  - Current black/white photography contrasts with tech elements, but feels superficial

### Design Direction and Inspiration

  - Draw from 70s-90s transition to digital design era
  - Incorporate grid systems, systemic design, strong typography
  - Balance human touch with confident AI expertise
  - Reduce playfulness in primary elements; consider motion for energy
  - Simplify typography, potentially moving to one primary font family
  - Data visualization and infographics important for showcasing metrics

### Project Timeline and Phases

1.  Kickoff (current meeting)
2.  Moodboarding (\~1 week)
3.  Concepting (\~2 weeks)
4.  Brand Identity Development
5.  Brand Guidelines (\~2 weeks)
6.  Final Deliverables: Figma file, PDF, necessary assets
