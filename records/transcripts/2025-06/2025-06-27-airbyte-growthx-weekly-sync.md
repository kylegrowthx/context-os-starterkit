## Key Topics

### Product Updates and Workflow Implementation

  - Marcel demonstrated new product; full implementation of workflows upcoming
  - Atlas (replacing Air Ops) expected to streamline content processes and resolve recurring issues
  - Strategy sprint kickoff will provide direction for new content workflows and quality improvements

### Content Strategy and Optimization

  - Focus on creating bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) intent content
  - Expanding content to cover various use cases and workflow automation tools
  - Competitive content strategy:
      - Software lists (e.g., "best ETL software")
      - Alternatives pages for competitors and adjacent products
      - "Versus" pages for known competitors with search volume
      - ["What is \[Competitor\]" articles for direct competitors](https://fathom.video/share/5EdvvgnEJHxu7M6cZkbYi5Vh2VjgSHXT?tab=summary&timestamp=529.0)

### Content Structure and Visuals

  - Adding comparison tables at the top of listicles for better visibility and citations
  - Incorporating pros and cons for each tool in comparison articles
  - Suggestion to include more in-article visuals and infographics (using AI tools and brand guidelines)
  - Emphasis on creating custom images to enhance content quality

### SEO and Linking Practices

  - [Discussion on whether to link to competitor homepages in "What is \[Competitor\]" articles](https://fathom.video/share/5EdvvgnEJHxu7M6cZkbYi5Vh2VjgSHXT?tab=summary&timestamp=529.0)
  - Airbyte team prefers not to link directly to competitors to avoid passing "link juice"
  - Consideration of A/B testing linking practices on less critical articles

### Tools and Analytics

  - GrowthX developing optimization tools and building best practices into Atlas
  - Airbyte using Profound for SEO analysis; offer to share insights with GrowthX team
