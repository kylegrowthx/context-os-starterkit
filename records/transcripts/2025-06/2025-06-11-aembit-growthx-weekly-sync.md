### New Content Strategy: Breadth vs Depth

  - Introduce classification of articles as "breadth" (easier, higher funnel) or "depth" (more technical, challenging)
  - Aim for 80-90% quality on breadth articles, with lower expectations for depth articles initially
  - Focus on improving breadth articles while continuing to work on depth articles
  - David to propose article topics with breadth/depth classification for Aembit's approval

### Topic Selection and Research

  - Prioritize topics with more available public information (e.g., secrets management, identity federation, zero trust)
  - Utilize search volume as a proxy for available information, but balance with other factors
  - Avoid forcing Aembit-specific CTAs or product mentions in general educational content
  - David to extend keyword research, especially focusing on mentioned vendors and topics

### Content Review and Publication

  - Aembit team (Apurva and Ashur) to review articles 1 and 2 for publication by end of week
  - GrowthX to focus on getting text content right before addressing illustrations or diagrams
  - Aim to publish a dozen articles to start seeing impact on performance

### GrowthX Improvement Plans

  - Jakub to consult with directors and engineering team for workflow improvements
  - Upcoming internal platform migration expected to improve overall content quality
  - New fact-checker workflow to be tested with Aembit content as a priority
