## Summary

GrowthX introduced Mara Leighton (Managing Director) and Vivek Shankar (Managing Editor) as the new Rapyd account leads, transitioning from Aida Knezevic who moved to client onboarding. The team showcased Atlas, GrowthX's new content platform, which features modular artifacts (company context, audience personas, writing guidelines), improved fact-checking, and streamlined article generation—with 38% traffic growth in week 23 and 2% month-over-month search visibility gains including 6 new keywords in top 3 and AI overview placements. Rapyd's strategic priorities include AI optimization (GEO) for AI search results, expanding surface area to platforms like Medium and forums, ABM-focused content for specific use cases, and potential exploration of FX as a growing market area, with an immediate need for structured reporting to measure content impact on pipeline.

## Context

Rapyd is a payments platform helping businesses receive money globally. GrowthX has been managing Rapyd's B2B content marketing for several months, delivering long-form interview-based content through their original Aeroops platform and now transitioning to Atlas. The relationship is high-touch, with regular weekly syncs covering content performance, strategic planning, and process improvements. This meeting represents a leadership transition on the GrowthX side—Mara Leighton brings 6+ years at Business Insider plus B2B content marketing experience with venture-backed startups, while Vivek brings fintech and institutional finance expertise relevant to payments. The team had recently completed a feedback session with Marcel and leadership, and this sync was called to introduce the new team and demonstrate the capabilities of Atlas before ramping up content production with 15 articles queued for publication.
