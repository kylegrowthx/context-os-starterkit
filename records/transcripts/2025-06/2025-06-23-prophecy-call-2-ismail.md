## Key Topics

### Prophecy Article Structure and SEO

  - Articles follow a template: SEO sections, main content, benefits, CTA
  - SEO sections fulfill algorithm expectations with definitions, examples, etc.
  - Main content differentiates by using webinar insights and platform leader language
  - Titles are long and specific to create intrigue for enterprise readers
  - Meta titles are shorter but still punchy, generated using Claude

### Content Generation Process

  - Ismail uses Gemini AI for large context processing of webinar transcripts
  - Custom prompts help generate article outlines and content
  - Process takes \~1.5 hours per article for Ismail due to AI proficiency
  - Saskia will receive video walkthrough and prompts to learn the process

### Prophecy Narrative and Positioning

  - Focus on resolving data engineering bottlenecks for enterprises
  - Emphasize self-service capabilities for business teams
  - Avoid overtly pushing Prophecy until final CTA section
  - CTAs drive to relevant e-book downloads, not direct sign-ups

### Image Creation Workflow

  - Use Midjourney AI to generate initial images from article sections
  - Compile images by Thursday for designer to brand
  - Replace placeholder images with branded versions before staging
