## Summary

Thiago da Costa and Andi Bailey reviewed Datagrid.ai's engagement with GrowthX, revealing a significant contract-execution mismatch: only 2 of 4 planned work streams are delivering value, with programmatic SEO and founder-led social content performing well while outbound campaigns were paused due to noise generation. ## Context

Datagrid.ai engaged GrowthX in late November 2024 for a $200k+ engagement spanning four work streams: programmatic SEO, AI-powered link building, outbound campaigns, and strategic partnerships. Thiago da Costa, the founder, leads Datagrid's sales and strategy. Andi Bailey, GrowthX's lead on the account, conducts this review six months into the contract to assess performance before the November renewal window. Datagrid is growing rapidly after ramping the business in January (expanding revenue and customer base), but the timing creates a dilemma: the team wants to conduct jobs-to-be-done research to unlock deeper customer insights, but Thiago is hesitant to interview customers during the delicate early phase of their product adoption. GrowthX is currently Datagrid's only dedicated marketing resource—Thiago plans to hire more marketing capacity in the second half of 2025.
