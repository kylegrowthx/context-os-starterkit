## Key Topics

### Hypergrowth Partners' Business Model and Challenges

  - Hybrid consultancy, talent firm, and VC model paid in equity
  - Currently do \~30 deals/year with 12% close rate
  - 90% of deal flow comes from VCs - need to impress them with content/presence
  - Challenge: Growing demand while maintaining quality of advisors

### Target Customers and Positioning

  - Late Series A B2B SaaS companies ($2-3M ARR, growth team, tier 1 VC)
  - Companies with traction/product-market fit but execution risk
  - Position as increasing chances of better round/valuation
  - Examples: Ramp (joined between A-B rounds), Zapier (no CMO during critical time)

### Content Strategy Goals

  - Showcase success stories and relevance in AI era
  - Highlight scarcity of top B2B marketing talent
  - Demonstrate value through concrete examples (e.g. pivots, cost savings)
  - Create content VCs want founders to see (e.g. growth frameworks)

### Content Style and Voice

  - Direct, transparent, actionable
  - Share specific wins, experiments, failures rather than general lessons
  - Dense yet warming casual style with moderate shorthand
  - Aim for "effortless quick high-level skim" that's subtly packed with detail

### Workflow Development

  - Leverage existing call recordings, LinkedIn posts, keynotes
  - Use AI to transcribe, extract key insights, generate content opportunities
  - Refine to match Guillaume's voice and style
  - Expand to capture insights from full partnership
