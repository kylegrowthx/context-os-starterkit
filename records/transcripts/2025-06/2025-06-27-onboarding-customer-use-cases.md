## Overview

- GrowthX primarily starts engagements with editorial content creation, focusing on longer-form website content to build a foundation for organic growth
- Programmatic SEO offers scalable opportunities for clients, with examples like Ramp's per diem pages and Vapi's voice AI agent demos
- Recent shift to 6-8 week strategy sprints before 12-month engagements to better align expectations and uncover opportunities

---

## Key Topics

### Editorial Content Focus

  - Longer-form content (e.g., blogs) is the primary starting point for most client engagements
  - Allows for dialing in voice, tone, and structure while gaining deep context about client personas
  - Creates a foundation for future growth in traffic, engagement, and conversion opportunities

### Programmatic SEO

  - Template-based approach for creating multiple pages around variants (e.g., cities, industries)
  - Examples:
      - Ramp: Per diem allowance pages for different states, with unique data per location
      - Vapi: Interactive voice AI agent demos for 3000+ use cases
  - More complex upfront but easier to scale once template is established

### Client Engagement Structure

  - Evolved from rolling 3-month contracts to 90-day pilots
  - Now implementing 6-8 week strategy sprints before 12-month engagements
  - Goals: De-risk longer engagements, align on impactful strategies, and define clear outputs

### Ideal Client Profile

  - Clear product-market fit
  - Strong go-to-market hypotheses
  - Understanding of organic growth value (often from past experiences)
  - Realistic expectations on timeline for results (not expecting revenue impact in 2-3 months)

### Challenges and Opportunities

  - Identifying programmatic SEO opportunities can be manual; potential for AI-assisted discovery
  - Scaling programmatic content creation across different CMS and technical setups
  - Ensuring smooth handoff from strategy sprint to execution phase
  - Customizing workflows and artifacts for specific client needs, especially for programmatic plays

---

## Action Items

- Continue refining the strategy sprint to execution phase handoff process
- Explore AI-assisted methods for identifying programmatic SEO opportunities
- Develop clearer processes for customizing workflows and artifacts for specific client needs
- Consider how to involve client engineering support for more complex programmatic implementations
