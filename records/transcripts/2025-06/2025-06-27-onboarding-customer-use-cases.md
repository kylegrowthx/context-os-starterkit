## Key Topics

### Editorial Content Focus

- Longer-form content (blogs or similar) is the primary starting point for most client engagements
- Allows for dialing in voice, tone, and structure while gaining deep context about client personas
- Creates a foundation for future growth in traffic, engagement, and conversion opportunities
- Creates feedback loops that allow the program to grow for months

### Programmatic SEO

- Template-based approach for creating multiple pages around variants (cities, industries, job roles)
- Examples:
  - Ramp: Per-diem allowance pages for different states, with unique county-level data for FY25
  - Vapi: Interactive voice AI agent demos for 3000+ use cases
- More complex upfront to set up but easier to scale once template is established
- Requires decisions about CMS structure, whether new content types are needed, and who handles technical implementation

### Client Engagement Structure

- Evolved from rolling 3-month contracts to 90-day pilots to current 6-8 week strategy sprints
- Strategy sprints precede 12-month engagements with no obligation beyond the sprint
- Goals: De-risk longer engagements, align on impactful strategies, uncover opportunities, and define clear outputs
- Allows for adjustment of scope between strategy and execution phases based on discoveries during the sprint

### Ideal Client Profile

- Clear product-market fit
- Strong go-to-market hypotheses
- Understanding of organic growth value from past experiences (especially those burned by over-reliance on paid ads)
- Realistic expectations on timeline for results (not expecting revenue impact in 2-3 months)

### Identifying Opportunities

- Jobs-to-be-done framework is central to discovery
- Opportunity identification is currently manual (using SEMrush) but candidates for AI-assisted methods
- Potential to blend qual and quant research using AI with search volume data
- Strategic thinking and rapid execution are key differentiators vs. platform-only competitors

### Challenges and Opportunities

- Identifying programmatic SEO opportunities can be manual; potential for AI-assisted discovery
- Scaling programmatic content creation across different CMS and technical setups
- Ensuring smooth handoff from strategy sprint to execution phase while maintaining perceived quality
- Customizing workflows and artifacts for specific client needs, especially for programmatic plays
- Determining when to involve engineering support for complex technical implementations
