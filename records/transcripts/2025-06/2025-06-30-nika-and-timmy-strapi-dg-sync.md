## Key Topics

### Strapi Content Creation Process

  - Target audience: front-end and back-end developers only
  - Use regular article generation SOP from web to view outline
  - No need to touch code; Strapi provides internal code review
  - Article length: ~2,500 words
  - Add SEO tags and an image section (use LLM for image suggestions)
  - Use direct headings (e.g., "What is..." instead of "Definition:")
  - Aim for scannable content with short paragraphs
  - CTA and intro should be brief; total article ~800 words or less
  - CTAs vary based on funnel stage (top, middle, bottom)

### DataGrid Content Creation

  - No specific templates for different article categories
  - CTAs tailored to funnel stage
  - For PSEOs:
      - No introduction needed
      - Meta title up to H1 (optional for PSEOs, necessary for editorials)
      - Always use "Create a free DataGrid account" as CTA
  - Fill in metadata: slug, meta description, and meta title
  - Configure field URL: copy "/blog/" and paste the slug
  - Move completed PSEOs to "Approved for Publishing" status

### Internal Linking Issues

  - Timmy's setup works without manual input
  - Nika experiencing issues; will try Timmy's approach (no manual directions)
  - Further testing needed to resolve discrepancies
