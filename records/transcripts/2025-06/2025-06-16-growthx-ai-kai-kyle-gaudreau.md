Kyle Gaudreau (GrowthX AI) and Kailash Sudhakar (Hasura) discussed a potential partnership to accelerate PromQL.io's organic growth from 11 backlinks (Ahrefs) to 50+ within quarters, using GrowthX's two-phase model: a 6-8 week strategy sprint to define content roadmap and topical clusters, followed by 12 months of execution with AI-driven content creation at scale. ## Context

Hasura is an API platform company that owned the GraphQL market (Hasura.io held a top-2 position with 30K GitHub stars on their open-source GraphQL engine). Over the past year, they've completely pivoted to focus on PromQL (a prompt-based query language) and DDN (advanced API platform), repositioning Hasura.io itself as an entry point to these new products. Kailash Sudhakar leads organic growth and SEO for this pivot at Hasura. He's a former agency owner with hands-on SEO and content experience, now managing Hasura's shift from a legacy GraphQL brand with strong organic presence to a new, low-authority property (PromQL.io has only 11 backlinks vs. Hasura.io's 76) that needs rapid organic traction to support larger, faster deal cycles. Hasura is actively hiring product marketers and expanding their content function to support this effort. Kyle Gaudreau from GrowthX AI—a content marketing and SEO agency founded by ex-Homebase leaders—was brought in by Ellie (likely from Hasura's product marketing) to present GrowthX's services as a potential replacement for Hasura's current SEO vendor (a Bangalore-based agency they're planning to transition away from).
