## Key Topics

### Pod Realignment and Structure Changes

  - New managing directors, managers, and editors joined recently
  - Pods realigned to match skillsets and create room for growth
  - Preparation for strategy sprint account handoffs in coming weeks
  - Atlas implementation as part of programmatic platform transformation
  - Goal: Share knowledge, deepen skillsets, and deliver "best hire" mindset

### Atlas Implementation and Training

  - Atlas replacing AirOps as core workflow tool
  - Training session with Daniel scheduled for today
  - Workflows ported over; start testing client workflows immediately
  - Pod testing sessions to be scheduled later this week
  - Engineering team on standby for bug fixes and support
  - Expectation: Generate at least one client-ready piece of content per client by week's end
  - Usage tracking implemented; feedback and bug reporting encouraged

### Audit Week Expectations

  - Learn and leverage Atlas as the primary tool
  - Transition clients internally with handoff meetings
  - Build client roadmaps for strategic, proactive management
  - Complete assigned Linear project checklist
  - Use Linear for bug reporting and improvement requests

### Strategic Shift in Client Management

  - Move from reactive to proactive approach
  - Raise the bar on deliverables using AI and programmatic tools
  - Focus on clear content strategy, client excitement, and publishing processes
  - Monitor key metrics: impressions, clicks, CTR, bounce rates
  - Regularly seek client feedback and address concerns promptly

### Communication and Transparency

  - Bi-weekly delivery org meetings planned for knowledge sharing and Q\&As
  - Emphasis on responsiveness and proactive communication with clients
  - Encourage raising concerns early to prevent potential churn
  - Linear to be used as the source of truth for project management and issue tracking
