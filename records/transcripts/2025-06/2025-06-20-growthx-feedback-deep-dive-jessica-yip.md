## Context

Yourco is a startup client engaged with GrowthX for content marketing and SEO services. Jessica Yip (product lead) and Brodie Meyer (likely commercial or operations lead) joined this meeting to provide structured feedback on the partnership. This is Andi Bailey's first comprehensive account review since joining GrowthX as customer operations lead one month prior, tasked with auditing processes, KPI measurement, and milestone tracking across all 30 GrowthX accounts. The feedback meeting was triggered by Yourco's concerns that despite GrowthX increasing impressions by 50% since January, actual website traffic and lead generation remain flat—well below their target of 10-15 leads per month (up 30-40% from their previous agency's 8/month baseline).
