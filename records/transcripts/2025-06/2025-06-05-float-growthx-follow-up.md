## Key Topics

### GrowthX Team and Approach

  - Led by experienced editors and SEO experts (e.g. former TechCrunch editor-in-chief, Apple blog lead)
  - Uses modular AI workflows with human expert oversight at key stages
  - Calibration phase to set strategy, voice, and content standards
  - Flexible process adapts to client needs (e.g. fact-checking for regulated industries)

### Float's Current Content Situation

  - Small team (SEO strategist, blog manager, content agency for premium content)
  - Averaging 5 posts/week, some AI experimentation (AirOps, Athena HQ)
  - Q1 focused on foundational content, Q2 on mid-funnel educational content
  - Seeing traction with corporate card content, mileage calculator

### Canadian Market Considerations

  - Need for Canada-specific focus in topics and tone
  - Lower search volumes but potentially less keyword difficulty
  - Challenge: ensuring realistic traffic projections based on Canadian data

### Content Strategy Discussion

  - GrowthX proposes balance of SEO-driven and brand/thought leadership content
  - Emphasis on addressing user pain points and questions, not just keywords
  - Adapting to evolving search landscape (e.g. AI answer engines impacting click-through rates)

### GrowthX Case Study: Ramp

  - Ongoing client relationship since August 2022
  - Created scalable content like expense categories and per diem calculator
  - Seeing success in both traditional search and AI answer engines
