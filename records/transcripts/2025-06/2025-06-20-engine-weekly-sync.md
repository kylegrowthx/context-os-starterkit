## Summary

GrowthX and Engine aligned on a major content strategy pivot—shifting from broad top-of-funnel coverage to high-intent, industry-specific topics that drive conversions, starting with a construction vertical sprint. The team developed custom writing guidelines emphasizing blue-collar directness, anti-corporate skepticism, operational realism, and respectful confidence, grounded in research of top industry writers and informed by Engine's brand campaign tone (hyperbole and extreme examples). GrowthX identified 1,000+ pages for refresh and enrichment, plus multiple topic clusters for phased rollout; next steps include leveraging Gong call data for authentic customer insights, sharing conversion data and top-performing ad CTAs, and completing the first article for publication within a week.

## Context

Engine is a travel management platform enabling corporate travel coordination with flexibility (FlexPro premium feature allows same-day cancellation and travel credits). GrowthX is in week two of an eight-week engagement to accelerate Engine's organic visibility and content-driven conversions. The team is establishing working rhythms and calibrating how fast to move—Marcel emphasized the collaborative nature of the engagement and the importance of feedback loops to avoid moving too fast or too slow. Engine's website has experienced a recent organic traffic dip that requires investigation; James emphasized the need to balance high-volume topics with those that actually drive conversions from decision-makers in corporate travel management and procurement. The relationship is collaborative, with Engine team members (including James Winter, Kali Wootton, and Rory Conroy) actively shaping strategy and contributing data and context.
