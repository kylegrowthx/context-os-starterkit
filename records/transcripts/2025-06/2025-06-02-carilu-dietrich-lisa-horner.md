## Summary

Marcel Santilli introduced GrowthX's AI-powered content and SEO capabilities to Lisa Horner and Carilu Dietrich at Appfolio, demonstrating how workflow automation and AI agents can help scale content production and rank for non-branded search terms. GrowthX's approach uses coding agents to execute AI workflows—essentially automating the thought processes of top marketers—with examples ranging from landlord knowledge hubs with state-specific content to per-diem calculators and competitive intelligence briefs. The team explored how Appfolio could leverage these tools to expand non-branded web traffic, shift positioning around "agentic" capabilities, and potentially augment their internal AI and content teams, with plans for a strategy sprint to determine the right fit.

## Context

Carilu Dietrich (a CMO advisor and investor) introduced Marcel Santilli (CEO of GrowthX, a B2B content marketing and AI services company) to Lisa Horner (VP of Marketing at Appfolio, a property management software company used by property managers and landlords). ## Overview

- growthx.ai offers AI-powered content creation and SEO optimization, focusing on external-facing content to prove ROI
- Their approach involves creating interconnected prompts and outputs ("artifacts") that feed into each other, enabling scalable content generation
- Appfolio sees potential in using these tools to expand non-branded web traffic and educate on new generic terms in their market
- Security and data sharing concerns exist but are mitigated by growthx.ai's use of only publicly available data

---

## Key Topics

### growthx.ai's Capabilities and Approach

  - Uses AI to automate content creation, SEO, and market research processes
  - Creates workflows that mimic thought processes of top team members
  - Employs a "strategy sprint" approach (4-8 weeks) to tailor solutions to client needs
  - Utilizes a team of experienced managing directors (e.g., former TechCrunch, Shopify executives)
  - Focuses on external content to prove ROI, but can also develop internal artifacts (personas, messaging, style guides)

### Content Creation and SEO Strategies

  - Identifies gaps in existing content and opportunities for updates (e.g., outdated blog posts)
  - Creates comprehensive content hubs (e.g., landlord resources including state-specific information)
  - Develops fact-checking processes to ensure accuracy, especially for location-specific content
  - Repurposes existing content (e.g., turning podcasts into enriched articles)
  - Aims to increase non-branded organic traffic

### Competitive Analysis and Research

  - Conducts in-depth competitor analysis using AI-driven research tools
  - Compiles detailed reports from hundreds of sources, adhering to specific research rules (e.g., using only government sources when required)
  - Utilizes multiple APIs and citation quality checks to ensure reliable information

### Potential Applications for Appfolio

  - Expand content strategy to capture more non-branded traffic
  - Develop educational content around new generic terms specific to Appfolio's market
  - Create state and locality-specific content, addressing compliance issues
  - Augment existing AI and content teams' capabilities through collaboration with growthx.ai

### Security and Data Concerns

  - growthx.ai primarily uses publicly available data, addressing most security concerns
  - Potential for Appfolio to use parts of growthx.ai's system locally for handling private information
  - Non-disclosure agreements in place for any strategy-related information sharing
