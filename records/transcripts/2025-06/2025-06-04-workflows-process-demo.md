## Key Topics

### Current Content Generation Challenges

  - Poor assignment briefs (mostly resolved)
  - Research phase issues:
      - Over-linking (40+ links in 2000 words)
      - Dead links and inaccurate citations
      - Hallucinated case studies and statistics
  - Limited granularity in feedback and polishing instructions
  - Difficulty reverting mistakes or making specific edits

### Nemanja's Workaround Process

  - Uses Perplexity for research generation with specific structure
  - Employs Claude for outlining, drafting, and polishing
  - Implements Paul Graham's style guide for Exec's preferred tone
  - Process takes 30-90 minutes per blog, depending on complexity
  - Allows for more granular control and efficiency than Aerops

### GrowthX Platform Improvements

  - Rebuilding client grids according to best practices
  - Expanding polishing instructions to include:
      - Voice and tone guidance
      - Content structure specifications
      - Brand messaging and compliance
  - Adding user context and AI context fields for better targeting
  - Exploring cascading feedback capabilities
  - Developing content scoring and link validation features

### Systematic Thinking and Editorial Mindset

  - Need for content managers to think more systematically about workflows
  - Importance of proactive intervention and clear articulation of issues
  - Shift towards hiring more editorial-minded content managers
  - Encouraging sharing of learnings and best practices across pods
