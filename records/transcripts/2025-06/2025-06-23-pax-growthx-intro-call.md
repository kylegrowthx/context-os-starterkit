## Context

Pax AI is a B2B SaaS startup that solves a specialized regulatory problem for importers and exporters. The company processes Duty Drawback claims — a U.S. government program that refunds tariffs and duties when companies export domestically manufactured goods or destroy imported materials. Rather than competing on price with traditional duty brokers (who take 6-12 months and work manually), Pax AI built an AI-powered platform that automates the entire workflow, making claims faster and cheaper. Kyle Gaudreau (Head of Growth at GrowthX) and Bruno Santilli (account lead) spoke with Penny Chen (likely operations or business development at Pax AI) to explore a content and SEO strategy engagement. The timing is urgent — recent tariff policy changes have dramatically increased demand and media attention around duty drawback, creating a window for Pax AI to establish market visibility and authority before larger competitors move in.
