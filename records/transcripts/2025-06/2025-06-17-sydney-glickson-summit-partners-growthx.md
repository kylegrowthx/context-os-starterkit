  - Not demand-constrained; being selective with clients and focusing on platform development

### Future Plans and Series B

  - Planning Series B raise in late summer/fall 2023
  - Potential focus on acquisitions (e.g., e-commerce agencies) to expand capabilities and client base
  - Building out platform for broader release, seeing high demand from current demo audiences
  - Exploring opportunities in creating expert data and replacing various service industries

### Team and Expertise

  - High-caliber team including ex-Apple, Shopify, TripAdvisor, HashiCorp executives and engineers
  - Attracting talent that would be cost-prohibitive for most companies to hire directly
