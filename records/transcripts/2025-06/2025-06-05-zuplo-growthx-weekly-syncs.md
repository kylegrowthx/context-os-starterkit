## Summary

Zuplo and GrowthX realigned their content strategy following a recent traffic drop caused by Google algorithm updates and the Memorial Day weekend. The team agreed to a three-pronged approach splitting efforts equally: 30% moat building (defending top-performing content), 30% content refreshes (revitalizing existing pages), and 30% new transactional articles (targeting commercial queries for better conversion). Key initiatives include implementing hero images on blog posts to improve EAT signals, placing 100 backlinks gradually (10-15/week), and prioritizing AI Engine Optimization (AEO) given that 5 recent customers came from ChatGPT mentions.
