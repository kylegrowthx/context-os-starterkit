## Overview

## Key Topics

### New Workflow Overview

  - Renamed steps for clarity (e.g., "keyword-driven title" instead of just "keyword")
  - More detailed company context and writing instructions
  - Removal of redundant steps (e.g., title selection)
  - Focus on providing over-detailed information initially, allowing for trimming later

### Research and Content Generation

  - Research URLs can be used to limit scope and improve credibility of sources
  - Adding specific URLs (e.g., HBR articles) can significantly reduce hallucinations
  - The brief generates research, which is then used for all subsequent steps
  - No additional external information is gathered after the research step

### Editing and Feedback

  - Edits should be made like substantive editing in a Google Doc
  - Use four slashes (//// ) to separate distinct pieces of feedback
  - The system chunks feedback to apply it more effectively

### Audience and Style Considerations

  - Current audience personas may not align with client expectations; further clarification needed
  - Existing client content seems focused on specific use cases rather than general audience education
  - Suggestion to create fundamental articles for internal linking and SEO improvement

### AI-Generated Content Concerns

  - Discussion on the future of AI content in search engine optimization
  - Potential preference of LLMs for finding information generated by other LLMs
  - Unreliability of current AI content detectors
