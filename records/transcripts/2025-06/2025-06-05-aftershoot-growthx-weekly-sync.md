## Context

This is a recurring weekly sync between GrowthX (a B2B content marketing agency) and AfterShoot, a photography SaaS company. GrowthX is producing technical photography content for AfterShoot's blog while also providing SEO strategy and performance analysis. After several months of content output, the teams identified operational friction points—primarily keyword approval bottlenecks and reporting gaps—that are limiting the pace of publication. Manaswini and David are driving process improvements on the AfterShoot side to remove decision paralysis, while Jakub is enhancing the SEO reporting dashboard to shift the conversation from tactical updates to strategic performance discussions.
