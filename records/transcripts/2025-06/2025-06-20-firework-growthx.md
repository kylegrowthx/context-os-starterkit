## Context

Firework is a video commerce platform in the e-commerce infrastructure space. This is a quarterly content strategy and performance review meeting between GrowthX (content marketing and SEO services) and Firework's internal team. Megan Dickey from GrowthX presented a comprehensive analysis of Firework's blog performance over recent months, examining lead volume, quality, conversion patterns, and content strategy alignment with their enterprise-focused positioning.

Firework's team expressed concerns about being pigeonholed into the "live shopping platform" category and emphasized their enterprise positioning (competing against Syndigo and Bazaarvoice for larger brands like Samsung and FP&G). GrowthX's recommendation was to double down on high-converting content types, improve call-to-action effectiveness, and shift terminology to position Firework as a "video commerce solution" or "24/7 video showroom" rather than a live shopping platform. Technical discussions also covered UTM tracking implementation, Webflow publishing infrastructure issues, and the importance of using real employee author profiles for blog credibility.

## Key Topics

### Lead Quality Analysis

  - U.S. leads show higher conversion rates and value compared to international leads
  - Demo forms indicate higher intent than free trials or gated content
  - Shopify-related content generates many leads but doesn't align well with enterprise focus

### High-Converting Content Strategy

  - Focus on luxury brand content, executive-level pieces, and industry-specific articles
  - Create more content on video commerce, ROI calculators, and executive guides
  - Develop a content calendar considering seasonal trends and industry focus

### SEO and Keyword Strategy

  - Create comparison and alternatives content for better commercial intent
  - Focus on enterprise-level competitors like Syndigo and Bazaarvoice
  - Move away from the term "live shopping platform" to avoid limitations
  - Use terms like "video commerce solution," "AI-powered," or "24/7 video showroom"

### Blog Performance and Technical Issues

  - Impressions vs. clicks mismatch is an industry-wide trend, not a major concern
  - Focus on improving click-to-trial conversion rates
  - Address Webflow publishing issues to clear backlog of unpublished articles
  - Implement year-agnostic URLs for evergreen content with proper redirects

### Author Profiles and Blog Attribution

  - Replace AI-generated author profiles (Natalia, etc.) with real employee profiles for credibility and trust
  - Use existing employee profiles (Ritesh, Sam, etc.) for blog post attribution and rotation
  - Implement a rotation system for author attribution to maintain variety and avoid appearance of single-author blog
  - Landing page with all active employee author profiles is available via Cameron Brown for reference
