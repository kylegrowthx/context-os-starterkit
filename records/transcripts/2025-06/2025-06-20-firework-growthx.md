## Key Topics

  - U.S. leads show higher conversion rates and value compared to international leads
  - Demo forms indicate higher intent than free trials or gated content
  - Shopify-related content generates many leads but doesn't align well with enterprise focus

### High-Converting Content Strategy

  - Focus on luxury brand content, executive-level pieces, and industry-specific articles
  - Create more content on video commerce, ROI calculators, and executive guides
  - Develop a content calendar considering seasonal trends and industry focus

### SEO and Keyword Strategy

  - Create comparison and alternatives content for better commercial intent
  - Focus on enterprise-level competitors like Syndigo and Bazaarvoice
  - Move away from the term "live shopping platform" to avoid limitations
  - Use terms like "video commerce solution," "AI-powered," or "24/7 video showroom"

### Blog Performance and Technical Issues

  - Impressions vs. clicks mismatch is an industry-wide trend, not a major concern
  - Focus on improving click-to-trial conversion rates
  - Address Webflow publishing issues to clear backlog of unpublished articles
  - Implement year-agnostic URLs for evergreen content with proper redirects

### Author Profiles and Blog Attribution

  - Replace AI-generated author profiles with real employee profiles
  - Use existing employee profiles for blog post attribution
  - Implement a rotation system for author attribution to maintain variety
