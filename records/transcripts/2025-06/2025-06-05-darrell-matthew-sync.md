## Key Topics

### Content Grid Optimization

  - New unstructured article generation grid introduced with improved user interface and clarity
  - Key sections: keyword-driven title, company context, prospects, value props, writing instructions, and audience personas
  - Grid designed to overcome default ranking-focused behavior and shift towards more editorial, client-specific content
  - Matthew to test new grid alongside existing VAPI grid to compare results

### Content Generation Challenges for VAPI

  - VAPI operates at the intersection of multiple industries (LLM models, voice agents, text-to-speech, STT)
  - Need for content specifically applied to voice technology setting, not generic articles
  - Client wants content on 200+ models available on their platform
  - Balancing keyword value with narrow-casting to voice-intent audience

### Grid Usage Best Practices

  - Spend time upfront on client-specific information
  - Manipulate the brief for most influence on final output
  - Include reputable article URL in research for better quality links and sources
  - Prune outline to reduce word count from \~5000 to 2000-2500 words
  - Use full slashes between distinct pieces of feedback in "Edits to make" section

### Internal and External Linking

  - Internal linking attempts made by default if no specific instructions given
  - External links can be manually specified for high-authority sources
  - Client may specify internal links they want included
