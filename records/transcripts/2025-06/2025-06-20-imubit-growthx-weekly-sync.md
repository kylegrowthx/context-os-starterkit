## Key Topics

### AIO Guide Outline and Target Audience

  - Guide to target buyer personas (executives, site champions)
  - Content should be high-level, graphics-heavy with short reads
  - Focus on educating prospects about AI optimization journey, not Imubit-specific offerings
  - Include sections on: what's at stake, approach considerations, security/reliability concerns, governance, and site readiness

### Content Strategy and Distribution

  - Integrate guide with existing blog content strategy
  - Consider creating industry-specific versions (e.g., cement, oil & gas) in the future
  - Use blog posts to drive traffic to the guide through banners or CTAs
  - Explore emerging markets (e.g., alternative fuels) for potential new use cases

### Website Structure and Industry Focus

  - Current website structure includes 5 main industries
  - "Process Industries" serves as a catch-all for other verticals
  - Open to exploring opportunities in new industries (e.g., food industry) as emerging markets

### Guide Development Approach

  - Start with one general version for process industries
  - Consider creating different cover pages for industry-specific appearances
  - Utilize real stories and scenarios from Imubit's delivery team knowledge base
  - Balance between providing valuable information and maintaining Imubit's competitive edge
