## Key Topics

### Sendoso's Content Needs

  - No aligned content strategy for 2-3 years
  - Aim to become a "content powerhouse" in relevant industries and ICPs
  - Focus on being thought leaders in direct mail and gifting for B2B SaaS
  - Explore expanding direct mail solutions to new industries

### GrowthX's Capabilities

  - AI-powered workflows for research, content creation, and visual assets
  - Human editorial team led by experienced managing directors
  - Comprehensive strategy sprint to define content direction
  - Ability to refresh existing content and create new material
  - Examples: 5x traffic growth for Swoogle, custom dev projects for Bolt

### Content Creation Process and Strategy Sprint

  - Initial 8-week strategy sprint to research, audit competitors, define personas, and calibrate content direction
  - Strategy sprint includes pre-work before kickoff and dedicated SWAT team for the first week to establish the engagement plan
  - Flexible pacing: can move fast if client feedback loops are quick (Webflow, Ramp model) or take multiple cycles to achieve calibration (Abnormal Security approach)
  - Ongoing weekly meetings and collaborative content development once strategy is locked
  - Potential for rapid scaling: 18 articles published in less than 2 months for Abnormal Security; 200+ pages refreshed for larger clients
  - Capability to extend across content types: blog articles, webinar extracts, podcast repurposing, visual assets (infographics, illustrations, cover images)

### Value Proposition and Engagement Model

  - Full editorial team with major-brand credentials (led by former TechCrunch editor, managing directors from Apple, Shopify, TripAdvisor) at fraction of traditional agency cost: ~$0.10-0.20/word vs. $2/word for traditional content agencies
  - Eliminates need to build in-house content team (estimated 3-5 FTEs + freelancers + tools to achieve same quality)
  - Augments existing strategy with execution capabilities through AI-powered workflows guided by human editors
  - Low-risk engagement model: 8-week strategy sprint with mutual opt-out at the end; no long-term commitment required before both parties align
  - Booking constraint: GrowthX keeps to only 2 kickoffs per week and books ~4 weeks out to ensure team dedication and quality pre-work
