## Key Topics

### Current Market Position and Impact

  - Recent article about Surge AI had less impact than expected, partly due to paywall
  - Website traffic increased from 500 to 5,000 views for two days after article
  - Received about five emails from potential new customers looking to switch from competitors

### Brand Positioning Strategy

  - Lean into anti-hype narrative, emphasizing Surge AI's under-the-radar success
  - Showcase technical expertise and research partnerships to instill trust
  - Balance academic gravitas with broader appeal to capture both researchers and wider audience

### Website Redesign Approach

  - Focus on showcasing research papers and technical prowess
  - Aim for a design that conveys trust and high-caliber expertise
  - Potential to create a blog that becomes a must-read resource for AI researchers

### Announcement Event Plan

  - Create a "moment" with a pre-recorded video event, aiming for high attendance
  - Content to be 50/50 split between company success story and technical insights
  - Capture detailed content in a full-day recording session, edit down to shareable format

### Amplification Strategy

  - Leverage networks and influencers to spread the announcement
  - Use LinkedIn and Twitter for initial teasers
  - Target timing around July 4th, balancing urgency with audience availability

### Target Audience Considerations

  - Primary focus on research scientists and technical decision-makers
  - Secondary goal of attracting top engineering talent
  - Aim to create broader awareness that could influence high-level decision-makers
