## Key Topics

### Client Background and Challenges

  - Aftershoot: Photography-focused company with multiple stakeholders
  - Very hands-on, detail-oriented approach slowing down content production
  - Multiple POCs (4+) involved in content approval process
  - Concerns about intent and persona targeting
  - Difficulty showing benefits due to slow publishing rate

### Content Strategy and Production

  - Shifted from product-focused to broader content strategy
  - 5-6 blog posts published per month
  - 40,000 words produced monthly, but slow approval process
  - Weekly process: 10 outlines submitted, 3-5 articles written
  - Photos sourced from Pexels/Unsplash or professional photographers

### SEO and Performance
