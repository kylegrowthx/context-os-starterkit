## Context

Aftershoot is a software platform for photographers that builds tools for managing shoots and content. The company has recently restructured its marketing organization and is actively involved in content strategy decisions. The engagement started as a GrowthX content marketing contract with 12 months planned, but the client requested a reduction to 9 months, and they're planning to hire a head of content internally within 6-12 months. This dynamic creates urgency: the team needs to demonstrate content value quickly while managing internal politics among multiple competing stakeholders (head of growth, content managers at different seniority levels, and a new SEO hire). The lack of video in client meetings and the client's perfectionist approach to content approvals suggest either previous legal/reputational concerns or bandwidth constraints. Building trust and streamlining the approval process is critical to this account's success.
