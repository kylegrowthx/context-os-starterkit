## Key Topics

### Pricing Negotiation

GrowthX received an initial seoClarity quote of $107k/year that exceeded budget expectations. Dave Capone's target is approximately $60k/year to gain approval from Marcel (GrowthX CEO). Identified cost-reduction strategies include cutting keyword count from 1,000 to ~500 per domain, deferring AI tracking features, and focusing on core platform capabilities initially. Mitul emphasized this is the "easy part" — the team can be creative with features and pricing to meet the target.

### Client Growth and Scalability

GrowthX currently monitors approximately 30 domains and faces rapid growth: 5 new clients are closing this week with more in pipeline. Dave emphasized the need for flexibility to add domains at the same per-domain cost as the business scales. Most clients are startups beginning with small sites (10-20 pages, with one example at 1,000 pages) that will grow significantly through GrowthX's programmatic content delivery methodology.

### Feature Priorities and Platform Requirements

Essential capabilities: rankings, reporting, search insights, market research, and API integration. API access is non-negotiable because GrowthX lacks an internal data center and cost constraints prevent near-term investment in one. Crawling capabilities are low priority; quarterly site audits are sufficient. Content capabilities are less critical since GrowthX already uses Scrunch for AI-related features.

### Competitive Positioning

GrowthX currently uses Scrunch ($300/month with 350 custom prompts, 3 personas, 5 page audits, 2 user licenses) for AI-related tracking. Mitul proposed including seoClarity's RKI (Rank Keyword Intelligence) feature in the proposal, though Dave indicated cost would be a limiting factor — Scrunch's pricing is competitive and declining.

### Implementation and Timeline

Dave is ready to sign the contract as early as Monday if seoClarity can deliver a proposal at the $60k price point. Mitul and Terry committed to completing a revised proposal that same day (Friday or Monday) and emailing it to Dave immediately.
