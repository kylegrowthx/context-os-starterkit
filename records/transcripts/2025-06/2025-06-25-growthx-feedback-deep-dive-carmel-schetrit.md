### Content Strategy and Performance

  - Traffic has grown, but time on site has decreased
  - Lead generation hasn't improved significantly
  - Shifting focus to more bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) content like downloadable guides
  - Need for better alignment between content format and strategic goals (e.g., making guides more actionable and technical)

### Data and Analytics Gap

  - Lack of comprehensive data insights is a major pain point
  - Current weekly reports lack actionable insights on content performance
  - Desire for funnel analysis, user journey mapping, and top-performing content metrics
  - Previous attempts at custom Google Analytics views were helpful but not sustained

### Business Context and Challenges

  - Siit operates in the internal service desk space, focusing on IT and ITSM
  - Facing competition from established players in a crowded market
  - High closing rate for trials/demos, but struggling to drive quality traffic and conversions
  - Considering broadening messaging beyond IT-specific focus to expand market reach

### Channel Performance

  - Direct inbound: highest volume of leads
  - Outbound sales: largest deal sizes
  - Paid ads: underperforming, exploring new strategies (e.g., Reddit)

### Technical Limitations

  - Current Webflow setup lacks flexibility for creating custom, polished content assets
  - Desire for more sophisticated design capabilities to enhance content presentation
