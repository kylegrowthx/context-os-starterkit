## Summary

Carmel Schetrit from Siit shared detailed feedback on GrowthX's content delivery over their first six months of partnership (January-June 2025), highlighting strong execution and team responsiveness but critical gaps in data insights and content strategy adaptation. The core issue: Siit is seeing traffic growth but flat or declining engagement metrics and lead generation, requiring a shift from broad top-of-funnel content to bottom-of-funnel assets with better conversion mapping. ## Context

Siit is an early-stage internal service desk platform competing in a crowded IT Service Management (ITSM) space dominated by established players like Jira Service Management. Carmel Schetrit joined Siit and engaged GrowthX in January 2025 to build organic demand through content marketing, initially focusing on IT-specific messaging. Siit has a strong product-market fit signal: high closing rates on trials and demos. The challenge is top-of-funnel — they need more qualified traffic and better conversion funnels. Carmel was referred to GrowthX through Marcel Santilli's network (via connections from Vercel and other shared contacts). Over six months, GrowthX has delivered consistent output and built strong working relationships (Carmel mentioned affection for team members Jess and Becerra), but gaps in analytics and strategy adaptation are creating friction as Siit moves into its next growth phase.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX Onboarding and Initial Performance

  - Carmel started with GrowthX in January 2025
  - Initial goals: generate more quality traffic and leads (top-of-funnel demand capture)
  - Onboarding process was well-organized but time-consuming for content calibration
  - Early challenges: AI-generated content lacked factual accuracy and business understanding
  - Current output pace is satisfactory; team is responsive and productive

### Content Strategy and Performance

  - Traffic has grown, but time on site has decreased
  - Lead generation hasn't improved significantly
  - Shifting focus to more bottom-of-funnel (BOFU) content like downloadable guides
  - Need for better alignment between content format and strategic goals (e.g., making guides more actionable and technical)

### Data and Analytics Gap

  - Lack of comprehensive data insights is a major pain point
  - Current weekly reports lack actionable insights on content performance
  - Desire for funnel analysis, user journey mapping, and top-performing content metrics
  - Previous attempts at custom Google Analytics views were helpful but not sustained

### Business Context and Challenges

  - Siit operates in the internal service desk space, focusing on IT and ITSM
  - Facing competition from established players in a crowded market
  - High closing rate for trials/demos, but struggling to drive quality traffic and conversions
  - Considering broadening messaging beyond IT-specific focus to expand market reach

### Channel Performance

  - Direct inbound: highest volume of leads
  - Outbound sales: largest deal sizes
  - Paid ads: underperforming, exploring new strategies (e.g., Reddit)

### Technical Limitations

  - Current Webflow setup lacks flexibility for creating custom, polished content assets
  - Desire for more sophisticated design capabilities to enhance content presentation
