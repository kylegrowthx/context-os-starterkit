  - Personal journey from CMO to AI company founder
  - How AI fits into various marketing disciplines (product marketing, demand gen, brand, partner marketing, etc.)
  - Quantum leap opportunities for AI in marketing

### Key Principles for AI Adoption in Marketing

1.  First principles thinking
      - Distilling tasks down to their essence and foundational elements
      - Understanding the "messy middle" of work and breaking it down to atomic components
      - Rebuilding work from first principles using decomposed insights
2.  Delegation skills
      - Ability to provide context so someone with no background can perform as well as you
      - Showing them where to fetch inputs, how to plan, how to execute, how to evaluate
      - Critical bottleneck—most people have never had to delegate, limiting their ability to instruct AI effectively
3.  Strong writing abilities
      - Essential for clear communication and precise instruction to AI tools
      - Non-negotiable skill (90% of marketing hires claim "I'm not good at writing" but it's learnable)

### GrowthX AI-Powered Tools

  - Developing workflow generation powered by AI coding agents that automate task decomposition
  - Core innovation: creating shared "artifacts"—rich context documents that act as shared organizational knowledge
  - Artifacts are tool-agnostic and work with ChatGPT, Claude, or any LLM; examples include writing guidelines, processes, briefs
  - Example: Engine.com writing guidelines artifact that dramatically improves output quality when provided as context
  - Key insight: success comes from spending less time tool-shopping and more time on planning and artifact creation
  - Soon giving Rapyd team access to these tools to test with their own workflows
