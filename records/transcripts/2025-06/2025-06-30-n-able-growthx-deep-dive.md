## Key Topics

### N-able's Current Challenges and Goals

  - Pivoting focus to cybersecurity solutions across endpoint, SOC, and backup
  - Targeting ambitious revenue growth through 2028
  - Need to improve top-of-funnel awareness and tell a comprehensive story
  - Struggling to acquire new MSP customers for AdLumen product
  - Desire to better promote COVE data protection product as an industry leader
  - Expanding target audience to include internal IT departments (IT Pros)

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Content Approach

  - Uses multiple AI tools and workflows to research, generate, and optimize content
  - Focuses on creating high-quality answers to specific user questions and pain points
  - Leverages tools like Scrunch to analyze AI search results and identify content opportunities
  - Aims for "compound effects" by repurposing content across multiple channels and use cases
  - Typical output ranges from 5-15 articles per week, with potential for programmatic content

### Content Strategy and Execution Process

  - 6-8 week initial sprint to define strategy, calibrate on voice/tone, and set content roadmap
  - Ongoing execution phase with daily publishing and continuous optimization
  - GrowthX team interfaces with technical experts, customers, and other stakeholders to deepen understanding
  - Focus on creating unique content experiences beyond typical solution pages and blog posts
  - Potential for programmatic content creation for location, industry, or role-specific content

### Measurement and Impact

  - N-able currently struggles with attribution and measuring content impact
  - GrowthX can provide strategic guidance on improving measurement, but not hands-on implementation
  - Focus on driving qualified leads and revenue, not just traffic
  - Challenges in multi-touch attribution becoming more complex with AI-driven search

### Operational Considerations

  - N-able team concerned about internal prioritization and resource allocation
  - GrowthX aims to minimize burden on client team while maintaining necessary collaboration
  - Typical engagement starts at $12k/month for editorial content, can increase to $15-20k with programmatic content
  - GrowthX can handle end-to-end content creation and publishing, including WordPress integration if needed
