## Key Topics

### Current Content Challenges

  - Prioritized Substack over internal blog due to bandwidth constraints
  - Third-party content on subdomain lacks brand voice and quality
  - Manual, time-consuming processes for research and editorial calendar development
  - Outdated content still appearing prominently on the blog

### AI-Assisted Content Creation Approach

  - Create detailed audience personas and brand voice guidelines
  - Utilize AI-powered research to gather trends and insights from various sources (APIs, forums, Substack)
  - Develop workflows to improve existing content and generate new ideas
  - Implement fact-checking and "brand police" systems to ensure quality and consistency

### Potential Benefits and Concerns

  - Accelerate content creation process while maintaining quality
  - Free up time for creative thinking and big-picture strategy
  - Address internal skepticism about AI's role in content creation
  - Demonstrate ROI and impact on visibility/SEO to justify the investment

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - 8-week strategy sprint to calibrate AI tools to The RealReal's specific needs
  - Involve expert human oversight in the development and refinement process
  - Gradual improvement of AI-generated content quality (aiming for 80%+ accuracy)
  - Potential to analyze and improve existing content across the site
