## Key Topics

### Homebase Business Update

  - ICP: Small businesses (20 employees or less) with hourly teams
  - Product suite: Hiring software (with new AI tool), onboarding, scheduling, time tracking, and payroll
  - Current priorities:
      - Increasing payroll adoption among existing customers
      - Attracting payroll-first buyers
      - Developing resources for business starters (e.g., restaurant name generators, Starter Center)

### GrowthX Partnership Feedback

  - Strengths:
      - Strong project management and account handling (Jess)
      - Reliable task completion (Becerra)
  - Areas for improvement:
      - Lack of visibility into AI workflow processes
      - Difficulty in collaborative problem-solving due to limited insight
      - Content team seems disconnected from AI flows producing work

### Content Strategy and Bottlenecks

  - Challenge: Multiple feedback rounds on deliverables (e.g., TLDRs for blogs)
  - Root cause: Potential misalignment in prompts, understanding of ICP, keyword intent
  - Desired solution: More collaborative workflow tweaking, reducing communication layers and wait times

### Reporting and Campaign Management

  - Current state: Basic performance updates during calls
  - Desired state:
      - More granular data analysis (e.g., page performance, keyword difficulty)
      - Proactive planning for content refreshes
      - Shift from production focus to campaign and impact focus

### Future Partnership Vision

  - Continue focus on Glossary Programmatic SEO
  - Develop capability to scrape insights from various platforms (Reddit, social media, podcasts)
  - Create a system for regular zeitgeist updates and language tracking

### GrowthX Platform Demo

  - New platform launch with improved visibility and workflow management
  - Custom AI editor with context-aware shortcuts
  - Advanced fact-checking and research capabilities
  - Improved cross-linking functionality using SEMrush data and content analysis
