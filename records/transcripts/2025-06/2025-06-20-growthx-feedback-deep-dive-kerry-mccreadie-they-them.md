## Summary

GrowthX had a strategic feedback session with Kerry McCreadie, Head of Organic at Homebase, to discuss the strength and future direction of their partnership. Kerry outlined Homebase's priority shift toward payroll adoption and resources for business starters, and gave critical feedback that while GrowthX's project management is solid, the partnership needs greater visibility into AI workflows, more collaborative tweaking of prompts, and proactive campaign-focused reporting rather than just production updates. Marcel demoed GrowthX's new platform, which includes AI-powered fact-checking, advanced cross-linking, and image generation workflows, and proposed a strategy recalibration session in July plus early access for Kerry to pilot the new platform.

---

## Context

Homebase is GrowthX's first customer and a strategic anchor account. They operate HR and payroll software for small businesses with hourly teams (20 employees or less), including hiring, onboarding, scheduling, time tracking, and payroll features. They recently launched a new AI hiring tool and are in H2 planning to build resources for business starters like restaurant name generators and a "Starter Center." Kerry McCreadie (they/them) leads their organic marketing and has been working with GrowthX for several months on content strategy and SEO, including the successful Glossary Programmatic SEO project. This was Andi Bailey's first customer meeting in her new role leading customer operations at GrowthX, and the main purpose was to collect detailed feedback on how the partnership is working and where GrowthX can improve delivery.

---

## Relevance

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- Kerry's feedback reveals a critical gap between GrowthX's project execution and customer insight into AI workflows. She needs visibility into prompts, ICP understanding, and model choices to collaborate effectively rather than go through multiple feedback rounds.
- The core bottleneck is not content quality but prompt alignment — TLDRs are failing because of mismatched ICP, keyword intent, and brand voice understanding, suggesting GrowthX needs content team input on the prompt side.
- Kerry wants monthly or quarterly workflow reviews to adapt to new models (Claude 4 vs Claude 3.5, etc.) and brand voice shifts, reducing async back-and-forth and wait times.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- Homebase signals strong account health and expansion opportunity: Kerry is enthusiastic about the new platform, has outlined ambitious H2 plans (Reddit scraping, Starter Center content hub, community launching), and wants to become an early pilot for new features.
- This is a reference-quality account — Kerry is openly strategic about GrowthX's roadmap and willing to co-pilot new capabilities in exchange for custom workflows (e.g., content refresh identifier tool she's built as a custom GPT).
- Renewal risk is low, but expansion opportunity is high: Reddit scraping, Starter Center content strategy, and potential CheckThat integration for fact-checking Homebase content are all greenfield.

**To CheckThat:**
- Kerry specifically called out CheckThat's fact-checking capabilities as something that "could get sold as a product" — she's interested in using it to verify Homebase's legal/payroll content claims.
- Homebase needs to scrape language and verify claims across domains (federal/state payroll law, for example), which is exactly CheckThat's use case.

      - Lack of visibility into AI workflow processes

  - Challenge: Multiple feedback rounds on deliverables (e.g., TLDRs for blogs)

  - Current state: Basic performance updates during calls
  - Desired state:

### Future Partnership Vision

  - Continue focus on Glossary Programmatic SEO

  - Create a system for regular zeitgeist updates and language tracking

### GrowthX Platform Demo
