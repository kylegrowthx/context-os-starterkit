## Summary

Vapi and GrowthX reviewed strong early SEO results from their content strategy: 4 blog posts ranking in the top 5 for targeted keywords, with another 6 in the top 40, plus 96 out of 100 custom agent pages generating impressions. The team discussed design refinements for agent pages (focusing on condensing the hero section and adding custom dropdowns), alignment on the custom agents listing page for Vapi's main website, and next steps for tracking conversions and accelerating publication toward 500-600 live pages over the next four weeks. GrowthX offered to help Vapi negotiate with conversion-tracking providers like Koala, while the team shifted focus from major redesigns to iterative improvements backed by performance data.

## Context

Vapi is an AI phone agent platform, and this weekly sync is part of an ongoing engagement with GrowthX on content strategy and SEO. The relationship started with GrowthX delivering SEO strategy, and this session reflects the transition into steady-state execution with measurable results. The team is now focused on scaling content output (targeting 500-600 custom agent pages live within four weeks) while maintaining quality and learning from early performance signals. Vapi is simultaneously managing a website redesign project and evaluating third-party agencies (WebStacks, Lightbox) for that work, which makes design alignment on the content pages particularly important to avoid future rework.
