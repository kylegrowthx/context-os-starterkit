## Summary

Marcel and Andi reviewed GrowthX's engagement with Teero, a dental staffing and services marketplace that has grown to $20-25M run rate in two years. Teero evolved from seeking AeroOps tool setup into a full-service content partnership, with Nate noting article quality has improved dramatically over six months (review time dropped from 2 hours to 5-20 minutes per batch). Key feedback: Nate wants earlier metric management aligned to revenue impact, prefers less frequent but longer strategy sessions with Loom videos and ad-hoc communication over weekly check-ins, and is open to post-publishing content review except for sensitive topics like hygienist pay. Marcel showed Nate an early preview of GrowthX's internal content creation platform with automated workflows and human review steps.

## Context

Teero is a venture-backed marketplace connecting dental practices with hygienists and staff, founded two years ago and now operating across multiple states. The company evolved from its original staffing model and is launching a second product (insurance verification for dental practices). Nate Anderson founded Teero and now runs day-to-day operations as a founder CEO with a lean team of 20 (no dedicated marketers). He originally reached out to GrowthX six months ago looking for help setting up AeroOps (an AI SEO tool), but the engagement evolved into a full-service content creation partnership. Review cycles have become much faster as the GrowthX team (led by Ada as account manager) built domain knowledge about the dental market and staffing business. Nate expressed strong appreciation for the work and noted Teero has become one of GrowthX's "zero to one" success stories in a well-defined vertical.
