## Context

This is an internal GrowthX delivery standup held during an active strategy sprint. George Haikal ran the meeting with direct reports Jo Kaminska, Marisol Smith, and Sadira Sittampalam to review progress across eight client accounts at varying stages of delivery. The broader context: GrowthX has shifted from trying to make rapid progress on everything to prioritizing "shipping"—completing and launching deliverables to get client feedback and signals, then moving to the next piece of work. This meeting reflected that discipline: decisions were made to reduce work-in-progress (WIP), double down on high-engagement accounts like Airbyte and Biologica, and align on what gets attention next.
