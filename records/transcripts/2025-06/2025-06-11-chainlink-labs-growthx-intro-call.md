## Summary

Kyle Gaudreau and Bruno Santilli from GrowthX pitched their end-to-end AI-powered content strategy and creation services to Chainlink Labs' growth team (Binh Tran-Tu and Kevin Ryu). Chainlink Labs is actively exploring generative engine optimization (GEO) and AI search visibility but currently manages this in-house with manual prompt generation, a data science team for auditing, and a content team for optimization. GrowthX presented their methodology (jobs-to-be-done framework, Scrunch for prompt tracking, proprietary platform with AI workflows) and pricing model (six-to-eight week strategy sprint for $20K), but neither party identified an immediate fit given Chainlink's large in-house teams and GrowthX's focus on comprehensive, end-to-end engagements. Both agreed to potentially reconnect if needs evolve, and Binh expressed interest in GrowthX's upcoming workshops and educational content.

---

## Context

Chainlink Labs (operating as Smart Contracts, Inc.) is a major blockchain infrastructure company serving diverse client segments from decentralized finance to enterprise institutions like major banks and central banks (ANZ, Central Bank of Brazil). Their growth team, led by Binh Tran-Tu and Kevin Ryu, runs demand generation and developer marketing programs with a particular recent focus on banking and capital markets (BCM). The company is at an early, defensive stage with generative engine optimization—trying to understand how to measure impact on LLMs, influence model outputs, and optimize workflows. Rather than hiring an external agency, they've built in-house expertise: a data science team audits findings, and a content team creates or optimizes content based on those signals.

GrowthX is a venture-backed (recent Series A) content and growth services company that pairs AI-powered workflows with expert talent. Kyle Gaudreau, VP of Customer Growth, came to GrowthX as the first customer after seeing workshops and has since moved into sales. Bruno Santilli, who recently joined from B2B industrial sales, represents the business development side. The company works with large customers like Ramp and Abnormal Security, combining agency services with a proprietary platform to deliver faster, more comprehensive content strategies.
