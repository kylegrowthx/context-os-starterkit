## Key Topics

### Current Affiliate Marketing Challenges

  - RAMP spends millions per month on affiliates with diminishing returns
  - Tier 1 affiliate sites prioritize larger competitors (e.g., Amex) over RAMP
  - Affiliate sites are being cited by LLMs at higher rates than other content types
  - RAMP's attempts to create localized content (e.g., accountants.ramp.com) have had mixed results

### GrowthX's Proposed Strategy

  - Create multiple niche websites targeting specific business types or localities
  - Focus on long-tail keywords and zero-volume searches
  - Utilize AI-driven content creation and workflow automation for rapid site development
  - Implement a multi-faceted approach: SEO, newsletters, lead magnets, and subscriber bases
  - Cross-link between sites to build authority and create a network effect

### Case Studies and Examples

  - CodeLibrary.gai: Launched in 5 days for AugmentCode, showing early indexing success
  - FindWeb.ai: Prototype for website template gallery with AI-generated content
  - Tiro.com: Achieved significant organic traffic growth in 6 months with 150 articles

### Potential Focus Areas for RAMP

  - SMB and mid-market companies (60% of RAMP's current revenue)
  - Persona-based or company type-based content
  - Locality-specific information for businesses in middle America
  - Finance-adjacent topics that appeal to decision-makers for corporate cards

### Implementation Considerations

  - Balancing immediate conversion goals with long-term authority building
  - Justifying investment to leadership by comparing to current affiliate spend
  - Potential for performance-based incentives in partnership with GrowthX
  - Need for careful planning to avoid obvious network manipulation
