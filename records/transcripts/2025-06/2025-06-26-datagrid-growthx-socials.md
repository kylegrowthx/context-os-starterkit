## Summary

GrowthX and Datagrid aligned on a fundamental shift in Thiago's social media content strategy: moving from AI-generated ideas to authentic, original thoughts grounded in his real experience and current thinking. The team identified that pre-written posts failed because they didn't reflect Thiago's voice or timely interests, making them difficult for him to endorse. Jason and Thiago agreed to replace the review-and-edit workflow with collaborative ideation sessions where they discuss concepts live, allowing ideas to emerge directly from Thiago's thinking before any writing happens. Key topics that emerged—AI replacing tasks not people, AI training bias, the "allowance pattern" for AI errors, and agent washing—will become the foundation for genuine, nuanced posts that align with Datagrid's positioning.

## Context

Datagrid is an AI-native platform that brings tools to AI agents, enabling cross-platform task execution without creating yet another silo of data. Thiago da Costa is Datagrid's founder and has achieved a previous exit. GrowthX is helping Datagrid build Thiago's thought leadership presence on social media as part of their broader content marketing engagement. The relationship is still in its early stages—Thiago has a growing but not yet established social presence, and the team is learning how to work together effectively. Mara Leighton and Jason Gong lead the writing and strategy work on GrowthX's side. This meeting happened because the initial content strategy—pre-written posts for Thiago to review—was failing. Posts weren't connecting with Thiago because they didn't sound like him and didn't align with what he was actively thinking about at any given moment. Rather than refine the existing workflow, the team decided to fundamentally change it to center on live conversation and collaborative thinking.

This philosophy—AI as executor not ideator—is foundational to CheckThat's positioning and will likely emerge as a key talking point in Thiago's posts about AI adoption, decision-making, and the gap between AI hype and reality.

- **To GrowthX Business Development:** Datagrid is solving a real enterprise pain point (AI adoption and cross-platform task execution) that Thiago articulated clearly: the demand for working AI solutions far exceeds supply, and most current solutions require manual human intervention. Thiago's growing social presence and thought leadership on these topics strengthen Datagrid's market position and represent significant reference and partnership value for GrowthX.

## Key Topics

### Content Creation Process

  - Current approach not capturing Thiago's voice or thought process
  - Need for live discussions to generate authentic, timely content ideas
  - Shift from reviewing pre-written posts to collaborative ideation sessions
  - Goal: Create content that Thiago would immediately want to publish

### AI Adoption Challenges

  - Enterprises rapidly adopting AI, but effective solutions still limited
  - Gap between AI potential and current software capabilities
  - New workforce entering with AI proficiency, while current workers struggle to adapt
  - Need for better AI tools that work with enterprise data, not just general knowledge

### AI Decision-Making and Trust

  - Exploring when and how to trust AI for critical decisions
  - Comparing AI mistakes to human errors in professional contexts
  - Considering the "allowance pattern" for AI errors vs. human errors
  - Balancing AI capabilities with human oversight in various industries

### AI Training and Limitations

  - AI predominantly trained on social media and publicly available content
  - Limitations in specialized knowledge domains (e.g., electrical engineering)
  - Challenges in training AI on proprietary or sensitive information
  - Trend towards using AI with specific knowledge bases rather than specialized models

### "Agent Washing" and AI-Native Solutions

  - Trend of adding superficial AI features to existing products
  - Parallel to early "cloud-washing" in software industry
  - Prediction that truly AI-native solutions will outpace retrofitted products
  - Datagrid's approach: integrating AI across tools rather than creating another silo
