## Key Topics

### Atlas System Demonstration

  - Clients (e.g., Rapid) impressed by comprehensive research and artifact generation
  - System creates detailed strategy sprint documents in Notion
  - Artifacts include company research, audience profiles, and writing guidelines
  - Potential to move from Notion to integrated system experience for clients

### Content Calibration Process

  - Manual process to determine client's preferred tone and style
  - Uses AI to generate author profiles, quotes, and style examples
  - Creates mood boards of words and sentences for client feedback
  - Aims for 80% direct communication style with customized examples

### Research and Assignment Generation

  - Ingests client's internal URLs and uses SEMrush for keyword data
  - Filters out brand mentions and irrelevant sections
  - Editors manually review and approve content opportunities
  - Assignments categorized by status (e.g., backlog, approved, in production)
  - Metadata includes topic clusters, group types, personas, and industries

### Workflow Optimization Opportunities

  - Potential for better integration between research and assignment generation
  - Opportunity to automate editor's manual review process
  - AI could prioritize top 10 recommendations for quicker human approval
  - Current human curation is cost-effective but could be enhanced with AI scoring

## Action Items

- Investigate integration of AI-driven scoring and prioritization for assignments
