## Key Topics

### Atlas Content Generation Platform

  - Recently launched internally, minimalist design but powerful and flexible
  - Demonstrates article generation workflow: topic input → assignment brief → research → outline → draft → fact-checking → internal linking
  - Customizable artifacts (audience personas, company context, writing guidelines) feed into content creation
  - Fact-checker flags unverified claims; can be customized (e.g., for tax-specific sources like IRS website)

### Town's AI-Driven Tax Preparation Process

  - AI analyzes client's financial documents and last year's tax return
  - Generates custom questionnaire for additional info needed
  - Automates adjustments typically done manually by humans
  - Uses tax calculator software to prepare federal and state forms
  - Human review and advisory still crucial, especially for complex cases
  - Goal: Reduce human work from 7-10 hours to 1 hour per return

### Client-CPA Interaction

  - Primarily email-based communication (70-80%)
  - Initial trust-building often requires phone/video calls
  - Frequency: 1 in 20 clients want quarterly calls; most prefer minimal interaction
  - Video summaries of tax returns reducing need for calls
  - Round-the-clock access to CPAs for urgent issues (e.g., IRS letters)

### Integrations and Client Base

  - Current: QuickBooks
  - Planned: Xero, FreshBooks, Gusto, Rippling, potentially ADP
  - Startup vs. SMB differences:
      - Startups often loss-making, lowering tax complexity
      - R\&D credits more relevant for startups
      - SMBs require more proactive planning due to profitability
