## Context

Zuplo is an API management platform serving developers. The company initiated a 6-month content engagement with GrowthX around 6 months ago, expecting to see measurable traction on search rankings and traffic by June 2025. However, Zuplo's investors and leadership have grown concerned about the ROI and the strategic direction: the landscape is shifting rapidly toward AI/LLM visibility, and Zuplo's data shows that 100% of their last 5 self-served customers came from ChatGPT, not traditional search. Adrian Machado is acting as the liaison between Zuplo's CEO and GrowthX, bringing informed technical perspective on both content strategy and developer marketing. This renewal discussion is happening at a critical inflection point where the company is reconsidering whether to continue with an agency partner, hire internal developer marketing expertise, or pursue a hybrid model that combines both.

## Key Topics

### Zuplo's Concerns with Current Strategy

  - Leadership questioning if GrowthX is the right tool/team for Zuplo's current growth stage
  - Investors pushing for focus on AI/LLM optimization over traditional SEO
  - 5/5 recent self-serve customers came from ChatGPT, highlighting the importance of AI visibility
  - Concerns about the impact and ROI of work done so far (6-month timeframe initially discussed)
  - Informational content not driving significant traffic relative to cost

### Shift in Content Strategy and Audience Engagement

  - Developer audience requires more "show, don't tell" content with code snippets and practical examples
  - GrowthX acknowledges need for different approach, including:
      - More tailored instructions for content creation
      - Post-processing step for reviewing code samples
      - Focus on synthesizing hard-to-find information from various sources

### Proposed Solutions and New Approaches

  - Create dedicated directories or microsites (e.g., CodeLibrary.ai for Augment Code) for more programmatic content
  - Shift focus from editorial to more aggressive, programmatic approaches (1,000+ pages vs. 100)
  - Leverage GrowthX's design and dev resources to build out new sections on Zuplo's site; set up directory structure in GitHub for faster deployment without learning client's custom CMS workflows
  - Potential directory plays: API directory, MCP directory, libraries and SDKs directory — each with 1,000+ pages of long-tail, rich-answer content
  - Explore partnerships with tools like Scrunch for better AEO metrics and insights (though noted these tools are "quite flawed" and early stage)
  - Implement hybrid approach: Zuplo builds internal developer advocacy (videos, reports, podcast) to generate insights and novelty; GrowthX synthesizes and amplifies programmatically

### Value of Agency Partnership in Evolving Landscape

  - GrowthX can stress-test strategies across multiple clients
  - Ability to bring insights and traction from other clients to benefit Zuplo
  - Flexibility to adjust approach and ensure Zuplo feels they're getting value
