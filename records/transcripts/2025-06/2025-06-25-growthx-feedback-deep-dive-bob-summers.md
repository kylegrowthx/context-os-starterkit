## Summary

Andi Bailey conducted a feedback call with Bob Summers, CEO of GoodCall.ai, to address serious concerns about GrowthX's delivery performance, account instability, and unresolved conflicts of interest with competing clients. Bob outlined six months of chaotic onboarding, frequent team changes, missed strategic direction, and the shock of discovering that GrowthX had signed Smith.ai and Vappy—direct competitors—without discussion. Despite his frustrations, Bob remains committed to the partnership and proposed a full contract reset aligned with GrowthX's new platform and methodology. Andi committed to developing a formal competitive client policy, ensuring Daryl presents the new strategic direction, and working with leadership to redefine the scope of work.

---

## Context

Bob Summers was Marcel's first paying SEO customer and an evangelist of Marcel's methodology. After successfully implementing Marcel's approach independently (growing GoodCall.ai's traffic from 1,000 to 100,000 monthly uniques), Bob signed on as a GrowthX client in January 2025 with high expectations and a unique contract structure: two months free service plus $120,000 in AI credits. However, the first six months have been characterized by chaotic onboarding with frequent team changes, subcontractors not following the GrowthX methodology, and the sudden departure of account lead Prateek with no communication. Bob also discovered that GrowthX had onboarded Smith.ai and Vappy—direct competitors in the voice AI space—without any discussion of conflicts of interest, triggering concerns about competitive strategy leakage. This is Bob's second conversation with GrowthX leadership (after a separate call with Marcel) and comes at a critical juncture as GrowthX launches a new platform designed to systematize content creation.
