## Key Topics

### Content Production and Publishing

  - 440 articles developed to date
  - 10 articles from previous week's backlog published
  - Plan to publish 20 articles (10 from current week + 10 from next week) before next meeting
  - New process: Prateek to share weekly update on GrowthX channel with links to published articles

### Lead Generation Analysis

  - US leads increased from 1-2 to 6 per month since engagement started
  - January 2025: 38 leads from US, 19 from India, 7 from UK, 4 from Canada
  - Concerning trend: Very few leads generated from blog posts published after September
  - Action items:
    1.  Identify highly relevant keywords for Firework's brand
    2.  Create topic-specific lead magnets (e.g., infographics, e-books) for each blog post

### SEO Performance Metrics

  - Impressions increased by 10% to 490k (up from 470k last week)
  - Clicks showed weak growth, increasing by about 1,000
  - Team to investigate reasons behind the recent decline in impressions and clicks

### Author Profiles for Blog Posts

  - Decision to transition from fake profiles to employee profiles
  - Concerns raised about maintaining consistency if employees leave
  - Action item: Cameron to test updating author profiles on 5 recent blog posts

### Blog Page Views Analysis

  - Overall upward trend since the beginning of the year
  - Strong performance in March (reason to be investigated)
  - Recent plateau/decline in page views since mid-May
  - Prateek to analyze organic search traffic separately from overall page views

### Multilingual Content Strategy

  - Plan to implement language switcher for blog posts (English, French, Spanish)
  - Cameron to work on technical implementation next week
  - Challenge: Ensuring accurate translations for all blog content
  - Proposed solution: Start with translating top 10-30 articles, potentially using AI with human review
