## Summary

GrowthX and Firework discussed critical performance gaps in their blog content strategy: while 440 articles have been developed and content production is on track (publishing 20 articles before the next meeting), blog posts published after September 2024 have generated zero leads—a concerning 8-month trend. The team identified two core issues: keywords aren't well-aligned with Firework's brand, and blog-specific lead magnets don't exist. Key decisions included transitioning author profiles from fake personas to real Firework employees for credibility, implementing multilingual language switchers (English/French/Spanish) for top articles, and investigating the root cause of the blog traffic plateau since mid-May. Critical next steps are refining keyword targeting, creating topic-specific lead magnets (infographics and e-books), and analyzing organic traffic patterns separate from campaign data.

---

## Context

Firework is a video commerce platform focused on shoppable video and short-form video content. GrowthX provides content marketing and SEO strategy services to Firework as part of the "Landbase" engagement—a major account focused on lead generation through blog content and organic search visibility. The partnership centers on developing and publishing a high-volume blog strategy to drive qualified leads for Firework's sales team. This weekly sync is the core operational touchpoint, tracking content production metrics, lead generation performance, and strategic initiatives. The relationship has been running successfully with US leads increasing from 1-2 per month to 6 per month, but recent blog content is underperforming on lead generation, signaling a disconnect between content topics and Firework's target buyer profiles.
