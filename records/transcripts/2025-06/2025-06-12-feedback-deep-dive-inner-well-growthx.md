## Summary

Marcel Santilli conducted a critical feedback session with Innerwell's leadership (Anthony Mangia and Andrew Berman) to address significant service failures in GrowthX's SEO delivery. Innerwell has lost ~50% of organic traffic since partnering with GrowthX—attributed to both AI-generated answers and execution shortcomings (high staff turnover, slow deliverable timelines, missing basic SEO hygiene like schema tags, and over-reliance on manual processes instead of programmatic automation). Marcel committed to an 8-week strategy sprint led personally by him with full engineering allocation, shifting the approach from manual agency work to programmatic content publishing and city-level SEO strategy (a 2-3x higher volume opportunity that GrowthX initially missed). Andi Bailey will schedule the strategy sprint kickoff for June 23rd with immediate internal recalibration starting the following week.

---

## Context

Innerwell is a telehealth/mental health provider operating in ketamine therapy, psychiatry, and general therapy services across multiple states with a strong local component (city-level targeting). The company generates 65% of paid traffic from Google and is experimenting with growing organic (SEO) as a complement to paid acquisition. GrowthX won the account as a high-touch services engagement but has struggled with execution due to rapid internal growth, organizational restructuring (new customer ops head Andi Bailey was introduced in this call), and the challenge of staffing new client accounts with inexperienced team members. This listening tour was Marcel Santilli's first direct conversation with Innerwell's leadership about account performance—a critical reset moment after months of unmet expectations.
