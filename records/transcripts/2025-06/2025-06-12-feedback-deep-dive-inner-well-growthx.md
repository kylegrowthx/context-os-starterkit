## Key Topics

### Current State of Innerwell's Business

  - Growing team and business
  - Experimenting with growth channels, trying to grow SEO alongside paid
  - 65% of paid traffic from Google, rest mostly from Meta
  - Lost \~50% of organic traffic since partnering with GrowthX

### GrowthX Service Issues

  - High employee turnover causing inconsistency
  - Lack of proactive strategy and opportunity identification
  - Slow delivery on promised deliverables (e.g., first report just received)
  - Link building progressing slower than expected
  - Feels more like traditional agency work than programmatic/software approach

### Strategy and Execution Concerns

  - Strategy rated 5-6/10, solid but requires too much client input
  - Lack of holistic opportunity assessment and prioritization
  - Absence of clear plan to address traffic loss due to AI-generated answers
  - Basic SEO hygiene issues (e.g., missing schema tags)

### GrowthX's Response and Plan

  - Acknowledge issues stemming from rapid growth and new team formations
  - Propose "strategy sprint" led by Marcel (CEO) to reset approach
  - Commit to more programmatic execution with proper engineering allocation
  - Plan to implement custom workflows and automate content creation/publishing

### SEO Strategy Refinement

  - Shift focus to city-level pages, recognizing higher search volumes
  - Analyze competitors like ZocDoc, Psychology Today, Talkspace for successful strategies
  - Commit to building out city-level landing pages programmatically
