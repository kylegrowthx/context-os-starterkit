## Context

Ambient.ai is a physical security and video analytics company founded by Stanford PhDs. Alberto Farronato recently joined as VP of Marketing. GrowthX has proposed a content marketing engagement to shift Ambient from inefficient paid advertising to a sustainable organic growth strategy built on personas, SEO, and vertical-specific campaigns. The engagement hinges on demonstrating that the Strategy Sprint delivers concrete research and insights that justify the $20K investment to an executive team that's action-oriented and skeptical of "overhead" spending. Ambient's unique challenge is that its customer personas cut across both industries (healthcare, enterprise, retail, etc.) and building types, requiring verticalized competitive analysis and content experiences. The company is also highly visual (computer vision product) and plans to hire for web redesign, making content strategy tightly coupled with that execution.
