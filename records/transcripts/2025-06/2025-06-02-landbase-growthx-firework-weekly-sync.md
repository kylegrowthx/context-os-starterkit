## Summary

GrowthX and Firework reviewed blog content performance and identified a critical strategic misalignment: 200+ articles published since September generated fewer than 2 leads each in the US market, with 40 articles per month proving ineffective despite improving global lead volume to 47 in May. Abhishek Anjanappa challenged the high-volume approach and directed a pivot toward quality content that positions Firework as a solution to tariff challenges (not just problem documentation) and emphasizes video as a differentiator. The team committed to normalized data analysis to identify when blogs reach peak engagement and which content types drive leads, addressing technical URL migration issues preventing week-by-week attribution tracking.

---

## Context

This is a weekly content performance sync between GrowthX (a content marketing services agency) and Firework, a platform specializing in video commerce and content solutions. Firework is a longstanding client generating paid content output (approximately 40 articles per month) targeting multiple markets. The meeting occurred during a challenging period: Prateek Gupta had been sick the previous week, creating a backlog of unpublished content, while the team was grappling with a fundamental strategic question — their high volume of blog content (200+ articles since September) was generating minimal leads, suggesting the current content strategy needed reevaluation rather than volume increases.
