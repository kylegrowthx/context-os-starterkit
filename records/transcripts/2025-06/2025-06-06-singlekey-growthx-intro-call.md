## Context

SingleKey is a Canadian B2B SaaS platform that has achieved dominant SEO performance in the Canadian market, generating approximately $300K MRR from organic search while maintaining current annual revenue of $10M (with 95% coming from Canada). The company is now pursuing aggressive US expansion to reach $50-100M in potential revenue, but faces the challenge of replicating their Canadian success in a more competitive market. They've completed extensive strategy work internally and partnered with a current agency at roughly $8K/month retainer, but are looking to accelerate execution velocity and modernize their approach. Kyle Gaudreau (who previously worked at Homebase Growing with mutual connections) connected with Rob Palumbo, SingleKey's representative, to explore whether GrowthX's AI-powered content operations could serve as their US execution partner. This is an exploratory first call to assess fit before moving toward deeper engagement discussions.
