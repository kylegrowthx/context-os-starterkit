## Key Topics

### Content Creation Workflow

  - Start with Airtable for article details and keywords
  - Use Perplexity for initial SEO-focused outline
  - Leverage Claude to refine outline and connect to target audience pain points
  - Integrate webinar content for proprietary insights
  - Aim for 60-90 minutes total time per article, with 45-60 minutes on research and outlining

### Target Audience Focus

  - Primary focus on data platform leaders and analytics line of business leaders
  - Avoid targeting data engineers or business analysts unless specifically relevant
  - Connect content to leadership challenges and decision-making, not technical details

### Differentiation Strategy

  - Use webinar content to add unique insights and language (e.g., "Blocked and Backlogged, Enabled with Anarchy")
  - Reference case studies (e.g., Amgen) to provide real-world examples without direct promotion
  - Balance SEO requirements with leadership-focused content

### Prophecy's Value Proposition

  - Position Prophecy as enabling governed self-service data access
  - Highlight how it bridges the gap between Databricks' data organization and user-friendly data retrieval
  - Emphasize the balance between self-service capabilities and maintaining data governance

### SEO and Content Structure

  - Include basic definitional sections for SEO (e.g., "What is data accuracy?")
  - Use comparison tables (e.g., data accuracy vs. integrity vs. completeness)
  - Adjust section titles to be more SEO-friendly while maintaining leadership focus
