## Summary

Megan Dickey (GrowthX's new SEO lead) and Abhishek Anjanappa (Firework) aligned on a major strategy shift for Firework's blog — after discovering recent content is underperforming on both lead generation and traffic (with older Q4/early Q1 posts delivering most leads), they decided to invest heavily in keyword research, content quality, and inline CTAs to drive the US market. Key decisions included leveraging Megan's 15-year journalism background to improve content, launching French and Spanish locale SEO (with templates for future locales), and establishing weekly reporting on leads, clicks, and impressions to track whether new content hits the same peaks as older pieces.
