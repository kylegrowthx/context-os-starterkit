## Overview

- FOSSA sought GrowthX's expertise to modernize and scale their content program using AI tools, aiming for faster, quality content production
- Technical nature of FOSSA's product, need for in-house data, and non-traditional publishing process (GitHub-based) led to decision to bring content creation in-house
- Suggestion for GrowthX: Consider offering subject matter experts for QA in specific industries to enhance content quality and client confidence

---

## Key Topics

### Initial Goals and Expectations

  - CEO connection or recommendation led to GrowthX partnership
  - Objectives:
      - Modernize and scale content program using AI tools
      - Learn from experts and jumpstart organic traffic efforts
      - No specific quantifiable metrics, but focus on repeatable, scalable AI-generated traffic assets
      - Emphasis on moving faster with quality content, both long-form editorial and PSEO tool-type assets

### Challenges and Reasons for Bringing Content In-House

  - Subject matter expertise: High bar for quality in technical product/audience
  - PSEO content quality reliant on internal data, difficult to communicate externally
  - Non-traditional content publishing process:
      - No CMS, content assets in GitHub repo
      - Manual publishing in Markdown and code
  - Inefficient to work with external agency given these factors

### Current Content Generation Approach

  - Andy using AI for \~40% of content creation
  - ChatGPT for deep research, treated as first draft
  - Not yet comfortable with 90%+ AI-generated content for longer-form editorial
  - Open to more AI involvement in PSEO content

### Feedback on GrowthX Partnership

  - Strong onboarding, despite initial competing priorities
  - Appreciated extra layer of QA for code snippets
  - Valuable learning experience, though ultimately decided in-house approach was necessary

### Future Content Strategy Considerations

  - Potential shift towards 40-50% AI involvement in content creation
  - Anticipation of increased focus on craftsmanship and quality
  - Suggestion for GrowthX: Offer true subject matter experts for technical industries

### GrowthX's Evolving Platform and Workflows

  - Transition to internal platform with custom code base
  - More specific prompting and better content control
  - Developing company-specific artifacts (personas, writing guidelines, technical guardrails)
  - Integration of deep research and evolving brand context
  - Potential for more accurate and tonally relevant content generation
