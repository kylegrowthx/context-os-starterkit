**Organic Growth Strategy Presentation**
- 381 content opportunities identified with 12 million combined monthly search volume
- Targeting low to very low difficulty topics ideal for quick wins
- Expected traffic capture rate: 10% of total search volume = approximately 1.2 million monthly visits

**Publishing Model**
- Conservative publishing pace: 125 editorial pages per month
- Aggressive sprint approach: 5 pages per day for 2-month period using "programmatic editorial" model
- Shift from pre-publish line editing to lightweight QA checks (pass/fail on critical issues only)
- Post-publish focus on pages generating clicks for optimization and enrichment

**Canva Case Study**
- Canva scaled from 250 pages (May 2015) to 6,600 pages (December 2015)
- 72x organic traffic growth in one year; 13x homepage traffic growth
- Homepage traffic decreased from 70% to 20%, indicating successful long-tail content strategy
- Non-branded organic traffic converts to brand searches at 5-7x higher rates

**Lovable's Current Position**
- 730,000 monthly visits, 98% branded, 91% from homepage
- Positioning: replicate Canva's early-phase organic expansion strategy
- Strategic focus: identify best 400-500 pages to power growth for next decade

**Risk Assessment**
- Minimal reputational risk as new content targets non-homepage areas
- Daily monitoring of impressions, click-through rates, session data
- Ability to prune or refactor underperforming pages
- QA process acts as gatekeeper to prevent major quality issues
