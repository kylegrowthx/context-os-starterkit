## Key Topics

### Appfire Situation

Appfire is at the mercy of Atlassian ecosystem changes — partner program shift, Atlassian moving more enterprise direct, resale margins declining. Catherine's mandate: get more control of the sales motion, build direct demand generation capability. Current site (~423 pages) has flat traffic. Company was "the man behind the curtain" — ranking in Atlassian-ecosystem searches but not on their own brand keywords or categories.

### GrowthX Pitch

Marcel walked through his career: IBM (SecurityIntelligence.com, highest ROI program companywide), HP (TechBeacon.com), ServiceTitan (CMO hire, $6M → $100M, IPO), Deepgram (24x traffic). Founded GrowthX 18 months ago, now ~100 employees, ~70 clients including Lovable, Webflow, Ramp, Brex, Navon, Sentinel One. Framing: website is the compounding asset that influences everything — AI fetch, SEO, conversion. GrowthX is an agency with an AI platform (Content OS + CheckThat), not just an agency.

### Strategy Sprint Model

Mandatory onboarding sprint before any content work. Week 1: context artifacts (brand, audience, tone, competitor references, terminology — up to 25 iterations). Week 3: full content strategy with keyword universe analysis, topic clusters, competitive gaps. 6-8 week lead time, only 2 kickoffs per week to maintain quality.

### CheckThat Opportunity

Marcel introduced CheckThat as an AI visibility platform. Catherine acknowledged Appfire has fallen behind — embedded too deep in Atlassian's shadow. She's interested in the audit even if not ready for full services. Marcel's framing: AEO can gain traction faster than SEO, and good content practices serve both. Will send a quick assessment.
