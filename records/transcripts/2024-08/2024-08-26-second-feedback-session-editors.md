  - Remove auto-generated titles; only include title in designated field

  - TLDR section should have bullet points and be placed before table of contents
  - Payroll CTA should be italicized and placed strategically

### Link Integration

  - Remove "tip" sections and integrate links naturally into relevant paragraphs
  - Ensure links are working and relevant to the content
  - Distribute links throughout the article, not just at the bottom

### Meta Information

  - Meta title: 15-60 characters
  - Meta description: 140-150 characters
  - Copy-paste generated title into the correct field

### Content Quality

  - Remove generic introductions (e.g., "What is payroll in Alabama?")
  - Delete irrelevant case studies or examples generated by AI
  - Ensure slug is simple (e.g., "how-to-do-payroll-in-utah")
