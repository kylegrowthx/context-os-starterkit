## Key Topics

### Product Overview

  - End-to-end solution: websites, registration, communications, session management, mobile apps
  - Excels at Tier 2 (complex conferences) and Tier 3 (webinars, repeatable events)
  - Differentiators: personalized registration logic, data mapping to CRM, flexible reporting

### Value Propositions

  - Control/flexibility with drag-and-drop setup and brand customization
  - Personalized attendee experiences via visibility logic throughout
  - Access to data insights and custom reporting within platform
  - Data portability with native CRM/martech integrations and open API
  - Predictable user-based pricing vs. per registration
  - Responsive in-house support team

### Competitors

  - Tier 1 (large events): Cvent, Regfocus, Bizzabo, Hopin
  - Tier 2 (conferences): Bizzabo, Hopin, Cvent, Regfocus
  - Tier 3 (webinars): Splash, Eventbrite, Bizzabo

### Content Strategy

  - Focus on event ROI, CRM/martech integrations based on market demand
  - 3 articles this week, 5 next week, then 10/week
  - Leverage value props and differentiation in calls-to-action
  - Provide competitor comparison content, value messaging
