## Overview

- Firework offers an integrated suite of video products for ecommerce: short videos, live showrooms, 1-on-1 video shopping assistants, and an AI virtual assistant called Ava
- Their solutions aim to elevate the online shopping experience with interactive video content, live demos, personalized assistance, and AI capabilities
- Key differentiators include global video infrastructure, enterprise compliance, integration with major platforms, and an ecosystem approach connecting their products

---

## Key Topics

### Product Overview

  - Firework is not just a video player - it has underlying video CMS, global CDN infrastructure, enterprise integrations
  - Four main products: short videos, showrooms, 1-on-1 video shopping, Ava virtual assistant
  - Products work together as an integrated ecosystem

### Short Videos

  - Embeddable video carousels, product cards for ecommerce product pages
  - Import videos from social, add product tags, customize placements
  - Addresses issues with GIFs, YouTube embeds taking traffic away

### Showrooms

  - Live, interactive video streaming for product launches, Q\&A, demos
  - Chat, product cards, giveaways to drive engagement
  - "Co-pilot" AI can answer common questions without human moderator
  - Encourages frequent live sessions to build user habits

### 1-on-1 Video Shopping

  - Replicates in-store personalized shopping assistance
  - Brand experts give live video walkthroughs, demos, measurements
  - Identifies high-intent shoppers to prioritize assistance
  - Option for Firework sales associates on-demand

### Ava Virtual Assistant

  - AI-powered video shopping assistant with human-like face/voice
  - Guides discovery with questions, shows product videos, compares options
  - Built on private knowledge base to ensure brand safety
  - Can transition to live human agent for high-value/complex requests
