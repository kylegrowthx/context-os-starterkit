## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Management

  - GrowthX has created a Notion workspace for project management, including:
      - Team and stakeholder information
      - Meeting logs
      - Task tracking
      - Competitive research
      - Performance reports
  - An Airtable has been prepared with:
      - Calibration articles for tone and voice
      - Personas and use cases
      - Competitor analysis
      - Content templates and clusters

### Competitor Landscape

  - Two main types of competitors identified:
      - RPA (Remote Process Automation) providers (e.g., Automation Anywhere)
      - Microsoft Power Automate (hybrid RPA and other automation)
  - AI agents are emerging as a related but distinct category

### Branding Considerations

  - Debating between keeping "knap.ai" or switching to "knapsack.ai"
      - Pros of "knap.ai": shorter, perceived as more legitimate
      - Pros of "knapsack.ai": easier to spell and remember, aligns with product name
  - Considering alternatives to the term "automations":
      - Potential options: guides, power boosts
      - Need to balance SEO considerations with user-friendly terminology

### Website Development

  - Current Webflow site is basic but functional
  - Exploring options with web development agencies (Web Stacks, Lightbox)
  - GrowthX may bring in freelancers to help create an MVP site in 2-3 weeks
  - Recommendation to use a "learn" section instead of a traditional blog structure
      - Example: deepgram.com/learn
      - Allows for future expansion beyond just blog posts

### Content Publishing Timeline

  - Can start with calibration articles as early as the end of this week
  - Aiming for 10 articles per week, ramping up to 20 articles in subsequent weeks
  - Earlier publishing allows for faster data collection and strategy refinement
