## Overview

- The initial calibration articles felt too basic/101-level and didn't align well with Swoogo's brand voice and target audience
- Molly will provide examples of articles that better match the desired tone and content level

- There will be an iterative, collaborative process for reviewing and refining the content strategy week-over-week

---

## Key Topics

### Initial Calibration Articles Feedback

  - Articles felt too 101-level, explaining basic concepts like "what is a hybrid event"
  - Content tone was slightly off from Swoogo's brand voice
  - Disconnect between the very broad/beginner audience targeted and Swoogo's ICP of event marketers

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Use 101-level content to build domain authority by targeting broad, top-of-funnel keywords
  - But focus main content efforts on middle/bottom-of-funnel topics tailored to ICP's interests
  - Iterative process - publish batches, get feedback, refine approach for next batch
  - Discard off-target articles, replace with better aligned ones
