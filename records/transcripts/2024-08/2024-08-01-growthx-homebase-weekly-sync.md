  - Idea to create high-value lead magnets like PDF guides and AI-powered calculators to capture more traffic

  - Prioritizing either a payroll calculator or schedule maker tool leveraging AI/LLMs
  - Payroll calculator aligns with Homebase's payroll product focus but may be more complex
