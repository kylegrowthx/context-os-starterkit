## Key Topics

### Target Audience and Goals

  - Primary focus: Cloud-based offerings for mid-market companies and web agencies
  - Aiming for 5% traffic increase, especially rebounding blog traffic
  - Current sign-up rate: \~4-5% for cloud accounts
  - Shift focus to self-serve subscriptions and product-led growth
  - Content goal: Increase from 12 to 50+ articles per month

### Content Strategy and Process

  - Current process involves community writers, keyword research, and technical review
  - Challenges with time-consuming review process for technical content
  - Opportunity to use AI for initial research, keyword topics, and outline creation
  - Focus on both technical Strapi-specific content and broader tech topics
  - Need for content addressing non-technical decision-makers (e.g., CMOs, agency leaders)

### Content Types and Priorities

  - Technical tutorials for Strapi users
  - Top-of-funnel content on trending topics and framework comparisons
  - Educational content on headless CMS benefits and use cases
  - Competitive comparison content (e.g., Strapi vs. alternatives)
  - Content modeling and editing experience from non-developer perspective

### Competitor Analysis

  - Key competitors: Sanity, Contentful, Storyblok
  - Analyze top-performing competitor content for ideas
  - Create targeted articles addressing "alternative to Strapi" searches
  - Develop product comparison pages with strong SEO and conversion optimization

### Traffic and Conversion Optimization

  - Focus on organic search due to challenges with paid advertising
  - Utilize HubSpot for conversion tracking
  - Request access to Google Ads and Search Console for data analysis
  - Integrate conversion data into content strategy decisions

### Collaboration and Workflow

  - GrowthX to provide calibration articles for feedback
  - Aim to produce 10 articles per week after initial calibration
  - Weekly performance updates (Mondays and Fridays)
  - Utilize Notion for documentation and Slack for communication
