## Overview

- GrowthX uses AI workflows to generate high-quality content at scale, adapting to the client's brand voice and industry
- They plant many content "seeds" and double down on what performs well based on data signals
- GrowthX also offers AI-powered outbound email campaigns for booking sales meetings

---

## Key Topics

### GrowthX Content Creation Process

  - Combines programmatic content generation with human review/editing by trained specialists
  - Starts with strategy, keyword research, competitor analysis to identify topics
  - Uses AI workflows to generate titles, outlines, drafts based on top-ranking content
  - Adapts content to client's brand voice through examples and calibration
  - Publishes quickly, monitors performance signals, and iterates/refreshes content

### Content Performance and Metrics

  - Primary KPI is increase in organic traffic and keyword rankings over time
  - Showed examples of traffic growth curves for other clients
  - For ProjectMark, would monitor metrics like sessions, rankings using tools like SemRush
  - Aim to build topical authority beyond just "construction CRM" queries

### AI-Powered Outbound Campaigns

  - GrowthX runs multi-channel outbound campaigns using AI for copy personalization
  - Tests many variants for best subject lines, messaging by audience
  - Handles email warmup, deliverability, meeting booking workflows
  - Shared example of insurance campaign driving hundreds of meetings per month

### Proposed Engagement for ProjectMark

  - Lighter, more turnkey content engagement to start (e.g. 30-40k words/month)
  - GrowthX handles end-to-end content production with some async feedback
  - Potential to add on outbound meeting campaigns managed by GrowthX
  - 3 month initial commitment to test and iterate the process
