## Key Topics

### Ad Keywords and Paid Strategy

  - Strapi discontinued paid ads in July due to:
      - Poor traffic generation (600 visitors/month from paid vs. 150k-180k organic)
      - Low-quality leads (negative scores in HubSpot)
      - Target audience (developers) less likely to click on ads
      - Limited budget compared to competitors (e.g., Sanity, Contentful)
  - Current ad spend reduced to $200-$300/month
  - Retargeting efforts also yielded poor results

### Organic Traffic and SEO Focus

  - Lost rankings for high-value keywords: React CMS, Next.js CMS, Angular CMS
  - Strategy shift to recoup organic traffic for frontend technology + CMS keywords
  - GrowthX to prioritize content around these topics

### Content Strategy and Templates

  - 10 new articles in progress, to be delivered by week's end
  - Focus on creating topic clusters for greater topical authority
  - Content types include:
      - Technical tutorials for Strapi users

      - Educational content on CMS use cases
      - Competitive comparison content

  - Templated approach for scalable content production (e.g., headless CMS for specific industries/use cases)
