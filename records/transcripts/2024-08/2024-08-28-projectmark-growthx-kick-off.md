### Current Traffic and Conversion Metrics

### Target Audience and Content Strategy

  - Primary focus: General contractors (80% of business), 50-500 employees
  - Secondary: MEP (Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing) subcontractors
  - Key roles: Directors (Business Development, Marketing, Pre-construction), CEOs, VPs, Senior Project Managers, Chief Estimators
  - Challenge: Balancing technical content with sales-oriented material in a B2B construction context
  - Opportunity: Explore pre-construction content, leveraging past success in this area

### Content Competitors and Mindshare

  - Direct competitors: Unanet, Buildertrend, Procore, AEC360
  - Indirect/content competitors: Monday.com, Salesforce, Pipeline CRM
  - Strategy: Create comparison content positioning ProjectMark against larger competitors
  - Opportunity: Target location-based searches (e.g., "general contractors in San Francisco") to reach both potential customers and their clients

### GrowthX Content Production Process

3.  Creation of detailed content assignments
4.  Prioritization and weekly content production
5.  Ongoing monitoring and content enrichment
