### Ramp's Goals and Metrics

  - Close 20,000 monthly user traffic deficit
  - Increase qualified traffic and email submissions

### Ramp's Current Content Strategy

  - Content pillars aligned with product categories (e.g., business credit cards, expense management)
  - Prioritization based on traffic opportunity and ranking potential
  - Mix of optimizing existing content and creating new content
  - Utilize insights from paid search team and product management

  - Multiple methods: freelance writers, in-house creation, Gushwork AI, contributor network

  - Dedicated managing editor responsible for end-to-end content production
  - Team of 16 specialized editors to augment managing editor's work
