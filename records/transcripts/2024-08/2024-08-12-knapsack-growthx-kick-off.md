## Overview

- Main goal is to drive 1,000 users to Knapsack's waitlist by end of August through organic content marketing
- Target audience is knowledge workers in finance and healthcare industries struggling with data privacy concerns when using AI tools
- Content strategy will focus on creating compelling use cases and automations that showcase Knapsack's value proposition
- Initial focus on creating landing pages for specific automations, with long-term goal of building a comprehensive automation library

---

## Key Topics

### Target Audience

  - Knowledge workers in finance (e.g. financial advisors, mortgage brokers) and healthcare (e.g. healthcare insurance) industries
  - Mid-level employees dealing with high information load and constraints
  - Unable to use existing AI tools due to data privacy/security concerns
  - Looking for AI solutions to boost productivity and impact without compromising data

### Goals and Metrics

  - Increase waitlist signups from 300 to 1,000 by end of August
  - Drive work email signups to identify potential enterprise customers
  - Long-term: 1,000 daily active users, with most being paid/enterprise tier
  - Achieve 1:10 CAC:LTV ratio through organic+paid efforts

### Content Strategy

  - Build library of compelling automation use cases and landing pages
  - Cover both efficiency (time-saving) and impact (better job performance) angles
  - Explore tangential topics like RPA that audience is researching
  - Leverage $0 search volume opportunities with high intent
  - Mix of high-intent low-volume and broader awareness topics

### Action Items

  - fwd.digital to provide 1-2 calibration articles in next 2 weeks for feedback
  - Mark to share design assets, product videos etc. to aid content creation
  - Decide on publishing domain - integrate with knap.ai or use separate Webflow site
