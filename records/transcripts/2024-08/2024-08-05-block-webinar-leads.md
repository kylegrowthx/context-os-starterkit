## Overview

- Utilized various data enrichment tools (AirOps, Clay, Hunter.io) to find LinkedIn profiles and company information for webinar attendees
- Developed a workflow to categorize leads into different statuses (potential client, need to set up meeting, partner, etc.)
- Explored ways to streamline the lead enrichment and outreach process for future webinars

---

## Key Topics

### Data Enrichment Process

  - Uploaded webinar registration list to AirOps and used PeopleDataLabs for initial data enrichment
  - Exported errors from PeopleDataLabs into a new list to try additional enrichment with Clay agents
  - Clay agents searched for LinkedIn profiles based on names, had mixed success
  - Imported enriched data back into AirOps, merged columns to consolidate LinkedIn profile links

### Evaluating Lead Quality

  - Looked at company domains, organic traffic, and keywords to gauge lead quality
  - Checked if companies and roles seemed legitimate based on available data
  - Identified some clear partners, potential clients, and companies to prioritize outreach

### Building a Workflow

  - Created a new "Status" field in AirOps to categorize leads (potential client, partner, back burner, etc.)
  - Aimed to consolidate quality leads into a single workflow view for outreach
  - Discussed displaying key properties like job title on the workflow view
