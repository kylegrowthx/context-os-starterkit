## Overview

- Double organic traffic and pipeline over the next 6 months while maintaining high lead quality
- Identify new content opportunities beyond current focus areas like web design/development
- Increase content volume and frequency while maintaining quality
- Explore interactive/programmatic content ideas leveraging Webstacks' technical capabilities

## Key Topics

### Current Content Strategy

  - Focused on web design topics driving to ebook on best website designs
  - Some content on headless CMS implementation driving to checklist download
  - Design-focused blogs perform well but are labor intensive
  - 20-30 new blogs per month, mix of internal and external writers

### Ideal Customer Profile

  - B2B tech/software companies, 100-5000 employees, funded/post-IPO
  - Target personas: VPs/Directors of Marketing, Web Strategists, Website Managers
  - Common triggers: website migration, rebranding, new marketing hire

### Success Metrics

  - Double organic traffic from \~12k/month to 24k+/month
  - Double pipeline from \~10 opps/month while maintaining 30% from organic
  - Increase diversity of traffic sources beyond top pages

### Content Creation Process

  - Keyword research \> Outline \> Writing \> Design \> Reviews \> Publish
  - Need to streamline review process, especially for client examples
  - Explore ways to refresh older content at scale
