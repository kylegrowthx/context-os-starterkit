## Key Topics

### Content/SEO Agency Path

  - Already decent traction, could keep building this niche
  - But many competitors, may get commoditized over time

### Athena Model (Services/Software)

  - Diversify into broader AI services/enablement/training
  - Treat agency as a cash cow to fund other initiatives
  - Would likely need to raise funding for this path
  - Risks around building software products

### Incentive Alignment

  - Explore revenue shares, equity stakes to incentivize partners
  - But equity complicates taxes/operations if unfunded
  - Tie incentives to sustained revenue generation

### AI Training Opportunity

  - With rapid AI evolution, opportunity to be definitive practical training source
  - Create curriculums, certifications, workshops for companies/employees
  - First-mover advantage if can build this out quickly
