## Key Topics

### Current Swoogo Outbound Strategy

  - Shifted from AE-led to SDR-led outbound efforts
  - Manually building targeted account lists (700 contacts across 300 accounts) in industrials industry
  - Light marketing touches to broader list
  - Lacks full ABM implementation (tailored content, messaging for specific industries)

### Proposed Improvements and Next Steps

  - Implement separate domain for outbound to protect main domain reputation
  - Explore trigger-based outreach (e.g., pricing page visits)
  - Develop systematic approach to prioritize accounts by ICP and intent signals
  - Automate account research and personalization using AI-assisted workflows
  - Consider expanding to other industries (e.g., B2B tech) with learnings from current approach

### AI-Assisted Outbound Tactics

  - De-anonymize website visitors for automated first touch (e.g., pricing page visits)
  - Scrape company websites and LinkedIn profiles for personalized outreach
  - Augment SDR/AE research tasks with AI-powered workflows
  - Potential to save significant time in account research and personalization

### ICP Scoring and Intent Signals

  - Framework presented for categorizing accounts by fit (good/bad) and intent (high/low)
  - Opportunity to refine ICP scoring based on closed-won deals and created opportunities
  - Discussion on balancing ICP fit with high intent signals for pipeline generation
