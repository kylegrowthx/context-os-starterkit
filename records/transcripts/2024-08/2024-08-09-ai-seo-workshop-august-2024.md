## Overview

- AI can dramatically speed up content creation and SEO optimization when used strategically
- Start by deeply understanding target audience and their intent, pain points, questions
- Use AI to generate high-quality outlines and first drafts, but still need human editors
- Continuously monitor, test, and iterate content to improve rankings
- AI is a tool to augment and accelerate, not fully replace, human expertise

---

## Key Topics

### Using AI for Audience Research

  - Demonstrate using perplexity.ai and you.com to research target audience
  - Prompt engineering to prime AI with audience context
  - Generating lists of audience goals, questions, pain points, terminology
  - Turning research into content topic ideas

### Keyword Research with AI Assistance

  - Using SEMrush and Airtable to identify keyword opportunities
  - Competitor keyword gap analysis
  - Evaluating keyword difficulty vs search volume
  - Identifying content refresh opportunities based on keyword rankings

### Building AI Content Workflows

  - Intro to Airops for building content generation workflows
  - Adapting an existing workflow template
  - Key workflow steps: research, outlining, drafting, human review, publishing
  - Generating FAQ content at scale from existing pages

### Continuous Content Optimization

  - Importance of monitoring rankings and iterating
  - Using AI to refresh and optimize existing content
  - A/B testing titles, outlines, content
  - Recovering traffic to declining pages
  - Acquiring backlinks to AI-generated content
