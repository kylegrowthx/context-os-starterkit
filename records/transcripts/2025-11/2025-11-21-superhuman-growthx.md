## Key Topics

### Superhuman's Content Scaling Challenge

  - **Primary Goal:** "Quality at scale"—increase content velocity while upholding the brand's high quality standards.
  - **Production Gap:** Current output is a "handful" of pages/month; the goal is to double or triple this.
  - **Key Use Cases:**
      - **Programmatic SEO:** Scale "how-to" content (e.g., "teacher cover letter") using templates to target specific user jobs-to-be-done.
      - **Legacy Content Refresh:** Automate updates for a large library of existing content.
      - **New Content Generation:** Create fresh content for evolving topical strategies across multiple sites.
      - **Paid Landing Pages:** Develop a turnkey solution for formulaic, re-skinned pages.

### GrowthX's Solution: Platform + People

  - **Model:** A service combining a proprietary AI platform with a dedicated team of experts.
  - **Core Technology:** An agentic AI workflow framework (open-sourcing next month) that uses "workflows as code" for rapid iteration.
      - **Research Supervisor Agent:** Performs deep research (e.g., 36-min runs for Biologica) to build a robust foundation, preventing AI hallucinations.
      - **Drafter Agent:** Generates drafts and self-evaluates against metrics (e.g., "groundness," writing guidelines) to iterate toward a high-quality output.
      - **Fact Checker Agent:** Breaks content into chunks, researches each passage, and suggests rewrites for inaccuracies.
  - **Human Team:**
      - **Engagement Manager:** Primary point of contact.
      - **Director:** Provides strategic oversight.
      - **Editor:** "Output bar raiser" who reviews every word to ensure quality.
      - **AI Design/Engineers:** Build custom workflows.
  - **Onboarding:** A 3-4 week process to define context, calibrate quality, and begin publishing.

### Pricing & Engagement Model

  - **Structure:** An 8-week strategy sprint followed by ongoing workstreams.
  - **Editorial Workstream:** \~$12-15k/month for \~5 articles/week (net new + refresh).
  - **Programmatic Workstream:** Pricing is custom based on scope, volume, and complexity (e.g., dev/design needs).
  - **Flexibility:** GrowthX is open to a flexible partnership, viewing Superhuman as a key lighthouse customer.
