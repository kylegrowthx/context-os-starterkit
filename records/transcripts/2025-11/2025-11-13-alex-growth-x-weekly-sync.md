## Key Topics

### Content Publishing & Imagery

  - **Problem:** Donald paused publishing 10 articles due to generic imagery.
  - **Solution:** Publish now, update later.
      - **Rationale:** Prioritize publishing frequency for a new blog; imagery is not a primary SEO signal.
      - **Process:** Growth X's design team will update images once Marta (Content Manager) returns from PTO next week.
  - **New Imagery Guidelines:**
      - **Comparison:** Two logos side-by-side.
      - **Alternative:** Logo of the main company being compared.
      - **Conceptual:** Imagery that visually represents the article's theme (e.g., a black hole for the "black hole of recruiting" article).

### Content Performance & SEO Strategy

  - **Problem:** The "Best AI Recruiting Tools" article saw a 44% week-over-week session drop.
  - **Solution:** Boost performance with internal linking, not an immediate refresh.
      - **Rationale:** Focus on monthly/quarterly trends for new articles.
      - **Actions:**
          - Add internal links from new articles to the top 4 performing posts.
          - Create a "Featured Blog Posts" section on the homepage to pass "link juice."
          - Ensure all new product pages link to relevant blog content.
  - **Rebranding & Content Alignment:**
      - **New Positioning:** Shifting from "AI recruiting platform" to "agency recruiting platform."
      - **Action:** Share new messaging with Growth X to update existing content and align future production.

### Technical SEO & Webflow Issues

  - **Problem:** High Webflow gigabyte usage from excessive image loads.
      - **Example:** One image loaded 1,600x more than its page was viewed in the last month.
      - **Hypothesis:** This is likely a hotlinking issue, not just large file sizes.
  - **Action:** Growth X will run a Semrush site audit to identify and fix technical SEO issues.
