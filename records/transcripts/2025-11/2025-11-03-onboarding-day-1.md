## Key Topics

### Onboarding Process

  - **Central Hub:** The `Handbook` in Notion contains all onboarding resources, organized by Day 1, Week 1, and Week 2.
  - **Task Management:** The `Onboarding` project in Linear tracks all tasks.
      - **Task Links:** Each task links directly to its corresponding Notion resource.
      - **Status Updates:** Update task status (e.g., `In Progress`, `Done`) using the dotted circle icon.
      - **Tags:** Tags (e.g., `Day 1`, `Week 1`) indicate task priority.
  - **Initial Manager:** Matthew Orpanza will guide the onboarding process.
      - **Action:** Matthew will assign an "onboarding buddy" for peer support.
      - **Note:** After onboarding, Gabrielle will be assigned to a permanent director.

### Company Tools & Systems

  - **Slack:** Primary communication tool for internal and external (client) channels.
  - **Geekbot:** A Slack integration for mandatory daily check-ins (priorities, blockers).
      - **Activation:** Alice will add Gabrielle to a pod's Geekbot *after* onboarding is complete.
  - **Linear:** Project management tool for tracking tasks and submitting IT tickets.
  - **Notion:** Knowledge management hub for SOPs, documents, and shared client workspaces.
  - **Loom:** Screen recording.
  - **Zoom:** Video conferencing.
  - **Fathom:** AI notetaker.

### Company Policies & Culture

  - **Time Overlap:** A 4-hour daily overlap with Pacific Time (PT) is required.
  - **PTO:** Unlimited PTO policy.
      - **Process:** Block time on the calendar and notify the team; no approval is needed.
  - **Referral Program:**
      - **Engineering:** Up to $10k USD.
      - **Non-Engineering:** Up to $5k USD.
      - **Condition:** Referred hire must remain employed for 6 months.
  - **IT Support:** Use the `#it-and-tools` Slack channel for issues.
      - **Process:** Post the issue and CC Alice or Yusuf (Head of IT).
  - **All-Hands Meeting:** The next meeting is tomorrow.
      - **Action:** Gabrielle must prepare a brief self-introduction (name, location, fun fact).

### Shared Login Setup

  - **Purpose:** The `team@growthxlabs.com` email is the standard login for all company tools.
  - **Process:**
    1.  Add the `team@growthxlabs.com` account in Gmail.
    2.  Use the 1Password extension to autofill credentials.
    3.  Enter the 2FA code from the Google Authenticator app.
