## Key Topics

### New SEO Strategy Proposal

  - Growth X proposed a long-tail content strategy to build domain authority and capture traffic.
  - **Content Pillars:**
      - **Glossary Definitions:** Low-risk, high-value content for LLM snippets.
      - **General Health Information:** Educational content on topics like perimenopause.
      - **Ingredient Deep Dives:** Detailed articles on product ingredients.
      - **Product Comparisons:** Articles comparing Biologica to competitors (optional).
  - **Data-Driven Approach:**
      - Strategy is based on standard keyword research plus Growth X's internal LLM visibility tool.
      - This tool analyzes competitor performance in LLM search to identify high-potential topics.

### Regulatory & Review Feasibility

  - **Primary Blocker:** The strategy's viability depends on whether Biologica can publish general health info without the standard medical review process.
  - **Proposed Solution:** Publish content as general information, completely separate from product claims, and include a "not medical advice" disclaimer.
  - **Risk Mitigation:** Growth X is exploring using Maggie's past feedback to build a knowledge base for automated content checks, reducing the need for manual review.
  - **Specific Concerns:**
      - **Ingredient Deep Dives:** Past content on this topic required extensive rewrites from Maggie for accuracy, raising concerns about scalability.
      - **Product Comparisons:** A philosophical decision for Biologica; not a mandatory part of the strategy.

### Implementation Plan

  - **Site Architecture:**
      - **Initial Plan:** Use the existing Shopify blog template and path (`/blogs/health`).
      - **Simplified Approach:** Publish all new content within the existing "Wellness Center" blog.
          - **Rationale:** Prioritizes content creation over complex architecture.
          - **Method:** Use tags (e.g., "glossary") to categorize articles. The Wellness Center page can then be updated to display content based on these tags.
  - **Publishing Cadence:**
      - **Rationale:** A slow, steady rollout avoids overwhelming Google's index and appearing unnatural for a new domain.
      - **Pacing:** 10–15 articles per week.
      - **Monitoring:** Track Google's indexing rate in Search Console to adjust the pace as needed.

### Pipeline & Content Quality

  - **Lynx Linking Error:**
      - **Issue:** A recurring error in the "Lynx" content pipeline caused incorrect links.
      - **Resolution:** The error was fixed in week 11. Growth X will review Biologica's article docket to identify and correct any remaining affected articles.
  - **Reviewer Feedback Analysis:**
      - Growth X will analyze Maggie's recent feedback to identify systemic issues vs. idiosyncratic preferences.
      - **Goal:** Integrate actionable insights into the content pipeline to improve future output quality.
