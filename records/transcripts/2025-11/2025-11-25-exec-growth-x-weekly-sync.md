### Landing Page Feedback & Corrections

  - **AI Role Play Page:**
      - **Issue:** SEO recommendations were applied to the outdated V1 page.
      - **Action:** Move all recommendations to the current V2 page.
      - **Goal:** Experiment with an aggressive page title (`Best AI Sales Roleplay Platform`) to test ranking for the high-volume keyword "Sales Training Platform."
  - **Coaching Platform Page:**
      - **Issue:** The page was optimized specifically for "sales coaching."
      - **Rationale:** This targeted a high-volume keyword.
      - **Action:** Make the main page use-case agnostic to support future products (e.g., call scoring). A separate, dedicated "Sales Coaching Software" page will be created.

### New URL Taxonomy & Structure

  - **Approved Structure:** A new URL hierarchy will create clear content clusters.
      - **Example:** `/ai-roleplays/sales/`
      - **Rationale:** Improves SEO by signaling topic authority to Google and simplifies analytics reporting.
  - **Industry Pages:**
      - **Decision:** Keep industry pages under the `/industries/` pillar.
      - **Rationale:** This structure is more flexible, allowing pages to cover multiple products (e.g., AI roleplays, call scoring) as the product suite evolves.

### Content & Backlink Strategy

  - **Content Production:**
      - **Status:** 40 AI roleplay comparison posts published in November, ahead of schedule.

  - **Performance (Nov 4–25):**
      - **Traffic:** 58 total sessions (45 organic) for the new content.
      - **Context:** This is a strong result for pages targeting zero-volume keywords, which typically take 3+ months to gain momentum.
      - **Reporting:** Garett requested GA4 conversion events (signups, demos) be added to the performance dashboard.
  - **Backlink Strategy Shift:**
      - **Past Strategy:** Build general domain authority by linking to the homepage.
      - **New Strategy:** Focus link equity on specific product pages to drive direct business value.
      - **December Plan:**
          - **Priority 1:** AI Role Play pillar page.
          - **Priority 2:** New Sales Roleplay page (Garett to launch by mid-Dec).
          - **Future:** L\&D and Customer Support pages will receive links in January.
