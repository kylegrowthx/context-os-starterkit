          - **Approach:** Publish articles based on competitive analysis and keyword gaps.

### Template Strategy: Rationale & Future

  - **Initial Focus → Website Templates:**
      - **Rationale:** Competitor analysis (Wix, Webflow) revealed website templates offer the highest search volume with the lowest competition.
      - **Goal:** Capture immediate traffic by targeting low-hanging fruit.
  - **Future Focus → SaaS/Internal Tool Templates:**
      - **Rationale:** Aligns with Lovable's core ICPs (entrepreneurs, PMs) and highest-retention users.
      - **Path Forward:** Requires analyzing user prompt data to identify specific build goals and pain points.
          - **Precedent:** This method was used successfully with Bolt to identify "trigger moments" (e.g., 5+ prompts) that correlated with higher retention.
