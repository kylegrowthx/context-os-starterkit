## Key Topics

### Competitor Analysis Update

- Aida Knezevic presented an updated analysis of Tableau's content strategy, showing that ~30% focuses on BI and analytics, AI-related content is weak, and ~70% covers data visualization techniques (Sankey diagrams, tornado charts) and product how-tos.
- **Recommendation:** Rather than create "Hex vs. Tableau" comparisons, Hex should create "Tableau vs. Power BI" competitor comparison pages to capture traffic without cannibalizing its own visibility.

### Content Calendar & Topic Review

- The "Content OS" Airtable is live with 106 topic assignments across multiple clusters.
- **Airtable Structure:**
  - **Topic Clusters:** High-level overview of target themes with target audience and product mapping.
  - **URL Tab:** Keyword gap analysis of 44,000 keywords grouped by competitor URLs (Databricks, etc.), showing keyword difficulty and search volume.
  - **Client Views:** Blog Ideas (for Hex review/approval), Drafts (in-progress content), Published Blogs (live content).
- **Review Process:** Hex reviews topics via Airtable comments (thumbs up/down or contextual feedback). Topics tagged with Aida for iteration. GrowthX will refresh existing Hex content (e.g., cohort analysis tutorial) by adding new SEO-optimized sections rather than creating competing pages.

### BI Content Strategy

- **Challenge:** How to create BI content that ranks for search intent without conflicting with Hex's "redefining BI" POV.
- **Solution:** Title and structure follow search intent (e.g., "What is Cohort Analysis?") but content embeds Hex's unique perspective (e.g., "Why traditional cohort analysis is flawed and how Hex approaches it differently").
- **Implementation:** Charles Schaefer to draft a BI/visualization POV doc that GrowthX will integrate into content outlines. This approach allows Hex to build authority while differentiating from traditional BI tools without cannibalizing keyword rankings.

### LLM Visibility Tracking

- **Scrunch (Synthetic Monitoring):** Simulates prompts based on Hex's company description, competitors, and target audiences (~1,100 responses collected so far). Current citation rate is ~4% (typical for Hex's site size). GrowthX can add more prompts from Hex customer conversations to improve coverage.
- **Looker Dashboard (Real Traffic):** Combines Search Console and GA4 data to show actual LLM referral traffic to Hex's pages. ChatGPT drives majority of traffic, followed by Perplexity and Claude. Blogs receive significant LLM traffic alongside homepage and templates.
  - **GA4 Issue:** Hex reports GA4 data as unreliable due to suspected bot activity and high "not set" traffic (~90%). GrowthX confirmed this is a common GA4 limitation and suggested considering alternative analytics (RudderStack, PostHog).
- **Meridian:** Hex recently onboarded Meridian (new platform, design partner, launched 2-3 weeks ago) which offers recommendations alongside monitoring. Teams will schedule comparison meeting to evaluate both platforms.
