## Key Topics

### The GrowthX Challenge: Bridging Editorial & Technical

  - **Role Focus:** Process-oriented, defining and articulating content quality to improve internal AI platforms.
  - **The "Delta":** The core task is identifying the gap between pipeline output and desired quality, then articulating it for engineers. This involves:
      - **Prompt Engineering:** Refining instructions for LLMs.
      - **Context Engineering:** Providing rich, relevant information.
      - **Engineering:** Filing detailed tickets when the issue is technical.
  - **The Goal → Time Compression:** Reduce human post-processing time from hours to 10–15 minutes per article.
      - **Example:** A clinically accurate article, reviewed by an OB, naturopath, and lawyer, can be produced with minimal human touch.
      - **Significance:** This efficiency is a "brain-breaker" for clients accustomed to slow, expensive content production.

### Candidate Background & Editorial Philosophy

  - **Experience:**
      - **Current:** Senior Tech Writer at TechRadar (Future Publishing), covering consumer AI.
      - **Prior:** Editor at iMore (Future Publishing), where he built a new how-to section.
  - **Editorial Judgment:**
      - Prioritizes context and accuracy; "better to not go in depth than go in depth and be wrong."
      - Applies the "done is better than perfect" principle, focusing on expert tone over endless tweaks.
  - **Process Improvement Example (iMore):**
      - **Problem:** No structure for evergreen how-to content.
      - **Solution:** Created a style guide and worked hands-on with freelancers to elevate content beyond dry, step-by-step instructions.
  - **Process Improvement Example (TechRadar):**
      - **Problem:** Needed to commission 40 articles in one month.
      - **Solution:** Focused all content on personal interactions with ChatGPT, a trend identified via Google Discover.
      - **Result:** February became the AI section's most successful month of the year.

### AI Familiarity & Adoption

  - **Workplace Usage:** Limited by Future's strict AI policy.
      - Uses a Notion/ChatGPT integration for daily trend scanning.
      - Uses ChatGPT as a conversational partner for headline ideation.
  - **Personal Experimentation:**
      - Actively experiments with new AI tools (e.g., AI web browsers).
      - Has successfully used prompt engineering to train LLMs to match his personal writing style.
  - **GrowthX Expectation:** A "voracious" adoption of LLM tools is vital.
      - This enables self-unblocking and building custom solutions, a key cultural shift from traditional media's resource-starved environment.

### GrowthX Culture & Operations

  - **Team:** \~20 Managing Editors.
  - **Workload:** 1–3 clients per ME, depending on complexity.
      - **Typical:** 2 clients, with 10–20 deliverables per week (pipeline-assisted).
  - **Culture:**
      - **Remote-First:** Fully remote; no "second-class" status for remote employees.
      - **Non-Toxic:** Leadership is committed to a genuine, collaborative environment.
      - **Aggressive Questioning Encouraged:** MEs are the "first users" of the product, and their feedback is critical for iteration.
