## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Rationale

  - **Goal:** Shift from expensive paid social to an organic content flywheel that drives traffic and users, while influencing AI search results (e.g., Perplexity, ChatGPT).
  - **Challenge:** Current content is too technical, creating a review bottleneck and limiting top/middle-of-funnel reach.
  - **Solution:** A topic cluster approach to build authority and scale content production to \~5 articles/week.
      - **Rationale:** Start with less technical content to accelerate production. As GrowthX learns the industry, pipelines will adapt to more technical topics.

### Proposed Topic Clusters

1.  **Competitor Alternatives & Comparisons:**
      - **Why:** Directly influences AI search rankings and provides a clear narrative for LLMs on Hex's differentiation.
      - **Note:** Hex is already building this content. The teams will collaborate to optimize it.
2.  **AI & Data Analytics:**
      - **Why:** Broad T/M-o-F topics to capture high-volume keywords and link to existing, high-quality thought leadership.
3.  **Modern Data Viz & Business Intelligence:**
      - **Why:** Capture keywords from competitors (Tableau, Databricks) and reframe Hex as a tool for business users.
      - **Focus:** Spotlight features like Data Apps, Threads, and Embedded Analytics.
4.  **Data Engineering & Infrastructure:**
      - **Why:** Address themes from competitor content (Databricks, Google Cloud).
      - **Focus:** Position Hex's Notebooks as enabling analysts/scientists to perform basic engineering tasks for self-serve analytics.
5.  **Shadow AI & Data Trust:**
      - **Why:** Address the risk of unauthorized employee use of LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude) and position Hex's Threads and Data Apps as the governance solution.
      - **Priority:** This cluster will be launched immediately.

### AI Visibility & Performance Tracking

  - **Scrunch Dashboard:** Tracks Hex's visibility in LLM responses vs. competitors (Power BI, Tableau, Databricks, Looker).
      - **Current State:** Hex has a 2% citation rate (LLM links to site), while competitors are at \~10%. This is expected given their larger content libraries.
  - **Looker Dashboard:** A 7-page report will track performance using data from Google Search Console and GA4.
      - **Key Features:** An LLM referral report and a cohort report to track the performance of individual clusters.
