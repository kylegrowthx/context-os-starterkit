## Key Topics

### Highspot's Content Challenges

  - **Current Process:** A piecemeal workflow using Profound for AI recommendations, requiring manual handoffs and assembly.
  - **Team Focus:**
      - **SEO (Claire):** SEO-driven blogs and landing page optimization.
      - **Content Marketing (Pam):** Global thought leadership (campaigns, ebooks, articles).
  - **Primary Need:** A single, integrated engine for faster, higher-quality content creation.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Service Model:** An end-to-end partner handling strategy, execution, and CMS publishing.
  - **Engagement Structure:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, $30-40k)**
          - **Goal:** Align on quality and build a content roadmap.
          - **Output:** 5-10 published articles.
          - **Decision Point:** Highspot can opt out after this phase.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12+ months, $15-18k/mo)**
          - **Output:** 5 articles/week (1,500–2,500 words each).
  - **Team Model:** A dedicated team (Managing Director, Managing Editor, Content Manager) with deep SEO/content backgrounds.

### GrowthX's Platform & Workflows

  - **Platform:** A proprietary internal tool used by GrowthX's team, not a client-facing SaaS product.
  - **Workflow Inputs:** "Artifacts" (e.g., ICP personas, brand guidelines) that provide context for the AI.
  - **LLM Orchestration:** Uses a mix of models (OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini) and specialized research tools (Perplexity, Exa, Tavli).
  - **Core Workflow Loop:**
    1.  **Brief Generation:** AI analyzes keywords, competitors, and intent to create a robust brief.
    2.  **Agentic Research:** Workflows run in a loop, scoring outputs and refining research until criteria are met.
  - **Client Collaboration:** Feedback is currently handled via Google Docs and Slack. A client-facing portal is planned for Q1 2025.

### Key Concern: Scoring & Visibility

  - **Highspot's Requirement:** A transparent, user-facing scoring system with direct competitor comparison, similar to Profound.
  - **GrowthX's System:** Internal-only scoring for quality and groundedness, with no direct competitor comparison feature for clients.
  - **Rationale:** Highspot needs this visibility to validate content performance and justify the investment.
