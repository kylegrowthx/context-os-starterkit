## Key Topics

### growthx.ai's Community-First Strategy

  - **Business Model:** Bootstrapped to $4M ARR before a Feb 2025 fundraise; on track for $15M ARR this year.
  - **AIledgrowth.com Vision:** A community-as-a-product designed to be a self-funding, net-neutral marketing and recruiting channel.
  - **Financial Model:** A $150 sign-up fee covers member acquisition cost (CAC). Monthly revenue ($30/mo) is reinvested to fund growth, creating a compounding effect.
  - **Current Status:** 68 paying members on Circle.so, acquired with minimal promotion, validating the model's potential.

### Dimitri's Experience: Airbnb Superhost Program

  - **Mandate:** Launch the Superhost program in LatAm (primarily Brazil) to increase revenue via guest recurrency.
  - **Strategy:**
      - **Enablement:** Held weekly virtual and monthly in-person meetups to recruit high-potential hosts (identified by product data) and share best practices.
      - **Scaling:** Created a Superhost ambassador program to train new hosts locally, leveraging the momentum of the 2014 World Cup and 2016 Olympics.
  - **Key Accomplishment:** Grew the program to nearly 5,000 members in the first year, far exceeding the 2,000-member goal.

### Dimitri's Experience: WeWork Brazil Launch

  - **Mandate:** Launch WeWork's Brazil operations and achieve \>75% occupancy for the first five buildings.
  - **Challenge:** Sell space before the physical product existed.
  - **Solution:** Built a pre-launch community around the "future of work" to generate excitement and leads.
      - **Result:** Launched at \>80% occupancy.
  - **Key Initiative: Curated Member Connections**
      - **Goal:** Differentiate WeWork as a business-driven community, not just an office space.
      - **Process:**
        1.  **Profiling:** Tracked all member companies in a spreadsheet by industry, size, and needs.
        2.  **Discovery:** Held bi-weekly conversations with 5–10 members to understand their challenges.
        3.  **Matching:** Made targeted introductions (e.g., VC to startup, service provider to HR lead).
        4.  **Tracking:** Measured connection success via follow-up meetings and business generated.
  - **Key Accomplishment:** Led the creation of WeWork's first NPS survey (in a Google Sheet) to measure member satisfaction. This process was later adopted company-wide.
