## Key Topics

### Current Operational Challenges

  - **Problem:** The company's ops are fragmented across Notion, HubSpot, and Linear, preventing a unified source of truth and accountability.
  - **Pain Point:** V1 systems persist too long because leadership lacks time for implementation and improvement.
  - **Goal:** Build systems that enforce processes and create accountability, moving beyond manual oversight to enable scaling.
  - **Context:** The company is growing rapidly (\~$15M revenue, 70 clients, 100 people) and needs to mature its operations.

### Automation Philosophy & Examples

  - **Core Principle:** Automate low-value admin tasks to free up human capacity for strategic work.
  - **Example:** Auto-create a project in a PM tool when a deal closes in the CRM.
  - **Implementation Strategy:**
      - Let teams feel manual pain first → validates the process before automating.
      - Implement changes incrementally → manages team adoption and reduces overwhelm.
  - **Small-Scale Example (Curated for You):**
      - **Problem:** Non-client-facing staff (merchandising team) lacked diplomatic communication skills for Zendesk tickets.
      - **Solution:** Created message snippets → provided a script for staff to use, building comfort and skill with client-facing language.

### Zendesk Rollout Case Study (Curated for You)

  - **Project Goal:** Roll out Zendesk to 40–65 people and integrate it with Slack for client ticket submission.
  - **Primary Success:** Smooth change management across multiple stakeholder groups (leadership, CSMs, merchandising team).
  - **Critical Mistake & Learning:**
      - **Incident:** Client data was sent to the wrong Slack channel due to an incorrect account tag.
      - **Root Cause:** A training gap. Emily assumed the "check account tag" step was obvious and didn't emphasize it enough for new users.
      - **Resolution:** The team contained the breach immediately. The incident served as a key learning opportunity to make training more explicit and redundant.
  - **Self-Assessment (Scale of 1–10):**
      - **CS Head:** 9/10 (High impact, but manager was slow to adopt the tool).
      - **COO:** 8–9/10 (High impact, but could have managed leadership's feature requests more tightly).

### Tooling & Reporting Experience

  - **Tooling Stack (Curated for You):** HubSpot, Notion, Linear, and Zendesk.
  - **Reporting Experience:**
      - **Curated for You:** Built all dashboards as the sole ops person.
      - **Airtable:** Scoped requirements for a data science team; built CS-specific dashboards in Airtable.
  - **Airtable Health Score Dashboard:**
      - **Method:** Used a junction table to create daily snapshots of customer health data.
      - **Value:** Enabled trending analysis to identify volatility (spikes, drops) in metrics like usage, which Emily called "big numbers."
  - **Current Tooling Experiments:**
      - **Motion:** AI-powered task scheduler.
      - **Obsidian:** Documentation and note-taking tool.
  - **Zapier Experience:**
      - **Use Case:** Connected HubSpot to Notion.
      - **Challenge:** Inconsistent data entry in HubSpot required manual workarounds.
      - **Limitation:** The free plan's scale limit prevented full adoption.
