This weekly sync brings together the Teero leadership team (primarily Nate Anderson, who leads content and strategy) with GrowthX, a B2B content marketing agency. ## Key Topics

### Monthly Performance Review (October)

  - **Organic Traffic:** +15% MoM, driven by the CDT code launch.
  - **Blog Traffic:** +6% MoM, with 5.11k clicks from SERPs.
  - **LLM Referrals:** Stagnant (\~30 clicks/week).
      - **Hypothesis:** The target audience (dental professionals) has low LLM adoption, making this a secondary focus.
  - **Leads:** Stagnant MoM, but growing QoQ.
      - **Top-performing pages:** "Dental Narratives for Insurance Providers" and "Dental Office Voicemail Greetings."

### Strategic Shift: Programmatic Content & Conversions

  - **Programmatic Content Engine:** The CDT code launch proves programmatic content drives "step-function" growth.
      - [**New Launch:** 2,000+ "dental hygienist salary in \[city\]" pages are already ranking on page one.](https://fathom.video/share/2Y_uzK7Ds7ZVdY9TdXK4qunexjV9tEBJ?tab=summary&timestamp=612.0)
      - **Goal:** Find more programmatic opportunities to replicate this success.
  - **Conversion Optimization:** The immediate priority is converting existing traffic into high-value leads for the new RCM (dental billing) service.
      - **Action:** Update CTAs on relevant blog posts to promote the RCM service and its dedicated landing page.
      - **Rationale:** This is a high-impact, low-effort change, as the traffic is already present.

### Technical Deep Dive: Programmatic Indexing

  - **Problem:** The new salary pages have an indexing gap.
      - **Finding:** 155 of the 2,000+ salary pages are not yet indexed (7.75% gap).
      - **Context:** This is a healthy rate for a large, new programmatic launch.
  - **Solution:** Create and link index pages in the footer.
      - **Action:** Build a central index page for the salary pages (e.g., by state/city) and add a link to it in the site footer.
      - **Rationale:** This improves site architecture and ensures Google's crawlers discover and index all pages regularly.

### New Content Approval Interface

  - **Demo:** Jessalynn demoed a new client-facing interface designed to streamline content approval.
      - **Features:** A simplified view with tabs for "Ready for Review" articles and one-click approval buttons.
      - **Automation:** A Slack bot will notify Nate when articles are ready for review.
  - **Feedback:** Nate confirmed the current Slack-based process is already efficient for his workflow.
