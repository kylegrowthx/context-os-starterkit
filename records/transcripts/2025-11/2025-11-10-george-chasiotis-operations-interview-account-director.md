## Key Topics

### The Account Director Role at GrowthX

  - **Model:** Shifting from a management role over specific "pods" to a pooled, strategic IC resource.
  - **Function:** Directors are called in via Linear tickets for high-level strategic work (e.g., technical SEO audits, AI strategy, shifting priorities).
  - **Rationale:** The change enables deep strategic thinking by removing directors from daily management tasks.

### Candidate Background & Experience

  - **Minutia Agency (2020–2023):**
      - Grew to 18 full-time staff and contractors.
      - Served 50+ B2B SaaS clients (PLG, SLG, Enterprise, Technical, Marketplaces, Vertical).
  - **LinkedIn Growth Agency (2023–Present):**
      - **Mistake:** Launched with three services (content, paid, signal-based outbound), causing buyer confusion.
      - **Correction:** Pivoted to a single, clear "LinkedIn Growth" service.
      - **Success:** Developed a scalable process for founder-led content:
          - Founder interview → AI-assisted transcript analysis → Content brief → Human-written draft → AI-assisted editing.
      - **Outcome:** Winding down due to slow market adoption, but the content system is a proven asset.

### Strategic Approach & Example

  - **Prioritization:** Focuses on high-impact work over client-requested tasks. Uses data to justify decisions and push back on low-value requests.
  - **Time-to-Value (TTV):** A key goal is achieving fast TTV to build client momentum.
      - **Established Clients:** Start with quick wins like content updates.
      - **Early-Stage Clients:** Focus on high-impact pages (e.g., alternatives, software listicles).
  - **Case Study: Promotional Products Client (2020)**
      - **Problem:** Initial content attracted low-value, small-volume orders.
      - **Solution:** Shifted strategy to target high-value use cases (e.g., company rebrands) and explicitly positioned content for bulk orders.

### Team Management & Operations

  - **Structure:** At Minutia, the team was organized into leads (SEO, Content) who managed ICs (strategists, writers).
  - **Client Communication:** Leads, not ICs, handled client communication to maintain a consistent strategic voice.
  - **Delegation:** Provided direction via a central Notion document detailing project goals and context. This gave leads autonomy for execution.
