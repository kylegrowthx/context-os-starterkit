## Overview

- **Content Strategy Pivot:** The current approach of repurposing random podcast transcripts is failing, yielding low engagement (e.g., 10 new subscribers) and lacking a clear purpose. The new strategy is to create highly focused content for specific audiences.
- **Persona-Driven Workflow:** To enable this pivot, the team will update the existing Notion persona doc with specific pain points and goals. This will allow growthx.ai to target content effectively by simply referencing a persona ID (e.g., "target Persona 2").
- **Distribution & Inspiration:** The team will analyze successful content creators to improve its LinkedIn distribution strategy and "piggyback" on their popular topics by offering a unique, potentially controversial, take.
- **New Content Format:** A new format will be introduced where Guillaume and Gonto record async "hot takes" (via Loom, Slack) on these popular topics for growthx.ai to turn into posts.

## Key Topics

### Problem: Current Content Underperforms

  - Recent posts, like those based on Noah's podcast, are not meeting quality standards.
  - This stems from a lack of clear direction, as growthx.ai receives only raw transcripts without goals, target audiences, or desired story arcs.
  - The result is low engagement, with subscriber growth declining from \~30 to \~10 per post.
  - **Conclusion:** The team must choose between two paths:
    1.  **Broad:** Create widely successful content to cast a wide net.
    2.  **Focused:** Create highly targeted, high-quality content for specific audiences.
  - The team agreed the current "in-between" approach is ineffective.

### Solution: A Persona-Driven Workflow

  - The new workflow will be built around the existing Notion persona doc.
  - **Action:** Guillaume and Julia will review and update the doc with specific pain points, frustrations, and goals for each persona.
  - **Action:** growthx.ai will then use this doc to target content by referencing a persona ID (e.g., "target Persona 2"), ensuring each piece has a clear purpose.
  - **Decision Point:** The team must decide whether to add a "Growth Operator" persona, which would expand the audience beyond the current focus on decision-makers (CEOs, VCs).

### Solution: Strategic Distribution & New Formats

  - The team will analyze successful creators (e.g., "California," "Cannabis") to improve its LinkedIn distribution strategy.
  - **Action:** The team will identify popular topics from these creators and use them as a "piggyback" opportunity.
  - **New Format:** Guillaume and Gonto will record async "hot takes" on these topics.
      - **Rationale:** This format is designed to be time-efficient and accommodate different time zones.
      - **Execution:** growthx.ai will then turn these recordings into posts, potentially highlighting diverging viewpoints for added interest.
