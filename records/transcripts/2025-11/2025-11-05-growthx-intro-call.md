## Key Topics

### Ipsy's Content & SEO Challenges

  - **Goal:** Increase editorial content velocity to improve SEO and capture visibility in AI search (AEO/GEO).
  - **Blockers:**
      - **Resource Constraints:** No internal bandwidth for high-volume content creation.
      - **Workflow Friction:** A recent blog migration to Contentful means publishing is currently owned by the tech team, slowing the process.
      - **Trust in AI:** Generic tools like Copy.ai require heavy human oversight to ensure brand voice and accuracy, negating their efficiency gains.
  - **Potential Scope Expansion:**
      - **PDPs:** 2,000+ product pages for Ipsy's e-commerce shop.
      - **Meta Content:** Titles and descriptions across the entire website.

### GrowthX's Solution: A Two-Phase Partnership

  - **Phase 1: 8-Week Strategy Sprint ($40k)**
      - **Goal:** Build a custom AI engine and content OS tailored to Ipsy's brand.
      - **Process:**
          - **Deep Dive:** Audit Ipsy's business, products, and target audience.
          - **Artifact Creation:** Develop custom inputs (e.g., audience profiles, product catalogs) to guide the AI.
          - **Content OS:** Build a Notion-based system for managing the content roadmap.
          - **Technical SEO:** Conduct an audit and advise on schema markup.
      - **Deliverable:** \~10 live articles by the end of the sprint to calibrate quality.
  - **Phase 2: 12-Month Growth Execution (Starts at $15k/mo)**
      - **Goal:** Scale content production to daily publishing.
      - **Pricing:**
          - **$15k/mo:** 5 articles per week.
          - **$18k–$20k/mo:** For higher volume.
      - **Process:**
          - **GrowthX:** Handles all research, writing, SEO optimization, and publishing.
          - **Ipsy:** Provides timely feedback on drafts.
          - **Analytics:** Tracks performance via Looker (traditional SEO) and Scrunch (AI visibility).

### GrowthX's AI Content Workflow

  - **Core Principle:** A multi-step, agentic workflow that ensures quality and brand alignment, unlike simple one-shot AI prompts.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Brief Generation:** An AI agent creates a detailed brief based on SEO analysis (keywords, competitors, intent) and the custom artifacts.
    2.  **Iterative Research:** An agent performs research, targeting specific objectives and sources (e.g., medical journals for Biologica). If quality thresholds are not met, the research is refined and re-run.
    3.  **Knowledge Graph:** Research is stored in a structured knowledge graph, making it easily retrievable for future content and ensuring consistency.
    4.  **Drafting & Review:** The AI drafts content, which is then reviewed against the custom artifacts and brand guidelines before being sent to Ipsy for feedback.
