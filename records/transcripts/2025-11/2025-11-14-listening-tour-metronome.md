## Key Topics

### White Paper Project Debrief

  - **Context:** The project launched during a period of significant team flux at Metronome, with most members having less than six months of tenure.
  - **Root Cause:** Quality issues stemmed from a joint failure in process.
      - **Metronome:** Missed initial review deadlines, preventing early calibration.
      - **growthx.ai:** Shipped content too quickly, exacerbating the problem.
  - **Outcome:** The content was rushed and of low quality ("D-grade" per Megan's assessment), but the team prioritized speed to market.

### Pricing Index Project: A New Standard

  - **Goal:** Drive real, tangible results for Metronome.
  - **Quality Requirement:** Accuracy is non-negotiable.
      - **Rationale:** Inaccurate pricing models would harm Metronome's prospects and customers.
  - **Scope:** The project includes both data-driven content and editorial sections ("Metronome's take").

### New Editorial Process & Lead

  - **Lead:** Will is now the editorial lead, with Megan providing support.
  - **Process Reset:** growthx.ai will implement a new process to ensure quality and build trust.
      - **Deep Calibration:** Work directly with Will to define tone, voice, and quality standards.
      - **Pipeline Transparency:** Share internal artifacts (e.g., instruction sets) and explain the content pipeline to build trust and show how feedback is used.
      - **Copy-First Review:** Deliver full copy, not just outlines, to enable review of the final product.
  - **Benchmark:** growthx.ai cited a client with a 4-layer review process (MD, ND, lawyer, co-founder) that, after initial calibration, now receives high-quality content with 70-80% engagement rates.
