## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Strategy

  - **Performance:** Clicks grew \~20% week-over-week for the second consecutive week.
  - **New Content:** 7 articles will be published this week.
  - **Keyword Strategy:**
      - **Self-Modeling:** Focus on the specific bottleneck it solves—both data science (multivariate analysis) and data analysis (univariate analysis)—not the generic "how to build an AI model."
      - **Brownfield:** Target this niche term (\~30 monthly searches) to reach existing plants.
      - **Closed-Loop Optimization:** Create more content on this high-performing topic, which drives strong CTRs and conversions.
  - **Content Directives:**
      - **"Coordinated Operating Strategies":** Avoid this specific phrase, as it is part of a future announcement. Use general terms like "coordinating teams" instead.
      - **"Full Automation isn't the goal":** Reframe as "Full automation isn't the *only* goal" to maintain the long-term vision of closed-loop optimization.

### New Initiative: Vertical-Specific Playbooks

  - **Objective:** Create gated content assets ("playbooks") to support demand-gen campaigns for Imubit's 2026 target verticals.
  - **Target Verticals:**
      - Refining (Oil & Gas)
      - Chem/Petrochem
      - LNG (Liquefied Natural Gas)
  - **Asset Concept:**
      - **Format:** A step-by-step guide or practical application, not a generic overview.
      - **Content:** Adapt top-performing articles for each vertical into a cohesive guide.
      - **Goal:** Attract warmer leads who are already interested in an implementation roadmap.
      - **Constraint:** Must align with the new content directive (closed-loop as a final stage).
  - **Action:** GrowthX will assess internal bandwidth for this new workflow and provide a timeline.
