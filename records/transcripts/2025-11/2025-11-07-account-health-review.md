## Context

    3.  Discuss nominated accounts.

      - **Impact:** This is creating client friction and risk.

### High-Risk Accounts

  - **Rapid (Churn Risk)**

  - **Superhuman (Upsell Opportunity Blocked by Internal Chaos)**
      - **Context:** The recent rebrand (Oct 29) created a massive upsell opportunity to serve the full product bundle (Mail, Grammarly, Coda, Go).

      - **Action:** Leverage Marcel's relationship with Yuri to gain clarity and influence the new content strategy.

      - **Concern:** Luke's experience with a different pricing model (Ramp's) could create friction with Engine's current $15k/mo, 5-article contract.
      - **Mitigation:** Over-deliver on performance and momentum to build a strong case for value.
