## Key Topics

### GrowthX Reorg & Director Role

  - **Problem:** Directors were getting "mired in the weeds" of production, preventing strategic work.
  - **Solution:** A new model separates responsibilities:
      - **Managing Editors:** Production (2–3 clients, 5 pieces/week).
      - **Engagement Managers:** Relationship owners (8–12 clients).
      - **Directors:** Pooled strategic resource.
  - **Director Responsibilities:**
      - High-level strategy (SEO, AEO).
      - Intervening for at-risk accounts.
      - Building playbooks for new processes.
  - **Work Management:** Directors will use a ticketing system (e.g., Linear) to manage requests from Engagement Managers.

### Candidate Background & Philosophy

  - **Experience:** B2B SaaS growth for technical audiences (devs, engineers) at PromptQL (formerly Hasura) and LaunchDarkly.
  - **Focus:** Connecting product value to audiences and building scalable growth systems.
  - **Core Belief:** Organic content is the foundation for an inbound engine; without it, sales teams must work 3–5x harder.

### Organic Attribution & Pipeline

  - **Challenge:** Demonstrating ROI for organic-only content.
  - **Strategy:**
      - **Proof Point:** Track traffic sources (Ahrefs, GA) to show content's impact on long-tail keywords and AI search.
      - **Client Framing:** Position content as the essential narrative that fuels inbound growth.

### "Zero-to-One" Process Example

  - **Project:** Created a content repurposing workflow from webinars.
  - **Tools:** Claude, Opus Pro, Descript.
  - **Process:**
      - **Input:** Long-form webinar video.
      - **Output:** 5+ pieces of content (short-form video, social snippets, blog posts).
      - **AI Training:** Fed Claude speaker transcripts to retain their voice.
  - **Results:** 30% social engagement increase, 15% blog traffic increase.

### Content for Technical Audiences

  - **Challenge:** Creating accurate content for clients at the forefront of their field.
  - **Solution:**
      - **AI Training:** Build a "brain" for AI tools by feeding them all available content from client experts.
      - **Trust Building:** Deliver consistent, high-quality content to earn client confidence and reduce micromanagement.

### Work Prioritization

  - **Method:** Prioritize work based on client impact.
  - **Hierarchy:**
    1.  High-spend accounts
    2.  At-risk accounts
    3.  Accounts with tight deadlines
  - **Adaptability:** Open to aligning with GrowthX's established prioritization methods.
