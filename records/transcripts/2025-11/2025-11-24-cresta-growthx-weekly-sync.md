## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Framework

  - GrowthX's strategy uses topic clusters to build authority, which improves visibility in both traditional SEO and AI search (LLMs).
  - LLMs favor structured content with features like:
      - FAQ sections
      - TLDR summaries
      - Original research and statistics
  - Performance will be tracked via a Looker dashboard (for referral data) and Scrunch (for LLM visibility).

### Competitor Analysis & Content Gaps

  - Cresta's blog traffic is currently driven by company news, not SEO.
  - Competitors cover thematic clusters that represent Cresta's content gaps:
      - AI/ML concepts & virtual agents
      - Contact center automation
      - Industry-specific content (healthcare, financial services, telecom)
      - Foundational glossaries

### Proposed Topic Clusters & Prioritization

  - **1. Contact Center Operations:** Broad appeal, high search volume. Targets all Cresta products.
  - **2. AI Agents:** High priority. A deal was recently won from a lead found on ChatGPT for this topic.
  - **3. Vertical-Specific Guides:**
      - **Priority Order:** 1) Travel & Hospitality, 2) Healthcare & Insurance, 3) Financial Services.
  - **4. Competitive Comparisons:**
      - **Tone:** Focus on Cresta's outcomes and positioning as an alternative, not on competitor deficiencies.
      - [**Accuracy:** Use a "Product Feature Matrix" artifact and an "accurate as of \[date\]" disclaimer.](https://fathom.video/share/pYJ4grfLM4EXzsjxszf3yjszTwR1KjNc?tab=summary&timestamp=1469.0)
      - **Review Process:** Initial review by Nicky & Devon; escalate risky content to Russell & Legal.
  - **5. Agent Performance & Coaching:** Targets the Agent Assist and Conversation Intelligence products.
  - **6. Conversational Insights (NEW):**
      - **Rationale:** Added to target executive CX buyers and position Cresta as a leader in using conversations for business insights (e.g., predictive CSAT).

### Website Structure for New Content

  - **Decision:** New SEO content, especially competitive comparisons, will live in a separate `/guides` section.
  - **Rationale:** This protects the main blog's brand integrity and prevents it from being diluted by a high volume of functional SEO articles.
  - **Implementation:** The `/guides` section will be linked in the main navigation (e.g., under "Resources") and footer to ensure indexability.

### Image Generation & Content Refresh

  - **Image Generation:** GrowthX will generate featured images using Cresta's brand guidelines.
      - **Action:** Nicky will invite Creative Director Steve to the Slack channel for initial style calibration.
  - **Content Refresh:** GrowthX will analyze existing blog posts to identify low-hanging fruit for improvement.
      - **Action:** Bulk-add SEO-friendly elements (FAQs, TLDRs) to existing content.
