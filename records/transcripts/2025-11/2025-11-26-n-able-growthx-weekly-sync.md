## Key Topics

### Product Feature Matrix Finalized

  - The matrix is complete, integrating documentation for all three key products:
      - N-Central
      - AdLumen MDR
      - Cove Data Protection
  - It maps each product's capabilities and differentiators to the ATT\&CK Lifecycle Framework.
  - **Purpose:** Serve as the single source of truth for accurate product messaging in all new content.

### New Content Review Workflow

  - **Problem:** The current email-based review process is inefficient, sending all drafts to all PMMs regardless of product relevance.
  - **Solution:** An Airtable automation will route drafts to specific PMMs based on the products mentioned.
      - **Logic:** A draft mentioning Cove → routes to the Cove PMM (e.g., Garrett).
      - **Multi-Product:** A draft mentioning multiple products → routes to all relevant PMMs.
  - **Process Flow:** PMM Review → Editorial/Brand Review.
      - **Rationale:** Get product facts correct first to avoid rework later.

### AI Pipeline Validation

  - **Goal:** Validate the new "agentic pipeline" by testing its ability to generate accurate product copy from the matrix.
  - **Method:** Regenerate 5 existing drafts from scratch using the new matrix.
      - **Rationale:** This creates a clear "before and after" comparison to prove the model's effectiveness and build PMM confidence.
  - **Scope:** Focus on product-specific sections; general language and tone are already approved.

### Blog Topic Prioritization

  - PMMs provided feedback on blog ideas, primarily noting topics to avoid.
  - **Assumption:** Topics without PMM feedback are approved for production.
  - **Action:** Elaine will review the full list and approve 10–15 topics by EOW next week.
      - **Rationale:** This provides a 3-week content runway for GrowthX.
