  - **Organic Traffic:** Upward trend in sessions, which is a more reliable metric than GSC data post-Google's parameter limit changes.
  - **Engagement:** High engaged sessions indicate users are finding and staying on pages.
  - **Keyword Growth:** Strong growth in Top 20 keywords, which drive the most clicks.
  - **Subcluster Performance:**
      - **Strong Growth:** Scheduling & Call-offs, Templates.
      - **High-Impact Drop:** "Employee Survey Examples" traffic is down 32%.
          - **Hypothesis:** Keyword cannibalization from new articles (e.g., "Anonymous Employee Survey") is spreading traffic.
          - **Action:** Investigate the mid-October refresh and confirm re-indexing.
      - **Strategy:** Consolidate fragmented survey content into comprehensive posts to improve ranking and traffic.

### New Content Strategy

  - **Competitor Keywords:** Target high-intent searches like "Beekeeper alternative."
      - **Rationale:** Capture users actively seeking alternatives to established tools.
      - **Differentiators:** Highlight Yourco's key advantages, such as being web-based (vs. app-only) for frontline workers without smartphones.
  - **Industry-Specific Content:** Create "Frontline Intelligence" content tailored to specific industries (e.g., logistics, manufacturing).
      - **Rationale:** Align with trending LLM searches and top articles, which show high user interest in industry-specific applications.

      - **Impact:** Requires more detailed "artifacts" (e.g., separate writing guidelines, proofreading checklists) to ensure high-quality outputs.
      - **Process:** Feedback from Jessica's comments is manually reviewed before being integrated into the AI system.
  - **Content Calendar:**
