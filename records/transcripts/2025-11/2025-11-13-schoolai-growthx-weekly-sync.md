## Summary

## Context

SchoolAI is an edtech platform that GrowthX has been supporting through ongoing content marketing and SEO services. This weekly sync brings together Jenn Peters and Megan Dickey from GrowthX with SchoolAI's product and content leads—Nikki Muncey and Nathan Buhler—to align on content strategy, track product launches, and manage the editorial pipeline. The meeting happened against the backdrop of two significant shifts: the end of a PSEO (programmatic SEO) contract that GrowthX was running, which created a gap that needed to be filled with alternative initiatives, and SchoolAI's internal pivot from positioning a monolithic 2.0 product launch to shipping features iteratively as they become ready. This meeting represents GrowthX's proactive approach to finding adjacent services and content strategies to maintain value delivery during the transition.
