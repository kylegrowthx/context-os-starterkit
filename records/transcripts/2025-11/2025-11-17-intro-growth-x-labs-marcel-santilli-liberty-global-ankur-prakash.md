## Key Topics

### Liberty Global: Context for the Partnership

  - **Structure:** A multinational TMT holding company (telecom, media, tech) with over $3B AUM in its venture arm.
  - **Portfolio Brands:** Virgin Media, O2, Telenet, Formula E, Formula 1, TripAdvisor.
  - **Investment Mandate:** Takes strategic investment positions and helps portfolio companies grow by becoming a customer and making introductions across its brand family.
  - **Call Origin:** An intro from Madrona suggested a dual opportunity for LG: an investment and a commercial partnership to help GrowthX access LG's brands.

### GrowthX: The "Services as Software" Model

  - **Mission:** Help brands become publishers by creating high-quality content at scale.
  - **Core Offering:** End-to-end content creation (strategy, publishing, LLM monitoring) for both traditional SEO and AI search (GEO).
  - **Engagement Model:** An 8-week strategy sprint followed by a 12-month growth execution phase.
  - **Team:** Combines AI workflows with human expertise, including a Chief Content Officer (former TechCrunch EIC) and SEO leads from TripAdvisor and SEMrush.
  - **Competitive Stance:** Views itself as an agency alternative, not a competitor to workflow tools like Profound or AirOps.

### The GrowthX Platform: "Atlas"

  - **Function:** An internal "content OS" that enables the "experts in the loop" model.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Artifacts:** Client-specific documents (e.g., ICPs, brand voice) that provide AI with essential context.
      - **Agentic Pipelines:** Workflows that run in a loop until a quality threshold is met.
      - **Research Engine:** Synthesizes info from multiple sources (Tavli, EXA, Anthropic, OpenAI) and stores it in a knowledge graph for future retrieval.
      - **Human Oversight:** Editors can inspect and modify any step in the workflow.
  - **Product Roadmap:**
      - **Atlas:** Will be opened to clients in the future.
      - **Check That:** A Profound competitor launching Jan/Feb 2026.

### Client Examples

  - **Augment Code:** Established the company as an expert in enterprise AI coding through editorial content, tool comparisons, and thought leadership.
  - **Ramp:** Created a programmatic vendor directory using Ramp's internal spend data, enabling the company to rank for searches like "Airtable vs. ClickUp."
  - **Webflow:** Built integration pages and definitions for technical terms.
  - **Lovable:** Created a templates library using the client's API to automatically generate and optimize presentation templates.
