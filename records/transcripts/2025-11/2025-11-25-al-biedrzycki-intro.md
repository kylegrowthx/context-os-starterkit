## Key Topics

### Manifold's Challenge: AEO Strategy for a Niche Platform

  - **Context:** Al, new Head of Marketing, is building the 2026 AEO strategy for Manifold, a life sciences AI platform.
  - **Platform:** Manifold's AI platform helps scientists synthesize complex R\&D data, bridging the gap between data sprawl and the need for new treatments.
  - **Target Audience:** Content must serve two personas:
      - **Bottoms-up:** Scientists (product users)
      - **Tops-down:** Business decision-makers (buyers)
  - **Constraints:** A small marketing team (1.5 people) requires a scalable solution to build domain authority.

### GrowthX's Solution: Hybrid AI + Human Content Engine

  - **Approach:** A hybrid model combining AI workflows for scale with human SMEs for accuracy.
  - **Team:** Includes a Chief Content Officer (ex-TechCrunch EIC) and editors with backgrounds at Ramp, Brex, and Webflow.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks)**
          - Focus: Context engineering and persona development.
          - Deliverable: Publish initial content to build momentum.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12 months)**
          - Deliverable: Scale content production.
          - Note: Production volume depends on the client's review and approval process.
  - **Workflow:**
    1.  **Brief Generation:** AI analyzes keywords, SERPs, and competitors to create a detailed brief.
    2.  **Drafting:** AI generates a draft using the brief, company context, and persona artifacts.
    3.  **Review & Validation:** Human editors check accuracy and suggest rewrites for unvalidated passages.
    4.  **Finalization:** Adds cross-linking, citations, SEO meta tags, and images.
    5.  **Publishing:** GrowthX handles staging and publishing on Webflow.
  - **Performance Tracking:**
      - **AEO:** Tracks LLM visibility and positioning against competitors via Scrunch.
      - **SEO:** Monitors engagement, clicks, and conversions via a custom Looker dashboard.

### AEO & Website Strategy

  - **GrowthX's Recommendation:**
      - **Scale Content:** Publish consistently to build domain authority, a prerequisite for AEO success.
      - **Segment Content:** Use separate sections (e.g., `/guides` for bottom-funnel, `/blog` for top-funnel) to guide user journeys.
      - **Drive Traffic:** Top-funnel content in LLMs should cite the website to drive direct traffic and conversions.
