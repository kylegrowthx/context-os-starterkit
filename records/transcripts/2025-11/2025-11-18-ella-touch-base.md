## Key Topics

### Atlas Platform Bottleneck

  - **Problem:** The Atlas platform is not scalable for a product-led future.
      - **Root Cause:** It lacks the ability to push global workflow updates.
      - **Impact:** Every content pipeline must be updated manually, one-off, which is unsustainable.
  - **Resource Misalignment:**
      - **Client Ops:** Fixes pipelines one-off but cannot perform root-cause engineering.
      - **Engineering:** Prioritizes long-term product vision over immediate internal tool improvements.
  - **Solution:** A clear launch timeline for Atlas as an external product, supported by more product and engineering resources.

### Inconsistent Client Handoffs

  - **Sales-to-Strategy Sprint Handoff:**
      - **Problem:** No standard process for setting client expectations or defining roles/responsibilities.
      - **Impact:** Creates misaligned expectations and stalls production.
      - **Andi's MVP Fix:** A client-facing project doc (PowerPoint) with a calendar and weekly deliverables, due next week.
  - **Strategy Sprint-to-Pod Handoff:**
      - **Problem:** Inconsistent handoffs cause pods to recalibrate strategy.
      - **Impact:** Wastes time and loses context from the initial 8-week sprint.
      - **Root Cause:** Inconsistent Atlas training and prompts, plus a lack of visibility into past prompt refinement.

### Inefficient Production Workflows

  - **Problem:** Many editors bypass Atlas for manual work (e.g., in Google Docs), undermining the platform's value and preventing the team from scaling.
  - **Root Cause:** A mix of personality (tech-averse vs. curious) and inconsistent training.
  - **Solution:** A new reorg will merge Content Manager and Managing Editor roles.
      - **Goal:** Force reliance on Atlas to meet new capacity targets (3–4 accounts per person by March).
      - **Support:** Panzer holds weekly Atlas troubleshooting sessions, but the technical depth can be off-putting.

### KPI & Reporting Gaps

  - **Problem:** Current reporting (clicks, impressions) is not meaningful to clients.
  - **Client Focus:** AI mentions and referrals.
  - **Solution:** Track AI mentions (quality, not just quantity) and integrate with client CRM data to enable true attribution.
