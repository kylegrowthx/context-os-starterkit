### Candidate Experience: Rapid7 Customer Health Score

  - **Background:** 7 years at Accenture (federal focus) → Rapid7 (commercial focus).
  - **Current Role:** CS Program Manager (new role since April) executing strategic initiatives for the VP of Operations.
  - **Primary Project:** Building a customer health score from scratch.
      - **Goal:** Unify siloed teams (Data, Product, Services) around a single definition of customer health.
      - **Metrics:** Product adoption, support tickets, NPS, and CSM ("Advisor") sentiment.
      - **Outcome:** Dashboards for CSMs and leadership to identify at-risk customers (for churn mitigation) and expansion opportunities.
  - **Key Contributions:**
      - **Cross-functional Alignment:** Facilitated design thinking workshops to build consensus on health indicators and their weighting.

      - **Process Improvement:** Created a formal "Strategic Initiatives Intake Process" to vet new projects by effort and impact, ensuring focus on top priorities.
  - **Collaboration with Data Team:**
      - **Process:** LeGrand provides UI/UX mockups (e.g., PowerPoint) to define requirements, leaving the technical implementation to the data team.
      - **Negotiation:** Balances development effort vs. user experience (e.g., chose Salesforce over Gainsight for a 5x faster build).
      - **Tools:** Gainsight, Tableau, Salesforce, Gong, Jira.
