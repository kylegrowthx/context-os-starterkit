## Key Topics

### Content Production & Review Process

  - **Problem:** The initial review process for DA-specific articles was a bottleneck due to factual inaccuracies ("hallucinations") from the AI agent.
  - **Solution:** A new, streamlined review process was implemented to accelerate publishing.
      - **DA-Specific Content:** JP will review for accuracy.
      - **General SEO Content:** Publishes directly to the CMS without manual review.
  - **Goal:** Publish 30+ articles by EOW. The long-term target is 30 articles/week.

### Reddit Monitoring & Strategy

  - **Context:** Reddit is a key source of brand perception, but past direct engagement was limited by bandwidth and difficulty measuring ROI.
  - **New Approach:** Shift from direct recruitment to brand perception management.
      - **Goal:** Counter negative narratives (e.g., "DA a scam") and shape public opinion.
      - **Method:** Monitor relevant threads and engage with comments to influence the conversation.
  - **Measurement Challenge:** Quantifying ROI is difficult.
      - **Proposed Solution:** Use indirect signals to build conviction.
          - **Surveys:** Ask new workers how they heard about DA.
          - **Unattributed Brand Traffic:** Analyze Search Console for generic searches (e.g., "data annotation") that lead to the site.

### LLM Sentiment Monitoring

  - **Tool:** GrowthX introduced a tool that tracks ChatGPT responses to specific prompts about DA.
  - **Purpose:** Provide directional data on how LLMs perceive the brand.
      - **Example Prompts:** "Who pays more?" or "Which has better work-life balance?"
  - **Measurement Challenge:** Direct attribution is impossible (LLM responses don't link).
  - **Proposed Solution:** Use this as a directional signal, not a primary metric.
      - **Refinement:** Segment monitoring for different worker types (generalists vs. professional experts) to understand their distinct concerns.

### SIFT Tool for LinkedIn Sourcing

  - **Tool:** GrowthX demoed SIFT, a tool for monitoring LinkedIn activity of target accounts.
  - **Use Case:** High-touch recruitment of professional experts.
      - **Workflow:** Monitor key ML researchers or engineers → Identify relevant posts → Initiate 1-on-1 outreach.
  - **Access:** Jason Gong will invite Nancy and Mason to the tool.
