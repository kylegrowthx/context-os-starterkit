## Key Topics

### The Problem: Fragmented Operations at GrowthX

  - **Business Context:** GrowthX is pivoting from a marketing agency to a SaaS company with a services layer.
      - **Current State:** A 1-year-old agency using an internal AI platform for content creation.
      - **Future State:** The platform will become an external SaaS product with a PLG model.
  - **Operational Challenges:**
      - **Tooling:** Fragmented across Linear (strategic projects), Notion (health scoring), and HubSpot (limited seats).
      - **Impact:** This forces reactive work, preventing strategic analysis of team efficiency, customer health, and forecasting.
      - **Goal:** Consolidate tools into a single platform for unified visibility and proactive planning.

### The Solution: Emily Gutzmer's Experience

  - **Background:** Moved from Customer Success (Airtable) to Operations, driven by an interest in the consultative, operational aspects of the work.
  - **Project 1: Zendesk Launch (Curated4U)**
      - **Problem:** No formal client request process.
      - **Solution:** Launched Zendesk to create a structured ticketing system.
      - **Scope:**
          - Designed workflows and routing logic.
          - Enabled new metrics: ticket resolution time, feedback categorization.
          - Managed change for both clients and internal teams.
      - **Challenges:**
          - **Leadership:** Inconsistent urgency from leadership.
          - **Adoption:** Reaching 90-95% adoption was difficult, as some team members clung to old, offline methods.
  - **Project 2: Standardized Onboarding (Curated4U)**
      - **Problem:** Inconsistent onboarding processes across clients.
      - **Solution:** Created a flexible, standardized framework.
      - **Method:**
          - Conducted 1-on-1s to understand team-specific pain points.
          - Built a bare-bones structure to ensure core steps were centralized.
          - Designed for future growth (e.g., platform-specific automations in Asana).
  - **Project 3: Customer Health Score (Curated4U)**
      - **Problem:** Inconsistent client data prevented comparative analysis.
      - **Solution:** Built a multi-pillar health score dashboard.
      - **Method:**
          - Standardized client data collection.
          - **Pillars:** Relationship Strength, Channel Expansion, KPIs (e-commerce), and a draft Deliverable Score.

### GrowthX's Operational Context

  - **1-Year Vision:** Design a delivery org that serves the new SaaS/PLG model, integrates with a subscription community, and maps a complex client ecosystem.
  - **Operational Change Process:**
      - **Current State:** Fast-paced, memo-driven (Amazon-style) process.
      - **Method:** Leadership owns OKRs; teams have autonomy to execute approved solutions.
      - **Pace:** Decisions on blockers are made in 1–2 weeks.
