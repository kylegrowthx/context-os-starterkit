## Context

Biologica is a women's health supplement brand founded by Joey Zellinger (co-founder of Allbirds) and his wife Liz, launching in December 2025. The company's core insight is that women's health supplements fail on two dimensions: they don't target specific hormonal life stages (perimenopause, postmenopause) and they're expensive and hard to take (e.g., 9 pills/day). Biologica's solution is a targeted, effervescent beverage format designed for specific hormonal stages, offering compliance and appeal that generic supplements like AG1 lack. GrowthX is managing the pre-launch content strategy to establish authority and legitimacy for the product launch, building a library of perimenopause/postmenopause content plus long-tail articles that avoid direct product mentions to mitigate regulatory risk. This is a weekly sync on execution and pipeline health leading up to launch.
