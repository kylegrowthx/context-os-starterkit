## Context

Homebase is a human resources and payroll software company. GrowthX is providing content marketing services to build out Homebase's "Question Center" — a new content vertical designed to answer common questions from small business owners about HR, payroll, and employment practices. This is a strategic initiative to drive organic visibility and provide authoritative guidance on topics relevant to Homebase's customer base. Matthew leads the GrowthX engagement and works closely with Kerry (content/product lead at Homebase) and Sonia (editorial/tone review) to finalize the content strategy and unblock production.

## Key Topics

### Problem: Inaccurate & Unauthoritative Content

  - The previous content pipeline scraped Homebase's existing content, which created two key issues:
      - **Outdated Data:** Facts and figures were pulled from old articles, leading to inaccuracies.
      - **Missing Context:** The original sourcing for statistics was lost, undermining the content's authority.

### Solution: New Sourcing & Content Strategy

  - **New Agentic Pipeline:** A new pipeline will conduct more thorough research, prioritizing primary sources like government and academic sites.
  - [**Academic In-Text Citations:** Data points will be cited academically (e.g., "According to \[Source\]") to build authority for this specific content format.](https://fathom.video/share/EBWTgP64x5AZ1gkiVvxF1BF7THWxSJom?tab=summary&timestamp=938.0)
  - **Methodology Blurb:** The final source list will be replaced with a blurb explaining the sourcing process.
      - **Rationale:** Aligns with Google's focus on transparency and methodology.
  - **Improved CTAs:** Calls to action are now more relevant to the article's topic (e.g., a "payroll" CTA on a payroll article).
  - **Tone & Transitions:**
      - **Tone:** The new tone is more authoritative but still matches the Homebase brand.
      - **Transitions:** The pipeline will be updated to add transition sentences between paragraphs and bulleted lists for better flow.

### Implementation & Cadence

  - **Production Cadence:** 10 articles per week.
  - **Initial Review Process:** The first few batches will include internal comments explaining the sources used to simplify the Homebase team's review.
  - **Homebase Review Focus:**
      - **Sonia:** Will conduct a final pass on the first batch for tone and flow.
      - **Kerry:** Will set up the Question Center on the website once the first drafts are ready.
      - **Both:** Should look for any missed queries or topics that could be added to articles.

### Related Topic: Content Refresh

  - **Finding:** An analysis of TLDRs revealed inconsistencies in existing content, such as mismatched numbers in listicles and incorrect dates.
  - **Action:** These findings will be included in Jacob's "Refresh Priorities" analysis, which will be shared next week.
