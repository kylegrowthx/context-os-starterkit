The partnership focuses on driving organic traffic and engagement through SEO-optimized content. This weekly sync is a standing meeting to review content pipeline progress, unblock approvals, and align on new content strategy. The meeting was timed after Ambient's recent product launch, which had consumed the team's bandwidth for several weeks.
