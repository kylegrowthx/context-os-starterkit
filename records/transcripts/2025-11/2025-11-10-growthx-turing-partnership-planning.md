## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Balancing Two Distinct Business Units

Turing has two business units with different content needs:

  - **Turing Intelligence (TI):** Enterprise-focused, building proprietary AI apps for clients like Pepsi.
      - **Goal:** Generate enterprise pipeline.
      - **Content:** Verticalized use cases (e.g., "AI for financial reporting").
  - **TA2 (AI Labs):** Sells to top AI labs (e.g., OpenAI) to improve their models.
      - **Goal:** Win 100% of business in key horizontals (e.g., coding, RL environments).
      - **Challenge:** Account penetration varies (e.g., 30% at OpenAI vs. 10% at Amazon), creating a need for content that drives expansion.

### The Solution: A Phased Content Strategy

  - **Phase 1: Turing Intelligence (TI) Focus**
      - **Rationale:** TI is an easier starting point and leadership's top priority for pipeline generation.
      - **Output:** 5 articles/week (new + refresh), with a focus on verticalized use cases.
  - **Phase 2: TA2 (AI Labs) Expansion**
      - **Timeline:** Begin TA2 work within \~60 days of kickoff to address leadership's focus on this 70-80% of engagements.
      - **Initial Scope:** Fill SEO gaps with "how-to" guides (e.g., "How to use a REST API to build X").
      - **Long-Term Scope:** Create derivative articles from existing case studies (e.g., a Salesforce AI project) to drive traffic to core assets.

### The Mandate: High-Quality, Multi-Purpose Content

  - **Requirement:** All content must be high-value and multi-purpose, not just SEO-optimized.
  - **Rationale:** This mandate from Phil ensures content can be repurposed for other uses (e.g., sales enablement) to demonstrate clear ROI.
  - **Action:** Turing will provide 3-5 examples of desired quality to set a clear benchmark.

### The Partnership Framework

  - **Pricing:**
      - **Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint):** $40,000 for 8 weeks, including 5-10 articles.
      - **Phase 2 (Execution):** $15,000/month for 12 months, delivering 5 articles/week.
      - **Opt-Out:** Turing can cancel after Phase 1.
  - **Process:**
      - **Review:** Bailey (TA2) and Nathan (TI) are primary reviewers, using Google Docs and Slack.
      - **Reporting:** A Looker dashboard will track metrics (clicks, impressions, conversions) and GEO performance (using Scrunch).
  - **Onboarding:** Turing will provide existing brand, product, and sales materials to accelerate growthx's ramp-up.
