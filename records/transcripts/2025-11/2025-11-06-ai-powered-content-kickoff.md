## Key Topics

### Turnstile Context & Goals

  - **Product:** A quote-to-cash platform spanning quoting, billing, reporting, and subscription management.
  - **ICP & Pricing:** Pre-seed to \~$30M ARR companies. Pricing is usage-based (\~0.5% of billing volume), not per-transaction.
  - **Competitors:**
      - **Primary:** Stripe Billing.
      - **Newer:** Tabs, Sequence, Orb.
      - **Indirect:** QuickBooks, HubSpot.
  - **Marketing Goal:** Build organic authority quickly to stand out in a crowded market and provide founders with content for social media.

### GrowthX Solution & Process

  - **Service:** End-to-end content creation (strategy, SEO/GEO, publishing) to build an organic engine and reduce reliance on paid channels.
  - **Team:** A dedicated pod (Managing Director, Editor, Content Manager) with deep marketing expertise (e.g., ex-TechCrunch, G2, TripAdvisor).
  - **Phased Engagement:**
      - **Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint):** Develops a content roadmap and "artifacts" (brand voice, ICP) to train the AI workflows.
      - **Phase 2 (Growth Execution):** Daily publishing begins, with the review process as the only bottleneck.
  - **Content Types:**
      - **Editorial:** Informational blog posts and thought leadership.
      - **Programmatic:** Scaled content (e.g., comparison pages) generated from a data source (e.g., Turnstile's CRM integrations, public pricing models).
  - **Workflow:** A "chain of thought" AI process with human oversight, using multiple LLMs (GPT-4, Perplexity) and fact-checking to ensure quality and credibility.

### Logistics & Collaboration

  - **Timeline:**
      - **Kickoff:** Week of December 8.
      - **Publishing:** Targeted to begin by the end of December.
  - **CMS:** Webflow is preferred. Turnstile must create a blog template and provide a user seat for GrowthX to stage and publish content.
  - **Collaboration:**
      - **Meetings:** Weekly syncs (can be bi-weekly).
      - **Communication:** A shared Slack channel for daily updates and feedback.
  - **Turnstile Preparation:** Provide brand guides, product one-pagers, ICP profiles, and a competitor list.
