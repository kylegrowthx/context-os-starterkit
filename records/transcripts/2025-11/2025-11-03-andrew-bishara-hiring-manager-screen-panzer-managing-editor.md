  - **Editorial Rubric:** Quality is judged by alignment with client brand, tone, and language.

  - **Process Improvement Example:** Standardized inconsistent promotional emails at The Personal Development School.

      - **Solution:**

          - Implemented a formal QA process to prevent launch errors by an external agency.
  - **Challenge:** Gaining team and management buy-in for new processes.

### AI Tooling Experience

  - **Content Generation:** Uses custom GPTs for emails, blogs, and SEO.
      - **Method:** Feeds brand-specific content (e.g., YouTube transcripts) to train the AI on the founder's voice and style.
  - **Gap Analysis:** Uses AI to identify missed opportunities (e.g., a missing schema markup).
  - **Key Learning:** Human oversight is non-negotiable.
      - **Why:** To prevent plagiarism, ensure brand alignment, and verify facts.
      - **Risk:** AI can pull competitor information or generate content inconsistent with brand values.

### The Managing Editor Role at GrowthX

  - **Core Function:** Operate GrowthX's internal AI platform, Atlas, to produce client content.
  - **Key Responsibilities:**
      - **Operate Pipelines:** Run Atlas's agentic workflows for editorial and programmatic SEO content.
      - **Edit Output:** Refine AI-generated content for client presentation.
      - **Improve Pipelines:**
          - **Inputs:** Refine instructions and context documents (e.g., style guides) for the AI.
          - **Collaboration:** File engineering tickets to fix platform bugs or build new features for client needs.
  - **Team Structure:** Internal-facing role; account strategists manage client communication.
  - **Onboarding:** Fast-paced ramp-up (est. 4 weeks) using Notion documentation and hands-on Atlas training.
