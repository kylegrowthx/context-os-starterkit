## Summary

Andi Bailey (GrowthX) conducted a screening interview with David McDowell, a Senior Director at Assembly Global with 10 years of SEO experience, for a strategic-only Director position. McDowell expressed strong interest in the role's focus on pure strategy over account management, and highlighted his success at getting buy-in for a non-branded SEO strategy at Chanel by framing it as a hedge against declining branded search. ## Key Topics

### GrowthX's New Director Role & Reorg

  - **Problem:** Directors were bogged down in production, preventing them from focusing on high-level strategy.
  - **Solution:** A reorg shifts Directors out of account ownership into a pure strategic execution role.
  - **New Director Responsibilities:**
      - Set client strategy and goals.
      - Align KPIs with contractual deliverables.
      - Conduct strategic audits (e.g., technical SEO, content refreshes) via a ticketing system.
      - Build playbooks to scale successful strategies across the team.
  - **Team Structure:**
      - **Engagement Managers:** Own client relationships and project management.
      - **Content Managers/Editors:** Execute content generation using the AI platform.

### Candidate Experience & Motivation

  - **Current Role (Assembly Global):** Senior Director leading SEO for 3 of 4 largest enterprise clients.
      - **Client Work:** Strategy, roadmap, and team oversight; reporting and storytelling.
      - **Internal Work:** Manages 3 directors; leads cross-channel collaboration efforts; helps evolve the agency's AI search offering.
  - **Motivation for GrowthX:**
      - **Platform:** Excited by the AI platform's ability to solve the client content scaling problem, a key challenge at his current agency.
      - **Role:** Seeks a strategic-only position to focus on high-impact thinking, free from day-to-day operations.

### Strategic Initiative Example: Chanel

  - **Challenge:** Secure buy-in from risk-averse luxury client Chanel for a non-branded SEO strategy.
  - **Approach:**
    1.  **Diagnosis:** Identified a major gap in non-branded search performance.
    2.  **Framing:** Positioned the strategy as a hedge against declining branded search interest, not a risk.
    3.  **Pilot:** Launched a successful 1-year pilot for one business division.
    4.  **Scaling:** Used pilot results to secure buy-in from other divisions and the global team.
  - **Execution:**
      - Coordinated internal teams (content, strategy) and an external vendor.
      - Managed weekly/bi-weekly syncs to align on research, page development, and post-launch plans.

### Playbook & Process Development

  - **Reporting Framework:** Built a Looker Studio tool to analyze performance drivers.
      - **Function:** Maps success/decline to specific page/query types.
      - **Insight:** Distinguishes between performance drops caused by search interest decline, SERP changes (e.g., CTR), or external factors (e.g., paid media).
  - **Metadata Optimization SOP:** Developed a playbook for scaling metadata optimization.
      - **Process:** Identify top revenue-driving SKUs → create AI prompts for optimization → launch with client.

### Q\&A: GrowthX's Challenges & Tools

  - **Primary Challenge:** Scaling the team to match the rapid pace of technology development.
  - **Director Reorg Rationale:** To free up Directors for strategic thinking, experimentation, and playbook creation, moving them away from production management.
  - **AI Visibility Tools:**
      - GrowthX uses Scrunch but is aware clients prefer Profound.
      - McDowell's agency also uses Scrunch but is considering switching to Profound due to data inconsistencies.
      - GrowthX is developing its own tool, "Check That," which leverages its internal AI workflows.
