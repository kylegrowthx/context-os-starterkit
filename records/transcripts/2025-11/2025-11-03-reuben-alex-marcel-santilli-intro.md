## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Model & Needs

  - **Core Philosophy:** Prove that AI can replace/augment human labor in knowledge work, starting with marketing.
  - **Agency as a Product:** A system of AI agents and human experts generates content at scale.
      - **Example:** For Webflow, produces 30–50 integration docs weekly.
  - **Rapid Growth:** $12–13M ARR in \~1 year; team is 80–90 people and client-constrained (next availability is late January).
  - **Key Needs:**
      - **AEO Playbook:** Define a standard, repeatable process for AI search optimization.
      - **Account Directors:** Strategic advisors for clients (VPs/CMOs) on growth.

### Reuben's AEO/GEO Approach

  - **Background:** 8 years in SEO (edutech, agency, FreshBooks, MaintainX) with a Computer Science degree.
  - **Strategy:** Prioritizes bottom-of-funnel content to reduce CAC.
      - **"Deal-breaker questions":** Yes/no questions that are critical for a prospect's evaluation (e.g., specific integrations).
      - **Acquisition content:** Listicles and comparison pages.
  - **Attribution & Measurement:**
      - **Primary:** UTM-tagged referral traffic from LLMs (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
      - **Secondary:** "How did you hear about us?" form and sales call analysis.
      - **Rationale:** Believes current tools (Profound, Scrunch) are directional, as their API limits (e.g., 1 SERP fanout) don't reflect real-world user behavior.

### Potential Roles & Fit

  - **AEO Playbook Lead:**
      - **Focus:** Internal strategy to standardize the AEO process across all client accounts.
      - **Rationale:** Ensures consistency and prevents issues that arise from a non-standardized approach.
  - **Account Director:**
      - **Focus:** Client-facing strategic advisor for marketing VPs/CMOs.
      - **Rationale:** Fills a key need for senior strategic leadership on client accounts.
  - **Reuben's Preference:** Open to both, but wants to remain hands-on with SEO/AEO strategy, not solely focused on client experience.
  - **Compensation:** Stated range of $155k–$160k CAD.
