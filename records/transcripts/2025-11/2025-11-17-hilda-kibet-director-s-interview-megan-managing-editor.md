## Key Topics

### Candidate Experience & Process Management

  - **Context:** Managed high-volume, 24/48-hour content delivery at Real and Limited Agency.
  - **Process:** Proactively batched client orders to create a buffer, ensuring content was with editors by the 24-hour mark.
  - **Onboarding:** Used initial client feedback to refine briefs, aiming for \~100% quality from the first draft to improve efficiency.
  - **Tooling:**
      - **Notion:** Built templates and documentation to standardize processes for a remote team.
      - **Asana:** Created automations (e.g., writer → editor handoff) to streamline workflows.
      - **SEMrush:** Used for SEO scoring, which was delivered to clients with the final content.

### Quality Control & Strategic Alignment

  - **Problem:** New writers produced technically sound content that lacked strategic alignment with client business goals (e.g., engagement, ranking).
  - **Solution:**
    1.  **Demonstrate:** Reworked a sample piece to show the "before and after" of strategic alignment.
    2.  **Standardize:** Added a mandatory "business goal" section to all content briefs.
    3.  **Train:** Used the reworked piece as a training example for the team.
  - **Origin:** This focus on strategic alignment was a direct result of critical feedback from a manager earlier in Kibet's career.

### GrowthX's Vision & Role Expectations

  - **Role Evolution:** The Managing Editor role now includes end-to-end production, from AI generation to final editing.
      - **Rationale:** Requires highly capable editors who need minimal oversight.
  - **Company Transition:** Shifting from a services-first agency to a tech-first platform.
      - **Tech Development:** Building in-house AI workflows and a new analytics platform.
      - **Product Vision:** Eventually offer two models: a self-serve platform and a hands-on service.
  - **Required Mindset:** A "tinkering mindset" is essential for providing feedback to the engineering team and iterating on AI prompts to improve tool output.

### Measuring Success

  - **Primary Metric:** Client renewals.
  - **Client-Facing Metrics:**
      - SERP rankings, clicks, and impressions.
      - LLM referrals (e.g., surfacing in Claude, ChatGPT).
  - **Internal Goal:** Optimize production volume (e.g., 5-20 posts/week) to balance client needs with maintaining high quality.
