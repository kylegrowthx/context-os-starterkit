## Key Topics

### The New Engagement Manager Role

  - **Context:** A new org structure redefines the role from "Account Strategist" to "Engagement Manager."
  - **Primary Function:** Own client relationships and renewals.
      - **Goal:** Be a trusted expert for client decision-makers.
      - **Focus:** Account health, project management, and coordinating internal teams.
  - **Account Load:** Scaling from 8 accounts to 12 by Q1 2026.
  - **Support Structure:**
      - **Pooled Directors:** Strategic experts (e.g., SEO audits, AI strategy) engaged by EMs for performance turnarounds.
      - **Delivery Team:** Manages all client deliverables.

### Candidate Experience & Approach

  - **Background:** 7+ years in account management, primarily with mid-size and enterprise clients.
  - **Renewal Strategy:** Uses data-backed QBRs to proactively manage client health and secure renewals.
  - **Communication Cadence:**
      - **Internal:** Weekly pod meetings (2 days before client calls) for alignment; weekly leadership syncs for full account downloads.
      - **External:** Weekly/bi-weekly client meetings; agendas sent 24 hours prior.
  - **SEO & Tech Skills:**
      - **SEO:** Experience with topic clusters, schema, and meta descriptions.
      - **Platforms:** Familiar with Looker, GA4, GSC, SEMrush, Screaming Frog, and Microsoft Clarity.

### Case Study: Watts Water Account Turnaround

  - **Problem:** A high-risk account facing churn.
  - **Solution:** Gained access to the client's Power BI data to prove ROI and identify strategic gaps.
  - **Outcome:** Expanded services by $10k/month by adding paid search and growing SEO content.
  - **Role:** Strategic lead and project manager; supported the SEO team on a 4,000-blog audit by creating AI-driven content clusters and alt image tags.

### Project Management & Quality Control

  - **Process:** Uses ClickUp (similar to Growthx's Linear) for project management.
    1.  **Briefing:** Creates a detailed brief with timelines and QA schedules.
    2.  **Team Alignment:** Confirms feasibility of dates with the team *before* setting client expectations.
    3.  **Handoff:** Pings the team on Slack to review the brief and ask questions.
  - **Quality Control (QC):**
      - **Internal Revisions:** Conducts internal reviews to ensure quality before client delivery.
      - **Scalable QA:** For high volume (e.g., 58 articles/week), would use a "biopsy" approach, scanning for known client pain points and operator-specific issues.

### Growthx's Expectations & Goals

  - **90-Day Success Metrics:**
      - **Month 1:** Own client accounts and strategy.
      - **Primary Goal:** Drive renewals and proactively flag accounts needing strategic intervention.
      - **Platform Curiosity:** Interest in AI workflows is key, as the company is evolving into a product-and-services model.
  - **Ideal EM Profile:** A strategic partner for client decision-makers, focused on KPIs and metrics that matter, not a content editor.
  - **2026 Department Goals:**
      - Scale account load from 8 to 12 per EM.
      - Improve production team efficiency through EM organization and prioritization.
      - Document and share effective EM processes department-wide.
