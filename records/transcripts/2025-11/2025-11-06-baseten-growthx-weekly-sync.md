## Summary

Baseten is ending its partnership with GrowthX, concluding that GrowthX's AI-driven content generation model cannot meet the technical depth required for Baseten's expert audience. Despite GrowthX's rigorous multi-LLM agentic pipeline (including brief generation, deep research, persona validation, and fact-checking), the inherent unpredictability of LLM training data limits output quality for highly technical segments. GrowthX will deliver three final article outlines and a complete export of the Content OS (all ideas, outlines, and research), while Baseten regroups internally to define a new content strategy addressing the core issue of low search indexability.

---

## Context

Baseten is an AI infrastructure platform that helps teams deploy and manage machine learning models. GrowthX began a content marketing partnership with Baseten to create technical content targeted at developers and ML engineers. However, Baseten has determined that GrowthX's AI-driven content generation model—which prioritizes search volume and SEO over technical depth—is not a good fit for their expert audience. The meeting was called to discuss the amicable conclusion of the partnership and the handoff of all project artifacts for Baseten's internal use.

## Key Topics

### Partnership Conclusion

  - Baseten is ending the partnership, as GrowthX's AI-driven model is a poor fit for the required content quality.
  - **Rationale:** The model's focus on search volume over technical depth fails to meet the high standards of Baseten's expert audience.
  - **Context:** This decision is not a reflection on the GrowthX team, but a mismatch in methodologies.

### GrowthX's AI Pipeline

  - Andi demonstrated GrowthX's agentic pipeline to show the rigor applied, despite the output mismatch.
  - **Process:** The pipeline uses multiple LLMs (e.g., Claude, Perplexity) and includes steps for:
      - Brief generation
      - Deep research
      - Persona & guideline validation
      - Fact-checking
  - **Constraint:** Even with this process, the final output quality is limited by the inherent unpredictability of LLM training data.

### Project Handoff & Future Strategy

  - Baseten will use all project artifacts to inform its new content strategy.
  - **Deliverables:**
      - Three final article outlines
      - Export of the "Content OS" (all ideas, outlines, research)
  - **Baseten's Path Forward:**
      - The core problem of low search indexability remains.
      - Marylise will meet with Mike to define a new strategy.
      - **Challenge:** The current team is at capacity, making internal content creation difficult.
  - **Future Collaboration:** Andi suggested a potential future partnership (e.g., 2026) if GrowthX offers clients direct access to its backend platform, enabling Baseten to apply its own expertise to guide the AI.
