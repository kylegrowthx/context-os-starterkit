## Key Topics

### Content Review Process

  - Five articles are ready for Maggie's review, updated to streamline the process.
      - **Updates:** Clinical framework, MD tags, and link checking.
      - **Goal:** Reduce Maggie's review time by ensuring links are correct.
  - Brian will send the list to Maggie for a spot-check to validate the new process.

### Long-Tail SEO Strategy

  - **Goal:** Drive traffic by answering common user questions via traditional SEO and LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).
  - **LLM Content Structure:**
      - **Proximity:** Place questions and answers within 500–800 words of each other.
      - **Format:** Use FAQs and TLDRs for easy LLM parsing.
      - **Outcome:** LLMs synthesize answers from Biologica's content and provide a source link, driving traffic.

### Regulatory Risk Mitigation

  - **Challenge:** Ensure all on-site content is DSHEA compliant.
      - **Rule:** Content must focus on structure/function claims, not disease claims.
      - **Exception:** Disease topics are allowed with a medical disclaimer stating the supplement is not intended to diagnose, treat, or cure.
  - **Solution:** Create a separate content category for purely informational topics.
      - **Content:** Focus on general health information (e.g., "What is perimenopause?").
      - **Product Mention:** No direct mention of Biologica's products or their use for specific conditions.
      - **Rationale:** This structure isolates the content from DSHEA risk, requiring only medical accuracy review, not regulatory review.

### Implementation & Logistics

  - **Topic Filtering:** GrowthX will filter the initial topic list to remove any that could imply product claims (e.g., "perimenopause supplements").
  - **Site Structure:** Brian will work with developers to decide where the new content will live.
      - **Option 1:** Integrate into the main blog as a new category.
      - **Option 2:** Create a separate content hub (e.g., `/blog/info/`).
  - **Blog Images:** Images will be updated by Friday to remove overlay text and use tighter crops.
