## Key Topics

### The Engagement Manager Role at GrowthX

  - **Function:** Pure account management; no editorial work.
  - **Core Responsibilities:**
      - Own client relationships, renewals, and account health.
      - Track performance against goals and guide strategy.
      - Coordinate with internal teams (Managing Editors, Directors).
  - **Team Structure:**
      - **Engagement Manager (EM):** Manages 3 Managing Editors (MEs).
      - **Managing Editor (ME):** Manages 2–3 accounts.
      - **Directors:** A pooled resource for strategic support (e.g., SEO analysis), engaged via a ticket system.
  - **Account Load:**
      - **Current:** 8 accounts.
      - **Projected:** Scale to 12 accounts by Q2 2026.
  - **Daily Cadence:**
      - Weekly/bi-weekly client calls.
      - Daily internal coordination (Slack, Monday.com).
      - Proactive performance monitoring; escalate issues to Directors as needed.

### Candidate Background & Experience

  - **Current Role:** Account Director at Monks (8 years).
  - **Client Experience:** Spotify, Uber, Sanofi, Warner Bros. Discovery, Marriott.
  - **Primary Focus:** Social media content (organic, campaigns, influencer).
  - **Warner Bros. Account Growth:**
      - Scaled the team from 23 to 40 people.
      - Grew the account from 9 to 12 brands across Latam.
      - Secured an 18-month contract renewal by identifying and addressing client needs.
  - **Spotify Campaign Example:**
      - Led a large-scale, cross-functional campaign (Strategy, Creative, Data, UX, Dev).
      - Built an in-app experience that scaled from Latam to the US, Spain, and Asia.

### Process & Problem-Solving

  - **Work Structure:**
      - Weekly client touchpoints.
      - Daily internal status reviews with Project Managers.
      - Proactive reminders (Slack) and task management (Monday.com).
  - **Problem-Solving Example (Spotify Campaign):**
      - **Problem:** Missed deadlines due to client delays in providing information and approvals.
      - **Action:** Proactively flagged risks in writing. When delays persisted, increased status calls from once to twice weekly to accelerate approvals.
      - **Result:** The campaign launched successfully on time.
