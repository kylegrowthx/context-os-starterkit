## Key Topics

### The GrowthX Account Strategist Role

  - **Full Ownership:** The role requires one person to own the entire client relationship, including renewals, upsells, and account health.
  - **Blended Expertise:** It combines deep SEO/content credibility with strategic account management.
  - **Team Model:** Works with an assigned editor (using AI tools like Surfer SEO) and can escalate complex issues to a "pooled" team of Director-level growth leaders.
  - **Success Metrics:** Measured by an evolving account health score with five dimensions:
    1.  Relationship strength
    2.  Contractual delivery
    3.  Content quality
    4.  Results (e.g., traffic)
    5.  ROI (e.g., leads, revenue)

### Dee Salvador's Experience & Fit

  - **Current Role (Kuno Creative):** SEO Program Manager.
      - **Focus:** SEO strategy, execution, and reporting for \~15 accounts.
      - **Structure:** A Marketing Strategist serves as the primary AM; Dee is a product specialist.
      - **Process:** Dee provides keyword research and content outlines; writers use Surfer SEO for drafts, which Dee then reviews for SEO quality.
  - **Previous Experience (Logical Position):** Held a full AM role, owning renewals and managing 50-60 smaller accounts.
  - **Career Motivation:** Left Grow Together to work on higher-end accounts with more growth opportunities.

### GrowthX's Evolution & Challenges

  - **Platform Strategy:** Building a tech platform to provide clients more control and create switching costs.
  - **Past Churn Drivers:**
      - **Bad Fit:** Clients lacking enthusiasm or trust in the process.
      - **Technical Complexity:** Clients with highly technical products requiring CTO-level review, creating bottlenecks.
      - **Organizational Complexity:** Slow internal approval processes delaying content publication and results.
      - **Strategic Misalignment:** Failing to connect SEO work to the client's broader business goals (e.g., leads vs. traffic).
