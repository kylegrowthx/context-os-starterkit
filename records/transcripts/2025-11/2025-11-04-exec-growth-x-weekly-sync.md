## Context

Exec is a B2B platform for AI-powered sales role-play and training, and GrowthX is the agency driving its content marketing and SEO strategy. The engagement, which began in February 2025, aims to increase Exec's visibility in both Google search and LLM (ChatGPT, Claude) results for keywords like "AI roleplay software" and "sales training platform." Early success with comparison articles (Exec vs. Hyperbound, Exec vs. MindTickle, etc.) demonstrated that high-intent, head-to-head content drives qualified leads immediately. However, Exec ranks below competitors in LLM results for broader platform keywords, likely because LLMs cite competitors' dedicated solutions pages rather than Exec's blog content. This meeting focused on approving a strategic pivot—building dedicated solutions pages optimized for commercial keywords and accelerating the backlink strategy to boost domain authority.
