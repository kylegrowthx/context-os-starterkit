## Summary

GrowthX and Enable aligned on a blog content partnership where GrowthX will produce approximately 5 SEO-driven blog posts per week during an 8-week calibration sprint, focusing on top and mid-funnel topics with high search intent (e.g., "ransomware recovery playbook"). The partnership will expand post-sprint to gated assets like whitepapers and guides to fill mid-funnel content gaps. All content requires Enable's technical review, brand alignment oversight, and legal/compliance clearance, particularly for security and threat-related topics.

## Context

Enable is a managed detection and response (MDR) and cybersecurity firm building out internal publishing capabilities. They operate with dedicated teams for corporate comms (Kim Cecchini), campaigns (Kerri Gordon), brand and creative (Jeff Baxter), content operations (Pete Roythorne), and social media (Kieran Brannan). Their brand identity is currently evolving, requiring careful coordination on messaging and style. GrowthX was engaged to help Enable become a publisher, producing SEO-optimized content that drives organic search visibility and helps Enable appear in AI-generated answers across generative search engines. This kickoff meeting established the operational workflow, oversight requirements, and collaboration model for the initial 8-week sprint.

## Key Topics

### Partnership Scope & Goals

  - **GrowthX's Role:** Produce \~5 blog posts/week after an 8-week calibration sprint.
  - **Primary Goal:** Drive SEO and Generative Engine Optimization (GEO/AEO) by capturing search intent for top/mid-funnel topics (e.g., "ransomware recovery playbook").
  - **Future Scope:** Post-sprint, the partnership will expand to gated assets (guides, white papers).
      - **Rationale:** Fill mid-funnel content gaps and support specific campaigns.
      - **Process:** Requires closer collaboration with Enable's campaigns and creative teams.

### Content Strategy & Planning

  - **Topic Selection:**
      - **GrowthX:** Keyword research to capture audience intent.
      - **Enable:** Strategic planning based on campaigns, thought leadership, and events.
  - **Alignment:** Post-sprint, the teams will align on a shared content calendar to ensure SEO-driven content supports Enable's strategic goals.
  - **Repurposing:** GrowthX will refresh old content but will not optimize Enable's in-progress pieces, which will be handled internally.

### Review & Approval Process

  - **Workflow:** GrowthX → Drafts via email → Pete Roythorne (Enable) → Review & Feedback → Final version → Pete publishes.
  - **Technical Review:** A broader team review for technical accuracy is required, especially during calibration.
  - **Legal/Compliance Review:** Required for claims, security trends, or compliance topics.
  - **Bylines:** Specific bylines are preferred over a generic "Enable" banner to avoid generic content.
  - **Threat Intel Content:**
      - **Context:** Enable's new Threat and Tell team will produce rapid-response content on current threats.
      - **Oversight:** GrowthX content on security trends requires extra oversight to align with Enable's messaging and avoid sensitive topics (e.g., nation-state actors).

### Brand & Creative Alignment

  - **Writing Guidelines:** GrowthX will use Enable's AI style guides, which are the most comprehensive resource.
  - **Brand Evolution:** Enable's brand identity is evolving, requiring close alignment.
  - **Creative Assets:**
      - **Blog Headers:** GrowthX will generate AI images based on existing blog styles.
      - **Gated Assets:** Requires hand-created designs and closer collaboration with Jeff Baxter.
  - **Action:** Jeff Baxter will provide a brand asset package to GrowthX.
