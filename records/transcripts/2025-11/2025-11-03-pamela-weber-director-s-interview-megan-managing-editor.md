## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Managing Competing Deadlines

  - **Context:** Manages deadlines for both internal SaaS projects (CSS) and external agency clients (Edgy Digital), including Toyota and Salesforce.
  - **Solution:** Proactive project scoping in ClickUp.
      - Creates a project card with a full brief and all necessary assets.
      - Assigns specific deadlines to each stakeholder (e.g., writer, designer).
      - This provides full workflow visibility, preventing surprises and enabling early intervention.
  - **Conflict Resolution:**
      - Negotiates priorities by asking which project best serves company goals.
      - Seeks win-win solutions (e.g., pushing a deadline from Thursday EOD to Friday noon).
      - Emphasizes collaboration over rigid adherence to individual deadlines.

### The Solution: Systemic Quality Control

  - **Process:** A built-in QA process prevents major errors.
      - Includes a deliberate review gap between final draft completion and final review.
      - This creates mental distance, allowing for a fresh perspective to catch overlooked issues.
  - **Attention to Detail:** Pamela's high standard for quality is constant.
      - Frequently spots minor errors on shared screens during calls and flags them for correction, even before content is published.

### The Mindset: Agile & Collaborative

  - **Feedback:** Views constructive criticism as a "great feedback loop" for professional growth.
  - **Example:** A content campaign's keyword strategy, partly generated by AI, was flagged by the CTO as not matching user intent.
      - **Action:** Pamela pivoted the strategy to new keywords, preventing wasted effort on an ineffective approach.

### The Fit: Pamela's Background & Role Alignment

  - **Experience:** Current role at CSS/Edgy Digital combines client management, content strategy, and AI marketing.
      - **AI:** Learned efficient AI use with InkFast and helped roll out the SmithOS agent.
  - **Role Alignment:** The position's requirements directly match Pamela's experience and interests.
      - **Editorial Judgment:** Shaping assignments based on strategy and pivoting as needed.
      - **Workflow Management:** Overseeing the full content production process.
      - **Human Oversight:** Providing the critical human insight needed to refine AI-generated content.
