## Context

GrowthX provides content marketing services to Smith.ai, a B2B communications software company specializing in AI-powered and human-staffed virtual receptionist services. Smith.ai has been in business for 10 years but recently launched an AI receptionist product, which led to an overly AI-focused positioning on their industry landing pages. Jo Kaminska has transitioned from a content contributor to Engagement Manager, becoming Smith.ai's primary point of contact for strategy and analytics. The conversation reflects Smith.ai's need to rebalance their go-to-market messaging after overcorrecting toward AI, and to align their content strategy with product capabilities that are increasingly hybrid (combining AI and human receptionists based on customer needs).
