## Context

SchoolAI is a GrowthX client focused on AI literacy and adoption in K-12 education. This weekly sync brings together the SchoolAI leadership team (Nikki Muncey, Nathan Buhler) and GrowthX content/SEO team (Jenn Peters, Megan Dickey) to review performance, align on editorial strategy, and address execution challenges. The meeting occurred at the start of November, following an exceptionally strong October where SchoolAI achieved record-breaking clicks (+10.7%) and impressions (+17%)—a significant result given industry-wide traffic headwinds from Google's AI Overviews. The team was reviewing what drove this success and planning future content pillars and marketing initiatives.

## Key Topics

### October Performance Review

  - **Record-Breaking Month:** October was the best-performing month ever, with clicks +10.7% and impressions +17% MoM.
      - This growth defies a broader industry trend of traffic loss since September, likely due to AI Overviews.
  - **Top Performers:**
      - **"AI for Math"** remains the \#1 traffic driver.
      - **"Peer and Self-Assessment"** (published Sep 24) is a breakout success, quickly generating high impressions.
  - **LLM Performance:**
      - **"AI for Math"** is also a top performer in LLM citations.
      - **"Best Free AI Tools"** is dropping in LLM performance, which is expected as it's a redirect and no longer meets user intent for top tool lists.
  - **"Formative Assessment" Niche:**
      - This content cluster dominates LLM citations, appearing in 50–100% of relevant AI Overviews.
      - **Why it works:** The strategy of targeting specific, long-tail queries is effective because competitors are not covering the topic in the same depth.

### Content Strategy & Execution

  - **New Content Pillars Approved:**
      - **Pillar 1: Subject-Specific AI**
          - **Goal:** Replicate the success of the "AI for Math" post.
          - **Examples:** "AI for Special Education," "AI Chemistry Help."
      - **Pillar 2: AI Literacy & Implementation**
          - **Goal:** Establish SchoolAI as a thought leader on responsible AI use.
          - **Examples:** "AI Literacy for Students," "AI Ethics in Education."
  - **Content Mix Shift:** The weekly content mix will change from 40% refreshes to 50%.
      - **Rationale:** Refreshes are showing strong results and are an efficient way to improve underperforming articles with high impressions but low clicks.
  - **Pending Approvals:**
      - **Platform Alignment Doc:** Awaiting review from Tucker.
      - **FAQ Doc:** Awaiting final review from Nikki.
      - **PSEO & Image Generation:** Awaiting decisions from Nikki.

### Reddit Engagement Strategy

  - **Problem:** SchoolAI has a minimal presence on Reddit, a key channel for teacher conversations.
  - **Impact:** This absence hinders brand awareness and limits citations in LLMs, which favor brands with strong online community engagement.
  - **Goal:** Increase SchoolAI's visibility and discussion on relevant subreddits (e.g., `r/edtech`, `r/instructionaldesign`).
  - **Potential Solutions:**
      - **Internal Resources:**
          - Ask the educator advocacy manager to engage the "Champions" program.
          - Use tools like **Gummy Search** to monitor conversations (GrowthX is exploring a partnership).
      - **External Resources:**
          - Evaluate a cold email offer for a Reddit monitoring service.
          - Consider Reddit ads.
