## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Refresh Strategy

  - Performance is improving, partly due to seasonality (entering high season).
  - **Focus:** Refreshing older content to boost LLM visibility and address traffic drops.
      - **"About" Pages:** Pages for PV and Cabo lost significant traffic (e.g., PV's "Time to Visit" page lost 6k views).
          - **Challenge:** Refreshing these pages risks keyword cannibalization with existing blog articles.
          - **Solution:** Use synonyms for section headers (e.g., "Cancun at Night" vs. "Cancun Nightlife") to differentiate content.
      - **Event Pages:** Require manual research for 2026 dates.
          - **Action:** Add the year to titles/meta titles (e.g., "Events in PV 2026") to improve search relevance.
          - **Process:** Update known events first, then research harder-to-find ones.

### Video Integration for SEO

  - New tour videos are embedded on tour pages (e.g., Extreme Adventure).
      - **Source:** Vimeo, chosen to avoid YouTube's post-video thumbnails.
  - **Opportunity 1: Embed in Articles**
      - **Action:** Mariana will embed Vimeo videos in relevant articles.
      - **Blocker:** Sanity may not accept raw embed codes.
      - **Workaround:** Use the existing embed code from tour pages.
  - **Opportunity 2: Leverage YouTube for Traffic**
      - **Action:** Upload videos to YouTube to capture new organic traffic from users researching destinations.
      - **Optimization:** Carlos suggested optimizing YouTube titles/descriptions with links to tour pages and articles.

### Contract Renewal & Performance Deck

  - **Goal:** Create a presentation for general management to support the contract renewal.
  - **Format:** A concise, graphic-rich PDF/Google Slides deck.
  - **Content:**
      - Key metrics: articles produced, keywords ranking, position improvement.

  - **Process:**
