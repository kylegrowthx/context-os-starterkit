## Context

ElevenLabs is an AI audio foundational model company (similar to Anthropic or OpenAI but focused on text-to-speech, voice cloning, dubbing, and sound effects) that went viral in 2023 with Peter Kokh's breakthrough TTS model. They now have three product lines: a creative platform for content creators, APIs for companies like VAPI and Chess.com, and an enterprise voice agents platform. Sam Sklar is driving their content strategy and reached out to GrowthX after a colleague referral mentioning GrowthX's work with similar companies (Lovable, Ramp, and VAPI). Despite having deep internal insights about voice agents, ElevenLabs lacks the bandwidth to produce high-quality thought leadership at scale and currently uses simple questionnaires + ChatGPT for case studies. This is an early exploratory call to assess whether GrowthX's services (known for training LLM systems on company context) could support both custom thought leadership and case study automation.

## Key Topics

### ElevenLabs Context & Needs

  - **Company:** AI audio foundational model company (text-to-speech, voice cloning, etc.) with \~$200M ARR and 300 employees.
  - **Products:**
      - **Creative Platform:** B2C/B2B tool for content creators.
      - **APIs:** Models for other companies to embed (e.g., VAPI, Chess.com).
      - **Agents Platform:** Enterprise voice agents for customer support, lead gen, and L\&D.
  - **Primary Need: Thought Leadership**
      - **Goal:** Establish ElevenLabs as the definitive expert in the voice agent space.
      - **Challenge:** Lacks the internal bandwidth to produce high-quality, shareable content despite having the insights.
      - **Example:** A whitepaper on "The state of voice agents for the telecommunications industry" that is so good it gets shared internally.
  - **Secondary Need: Case Studies**
      - **Challenge:** High demand from SMB/mid-market customers (20+/week) for case studies on their use cases.
      - **Current Process:** Inefficient; uses a simple questionnaire and ChatGPT, yielding inconsistent quality.
      - **Goal:** Automate production to provide sales with a long-tail library of use cases across industries and regions.

### GrowthX Proposed Solution

  - **Standard Model:** An 8-week strategy sprint to build a knowledge base ("artifacts"), followed by 12 months of daily, agentic content production (SEO, programmatic).
  - **Proposed Hybrid Engagement:**
      - **Custom Thought Leadership:** A custom workflow for the high-quality whitepapers.
      - **Standard SEO Content:** Run in parallel to build foundational knowledge and train the system, which directly benefits the custom thought leadership work.
