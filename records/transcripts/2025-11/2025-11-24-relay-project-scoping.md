## Summary

Relay and GrowthX scoped a major new interactive content hub targeting the home services vertical (plumbers, roofers, contractors, etc.), designed to capture high-intent AI-referred traffic (AEO/SEO) and convert it to qualified leads. ## Overview

- **Project Goal:** Build a dynamic content hub for the home services vertical, moving beyond static articles to capture high-intent AEO/SEO traffic and convert it to leads.
- **Strategic Decision:** Integrate the hub into the existing Contentful blog via a subfolder. This avoids SEO issues with a new subdomain and aligns with a Q2 brand campaign targeting the same audience.
- **Key Features:** The hub will feature interactive elements (quizzes, calculators) and multimedia (podcasts, custom research) to create a "delightful" experience.
- **Partnership Scope:** Relay needs a partner for end-to-end delivery (strategy, design, dev, motion graphics) to supplement its small internal team and meet an ambitious Q1 launch target.

## Key Topics

### Project Goal & Strategy

  - **Objective:** Create a niche content hub for the home services vertical (e.g., plumbers, roofers) to capture high-intent AEO/SEO traffic.
  - **Rationale:** Capitalize on the high conversion rates of AI-referred traffic by providing an engaging destination that is more effective than a standard blog post.
  - **V1 Scope:** Focus on qualified lead capture. The nurture funnel can be built later.

### Technical & Platform Approach

  - **Platform:** Integrate the hub into the existing Contentful blog using a subfolder.
  - **Rationale:** This approach is preferred over a new subdomain to avoid SEO penalties and keep the content within the core Relay web ecosystem.
  - **Development:** Relay's two in-house devs prefer custom builds over third-party tools for interactive elements.

### Content & Features

  - **Content Focus:** Deep-dive into specific home services niches, moving beyond general topics.
  - **Interactive Elements:**
      - **Quizzes:** To increase engagement.
      - **Calculators:** To provide value.
          - **Constraint:** Must use "imaginary numbers" due to compliance rules against offering fake predictions with real data.
  - **Multimedia:**
      - **Original Research:** A smaller, niche survey for home services to supplement the annual "Cash Flow Compass" report.
      - **Podcast Integration:** Feature relevant episodes, like the one with Brian Scudamore.
  - **Visuals:** Primary Relay branding, not a sub-brand. The project may include a blog facelift to improve the overall brand experience.

### Partnership & Timeline

  - **Relay Team:** Bethany Cantor, Dave White, Carolyn (Lead Brand Designer), Katie, and a new content marketing hire. Nick (VP of Marketing) and Finance will be involved for approvals.
  - **Partnership Rationale:** Relay needs a partner for end-to-end delivery (strategy, design, dev, motion graphics) to supplement its small internal team and meet an ambitious timeline.
  - **Timeline:**
      - **Proposal:** Required within the next month for 2025 budget planning.
      - **Launch Target:** End of Q1 2025.
      - **Rationale:** Align the hub's launch with a major Q2 brand campaign targeting the home services audience.
