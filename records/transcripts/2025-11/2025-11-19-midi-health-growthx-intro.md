## Overview

- **Midi's Challenge:** Scale content production while maintaining the scientific accuracy required for its sensitive medical niche (perimenopause/menopause), a key concern with AI tools.
- **GrowthX's Solution:** A hybrid model using AI for speed and human experts for quality. It starts with an 8-week strategy sprint (5–10 articles) to prove value before a 12-month commitment.
- **Core Offer:** \~20 high-quality editorial articles/month. This is a deliberate choice over high-volume AI output, as GrowthX believes quality is more effective for SEO and LLM visibility.
- **Custom Work:** Additional workstreams (e.g., calculators, programmatic content) are scoped separately, adding $9–15k/month to the base cost.

## Key Topics

### Midi Health's Content Challenge

  - **Goal:** Establish Midi as the authoritative leader in midlife women's health (40+).
  - **Current Process:** A rigorous, manual workflow ensures accuracy:
      - SEO analyst identifies keywords.
      - Health writers create content.
      - Midi clinicians provide expert input.
      - Each piece receives two medical reviews.
  - **Performance:** This process has successfully built domain authority and driven rapid SERP growth.
  - **Exploration:** Midi is evaluating AI tools (e.g., SEMrush, Profound) but is cautious about scaling with AI due to the critical need for scientific accuracy.

### GrowthX's Hybrid Content Engine

  - **Origin:** Founded by Marcel, who built successful organic content engines at IBM, HP, and Deepgram, where he began automating parts of the process with AI.
  - **Model:** Blends AI automation with human expertise.
      - **AI:** Accelerates research, brief generation, and drafting.
      - **Human:** Provides strategic direction, expert review, and final editing.
  - **Team:** Includes former TechCrunch EIC and professionals with backgrounds in SEO, content marketing, and journalism.
  - **Growth:** Founded \~1 year ago, now with \>60 clients and $15M ARR.

### Partnership Structure & Pricing

  - **Phase 1: 8-Week Strategy Sprint**
      - **Purpose:** Prove value and build a custom content engine before a long-term commitment.
      - **Deliverables:** 5–10 published articles and a 3–6 month content roadmap.
  - **Phase 2: 12-Month Contract**
      - **Commitment:** Requires a 12-month agreement.
      - **Volume:** \~20 articles/month (a mix of net new and refreshes).
      - **Rationale:** Prioritizes quality over quantity, as GrowthX believes this is more effective for long-term SEO and LLM visibility.
  - **Pricing & Custom Workstreams**
      - **Base Price:** Covers either the editorial or programmatic workstream.
      - **Add-ons:** Additional workstreams (e.g., calculators, newsletters) are scoped separately, adding $9–15k/month depending on complexity.

### GrowthX's Workflow & Examples

  - **Workflow:** Uses an internal platform to manage the content process.
      - **Inputs:** Managing editor instructions, brand artifacts (personas, style guides), and a research knowledge base.
      - **Process:** AI agents generate research plans, execute research, and draft content.
      - **Quality Control:** Human editors review all drafts, making inline changes and using context-aware tools for suggestions.
  - **Examples:**
      - **Biologica:** A similar health client for whom GrowthX built a custom workflow to handle medical claims and compliance disclaimers, replicating their expert review process.
      - **Ramp:** Built a programmatic vendor directory (e.g., "Airtable vs. ClickUp") to establish authority in business spending.
      - **Webflow:** Created programmatic definition and integration pages.
