## Context

GrowthX is a one-year-old B2B marketing agency that delivers high-volume content services (primarily blog content for SEO) using proprietary AI workflows. The company operates in "dogfooding" mode — testing the AI content platform internally on client work before launching it as an external SaaS product. Currently, Andi Bailey leads customer operations, managing a 40–50-person delivery organization spread across teams of 4–5 people supporting 4–5 accounts each (1.2 people per account ratio). The company is scaling quickly (adding two clients per week) while simultaneously building the internal platform. Andi opened the search for a Client Operations Manager to move off the tactical, weeds-level work of capacity planning and process implementation. GrowthX has several operational pain points: fragmented tooling (Linear for project tracking, Notion for health scoring, Figma for planning), limited visibility into team capacity and client health, and no unified data flows — exactly the type of problem Leah has solved multiple times at previous companies.
