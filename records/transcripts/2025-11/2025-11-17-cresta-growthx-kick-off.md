## Summary

Cresta and GrowthX kicked off a content partnership focused on scaling Cresta's organic visibility through SEO and AI Engine Optimization (AEO). GrowthX will produce broadly discoverable content—competitive comparisons, glossaries, and educational resources—while Cresta's internal teams handle hyper-technical product content. The engagement targets Cresta's 8–20% of pipeline already flowing through ChatGPT and other AI channels; the team decided to implement an AI visibility tracking tool in week 1 and deliver writing guidelines, company context, and personas by end of week 1, followed by a full content strategy and topic clusters by week 2.

---

## Context

Cresta is a conversational AI platform helping enterprise contact centers improve revenue, cut costs, and differentiate customer experience through three core capabilities: conversation intelligence (extracting insights from customer conversations), agent assist (co-pilots for human agents), and conversational AI agents (full automation). The company is experiencing triple-digit annual growth and expanding from Fortune 500/100 enterprise customers into mid-market (250+ employees) and new geographies (APAC, EMEA). Russell Banzon leads sales, Devon Mychal oversees product marketing and content, Nicky Budd-Thanos heads content, Annie Andrews manages digital marketing and paid media, and Shreha Jain runs marketing operations and integration strategy. GrowthX is onboarding Cresta through a three-week discovery sprint (managed by Erik O'Brien and George Haikal) to understand their business, competitive positioning, and customer journey before building a scalable content and SEO strategy. The partnership is timely: Cresta's team lacks dedicated internal SEO/AEO expertise, previously worked with an underperforming SEO agency, and needs to automate content production to feed both traditional search and AI-powered discovery channels.

## Overview

- **Goal:** Build a scalable content engine for traditional SEO and AEO to support Cresta's triple-digit growth and market expansion.
- **Focus:** Content for broad discoverability (e.g., competitive comparisons, glossaries), not deep technical product content, which remains an internal function.

## Key Topics

### Why Now? The Need for Scalable Content

  - **Company Growth:** Cresta is growing triple-digits annually and expanding from large enterprise (Fortune 500/100) into mid-market (500+ employees) and new regions (APAC, EMEA).
  - **Channel Shift:** AEO (AI Engine Optimization) is a critical new channel, already driving 8–20% of pipeline from ChatGPT, but without a formal strategy or measurement.
  - **Team Bottleneck:** The small marketing team lacks the capacity and specialized expertise (SEO/AEO) to produce content at the required scale.

### Cresta's Value Proposition

  - **Business Outcomes:**
      - **Grow Revenue:** Improve conversion in sales and service-to-sales motions.
      - **Save Money:** Increase operational efficiency via agent assist and AI automation.
      - **Differentiate CX:** Enable personalized, proactive experiences and better VoC insights.
  - **Customer Perception (The "Why Cresta"):**
      - **Exceptional Partnership:** Deeply understands customer KPIs and provides forward-deployed PMs/engineers to ensure measurable ROI.
      - **End-to-End Platform:** A single partner for the entire AI CX journey, from agent augmentation to full automation.

### Customer Profile & Trigger Moments

  - **Ideal Customer Profile (ICP):**
      - **Verticals:** Financial Services, Healthcare (payer), Telco, Travel, Retail, Home Services.
      - **Size:** Historically 5k+ employees, now expanding to 250+ employees.
      - **Use Cases:** High-volume contact centers for sales, customer care, service-to-sale, churn retention, and collections.
  - **Trigger Moments:**
      - **"Do More with Less":** A primary driver, covering both cost-cutting and managing rapid growth without hiring.
      - **Executive Mandate:** CIOs/CEOs are tasked with implementing AI and seek proven solutions.
      - **Digital Transformation:** Leaders need to integrate customer data across digital channels.
      - **VoC Bottleneck:** Manual analysis of conversations is too slow to drive business decisions.

### Sales Objections & Competitive Landscape

  - **Primary Objections:**
      - **Price:** Cresta is more expensive than bundled, less-specialized solutions from incumbents (e.g., Genesys).
      - **"Build vs. Buy":** Prospects question the value of a vendor vs. building an AI agent in-house.
      - **Integrations:** Missing out-of-the-box support for platforms like Zendesk Chat, TalkDesk, and Nextiva.
  - **Competitive Landscape:**
      - **Incumbents:** Genesys, Nice, Variant, Nexidia offer bundled, less mature features.
      - **AI Agent Specialists:** Sierra, Decagon.
      - **Dev Tools:** Bland, Vapi (Cresta is not a self-service SDK).

### Content Strategy & Process

  - **Current Process:**
      - Content is driven by a 6–8 week product release schedule.
      - Hyper-technical content is written by Product/Engineering, with Content providing editorial polish.
      - Other content (webinars, e-books) is created by the Content Lead.
  - **GrowthX's Role:**
      - **Focus:** Broadly discoverable content for SEO/AEO.
      - **Examples:** Competitive comparison pages, sitemap glossaries, and general educational content.
      - **Rationale:** This avoids the deep product nuance that slows down external teams and leverages Cresta's internal expertise for technical content.
