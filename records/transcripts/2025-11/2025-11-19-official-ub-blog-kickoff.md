## Summary

Udemy, working with GrowthX, kicked off the Official UB Blog project to establish a content hub for business topics, with an MVP launch targeted for Q4 focusing on core blog functionality and article content, deferring the complex mixed-content card layout to V2 (Jan/Feb). The team aligned on a two-card-type strategy (free blog articles vs. gated resources from the Resource Center), with article card descriptions auto-populating from a mandatory WordPress "content summary" field to improve LLM/AI visibility. Nicole and Stef will decide the blog name asynchronously, Justin will create an Asana project to track tasks and launch a weekly working session in two weeks, Kristina will deliver homepage and category page mockups within two weeks, and Kevin will begin staging the core blog build immediately.

---

## Context

Udemy is launching a new Official UB (University Business) blog, a strategic content hub to improve AI visibility and support lead generation through gated resources. The project is led by Justin Luebke (Udemy) with design support from Kristina Vinogradova and development from Kevin Kurtz. GrowthX (represented by Kyle Gaudreau) provided the initial scope and wireframe to establish the blog architecture. The initiative is part of a broader effort to shift content strategy toward specialized verticals and differentiate free blog articles from premium gated resources, aligning with Udemy's goal to drive qualified leads while maintaining SEO value through content summaries that improve LLM/AI visibility.

## Key Topics

### Scope & Wireframe Review

  - Justin reviewed the scope and wireframe from GrowthX.
  - **Key Features:**
      - Homepage with a featured article and topic categories.
      - Category pages filtering articles by topic.
      - Article pages using the existing sticky Table of Contents (ToC) template, plus new elements:
          - Author box
          - Related resources section
          - Customizable CTA cards
          - Breadcrumbs

### Content Card Strategy

  - **Problem:** The wireframe's card descriptions are missing from current articles.
  - **Solution:** Auto-populate descriptions from a new "content summary" field in WordPress.
      - **Rationale:** This field is already being added to new content and is valuable for LLM/AI visibility.
      - **Implementation:** The field will become mandatory for new content to ensure all cards have a description.
  - **Card Design:**
      - Two distinct card types will be used:
        1.  **Blog Article Cards:** For free, fully accessible content.
        2.  **Resource Cards:** For gated content from the Resource Center.
      - **Goal:** Make resource cards visually distinct to drive lead generation.

### Timelines & MVP Strategy

  - **Challenge:** A Q4 launch is aggressive and depends on rapid design and development.
  - **Solution:** Launch an MVP to de-risk the timeline.
      - **V1 (Q4 MVP):** Core blog functionality only.
      - **V2 (Jan/Feb):** Add the complex mixed-content card layout.
  - **Development:** Kevin's team can start building the existing article template asynchronously while designs are finalized.

### Blog Naming & Copy

  - **Naming Process:** Nicole and Stef will decide the blog name asynchronously.
      - **Rationale:** This streamlines decision-making to accelerate the project.
      - **Consideration:** The name should be ownable and work for future localization.
  - **Copy Needs:**
      - Blog name
      - SEO blurbs for category pages
      - "Content summary" for all articles
  - **Workflow:** Justin will draft copy for Nicole's review.
