## Summary

GrowthX and Aembit reviewed content production progress and organic traffic metrics, with overall blog traffic growing steadily (users: 2.54k to 3.07k; engagement time: 43s to 53s) while Aembit-specific articles remained flat. The team identified SEMrush keyword tracking as a critical gap for measuring SEO progress beyond GA4 and agreed to define a priority keyword list for setup. GrowthX also announced a role realignment where Ehtisham will focus exclusively on content production and publishing, with a new customer-facing team member (Vivek) joining future meetings to handle reporting and analysis.
