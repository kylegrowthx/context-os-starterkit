## Key Topics

### BCV's Content Challenges

  - **Firm-level:** Difficulty getting partners to create content and achieving readership in a competitive VC landscape.
  - **Portfolio-level:** High demand for expert resources on GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) and AIEO (AI-Enhanced Engine Optimization).
  - **Strategic:** Deciding how to allocate finite resources (time, budget) between written content and video for maximum impact.

### GrowthX's Solution: Platform + Talent

  - **Founder's Background:** Marcel Santilli's career is built on scaling content as a media company, not just marketing.
      - **HP:** Grew a contributor network to 1M+ visitors/mo with $0 ad spend, generating \>50% of software leads.
      - **Service Titan:** Built "Toolbox" (podcast, tools) to serve contractors, now a majority of their pipeline.
      - **DeepGram:** Used AI workflows to 24x traffic in 3 months.
  - **Company Growth:**
      - **Revenue:** Nearing $50M ARR (cash-flow positive for 2 months).
      - **Team:** \~90 people, including 55 in delivery (editors, SEO, engineers).
  - **Core Philosophy:** Content is the "atomic unit" of all marketing.

### The GrowthX Process: Strategy First

  - **8-Week Strategy Sprint (\~$30k):**
      - **Purpose:** De-risk engagement by defining strategy, calibrating quality, and setting up the workspace.
      - **Outcome:** A complete content strategy and artifacts, valuable even if the client doesn't continue.
      - **Continuation Rate:** \~80–85% of clients proceed to recurring workstreams.
  - **"Context Engineering":**
      - **Process:** Deep research and weekly team syncs to understand the client's business, audience, and goals.
      - **Rationale:** This deep context is the foundation for effective AI agents; it's about providing the right "big picture," not just prompt engineering.
  - **Case Study: Lovable**
      - **Problem:** Needed to scale content for broad reach, not just incremental clicks.
      - **Solution:** Built a high-volume, high-intent "guides" section.
      - **Impact:** A technical audit during the sprint found and fixed thousands of malicious subdomain pages that risked a Google domain ban.

### Execution: AI Agents & Continuous Refresh

  - **Agentic Workflow Framework:**
      - **Research:** A robust researcher agent runs for \>30 minutes per piece, using high-compute ($5/piece) to filter out low-quality sources.
      - **Drafting:** A custom drafter agent uses "evals in the loop" to refine content based on guidelines and roundness.
      - **Editing:** A human editor is always in the loop, with the client's team involvement level being flexible.
  - **Continuous Asset Management:**
      - **Rationale:** Content is a decaying asset requiring constant updates to maintain "AI visibility" in search results.
      - **Cadence:** Daily publishing and refreshing (500–1k pages/week) across all clients.
      - **Examples:**
          - **Ramp:** Built a vendor catalog and editorial content.
          - **Augment Code:** Achieved \>20x AI visibility.
          - **Grammarly:** Performed surgical updates across all relevant pages after the Superhuman merger.

### Client Profile & Pricing

  - **Ideal Client Profile (ICP):**
      - **Established:** Must have an existing website and some domain authority to ensure faster signal generation.
      - **Strategic:** Views content as a long-term asset build, not a transactional ad spend.
      - **Clear:** Has a defined audience and internal decision-maker for quality and goals.
  - **Pricing Model:**
      - **Phase 1:** 8-week Strategy Sprint (\~$30k).
      - **Phase 2:** Recurring monthly retainers based on workstreams (e.g., editorial, templates).
