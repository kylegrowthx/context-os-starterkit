## Summary

Jason Gong interviewed Shweta, a long-time community member and tech writer with 15-20 years of experience, to understand her use cases and gather feedback on the AI-Led Growth community. Shweta's unique perspective spans B2B SaaS content writing (HR Cloud, Thomson Reuters, Forbes Advisor) and non-SaaS businesses—a resort and restaurant where she uses AI for planning, investor communications, and marketing strategy. The conversation revealed strong demand for build sessions, podcasts, text transcripts for faster review, a more intuitive prompt library, and opportunities for non-SaaS creators to share their AI use cases. Jason proposed a community feature showcasing Shweta's diverse AI applications and committed to creating build sessions on tool comparisons (Perplexity vs Claude vs ChatGPT) and Manus multi-agent workflows.

## Context

Shweta is a founding member of the AI-Led Growth community, having joined in its early stages when membership was under 70. She discovered the community through a LinkedIn post by Marcel about GrowthX's content strategy work and was drawn to how GrowthX's thought process aligned with her own approach to writing and AI integration. As a writer, content marketing consultant, and non-digital entrepreneur, Shweta represents a different audience profile than the typical B2B SaaS founders in the community. She's currently writing for HR Cloud (an AI-first company), previously worked with Thomson Reuters (strict no-AI policy) and Forbes Advisor, and co-runs a resort and restaurant business with her husband in a tier-three city in India where she uses AI to overcome local resource constraints. Her perspective is valuable to the community because she applies AI across diverse business models—not just SaaS.
