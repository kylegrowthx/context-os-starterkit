## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Approval

  - GrowthX presented the Content OS (Airtable) with topics from a keyword gap analysis.
      - **Goal:** Build topical authority (top-of-funnel) and capture bottom-of-funnel interest (e.g., listicles).

  - The initial goal is to approve 8–10 topics to create a buffer for the production cadence.
  - **Decision:** The first blog will be a refresh of the "Leveraging AI for Efficient PCI Assessments" post.
      - **Rationale:** Chosen as a low-risk, factual topic to calibrate on voice and tone before scaling.
      - **Keyword:** Optimize for "AI PCI compliance."
      - **Cluster:** Cybersecurity and Privacy.

### AI Visibility Tracking ("Scrunch")

  - GrowthX demoed its "Scrunch" tool, which tracks AI presence by sending 25 daily prompts to 7 platforms (e.g., Perplexity, Google).
      - **Fieldguide Presence:** \~10% (cited in \~10% of responses).

  - **Future:** The prompt list will expand to cover product deep-dive topics. The north star metric will be "LLM referral rate" in Google Analytics.
