      - Aida detailed the agentic AI pipeline to explain how content is produced and ensure quality.
      - **Process:**
        1.  **Context:** The LLM uses SpecterOps' company context, audience personas, and writing guidelines.
        2.  **Research:** Uses EXA (chosen over Perplexity for better quality in complex industries like cybersecurity).
        3.  **Self-Evaluation:** The AI grades its own research and re-researches if quality standards are not met.
        4.  **Drafting:** Generates an article from an outline, adding internal and external links.
        5.  **Validation:** The AI validates the draft against writing guidelines and proofreads for style/grammar.
        6.  **Fact-Checking:** Verifies all factual claims against the research document, flagging or removing unverified statements.
