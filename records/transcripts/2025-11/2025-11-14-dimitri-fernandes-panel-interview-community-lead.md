## Context

Dimitri Fernandes is a community strategy consultant with deep experience building professional communities, most notably at Endeavor Brazil where he established high-trust networks of influential entrepreneurs. He was brought in by Marcel and Jason to consult on GrowthX's newly-launched AI-led growth community, which launched officially during the week of this call (November 2025). The community achieved 170 paid members (at $300-500 per workshop) in its initial phase through three sequential workshops conducted by Marcel, with members actively sharing resources and learning in a Slack community. Dimitri was tasked with designing a scalable operating system that preserves what's working—high-quality content delivery and member quality—while adding structure for peer contribution and advocacy.

---

## Summary

Dimitri Fernandes presented a five-stage community operating system (Discover → Join → Engage → Contribute → Advocate) designed to scale GrowthX's AI-led growth community beyond its current 170 paid members. Key outcomes: consensus that vetted member quality matters more than volume; agreement to balance content-first positioning with peer connections and subgroup dynamics; and alignment on spending $1M over six months on platform infrastructure, high-quality content acquisition, and in-person brand events (South by Southwest, OpenAI summits). Marcel emphasized the long-term mission is positioning GrowthX as the trusted retooling resource for knowledge workers learning AI-native work principles, not merely a community platform.
