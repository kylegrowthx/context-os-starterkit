## Key Topics

### LandingAI's Context & Needs

  - **Company Pivot:** Shifted from computer vision to agentic document extraction, an API product with strong market reception.
  - **Target Audience:**
      - **Developers:** Enterprise users processing high document volumes.
      - **Business Decision-Makers:** Seeking process optimization and insights.
  - **Content Goal:** Build a growth plan for the new document extraction product, aligning with a website refresh in the next two months.

### GrowthX's Service & Model

  - **Core Offering:** An end-to-end content engine for SEO/AEO, focused on getting clients "in the answer" of AI search.
  - **Service Scope:**
      - **Included:** Editorial & programmatic content (strategy, creation, publishing, monitoring).
      - **Excluded (Initially):** Link building, social media, paid media, sales lead gen.
      - **Add-ons:** Custom workstreams (e.g., newsletters) are possible after 3–6 months for an additional $9k–$15k/mo.
  - **Pricing & Structure:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-week Strategy Sprint):** $30k. Delivers a technical SEO audit, content roadmap, and 5–10 articles to calibrate quality.
      - **Phase 2 (12-month Growth Execution):** $15k/mo. Delivers 5 net new or refreshed articles per week.
  - **Team & Reporting:**
      - **Team:** Engagement Manager (POC), Managing Editor, and Managing Directors (for strategic support).
      - **Reporting:** A Looker dashboard tracks sessions/conversions; Scrunch tracks AI search metrics (answers, citations, sentiment).

### Strategic Mismatch: Content Quality vs. Novelty

  - **LandingAI's Concern:** The need for novel, thought-leadership content to stand out in a competitive space (vs. Google, Amazon, Microsoft).
  - **GrowthX's Position:**
      - Initial focus is on creating the *best answer* to existing search demand, not necessarily novel content.
      - Novelty is a later-stage capability, requiring a deep knowledge base built over time.
      - Technical content (e.g., code snippets) is possible but requires a clear SEO opportunity first.
