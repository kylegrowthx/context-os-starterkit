## Key Topics

### Process & Workflow

  - **Review Bottleneck:** Udemy's internal review process is the main bottleneck.
      - **Cause:** Reviewers have full schedules, delaying feedback.
      - **Solution:** Adopt a "fix later" model to unblock publishing.
  - **Content Delivery:** Katya will deliver content by Wednesday instead of Thursday to provide more review time.
  - **Meeting Cadence:** The sync moves to Wednesdays at 9 AM PT. A bi-weekly cadence will be considered once the process is stable.

### Content Strategy & Quality

  - **Content OS Update:** A new column maps content to pillar pages, using the UB scorecard for alignment.
  - **Internal Linking:**
      - **Challenge:** Manually adding links to unpublished, related content is inefficient.
      - **Solution:** Katya will investigate a dev artifact to enable automated placeholder links.
      - **Guideline:** Prioritize linking to Udemy Business content over consumer blogs.
  - **Formatting:** Justin requested more H3s to improve readability and break up long sections.
  - **Content Duplication:** The team will avoid duplicating content.
      - **Example:** Udemy's "MCP article" was a one-off product launch piece.
      - **Guideline:** Growth X will avoid AI articles to prevent conflicts.

### Image & Visuals

  - **Goal:** Reduce Udemy's design team lift by having Growth X create on-brand images.
  - **Action:** Justin will provide Udemy's standard image overlays for Growth X to use.
  - **Future Initiative (2026):** Add in-text visuals (tables, infographics) to improve readability.
      - **Constraint:** Visuals must be localization-friendly (e.g., text-free) or easily translatable.

### GA4 Access

  - **Status:** The process is blocked by legal.
  - **Blocker:** Udemy requires a custom Data Processing Agreement (DPA), not Growth X's standard one.
  - **Next Step:** Udemy sent its DPA to Growth X for redlining.
