## Key Topics

### Invert's Marketing Challenge

  - **Product:** Bioprocess analytics platform (the "Tableau for bioprocess") that ingests, harmonizes, and analyzes biopharma data.
  - **Market:** Series A startup selling enterprise deals (1–2 year cycles), shortened to \~6 months via a 3-month proof of concept (POC).
  - **Problem:** No marketing engine exists.
      - Sales relies on personal networks; no inbound leads.
      - No content assets (e.g., webinars, white papers) for lead capture.
      - Blog is inactive (1 post/3 months).
      - Veronica French (Head of Marketing) is using AI tools (e.g., bear.ai) to hack together 2 posts/week to build a presence.
  - **Target Audience (ICP):**
      - **Executive:** Digital/innovation leaders driving transformation.
      - **Scientist:** End-users and managers (Heads of Process Development, Process Engineers, Data Scientists).
      - **IT:** Part of the buying circle but not the entry point.

### GrowthX's AI Content Engine

  - **Service:** End-to-end content creation and publishing, combining AI workflows with human experts.
  - **Methodology:**
      - **SEO:** Standard keyword-driven strategy.
      - **GEO:** Audience-first approach that answers all ICP questions to build authority and capture AI Answer traffic.
  - **Execution:**
      - **Team:** Dedicated Managing Director, Managing Editor, and Content Manager.
      - **Publishing:** Direct access to CMS (e.g., Webflow) to stage and publish content.
  - **Phased Rollout:**
      - **Phase 1:** Generate foundational artifacts and publish 5–10 initial articles.
      - **Phase 2:** Scale to 5 articles/week (new + refreshes).
  - **Capabilities:**
      - Beyond blogs → technical docs, compete pages, programmatic content (e.g., Ramp's vendor spend pages).
      - Includes image generation, tables, and infographics.
