  - Nate requested a walkthrough of the new Looker Studio dashboard to understand its metrics and attribute growth.
  - **Goal:** Quantify GrowthX's monthly impact and attribute specific growth to their work.

### Key Dashboard Metrics

  - **Non-branded Search:** Traffic from non-brand-specific Google queries.
      - **Significance:** The best indicator of GrowthX's direct impact, as it shows performance for queries not already targeting "Teero."
  - **LLM Traffic:** Traffic from large language models (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity).
      - **Significance:** Now accounts for \~50% of non-branded traffic, making it a critical metric.
  - **Engagement Rate:** Measures user activity beyond a page visit (e.g., clicking links).
      - **Significance:** A 53% engagement rate on LLM traffic is a strong signal of content quality.
  - **Sessions:** A user visit ending after 30 minutes of inactivity.
  - **Conversion Events:** Jessalynn noted a tracking error (29k "office open" events is too high) and will investigate.
