## Key Topics

### Spendesk's Content Bottleneck

  - **Primary Pain Point:** A slow design agency blocks rapid-fire creative needs for ads and campaigns.
  - **Content Production:**
      - **Current Output:** \~1 blog/week (2 at a push).
      - **Constraint:** The 3-person team is pulled into other tasks (translations, emails, landing pages), limiting time for blog creation.
  - **Current AI Tool (Dust):**
      - Effective for generating content from internal data (e.g., Notion).
      - Ineffective for net-new topics (e.g., e-invoicing), requiring heavy fact-checking and risking AI "hallucinations."
  - **Language Needs:** English, French, German, and Spain-Spanish.
      - **Process:** AI-translate English drafts → native team sense-check/localize.

### GrowthX's AI-Human Content Engine

  - **Model:** A managed service, not a self-serve platform.
  - **Core Workflow:**
      - **1. Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, $30k):**
          - Defines keywords, topic clusters, and competitor analysis.
          - Creates "artifacts" (e.g., detailed persona profiles) to provide context for AI workflows.
      - **2. Growth Execution (12 months, $15k/month):**
          - **Output:** 5 articles/week (editorial or programmatic).
          - **Process:** AI-driven research and drafting, with human editors for review, fact-checking, and final edits.
          - **Rationale:** This human-in-the-loop model prevents the "obviously AI-written" quality issues that Google's algorithm punishes.
  - **Client Examples:**
      - **Ramp:** Started with blog content, then expanded to programmatic assets like a vendor directory and comparison pages.
      - **Augment Code:** Achieved 3-5x organic traffic growth.

### Partnership Structure & Pricing

  - **Phased Approach:**
      - **Phase 1 (First \~6 months):** Focus on blog content to build domain expertise and SEO authority.
      - **Phase 2 (Post-6 months):** Expand to premium assets (e.g., ebooks, guides).
  - **Pricing:**
      - **Strategy Sprint:** $30,000
      - **Growth Execution:** $15,000/month for 5 articles/week.
