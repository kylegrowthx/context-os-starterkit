## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency working with Logic, a workflow automation platform. Logic's goal is to increase brand visibility in search results and large language models (LLMs) through strategic content embedding and optimization. This weekly sync between Bailey Seybolt (GrowthX) and Azzam Aijazi (Logic) covers content performance, publishing workflows, and LLM-focused strategies. The team is testing new approaches to content optimization including per-article FAQs, workflow embeds, and long-tail keyword research specifically targeting LLM citation patterns.
