## Key Topics

### SEO Audit Findings

  - An audit combining GSC and Ahrefs data identified three content issues causing traffic declines:
      - **1. Significant Traffic Loss:** Pages with old publish dates and major keyword/position drops.
          - **Action:** Full Refreshes (e.g., "partner page examples").
      - **2. Pages Just Outside Top 10:** Articles in positions 2–4 or 11–20.
          - **Action:** Minor Refreshes.
      - **3. Keyword Cannibalization:** Multiple articles competing for the same keywords (e.g., "ebook examples" vs. "ebook best practices").
          - **Action:** Merge into a single, comprehensive article.

### Proposed Refresh Strategy

  - **Minor Refreshes:** The primary tactic for quick wins.
      - **Process:** Reset publish date + add a TLDR/FAQ section.
      - **Rationale:** The new date signals freshness to Google, while new content provides LLM fodder.
      - **Efficiency:** An estimated 8 Minor Refreshes can be done for every 1 Full Refresh.
      - **Goal:** Drive a \~15% click increase, based on past client success.
  - **Prioritization:** The refresh list will be ordered by traffic/keyword volume to maximize impact.
  - **Content Relevance:** Jesse will flag irrelevant topics (e.g., "SEO internship") to de-prioritize them.

### LLM Traffic Analysis

  - **Finding:** GA4 data shows unusually high LLM referral traffic, with one article receiving 60% of its clicks from LLMs.
  - **Hypothesis:** External citations (e.g., partner directories) are now a critical SEO factor, as LLMs use them to source information.
  - **Action:** Jakub will analyze LLM traffic trends by content cluster to inform future strategy.
