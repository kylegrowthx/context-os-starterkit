## Summary

Will Proctor and Danni Roseman conducted user research with Gabrielle Sun, an instructional designer and aspirational founder from Portland who joined the AILA growth community after discovering it through a Luma event on AI search and content quality. Gabrielle highlighted critical feedback on the community platform: the Circle platform's 20+ navigation tabs create analysis paralysis and discourage engagement, she values direct access to Marcel and Jason's thinking above all else, and she identified two high-impact improvements—cohort-based Q&A sessions with founders of the community and a personalized onboarding flow based on user personas. The team also surfaced that a "celebrate" channel showing member wins could create positive peer pressure and reinforce community value.

---

## Context

Gabrielle Sun is an instructional designer at a Fortune 500 company in Portland, Oregon who specializes in training content and change management. She's building a side project and actively exploring the startup ecosystem with the goal of either joining a startup as a founding member within 1-2 years or launching her own venture. She discovered the AILA growth community after attending a Luma event where Marcel and Jason demonstrated practical AI prompts for founder research—specifically founder persona frameworks and company contact discovery. The prompts impressed her enough to join the community, and while she's consumed the initial Luma recording and the "Zero to 7 Million" video, her engagement has been minimal beyond browsing the general chat. This call was part of GrowthX's ongoing community development research to understand what keeps members engaged and what barriers prevent content consumption.

---

## Relevance

**To GrowthX Community Development:**
- **UX friction is a critical barrier to engagement.** The 20+ Circle navigation tabs create decision paralysis that prevents members from discovering relevant content, even aspirational founders with clear learning goals. Persona-based onboarding with a curated first 3-5 pieces of content is essential to improve time-to-value.
- **Cohort-based Q&A with Marcel and Jason is the highest-ROI engagement tactic.** Gabrielle ranked direct access to Marcel and Jason's thinking above networking or content, and referenced a comparable community where a founder offered monthly coaching Q&As—even charging for them—which proved highly sticky. A bi-weekly or monthly cohort-based format where members submit questions in advance could significantly improve retention.
- **A "celebrate" channel with member wins creates positive peer pressure.** Gabrielle observed this in other communities (e.g., celebrating course sales, milestone launches) and found it motivated continued engagement by making community value tangible and rewarding.
- **Personas for the community are more complex than "marketers."** Gabrielle identified herself as an "aspirational founder" separate from growth hackers or traditional marketers, suggesting the onboarding and content strategy need to bucket members by persona and surface the most relevant pathways first.

**To CheckThat / AI Visibility:**
- Gabrielle's immediate application of Marcel's founder research prompts (using Perplexity and Claude to test them) demonstrates strong product-market fit for AI-driven founder and company research workflows in the aspirational founder segment.

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- Low-friction community content can serve as a lead magnet and proof point for GrowthX services. Gabrielle's interest in learning from Marcel and Jason's methodology could translate to higher-ticket consulting engagements as she moves closer to founding.

### Feature Requests & Engagement Tactics

  - **Cohort-Based Q\&A Sessions:**
      - **Format:** Bi-weekly or monthly calls with Marcel & Jason.
      - **Process:** Members submit questions in advance for direct answers.
      - **Rationale:** Provides personalized guidance and direct access to experts, a format proven highly engaging in other communities.
  - **Personalized Onboarding:**
      - **Concept:** A guided path tailored to user personas (e.g., "Aspirational Founder," "Growth Marketer").
      - **Action:** Directs new users to the most relevant content to accelerate time-to-value.
  - **"Celebrate" Channel:**
      - **Concept:** A dedicated channel for members to share their wins and milestones.
      - **Rationale:** Creates positive peer pressure and reinforces the community's value by showcasing tangible results.
