## Key Topics

### Stonly's Challenge & Goal

  - **Context:** Stonly's 1-person content team has historically focused on thought leadership (webinars, PDFs), not SEO.
  - **Goal:** Build brand presence in AI search, aiming for Stonly to appear as the top result for queries like "best knowledge-base software."
  - **Past Vendor:** Stonly had planned to sign with Fathom for its tech + strategy support but was blocked when Fathom was acquired by HubSpot, a platform Stonly doesn't use.

### GrowthX's Solution & Process

  - **Model:** An end-to-end service combining a proprietary platform with expert human editors.
  - **Process:**
      - **1. Strategy Sprint (8 weeks):** GrowthX becomes an expert on the client's business, brand, and audience.
          - **Deliverables:** Personas, brand guidelines, topic clusters, and 10 published articles.
      - **2. Growth Execution:**
          - **Volume:** 5 articles/week (new or refreshed).
          - **Workflow:** AI generates content from detailed briefs, which human editors then review, refine, and command.
          - **Quality Control:** Includes automated fact-checking against a confidence interval and validation against custom writing guidelines.
  - **GEO Optimization:**
      - **Strategy:** Focuses on audience intent and structuring content (e.g., tables, clear headings) for LLM consumption.
      - **Monitoring:** Uses Scrunch (transitioning to a proprietary tool) to track citations, sentiment, and position in AI search results.

### Pricing & Logistics

  - **Strategy Sprint:** $30,000 for 8 weeks (includes 10 articles).
  - **Growth Execution:** $15,000/month.
  - **Team:** Dedicated account person, managing editor, and strategy directors.
  - **Meetings:** Weekly syncs to review progress and analytics.
  - **Timeline:** Earliest kickoff is late January.
