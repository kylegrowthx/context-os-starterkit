## Summary

GrowthX delivered exceptional Q3 results with a 55% QoQ run rate increase to ~$15M, 22 new logos, and 80% pilot conversion, fueled by strong unit economics and a SAFE funding round from HubSpot, Webflow, and Proof VC. The company announced a major delivery org restructure separating client-facing Engagement Managers from production Editors, shifting Directors to a shared resource model focused on strategic work, and introducing more Forward Deployed Engineers to improve scalability and unlock predictable renewals. The transition moves into effect on December 1st with a completed account handoff by November 24th.

---

## Context

GrowthX is a B2B content marketing services company with $200k+/year client engagements. The company had just completed a board meeting and used this all-hands to share quarterly results and strategic updates to the full team. The delivery org restructure was the centerpiece—CEO Marcel Santilli explained that while the company has exceptional growth and demand, the delivery organization faces a capacity bottleneck where adding more people doesn't proportionally increase output, and renewal predictability has suffered as a result. The restructure aims to improve efficiency, clarify customer relationships, and enable scalability by separating roles and building shared resource models similar to those already in use with forward-deployed engineers and client ops.

## Key Topics

### Financials & Performance

  - **Q3 Growth Metrics:**
      - Run Rate: +55% QoQ → \~$15M (projected EOY).
      - New Logos: 22 added, all from inbound/word-of-mouth.
      - Pilot Conversion: \~80%, up from prior quarters.
  - **Financial Position:**
      - Cash Flow: Positive for the last two months while still investing aggressively.
      - Funding: Secured a SAFE from HubSpot, Webflow, and Proof VC, replenishing 2025 cash burn.
      - Gross Margin: Nearing 70%, reflecting a tech-first model over a pure services model.
  - **New Business Wins:**
      - Cresta ($175M Series D): Editorial work.
      - Navon ($6B IPO): Content to help reverse a 25% stock drop.
      - Turnstile (Series A): Full quote-to-cash platform.
  - **Pipeline Highlights:**
      - Brex: $40K Phase 1 + $35K/mo Phase 2 → \>$300K annual potential.
      - Ramp: Building a new domain, "the Cashflow Desk."
      - Zapier: Scoping a partnership to build their templates library.

### AI-Led Growth Community Launch

  - **Purpose:** A self-funded marketing initiative to build the next generation of AI-native growth operators.
  - **Initial Results:**
      - Sign-ups: \~8,000 in the first week.
      - Paying Customers: 155.
      - Revenue: $40K ARR / $3.5K MRR.
  - **Strategy:** Reinvest all revenue to create the best resources and potentially recruit future talent directly from the community.

### Delivery Org Restructure

  - **Problem:** Diminishing returns on headcount. Adding more people does not proportionally increase capacity, creating a bottleneck that hinders growth and makes renewals unpredictable.
  - **Solution:** A new model separating client-facing from production roles to improve efficiency and scalability.
  - **New Roles & Structure:**
      - **Engagement Managers (EMs):** Primary client contact, owning the commercial relationship and a book of business.
      - **Editors:** Non-client-facing production experts focused on quality and output.
          - **Growth Path:** A senior IC path exists for Editors, separate from management.
      - **Directors:** Shift to a shared resource model, focusing on high-impact strategic work across accounts, not daily management.
      - **Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs):** More FDEs will be added to provide leverage and enable custom work.
      - **Shared Editorial Team:** A central pool for production capacity, new employee training, and context-building.
  - **Account Health Score:** A new 4-vector metric to guide strategic work and ensure predictable renewals.
      - **Strategy:** Client alignment on the 6-month growth plan.
      - **Quality:** Content exceeding client and audience expectations.
      - **Output:** Efficient delivery meeting contractual obligations.
      - **Performance:** Measurable outcomes justifying the investment.
      - **Relationship:** Client perception and stakeholder enablement.

### Implementation Timeline

  - **Today:** Andi will share the new org chart and role descriptions.
  - **This Week:** Individual role assignments will be sent out.
  - **By Monday, Nov 24:** Account handoff documents must be completed and shared with clients.
  - **Dec 1:** New roles become active, with EMs and Editors assuming full responsibility.
  - **Dec 12:** All transitions must be solidified before the holiday break.
