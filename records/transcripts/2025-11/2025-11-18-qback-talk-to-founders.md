## Summary

Harshit Jain detailed his impressive journey in product marketing, notably scaling revenue from $3 million to $50 million at Observe in three years. He has also built marketing functions from the ground up at Lead Square and Hevo, achieving significant growth at Hevo. Harshit shared his experience of founding Qback, which closed in December 2024 due to founder misalignment rather than financial issues. Currently at E6, he is developing the product marketing function in an early-stage company competing with major players. Harshit emphasized the transformative impact of AI on marketing and expressed a willingness to collaborate on educational resources in the AI Growth community, which aims to create a network of expert contributors and hire talent from within.
