### New Agentic Content Pipeline

  - An agentic AI pipeline is live to improve content quality and accuracy.
  - **Key Upgrades:**
      - **Research:** Uses EXA/Tavily (vs. Perplexity) for more relevant, authority-constrained searches (e.g., government sites).
      - **Self-Correction:** The AI generates research questions, grades its own answers (1-5 score), and re-researches low-confidence topics.
      - **Source Validation:** Requires primary sources for all statistics; cannot use stats from secondary sources like blog posts.
  - **Risk Mitigation:**
      - **Flagging Unverified Claims:** The AI flags claims it cannot verify with credible sources.
      - **Manual Review:** Rimkus requested a pipeline step to explicitly annotate these flagged claims for manual review by a technical expert.
      - **Rationale:** This ensures accuracy, builds internal credibility, and reduces legal/financial risk.
