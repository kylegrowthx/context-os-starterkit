## Key Topics

### PMM Feedback & Monday's Meeting

  - A blog "missed the mark" because PMMs had not filled out the Notion documentation.
  - The upcoming Monday meeting will address this by:
      - Reviewing existing feedback.
      - Re-reviewing the Notion docs to clarify input requirements.
      - Showcasing the new agentic pipeline to demonstrate process improvements.

### New "Agentic" AI Pipeline

  - A new content generation pipeline is now active, designed to improve quality and accuracy.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **Self-Correction:** An agent grades its own draft against artifacts (personas, guidelines) and revises to fix errors.
      - **Advanced Fact-Checking:** Replaced Perplexity with EXA, a more powerful search API.
  - **Initial Results:** One test article showed improved flow and overall quality.

### Blog Strategy: "What Is" Sections

  - **Decision:** Remove "What is" H2s from blogs.
  - **Rationale:**
      - Blogs will focus on thought leadership and specific questions.
      - Educational content will move to a dedicated directory.
      - This targets higher-quality, consideration-stage traffic over general, top-of-funnel clicks.
  - **Implementation:** Use the intro for a brief definition, then start with a more specific H2.

### Content Strategy & Topic Approval

  - **Problem:** N-able was approving individual topics without a clear strategic context.
  - **Solution:** N-able will review the master **Content Strategy Doc** first.
      - This document defines the core themes and topic clusters.
      - Feedback on the doc will guide future keyword research and topic generation.
  - **Action:** N-able will pause all topic approvals until the strategy doc review is complete.

### Product Accuracy & Feedback Loop

  - **Problem:** PMMs need a clear feedback loop to ensure their specific product language and accuracy notes are incorporated.
  - **Issue:** Product mentions were sometimes removed from drafts, making it unclear if feedback was applied.
  - **Solution:** Create a separate **"Product Accuracy" artifact**.
      - This artifact will contain specific rules and guidelines for all product mentions.
      - It will be integrated as a dedicated step in the agentic pipeline, preventing AI from making false claims.
      - GrowthX has prior experience with this for a client in the healthcare supplement space.

### Proposed Gated Asset: AI Threat Detection

  - GrowthX proposed a gated asset for lead generation: **"How SMBs Can Improve AI Threat Detection."**
  - **Structure:**
    1.  **Setting the Threats:** Repurposes annual threat report data (voice clone, synthetic video, etc.).
    2.  **Detection Framework:** Expands on a brief table from the report into a full narrative.
    3.  **Implementation:** Details how to apply the framework, including where to position N-able solutions (e.g., Add Lumen MDR).
  - **Status:** The asset can replace weekly articles under the standard contract. The outline is ready for PMM review, especially with security PMM Jen.

### SEO Feedback

  - **Internal Links:** Fix broken links (e.g., anchor text "MSP owners" linking to "incident response best practices").
  - **Content Variety:** Vary opening stats across cyber resilience articles (e.g., stop reusing the "4.4 million" stat).
  - **AI Overviews:** To optimize for generative AI, GrowthX uses practices like:
      - "Bottom line up front" writing.
      - One thought per sentence/paragraph.
      - FAQs and bulleted summaries.
