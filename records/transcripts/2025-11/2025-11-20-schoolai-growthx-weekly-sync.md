### "AI for Math" Article Strategy

- **Problem:** The article's top rank for the broad query "AI for math" attracts significant student traffic.
- **Risk:** High bounce rates from students seeking personal help could signal low intent to Google, risking the article's high rank.
- **Dilemma:** The article is a key asset, driving the most organic traffic and serving as a top LLM citation source.
- **Potential Solutions:**
    - **Create new, teacher-specific content:** Avoids modifying the high-performing original.
    - **Add on-page student CTA:** Redirects students to relevant resources or encourages them to ask their teacher about SchoolAI.
    - **Reposition for students:** Aligns content with the future student-facing product.

### Holiday Content Strategy

- **Context:** GrowthX is creating a content buffer for the holidays.
- **Decision:** Pause publishing during low-traffic weeks (Thanksgiving, Christmas) to avoid wasted effort.
- **Rationale:** Google Search Console data from last year shows a massive traffic drop during these periods.
- **Action:** Use the content buffer to schedule occasional posts for consistency.
