## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Inefficient Page Revamp Process

  - Outreach is updating 164 website pages to align messaging with recent product launches.
  - The current process is inefficient:
      - SMEs provide content feedback in Word docs.
      - Hadassah manually transfers this to a spreadsheet for SEO review.
      - This creates a risk of inconsistent SEO application and missed opportunities.

### The Solution: A Collaborative, SEO-Driven Workflow

  - A new, collaborative workflow was established to streamline the process:
    1.  **SME Review (Outreach):** SMEs update page copy for accuracy.
    2.  **SEO & Content Recommendations (GrowthX):**
          - Review SME-updated docs for technical SEO (titles, metadata, linking).
          - Provide strategic content suggestions, including competitor analysis.
          - Flag design/schema opportunities for Kendra's team.
    3.  **Implementation (Outreach):** Andrew and Kendra's team implement all changes on the website.

### Coordination & Timeline

  - Hadassah will manage the project in batches via a central spreadsheet, which will track:
      - Page URLs
      - Links to SME-updated docs
      - Status (e.g., "Ready for GrowthX," "Complete")
  - **Resource Allocation:** GrowthX can pause blog production to accelerate work on larger batches.
  - **Immediate Action:** GrowthX will complete SEO updates (meta titles/descriptions) for pages already content-updated by Outreach.
