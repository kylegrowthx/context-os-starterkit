## Key Topics

### Partnership Model & Expectations

  - GrowthX's model is a partnership where Hilti provides the strategic foundation, and GrowthX executes at scale.
  - **Hilti's Role:**
      - Provide all foundational brand assets: ICP, personas, brand guidelines, product marketing materials.
      - Define key business topics for authority (e.g., "FireStop").
  - **GrowthX's Role:**
      - Use Hilti's assets to build internal "artifacts" (language models) that ensure brand voice consistency.
      - Identify SEO/GEO opportunities and create content at scale (target: 5 articles/week).
      - Provide guidance on tailoring content to specific personas (e.g., C-suite vs. IC).

### Engagement Structure & Timeline

  - The engagement is structured in two phases:
    1.  **8-Week Strategy Sprint:**
          - **Goal:** Drive rapid results in the U.S. market.
          - **Deliverables:** A content roadmap with SEO/GEO opportunities and sample articles.
          - **Why:** Proves value and builds confidence for the long-term partnership.
    2.  **12-Month Growth Execution:**
          - **Goal:** Scale content production to 5 articles/week.
          - **French/German Content:** Begins \~3 months into this phase.
          - **Why:** Allows the team to master the U.S. workflow before building new market capabilities.

### French & German Content Strategy

  - GrowthX can create content for these markets but has not done so before.
  - **Method:** AI-driven translation and market-specific research, not native speakers.
  - **Why Phased:** This approach is a test case. A sample article cannot be provided pre-engagement because the team is fully focused on delivering for current clients.

### Performance & Feedback Loop

  - Performance is tracked via bi-weekly reviews of Google Analytics and Google Search Console data.
  - **Why:** Enables data-driven pivots to focus on high-performing topics.
  - The feedback loop focuses on content performance (clicks, conversions), not refining Hilti's core ICP or brand voice.
  - **GrowthX's Rationale:** Time is better spent on content quality and structure for AI search than on endless brand voice refinement, which offers lower ROI.
