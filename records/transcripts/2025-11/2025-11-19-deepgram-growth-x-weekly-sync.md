## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Tracking

  - **Problem:** The Looker dashboard's performance view is skewed by low-value traffic from "AI apps" pages, obscuring the impact of new content.
  - **Solution:** Erik will create a new dashboard view that filters out this traffic, enabling accurate measurement of new blog performance.
  - **Goal:** Use this data to inform technical SEO optimizations (e.g., internal linking) and guide the long-term content strategy.

### Strategic Content Revamps

  - **Competitor Pages:** A full revamp is planned to replace generic text with data-rich tables and charts, directly comparing Deepgram's capabilities against competitors.
      - [**Rationale:** To capture high-intent "Deepgram vs. \[Competitor\]" search queries and outperform current text-heavy pages.](https://fathom.video/share/C-wPE5pkWaQSsa1xEq6HLPnz8fD7UxE-?tab=summary&timestamp=317.0)
  - **Language Pages:** New pages for 35+ STT and \~10 TTS languages are in development.
      - **Rationale:** To capture low-competition, high-value terms (e.g., "Spanish speech-to-text").
      - **Status:** A wireframe for the Spanish STT page is ready for Troy's SEO review. The pages are designed for scalability, using a mix of static and dynamic elements managed via a spreadsheet and Loom tutorial.

### Q1 2026 Content Focus: Text-to-Speech (TTS)

  - **Driver:** A new TTS model launch and a strategic goal to increase TTS revenue share in 2026.
  - **Benchmark:** Eleven Labs, a market leader, will be analyzed for SEO strategy, with the caveat that Deepgram's focus is B2B, not consumer/creator.
  - **Status:**
      - Will is writing 5 new TTS pieces for Jose's review this week.
      - Hasan will provide 12 more TTS topics for Will to research.

### Content Amplification Strategy

  - **Goal:** Maximize ROI on content by promoting it beyond the blog.
  - **Current Channels:**
      - **Deepgram Digest:** Product news newsletter; Hasan includes new blogs.
      - **AI Minds:** Bi-weekly thought leadership newsletter; Jose owns.
  - **New Opportunities:**
      - **Targeted Email Campaigns:** Send hyper-relevant content to specific user segments (e.g., TTS users, language-specific audiences).
      - **Social Media:** Use content for LinkedIn retargeting and organic posts.
      - **Syndication:** Explore editorial sponsorships in relevant newsletters.
