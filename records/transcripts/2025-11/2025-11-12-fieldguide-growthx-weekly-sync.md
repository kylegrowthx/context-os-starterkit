## Key Topics

### Content Hub Location

  - **Decision:** The content hub will be a subfolder within the existing `/resources-library/` path.
  - **Rationale:** This location was requested by Fieldguide's CMO. Aida confirmed it is an acceptable SEO compromise, as it leverages the parent folder's existing authority. A new standalone subfolder would lack authority and risk SEO performance.
  - **Authority Consideration:** Fieldguide's blog section has an authority score of 31, but the blog cannot be modified per Fieldguide policy. Resources Library subfolder has no independent authority score yet, but will inherit from the parent.
  - **Contingency:** A dedicated subfolder can be revisited once the new content demonstrates strong performance.

### New Workflow & Production

  - **Project Management:** All content tasks will be managed in Monday.com to standardize workflow and track status.
      - **GrowthX:** Create blog task, update with link, assign to Amanda (Fieldguide's content manager), and set a due date one week out to trigger notifications.
      - **Fieldguide:** Update task status as content moves through review and approval phases.
  - **Creative Assets:** GrowthX will now produce all creative assets for the blogs, expanding scope beyond writing.
      - **In Progress:** Aida prepared sample graphics for the "PCI compliance" blog. Will share with Yasine for internal Fieldguide review before forwarding to Fieldguide's creative team for feedback.
  - **Production Cadence:**
      - **This week:** 3 blogs in progress; 1 ready by end of week after Yasine's final approval.
      - **Next week:** 5 blogs scheduled.
  - **CMS Access:** Yasine will grant GrowthX (Aida and Erik) direct HubSpot access to stage content under Resources Library.

### Performance Dashboard & Analytics

  - GrowthX presented a new Looker dashboard that combines GA4 and Search Console data to track content performance and inform strategy refinement.
  - **Dashboard Pages:**
      - **Engagement:** Displays sessions vs. engaged sessions. Engaged sessions defined as 10+ seconds on page, clicked a link, or scrolled. High engagement rate (>50%) is a key SEO signal. Current site-wide engagement is average (~50%).
      - **Cohorts:** Primary page for measuring GrowthX content performance. URLs grouped by topic cluster. Will show which clusters drive the most traffic once content is published.
      - **Query Report:** Identifies keywords where Fieldguide ranks on page 2-3 but lacks dedicated content. Highlights quick-win opportunities for new blog posts that can rank on page 1. Example: GrowthX's first client (dental staffing) ranked for "TRO reviews" but had no content about it; created blog combining user reviews, which ranked immediately in position 1 and continues to drive leads.
      - **Conversion Events:** Tracks standard GA4 events. Yasine flagged "file download" event as unclear source and will investigate.
      - **LLM Referral Traffic:** Tracks traffic from ChatGPT and Perplexity. Majority from ChatGPT, with Perplexity growing. Blogs currently underperforming on LLM traffic relative to homepage.
  - **Data Integration Notes:**
      - **GA4:** Includes all traffic sources (organic Google, direct, LLMs).
      - **Search Console:** Organic Google traffic only. Data will differ between platforms.
  - **Indexing Timeline:** New content typically indexed by Google within a couple of days. Impressions appear within 7-14 days. Clicks may be immediate depending on keyword demand. Full impact visible after 4 weeks of consistent publishing.
