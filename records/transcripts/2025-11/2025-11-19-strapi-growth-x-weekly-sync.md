## Summary

GrowthX and Strapi conducted their weekly content and performance sync, reviewing seven delivered and seven scheduled articles alongside ongoing FAQ rollouts and comparison page optimization work. The team approved prioritizing the top 5 non-"Strapi vs." comparison pages for table-repositioning before year-end, rejecting a broader fix as too costly for low-traffic pages. Strapi continues outperforming WordPress across all tracked categories (including "content management for developers"), with November traffic on pace to match October and non-branded impressions rising. GrowthX will begin monitoring key subreddits (Next.js, etc.) to identify top-of-funnel content topics as a workaround for the delayed "Kappa" content-gap tool, which engineering has deprioritized due to the Q1 2025 Atlas launch.

---

## Context

Strapi is a headless CMS that competes directly with WordPress and other traditional content management systems. GrowthX is executing a multi-quarter content and SEO strategy to improve Strapi's online visibility and competitive positioning, particularly targeting developer audiences. This weekly sync is the operational heartbeat of that partnership — Vivek Shankar (GrowthX content lead) briefs the Strapi team (Victor Coisne, Paul Bratslavsky, Theodore Onyejiaku, and Golzar Yaghoubpour) on content delivery, performance metrics, competitive trends, and emerging needs. The meeting reflects both teams' focus on sustainable content programs, data-driven decision-making (rejecting expensive low-ROI tasks), and finding creative workarounds when engineering resources are constrained.

  - **Comparison Page Table Fix:**
      - **Problem:** The large comparison table appears at the top of some pages, pushing key text below the fold.
      - **Solution:** Move the table below the text.
      - **Decision:** Prioritize the top 5 non-"Strapi vs." pages before EOY.

  - **FAQ Rollout:**
      - **Integration Pages:** FAQs are live on 53 pages; more are queued.
      - **Feature & Capability Pages:** FAQs are underway.
      - **Internal Linking:** Adding links to docs/reference pages within FAQs to provide deeper context and boost citation potential.

      - The footer FAQ link is being replaced with an experimental "About Strapi" page.
      - Content is being generated for review by EOW.
  - **Metadata Audit:**
      - **Request:** Audit metadata for all blog articles.
      - **Status:** GrowthX currently updates metadata only during content refreshes; a full audit is a new request.
  - **"Kappa" Content Gap Tool:**
      - **Status:** On hold.
      - **Reason:** Engineering's focus on the Q1 2025 Atlas launch deprioritizes custom requests.
      - **Impact:** Delays automated topic identification from social listening.

### Performance Review

  - **November Traffic:**
      - On pace to match October's performance.
      - Non-branded impressions are rising.
      - Conversion events remain stable.
      - Overall LLM traffic is increasing.
  - **Scrumge (Competitive Analysis):**
      - Strapi now leads WordPress in all tracked categories, including "content management for developers" and "customizable admin interfaces."
  - **GrowthX Integration Pages:**
      - **Performance:** Traffic has plateaued.
      - **LLM Traffic:** Driven by branded queries (e.g., "how to integrate Strapi with X").
      - **Plan:** Refresh top pages (e.g., AWS, Angular) to signal maintenance and potentially boost sessions.
      - **Clarification:** The performance analysis will be re-run using a confirmed list of pages written solely by GrowthX.
