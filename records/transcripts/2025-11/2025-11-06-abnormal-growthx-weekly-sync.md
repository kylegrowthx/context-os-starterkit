## Key Topics

### Performance & Strategic Direction

  - **Performance:** Engaged impressions grew 38% WoW (vs. 34% last week).
  - **CTR Insights:**
      - **High-CTR Pages:** Blog, repository comparisons, and CISO pages.
      - **Top 6 Pages:** All contain "GPT" (e.g., `worm GPT`).
  - **Strategic Goal:** Drive more clicks and business leads, with clicks as the primary metric.

### New Content Initiatives

  - **1. High-CTR Topic Expansion**
      - **Rationale:** Double down on proven topics (GPT, fraud, scams) that convert well.
      - **Execution:** Use the glossary format for terms not directly tied to the Abnormal solution (e.g., `Hack GPT`) to avoid brand confusion.
  - **2. B2C Keyword Exploration**
      - **Rationale:** Capture high-volume B2C traffic (e.g., `spam email`) to meet volume goals.
      - **Trade-off:** Higher volume, but less persona-specific.
      - **Example:** "Microsoft email scams" article drove significant growth.
  - **3. "Cyber Career Guide" Content Branch**
      - **Proposal:** A new content hub targeting cybersecurity career keywords.
      - **Rationale:** High volume, low-to-medium keyword difficulty.
      - **Initial Concern (Abdallah):** Attracting job-seekers, not prospects.
      - **Resolution:** Test the concept and track traffic behavior (e.g., time on site) to validate the audience.
  - **4. Webinar & Podcast Repurposing**
      - **Webinars:** Create blog posts from webinars to drive traffic to the full recording.
      - **Podcasts:** Emily's team will continue managing podcast recap blogs. GrowthX can provide SEO optimization support.
  - **5. CISO-Authored Substack Newsletters**
      - **Proposal:** Launch personal Substack newsletters for CISOs Mike Leach and Patricia.
      - **Execution:** GrowthX will use AI to repurpose their content (podcasts, webinars).
      - **Ownership:** Newsletters will be branded to the CISOs (e.g., "Mike Leach from Abnormal") to build their personal brands while clearly sponsored by Abnormal.
      - **Goal:** Drive clicks to the main Abnormal blog.

### Content Operations

  - **Reddit Strategy:** Focus on monitoring ICP subreddits for content ideas, not just brand mentions.
  - **Medium Workflow:** Engineering will provide a timeline for repurposing the Medium workflow by EOW.
  - **Content Refresh:** Proceed with approved content refreshes. Emily's team will review retroactively to avoid blocking publication.
  - **YouTube SEO:** GrowthX will provide SEO descriptions for Abnormal's YouTube videos to improve organic search discovery.
