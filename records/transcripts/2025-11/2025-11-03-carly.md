## Key Topics

### GrowthX & "AI Led Growth" Community

  - **GrowthX Agency:** An AI-native marketing agency proving a new, more efficient way to work.
      - **Efficiency:** Claims to be 4–5x more efficient than traditional SEO agencies.
      - **Productization:** The agency's internal platform will eventually be released as a standalone product.
  - **"AI Led Growth" Community:** The agency's primary marketing vehicle.
      - **Goal:** Evangelize AI in growth and marketing, becoming the go-to place for upskilling.
      - **Model:** A hybrid of Reforge (structured content) and Exit 5 (peer discussion).
      - **Audience Tracks:**
          - **Operator Track:** Marketers and growth professionals.
              - **Status:** 70 paying members at $200/year for workshop recordings.
          - **Exec Track:** Senior leaders and potential agency clients.
              - **Status:** Free; modeled after Hampton, with private dinners and fireside chats.
  - **Community Need:** A dedicated leader to own strategy, growth, and operations.

### Pavilion's Community Success Model

  - **Problem Statement:** A clear, unifying narrative ("The average tenure of a VP of sales is 18 months") that created camaraderie by framing a shared challenge.
  - **"Give to Get" Culture:** The core value, enforced via:
      - **Onboarding:** Members accept a code of conduct explaining the value.
      - **Messaging:** Regular reminders in newsletters and Slack.
      - **Enforcement:** Proactive moderation with warnings and expulsions for non-compliant behavior.
  - **Key Metrics:**
      - **Retention:** The ultimate measure of value; monthly members provide a faster feedback loop.
      - **NPS:** Trailing 90-day NPS is a leading indicator of member satisfaction.
      - **Annual Surveys:** Used to measure specific outcomes (e.g., new job, raise).
      - **Member Health Score:** A composite metric tracking engagement across all platforms.
  - **Engagement Channels:**
      - **Slack:** The \#1 ranked member value; \~50% monthly active users.
      - **Pathfinder Bot:** An AI bot (powered by Tribble) that answers questions by pulling from all community content (Slack, events, courses). It also suggests relevant members to connect with.
      - **In-Person Events:** Chapters and private dinners.
      - **1-on-1 Matching:** A tool (Orbit) to facilitate member connections.

### Outcome: Carly Cannot Join

  - **Reason:** A 12-month non-compete from Pavilion.
  - **Rationale:** The community's paid model and exec track create a direct, competitive overlap.
  - **Offer:** To serve as an informal advisor, providing feedback and connecting Jason with candidates.
