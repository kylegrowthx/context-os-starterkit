### SEO Experiment: "TLDR" Sections

  - **Feedback:** The current "TLDR" format is jarring and feels robotic.
  - **Goal:** Improve human readability while retaining SEO benefits.
  - **Proposed Changes:**
      - **Formatting:** Use Ghost's native modules (e.g., callout boxes) to visually separate the summary.
      - **Title:** Experiment with more user-friendly labels (e.g., "Here's what you need to know").
  - **Rationale:** The "TLDR" structure is the key SEO element, not the specific title.
  - **Supporting Data:** A large-scale test with client Airbyte (30 control vs. 30 experimental posts) shows early signals of higher average click-through and better search positioning.

### New SEO Question: AI-Generated Apps

  - **Question:** How to make AI-generated apps on Bubble SEO-indexable to increase platform authority?
  - **Challenge:** AI-generated content is often not indexed by search engines.
      - **Precedent:** GrowthX's work with client Lovable showed their AI-generated apps had no SEO visibility. Retroactively fixing this is a significant effort.
  - **Current Bubble Showcase:** The `bubble.io/app-gallery` features user-submitted apps selected by Bubble, not automatically generated ones.
  - **Idea:** A "remixable template" system could incentivize users to create showcase-worthy apps by offering credits for template usage.

### Request: AI Image Generation Process

  - **Request:** Bubble's design team is interested in the process and prompts used for AI-generated blog artwork.
  - **Goal:** Learn from GrowthX's workflow to improve internal efficiency.
  - **Action:** Erik will ask designer Katya to share her multi-step process and the "master prompt" used for Bubble's images.
