## Key Topics

### Boulevard's Evaluation Context

  - Boulevard is in the final stage of evaluating two firms for content services, with a decision expected in \~1 week.
  - This call focused on the commercial and scope details of GrowthX's Statement of Work (SoW).

### GrowthX's Service Model

  - GrowthX's model is a standardized, two-phase process:
      - **Phase 1: 8-Week Strategy Sprint**
          - **Goal:** Develop a 3–6 month content roadmap and publish initial articles.
          - **Process:** Kickoff calls → resource gathering → SEO/competitor audits → keyword analysis → topic cluster development.
          - **Deliverables:** A detailed strategy and content plan in a shared Notion/Airtable workspace.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
          - **Goal:** Publish 5 articles/week (new or refreshed) based on the strategy.
          - **Deliverables:**
              - 5 published-ready articles/week.
              - Performance tracking via a Looker dashboard.
              - AI search performance reporting (e.g., prompt tracking).
              - Weekly/bi-weekly syncs and monthly reviews.

### Scope & Deliverables Clarification

  - **Content Creation:** The 5 articles/week include the full process: research, brief creation, drafting, and final publication.
  - **AI Role:** AI generates initial drafts, but the process is heavily human-driven, with manual keyword analysis, brief creation, and final review to ensure quality and brand voice.
  - **Programmatic Content:** This is a potential pivot, not an add-on. If editorial opportunities are exhausted, the engagement can shift to building a programmatic content engine (e.g., calculators, location pages) at no additional cost.
  - **Out of Scope:**
      - Active backlinking campaigns.
      - Technical SEO implementation (the Strategy Sprint includes an audit report, but not the execution of its recommendations).

### Account Team Structure

  - The core team has three points of contact:
      - **Engagement Manager:** Day-to-day contact and strategy lead.
      - **Managing Editor:** End-to-end content creation.
      - **Managing Director:** Pooled resource for high-level strategy and performance intervention.
  - The editor from the Strategy Sprint continues through Growth Execution to ensure continuity.
