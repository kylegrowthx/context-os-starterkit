### The Challenge at GrowthX

  - **Context:** GrowthX is a 1-year-old services business scaling rapidly, onboarding \~2 clients/week.
  - **Problem:** The delivery org's current structure is not scalable. Hiring this role is a key part of a reorg to improve efficiency and cost tracking, a priority for investors.
  - **Goal:** Implement a formal project management tool to enable:
      - Capacity planning
      - Cost tracking
      - Progress tracking against contractual deliverables
      - Automated reporting
  - **Future:** The role will also support the launch of a new self-serve product platform, offering significant growth opportunities.

### Sierra Straube's Experience & Approach

  - **Career Path:** Customer Support → Customer Success (Brightwheel) → CS Ops Manager (Resilience).
  - **Brightwheel:** Developed a webinar series to segment new clients into high-touch vs. low-touch onboarding paths, directly leading to the creation of an ops role.
  - **Resilience:** As the second CS hire, built the entire CS Ops function from the ground up, focusing on scalable processes and workflows.

### Key Projects & Methodologies

  - **Gainsight Implementation:**
      - Led implementation as a first-time administrator, learning the tool while simultaneously training the team.
      - Managed change with a weekly demo/SOP cadence and an "85/15 rule":
          - Build features to 85% completion.
          - Demo for team feedback.
          - Finalize based on input to ensure the tool serves end-users.
  - **Automated Sales-to-CS Handoff:**
      - Partnered with Sales Ops to automate the handoff process from email to a HubSpot ticketing queue.
      - **Result:** Eliminated friction and information loss by providing CSMs with all necessary client context immediately upon deal close.
  - **Customer Feedback Loop:**
      - Built a HubSpot ticketing system for CSMs to capture client feedback.
      - Analyzed monthly data to inform product roadmap decisions, reprioritizing features based on client need.
  - **Churn & Renewal Analysis:**
      - Led monthly sessions using product usage and churn risk data to identify trends and apply learnings across the team's accounts, improving renewal rates.
