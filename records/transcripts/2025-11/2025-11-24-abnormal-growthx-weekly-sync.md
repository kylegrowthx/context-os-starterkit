## Summary

GrowthX and Abnormal Security reset their Q4 SEO forecast to 15k clicks/month by February (2x from ~7.5k current) and aligned on a major strategy shift from high-volume new content creation to prioritizing content refreshes, which have proven highly effective for both traditional SEO and AI Answer Engines. The team identified a critical GA4 tracking bug affecting ~100 pages including 92 GrowthX-written articles, agreed to launch a webinar-to-article repurposing experiment, and established a weekly Reddit monitoring cadence to inform Abnormal's editorial roadmap.

## Context

## Key Topics

### Performance & Forecast Reset

  - **Current Performance:** Clicks are growing 90% since August, with editorial content showing strong acceleration (nearly 3x growth).
  - **Forecast Reset Rationale:** The original forecast was overly aggressive, especially its reliance on the glossary. A more realistic forecast is needed to provide a useful benchmark for measuring success.
  - **New Forecast:** The new target is 15k clicks/month by February, a 2x increase from the current \~7.5k. Abdallah confirmed this aligns with his internal goals.

### New Strategy: Content Refreshes

  - **Core Strategy:** Shift focus to refreshing existing content, which is a proven tactic for driving SEO growth and is now favored by AI Answer Engines (AEO).
  - **Execution Plan:**
      - **Phase 1 (Low Hanging Fruit):** Refresh GrowthX-written articles in positions 11–20 to push them onto page one.
      - **Phase 2 (Full Rebuild):** Re-evaluate underperforming content to fix intent mismatches, not just refresh the same approach.
  - **Abnormal's Input:**
      - **Visuals:** Add diagrams and graphics to improve UX and rank in image search.
      - **Approval:** Abdallah approved the refresh strategy, starting with GrowthX-owned content to simplify the approval process.

### New Experiment: Webinar Repurposing

  - **Opportunity:** Repurpose Abnormal's high volume of webinars (15–20+ per quarter) into SEO-optimized articles.
  - **Ownership:** Abdallah's team will own the experiment, providing videos and transcripts.
  - **Execution:** Create 1–3 articles per webinar, linking back to the original video.
  - **Distribution:** Use the new articles as more engaging success pages in post-webinar nurture streams.

### Reddit Strategy

  - **Goal:** Build a consistent, educational presence on Reddit.
  - **Current Status:** GrowthX uses Reddit monitoring (via Gummy Search) to inform its editorial roadmap.
  - **Decision:** GrowthX will provide weekly Reddit monitoring reports to Abnormal. Abnormal will then use this data to inform its own internal Reddit content creation.

### Critical Blocker: GA4 Tracking Issue

  - **Problem:** \~100 pages (including 92 GrowthX articles) are not being tracked by GA4, preventing accurate performance analysis.
  - **Cause:** Suspected GTM rule error.
  - **Resolution:** Abnormal's web team will investigate and fix the issue.
