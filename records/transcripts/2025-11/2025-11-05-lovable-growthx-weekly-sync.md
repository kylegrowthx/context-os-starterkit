## Context

This is the ongoing weekly sync between GrowthX (a B2B content marketing agency) and Lovable (an AI-powered web builder). GrowthX is providing content marketing and SEO/AEO services for Lovable to drive visibility in AI search platforms and attract builders to the platform. The team includes George Haikal from GrowthX (leading the account), and Rachel Hepworth and Ceci Stallsmith from Lovable's product and growth leadership. This is a strong working relationship with significant momentum on multiple initiatives.

---

## Relevance

- **To GrowthX Delivery:** Lovable is validating the comparison article SEO strategy at scale — 13 articles live with citations already appearing in Perplexity and Sell AI after 1.5 weeks, proving the AEO approach works for AI visibility. Cadence is 5 articles per week with a documented workflow using GrowthX's internal AI visibility tool. This is a replicable model for other clients.
