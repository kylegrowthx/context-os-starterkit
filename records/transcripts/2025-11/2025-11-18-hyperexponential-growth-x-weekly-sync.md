## Key Topics

### Content Review Process

  - **Problem:** The current review process is too slow. One article took 35 minutes to review, an unsustainable pace for the 11 articles in Franki's queue.
  - **Root Cause:** Paragraphs often lack a clear "so what" statement, leaving sentences feeling disconnected.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will use Franki's detailed comments as training data for its AI pipeline to improve content quality.
  - **Context:** Refresher articles, which have a lighter-touch pipeline focused on stat updates and tone adjustments, are expected to be much faster to review.

### New "Versus" Content Series

  - **Goal:** Create a series of high-value comparison articles (e.g., "HX vs. Federado") to capture low-funnel search intent.
  - **Process:**
    1.  **Input:** Product Marketing (PMM) will provide comparison grids and product context.
    2.  **Output:** GrowthX will write editorial articles that include a comparison table.
  - **Action Plan:**
      - **GrowthX:** Create a sample article with existing info to identify knowledge gaps.
      - **Franki:** Lead a Tuesday meeting with PMM (Chris, Mehmet) to provide the necessary context.

### PSEO (Programmatic SEO) Strategy

  - **Goal:** Use AI to identify and scale programmatic content opportunities.
  - **Method:** Jakub used a custom GPT, fed with HX personas and product docs, to generate \~12 PSEO ideas.
  - **Prioritization Criteria:**
      - **Business Alignment:** How well the topic aligns with HX products.
      - **SEO Impact:** Keyword potential, difficulty, and competition.
      - **Original Content:** Availability of internal data or artifacts to enrich the content (e.g., Jamie Wilson's session transcripts).
  - **Next Step:** The team will review Jakub's document to select 2–3 MVP ideas for a small-batch test before scaling.

### Editorial Content & Themes

  - **Specialized Market Segments:** Franki proposed creating foundational content on specific lines of business (e.g., aviation insurance) to address a sales team need.
  - **Rationale:** While keyword volume is low, these topics are high-intent and build authority.
  - **Decision:** This will be explored as a future PSEO opportunity.
  - **Software Listicles:** Jakub recommended prioritizing "best of" and "alternatives" listicles as a fast way to build a base of low-funnel content.
