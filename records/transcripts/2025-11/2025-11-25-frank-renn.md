## Summary

GrowthX's CEO has reengaged with the website and is driving an urgent repositioning: shift the homepage from a person-focused to platform-focused brand identity to counter new AI competitors. Renato briefed Frank (head of design at thirty-five studio) on a tight deadline — homepage draft due Friday, full build next week — and walked through design direction, content strategy, and inspiration (Lagoore for clean whitespace, Harvey for hybrid platform+services model). The team will use the existing design system but flatten the aesthetic, increase whitespace, and adapt the About page layout to accelerate delivery without building a new design system from scratch.

## Key Topics

### Strategic Pivot: Platform-First Positioning

  - **Problem:** Intense competition from new AI startups requires a brand repositioning.
  - **Solution:** Shift the homepage focus from a person to the platform itself.
  - **Inspiration:**
      - **Lagoore:** For a clean, whitespace-heavy aesthetic.
      - **Harvey:** For its hybrid model (platform + services) and feature-focused homepage, which mirrors growthx's own structure.
  - **Goal:** Create a "mysterious" homepage that drives contact for more information.

### Homepage Redesign Plan

  - **Scope:** Focus exclusively on redesigning the homepage.
  - **Visuals:**
      - **Adapt, don't rebuild:** Use the current design system but adjust the layout.
      - **Aesthetic changes:**
          - Increase whitespace and reduce information density.
          - Adopt a "flatter" look (CEO feedback).
          - Retain existing colors and grids; drop serifs.
  - **Content & Structure:**
      - **Source:** New content is being written by Marcel; Renato will provide a draft ASAP.
      - **Structure:**
          - Opening video (likely the CEO).
          - Validation block.
          - Platform showcase with animated mock UI.
  - **Process:**
      - **Rationale:** The tight timeline (draft by Friday) prevents building a new design system.
      - **Action:** Frank will adapt the existing "About" page layout for the homepage skeleton to accelerate the process.

### Referral: `smith.di`

  - **Context:** `smith.di`, an existing growthx client, contacted Frank's studio for branding work.
  - **Source:** Renato made the referral after `smith.di` asked for recommendations for a branding partner.
  - **Rationale:** Renato advised using a smaller studio over a large San Francisco firm for better quality and results.
