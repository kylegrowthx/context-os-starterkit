  - **Launch Plan:** The community launch is tomorrow. The team will amplify Marcel's central LinkedIn post.

### Platform & Tooling

  - **Course Feedback:** Marissa suggested adding a "how to get the most out of this course" blurb at the beginning to front-load information and improve retention.
  - **Community Structure:** The community's discussion channels will be simplified to one or zero.
      - **Rationale:** To prevent a "dead" feeling and focus member activity.
  - **Traffic Strategy:**
      - **`ailygrowth.com`:** The long-term organic content machine.
      - **Direct Circle Links:** For warm conversations, as they convert better by offering immediate, valuable content.
      - **Future State:** Edin is exploring using the Circle API to pull dynamic, gated content onto `ailygrowth.com`.
  - **EventHapply Setup:** Kyle's top priorities are setting up EventHapply and its Zoom integration.
      - **Rationale:** To enable automated post-event sequences defined in Gloria's FigJam.
  - **Enrichment:** Always-on enrichment will be deprioritized for now.
      - **Rationale:** The immediate need is for mass outbound campaigns, not inbound lead scoring.
