## Key Topics

### Blog Indexing & Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** New blog articles are not being indexed quickly by Google, requiring manual submission.
  - **Root Cause:** The blog's "learn series" pages are sorted "oldest-first," hiding new content from Google bots and making the site appear stale.
  - **Solution:**
      - Change the sort order to "newest-first" on all blog category pages.
      - Add publish/refresh dates to article snippets for better crawl signals.
      - Increase articles per page from 5 to 8–10 to expose more content.
  - **Content Pipeline:**
      - 10 new comparison articles (e.g., "MindTickle Alternatives") were published.
      - Production will increase to 12 articles/week to build a buffer for the holiday season.
      - Garett will review all comparison articles to ensure Exec's positioning is strong and CTAs are prominent.

### Backlink & On-Page SEO Strategy

  - **New Backlink Strategy:** Shift from general homepage links to a focused, bottom-up approach targeting specific landing pages with unique anchors.
  - **Immediate Priorities:**
      - **Sales AI Roleplay Page:** Pause backlink efforts for "sales roleplay software" to focus all link equity on a new, high-priority page launching within two weeks. Garett has sent the draft copy to Jo for on-page SEO optimization.
      - **Existing AI Roleplays Page (`/ai-roleplays`):** Implement on-page SEO edits first. A URL slug change to `/ai-roleplay-software` is deferred to avoid disrupting existing links, but remains an option if initial optimizations are insufficient.
      - **Coaching Platform Page:** This page is a good candidate for backlink efforts in the interim.
  - **Keyword Intent:** To avoid ranking for unrelated "roleplay" topics (e.g., cosplay), all content will use corporate/business-focused language to signal clear intent to Google.

### Future Content & Information Architecture

  - **Head Term Expansion:** Exec will create new landing pages or blog posts for high-intent head terms identified by GrowthX and Garett.
  - **Initial Focus:** Sales-related terms (Enablement, Onboarding, Training) and industry-specific terms (L\&D, Customer Support).
  - **Execution:** Exec will use existing design blocks to accelerate page creation.
  - **Platform Page Re-optimization:** The `/platform` page will be re-optimized for "Learning Experience Platform" (LEP) to align with a recognized industry category.
      - **Rationale:** This positions Exec within a familiar framework for buyers and allows for better directory listings.
      - **Action:** Update page content and consider changing the URL slug from `/platform` to `/learning-experience-platform` since the page has low existing link equity.
