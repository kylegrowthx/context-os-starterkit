  - **"Birth Injury" Topics:** On hold pending an ICP check with sales/Pedro.
      - **Rationale:** To confirm alignment with Tavrn's target customer profile and assess sensitivity.
  - **"Case Management Integrations" Content:** Paused until features are live.

      - **Function:** A new tool allowing customers to customize demand letters via free-text commands.
      - **Significance:** Solves a key customer pain point—the need for manual, specific edits—and leverages existing high-traffic demand-related content.
