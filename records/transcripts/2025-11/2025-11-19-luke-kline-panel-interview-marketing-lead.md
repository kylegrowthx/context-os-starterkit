## Relevance

**Community & Retention Strategy (Product)**
- 7-day post-signup critical window for proving value; GrowthX's current onboarding (on-demand calls) is non-scalable
- Champion framework: identify top 15-20 engaged members as word-of-mouth engine with exclusive access and sharing incentives
- Automated onboarding sequence followed by human touch for engaged members

**Content-Led Growth (Marketing)**
- High-quality, original gated content (deep competitor breakdowns, unique data analysis) as primary acquisition driver
- Luke's data: 2 pieces of in-depth content drive majority of paid signups; everything else is supporting channel
- Contrarian positioning and original insights drive member acquisition over generic content

**Influencer Co-Marketing (Channels)**
- Co-hosted workshops/roundtables (not paid promotion) with influencers your audience trusts
- High-value content benefits both audiences; drives signups through landing pages and follow-up sequences
- Luke example: Scott Prinker workshop yielded 500 signups

**Channel Testing & Focus (Execution)**
- Don't chase all channels simultaneously; pick one, master it, then expand
- Quality over quantity: 500 super-engaged members beats 5,000 lukewarm ones
- LinkedIn currently underperforming for GrowthX; need systematic testing of organic channels (LinkedIn, video, events)
