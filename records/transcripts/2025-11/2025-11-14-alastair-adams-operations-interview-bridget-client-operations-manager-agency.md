## Key Topics

### The Opportunity: Services-to-Platform Transition

  - The company is evolving from a profitable services agency (\~60 clients) to a platform-based business.
  - **Initial Focus:** Build foundational ops for the services side (e.g., client health tracking, playbooks).
  - **Future Focus:** Support the new platform with data modeling, usage analytics, and insights.
  - **Current State:** Client health tracking is subjective ("vibes"); metrics like churn and expansion are tracked, but a formal system is needed.
  - **Culture:** High autonomy is expected; the role requires self-management and identifying high-impact problems to solve.

### Handshake Experience: Data & Automation

  - **Context:** Handshake EMEA operations; role expanded from enablement to cover all go-to-market ops due to a US-based org gap.
  - **Key Project: Student/Job Market Balance Dashboard**
      - **Problem:** Poor employer sentiment from low platform usage.
      - **Solution:** Built a Looker dashboard (BigQuery backend) analyzing student vs. job demand by location and major.
      - **Impact:** Became the most-viewed dashboard, enabling sales to adapt strategy by targeting universities with in-demand majors or employers to fill student gaps.
  - **Key Project: CSM Slide Deck Automation**
      - **Problem:** Manual CSM slide deck creation was time-consuming.
      - **Solution:** Implemented **Matic** to connect to the database and auto-populate slides.
      - **Impact:** Reduced deck creation time by 95% (from hours to \~2 minutes).
  - **Other Automation:** Used Zapier for data-triggered alerts (e.g., low usage) to enable proactive customer success.

### Work Style & Feedback

  - **Ideal Workday:** Focus on data analysis to generate insights and build strategic tools.
  - **Performance Feedback:**
      - **GM:** Reliable, trustworthy, and proactive in surfacing insights.
      - **Director:** Needs to improve visibility into work to increase perceived impact.
  - **Recognition:** Won the "Move the Marketplace" award for the greatest org-wide impact.
  - **Reason for Leaving:** Layoff from Handshake's EMEA team (90% reduction) as the company shifted focus to a new AI product.
