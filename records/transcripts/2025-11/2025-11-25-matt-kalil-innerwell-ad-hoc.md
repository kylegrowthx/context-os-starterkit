## Key Topics

### Innerwell: Business Context

  - **Service:** Telehealth ketamine therapy.
  - **USP:** At-home lozenge delivery, avoiding clinic visits required for IV/IM treatments.
  - **Other Offerings:** EMDR, psychedelic integration, general therapy, and psychiatry.

### Content Strategy Pivot

  - **Problem:** The original "condition-led" strategy failed to rank, as keywords like "what is ADHD" were dominated by high-authority sites (.gov, .edu).
  - **Solution:** The new "treatment-led" strategy builds authority around specific services.
      - **Current Focus:** The Ketamine Therapy cluster, Innerwell's core USP.
      - **Future Focus:** The EMDR cluster.
  - **Goal:** Establish a repeatable content rubric with the Ketamine Therapy cluster, then apply it to other services.

### Ketamine Therapy Cluster Structure

  - The cluster is built around three user intent stages:
    1.  **General Awareness:** Top-of-funnel content (e.g., "Does ketamine show up on a drug test?").
    2.  **Treatment Access:** Mid-funnel content on logistics (e.g., insurance coverage, telehealth eligibility).
    3.  **Programmatic Series:** High-intent articles targeting users actively seeking treatment.

### Programmatic Workflow: "Ketamine Therapy for \[Condition\]"

  - **Purpose:** Target high-intent users with a scalable, templated approach.
  - **Standard Article Structure:**
      - [What is \[Condition\]?](https://fathom.video/share/ZGbqdi5VKydywuvUtz5AsE2Y9dgwh2-k?tab=summary&timestamp=466.0)
      - Traditional treatments & limitations.
      - How does ketamine therapy work?
      - How Innerwell approaches it (the conversion point).
      - Is it right for me?
      - **CTA:** "Try with Innerwell" → Free assessment.
  - **Generation Process:**
      - **1. Research:** Analyze top-ranking SERP results to identify key elements and find opportunities for "information gain."
      - **2. Draft (Atlas):** Use a neutral template to generate an initial draft.
      - **3. Restructure (Claude):** Use Claude to align the Atlas output with the standard template, providing "gold standard" examples for reference.
      - **4. Post-Processing (Manual):**
          - **Internal Links:** Manually add \~5 links from the "Internal Linking Repo" to top-performing pages, as LLMs are unreliable for this.
          - **Quality Control:** Use the "On-Page Checklist" to ensure all SEO and formatting standards are met.
