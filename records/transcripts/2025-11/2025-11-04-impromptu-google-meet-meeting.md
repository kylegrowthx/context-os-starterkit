## Key Topics

### Tavern: Reporting Strategy for New Clients

  - **Looker:**
      - **Focus:** "Overall Sessions" (purple bar), viewed YTD to show long-term trend.
      - **Rationale:** "Engaged Sessions" is ambiguous; YTD view provides context for early spikes.
      - **Finding:** Tavern's early traffic (200 clicks in 7 weeks) is exceptionally strong.
  - **GSC:**
      - **Focus:** Clicks and impression growth.
      - **Rationale:** Impression data is less reliable post-Sept 15th update, but early impression growth is a key indicator of future traffic.
      - **Finding:** Strong branded search growth suggests effective external marketing (e.g., influencers, paid ads).
  - **Scrunch:**
      - **Guideline:** Defer reporting until there's meaningful data to avoid setting false expectations.
      - **Action:** Align Scrunch prompts with Tavern's feature-led content clusters (e.g., "retrieval," "demand").
      - **Rationale:** This creates a direct link between content strategy and LLM performance, enabling clear diagnosis of what's working.
  - **Reporting Cadence:**
      - **Early Stage:** Weekly check-ins to build momentum with small wins (e.g., SERP features).
      - **Mature Stage (Months 3-4):** Shift to monthly deep-dive reports.
      - **Rationale:** This timing aligns with when performance data becomes stable enough to identify underperforming content and inform strategy pivots.

### InnoWell: Performance & Cadence

  - **Redirect Analysis:**
      - **Problem:** The current Looker setup obscures the impact of recent redirects.
      - **Solution:** Create a Looker dashboard that cohorts traffic by URL type:
          - Old Interwell URLs
          - Old Growthx URLs
          - New Growthx URLs
      - **Rationale:** This dashboard will prove that traffic successfully migrated to the new URLs and is now stabilizing/growing.
      - **Blocker:** Awaiting access from Akina to execute the final batch of redirects.
  - **Content Cadence:**
      - **Problem:** The current 2 articles/month cadence is insufficient to meet growth expectations.
      - **Finding:** Programmatic content creation is highly scalable (e.g., 2 articles in 45 mins).
      - **Action:** Matthew will assess capacity to increase the cadence, targeting 10 articles/week by EOY.

### Goodwill: Strategy Pivot

  - **Problem:** The glossary content is low-converting and being cannibalized by LLMs.
  - **Opportunity:** Tool pages are high-converting and offer a scalable programmatic strategy.
  - **Recommendation:** Pause glossary production and reallocate resources to tool pages.
  - **Rationale:** This pivot directly supports the client's conversion goals and enables a more efficient content strategy.
