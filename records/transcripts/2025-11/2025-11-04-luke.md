## Key Topics

### The "Software Eating Labor" Vision

  - GrowthX's core philosophy is that AI will replace labor, not just add SaaS features.
  - This model captures the full value of a person, not just a tool, enabling a \~$15k/mo price point that closes deals in a week.
  - **Contrast with AirOps:** AirOps is a feature-based SaaS tool. GrowthX is a service that replaces entire teams, with its platform as the internal engine.

### The AI-Human System

  - The model combines specialized AI agents with a human-in-the-loop for quality and strategy.
  - **AI Agents:**
      - Research, writing, and opportunity surfacing.
  - **Human Roles:**
      - **Editor:** Final writing review.
      - **Domain Expert:** Subject matter check (e.g., a developer for Webflow content) to ensure accuracy.

### Current Service & Traction

  - **Webflow Case Study:**
      - **Project:** Create hundreds of detailed integration guides (e.g., Webflow + Stripe).
      - **Process:** A dedicated "lane" (content factory) was built, calibrated for quality, and now publishes 30–40 articles/week.
      - **Outcome:** The content is highly useful, driving long user sessions and being cited on sales calls.
  - **Growth & Retention:**
      - Grew to \~$14M ARR in one year.
      - 80%+ retention rate on annual contracts.
  - **Go-to-Market:**
      - Inbound-only via socials and workshops; outbound is paused due to capacity limits.
      - **Future Plan:** Launch a self-serve, product-light package (\~$12k/yr) to enable scalable outbound.

### Product Roadmap: "Content OS"

  - The internal platform will become a public product.
  - **Core Function:** Treat content as a product, not a one-off asset.
  - **Features:**
      - Tracks performance of individual content pieces.
      - Surfaces opportunities for improvement (e.g., keyword gaps).
      - Triggers AI-driven workflows (e.g., rewrites, link additions).
  - **Goal:** Provide a central "codebase" for marketing artifacts, similar to how engineers use a codebase.

### Marketing Role & Culture

  - **Primary Need:** A marketing leader to define GrowthX's messaging and connect its disparate offers into a coherent GTM strategy.
  - **Culture:**
      - High trust and full ownership for all team members.
      - Remote-first with a strong emphasis on proactive communication to align the team on new concepts.
      - CEO Marcel's marketing background ensures strong buy-in and resources for experimentation.
