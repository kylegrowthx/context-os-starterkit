## Key Topics

### Campaign Performance & Strategy

  - **Strong Performance:**
      - **LinkedIn (HeyReach):** Connection acceptance rate \>20%; reply rate \~20% (on KPI).
      - **Email (Instantly):** 40 positive leads generated; 1 positive reply per 217 leads (nearly 2x the 1:400 market benchmark).
  - **Strategic Adjustments:**
      - **Volume:** Increased email infrastructure to scale volume, as LinkedIn outreach is capped at 60 connection requests/day.
      - **Sender Alias:** Jason Gong is now the primary sender (75% of volume) to avoid setting false expectations from Marcel's name. Mark Lim will compare reply rates for both aliases.
      - **Repetition:** Re-engaging past webinar invitees is acceptable, as the low-commitment offer is unlikely to cause annoyance.

### List Building & Management

  - **New Lists:** Understory is building lists from high-intent sources.
      - Pavilion & Exit 5 member directories
      - Competitor event attendee lists
      - Competitor LinkedIn page followers (e.g., Profound, Scrunch)
  - **Centralized Storage:** All lists will be consolidated in a Google Sheet and GrowthX's Clay instance, with a "Source" column for clear tracking.

### New Strategy: Evergreen Intent Engine

  - **Goal:** Automate outreach to warm leads who show intent by engaging with LinkedIn content.
  - **Tool:** Sift, which monitors LinkedIn accounts (GrowthX, competitors) and filters engagements by ICP.
  - **Workflow:**
    1.  **Sift → Clay:** Push ICP-filtered engagement data into a Clay table.
    2.  **Clay Enrichment:** Add account data and apply custom logic.
    3.  **Clay → Outreach:** Push enriched leads to Instantly or HeyReach for automated, personalized campaigns.

### Process & Future Growth

  - **Process Improvement:** To prevent project stalls, Jason Gong will provide clear, prioritized project definitions. Understory will proactively flag optimization opportunities for Jason's approval.
  - **Future Growth:**
      - **Hiring:** Jason Gong is hiring a demand gen/GTM specialist to increase internal capacity.
      - **Ads:** GrowthX will consider paid ads once the community motion is repeatable. Understory's ad management service requires a minimum $10k/channel ad spend.
