### Process Improvement: Technical Accuracy

  - **Problem:** Ensuring technical accuracy is a challenge, as identified by Asher.
  - **Solution:** A new process was piloted on the "Securing MCP server" article.
      - **Process:** An AI tool cross-referenced the article against 7+ external sources.
      - **Result:** Identified one minor section needing revision, which was completed.
  - **Decision:** This process will be applied to all future articles to guarantee accuracy.

  - **Problem:** The "AI workloads" tag is too broad for specialized articles on "agentic AI."
  - **Rationale:** "Agentic AI" is a distinct, high-volume keyword that deserves its own tag for better organization and SEO targeting.
  - **Decision:** Add an "Agentic AI" tag to the content management system.
