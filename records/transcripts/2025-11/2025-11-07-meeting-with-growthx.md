## Key Topics

### Zipsec's SEO Challenge & Strategic Goals

  - **Challenge:** Zipsec has a zero SEO baseline and needs a strategic partner to build a foundation for long-term growth.
  - **Strategic Goals:**
      - **Partner Keywords:** Rank for terms like "Jamf," "Intune," and "Okta" by positioning Zipsec as the easier, faster way to implement these tools.
      - **MSP Preemption:** Position Zipsec as a superior alternative to Managed Security Providers (MSPs).
  - **Target Audience:** Small to mid-market companies (10–250 employees).

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Content Engine:** Publish 5 educational articles/week (definitions, listicles, comparisons) to build domain authority.
  - **Backlinking:** Not a core service. GrowthX's success (e.g., Tiro, Betagrid) relies on a high-volume content engine, not paid links.
  - **Fact-Checking Process:** A proprietary agentic workflow ensures accuracy and credibility.
      - **Mechanism:** The system extracts passages from drafts and validates them against a confidence threshold.
      - **Outcome:** Flags nuanced statements (e.g., specific percentages) as potentially unreliable, prompting a human editor to rewrite for accuracy.
  - **Technical SEO:** GrowthX will provide recommendations for SEO-optimized blog templates and will handle all content staging, publishing, and meta-tagging.

### Logistics & Pricing

  - **Reporting:** A Looker dashboard will track traffic, engagement, and LLM visibility (citations, prompts).
  - **Attribution:** GrowthX can hook into Zipsec's existing attribution to track downstream metrics like conversion rates.
  - **Review Process:** A single point of contact is needed to prevent content review from becoming a bottleneck.
      - **Initial:** Dave Katz.
      - **Future:** A new Content Marketing Engineer (hiring in progress).
  - **Pricing:** A "friends and family" discount was offered.
      - **Phase 1:** $25k/mo (down from $30k).
      - **Phase 2:** $12k/mo (down from $15k).
