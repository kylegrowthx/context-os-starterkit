## Context

This is a 1:1 between Daniel Lopes (VP Product at GrowthX) and Renato Valdes Olmos (CEO at GrowthX). The meeting covers GrowthX's two strategic product initiatives — CheckNet (a competitive intelligence platform built on GrowthX's AI visibility research) and Content OS (an internal content operations platform for existing clients). Daniel walked through product roadmaps, launch milestones, go-to-market positioning decisions, and the two-tier service model GrowthX is adopting to serve different customer segments — those who want full managed service and those who want to self-operate with assistance. The conversation also touched on competitive validation from Graphite's pivot and staffing plans for both products.

## Key Topics

### Market Context & Competitor Intel

- **Graphite.io Pivot:** Graphite laid off its entire product team to focus on a full-service, human-led model.
  - **Significance:** This validates our hybrid approach, as Graphite sunset a powerful client-facing platform to avoid competing with internal teams.
- **Client Segmentation:** Two primary client types inform the service model:
  1. **"Do it for me" (e.g., Lovable):** High budget, low time; trusts us to run the platform.
  2. **"Internal teams" (e.g., Grammarly):** Has internal teams; concerned about job displacement.

### Content OS: Strategy & Roadmap

- **Service Model:** A two-tiered model addresses the client segments.
  - **"We operate for you":** Full-service, team-operated model for clients like Lovable.
  - **"You operate yourself":** Co-piloting model for clients with internal teams.
- **Mandatory Onboarding:** Both models require a Solutions Engineer for initial setup and calibration.
  - **Tasks:** Custom content types, CMS publishing workflows, and core artifact calibration (company, personas, writing guidelines).
- **Phased Launch Plan:**
  - **December:** Launch core features (Pages, Opportunities, Assignments) for *new content only*.
    - **Scope Cuts:** Optimization features are out of scope for now. The Workflow/Pipelines module is feature-flagged for full-service clients only.
    - **Feature-Flagged:** Knowledge Base and Assistant modules are hidden due to complexity.
  - **January:** Focus on workspace setup and user management.
    - **Deliverables:** Workspace calibration (artifacts), a basic "client" user role, LLM opportunity imports, and a basic optimization assignment type.
  - **February 1st:** Target milestone for a usable client product.

### CheckNet: Competitive Intelligence Product

- **Launch:** Public index and prompt management to launch in late November/early December.
- **Public Index:** A free, public site with ~25,000 companies and 14 days of data from "evaluation" prompts.
- **Private Area (Paid):**
  - **Prompt Management:** Access to a full library of prompts (Awareness, Comparison, etc.) and the ability to create custom prompts.
  - **Customization:** Control over competitor selection and longer data retention periods.
- **Monetization:** Webflow has expressed interest in licensing the data.

### GrowthX Website & Operations

- **Ownership:** The site must move from the "raise" domain to our own ownership.
- **Development Speed:** Requires dedicated resources to accelerate development.
- **Proposal:** Renn sent a proposal outlining options for dedicated resources.
