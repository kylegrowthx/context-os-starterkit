  - **Publishing Process:**

  - **Post-Publication Edits:** Published articles can be edited later to correct any flagged issues.

### Product Messaging Alignment

  - **Problem:** The "Gun Detection" article confused two distinct products: Access Intelligence and Threat Detection.
  - **Access Intelligence Product:**
      - **Function:** Filters false door sensor alerts (e.g., Door Forced Open, Door Held Open).
      - **Key Stat:** **95% alarm reduction** is specific to this product.
      - **Rationale:** Saves security teams significant time by reducing noise from finicky sensors.
  - **Threat Detection Product:**
      - **Function:** Detects external threats (e.g., tailgating, brandishing).
      - **Rationale:** Requires advanced adjudication beyond simple sensor data.
  - **Clarification:** Tailgating is now a Threat Detection feature, not Access Intelligence.
  - **Out of Scope:** Ambient's system does not currently detect shoplifting.
