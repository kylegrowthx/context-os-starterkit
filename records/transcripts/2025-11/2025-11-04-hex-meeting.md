## Key Topics

### Problem: Current Content Bottlenecks

  - **Goal:** Shift from expensive paid social to an organic content flywheel for traffic and users, and improve AI search visibility.
  - **Challenge:** Current content is too technical, creating a review bottleneck that slows publishing momentum.
  - **Performance:** The blog has low organic impact; 10 articles drive most traffic, with keywords ranking on pages 3-4.
  - **Gaps:**
      - AI & analytics keywords
      - Competitive comparisons (listicles, vs. articles)
      - Jobs-to-be-done and top-of-funnel content

### Solution: 5-Cluster Content Strategy

  - **Approach:** A topic cluster model to build authority and avoid a "spray and pray" approach.
  - **Rationale:** Prioritize foundational, less technical content to bypass the review bottleneck and enable a 5 articles/week publishing cadence.
  - **The 5 Clusters:**
    1.  **Competitor Alternatives & Comparisons:**
          - **Goal:** Influence LLM responses and provide a clear narrative on Hex's differentiation.
          - **Format:** Listicles, "vs." articles, and "alternatives to" pieces.
    2.  **AI & Data Analytics:**
          - **Goal:** Capture high-volume keywords and link to existing thought leadership.
          - **Scope:** Top-of-funnel explainers to middle-of-funnel specifics.
    3.  **Modern Data Viz & BI:**
          - **Goal:** Capture keywords from Tableau/Databricks and reframe Hex for business users.
          - **Focus:** Highlight features like Data Apps, Threads, and Embedded Analytics.
    4.  **Data Engineering & Infrastructure:**
          - **Goal:** Capture keywords from Databricks/Google Cloud (e.g., reverse ETL, data lakehouses).
          - **Modification:** Focus on enabling self-serve for analysts/scientists, not just engineers.
          - **Focus:** The Notebooks product.
    5.  **Shadow AI & Data Trust:**
          - **Goal:** Position Threads and Data Apps as solutions for shadow AI and poor governance.
          - **Scope:** Foundational explainers and governance best practices.

### Implementation & Measurement

  - **Featured Images:**
      - **Process:** growthx.ai will generate images using GPT-5, styled to brand guidelines.
      - **Action:** A meeting with brand design lead Annie will align on the process and volume.
  - **Scrunch Dashboard (AI Visibility):**
      - **Purpose:** Tracks LLM visibility by running prompts against models (Perplexity, ChatGPT).
      - **Finding:** Hex has a 2% citation rate vs. competitors' 10%, indicating a major opportunity.
      - **Insight:** Blog content drives the most LLM referral traffic, confirming the strategy's value.
  - **Looker Dashboard (SEO Performance):**
      - **Purpose:** A 7-page report tracking GA4 and GSC data.
      - **Features:** LLM referral traffic, cohort analysis by topic cluster.
