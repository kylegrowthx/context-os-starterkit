## Key Topics

### Background & Motivation

  - George Chasiotis scaled his first agency, Minuttia, to 19–20 people before losing interest.
  - A second agency, co-founded this year, is winding down by year-end.
  - **Motivation:** Seeking a new challenge; GrowthX is his first priority.

### Case Study: SwagDrop (SEO for B2B Leads)

  - **Context:** A promotional products client was struggling during COVID.
  - **Strategy:**
      - **Initial:** Build topical authority with listicles, which dominated SERPs at the time.
      - **Refinement:** Pivot from broad traffic to high-value B2B leads by targeting specific use cases (e.g., "rebranding for 1,200 employees").
  - **Outcome:** SEO became the \#1 lead source, beating competitors like Swag.com. The team also suggested the rebrand to "SwagDrop.com."
  - **Learned Lesson:** An HR glossary diluted the site's focus. **Insight:** Target audiences with specific content (e.g., "Bamboo HR alternatives") and retarget, rather than creating broad, dilutive resources.

### Case Study: MailSearch (AEO & Brand)

  - **Context:** A technical SaaS client with a strong GitHub repo.
  - **Observation:** New content ranked on Google and in LLMs within hours.
  - **Hypothesis:** AEO success requires a strong brand, not just SEO tactics.
      - **Brand Strength:** A robust GitHub repo provided LLMs with deep product context.
      - **Reputation:** High G2 review counts correlate with better LLM performance.
  - **Recommendation:** For brands lacking this foundation, run campaigns to build online reputation (e.g., G2 reviews) to accelerate AEO results.

### Strategic Frameworks

  - **Short- vs. Long-Term Strategy:**
      - **Existing Content:** Start with content updates, then identify and fill gaps.
      - **New Site:** Prioritize high-intent pages (alternatives, comparisons, category lists).
  - **Content Audit & Prioritization:**
      - **Method:** An "opportunity score" system to prioritize content updates.
      - **Process:** Weight business data (leads, revenue) with organic data (keywords, sessions) to identify high-value pages for refresh.
      - **Gap:** Current tools (ClearScope, Profound) lack business data integration.
  - **Identifying & Fixing Strategic Misalignments:**
      - **Problem:** At Toggle (time tracker), new content failed to perform despite a strong brand.
      - **Root Cause:** A large volume of underperforming content (content debt) from 5–10 years was dragging down overall site performance.
      - **Solution:** A major content audit, pruning, and consolidation effort was required before new content could succeed.
