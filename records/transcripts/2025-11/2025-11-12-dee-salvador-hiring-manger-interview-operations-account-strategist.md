## Key Topics

### The Evolving Account Strategist Role

  - **Business Model:** GrowthX is an agency using an internal AI platform to generate organic blog content (5 articles/week). This platform will eventually become an external product.
  - **New Team Structure:**
      - **Account Strategist:** Owns 8–9 accounts. Manages client relationships, scope, and renewals.
      - **Managing Editor:** Creates content for 2–3 clients using the AI platform.
      - **Directors:** A pool of floating experts (SEO, AEO) providing deep strategic support on demand.
  - **Focus:** The role is a client success function, not sales. It requires marketing expertise to present as an expert and build trust.

### Candidate Profile: Dee Salvador

  - **Background:** Psychology degree; Master's in Clinical Mental Health Counseling.
  - **Current Role (Kuno Creative):** SEO Program Manager.
      - Main POC for all accounts, from pitches to execution.
      - Collaborates with content writers on outlines and keywords.
      - Involved in renewal discussions.
  - **Past Leadership Roles (Neil Patel Digital, Grow, Logical Position):**
      - Managed teams of 5–9 SEO strategists.
      - Oversaw large account portfolios (70–90 accounts).
      - Shifted from leadership to a strategic role to focus on implementation and direct results.

### Account Management Philosophy & Skills

  - **Core Philosophy:** Build trust and transparency through active listening and invested questioning.
  - **Project Management:**
      - Uses calendar time-blocking and Basecamp for task tracking.
      - Prioritizes by identifying and resolving easy roadblocks first to maintain momentum.
  - **Client Management Example:**
      - **Problem:** A client's glossary page was off-topic after a content writer was removed from scope.
      - **Action:** Dee transparently explained the situation, proposed repurposing the content as an educational blog, and managed the 3-week process to publication.

### GrowthX Context & Challenges

  - **Client Pain Points:**
      - **AEO Optimization:** The primary driver for new clients.
      - **Perfectionism vs. Velocity:** Clients often prioritize minor edits over rapid publishing, hindering AEO goals.
  - **Internal Tools:**
      - **SEO:** SEMrush, Scrunch
      - **Analytics:** GSC, GA4
      - **Project Mgmt:** Linear (engineering), Airtable (client updates)
      - **Communication:** Slack, Notion, Google Docs
  - **Culture & Growth:**
      - **Challenge:** Building morale is difficult with a global, remote team and a high-growth phase requiring significant focus on work.
      - **Support:** Training and certifications are approved on a case-by-case basis if relevant to the role.
