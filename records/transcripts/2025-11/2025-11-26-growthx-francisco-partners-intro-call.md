## Key Topics

### The Problem: FPO's Content Partner Gap

  - FPO's Marketing Operating Partner, Jim Kingsbury, needs to replace X Funnel, a top-performing AI search partner that was acquired by HubSpot.
  - The portfolio has diverse needs, requiring a new set of vetted partners.

### The Solution: GrowthX's End-to-End Model

  - GrowthX is an end-to-end agency that handles the full content lifecycle:
      - SEO/GEO strategy
      - Content creation
      - CMS staging & publishing
      - LLM visibility & monitoring
  - **Goal:** Enable clients to scale high-quality content that feels human-written, solving for time-to-value, quality control, and cost management.
  - **Team:** AI workflows are guided by human experts, including a Chief Content Officer who was a former editor-in-chief at TechCrunch.

### The Process: Two-Phase Engagement

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks)**
      - **Goal:** Build the context engineering needed for effective AI workflows.
      - **Client Commitment:** Low (1-hr kickoff, 45-min deep dive, 15-30 min CMS access).
      - **Deliverables:**
          - Context artifacts (e.g., writing guidelines, persona docs)
          - 10–20 published articles
          - 3–6 month content roadmap
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12 months)**
      - **Goal:** Scale content production based on the roadmap.
      - **Production:** Targets \~5 articles/week.
      - **Workflow:** GrowthX handles all execution; clients only review drafts and provide feedback.
      - **Scope Expansion:** Typically starts with editorial (blog) and expands to programmatic content (e.g., vendor directories, calculators) or distribution channels (e.g., newsletters).

### The Challenge: Ensuring Technical Authority

  - **FPO's Concern:** B2B SaaS companies require deep technical expertise to avoid publishing content that damages their brand, even if it performs well for SEO.
  - **GrowthX's Solution:** A multi-step workflow designed for technical accuracy.
    1.  **Artifacts:** Initial strategy sprint defines brand guidelines, personas, and source requirements.
    2.  **Agentic Research:** AI agents conduct deep research (e.g., 38 mins for one guide), using tools like Excel and Tably, and re-running searches to fill gaps.
    3.  **Knowledge Graph:** Research is stored for semantic retrieval, speeding up future content creation.
    4.  **Fact-Checking:** An automated fact-checker validates every passage; human editors review and approve suggested rewrites for inaccuracies.
    5.  **Focus:** The goal is authoritative, SEO-optimized content. GrowthX can free up internal experts to focus on thought leadership.

### The Proof: Client Success & ROI

  - **Key Metrics:** GrowthX tracks ROI via traffic, engagement, and conversions, reported in custom Looker dashboards.
  - **Example Results:**
      - **Augment Code:** Tripled organic traffic since starting in June.
      - **Ramp:** Built a programmatic vendor directory and per diem calculator, expanding Ramp's search presence to competitive terms (e.g., "ClickUp vs. Airtable").
      - **Webflow:** Started with developer docs and is now expanding to a templates library.
