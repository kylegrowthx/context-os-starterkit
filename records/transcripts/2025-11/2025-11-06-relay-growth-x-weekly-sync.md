  - **Goal:** Increase content velocity before the holidays; new topics are needed beyond the current AI focus.
  - **New Cluster 1: Accountants & Bookkeepers (A\&B)**
      - **Audience:** Small business accountants and bookkeepers.
      - **Rationale:** A key referral channel for new SMB clients. Content will focus on firm efficiency and how Relay helps their clients.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will conduct keyword research. Zoe will provide messaging docs from the A\&B team.
  - **New Cluster 2: Financial Planning (for Jan 2026 Product Launch)**

### 2026 Strategic Vision: Interactive Content

  - **Concept:** An immersive, interactive "mini-site" for a specific vertical (e.g., home services).
  - **Features:** Deep-dive content, calculators, quizzes, and animations.
  - **Goal:** Create a full-funnel experience from top-of-funnel discovery to registration.
