## Key Topics

### Problem: Inconsistent Quality & QA Gaps

  - **Context:** Bishara's current role involves managing competing deadlines for SEO, social media, and daily video publishing.
  - **Challenge:** A landing page was created with content that mismatched its promotional email.
      - **Impact:** This inconsistency risked confusing customers and damaging brand trust.
      - **Root Cause:** QA was an afterthought, occurring too late in the process.
  - **Challenge:** Video content was published with grammatical errors and internal titles.
      - **Impact:** This damaged the brand's professional image.
      - **Root Cause:** No formal QA process existed for video content.

### Solution: Structured Workflows & AI Scaling

  - **Solution 1: Email/Landing Page QA Process**
      - **Process:** Content Writer → Bishara (initial review) → Marketing Manager → Bishara (final review of agency test email) → Agency schedules.
      - **Outcome:** Eliminated quality errors.
      - **Refinement:** After training the content team to self-manage the process, Bishara streamlined it by removing his review step to prevent QA bottlenecks.
  - **Solution 2: Video QA Process**
      - **Action:** Bishara and the Marketing Manager built a new workflow to ensure all video content is reviewed before publication.
      - **Outcome:** The new process is now "smooth" and has eliminated quality issues.
  - **Solution 3: AI-Powered Blog Scaling**
      - **Goal:** Increase blog output from 1 to 2 posts/week without increasing writer workload.
      - **Method:** A custom ChatGPT generates drafts from video transcripts, which are fed in to ensure a natural, on-brand voice.
      - **Process:** Bishara provides topics/keywords → Writers use AI to draft → Bishara performs final QA.
      - **Outcome:** The content pipeline is now covered through mid-December.

### The Managing Editor Role at GrowthX

  - **Responsibilities:**
      - Implement client strategy set by the Director.
      - Manage 1–5 client accounts with varying content needs (e.g., 5–26 pieces/week).
  - **Evolving Structure:**
      - **Current:** Content Manager runs workflows → Managing Editor edits in Google Docs.
      - **Future:** The role will be end-to-end (workflow + editing) to streamline the process.
  - **Success Metrics:**
      - Deliver high-quality content with minimal client feedback.
      - Drive measurable client outcomes to secure renewals.
  - **Tools:**
      - **SEO:** SEMrush
      - **Analytics:** Looker (until a proprietary tool launches in \~1 month)
      - **Collaboration:** JIRA, Notion

### Logistics & Availability

  - **Required Availability:** 9 AM–1 PM PT for client calls and team syncs.
  - **Bishara's Travel:**
      - **Dates:** Feb 19–Mar 15, 2025 (Australia).
      - **Impact:** The 9 AM–1 PM PT window will be difficult to maintain due to the time difference.
      - **Resolution:** Bishara can take this period as unpaid vacation.
  - **Contractor Vacation Pay:** Bishara asked if contractors are paid for vacation.
      - **Resolution:** Megan Dickey will ask HR for clarification.
