GrowthX and Aembit reviewed content production blockers, traffic metrics, and proposed a new `/guides` section to accelerate publishing velocity. ### Content Production & Blockers

  - **Published:** 2 articles, 5 glossary pages.
  - **Awaiting Aembit Review:**

      - 2 article outlines: "IAM for Agentic AI" & "Workload Access Management."

  - **Quality Control:** GrowthX's system now cross-references a dedicated knowledge base to ensure technical accuracy for Aembit-specific claims.

### Traffic & Performance Analysis

  - **Overall Organic Traffic:** Shows growth since June.
      - **Metrics:** 293 active users (up from last 3 weeks), 39% increase in views, higher average time on page.
      - **Opportunity:** Increase internal linking to raise the pages-per-visit metric from \~1.5 to 2+.
  - **GrowthX-Only Content:** Experienced a dip in impressions and clicks in early November.
      - **Cause:** Likely due to a slower publishing pace and seasonal factors.
      - **Context:** The long-term trend is positive, but consistent publishing is needed to maintain momentum.
  - **Glossary Traffic:**
      - **Baseline:** 7 active users, 25 views across 12 pages.
      - **Note:** A previous traffic surge was attributed to AI agent scraping, not human engagement.
  - **LLM Traffic:**
      - **Status:** Growing but not yet substantial.
      - **Sources:** Primarily Perplexity and ChatGPT.
      - **Action:** GrowthX will continue optimizing content for AI overviews and listicle formats.

### Proposed Strategy: `/guides` Section

  - **Problem:** Aembit's internal review process slows content production, creating a bottleneck.
  - **Solution:** Create a separate `/guides` section for GrowthX to publish high-volume SEO content (e.g., listicles, comparisons) at a faster pace.
  - **Rationale:** This separates fast-moving SEO content from Aembit's more curated blog, allowing both to operate at their optimal pace.
  - **Precedent:** This model is successful for clients like Augment Code, Lovable, and Prophecy.
