## Key Topics

### Content OS & Strategy Review

  - GrowthX presented the new Content OS in Airtable, featuring 60+ blog ideas clustered by topic.
  - **David's Feedback:**
      - **"Straightforward Targets":** The current topics feel too obvious.
      - **"Sales Guidance":** More content is needed on sales teams and the "automated workflow with agents for sales AGI."
  - **GrowthX's Rationale:** The 8-week sprint prioritizes foundational content to build organic traffic quickly. "Harder" topics are a quarterly strategy, not an immediate focus.
  - **Keyword Strategy:**
      - **Primary Goal:** Balance high-volume, high-difficulty keywords (e.g., "AI agents," 27k searches/mo) with low-volume, high-intent keywords (e.g., "AI workplace automation agents," 10 searches/mo).
      - **Why:** Low-volume keywords, while niche, have a higher audience overlap and are easier to rank for, driving qualified leads.
      - **Data Context:** SEMrush data is a conservative estimate. Actual traffic will be higher because articles rank for many related keywords, and AI-related search volume is growing faster than historical data reflects.

### AI Search Optimization (AEO)

  - **Goal:** Appear in AI chatbot answers (e.g., ChatGPT) for direct user queries.
  - **Method:** AEO is achieved via content structure, not keyword difficulty.
      - **Format:** Skimmable, chunked paragraphs with one idea per sentence.
      - **Elements:** TLDRs and FAQs that AI models can easily pull for answers.
  - **Key Insight:** AEO performance is uncorrelated with Google rank. A page 4 article can be a top AI answer if it's structured well.
  - **Actionable Framework:** Evaluate topics by asking, "Could this article help a user answer a question they'd pose to ChatGPT?"

### Airtable Review Process Refinement

  - **Problem:** The team needs a clearer way to prioritize topics beyond a simple thumbs-up/down.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will tag topics in Airtable to create a two-tiered system for review.
      - **Foundational:** Core topics for the 8-week sprint.
      - **Extension:** Strategic reach goals for future consideration.
  - **Workflow:** Read AI will review the "extension" list to debate and stack-rank strategic priorities.
