### GrowthX's AI-Powered SEO Solution

  - **Core Offer:** On-site content creation (editorial & programmatic) at scale.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, $40k)**
          - Develop audience personas and brand voice artifacts.
          - Build a "Content OS" (roadmap) and deliver \~10 articles.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12-month retainer, $15k/mo)**
          - **Editorial:** 5 articles/week.
          - **Programmatic:** High-volume content (e.g., 100 articles/week).
  - **Quality Control:**
      - **Fact-Checking:** An AI workflow validates all claims against a pre-approved knowledge base.
      - **Human Oversight:** Managing editors review and refine all AI-generated content.
  - **Add-on Services ($9k–$15k/mo):**
      - Microsites & template libraries (e.g., Lovable project).
      - Newsletters.
      - Off-site content (e.g., Reddit posts).

  - **Timeline & Results:**
      - **Kickoff:** Within weeks of contract signing.
      - **Results:** Tangible wins expected in 3–4 months, faster for sites with high domain authority.
      - **Proof Point:** AugmentCode saw 2–3x traffic growth from new guides, driving 300 signups/week.
