## Key Topics

### Onboarding Process & Tracking

  - **Central Hub:** The "Company-Wide Onboarding" page in Notion contains all resources (articles, videos, meeting invites).
  - **Task Tracking:** The onboarding project in Linear duplicates these tasks for tracking.
      - [**Access Path:** `Projects` → `My Projects` → `[Your Onboarding Project]` → `Issues`.](https://fathom.video/share/bmGLFNzKo1amEzwAgsHuDDNUnaxALosu?tab=summary&timestamp=1009.0)
      - **Task Status:** Update each task's status (e.g., "Done") using the dotted circle icon.
      - **Project Status:** Mark the entire project "Completed" in the right-side properties panel when finished.

### Core Tools & Usage

  - **Slack:** Day-to-day communication.
      - **Geekbot:** Daily stand-ups.
          - **When:** Post-onboarding, after pod assignment.
          - **Why:** Replaces manual check-ins as the official way to report progress and blockers.
  - **Linear:** Project management and engineering tickets.
  - **Notion:** Knowledge hub for SOPs and client docs.
  - **Fathom:** AI note-taker for Zoom.
      - **Why:** Ensures all internal meetings are automatically accessible to the team without manual sharing.
      - **Exception:** Limit recordings for company-wide calls (e.g., All-Hands) to avoid duplication.
  - **Loom:** Screen recording for task demos.

### Company Policies & Logistics

  - **Work Overlap:** 4-hour minimum overlap with Pacific Time.
      - **Action:** Alice will clarify if this is a fixed block (e.g., 8 AM–12 PM PST) with Bridget (VP of Ops).
  - **Time Off (PTO):** Unlimited PTO; no pre-approval needed.
      - **Process:** Block time on the calendar with sufficient notice (1–2 weeks) and add your team.
  - **Employee Referrals:**
      - Engineering roles: up to $10k bonus.
      - Non-engineering roles: up to $5k bonus.
      - **Condition:** Referred hire must stay 6 months.
  - **IT Support:** Post issues in the `#it-and-tools` Slack channel.

### Team & Reporting Structure

  - **Danni:** Reports to **Jason Gong** (Growth Team).
  - **Victor & Kalil:** Report to **Matthew "Panza"** (temporary manager).
      - **Role:** Guides onboarding until pod assignment.
      - **Action:** Panza will assign an onboarding buddy for informal support.
