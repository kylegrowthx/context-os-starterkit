## Key Topics

### Target Audience & Product-Market Fit (PMF)

  - **Primary Audience:** Mid-market staffing firms (10+ recruiters in US/CA/UK).
      - **Rationale:** They have a clear, measurable ROI from Alex's 24/7 candidate outreach, which directly increases placements and revenue.
      - **Exclusion:** Firms with outsourced recruiters (e.g., India-based) are not a good fit, as the ROI is less pronounced.
  - **Secondary Audience:** Large enterprise customers.
      - **Challenge:** PMF is not yet established. Enterprise buyers focus on metrics like time-to-hire, not revenue, and require social proof ("who else is using this?").
      - **Status:** A few deals are in the pipeline that could provide the needed social proof.

  - Alex is rebranding from an "AI Interviewer" to an "Agentic Recruiting Platform" built from four specialized AI agents.
