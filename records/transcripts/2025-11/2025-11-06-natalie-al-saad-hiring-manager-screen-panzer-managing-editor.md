## Key Topics

### The Role: Managing Editor at GrowthX

  - **Focus:** Process-oriented role combining content editing with prompt engineering for GrowthX's internal tool, Atlas.
  - **Daily Tasks:**
      - Editing content for client delivery.
      - Operating content pipelines via Atlas.
      - Iterative improvement: Identifying pipeline flaws and filing tickets for dev cycles.
  - **Core Responsibilities:**
      - **Context Engineering:** Providing LLMs with authoritative sources (e.g., client docs) to ensure accuracy.
      - **Prompt Engineering:** Translating client needs into precise, actionable instructions for the LLM.
      - **Observability:** Using Atlas to trace the LLM's decision-making process to diagnose issues.
