## Overview

- **Navan's core value is driving adoption** by eliminating reasons for users to book outside the platform (e.g., loyalty points, mid-trip support), which solves the industry's common problem of \>50% off-platform spend.
- **A proprietary content engine** sources inventory from multiple sources (not just a GDS) to find the most competitive prices and offer more options, directly improving savings and adoption.
- **Unique policy features** like dynamic thresholds (adapting to market prices) and Navan Rewards (incentivizing users to save) enable flexible, cost-effective travel programs.
- **The 24-hour void period** for out-of-policy bookings secures prices while awaiting approval, preventing cost increases that often occur with legacy systems.

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Low Adoption & Off-Platform Spend

  - Business travel's main challenge is low platform adoption, with \>50% of spend often occurring off-platform.
  - This creates a lack of visibility for finance and accounting, as expenses are filed manually and often months late.
  - Navan's strategy is to eliminate all reasons for users to book outside the tool, ensuring policy compliance and spend visibility.

### The Solution: A Seamless, Integrated Platform

  - **User Experience (OBT):** A mobile-first online booking tool (OBT) with a consumer-grade interface.
  - **Automated Provisioning:** User data (cost centers, entity) is synced directly from HR systems, ensuring policies are always correct.
  - **Delegate Booking:** A single interface allows assistants to book for multiple travelers, regardless of location.
  - **Trip Management:** A single view for all trip details (flights, hotels, invoices) with in-app actions like seat selection and cancellations.
  - **Loyalty Program Integration:** Users earn and apply their personal loyalty points and benefits (e.g., free seat selection) directly in the tool, removing a key reason for booking elsewhere.

### Core Differentiators: Content & Policy

  - **Proprietary Content Engine:**
      - **Problem:** Most tools rely on a single Global Distribution System (GDS), which limits options and pricing.
      - **Solution:** Navan's engine connects to multiple sources, comparing fares to find the most competitive price and offering a wider selection.
  - **Dynamic Policy Engine:**
      - **Problem:** Static policies are either too restrictive in high season (blocking necessary travel) or too loose in low season (allowing overspending).
      - **Solution:** Dynamic thresholds adapt to real-time market prices, enabling travel during peak demand while ensuring cost control during off-peak periods.
  - **Navan Rewards:**
      - **Problem:** Users have no incentive to save the company money.
      - **Solution:** Gamifies savings by rewarding users with cash for personal travel when they book below the policy threshold.
      - **Why it works:** Creates a win-win-win: user earns, company saves, and Navan retains spend for personal trips.

### Mid-Trip Support & Expense Management

  - **Disruption Handling:** Automated workflows and in-house agents manage flight delays and cancellations, preventing costly offline interactions.
  - **Expense Capture:** Integrates with services like Uber/Lyft for automatic expense capture.
  - **Policy-Driven Cards:** Navan cards enforce policy at the point of sale (e.g., declining food purchases when not traveling), providing real-time control.
