## Context

This is a screening interview for an Account Executive role at GrowthX, conducted by Tyler Pavlas (founding AE at GrowthX). Justin Collins is an experienced enterprise software salesperson who spent his career at Jasper, an AI-powered content creation platform founded in 2022. He was hired as the second AE (one month after the founding AE) and built GrowthX's enterprise sales motion from scratch, scaling from a single $50K deal to $32M in enterprise ARR while the company transitioned from a prosumer-heavy model ($50M ARR) to enterprise-focused after ChatGPT's 2022 launch disrupted inbound demand.

Justin's core expertise is consultative selling and ROI-based positioning—translating time savings into dollar value to create executive-level business cases (e.g., Pilot Flying J saved $160K/year, which he used to expand from a $64K initial deal to multi-six-figure). The conversation explored whether his approach to building personalized sales processes, handling complex deal dynamics (mid-market, SMB, enterprise across verticals), and managing executive alignment fit GrowthX's current need: a partner to scale inbound demand through personalized sales execution while managing delivery constraints.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Sales Challenge

  - **High Inbound Demand:** GrowthX has strong interest from B2B software companies (e.g., Zapier, Brex, Webflow), but delivery capacity is the bottleneck.
  - **Need for Personalization:** Tyler's key challenge is building a personalized sales process (e.g., custom content refreshes) to increase prospect confidence, a task currently deprioritized to focus on client success.
  - **Ideal Client Profile:**
      - **Domain Authority:** Trust GrowthX to scale content without micromanagement.
      - **Content Type:** SEO-focused, not highly technical thought leadership.
      - **Executive Buy-in:** A champion who sees the vision and is a collaborator, not a roadblock.

### Justin's Experience at Jasper

  - **Role:** \#2 Account Executive at Jasper, hired one month after the founding AE.
  - **Growth:** Helped scale enterprise ARR from $50k to $32M.
  - **Context:** The company's business model shifted from prosumer-heavy ($50M ARR) to enterprise-focused after ChatGPT's launch dried up inbound leads.
  - **ACV Evolution:** Grew from an initial $15k to a $75k–$150k range, driven by adding solutions architect services.

### Key Strategy: Monetizing ROI

  - **Core Tactic:** Translate time savings into a direct dollar value to create a compelling business case for leadership.
  - **Method:** Use Glassdoor data to calculate the cost of equivalent headcount that Jasper replaces.
  - **Pilot Flying J Case Study:**
      - **Challenge:** Corporate skepticism toward AI.
      - **Land:** Won a \~$64k deal by building trust through hands-on trials and showing immediate productivity gains.
      - **Expand:** Used the ROI case (monetizing 3–5 hrs/user/wk saved → $160k/yr) to secure a multi-six-figure expansion, making Pilot a major client and public case study.
  - **Lesson Learned (ClickUp):** Justin missed connecting with leadership early, which led to churn. This reinforced the need for executive alignment to secure long-term partnerships.

### Pipeline Generation

  - **Inbound-to-Outbound Shift:** Justin adapted from managing high inbound volume (16 demos/wk) to building an outbound process from scratch post-ChatGPT.
  - **Successful Campaign:** Created a 3-day trial campaign for churned/lost business prospects.
      - **Process:** Automated outreach → 3-day platform access with daily hands-on support from Justin.
      - **Rationale:** The high-touch, personalized experience built trust and converted prospects by demonstrating value directly.
