## Key Topics

### Project Overview

  - **Concept:** A hub-and-spoke index of company pricing models.
  - **Purpose:** Blend organic content, link-building, and brand authority.
  - **Initial Scope:** Launch with \~20 pages, focusing on major players.
  - **Data Source:** All data is public (e.g., company pricing pages, blogs).
  - **Delivery:** Content is delivered as human-readable Google Docs for review and as JSON for technical ingestion.

### Page Structure & Content

  - Each company page will include:
      - **Pricing Models:** Current models used.
      - **Pricing History:** A timeline of past changes.
      - **Customer Sentiment:** Scraped highlights/lowlights from sources like G2.
      - **Metronome's Take:** An AI-generated snippet providing Metronome's expert opinion.
  - **Editorial Calibration:** The "Metronome's Take" pipeline was adjusted from a "hot takey" tone to a neutral/positive stance to align with brand voice.

### Strategic Goals & Future Vision

  - **Near-Term (Launch):** SEO/AEO play to capture traffic for searches like "OpenAI pricing."
  - **Mid-Term:** Mid-funnel asset for campaigns (e.g., "See what your competitors are pricing").
  - **Long-Term:** Onboarding ramp for the Q1 self-service product, potentially evolving into a sandbox deployment tool.
  - **Future Content:** The AI-driven process enables high-volume content creation (e.g., articles on pricing models) to build a dense, authoritative content network.
