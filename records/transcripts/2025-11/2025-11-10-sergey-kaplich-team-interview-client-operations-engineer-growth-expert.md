## Key Topics

### Background & Motivation

  - **Career Path:** Evolved from marketing to frontend engineering after being inspired by developer colleagues.
  - **Dual Passions:** Combines marketing experience with a love for building products using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
  - **Motivation for GrowthX:** Inspired by the CEO's video, which articulated content marketing pain points Sergey had personally experienced.

### Problem-Solving: Unclear Requirements & Scope Creep

  - **Scenario:** A backend manager mis-estimated a frontend project's complexity, assigning a 1-month timeline for a 3-month scope.
  - **Action:** Sergey identified the mismatch early and proactively communicated the issue to stakeholders.
  - **Proposed Solutions:**
      - **Option 1 (Chosen):** Prioritize features for a 1-month MVP, deferring others.
      - **Option 2 (Rejected):** Deliver full scope in 1 month with no testing.
      - **Option 3:** Add more engineers to meet the original deadline.
  - **Outcome:** The project launched successfully in 3 months. The manager acknowledged the initial mis-estimation, highlighting the value of early communication.

### Process: Balancing Speed & Quality

  - **Philosophy:** "Always be launching" MVPs to get work into users' hands quickly.
  - **Validation Tools:**
      - **LogRocket:** Used to record and analyze user sessions in a React app.
      - **Hotjar & Google Analytics:** Used for landing page and website analytics.
  - **Direct User Feedback:** Values direct user observation to uncover pain points and inform design/engineering improvements.

### Application: Strategic AI Content Generation

  - **Project 1 (HEIC Converter):**
      - **Method:** Generated blog posts entirely with AI, guided only by keywords.
      - **Result:** 0 traffic.
      - **Lesson:** Raw AI content without human guidance is ineffective.
  - **Project 2 (Blue Sky Directory):**
      - **Method:** Analyzed Google traffic to identify user needs (e.g., "how to bookmark posts").
      - **Action:** Used AI to generate blog posts specifically addressing those needs, optimizing for keywords and style.
      - **Result:** Hundreds of monthly users from Google and ChatGPT.
      - **Lesson:** Strategic AI application, informed by data, drives results.

### GrowthX Context (from Stevie)

  - **Stevie's Role:** Created to bridge technical and client ops needs; involves prompt engineering, project management, and technical SME support.
  - **Company Culture:** Fast-paced, scrappy, and experimental.
  - **Key Skill:** QA and a strong sense of ownership are critical, as the team moves quickly and everyone is expected to contribute to quality.
