  - **Problem:** Content approval delays were slowing the publishing cadence.
  - **Solution:** A new workflow was established to create a dedicated review window for Joel.
      - **New Cadence:** GrowthX will deliver content for approval by **Thursday EOD**.
      - **Rationale:** This gives Joel a buffer on Thursday and a clear review window on Friday, which is a lighter meeting day.
  - **Content Updates:**
      - **Conference Posts:** To preserve SEO ranking, future posts will be updated with new dates/venues instead of being rewritten.
      - **NGINX Content:** The "Employee Expense Cards" topic is paused due to an uncertain NGINX launch timeline. It will be replaced by a "Refunds and Travel Credits" article.

### New FAQ/Glossary Experiment

  - **Proposal:** A new FAQ hub designed to capture LLM traffic and improve SEO.
  - **Methodology:**
      - **Research:** Scrape Reddit for high-engagement questions to identify user pain points.
      - **Structure:** Each page will focus on a primary question, with related questions and answers below.
  - **MVP Plan:**
      - Launch with 10–20 pages to test for clear traction (e.g., citation growth).
      - **Rationale:** This low-risk approach avoids major structural changes to the site's existing FAQ page, which receives significant traffic (\~1,400 sessions/month).
  - **Team Feedback:**
      - Barry and Luke confirmed this approach was effective at previous companies.
      - Joel will share an offline resource on using ChatGPT for deep research to surface questions.
