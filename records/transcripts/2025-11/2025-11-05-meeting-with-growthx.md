## Key Topics

### Partnership Goals & Scope

  - **Strategic Reset:** Re-evaluate SEO and content strategy post-acquisition.
  - **Content Scaling:** Increase output while balancing SEO and AI search optimization.
  - **Internationalization:** Improve non-English content strategy beyond simple translation.
  - **Scope:** Blog and resource content; Pier 39's team retains product/use case pages.

### GrowthX's Two-Phase Approach

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint ($20k)**
      - **Goal:** Validate quality and roadmap before committing to a full partnership.
      - **Deliverables:**
          - **Artifacts:** Voice/tone guides, competitor analysis, topic clusters.
          - **Content:** 10 articles to demonstrate quality.
          - **Roadmap:** 12-month content plan.
      - **Purpose:** Artifacts serve as internal resources and as "context engineering" for GrowthX's AI workflows.
  - **Phase 2: Daily Publishing ($10k/mo)**
      - **Goal:** Execute the Phase 1 roadmap at scale.
      - **Cadence:** \~20 articles/month, a mix of net-new and refreshes based on potential ROI.
      - **Process:** GrowthX drafts → Pier 39 reviews → GrowthX publishes to Contentful.

### AI Search & SEO Strategy

  - **Dual Optimization:**
      - **SEO:** Keyword analysis (volume, difficulty) to outrank competitors.
      - **AI Search:** Audience-centric authority building to appear in AI answers.
  - **Content Structure:** Optimized for AI with clear headings, FAQs, and listicles.
  - **Measurement:**
      - **Tool:** Scrunch dashboard for tracking citations and presence.
      - **Metrics:** Prompts cited, citation sessions, clicks from AI search.
      - **Rationale:** AI clicks convert at 0.4x the rate of traditional search, making them a high-value target.

### International & Visual Content

  - **Geographic Strategy (France, Germany):**
      - **Approach:** Custom personas and use cases for each market, not just translation.
      - **Status:** GrowthX to confirm translation process and "one article, three versions" cadence.
  - **Rich Media:**
      - **Inclusion:** AI-generated cover images and infographics are standard.
      - **Process:** AI generates options from brand guidelines → editor selects → client reviews.
