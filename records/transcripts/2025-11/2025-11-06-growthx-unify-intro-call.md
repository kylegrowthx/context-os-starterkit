## Context

Unify is a GTM platform founded by Austin Hughes, who previously built the growth engine at Ramp during its hyper-growth phase. The platform consolidates intent signals, prospecting, and email sequencing into one streamlined tool, positioning itself as an alternative to multi-tool stacks like Sixth Sense + Zoom Info + Outreach. Charlie O'Neil joined as an early BDR and moved to the growth team, now managing automated outbound, inbound funnel optimization, and increasingly, organic content strategy.

Unify currently operates an automated content generation system using Profound (an AI platform) and n8n (automation tool) that publishes AI-generated articles to an "Explore" page for SEO/AEO. However, the system produces high-volume, low-quality output: AI-sounding tone and frequent hallucinations with incorrect citations that require Charlie to manually fact-check and rewrite 3+ articles nightly. This bottleneck is unsustainable as Unify scales, prompting the outreach to GrowthX. Charlie is the primary decision-maker on this call; Austin was invited but unable to attend due to founder commitments, though he has used GrowthX's services previously at Ramp.

## Key Topics

### Unify's Content Challenge

  - **Background:** Unify's founder, Austin, previously built the growth engine at Ramp. Unify is a streamlined GTM platform consolidating intent signals, prospecting, and sequencing into one tool.
  - **Current Workflow:** An automated system using Profound and n8n generates articles for the "Explore" page.
  - **Key Issues:**
      - **Quality:** Content has an unengaging, "AI-sounding" tone.
      - **Accuracy:** Frequent AI hallucinations and incorrect citations require extensive manual fact-checking.
      - **Bottleneck:** Charlie spends late evenings editing 3+ articles/day, making the workflow unsustainable.
  - **Goal:** Publish 5–7 high-quality articles/week to improve SEO/AEO and build long-term organic authority.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Model:** An end-to-end partnership, not just an agency, providing strategy, creation, and publishing.
  - **Core Differentiator:** Combines complex AI workflows with expert human oversight (Managing Director, Editor, Content Manager) to ensure quality and accuracy at scale.
  - **Process:**
      - **Strategy Sprint:** Develops an SEO/GEO roadmap based on ICP analysis.
      - **Execution:** Publishes daily to the CMS, with Unify's only task being draft review.
  - **Content Examples:**
      - **Ramp Vendor Directory:** Interactive charts with spend data to establish authority.
      - **Town (Accounting):** Accurate tax advice using a fact-checker agent and integrated brand quotes.
      - **Augment Code:** Technical content (e.g., "What is a REST API?") to capture long-tail search intent.

### Pricing & Logistics

  - **Pricing:**
      - **Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint):** $30k for a 4-week engagement, delivering a 6-month roadmap and 10 published articles.
      - **Phase 2 (Growth Execution):** $15k/month for ongoing content creation (target: 5–7 articles/week).
  - **Timeline:** GrowthX has high demand and can only commit to two new clients per week. A decision by end-of-month would secure a January start.
  - **Assets:** Unify has a comprehensive brand hub (built with Profound) containing voice guidelines, competitor analysis, and other inputs, which could accelerate the kickoff.
