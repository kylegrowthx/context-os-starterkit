**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- PSEO adoption is becoming a client expectation after the Ramp case study (600 pages in H1); Hyper Exponential sees PSEO as a path to faster growth, but viability depends on domain authority and niche selection (e.g., actuarial insurance with low-intent keywords have low competition potential).

## Key Topics

### Hyper Exponential (Flagged)

  - **Problem:** High-maintenance client with frequent scope changes and excessive line-editing on drafts.
  - **New Request:** Client is fixated on PSEO, likely influenced by a past GrowthX case study (Ramp).
  - **Opportunity:** Pivot to a scalable PSEO strategy to bypass editorial approval bottlenecks.
      - **Viable Niche:** Actuarial insurance topics have low competition (SERPs show PDFs) and high intent-match.
      - **Scalable Format:** A "topic + line of business" template could generate 50+ pages from one approved format.
  - **Action:** Jakub will scope a formal PSEO strategy proposal.

### Biologica (Renewal Risk)

  - **Problem:** High-touch content requires extensive legal and medical review, consuming \~50% of a writer's time.
  - **Renewal:** Jan 29. The account is not auto-renewing.
  - **Proposed Solution:** A new long-tail strategy focused on glossary and informational content.
      - **Rationale:** This content avoids specific medical claims, bypassing the high-cost review process.
      - **Status:** The client is aware of and interested in this lower-touch approach.
  - **Decision:** The team will evaluate if the account is worth retaining. The discussion will frame the choice as keeping Biologica vs. replacing it with a more profitable, lower-effort client.

### Sentinel One (Low Performer)

  - **Problem:** Editorial work is blocked by client topic selectivity and unreliable keyword tools (SEMrush, Ahrefs).
  - **Mitigation:** The large volume of programmatic CVE pages (1,000+ launched) fulfills contractual obligations and provides a buffer.
  - **Path Forward:** Kyle coached the team on engaging the client to understand their strategic priorities and proposing content refresh projects.
  - **Transition:** The account is transitioning from Katya to Bailey and Yana. The change is driven by scheduling (calls were at 7 AM for Katya), not performance.

### Interwell (Low Performer)

  - **Problem:** Organic traffic has fallen for weeks due to client-side errors (e.g., incorrect redirects).
  - **Client Pressure:** Akina (client POC) reports "pressure from above" to resolve issues.
  - **Status:** The team is working overtime to fix the redirects.
  - **Renewal:** March 3. A reset conversation is needed in January to align on the next three months.

### New Renewal Process

  - **Goal:** Formalize renewal decisions to proactively manage account health and profitability.
  - **Process:** Directors will submit a formal recommendation 90 days before renewal, outlining:
      - Account state
      - Recommendation (renew/don't renew)
      - Rationale and terms
