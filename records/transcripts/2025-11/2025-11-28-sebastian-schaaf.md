### Problem: Template Page Creation for Community Projects

  - **Goal:** Create template pages for high-quality community projects to diversify the template gallery beyond just blogs and portfolios.
  - **Challenge:** `growthx.ai`'s automated workflow requires the project's chat history for analysis, which is not available for public projects.
  - **Solution:** Sebastian will remix projects with their chat history and transfer them to `growthx.ai`'s workspace, providing the necessary context.

### Solution: Template Page Generation & V2 Design

  - **Wave 1:** `growthx.ai` will create pages for a 12-project list (7 existing + 5 new community projects).
  - **V2 Page Design:** `growthx.ai` will propose a V2 template page design to balance SEO with user experience.
      - **Rationale:** The current SEO-focused pages are not optimized for quick user comprehension.
      - **Goal:** Help users determine relevance within 2-3 seconds.
      - **Example Tweak:** Add "Who is this for?" boxes at the top to highlight personas (e.g., "Photographers," "Marketers").

### Strategic Alignment: Template Production

  - **`growthx.ai`'s 5-Template/Week Production:**
      - **Efficiency:** Creating variations of an existing template type is faster than building a new one from scratch.
      - **Strategy:** Use new template categories from community projects (e.g., e-commerce) to inform their own production, creating variations to accelerate content growth.
  - **Lovable's Template Vision:**
      - **Goal:** 100 high-quality templates by EOY (via agency & partners) to populate the new gallery.
      - **Landing Page:** Feature a curated selection of 3–6 templates.
      - **Dashboard:** Provide a "Discover" section with "Apps" and "Templates" links.
