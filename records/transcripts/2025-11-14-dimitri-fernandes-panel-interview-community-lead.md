## Summary

Panel interview with Dimitri Fernandes, a community strategy expert, discussing the vision and operating model for GrowthX's AI-led growth community. Dimitri outlined a community structure that moves members through discovery, onboarding, engagement, contribution, and advocacy stages, emphasizing the importance of human touch alongside automation. Marcel and Dimitri discussed the balance between content-driven growth and community connection, with Marcel sharing that the community has achieved 170 paid members after initial workshops while maintaining quality standards for practitioners.
