## Key Topics

### Current AI Landscape and Challenges

  - Marcel's company processing \~3M words/month for clients (\~1B tokens)
  - Outgrowing existing platforms (AirOps, human loop, pipe training) at scale (100+ workflows)
  - Platforms good for prototyping, struggle with high-volume production (10k+ processes/day)
  - Balance between simplicity and power as user needs evolve

### Proposal Generation Tool Concept

  - Leverage historical RFP responses, ProjectMark data, and company info
  - Multi-step workflow:
    1.  Ingest historical proposals
    2.  Gather company data (core values, etc.)
    3.  Upload new RFP
    4.  Auto-generate responses using historical + current data
  - Potential to save construction companies significant time

### Technical Implementation Insights

  - N8N recommended over AirOps for better retrieval and vector DB capabilities
  - Custom knowledge bases crucial for efficient retrieval
  - Vector DBs preferred over regular DBs for this use case
  - Prototype could be built in \~1 day with proper data setup

### Monetization Strategy

  - Marcel suggests service-first approach:
      - Position as "AI + construction experts" service
      - Charge premium ($10k/month vs $20k/year for software-only)
      - Transition to software product later
  - Emphasize early adopter benefits, showcase rapid turnaround
