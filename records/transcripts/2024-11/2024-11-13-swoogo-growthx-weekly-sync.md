## Key Topics

### Content Strategy and Performance

  - Approaching 1,000 weekly blog visitors (142 published articles)
  - Keyword research based on competitor analysis, paid ad keywords, and "land and expand" strategy
  - Content prioritization balances product-tied articles (\~100) with top-of-funnel keywords (\~42)
  - Significant increase in demo submissions from blog content (Q2: 9, Q3: 17, Q4 to date: 11)

### SEO and Domain Authority

  - Swoogo experiencing steeper domain authority increase than typical for similar timeframes
  - Increased "optimizable and sellable surface area" for future conversion optimization
  - Expectation of lift in brand searches over next 6-12 months due to consistent content quality

### Blog Optimization and User Experience

  - Current blog posts not featured on homepage to avoid overshadowing company news
  - Need for a cohesive blog strategy balancing SEO content with promotional material
  - Consideration of content hubs to organize different content initiatives (e.g., blog, directory, forms)
  - Potential for stronger CTAs and lead magnets to improve conversion rates (currently \~0.04%)

### Content Collaboration and Workflow

  - Rebecca (new Director of Branding Content) to review existing blogs and suggest new topics
  - Weekly content planning: 15 articles (10 new, 5 refreshes) proposed by Prateek
  - Opportunity for Rebecca to directly request specific blog topics through Airtable

### Future Initiatives

  - Q1 2025 plans: Implement user identification tools (e.g., Koala, R\&B2B) for blog traffic analysis
  - Optimize blog post layouts and user variants to drive engagement
  - Explore A/B testing opportunities, particularly for demo forms
  - Develop a more strategic approach to featuring SEO content alongside company news
