## Key Topics

### Unusual Ventures' Approach

  - Founded \~8 years ago to address gaps in traditional VC support

  - Provides experienced professionals to work directly with portfolio companies
  - Aims to improve success rates for early-stage startups (currently only \~9% of Series A companies reach Series B)

### GrowthActs' Services and Methodology

  - Offers repeatable "growth engine" workstreams at $9,000/month
  - Services include editorial content, programmatic SEO, and AI-enhanced outbound marketing
  - Utilizes AI workflows with human experts in the loop for quality control
  - Achieved $3 million ARR in 5 months with 25 clients

  - HumanSignal: An Unusual Ventures portfolio company in AI quality assurance

### Startup Challenges and Solutions

  - Founders often struggle with hiring for roles outside their expertise
