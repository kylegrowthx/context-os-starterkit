## Overview

## Key Topics

### Content Creation Process

  - AI-assisted research and drafting, followed by human editing (1+ hour per piece)
  - Custom instructions and blacklisted words to avoid AI-generated content issues
  - Fact-checking process and trusted source database to ensure accuracy
  - Knowledge base creation for client-specific information and guidelines

### Quality Assurance

  - Multiple layers of review: AI assistance, human editing, fact-checking
  - Emphasis on avoiding mistakes related to location-specific information
  - Custom instructions to address sensitive topics (e.g., marine mammal centers)
  - Continuous calibration and improvement based on client feedback

### SEO Strategy

  - Focus on creating content clusters and interlinking strategy
  - Utilization of AI to identify relevant internal linking opportunities
  - Emphasis on long-term growth and compounding results (3+ months for significant impact)
  - Ability to refresh and optimize existing content

### Collaboration Process

  - Kickoff meeting to deep dive into client's business, audience, and offerings
  - Development of content strategy and roadmap within first two weeks
  - Regular communication and feedback loops with clients
  - Option for clients to review content or trust the process after initial calibration

### Content Management

  - Use of a "Content OS" to track progress and status of all content pieces
  - Integration with client's CMS for publishing and optimization
  - Flexibility in content placement (blog, travel guides, sustainability sections)
