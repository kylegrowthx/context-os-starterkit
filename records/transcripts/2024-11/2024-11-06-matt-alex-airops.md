## Key Topics

### fwd.digital Business Challenges

  - Struggling with client retention due to short-term (3-month) engagements
  - Difficulty in scaling AI enablement services due to high time investment
  - Challenges in presenting and collaborating on workflows with clients
  - Recruiting high-caliber talent ($7-8k/month) to meet custom client requests

### AirOps Platform Development

  - Introducing "co-pilot" feature for improved workflow building and debugging
  - Anticipating significant improvements in build time (30-70% reduction) by January
  - Focus on making the workflow builder less intimidating for new users

### Partnership Structure and Economics

  - Need for a core agreement on platform usage and pricing
  - Considering tiered approach: experimental credits + fair-cost usage pricing
  - Potential for applying credits from client accounts to fwd.digital's account
  - Last month's task usage was \~$13.5k, which may need optimization

### Client Engagement and Co-selling

  - Challenges in upselling AirOps to content service clients
  - Proposal to share logos and case studies for mutual benefit
  - Need for clearer process on introducing AirOps to high-potential clients (e.g., Ramp)

### AI Enablement Services

  - High demand but low follow-through from clients after workshops
  - Difficulty in scaling due to intensive time investment and low client engagement
