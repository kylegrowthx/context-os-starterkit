## Overview

- Need to improve conversion rate optimization (CRO) on blog to generate more leads and signups from organic traffic

## Key Topics

### Content Performance and SEO Progress

  - New record for clicks this week, though impressions dipped slightly
  - 700 visits in last few months went to 404 pages; redirects being implemented
  - 123 new articles published, generating 120-123 weekly visits
  - 45 content refreshes done, generating \~40 weekly visits
  - Total weekly organic traffic around 160-200 visits

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Strategy

  - Current blog sidebar CTAs (book demo, talk to sales) not converting well
  - Recommendations:
      - Add conversion panels within article content (2-3 times per post)
      - Implement exit-intent popups
      - Create lead magnets like case studies, ebooks, free credits
      - Test irresistible offers (e.g. 3 months free) to validate traffic quality
  - Arena team working on CRO campaign with new banners across site

### Content Strategy Adjustments

  - Slow down publishing new content, focus on optimizing existing articles
  - Prioritize adding lead magnets and conversion elements to top-performing posts
  - Organize content by funnel stage (top, middle, bottom) to align CTAs
  - Ensure 3-4 internal links per article for better interlinking

### Backlink Strategy Development

  - Start outreach to gain quality backlinks, improving domain authority
  - Target content managers at relevant companies for backlink exchanges
  - Use outbound email campaigns to scale backlink outreach efforts
  - Consider some paid placements on high-authority sites

### Visitor Identification and Lead Nurturing

  - Implement Rev2B to identify company/visitor data from blog traffic
  - Use identified leads for targeted outbound campaigns and LinkedIn networking
  - Create multi-channel nurture flows (blog \> email \> LinkedIn) for identified leads
