## Overview

---

## Key Topics

### Current SEO Performance Analysis

  - Impressions steadily increasing, but CTR and clicks remain static
  - Overall website CTR: \~2%; blog articles CTR: \~0.4% (last 28 days)
  - Ranking well (1st-8th position) for targeted construction CRM keywords
  - Best performing article: "Construction Sales Process" (2-3 clicks)
  - Weekly impressions hovering around 500, with minimal growth due to slow publishing

### Content Strategy Challenges

  - Narrow focus on "construction CRM" limits keyword opportunities
  - Risk of keyword cannibalization due to similar topics
  - Slow publishing frequency (3-week delay) hinders fresh content ranking
  - Founder's detailed review process causes significant publishing delays
  - Limited data collection due to delayed publishing affects strategy optimization

### Proposed Strategy Shift

  - Expand focus beyond "construction CRM" to broader construction-related topics
  - Target higher search volume, top-of-funnel keywords (e.g., construction marketing, sales, finance)
  - Increase content production to 15-20 pieces per week (from current 10-12)
  - Implement a tiered review process:
    1.  Publish top-of-funnel content quickly
    2.  Maintain rigorous review for bottom-of-funnel articles
    3.  Review high-traffic top-of-funnel content post-publication

### Keyword and Content Ideas

  - New keyword targets with \~700,000 search volume, including:
      - Construction engineering
      - Writing proposals for construction projects
      - How to increase sales in construction business
  - Planned topics: SEO for construction firms, construction marketing
  - Leverage upcoming customer case study for "increasing sales" content
