## Key Topics

### Content Performance Analysis

  - Week-over-week metrics: 25% increase in clicks, improved average CTR
  - Month-over-month: Better total clicks and impressions
  - Comparison between AI-generated (95 articles) and human-written (15 articles) content:
      - AI: 435 clicks, 180K impressions
      - Human: 225 clicks, 40K impressions
  - Team acknowledges different strengths: AI good for top-funnel, humans for bottom-funnel content

### Content Strategy Refinement

  - Current focus: CMS comparison topics (30+ in pipeline)
  - Metadata optimization for 10 high-impression, low-click articles
  - Commercial panels implemented across 100+ articles
  - New direction:
      - Reduce new content volume to focus on enriching existing high-potential pieces
      - Add visuals, examples, and improve formatting
      - Aim for content quality that team would confidently share with clients

### Quality Improvement Initiatives

  - Create a curated list of exemplary websites for visual references
  - Incorporate design evaluation criteria from "Best B2B SaaS Websites" asset
  - Explore adding video content and UI walkthroughs for products like Storyblock
  - Develop a "trust-to-sources" engine to incorporate thought leaders and credible sources

### Workflow Adjustments

  - Treat each blog post as a product with its own backlog
  - Focus on high-value, well-performing content for deeper improvements
  - Balance between continuing to plant "seeds" and enriching existing content
  - Aim for "Nikan-worthy" content quality as the new standard
