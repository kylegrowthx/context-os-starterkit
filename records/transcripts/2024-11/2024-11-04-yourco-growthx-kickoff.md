## Key Topics

### Current Growth Metrics and Goals

  - Yourco approaching $1M ARR, aiming to triple to $3M in 12 months
  - Currently getting 6-7 SEO-driven leads per month, targeting 15+
  - Top growth channels: SEO (40%) and outbound email (60%)
  - Partner channel showing recent traction (4 leads that morning)

### Target Customer Profile

  - Industries: Manufacturing, construction, transportation/warehousing, agriculture
  - Company size: SMB (\<200 employees) and mid-market (200-2000+ employees)
  - Key roles: HR titles (HR manager to CHRO), IT, operations, communications
  - Pain point: Ineffective communication with non-desk workforce

### Product Use Cases

  - Primary: Emergency notifications, employee engagement surveys, benefits communication
  - Expanding: Operational use cases (scheduling, SOPs, safety protocols)
  - Unique feature: Automatic translation for multilingual workforces

### SEO Strategy Discussion

  - Focus on building topical authority around industries
  - Create content clusters based on use cases, adapting for different industries
  - Develop templates, guides, and resources valuable to both prospects and current users
  - Address industry-specific compliance and regulation topics (e.g. union-related content)

### GrowthX Approach

  - Phased strategy: Establish high-quality publishing cadence → Refine and enrich content → Optimize conversion
  - Emphasis on creating "irresistible offers" to capture leads (e.g. industry-specific guides)
  - Focus on human-centric content creation, not chasing SEO trends
