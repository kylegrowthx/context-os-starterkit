## Key Topics

### Website and App Updates

  - Knapsack app now publicly available for download on website
  - Site redesign live with updated fonts, maintaining same fundamental structure
  - Planning to move off Webflow in the future

### SEO and Content Performance

  - 173 clicks total, 70k impressions, 1% CTR (improved from \<1%)
  - Organic traffic \~508/month, spike since Nov 3rd
  - 47 clicks this week despite lower impressions due to publishing delay
  - Rankings improving: more blogs in top 5 positions on Google
  - Mix of top, middle, and bottom of funnel content planned

### Outbound Marketing Strategy

  - Shifting focus to outbound efforts using SmartLead
  - Previous email campaigns (via AI SDR) had high open rates but low engagement
  - New approach: test various subject lines and email copy, aim for 60-70% open rates
  - Target ICPs: Independent Financial Advisors, RIAs (Registered Investment Advisors)
  - Key messaging: data privacy, AI automations to save time

### Knapsack's Unique Selling Points

  - Files never stored elsewhere, Knapsack doesn't see user files
  - Need to emphasize privacy features similar to Apple's approach
  - Consider creating visual comparison of Knapsack vs other AI tools for privacy
  - Update compliance page with FAQ for IT/legal teams
