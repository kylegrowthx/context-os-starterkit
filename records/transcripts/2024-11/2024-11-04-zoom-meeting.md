## Key Topics

### Metadata Client Overview

  - Company recently "slashed"; running on fumes with minimal marketing team
  - Organic traffic halved over past couple years; lowest in a long time
  - Existing topical authority could help quick wins with consistent publishing
  - Package: $6k/month, aiming for 5 articles/week (not 15 as with larger clients)

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Utilize keyword gap analysis and competitive research
  - Develop clusters for easier alignment (vs. reviewing 1000 individual assignments)
  - Custom GPT/ChatGPT used for cluster generation and template creation
  - Consider supplementing with "missed opportunities" based on industry knowledge
  - Focus on benchmarking, examples, and baselines using Metadata's unique ads data

### Process Improvements

  - Need to reduce noise in competitive URL filtering (500 to 100 most relevant)
  - Current process too compute-intensive (200,000 credits for Boost My School)
  - Aim for programmatic approach to streamline filtering and relevance assessment

### Client Presentation Strategy

  - Develop clean, presentable Airtable with 3 well-developed clusters (quality over quantity)
  - Schedule 1-hour follow-up meeting to present strategy, get alignment, and review calibration live
  - Start with smaller, recommended list; have additional opportunities ready for discussion

### Yorco Client Approach

  - More industry and use-case based strategy (vs. competitor-focused)
  - Significant overlap with Upkeep client (maintenance management, HR focus)
  - Target deskless workers in manufacturing; leverage industry terminology and compliance topics
  - Potential for massive growth due to lack of strong content players in the space
