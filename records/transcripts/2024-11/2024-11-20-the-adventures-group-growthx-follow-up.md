## Key Topics

### Content Creation and Analytics Workflow

  - Moving away from AirOps to custom workflows for 10M+ words/month
  - GrowthX will handle all aspects: editing, strategy, reporting, and execution
  - Weekly reports from GA and Search Console data
  - Potential for future automation of reporting and workflows

### Multilingual Content Strategy

  - Content must be published in both English and Spanish simultaneously
  - GrowthX to add Spanish-speaking editor (Mariana) for review and localization
  - Adjusted scope: Reduce English content volume by half, add Spanish translation
  - Client base: 87-90% English speakers, 10-12% Spanish speakers

### Technical Integrations

  - Sanity CMS integration is feasible; GrowthX experienced with headless CMSs
  - Image management: Current storage in Dropbox (1000+ images)
  - Potential use of Sanity's CDN for programmatic image handling
  - Possibility of custom photo assignments for unique content

### Future Expansions and Add-ons

  - Potential for newsletter integration with HubSpot (not in initial scope)
  - Additional CRO and campaign strategies possible after 3-month mark
  - GrowthX open to guiding or potentially executing additional projects

### Project Kickoff and Timeline

  - Proposed start date: December 16th
  - Intensive kickoff week: 1-hour kickoff, 1-2 hour deep dive sessions
  - 3 weeks for strategy development and calibration articles
  - Full content production to begin first week of January
