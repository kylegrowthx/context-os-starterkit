## Key Topics

  - Initially discussed $6K/month for outbound, but Marcel explained the true value and scope of their services

  - Rachael emphasized willingness to pay full price for quality services
  - Plan to potentially expand services in Q1 2024 after demonstrating initial results

### Content Creation (Work Stream 1)

  - Identified as a top priority to support the new website launch
  - $9K/month budget already approved by Rachael's boss
  - Marcel mentioned hiring four new managing editors, with a dedicated person for Transmit Security

### Outbound Marketing (Work Stream 6)

  - Includes end-to-end campaign management, hyper-personalized messaging, and performance measurement
  - Marcel emphasized the comprehensive nature of their approach, including IP warming, domain setup, and lead enrichment
  - Rachael sees this as an opportunity to test and extend their outbound efforts efficiently

### AI and Automation in Sales

  - Discussed the limitations of current tools like Outreach and the need for more personalized, AI-driven approaches
  - Highlighted the shift towards highly tailored, one-sequence-per-prospect strategies
  - Rachael expressed interest in leveraging AI to show quick results within tight timeframes

### Vendor Onboarding Process

  - Transmit Security requires a security review for all vendors
  - Includes background checks for team members and detailed questionnaires
  - Marcel requested a heads-up on potential roadblocks in the security review process
  - Rachael to send security review questions to Marcel for preparation

### Website Design Discussion

  - Briefly touched on website design options (Lightbox vs. No Boring Design)
  - Rachael leaning towards No Boring Design due to professionalism and cost-effectiveness
  - Marcel shared candid feedback about challenges with Lightbox, advising caution
