## Overview

- Arena.im is experiencing increased impressions but no corresponding increase in sign-ups (still 5-10 per day)
- Team is shifting focus to conversion rate optimization (CRO) and improving content quality
- GrowthX team to provide more strategic support in content enrichment and CRO efforts
- Need to better understand traffic quality and user journey to improve conversion rates

## Key Topics

### Marketing Team Changes

  - PA is no longer leading marketing; Paulo Martins taking over
  - Kyle Gaudreau introduced as new GrowthX team member with CRO expertise

### Content Performance

  - Best week yet for clicks (144) and third-best for impressions (9,000+)
  - 125 articles published, 120 indexed
  - Discrepancy noted between Google Search Console and Looker Studio data

### Conversion Rate Optimization (CRO) Efforts

  - Implementing widgets and in-content CTAs
  - Adding promotional offers (e.g., 3 months free)
  - Considering more strategic placement of CTAs and lead magnets
  - Team cautioned about expecting significant lift from end-of-article CTAs alone

### Content Strategy Shift

  - Proposing to reprioritize content production:
    1.  Focus on enriching top-performing content
    2.  Create authoritative pillar content
    3.  Develop content that can be repurposed (e.g., infographics, lead magnets)
  - Andrej to work on internal linking strategy for top 20 performing articles

### Traffic and Sign-up Analysis

  - Need to correlate increased impressions/clicks with actual website traffic
  - Aim to understand if traffic is convertible and identify improvement areas
  - Kyle suggested separating blog traffic analysis to establish a baseline conversion rate
