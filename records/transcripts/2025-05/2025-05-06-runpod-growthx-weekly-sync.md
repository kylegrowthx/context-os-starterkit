## Context

RunPod is a cloud computing and infrastructure platform serving developers. GrowthX has been providing B2B content marketing services to RunPod, targeting their developer audience through organic search and content strategy. The relationship has been strained around content quality, strategy alignment, and RunPod's ability to execute on the strategy independently. In this meeting, GrowthX learned that RunPod canceled its engagement, prompting a discussion about how to transition the work and what happens to the existing content pipeline over the final 30 days.
