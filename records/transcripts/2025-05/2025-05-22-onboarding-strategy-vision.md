## Overview

- GrowthX aims to systematize high-quality content creation through AI-augmented workflows, enabling clients to build "authority funnels"
- The company's approach combines first principles thinking with effective delegation to AI systems
- GrowthX is building a platform to capture editor interventions and continuously improve AI outputs
- Team members should focus on adding value through deep understanding and context, not just surface-level edits

---

## Key Topics

### Company Background and Vision

  - Marcel developed content workflows at DeepGram to systematize high-quality content creation
  - GrowthX distills best practices into AI-augmented workflows to scale content production
  - Goal is to help clients become publishers and build "authority funnels" to lead audiences to products

### AI-Native Approach

  - Two key skills: first principles thinking and effective delegation
  - Break down tasks to core components before rebuilding with AI assistance
  - Provide clear context, goals, and examples when delegating to AI systems
  - Use day-to-day work as R\&D to experiment and improve processes

### Platform Development

  - Moving from Airtable/Google Docs to custom "Task Hub" with Notion-like editor
  - New platform will capture editor interventions to improve AI outputs over time
  - Aim to reduce manual editing and increase automated options (e.g. choosing between AI-generated versions)

### Content Quality and Editing

  - Focus on understanding why AI outputs fall short rather than extensive manual rewrites
  - Experiment with adjusting research instructions, outlines, or polishing steps
  - Consult with experienced team members on successful approaches for different clients
  - Aim to work ahead on content rather than producing last-minute

### Handling AI Limitations

  - When stuck, leverage internal support from directors and engineering team
  - Submit tickets for engineering investigation of persistent issues
  - Experiment and compare to successful examples before escalating
