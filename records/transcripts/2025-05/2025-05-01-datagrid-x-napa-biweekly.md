## Key Topics

### Assembly AI Setup and Integration

  - Connected Thiago's LinkedIn account to Assembly AI
  - Set up Slack notifications for post approvals (preferred over email)
  - Resolved initial connection issues related to different email addresses
  - Demonstrated the approval process and user interface

### Content Strategy and Posting Schedule

  - Focus on LinkedIn as the primary platform due to easier growth potential
  - Will cross-post to Twitter without additional optimization
  - Team will handle posting cadence, aiming for Monday through Friday schedule
  - Content slates typically ready by Friday, with flexibility for timely posts

### Approval and Posting Process

  - Thiago receives Slack notifications for posts ready for approval
  - After approval, posts are automatically queued for scheduling
  - Team manages optimal posting times throughout the week
  - First approved post scheduled for the following day

### Future Content Ideas and Collaboration

  - Team will send questions in advance of biweekly meetings for content brainstorming
  - Considering video content for future posts, as LinkedIn favors video format
  - Potential series on upcoming 1000-person rollout as a use case
  - Thiago can share ideas via Slack, voice memos, or directly in Assembly AI's ideas tab

### Platform Comparisons and Features

  - LinkedIn easier to grow on compared to Twitter
  - LinkedIn allows basic text formatting (bold, italics) but limited rich content options
  - Articles feature on LinkedIn can be valuable for longer-form content
  - Twitter growth requires more active engagement and time investment
