## Summary

Siit's organic traffic, demos, and key page visits are declining across all channels—likely due to website indexing changes and lack of French localization. The team agreed to pivot toward bottom-of-funnel content, launching a test with 5 long-form (2000-3000 word) comparison pages and 5 shorter pieces this week while maintaining glossary content for top-funnel interlinking. GrowthX will establish a 3-5 backlinks-per-week program from domain-authority-50-100 sites, and Branko recommended optimizing product pages and use cases for AI search visibility (ChatGPT, Perplexity) to capture traffic that may be shifting from traditional search.

---

## Context

Siit is a Webflow-based ITSM (IT service management) platform positioned for companies like banking/fintech and professional services. GrowthX has been leading their content strategy to drive organic traffic and qualified demos—a long-term engagement where Branko Kral recently joined as director to support strategy and execution. The sync happened as Siit was experiencing performance headwinds: Carmel (Siit's marketing lead) had been on vacation for a few weeks, they'd recently onboarded a new PPC contractor, and they're preparing for a closed-beta AI agent product launch on Monday plus a major IT trade show in London mid-week. The conversation centered on diagnosing the traffic decline and reorienting content strategy toward decision-stage buyers rather than broad awareness, aligning with Siit founder Shalom's push for bottom-of-funnel focus.
