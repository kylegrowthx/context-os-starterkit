## Key Topics

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Content Platform

  - Built on Marcel's expertise in organic growth and content marketing
  - Uses AI workflows to distill expert knowledge into repeatable processes
  - Combines AI automation with human interventions for high-quality output
  - Platform includes a task hub, learn engine, and workflow management system
  - Processed \~500,000 workflow runs last month

### Business Model and Team Structure

  - 60-person team split into "build" (20 people) and "deliver" (40 people) sides
  - Build team: Principal engineers and former CTOs/co-founders
  - Deliver team: Experienced content leaders (e.g., former TechCrunch editor, Apple blog manager)
  - Services-led approach provides revenue to fund platform development

### Funding and Growth

  - Raised $12M Series A from Madrona Venture Group, skipping seed stage
  - Strong inbound interest from brands, minimal outbound sales efforts so far
  - Customers include Ramp, Webflow, and other "awesome legit brands"
  - Focused on reasonable valuation to benefit employees and maintain optionality

### Future Directions and Market Opportunity

  - Potential to expand platform beyond content into areas like recruiting
  - Building company in an "AI-native way," solving internal needs that could become new product lines
  - Exploring events and networking opportunities with VCs and CMOs
