## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Refinement

- Blend SEO-optimized articles with more focused, middle-funnel content
- Experiment with different approaches to find optimal traffic/relevance balance
- Continue producing longer, keyword-rich pieces for SEO, but also create shorter, targeted articles
- Aim for ~7-minute read time for most articles; longer pieces acceptable for "ultimate guides"

### Article Feedback and Improvements

- Add Shaped segue/CTA to article conclusions
- Ensure content aligns with Shaped's core value propositions (e.g., real-time capabilities)
- Improve article structure: strong, engaging intros; avoid concept overload in latter half
- For technical topics (e.g., vector search), clearly separate concepts to avoid conflation

### Publishing Process

- GrowthX team to gain Webflow CMS access (team@growthx.ai)
- Nic to conduct 30-minute training on Shaped's custom CMS setup
- Initial articles to be submitted as drafts for approval before publishing
- Goal: Start publishing soon to gather data on performance

### New Content Planning Workflow

- David to send 7 outlines weekly (5 SEO, 2 mid-funnel) by Friday COB
- Shaped team to review, approve, or suggest changes
- Allows for collaborative planning and alignment on content direction

### SEO vs. Audience-Specific Content Balance

- Continue SEO focus (using tools like Surfer SEO) for traffic generation
- Incorporate more targeted pieces (e.g., Teemo-style articles) for engagement
- Explore product-specific content (e.g., Spotify, Netflix recommendation systems)
