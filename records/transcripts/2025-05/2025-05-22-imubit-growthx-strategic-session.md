## Summary

GrowthX and Imubit aligned on a major content strategy pivot to drive top-of-funnel traffic while protecting brand integrity, agreeing to separate "Articles" (SEO-focused, high-volume keyword content) from "Blog" (domain expert short-form pieces). The team will create dual article versions—one mentioning Imubit and one without—to test messaging balance, and develop a thought leadership piece on industrial process optimization trends with input from Imubit's subject matter experts. Focus will shift from oil & gas refining to broader industrial processing verticals, with "industrial process optimization" positioning Imubit's solution more directly than the broader "industrial AI" category.

---

## Context

Imubit is an industrial AI software company serving process manufacturers (oil & gas refining, petrochemicals, mining, etc.) with predictive maintenance and process optimization solutions. GrowthX has been engaged to drive organic growth through content marketing and SEO strategy. The Imubit team—led by Rebecca Murtagh (CEO), with Allison Buenemann (Product Marketer) and Tim Yandel as key stakeholders—has been reviewing content drafts created by Mariana Montezzana and the GrowthX team, raising concerns about brand dilution from high-volume keyword content and ensuring alignment with their buyer's journey and value proposition. This meeting brought in Dave Capone (GrowthX's managing director for Mariana's pod) alongside other GrowthX strategists to resolve content positioning tensions and plot next-phase content strategy.

## Relevance

### To GrowthX Delivery

- **Content architecture shift**: Dual-content model (SEO-focused "Articles" + brand-safe "Blog") is a replicable framework for B2B clients concerned about keyword targeting vs. brand positioning—test and document this approach for other industrial/technical verticals.
- **Two-version article strategy**: Creating versions with and without client mentions validates whether keyword-driven content converts better when decoupled from obvious brand placement; captures learning on SEO + brand balance.
- **Subject matter expert (SME) integration**: Rebecca and Allison insisting on SME input (non-CEO preferred) for predictions content highlights the need for client collaboration on thought leadership; this workflow should be documented as a service offering.
- **URL structure and cross-linking**: Static filtered views (/articles, /blog) + internal linking strategy is a core SEO best practice now being actively implemented; document as repeatable delivery process.

### To GrowthX Business Development

- **Imubit account expansion**: Engagement breadth signals a deepening relationship (Dave's involvement, expanded content program, multi-vertical planning); indicates room for larger scope or upsell.
- **Sector expertise building**: Industrial processing, predictive maintenance, and process optimization are adjacent verticals to GrowthX's home services content focus; winning Imubit could unlock referrals or case studies in manufacturing/industrial.
- **Retention risk mitigation**: Imubit's emphasis on brand and buyer journey fit shows they value strategic partnership over just keyword volume; maintaining alignment on these principles is key to renewal.

## Key Topics

### Content Strategy Alignment

  - GrowthX creating top-of-funnel content to drive traffic; Imubit team concerned about brand dilution
  - Agreed to separate "Articles" (SEO-focused) from "Blog" (domain expert short-form content)
  - Will create two versions of articles - one mentioning Imubit, one without - to find right balance
  - Focus on "industrial AI" as key term to own, but "industrial process optimization" closer to solution

### Website Structure for SEO

  - Need static URL for filtered article/blog pages (e.g. /articles, /blog)
  - Cross-link articles with other relevant content (guides, blogs) for SEO benefit
  - Ensure new content discoverable via site navigation and sitemap

### Expanding Market Focus

  - Shift from oil & gas refining to broader "industrial processing" vertical initially
  - Gradually add content for sub-verticals (petrochemical, mining, etc.) to expand reach
  - Maintain "industrial" as top-level vertical to stay horizontal for now

### Thought Leadership Content

  - Develop "State of Industrial Process Optimization" report with stats, predictions, client data
  - Update annually to build authority and attract backlinks
  - Consider having subject matter expert (vs. CEO) provide commentary for faster turnaround

### SEO and AI Search Considerations

  - Long-form content still important for SEO despite AI search growth
  - AI search allows for longer, more contextual queries
  - Rankings in AI overviews may differ from traditional search results
  - Image optimization important as Imubit image appearing prominently for some queries
