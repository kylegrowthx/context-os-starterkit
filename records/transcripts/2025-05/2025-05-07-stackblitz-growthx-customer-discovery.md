## Summary

George Haikal led a discovery call with Monika Rozanska from Stackblitz (formerly Bolt) to align on user research strategy and content initiatives. George offered to conduct 10 structured user interviews to help Stackblitz identify high-priority user segments and inform product strategy around engagement, retention, and feature prioritization. ## Context

Stackblitz is a developer platform for building and deploying web applications with AI. The company has 750k monthly active users and recently hired a data team to build infrastructure for understanding user behavior, activation, engagement, and retention. Monika Rozanska, who leads data/analytics at Stackblitz, was in San Francisco for a company offsite during this call. George Haikal, Head of Growth at GrowthX, initiated this customer discovery meeting to understand Stackblitz's user research needs and position GrowthX's services (user interviews, content strategy, templates, and educational materials) as a way to support their growth initiatives.

This call came at a strategic moment for Stackblitz: they had just launched an in-product user survey (receiving 50k responses) and were attempting to segment users and define their ideal customer profile. They're also in the midst of building out educational content, revamping help center documentation, and planning an "AI Builder Bootcamp" educational website. GrowthX's user research methodology and content marketing expertise align well with Stackblitz's immediate needs around understanding what makes users successful long-term.
