## Summary

Jessica Yip and Prateek Gupta reviewed Yourco's content portfolio (147 articles published, with 25 new and 22 refreshed pieces) and discussed a strategic expansion from frontline communication to adjacent verticals including operations, productivity, training, development, and leadership. Both identified a 10-20% drop in organic clicks across properties — driven largely by Google's AI overviews — and agreed on a multi-pronged response: SEO audits of top-performing pages, implementation of FAQ schema markup in Prismic CMS, resolution of keyword cannibalization and broken links, and a shift toward long-form resource pages on proven topics like translation, safety, and compliance.

## Context

Yourco is a frontline workforce platform. GrowthX is delivering content marketing services to support Yourco's SEO and lead generation — part of a broader content marketing engagement. Jessica Yip leads content strategy for Yourco; Prateek Gupta is the GrowthX SEO specialist managing content performance and technical optimization. These weekly syncs track content output, SEO metrics, GA4 attribution data, and strategic refinements to the content roadmap. The meeting reflects Yourco's maturation: having built strong traction in frontline communication content, the team is now broadening topical coverage while also confronting industry-wide SEO headwinds from AI overviews displacing organic clicks.
