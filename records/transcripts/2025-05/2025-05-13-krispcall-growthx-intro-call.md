## Context

KrispCall is a cloud-based telephony application competing against well-funded players like Nextiva, OpenPhone, and Dialpad. They've built strong organic performance over the past three years—80-90% of their 400K monthly users come from organic search—but face a maturation challenge. Their in-house team of 35 (SEOs, content writers, link builders, technical specialists) was built from scratch with junior talent, and programmatic automation efforts haven't yielded expected returns. The market is shifting: AI overviews in Google Search and LLM-powered search platforms (Perplexity) are fragmenting where visibility happens, and KrispCall is seeing only minimal traction (~1K monthly clicks from LLM sources). Internally, they're under pressure to hit revenue targets (70-80% from organic), so Sabin is exploring agencies after disappointing ROI from in-house tooling investments. GrowthX represents their third agency contact. This is a potential strategic fit: KrispCall needs both better content execution and AI visibility expertise.
