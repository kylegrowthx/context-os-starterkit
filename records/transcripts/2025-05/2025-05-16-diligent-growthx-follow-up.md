## Overview

- Diligent's organic search traffic is down 20% YoY, despite producing new content and running awareness campaigns
- Current content creation process is reactive and lacks a systematic approach to SEO optimization
- GrowthX proposes a structured content strategy to scale production and learn faster from content performance
- Technical limitations in Diligent's CMS and ongoing web redesign project may impact content publishing velocity

---

## Key Topics

### Current Content Performance and Challenges

  - Organic search traffic down 20% year-over-year
  - $100,000 spent on awareness campaign with little lasting traffic impact
  - Shifting focus between governance, ESG, risk, and compliance has led to inconsistent SEO performance
  - Current content creation is reactive, lacking an editorial calendar or consistent output targets

### Content Types and SEO Optimization

  - SEO-focused content: Hub and spoke model, driving traffic and keyword ranking
  - Editorial blogs: Thought leadership, minimal SEO work
  - Product/announcement blogs: Minimal to no SEO optimization
  - Gated content (videos, podcasts, guides, case studies): Focus on lead capture, landing pages lack SEO juice

### GrowthX's Proposed Approach

  - Systematic "seed planting" strategy: Refreshing existing content and creating new pieces
  - Goal: 5-10 new content pieces weekly to accelerate learning and optimization
  - Tailored voice and style guide to maintain consistency
  - Assigned managing editor and director to ensure focus and avoid conflicts of interest

### Implementation Challenges and Considerations

  - Diligent's CMS limitations hinder rapid content publishing
  - Ongoing web redesign project (until end of December) may impact content strategy execution
  - Resources section redesign planned for Q3
  - Need to bridge the gap between content creation and technical limitations

### Priorities and Next Steps

  - Focus on web and organic traffic improvement as top priority
  - Structure SOW to demonstrate clear value and facilitate future change orders
  - Start with a focused approach for the next three months to show results before scaling
  - Address product and solution pages, which have seen a 20% drop in organic traffic
