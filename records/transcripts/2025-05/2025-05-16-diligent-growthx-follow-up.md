## Summary

GrowthX and Diligent discussed a content strategy overhaul to address a 20% year-over-year drop in organic traffic and $100k in awareness spend that generated minimal lasting impact. Marcel pitched a systematic "seed planting" approach with a target of 5–10 new or refreshed content pieces weekly, supported by GrowthX's editorial team led by Matthew (former TechCrunch editor-in-chief) to maintain voice consistency and publishing velocity. Shathabi flagged critical constraints: Diligent's CMS limitations, an ongoing web redesign through December (managed by Lastref), and the need to show metrics-driven results within three months before scaling further, with product and solution pages experiencing a 20% traffic decline.

---

## Context

Diligent is a governance, risk, and compliance software company that has been struggling with organic search visibility despite ongoing content production. The company has historically rotated focus between governance, ESG, risk, and compliance content—a merry-go-round that prevents sustained rankings in any vertical. John Habib leads Diligent's content operations, managing a Resource Center with multiple content types (SEO-focused hubs, editorial blogs, product announcements, gated assets). Shathabi Ravindra owns growth metrics and is accountable for customer acquisition cost (CAC) and return on ad spend (ROAS), making the 20% organic decline personally significant. GrowthX proposed a three-month proof-of-concept engagement starting with constrained scope—avoiding the aggressive 5–10 pieces per week target initially due to CMS tech debt—to demonstrate value before scaling. The web redesign project (managed by Lastref, Brad Taylor) is a critical dependency that will unblock the Resources section redesign in Q3.

## Key Topics

### Current Content Performance and Challenges

- Organic search traffic down 20% year-over-year
- $100,000 spent on awareness campaign with little lasting traffic impact
- Shifting focus between governance, ESG, risk, and compliance has led to inconsistent SEO performance
- Current content creation is reactive, lacking an editorial calendar or consistent output targets

### Content Types and SEO Optimization

- SEO-focused content: Hub and spoke model, driving traffic and keyword ranking
- Editorial blogs: Thought leadership, minimal SEO work
- Product/announcement blogs: Minimal to no SEO optimization
- Gated content (videos, podcasts, guides, case studies): Focus on lead capture, landing pages lack SEO juice

### GrowthX's Proposed Approach

- Systematic "seed planting" strategy: Refreshing existing content and creating new pieces
- Goal: 5-10 new content pieces weekly to accelerate learning and optimization
- Tailored voice and style guide to maintain consistency
- Assigned managing editor and director to ensure focus and avoid conflicts of interest

### Implementation Challenges and Considerations

- Diligent's CMS limitations hinder rapid content publishing
- Ongoing web redesign project (until end of December) may impact content strategy execution
- Resources section redesign planned for Q3
- Need to bridge the gap between content creation and technical limitations

### Priorities and Next Steps

- Focus on web and organic traffic improvement as top priority
- Structure SOW to demonstrate clear value and facilitate future change orders
- Start with a focused approach for the next three months to show results before scaling
- Address product and solution pages, which have seen a 20% drop in organic traffic
