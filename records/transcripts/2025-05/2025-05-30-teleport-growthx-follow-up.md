## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Approach and Expertise

  - Focus on helping brands operate like publishers and scale quality content
  - Blend of automation and expert-in-loop processes
  - Start with "editorial layer" for longer-form content to build foundation
  - Team includes ex-TechCrunch editor (Matthew Panzerino), SEO experts (e.g., Dave from Fanatics/TripAdvisor), and former SEMrush lead editor

### Content Strategy and Production Process

  - 6-8 week strategy sprint to develop editorial roadmap
  - Use of Airtable for "Content OS" to track topics, keywords, and content status
  - Proprietary workflows combine AI tools with human expertise for rapid production
  - Calibration period to align on brand voice, style, and content quality

### Differentiation and Tools

  - Custom workflows and AI integration enable high-quality, rapid content creation
  - Human-in-the-loop steps throughout the process
  - Continuous refinement of inputs based on client feedback and performance data
  - Focus on answering user questions effectively to build brand equity

### SEO and Traffic Growth

  - Example shown of Abnormal Security's content success
  - Discussion of StrongDM's recent content acceleration and traffic growth
  - Analysis of Teleport's current content strategy, noting opportunity in blog content

### Engagement Timeline and Scaling

  - Weeks 4-6: Establish roadmap, personas, and initial content pieces
  - Weeks 8-12: Ramp up to 5-15 articles per week, based on strategy and needs
  - Focus on laying foundation before optimizing for conversions
