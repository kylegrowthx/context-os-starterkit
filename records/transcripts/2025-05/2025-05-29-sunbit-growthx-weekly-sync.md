## Context

Sunbit is a fintech client where GrowthX is running a multi-year B2B and B2C content strategy focused on two distinct audiences: retail consumers (160+ million people with teeth) and business decision-makers (200,000 U.S. dental practices). The relationship began with B2C content (dental, auto service, central tips) and has matured to the point where Sunbit's B2B stakeholders are eager for content targeting their internal teams and business partners. This weekly sync is the primary synchronization point for strategy, SEO metrics, and content pipeline planning. Sunbit's internal stakeholders are enthusiastic but have limited visibility into content development timelines, creating an opportunity for GrowthX to deliver surprising volume and quality to build excitement and deepen the partnership.
