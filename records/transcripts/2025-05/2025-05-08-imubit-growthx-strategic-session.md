## Overview

- Imubit offers one main product: the "Optimizing Brain for Your Plant", an AI-driven closed-loop optimization solution for industrial processes
- Content strategy will focus on creating awareness around closed-loop AI optimization, bridging IT/OT gaps, and addressing industry pain points
- Two calibration articles were selected to align on tone and messaging before scaling up content production
- GrowthX will leverage a mix of high-volume and niche keywords to drive traffic while educating on Imubit's specific solution

---

## Key Topics

### Imubit's Product and Market Position

  - Single product: "Optimizing Brain for Your Plant" - AI-driven closed-loop optimization for industrial processes
  - Leading in closed-loop AI optimization, a newly defined category (announced fall 2024)
  - Competitors: Advisory AI models (open loop), closed but non-AI solutions, and status quo (e.g., GE, Honeywell, Siemens)
  - Target industries: Cement, chemicals, oil & gas, metals, mining, materials, and general process industries

### Content Strategy and Keyword Approach

  - Mix high-volume keywords (e.g., digital transformation) with niche terms (e.g., closed-loop optimization)
  - Focus on top-of-funnel content to educate on AI in industrial processes
  - Address pain points: skills gap, workforce empowerment, sustainability
  - Create content clusters: Industrial AI/digital transformation, optimization/efficiency, sustainability/safety/compliance, empowering workforce

### Target Audience and Personas

  - Primary researchers: Engineers, technical managers (users, not buyers)
  - Decision-makers: Plant managers, site leadership
  - Need to address both IT and OT (Operational Technology) perspectives

### Calibration Articles Selected

1.  "Data-driven industries: How we went from fixing problems to avoiding them"
2.  "Beyond the hype: Real-world applications of AI in process industries"

### Multilingual Content Consideration

  - Potential for future expansion into Spanish and German content
  - Low-effort approach to localize key landing pages and high-performing content

### Visual Content Strategy

  - Combine stock photos of industrial environments with product screenshots
  - Potential for AI-generated conceptual images and statistics visualizations
  - Possibility of creating GIFs for product demonstrations
