## Key Topics

### Haley's Podcast Production Business

- Runs Haynow Media, a podcast production company agency serving B2B and small businesses
- Helps clients build YouTube presence and grow podcast downloads through SEO expertise and social media
- Team includes SEO experts focused on backlinks and podcast download growth
- Clients typically can't distinguish between podcast downloads and YouTube/video growth
- Saw tremendous growth mostly through social media; looking to diversify offerings for greater client value

### GrowthX's Core Business Model

- Specializes in creating content engines through editorial and programmatic website content
- Breaks down content creation into specific workflows with human expertise at key decision points
- Built proprietary platform to handle complex parallel processing and parallel dependency chains
- Reduces work that typically takes hours to just minutes
- Works with customers like Galileo AI, Reddit, Ramps, Homebase
- Base engagement: $9,000/month for 5-8 articles/week with 12-month minimum commitment
- Primary focus on helping clients become publishers through their own networks (website, social)

### Podcast-to-Article Repurposing (Galileo AI Case Study)

- Galileo AI started weekly podcast in fall 2024; GrowthX took transcripts and created SEO articles
- Articles embed video, extract quotes with attribution, and create articles that tell the podcast's story
- Workflow creates articles with SEO value and extended distribution beyond the podcast itself
- Results: Galileo is "one of our happiest customers" seeing organic impact; considering additional work with GrowthX
- Podcast attribution is hard to break down separately, but holistically seeing strong results across organic channels

### Partnership Possibilities and Limitations

- White-labeling: Not currently offered and not a core business model for GrowthX
- Referral partnership: Kyle expressed openness to referring Haley's podcast production services to GrowthX customers who want to start podcasts; Haley open to referring GrowthX to her podcast clients who need content distribution and SEO
- Customization: GrowthX can customize workflows, but tries to maintain focus on editorial and programmatic content to provide business focus
- Pricing barrier: $9,000/month starting point and 12-month minimum is tough for earlier-stage clients in Haley's portfolio
