## Key Topics

### SEO Strategy and Reporting

  - Focus on organic pipeline growth and data requests over traffic
  - Macro analysis: competitor strength, market strategies
  - Micro analysis: customer pain points, buying decisions, alternatives
  - Use of tools like Ahrefs or SEMrush for keyword gap analysis
  - Strategy document includes goals, challenges, opportunities, and focused tactics
  - Weekly reports with short-term goals, using templates in GSE or Looker

### Client Onboarding and Relationship Management

  - Kickoff call with managing director/editor to present initial research
  - Weekly recurring calls to action strategy
  - First 3 months: focus on early signals, iterating strategy, and pivoting if needed
  - "Calibration articles" to fine-tune content to client's voice and style
  - Building trust crucial for smaller teams to share proprietary data

### Managing Editor Role and Onboarding

  - First 3 weeks: educational tutorials, test exercises, shadowing
  - Gradual easing into client relationships over 12 weeks
  - Managing editors typically handle 3-5 clients
  - Senior managing editors may take on higher volume
  - Company prioritizes quality and prevents overwhelm

### Company Culture and Growth

  - Rapid hiring and scaling (3x growth recently)
  - Collaborative environment with open communication
  - Focus on retention for both employees and clients
  - Venture-backed, allowing for quality focus and manageable workloads
