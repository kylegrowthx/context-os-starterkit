## Key Topics

### AppBroda's Business Overview

  - 4-year-old bootstrapped adtech company with 20 employees
  - Primary business: ad serving for mobile gaming publishers (e.g., Zynga, Voodoo)
  - High ACV ($20K-$300K), serving \~60 clients out of 400-500 potential companies
  - Geographic spread: 40% Europe, 30% China, rest in US and Southeast Asia

### New Product Vertical

  - Developing MarTech tools for user acquisition optimization
  - Target: UA managers and creative ops teams in gaming, consumer apps, and D2C brands
  - Focus: Productivity and workflow improvements for UA processes
  - Potential market: \~20,000 companies, lower ACV than current business

### GrowthX's Offering

  - AI-powered content creation service with human expertise
  - Two main content types: Editorial (long-form, point of view) and Programmatic (template-based, repeatable)
  - 12-week initial engagement process, including strategy development and content calibration
  - Pricing: Starts at $9K/month with 12-month engagements (90-day pilot period)

### Content Strategy and Impact

  - Primary focus on SEO, but aims for broader content strategy impact
  - Additional channels: Social media, interview-based content (e.g., customer stories)
  - Case studies: Abnormal Security (1300+ content assignments), Tiro (new blog growth)

### AppBroda's Current Needs

  - Recognizes importance of content strategy for new product launch
  - Not ready for significant SEO investment due to pre-product market fit stage
  - Limited resources to dedicate to content management and strategy currently
