## Summary

Jason and Bruno from GrowthX conducted an initial discovery call with Aishwarya Goel, co-founder of Inferless, a seed-funded ML model deployment platform. Aishwarya explained that Inferless generates most of its leads through Google search (including Perplexity), Reddit, and SEO, and wants to improve conversion rates by attracting ICP-aligned developers rather than broad traffic. Jason pitched GrowthX's AI-augmented content approach using expert SEO practitioners and multi-stage research workflows, contrasting it with traditional PR agencies. Both parties agreed to a follow-up call where GrowthX will prepare competitive gap analysis and discuss Inferless' 12-month growth goals.

## Context

Inferless is a 17-person, seed-funded startup founded by Aishwarya Goel and a co-founder with strong ML infrastructure expertise. The company provides custom ML model deployment solutions tailored for companies migrating from AWS and GCP, competing in a niche against hyperscalers. They target Series A+ and mid-market companies in North America with a product-led growth motion where ML engineers and data scientists discover and deploy models, with CTOs joining conversations at higher spend levels. Customers spend $100–$10,000/month. The company has been steadily generating leads through technical content (which has landed on Hacker News and Reddit) and organic SEO for terms like "serverless GPU," and recently opened the platform to design partners after spending the first year building product. This is GrowthX's first conversation with Inferless; they found each other through LinkedIn and the GrowthX website.
