## Context

Discern is a B2B SaaS platform focused on automating multi-state business compliance — particularly filing services for registered agents, franchise taxes, annual reports, and foreign registrations. Mike Bosserman (CEO/Founder) engaged GrowthX for programmatic SEO content that would position Discern as the solution for businesses navigating complex compliance obligations across multiple states. Unlike consumer-facing platforms, Discern targets sophisticated business owners and compliance professionals who already manage multiple entities and want to outsource repetitive filing and reporting.

This session was the strategic planning phase following preliminary SEO audits. Carrie Chowske had conducted competitive analysis, identified keyword opportunities, and mapped content clusters that aligned with Discern's ICP (businesses with multi-state operations). The meeting's purpose was to align on approach, define content pillars, resolve tactical questions about audience targeting (multi-state businesses versus first-time LLC creators), and establish next steps for content template development in Webflow.
