## Overview

- Need to transition from relying on director-level experts to a more scalable model with tiered service levels and specialized pods
- Importance of setting clear client expectations around engagement model and KPIs from the start
- Opportunity to develop more standardized onboarding processes, templates, and client-facing dashboards
- Long-term vision is to build an AI-powered platform that transforms knowledge work, with the agency as a proving ground

---

## Key Topics

### Current Onboarding Process

  - Heavy reliance on director-level pod leaders for discovery/strategy
  - Time-intensive custom AI model refinement during foundation/calibration
  - Challenge: Can't scale hiring director-level expertise indefinitely

### Proposed Improvements

  - Advanced customer segmentation: Specialized pods by industry
  - Split foundation and calibration stages to increase client buy-in
  - Transition to "maintenance mode" post-calibration with tiered support
  - Standardized templates for onboarding, expectations, project plans
  - Consider client-facing dashboards focused on agreed KPIs

### Scaling Challenges

  - Clients get attached to high-touch service from directors
  - Multiple work streams for larger clients make it hard to reach steady state
  - Need to balance high-touch service for top clients vs. scalable model for growth
  - Difficulty in attributing results directly to GrowthX's work

### Long-term Vision

  - Build AI-powered platform to transform knowledge work
  - Agency serves as proving ground for 10x efficiency gains
  - Exact product/business model still evolving
