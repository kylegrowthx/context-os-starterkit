## Context

Darrell Etherington and Megan Dickey are new directors joining GrowthX, a B2B content marketing agency with ~50 customers and $200k+ engagements. This was their first formal onboarding meeting with Bridget McGillivray, Head of Operations. GrowthX is in a scaling phase—they're delivering high-touch, customized content and SEO work to clients while simultaneously building underlying infrastructure. The company has recently hired a team and is trying to move away from ad hoc processes toward documented workflows, though given the rapid growth, many processes remain incomplete or frustrating to use. This meeting established the foundational context both directors need to be effective: understanding the onboarding path, discovering which tools and communication channels to use, and absorbing GrowthX's strategic vision (which is Marcel's responsibility, via video).
