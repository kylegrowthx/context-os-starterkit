## Context

Hasura is a GraphQL-focused startup transitioning to a new product and brand identity. Originally built around open-source GraphQL, the company has pivoted multiple times — first to DDN and SuperGraph, now committing entirely to PromQL, a data agent that connects LLMs to enterprise data sources for conversational, accurate AI applications. Ellie Ebrahimi, who joined Hasura approximately nine months ago, discovered GrowthX through peer referrals at a CMO networking event and reached out to Maurice Maxwell, leading to this follow-up conversation.

GrowthX brought Kyle Gaudreau (VP of Customer Growth, formerly on delivery/strategy side) and Bruno Santilli (GrowthX team member, currently based in Brazil) to present the company's unique positioning. Kyle has deep growth and content strategy experience from companies like Homebase and was GrowthX's first customer before joining the team in October 2024. Hasura urgently needs strategic content help and SEO expertise to support their imminent rebrand migration — not just technical audits, but end-to-end content strategy and execution.
