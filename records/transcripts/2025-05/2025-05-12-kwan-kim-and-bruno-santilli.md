## Key Topics

### Terratrue's Current Marketing Situation

- One predictable go-to-market motion: outbound sales (outsourced to agency)
- Exploring organic content strategies as next investment area
- Considering modern marketing approaches, especially with AI advancements
- Targeting late Q3 for potential engagement with new content strategies

### GrowthX's Service Overview

- Focus on building repeatable content engines using AI-powered workflows
- Two main content types: editorial and programmatic SEO
- Editorial content (60-70% of engagements) for deeper understanding of voice, tone, and personas
- Programmatic SEO (30-40% of engagements) for high-volume, templatized content

### GrowthX's Differentiators

- Proprietary platform and workflows with human-in-the-loop steps
- End-to-end ownership of content creation process, including images and staging
- High-level expertise: Managing Directors, Managing Editors, and Content Managers with industry experience
- Efficient operations allowing for competitive pricing and margins

### Content Creation Process

- 10+ workflows for a single piece of content
- Includes research, briefing, internal linking, meta tags, etc.
- Client involvement through feedback on outputs, not direct platform interaction
- Use of Notion and Slack for async communication and collaboration

### Pricing Structure

- Editorial content: Starts at $9k/month for 50,000 words (about 8 articles/week)
- Programmatic SEO: Starts at $12k/month for 1200 pages/year
- Flat pricing structure, not adjusted based on company size
