## Key Topics

### Content Performance Update

  - Recovery in site clicks and impressions after previous week's drop

  - GrowthX content performance: clicks down, impressions up by 1,700+
  - Top performing page: "AI homework helper tools" with 300+ clicks over other pages

### New Product Experience Launch

  - Complete rebuild of SchoolAI product, launching in June
  - New space pages with more content, learning outcomes, standards, and agendas
  - Redesigned user interface with "Dot" AI assistant and power-up stage
  - Improved SEO potential with more indexable content on space pages

### Indexing Strategy for Spaces

  - Product team to build a manifest for controlling space indexing
  - Nikki to provide CSV of spaces to preserve for indexing
  - High-performing spaces to remain indexed; others to be no-indexed
  - Future automated system based on launch frequency and quality score
  - WWW pages to be 301 redirected to corresponding app.schoolai.com space pages

### Website Platform Transition

  - Plans to move away from Framer after September due to limitations
  - Considering hiring a developer to start building a new website
  - Exploring CMS options like Webflow and Strapi
  - Need to preserve SEO work during the transition

### SEO Considerations for New Product

  - Canonical tags to be used for remixed spaces to avoid duplicate content issues
  - No-index and canonical strategy for remixed spaces to consolidate SEO value
  - Content for indexed spaces to be AI-generated based on teacher inputs and Dot's prompting
