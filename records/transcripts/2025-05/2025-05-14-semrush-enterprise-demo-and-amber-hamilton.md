## Summary

Kevin Batchelor (SEMrush solutions engineer) demonstrated SEMrush's new AIO (AI Optimization) platform to GrowthX, showing how it tracks AI-generated content performance across ChatGPT, Gemini, and Grok. The platform generates prompts from keywords, monitors share of voice, analyzes sentiment trends, and flags outdated information in AI responses—features designed to solve AEO problems similar to Scrunch and Profound. GrowthX is currently spending ~$5k/month on SEMrush with 50+ seats and indicated they're running a pilot with competitors; Kevin proposed a follow-up deep-dive on Enterprise platform setup, project prioritization, and ROI forecasting for agency use cases, with Amber to coordinate pricing research and a Monday meeting including Kirkland from GrowthX.
