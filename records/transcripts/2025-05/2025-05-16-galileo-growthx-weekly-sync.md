- Conor expressed significant concern about SEO impact due to how Sanity instance was configured; brand will be elevated long-term but short-term risk of ranking damage

- Galileo is recreating and updating images for key content pieces (mastering agency, leaderboard, etc.)

- New direction: embed podcast quotes and video content into existing blogs to improve SEO and capture more LLM and search visibility
- Campaign team member starting June 2nd will focus on enriching content and aligning it more tightly with sales funnel; expect significant June uplift in content operations

### Video Content Integration

- Conor proposing to identify top 10 podcast videos and top 5 how-to videos for blog embedding (e.g., "Monitor your LangChain agent with Galileo" tutorial embedded in LangChain-related blogs)
- GrowthX has scraped all Galileo YouTube videos and built automatic insertion capability into their workflow to match videos to topically relevant blogs
- Exploring webinar content for potential integration as well, though some content may not align topically
- Strategy rollout planned for June alongside new campaign team member onboarding
