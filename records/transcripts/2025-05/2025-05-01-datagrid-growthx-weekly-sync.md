## Context

Datagrid is an AI-powered operational software platform serving construction companies (subcontractors, general contractors, developers) with use cases spanning project management, finance, sales, and HR workflows. They've already achieved $2M+ ARR and are deployed at scale—some customers going live across 1,000+ users. GrowthX provides content marketing and SEO services for Datagrid, managing a growing content hub of 75+ articles. The relationship is strong and strategic: GrowthX and Datagrid are aligned on Datagrid's long-term positioning challenge. Datagrid recognizes that their best growth (faster sales cycles, happier customers) comes from positioning around operational use cases rather than the construction industry itself—yet their website and messaging still lean heavily on industry-specific language. They need help validating this hypothesis through structured customer discovery and translating findings into compelling messaging that works across multiple verticals.
