## Key Topics

### ELSA's Business Model

  - AI-powered English learning platform for both B2C and B2B markets
  - B2C: Subscription-based app for individual users (presentation skills, interview prep, travel, etc.)
  - B2B: Bulk license sales to companies (100-1000 licenses)
  - Global presence: HQ in US (Bay Area), research scientists in Europe
  - Specialized English courses for different purposes, job functions, and industries
  - AI-driven personalized learning paths

### GrowthX's Services

  - Content marketing agency using AI-enhanced workflows
  - Services include content creation, SEO optimization, social media management, and LLM citation tracking
  - Unique approach: Mix of AI workflows and expert human oversight
  - Content creation process: Research, outline, drafting, style adaptation, internal linking, and human review
  - Pricing: $9,000 for 50,000 words/month (for content and strategy)

### AI-Powered Content Creation

  - GrowthX uses AI to create higher quality content at larger volumes
  - Publish more content initially, then double down on what works best
  - Generate 50-80 articles per month vs. traditional 12 articles/month
  - AI assists in creating infographics and pulling quotes
  - Faster results due to higher volume and ability to optimize based on performance

### SEO and Off-Page Strategies

  - GrowthX offers full-service SEO, including both on-page and off-page optimization
  - Off-page SEO focuses on influencing citations and mentions across various platforms
  - Track and influence how brands are mentioned in AI language models (e.g., ChatGPT, Perplexity)
  - Identify key sources driving product perception and work to influence them
  - Link building evolving towards broader brand/product marketing and reputation management

### LLM Citation Tracking

  - Monitor brand mentions in AI language models like ChatGPT
  - Create list of relevant prompts based on customer research
  - Track mentions over time to gauge impact on referral traffic
  - Attribution sits between highly trackable ad spend and less quantifiable brand awareness campaigns
