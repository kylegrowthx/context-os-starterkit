## Summary

Bridge Legal (a legal tech platform serving both law firms and litigation funders) reached out to GrowthX exploring content-driven inbound marketing after relying primarily on outbound sales. Kyle Gaudreau walked through GrowthX's hybrid software-plus-services model: an 8-week strategy sprint ($20K) to develop editorial roadmap, competitive analysis, and brand voice guidelines, followed by 12-month execution producing 5-15 articles per week at $15K/month. Alex Sha noted Bridge Legal already has existing brand and competitor research from a prior agency engagement, positioning them potentially to move faster, though Kyle emphasized the strategy sprint remains essential to ensure content is purpose-built for editorial content generation, not just website branding.

## Context

Bridge Legal is a B2B legal technology company serving two distinct customer segments: law firms seeking to improve their litigation processes, and litigation funders (institutional investors) looking to manage their litigation asset portfolios. The company currently relies entirely on outbound sales for customer acquisition, with distinct sales teams targeting large law firms and institutional fund managers. While they have a B2C marketing team, they lack any B2B inbound marketing motion and have minimal web presence in their B2B-focused segments (the litigation funding platform is currently hidden from public access). Alex Sha is the co-founder and CTO leading B2B marketing strategy, and Mia Ye is a product manager reporting to Alex. The company recently invested in external agency work to develop brand guidelines and competitor research, but recognized they lack an editorial content engine to drive organic inbound lead generation—the core gap GrowthX specializes in filling.

## Key Topics

### Bridge Legal's Current Marketing Approach

  - Primarily uses outbound marketing for B2B customers (law firms and litigation funders)
  - Targets large law firms, considering expansion to smaller firms
  - Has a B2C marketing team, but lacks B2B inbound strategies
  - Recently worked with an agency to develop brand guidelines and competitor research

### GrowthX's Offering

  - Combines AI-powered platform with expert services for content creation
  - Focus on editorial-style content to build organic traffic
  - Typical output: 5-15 published articles per week
  - Uses Airtable as a "Content OS" to manage content strategy and production
  - Offers both strategy development and execution phases

### Engagement Structure

1.  Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, $20K):
      - Develop editorial roadmap
      - Competitive gap analysis
      - Voice, tone, and structure guidelines
      - 3 sample content pieces for calibration
2.  Growth Execution (12 months, typically $15K/month):
      - Ongoing content production and publishing
      - Flexible pricing based on content volume needs

### ROI and Metrics

  - Early focus on impression and click growth
  - Evolves to conversion metrics and revenue impact over time (around 6 months)
  - Example: Tiro (B2B dental marketplace) saw significant organic traffic growth within 3 months

### Content Approval Process

  - Initially requires more client involvement for feedback and approval
  - Gradually transitions to lighter touch as trust is built
  - GrowthX's managing editors ensure quality before publishing
