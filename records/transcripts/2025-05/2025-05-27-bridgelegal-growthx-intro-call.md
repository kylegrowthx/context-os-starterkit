## Key Topics

### Bridge Legal's Current Marketing Approach

  - Primarily uses outbound marketing for B2B customers (law firms and litigation funders)
  - Targets large law firms, considering expansion to smaller firms
  - Has a B2C marketing team, but lacks B2B inbound strategies
  - Recently worked with an agency to develop brand guidelines and competitor research

### GrowthX's Offering

  - Combines AI-powered platform with expert services for content creation
  - Focus on editorial-style content to build organic traffic
  - Typical output: 5-15 published articles per week
  - Uses Airtable as a "Content OS" to manage content strategy and production
  - Offers both strategy development and execution phases

### Engagement Structure

1.  Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, $20K):
      - Develop editorial roadmap
      - Competitive gap analysis
      - Voice, tone, and structure guidelines
      - 3 sample content pieces for calibration
2.  Growth Execution (12 months, typically $15K/month):
      - Ongoing content production and publishing
      - Flexible pricing based on content volume needs

### ROI and Metrics

  - Early focus on impression and click growth
  - Evolves to conversion metrics and revenue impact over time (around 6 months)
  - Example: Tiro (B2B dental marketplace) saw significant organic traffic growth within 3 months

### Content Approval Process

  - Initially requires more client involvement for feedback and approval
  - Gradually transitions to lighter touch as trust is built
  - GrowthX's managing editors ensure quality before publishing
