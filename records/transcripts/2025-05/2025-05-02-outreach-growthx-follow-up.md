## Key Topics

### SEO Content Audit

- Analyzed 347 Outreach blog posts for optimization opportunities
- Identified potential to 8x traffic through content refreshes and improvements
- Examined 554 competitor content gaps (Gong, Flurry, Sales Loft), categorized by difficulty (easy, average, hard)
- Opportunity to potentially 5x traffic by addressing easy-to-target content gaps
- Proposed 12-month retainer model with approximately 5 new or refreshed pieces weekly

### Editorial Strategy Alignment

- Proposed developing a unique editorial mission and voice for Outreach's content
- Suggested creating content that appeals to aspirational readers (e.g., "for people who sign the board deck," own renewal numbers, debug forecasts)
- Emphasized aligning content with Outreach's five campaign themes, ensuring one unified strategy, creative direction, messaging, and audience per theme
- Recommended deep audience analysis to better understand pain points and content needs
- Will develop voice and style guide collaboratively so content flexes Outreach's brand beyond flat pieces

### Content Creation and Repurposing

- Focus on creating high-quality, in-depth content that can be repurposed into various formats (PDFs, social, guides, etc.)
- Example: Transforming basic "interview questions" article into comprehensive guide with custom research, quotes, and sample dialogues
- Capability to convert webinars/podcasts into detailed articles, enhancing SEO value and user experience
- Content must start with SEO foundation to unlock repurposing potential; pure blog-first content is harder to leverage across channels

### Technical SEO and Content Management

- Offer technical SEO expertise, including internal linking, schema, and meta tags
- Can handle content staging and publishing to minimize client workload
- Team includes technical SEO experts and hands-on implementers (e.g., former TripAdvisor technical SEO leads)
- Flexible in adapting to Outreach's existing SEO brief templates and processes

### Collaboration and Review Process

- Weekly meetings and daily Slack communication for alignment
- Flexible approval process for content ideas and assignments
- Preference for batched approvals to maximize efficiency and reduce context switching
- Can adapt to client preference: some prefer batch-based phases, others prefer weekly cadence
- Willing to be proactive (recommending topics, identifying gaps) while staying responsive to internal business signals Outreach may not share
