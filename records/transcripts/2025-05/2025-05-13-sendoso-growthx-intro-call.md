  - Team includes industry veterans (e.g., ex-TechCrunch editor-in-chief, former Fanatics/TripAdvisor SEO lead)

### AI Content Generation Concerns

  - GrowthX stresses their quality-first approach, iterative calibration process with clients
  - Multiple human touchpoints in workflows to ensure relevance, accuracy, and brand alignment

### Content Strategy Opportunities for Sendoso

  - Leverage Postal acquisition for expanded content/media opportunities
  - Explore topic areas beyond manual sales gifting (e.g., ABM, outbound marketing, AI experiences)
  - Create comprehensive guides and repurpose social content into deeper, educational pieces
