## Key Topics

### Role Overview and Requirements

  - Content manager role focuses on semi-automated workflows with human intervention
  - Emphasis on editorial mindset, not just writing skills
  - Need for articulating where output is lacking and how to improve it
  - Ability to think architecturally about recurring issues and solutions
  - Experience with editing other writers' work is crucial

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Use of internal platform (ContentOS) for article creation
  - Workflow steps: assignment creation, outlining, rough drafts, polishing, fact-checking
  - Customization of workflows based on client needs (e.g., healthcare regulations)
  - Importance of providing specific, actionable feedback to improve workflows

### Fernanda's Relevant Experience

  - Currently an editorial lead at Hogarth, managing a team of 9 editors/writers
  - Experience in healthcare industry content (Abbott, Centene)
  - Familiar with using ChatGPT and other AI tools for content creation
  - Skilled in providing feedback and developing resources/processes for clients

### Typical Workday and Expectations

  - Asynchronous work with some overlap in PT/ET for meetings
  - Managing content for 1-3 clients, usually weeks ahead of publication
  - Using Airtable for task organization and prioritization
  - Running workflows to generate assignments, outlines, and drafts
  - Reviewing and refining content at various stages
  - Collaborating with Managing Editors for final review
  - Average output of 5-7 pieces of content per day

### Content Quality and Improvement Process

  - Creating detailed writing instruction documents for clients
  - Continuous refinement of workflows based on feedback and analysis
  - Goal to narrow the gap between initial output and publishable content
