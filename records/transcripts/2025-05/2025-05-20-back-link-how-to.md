## Key Topics

### Characteristics of Good Backlinks

  - High domain authority score
  - Reputable, non-sketchy sources
  - Relevant anchor text and placement
  - Natural link profile (not spammy)
  - Ideally from sites that don't sell links (e.g. major publications)
  - Generate actual referral traffic

### Backlink Acquisition Process

  - Using Loganix platform to select and purchase backlinks
  - Providing URLs and anchor text to Brainy Beast for manual outreach
  - Aiming for an average cost of $250 per link, balancing budget constraints
  - Targeting a mix of homepage, service pages, and key content pieces
  - Considering factors like domain rating, traffic, and relevance when selecting

### Backlink Strategy Considerations

  - Balancing different types of backlinks for a natural profile
  - Focusing on niche-relevant sites when possible
  - Promoting both homepage and specific content/service pages
  - Aligning backlink strategy with overall content goals and performance
  - Potential to develop an internal backlink exchange network among clients

### Expanding Content Distribution

  - Backlinks are a priority but other distribution methods discussed:
      - Email marketing
      - Social media content repurposing
      - Creating bonus content for clients (e.g. playlists, meditations)
  - Aim to position growthx.ai as more than just a content production service

### Team Roles and Responsibilities

  - Managing Editors (Jess, Jürgen) to oversee backlink strategy
  - Senior Content Managers (e.g. Becerra, Matt) could potentially assist
  - Branko to lead development of backlink processes/offerings
  - Content Managers to focus on individual content pieces
