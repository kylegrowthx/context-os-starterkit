## Context

Andi Bailey was recently hired as Head of Customer Operations at GrowthX, joining a team managing content marketing projects across multiple client accounts. The onboarding call involved Marcel Santilli (CEO) and Bridget McGillivray (internal stakeholder) walking Andi through the operational landscape. The context is urgent: the organization has grown but operational infrastructure hasn't scaled proportionally. The client database (in Notion) is outdated and unreliable, processes vary inconsistently across directors and pods, and there's visibility gaps into account health (evidenced by recent surprise risks like GetPod's churn). The organization uses Airtable for content tracking, Linear for project management, and is trying to measure CM/ME productivity and tie output to business metrics.

## Key Topics

### Client Database and Communication Standards

  - Current client database in Notion is outdated and not used as source of truth
  - Need to standardize update process (e.g. directors update by Friday EOD)
  - Implement clear cadences for client updates, meetings, and follow-ups
  - Ensure directors are following established processes consistently

### Project Management and Delivery Improvements

  - Many directors lack clear strategy and organized workflow management
  - Standardize use of Airtable for content tracking and assignment management
  - Implement consistent content strategy approach (clusters, assignments, calibration)
  - Ensure basics like published URL tracking for metrics/results visibility

### Productivity and Resource Allocation

  - Investigate why CMs reportedly only produce ~8 content pieces/week
  - Have CMs/MEs record Loom videos of their workflows to identify inefficiencies
  - Consider implementing time tracking to understand resource allocation
  - Goal: Optimize cost per quality output and improve overall efficiency

### Standardization and Scaling

  - Work on templating and standardizing processes across accounts
  - Audit "best" and "worst" accounts from each director to identify gaps
  - Develop plan to efficiently onboard strategy sprint clients into maintenance
  - Balance standardization with flexibility for unique client needs

### Metrics and Reporting

  - Develop centralized view of key metrics per account (publishing volume, traffic, etc.)
  - Consider building health metrics into CRM (e.g. HubSpot) for upsell conversations
  - Track human cost per quality output as a key KPI
  - Implement "controllable inputs" mentality to drive desired outcomes
