## Overview

- Sales process needs adjustment: Clearer scoping and expectation-setting with clients before closing deals
- Delivery team faces challenges: Untrained staff, misaligned expectations, and inefficient workflows
- Workflow improvements needed: Formalize process for iterating on and managing the content production system
- Human-side enhancements: Better training, onboarding, and performance management for content managers (CMs) and managing editors (MEs)

---

## Key Topics

### Sales Process Misalignment

  - Current approach: Vague promises leading to misaligned client expectations
  - Proposal: Implement more structured, transparent scoping before closing deals
      - Example: Detailed statement of work (SOW) outlining deliverables, timelines, and expectations
  - Consider different pricing/packaging for fixed-scope vs. open-scope projects
  - Potential solution: Pre-close alignment session to review detailed project timeline with clients

### Delivery Team Challenges

  - Organization is new (4-5 weeks old), lacking established processes and expertise
  - Understaffing issues for complex, custom projects (e.g., StackBlitz, BAPI)
  - Need for better skills assessment and training before assigning team members to projects
  - Suggestion: Implement a "bootcamp" or training period before putting new hires on client work

### Workflow Improvement Process

  - Current state: Ad-hoc requests to Daniel or Kirkland for changes
  - Proposal: Formalize weekly or bi-weekly grooming sessions
      - Involve key stakeholders (directors, MEs, engineers)
      - Review backlog, prioritize improvements, and align on roadmap
  - Need for better visibility into ongoing workflow improvements
      - Suggestion: Create a high-level board or dashboard for tracking progress
  - Consider implementing regular async updates on workflow changes

### Content Quality and Efficiency

  - Issue raised: CMs spending significant time (avg. 30 min) editing AI-generated content
  - Potential solutions:
      - Improve prompts to reduce common issues (e.g., removing unnecessary "future" sections)
      - Implement better tracking of time spent on content editing
      - Consider adding a copy-editing step for high-value clients (similar to Animals agency approach)
