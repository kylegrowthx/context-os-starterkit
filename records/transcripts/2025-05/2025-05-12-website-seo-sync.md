## Summary

GrowthX's Prateek Gupta synced with Firework's content and SEO teams on multilingual SEO strategy for Firework's website. The team prioritized creating a sitemap for the Japanese site (jp.firework.com) due to high search volume (296+ clicks), and agreed to defer French/Spanish sitemaps until more content is translated. They mapped out a phased content translation approach: identify top 50 evergreen blog posts, translate 10 each to French and Spanish, measure indexing rates after one week, then scale to 5-10 posts per week.
