## Key Topics

### Pre-Sales Process Improvement

  - Implement a system for Owen to prepare detailed notes before calls, including:
      - Company fit assessment
      - SEO visibility and authority score
      - Key stakeholder information
      - Relevant background details
  - Utilize Fathom for call recordings and transcripts to gather context
  - Create and leverage "leave-behind" materials (e.g., videos, audits) to enhance prospect engagement

### Post-Call Follow-up Optimization

  - Owen to draft follow-up emails based on Fathom call recaps
  - Bruno to review and add SOW if the opportunity is qualified
  - Use Adio comments to communicate next steps and maintain deal history
  - Prioritize booking next meetings for qualified opportunities

### Pipeline Management and Hygiene

  - Regular review and cleanup of open deals in the pipeline
  - Focus on moving high and medium intent opportunities forward
  - Close or reassess low-intent opportunities
  - Ensure proper documentation of closed-lost reasons

### Team Communication and Rituals

  - Implement daily stand-ups (15 minutes) to discuss priorities and blockers
  - Conduct regular pipe review meetings to assess deal progress and next steps
  - Use Slack and Adio comments for clear, asynchronous communication

### Automation and Process Refinement

  - Explore options for automating task creation in Adio when new deals are created
  - Continuously refine processes based on team feedback and efficiency gains
  - Consider implementing Linear for project management templates (e.g., 12-week engagement outline)
