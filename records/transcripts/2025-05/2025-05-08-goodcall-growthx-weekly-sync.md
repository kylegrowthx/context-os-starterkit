## Key Topics

### Website Improvements

Bob addressed critical on-page SEO issues this week. He fixed H1 tag duplication per page, moved tables of contents below images (which were previously squeezing page layout on mobile), and removed duplicate content scattered across articles. JavaScript rendering issues were also resolved. Bob added footer links to all major cluster categories (salons, BPOs, answering services) across the entire site, improving internal linking structure. The main outstanding issue is mobile responsiveness, which still needs work. Bob used SEMrush's site health tool to identify and prioritize further improvements like fixing broken redirects.

### Content Strategy and Publishing

Prateek published 45 articles this week, roughly double the usual 25/week rate, to clear a one-week backlog. He's planning to close the remaining backlog this week. He completed Bulova reviews and comparisons and is now pivoting to a new targeted campaign: creating content across 50 new industries identified for answering services. Out of a list of 99 industries Bob provided, 35 were already covered, 64 were duplicates. Prateek is focusing the remaining 50 industries starting this week. He noted that BPO and Ceylon content have underperformed so far despite 25 articles each; the team agreed to give these categories two more weeks before deciding whether to pivot strategy.

### SEO Performance

Clicks increased from 90 to 140 week-over-week, a 50% improvement driven almost entirely by the answering services category. The "Funnelive service" article was a standout, generating 11 clicks on its own. Healthcare appointment scheduling continues to be the top performer overall. Impressions grew slightly but the real leverage is in click growth, particularly in the answering services vertical.

### Link Building

Link building outreach to relevant website owners has started. Goodcall is expecting high-quality links to come in before June. On a month-to-month basis, the goal is to reach 20 links/month, which should help clear a historical link-building deficit while establishing a sustainable pace going forward.

### Keyword Rankings

Goodcall now has 75+ keywords in the top 10 of Google results, with 101 keywords in positions 1-3 (per SEMrush data) and 532 keywords in positions 4-10. This represents significant growth. The team is also seeing growth in SERP features, particularly Google's AI Overviews.

### AI Views Impact and Industry Trends

Google expanded AI Overviews coverage at the end of April. Goodcall is appearing in AI views frequently, which has brand visibility value, but the click-through impact is mixed. Prateek noted that on some of his other client projects, clicks have dropped 10-20% due to AI views capturing search traffic that previously went to organic results. The hypothesis is that impressions in AI views may build brand recall over time, but the immediate impact is a trade-off between visibility (impressions) and direct traffic (clicks). Bob will be speaking at the Twilio Developer Conference next week (brand awareness focus) and a call center industry conference in roughly five weeks (high-intent audience with potential for an enterprise landing page).
