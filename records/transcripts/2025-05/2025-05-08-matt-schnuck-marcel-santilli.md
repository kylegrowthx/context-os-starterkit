- Utilizes a grid-based system with various workflows for different content creation stages
- Combines multiple AI models (Claude, ChatGPT, Perplexity) for optimal results at each stage
- Includes steps for brief creation, research enrichment, outline generation, and article drafting
- Human oversight from managing directors, editors, and content managers throughout the process
- Flexibility to swap AI models as needed for best performance (e.g., switching from You.com to Perplexity for research)

- Targets high purchase intent keywords, differentiating from direct competitors like HIMS and Roman
