## Summary

Dimmo and GrowthX aligned on removing the video gate from Dimmo's product comparison site to improve conversion rates — finding that of 162,556 visitors since launch, only 5,708 completed the gate form, and in the past week alone 250 users tried to watch videos but abandoned because of the gate. They also agreed to explore alternative lead capture methods (pop-ups, Gmail sign-in, feature-gated content) and discussed publishing all demo videos to a YouTube channel for additional SEO benefit. GrowthX needs to resolve a webhook issue blocking the publication of 75 new product review pages, with 30 pages ready and 30 more expected by week's end.

---

## Context

Dimmo is a B2B SaaS company in the product comparison / AI demo space. GrowthX is delivering a content marketing engagement to drive organic traffic to Dimmo's product demo pages, which showcase side-by-side comparisons of software tools. Dimmo launched their content hub in May 2024 and has amassed 107 live product review pages with 75 more in the pipeline. The site is currently generating 22 keywords in top 10 Google positions, though some high-intent product pages (Copy.ai, Thena, Trumpet) have seen recent ranking drops, possibly due to recent meta title changes. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's ongoing delivery cadence to coordinate content publishing, technical SEO fixes, and strategic direction on lead capture and distribution.
