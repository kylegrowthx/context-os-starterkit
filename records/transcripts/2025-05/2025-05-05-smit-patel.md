## Context

Pieces is a developer tool startup founded to build long-term memory infrastructure for AI — a desktop application that captures everything users do (screens, browser activity, files, applications) and converts it into a searchable vector database accessible through LLMs like ChatGPT, Claude, and others. Smit Patel, formerly an early employee at Postman and Datadog who saw both companies scale from near-zero to hundreds of millions in revenue, joined as CEO less than a year ago to rebuild the growth and positioning functions from scratch. When Smit arrived, Pieces had a major SEO problem: the previous leader had hired low-quality external writers and scaled content volume without regard for quality, resulting in high traffic but poor engagement — "20 dad jokes" that brought visitors who weren't interested in the product. Smit immediately shifted to in-house content creation by engineers and developer advocates, trading short-term traffic for better engagement and brand fit. The company's primary growth driver remains organic search, and they recently ran paid ads experiments that generated volume but without high-quality conversions. As a venture-backed company, Smit is focused on growth metrics that signal strong fundamentals for fundraising — specifically signups and downloads — and is exploring partnerships like GrowthX to scale quality content production efficiently.

  - 12-week documented process for developing content strategy
  - Combines editorial and programmatic SEO approaches
  - Creates a "Content OS" with topical opportunities and keyword clusters
  - Uses AI-assisted workflows with human oversight for content creation
  - Focuses on understanding client's product, personas, and industry deeply
  - Includes calibration phases in early phases to gather feedback and refine outputs
  - Human-in-the-loop steps throughout to ensure quality and adapt to feedback

### SEO Performance Expectations

  - Typical results timeline: initial spike around month 3, compounding growth thereafter
  - Example: Customer with no blog reached 100+ daily clicks within 3 months
  - Case study: Zooplo (API management) saw growth from 200 to 350+ daily clicks in a few months starting early February

### AI-Generated Content and SEO

  - GrowthX believes Google focuses on user experience and content quality, not detection of AI-generated content
  - Recent algorithm changes target broad, unfocused content strategies (e.g., affiliate sites, HubSpot/Zapier-style authority-building for unrelated topics)
  - Google rewards understanding "what is your website about" and creating authoritative, relevant content for specific personas
  - Focus should be on deep persona understanding and answering specific customer questions, not chasing vanity traffic metrics

### GrowthX's Case Studies and Experience
