## Key Topics

### growthx.ai's Business Model

  - Internal platform development for content creation, not SaaS
  - $7M run rate, profitable, recently raised $12M Series A
  - 40 employees, fully remote, global team
  - Clients pay for content output, not software access

### Content Creation Process

  - AI workflows act as "writers", human managers as editors
  - Workflows include outline generation, keyword research, article writing, post-processing
  - Highly customizable for each client's specific needs and brand voice
  - Enables high-leverage content production (e.g., 40 articles edited in one day)

### Role of Senior Content Manager

  - Acts more as an editor than a writer, managing AI workflows
  - Articulates differences between good and bad content to improve processes
  - Bridges gap between content needs and engineering implementation
  - Requires high agency and ability to synthesize disparate skills

### Types of Content Produced

  - Programmatic SEO (e.g., 50+ state-specific pages for legal startup)
  - Editorial/organic content for authority building and top-of-funnel traffic
  - Custom publication creation for clients (e.g., sponsored industry news site)

### Future Vision

  - Expand beyond content to other business processes (sales, HR, operations)
  - Eventually offer platform publicly as a tool for knowledge workers
  - Potential to revolutionize media landscape, enabling solo creators to compete with large newsrooms
