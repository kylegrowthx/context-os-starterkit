## Key Topics

### ComplyAdvantage's Current Marketing Approach

  - 60% of revenue from inbound marketing, but not sustainable long-term
  - Shifting focus to support outbound sales efforts
  - SEO program in "maintenance mode", focused on refreshing existing content
  - Organic search drives smaller deals (startups, early-stage companies)
  - Paid advertising is a significant budget item, based on cost per MQL

### GrowthX's AI-Powered Content Creation Offering

  - Uses AI workflows to scale content creation (8-16 pieces/week)
  - Human-in-the-loop process for quality control and brand alignment
  - Experience with regulated industries and technical content
  - Fact-checking process to ensure accuracy
  - Initial calibration phase to align with brand voice and requirements

### Content Repurposing and Distribution Strategy

  - Repurpose existing content (e.g. podcasts, webinars) for SEO and multi-channel distribution
  - Create comprehensive, engaging content experiences from audio/video content
  - Optimize for both traditional search and AI-powered search tools (e.g. Perplexity, ChatGPT)

### ComplyAdvantage's Concerns and Constraints

  - Skepticism about AI-generated content uniqueness and quality
  - Concerns about accuracy in highly regulated industry
  - Budget constraints - $9k/month would be 2x most expensive marketing tool
  - Short-term focus on revenue goals and reliance on paid acquisition
