## Key Topics

### Hasura's Business Pivot

  - Shifting focus from API product to AI-centric offerings
  - Deal cycle reduced from 12-18 months to 3-6 months
  - Average deal size increased from $60K to $300K+
  - Transitioning from inbound to outbound sales, aiming to return to inbound motion
  - Purchased PromptQL.io/com domain for rebranding efforts

### GrowthX's Content Strategy Approach

  - Utilizes AI workflows to scale high-quality content production
  - Employs a team of experienced editors, SEO specialists, and technical experts
  - Offers a comprehensive "Content OS" for strategic planning and execution
  - Aims for daily content publication, significantly higher than typical 2-5 pieces per month
  - Focuses on creating in-depth, AI-friendly content that performs well with LLMs

### GrowthX's Engagement Model

  - 8-week strategy sprint for $20,000
  - Includes company research, strategy development, style guide creation, and content calibration
  - Proposes a 12-month commitment after the sprint, based on mutual agreement
  - Flexible approach, sometimes recommending companies to implement strategy independently if not ready for full engagement

### Hasura's Content Challenges

  - Current content strategy lacks cohesion due to managing two product lines
  - Struggling to identify and target the right audience (technical vs. go-to-market)
  - Need for a systematic approach to content creation and strategy
  - Difficulty in producing timely, relevant content for marketing initiatives
