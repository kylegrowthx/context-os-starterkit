## Context

Hellobdv is building a women's supplement brand with a differentiated approach: products designed for specific life stages and age cohorts, with clinically-backed dosages and a novel form factor to improve compliance. They're targeting segments like perimenopause and menopause and plan to launch at the end of September. The company has already done significant product research and has an in-house doctor for medical review, but faces resource constraints around scaling content production. Jason Gong found Hellobdv through LinkedIn and recognized a fit with GrowthX's core value proposition: AI tools alone create low-quality content, but combined with domain expertise and a disciplined process (research → outline review → fact-check → expert review), they can produce high-quality, trustworthy content. GrowthX has relevant experience with Interwall (ketamine therapy content) and Abnormal Security (high-accuracy security content), both regulated or trust-sensitive spaces.
