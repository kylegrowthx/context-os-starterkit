  - Elvis will export graphics and share in Slack; plans to test refined approach before wider rollout
  - Mara flagged that GrowthX is also developing range of AI image types — team open to suggestions on image styles to standardize

### Upcoming Superhuman Features & Campaigns

  - "Summer of AI" campaign launching in June (internal AI product campaign currently in flight since ~2-3 weeks prior)
  - New vacation responder feature: Auto-detects "Out of Office" events from calendar data (deterministically, not AI-powered) and prompts user the business day before vacation starts; prefills date and default message for easy enabling (addresses user pain point of frantically setting responder before travel)
  - Significant calendar functionality improvements planned for July launch to achieve feature parity with Outlook (major blocker for Outlook users who rely on Outlook desktop for unified email + calendar experience)
  - Planned calendar product launch with dedicated landing page in Q3; team exploring AI-native angle for calendar (how AI helps with availability sharing and meeting scheduling, not just parity features)
  - Morgan planning webinar with other AI-native tools (estimated June) — generates strong interest and content collaboration opportunities
  - Recent AI email webinar (2 days before this sync) generated tons of questions, particularly around calendar features; strong signal for Q3 calendar content demand

### Content Collaboration Opportunities

  - Repurpose webinar content into articles — Elvis notes this has worked well historically, and Superhuman has "a decent amount" of webinar material already available
  - Superhuman will post AI email webinar blog post (not yet live, but Morgan can share link); GrowthX will monitor resources page (Alex leading website overhaul) for webinar archive
  - Expand Q3 content strategy to include calendar productivity topics and AI-native calendar angles (beyond feature parity positioning)
  - Superhuman to provide positioning and high-level strategy for calendar work to guide GrowthX content production (dependency: must be completed before GrowthX can execute on Q3 content)
