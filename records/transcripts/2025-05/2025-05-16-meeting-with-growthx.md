## Summary

Bruno Santilli from GrowthX presented the company's AI-assisted content creation services to Josh Payne at Onward, a bootstrapped 10-15 person team focused on efficient growth. GrowthX is shifting from traditional 12-month contracts to a strategy-first model, proposing a 2-month engagement ($20-30k) for full assessment and go-to-market strategy, followed by a $9k/month option for ongoing editorial services producing 7-8 articles per week. Josh expressed interest but pushed back on the timeline, wanting to see content samples and results within a shorter pilot period rather than committing to a lengthy assessment phase.

---

## Context

Onward is a bootstrapped SaaS company with 10-15 employees focused on efficient, sustainable growth over rapid expansion. They have published case studies on their website with previous help from a content marketer and want to develop their blog with long-form SEO pillar content, comparison pieces (Onward vs. competitors like Route and Corso), and strengthen their organic presence. Their founder, Josh Payne, is interested in scaling their content and marketing efforts but is being deliberate about partnerships and ROI given their bootstrap constraints. GrowthX is a rapidly growing B2B content marketing and AI visibility services company ($7-8M ARR, 60-65 employees) led by founder Marcel Santilli (with brother Bruno leading sales). Bruno joined the company in January 2026 and was pitching GrowthX's content services as a potential solution for Onward's scaling needs.
