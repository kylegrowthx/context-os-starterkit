## Key Topics

### GrowthX.ai's Business Model and Needs

  - AI-powered growth agency serving B2B clients like Webflow, AppNormal Securities, Ramp, and Reddit
  - Offers end-to-end SEO services, including link building, which they currently outsource
  - Seeking a reliable partner for consistent, high-quality link building across multiple clients
  - Flexible on engagement model, from transactional to strategic partnership

### Loganix's Service Offerings

  - Core business (70%) is link building, with additional SEO services available
  - Two main engagement models:
    1.  Transactional: Fixed-price "authority links" ($400-$600 per link)
    2.  Strategic Partnership: Publisher fee plus margin, more flexible pricing ($200-$1000+ per link)
  - Customizable workflow integration (Asana, spreadsheets, Notion, etc.)
  - Additional services: local SEO, keyword research, content creation

### Link Building Process and Expectations

  - Average turnaround time: 8-10 days for standard requests
  - Longer timelines for specialized niches (e.g., European markets, crypto)
  - Flexible approval process: client can review and approve each link or set criteria for automatic approval
  - Loganix can source new publishers for specific client needs

### Pricing and Quality Considerations

  - Link prices vary based on metrics (Domain Rating, traffic, etc.) and publisher relationships
  - Higher-end clients (e.g., HubSpot) may require more authoritative links (DR 70+) for competitive keywords
  - Loganix recommends against very low-cost links ($190) for most clients, as they may not be effective
