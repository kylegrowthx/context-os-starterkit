## Summary

VerticalScope and GrowthX explored a potential partnership to accelerate content production across VerticalScope's 1200+ enthusiast forum communities using AI-powered workflows. GrowthX specializes in editorial and programmatic content generation, while VerticalScope seeks to solve declining engagement post-COVID by increasing content velocity and quality—particularly in automotive forums where posting volumes are declining. ## Context

VerticalScope operates a massive network of 1200+ niche community forums (Ben'sWorld.org being a classic car example) reaching approximately 120 million users per month with over 2 billion posts of user-generated content. CEO Rob Laidlaw and VP Chris Goodridge are exploring how to leverage AI to increase content velocity and quality in their communities, which have faced headwinds from post-COVID user attention shifts and Google algorithm volatility. GrowthX, founded by Marcel Santilli, specializes in AI-powered content workflows that break content creation into repeatable processes. Kyle Gaudreau, VP of Customer Growth at GrowthX, had previously been GrowthX's first customer and was excited to explore this opportunity with the VerticalScope team. This meeting was initiated after Chris received a recommendation to contact GrowthX.
