## Context

This is a fundamental business transformation that requires comprehensive SEO strategy to protect their search visibility and organic traffic during the transition. Talia Moyal (Head of Product at Gitpod) reached out to GrowthX specifically because they recognized SEO expertise was critical for this migration and wanted to understand best practices. Dave Capone, who brings 20+ years of SEO experience including large-scale site migrations for companies like Fanatics, Red Ventures, and TripAdvisor, was introduced to lead the account. The team is still in early planning stages and determining where external support (GrowthX) should focus versus internal execution.
