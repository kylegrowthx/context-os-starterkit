## Context

James Winter is an interim executive at Engine.com, a B2B business travel platform for SMB and mid-market companies in blue-collar industries (construction, healthcare, energy, utilities). Engine.com generates revenue through hotel booking take rates and recently launched a groups product that unexpectedly gained traction in the weddings vertical.
