## Summary

GrowthX and Firework analyzed May blog performance and discovered a critical conversion challenge: only 10 out of 37 leads came from the US despite 5,500 monthly clicks, indicating a 0.67% click-to-lead conversion rate. The team discussed pivoting from high volume (10 blogs/week) to higher quality content focused on US lead generation, competitive analysis against Salesify and Bazaar Voice, and creating event-driven content around VivaTech. Prateek committed to delivering US-specific analytics, March/April blog performance reports, and early VivaTech blogs next week.

## Context

This is a weekly operational sync between GrowthX's content delivery team (led by Prateek Gupta) and Firework, an AI-powered user-generated content platform. Firework hired GrowthX to execute a blog-driven content strategy aimed at generating leads and building authority through SEO. The relationship is collaborative—Firework drives strategy direction while GrowthX executes content production. The meeting reflects ongoing tension between volume and quality: Firework's engineering team (led by Abhishek Anjanappa) is pushing for deeper analysis to understand why conversion rates are poor, while GrowthX is operationally focused on meeting production targets. The team also expanded to include French and Spanish website localization, adding complexity to attribution and audience segmentation.
