## Context

Imubit is an industrial AI platform focused on process optimization and operational efficiency in oil and gas, cement, steel, and other heavy industries. They recently overhauled their go-to-market strategy, transitioning from an AE-focused strategic sales model to inbound lead generation via a "value assessment" approach (rebranded from "request a demo"). This is a major operational shift — their AEs historically had no inbound pipeline and are still adjusting to handling web-generated leads. The team launched the value assessment campaign just two weeks before this call and already received 34 requests with 11 qualified leads, signaling strong market demand. GrowthX is now their strategic content and SEO partner, brought in specifically to shape Imubit's content narrative around their industrial AI platform, particularly as they roll out updated website navigation and industry pages that launched the day of this meeting.
