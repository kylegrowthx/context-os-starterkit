## Key Topics

### Current Challenges and Opportunities

  - Udemy has 250,000+ courses and 98% of instructors opted into AI policies
  - Need to better utilize course transcripts and data for SEO and content creation
  - Aim to shift perception from affordable, promo-driven courses to higher-value offerings
  - Focus on career accelerators and enterprise-level AI fluency programs

### AI-Powered Content Strategies

  - Create topic hubs and dynamically generated pages for better SEO and user experience
  - Develop salary estimators and career path tools using course and market data
  - Repurpose podcast content (e.g., Leading Up) into frameworks and course recommendations
  - Implement AI-driven newsletter curation for millions of subscribers

### Course Curation and Creation

  - Build an AI Accelerator program with \~800 curated courses on AI fundamentals
  - Develop persona-based learning paths (e.g., dev, business skills)
  - Identify content gaps and leverage third-party data or instructor outreach to fill them
  - Explore cohort-based learning models for higher-end offerings

### Instructor and Expert Focus

  - Highlight instructor expertise and real-world experience more prominently
  - Consider expert-led cohorts and lightning sessions for specialized topics
  - Curate top experts in fields like AI and research for premium offerings

### Market Positioning and Growth

  - Drive higher ASP and deal values in enterprise accounts (UB side)
  - Diversify focus areas beyond traditional tech courses to include business skills and AI
  - Address changing buyer behavior and competition from free online learning resources
