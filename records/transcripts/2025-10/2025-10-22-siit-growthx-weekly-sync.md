## Context

Siit is an AI-powered service desk platform that centralizes data from internal tools to drive operations and efficiency. GrowthX is providing B2B content marketing services to support Siit's go-to-market strategy. Carmel Schetrit (Head of Marketing at Siit) and Matthew Alves-Hill (GrowthX) are in a weekly sync to align on content performance and plan the Q4 content roadmap. Siit is preparing for a Series A fundraise and is shifting its product narrative to emphasize the AI and agentic capabilities, moving beyond the "service desk" positioning competitors are using. This weekly meeting focuses on tightening the content strategy to support this narrative shift while addressing immediate performance gaps in the tool pages — which are driving strong traffic but failing to convert to pipeline.

  - **Proposed Solution:** GrowthX will test stronger CTAs on Tool pages to improve conversion.
      - **Rationale:** The current "Alternatives" section at the end of pages is not an effective conversion driver.

### Solution: Q4 Content Strategy Pivot

  - The Q4 strategy will pivot to an "AI Service Desk" cluster, prioritizing the AI story.
