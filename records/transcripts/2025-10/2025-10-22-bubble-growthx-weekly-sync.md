## Key Topics

### Content Refresh Strategy

  - **Prioritization:** GrowthX's Airtable audit (GSC, SEMrush data) will guide refresh priorities.
  - **"Vs." Articles (GrowthX):**
      - **Why:** Requires deep research into competitor feature sets, a task suited for GrowthX's tools (e.g., Perplexity AI).
      - **Goal:** Establish a quarterly refresh cadence to keep content current.
  - **"How-to" Guides (Bubble):**
      - **Why:** Requires internal resources for accurate, in-platform screenshots and examples.
      - **New Format:** Shift from outdated, screenshot-heavy guides to a video-first approach.
          - **Text:** General development principles.
          - **Video:** Embedded, Bubble-specific tutorial.
      - **Validation:** GrowthX will analyze SERPs to confirm the SEO viability of this format.
  - **`bubble.io/how-to-build` Page:**
      - **Why:** This landing page for \~60 outdated tutorials needs a strategic decision.
      - **Options:** Refresh relevant guides to the new video format or kill low-value pages.
      - **Timeline:** Refresh work can begin after the redesigned property editor enters beta in December.

### "How to Design a Great Website" Refresh

  - **Purpose:** A test run to align an existing article with new Bubble messaging.
  - **Key Changes:**
      - **Structure:** Moved "web design trends" section to the top.
      - **SEO:** Condensed opening paragraphs after H2/H3s for better LLM visibility and skimmability.
      - **Messaging:** Updated Bubble mentions and linked to the AI website generator.
      - **Completeness:** Added UX/page speed topics to match top-ranking content.
      - **Integrations:** Mentioned Hotjar and Mixpanel as Bubble plugins for user testing.
  - **Feedback:** Bubble requested strategic comments on future drafts to explain the rationale behind edits (e.g., SEO, messaging).

### New Looker Dashboard

  - **Purpose:** Track content performance using data from GA4, GSC, and SEMrush.
  - **Key Metrics:**
      - **Engaged Sessions:** Sessions \>10s or with a scroll/click. Goal: \>50% of total sessions.
      - **Non-branded Clicks:** Traffic from non-brand queries; a key indicator of new content's SEO impact.
      - **LLM Referrals:** Traffic from AI sources (ChatGPT, Perplexity). The "best no-code app builder" post is the top performer.
  - **Note:** The dashboard's GA4 conversion events are not used by Bubble's internal Segment-based tracking and can be ignored.
