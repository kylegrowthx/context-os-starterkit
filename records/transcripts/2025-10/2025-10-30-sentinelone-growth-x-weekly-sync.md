  - **Launch Timeline:** The launch is delayed to next Thursday (tentative).
      - **Friday:** Marcus and George demo the instance to SentinelOne's web team.
      - **Monday:** SentinelOne's India-based QA and SEO teams begin a full review.

      - **Rationale:** SentinelOne's indexing is fast (e.g., 800 of 929 French pages indexed in 2–3 days), enabling a faster rollout.
  - **New Strategy → Build a Comprehensive Database:**
      - **Goal:** Create a definitive vulnerability database by publishing all CVEs from 2020 onward.
      - **Execution:** Publish CVEs year-by-year (e.g., all of 2025, then all of 2024).
      - **Data Source:** Use SentinelOne's NVD API pull to ensure the database is complete, not limited to SEO tool-driven lists.

### CS101 Content & Workflow

  - **Q3 Revisions:** 7 article revisions will be delivered today (Thursday) to meet the Q3 deadline.
  - **New Articles:** 5 new articles are bumped to next week.
  - **ContentStack Workflow:**
      - **Process:** Draft in Google Docs → Get final approval → Push final version to ContentStack as a draft.

  - **Topic Strategy Feedback:**
      - **Requirement:** Topic suggestions must target unique, high-volume keywords.
      - **Rationale:** SentinelOne's demand-led strategy requires focusing on topics with proven search volume.
