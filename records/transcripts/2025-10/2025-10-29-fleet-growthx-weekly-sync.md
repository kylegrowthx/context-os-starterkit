## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Topic Generation

  - **Initial Strategy:** Mirror competitor Jamf to quickly close keyword gaps.
      - **Method:** Aida used keyword gap analysis to identify topics Jamf covers but Fleet does not.
  - **Brock's Feedback:** This approach risks missing Fleet's unique advantages over Jamf.
      - **Example:** Fleet's cross-platform policies vs. Jamf's single-platform extension attributes.
  - **Decision:** Combine both approaches.
      - **Action:** Brock will provide a list of \~50 product-led topics.
      - **Rationale:** This ensures coverage of both competitive parity and unique features.

### Tone & Voice Refinement

  - **Goal:** Refine the AI's output to match Fleet's desired voice.
  - **Initial Test:** An AI analysis of the Stripe case study flagged some jargon.
      - **Context:** The article was a rushed rewrite of a jargon-filled original, explaining the AI's flags.
  - **Brock's Guidance:** The desired tone is direct and logical ("Spock-like"), not "frothy" or overly conversational.
      - **Action:** Brock will provide more technical articles as better examples.
      - **Note:** The "case study on Fleet" article was also cited as a good example of the desired conversational style for roadmap discussions.

### Messaging Challenge: "Linux MDM"

  - **Problem:** The term "Linux MDM" is a misnomer, as no formal MDM specification exists for Linux.
  - **Dilemma:** Using the term could capture search traffic (\~100/mo) but risks undermining Fleet's authority.
  - **Solution:** A nuanced, educational approach.
      - **Method:** Acknowledge the term's informal use ("Kleenex" metaphor) while explaining why a formal Linux MDM doesn't exist.
      - **Rationale:** This strategy captures search intent while establishing Fleet as an expert.

### Content Calendar & Workflow

  - **Tool:** Airtable is the central content database.
  - **Views:**
      - `Ideas to Approve`: For topic feedback.
      - `Published Blogs`: For completed content.
      - `Ready to Review`: Will be added for content awaiting Brock's approval.
  - **Workflow:** Brock will use Airtable's comment feature to provide feedback on topics.
