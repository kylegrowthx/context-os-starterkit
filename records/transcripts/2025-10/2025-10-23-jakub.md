## Key Topics

### Process Gaps & Inconsistencies

  - **Problem:** Rapid growth outpaced process standardization, creating inconsistent, ad hoc workflows that waste resources and prevent scaling.
  - **Example 1: Content Audits**
      - **Issue:** No standard process for content audits and refreshes.
      - **Resource:** Jakub created a V1 playbook (using ChatGPT) for Ada and Eric, which Talal will review and improve.
  - **Example 2: Organic Content Strategy**
      - **Issue:** Inconsistent keyword research and clustering methods across MEs.
      - **Worst Practice:** Exporting raw SEMrush lists (300–3,000 keywords) for clients to sort.
      - **Best Practice:** Matt's Interwell process, which translates high-level goals into organized topics.
      - **Resource:** Jakub is developing an Organic Strategy Framework to standardize this.

### Content Quality Decline (Aerobs → Atlas)

  - **Problem:** Content quality declined significantly after the migration from Aerobs to Atlas.
  - **Specific Issues:**
      - **Hallucinations:** Made-up stats and irrelevant citations.
      - **Redundancy:** Repetitive language, especially in longer articles.
      - **Technical Writing:** Struggles with complex topics (e.g., Flowsome, Webstack).
      - **Cannibalization:** New content competes with existing pages, wasting resources.
  - **Resolution Process:** MEs flag issues via tickets to ENG or discuss workarounds in Slack (e.g., using Cloud projects).

### Strategic Initiatives

  - **1. Playbook Library:**
      - **Goal:** Centralize all successful, client-agnostic workflows (e.g., audits, refreshes) into a single, searchable database.
      - **Rationale:** Capture and scale knowledge that is currently lost after one-off client use.
      - **Long-Term Vision:** A public-facing library for lead generation.
  - **2. Experiment Tracker:**
      - **Goal:** Create a data-backed database of proven SEO/CRO experiments.
      - **Rationale:** Move from ideas to validated results to confidently recommend tactics to all clients.
      - **Method:** Systematically track experiments across clients to gather shareable data for marketing (e.g., LinkedIn, webinars).
  - **3. Cross-Client Data Analysis:**
      - **Issue:** No macro-level analysis of performance data (e.g., GSC, GA) across clients.
      - **Status:** A project by Panzer may be in progress; integration with Atlas dashboards is a potential path.
