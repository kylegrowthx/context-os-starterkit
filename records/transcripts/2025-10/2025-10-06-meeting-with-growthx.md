## Context

Leapsome is an HR/people operations software company (sells primarily into SMB segment) that recently made a strategic shift away from traditional agency partnerships for content marketing. They initially hired an agency but discovered AI-powered approaches could be more efficient; that agency subsequently dismantled due to changing market dynamics. The team has been experimenting with ChatGPT internally but hit scaling challenges: poor memory recall beyond 1000 words, hallucinations, and unreliable source attribution. A few months ago, they began working with Copy.ai, but experienced quality issues due to lack of human marketing expertise in the loop during workflow setup. Willow Thompson made the GrowthX connection through George Mayne, who works at GrowthX and previously worked with Willow at Pitch. This meeting was called to explore whether GrowthX's hybrid AI + human expert model could solve Leapsome's core problem: scaling high-quality, brand-aligned content at speed.
