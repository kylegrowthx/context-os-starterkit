  - **Problem:** The article review queue in Notion was blocked, slowing publishing velocity.
  - **Solution:** A new workflow was established to streamline reviews:
      - **Assignment:** David/Franki assign articles to the relevant SME.
      - **SME Action:** The SME leaves comments and moves the article to "Needs Edits."
      - **GrowthX Action:** Bailey's team makes revisions and moves the article to "Final CMO Review."
      - **Tracking:** A "time in stage" tracker will be added to monitor velocity.
