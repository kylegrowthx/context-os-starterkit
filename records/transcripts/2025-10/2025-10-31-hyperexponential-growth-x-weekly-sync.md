## Key Topics

### Content Quality & Strategy

  - **Problem:** Initial AI-generated content was too self-serving and lacked depth, leading to titles like "practical framework" and aggressive angles like "why integration beats best-of-breed tools."
  - **Solution:** A new agentic pipeline was approved for its superior quality, featuring more citations from credible sources (Accenture, BCG) and a more actionable FAQ format.
  - **Decision:** The strategy will pivot to SEO-driven educational content. Articles with self-serving angles will be revised to be more helpful and less product-focused.

### Content Review Process

  - **Problem:** The article review queue in Notion was blocked, slowing publishing velocity.
  - **Solution:** A new workflow was established to streamline reviews:
      - **Assignment:** David/Franki assign articles to the relevant SME.
      - **SME Action:** The SME leaves comments and moves the article to "Needs Edits."
      - **GrowthX Action:** Bailey's team makes revisions and moves the article to "Final CMO Review."
      - **Tracking:** A "time in stage" tracker will be added to monitor velocity.

### New Content Pipeline & Future Projects

  - **New Topics:** GrowthX generated \~40 new SEO-focused article ideas in Airtable, mixing high-volume general topics with low-volume, high-intent keywords.
  - **Podcast Repurposing:** Transcripts will be used for quotes and insights in new articles. Short-form video clips will also be embedded to boost engagement.
  - **"Recipe Book" Project:** A long-term project was approved to create a programmatic "recipe book" of practical AI agent use cases.
      - **Goal:** Inspire users with concrete examples of how to apply HX agents.
      - **Inspiration:** Zapier's integration pages and Clay's agent examples.
      - **Ownership:** Jakub (GrowthX) will scope the project, which will require engineering support.
