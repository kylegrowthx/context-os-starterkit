### Internal Linking Best Practices

  - New guidelines will be implemented to improve user experience and SEO.
  - **Link Density:** Target 3–4 internal links per 1,000 words.
      - **Rationale:** Avoids overwhelming users and is more effective for bots.
  - **Anchor Text:** Do not link out on a page's target keyword.
      - **Rationale:** Prevents cannibalization and maintains focus.
  - **User Flow:** Link down the funnel (e.g., "what is" → software comparison) to guide users naturally.
