## Key Topics

### Panther's Content Challenges

  - Bandwidth limitations: Need to produce wide breadth of content (blogs, sales enablement, product pages, white papers)
  - Reactive vs. proactive approach: Currently focused on product launches, not strategic topics
  - Technical depth: All content requires high level of technical accuracy
  - Resource optimization: Small marketing team trying to balance multiple priorities

### GrowthX's Offering

  - Two-phase approach: 8-week strategy sprint + 12-month growth execution
  - AI-powered workflows with expert human oversight
  - Focus on creating content for AI search visibility (AEO/GEO)
  - Daily publishing capability, wide topic coverage
  - Handles content creation, staging, and CMS publishing
  - Analytics for LLM visibility and performance tracking

### Content Creation Process

  - Uses sophisticated AI workflows beyond simple prompts
  - Incorporates client-specific artifacts (personas, style guides, product info)
  - Multi-step process: brief generation, research, outlining, drafting, fact-checking
  - Human experts review and refine content throughout
  - Can integrate client team quotes and insights

### Technical Content Capabilities

  - Successfully working with technical clients (e.g., Augment Code, Abnormal Security)
  - Can handle technical subject matter, but not code snippets or cutting-edge developments
  - Uses client-provided resources and approved sources for accuracy
  - Focuses on thought leadership, product comparisons, and industry trends

### Proof of Concept and Next Steps

  - GrowthX to provide workshop recording for team review
  - Will create a POC content piece based on Panther's guidelines
  - Team to internally discuss and evaluate the offering
  - Potential future meeting with GrowthX CEO and Panther leadership
