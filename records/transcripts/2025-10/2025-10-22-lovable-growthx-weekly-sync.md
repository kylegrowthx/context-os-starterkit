## Key Topics

### GrowthHackers Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** GrowthHackers' content, live due to a 3-month cancellation clause, is low-quality and creates brand risk.
      - **Low-Quality Content:** Pages are poorly written and provide incorrect information (e.g., "Lovable export code" page doesn't explain GitHub sync).
      - **Poor User Experience:** Users searching for support land on irrelevant pages.
      - **Minimal SEO Value:** Traffic is almost entirely branded; non-branded traffic is negligible (\~64 sessions).
  - **Decision:** Sunset the content immediately.
      - **Action:** Redirect the entire subdirectory to the homepage.
      - **Rationale:** This removes the brand risk and avoids potential Google penalties.

### Guides Content Launch & Workflow

  - **Launch:** The new Guides section is live with 8 articles.
  - **Goal:** Publish 5+ articles/week to build authority.
  - **Workflow Bottleneck:** The current PR process, requiring Furkan's review for every article, creates a 1–1.5 day delay.
  - **Solution:** Request direct merge access for content publishing to streamline the workflow.

### Template Library Strategy

  - **Goal:** Build authority in the template category by prioritizing high-impact work.
  - **Analysis:** GrowthX analyzed 8,000 competitor template URLs (Webflow, Wix, Envato) to quantify market opportunity.
      - **Website Templates:** High volume (1.1M US desktop searches/mo) and low difficulty (avg. 27/100).
      - **App Templates:** Lower volume and higher difficulty.
  - **Strategy:**
      - **Phase 1:** Focus on high-volume, low-difficulty website templates to secure early wins and build authority.
      - **Phase 2:** Tackle more complex app templates.
  - **Rationale:** This strategy aligns with a PLG model for broad top-of-funnel awareness, while complex app templates are better suited for a sales-led approach.

### AI-Enhanced Optimization (AEO)

  - **Process:** GrowthX used its proprietary platform, CheckVav, to generate 13,000 AEO prompts.
      - **Method:** Analyzed Reddit, forums, and Google Search to create user-question prompts.
      - **Value:** Provides free, unlimited tracking, replacing tools that would cost \~$5k/month.
  - **Next Step:** Track prompt performance for \~7 days to gather sufficient data before pruning or doubling down on specific prompts.

### Partnership & Collaboration

  - **Bug Report:** Marcel reported a bug in the "edges" function, which has shown a "Houston, we have a problem" page for 4 days since the "AI and cloud" tab was introduced.
  - **Collaboration Opportunity:** Marcel offered to co-host a live class for marketers on building a blog with Lovable.
      - **Outcome:** Ceci and Rachel agreed, seeing this as a key opportunity for "marketing to marketers" and to feature Marcel as an "expert vibe coder ambassador."
