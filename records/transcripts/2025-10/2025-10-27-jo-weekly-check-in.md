## Key Topics

### Smith Content Strategy

  - **Challenge:** Smith is a highly detail-oriented client (Madi) who flags minor issues (e.g., formatting, font) as major problems, creating significant review friction.
  - **Solution:** Proactively derisk the process by ensuring all content is perfect before submission.
      - **Tone:** Must be educational and non-promotional.
          - **Tool:** Use the "Call Flow Design Hub guidelines" in Claude or the "Smith SEO" GPT to check for the correct tone.
          - **Process:** Analyze the last 5 customer calls with ChatGPT to understand specific expectations.
      - **Accuracy:** Verify all facts and stats, especially in external links.
      - **Style:** Adhere strictly to the style guide (e.g., correct m-dash spacing).
      - **Internal Linking:** Ensure anchor text is precise.
  - **Priority:** The "Call Flow Design" cluster is the top priority.
  - **New Pipeline:** A new pipeline is needed to enrich high-performing industry pages (e.g., legal) that Madi wants to deepen. Usman will create a ticket for this.

### Exec Comparison Posts Strategy

  - **Goal:** Create templated editorial posts comparing Exec to competitors (e.g., Second Nature).
  - **Research Process:**
    1.  **Review Messaging Doc:** Read the new Exec messaging doc to understand key differentiators.
    2.  **Build Feature Matrix:** Use Perplexity/ChatGPT for deep research on all platforms.
          - **Output:** A table comparing features across all competitors.
          - **Requirement:** Cite all sources to prevent hallucinations.
    3.  **Analyze Customer Calls:** Use Fathom to summarize the last 3 Exec calls, focusing on competitor discussions.
  - **Positioning:**
      - **Exec's Strength:** Unmatched customization for enterprise-level role-plays.
      - **Competitor's Strength:** Often cheaper tools for smaller businesses.
      - **Strategy:** Frame the comparison around market focus (enterprise vs. SMB) to highlight Exec's value.
  - **Template & Assets:**
      - **Template:** Usman must share the draft template with Jo for review *before* writing.
      - **Assets:** Request a "screenshot pack" and customer quotes from Garrett.

### Workflow & Process Improvements

  - **"One Tab, One Version" Rule:** To eliminate confusion, each Google Doc must contain only the final, approved version of an article. Old versions must be moved to a separate storage location (e.g., Mac Notes).
  - **Ticket Analysis Process:**
      - **Action:** For any ticket with a "call for context" link, analyze the transcript with ChatGPT to extract all necessary information before starting work.
