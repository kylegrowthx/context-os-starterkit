## Summary

Tyler Pavlas from GrowthX presented a comprehensive partnership proposal to Routable's leadership — Omri Mor (CEO), Nicole Lindenbaum, and Natalie Hayes (new Director of DemandGen) — covering end-to-end SEO, GEO strategy, and AI-powered content creation services. GrowthX outlined a two-phase engagement: an 8-week strategy sprint to develop brand voice artifacts and content roadmaps, followed by a 12-month delivery phase combining sophisticated AI workflows (OpenAI, Claude, Perplexity) with expert editorial oversight. Routable's primary need is refreshing existing content and developing unique perspectives rather than building internal SEO muscle, with Q1 2026 targeted as a potential start date pending internal alignment among Omri, Nicole, and Natalie around priorities and capacity.

## Context

Routable is a B2B fintech/expense management company in the business travel and spend management space. Omri Mor, Routable's CEO, learned about GrowthX through a customer connection and initiated an exploratory conversation about a month prior to this call. Natalie Hayes joined Routable as Director of DemandGen approximately one month before this meeting, making her the primary decision-maker for content and demand generation strategy going forward. The partnership exploration centers on GrowthX's capability to fill a critical gap: Routable has SEO activities in place and ranks for key terms, but lacks the operational muscle to adapt quickly to search landscape changes (particularly AI search), refresh aging content regularly, and develop original perspectives. Routable is considering timing around Q1 2026 as a potential start, pending internal alignment among the leadership team on strategic priorities.
