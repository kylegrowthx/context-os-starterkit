## Context

SmithAI is an AI and Virtual Receptionist platform that GrowthX is supporting with a comprehensive content and SEO strategy. This is a weekly tactical sync between Maddy Martin (SmithAI's Head of Content/Product) and the GrowthX team (Jo Kaminska, Megan Dickey) to review progress on industry pages, VR content refreshes, and SEO reporting. SmithAI's sales are shifting heavily toward AI Receptionists over Virtual Receptionists, and Maddy wants to ensure the content reflects the specific needs of different verticals—not generic top-funnel positioning. GrowthX solved this by introducing the ETSA researcher tool, which can independently research industries like Home Services, Legal, and Real Estate.
