### YouTube Strategy Overhaul

  - **Problem:** The current channel, a mix of podcasts, webinars, and short-form videos, sends mixed signals to the YouTube algorithm, hurting organic growth.
  - **Solution:** Split the channel into two distinct entities.
      - **Repository Channel:** For long-form content (podcasts, webinars).
      - **Organic Channel:** For short, high-cadence videos optimized for discovery.
  - **New Production Framework:**
    1.  **Title:** Start here; it's the foundation.
    2.  **Thumbnail:** Must be an accurate representation of the content.
    3.  **First 30 Seconds:** Crucial for audience retention.
  - **ICP Definition:** A formal ICP document is needed to guide content strategy. Will will draft profiles for two segments:
