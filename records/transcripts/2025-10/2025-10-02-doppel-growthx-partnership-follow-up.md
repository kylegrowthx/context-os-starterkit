## Summary

Tyler Pavlas walked Doppel's Joel Silverstein and Rodolfo Yiu through GrowthX's content creation process and pricing, clarifying how the service differs from building custom GPTs or using tools like AirOps. The core differentiator is GrowthX's combination of strategic artifacts, proprietary knowledge bases with fact-checking workflows, and human editorial oversight. ## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Uses AI workflows with deep context integration (knowledge base, custom instructions)
  - Human experts (managing editor, content manager) oversee and refine AI outputs
  - Aims for high-quality, brand-aligned content without requiring extensive client review
  - Can integrate client's proprietary data into content creation process

### Content Publishing and Management

  - GrowthX handles end-to-end process, including CMS posting (initially manual, potential for automation)
  - Streamlined review process crucial for efficiency; multiple reviewers can slow down publication
  - Offers content performance tracking via Looker dashboards and Scrunch for LLM visibility

### SEO and Performance Metrics

  - Uses Google Search Console data for organic search performance
  - Scrunch for LLM content performance tracking
  - Can customize analytics reporting based on client needs
  - Emphasizes long-term investment (6+ months) for meaningful organic traffic growth

### Competitive Considerations

  - GrowthX works with multiple clients in similar spaces (e.g., CodeRabbit, Spectre Ops)
  - Manages potential conflicts by not staffing same team members on competing accounts
  - Doppel identified ZeroFox as a major competitor in their space

### Pricing and ROI Discussion

  - Standard pricing: $30K strategy sprint + $15K/month retainer
  - Some flexibility on payment terms; potential for $12K/month with annual commitment
  - Compared to in-house costs or traditional agency pricing ($2,000-$2,500 per article)
