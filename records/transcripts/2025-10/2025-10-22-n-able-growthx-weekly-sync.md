  - N-able approved GrowthX's proposal to repurpose its annual threat report into smaller, gated guides.
  - **Rationale:** To capture specific search intent (e.g., "AI threat detection trends") and drive SEO by linking to these guides from relevant blog posts.

### Looker Dashboard Walkthrough

  - GrowthX reviewed the Looker dashboard, which tracks performance from GA4 and GSC.
  - **Key Reports:**
      - `Overview`: Sessions vs. Engaged Sessions (currently low, \~50%).
      - `Non-Branded`: Organic search traffic from non-branded queries.
      - `Cohort`: Tracks performance of GrowthX-published content by topic cluster.
      - `LLM Referral Traffic`: Shows traffic from AI platforms (e.g., ChatGPT).
  - **N-able Feedback:**
      - **Subdomain Filtering:** Add a filter for the main marketing site (`www`) to exclude subdomains like `docs` and `careers`.
      - **Non-Branded Filter:** Exclude product themes (e.g., "MDR") in addition to brand terms.
      - **Impression Filtering:** Exclude "junk" impressions (e.g., from the letter "N") to clean up data.
      - **Conversion Events:** N-able will specify which GA4 events are most important to track.
