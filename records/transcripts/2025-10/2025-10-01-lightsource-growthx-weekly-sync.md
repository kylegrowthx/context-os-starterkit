## Context

LightSource is an AI-native procurement platform focused on direct materials procurement — a significantly underserved market segment. Unlike competitors (SAP, Oracle, Kielvar) that dominate indirect procurement and tail-spend management, LightSource targets the higher-value, more complex world of component sourcing and supply chain optimization. This weekly sync is part of GrowthX's ongoing engagement with LightSource to develop and execute a comprehensive content marketing strategy that establishes LightSource as a thought leader in the direct materials procurement and supply chain AI space. The relationship is active and strategic: GrowthX will generate content pillar pages and blog articles using LightSource's category research, personas, and manifesto, with LightSource team reviewing and approving drafts through their Framer staging site before publication.
