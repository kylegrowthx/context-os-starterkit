## Summary

GrowthX presented a pricing proposal for RAMP's CashFlow Desk project — an 8-week strategy sprint at $18K total, followed by ongoing execution at $12K/month for five articles per week. RAMP's team will brief their leadership (George) on the proposal and provide initial feedback by Monday, with flexibility built in to opt out after seven weeks of the strategy sprint if the AEO performance strategy doesn't validate, or pivot to a bundled package combining CashFlow Desk and reduced RAMP.com work at $18-19K/month.

## Context

RAMP (the client) is a financial operations platform that processes business spending. GrowthX has been working with RAMP on their primary domain (RAMP.com) for some time, currently delivering approximately 25 articles per week at $9K/month. In this meeting, GrowthX proposed expanding the engagement to launch CashFlow Desk, a separate off-domain property focused on AI-enabled visibility into cash flow management. The meeting was held to discuss pricing, scope, and performance validation approach for the CashFlow Desk initiative, with key decision-makers Matt Angelosanto and Victoria Naef representing RAMP.
