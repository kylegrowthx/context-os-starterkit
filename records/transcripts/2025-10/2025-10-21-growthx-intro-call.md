## Key Topics

### Vanco's Content Scaling Challenge

  - **Current Process:** Jordan manually uses prompt sequences and GPTs for new content.
  - **Primary Bottleneck:** Manually updating existing content is more time-consuming than creating new content.
  - **Automation Blocker:** Vanco's AI Governance Board prevents direct HubSpot integrations with LLMs (e.g., ChatGPT, Claude), forcing manual publishing.
  - **Strategic Context:** A business unit split in January creates two sites:
      - **Existing Site:** Faith & Nonprofit content.
      - **New Site:** Education content (new domain, requires rapid authority building).
  - **Content Goal:** Increase comparative content for high-converting, lower-volume keywords, especially for the EDU market.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Service Model:** An end-to-end partner for SEO/GEO strategy, content creation, and publishing.
  - **Methodology:** A hybrid model using proprietary AI workflows with expert oversight.
      - **AI:** Uses agentic research, custom knowledge graphs, and multiple LLMs (e.g., Perplexity, Claude, specialized tools like Tavly).
      - **Experts:** A dedicated team (Managing Director, Editor, Content Manager) ensures quality and brand alignment.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1 (8-week sprint):** SEO/GEO audit, strategy, roadmap, and \~10 articles to calibrate quality.
      - **Phase 2 (Ongoing):** 5 articles per week, with work split across Vanco's two sites.
  - **Pricing:**
      - **Phase 1:** $30k (startups) or $40k (enterprises).
      - **Phase 2:** $15k/month for 5 articles/week.
  - **Analytics:**
      - **Current:** Uses a Scrunch dashboard.
      - **Future (Jan 2026):** Launching a proprietary platform, with access retained by clients post-contract.

### Strategic Mismatch: Infrastructure Ownership

  - **Vanco's Requirement:** A "closed loop" solution built within Vanco's secure, internal infrastructure.
      - **Rationale:** Security concerns (payments industry) and a desire to build long-term, transferable internal capabilities.
  - **GrowthX's Model:** Workflows are built and run on GrowthX's proprietary platform.
      - **Client Deliverables:** Vanco would own the content, artifacts (e.g., persona docs), and assignment roadmap.
      - **Workflow Access:** Workflows are not exportable but can be transparently explained.
  - **Outcome:** Jen Hoitsma expressed that a partnership would be more compelling if it involved building the infrastructure *in Vanco's environment*, not just delivering content.
