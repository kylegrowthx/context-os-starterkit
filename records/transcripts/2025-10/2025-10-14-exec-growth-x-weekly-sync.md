## Context

Exec is GrowthX's primary software client — an AI roleplay platform for sales training and customer-facing AI agents. This is a core GrowthX client relationship. The weekly sync reviews SEO performance, content strategy, and prompt development for Exec's platform. GrowthX had previously been focused on broad, top-of-funnel content but discovered that bottom-of-funnel competitor comparison content is undersaturated in SERPs and drives high-intent, conversion-ready traffic. Exec's SEO has shown stable growth with spike events that GrowthX is still investigating, and long-tail keyword pages are individually converting. The team is now pivoting to fill a competitive content gap before Exec's direct competitors do.
