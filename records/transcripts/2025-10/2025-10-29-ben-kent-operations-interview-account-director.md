## Key Topics

### The Evolving Account Director Role at GrowthX

  - The company is evolving its structure to support two business models: a traditional services org and a new product-led org built around a proprietary AI content platform.
  - **New Operating Model:**
      - **Pods:** Account Manager (daily comms, PM) + Managing Editor (AI platform ops).
      - **Directors:** Pooled resource for high-value strategic work (e.g., SEO audits, content strategy).
      - **Rationale:** Optimizes director time for strategic tasks that don't require weekly client meetings, while allowing for flexible director assignment based on client needs (e.g., technical vs. editorial focus).

### Candidate Experience & Strategic Approach

  - **SEO Philosophy:** Views generative AI as making SEO more critical than ever, not obsolete.
      - **Example:** Educated Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center's board on SEO's long-game value to manage unrealistic expectations.
  - **Strategic Initiative Example (Sesame Workshop):**
      - Led the migration and redesign of `sesameworkshop.org`.
      - **Process:** Coordinated calendars and deliverables across internal teams and a third-party engineering firm.
      - **Challenge:** Managed a partner at Known who proposed unfeasible projects.
          - **Resolution:** Used side chats to realign on scope and budget.
          - **Outcome:** The partner later recommended Kent for continued work after Known's SEO department was laid off, demonstrating a successful relationship recovery.
  - **Management Experience:**
      - Built SEO departments from scratch at Known and MRM.
      - No direct reports at Known due to budget cuts, but partnered closely with analytics and engineering teams.
      - Supported strategists at MRM on client kickoffs (e.g., Benjamin Moore).

### GrowthX's Client Strategy & AI Platform

  - **Client Onboarding:** All new clients complete an 8-week "strategy sprint."
      - **Goal:** Deep dives with client teams (product, engineering, sales) to create "artifacts" (e.g., tone guides) that inform AI workflow guidelines.
  - **AI Platform:** Uses templated pipelines with "Madlibs" for customization.
      - **Process:** Directors define rules (e.g., research sources, competitor exclusions) by referencing client-specific documents, not by writing code.
