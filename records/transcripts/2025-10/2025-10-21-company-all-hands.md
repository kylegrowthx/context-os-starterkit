## Key Topics

### Pipeline & Sales Update

      - **Hex:** Series C ($70M) data team platform (win influenced by a positive referral from Airbytes).
  - **ICP Refinement:** Sales is now targeting more established companies, moving beyond simple blog-building clients.
  - **Warriors Tickets:** A proven conversion tool; all prospects sent to a game have converted to customers.

### Customer Health & Retention Strategy

  - **New Retention Framework:** A formal process will use two tools to prioritize delivery resources and improve renewal outcomes.
      - **1. Customer Health Scorecard:** Measures four vectors to provide a holistic view of account status:
          - **Quality:** Client perception of work.
          - **Output:** Volume and timeliness of deliverables.
          - **Performance:** Achievement of client-specific results (e.g., organic traffic).
          - **Relationship:** Strength of the client-side connection.
      - **2. ICP Fit Scoring:** Automatically scores clients on five dimensions to identify core partners.
          - **Rationale:** Focus resources on high-fit clients and push back on custom requests from low-fit clients.
  - **Recent Renewal Progress:**
      - **Yorco:** Auto-renewing quarterly; goal is to convert to an annual contract.
      - **Strappy:** Renewing flat; contract is being sent.
      - **Ramp:** Discussing a custom website build and future strategy sprints.
  - **Client Exits & Learnings:**
      - **CodeRabbit:** Partnership ended at week 4.
          - **Reason:** Client was a poor ICP fit with misaligned expectations and a low-trust relationship.
          - **Significance:** Demonstrates the new strategy's use in proactively ending engagements that are not set up for success.
      - **Homebase:** Partnership ends in December.
          - **Reason:** Bringing work in-house, but using Ahrefs for "their own thing."
          - **Significance:** A competitor (Ahrefs) was engaging the client for nearly a year without the delivery team's knowledge, highlighting the need for stronger relationships to uncover such threats early.

### Product & Platform Demos

  - **Product Engineering Tracks:** The team is focused on five key areas:
      - **Looker Exit:** Replacing Looker for internal reporting.
      - **Analytics Integration:** Integrating Google Analytics and Fathom (GDPR-compliant alternative) to track traffic and LLM visits.
      - **Content Inventory:** Replacing Airtable with a new system for managing content, opportunities, and assignments.
      - **CheckDash:** Launching the AI visibility product.
      - **Agentech Pipeline:** Rolling out new pipelines (approx. 5 per week).
      - **Output Framework:** Finishing the new workflow writing system for a full migration next week.
  - **CheckDash Demo (Marcel):**
      - **Goal:** Become the open standard for AI visibility, similar to SimilarWeb for traffic or G2 for products.
      - **Functionality:** Tracks AI visibility for specific buying categories by monitoring top prompts across sources like ChatGPT and Flexing.
      - **Model:** A public leaderboard shows rankings for common categories. Users get free access to these "category prompts" and can pay for custom prompts.
      - **Launch:** Internal launch of the public-facing site is planned for this week/next to begin indexing pages for SEO.
  - **Content Inventory Demo (Brad & Daniel):**
      - **Goal:** Replace Airtable with an integrated system for managing content strategy and execution.
      - **Workflow:**
          - **Pages:** Crawls a site, scrapes content, and enriches it with AI-generated data (e.g., content score, technical issues).
          - **Opportunities:** Generates an "inbox" of ideas from the inventory, competitor analysis (via SEMrush), and trends (Reddit, LinkedIn).
          - **Assignments:** Converts accepted opportunities into a linear, task-based dashboard for the delivery team, abstracting away pipeline complexity.
  - **Technical Blog Demo (Daniel):**
      - **Purpose:** The internal technical blog serves as a testing ground for the new platform, producing high-quality, structured content.
      - **Key Insight:** The platform can generate complex, detailed articles (e.g., a 1.5-hour comparison of databases) and react to real-time events (e.g., Claude Haiku launch).
      - **Pipeline Customization:** Users can modify inputs (e.g., outlines, writing guidelines) and rerun only specific steps without rerunning the entire research phase, which can be time-consuming.
