## Context

Okta is a customer of GrowthX's B2B content marketing services. This is a weekly working session between Okta's go-to-market team (led by Rachael Tiow) and GrowthX's delivery team (Kyle and Sydney). Rachael is driving a major initiative to build targeted account lists and related artifacts (detailed strategic documents) for enterprise and upper-mid-market retail accounts across US, UK/Ireland, and Australia/NZ markets—part of a shift to focus Okta's sales efforts on six-figure deals rather than SMB. GrowthX is creating the artifacts based on account research and data from Clay, with Sydney and Kyle validating the data and building structured documents that Okta's demand gen and ABM teams can use for webinar topics, content strategy, and account-level campaigns. The relationship is collaborative but there's underlying tension: Rachael is frustrated with the quality and relevance of content coming from Okta's internal marketing teams, who she believes don't understand Okta's customers or how to articulate value beyond the product itself.
