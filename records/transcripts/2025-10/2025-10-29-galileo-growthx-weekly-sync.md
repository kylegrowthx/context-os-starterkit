### Performance & Analytics Deep Dive

  - **Engagement Rate (ER) Drop:**
      - A site-wide ER drop has occurred since early August, now hovering just above the 50% industry benchmark.
      - The drop is concentrated on blog pages, with some showing massive declines (e.g., 20–40%).
      - The homepage ER remains high at 85%.
      - **Hypothesis:** A GA4 tag manager change in late August (to remove duplicate pageviews) may have inadvertently "fixed" a previous tracking anomaly, making the prior high ERs appear inflated.
      - **Action:** Jackson will investigate the tag manager history to confirm the timeline and impact.
  - **Traffic & Content Performance:**
      - Overall traffic is up, likely driven by a recent ebook sponsorship.
      - **Anomaly:** The "one-page multi-context processing" blog saw a 1,000% session jump (to 75) but a 4% ER, suggesting bot traffic or a keyword mix-up.
      - **Success:** The "AI evaluations" pillar page saw a 200% session jump with a strong 70% ER, validating the pillar strategy.
      - **SEO Metrics:**
          - Non-branded impressions decreased slightly, attributed to Google's bot traffic cleanup.
          - The Observability content pillar jumped 19 spots to \#20.
          - LLM referral traffic is up, near its all-time high.
          - Galileo's citation count increased from 3% to 5%, while competitors' remained flat.

### Reddit & Medium Program Proposal

  - **Context:** GrowthX is piloting a full Reddit management service for other clients and is now scoping a formal offering.
  - **Proposed Phased Approach:**
      - **Phase 1 (Immediate & Free):** GrowthX will monitor relevant subreddits to provide content strategy insights.
      - **Phase 2 (Q1 2025, \~$8–10k/mo):** A full management service, including seeding conversations, replying to comments, and cross-publishing to Medium.
  - **Urgency & Flexibility:**
      - GrowthX can accelerate the full program if Galileo's need is urgent and the budget is approved.
      - Jackson will discuss the timeline and budget with Connor, noting internal pressure for a Reddit presence.
