## Context

GrowthX is an AI-powered content marketing platform and services company founded by Marcel Santilli (formerly CMO at Service Titan and growth lead at DeepGram, SolarWinds, and Schrödinger) with CTO Daniel Lopes (IFTTT veteran, Basecamp product leader). The company combines a forward-deployed services model with underlying software infrastructure to deliver content strategy, creation, and optimization for B2B SaaS companies. Peak XV Partners is a prominent Southeast Asia-focused venture capital firm making a strategic expansion into global SaaS investments. This meeting was a deep-dive pitch following an earlier in-person discussion and customer reference calls, with Peak XV's broader investment committee joining to evaluate the opportunity alongside the core investment team (Rishen Kapoor, Aakash Kapoor, and others).

## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Business Model and Growth

  - Offers AI-powered content marketing as a managed service with underlying tech platform
  - $11.5M+ in ARR, $2M+ signed in last 30 days
  - 60+ customers including Ramp, Airbyte, Webflow
  - 70%+ gross margins, trending towards 74-75% by year-end
  - Cashflow positive in September ($98k), biggest sales and ARR add month
  - No outbound marketing, first sales hire closed $3M in 6 weeks

### Product and Service Offering

  - AI-powered content creation workflows and platform
  - Forward-deployed team of strategists, editors, and content creators
  - Services include editorial content, programmatic content (e.g. product catalogs), newsletters
  - Expanding into other areas like ABM, competitive intel, product marketing

### Customer Case Studies

  - Ramp: 900+ pages in 14 months, driving significant organic growth
  - Airbyte: Expanded from $12k to $40-45k monthly contract
  - Augment Code: Grew from $10k to $50k monthly across 4 workstreams

### Team and Hiring Strategy

  - Remote-first approach to access top talent globally
  - Mix of ex-founders, CTOs, and experienced product/engineering leaders
  - 40-person delivery team serving 60 clients
  - Aiming for 1 person to operate 5-10 clients as efficiency improves

### Technology Platform

  - Custom workflows and AI agents for content creation and optimization
  - Internal tools for context engineering, research, and content management
  - Building towards a "Content OS" that clients can eventually self-serve on

### Fundraising Plans

  - Considering $30-50M raise
  - Not urgently needed due to capital efficiency and profitability
  - Optimizing for right strategic partners over speed
  - HubSpot confirmed as potential strategic investor

### Peak XV Partners' Perspective

  - Strong interest based on past successes with services/platform hybrids (e.g. GlobalLogic, Mu Sigma)
  - Experience with marketing tech investments (Appier, Insider)
  - Alignment on long-term thinking and customer-centric approach
