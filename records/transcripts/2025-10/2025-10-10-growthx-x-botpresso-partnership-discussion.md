GrowthX and Botpresso explored a partnership to handle SEO audits for GrowthX clients at scale — Botpresso would provide abbreviated technical SEO audit checklists for SaaS companies, with typical audits taking 50-60 hours for mid-sized sites (5,000-15,000 pages). ## Context

GrowthX and Botpresso are independent but complementary agencies in the B2B content and SEO space. GrowthX specializes in end-to-end content strategy and creation for Series A-B SaaS companies, producing 5-50 content pieces per week depending on the client. They already offer SEO audits as part of their scoped work but are growing fast enough that the in-house team is getting stretched — Andi is looking for ways to scale the audit capability without hiring faster than sustainable. Botpresso is a technical SEO consultancy founded by Nitin Manchanda, already working with shared clients like Airbyte. They do full-depth technical SEO audits covering on-page and technical issues, and have built custom tools (Chrome extension, Python-based duplication checkers) to support the work. The meeting was called by Marcel (who didn't make it, finishing a webinar) to explore whether Botpresso could handle the initial audit work for GrowthX clients before kickoff, so GrowthX can focus on content strategy and execution.
