## Key Topics

### Current State: Workflow & Scaling Challenge

  - **Process:** Growth X uses AI researcher workflows to create consistent account-based briefs and guides for SDRs.
  - **Challenge:** Delivering long-form documents is unscalable for Okta's 300+ SDRs.
      - **Goal:** Find a system to deliver insights directly into SDR workflows (e.g., Clay, spreadsheets), avoiding direct CRM writes.
  - **Feedback Loop:** Currently ad hoc, relying on Rachael's field updates.
      - **Impact:** The team's primary feedback source, Nick Winkler, is moving to an AE role.

### Q4 Strategy: Retail & ABX

  - **Retail Focus:** EMEA's Q4 priority on retail makes the existing work highly relevant.
  - **New Initiative: ABX Kit:**
      - **Goal:** Enable marketers to use account insights for content creation (blogs, ads, roundtables), moving from loose messaging to targeted campaigns.
      - **Rationale:** The initiative is an experiment to weave ABM and sales into a true Account-Based Experience (ABX).
  - **Artifact Validation:** The team will test new artifacts (Hospitality, FinServe, Healthcare) to ensure they are self-explanatory for new SDRs or marketers.

### Future Work: SaaS Vertical

  - **Timeline:** Work begins in November.
  - **Key Challenge:** Clearly defining the value proposition for SaaS vs. SaaS R0 and Workforce Identity.
  - **Scope:** Create foundational artifacts for all business segments (SMB, Commercial, Enterprise), despite the current focus on Enterprise.
