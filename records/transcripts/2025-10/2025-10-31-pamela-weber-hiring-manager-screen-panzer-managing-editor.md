## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Content Strategy & ME Role

  - **Goal:** Build quality content at scale by applying editorial principles to AI-driven workflows.
  - **ME Role:** Pipeline management, not traditional editing.
      - **Primary Objective:** Optimize AI pipelines to reduce human editing time to a target of 10–15 minutes per article.
      - **Responsibilities:** Identify performance gaps, improve instructions/contextual artifacts, and file engineering tickets for tool enhancements.
  - **Tooling Philosophy:** Tool-agnostic.
      - **Primary Tool:** Internal platform, Atlas.
      - **Auxiliary Tools:** Use external tools (e.g., custom GPTs) for prototyping or filling gaps in Atlas.
      - **Core Principle:** Automate any task performed manually more than once.

### Candidate Experience & AI Workflow

  - **Background:** 11 years in content, including 6 at a marketing agency (Toyota, Lexus) and 5 as Content Lead at Inc. (an AI SaaS).
  - **AI Workflow Evolution:**
      - **Initial Stack:** Inc. (generation), Gemini (fact-checking), Claude (tone), ChatGPT (legal/formal content).
      - **Current Stack (SmithOS):** An AI agent product enabling a recursive, multi-agent workflow.
          - **Process:** Keyword research → human review → outline generation → human review → drafting.
          - **Rationale:** Human intervention at key stages is critical to prevent wasted work on flawed research or outlines.
  - **Performance Improvement Example:**
      - **Problem:** Initial AI agent campaign (150 articles) lacked explicit instructions and feedback loops.
      - **Solution:** Implemented detailed briefs (structure, sources, exclusion phrases) and a recursive, multi-agent architecture.
      - **Result:** Output increased to 600 articles/month, driving 66% QoQ organic traffic growth.

### Content Quality & Cadence

  - **Quality Standard:** "Is it something I would want to read?"—content that is engaging, valuable, and connects with humans.
  - **Shipping Decision:** A balance between perfection and deadlines, considering dependencies on other teams (e.g., graphic design).
  - **GrowthX Cadence Varies by Complexity:**
      - **High-Touch (Technical/Medical):** 5–10 articles/week.
      - **Top-of-Funnel (SEO-centric):** 5–10 articles/week.
      - **Programmatic (Directories):** 20–30+ pieces/week (more data management than editing).
