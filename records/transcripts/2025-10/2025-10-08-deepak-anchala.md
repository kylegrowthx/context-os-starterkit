## Key Topics

### Adopt.ai's Current Content Strategy

- Publish blogs, host webinars and podcasts, and maintain social media (company and founder pages)
- Currently producing 6-7 blogs/month: 5 in-house (engineers and internal team), 2 via external agency
- Strategic bet on long-tail keyword pillars with low search volume but high future relevance to agent-builder category
- Challenged by category immaturity: agent builder keywords are dominated by Microsoft and OpenAI; true category keywords will grow as market develops
- Experimented with AI workflows but found quality doesn't meet their technical standard for credibility
- Not seeing enough SEO or AEO traction despite content efforts—users are looking at Reddit, YouTube, LinkedIn, and other surfaces for information

### GrowthX's Offering

- End-to-end service: strategy sprint (deep research, ICP/competitor/product audits), followed by execution phase publishing 5 pieces/week (one per business day)
- Hybrid AI workflows with expert humans in the loop: TechCrunch editor-in-chief, ex-TripAdvisor SEO leaders, G2.com builders on staff
- Custom knowledge base built from research, customer resources, and semantic retrieval via graph databases
- Persona and product/services artifacts fed into all workflows to ensure relevance and technical accuracy
- Three-person team per account: managing director (strategy), managing editor (quality oversight), content manager (execution)
- Platform capabilities: brief generation informed by SERP data, keyword research, competitor analysis, deep research with fact-checking, outline and draft generation, expert review steps
- Analytics and LLM visibility tracking via Scrunch partnership; building native analytics into their platform
- Staging and publishing handled by GrowthX (saves customers the last-mile execution work)

### GrowthX's Platform and Process

- Strategy sprint phase: conduct deep research audits, build persona/product/ICP artifacts, establish kickoff with customer, research competitors, define quality bar
- Execution phase: create a content OS with topic clusters, generate SEO assignments with opportunity scoring, and execute 5 pieces/week
- Example workflow (health supplements brand): keyword research → organic search results & SERP factors → competitor analysis → brief generation → deep research with credible source validation → outline → draft → fact-checking with confidence intervals → expert review (custom based on customer role, e.g., CMO review) → image creation
- Custom workflows based on customer needs: e.g., glossary pages, tool comparisons, thought leadership, ROI guides
- Today: workflows are codified and customizable; team does staging/publishing; feedback via Google Docs with customer approval gates
- Vision for Q1 2026: customer access to platform for project management visibility, analytics (project assignments, topic clusters, performance tracking), and eventually content creation collaboration
- Differentiated from tools like Aero by service layer (white-glove execution, strategic focus), workflow customization, time-to-value, and the fact that GrowthX handles the entire end-to-end vs. requiring customer to own task management

### GrowthX's Client Base and Growth Metrics

- Mix of seed-stage to later-stage SaaS, but recommend clients have: established CMS/blog setup, defined ICP, and confidence in target topics
- Technical clients with visible traction: Airbyte (Marcel worked there as CMO), Deepgram (Marcel's former company; GrowthX started here with API experiments), WebStacks, VAPI, AugmentCode, SentinelOne, Surge.ai
- Content examples showcased: Town (built entire blog, seed stage at intake, 3x traffic since June), Abnormal Security (thought leadership + ROI content + custom glossary pages), Augment Code (guides, tool comparisons, enterprise AI coding narrative control, 3x traffic since June, showing up in AI Answers in multiple places)
- Worked with seed-stage companies (e.g., Town) and helped set up blogs from scratch
- Two kickoffs per week; booked through Thanksgiving (October 2025)
- Conversion uplift from AI search visits: 4.4x vs. traditional search
