## Summary

Mara and Victor negotiated Strapi's flat renewal with GrowthX while addressing Victor's frustration with delayed CAPA integration — Mara committed to prioritizing an engineering review within a week. The team aligned on an 80-20 split favoring top-of-funnel content with success measured by articles hitting 1,000+ views and signups, moving away from lower-performing middle-of-funnel pieces. Strapi wants greater autonomy over metadata and content refreshes using their new AI automation, plus self-serve access to GrowthX's Atlas workflow tool to reduce dependency on GrowthX's engineering resources.

---

## Context

Strapi is a leading open-source headless CMS and a multi-year GrowthX client. Victor Coisne is their VP of Product and a strong advocate for GrowthX within developer and DevTool communities — he recently spoke about GrowthX on a DevTool scaling podcast. Mara Leighton is Victor's main GrowthX contact and handles account delivery. This meeting was their renewal conversation for 2026. The relationship is strong but tension exists around engineering capacity: Strapi has requested CAPA integration (a feature that feeds content into an LLM's knowledge base) for some time, perceiving delays as GrowthX "dragging their feet," while Mara is navigating budget constraints ("flat renewal") and competing product priorities. Both parties see content strategy and partnership depth as core to the relationship.
