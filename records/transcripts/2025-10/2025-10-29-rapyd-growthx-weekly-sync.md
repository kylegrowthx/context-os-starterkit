## Key Topics

### Content Performance Review

  - **Traffic & Impressions:** Overall traffic is up. Non-branded impressions are slightly down, a trend attributed to Google's bot traffic cleanup.
  - **Top Performers:** Recent content is driving results, with PIX and stablecoin articles gaining momentum.
  - **Cohort Rankings:** Most content cohorts are ranking in the top 10.
      - **Underperforming:** "Business Strategy" and "High Opportunity Industries" cohorts need refreshing.
  - **LLM Referral Traffic:** Low, as expected. This is because current content is primarily "trends" articles, which users are less likely to click through from LLM summaries than from comparison pages.

### Strategic Shift: Content Priorities

  - **Problem:** The content queue contains many "foundational" articles that are difficult to review and don't align with current strategic goals.
  - **Solution:** Deprioritize these foundational articles to focus on content that drives results and aligns with Rapyd's strategic initiatives.
  - **New Priorities:**
    1.  **Competitor Comparison Articles:** High-intent content expected to drive conversions.
    2.  **Country Payment Method Guides:** Proven traffic drivers.
  - **Next Batch of Countries:** UK, Germany, France, Italy, Poland, and Iceland.
      - **Rationale for Iceland:** A key market where Rapyd is the largest payment processor.

### Process Improvement: De-risking Country Guides

  - **Problem:** Country guides contain specific regulatory/legal advice (e.g., licensing, FX controls) that creates legal liability and requires lengthy compliance reviews.
  - **Solution:** Edit the guides to remove this risky content and add a standard disclaimer.
      - **Disclaimer:** "This information is for informational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice."
  - **Outcome:** The refined guides will serve as a template for future country articles, streamlining the review process and enabling faster rollout.

### Persona Document Refinement

  - **Problem:** The persona document is too broad and granular, losing focus on core audiences.
  - **Solution:** Mark is narrowing the document to better align with the core audience and their motivations.
  - **Next Step:** The refined personas will be used to map specific content to each audience, ensuring better targeting.

### Contract Renewal

  - **Status:** The renewal contract has not yet been sent.
  - **Action:** GrowthX will send the contract for review.
