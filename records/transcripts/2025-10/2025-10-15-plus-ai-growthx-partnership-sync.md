## Overview

## Key Topics

### Plus AI's Current Setup and Needs

  - Using Webflow CMS for template pages, but may need a more scalable solution for thousands of pages
  - API available for generating presentations (.pptx files)
  - Seeking help with keyword research, content generation, and SEO optimization for template pages
  - Open to editorial content ideas, though feeling they've covered low-hanging fruit

### GrowthX's Capabilities and Constraints

  - Confident in building high-quality workflows for content generation
  - Experienced in creating programmatic pages with SEO-optimized content
  - Limited capacity for custom design/engineering work due to other client commitments
  - Suggested focusing on strategy, workflow building, and content quality review

### SEO Strategy Discussion

  - Importance of creating category/listing pages (e.g., "construction website templates")
  - Need for multiple indexable pages per category/filter
  - Potential for 5-50 presentations per category
  - Discussed refreshing and updating existing content regularly

### Proposed Hybrid Approach

  - Focus on programmatic template page generation
  - Include editorial content refresh and creation
  - Start with launching 20 new templates and refreshing existing pages
  - Leverage Plus AI's presentation library for content examples

### Technical Integration

  - Plus AI can provide API access for presentation generation
  - GrowthX to create workflows for content generation and SEO optimization
  - Discussed embedding options (PowerPoint, Google Slides) and potential SEO implications
