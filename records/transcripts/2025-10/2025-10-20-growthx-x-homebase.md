## Key Topics

### Homebase's Decision to Go In-House

  - Homebase plans to bring its SEO and GEO content programs in-house.
  - **Rationale:**
      - **High Overhead:** The current GrowthX process requires more internal management and revision cycles than Homebase's own systems.
      - **Internal AI Push:** A strategic directive from leadership (John, Ray) to build and own internal AI infrastructure.
      - **Cost Efficiency:** A belief that internal ownership will yield more work product from available funds than the current scope allows.

### Feedback on GrowthX Engagement

  - **Process Inefficiency:** The team prefers building and running their own Aerofs flows over the high-revision GrowthX process.
  - **Post-Migration Quality Drop:** The migration to the new Becerra system required re-coaching to restore prior content quality.
  - **Feedback Loop Issue:** Feedback feels applied directly to a flow, not translated by an "AI engineer."
      - **Result:** Subsequent drafts often don't address feedback, creating friction and slowing progress.
  - **Reporting Gap:** Performance reporting has never been delivered.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solutions

  - Marcel presented two options to address Homebase's concerns and retain the relationship.
  - **Option 1: "Bake-Off" Strategy Sprint**
      - **Proposal:** Onboard Homebase as a "new client" with a dedicated team and the new, successful onboarding process used with clients like Lovable.
      - **Terms:** A short sprint with clear goals; if quality isn't significantly better, the contract ends early.
  - **Option 2: Direct Platform Access Pilot**
      - **Proposal:** Pause billing and provide early access to GrowthX's upcoming platform for Homebase to build and own workflows directly.
      - **Platform Details:**
          - **Open Source Framework:** Workflows are built in code using the `output.ai` framework, enabling full ownership and exportability.
          - **API Access:** Workflows can be exposed via API, allowing integration into tools like n8n for custom orchestration.
          - **Advanced Capabilities:** Includes agentic research (with confidence scoring), programmatic infographics, and site-wide monitoring.
  - **Contract Exit Options**
      - **Option A (Cancellation):** Pay a 50% cancellation fee on the remaining \~10 months of the contract to terminate early.
      - **Option B (Redeployment):** Redirect the remaining contract value to a different scope, such as building advanced workflows for Homebase to own.
