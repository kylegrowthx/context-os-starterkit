## Overview

- **GrowthX content is driving all organic growth,** increasing its share of site traffic while overall site traffic remains flat.
- **Top-of-funnel content (e.g., salary guides, templates) is a key driver** of qualified leads (SQUOs), challenging the assumption that such content yields low-intent traffic.
- **A critical tracking gap prevents measuring content ROI;** GrowthX will now track the `HubSpot form demo request ICP` event to connect content to qualified conversions.
- **The engineering content strategy will be refined** to target high-fit segments (Structural, MEP) while accepting "splash damage" from broad terms (Civil) that will be filtered by the demo form.

---

## Key Topics

### Organic Performance & Content ROI

  - Overall site traffic is flat, but GrowthX-published content is growing steadily, increasing its share of traffic without cannibalizing existing keywords.
  - LLM citations for GrowthX content grew 67%, indicating strong optimization for AI crawlers.
  - **Key Insight:** Top-of-funnel content is driving qualified leads.
      - **Templates:** Drove 6 SQUOs in the last 3 months (3 from "Project Staffing Plan," 3 from "Gantt Chart").
      - **Salary Guides:** Account for 5 of the top 10 keywords by growth.
  - This success challenges the assumption that resource-based content yields only low-intent traffic, validating an "abundance mindset" for content creation.

### Tracking & Measurement Gap

  - A critical gap prevents measuring content ROI by connecting organic traffic to qualified conversions.
  - **Problem:** The current GTM setup sends traffic sources to Metabase but lacks a specific conversion event for GrowthX to track.
  - **Solution:** GrowthX will now track the `HubSpot form demo request ICP` event, which automatically filters out non-ICP leads, providing a clear signal of qualified intent.
  - **Action:** Jessica will confirm this is the correct event trigger.

### Website UX & Authority

  - **Problem:** Feature/benefits pages have low traffic, with only the "Project Management" page ranking in the top 15.
  - **Hypothesis:** This is caused by a vague "Get Started" CTA and an overwhelming navigation menu.
  - **Recommendation:**
      - **Homepage CTAs:** Use clear, above-the-fold CTAs linking directly to feature pages.
      - **Navigation:** Simplify the menu to reduce decision fatigue.
      - **CTA A/B Test:** Test the "Get Started" button with more specific copy (e.g., "Request a Demo").
  - **Recommendation:** Create author pages to build authority and improve SEO.
      - **Structure:** A main author index page → individual author bio pages.
      - **Action:** Update all past articles with author links during content refreshes.

### Engineering Content Strategy

  - **Goal:** Refine the engineering content strategy based on ICP fit.
  - **High-Fit Segments:** Structural, Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing (MEP) engineering firms.
  - **Low-Fit Segments:**
      - **Civil Engineering (Govt-funded):** Poor fit due to complex, non-cloud-friendly invoicing and contracting workflows.
      - **Surveyors/Contractors:** Poor fit due to short engagement cycles.
  - **Decision:** Continue targeting broad engineering terms (e.g., "Civil Engineer") to capture top-of-funnel traffic, accepting "splash damage" from non-ICP leads. These leads will be filtered by the demo form's routing logic.
  - **Constraint:** Any content effort for Civil Engineering must be matched by a similar effort for Structural Engineering, a proven high-conversion segment.

### Reddit & Content Repurposing

  - GrowthX is now posting content on Reddit via the `u/Monograph` user account.
  - The `r/Monograph` subreddit was secured to prevent squatting but is inactive.
      - **Rationale:** Managing a subreddit requires significant resources (moderation, curation) that are not available.
  - Jessica will repurpose top-performing content for LinkedIn ads and newsletters.
      - **Test Plan:** Run campaigns for both proven content and new AI-focused content to prime the audience for the upcoming AI product launch.
