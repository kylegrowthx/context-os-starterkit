## Overview

- **Performance Crisis:** Organic traffic has been declining since April, with a 5% QoQ session drop and a 30% QoQ conversion rate decline.
- **Process Bottleneck:** A 2–3 month internal content approval process limits output to \~4 articles per quarter, creating a large backlog.
- **GrowthX Mandate:** Take over all SEO content production (net new & refreshes) to scale output to \~5 pieces/week and reverse performance trends.
- **Strategic Focus:** Drive AI authority and balance the content funnel. The plan will leverage high-intent ChatGPT traffic (up 250% QoQ) while fixing the high-volume, low-converting glossary page.

---

## Key Topics

### SEO Performance & Challenges

  - **Organic Traffic Decline:** Steady decline since April.
  - **Conversion Rate Drop:** Organic conversion rate down 30% QoQ.
  - **Glossary Page Underperformance:**
      - Traffic cut in half over 18 months (20k → 10k sessions/mo).
      - High traffic, but low conversion due to top-of-funnel (ToFu) content.
  - **ChatGPT Traffic:**
      - Up 250% QoQ, driving the highest conversion rate of all channels.
      - This high-intent traffic is a key growth opportunity.

### Content Strategy & Process

  - **Current Process:**
      - A 2–3 month approval cycle (PMM → Brand → Web Team) limits output to \~4 articles/quarter.
      - This has created a large backlog of unproduced content in the "SEO Strategy Master Sheet."
  - **GrowthX Mandate:**
      - Fully own all SEO content production (net new & refreshes).
      - Scale output to \~5 pieces/week.
      - The goal is for Redis teams (PMM, Brand) to be completely hands-off.
  - **Content Priorities:**
      - **High-Performing:** Technical deep-dives (e.g., "AI agent memory," "fuzzy matching") and data-driven competitor analyses.
      - **Strategic Focus:** Build authority in AI infrastructure.
      - **Funnel Balance:** Address the current imbalance by creating more middle-of-funnel (MoFu) and bottom-of-funnel (BoFu) content.

### Implementation Plan

  - **Content Management:** GrowthX will use Airtable as its "Content OS" to manage the full production pipeline.
  - **Keyword Strategy:**
      - GrowthX will conduct a keyword gap analysis to find competitor keywords Redis doesn't rank for.
      - This will inform a new, focused target keyword list.
      - Redis's Ahrefs account has \~200 slots open to track these new keywords.
  - **Reporting:** GrowthX will build a 7-page Looker dashboard to track performance by topic cluster, pulling data from GA4 and GSC.
