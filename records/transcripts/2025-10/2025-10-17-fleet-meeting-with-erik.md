## Key Topics

### Mini-Sprint Package Development

  - Creating a 1-2 week package for client onboarding and initial strategy
  - Includes: kickoff call, artifact creation, SEO/site/AEO audits, content strategy in Airtable
  - Priced at \~$15-20K, aimed at companies not ready for full agency service
  - Scalable model: potential to do 20 mini-sprints/month with team expansion
  - Aligns with improving Atlas platform and AI-led growth community

### Fleet Client Onboarding

  - Client team energetic and engaged; less complex than recent cybersecurity clients
  - Primary focus: AI visibility (top metric) and meetings booked (long-term goal)
  - Need to simplify 40+ roles/titles into two main audiences: decision-makers and tool users
  - Key contacts: Brad (technical), Irene, Lupin, Mike T (brand)
  - CMS likely GitHub-based; team to follow up on specifics

### Artifact Creation Process Improvement

  - Current: Raw transcript → What We Heard doc → Artifact creation
  - Proposed: Raw transcript → Enhanced persona doc → Better-informed artifact
  - Goal: Capture more detailed insights from kickoff calls in a structured format
  - Enables potential future automation of artifact creation

### Agentic Pipeline Implementation

  - Push to set up agentic pipeline for all new clients from the start
  - Creating a template for good artifacts and sample pipeline
  - Standardizing process to ensure consistent quality and efficiency
