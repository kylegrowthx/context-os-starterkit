## Context

This is an internal strategy sync between George Haikal (GrowthX CEO) and Erik O'Brien (GrowthX delivery) following GrowthX's kickoff with Fleet, a new client. The team is working to formalize a new business model—a pre-engagement "mini-sprint" package designed to serve mid-market companies that need foundational AI visibility and content strategy work before committing to a full managed service engagement. The broader context: GrowthX has been learning from recent complex clients (Specter Ops, Enable, Base 10, Surge in cybersecurity) and wants to systematize the onboarding process so that future engagements move faster and require less back-and-forth on basics like system access and artifact creation. This meeting sets the direction for rolling out the mini-sprint model and reflects George's push to make GrowthX's delivery more repeatable and scalable.
