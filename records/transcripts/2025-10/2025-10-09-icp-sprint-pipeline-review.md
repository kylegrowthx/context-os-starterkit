## Key Topics

### Pipeline Update

  - Red signed (30-18 standard editorial setup)
  - Cresta progressing well (9K for phase two)
  - Viscom and Read.ai as solid backups for November 3rd
  - Strategic logos (Zapier, Kirkland, Ramp, Webflow) progressing, targeting December 1st
  - Navan experiencing procurement challenges (MSA, DPA complications)

### Sprint Duration Adjustments

  - Extending sprints to 10 weeks for December 1st, 8th, and 17th starts
  - Considering 12-week sprints for more complex projects (e.g., Ramp, Navan)
  - Rationale: Holiday season impact and need for extended timelines on certain projects
  - Example: DeepGram in week 7 without full results, would benefit from 12 weeks

### ICP Qualification Challenges

  - SpecterOps highlighted as a problematic client due to:
      - Highly technical, security-focused content
      - Lack of brand guidelines, voice, tone, or style documentation
      - Multiple stakeholders required for content approval (CTO, CEO, CPO, Product Marketing)
      - Desire to reposition with a new, unsearched perspective
  - Discussion on potentially disqualifying similar clients in the future
  - Need for more thorough pre-qualification process

### Sales Process Improvements

  - Exploring options for quick proof-of-concept content creation
  - Debate on the feasibility and value of creating sample content pre-sprint
  - Focus on improving artifact generation, site audits, and SEO/GEO audits pre-sprint
  - Suggestion to prioritize other sales process improvements before diving deeper into SEO/GEO expertise

### Inbound Lead Management

  - Proposal to implement an application form on the website
  - Automate initial screening process to reduce manual effort
  - Improve lead quality and reduce time spent on unqualified prospects
