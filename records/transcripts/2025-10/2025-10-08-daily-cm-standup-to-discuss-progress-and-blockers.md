### Case Study Template and Execution

**Template approach:** Use Sunbit's published case studies as primary source. Generalize and anonymize (e.g., "family-run dealership in California" instead of specific shop name). Make publishable as-is, but highlight customization points where Sunbit can insert real customer names and metrics.

**Length:** 700-800 words per case study.

**Customization strategy:** Leave specific details (shop name, location, exact metrics like "68% sales increase") as highlighted editable sections. Include comment tags for Sunbit team: "We used [source] for this case study. You can customize these highlighted parts with real customer names and metrics if desired, or publish as-is."

**Example structure:** "A family-run auto repair shop in Phoenix was able to increase foot traffic/sales by 68% after implementing Sunbit financing" — Sunbit changes to real shop name, real location, real percentage.

**Execution:** Saskia demonstrated successful case study format. Start with base prompt for styling/formatting, then add case study-specific instruction: "Use real case study information from their website. Generalize/anonymize locations and names. Highlight customizable sections."

**Lesson:** Case study articles showing promise for B2B content conversion. Replicating approach across 25 articles.

### AI Impact on Content Strategy

**Market shift:** AI (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) now dominates top-of-funnel search behavior. Users no longer Google basic questions—they ask AI directly for quick answers. Traditional top-of-funnel SEO (keyword rankings, paid search, cookies) increasingly broken.

**Strategic implication:** GrowthX shifting focus to middle and bottom-of-funnel content where AI doesn't solve transactional/commercial problems. Content that drives consideration and purchase decisions, not just awareness.

**Sunbit case studies fit:** Case study content addresses comparison, proof of results, and customer education—traditionally bottom/middle-funnel territory where Google traffic still converts.
