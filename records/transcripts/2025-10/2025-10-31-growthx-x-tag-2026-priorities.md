## Key Topics

### 2025 Performance & Market Context

  - **Sales Downturn:** Vallarta sales are down \~30% YoY, with October as the lowest month.
  - **Market-Wide Factors:**
      - **Travel Alerts:** A new Canadian travel alert for Jalisco & Nayarit, amplified by media blogs, negatively impacts traveler perception.
      - **Negative Press:** A US Transportation Safety Board (TSB) cancellation of flights to Mexico City's AIFA airport creates general consumer anxiety about travel to Mexico.
      - **Competitive Shifts:** Search intent is rising for other destinations (e.g., La Paz), indicating a shift in traveler interest.
  - **Content Performance:**
      - **Strong Organic Growth:** Organic + Direct traffic share grew from 43% (Sept 2024) to 61% (Sept 2025), showing content is successfully capturing top-of-funnel interest.
      - **Fathom Revenue Drop:** Fathom revenue fell 32% YoY ($312k → $200k), a primary driver of the overall sales decline.

### 2026 Content Strategy Shift

  - **Phase 2 Focus:** Shift from high-volume publishing to quality and refinement.
  - **Key Initiatives:**
      - **Content Refresh:** Update top-performing articles (e.g., "Is it safe?", "Hurricane season") to maintain freshness for AI search and user relevance.
      - **Consolidation:** Redirect redundant articles to strengthen key pages.
      - **Pillar Pages:** Build new destination landing pages (e.g., Marietas) to provide a comprehensive, non-blog perspective.
  - **Strategic Partnership:** A new collaboration with the Puerto Vallarta Tourism Bureau (PVTB) will drive mutual growth through content and influencer exchanges.

### New Initiative: Tour Page Optimization

  - **Goal:** Improve conversion at the point of sale by optimizing underperforming tour pages.
  - **Prioritization:** Target pages outside the top 3 organic search results, as top-ranking pages are already performing well.
  - **Execution:**
      - **Content Focus:** Add rich, keyword-dense details (e.g., specific marine life, reef names) to improve SEO and user engagement.
      - **Information Gathering:** Isabel will provide a list of questions for each tour to help the content team source details from internal experts.
