## Key Topics

### Content Strategy for AI Receptionist Knowledge Hub

  - 6 core content clusters established in Airtable, aligned with mock-up design
  - Call flow design cluster currently in focus
  - Potential for 3,000-5,000+ traffic from identified keywords
  - Low keyword difficulty aligns well with Smith.ai's domain authority

### Virtual Receptionist (VR) Content Opportunity

  - VR searches down 80% (500 searches Jan-May to 110 searches Jun-Oct), but VoIP competitors dominating with high domain authority (e.g., RingCentral 85-90 DA)
  - Opportunity to capture less competitive VR space with legal industry focus:
      - Refresh existing VR content targeting practice areas slower to adopt AI: personal injury, family law, immigration
      - Target multi-location legal firms (10-20 support staff, Boston/Chicago/LA footprints)
      - Position VR as compliant option for regulated practices concerned about AI liability
      - Pair content strategy with lowered PPC costs (keywords down from $2,000+ CPC to affordable rates)

### Call Flow Design Content Feedback

  - **Intro needs IVR comparison**: Readers familiar with traditional phone routing (IVRs, call trees) need context on what's new about call flow design before diving into technical details
  - **Custom designs required**: Version 2 should use SmithAI-specific imagery rather than generic existing designs — text-heavy content without visuals doesn't convey authority
  - **Positive reception**: Reduced self-promotion and improved citation practices are working; legal/technical audience expects substantive sourcing
  - **Tone guidance needed**: Write for human reader (especially legal/technical audiences), not for GPT title generation

### Q3 Website Performance Report

  - **Engagement exceptional**: 80-85% engagement rate across sessions (significantly above typical 50% for organic + referral mix)
  - **Homepage dominates conversions**: Homepage driving most clicks for both AI and human receptionist CTAs — suggests landing pages underperforming relative to hub
  - **AI receptionist momentum**: 13,000+ events Q3 vs Q2, particularly from homepage — strong business signal that paid/organic investment paying off
  - **New traffic source emerging**: ChatGPT (largest AI source, upward trend Q3 vs Q2), plus smaller but high-engagement sessions from Copilot (12 sessions, 11 engaged) and Gemini — opportunity to optimize for AI search intent
  - **Industry refresher challenge**: Traffic down quarter-to-quarter on industry refresher cluster, but engagement rate improving — suggests quality upgrade working despite volume decline
