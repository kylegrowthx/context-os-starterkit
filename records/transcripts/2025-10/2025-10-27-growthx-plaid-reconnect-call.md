## Key Topics

### Plaid's Content Challenges & Goals

  - **Team & Focus:** Tom Sullivan (Content Strategist) and Richie Lauridsen (Organic Growth) manage the `plaid.com/resources` hub.
  - **Primary Need:** Scale content on new topics where Plaid lacks visibility, such as:
      - AI & Fintech
      - New lending types
      - Gaps in core product coverage
  - **Current Bottlenecks:** Slow internal review processes hinder content velocity.
  - **Strategic Goal:** Develop interactive, tool-based content (e.g., calculators) to drive engagement and improve AI search visibility.
  - **AI Usage:** Plaid uses AI to *enhance* human-led content (e.g., outlining, style checks), not for full generation, to maintain a high standard of subject matter expertise.

### GrowthX's Solution: Hybrid Content Model

  - **Approach:** A hybrid model combining AI workflows for speed with expert human oversight for quality.
  - **Team Structure:** Each client gets a dedicated team (Managing Director, Editor, Content Manager) with deep SEO/content backgrounds (e.g., ex-TechCrunch, G2).
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1 (Strategy Sprint):** A 4-week sprint to build context artifacts (ICP, brand voice) and a content roadmap, with publishing starting by week 4.
      - **Phase 2 (Growth Execution):** Focus on high-velocity publishing (e.g., daily) or a smaller volume of high-quality pieces, depending on client goals.
  - **Platform:** An internal platform built by GrowthX's founder (ex-CMO at Scale, HashiCorp) to solve the content scaling challenges he faced.
      - **Features:** Custom knowledge bases, source-specific research, and fact-checker agents.
  - **Deliverables:** Includes cover images and infographics; custom design assets are negotiable.

### Ramp Case Study: Proof of Concept

  - **Relevance:** Demonstrates GrowthX's ability to execute on Plaid's goal of creating interactive, tool-based content.
  - **Project:** Created 900+ programmatic pages for Ramp, including:
      - **Vendor Directory:** Reviews, comparisons, and alternatives pages for business tools.
      - **Interactive Tools:** Per diem calculator, expense classifier, and an interactive map.
  - **Key Tactic:** Populated pages with Ramp's internal spend data, visualized in interactive charts, to create unique, authoritative content.

### Logistics & CMS Publishing

  - **CMS:** Plaid uses Contentful, which has a steep learning curve for resource articles.
  - **GrowthX Capability:** The team has experience with Contentful and can handle staging/publishing, contingent on security access.
  - **Onboarding:** Requires a meeting with Plaid's web team to understand the specific Contentful setup.
