## Key Topics

### N-able's Repositioning Strategy

  - Shifting from IT security/management to cyber resilience and cybersecurity
  - Acquired AdLumen for threat detection and response capabilities
  - Now offers full "before, during, and after" attack solution suite
  - Unique positioning as one of few vendors with comprehensive resilience offerings
  - Addressing growing cyber threats, especially in SMB market

### Key Differentiators

  - Vendor-agnostic approach, integrating with other solutions
  - Best-in-class tools across portfolio
  - Partnership mentality vs. vendor relationship
  - Focus on reducing customer labor costs through automation and efficiency
  - Strong customer success program (won Stevie award in 2024)
  - Pricing models that encourage product usage (e.g., per node/agent pricing)

### Customer Base and Market Focus

  - 85-90% MSP clients, 10-15% mid-market IT departments
  - Expanding focus on mid-market, especially with UEM solutions
  - Channel-driven approach for AdLumen (distribution, resellers, VARs)
  - Different sales strategies for MSP vs. mid-market (displacement vs. consolidation)

### Product Portfolio

  - Unified Endpoint Management (UEM/RMM): Core control panel for MSPs
  - Cove: Data protection and backup solution
  - AdLumen: Managed Detection and Response (MDR)
  - Emphasis on selling full suite, but customers often adopt incrementally

### Brand Voice and Messaging

  - Shifting from "fun startup vibe" to more serious, trustworthy advisor
  - Maintaining positive outlook and high-road approach vs. competitors
  - Emphasizing partnership, reliability, and trust
  - Balancing serious cybersecurity messaging with positive future outlook

### Content and Marketing Needs

  - Increased brand awareness beyond RMM/platform perception
  - End-user focused content to drive mid-market adoption
  - SEO optimization for vast keyword opportunities
  - Scalable content creation and review process
  - "Snackable" content (listicles, best practices) for paid social
  - Case studies showcasing full product portfolio impact
