## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Rapid Growth Strategy

  - Focused on building relationships and trust through high-value workshops
  - Gave away extensive knowledge, templates, and processes openly
  - Leveraged social proof from high-profile clients (e.g., Ramp, Webflow) to attract more business
  - Minimal traditional marketing; growth primarily through word-of-mouth and relationships

### Content Creation and Thought Leadership

  - Emphasis on creating in-depth, practical content that addresses specific pain points
  - Examples: whiteboarding videos, technical guides, glossaries
  - Importance of founders/CEOs being active in content creation and social media
  - Focus on simplifying complex topics and clearly articulating solutions to industry problems

### Case Study: Augment Code

  - GrowthX produced 120+ guides in 3 months
  - 10x increase in AI visibility
  - Significant traffic growth (300 new visitors/day) to guide and MCP sections
  - Improved search rankings and mentions in AI-generated answers

### Nexla's Current Content Strategy

  - Saket writes weekly/bi-weekly on relevant industry topics
  - Content leads to media interviews and thought leadership opportunities
  - Focus on data and AI challenges, industry analysis, and practical insights
  - Potential areas for improvement: increased cadence, more channels, varied content types

### Strategies for Building Authority

  - Consider creating a cohort-based course on Maven or similar platform
  - Develop a "mastermind" course outline based on industry demand
  - Use customer network to gain initial traction and validation
  - Iterate and improve course content based on feedback
  - Combine long-form content with social media posts and website optimization
