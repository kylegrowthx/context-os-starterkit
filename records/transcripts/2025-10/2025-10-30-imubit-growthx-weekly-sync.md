## Key Topics

### Abnormal Security Content Strategy

  - Mariana is completing a 2-month keyword/title plan for Abnormal Security.
  - **Strategic Pivot:** Shifting focus from "glossary" to "editorial" content.
      - **Rationale:** Editorial topics have lower keyword difficulty (KD) and are growing much faster.
      - **Target:** Keywords with \~300 volume and KD \<35.
  - **Career Guide:** A new project with a promising average KD of 35.
  - **Client Alignment:** Mara will share the memo with Megan to gather feedback before a broader presentation, ensuring priorities are aligned.

### The Adventurers Group (TAG) Workflow

  - **Challenge:** Article generation is slow (\~50 min/article), with research taking \~18 min and drafting \~31 min.
  - **Solution:** A new Atlas "Knowledge Base" now automates tour selection, replacing a manual process.
      - **Process:** Mariana extracted data from 130+ tour pages into a Notion database, then uploaded it to the Atlas Knowledge Base.
      - **Functionality:** The AI can now instantly recommend tours based on a query (e.g., "tours for friends in Cabo").
  - **Programmatic SEO (PSO):** Not a current priority.
      - **Rationale:** The post-generation editing workflow remains manual, and editorial content is already performing well.
      - **Principle:** PSO should solve a business problem, not be implemented for its own sake.
