## Overview

- **The Problem:** Firms use a fragmented tech stack (e.g., Thomson Reuters, Shirlink) for audit/advisory, creating risk, inefficiency, and high costs.
- **The Solution:** Fieldguide is an end-to-end platform with embedded "professional-grade agents" that automate manual work, freeing practitioners for high-value judgment.
- **The Differentiator:** Domain-expert AI built by practitioners for practitioners, trained on 27,500 engagements. This contrasts with rigid legacy tech and generic AI tools.
- **The Outcome:** Increased capacity and higher quality work, enabling firms to grow revenue without adding headcount (e.g., one customer projected nearly $1M in incremental revenue).

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Fragmented Tech Stacks & Industry Pressures

  - The industry relies on outdated legacy tech (e.g., Thomson Reuters, Caseware) supplemented by siloed point solutions (e.g., Shirlink for portals, DataSnipper for evidence).
  - This creates a fragmented tech stack with significant drawbacks:
      - **Risk:** Introduces errors and data loss.
      - **Cost:** High maintenance and vendor management.
      - **Inefficiency:** Slows down workflows.
  - **Industry Pressures Driving Change:**
      - **Market:** Increasing financial complexity (e.g., digital assets) and regulatory pressure.
      - **Talent:** A talent shortage and declining CPA exam participation.
      - **Competition:** Firms must differentiate on service quality, as price margins are tight.
      - **Client Expectations:** Clients demand fast, transparent, and non-disruptive engagements.

### The Solution: End-to-End Platform with Domain-Expert AI

  - Fieldguide consolidates the entire engagement workflow into one platform, eliminating the need for multiple tools.
  - It features embedded "professional-grade agents" built with deep domain expertise.
      - **Rationale:** To provide credible, reliable automation that contrasts with generic "chatbots" or "copilots."
      - **Training Data:** The AI is trained on a corpus of 27,500 audit engagements, giving it a deep understanding of best practices.
  - **The "Human-First" Philosophy:** The goal is to amplify human judgment, not replace it.
      - **Analogy:** An "Iron Man suit" for auditors, automating repetitive tasks so practitioners can focus on high-value analysis.

### Key Differentiators & "Wow" Features

  - **Domain-Expert AI:** The most important differentiator. The agents "know what good looks like" and can flag issues like missing data before it reaches the team.
  - **Flexibility & Speed:** Unlike rigid legacy tech, Fieldguide adapts to a firm's specific methodologies, enabling them to grow and innovate faster.
  - **Customer Collaboration:** Customers feel they have a direct influence on the product roadmap, fostering a strong partnership.
  - **"Wow" Feature: The Testing Agent:**
      - Automates the most time-consuming part of an engagement—evidence testing—which can take weeks manually.
      - **Process:** The agent compares data, documents results, and flags discrepancies with a single click.
      - **Impact:** Frees auditors to apply their expertise to analyze results, not perform manual checks.

### Buyer Personas & Value Proposition

  - **Core Buyers:**
      - **Partners:** Own a book of business; motivated by growing revenue and delivering high-quality work.
      - **Managing Partners & C-suite:** Oversee the entire firm; motivated by cost management, risk reduction, and simplifying the tech stack.
  - **Value Proposition:**
      - **Increased Capacity:** Automates manual work → teams can do more with existing headcount.
      - **Higher Quality:** Domain-expert AI catches errors → improves accuracy and reduces rework.
      - **Revenue Growth:** Increased capacity and quality → enables firms to win more clients.
      - **Risk Reduction:** A single, trusted platform → eliminates data fragmentation and errors.

### Go-to-Market & Awareness Triggers

  - **Sales Motion:** Traditionally bottom-up (practitioners), but Fieldguide is actively building a top-down motion (C-suite).
  - **Awareness Triggers:**
      - **Burning Pain:** The most common driver is the daily frustration of a fragmented tech stack.
      - **Strategic Mandates:** Top-down directives for AI transformation or tech stack overhauls (e.g., M\&A, legacy system sunsets).
      - **Competitive Shopping:** Firms often evaluate new tech annually to stay competitive, even without an immediate purchase plan.
  - **Fieldguide's AI Maturity Framework (AMF):**
      - A strategic tool to help risk-averse firms adopt AI confidently.
      - **Rationale:** Addresses the high cost of failed tech implementations, which damage reputation and trust.
      - **Positioning:** Differentiates Fieldguide as a trusted partner committed to customer success.
