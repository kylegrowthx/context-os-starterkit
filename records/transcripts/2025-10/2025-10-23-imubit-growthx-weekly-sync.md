### Website Migration & Content Strategy

  - **Problem:** The current site has significant technical and content issues, including:
      - Inconsistent styling (e.g., mismatched corners).
      - Duplicated pages created without a clear strategy.
      - Broken legacy pages with non-functional buttons.

      - GrowthX will review the sitemap to identify valuable content (e.g., case studies, images) for repurposing and propose a redirect strategy for legacy pages.

      - **"Operating Strategy":** Merging human expertise with AI.
      - **"Data Readiness":** A middle-of-funnel series on preparing for AI optimization.
          - **Tone:** It's okay to start with imperfect data; a historian is not a prerequisite.

      - **"Industry 5.0":** Exploring AI + human collaboration.
  - **Messaging Stack:** A new messaging stack is in development to provide a consistent framework for discussing Imubit's solution.
      - **Goal:** Define consistent terminology and map the solution to a customer maturity journey (Define → Measure → Augment → Automate).

  - **Performance:** Sessions are up 6% and engaged sessions are up 9%.

      - **Impact:** This does not inflate Scrunch's LLM visibility metrics, which are based on simulated queries, not live traffic.

      - **Action:** Mara will investigate the root cause.
