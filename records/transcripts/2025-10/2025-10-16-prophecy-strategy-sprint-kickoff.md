## Key Topics

### Prophecy's Pivot and New Target Personas

  - Shifting focus from data engineers to data analysts and analytics leaders
  - Three key personas: end-user analysts, analytics leaders (buyers), and data platform teams (gatekeepers)
  - Targeting mid to large enterprises, especially those using Databricks
  - Main value prop: Enabling analysts to access and work with data in Databricks while maintaining governance

### Content Strategy Approach

  - Focusing on both ends of the intent spectrum: informational "what is/how to" content and high-intent comparisons/alternatives
  - Aiming to build topical authority for new audience while also targeting high-conversion keywords
  - Balancing CEO's content preferences with SEO/AEO opportunities
  - Leveraging GrowthX's agentic workflows for high-quality, persona-aligned content

### Website and Branding Updates

  - Transitioning from prophecy.io to prophecy.ai
  - New homepage design to clearly communicate Prophecy's value proposition
  - Considering creation of a unified "Get Started" page to streamline user journeys

### Competitive Landscape and Keyword Opportunities

  - Identified 14,000+ relevant keywords from analyzing just two competitors
  - 670 high-potential, low-difficulty keyword opportunities identified
  - Focus on topics like "AI tools for data visualization" and "Tableau alternatives" for quick wins

### GrowthX Engagement Goals

  - Demonstrate value of new approach within \~47 days to secure contract extension
  - Leverage agentic workflows and eval loops to ensure high-quality, persona-aligned content
  - Balance quick wins (e.g. listicles, comparisons) with more in-depth, technical content
