## Context

JioHotstar is a major streaming platform in India, formed from a merger of Jio Cinema and Disney Hotstar. They are the #1 streaming partner in India with regional presence across Europe and other territories. The company was referred to GrowthX by an internal resource and is actively exploring partnerships to improve brand visibility in AI services, particularly ChatGPT and Perplexity, for generic entertainment queries. Their website currently has no dedicated blog section, though one is planned for their roadmap, which limits immediate content publishing options for a potential GrowthX engagement.
