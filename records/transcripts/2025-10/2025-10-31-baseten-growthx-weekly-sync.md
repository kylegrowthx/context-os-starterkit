## Context

Baseten is a platform for ML deployment and inference. GrowthX is providing content marketing services to help Baseten drive awareness and authority in AI/ML. This is a trial-period engagement that runs through early November 2025, making the next few weeks critical for demonstrating value before renewal discussions. Marylise Tauzia is Baseten's point person; the team is highly quality-conscious about their public-facing content, which initially created friction when GrowthX's early article drafts felt "robotic" to Marylise. The conversation reflects GrowthX's solution: a separate Learning Center allows AI/SEO-optimized content without compromising the blog's brand voice. Baseten plans to hire their own designer to review and finalize templates, emphasizing their control over visual presentation and messaging consistency.
