## Overview

- **Role:** The Account Strategist (AS) is the PM for 3–5 clients, managing deliverables and quality within a pod structure. The AS connects the client to the Managing Editor (ME) and executes the high-level strategy set by the Account Director (AD).
- **Candidate:** Carly Piekos has 7+ years of SEO/content experience, managing enterprise clients (Amex, Boeing) at iCrossing. Her background includes technical SEO, PM, and process development, including creating a new CSM onboarding at Conductor that cut training time by two months.
- **Company Need:** GrowthX needs to formalize its processes to support rapid growth. The ideal candidate will help define "jobs to be done" and build scalable systems, moving beyond the current ad-hoc workflows.
- **Onboarding & Success:** Onboarding is a 1-week company intro, followed by shadowing 1–2 clients in Week 2. Success is defined by client retention and building strategic relationships that uncover future needs, not just delivering on contracts.

## Key Topics

### GrowthX: Company & Role Context

  - **Model:** Agency built on an internal content platform, which will eventually become an external, tiered self-serve product.
  - **Team Structure:** Pods are the core unit, with titles evolving.
      - **Account Director (AD):** Manages \~3 pods; sets high-level SEO strategy.
      - **Account Strategist (AS):** Manages 3–5 clients; PMs deliverables, quality, and KPIs.
      - **Managing Editor (ME):** Creates content in the platform.
  - **Client Onboarding:** A separate 8-week "strategy sprint" team handles initial setup (calibrating content, defining stakeholders). This ensures the AS receives a client with a clear strategy, enabling a smooth continuation of work.

### Candidate Experience & Skills

  - **Background:** 7+ years in SEO/content, spanning in-house, agency, and SaaS roles.
  - **Current Role (iCrossing):** SEO Manager for American Express and Boeing.
      - **Responsibilities:** Full project management, client communication, and deliverable creation.
      - **Coordination:** Manages internal teams (analysts, UX, dev) and uses client-specific tools (Confluence for Amex, Jira for Boeing) across three separate laptops.
  - **Process Improvement:**
      - **Conductor:** Created a new CSM onboarding program that cut training time by two months.
      - **Motivation:** Driven by a passion for building useful, scalable systems.

### Project Management & Problem-Solving

  - **Client Management:**
      - Manages multiple enterprise clients by structuring the week around allotted hours per client.
      - Proactively communicates bandwidth and negotiates timelines, offering to push non-critical tasks to the next week.
      - Provides weekly progress updates (Thursdays) to maintain transparency.
  - **Problem-Solving Example:**
      - **Challenge:** A critical PowerPoint deliverable for Amex went off-track due to conflicting, subjective feedback from multiple internal stakeholders and the client contact.
      - **Action:** Piekos took ownership, consolidated feedback by prioritizing the primary client contact's input, and delivered the project on time.
      - **Outcome:** The client was impressed and used the deliverable for their own internal presentations.

### GrowthX: Onboarding & Success Metrics

  - **90-Day Success Metrics:**
      - **Client Retention:** The primary North Star metric.
      - **Process Definition:** Contribute to formalizing "jobs to be done" and scalable workflows.
      - **Strategic Relationships:** Build trust to uncover future client needs, moving beyond simple task execution.
  - **Onboarding Process:**
      - **Week 1:** Company orientation.
      - **Week 2:** Begin shadowing 1–2 clients, aiming for a smooth handover with a director.
  - **Sales-to-Ops Handoff:**
      - **Process:** The salesperson is highly collaborative, sharing contract language and scoping concerns with the ops team.
      - **Rationale:** A strong pipeline allows the company to be selective, ensuring a good client fit and preventing over-promising.
  - **SEO Strategy:**
      - **Current:** Account Directors set high-level strategy within their pods.
      - **Future:** A new SEO Strategist hire will centralize this function, potentially allowing Account Strategists to focus on execution.
