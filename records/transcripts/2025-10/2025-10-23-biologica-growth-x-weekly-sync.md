## Key Topics

### Content Workflow & Quality

  - **Process:** To avoid rework, minor edits will be applied *after* medical review. Systemic issues will be fixed in the content pipeline.
  - **Pacing:** Growth X is catching up by delivering 6 new articles, all produced post-pipeline adjustment.
  - **Quality:** The "Iron" article's opening was praised as relatable.
  - **CTAs:** The current multi-product CTA format is an improvement. Growth X is open to tweaks, like calling out one product in-text while showing others as cards.

### AI Image Feedback

  - **Principle:** Images must be "lifelike" and unobtrusive.
  - **"Good" Examples:** Simple, realistic photos (e.g., flowers) that don't distract.
  - **"Bad" Examples:**
      - **"Soap opera effect" visuals:** Look obviously AI-generated.
      - **Unrealistic physics:** Water ripples in a glass that wouldn't occur.
      - **Distracting imagery:** An "x-ray" of a pear or a flower with an impossible shadow.
  - **Delivery:** Growth X will provide \~2 image options per article in Airtable.
  - **Backup Plan:** If options are unsuitable, publish with a placeholder and replace it later from the growing Shopify media library.

### New AEO Content Strategy

  - **Goal:** Capture AEO visibility with non-product content relevant to the ICP.
  - **Initial Scope:** Editorial-style articles for a quick win.
  - **Technical Needs (Editorial Articles):**
      - A separate Shopify blog (via a new breadcrumb).
      - Support for standard text blocks and an FAQ section.
      - **Constraint:** Shopify's 50-block-per-page limit requires dev agency consultation.
  - **Future Scope (Data-Driven Directory):**
      - A more complex project requiring a custom data pipeline and dev cycles.
      - **Example:** A directory of supplement ingredients.
