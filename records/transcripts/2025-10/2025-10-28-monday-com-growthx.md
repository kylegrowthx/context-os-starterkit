## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Current State & Pain Points

  - **Business:** High-touch services org (e.g., delivering 5 weekly blog articles per client) with flat monthly fees.
  - **Current Tools:** Notion, Linear, and Airtable.
  - **Core Problems:**
      - **Capacity Planning:** Notion lacks the dynamic visibility needed to reassign clients and model workload changes.
      - **Client Health:** No central system for tracking health, making it difficult to spot at-risk accounts.
      - **Scope Creep:** No visibility into "off-the-books" work, making it impossible to quantify its impact.
      - **Communication:** Managing 37+ client Slack channels is unsustainable.

### monday.com's Proposed Solution

  - **Product:** monday.com Work Management (not Sales CRM, as HubSpot is used for sales).
  - **Core Functionality:**
      - **Boards:** Track clients, projects, and tasks.
      - **Dashboards:** Aggregate data for high-level KPIs and reports.
      - **Workload Widget:** A dynamic bubble chart to visualize team capacity, enabling managers to reassign tasks and instantly see the impact on individual workloads.
  - **Integrations:**
      - Native: HubSpot, Slack, Gmail, Outlook, Zoom.
      - Open API: Enables custom connections, e.g., to Linear or Notion.

### AI & Client Health Scoring

  - **Sentiment Analysis:** An AI column can analyze Slack messages pushed to monday.com to gauge client sentiment (e.g., positive, negative, neutral).
  - **Custom Health Score:** A formula or AI column can compute a single client health score from multiple data points (e.g., meeting frequency, deliverable status, sentiment).
  - **Reporting:** The health score can be visualized on a dashboard to show account status (e.g., Healthy, At-Risk, On Track).

### Pricing, Onboarding & Timeline

  - **Pricing:** Enterprise plan is $52/user/month, billed annually.
      - **40 Users:** \~$25,000/year.
      - Volume discounts are available.
  - **Onboarding:** Implementation is a separate cost, not included in the plan.
      - **Standard Partner:** \~$250/hour.
      - **monday.com Internal Team:** \~$350/hour.
      - **Alternative:** Daniel can connect GrowthX with a "platinum level" partner who offers free onboarding, paid by monday.com as part of the deal.
  - **Timeline:**
      - **Decision:** By year-end.
      - **Setup:** 4–6 weeks, to be led by a new Ops hire.
