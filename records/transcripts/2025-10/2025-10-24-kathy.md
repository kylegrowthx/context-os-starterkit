## Key Topics

### GrowthX's Vision & Strategy

  - **Core Mission:** Improve knowledge work for non-technical people.
  - **Dual Model:** An agency (GrowthX) and a product company (unnamed competitor to SEMrush/Ahrefs).
  - **Competitive Edge:** The agency model provides real-world experience, enabling GrowthX to build a more effective product than tool-only companies.
  - **GTM Strategy:** A "detached brand" evangelizes AI/growth, serving as an organic marketing vehicle for GrowthX.

### Hiring Need 1: Director (Delivery)

  - **Problem:** GrowthX lacks a repeatable model for its dev tool clients (e.g., Webflow, Strapi), and the team has limited technical expertise.
  - **Role:** A Director to act as a strategic counterpart to client founders/CMOs.
  - **Responsibilities:**
      - Define and standardize client playbooks (e.g., QBRs).
      - Own a portfolio of dev tool accounts.
  - **Team Structure:** Director → Account Strategist → Managing Editor.

### Hiring Need 2: GTM Lead

  - **Problem:** GrowthX's marketing efforts (community, events, demand gen) are siloed and need a unified strategy.
  - **Role:** A GTM lead to create connective tissue between all marketing lanes.
  - **Responsibilities:**
      - Drive marketing strategy and messaging.
      - Unify community, events, and demand gen.

### Kathy's Background & Fit

  - **Status:** Left GrowthBook; now consulting and building Spivit (an event meeting-setup tool).
  - **Fit for Director:** Technical background and PMM experience make Kathy well-suited to manage dev tool clients and push back strategically.
  - **Fit for GTM Lead:** PMM skills are valuable for building a "publication machine" to turn expert knowledge into content at scale.
  - **Shared Vision:** Kathy proposed an AI agent to automate content creation from product artifacts (e.g., PRDs), aligning with GrowthX's product vision.
