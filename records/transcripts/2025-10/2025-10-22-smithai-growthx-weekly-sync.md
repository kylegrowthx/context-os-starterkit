Maddy Martin, Smith.ai's director of product marketing, leads strategy on the client side; Megan Dickey and Jo Kaminska manage the engagement from GrowthX, while Julian Wan handles design. However, authenticity requires specificity — understanding each industry's actual workflow, pain points, and terminology — which their current pages lack entirely.
