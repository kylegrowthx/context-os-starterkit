## Context

Fieldguide is a B2B SaaS platform for auditors—built by practitioners for practitioners—that automates audit workflows across the entire lifecycle (data request through reporting) with AI. The platform delivers massive efficiency gains: 50% time cuts, 70% manual task elimination. Currently serving ~100 customers primarily from the top of the IPA 500 list (large firms), Fieldguide is targeting expansion into mid-market financial audit, specialty advisory (SOC/HITRUST control audits), and Canada. The buying triggers are top-down CEO/board mandates to adopt AI, competitive pressure from peer firms, and inability to scale without hiring more staff. This partnership with GrowthX addresses a key gap: organic search is strong for Fieldguide's branded terms, but the company struggles to reach unaware audiences searching for solutions in LLMs. The meeting served as the kick-off to GrowthX's "Strategy Sprint"—a deep-dive discovery and onboarding process designed to align on shared OKRs, set both teams up for success, and accelerate execution.

## Key Topics

### The Challenge: Scaling Organic Growth for Aggressive Targets

  - **Objective:** Meet aggressive ARR and pipeline targets for next year, which require triple-digit YoY growth.
  - **Problem:** Current organic search is strong for branded terms but fails to capture new, unaware audiences.
  - **Solution:** Scale content production to increase LLM search visibility and attract new leads.
      - **Rationale:** Jenny Cota discovered HockeyStack (a GrowthX client) via ChatGPT, validating the channel's potential.
  - **Context:** This content initiative complements an existing SEM and SEO strategy.

### The Solution: Fieldguide's Core Differentiators

  - **Practitioner-First AI:** Built for auditor workflows, not the other way around.
      - **Team:** Practitioners and AI experts collaborate on development.
      - **Ethos:** "AI + people," not AI replacing people.
  - **Unified End-to-End Platform:** The only one in the market, covering the entire audit lifecycle from data request to reporting.
  - **Massive Efficiency Gains:**
      - Cut audit times by 50% (e.g., 240 hrs → 120 hrs for one engagement).
      - Eliminate up to 70% of manual tasks.
      - Automate a 40-hour "rock report" in 40 seconds.
  - **Security & Governance:** Addresses auditor risk aversion with full transparency and certifications (ISO 42001, SOC 2).
  - **Strategic Positioning:** Occupies the middle ground between legacy tech (e.g., Thompson Reuters) and new entrants lacking industry depth.

### The Audience: Who Fieldguide Serves

  - **Current Customers:** \~100 logos, primarily from the top of the IPA 500 list (large firms).
  - **New Target Segments:**
      - **Mid-Market:** Growing traction for financial audit.
      - **Specialty Advisory Firms:** Focus on control audits (SOC, HITRUST).
      - **Canada:** International expansion is a key growth driver.
  - **Buying Triggers:**
      - Top-down directives from boards/CEOs to adopt AI.
      - Competitive pressure from peer firms using Fieldguide.
      - Inability to scale without hiring more staff.

### The Strategy: Content Hub & Performance Metrics

  - **New Content Hub:** A separate section from the existing blog, which is reserved for CEO thought leadership and product updates.
      - **Rationale:** Allows for high-volume, data-driven SEO content without being tied to a specific author.
  - **Q4 OKR:** Jenny Cota shared an OKR to track progress, which will be refined with GrowthX.
  - **Baseline Measurement:** GrowthX will establish a baseline for LLM-referred traffic using a new AI visibility tool and GSC/GA4 data.
  - **High-Performing Content:**
      - **ABM Direct Mail:** Video cards with product demos drive nearly 50% of current pipeline.
      - **Digital Assets:** "AI Maturity Framework," "Practitioner's Guide to AI," and the "Audit & Advisory Benchmark Report."
