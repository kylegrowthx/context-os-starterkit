## Key Topics

### GrowthX Service Overview

  - End-to-end partner for content creation, SEO, and organic growth
  - Core focus: Creating high-quality, scalable content for marketing sites (editorial and programmatic)
  - Services include strategy, content roadmap, brand voice development, and daily execution (5 articles/week)
  - Last-mile execution: staging and publishing in client's CMS
  - Analytics and LLM visibility monitoring included

### Differentiation from Competitors

  - Faster time-to-value compared to DIY tools like Aerox
  - More sophisticated, custom workflows tailored to each client's brand and technical needs
  - Cost-effective at scale (no pass-through costs for API calls, tokens, etc.)
  - Ability to publish daily with consistent quality across various topics
  - Combines AI-powered generation with human expertise and oversight

### Client Examples and Results

  - Town (seed-stage): Created highly accurate tax advice content
  - Augment Code: Produced 100+ technical guides in 3 months, 3x blog traffic
  - Augment Code saw 2x organic traffic growth in 3-4 months
  - Multiple citations of GrowthX-created content in AI search results

### Strategy and Workflow

  - Comprehensive strategy sprint: SEO audit, content roadmap, brand voice development
  - Two-phase approach: Strategy sprint followed by daily execution
  - Utilize custom knowledge bases and artifacts for highly relevant content
  - Incorporate client quotes and technical details for authenticity
  - Focus on key GEO factors: well-researched, attributed, comprehensive, and fresh content

### Technical Capabilities

  - Built on learnings from Aerox, with improvements based on scaling pain points
  - Custom platform (Content OS) for managing assignments, topics, and keyword analysis
  - Direct CMS integration for staging and publishing
  - Ability to work in multiple languages (case-by-case basis)

### Team Structure

  - 70-80 total employees
  - Large Engineering/Product Development (EPD) and delivery teams
  - 3 team members assigned per client in growth execution phase
  - Staff members typically work on 4-5 clients maximum
