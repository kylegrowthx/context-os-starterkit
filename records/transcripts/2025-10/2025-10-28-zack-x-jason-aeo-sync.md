## Key Topics

### growthx.ai's AEO Strategy

  - **Model:** A "technology agency" that builds and operates AI-driven "content machines" for clients, focusing on outcomes over just providing a platform.
  - **Process:**
      - **Automation:** Workflows combine AI agents (research, writing) and human experts (e.g., a full-stack engineer for Webflow's dev docs).
      - **Volume:** Produces 30–40 articles/week for clients like Webflow and Ramp.
      - **Maintenance:** The "machines" also update content to keep pace with rapid product changes (e.g., Webflow's quarterly rebrands).
  - **Measurement:**
      - **North Star:** Bottom-of-funnel metrics (e.g., signups).
      - **Leading Indicators:** SEO metrics (rank, impressions, clicks) and AEO mentions.
      - **AEO Goal:** Win indirect brand mentions in LLM answers, as direct links are rare.
  - **Tactics:**
      - **Research:** Map target prompts and analyze citation sources (Reddit, public forums).
      - **Reddit Strategy:** Ranges from standard commenting to "gray hat" methods like seeding and steering discussions.
      - **Vendor Directory Example:** growthx.ai created the content for Ramp's vendor and expense category directories, which Profound now uses for its marketing.

### Faire's AEO Initiatives

  - **Context:** Faire is dedicating Q4 to AEO experiments, building on significant organic growth from ChatGPT without prior specific effort.
  - **Primary Goal:** Retailer acquisition and signups.
  - **Content Focus:**
      - **Informational:** Establish authority on wholesale/retail topics.
      - **Transactional:** Capture long-tail product searches (e.g., "wholesale Halloween candles").
  - **Key Finding:** Hyper-specific, long-tail pages (e.g., collections) perform best in both SEO and AEO.
  - **Experimentation:**
      - **On-site:** Create new, hyper-specific pages to test citation and signup rates.
      - **Off-site:** Influence citations on other sites via affiliate and community teams.
      - **PDPs:** Test optimizing Product Detail Pages (PDPs) for AEO, a challenge as brands control the content.

### AEO's Cross-Functional Challenge

  - **Problem:** AEO is often owned by SEO teams but requires a broader skillset (PR, community, PMM) and cross-functional coordination.
  - **Faire's Approach:** Zack is actively promoting a cross-functional model, which the company has embraced.
  - **Measurement Challenge:** Balancing direct signups (for transactional content) with indirect awareness (for informational content).
