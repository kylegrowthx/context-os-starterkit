### Publishing Automation Blocker

  - **Problem:** The publishing automation feature is blocked, preventing all new content from going live.
  - **Impact:** A large content backlog is accumulating, preventing Sunbit from realizing value from the service.
  - **Urgency:** Jason stressed that this is the top priority for making the partnership work, as the delay is "bordering on unacceptable."
  - **Required Action:** Ehtisham must get an ETA from developers and push for expedited completion.

### Content Production & Standardization

  - **New Batch:** 25 articles are complete, including 9 case studies and 16 B2C cost articles.
  - **Future Pipeline:** The case study volume will increase, supported by \~70 customer data points available for future content.
  - **Standardization for Automation:** To enable the publishing webhook, content cluster names must be updated to match Sunbit's blog categories.
      - B2B Auto → `auto`
      - B2B Dental → `dental`
      - B2C Auto → `automotive tips`
      - B2C Dental → `dental tips`
  - **Case Studies:** These will be published to the main blog and do not require a separate cluster name.

### Organic Traffic & Analytics

  - **Performance (Oct 12–18):**
      - Sessions: 425 (down from 433)
      - Active Users: 387
      - New Users: Stable
      - Engagement Time: Stable
  - **Issue:** Key event tracking appears broken, showing an inflated 325 events (vs. a typical 46–98).
      - **Cause:** All events are misattributed to "shop directory views," even on B2B articles where "become a partner" clicks are expected.
      - **Significance:** This renders event data unreliable for ROI analysis.
  - **Trend:** The dip is attributed to ranking drops on a few articles. There are no 404 errors, a positive sign.
