## Key Topics

### The Problem: Forecasting Gap

  - The current forecast is based on a small, 180-topic backlog that will be exhausted in \~2 months.
  - This is insufficient for the client's goal of hundreds of thousands of monthly visitors.
  - **Requirement:** A 6-month content plan is needed to create a more compelling forecast.

### Current Process: Inefficient & Limited

  - **Topic Clusters:** Defined based on the ICP (coders) and brainstorming, with AI (Claude) used to map cluster options.
  - **Keyword Research:** A bottom-up workflow:
    1.  **Generate:** Use Claude to brainstorm keyword ideas for a cluster.
    2.  **Validate:** Manually plug ideas into SEMrush to check for search volume and difficulty.
  - **Limitation:** This process is too slow for generating hundreds of ideas and risks AI "hallucinations" (keywords with no search volume).

### Proposed Solution: Top-Down Keyword Research

  - A top-down approach to efficiently generate a large-scale backlog.
  - **Method:**
    1.  Map the entire search landscape the client needs to own.
    2.  Use tools like Google Autocomplete and "People Also Ask" to find emergent themes.
    3.  This yields a much larger keyword universe, enabling a 600+ topic backlog.

### Immediate Action: Programmatic Salary SEO

  - Adding this play to the forecast will significantly boost projected traffic.
  - **Goal:** Identify the top 100 high-volume keywords for salary pages.
  - **Method:**
    1.  Analyze competitor salary pages (e.g., Indeed, Levels.fyi) using Screaming Frog.
    2.  This reveals the scale of potential URLs and keywords to target.

### Future Application: Augment Client

  - The Augment client has similar 10x growth goals and also needs a larger content backlog.
  - **Plan:** Document the new process for Data Annotation, then adapt it for Augment.
  - **Priority:** Data Annotation is the immediate focus.
