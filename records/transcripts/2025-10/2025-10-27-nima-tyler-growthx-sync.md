## Key Topics

### The Access Dilemma

  - **Problem:** Navan's security policy requires a company laptop for G Suite access (GSC, GA4), which conflicts with GrowthX's agency model.
  - **Constraint:** GrowthX cannot use Navan-issued laptops, as this would classify them as "contingent workers" and create significant delivery problems.
  - **Proposed Workaround:** GrowthX can operate without direct access if Navan provides a dedicated resource to pull reports and publish content.

### CMS Publishing Workflow

  - **Requirement:** A dedicated Navan resource for timely publishing is critical to avoid project bottlenecks.
  - **Context:** GrowthX targets 5 articles/week, with publishing starting in weeks 4–6.
  - **Rationale:** Delays of several days would prevent GrowthX from scaling content production and achieving desired results.

### Analytics & Reporting

  - **Preference:** Direct access to GSC and GA4 is preferred to enable GrowthX to update a shared Looker dashboard and track performance.
  - **Rationale:** Direct access allows GrowthX to make data-driven strategy adjustments, which is difficult when "flying blind."
  - **Alternative:** Navan's SEO owner, Moran (Tel Aviv), could pull and send reports, though time zone overlap with a US-based GrowthX team is limited.

### AI Tool Transparency

  - **Requirement:** Navan's security policy demands a list of all AI models used in GrowthX's platform.
  - **Rationale:** This is a strict policy; Navan previously blocked vendor X-Funnel for failing to disclose its AI models.
  - **GrowthX Platform:** A proprietary platform using best-in-class models (e.g., Claude Sonnet 4.5, GPT).
  - **Offer:** GrowthX can set up a Scrunch dashboard for Navan to provide end-to-end visibility into the AI workflow.

### Collaboration & Review Process

  - **Collaboration:** A private Slack channel hosted by GrowthX is the preferred and most efficient method for communication.
  - **Content Review:** The process uses an email alias for initial editorial review, with final approval from Rich, Libby, or Andrew.
  - **Goal:** Limit the number of reviewers to streamline the process and prevent delays.

### Contract Status

  - **Security Addendum:** Final review in progress at GrowthX.
  - **MSA & SoW:** In a good place; Mithil is confirming PO structure and payment terms.
