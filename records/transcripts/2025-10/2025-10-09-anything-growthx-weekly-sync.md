## Context

Anything (formerly Create) is an AI-powered no-code app builder targeting entrepreneurs, tech professionals, and small agencies seeking to build mobile and web applications without coding. The company recently rebranded from Create to Anything, losing significant SEO authority in the process (domain authority dropped from 60+ to 21). GrowthX is a B2B content marketing agency engaged for an 8-week content strategy and production sprint. Aida Knezevic, GrowthX's editorial account strategist, joined the team to manage content strategy and the editorial process alongside managing editor Nathalie Schrans. This weekly sync follows the kickoff meeting and focuses on presenting GrowthX's initial content strategy framework, demonstrating Atlas (GrowthX's in-house content generation platform), and gathering Dhruv's feedback on strategic direction before keyword research and content calendar development begins.
