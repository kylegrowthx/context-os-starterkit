## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Blockers

  - KPIs show slow growth, a direct result of low publication volume.
  - The primary blocker is a slow review process, which delays content production.
  - **Solution:** Streamline the review process to increase publication velocity.

### New Review Process

  - **Live Idea Approvals:** Ideas will be reviewed and approved live during syncs to accelerate the pipeline.
  - **Pre-Assignment:** GrowthX will pre-assign reviewers for ideas and articles, following Alberto's rubric:
      - Atul: 50%
      - Mauricio: 30%
      - Alberto: 20% (for competitive/vendor-focused topics)
  - **Reminders:** Automated email/Slack reminders will be set for pending reviews.

### Live Idea Review

  - A live review session approved several ideas, moving them to "In Production."
  - **Key Decision:** The keyword "predictive" was changed to "proactive" to align with Ambient's brand messaging.

### New Lead-Gen Strategy: White Papers

  - **Goal:** Create 3-4 gated white papers to generate leads, compensating for a light event quarter.
  - **Content Source:** Repurpose existing blog content.
  - **Proposed Topics:**
      - A general "How to implement agentic physical security" guide.
      - Vertical-specific guides (Education, Healthcare, Energy).
  - **Title Strategy:** Use "agentic physical security" to build the new category, as it's seen as fresher and more engaging than "AI in physical security."
  - **Promotion:** Ambient will create landing pages and promote the assets via ads and email marketing.
