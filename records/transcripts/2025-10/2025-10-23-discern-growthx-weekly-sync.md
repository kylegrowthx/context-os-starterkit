## Key Topics

### Entity Detail Guides

  - **Problem:** Users struggle to find the unique identifiers (e.g., entity number, formation date) needed for self-service filings on Discern.
  - **Solution:** Create short, scannable guides for all 51 jurisdictions (50 states + DC) showing users how to find this information on the Secretary of State (SOS) website.
  - **Content Structure (Ademilade's Proposal):**
      - **BLUF Summary:** A top-level paragraph with the immediate answer: "Go to the SOS website and search for your entity name."
      - **Summary Table:** A scannable table detailing required info, where to find it, and its expected format (e.g., number of digits).
      - **H2 Sections:** Detailed instructions for finding each data point.
      - **SOS Links:** Direct links to each state's SOS business search page.
      - **CTA:** A final section on onboarding entities to Discern.
  - **Critical Instruction:** Guides must advise users to **copy and paste** the exact entity name from the SOS website.
      - **Rationale:** Minor variations (e.g., "Discern Inc." vs. "Discern, Inc.") are treated as different legal entities by the state, causing filing rejections.

### Healthcare Content Strategy

  - **Target Audience:** Healthcare businesses, which are typically more heavily regulated and require more state registrations.
  - **Key Pain Points Discern Solves:**
      - **High Entity Volume:** Physical healthcare models (e.g., urgent care) require many legal entities (e.g., PCs) for each location, creating a significant compliance burden.
      - **Complex Entity Types:** Professional Corporations (PCs) and Professional Limited Liability Companies (PLLCs) are poorly served by the industry, leading to frequent compliance issues.
  - **Proposed Content Buckets:**
      - General Healthcare Compliance
      - Home Healthcare Businesses
      - Professional Corporation (PC) Compliance
      - Professional Limited Liability Company (PLLC) Compliance
