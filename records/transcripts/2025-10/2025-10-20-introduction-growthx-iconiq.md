## Overview

- **Goal:** ICONIQ seeks a partner to build an "AEO" (AI Engine Optimization) strategy, shifting its brand perception from growth-stage-only to a top early-stage AI investor.
- **Solution:** GrowthX offers a white-glove service that provides both strategy and execution, solving ICONIQ's key challenge of lacking internal writing bandwidth.
- **Process:** A two-phase model: an 8-week strategy sprint (delivering artifacts and sample content) followed by a 12-month execution phase for daily publishing.
- **Constraint:** ICONIQ's strict SEC compliance process prohibits updating old content, which will require a new-content-only approach and a heavy review burden.

## Key Topics

### ICONIQ's Challenge & Goal

  - **Primary Goal:** Shift brand perception to be known as a top early-stage and AI investor.
  - **Desired Outcome:** Appear in AI search results (e.g., ChatGPT) for queries like "best investors for AI."
  - **Internal Constraint:** Small marketing team (4 people) lacks the bandwidth for content creation.
  - **Previous Evaluation:** Profound was rejected; it identified problems but offered no execution support.

### GrowthX's Solution & Process

  - **Differentiator:** A white-glove service providing full execution (strategy → publishing), unlike tools like Profound.
  - **Methodology:** AEO requires a large content surface area to answer varied user queries across LLMs.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1: 8-Week Strategy Sprint**
          - Create foundational artifacts (personas, brand voice) to guide AI workflows.
          - Publish sample articles to demonstrate quality before a long-term commitment.
      - **Phase 2: 12-Month Execution**
          - Publish content daily, combining net-new articles with refreshes of existing content.
          - **Note:** The refresh component is blocked by ICONIQ's compliance rules.
  - **Pricing:** \~$15k/month per workstream (e.g., editorial, programmatic, newsletter).

### ICONIQ's Content & Compliance Constraints

  - **Current Content:**
      - In-depth research reports using ICONIQ's proprietary database.
      - Investment announcements.
      - Repurposed video content (founder stories).
  - **Compliance Constraint:** As a registered investment advisor, ICONIQ cannot update old content.
      - **Rationale:** To avoid SEC scrutiny over changes to historical records.
      - **Impact:** Blocks GrowthX's standard content refresh strategy.
  - **Internal Process:** All new content requires review by ICONIQ's compliance team, which can be a bottleneck and may impact the content's tone.
