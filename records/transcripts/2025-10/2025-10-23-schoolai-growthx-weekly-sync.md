## Key Topics

### Content Pipeline & Performance

  - **Editorial Article Shortage:** The editorial pipeline is low (1 article staged vs. 10/week target), likely due to report card season. Nikki pinged the team to submit finished articles.
  - **October Performance:**
      - Clicks are up 4.9% WoW, projecting \~128K for the month.
      - This is a strong result given widespread industry volatility, including a Google Search Console data freeze from Oct 15–17.
  - **Reporting Pivot:**
      - **Decision:** Shift performance reporting to a month-over-month (MoM) view, starting with the November sync.
      - **Rationale:** To gain strategic insights from larger trends, moving beyond weekly noise. Weekly anomalies will still be flagged.

### PSEO Strategy & Implementation

  - **Problem:** PSEO is blocked by SchoolAI's internal capacity, not a strategic concern.
      - **Bottlenecks:** Image creation (Carrington) and content review.
  - **Proposed Solution: AI-Generated Images**
      - GrowthX can use its workflow to generate PSEO images, solving the design bottleneck.
      - **Method:** Feed the workflow SchoolAI's brand guidelines, reference image URLs, and product assets (e.g., dashboard screenshots, "space tiles") for integration.
  - **Proposed Solution: AI-Assisted Content Review**
      - Nikki will test using SchoolAI's new Chrome extension (in beta) to review PSEO articles.
      - **Goal:** Automate checks for factual accuracy and product capability claims.
      - **Enabler:** An updated product capabilities doc will serve as the source of truth for the AI to prevent "hallucinations."

### New Growth Opportunities

  - **Diversify Top Content:**
      - **Problem:** The "AI for Math" article accounts for \~50% of blog sessions, creating a single point of failure.
      - **Opportunity:** Replicate its success formula by creating similar guides for other subjects.
      - **Resource:** Nikki will provide the "Starter Packs" database (coach-created lists of Spaces for specific subjects) to inform new content.
  - **LLM Discovery of Spaces:**
      - **Finding:** A SchoolAI "Space" ("Historic figure chatbot") was cited by an LLM, generating 6 sessions.
      - **Significance:** This confirms individual tools can be discovered, not just the platform.
      - **Opportunity:** PSEO for Spaces could be a major differentiator, enabling SchoolAI to break out of the commoditized competitor cluster.
