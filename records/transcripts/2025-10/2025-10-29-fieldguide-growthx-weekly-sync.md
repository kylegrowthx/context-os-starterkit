## Key Topics

### Current State & Goals

  - **Problem:** Fieldguide's blog has minimal organic traffic (13 visitors/mo) and lacks a scalable content engine.
  - **Goals:**
      - Establish visibility in AI tools (ChatGPT, Perplexity).
      - Build non-branded organic traffic to reduce paid ad spend.
      - Create a scalable publishing engine (5 blogs/week).

### Proposed Content Strategy

  - **Approach:** Use topic clusters to build authority efficiently, avoiding a "spray and pray" method.
  - **Rationale:** Mapping clusters to solutions creates a natural internal linking structure and a clear user path to product pages.
  - **6 Proposed Clusters:**
    1.  **AI-powered audit automation:** High-level AI topics + commercial keywords (e.g., "Onspring vs. Audit Board vs. Fieldguide").
    2.  **SOC audits:** Maps to SOC audits & readiness solutions.
    3.  **Cybersecurity & privacy compliance:** Maps to IT audit & cybersecurity solutions.
    4.  **Financial audits & SOC compliance:** High-volume keywords (>100k searches/mo); positions Fieldguide as essential tech.
    5.  **Risk advisory & internal audit:** Maps to risk advisory & internal audit solutions.
    6.  **Software comparisons & buying guides:** Focused on tool evaluations (e.g., "best SOC 2 audit software").

### Recommended Priority Clusters

  - **#1: AI-powered audit automation** → Highlights Fieldguide's core differentiator.
  - **#6: Software comparisons & buying guides** → Drives immediate rankings and AI visibility.
  - **Requirement:** This cluster requires a "Product Feature Matrix" artifact from Fieldguide detailing differentiators vs. competitors.

### Content Workflow & Assets

  - **Process:** GrowthX uses its Atlas platform (AI + human-in-the-loop) for drafting, then sends Google Docs to Fieldguide for review.
  - **Publishing:** GrowthX will publish approved articles directly to Fieldguide's HubSpot CMS.
  - **Featured Images:** GrowthX can auto-generate abstract images using AI; requires access to Fieldguide's brand assets (fonts, colors).
  - **Future Tactic:** Add inline CTAs to blog posts to capture user intent and drive traffic to solutions pages.
