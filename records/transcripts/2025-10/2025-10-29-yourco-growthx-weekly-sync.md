## Key Topics

### Performance Analysis: Subcluster Growth

  - Analysis of individual subclusters reveals a clear correlation: new content → immediate traffic growth.
  - **Scheduling & Call-offs:** Strong growth and engagement.
      - **Why:** The focus on "call-offs" addresses a specific, high-pain point for the audience.
      - **Proof:** Fixing an indexing issue on a call-offs article (by changing its slug) immediately drove traffic, confirming the content's value.
  - **Templates:** Growth driven by new content, primarily for safety and engagement.
  - **Surveys & Feedback:** A spike in May followed the publication of four new articles, then traffic normalized.
      - **Why:** Google's temporary preference for new content, not a sudden surge in market demand.
  - **Translation & Multilingual:** Steady growth, but analysis shows the "Cost of translation" article is the primary driver.
      - **Why:** It attracts users with high-intent queries (e.g., "looking for translators").

### Problem: Traffic Attracting Smaller Businesses

  - **Observation:** Recent SEO/SEM efforts generated \~20 leads, but all were from small companies.
  - **Hypothesis:** The current tactical content (e.g., "employee texting") attracts SMBs because it solves immediate, hands-on problems for individuals in multi-role positions.
  - **Goal:** Attract enterprise leads, who require content focused on high-level business benefits and strategic value.

### Solution: New Content Strategy for Enterprise

  - **Frontline Intelligence:** The new topic will be used to create conceptual, benefit-driven content.
      - **Why:** To shift focus from tactical execution to strategic value (e.g., "frontline workforce visibility as a game changer").
  - **Competitive Content:** A future strategy to target enterprise users already on competitor platforms (e.g., Beekeeper).
      - **Why:** To highlight Yourco's specific advantages (e.g., SMS-based communication, no app download) over competitor pain points (e.g., low app adoption).
      - **Approach:** Focus on niche use cases and specific problems (e.g., "alternatives to Beekeeper for low adoption"), not broad comparisons.
      - **Timing:** Develop this content after the Frontline Intelligence product is mature enough to showcase its unique analytical capabilities.

### Technical & Reporting Updates

  - **Indexing Issue Resolved:** Only one page remains unindexed.
      - **Why:** The fix (changing a page's slug) worked, proving the problem was related to Google's difficulty distinguishing between similar URLs.
  - **Looker Reporting:** All old, non-GrowthX content is now included in the cohort and subcluster reports.
      - **How:** Uncheck the "non-content" filter to view the full content set.
