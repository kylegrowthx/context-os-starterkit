## Summary

Exec and GrowthX aligned on a major SEO strategy shift: moving from broad, generic content targeting to creating dedicated "money pages" for high-intent commercial keywords like "sales training software" and "AI roleplay for sales," with internal linking from the homepage to distribute ranking power. The team acknowledged a $48k/year backlink-building workstream that had lacked visibility, and Megan committed to delivering a dashboard and monthly strategy report showing ~10 links per month and authority score targets (aiming for backlinks from sites with SEMrush Authority ≥31, with TechCrunch-level sites being most valuable). GrowthX will support the new strategy through keyword analysis for each solutions page and help Exec create data-driven content to earn high-quality backlinks naturally.

---

## Context

Exec is an AI-powered conversational platform offering role-specific solutions (sales training, learning & development, customer support) and industry-specific verticals (dealerships, insurance, real estate). GrowthX has been managing their SEO and content strategy for 11 months, primarily delivering bottom-of-funnel comparison content and buyer guides focused on "AI roleplay" topics. However, Sean and Garett (Exec's product and go-to-market leads) raised a critical concern: after 11 months, Exec isn't ranking for their core, high-intent keywords — a significant miss on "table stakes" traffic. This meeting represented a strategic pivot point: the team agreed to shift from content-only tactics to a structured "pillar-cluster" model centered on dedicated solutions pages that would compete directly with competitors like Second Nature (who successfully rank for both "AI roleplay" and "sales training software").

## Key Topics

### SEO Performance Gap

  - **Problem:** After 11 months, Exec ranks poorly for high-intent keywords like "AI roleplay for sales," failing to capture table-stakes traffic.
  - **Goal:** Win on these core keywords, not just niche or long-tail terms.
  - **Competitor Benchmark:** Second Nature successfully ranks for both "AI roleplay" and "sales training software," demonstrating the viability of this dual approach.

### Backlink Workstream Visibility

  - **Concern:** Exec has no visibility into the $48k/year backlink building workstream.
  - **GrowthX Response:** Megan confirmed the work is happening (\~10 links/mo) and will deliver a report.
  - **Analysis (Jo):**
      - Exec's SEMrush Authority Score is 31, a reliable metric.
      - **Target:** Acquire backlinks from sites with Authority Scores ≥31; scores \>50 (e.g., TechCrunch) are most valuable.
      - **Opportunity:** Create data-driven content (e.g., "State of AI Roleplay" report) using Exec's unique insights to earn natural, high-quality backlinks.

### New SEO Strategy: Solutions Pages

  - **Decision:** Shift from generic content to dedicated "money pages" targeting high-intent commercial keywords.
  - **Rationale:** The homepage cannot rank for specific terms like "sales training software" without diluting its broader message.
  - **Page Plan (Sean):**
      - **Role-Specific:** `/sales-training`, `/lnd`, `/customer-support`
      - **Industry-Specific:** `/dealerships` (live), `/insurance`, `/real-estate`
  - **Implementation:**
      - **Internal Linking:** Link new solutions pages from the homepage to distribute "link juice" and improve ranking potential.
      - **Blog Support:** Update existing blog posts with callouts and links to the new solutions pages.
      - **Pillar-Cluster Model:** Adopt a bidirectional linking structure where all pages link to and from the central "money page" to maximize juice distribution.
