### New Tool: Scrunch AI Visibility Dashboard

  - **Purpose:** Track N-able's AI visibility (links, mentions) across LLMs (Claude, Gemini, etc.).
  - **Method:** Pings LLMs with 175+ prompts to simulate user queries.
  - **Key Metrics:**
      - **Citations:** % of responses with a link to N-able's site.
          - **Baseline:** 0% for N-able (expected).
          - **Benchmark:** 2–10% for established clients.
          - **Driver:** A large library of educational content establishes a site as an "answer-rich resource."
      - **Presence:** % of responses mentioning the N-able brand.
  - **Limitation:** Claude's default setting does not cite sources, which skews its citation rate to 0% for all tracked entities.
  - **Action (GrowthX):** Send N-able a PDF report and the full list of tracked prompts.

### AI Citation Strategy

  - **Question (Andrew):** What drives AI citations?
  - **Answer (GrowthX):**
      - **Topic Clusters:** Builds topical authority, which increases LLM referral traffic.
      - **Standard SEO:** Good practices (FAQs, tables) also improve visibility.
      - **Content Length:** Less critical than comprehensiveness. A shorter page can be cited if it fully answers the user's intent.
  - **Future Research:** GrowthX is compiling a report on AI citation factors, due by EOY.
