## Summary

Daniel Li, CEO of Plus AI, met with Tyler Pavlas to explore a potential partnership for SEO and content initiatives. Plus AI is an AI presentation creation tool for professional users (consultants, account executives) approaching $6M ARR, but has experienced declining organic traffic since June Google updates. Tyler and Daniel discussed two key programmatic opportunities: creating 1,000+ landing pages showcasing presentation templates with keywords, prompts, and visual previews, and building a SlideShare-style library of user-generated presentations. Tyler committed to evaluating Plus AI's needs against GrowthX's current capacity and returning within one week with a clear answer on editorial potential and programmatic execution timeline.

## Context

Plus AI is a venture-backed AI tool that helps professionals create native PowerPoint and Google Slides presentations using AI. Daniel Li is the co-founder and CEO, with only four full-time employees but strong profitability approaching $6M ARR. The company targets professional users like consultants and account executives who live inside Microsoft Office, positioning itself differently from competitors like Gamma (broader Canva-like audience) and Beautiful AI. Daniel is also a venture partner at Madrona Ventures, where he met GrowthX CEO Marcel Santilli at the Madrona CEO Summit. During that summit conversation, Marcel showcased GrowthX's programmatic content work for Figma and Lovable, which inspired Daniel to reach out to explore similar opportunities for Plus AI. The call was Tyler's first conversation with Daniel post-Summit.
