## Context

Adventures Group operates three tour destinations across Mexico: Cancun, Puerto Vallarta (Vallarta), and Cabo. Each is a separate business with its own audience and performance metrics. The relationship with GrowthX spans content marketing and SEO strategy for all three properties. Mariana Montezzana leads the GrowthX side, working closely with Isabel Luna (Adventures Group director) and Angelica Briseño (Cancun property manager) to develop content that converts tourists into tour bookings.

The meeting happens at a seasonal inflection point—Cancun is emerging from low season (post-Sargassum algae blooms) while whale watching season approaches. Adventures Group is also launching premium experiences: a new Rhythms of the Night show with a fine-dining gastronomic journey option, creating content opportunities. The team is simultaneously rolling out a new technical categorization system for the blog, adding structural improvements alongside content strategy adjustments.

### Content Strategy & Performance

  - **Problem:** High-traffic articles on general topics (e.g., "Facts About Macaws") attract a non-tourist audience, yielding low conversion.
  - **Solution:** Shift focus to high-intent, tour-specific content to drive direct revenue.
      - **Example:** "Curiosidades Sobre Cameos" (Camels) performs well because it directly links to a core tour.
      - **Example:** "Facts About Macaws" is low-conversion because macaws are a park feature, not the tour's focus.
  - **Successes:**
      - **Seasonal Content:** "Packing List for November in Cancun" already ranks \#10, validating the strategy for time-sensitive topics.
      - **Whale Watching:** Articles were refreshed 1.5 months ago in anticipation of the upcoming season.
