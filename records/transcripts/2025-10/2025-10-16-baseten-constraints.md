## Key Topics

### Content Accuracy and Recency Challenges

  - Baseten requires content less than 3-6 months old due to rapid industry changes
  - Initial AI-generated outline deemed worse than "college student with Google search"
  - Perplexity and initial pipeline incorrectly validated the outline's accuracy
  - Difficulty in producing super-accurate technical content highlighted

### AI Pipeline Improvements

  - Current pipeline not agentic; new agentic pipeline now in place
  - Proposal to add constraints, restriction courses, and expert review to the pipeline
  - Option to pass recency parameters (e.g., only content from September forward)
  - Exploration of AI watch directory and other data sources for up-to-date information

### Content Strategy Adjustments

  - Shift towards high-level, educational, "glossary-style" content
  - Focus on decision processes rather than specific model comparisons
  - Explore creating a separate website or subdomain for less scrutinized content
  - Consider seeding content on platforms like Reddit for AI mentions

### Human Expert Involvement

  - Necessity of involving domain experts for review and validation
  - Challenges in finding affordable, knowledgeable reviewers (e.g., $2000/month part-time)
  - Potential candidate identified who has written for vector database companies

### Client Expectations and Relationship

  - Baseten approved topics but rejected the execution
  - Need to align on content creation process and quality checks
  - Importance of showing the pipeline and human-in-the-loop processes to build confidence
  - Possibility of price renegotiation based on increased complexity
