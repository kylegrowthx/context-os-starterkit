## Summary

Syl from Augment Code met with Liz Kushnereit and Jason Gong from GrowthX in person to align on a major strategic pivot: Augment is shifting from self-serve to enterprise sales-led growth, aiming to double sales-led revenue within three months. This requires a complete content strategy overhaul — moving from general developer content to targeted messaging for engineering leaders at 50+ engineer companies, with success metrics tied to PQAs (Pre-Qualified Accounts), meetings booked, and enriched user trials. GrowthX will shift from broad awareness content to content that directly fuels Augment's outbound motion through webinars, blog posts, social channels (Reddit, YouTube), and event content aligned to Augment's ICP and sales team needs.

---

## Context

Augment Code is an AI code assistant with a differentiated "Context Engine" for superior code retrieval and indexing in large codebases — the primary advantage over competitors like Cursor. They built significant self-serve motion early (losing $4M/week due to cost-per-user dynamics with cheap competitor pricing), but have now decided self-serve is cost-prohibitive. The market shift (OpenAI's codex pricing) and internal board-level goals have forced a complete GTM pivot to enterprise sales-led growth. They've scaled from 6 AEs overfed by inbound to 15 AEs (with 12 more joining in 2 months), and now need an outbound engine to fill the pipeline. Current sales-led revenue is ~$11M ARR; goal is to double it in Q4. GrowthX is Augment's content and demand generation partner, responsible for fueling this new outbound motion through targeted content, webinars, and lifecycle marketing that feeds Augment's sales team with engaged prospects.
