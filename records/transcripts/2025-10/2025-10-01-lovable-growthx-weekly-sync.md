## Context

Lovable is a no-code platform that has engaged GrowthX for a comprehensive content marketing and SEO strategy. The relationship is six months into a strategic engagement focused on driving organic, non-branded traffic to the Lovable website. The team is addressing a critical business challenge: 80-90% of Lovable's users abandon the product after only 5-10 prompts due to either low intent or getting stuck, which creates an opportunity for educational content to improve onboarding and product stickiness. This is the weekly sync to review progress on the content strategy rollout, the template library feature development, and technical infrastructure requirements for scaling content operations.
