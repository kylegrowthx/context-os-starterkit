## Summary

Jason Gong and Neesha Tambe scoped an initial engagement for Neesha to lead AI-led Growth's community initiatives over the next two months with ~25 hours/week availability. They identified two primary focus areas: developing a cohesive content strategy for podcasts and workshops (currently ad hoc), and optimizing the community journey from discovery to member retention through clear onboarding and engagement rituals. ## Context

AI-led Growth is a B2B content marketing agency that generates significant revenue through client engagements and is building CheckThat, an AI visibility index for B2B SaaS. The company has a growing community initiative around AI adoption and content creation methods, currently serving as the primary marketing channel for customer acquisition. Neesha Tambe is a community and content strategy consultant who was brought in to professionalize and scale the community, which currently operates in a largely ad hoc manner across podcasts, workshops, and content without strategic coherence. The team supporting community includes Will Proctor (videographer/production), an editor, Claudia Ring (fractional marketing), Jason Gong (part-time leadership), and a newly hired events lead named Gloria. This call was their initial scoping conversation to define project scope and identify priorities for the first two months.
