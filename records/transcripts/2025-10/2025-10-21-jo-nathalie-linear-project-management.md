## Overview

- **Automate recurring work with Linear's recurring tasks.** For templated services like Strategy Sprints, this saves significant setup time.
- **Use a "pull" system for content production.** The Managing Editor updates Conan OS status to "In Production" on Fridays, giving CMs a live link to their Monday work without needing direct assignments.
- **Standardize processes with internal docs.** Link to a "Standards & Processes" canvas in Slack channels to document client-specific quirks and avoid repetitive explanations.

## Key Topics

### The Problem: Inefficient Project Management

  - The current Linear project for Strategy Sprints is outdated, containing irrelevant tasks and incorrect stages.
  - This leads to excessive back-and-forth communication and missed administrative tasks (e.g., sending sync agendas).

### Solution: Jo's Linear Workflow

  - **Recurring Tasks:** Automate predictable work (e.g., weekly content generation, performance updates) to save setup time.
      - [Use a consistent naming convention (e.g., `[Weekly]`) for clear identification.](https://fathom.video/share/14dZx-dcf6H-fcV-eydfifFuLu9HmLg2?tab=summary&timestamp=54.0)
      - Set all recurring tasks to appear on the same day (e.g., Friday) to create a focused batch-processing window.
  - **Content Production "Pull" System:**
      - **Friday:** The Managing Editor updates Conan OS status to "In Production" and adds the live view link to the corresponding Linear ticket.
      - **Monday:** CMs access the ticket for their work; no direct assignments are needed.
  - **Internal Communication & Documentation:**
      - **Task Comments:** Used for all internal communication, including Google Doc links for content reviews.
      - **Slack:** Used for general discussion, with a "Standards & Processes" canvas in each client's internal channel to document unique guidelines.

### Recommendation: Linear Templates

  - **Rationale:** Templates standardize repetitive communications, saving time and ensuring consistency.
  - **Suggested Templates:**
      - **Customer Communication:** For recurring updates.
      - **Request for Materials:** For client requests.
      - **Handoff:** To guide the transfer of projects to execution pods.
