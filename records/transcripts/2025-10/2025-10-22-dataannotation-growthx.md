## Key Topics

  - **Tools:** Airtable for content strategy (topics, keywords), Notion for docs.
  - **Approval Process:**
      - **Decision:** Prioritize publishing speed over pre-approval.
      - **Rationale:** The cost of a post-publish correction is low; the opportunity cost of delaying SEO indexing is high.
      - **Exception:** Surge AI will scan brand-sensitive articles (e.g., "Is DA a scam?") before publication.

### Technical Implementation

  - **Platform:** Webflow.
  - **URL Conflict:** Webflow's URL structure prevents the desired path: `/blog/categories/blog-detail`.
  - **Solution:** Create a separate `/career` collection for evergreen content (e.g., interview guides). This keeps brand-focused posts on the main `/blog` path.
  - **Publishing Process:** GrowthX will upload articles to Webflow drafts for Surge AI review.
  - **Image Generation:** GrowthX will create meta images; Surge AI will provide brand guidelines.

### Strategy & Ramp-Up

  - **Initial Focus:** Rewrite existing blog posts, as this work is unblocked by new site development.
  - **Ramp-Up Schedule:**
      - **Week 1:** 5 articles.
      - **Week 2:** Evaluate performance → ramp to 20 articles.
      - **Goal:** Reach 30–50 articles/week to cover the large keyword cluster.
  - **Performance Timeline:**
      - **SEO:** A long-term channel. Expect 3–4 weeks for initial traffic and 4–6 months for top rankings.
      - **Forecasting:** GrowthX will provide a forecast mapping the keyword cluster to projected signups.

### Author Authority & Reddit Strategy

  - **Author Authority:** Ghostwrite articles under DA worker names to build author authority, a key SEO signal.
  - **Reddit Strategy:**
      - **Monitoring:** Track relevant subreddits for questions to answer.
      - **Seeding:** Post content to Reddit to aid Google ranking and AI prompt training.
      - **Discussion:** Use vendors to seed discussions and build momentum.
      - **Rationale:** Engage Reddit's active community, a platform where Surge AI has a strong organic presence.
