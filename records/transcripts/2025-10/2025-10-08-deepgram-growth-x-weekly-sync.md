## Summary

GrowthX and Deepgram aligned on content production timelines and SEO performance tracking, with three articles ready to publish Wednesday through Friday and five more in progress. The team shifted the PSEO strategy for language-specific pages from case studies to use cases to address low language coverage, drawing inspiration from Eleven Labs' approach and focusing on reusing existing CMS components. Key concern emerged around demonstrating the business value of content pruning to stakeholders — the team agreed to focus on MQL attribution rather than raw traffic metrics and to prune content in sets of 20-30 pages at a time to minimize disruption.
