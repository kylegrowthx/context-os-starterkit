## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Strategy

  - **Overall Site Performance:**
      - Clicks & impressions down \~2% WoW, but the decline is moderating from \~6% drops earlier in October.
      - This aligns with a market-wide trend of search volatility.
      - Stabilization is expected \~1 month after the Sept 22 Google spam update.
  - **Non-Branded Content:**
      - Grew 2.8% WoW, a counter-trend to the overall site decline.
      - Likely driven by strong performance from recently refreshed content.
  - **LLM Visibility:**
      - Traffic rebounded sharply last week, though top positioning dropped 10 points.
      - **Insight:** Mid-tier rankings (2-4) are valuable for driving clicks, as users compare options. The top result often provides the answer directly, reducing click-throughs.
  - **Content Strategy:**
      - **Freshness:** Refreshing old content is a key signal for improving LLM visibility.
      - **External Linking:** The current policy of minimal external linking hurts SEO authority.
          - **Rationale:** Signals low trust to Google and limits the use of authoritative stats.
          - **Solution:** GrowthX can block specific competitor domains to mitigate risk.

### Technical SEO & Data Integrity

  - **GA4 Data Duplicates:**
      - Duplicate traffic sources in GA4 (e.g., multiple "organic" channels) cause inaccurate reporting in Looker.
      - **Action:** Outreach's technical team must consolidate these sources.
  - **UTM Parameter Crawl Waste:**
      - Google is wasting crawl budget on non-indexed URLs created by UTM parameters.
      - **Impact:** Harms technical SEO health and site trust.
      - **Action:** Outreach's technical team must fix this by de-indexing or disallowing these URLs.

### Team Enablement & Data Clarification

  - **Gong Analysis Discrepancy:**
      - **Issue:** A prompt ("platforms that unify prospect engagement...") showed a "perfect score" in an old LLM report but "zero presence" in the new Gong analysis.

  - **LLM Training Request:**

      - **Action:** Megan will explore hosting a session, potentially timed with the launch of GrowthX's new LLM tool.
