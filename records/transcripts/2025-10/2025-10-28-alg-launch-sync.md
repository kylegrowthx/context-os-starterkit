      - **Rationale:** A simpler site provides a clear hypothesis for conversion testing and unblocks immediate public promotion.
  - **Implementation:**
      - **Source of Truth:** The Notion document will be the definitive source for website copy.

### Community Pricing Model

  - **Decision:** Adopt a new pricing model to improve churn visibility and payment health.
  - **New Structure:** $299 one-time upfront fee + $30/month subscription.

      - The low monthly fee provides early signals of payment issues or churn, preventing surprises at the end of a 12-month contract.
      - A member who cancels must pay the upfront fee again to rejoin.
