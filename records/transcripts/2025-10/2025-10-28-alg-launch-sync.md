## Summary

GrowthX aligned on a "progress over perfection" launch strategy for the ALG community membership, deciding to simplify the initial website to feature only the core membership offering rather than bundling GrowthOS and Masterclass. The team committed to a new pricing model ($299 upfront + $30/month) to provide early churn signals, and locked in the Q4 webinar strategy: "AI Native Marketing in the Wild" co-promoted with Lovable on November 18-19, with a strong hook around AI-driven growth case studies. Immediate next steps include updating the website, implementing the new pricing structure, finalizing the webinar partnership with Lovable (Cecilia), and scheduling a working session between Marcel and Gloria to refine the webinar landing page.

## Context

GrowthX is launching ALG (a collective/community membership platform) as a new revenue stream and strategic product focus. This is an internal product launch involving Marcel Santilli (founder), Jason Gong (product/engineering lead), Gloria Willis (content/marketing strategy), and Edin Abazi (design/build). The meeting was called as a "forcing function" to unblock the website launch this week and ensure alignment on messaging and pricing before the public announcement. Marcel has emphasized that the community is not a one-time event launch but an ongoing daily commitment that will be built through consistent content, promotion, and community nurturing over months—the website launch is just the opening move that allows Marcel to begin publicly talking about the initiative and coordinating initial members to start promoting it.

## Relevance

**To GrowthX Business Development:**
- ALG community launch is the focal point of Q4 2025 strategy, positioning GrowthX as a platform for AI-native growth and marketing. The simplified launch (membership-only, no bundled products) reduces friction and provides a clear first hypothesis for conversion testing.
- Pricing model ($299 upfront + $30/month) generates early churn signals and recurring revenue; the upfront fee requirement for rejoin prevents one-time-pay-and-churn abuse.
- Network leverage: testimonials and co-promotion from advisors and customers (Carol, Mario, Victor from Strapi, G, Gonto, Rachel, Victoria) build social proof at launch.
- Newsletter performance (52% open rate, 10% CTR) positions it as a complementary funnel for non-paying subscribers; promoting the newsletter heavily reduces conversion friction.

**To GrowthX Delivery:**
- The webinar strategy ("AI Native Marketing in the Wild" on Nov 18-19 with Lovable) becomes the lead generation engine for community membership. Marcel will deliver hands-on workshop content with a strong hook (e.g., "24x traffic for Deepgram in 3 months") plus templates and prompts as leave-behinds.
- The backend offer (community membership with step-by-step training) bundles the webinar value with deeper, ongoing access—a 10-hour version vs. self-service templates.

**To CheckThat:**
- CheckThat gets a soft mention at the webinar's end ("if you're thinking about visibility") with credit offers, not as a primary focus. This defers CheckThat promotion and avoids diluting the AI Native Marketing positioning.

## Overview

- **Simplify the Launch:** The initial website launch will focus solely on the core community membership. This "progress over perfection" approach avoids overwhelming visitors with multiple products (GrowthOS, Masterclass) and unblocks immediate promotion.
- **New Pricing Model:** The community will adopt a $299 upfront fee + $30/month subscription. This structure provides early churn signals and ensures members' payment info remains active.
- **Webinar Strategy:** The Q4 webinar will be a hands-on workshop on "AI Native Marketing," co-promoted with Lovable. It will feature a strong hook (e.g., "24x traffic in 3 months") and a community offer, with only a soft mention of the "Check That" tool.
- **Leverage Network:** The launch will feature testimonials from key advisors and customers (e.g., Carol, Mario, Victor from Strapi) to build instant credibility.

## Key Topics

### Website Launch Strategy

  - **Problem:** The current launch plan is too complex, featuring multiple products (GrowthOS, Masterclass) and confusing CTAs.
  - **Decision:** Simplify the launch to focus on the core community membership.
      - **Rationale:** A simpler site provides a clear hypothesis for conversion testing and unblocks immediate public promotion.
  - **Implementation:**
      - **Source of Truth:** The Notion document will be the definitive source for website copy.
      - **Testimonials:** Feature photos and quotes from advisors and customers (e.g., Carol, Mario, Victor from Strapi, G, Gonto) to build instant credibility.
      - **Newsletter CTA:** Promote the newsletter as a clear alternative for non-members, leveraging its high performance (52% open rate, 10% CTR).

### Community Pricing Model

  - **Decision:** Adopt a new pricing model to improve churn visibility and payment health.
  - **New Structure:** $299 one-time upfront fee + $30/month subscription.
  - **Rationale:**
      - The low monthly fee provides early signals of payment issues or churn, preventing surprises at the end of a 12-month contract.
      - A member who cancels must pay the upfront fee again to rejoin.

### Q4 Webinar & Event Strategy

  - **Webinar:**
      - **Theme:** "AI Native Marketing in the Wild."
      - **Date:** Move to Nov 18 or 19 to avoid the Thanksgiving week slowdown.
      - **Partnership:** Co-promote with Lovable to leverage their audience. Marcel will contact Cece (Cecilia) at Lovable.
      - **Content:** A hands-on workshop on practical AI marketing workflows.
      - **Hook:** A compelling, standalone value proposition (e.g., "24x traffic for Deepgram in 3 months") to drive registrations.
      - **Offer:** A backend offer to join the community for the full, step-by-step training.
      - **"Check That" Tool:** Mentioned only as a soft CTA at the end, not as a primary focus.
  - **In-Person Event:**
      - The half-day event on Dec 4 remains a key priority.
