## Context

Turing is a company operating four distinct business units: Turing Intelligence (TI, serving enterprises deploying AI), TI Talent (scaling businesses using staff augmentation), TA2 (serving AI researchers and labs), and a separate Talent Audience (developers and engineers seeking contract work). The call was convened by Weston Hancock (SEO and growth lead) to evaluate GrowthX as a content partner for scaling organic search traffic across these business units. Turing's marketing team is distributed and resource-constrained, with a small internal team producing 1-3 editorial pieces per week across multiple business units using manual AI workflows (custom GPT trained on brand guidelines). The company is looking to accelerate content production, improve AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) strategy to capture AI search traffic, and repurpose content across multiple formats (social, newsletters, gated assets). Tyler Pavlas, GrowthX's VP, presented the company's platform and content creation process—which combines agentic workflows for brief generation, deep research, drafting, and fact-checking with human expert oversight.

## Key Topics

### Turing's Content Challenges & Needs

  - **Current AI Use:** Manual, task-specific GPT use for revisions and derivative pages; no automated workflows.
  - **Strategic Needs:**
      - Competitive analysis & foundational research for content briefs.
      - AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) strategy to capture AI search traffic.
  - **Repurposing & Distribution:** High interest in repurposing long-form content (e.g., research reports) into social posts, newsletters, and FAQs.
  - **Design Support:** Need for design help (e.g., infographics) to offload work from a small internal team.
  - **Gated Content:** Interest in creating gated assets for demand generation, separate from organic content.

### GrowthxAI's Solution & Process

  - **Core Offering:** An end-to-end partner providing an internal platform and expert team for scalable, quality content.
  - **Process Overview:**
      - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, est. $15k)**
          - Defines ICP, brand voice, and custom AI workflows.
          - Ingests Turing's resources to build a custom knowledge base.
          - Publishes 5-10 articles to establish the quality bar.
          - Turing can opt out after this phase.
      - **Phase 2: Growth Execution**
          - Scales to daily publishing based on the roadmap from Phase 1.
          - Additional workstreams (e.g., newsletters) can be added for $9-15k each.
  - **Content Creation Workflow:**
      - **Brief Generation:** Agentic workflow analyzes SERP, competitors, and search intent.
      - **Deep Research:** Uses tools like EXA/TAP and a custom researcher; loops until a quality threshold is met.
      - **Drafting:** Generates content, adds cross-links, and validates against guidelines.
      - **Fact-Checking:** Verifies every passage against sources; suggests rewrites if confidence is low.
      - **Human Oversight:** Experts are in the loop for edits and to guide the AI with instructions.

### Turing's Audience & Prioritization

  - **Four Distinct Audiences:**
    1.  **Turing Intelligence (TI):** Enterprises using deployed AI.
    2.  **TI Talent:** Scaling businesses using staff augmentation.
    3.  **TA2:** AI researchers and labs.
    4.  **Talent Audience:** Developers seeking work.
  - **Initial Priorities:**
      - **TI & TI Talent:** Highest priority due to clear organic growth paths and budget. The core team for this is Tara and Nathan.
      - **TA2:** Lower priority; the audience is highly technical and small.
      - **Talent Audience:** High need (neglected content since 2022) but low budget appetite for organic growth ROI.
