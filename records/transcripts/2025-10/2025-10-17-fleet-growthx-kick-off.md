## Key Topics

### Current Market Positioning

  - Fleet positioned as modern, enterprise-ready alternative to legacy MDMs like Jamf
  - Key differentiators: open-source, multi-platform, flexible deployment (cloud/on-prem), GitOps-friendly
  - Target replacing $40M of Jamf's European revenue, leveraging aversion to Microsoft

### Content Strategy and SEO

  - Previous minimal SEO investment; now aiming for high-volume content creation
  - Will start with 1 calibration article, ramp up to 5/week by weeks 6-8
  - Articles may live in a separate, potentially hidden "guides" section to avoid disrupting main blog
  - Focus on topics like Jamf migration, on-premise deployment, compliance (PCI, FedRAMP)

### Customer Objections and Concerns

  - Perception of Fleet as not mature enough for enterprise MDM
  - Budget constraints pushing towards "free" solutions like Microsoft Intune
  - Need for on-premise deployment options (e.g., government, finance, retail sectors)

### Internal Review Process

  - Irena: Content calendar management, liaison with Mike T.
  - Brock: Technical accuracy review
  - Mike T: Final approval, tone/voice consistency (1 business day turnaround)
  - Sam: CMS access, Google Analytics setup
