## Context

John Knightly (who worked with Marcel in prior roles) rejoined the organization in January 2025 as a growth leader, bringing James Levin as a growth marketing consultant-turned-full-time leader. Chanel Chambers joined as Product Marketing lead around June 2025. The company operates across six solution areas (including data security, identity security, etc.) but had been performing poorly in organic search (e.g., ranking page 6 for DSPM despite strong market position). The previous marketing team chased vanity metrics (ranking for unrelated keywords like PowerShell scripts) without driving conversions. The new leadership's mandate is to focus ruthlessly on conversion-oriented content and organic pipeline generation. John had attended a GrowthX workshop months earlier and remembered the impact of AI visibility for high-intent content; this reconnect call was to explore whether GrowthX could help Netwrix scale quality content across their solution categories while their new CMS launches.
