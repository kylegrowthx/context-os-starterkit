## Key Topics

### Strella's Need for a Growth Partner

  - **Context:** Strella, a B2B SaaS for B2C customer insights, recently closed its Series A.
  - **Goal:** Use Series A funds to establish predictable, scalable growth channels beyond current founder-led sales and word-of-mouth.
  - **Requirement:** A partner to build the organic content channel from scratch ("zero to ten") because internal bandwidth is insufficient for a platform-only approach.
  - **ICP:** B2C companies (CPG, tech) with 150–2,000 employees in the US.
  - **Target Roles:** Product, UX Research, Market Research, Consumer Insights, and Design teams.
  - **Competitors:**
      - **Direct:** Listen Labs, Outset (Series A).
      - **Keyword Targets:** Maze, UserTesting, Dovetail (large authority scores).

### GrowthX's Partnership Model

  - **Pitch:** An end-to-end partner providing strategy, content creation, publishing, and AI visibility monitoring.
  - **Process:**
      - **Phase 1: 8-Week Strategy Sprint ($30k)**
          - **Goal:** Create foundational artifacts (personas, brand guides) to feed AI workflows.
          - **Deliverables:** \~10-20 published articles and a 12-month execution roadmap.
          - **Off-Ramp:** Strella can opt out at the end of the sprint, keeping all deliverables.
      - **Phase 2: 12-Month Growth Execution ($15k/mo)**
          - **Goal:** Daily editorial content publishing.
          - **Includes:** AI visibility monitoring (e.g., Profound, Scrunch), which Tyler estimated at \~$3k/mo if purchased separately.

### Scope & Pricing Clarification

  - **The $15k/mo plan is for editorial content only.**
  - **Advanced Workstreams:** Programmatic SEO, custom tools (e.g., calculators), or new site sections require an additional $9k–$15k/mo per workstream.
  - **Exception:** Programmatic work can be included in the $15k/mo plan if Strella provides the engineering/design support to build the initial CMS structure.

### Strella's Decision Factors

  - **Primary Blocker: Timeline**
      - **GrowthX Availability:** Earliest kickoff is Q1 2025.
      - **Strella's Urgency:** Feels "behind" and needs to launch the channel sooner.
      - **Impact:** This timeline makes GrowthX a less viable option than a competitor offering an earlier start.
  - **Secondary Factor: Cost**
      - The $30k sprint cost and $15k/mo execution fee are key figures for David to discuss internally with Priya.
