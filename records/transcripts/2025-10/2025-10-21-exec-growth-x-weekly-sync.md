## Key Topics

### Bottom-Funnel Competitor Content

  - **Problem:** The initial plan for 120+ bottom-funnel articles mixed AI roleplay platforms (e.g., Hyperbound) with human coaching marketplaces (e.g., Torch).
  - **Rationale:** This mix is confusing for users and misrepresents market segments. Exec is a unique competitor in both spaces.
  - **Solution:** Segment the content strategy into two distinct phases:
      - **Phase 1 (Priority): AI Roleplay Platforms**
          - Compare Exec vs. priority platforms.
          - Compare priority platforms against each other.
      - **Phase 2 (Follow-up): Human Coaching Marketplaces**
          - Compare platforms like BetterUp, Torch, CoachHub, and GrowthSpace against each other.
  - **Implementation:** GrowthX will send a filtered list of topics for Exec approval before production begins.

### Auto Dealership Landing Page (LP)

  - **New Initiative:** Exec is launching a new LP for auto dealerships, featuring 40 pre-built scenarios.
  - **Purpose:** A mid-funnel sales tool for outreach, not an SEO play.
  - **Potential Conflict:** The new LP could cannibalize traffic from the existing, high-ranking blog post, "AI role plays for car dealerships."
  - **Action Plan:**
    1.  **Refresh:** Update the blog post to align messaging with the new LP.
    2.  **CTA:** Update the blog post's CTA to link directly to the new LP.
    3.  **Monitor:** Track performance of both pages post-launch.
    4.  **Consider Merge:** If the LP outperforms the blog post for leads, merge the blog post into the LP via a 301 redirect.

### Content Operations & SEO

  - **Publishing:** 18 articles from the content audit are now live; 10 more are in progress.
  - **Redirects:** The 12 redirects identified in the audit are pending completion by Exec to improve site structure.
  - **Thought Leadership:** New articles will be integrated into existing user-facing categories, not a new "Thought Leadership" category.
      - **Rationale:** Existing categories are more useful for user navigation.
      - **Distribution:** Repurpose for other channels (newsletter, LinkedIn) as they are not SEO-focused.
