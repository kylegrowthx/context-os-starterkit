## Key Topics

### Boulevard's Objectives & Concerns

  - **Primary Goal:** Lift organic traffic and generate leads, not just traffic, with a scalable, data-driven strategy.
  - **Content Strategy:** A clear approach for managing 700+ existing articles, including refreshing and potentially removing underperforming content.
  - **Technical SEO:** Clarification on GrowthX's role in implementing technical optimizations vs. providing recommendations.
  - **AI Search Traffic:** A key concern is how to capture traffic and leads from AI overviews, given data suggesting low click-through rates to source websites.

### GrowthX's Solution & Approach

  - **Core Service:** An end-to-end partner for SEO and AEO (AI Engine Optimization), creating a content engine to become the "best answer" across search platforms.
  - **Methodology:** Combines traditional keyword research with an audience-first AEO approach to build a large, high-quality content surface area.
  - **Proprietary Platform:** An internal tool orchestrates AI workflows (using models from OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) for deep research, outlining, and drafting.
  - **Human Oversight:** A dedicated team (Managing Director, Managing Editor, Content Manager) refines all AI output, ensuring brand voice, accuracy, and strategic alignment.

### Engagement Model & Logistics

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks, opt-out)**
      - **Goal:** De-risk the partnership and demonstrate quality.
      - **Deliverables:** Technical audit, content audit, \~10 articles.
  - **Phase 2: Growth Execution (12 months)**
      - **Cadence:** Daily publishing (5+ articles/week), a mix of net-new and refreshes.
      - **Workstreams:**
          - **Editorial:** Standard long-form content.
          - **Programmatic:** Swappable alternative for data-driven page generation (e.g., location-based content).
      - **Pricing:** Starts at $15k/month for one workstream; custom workstreams (e.g., tools) are additional ($9k–$15k).

### Scope & Boundaries

  - **Technical SEO:** GrowthX provides recommendations; implementation is TBD.
  - **Off-Page SEO:** Not a standard offering. Backlinking may be explored after 3–6 months but is not committed upfront.
  - **Content Repurposing:** GrowthX's content is available for Boulevard's team to repurpose; they do not manage social media or PR execution.
  - **Customer Interviews:** Not included; Boulevard's team must provide transcripts for case studies.
  - **Image Workflow:** GrowthX generates AI-based cover images. Integration with Boulevard's DAM is not standard but can be explored.
