## Context

Rachael Tiow is a strategist at Okta working on sales enablement. GrowthX has been engaged to develop a suite of sales enablement artifacts—account briefs, prospecting resources, and instructional materials—that will help Okta's SDRs understand how Okta's identity solutions connect to business outcomes in specific industries. The project has revealed a knowledge gap: many SDRs don't grasp the relationship between identity, personalization, modernization, and loyalty programs, leading to weak prospecting efforts (one AE asked why Lululemon would buy Okta). GrowthX is building structured artifacts informed by persona docs, win notes, case studies, and industry-specific insights to address this gap. The team is using tools like Gem (for individual SDR implementations) and Notebook LM as a source of truth for the underlying context.
