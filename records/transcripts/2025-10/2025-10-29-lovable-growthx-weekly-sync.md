## Key Topics

### User Activation & Intent Data

  - **Problem:** The sign-up-to-activation rate is declining.
  - **Root Cause:** A lack of user intent data prevents effective onboarding and template development.
  - **Solution:** Gather intent data to understand *who* users are and *what* they are trying to build.
  - **Data Gathering Methods:**
      - **Surveys:** Ask users about their goals during onboarding.
      - **Session Analysis:** Analyze user sessions to identify common workflows and pain points.
      - **Prompt Analysis:** Use AI to summarize user prompts and infer intent.
  - **Onboarding Experiment:** Test asking intent questions during the first co-generation step to avoid blocking sign-up.

### Content & Template Strategy

  - **Goal:** Increase content volume while maintaining quality.
  - **New Hire Project:** Joanna will lead a project to gather use case data, focusing on:
      - **B2B:** Sales tools (e.g., pipeline command centers, account research apps).
      - **Consumer:** Common website types (e.g., portfolios, blogs).
      - **Engineers:** Investigate this large user segment's needs.
  - **Template Library:**
      - `lovable.dev/templates` is live with 7 templates.
      - **MVP:** No categories; all templates are general websites.
      - **Next Batch:** 5 portfolio websites.
      - **Future:** Add categories and filters as the library grows.
  - **Editorial Content:**
      - 8 articles published; 5 more by EOD.
      - **Early Signal:** Competitive comparison articles are performing well → will be prioritized.

### Technical Audit & Security Risk

  - A technical audit identified a critical security risk.
  - **Finding:** 213 URLs are flagged by Google as phishing or deceptive.
  - **Risk:** Could result in a sitewide penalty from Google.
  - **Action:** Ceci will send the list of URLs to Igor (security) for immediate takedown.
