## Key Topics

### User Activation & Intent Data

  - **Problem:** The sign-up-to-activation rate is declining. Activation is defined as users staying active for a certain number of days and performing a certain number of actions (not just shipping a project).
  - **Root Cause:** A lack of user intent data prevents effective onboarding and template development. People sign up but don't know what they want to build.
  - **Solution:** Gather intent data to understand *who* users are and *what* they are trying to build, then use that to proactively suggest templates or guide them directly.
  - **Data Gathering Methods:**
      - **Surveys:** Ask users about their goals during onboarding or first co-generation.
      - **Session Analysis:** Analyze user sessions to identify common workflows and pain points.
      - **Prompt Analysis:** Use AI to summarize user prompts and infer what they're trying to build, even if they don't explicitly state it.
  - **Onboarding Experiments:** Test asking intent questions during the first co-generation step (which typically takes 1 minute) to gather data without blocking sign-up. Some debate exists about whether to ask during signup vs. during first co-gen, but team will experiment with both approaches.

### Content & Template Strategy

  - **Goal:** Increase content volume while maintaining quality. Current workflow can produce 5 templates per week; GrowthX is working to ramp this number up.
  - **Joanna's Discovery Project:** New hire Joanna (starting Monday, based in Stockholm) will lead JTBD discovery across three segments:
      - **B2B Sales:** Pipeline command centers, account research tools, deal forecasting apps, call companion apps, Salesforce extenders with inline compose.
      - **B2B Marketing:** Campaign generators, web personalization tools, marketing brief-to-asset generators.
      - **Consumer & Engineers:** Understanding what these large user segments are building and what templates would accelerate their workflows. Engineers are Lovable's second-largest user segment; the team is unclear what they need.
  - **Template Library:**
      - `lovable.dev/templates` is live with 7 templates; currently hidden.
      - **MVP:** No categories; all templates are general websites.
      - **Next Batch:** 5 portfolio websites (next week).
      - **Growth Path:** Add categories and filters as library grows. Potential for 50+ templates across multiple categories by ramping workflows.
      - **Discovery:** Will track who searches for templates and what's working to double down.
  - **Editorial Content:**
      - 8 articles published; 5 more shipping by end of day.
      - **Early Signal:** Competitive comparison articles are showing outsized clicks and impressions in GSC, already getting cited. Will be prioritized going forward.
      - **Unblocked:** Furkan approving PRs, so no blocker on editorial side.

### Technical Audit & Security Risk

  - A technical audit identified a critical security risk.
  - **Finding:** 213 URLs are flagged by Google as phishing or deceptive.
  - **Risk:** Could result in a sitewide penalty from Google.
  - **Action:** Ceci will send the list of URLs to Igor (security) for immediate takedown.
