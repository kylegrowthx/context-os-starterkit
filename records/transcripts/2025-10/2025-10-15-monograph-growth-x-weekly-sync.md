## Summary

GrowthX and Monograph pivoted from the Monograph Method research to support Monograph's Q1 product launch, which will introduce three-tier pricing, a new CRM product (internally called "Leads"), and heavier emphasis on AI capabilities. The content strategy will shift to two phases: first establishing GrowthX authority in CRM/AI topics through general content, then supporting the product launch with feature-specific content 2-4 weeks before release.
