The company faces a significant challenge with value selling: many potential customers don't yet know to search for or ask about advanced features like vertical web sets or AI-powered personalized agents. ### Initial Content Strategy

  - GrowthX delivered an Airtable "Content OS" with 53 blog ideas from keyword research.
  - **Research Sources:**
      - **SEMrush:** Keyword gap analysis vs. competitors.
      - **Manual Research:** Based on audience pain points.
  - **Content OS Structure:**
      - `Assignments`: Blog ideas with keyword data (volume, difficulty, meta info).
      - `Topic Clusters`: High-level descriptions of content themes.
      - `URLs`: Reference list of competitor and internal URLs.
      - `Target Keywords`: The specific keywords each new article will target.

### Strategy Pivot: Addressing Unarticulated Needs

  - **Problem:** You.com's "value selling" model creates a challenge for keyword research. Customers often don't know to search for solutions like "vertical web set" or "AI-powered personalized cruise app."
  - **Solution:** The content strategy will pivot to address this by targeting two distinct audiences:
      - **Developers (API):** Content will answer direct, known-painpoint queries (e.g., "search API").
      - **Enterprise (End-to-End):** Content will use value-selling prompts to surface unarticulated needs.
  - **Execution:** Erik will tag topics in the Content OS by audience (Developer vs. Enterprise) to enable audience-specific reporting in Scrunch.com.

      - You.com approves 5–10 topics by EOW.

  - **Phase 2 (Ramp-up):**
