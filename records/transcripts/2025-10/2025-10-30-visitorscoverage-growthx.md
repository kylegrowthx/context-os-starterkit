## Context

The company is fully digital and depends entirely on organic traffic for customer acquisition, making SEO mission-critical to the business. ## Key Topics

### VisitorsCoverage's Challenge: Adapting to AI Search

  - VC is a 20-year-old travel insurance marketplace (VisitorsCoverage.com) with a SaaS platform (Trappy.io).
  - **Primary Goal:** Develop an AEO strategy to capture long-tail queries in AI answers.
  - **Key Constraint:** Avoid harming established SEO performance, which is critical for the business.
  - **Current Tools:** Using SEMrush, Ahrefs, and other tools, but feels "tired of trying" to find a clear path forward.

### GrowthX's Solution: AI-Powered Content Engine

  - GrowthX is an end-to-end partner that scales quality content using AI workflows and expert oversight.
  - **Founder:** Marcel, a former CMO (HashiCorp, Scale), built the platform to automate his organic growth process.
  - **Expertise:** The Chief Content Officer is the former editor-in-chief of TechCrunch.
  - **Content Scope:**
      - **Editorial:** Net new content and refreshes (target: 5 articles/week).
      - **Programmatic SEO:** Scalable pages (e.g., city guides, comparison pages, glossaries).
      - **Interactive Tools:** Case-by-case development (e.g., per diem calculators).

### Engagement Model & Team Structure

  - **Phase 1: Strategy Sprint (8 weeks)**
      - **Goal:** Build a custom knowledge base via expert interviews to train AI workflows.
      - **Deliverable:** A content roadmap and initial articles for quality validation.
      - **Opt-Out:** VC can exit before Phase 2.
  - **Phase 2: Cadence of Growth (12 months)**
      - **Commitment:** 12-month term, required to justify the significant resource investment in Phase 1.
      - **Team:** A dedicated 3-person team: Managing Director (strategy), Managing Editor (deliverables), and Content Manager (execution).
      - **Client Review:** VC's only task is providing timely feedback to prevent bottlenecks.
  - **Performance:** \~95% of clients convert from Phase 1 to Phase 2.
