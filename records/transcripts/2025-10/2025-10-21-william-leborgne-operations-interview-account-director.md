## Key Topics

### Challenge: Stalled Organic Engine at AirDNA

  - **Problem:** AirDNA's organic engine was stalled and over-reliant on branded traffic (80:20 branded:non-branded mix).
  - **Mandate:** "Right the ship" and grow non-paid channels to lower CAC.

### Solution: AI-Powered Organic Growth

  - **Blog Performance:**
      - Grew organic visits from 6k to 32k/mo.
      - Shifted traffic mix to a healthier 55:45 branded:non-branded.
      - Drives 11% of core business registrations and subscriptions.
  - **High-Impact Projects:**
      - **Programmatic Pages (70k):** City-specific data pages drive \~25% of organic registrations.
      - **Airbnb Calculator:** A high-utility tool for estimating rental income.
  - **AI-Driven Efficiency:**
      - Used AirOps to cut content budget by 75% while increasing production.
      - Built 20+ workflows, including a unique localization process:
        1.  **Cultural/Legal Check:** Verifies content suitability for the target market.
        2.  **Local Optimization:** Rewrites the article for local keywords.
        3.  **Data Integration:** Merges key data points from the original English article.
      - This workflow enables one French and one Spanish reviewer to manage 22 and 16 articles/mo, respectively.

### New Challenge: Winning in AI Search

  - **Problem:** AI Overviews (AIOs) are causing click-through rates (CTRs) to plummet (e.g., from 2.4% to 0.8%).
  - **Response:**
      - Developed a 2026 playbook for winning in AI search.
      - Experimenting with new content formats (e.g., micro-content, multi-answer articles) to increase LLM citations.
      - Repurposing content across channels (e.g., 109 YouTube videos from top blogs) to diversify traffic sources.

### Proposed Solution: Strategic Framework for GrowthX

  - **Problem:** GrowthX needs a more repeatable framework to communicate strategy and value, moving beyond a "busy shop" perception.
  - **Proposed Framework:**
      - **"2 Bets" (80% Capacity):** High-conviction, strategic initiatives.
      - **"20% Swings" (20% Capacity):** Experiments that can become future "Bets."
  - **Rationale:** This structure provides a clear narrative for clients, connecting activities to objectives and demonstrating proactive value.
