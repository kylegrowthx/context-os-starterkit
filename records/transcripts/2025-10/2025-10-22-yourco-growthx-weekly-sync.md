  - **Goal:** Merge articles into pillar content to improve SEO authority and reduce internal competition.
  - **Survey Results:** Merge 4 articles → 1 pillar: "How to take action on employee survey results."
      - **Rationale:** Consolidates related content; the "Examples of employee survey feedback" article will remain separate due to its distinct keyword focus.
  - **Survey Distribution:** Merge 5 articles → 1 pillar: "How can you distribute a questionnaire?"
      - **Rationale:** Broaden scope beyond just distribution to cover best practices and response rates.
  - **Employee Communication:** Merge 3 articles → 1 pillar.
      - **Rationale:** Differentiate from the Resources page by adding technical depth (e.g., Yourco's multi-HRIS integration).
  - **Enrichment Prioritization:**
    1.  Articles with the most clicks.
    2.  Articles with the oldest "last updated" date.
  - **Dated Content:** Articles with specific years (e.g., "Overtime Rules 2025") will not be updated unless laws change.
      - **Rationale:** Users prefer official sources for legal info, and these articles often become zero-click searches.
      - **Strategy:** Focus on practical tips (e.g., "How to communicate new overtime rules") to provide unique value.

### Frontline Intelligence Content Strategy

  - **Angle:** Focus on HR pain points and insights, not generic tech terms.
  - **Keywords:** Target HR-specific terms like "employee sentiment," "turnover rate," and "predictive HR analytics."
  - **Content Focus:**
      - How to identify and analyze key HR metrics.
      - How to download and analyze survey reports.
  - **Enrichment Mandate:** Integrate Frontline Intelligence insights into all relevant article updates.
