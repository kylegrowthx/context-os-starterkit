## Context

Prophecy is a B2B AI data preparation and analysis platform that recently underwent significant organizational change. The marketing team was restructured: Ashley and Lance departed, leaving Wilton Kuffel to lead a lean but strong marketing team. Simultaneously, the product focus shifted downstream—from data engineers to data analysts and analytics leaders—with the Prophecy Express offering (fixed-cost, objective-based package for teams of 20) becoming a key go-to-market vehicle. The new website relaunches on Friday, and Raj (CEO, based in India for the next two months) is the acting head of product marketing, holding a clear vision for the company's direction. Prophecy has been working with GrowthX on SEO and content for some time but hasn't seen the growth results that should justify continued partnership. The relationship is at an inflection point: Prophecy needs to demonstrate AEO and GEO wins quickly to justify continued spend, and Wilton and Cody recognize that previous content efforts were product-marketing focused rather than signals-driven growth marketing.
