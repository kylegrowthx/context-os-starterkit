## Context

Biologica is a health/wellness product company (appears to be supplements or similar health products) preparing to launch their website by end of November 2025. GrowthX was engaged to create high-quality, medically-reviewed thought leadership content to establish authority at launch. The partnership began with the expectation of initial back-and-forth during setup, but Biologica found the editing burden significantly heavier than anticipated—with Brian and his colleague Liz (a former lawyer and strong writer) spending 30-60 minutes per article reviewing content. Quality initially improved, dipped significantly in August, and has shown recent signs of recovery. Brian raised concerns that despite extensive feedback from Biologica and GrowthX's human review process, feedback often appears unaddressed in subsequent batches, making the $12k/month engagement feel costly relative to output quality.
