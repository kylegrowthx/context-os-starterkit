## Key Topics

### Conversion.ai's Challenge

  - **Problem:** Over-reliance on cold outbound leads, which are harder to close than inbound.
  - **Goal:** Build an inbound channel via content marketing, but lacks the internal bandwidth.
  - **Requirement:** A "white-glove" partner to act as an interim growth/content lead, not a low-cost point solution.
  - **Content Priorities:**
      - **Editorial:** High-quality blog content for SEO and topical authority.
      - **Programmatic:** Landing pages, lead magnets, and sales collateral.
      - **Technical SEO:** Interlinking, meta-data, and backlink acquisition.

### GrowthX's Proposed Solution

  - **Service Model:** A managed editorial service, starting with a 3–6 month engagement before expanding to programmatic work.
  - **Process:** End-to-end service (strategy → publishing) powered by an internal platform and human experts (MD, Editor, Manager).
  - **Pricing:**
      - **Strategy Sprint:** $30k for 8 weeks.
          - **Deliverables:** SEO analysis, content roadmap, and ≥10 published articles.
          - **Rationale:** Secures commitment for the upfront strategic investment.
      - **Ongoing:** $15k/month for daily publishing.
  - **Workflow:**
      - **Inputs:** Client resources (decks, calls) and deep research (Exa, Tavli) feed AI-powered workflows.
      - **Outputs:** Includes internal/external linking, images, and direct CMS publishing (Webflow).

### The Scheduling Conflict

  - **GrowthX's Availability:** Booked through EOY 2025; first kickoff is late Jan 2026.
  - **Conversion.ai's Urgency:** The 2–3 month delay conflicts with leadership pressure to accelerate growth.
