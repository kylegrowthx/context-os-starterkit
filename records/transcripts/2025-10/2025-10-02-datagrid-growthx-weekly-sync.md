## Context

The company launched in October 2025 and has grown rapidly, with CEO Thiago da Costa and contract lead Kaitlin Quimby (from Toric) partnering with GrowthX's content team (led by Vivek Shankar and Mara Leighton) on a content marketing engagement. The relationship is strategic for both sides—Datagrid wants to drive qualified traffic to support enterprise sales, while GrowthX is using the engagement to develop depth in enterprise software and government/compliance-heavy use cases.
