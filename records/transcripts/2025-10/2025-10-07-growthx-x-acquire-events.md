## Summary

GrowthX and event planning firm Acquire discussed high-end experiential marketing for two distinct use cases. First, they outlined Surge's needs for exclusive, prestigious events ($55-75k for 20 people) designed to position the company's founder among frontier AI leaders like Meta and Anthropic CTOs. Then they shifted to GrowthX's own CMO dinners ($10-15k range, 10-15 attendees) for deal closing and client retention, with interest in testing virtual event experiences to expand beyond San Francisco. Acquire committed to creating detailed proposals for both the Surge flagship experience and the GrowthX CMO options (in-person and virtual).

---

## Context

GrowthX is exploring a strategic partnership with Acquire Wine, a full-service event planning firm with deep expertise in Michelin-starred culinary experiences, high-profile venues (private residences, exclusive clubs), and go-to-market support. This meeting brought together GrowthX's leadership (Jason Gong, head of growth; Gloria Willis, VP of operations; and Liz Kushnereit, account lead for GrowthX's largest client) with Acquire's principals (Haley Moore and Gina Holly) to establish a framework for two distinct event programs.

The backdrop is GrowthX's engagement with Surge, a data annotation and reinforcement learning company that scaled to unicorn status ($1B+ valuation in five years) and is now positioning itself as a preferred vendor to frontier AI labs. Surge's business depends on relationships with CTOs and executives at Meta, Anthropic, and similar companies—relationships that need to be cultivated at the executive level. GrowthX also wants to formalize its own business development muscle through recurring CMO dinners that drive retention and deal velocity. Both use cases require operationalizing experiences that feel exclusive and memorable.

## Key Topics

### Surge Event Requirements

  - Exclusive, private venues (residences, clubs) with modern, buzzworthy appeal
  - High-profile guests from frontier AI labs (e.g., CTO of Meta)
  - Elevated experiences with top-tier food, wine, and service
  - Potential thought leader or speaker as draw
  - Goal: Establish credibility and position Surge founder among top AI company leaders
  - Budget: Flexible, prioritizing best possible experience (\~$55-75k for 20 people)

### GrowthX CMO Dinners

  - More modest than Surge events, but still unique and high-quality
  - Typically 10-15 guests, mix of prospects and current clients
  - Previous budgets: $200-600 per person, open to higher for special occasions
  - Purpose: Close deals and strengthen client relationships
  - Interest in exploring virtual options for geographic expansion

### Event Planning Services

  - Acquire offers full-service planning: venue sourcing, chef selection, decor, gifts
  - Go-to-market support available to help fill events with target attendees
  - Virtual event capabilities with curated food/drink experiences
  - Pricing varies widely based on location, guest count, and level of service
