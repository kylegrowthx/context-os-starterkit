## Key Topics

### Project Status & Blockers

  - The project was paused; the next step is a wireframe for a "well-converting blog."
  - The wireframe is for layout and debate, not final design.
  - **Blocker:** The project is stalled until the wireframe is approved, which will then unblock the kickoff call with Udemy's dev and design teams.

### Blog Structure & Wireframe Requirements

  - The wireframe must include a homepage and category pages.
  - **Category Page Requirements:**
      - Dedicated pages with a paragraph-long content chunk.
      - URLs should *not* contain the category (e.g., `/blog/article-title`).
  - **Inspiration:** The Coursera blog was cited as a good example.

### Content Strategy & Focus

  - **Priority:** Deepen AI keyword research for topics like "upskilling" and "AI literacy."
  - **Constraint:** New research must be structured to avoid content overlap with existing categories.
  - **Future Expansion:** GrowthX is open to researching non-AI topics like "Technical Upskilling" and "Leadership & Management" after the initial AI focus.

### SEO Strategy: Google vs. LLMs

  - **Primary Focus → Google SEO:**
      - **Rationale:** Google drives \~95% of traffic vs. ChatGPT's \<250k monthly visits.
      - **Goal:** Capture AI Overviews on Google.
  - **Secondary Focus → LLM Citations:**
      - **Tracking Gap:** No UB-specific prompts are tracked in BrightEdge; the current list is for the consumer business.
      - **Action:** Create a list of UB-specific prompts for BrightEdge tracking.
      - **Exploration:** A supplemental tool is being considered for persona-based LLM tracking (a feature BrightEdge lacks).
  - **Process:** Write solid content for users first; LLM citations are expected to follow.

### Cross-Promotion Mandate

  - **Source:** A directive from CMO Genefa.
  - **Goal:** Drive lead-gen by cross-promoting between the consumer and UB blogs.
  - **Rationale:** Individual learners are often decision-makers for their teams.
  - **Requirement:** The new blog structure must include a strategic mechanism for cross-promotion, not just simple banners.

### Operational Updates

  - **Page Speed:** Dev Kevin is in a 2-week sprint to fix page speed on the WordPress site, starting with high-priority pages.
  - **Authorship Process:**
      - **Pillar Content:** Published under a senior leader's name (e.g., Steph) and always reviewed.
      - **Non-Pillar Content:** Published under copywriter Jay's name.
          - All pieces will be reviewed by a small internal team initially.
          - The process will be delegated to Jay once it's honed.
  - **Content Calendar:** GrowthX is building a shared calendar in Airtable to align with Jay's work and provide visibility.
