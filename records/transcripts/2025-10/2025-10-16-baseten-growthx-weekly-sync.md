### Content Creation Process Improvements

  - GrowthX acknowledged previous inaccuracies in outlines and implemented new measures:

  - GrowthX to provide:
      - Detailed approval process and inputs for future outlines

      - Exploration of off-site publishing options to build BaseTen's authority

### Analytics Dashboard Review

  - New Looker dashboard integrates Google Search Console and Google Analytics 4 data
  - Key metrics include:
      - Overall sessions vs. engaged sessions (engagement rate target: \>50% for long-form content)
      - Landing page performance by traffic source
      - Query ranking distribution (branded vs. non-branded)
      - Conversion events (specific definitions to be clarified with GA4 setup team)

  - BaseTen team interested in incorporating PostHog data and understanding search queries leading to LLM referrals

### Content Strategy Considerations

  - BaseTen emphasized the need for very recent information (3-6 months max) due to rapid industry changes
  - Discussion on publishing content outside BaseTen's domain to increase authority and influence LLMs
  - GrowthX exploring experimental options for seeding content in authoritative sources
