### GrowthX's Platform & Service Model

  - **Origin:** Marcel Santilli's prior success at Deepgram (24x traffic, 4x revenue) by scaling content production with early AI workflows.
  - **Core Insight:** Deliver outcomes as a service, not just tools. This abstracts away AI complexity (prompt engineering, tool integration) for customers.
  - **Platform Architecture:**
      - **Agentic Workflows:** Codified processes (research, writing, fact-checking) run by AI agents.
      - **Context Engineering:** "Artifacts" (documents on company, products, personas) ground the AI in specific business knowledge, preventing generic outputs.
      - **Knowledge Graph:** Stores all research and content as semantically linked nodes for accurate, efficient retrieval.
      - **Fact-Checking Agent:** Verifies claims passage-by-passage, flagging or rewriting statements with low confidence (e.g., changing "14.3% gains" to "suggests improvement" if the specific figure isn't universally established).
  - **Business Status:** \~60 customers, \>$13M revenue, cashflow positive.

### Proposed ServiceNow Pilot

  - **Problem:** ServiceNow's premium thought leadership is underutilized, lacking integration into practical content that captures AI search citations.
  - **Solution:** A pilot project to enrich existing assets with practical, search-optimized content.
      - **Process:** Compile existing thought leadership into a GrowthX knowledge base → Use it to generate new guides and articles that capture AI citations for high-intent queries.
  - **Pilot Focus:** A specific "buying center" (e.g., CISOs) to create a content suite guiding them from awareness to purchase.
  - **Strategic Alignment:** Supports ServiceNow's shift from selling individual solutions to the full platform.
