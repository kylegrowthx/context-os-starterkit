## Context

George Haikal (VP/founder at GrowthX) reached out to Neil Madsen (Fabius co-founder) through a referral from Bridget McGill at GrowthX. GrowthX is a Series A B2B content marketing agency that has grown through high-touch, repeatable service delivery. George has spent significant effort defining their ICP through analyzing churn, customer research, and testing — identifying five core dimensions (decision maker clarity, content expertise, approval complexity, etc.) that indicate which clients are easiest and most profitable to serve. The challenge is scale: George needs to systematically apply these qualification criteria across all customer touchpoints — sales calls, discovery sessions, and post-sale delivery meetings — without depending on him personally to review every interaction. He's building standardized sales processes and discovery questions, but needs a way to monitor whether the team is actually asking those questions and capturing the signals he's identified. This is where Fabius comes in.
