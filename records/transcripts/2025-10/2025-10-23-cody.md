## Key Topics

### Content Strategy & Publishing Model

  - **Challenge:** Raj's quality concerns conflict with the high-volume content needed for AEO/GEO.
  - **Solution:** A two-tier publishing model, inspired by the Augment customer example.
      - **Main Blog:** Reserved for high-quality, product-centric content.
      - **`/guides` Section:** A new folder for high-volume AEO/GEO content.
  - **Rationale:** This structure meets SEO requirements (findable, linked, sitemapped) while allowing for rapid, unconstrained publishing.

### AEO Measurement & Topic Prioritization

  - **Challenge:** AEO volume is unquantifiable; no standard tools exist for prompt-based search data.
  - **Solution:** Measure success directionally using a blend of signals:
      - Referral traffic from AEO placements
      - Brand search volume
      - Overall SEO lift
  - **Topic Prioritization (from Reddit research):**
      - **High Priority:** Topics \#2 (AI tool gaps), \#3 (data cleaning), \#5 (self-service analytics) → Direct product relevance.
      - **Strategic Opportunity:** Topic \#1 (dashboard adoption) → Aligns with PR strategy by addressing analytics leader pain points (e.g., managing request backlogs).
