## Key Topics

### Vendor Page Strategy Shift

  - Vendor page work is paused for a new design launch in \~2 weeks.
  - **New Design:** More data-driven, less content-heavy, with a 5-bullet summary for social sharing.
  - **Strategic Pivot:** Testing a shift from an SEO play to a data product.
      - **Rationale:** Driven by a different RAMP department leveraging new data resources.
  - **Future Role for GrowthX:** Uncertain.
      - **Contingency:** If RAMP reverts to the original design, GrowthX support would be needed.

### Cashflow Desk Project: Contract Terms

  - **RAMP's Concern:** The 8-week validation period in the proposal starts at the site build, preventing performance validation before a year-long commitment.
  - **RAMP's Proposal:** Start the 8-week validation period at content publication.
      - **Rationale:** This aligns the validation window with the ability to measure results.
  - **GrowthX's Concern:** A 30-day opt-out is too ambiguous for a project without defined KPIs.
      - **Rationale:** Success metrics (e.g., citation rate) are undefined, making it impossible to know what performance is required to avoid an opt-out.

### Strategic Alignment & Path Forward

  - **RAMP's Strategic Focus:**
      - **Earned AEO (Cashflow Desk):** The primary focus, seen as a scalable alternative to expensive affiliate placements.
      - **Owned AEO (RAMP.com):** Now fully staffed internally, reducing the need for external support on traditional content.
  - **GrowthX's Position:**
      - **Rationale for RAMP.com Work:** Continuing work on the main domain is critical for business continuity and case study development.
      - **Proposed Solution:** Add a flexible RAMP.com workstream (e.g., tools, calculators) to de-risk the engagement, as it's tied to a proven asset.
  - **Agreed Path Forward:**
      - **Validation Window:** GrowthX will confirm with Marcel if the 8-week period can start at publication.
      - **Benchmarks:** RAMP will provide citation rate data from a proxy site ("new facts") to define success metrics.
      - **Executive Alignment:** Marcel (GrowthX) and George (RAMP) will connect to resolve the opt-out clause and define the RAMP.com workstream.
