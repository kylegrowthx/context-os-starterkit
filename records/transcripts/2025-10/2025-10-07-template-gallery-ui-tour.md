## Summary

GrowthX presented designs for an SEO-optimized template gallery and detail pages for Lovable, featuring filters, sticky navigation, and description-rich layouts to drive organic search traffic. Felix shared Lovable's evolving brand direction and new background gradient treatments, while Sebastian flagged a critical misalignment: GrowthX is building prompt-generated templates with an SEO-first template page structure, whereas Lovable is developing designer-built, high-quality templates for a marketplace and landing page integration. The team agreed these are two separate tracks that may need to coexist or be merged, and scheduled a follow-up with Marcel and George to align on quality standards, template strategy, and page structure.

---

## Context

GrowthX is engaged by Lovable for growth marketing, with a specific mandate to make Lovable's templates rankable and drive organic traffic through SEO. Marcel pitched the concept of a template gallery with detail pages that would be indexable on Google, allowing users to discover templates through search. Georgemaine (GrowthX's designer/full-stack engineer) spent a week prototyping gallery and detail page designs, trying to balance Lovable's visual design language (floating cards on gradient backgrounds) with SEO requirements (text-heavy layouts for keyword targeting). This meeting served as the first detailed design review with Felix (Lovable's design lead) to get early feedback before implementation, and revealed that Sebastian—who leads Lovable's internal template initiatives for their builder ecosystem and eventual marketplace—was working on overlapping template page designs with different objectives and quality standards. The misalignment needed to surface immediately to prevent duplicate work and wasted effort.
