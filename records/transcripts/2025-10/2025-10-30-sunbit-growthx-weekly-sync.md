## Key Topics

### Content Performance & Strategy

  - **Organic Growth:** Since content publishing began in April, the site has seen:
      - **68% YoY increase** in organic search traffic.
      - **Average search position drop** from 88 to 4.5.
      - **Significant increases** in time on site and page depth.
  - **Publishing Cadence:**
      - **Goal:** Publish \~2–3 articles/day (vs. 10/day) to maintain a natural pace for search bots.
      - **Backlog:** \~100 articles are with the legal team; approval will replenish the "ready to publish" queue.
  - **Content Feedback:**
      - **Pattern:** Articles about Sunbit or finance receive more scrutiny.
      - **Process:** GrowthX is using a running feedback document to improve future content.

### AI Visibility & New Initiatives

  - **Current AI Visibility (via Scrunch tool):**
      - **Brand Mentions:** Sunbit appears in 9% of AI responses, compared to CareCredit's 30%.
      - **Sentiment:** Generally positive when Sunbit is mentioned.
  - **Strategic Response:**
      - **Competitor Focus:** Cherry Technologies is an emerging threat; new "Sunbit vs. Cherry" articles will be created to influence LLMs.
      - **Technical Improvement:** JSON schema will be added to page headers to improve AI parsing.
  - **Approved Experiments:**
      - **LLM-Specific FAQs:** Create dedicated Q\&A pages for LLMs.
          - **Option 1:** Publish as standard pages.
          - **Option 2:** Mark as `noindex` and reference only in `LLMS.txt` for exclusive LLM access.
      - **Case Study Test:** Publish case studies as blog posts to test traffic and conversion. If successful, the separate case studies section will be retired.
