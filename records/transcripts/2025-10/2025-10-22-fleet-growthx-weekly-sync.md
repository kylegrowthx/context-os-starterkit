## Key Topics

### The Core Problem: Inaccurate LLM Data

  - **Challenge:** LLMs provide inaccurate, outdated information about Fleet, including the claim that it is "not an MDM."
  - **Root Cause:** A lack of foundational content on Fleet's site for LLMs to ingest.
  - **Goal:** Correct the narrative by building a comprehensive content library that educates LLMs on Fleet's current capabilities and enterprise positioning.
  - **Target Audience:** Enterprises (\>700 employees) with significant Mac fleets evaluating MDM solutions.
  - **Success Metric:** LLM visibility tracked via a Scrunch dashboard, which monitors Fleet's presence in industry-relevant prompts vs. competitors.

### Content Strategy & Topic Clusters

  - **Analysis:** Fleet's current "guides" are product-driven with minimal organic visibility (19 URLs ranking for \~30 keywords).
  - **Competitor Gap:** Competitors (Jamf, SimpleMDM) succeed with foundational content ("what is X") and commercial-intent content (comparison listicles) that informs LLMs.
  - **Proposed Clusters (Draft):**
    1.  **MDM Fundamentals:** Foundational definitions (e.g., MDM vs. MAM).
    2.  **Commercial Intent:** Comparison and alternative listicles.
    3.  **Apple Ecosystem Management:** Apple-specific topics (e.g., Apple Business Manager).
    4.  **Cross-Platform Device Management:** Multi-OS management (Mac, Windows, Linux).
    5.  **Enterprise Security & Compliance:** Topics for enterprise buyers (e.g., encryption, compliance).

### Feedback & Revisions

  - **Cluster 3 & 4 → Merge:** Brock requested merging the "Apple Ecosystem" and "Cross-Platform" clusters.
      - **Rationale:** Separating them conflicts with Fleet's core value of simplifying management across all OSs. It risks reinforcing platform distinctions, which could become outdated information, similar to the "not an MDM" narrative.
  - **New Cluster → Zero-Day Support:** A new cluster will focus on "Zero-Day Support" for major OS updates.
      - **Rationale:** This is a critical enterprise concern. Delayed compatibility prevents customers from applying security patches, creating compliance risks.
      - **Keyword Focus:** "Zero-day support for major OS updates."

### Content Generation Process

  - **Process:** Human-led workflow to ensure accuracy and quality.
    1.  **Keyword Research:** Manual research by GrowthX.
    2.  **Outline Generation:** Atlas platform analyzes top-ranking content via SEMrush API.
    3.  **Research Report:** Atlas compiles a report using Perplexity.
    4.  **Fact-Checking:** An automated fact-checker reviews the draft.
    5.  **Human Editing:** A human editor reviews for accuracy, tone, and messaging.
  - **Ramp-Up:** 1 calibration article → 3 articles/week → 5 articles/week.
