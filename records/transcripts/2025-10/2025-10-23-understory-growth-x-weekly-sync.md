  - **Cadence:** 5 articles/week, with unreviewed articles auto-publishing to maintain speed.
  - **Feedback:** Understory requested direct tags on specific sections needing review, as they cannot read every article in full.

### SEO & Analytics

  - **Sitemap Error:** A critical sitemap error is being fixed by EOW.
      - **Problem:** Posts were mapped to `/post` instead of `/blog`.
      - **Impact:** This blocked Google from indexing content, skewing all traffic data.

      - **2025 Sessions:** \~10,000 (higher than Understory expected).
      - **Traffic Source:** \~50/50 split between LinkedIn and Google.
  - **LLM Traffic:** The Looker report shows Claude and ChatGPT are the top LLM sources for mentions and traffic.

### New Airtable Content OS

  - **Purpose:** Centralizes content management for faster feedback and better visibility.
  - **Key Features:**
      - **`Topics Pending Approval`:** Understory can use dropdowns to approve, revise, or add notes.
      - **`Content Calendar`:** Understory can adjust publication dates for future articles.
      - **`Articles for Review`:** Understory can add notes for specific edits.

### New Content Formats

  - **Podcast Summaries:** The initial draft was approved with key feedback:
      - Embed the YouTube video directly in the article.
      - Add a "TLDR" (Too Long; Didn't Read) section.
      - Hyperlink chapters to specific timestamps in the YouTube video (GrowthX to investigate feasibility).
      - Add a shout-out section for the guest's company.
      - Prioritize older episodes first.
  - **Case Studies:** Understory will create written case studies now for faster publication, rather than waiting for video testimonials.
  - **"Working in Public" Series:** Alex proposed a series documenting the SEO journey, leveraging the team's combined 35k social media followers.
