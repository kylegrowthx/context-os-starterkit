  - **Action:** Outreach will build a link-building strategy to get included in these competitor-cited blogs.

### Website Refresh: Collaboration

  - **Context:** Outreach's manual website refresh (led by Paul) provides an opportunity for strategic collaboration.
  - **Goal:** Optimize core platform pages for both Google SEO and LLM retrievability.
  - **GrowthX Support:** GrowthX will provide strategic guidance on page structure, metadata, and content optimization.
  - **Execution:** GrowthX's Atlas workflow tool can scale the refresh, similar to a project where they updated hundreds of landing pages for another client.
  - **Prioritization:** The Q2 vs. Q3 LLM visibility report will identify pages with the biggest drops, guiding where to focus refresh efforts first.
