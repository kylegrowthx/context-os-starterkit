## Key Topics

### SPS Commerce's Current Situation and Needs

  - Merging three companies and their respective websites
  - Primary focus on website migration, followed by SEO/AEO audit
  - Uncertain about long-term content creation needs due to ongoing reorganization
  - Looking for analysis, direction, and optimization of existing content

### GrowthX's Capabilities and Approach

  - Two-phase process: strategy sprint (phase 1) and growth execution (phase 2)
  - Utilizes AI for content creation while maintaining human expertise oversight
  - Offers daily publishing against a collaboratively developed content roadmap
  - Provides analytics and LLM visibility tracking for created content
  - Uses internal platform for workflow management, soon to be accessible by clients
  - Conducts technical audits and makes recommendations for improved visibility

### GrowthX's Content Creation Process

  - Uses AI-driven research and fact-checking processes
  - Creates persona artifacts to inform content relevance
  - Develops assignments based on keyword opportunities and difficulty
  - Implements multi-step content creation workflow with quality checks

### Case Studies and Examples

  - Showed success stories for clients like Monograph, Engine, Town, Augment Code, Tiro, and Ramp
  - Demonstrated significant traffic increases (e.g., 2.5x for Augment Code, 10x for Tiro)
  - Showcased ability to create interactive tools and programmatic content (e.g., Ramp's per diem calculator)
