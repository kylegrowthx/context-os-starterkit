## Context

Outreach (a sales engagement platform) is a key client of GrowthX, which handles their content strategy and SEO. This weekly sync is a standing meeting between Outreach's marketing team (led by Hadassah Pegado, director of marketing) and GrowthX's content delivery team (Jenn Peters, Megan Dickey). The meeting happens at a critical inflection point: traditional organic search is shifting as LLMs become the primary interface for discovery. Outreach's content visibility depends increasingly on being cited in LLM responses (especially ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude), not just ranking in Google. Jenn Peters leads the performance analysis and macro SEO landscape updates, while Megan handles LLM visibility tracking via Scrunch and manages the competitive intelligence work.
