## Key Topics

### Content Performance Overview

  - Overall site performance: Clicks decreasing, impressions increasing
  - Non-branded performance showing slight decline
  - Top blog posts (e.g., sales email templates) performing well after refreshes
  - Potential strategies:
      - A/B testing SERP-optimized titles and meta descriptions
      - Updating outdated content and meta information
      - Leveraging LLM visibility tactics

### LLM Visibility and Strategy

  - Steady growth in LLM citations, up across major platforms (except slight dips in Copilot and Gemini)
  - Top landing pages from LLM traffic identified, with homepage leading
  - Discussion on Scrunch tool limitations and interpretation of "responses" metric
  - Focus on identifying where competitors (especially Gong) are mentioned but Outreach isn't
  - Plan to export competitor presence data for team review and content strategy development

### Content Pipeline and Guidelines

  - New workflow incorporating in-house built agentic pipeline system
  - Writing guidelines document shared to improve content creation process
  - Discussion on potential new content topics:
      - Call coaching or manual version of the score
      - Deal personalization (vs. email personalization)
      - Real-time suggestions during sales meetings

### Proprietary Data Content Strategy

  - Plans to create reports using Outreach's proprietary data
  - Aim to boost content uniqueness and authority in the industry
  - Potential to refresh existing articles or create new pillar pages with this data
