## Background

Ryan Singer is the creator of Shape Up, a popular product development methodology used at Basecamp. His work focuses on simplifying complex problems and designing products that feel natural to use.

---

## Relationship to GrowthX

Ryan is advising GrowthX on product strategy for their content performance platform (CheckThat). He conducted a multi-session product shaping engagement to help GrowthX think through how to present complex data in an intuitive way that drives daily engagement. The core challenge he helped address: how to make content performance tracking as simple and addictive as Trello while maintaining Salesforce-level depth.

---

## Key Insights from Sessions

- **Core Problem:** Teams publish content but "fly blind" on whether it works. They need to connect content work to outcomes without jumping between multiple disconnected tools.
- **Design Philosophy:** Stop thinking in tabs and sections. Build a "daily visit" screen first—something that makes you feel bad if you don't check it every day.
- **Strategy:** Nail Trello-level simplicity at the surface with Salesforce-level depth underneath.
- **Content Half-Life:** With AI, pages need continuous improvement, not one-time publishing. The 1% rule applies: top 1% of pages drive 20%+ of results.
- **LLM Traffic:** LLM referral traffic converts 3-7x higher than typical search because users arrive pre-sold.
