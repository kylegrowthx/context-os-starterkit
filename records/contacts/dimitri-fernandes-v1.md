## Key Expertise

**Community Framework:**
- Community lifecycle: Discovery → Onboarding → Engagement → Contribution → Advocacy
- Five core principles: trusted space, loop structure, human touch, measurement, and qualitative belonging
- Member personas and vetting for quality professional communities
- Cross-platform integration and automation with human layer support

**Strategic Recommendations for GrowthX:**
- Establish vetting criteria for paid membership to build exclusivity and quality
- Move beyond content-centric model to member-to-member connections and peer groups
- Create structure for subgroups and industry-specific roundtables (e.g., CMOs in fintech)
- Balance automation/AI with human touch through community leads, moderators, and volunteer advocates
- Measure both quantitative activity (engagement, contribution rate) and qualitative outcomes (belonging, value delivery)
- Design workflows for member journey from discovery through advocacy, emphasizing first-month value delivery
