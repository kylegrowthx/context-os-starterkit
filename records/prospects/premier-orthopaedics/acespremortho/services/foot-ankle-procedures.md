Understanding Foot & Ankle Procedures
-------------------------------------

Foot and ankle procedures encompass a wide range of treatments aimed at diagnosing and treating conditions affecting the feet and ankles, including bunions, plantar fasciitis, Achilles tendon injuries, fractures, and arthritis. At Premier Orthopedics, our podiatrists are experts in both conservative and surgical techniques, addressing the underlying cause of foot and ankle disorders to restore function and alleviate pain.

When Is Foot & Ankle Surgery Needed?
------------------------------------

Foot and ankle surgery may be recommended when conservative treatments such as medication, orthotics, or physical therapy fail to provide adequate relief or when the condition significantly impairs daily activities or quality of life. Common scenarios where surgery may be necessary include:

### Bunions

A bony bump that forms on the joint at the base of the big toe, causing pain and deformity that may require surgical correction.

### Plantar Fasciitis

Inflammation of the plantar fascia, a thick band of tissue that runs across the bottom of the foot, often resulting in chronic heel pain that may necessitate surgical intervention.

### Achilles Tendon Injuries

Inflammation of the plantar fascia, a thick band of tissue that runs across the bottom of the foot, often resulting in chronic heel pain that may necessitate surgical intervention.

### Foot & Ankle Fractures

Broken bones in the foot or ankle that may need surgical fixation to ensure proper healing and alignment.

### Arthritis

Severe arthritis in the foot or ankle joints that may necessitate joint replacement or fusion surgery to alleviate pain and improve mobility.

Benefits Of Foot & Ankle Procedures
-----------------------------------

Foot and ankle procedures offer numerous benefits for patients suffering from debilitating conditions, including:

### Pain Relief

Surgery can effectively alleviate chronic pain caused by structural abnormalities or injuries, allowing patients to resume their normal activities with reduced discomfort.

### Improved Mobility

By correcting deformities and restoring stability to the foot and ankle, surgery can improve overall function and mobility, enabling patients to move more freely and comfortably.

### Preventing Further Damage

Surgical intervention can help prevent further damage to the foot and ankle structures, reducing the risk of complications and long-term disability.

### Enhanced Quality Of Life

Relief from pain and improved mobility can significantly enhance patients’ quality of life, enabling them to engage in activities they enjoy and participate more fully in daily life.

Types Of Foot & Ankle Procedures Offered At Premier Orthopedics
---------------------------------------------------------------

Premier Orthopedics offers a comprehensive range of foot and ankle procedures, including:

### Conservative Treatments

Our podiatrists prioritize conservative treatments whenever possible, including orthotics, physical therapy, and medication management to relieve pain and improve function.

### Minimally Invasive Surgery

Minimally invasive techniques involve smaller incisions, reduced tissue disruption, and faster recovery times compared to traditional open surgery. Procedures may include arthroscopy for joint issues and endoscopic surgery for tendon repairs.

### Bunion Surgery

Surgical correction of bunions to realign the bones and soft tissues, reducing pain and restoring normal foot shape and function.

### Achilles Tendon Repair

Surgical repair of Achilles tendon tears or ruptures to restore strength and flexibility to the tendon.

### Fracture Fixation

Surgical stabilization of foot and ankle fractures using plates, screws, or pins to ensure proper healing and alignment.

### Joint Replacement & Fusion

For severe arthritis or joint damage, joint replacement or fusion surgery can alleviate pain and improve mobility by replacing or fusing the affected joint.

Recovery Process After Foot & Ankle Procedures
----------------------------------------------

The recovery process following foot and ankle procedures varies depending on the type of procedure performed, the patient’s overall health, and other individual factors. However, there are some general guidelines that apply to most patients:

### Hospital Stay

Some procedures may require a short hospital stay for monitoring and post-operative care, while others may be performed on an outpatient basis, allowing patients to return home the same day.

### Pain Management

Our team will provide pain management strategies to help alleviate discomfort during the recovery period, including medication, ice therapy, and activity modification.

### Physical Therapy

Physical therapy plays a crucial role in the rehabilitation process, helping patients regain strength, flexibility, and mobility in the foot and ankle. Our skilled therapists will develop a personalized exercise program tailored to each patient’s specific needs and goals.

### Follow-up Care

Regular follow-up appointments with your podiatrist are essential for monitoring your progress, assessing healing, and addressing any concerns or complications that may arise during the recovery process.
