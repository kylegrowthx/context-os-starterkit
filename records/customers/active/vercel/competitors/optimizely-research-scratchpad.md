## Question 1: Company Overview, Founding, History, Key Milestones

### Founding & History

**Founded:** 2010 by Dan Siroker and Pete Koomen (both formerly of Google)
**Y Combinator:** Completed Y Combinator Winter 2010
**Original Name/Business:** OptimizelyX (website optimization platform)
**Initial Focus:** A/B testing and website optimization for marketing teams

**Key Pivot (October 2020):**
Episerver (CMS vendor, itself acquired by Insight Partners in 2018) acquired Optimizely for the experimentation/optimization platform.

**Rebranding (January 2021):**
Episerver announced it would rebrand the combined entity as "Optimizely," making the experimentation platform the flagship product.

**Current Structure (2025-2026):**
Optimizely is now a portfolio company of Insight Venture Partners, operating as a unified "Digital Experience Platform" (DXP) consolidating CMS, experimentation, personalization, content marketing, and commerce.

### Leadership

- **CEO:** Alex Atzberger (since 2019; led company through acquisition and platform consolidation)
- **Co-Founders (Original):** Dan Siroker, Pete Koomen (both left or evolved roles post-acquisition)
- **CFO:** Iain Hassall
- **CMO:** Dorian Kendal

### Company Metrics (2024-2025)

## Question 8: Community Sentiment & Market Perception

- Integration capability (analytics, data platforms)

- Very high enterprise pricing; no transparent mid-market tier

## Edge Computing & Serverless

**Optimizely Edge Agent:**
- Dual-mode operation (edge for GET requests, serverless for POST)
- Edge mode: Sub-50ms latency at CDN PoPs
- Agent mode: Serverless, auto-scaling backend
- Supports Fastly Compute@Edge, Cloudflare Workers, Vercel Edge Functions, AWS Lambda@Edge, Akamai

**Performance:** 
- 2x slower than Vercel Edge Config when not using edge mode (requires network roundtrip)
- Flicker-free execution for Web Experimentation
- Full experimentation capability entirely at the edge
