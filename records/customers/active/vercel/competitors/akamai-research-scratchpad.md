## 1. Company Overview & Founding History

### Founding Story
Akamai was born from a challenge posed by Tim Berners-Lee (inventor of the World Wide Web) at MIT in early 1995. He foresaw internet congestion and challenged colleagues to invent a fundamentally new way to deliver content.

**Key Founders:**
- **Dr. Tom Leighton**: MIT Professor of Applied Mathematics, expert in parallel algorithms, co-founder in 1998, became CEO in 2013
- **Danny Lewin**: Undergraduate at MIT, worked with Leighton to develop distributed routing algorithms

The name "Akamai" comes from a Hawaiian dictionary, meaning "intelligent." The company was formally founded in 1998 and went public in October 1999.

**Timeline:**
- 1998: Founding
- 1999: IPO (NASDAQ: AKAM)
- 2015: Introduced Akamai Intelligent Edge Platform concept
- 2020-2025: Transformation from CDN-only to "cloud and security company"

## 5. Product & Feature Analysis

### Core Product Lines

**1. Delivery Network (CDN)**
- **Global CDN:** 4,100+ PoPs across 131+ countries
- **Edge Caching:** Intelligent cache management with no manual configuration
- **Image Optimization:** Automatic resizing, format conversion, compression
- **Video Delivery:** Sophisticated caching and rules for streaming
- **Dynamic Site Acceleration:** Route optimization for dynamic content

**2. Security (51% of 2024 revenue)**

**Web Application Firewall & DDoS:**
- **App & API Protector (WAF):** Cloud-delivered WAF with auto-updating, self-tuning rules
- **Prolexic DDoS:** Layer 3, 4, and 7 DDoS mitigation
- **Bot Manager:** Bot detection and management; Bot Manager Premier for advanced detections
- **Behavioral DDoS Engine:** Sophisticated volumetric attack defense
- **API Security:** Automated API discovery, protection, and throttling
- **Real-time request inspection:** All traffic scanned for threats

**Identity & Access Security:**
- **Akamai Identity Cloud:** Access management, SSO, MFA
- **Akamai Guardicore Platform:** Microsegmentation, Zero Trust network access, DNS firewall, threat hunting

**Threat Intelligence:**
- Managed Threat Hunting
- Intelligence feeds from 4,100+ PoPs

**3. Edge Computing & Serverless**

**EdgeWorkers:**
- JavaScript execution at edge (V8 isolates)
- 300-second max execution (vs. Vercel's 60 second default)
- Low cold starts
- Use cases: A/B testing, geolocation, personalization, auth, redirects
- Free tier available but limited (bundled with enterprise contracts)

**Akamai Inference Cloud (New 2024):**
- Edge AI inference platform
- NVIDIA RTX PRO Servers with RTX 6000 Blackwell GPUs
- Kubernetes, vLLM, KServe, NVIDIA NIM stack
- ~20 initial locations; planned expansion
- Model-aware security and threat protection

**Gecko (Generalized Edge Compute):**
- Distributed edge computing platform
- Deployed in 100+ cities globally
- 41 core datacenters in 36 cities as of 2024

**4. Cloud Infrastructure (Compute: 16% of 2024 revenue)**

**Akamai Connected Cloud (formerly Linode):**
- Cloud VMs, Kubernetes, Bare Metal
- 41 datacenters across 36 locations globally
- Low cost compared to AWS/Azure/GCP
- 25% year-over-year growth in compute segment

## 6. Pricing & Packaging

### CDN & Delivery Pricing

- **Base Rates:** $0.035–$0.049 per GB (varies by volume and geography)
- **Pricing Model:** No public pricing; enterprise-only with custom quotes
- **Minimum Contracts:** 12-month minimum commitments typical
- **Volume Discounts:** Negotiated based on committed usage levels
- **Transparency:** Low (no public pricing calculator)

**vs. Competitors:**
- Cloudflare: Free tier + $20/month Pro tier + enterprise custom
- Vercel: Free Hobby (non-commercial) + $20/user Pro + enterprise custom
- AWS CloudFront: Usage-based pay-as-you-go, typically cheaper at small scale

**Key Differentiator:** Akamai has no free tier; not suitable for hobbyist/small projects.

### Security Product Pricing

- **Bundle Pricing:** Security products typically bundled with CDN contracts
- **WAAP Suite:** Often packaged as part of larger enterprise deals
- **Bot Manager:** Available as add-on to CDN contracts
- **Guardicore:** Usually sold as separate module in larger deals

### Akamai Connected Cloud (Compute) Pricing

- **VMs:** Competitive with Linode pricing pre-acquisition (~$5-100/month)
- **Growth:** Compute segment up 25% YoY 2024
- **Model:** Simpler, more transparent than Akamai CDN pricing
