## Summary

Partnership kickoff meeting for Giga's 8-week Strategy Sprint engagement with GrowthX. Morgan (CEO, ~2 weeks in role) and Kyle (CRO, ~3 months in role) shared the company's positioning challenges, competitive landscape, sales dynamics, and content needs. The contract was signed same-day as the sales call — fastest close Tyler has seen.

### Key Themes

1. **Zero brand awareness** — no organic content presence; missing ~95% of potential deals
2. **CS sameness** — market flooded with generic "agentic CX" claims; Giga blends in
3. **Enterprise readiness perception** — small team makes risk-averse CTOs hesitant
4. **Infrastructure layer vision** — Giga wants to be "DevOps for AI agents" not just another CX vendor
5. **Crisis management** — ransomware-related negative SEO articles need suppression
6. **Sales team undefeated** — <3 months old, haven't lost a deal where they have a seat
