## Methodology

- **TAM**: Top-down from industry reports + bottom-up validation from customer counts × average spend
- **SAM**: Filter TAM by ICP criteria (B2B tech, $10M+ raised, geography, willingness to pay)
- **SOM**: Bottom-up from realistic penetration rates, sales capacity, pricing, and growth trajectory
- **Competitive analysis**: Map players by funding, revenue (if known), positioning, and threat level
- **Growth rates**: Use multiple sources and triangulate; flag when estimates diverge significantly
