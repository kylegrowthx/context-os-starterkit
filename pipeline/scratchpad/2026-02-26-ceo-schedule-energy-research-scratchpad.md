# CEO Schedule & Energy Optimization — Research Scratchpad

## Topic Definition
How to organize a startup CEO's schedule and calendar based on energy management, not just time management. Optimized for a Series A founder with ADHD who operates at high intensity.

## Learner Profile
- **Who:** Marcel Santilli — CEO/Founder, GrowthX (Series A, B2B content marketing, ~$12M ARR)
- **Context:** ADHD, systems-thinker, high-intensity, context-switching is especially costly, protected family time 4:30-7:30pm, energized by strategic depth and teaching, drained by context switching and purposeless meetings
- **Goal:** Build a calendar architecture that optimizes for energy, not just time — sustaining peak output while preventing burnout
- **Use case:** Both personal implementation and instruction for EA/AI agents managing his calendar
