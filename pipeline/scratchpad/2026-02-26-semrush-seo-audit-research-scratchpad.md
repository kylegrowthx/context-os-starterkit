# SEMRush SEO Audit — Research Scratchpad

## Topic
How to do a comprehensive SEO audit using SEMRush (app and API)

## Learner Profile
- **Role:** GrowthX team members (content marketers, strategists)
- **Goal:** Run full SEO audits independently on client domains
- **Context:** Training material for GrowthX service delivery. Equal coverage of SEMRush UI and API.

## Audience Analysis

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO is learning this? | GrowthX team — marketers and strategists who need to audit client domains |
| WHAT do they care about vs noise? | Practical workflow, actionable findings, client-ready outputs. Not academic SEO theory. |
| WHY do they need this? | To run audits that surface real problems and opportunities for B2B clients |
| WHAT sources would they trust? | SEMRush official docs, recognized SEO practitioners, data-backed guides |
| WHAT should be excluded? | Beginner "what is SEO" content, outdated pre-2024 techniques, black-hat tactics |
