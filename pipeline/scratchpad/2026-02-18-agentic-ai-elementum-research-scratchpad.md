## Research Questions

### 1. Foundations
- What is the precise definition of "Agentic AI" vs "Generative AI"?
- What is the difference between "Probabilistic" (LLM) and "Deterministic" (Rules/Code) execution in an enterprise context?
- Why is "Agent Sprawl" a problem?
- What is "Zero Persistence" in the context of data privacy and AI?

### 2. Frameworks & Architecture
- What is a "Control Plane" for AI agents? How does it differ from simple monitoring?
- What are the architectural patterns for "Orchestration" (e.g., ReAct, Plan-and-Solve)?
- How does "Human-in-the-loop" fit into agentic workflows?

### 3. Competitive Landscape (The "Against" Narrative)
- How does UiPath define "Agentic Automation" (Maestro)?
- What is ServiceNow's "Agentic AI" strategy?
- How does IBM define "Agentic AI" (since they own the educational layer)?
- What is the "SaaSpocalypse" narrative?
