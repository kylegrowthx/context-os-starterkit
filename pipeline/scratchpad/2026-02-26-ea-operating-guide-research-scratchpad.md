## Audience Analysis

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO is learning this? | A human EA and AI agents supporting a high-intensity startup CEO |
| WHAT do they care about vs noise? | Actionable operating procedures, decision frameworks, communication protocols. NOT generic admin tips. |
| WHY do they need this? | To anticipate needs, protect CEO time, manage information flow, and amplify CEO leverage |
