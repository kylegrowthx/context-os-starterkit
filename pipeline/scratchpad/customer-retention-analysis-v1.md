### Churn Analysis Summary

**Primary Churn Drivers:**
1. **Cost/Value perception** (SchoolAI) - felt could get cheaper alternative
2. **New decision-maker** (Superhuman) - new VP chose different direction
3. **Service delivery** (Exec) - poor backlinking performance
4. **Timing/ROI** (Biologica) - survival mode, needed immediate returns
5. **Channel shift** (Rapyd) - moved budget to events

**Gaps Identified:**
- Surge AI ($2.76M) - No churn reason recorded. Critical post-mortem needed.
- Teero, Okta - No churn reasons documented
- Poor renewal management cited in SchoolAI loss

**Recoverable Accounts:**
- **Biologica** - Door open to restart in 2-3 months

## Key Insights

1. **Surge AI Churn Impact**: Single account represents 77% of all churned ARR ($2.76M of $3.61M). No churn reason documented - critical gap.
2. **Average Customer Tenure at Churn**: 8 months
3. **Top Churn Drivers**:
   - Cost/value perception (can get cheaper)
   - New decision-makers choosing different direction
   - Service delivery issues (backlinking performance)
   - ROI timing misalignment
   - Channel shift (events vs content)
4. **Documentation Gap**: 3 of 8 churned accounts have no churn reason recorded (Surge AI, Teero, Okta)
