# SEO Ranking Factors Research Scratchpad

## Research Configuration
- Topic: SEO ranking factors — comprehensive evidence-based guide biased toward 2025-2026 studies
- Learner Profile:
  - Role: B2B content marketing agency CEO (expert practitioner)
  - Goal: Master the evidence base on what actually drives rankings in 2025-2026, build defensible methodology
  - Context: Runs $200k+/year content engagements; needs to separate signal from noise, understand what Google has confirmed vs. correlated vs. speculated
  - Time Available: 8-12 hours
- Research Mode: exploratory
- Target Sources: 800+
- Search Depth: advanced
- Started: 2026-02-19

---

## Audience Analysis

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO is learning this? | Agency CEO who lives in SEO daily — wants the evidence hierarchy, not beginner explanations |
| WHAT do they care about vs noise? | Studies with methodology, confirmed vs. correlated factors, Google leaks/patents, practitioner-tested insights. NOT listicles of "200 ranking factors" without evidence |
| WHY do they need this? | To build an evidence-based SEO methodology for client strategy, differentiate the agency, train team on what actually matters |
| WHAT depth is appropriate? | Expert — wants to cite studies, understand effect sizes, know where consensus exists and where debate lives |
| WHAT sources would they trust? | Peer-reviewed studies, large-scale correlation analyses (Backlinko, Semrush, Ahrefs), Google's own documentation, antitrust trial revelations, practitioners who test at scale |
| WHAT should be excluded? | Unsourced "top 200 factors" lists, pre-2024 studies unless foundational, speculation presented as fact |
