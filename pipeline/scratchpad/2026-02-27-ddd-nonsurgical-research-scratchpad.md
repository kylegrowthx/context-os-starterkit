# DDD Non-Surgical Solutions — Research Scratchpad

**Topic:** Degenerative Disc Disease: Non-Surgical Solutions for Long-Term Pain Relief
**Started:** 2026-02-27
**Target Sources:** 30+
**Status:** Phase 1 — Generating research questions

## Learner Profile
- **Role:** Patient or caregiver, no medical background
- **Goal:** Understand DDD and all non-surgical options to make informed decisions
- **Context:** Diagnosed or suspected DDD, wants to avoid surgery if possible

## Research Questions

### 1. Foundations (Critical)
- Q1: What is DDD — what's actually happening in the spine, in plain language?
- Q2: How common is DDD, and who gets it?
- Q3: What are the symptoms and how is it diagnosed?
- Q4: What's the natural history — does it always get worse?
- Q5: What are the biggest misconceptions patients have about DDD?

### 2. Frameworks & Treatment Models (Critical)
- Q6: What is the standard clinical treatment pathway for DDD?
- Q7: What treatment frameworks or guidelines do spine specialists follow?
- Q8: How do clinicians decide which non-surgical treatment to try first?
- Q9: What is the evidence hierarchy for non-surgical DDD treatments?

### 3. Physical Therapy & Exercise (Critical)
- Q10: What specific exercise protocols have the strongest evidence for DDD?
- Q11: Core stabilization vs. general exercise — what does the research say?
- Q12: What role does aquatic therapy play?
- Q13: How long does PT take to show results?

### 4. Pharmacological Treatments (High)
- Q14: NSAIDs, acetaminophen, muscle relaxants — comparative effectiveness?
- Q15: What about topical treatments and patches?
- Q16: Current guidelines on opioid use for DDD?
- Q17: Any newer drug therapies showing promise?

### 5. Injection Therapies (High)
- Q18: Epidural steroid injections — how effective, how long do they last?
- Q19: Facet joint injections and medial branch blocks — evidence?
- Q20: What are the risks and limits of repeated injections?

### 6. Complementary & Lifestyle Approaches (High)
- Q21: Chiropractic care — evidence for DDD specifically?
- Q22: Acupuncture — what do the systematic reviews say?
- Q23: Yoga and Pilates — evidence for disc-related back pain?
- Q24: Weight management, smoking cessation, ergonomics — impact on DDD?
- Q25: Cognitive behavioral therapy and pain psychology — evidence?

### 7. Emerging & Regenerative Therapies (Medium)
- Q26: PRP (platelet-rich plasma) — current evidence level?
- Q27: Stem cell therapy — where does the science actually stand?
- Q28: What other regenerative approaches are in clinical trials?

### 8. Key Practitioners & Trusted Sources (High)
- Q29: Who are the leading researchers/clinicians in non-surgical DDD treatment?
- Q30: What are the best patient-facing resources?

### 9. Real Patient Outcomes (High)
- Q31: What percentage of DDD patients improve without surgery?
- Q32: What does "long-term pain relief" actually look like?
- Q33: When should a patient seriously consider surgery?
