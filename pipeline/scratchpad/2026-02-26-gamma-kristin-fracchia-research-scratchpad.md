## GAMMA COMPANY

### Founding & Origins
- Founded 2020 by Grant Lee (CEO), James Fox, Jon Noronha
- All three were former Optimizely colleagues
- Grant was in London doing advisory work, drowning in slide decks — idea crystallized
- Relocated to SF because he believed it would remain the innovation hub
- Background: finance, consulting, tech

### Early Struggles (2020-2022)
- Raised $7M seed (Oct 2021)
- Built PowerPoint alternative for 3 years
- Only 60,000 users by late 2022
- Burning through cash, ~12 months runway left
- "The market had spoken, and nobody wanted the product"

### The AI Pivot — March 2023
- Integrated generative AI in March 2023
- Gained 10 million users in 9 months post-pivot
- Nearly couldn't make payroll 2 weeks before AI launch (March 2023 bank volatility)
- Quote from Grant: "sitting in one of the corners of that two-bedroom apartment, two weeks away from our biggest launch — the AI launch — and not knowing if we could even make payroll"
- One investor called it "the dumbest idea he had ever heard"

### Growth Timeline
- March 2023: AI relaunch → 3M users in 3 months
- Late 2023: 10M users
- Jan 2024: 17M users
- End 2024: $30.5M ARR
- April 2025: 50M users, $50M ARR
- October 2025: $102M ARR, 70M users, 600K+ paying subscribers
- Feb 2026: 70M+ users, 400M+ presentations created, 1M+ pieces of content per day

### Funding
- Seed: $7M (Oct 2021)
- Series A: $12M led by Accel (May 2024)
- Series B: $68M led by Andreessen Horowitz at $2.1B valuation (Nov 2025)
- Total raised: ~$87M
- Profitable since 2023

### Team
- 50-52 people (as of late 2025)
- All 10 original employees still at company after 5 years
- 1/3 of early team = designers
- "A 50-person team moved like a 200-person team"

### Product
- AI-powered presentation, website, and document builder
- Gamma 3.0: built-in AI design agent
- AI image editing, Gamma Agent, API, Gamma for Teams, Gamma Ultra
- Orchestrates external AI models: Perplexity (outlines), Claude (reasoning), GPT-4 (visuals)
- Can swap models in days vs competitors' months

### Growth Mechanics
- PLG: freemium → instant output → emotional "wow" → habit → conversion
- "Made with Gamma" watermark = viral loop
- Every shared deck = marketing asset
- Solving "first mile" (idea → draft), users handle "last mile" (editing → polishing)
- From Optimizely DNA: internal experimentation culture, test everything
- Zero dollars on advertising

### Creator Marketing Strategy (Key Insights)
- 6 agencies, 150+ creators across TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube
- 70% of spend on micro-influencers (10K-100K followers) for higher engagement
- Reserve budget for mega-influencers during product launches
- Founder (Grant) personally onboarded early creators 1:1 on Zoom
- Collaboration > transactions: top creators contribute product feedback and campaign ideas
- Surveys through agencies to refine messaging
- Key finding: technical AI specs underperform vs. outcome-focused storytelling
- Quote: "We've seen how powerful creator-led storytelling can be. When someone explains Gamma in their own voice — through their own use case — it clicks in a way no brand ad ever could."
- Q2 2025 results: 1.36M+ impressions across 36 LinkedIn and newsletter posts
- Strong traction in AI, productivity, solopreneur niches
