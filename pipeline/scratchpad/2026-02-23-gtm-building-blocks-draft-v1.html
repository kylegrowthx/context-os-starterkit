The Model — Service-as-Software

Services → Technology

Service delivery generates data. Data improves AI. Better AI improves delivery. Each project reduces marginal costs.

Technology → Marketplace

Platform makes experts efficient. Better results attract more experts. Talent network grows.

Marketplace → Services

Elite talent enhances reputation. Reputation attracts customers. More customers mean more data. Cycle continues.

Key Tensions & Open Questions

  • Product focus risk: Marcel & Daniel's attention spread across too many products. No independent product leaders yet.
  • Capacity bottleneck: Scaling from 2→4 kickoffs/week requires 2x pipeline and significant hiring ahead of demand.
  • Churn sensitivity: At current scale, every point of monthly churn compounds hard against growth targets.
  • CheckThat vs. ContentOS: Clear separation needed, but integration is the value. Threading this needle is the GTM challenge.
  • Service-as-software positioning: Market hasn't fully understood the category. Education cost is real (hence AI Led Growth).
  • $7M → $22M in one year: ~3x growth requires all levers pulling simultaneously — kickoffs, conversion, ACV, retention, expansion.
