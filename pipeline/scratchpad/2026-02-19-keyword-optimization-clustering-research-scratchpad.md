# Keyword Optimization & Clustering Research Scratchpad

## Research Configuration
- Topic: Keyword optimization and keyword clustering — best practices, evidence-based approaches, tool methodologies, mental models, and frameworks
- Learner Profile:
  - Role: B2B content marketing agency CEO (deep practitioner)
  - Goal: Master the discipline — understand what works, why it works, how tools think about it, and build a defensible point of view
  - Context: Runs $200k+/year content marketing engagements; needs both strategic depth and tactical precision
  - Time Available: 5-10 hours
- Research Mode: exploratory
- Target Sources: 50+
- Search Depth: advanced
- Started: 2026-02-19

---

## Audience Analysis

| Question | Answer |
|----------|--------|
| WHO is learning this? | Agency CEO who already does keyword work daily — wants the *why* behind best practices, not beginner intros |
| WHAT do they care about vs noise? | Evidence-based approaches, tool methodologies (how Ahrefs/Semrush/Surfer think about clustering), studies that prove what works, mental models for keyword architecture. NOT basic "what is a keyword" content |
| WHY do they need this? | To build a defensible methodology for client engagements, train team members, and differentiate the agency's approach |
| WHAT depth is appropriate? | Expert-level — wants to understand the algorithms and thinking behind tools, not just how to click buttons |
| WHAT sources would they trust? | SEO practitioners who've tested at scale (Cyrus Shepard, Kevin Indig, Eli Schwartz, Ahrefs/Semrush research teams), academic/research-backed content, tool documentation on methodology |
| WHAT should be excluded? | Beginner SEO tutorials, "10 tips for keywords" listicles, outdated pre-BERT keyword density content, B2C-only examples |
