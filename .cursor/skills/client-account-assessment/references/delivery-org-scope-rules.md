# GrowthX Delivery Org Scope Rules

These rules define what belongs in delivery leadership reports vs. what belongs to other leaders. The Delivery Org encompasses both the **Engagement Manager (EM) layer** and the **Managing Editor (ME) / Production layer**.

## Delivery Org Domain (Include in Reports)

The Delivery Org covers everything related to client engagement execution: EM account management, ME production, content quality, deliverable cadence, and team coordination.

### EM / Account Management Layer

**Always include**:
- EM account assignments and workload
- Client relationship health and communication patterns
- Sync cadence and meeting effectiveness
- Escalation management and resolution
- Sprint-to-Growth Execution conversion quality
- Client onboarding and handoff execution
- EM coordination with MEs and production teams
- Cross-functional requests from clients (design, dev, analytics)
- Strategic sprint delivery and artifact quality
- Account risk identification and mitigation

### ME / Production Layer

**Always include**:
- ME performance, capacity, and assignments
- CM performance and workload
- Content quality issues, editorial standards, pipeline improvements
- Deliverable cadence and timing (time from Atlas exit to client-ready)
- Production Team Weekly updates
- Team Lead Alignment (sprint leads, production leads)
- Lead Managing Editor effectiveness
- ME 1:1s and skill development
- Capacity planning and account assignment decisions
- Pipeline and artifact improvements
- ME onboarding and ramp-up progress
- Sprint-to-production handoff quality
- Content refresh pipeline health
- Production tool improvements (Atlas, capacity planner)

## Not the Delivery Org's Domain (Exclude or Mention Briefly)

These belong to other leaders (Andi, Kyle, Marcel, George, etc.):

- **Renewal negotiations** — Andi/Marcel's purview (note renewal dates for context, but do not assess negotiation strategy)
- **CheckThat.ai rollout/pricing** — Andi/Marcel's decision
- **Client CEO interventions** — Marcel's layer
- **EM hiring and headcount decisions** — Andi/Bridget's purview (note staffing gaps if they impact delivery)
- **Deal desk / new sales** — Kyle/sales team
- **Product roadmap decisions** — Marcel/product
