# B2B SaaS Product Marketing Study Guide — Research Prompt

## Topic
**B2B SaaS Product Marketing Mastery** — positioning, messaging, go-to-market, competitive intelligence, launches, and sales enablement for startups

---

## Research Scope

### Phase 1: Foundation Research
- Core frameworks (positioning canvases, messaging hierarchies, value prop structures)
- Mental models for strategic PMM thinking
- Traits/skills that distinguish exceptional product marketers
- Top authorities & practitioners (current and historical)

### Phase 2: Massive Source Discovery (Target: 500+)
- 📚 Books & guides
- 📝 Blogs & newsletters
- 🎙️ Podcasts & transcripts (Lenny's Podcast priority)
- 🎓 Courses & workshops
- 📊 Academic research, HBR, studies
- 🐦 X/Twitter threads from practitioners
- 💼 LinkedIn posts & articles
- 🎬 YouTube talks, conference presentations
- 📄 PDFs, whitepapers, case studies
- 🏢 Company examples (real positioning/messaging teardowns)

### Phase 3: Your Project Context
- Deep scan of your folder for saved sources, notes, preferences
- Extract and analyze any Lenny's podcast transcripts
- Identify patterns in what resonates with you

### Phase 4: Synthesis & Curation
- From 500+ sources → select top 100 most valuable for the guide
- Selection criteria: authority, practicality, uniqueness, depth, recency

## Research Approach

```
Explore your context → Web search (broad) → Follow citation trails →
Find experts → Mine their content → Check their recommendations →
Social media deep dives → Podcast transcript analysis →
Academic/research sources → Synthesize patterns → Curate final 100
```

---

## Selection Criteria for Final 100

1. **Authority** — Is this person/source respected in the field?
2. **Practicality** — Can you apply this immediately?
3. **Uniqueness** — Does it offer a distinct perspective?
4. **Depth** — Is it substantive or surface-level?
5. **Recency** — Is it current (or timeless)?
6. **Proof** — Are there real examples/case studies?
